**Introduction**

The 2022 Buick Encore GX is a subcompact SUV that offers a combination of style, performance, and practicality. It comes with a standard 1.2-liter turbocharged three-cylinder engine producing 137 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque, paired with a CVT; a 155-horsepower and 174 lb-ft 1.3-liter turbocharged three-cylinder is optional. It also features a 2.0-liter turbo-four engine with 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, mated to a 9-speed automatic transmission. Overall, the Encore GX is well-received for its stylish design, fuel efficiency, and comfortable interior, but some common issues have been reported.

**Engine**

**1.2-liter Turbocharged Three-Cylinder**

* **Engine Misfire**
    * Description/Symptoms: Irregular engine operation, rough idling, loss of power
    * Solution: Replacing spark plugs or ignition coils
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive oil consumption, whining or whistling noises from the engine
    * Solution: Replacing the turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.3-liter Turbocharged Three-Cylinder**

* **No Issues Found**

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **Timing Chain Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, metal shavings in the oil, loss of engine power
    * Solution: Replacing the timing chain and related components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **Transmission Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay in acceleration, loss of power
    * Solution: Replacing or rebuilding the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Excessive Body Roll**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive leaning of the vehicle during turns or cornering
    * Solution: Replacing or upgrading the suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharge, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Identifying and repairing electrical faults or replacing the battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, audio system issues
    * Solution: Updating the software, replacing the infotainment module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof Trim Peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Peeling or bubbling of the roof trim around the sunroof
    * Solution: Replacing the roof trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: Formation of rust on the vehicle's body or suspension components
    * Solution: Applying rust-resistant coatings or repairing affected areas
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of the rust
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Steering Wheel Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or creaking noises coming from the steering wheel
    * Solution: Replacing or tightening the steering wheel components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or vibration from the dashboard
    * Solution: Tightening loose screws or panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $Free - $100+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2022 Buick Encore GX has received generally positive reviews for its performance, fuel efficiency, and stylish design. However, some specific issues have been reported, particularly with the 1.2-liter turbocharged three-cylinder engine and CVT transmission. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, and it's recommended to research specific model years and consult with a qualified mechanic for more detailed information.
