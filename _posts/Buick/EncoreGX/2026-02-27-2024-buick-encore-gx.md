# 2024 Buick Encore GX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Buick Encore GX offers a stylish design and modern cabin tech, appealing to subcompact SUV buyers seeking a touch of upscale comfort. However, early owner reports highlight a series of electronic and mechanical glitches that prospective used-car buyers should inspect closely before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 1.2L Turbo I3

A 1.2-liter turbocharged inline-three engine producing around 137 horsepower, paired exclusively with a continuously variable transmission (CVT) and front-wheel drive. It offers efficient daily commuting performance, though early production models feature minor owner complaints regarding initial drivability.

**Engine Misfire and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may start with a severe misfire and subsequently shut off without displaying any check engine lights, or experience hesitations after refueling.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician diagnose the fuel system, ignition coils, and software calibration for any fault codes.

### 1.3L Turbo I3

A larger 1.3-liter turbocharged inline-three engine generating approximately 155 horsepower, available with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. Front-wheel drive versions utilize a CVT, while all-wheel-drive variants are matched with a 9-speed automatic transmission.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

A continuously variable transmission utilized on front-wheel-drive configurations designed to maximize fuel economy. Used buyers should test drive thoroughly to ensure smooth acceleration without hesitation or shuddering.

**Abnormal Shuddering and Shifting Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience unexpected shuddering during highway acceleration and find that the transmission fails to properly engage or shift into gear.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a dealer or specialist to inspect transmission fluid condition, software updates, or internal component wear.

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A traditional 9-speed automatic transmission paired exclusively with all-wheel-drive models, offering responsive gear changes and enhanced traction capability in adverse weather.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides standard stopping power, though some owners report early acoustic discomfort and aggressive pad bite at low speeds.

**Premature Squealing and Grabbing**
- **Symptoms:** Brakes emit constant squeaking noises and may feel overly grabby or exhibit occasional hesitation at slow parking speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and calipers for uneven wear, glazing, or hardware alignment issues.

## Electrical

### Instrument Panel and Infotainment Display

The vehicle relies heavily on digital displays for instrumentation, which have exhibited recurring software and screen blackout glitches in early owner experiences.

**Blank Screen or Intermittent Blackout**
- **Symptoms:** The digital instrument cluster and infotainment screen suddenly go blank while driving or upon vehicle startup, hiding critical readouts like speed and fuel level.
- **Solution:** Check for available dealer software flashes or infotainment module updates designed to resolve display instability.

### Rear-View Camera

Advanced safety and visibility aids contribute to parking ease, though electronic camera feedback failures have been noted.

**Blurry or Solid Red Backup Display**
- **Symptoms:** When shifting into reverse, the backup camera presents a heavily blurred picture or transitions to a solid red screen.
- **Solution:** Inspect the camera lens, wiring harness connection, and display module for faults or water intrusion.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V744000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Encore+GX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The software in the Virtual Cockpit Unit Module (VCU) may cause the instrument panel display to intermittently go blank at vehicle startup or while driving. A blank instrument panel will not show critical information, such as vehicle speed and warning lights, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a pre-owned 2024 Buick Encore GX, pay close attention to electronics, dashboard displays, and transmission behavior during a test drive. Verifying that all open software updates and recalls have been completed by a certified dealer will significantly reduce the risk of unexpected issues.
