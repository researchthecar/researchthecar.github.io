**Introduction to the 2020 Buick Encore GX**

The 2020 Buick Encore GX is a subcompact crossover SUV with a bold design and a comfortable interior. It comes standard with a 1.3-liter turbocharged three-cylinder engine that produces 155 horsepower and 174 lb-ft of torque. A 1.2-liter turbocharged three-cylinder engine with 137 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque is also available. Both engines are paired with a continuously variable automatic transmission (CVT).

People like the Encore GX for its stylish exterior, well-equipped interior, and comfortable ride. However, some complaints have been made about its lack of power and fuel economy.

**Engine**

**1.3-liter Turbocharged Three-Cylinder Engine**

**Engine Stalling**
Description/Symptoms: The engine stalls intermittently while driving.
Solution: Replace the engine control module (ECM) and update the software.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Automatic Transmission (CVT)**

**Hesitation and Jerking**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitation and jerking during acceleration.
Solution: Update the transmission software.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly when the vehicle is parked.
Solution: Replace the battery or alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Door Handle Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Door handles break or become loose.
Solution: Replace the door handles.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Infotainment System Glitch**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system freezes or crashes.
Solution: Update the infotainment software.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2020 Buick Encore GX is a reliable vehicle with few reported issues. The most common problems are with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. However, these issues are typically minor and can be fixed at a reasonable cost.
