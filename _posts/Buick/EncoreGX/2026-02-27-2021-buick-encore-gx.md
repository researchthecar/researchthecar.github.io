# 2021 Buick Encore GX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Buick Encore GX offers a stylish and compact crossover package with efficient powertrain options, making it a popular choice for urban and suburban commuters. However, used car buyers should carefully inspect potential purchases for a few recurring complaints, notably concerning annoying shifter-to-park warning glitches and occasional electrical or powertrain hiccups.

---

## Engine

### 1.2L Turbocharged 3-Cylinder

The base 1.2-liter turbocharged three-cylinder engine produces 137 horsepower, paired exclusively with a continuously variable transmission (CVT) and front-wheel drive. It offers adequate efficiency for daily commuting, though proper maintenance of emissions systems is vital.

**Evap System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light and trouble codes pointing toward an evaporative emissions leak or detection pump failure.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the EVAP system and replace faulty detection pumps or hoses as needed.

### 1.3L Turbocharged 3-Cylinder

The available 1.3-liter turbocharged three-cylinder engine generates 155 horsepower and 174 lb-ft of torque, providing a bit more pep than the base engine. It can be paired with front-wheel drive (via a CVT) or all-wheel drive (via a 9-speed automatic transmission).

**Limp Mode and Sensor Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden entry into limp mode, reduced engine power, and sluggish acceleration often triggered by mass air flow or LIN bus communication faults.
- **Solution:** Inspect wiring harness and sensors according to updated service bulletins, or replace faulty control modules.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The front-wheel-drive variants utilize a CVT designed to maximize fuel economy. Routine fluid checks are recommended to ensure longevity.

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

All-wheel-drive models feature a traditional 9-speed automatic transmission, offering smooth gear transitions and robust performance under varying road conditions.

**Unexpected Gear Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of forward gear engagement and transmission slipping while driving, creating a safety risk.
- **Solution:** Have a professional transmission specialist inspect internal components, valve bodies, or replace the transmission if mechanical failure is present.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake System

The braking system provides stopping power suited for a compact SUV, but owners should watch for occasional electronic control module faults and stiff pedal complaints.

**Stiff Pedal and Control Module Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal becomes excessively hard and difficult to depress, accompanied by warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Inspect the brake system control module and hydraulic booster components, replacing faulty units as necessary.

## Electrical

### Shifter Microswitch and Sensor

Electrical and cabin control electronics generally function well, though specific shifter feedback components are prone to premature wear or sensor miscommunication.

**Shift to Park Warning Message**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle displays a 'Shift to Park' message and sounds a continuous chime even when already shifted into park, sometimes preventing the car from locking or draining the battery.
- **Solution:** Replace the shifter assembly or microswitch mechanism.
- **Cost:** Approximately $1,000 out-of-pocket at a dealership

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V588000: Service Brakes](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Encore+GX&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Sensor connection contamination in the electronic brake boost system may cause an interruption of communication between the sensor and the brake boost system. If communication with the sensor is interrupted, the electronic brake boost assist could be lost.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the electronic brake boost module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V440000: Equipment:Mechanical:Accessory Scissor/Screw/Bottle Jack/Lift:Stand](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Encore+GX&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A fractured jack can cause the vehicle to collapse, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the jack, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Buick Encore GX is a comfortable, versatile subcompact SUV that appeals to daily drivers looking for modern tech and decent fuel economy. While major mechanical catastrophes are uncommon, used buyers should thoroughly test the electronic systems—specifically checking for the nagging 'Shift to Park' glitch and verifying proper engine and brake module operation before finalizing a purchase.
