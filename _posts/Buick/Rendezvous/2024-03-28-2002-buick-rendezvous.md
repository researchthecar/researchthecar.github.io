**Introduction:**
The 2002 Buick Rendezvous is a mid-size SUV that offers a blend of comfort, versatility, and performance. It is available with two engine options, a 3.4L V6 and a 3.8L V6, and three transmission options, including a 4-speed automatic, 5-speed automatic, and a CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission). Despite its overall reliability, the Rendezvous has faced some common issues reported by owners.

**Engine**
**3.4L V6**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking from the intake manifold gasket, causing overheating and coolant loss.
Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**3.8L V6**

**Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the valve cover gasket, causing oil consumption and a burning smell.
Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

**Shift Solenoid Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or refusing to shift, leading to performance issues.
Solution: Replace the shift solenoid(s).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Automatic**

**Torque Converter Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission shuddering or slipping, accompanied by a burning smell and loss of power.
Solution: Replace the torque converter.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**CVT**

**Belt or Pulley Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or whining, leading to reduced performance and potential failure.
Solution: Replace the CVT belt or pulley.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Strut Mount Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Noises and vibrations while driving, particularly over bumps or rough roads.
Solution: Replace the strut mount(s).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Discharge**
Description/Symptoms: Battery draining quickly, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
Solution: Check for electrical draw and replace battery if necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Door Handle Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Door handle breaking or becoming detached, making it difficult to open the door.
Solution: Replace the door handle.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**HVAC Fan Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Heating or air conditioning system not working properly due to a faulty fan motor.
Solution: Replace the HVAC fan motor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
While the 2002 Buick Rendezvous offers a comfortable and versatile driving experience, it has faced some common issues that owners should be aware of. The most severe issues include transmission failure, which can be costly to repair, and electrical problems that can impact the vehicle's reliability. Overall, the Rendezvous has received mixed reviews, with owners praising its spacious interior and smooth ride, but expressing concerns about its reliability and fuel efficiency.
