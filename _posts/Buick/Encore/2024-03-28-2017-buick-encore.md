**2017 Buick Encore Overview**

The 2017 Buick Encore is a compact SUV that is available with a choice of two engines and two transmissions:

* **Engine:**
    * 1.4L turbocharged four-cylinder (138 horsepower, 148 lb-ft of torque)
    * 2.5L four-cylinder (182 horsepower, 172 lb-ft of torque)
* **Transmission:**
    * Six-speed automatic
    * CVT

The Encore is praised for its stylish exterior and comfortable interior. However, some owners have reported issues with various components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**1.4L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, stalling, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Boost Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whistling noise from engine bay
    * Solution: Repair or replace turbocharger boost hose
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5L Four-Cylinder**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough shifting, hesitation, slipping gears
    * Solution: Fluid flush, replace solenoid pack
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Complete loss of power, no gears engaging
    * Solution: Replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**CVT**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise over bumps, reduced ride quality
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive bouncing, instability when driving
    * Solution: Replace rear shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead prematurely, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Diagnose electrical system, repair or replace faulty component
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Harness Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical problems, such as lights flickering or radio malfunctioning
    * Solution: Repair or replace electrical harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Small cracks appear on windshield
    * Solution: Replace windshield
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Missing or Broken Body Panel Clips:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Body panels rattle or are loose
    * Solution: Replace missing or broken clips
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Latch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Latch breaks or becomes loose, causing center console to open while driving
    * Solution: Replace center console latch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Heater Core Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant smell inside cabin, fogging windows
    * Solution: Replace heater core
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Buick Encore has a mix of positive and negative reviews. While it is praised for its stylish design and comfortable cabin, it also has several reported issues. The severity of the issues varies, with some being relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be quite costly. Potential buyers should carefully consider these issues before purchasing an Encore.
