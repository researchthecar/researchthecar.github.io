# 2017 Buick Encore Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Buick Encore is a subcompact luxury crossover that offers a quiet cabin and easy maneuverability, making it popular for urban driving. However, used car buyers should carefully evaluate its mechanical history, as owner reports highlight potential concerns regarding turbocharger performance, electrical reliability, and braking behavior.

---

## Engine

### 1.4L Turbocharged I4

The 1.4-liter turbocharged inline-four engine produces around 138 to 153 horsepower depending on the specific tune, providing adequate zip for city driving but drawing occasional owner complaints regarding turbo and stalling issues.

**Engine Stalling and Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles may experience sudden engine stalling, a loss of motive power, and entry into limp mode accompanied by smoke or diminished acceleration.
- **Solution:** Inspect the turbocharger assembly and associated intake components; replace faulty turbo units or control valves as necessary.

**Engine Misfire**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light, rough idling, and diagnostic trouble codes indicating cylinder misfires.
- **Solution:** Perform diagnostic testing to identify failing ignition coils, spark plugs, or fuel delivery components and replace affected parts.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional six-speed automatic transmission is paired with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, generally offering smooth gear changes though some drivers have reported roll-back tendencies on inclines.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system generally provides standard stopping power, but periodic warnings and momentary pedal resistance issues have been noted by owners.

**Brake Assist and Pedal Response Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of the brake warning light accompanied by a temporary lack of responsiveness or firm pedal feel when applying brakes.
- **Solution:** Inspect the braking system sensors, brake booster, and hydraulic lines; replace malfunctioning components or sensors.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Power Steering

Steering provides light and agile handling for tight spaces, though cold-weather power assist issues and column adjustments warrant inspection during a pre-purchase test drive.

**Intermittent Power Steering Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Stiff steering wheel operation, particularly in colder ambient temperatures, or a tilt steering column lever failing to stay locked in position.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electric power steering motor and steering column locking mechanism, replacing worn components or the rack and pinion assembly if required.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Occupant safety features include standard airbags and seat belts, though proper latch and retractor operation should be verified on any used model.

**Seat Belt Retractor and Latch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Front driver or passenger seat belts failing to retract properly, latch securely, or work as intended during normal use.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt assemblies and latch mechanisms; replace faulty seat belt units under applicable warranty or safety coverage.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V651000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Encore&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test. During this test, deployment of the frontal air bags and the seat belt pretensioners would not occur in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V437000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Encore&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** While being previously remedied for recall 16V-651, the affected vehicles may not have received the complete software update necessary to remedy the recall condition. Without the update, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2017 Buick Encore, prospective buyers should prioritize a thorough inspection of the turbocharger system, electrical accessories, and steering functionality. While its compact size and comfortable ride make it an appealing daily driver, verifying service records and conducting a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection can help mitigate potential repair risks.
