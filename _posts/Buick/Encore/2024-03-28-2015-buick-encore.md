# 2015 Buick Encore Summary

The 2015 Buick Encore is a compact crossover SUV that offers a comfortable ride and a spacious interior. It is available with a choice of two engines: a 1.4-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 138 horsepower and 148 lb-ft of torque, or a 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 182 horsepower and 172 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a six-speed automatic transmission.

The Encore has received generally positive reviews from critics, who praise its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and good fuel economy. However, some critics have complained about its lack of power and its uninspired interior design.

**Engine**

**1.4-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:** Description/Symptoms: The engine may consume an excessive amount of oil, which can lead to low oil levels and engine damage. Solution: The engine may need to be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+ Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Failure:** Description/Symptoms: The timing chain may fail, which can cause the engine to stop running. Solution: The timing chain will need to be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+ Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 Buick Encore is a generally reliable vehicle with a few potential issues. The most serious issue is the excessive oil consumption that can affect the 1.4-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine. The other issues are relatively minor and can be fixed for a reasonable cost. Overall, the Encore is a good choice for consumers who are looking for a compact crossover SUV that is comfortable, spacious, and fuel-efficient.
