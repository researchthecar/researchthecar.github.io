# 2022 Buick Encore Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Buick Encore, buyers will find a compact crossover powered by a single standard engine option paired with an automatic transmission. While overall owner feedback is relatively limited, prospective buyers should review available reliability reports and inspect the vehicle thoroughly during a test drive to ensure all powertrain and safety systems operate smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 1.4L Turbocharged I4

The 2022 Buick Encore comes standard with a 1.4-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing around 155 horsepower, offering punchy low-end torque for city commuting. Overall, this powerplant is generally conventional, though owners have reported occasional electronic management and emissions-related hiccups.

**Engine Power Loss and Drivability Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report that the vehicle may suddenly lose power while driving and lunge forward violently, accompanied by check engine lights and diagnostic trouble codes.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose stored engine management codes and inspect the electronic control systems or related emission components.

**Evaporative Emissions System Codes**
- **Symptoms:** The check engine light may illuminate on the highway, triggering specific diagnostic trouble codes related to the EVAP purge system.
- **Solution:** Inspect the evaporative emissions components, purge pump, and associated lines for proper operation.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 6-speed automatic transmission channels power to either the front wheels or all four wheels, delivering smooth everyday gear changes. It typically exhibits standard durability under normal driving conditions.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety equipment generally performs as expected, though owners should physically inspect interior components like seat belts for abnormal wear.

**Frayed Seat Belts**
- **Symptoms:** Front or rear seat belt webbing may show premature signs of fraying and unraveling at relatively low mileage.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt webbing during a pre-purchase examination and replace any compromised assemblies immediately.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Buick Encore is a tidy subcompact crossover that serves well as an urban commuter, provided it passes a thorough mechanical inspection. Used car buyers should pay close attention to engine drivability, monitor for any active check engine lights, and verify that all safety belts and cabin features are in pristine condition before finalizing a purchase.
