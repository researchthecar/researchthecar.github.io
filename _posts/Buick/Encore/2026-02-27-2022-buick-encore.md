# 2022 Buick Encore Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Buick Encore, buyers should be aware of a few notable owner-reported issues, particularly concerning gear selection and power delivery. While many drivers enjoy its compact size and fuel economy, reviewing common complaints can help you spot potential problems during a test drive and determine if a specific vehicle has been well-maintained.

---

## Engine

### 1.4L Turbocharged I4

The 1.4L turbocharged four-cylinder engine provides efficient everyday performance for city commuting. Buyers should listen for unusual noises and verify smooth acceleration during a test drive.

**Sudden Loss of Power**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle loses power unexpectedly while driving, causing it to lurch forward and illuminating the check engine light.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the engine management computer (EMC) and related sensors for fault codes and update or replace faulty components.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission generally delivers smooth shifts, though owners have reported persistent electronic gear-selector communication glitches.

**Shift to Park Warning and Electrical Drain**
- **Symptoms:** When shifting into park and turning off the vehicle, the dashboard displays a 'Shift to Park' warning, preventing the car from fully shutting off and draining the battery.
- **Solution:** Inspect the shifter assembly and transmission range sensor, and replace faulty switches or wiring connectors as needed.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

**Frayed Seat Belts**
- **Symptoms:** Front or rear seat belt webbing exhibits premature fraying and unraveling.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt assemblies for wear and replace any damaged restraint components immediately.

### Rear Seats

**Missing LATCH Anchors**
- **Symptoms:** Lower LATCH child safety seat anchors cannot be found or are missing despite being referenced in the owner's manual.
- **Solution:** Verify the physical presence of lower anchors in the rear seats prior to attempting to install child safety seats.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Buick Encore offers a maneuverable and comfortable ride, but potential buyers should thoroughly check the vehicle's electrical systems and gear selector functionality. Pay close attention to the shifter during your test drive to ensure the vehicle correctly recognizes the 'Park' position and shuts down properly without accessory drain.
