**Introduction**

The 2004 Smart Cabrio is a compact convertible with a unique design and efficient fuel economy. It is powered by a 0.7-liter turbocharged gasoline engine, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The Cabriolet is praised for its agility, handling, and low operating costs, but some drivers have reported reliability issues.

**Engine**

**0.7L Turbocharged Gasoline Engine**

* **Cylinder Head Warping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Warping of the cylinder head can cause coolant leaks, engine overheating, and performance issues.
    * Solution: Replacement of the cylinder head.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the timing chain can lead to engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Faulty solenoids can cause shifting problems, such as delayed or hard shifting.
    * Solution: Replacement of the solenoids.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Control Arm Bushings Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn control arm bushings can lead to excessive vibration, noise, and poor handling.
    * Solution: Replacement of the control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the alternator can lead to loss of electrical power and battery discharge.
    * Solution: Replacement of the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the starter solenoid can prevent the engine from starting.
    * Solution: Replacement of the starter solenoid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Convertible Top Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Problems with the convertible top may include leaks, tears, or difficulties opening or closing.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of the convertible top.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Panel Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard panel may crack over time, causing cosmetic issues.
    * Solution: Replacement of the dashboard panel.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the power window motor can prevent the window from being opened or closed.
    * Solution: Replacement of the power window motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2004 Smart Cabrio is a fun and efficient vehicle, but it has some reliability issues that owners should be aware of. The most common issues include engine problems, transmission issues, and electrical issues. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, so it's important to have the vehicle regularly inspected and maintained to prevent major problems.
