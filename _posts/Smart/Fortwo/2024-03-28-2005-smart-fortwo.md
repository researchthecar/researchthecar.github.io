**Introduction:**

The 2005 Smart Fortwo is a compact, two-seat city car known for its exceptional fuel economy and maneuverability. It was offered with a single engine option and a choice of two transmissions: a 5-speed manual or a 6-speed automated manual.

**Engine:**

* **0.8L 3-Cylinder Gasoline Engine**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission:**

* **5-Speed Manual**
    * **Shift Linkage Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty or inability to shift gears, grinding noises.
        * Solution: Replace the shift linkage.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **6-Speed Automated Manual**
    * **Clutch Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clutch slipping, difficulty engaging gears.
        * Solution: Replace the clutch.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Rear Suspension Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, reduced handling stability.
    * Solution: Replace the rear suspension springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Front Strut Mount Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or grinding noises from the front of the vehicle, poor handling.
    * Solution: Replace the front strut mount bearing.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery warning light illuminated, electrical system problems.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine fails to start, clicking or grinding noises when trying to start.
    * Solution: Replace the starter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Plastic Trim Panel Damage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Broken or damaged plastic trim panels on the exterior of the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the damaged trim panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Interior Door Handle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty or inability to open the door from the inside.
    * Solution: Replace the interior door handle.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2005 Smart Fortwo has a few common issues, primarily related to the transmission, suspension, and electrical system. The severity of the issues ranges from minor inconveniences to more costly repairs. Overall, the Fortwo is generally a reliable and economical vehicle, but it is important to be aware of these potential problems.
