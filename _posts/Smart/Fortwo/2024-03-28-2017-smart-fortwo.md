**Introduction**

The 2017 Smart Fortwo is a compact city car with a rear-mounted engine. It is available with a 1.0L gasoline engine and a 5-speed manual transmission or a 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The electric version of the Smart Fortwo, known as the Fortwo Electric Drive, is powered by an electric motor and a single-speed transmission.

People like the Smart Fortwo for its maneuverability, fuel efficiency, and affordability. However, it is not without its problems. Some of the most common issues reported by owners include:

**Engine**

* **1.0L Gasoline Engine**
    * **Faulty intake manifold:** Can cause rough idling, stalling, and loss of power
    **Solution:** Replace intake manifold
    **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
    **Recalls:** None Found
* **6-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shifting problems:** Can cause difficulty shifting gears or slipping out of gear
    **Solution:** Replace transmission control module or clutch pack
    **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500
    **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **No issues found**

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension:** Can cause clunking or knocking noises
* **Solution:** Replace lower control arms or bushings
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery:** Can fail prematurely
* **Solution:** Replace battery
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield:** Can crack or chip easily
* **Solution:** Replace windshield
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Steering wheel:** Can become loose or rattle
* **Solution:** Tighten or replace steering wheel bolts
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Smart Fortwo is a reliable and affordable city car. However, it is not without its problems. The most common issues include engine and transmission problems, as well as suspension, electrical, and exterior issues. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they can all be repaired by a qualified mechanic.
