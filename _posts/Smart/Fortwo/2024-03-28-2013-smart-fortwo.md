**2013 Smart Fortwo**

The 2013 Smart Fortwo is a two-seater microcar known for its compact size and fuel efficiency. It comes with a single engine option and two transmission options:

- 1.0L 3-cylinder gasoline engine (70 horsepower, 68 lb-ft of torque)
- 5-speed automated manual transmission
- 6-speed dual-clutch transmission (DCT)

**Engine**

* **1.0L 3-cylinder gasoline engine**
    - **Oil leaks:** Seals may fail, leading to oil leaks.
       - Replace seals
       - $200 - $400+
       - Recall: None Found
    - **Timing chain issues:** The timing chain may stretch or break, causing engine damage.
       - Replace timing chain
       - $1,000 - $2,000+
       - Recall: None Found
    - **Misfire:** Ignition coils or spark plugs may fail, causing misfires.
       - Replace ignition coils or spark plugs
       - $100 - $300 per cylinder
       - Recall: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-speed automated manual transmission**
    - **Clutch problems:** The clutch may slip or wear out prematurely.
       - Replace clutch
       - $500 - $800+
       - Recall: None Found
    - **Shifting issues:** The transmission may struggle to shift gears smoothly.
       - Reset transmission control module
       - Free
       - Recall: None Found
* **6-speed dual-clutch transmission (DCT)**
    - **Jerking or hesitation during shifts:** The DCT may experience jerky or hesitant shifts.
       - Software update or clutch replacement
       - $500 - $1,000
       - Recall: N160917000

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

       - Replace battery
       - $100 - $200
       - Recall: None Found
* **Alternator issues:** The alternator may fail, leading to electrical system problems.
       - Replace alternator
       - $500 - $700+
       - Recall: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Door lock issues:** The door locks may malfunction, making it difficult to open or close the doors.
       - Replace door lock actuators
       - $200 - $400+
       - Recall: None Found
* **Window regulator issues:** The window regulators may fail, causing the windows to become stuck or inoperable.
       - Replace window regulators
       - $300 - $500 per window
       - Recall: None Found
* **Air conditioning issues:** The air conditioning system may malfunction, leading to poor cooling or heating.
       - Recharge or repair AC system
       - $100 - $500+
       - Recall: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2013 Smart Fortwo has some common issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. However, the issues are not widespread and the repairs are generally affordable. Owners of this vehicle enjoy its compact size and fuel efficiency, but they may experience occasional mechanical problems.
