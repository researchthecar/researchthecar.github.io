**Introduction:**

The 2009 Smart Fortwo is a compact city car renowned for its small size, maneuverability, and fuel efficiency. It is offered with a single engine option, paired with either a 5-speed automated manual transmission or a 6-speed manual transmission.

## **Engine:** 1.0L I3 Gasoline

- **Timing Chain Tensioner Issue**
  - Description/Symptoms: Premature wear or failure of the timing chain tensioner can cause a rattling noise during engine operation and potentially lead to engine damage.
  - Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and timing chain.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Transmission:**

### **5-Speed Automated Manual**

- **Shifting Problems**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty or hesitation when shifting gears, transmission slipping or shuddering.
  - Solution: Replacement or repair of the transmission control module or valve body.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

### **6-Speed Manual**

- **Clutch Pedal Binding**
  - Description/Symptoms: Clutch pedal becomes difficult to depress or release smoothly, causing difficulty in gear changes.
  - Solution: Replacement or adjustment of the clutch master cylinder or slave cylinder.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Suspension**

- **Front Shock Absorber Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged front shock absorbers lead to poor ride quality, reduced handling, and potential tire wear.
  - Solution: Replacement of the shock absorbers.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Electrical**

- **Battery Drain**
  - Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly even when the vehicle is not in use, causing difficulty starting.
  - Solution: Diagnosis and repair of faulty electrical components or modules.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Exterior**

- **Headlight Condensation**
  - Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlight assemblies, impairing visibility.
  - Solution: Replacement of the headlight assemblies or repair of seals.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Interior**

- **Dashboard Rattle**
  - Description/Symptoms: Rattling or vibration from the dashboard during driving, particularly on rough roads.
  - Solution: Identification and tightening of loose components or installation of sound dampening material.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Other**

- **Fuel Pump Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power or engine stalling while driving due to a malfunctioning fuel pump.
  - Solution: Replacement of the fuel pump.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2009 Smart Fortwo has some notable issues, particularly with its engine and transmission components. The expenses associated with these issues can be substantial, but the compact size and fuel efficiency of the vehicle may still appeal to some buyers.
