**Introduction:**

The 2008 Smart Fortwo is a subcompact city car available with a single engine and transmission option. The 1.0-liter gasoline engine generates 70 horsepower and 68 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed automated manual transmission. Drivers appreciate its fuel efficiency, maneuverability, and affordable price, but criticize its cramped interior, limited cargo space, and sluggish performance.

**Engine**

* **1.0L Gasoline Engine**
    * **Head Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, hard starting, rough idle
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Automated Manual Transmission**
    * **Shifting Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, delayed shifts, grinding noises
        * Solution: Adjust or replace shift actuator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system not charging, battery not holding a charge
        * Solution: Replace alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: Doors not locking or unlocking, key not turning in lock
        * Solution: Replace door lock actuator or key cylinder
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Window Regulator Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Windows not moving up or down smoothly or being trapped in the closed position
        * Solution: Replace window regulator motor
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Dashboard Rattle**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noisy rattling coming from the dashboard
        * Solution: Tighten or replace loose screws or panels
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2008 Smart Fortwo is known for its fuel efficiency and affordability, it has a history of issues with its engine, transmission, and electrical system. These problems range in severity from minor annoyances to potential engine damage. The estimated cost of repairs can be significant, especially for more serious issues such as head gasket failure or alternator failure.
