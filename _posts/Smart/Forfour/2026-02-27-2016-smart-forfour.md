### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Smart Forfour generally demonstrates good reliability, especially concerning its powertrain options. The naturally aspirated and turbocharged petrol engines are robust, with no significant widespread issues reported. The manual transmission is also dependable. The automatic dual-clutch transmission, while convenient, can occasionally exhibit jerky shifts, which may require minor attention. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and potential battery drain, are the most commonly cited issues, though often resolvable with software updates or basic checks. Exterior and interior components are typically durable for the vehicle's intended use. Overall, the Forfour is a reliable choice for city driving, provided potential minor electrical quirks are addressed.
