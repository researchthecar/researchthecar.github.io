The 2016 Smart Forfour is a quirky and compact city car known for its maneuverability and fuel efficiency. It comes with several engine options, including a 1.0L three-cylinder engine producing around 70 horsepower and 67 lb-ft of torque and a turbocharged 0.9L three-cylinder engine producing around 90 horsepower and 100 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a five-speed manual and a six-speed dual-clutch automatic. People generally appreciate the Forfour's ease of parking in tight spaces and its distinctive styling, while common complaints revolve around its limited cargo space, relatively underpowered engines, and somewhat firm ride.

**Suspension**

*   **Rear Shock Absorbers Failing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Excessive bouncing, reduced ride comfort, and poor handling, especially over bumps.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the rear shock absorbers.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Central Locking System Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Doors not locking or unlocking properly, or only locking/unlocking intermittently. Can affect all doors or just a single door.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis and repair/replacement of the central locking module or door lock actuators.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $450+ depending on component.
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Battery Drain**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The battery dies prematurely, especially if the car sits for a few days. This can be caused by parasitic draw from various systems.
    *   Solution: Diagnose and eliminate the source of the parasitic draw. This may involve checking the car's systems one by one, and can be difficult to resolve.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Panoramic Roof Issues (If Equipped)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Water leaks around the panoramic roof seal, difficulty opening or closing the sunshade, or motor failure for the sunshade.
    *   Solution: Resealing the panoramic roof, repairing or replacing the sunshade motor or mechanism.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $800+ depending on the specific issue.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Dashboard Rattles**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rattling noises emanating from the dashboard area, especially on rough roads.
    *   Solution: Locating and securing the source of the rattle. This might involve adding foam padding or tightening loose screws.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+ depending on complexity.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Smart Forfour appears to have some issues, particularly regarding the suspension components. The rear shock absorber failures can impact ride quality and handling, while the electrical issues can cause various inconveniences. The exterior and interior issues are less severe but can still be annoying. Overall, the cost of repairs varies depending on the specific problem.
