**Introduction:**

The 2016 Smart Forfour is a compact, four-door hatchback available with a single 1.0-liter three-cylinder engine and a five-speed manual or six-speed dual-clutch transmission. Praised for its fuel efficiency, maneuverability, and affordability, the Forfour has received criticism for its limited power, cramped interior, and lack of certain features.

**Engine**

**1.0-Liter Three-Cylinder**

* **Engine Misfiring:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Irregular engine operation, loss of power, rough idling
    * Solution: Replace faulty spark plugs or ignition coils
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, reduced engine power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
* **Oil Leak from Oil Filter Housing:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from oil filter housing area
    * Solution: Replace oil filter housing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    
**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual**

* **Difficulty Shifting Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard to shift into or out of gears
    * Solution: Adjust or replace shift linkage, or repair internal transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1000+

**Six-Speed Dual-Clutch**

* **Transmission Clutch Pack Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, shuddering during acceleration, difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace clutch pack
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+
    
**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly even when the car is parked
    * Solution: Inspect electrical system for parasitic draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Faulty Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) Sensor:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inaccurate or intermittent tire pressure readings
    * Solution: Replace faulty sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Airbag Control Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Airbag warning light illuminated, airbags may not deploy in a collision
    * Solution: Replace airbag control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
* **Faulty Power Window Switches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows malfunctioning or not responding
    * Solution: Replace faulty switches
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2016 Smart Forfour has relatively few reported issues. The most serious and expensive issue is the transmission clutch pack failure, although this primarily affects the dual-clutch transmission. Most other issues are relatively minor and affordable to fix. However, the Forfour's cramped interior and limited power may be significant drawbacks for some drivers.
