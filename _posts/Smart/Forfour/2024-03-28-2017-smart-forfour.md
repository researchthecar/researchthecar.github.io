**Introduction to the 2017 Smart Forfour**

The 2017 Smart Forfour is a subcompact hatchback available with two engine options: a 1.0-liter turbocharged three-cylinder and a 1.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder. Both engines are paired with a six-speed manual or an optional seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The Smart Forfour is praised for its maneuverability, fuel efficiency, and compact size, while some criticisms include its limited rear-seat space and lack of available features.
