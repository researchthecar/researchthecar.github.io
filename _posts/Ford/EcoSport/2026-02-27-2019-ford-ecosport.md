# 2019 Ford EcoSport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Ford EcoSport, buyers will find a subcompact crossover designed primarily for city driving, offering nimble handling and an elevated seating position. However, prospective owners should review its mechanical history closely, as owner complaints and service records highlight several recurring trouble spots ranging from powertrain hiccups to electrical glitches.

---

## Engine

### 1.0L EcoBoost Turbocharged 3-Cylinder

The 1.0-liter turbocharged three-cylinder engine produces approximately 123 horsepower and is paired exclusively with front-wheel drive. While fuel-efficient for daily commuting, this engine has accumulated notable complaints regarding drivability disruptions and unexpected power loss.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles may unexpectedly lose power while driving at highway or city speeds, occasionally triggering a wrench icon, reduced engine power mode, or complete stalling.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fuel system components, oil pressure sensors, or associated powertrain recalls, and replace failing parts as required.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden engine failure or inability to start, often linked to internal fuel pump performance issues.
- **Solution:** Replace the defective fuel pump assembly.

### 2.0L Ti-VCT 4-Cylinder

The larger 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine generates around 166 horsepower and comes paired with standard intelligent four-wheel drive. It offers more robust highway performance than the base turbo three-cylinder.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The traditional 6-speed automatic transmission is utilized across EcoSport models to deliver power smoothly to the wheels, though some drivers experience hesitation or rough shifting behavior.

**Abnormal Shifting and Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may jerk, hesitate, or shift harshly when the driver depresses the accelerator pedal at various speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission control modules, update software, or repair internal mechanical components if wear is detected.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Accessory Electronics

Electrical and infotainment complaints frequently center around failing display modules and unresponsive cabin electronics.

**APIM and Display Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen display may go completely dark, reboot continuously, lose Bluetooth connectivity, or freeze, disabling the backup camera and radio functions.
- **Solution:** Reset, update, or replace the Accessory Protocol Interface Module (APIM).

## Exterior

### Tailgate Latch and Swing Gate

Cabin hardware is generally straightforward, though the side-hinged rear tailgate latch is a known trouble spot for false warnings and latching difficulties.

**Tailgate Ajar Warning and Inadvertent Opening**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard warning lights may falsely indicate that the rear swing gate is open, or the latch may fail to secure the door tightly while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect, adjust, or replace the rear latch mechanism and door sensors.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V023000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=EcoSport&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 207, "Seating Systems," and 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages." A loose seat back can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Ford has notified owners, and dealers will replace the front seat assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V905000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication:Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=EcoSport&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A loss of oil pressure can result in an engine stall and a loss of power braking assist, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the oil pump tensioner assembly and oil pump drive belt, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Ford EcoSport is an approachable subcompact crossover for buyers seeking an elevated driving position and modern features in a tidy footprint. However, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect service histories, test for smooth transmission operation, and check that all safety recalls and infotainment functions are fully addressed before purchasing.
