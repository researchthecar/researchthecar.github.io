# 2022 Ford EcoSport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Ford EcoSport represents the final model year for this subcompact crossover in North America, offering a high-riding seating position and nimble city dimensions. Buyers exploring the used market should review specific owner-reported concerns across powertrain options and cabin safety features to ensure a sound purchase.

---

## Engine

### 1.0L EcoBoost Turbo Inline-3

The 1.0-liter turbocharged three-cylinder engine produces approximately 123 horsepower and is paired exclusively with all-wheel drive. While efficient for urban commuting, owners should monitor for potential exhaust and power delivery issues.

**Cracked Catalytic Converter**
- **Symptoms:** The check engine warning light illuminates while driving due to a cracked catalytic converter.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a qualified technician and replace the damaged catalytic converter as necessary.

**Intermittent Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle frequently experiences unpredictable losses of power during acceleration or when shifting out of driveways.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic testing by a professional is recommended to isolate fuel delivery, turbocharger, or sensor faults.

### 2.0L Ti-VCT Inline-4

The naturally aspirated 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine generates around 166 horsepower and provides standard all-wheel drive capability. It offers straightforward mechanical operation compared to the smaller turbo option, though overall performance remains modest.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed SelectShift Automatic

The conventional 6-speed automatic transmission is paired with both engine options across the EcoSport lineup. It generally offers more traditional shifting behavior than dual-clutch units, but buyers should verify service history and check for any open manufacturer campaigns.

## Exterior

### Body Structure and Trim

Exterior body components require attention to ensure weather seals and trim remain properly secured.

**Detaching Roof Rail Trim**
- **Symptoms:** Exterior roof rail trim can loosen and detach from the vehicle while driving, creating a road hazard.
- **Solution:** Inspect roof rail trim clips and fasteners, and replace or re-secure loose components.

**Swing Gate Ajar Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The rear swing gate ajar warning light illuminates unexpectedly while the rear door unlatches slightly during transit.
- **Solution:** Adjust or replace the swing gate latch mechanism and door sensor to ensure a secure seal.

### Exterior Lighting

Lighting hardware components can experience mounting fatigue over time.

**License Plate Lights Detaching**
- **Symptoms:** The rear license plate light assemblies repeatedly loosen and fall out of position.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged mounting clips or housing units to securely retain the license plate lights.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Air bag system complaints focus on warning indicators and isolated deployment concerns.

**Restraints Indicator Warning and Non-Deployment**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported the restraints indicator warning lamp illuminating unexpectedly, or side air bags failing to deploy during an impact.
- **Solution:** Have the supplemental restraint system scanned for diagnostic trouble codes and inspect all associated wiring and sensors.

### Seat Belts

Rear seatbelt functionality is critical for passenger safety and should be thoroughly checked during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Rear Seatbelt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rear seatbelts may fail to function properly, leaving functional restraints unavailable for passengers.
- **Solution:** Inspect all seatbelt assemblies and replace faulty retractors or latch mechanisms immediately.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V905000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication:Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=EcoSport&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of oil pressure can result in an engine stall and a loss of power braking assist, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the oil pump tensioner assembly and oil pump drive belt, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V126000: Power Train:Axle Assembly:Axle Shaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=EcoSport&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash. In addition, a disconnected half shaft can result in a vehicle rollaway if the parking brake is not applied, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the half shafts as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Ford EcoSport provides compact dimensions and decent feature content for daily driving, but used-car shoppers should perform a thorough inspection. Pay close attention to powertrain operation, rear seatbelt functionality, exterior trim condition, and any warning lights on the dashboard before finalizing a purchase.
