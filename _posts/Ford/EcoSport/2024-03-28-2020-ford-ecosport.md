**2020 Ford EcoSport**

The 2020 Ford EcoSport is a subcompact SUV that offers a choice of two engine options: a 1.0-liter EcoBoost three-cylinder and a 2.0-liter four-cylinder. The 1.0-liter engine produces 123 horsepower and 125 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.0-liter engine produces 166 horsepower and 149 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a six-speed manual or a six-speed automatic.

The EcoSport is praised for its fuel efficiency, affordability, and spacious interior. However, it has also received criticism for its lack of power, cramped rear seat, and unrefined ride quality.

### **Engine**
- 1.0-liter EcoBoost three-cylinder

  - **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to skip or break, resulting in catastrophic engine damage. Symptoms include a rattling noise from the engine, reduced power, and a check engine light.
    - Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and timing chain.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    - Recalls: None Found

### **Transmission**
- Six-speed Automatic

  - **Transmission Shudder**
    - Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shudder or vibrate when shifting gears, especially at low speeds.
    - Solution: Flush the transmission fluid and replace the filter. In some cases, the transmission may need to be rebuilt or replaced.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

### **Suspension**
- No Issues Found

### **Electrical**
- No Issues Found

### **Exterior**
- **Tailgate Latch Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: The tailgate latch can fail, causing the tailgate to open unexpectedly while driving.
    - Solution: Replace the tailgate latch.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    - Recalls: 20V-201

### **Interior**
- **Climate Control Malfunction**
    - Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, causing the temperature to fluctuate or the air to not blow properly.
    - Solution: Replace the climate control module or actuator.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    - Recalls: None Found

### **Other**
- **Fuel Pump Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: The fuel pump can fail, causing the engine to stall or run poorly. Symptoms include difficulty starting the engine, reduced power, and a check engine light.
    - Solution: Replace the fuel pump.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 2020 Ford EcoSport has a limited number of reported issues, with the most severe being the timing chain tensioner failure and transmission shudder. However, the estimated cost to fix these issues can be significant. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before purchasing an EcoSport.
