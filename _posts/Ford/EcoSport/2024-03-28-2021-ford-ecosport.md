**2021 Ford EcoSport**

The 2021 Ford EcoSport is a subcompact SUV that offers a spacious interior, versatile cargo space, and a fuel-efficient engine. It is available with two engine options: a 1.0-liter EcoBoost three-cylinder and a 2.0-liter four-cylinder. Both engines are paired with a six-speed automatic transmission.

Owners of the 2021 EcoSport have reported various issues, but none of them are considered widespread or severe. Some drivers have experienced minor engine problems, while others have encountered occasional transmission issues. The suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components have generally been reliable.

**Engine**

* **1.0-Liter EcoBoost Three-Cylinder**

  * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil may leak from the valve cover gasket, causing a drop in oil levels and potentially leading to engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **2.0-Liter Four-Cylinder**

  * **No Major Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Automatic**

  * **Shifting Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or jerk when shifting gears, especially during acceleration or deceleration.
    * Solution: Update the transmission control module (TCM) software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $100 (depending on labor costs)
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Major Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, requiring multiple jump-starts or replacement.
    * Solution: Inspect the battery connections and charge or replace the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+ (depending on battery type)
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door lock actuator may fail, preventing the door from locking or unlocking properly.
    * Solution: Replace the door lock actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may rattle while driving, especially over rough roads.
    * Solution: Add insulation or padding to the affected area.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Ford EcoSport is generally a reliable vehicle with few major issues reported by owners. The engine and transmission problems are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix. The suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components have also been mostly trouble-free. Overall, the EcoSport offers a good value for its price point and is a suitable option for those seeking a compact SUV with a spacious interior and fuel efficiency.
