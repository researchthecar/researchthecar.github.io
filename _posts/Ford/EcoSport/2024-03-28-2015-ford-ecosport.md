**Introduction**

The 2015 Ford EcoSport is a subcompact SUV that offers a 2.0-liter Ti-VCT engine with a 6-speed SelectShift automatic transmission. It produces 160 horsepower and 149 lb-ft of torque. The EcoSport is praised for its compact size, affordable pricing, and spacious interior, but has been criticized for its lackluster performance and poor fuel economy.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter Ti-VCT**

* **Engine misfiring**
Description/Symptoms: The engine will misfire under acceleration, causing a loss of power and rough idling.
Solution: Replace spark plugs and ignition coils.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
Recalls: NHTSA 15V467000

* **Intake manifold gasket failure**
Description/Symptoms: A faulty intake manifold gasket can cause a loss of coolant and a check engine light.
Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-speed SelectShift Automatic**

* **Transmission shudder**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission will shudder during gear shifts, especially when the vehicle is cold.
Solution: Update transmission control software.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $100
Recalls: None Found

* **Premature transmission failure**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may fail prematurely, causing the vehicle to lose all forward gears.
Solution: Replace transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut mount bearing failure**
Description/Symptoms: The strut mount bearings may wear out prematurely, causing a clunking noise and loss of suspension stability.
Solution: Replace strut mount bearings.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

* **Premature rear axle bearing failure**
Description/Symptoms: The rear axle bearings may wear out prematurely, causing a whining noise and loss of stability.
Solution: Replace rear axle bearings.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to lose power.
Solution: Replace battery and inspect electrical system for parasitic draw.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

* **Headlight switch failure**
Description/Symptoms: The headlight switch may fail, causing the headlights to not work.
Solution: Replace headlight switch.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear liftgate latch failure**
Description/Symptoms: The rear liftgate latch may fail, causing the liftgate to not latch properly.
Solution: Replace rear liftgate latch.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: NHTSA 17V459000

* **Side mirror glass detachment**
Description/Symptoms: The side mirror glass may detach from the mirror housing.
Solution: Replace side mirror glass.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattles**
Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may rattle while driving.
Solution: Inspect and tighten loose panels or components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $100

* **Audio system glitches**
Description/Symptoms: The audio system may experience glitches or freezes.
Solution: Update audio system software or replace audio unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Summary**

The 2015 Ford EcoSport has several potential issues that range from minor annoyances to serious mechanical failures. The most common issues include engine misfiring, transmission shudder, suspension problems, and electrical issues. The severity of these issues can vary, but some can be expensive to repair and lead to a significant decrease in the vehicle's value.
