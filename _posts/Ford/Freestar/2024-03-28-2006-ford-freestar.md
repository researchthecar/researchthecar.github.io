**Introduction**

The 2006 Ford Freestar is a minivan that was offered with three engine options: a 3.9L V6, a 4.2L V6, and a 4.6L V8. Transmission options included a 4-speed automatic and a 5-speed automatic. The 3.9L V6 produced 193 horsepower and 265 lb-ft of torque, while the 4.2L V6 produced 201 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque. The 4.6L V8 produced 231 horsepower and 292 lb-ft of torque. The Freestar was praised for its spacious interior and comfortable ride, but criticized for its lack of power and poor fuel economy.

**Engine**

**3.9L V6**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Timing chain tensioner failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from engine, reduced engine power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner and chain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: N/A

**4.2L V6**

* **No major issues found**

**4.6L V8**

* **Spark plug blowout**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfiring, rough idle, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs and inspect threads for damage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Intake manifold runner control valve failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold runner control valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A
