**Introduction:**
The 1996 Ford Contour was a mid-size sedan offered with two engine options: a 2.0L DOHC 4-cylinder and a 2.5L DOHC V6. Transmission choices included a 5-speed manual and a 4-speed automatic. The Contour was praised for its sporty handling, comfortable interior, and affordability. However, it also had its share of issues.

**Engine**

**2.0L DOHC 4-cylinder (130 hp, 140 lb-ft torque)**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, reduced engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5L DOHC V6 (165 hp, 165 lb-ft torque)**

* **Lower Intake Manifold Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace lower intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual**

* **Synchronizer Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace synchronizers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-speed Automatic (AX4S)**

* **Transmission Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping gears, delayed shifting, no engagement
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise over bumps, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Body Control Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of electrical components, dashboard malfunctions
    * Solution: Replace body control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Handle breaks or sticks, door cannot be opened
    * Solution: Replace door handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door Panel Detachment:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door panel becomes loose and rattles
    * Solution: Reattach door panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 1996 Ford Contour had a mix of minor and serious issues. The transmission issues could be costly to repair, while the other problems were relatively affordable to fix. Overall, the Contour was a decent vehicle but suffered from some reliability concerns, particularly in its transmission and engine.
