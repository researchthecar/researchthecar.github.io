**Introduction**

The 2008 Ford E-350 Super Duty is a full-size van offered with a choice of three gasoline engines and two diesel engines, paired with either a 4-speed automatic or 5-speed automatic transmission. It is popular for its spaciousness, towing capacity, and versatility. However, like any vehicle, it does have some known issues.

**Engine**

* **5.4L V8**
    * **Cam phaser failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The cam phasers, which control variable valve timing, can fail. This can lead to a variety of symptoms, including reduced engine power, rough idling, and increased fuel consumption.
        * Solution: The cam phasers and timing components need to be replaced. 
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: N/A
    * **Intake manifold gasket failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold gasket can fail, causing a vacuum leak. This can lead to reduced engine power, rough idling, and increased fuel consumption. 
        * Solution: The intake manifold gasket needs to be replaced. 
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: N/A
* **6.8L V10**
    * **No Issues Found**
* **6.0L PowerStroke Diesel**
    * **Turbocharger failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger can fail, resulting in reduced engine power and increased exhaust smoke.
        * Solution: The turbocharger needs to be replaced. 
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
        * Recalls: N/A
    * **Injector failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The fuel injectors can fail, causing rough idling, reduced engine power, and increased fuel consumption.
        * Solution: The fuel injectors need to be replaced. 
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: N/A
* **7.3L PowerStroke Diesel**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **4-speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting gears.
        * Solution: The transmission may need to be rebuilt or replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $4,000
        * Recalls: N/A
* **5-speed Automatic**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Exterior**

* **Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: The body panels, especially around the wheel wells and undercarriage, can rust. 
    * Solution: Rust prevention and treatment. 
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the severity of the rust.
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **HVAC issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) system may not work properly. 
    * Solution: The HVAC system may need to be repaired or replaced. 
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Door lock actuators**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door lock actuators may fail, causing the doors to lock or unlock unexpectedly. 
    * Solution: The door lock actuators need to be replaced. 
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per door
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

The 2008 Ford E-350 Super Duty is a capable and versatile van, but it does have some potential issues that buyers should be aware of, such as engine and transmission problems. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the age and condition of the vehicle. Regular maintenance and preventive care can help to reduce the risk of experiencing these issues.
