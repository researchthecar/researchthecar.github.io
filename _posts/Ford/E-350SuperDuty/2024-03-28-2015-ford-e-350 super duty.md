**Introduction:**

The 2015 Ford E-350 Super Duty is a full-size commercial van available in multiple configurations. It boasts a powerful engine and a spacious cargo area, making it a popular choice for businesses and contractors. Engine options include a 6.8L Triton V10 or a 6.2L V8, both paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The vehicle offers excellent towing and payload capacity but has received some complaints regarding reliability and comfort.

**Engine**

**6.8L Triton V10**

* **Misfiring**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, hesitation during acceleration, reduced fuel efficiency
    * Solution: Replace ignition coils or spark plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, engine overheating, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Slipping Gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or fails to engage, rough shifting
    * Solution: Replace internal transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping transmission, overheating, shuddering
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or rattling sounds when driving over bumps
    * Solution: Lubricate or replace suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle fails to start, dim headlights
    * Solution: Replace battery or identify electrical drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery warning light illuminated, electrical system failure
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Body Panels**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on metal surfaces
    * Solution: Clean and treat affected areas
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Depends on severity
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the dashboard while driving
    * Solution: Tighten loose components or replace trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Ventilation Fan Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: No air flow from ventilation system
    * Solution: Replace blower motor or control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2015 Ford E-350 Super Duty has received mixed reviews from owners. While the powerful engine and spacious cargo area are praised, issues with reliability, comfort, and electrical components have been reported. The estimated cost of repairs for the identified issues varies depending on the severity of the problem and the specific parts involved.
