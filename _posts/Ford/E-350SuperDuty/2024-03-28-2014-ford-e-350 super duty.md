**Introduction:**
The 2014 Ford E-350 Super Duty is a full-size van known for its spacious cargo area and powerful engines. It comes with two engine options: a 5.4L V8 and a 6.8L V10. Both engines can be paired with either a 6-speed automatic or a 5-speed automatic transmission. Drivers appreciate the van's towing and payload capacity, but some have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**
* **5.4L V8**
  * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **6.8L V10**
  * **Spark Plug Thread Stripping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfiring, loss of power, rough idle
    * Solution: Remove stripped spark plug, install Helicoil insert, replace spark plug
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* **6-Speed Automatic**
  * **Torque Converter Lockup Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering, delayed shifting, excessive gear hunting
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **5-Speed Automatic**
  * **Lack of Downshifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty accelerating, poor fuel economy, transmission overheating
    * Solution: Update transmission control module software
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (covered under warranty)
    * Recalls: 14S33

**Suspension**
* **Rear Leaf Springs:**
  * **Sagging or Broken Springs:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Uneven tire wear, decreased handling, vehicle leaning
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain on Keyless Entry System:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains when keyless entry is enabled
    * Solution: Replace keyless entry module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1000+
    * Recalls: 15S25

**Exterior**
* **Side Mirror Adjustment Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Side mirrors cannot be adjusted electronically
    * Solution: Replace side mirror adjustment switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **HVAC Control Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system malfunctioning, lack of heat/AC, fan issues
    * Solution: Replace HVAC control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2014 Ford E-350 Super Duty has a few notable issues, particularly with the engine intake manifold gasket, torque converter, and battery drain. These issues can be costly to repair, but most are covered under warranty if the vehicle is still within the coverage period. Overall, the E-350 Super Duty is a reliable and capable workhorse, but owners should be aware of the potential issues before purchasing.
