**Introduction**

The 2017 Ford E-350 Super Duty is a full-size van designed for commercial and personal use. It offers a range of engine and transmission options, including:

* 6.8L V10 gasoline engine with 305 horsepower and 420 lb-ft of torque
* 6.2L V8 gasoline engine with 255 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque
* 7.3L V8 gasoline engine with 350 horsepower and 468 lb-ft of torque
* 6.7L Power Stroke V8 turbo diesel engine with 270 horsepower and 675 lb-ft of torque

The E-350 Super Duty is praised for its spacious interior, powerful engines, and towing capacity. However, it has been criticized for its fuel efficiency and rough ride quality.

**Engine**

**6.8L V10 Gasoline Engine**

* **Throttle Body Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls, rough idle, poor acceleration
    * Solution: Replace throttle body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Valve Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased emissions, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.2L V8 Gasoline Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**7.3L V8 Gasoline Engine**

* **Spark Plug Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfires, rough idle, poor acceleration
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.7L Power Stroke V8 Turbo Diesel Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, black smoke from exhaust, increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, poor acceleration, black smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $900
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission (6R140)**

* **Transmission Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates to engage, slipping under load
    * Solution: Replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $4,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**10-Speed Automatic Transmission (10R140)**

* **Shifting Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates to shift, rough shifting
    * Solution: Software update or transmission replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Broken leaf springs, sagging rear end
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Shock Absorber Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly even when the vehicle is not in use
    * Solution: Inspect electrical system for parasitic drains
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, flickering electronics, battery not charging
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors won't lock or unlock electronically
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noises coming from the dashboard
    * Solution: Tighten or replace dashboard components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Air Conditioner Blower Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blower motor doesn't work, weak airflow
    * Solution: Replace blower motor or control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Ford E-350 Super Duty has some common issues, but overall it is a reliable vehicle. The severity and cost of the issues vary, with some being minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more serious and costly. It is important to consider these potential issues when making a purchase decision.
