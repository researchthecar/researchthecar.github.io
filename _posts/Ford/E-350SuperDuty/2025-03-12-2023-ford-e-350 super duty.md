The 2023 Ford E-350 Super Duty is a full-size van primarily used for commercial purposes, offering substantial cargo and passenger capacity. Engine options include a 7.3L "Godzilla" V8 producing 350 horsepower and 468 lb-ft of torque paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission. Owners often praise its reliability, capability, and practicality for fleet use or heavy-duty hauling. Common criticisms include its size and maneuverability in urban environments, fuel economy, and basic interior features compared to passenger-focused vans.

**Engine**

**7.3L "Godzilla" V8**

*   **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported higher than expected oil consumption, requiring more frequent top-offs between oil changes. There is no indication of leaks.
    *   Solution: Monitor oil levels regularly. Consider switching to a different brand or viscosity of oil. In severe cases, engine repair or replacement may be necessary, but this is not common.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix:
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**10-Speed Automatic**

*   **Harsh Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Reports of harsh or jerky shifts, particularly at lower speeds. This may be more noticeable during acceleration or deceleration.
    *   Solution: A software update or reprogramming of the transmission control module (TCM) may resolve the issue. If the problem persists, inspection of the valve body or replacement may be required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix:
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The Ford SYNC infotainment system occasionally freezes, becomes unresponsive, or crashes. This can affect navigation, audio, and other connected services.
    *   Solution: Perform a system reset. Check for software updates. If the problem continues, consult a Ford dealer for diagnosis and possible hardware replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix:
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Backup Camera Malfunction**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Backup camera displays a black screen, distorted image, or fails to activate when the vehicle is shifted into reverse.
    *   Solution: Check the camera connection. The Camera module might need replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix:
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Sliding Door Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing the sliding door, or excessive noise during operation.
    *   Solution: Inspect the door tracks and rollers for debris or damage. Lubricate the tracks and rollers. Adjust the door alignment if necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix:
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Rattling/Squeaking Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rattling or squeaking noises emanating from various interior panels, especially while driving on rough roads.
    *   Solution: Identify the source of the noise. Tighten loose panels or apply felt tape to areas of friction.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix:
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Ford E-350 Super Duty appears to have relatively few widespread and severe issues reported thus far. The most common concerns relate to the transmission and electrical systems. While some owners have experienced harsh shifting or infotainment issues, these are often resolved with software updates or minor repairs. The "Godzilla" V8's tendency for excessive oil consumption is concerning and can result in high repair costs. Overall, the 2023 E-350 Super Duty seems to be a reliable workhorse, but potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues and check for any applicable recalls before purchasing.
