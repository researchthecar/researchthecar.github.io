Here's a breakdown of common issues and complaints for the 2024 Ford E-350 Super Duty, organized by category and specific engine/transmission configurations where applicable.

The 2024 Ford E-350 Super Duty is a robust and reliable workhorse, praised for its powerful engine options and towing capabilities. It is available as a cutaway, stripped chassis, or a passenger/cargo van. Engine options include a 7.3L "Godzilla" V8 producing 350 horsepower and 468 lb-ft of torque, and a 6.8L V8 producing 275 horsepower and 405 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission. While many owners appreciate the E-350 Super Duty's performance and utility, some common complaints revolve around electrical glitches, ride quality, and fit-and-finish issues.

    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system (if equipped) may randomly freeze, become unresponsive, or reboot while driving. This can be disruptive and potentially distracting.

**Transmission**

**10-Speed Automatic (Both Engine Options)**

*   **Harsh Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported experiencing harsh shifting, particularly between certain gears. Hesitation when accelerating or a noticeable delay when shifting can also occur. This can be related to valve body issues.
    *   Solution: A software reflash of the transmission control module (TCM) may resolve the issue. In some cases, a valve body replacement is required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $3000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Side View Mirror Vibration**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported that their side view mirrors shake or vibrate excessively when driving at highway speeds. This can reduce visibility and be distracting.
    *   Solution: Tightening the mirror mounts may provide a temporary fix. In severe cases, replacing the side view mirrors may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500+

**Interior**

*   **Rattling/Squeaking Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported various rattling and squeaking noises emanating from the interior, especially in the dashboard and door panels. This can be caused by loose trim pieces or components.
    *   Solution: Identifying the source of the noise and securing loose trim pieces or components may resolve the issue. Applying felt tape or foam padding can also help dampen vibrations.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (DIY) - $200+

**Suspension**

*   **Rough Ride Quality**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The E-350 Super Duty, particularly when unloaded, can exhibit a rough ride quality, especially over bumps and uneven road surfaces.
    *   Solution: Upgrading to aftermarket shocks or adding helper springs can improve ride quality. Adjusting tire pressure to the recommended level is also important.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1000+

**Summary**

The 2024 Ford E-350 Super Duty generally proves to be a dependable vehicle. However, potential buyers should be aware of the electrical issues and potential transmission problems. While interior and exterior issues are often minor annoyances, they should still be taken into account. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, but being informed can help potential buyers make a more confident purchase decision.
