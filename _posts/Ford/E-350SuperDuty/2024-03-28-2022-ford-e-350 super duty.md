## 2022 Ford E-350 Super Duty

The 2022 Ford E-350 Super Duty is a heavy-duty commercial van that offers a spacious interior, robust performance, and advanced features. It comes standard with a 6.8-liter V10 engine that delivers 362 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque. Optional engine choices include a 7.3-liter V8 and a 6.7-liter Power Stroke diesel V8. Transmission options include a 6-speed automatic and a 10-speed automatic.

Despite its strengths, the E-350 Super Duty has faced some reported issues affecting various components.

### Engine
**6.8-liter V10**
- **Spark Plug Failure:** Premature failure of spark plugs can lead to engine misfires, reduced power, and increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of spark plugs.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500
- **Recalls:** None Found

**7.3-liter V8**
- **No Issues Found**

**6.7-liter Power Stroke Diesel V8**
- **Injector Sticking:** Fuel injectors can stick, causing rough idling, poor acceleration, and reduced fuel economy.
- **Solution:** Replacement of fuel injectors.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $800 - $1,500+
- **Recalls:** None Found

### Transmission
**6-speed Automatic**
- **Slipping Gears:** The transmission may slip out of gear, causing a loss of power and potential safety hazards.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of transmission.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,500 - $3,500+
- **Recalls:** None Found

**10-speed Automatic**
- **No Issues Found**

### Suspension
- **Leaf Spring Breakage:** Rear leaf springs can break, affecting ride quality and handling.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaf springs.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
- **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V550000

### Electrical
- **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain prematurely, causing difficulty starting the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of electrical system.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+
- **Recalls:** None Found

### Exterior
- **Corrosion on Tailgate:** The tailgate may experience corrosion, leading to rust and compromised structural integrity.
- **Solution:** Replacement of tailgate.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
- **Recalls:** None Found

### Interior
- **HVAC Malfunction:** The heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) system may malfunction, leading to problems with temperature control.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of HVAC components.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $800
- **Recalls:** None Found

### Summary
The 2022 Ford E-350 Super Duty has faced some reported concerns, particularly involving the 6.8-liter V10 engine, 6-speed automatic transmission, and suspension. The severity of these issues varies, but some can potentially lead to significant repair costs. Additionally, corrosion on the tailgate and HVAC malfunctions have also been reported.
