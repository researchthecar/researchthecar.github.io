## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Intermittent Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power to various accessories or the entire vehicle, dashboard warning lights may illuminate.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of wiring harness, relays, fuses, or alternator issues. Replacement of faulty components.
- **Cost:** $150 - $600

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery repeatedly dies, requiring jump starts. Lights may stay on or accessories malfunction.
- **Solution:** Identify parasitic draw from faulty modules, aftermarket accessories, or worn wiring. Repair or replace faulty components.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500

## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Air conditioning or heating not working effectively, unusual noises from the vents, or blower motor failure.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of blower motor, blend door actuators, refrigerant leaks, or control modules.
- **Cost:** $250 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Ford E-350 Super Duty demonstrates strong mechanical reliability, with its V8 engines and automatic transmission generally performing without significant widespread issues. The primary areas of concern tend to be in the electrical system, specifically intermittent power loss and battery drain, which can be attributed to wiring, relays, or the alternator. Interior issues are less common but can involve the HVAC system. Overall, the E-350 is a dependable vehicle for its intended purpose, with most reported problems being manageable and repairable, especially when proactive maintenance is performed.
