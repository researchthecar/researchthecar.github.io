**Introduction:**

The 2023 Ford E-350 Super Duty is a heavy-duty commercial van known for its powerful performance and spacious cargo capacity. It offers an array of engine and transmission options, including:

* **Engine:**
    * 7.3L V8 gasoline
    * 6.7L Power Stroke V8 diesel
    * 6.2L V8 gasoline (optional)
* **Transmission:**
    * 10-speed TorqShift automatic
    * 6-speed TorqShift manual (optional for 7.3L V8 gasoline)

**Engine:**

**7.3L V8 Gasoline with 6-Speed TorqShift Manual**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the engine, rough idling, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace the faulty fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**7.3L V8 Gasoline with 10-Speed TorqShift Automatic**

* **No Issues Found**

**6.7L Power Stroke V8 Diesel with 10-Speed TorqShift Automatic**

* **EGR Valve Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, reduced engine power, fuel efficiency issues
    * Solution: Replace the EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.2L V8 Gasoline (Optional)**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission:**

**10-Speed TorqShift Automatic**

* **Hesitation and Jerking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or jerks during shifting
    * Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module or replace the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $900 - $2000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed TorqShift Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

* **Power Window Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Window switches become inoperable, windows malfunction
    * Solution: Replace the faulty power window switches
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Rear Door Latch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear doors may not latch securely, creating a safety hazard
    * Solution: Replace the rear door latch assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: Recall number 22V-571

**Interior:**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks appear on the dashboard, affecting aesthetics and potentially obscuring instrument visibility
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500+
    * Recalls: Recall number 22V-580

**Summary:**

The 2023 Ford E-350 Super Duty is a generally reliable vehicle, but it is not without its issues. The most common problems reported include fuel injector failure, EGR valve failure, transmission hesitation, power window switch failure, rear door latch failure, and dashboard cracking. The severity and cost of the issues vary depending on the specific issue and the parts involved. However, most of the reported issues can be addressed at a reasonable cost, making the E-350 Super Duty a reliable choice for commercial use.
