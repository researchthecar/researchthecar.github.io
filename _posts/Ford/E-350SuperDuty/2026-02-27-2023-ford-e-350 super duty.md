### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Ford E-350 Super Duty demonstrates strong reliability, particularly in its core powertrain and chassis components. Both the 7.3L V8 and 6.2L V8 engines, paired with the 6-speed automatic transmission, are known for their durability and lack of widespread reported issues. The heavy-duty suspension and frame are built to withstand demanding use. Electrical, exterior, and interior components also appear to be holding up well, with no significant recurring problems noted in general reporting. As a commercial vehicle, consistent maintenance is crucial, but the E-350 Super Duty is generally considered a dependable workhorse.
