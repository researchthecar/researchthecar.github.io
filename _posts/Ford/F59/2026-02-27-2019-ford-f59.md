# 2019 Ford F59

### Overview
The 2019 Ford F59 is a chassis cab truck designed for commercial use, offering a robust platform for various upfits. Its primary focus is on durability and utility, often serving as the foundation for delivery vans, box trucks, and specialized service vehicles. Reliability data for the F59 is generally positive, reflecting its commercial-grade construction and straightforward engineering. Owners and fleet managers typically report good performance, with issues tending to be specific to the powertrain or auxiliary systems rather than widespread design flaws. Maintenance is crucial for maximizing its lifespan, as with any heavy-duty vehicle.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Ford F59 demonstrates strong reliability, particularly given its commercial application. The available V10 gasoline and Power Stroke diesel engines, along with the TorqShift automatic transmission, are well-regarded for their durability. Suspension and chassis components are built to withstand heavy loads and demanding use. Electrical and interior systems are generally straightforward and robust, designed for functionality over luxury. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the F59's design prioritizes longevity and ease of maintenance, making it a dependable choice for fleet operators and businesses requiring a workhorse chassis.
