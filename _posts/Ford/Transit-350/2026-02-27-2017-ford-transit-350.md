### Standard Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start, dashboard warning lights illuminate unexpectedly, accessories not functioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of parasitic draw, battery replacement if necessary.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light on the dashboard, electrical components malfunctioning, engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Alternator replacement.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Ford Transit-350 generally exhibits good reliability, particularly with its gasoline engine options and automatic transmission. The most frequently cited technical concerns tend to be associated with the 2.2L TDCi diesel engine, specifically related to the turbocharger and fuel injectors, which can lead to performance issues and require costly repairs. Electrical system issues, such as battery drain and alternator failure, are also noted as potential problems that can impact daily operation. However, these are not universally reported across all vehicles. For the most part, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are considered robust and free from widespread defects. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any emerging symptoms are key to ensuring the longevity and reliability of this commercial vehicle.
