# 2022 Ford Transit-350

### Overview
The 2022 Ford Transit-350 is a versatile and popular commercial van known for its robust build and utility. As a workhorse vehicle, its reliability is paramount for businesses. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, examining key areas like the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical systems, exterior, and interior. While generally considered dependable, specific components and powertrain configurations can exhibit varying degrees of reported concerns. Understanding these potential weak points can help owners and fleet managers proactively address issues and maintain optimal operational efficiency.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L EcoBoost V6
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant smell, visible leaks under the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace leaking hoses, water pump, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

### 2.0L EcoBlue Diesel
**DPF (Diesel Particulate Filter) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** DPF regeneration, cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000

**Fuel Injector Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, engine misfires, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Injector cleaning or replacement.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,200

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Ford Transit-350 generally demonstrates good reliability, particularly with its standard powertrain options. The 3.5L PFDI V6 engine and its associated 10-speed automatic transmission have shown fewer reported issues. The 3.5L EcoBoost V6, while powerful, can occasionally present concerns with its turbochargers and cooling system. The 2.0L EcoBlue Diesel engine, common in some configurations, may experience DPF and fuel injector issues, which are not uncommon for diesel powertrains. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment glitches and battery drain, are present but typically manageable. Overall, the Transit-350 is a solid choice for commercial use, with most potential issues being repairable and not indicative of systemic failure across the model range.
