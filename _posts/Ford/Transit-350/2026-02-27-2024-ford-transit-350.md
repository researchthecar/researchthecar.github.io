### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Ford Transit-350 demonstrates strong initial reliability, with no significant widespread mechanical failures reported for its primary engine and transmission options. The 2.0L EcoBlue Diesel and 3.5L EcoBoost V6 engines, paired with the 10-speed automatic transmission, appear robust. Suspension components are also holding up well under typical commercial use. The most commonly noted, though not widespread, issues are minor electrical glitches within the infotainment system, typically resolved with software updates. Exterior and interior components are generally performing as expected. Overall, the Transit-350 is positioned as a dependable workhorse for commercial applications, with potential buyers advised to monitor for any emerging electrical system quirks.
