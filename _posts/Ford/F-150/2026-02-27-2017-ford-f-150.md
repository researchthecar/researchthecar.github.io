# 2017 Ford F-150 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Ford F-150 is a versatile and highly capable full-size pickup truck known for its lightweight aluminum body and diverse array of robust engine and transmission configurations. While it remains a top choice in the used truck market for towing and daily utility, prospective buyers should review powertrain options carefully and inspect for any historical owner-reported mechanical quirks.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Ti-VCT

This naturally aspirated base V6 engine produces roughly 282 horsepower and 253 lb-ft of torque. It offers reliable, basic utility for everyday driving without the complexity of turbochargers.

### 2.7L EcoBoost V6

A twin-turbocharged direct-injection engine delivering about 325 horsepower and 375 lb-ft of torque. It balances spirited performance with respectable fuel efficiency for light-to-moderate duty towing.

### 3.5L EcoBoost V6

The second-generation high-output twin-turbo V6 pushes out 375 horsepower and an impressive 470 lb-ft of torque. It is a popular choice for heavy towing, though buyers should verify proper maintenance history on forced-induction systems.

### 5.0L V8 Ti-VCT

A traditional naturally aspirated V8 generating around 385 horsepower and 387 lb-ft of torque. Renowned for its classic V8 sound and durable architecture, it remains a favorite for traditional truck buyers.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic

A traditional six-speed automatic transmission paired with select standard engines. It provides predictable shifting and long-term mechanical simplicity for used buyers.

### 10-Speed SelectShift Automatic

A modern ten-speed automatic transmission paired with EcoBoost and V8 engines to optimize fuel economy and power delivery. Prospective buyers should test-drive to ensure smooth shifting across all gears.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V894000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=F-150&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** When the heater is plugged in, prolonged corrosion could cause a resistive short, increasing the risk of overheated or melted wiring which can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will disable the block heater cable, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V278000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=F-150&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A damaged cable could cause a resistive short, increasing the risk of overheated or melted wiring, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will disable the engine block heater from being used. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V568000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=F-150&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If a front seat belt pretensioner deploys as the result of a crash, the sparks may ignite materials such as carpeting or insulation within the B-pillar area. A vehicle fire could result if materials ignite inside the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will remove the B-pillar insulation material and wiring harness tape, and install heat resistant tape, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V652000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=F-150&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A door that opens while driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the door latch actuation cables and repair if necessary, and install water shields over the door latches, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V529000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=F-150&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The air bag inflator within the passenger frontal air bag module may rupture in the event of a crash. An inflator rupture may result in metal fragments striking the vehicle occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V526000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=F-150&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages." If the left rear inflatable seat belt buckle separates from it's mounting bracket, the seat occupant may not be adequately restrained, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the seat belt assembly, replacing the attaching rivet as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V331000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=F-150&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** These accessory door handle covers may cause the doors to unlatch in a side impact collision. As such, these vehicles may fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 206, "Door Locks and Door Retention Components." If the doors unlatch and open in a side impact collision, the occupants have an increased risk.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will remove the accessory covers and refund the purchase price. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

- **Issue:** Several gauges, including the transmission gear position indicator, may not illuminate. ## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Ford F-150 provides a compelling mix of performance, towing capacity, and modern powertrain engineering. When shopping for a used model, take time to evaluate the specific engine and transmission combination that best fits your workload, and ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection is completed.
