# 2014 Ford Transit Custom

### Overview
The 2014 Ford Transit Custom is a popular commercial vehicle known for its practicality and versatility. As with any vehicle, especially those used for commercial purposes, reliability is a key concern for owners and fleet managers. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into common problem areas and their potential resolution costs. The Transit Custom generally performs well, but specific components and engine/transmission variants have shown varying degrees of susceptibility to certain faults. Understanding these potential issues can help in proactive maintenance and informed purchasing decisions.

---


## Engine

### 2.2L TDCi Diesel (various power outputs)
**EGR Valve Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, rough idling, engine warning light, increased emissions.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Whining or whistling noises, loss of power, black smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Glow Plug Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, especially in cold weather, engine warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty glow plugs.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears, juddering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch kit (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200

**Gear Selector Cable Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty selecting gears, loose gear shifter.
- **Solution:** Adjustment or replacement of gear selector cables.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle not starting, dim lights, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to find parasitic drain, repair or replace faulty component, or replace battery.
- **Cost:** $150 - $600

**Sensor Failures (e.g., ABS, Parking Sensors)**
- **Symptoms:** Warning lights on dashboard, malfunctioning systems.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty sensor.

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Ford Transit Custom demonstrates a generally solid reliability record, particularly for its intended commercial use. The most frequently reported technical issues tend to center around the 2.2L TDCi diesel engine, with EGR valve problems, turbocharger issues, and glow plug failures being the primary concerns. Transmission-wise, the manual gearbox can experience clutch wear and gear selector cable problems, while the automatic variant appears more robust. Electrical gremlins, such as battery drain and sensor faults, are also noted but are not overwhelmingly prevalent. Exterior and interior components are typically durable. Overall, with diligent maintenance and attention to the known engine-related weak points, the 2014 Transit Custom remains a dependable workhorse.
