# 2019 Ford Transit Custom

### Overview
The 2019 Ford Transit Custom is a popular commercial van known for its versatility and practicality. Generally, it offers a solid reliability record, especially considering its demanding operational environment. Owners and fleet managers often praise its robust build and functional design. However, like any vehicle, specific components can experience issues. This report focuses on documented technical reliability data, excluding routine maintenance and normal wear and tear, to provide a clear picture of potential concerns for the 2019 model year across its various configurations.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L EcoBlue Diesel (105 PS)
**DPF (Diesel Particulate Filter) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated DPF warning light, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration, DPF cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200

**AdBlue System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** AdBlue warning light, engine starting issues, reduced power.
- **Solution:** Refill AdBlue tank, check for leaks or sensor faults, potential SCR catalyst cleaning or replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1500

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears, unusual noises.
- **Solution:** Clutch replacement.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


### 6-Speed SelectShift Automatic Transmission
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Delayed gear changes, rough shifting, occasional jerking during acceleration or deceleration.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter change, software update, or internal transmission repair.
- **Cost:** $250 - $1500

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start, dashboard warning lights flickering.
- **Solution:** Diagnose parasitic draw, check battery health, replace faulty module or component causing drain.
- **Cost:** $150 - $800

**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software update, system reset, or replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Ford Transit Custom demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly in its suspension, exterior, and interior components, which show no widespread significant issues. The primary areas of concern revolve around the diesel engines, specifically the DPF and AdBlue systems, which are common to modern diesel vehicles and can be influenced by driving patterns. Transmission issues, while not rampant, can occur, especially with the automatic gearbox. Electrical gremlins, such as battery drain and infotainment glitches, are also noted but generally manageable. Regular maintenance and mindful operation, especially for diesel variants, are key to ensuring long-term dependability.
