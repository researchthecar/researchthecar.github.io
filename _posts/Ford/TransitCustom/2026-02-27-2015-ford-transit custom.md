# 2015 Ford Transit Custom

### Overview
The 2015 Ford Transit Custom is a popular commercial van known for its practicality and versatility. As a workhorse vehicle, its reliability is a key concern for fleet operators and small business owners. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond standard maintenance, examining common problem areas across its various engine and transmission configurations. While generally robust, certain components have shown a propensity for specific faults that can impact operational uptime and repair costs. Understanding these potential issues allows for proactive maintenance and informed purchasing decisions.

---


## Engine

### 2.2L TDCi Diesel (various power outputs)
**EGR Valve Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, rough idling, engine warning light illumination, increased emissions.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Whining or whistling noises, loss of power, black smoke from exhaust, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Glow Plug Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, especially in cold weather, engine warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty glow plugs.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears, unusual noises when engaging clutch.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch kit (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200

**Gear Selection Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Stiff or notchy gear changes, difficulty selecting specific gears.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential adjustment or replacement of shift linkage components.
- **Cost:** $250 - $600

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle not starting, dashboard warning lights flickering.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw and repair of faulty component or wiring.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400

**Sensor Failures (e.g., ABS, Speed Sensor)**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated warning lights (ABS, traction control, check engine), incorrect speed readings.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty sensor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Ford Transit Custom generally exhibits good reliability, particularly with its robust 2.2L TDCi diesel engine. The most commonly reported issues revolve around the EGR valve and turbocharger, which are not uncommon for diesel engines of this era and can be costly to repair if neglected. Manual transmission clutches can wear with heavy use. Electrical gremlins, such as battery drain or sensor failures, are also noted but are typically diagnosable and repairable. Overall, the Transit Custom is a dependable van, but owners should be aware of potential EGR and turbocharger maintenance needs to ensure long-term operational efficiency.
