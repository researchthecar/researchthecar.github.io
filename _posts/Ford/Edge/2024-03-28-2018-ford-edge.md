**Vehicle Introduction:**

The 2018 Ford Edge is a midsize SUV offered with a choice of three engine options: a 2.0L EcoBoost turbocharged four-cylinder, a 2.7L EcoBoost turbocharged V6, and a 3.5L naturally aspirated V6. The 2.0L EcoBoost produces 250 hp and 275 lb-ft of torque, the 2.7L EcoBoost produces 335 hp and 380 lb-ft of torque, and the 3.5L V6 produces 280 hp and 250 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include an 8-speed automatic for all engines. The Edge is known for its stylish exterior, spacious interior, and comfortable ride. However, certain issues have been reported by owners.

**Engine**

**2.0L EcoBoost**
* **Valve Cover Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the valve cover gasket, causing oil consumption and reduced engine performance.
    * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased exhaust smoke, and loud whistling noises from the engine.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.7L EcoBoost**
* **Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Severe coolant leak, white smoke from the exhaust, and overheating.
    * Solution: Replace the head gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, reduced power, and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and timing chains.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.5L V6**
* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**
* **Shifting Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifting, slipping gears, or loss of power.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the transmission components, such as solenoids or clutches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise and vibrations from the suspension, reduced handling and stability.
    * Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Harness Faults:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Various electrical issues, such as flickering lights, malfunctioning gauges, or engine starting problems.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the faulty wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors, doors failing to remain shut.
    * Solution: Replace the door lock actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks from the sunroof, causing interior damage.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the sunroof seals or drain tubes.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Ineffective heating or cooling, fan noise, or temperature fluctuations.
    * Solution: Repair or replace climate control system components, such as the blower motor or control panel.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Creaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises and creaks from the dashboard, especially in cold weather.
    * Solution: Apply lubricant to the dashboard components or repair any loose connections.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2018 Ford Edge has received mixed reviews from owners. While some praise its stylish design and spacious interior, others have encountered various issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity of these issues varies, but some, such as head gasket failure or turbocharger failure, can be costly and time-consuming to repair. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
