**Introduction:**

The 2014 Ford Econoline Wagon is a full-size van powered by a standard 5.4L V8 engine that produces 300 horsepower and 363 lb-ft of torque. It comes paired with a standard 6-speed automatic transmission. The Econoline Wagon is known for its spacious interior and versatility, but it has also been met with some criticism regarding its dated design and poor fuel economy.

**Engine**

**5.4L V8**
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
  * **Description/Symptoms:** Coolant leak, rough idle, engine overheating
  * **Solution:** Replace intake manifold gaskets
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
  * **Recalls:** 14V-261

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
  * **Description/Symptoms:** Engine rattle, reduced power, engine stalling
  * **Solution:** Replace timing chain tensioner
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $800 - $1,200+
  * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**
* **Transmission Slipping**
  * **Description/Symptoms:** Delayed or hesitant shifting, loss of power
  * **Solution:** Repair or replace transmission
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,000+
  * **Recalls:** 14V-262

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise**
  * **Description/Symptoms:** Clunking or rattling noises when driving over bumps
  * **Solution:** Replace worn ball joints or bushings
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600+
  * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Rear Axle Seal Leak**
  * **Description/Symptoms:** Oil leak from rear axle
  * **Solution:** Replace rear axle seal
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
  * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
  * **Description/Symptoms:** Rapid battery discharge, difficulty starting vehicle
  * **Solution:** Diagnose and repair electrical system issue
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+
  * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sliding Door Cable Failure**
  * **Description/Symptoms:** Sliding door fails to open or close properly
  * **Solution:** Replace faulty cable
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
  * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Dashboard**
  * **Description/Symptoms:** Cracks in the center console
  * **Solution:** Replace center console
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800+
  * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 2014 Ford Econoline Wagon has some notable issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. These problems can be costly to repair and can impact the reliability and performance of the vehicle. However, the Econoline Wagon remains a versatile and spacious option for those who require a large van.
