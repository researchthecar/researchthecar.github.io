**2011 Ford Econoline Wagon**

The 2011 Ford Econoline Wagon is a full-size van offered with several engine and transmission options. It is popular among families and businesses for its spaciousness and versatility. However, like all vehicles, it has its share of reported issues.

**Engine**

* **5.4L Triton V8**
    * **Intake manifold gasket failure:** Leaks coolant, causing overheating and engine damage. Requires replacement of the intake manifold gasket. $800 - $1,200+
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure:** Causes the timing chain to skip, resulting in engine damage. Requires replacement of the timing chain tensioner and related components. $1,500 - $2,500+
* **4.6L Triton V8**
    * **Spark plug failure:** Causes misfires and reduced engine performance. Requires replacement of the spark plugs. $100 - $200+
* **3.7L Duratec V6**
    * **No significant issues reported**

**Transmission**

* **5-speed TorqShift automatic**
    * **Transmission slipping:** Transmission fails to engage gears properly, resulting in erratic shifting and loss of power. Requires transmission repair or replacement. $1,500 - $3,000+
* **6-speed TorqShift automatic**
    * **No significant issues reported**

**Suspension**

* **Worn shock absorbers:** Reduced handling and ride quality, increased braking distance. Requires replacement of the shock absorbers. $500 - $800+
* **Leaking struts:** Fluid leakage affects ride quality and handling. Requires replacement of the struts. $800 - $1,200+

**Electrical**

* **Electrical shorts:** Can cause various electrical issues, such as flickering lights, power loss, and battery drain. Requires diagnosis and repair of the short circuit. $200 - $500+
* **Battery failure:** Premature battery failure can result in starting problems. Requires replacement of the battery. $100 - $200+

**Exterior**

* **Plastic molding warping:** Exterior plastic moldings may warp or crack, affecting the appearance of the vehicle. Requires replacement of the affected moldings. $500 - $1,000+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattles:** Rattles or squeaks from the dashboard can be caused by loose components or poor assembly. Requires disassembly of the dashboard and tightening or replacing loose parts. $500 - $1,000+
* **Air conditioning vents not blowing cold air:** Can be caused by a faulty compressor, refrigerant leak, or blockage in the ventilation system. Requires diagnosis and repair of the affected component. $500 - $1,500+
* **Power window failure:** Windows may fail to operate due to faulty switches, motors, or wiring. Requires repair or replacement of the affected components. $200 - $500+

**Summary**

Overall, the 2011 Ford Econoline Wagon has a range of issues that vary in severity and cost. Some issues, such as intake manifold gasket failure and transmission slipping, can be costly to repair. However, many other issues are relatively minor and can be addressed at a lower cost. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and consider their budget when purchasing and maintaining this vehicle.
