# 2002 Ford F-350 Super Duty

### Overview
The 2002 Ford F-350 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck known for its robust construction and towing capabilities. As a workhorse vehicle, its reliability is generally considered strong, particularly with its larger engine options. However, like any vehicle of this age and class, certain components can experience wear or specific issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented common problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers. The data covers various engine and transmission configurations available for this model year, offering a detailed look at its technical reliability.

---


## Engine

### 5.4L Triton V8
**Spark Plug Blowout**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of engine power, rough idling, misfires, audible popping sound from engine.
- **Solution:** Requires specialized tools to extract broken spark plug and re-tap the cylinder head. Often involves replacing spark plugs with updated designs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Camshaft Position Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check Engine Light illumination, rough idle, stalling, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the camshaft position sensor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


### 6.8L Triton V10
**Spark Plug Blowout**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of engine power, rough idling, misfires, audible popping sound from engine.
- **Solution:** Requires specialized tools to extract broken spark plug and re-tap the cylinder head. Often involves replacing spark plugs with updated designs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Camshaft Position Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check Engine Light illumination, rough idle, stalling, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the camshaft position sensor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


### 7.3L Power Stroke V8 Diesel
**Injector O-ring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, white smoke from exhaust, loss of power, fuel smell in oil.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the injector O-rings and seals.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whining or whistling noises from the turbo.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential rebuild or replacement of the turbocharger.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500


## Transmission

### 4R100 Automatic (4-Speed)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, shuddering during acceleration, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter and potentially transmission fluid flush.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission slipping, harsh shifting, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Installation of an auxiliary transmission cooler.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


### ZF6 Manual (6-Speed)
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the clutch kit (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Leaf Spring Sagging/Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** Rear end sag, uneven ride height, clunking noises over bumps.
- **Solution:** Replacement of damaged or worn leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800 per axle

**Ball Joint Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or popping noises when turning, uneven tire wear, loose steering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per side


## Electrical

### Standard System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, dead battery, electrical system malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Instrument Cluster Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Inaccurate gauge readings, flickering lights, non-functional gauges.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Exterior

### Standard Body
**Tailgate Latch Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty opening or closing the tailgate, tailgate not latching securely.
- **Solution:** Lubrication or replacement of tailgate latch mechanisms.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300


## Interior

### Standard Cabin
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents or weak airflow, regardless of fan speed setting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor and resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power locks not working on one or more doors, manual operation required.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty door lock actuator.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300 per door


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2002 Ford F-350 Super Duty generally exhibits good reliability, especially the robust 7.3L Power Stroke diesel engine. However, owners should be aware of potential issues such as spark plug blowout on the gasoline V8s, and common wear items like suspension components and the 4R100 transmission's torque converter, particularly under heavy use. Electrical components like the alternator and instrument cluster can also be points of failure. While not overwhelmingly problematic, addressing these known issues proactively can help maintain the truck's legendary work capability and longevity. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to symptoms are key to a positive ownership experience.
