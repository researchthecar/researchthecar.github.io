**Introduction**
The 2017 Ford F-350 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck offered with a range of engine and transmission options. It is known for its powerful engines, capable towing capacity, and rugged construction. However, like any vehicle, it has experienced some issues reported by owners.

**Engine**
- **6.7L Power Stroke Turbo Diesel V8 (440 hp, 925 lb-ft torque)**
  - **Fuel Injector Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, decreased fuel economy
    - Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
- **6-Speed Automatic Transmission (TorqShift)**
  - **Torque Converter Shudder**
    - Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shudder felt when accelerating or shifting gears
    - Solution: Replace torque converter
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
- **Leaf Spring Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Broken or cracked leaf springs, resulting in sagging or uneven ride height
    - Solution: Replace leaf springs
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+ per side
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Rear Axle Seal Leak**
    - Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil from the rear axle differential
    - Solution: Replace axle seal
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
- **Faulty Battery Sensor**
    - Description/Symptoms: Inaccurate battery level readings, leading to premature battery failure or starting issues
    - Solution: Replace battery sensor
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
- **Rust on Frame**
    - Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on the steel frame, particularly on older models
    - Solution: Sand and paint affected areas, or replace rusted components if necessary
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of corrosion
    - Recalls: 19S30

**Interior**
- **Heater Core Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: No heat or insufficient heat output from the heater vents
    - Solution: Replace heater core
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Power Window Switch Malfunction**
    - Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or non-functional power windows
    - Solution: Replace power window switch
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 2017 Ford F-350 Super Duty is a capable and reliable workhorse. However, it has experienced some issues, primarily related to its engine, transmission, and suspension. The severity of these issues varies, with some being relatively minor and others requiring more extensive repairs. Overall, the cost of addressing these issues can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars. It is important to stay informed about potential problems and perform regular maintenance to ensure the longevity and reliability of the vehicle.
