# 2019 Ford F-350 Super Duty

### Overview
The 2019 Ford F-350 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck engineered for demanding work and towing applications. It offers robust performance with a choice of powerful gasoline and diesel engines, paired with durable transmissions. Reliability for this model year is generally strong, reflecting Ford's commitment to building work-ready vehicles. While specific component failures are infrequent, potential areas to monitor include certain powertrain elements under extreme use and typical electronic accessory functions. Overall, the F-350 Super Duty is considered a dependable option for those requiring significant hauling and towing capabilities, with a focus on mechanical robustness.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Ford F-350 Super Duty demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission. The gasoline 6.2L V8 and the 6-speed automatic transmission are generally trouble-free. The 6.7L Power Stroke diesel, while powerful, has a few documented issues such as DPF problems and EGR cooler leaks, which are not uncommon for modern diesel engines and can be costly to repair. Electrical gremlins, primarily related to the infotainment system and occasional battery drain, are the most frequently cited non-powertrain issues. Overall, for a heavy-duty truck, the F-350 offers a dependable ownership experience, with most potential issues being manageable or related to the complexities of diesel emissions systems.
