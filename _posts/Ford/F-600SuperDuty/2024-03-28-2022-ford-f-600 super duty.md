**Introduction**

The 2022 Ford F-600 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck offered with a choice of two engines: a 6.7-liter Power Stroke V8 turbodiesel and a 7.3-liter Godzilla V8 gasoline engine. Transmission options include a 6-speed automatic and a 10-speed automatic.

Owners appreciate the F-600 Super Duty for its powerful engines, capable towing and payload capacities, and comfortable interior. However, some owners have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**6.7-Liter Power Stroke V8 Turbodiesel**

**Fuel Injector Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, rough idling, decreased engine power, increased fuel consumption
Solution: Replace fuel injectors
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: N/A

**7.3-Liter Godzilla V8 Gasoline**

**No Significant Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

**Delayed Shifting**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitation or slipping during gear changes
Solution: Update transmission control module software or replace transmission components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension Strut Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises, excessive vibration, uneven tire wear
Solution: Replace front suspension struts
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges prematurely, difficulty starting vehicle
Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system, replace battery if needed
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

**Tailgate Cable Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Tailgate won't open or close properly
Solution: Replace tailgate cable
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

**Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: A/C or heating system not working correctly
Solution: Diagnose and repair climate control system components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

While the 2022 Ford F-600 Super Duty is a generally reliable vehicle, some models have experienced specific issues with fuel injectors, transmission shifting, suspension struts, electrical system, tailgate cables, and climate control systems. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the problem and the necessary repairs. Owners should be aware of these potential problems and have their vehicles inspected by a qualified mechanic if any concerning symptoms arise.
