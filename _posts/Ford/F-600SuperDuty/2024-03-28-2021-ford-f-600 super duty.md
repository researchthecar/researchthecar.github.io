## 2021 Ford F-600 Super Duty

The 2021 Ford F-600 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck that offers a powerful engine and impressive towing and payload capacities. It comes with a standard 6.7L Power Stroke V8 diesel engine that produces 450 horsepower and 1,050 lb-ft of torque. The F-600 Super Duty is also available with a gasoline-powered 6.8L V10 engine that generates 385 horsepower and 430 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 10-speed TorqShift automatic transmission. The F-600 Super Duty is praised for its impressive capabilities and rugged design, but some owners have reported some issues with the vehicle.

### Engine
- **6.7L Power Stroke V8 Diesel Engine**
    - **Turbocharger Failure:**
      - Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger fails, leading to reduced engine power, black smoke from the exhaust, and a check engine light.
      - Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
      - Recalls: None Found
    - **Injector Failure:**
      - Description/Symptoms: One or more fuel injectors fail, causing rough idling, poor acceleration, and increased fuel consumption.
      - Solution: Replace the faulty fuel injectors.
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
      - Recalls: None Found
- **6.8L V10 Gasoline Engine**
    - **No Issues Found**

### Transmission
- **10-Speed TorqShift Automatic Transmission**
    - **Transmission Slipping:**
      - Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips or hesitates during gear changes, causing rough shifts and loss of power.
      - Solution: Replace the transmission fluid or repair/replace the transmission.
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
      - Recalls: None Found

### Suspension
- **No Issues Found**

### Electrical
- **Battery Drain:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
  - Solution: Inspect and test the electrical system to identify and repair any parasitic drain.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

### Exterior
- **Door Handle Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The exterior door handles break or become loose, making it difficult to open the doors.
  - Solution: Replace the door handles.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Tailgate Latch Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The tailgate latch fails, allowing the tailgate to open unexpectedly.
  - Solution: Replace the tailgate latch.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
  - Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V424000

### Interior
- **Climate Control Issues:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The climate control system malfunctions, leading to incorrect temperature settings or failure to control the temperature.
  - Solution: Repair or replace the climate control module or actuators.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Infotainment System Freeze:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system freezes or becomes unresponsive.
  - Solution: Reset or update the infotainment system software.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+
  - Recalls: None Found

### Summary

Overall, the 2021 Ford F-600 Super Duty is a capable and powerful truck. However, some owners have reported issues with certain components, such as the turbocharger, transmission, and electrical system. The severity and cost of repairs can vary depending on the issue and the extent of the damage. It's recommended to perform regular maintenance and address any issues promptly to ensure the vehicle's reliability and safety.
