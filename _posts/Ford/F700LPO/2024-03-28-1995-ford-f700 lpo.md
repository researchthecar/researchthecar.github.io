**Introduction:**

The 1995 Ford F700 LPO is a heavy-duty pickup truck powered by a 7.3L Power Stroke diesel engine paired with a 5-speed manual transmission. It boasts 215 horsepower and 425 lb-ft of torque, making it a capable workhorse. While praised for its durability and towing capacity, it has also faced some issues.

**Engine**
**7.3L Power Stroke Diesel**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, rough idling, decreased engine power.
  - Solution: Replace fuel injectors.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: 98V267000
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Loss of boost pressure, reduced engine performance, excessive smoke.
  - Solution: Replace turbocharger.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**5-Speed Manual**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid, difficulty shifting gears.
  - Solution: Replace transmission seals or gaskets.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Broken leaf springs, sagging rear end.
  - Solution: Replace leaf springs.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, battery not charging, electrical system issues.
  - Solution: Replace alternator.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Frame and Body:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on metal components, reduced structural integrity.
  - Solution: Treat rust with rust inhibitor, replace affected parts.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Instrument Cluster Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning gauges, incorrect readings.
  - Solution: Replace instrument cluster.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1995 Ford F700 LPO faces moderate-to-severe issues that can be costly to repair. Major engine and transmission problems, such as fuel injector failure and turbocharger failure, can significantly impact performance and reliability. Suspension issues like leaf spring breakage can compromise safety. While electrical and interior issues are less severe, they can still be inconvenient. Buyers should be aware of these potential issues and factor the cost of repairs into their purchase decision.
