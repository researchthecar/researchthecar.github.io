**1992 Ford F700 LPO**

The 1992 Ford F700 LPO is a heavy-duty pickup truck that was offered with a variety of engine and transmission options. The most popular engine option was the 7.3L V8 diesel, which produced 215 horsepower and 425 lb-ft of torque. Other engine options included a 6.9L V8 diesel, a 7.5L V8 gasoline, and a 9.0L V8 gasoline. The F700 LPO was also offered with a wide range of transmission options, including a five-speed manual, a six-speed manual, and a three-speed automatic.

Owners of the 1992 Ford F700 LPO generally praise the truck's powerful engines, rugged construction, and spacious interior. However, some owners have reported experiencing certain issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**7.3L V8 Diesel**

* **Turbocharger failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased exhaust smoke, whistling noise
  * Solution: Replace turbocharger
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
* **Fuel injector failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, rough idling, decreased fuel economy
  * Solution: Replace fuel injector
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual**

* **Grinding gears**
  * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise when shifting
  * Solution: Replace worn gears or synchronizers
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+

**Suspension**

* **Leaf spring breakage**
  * Description/Symptoms: Sagging suspension, rough ride, decreased handling
  * Solution: Replace leaf springs
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, dim lights, electrical system malfunctions
  * Solution: Replace alternator
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* **Battery drain**
  * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies quickly, even when the vehicle is not running
  * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system draw
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Rust on body panels**
  * Description/Symptoms: Surface rust, bubbling paint, holes in body panels
  * Solution: Repair or replace rusted panels
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on extent of damage

**Interior**

* **Dashboard cracking**
  * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on dashboard, especially in areas exposed to sunlight
  * Solution: Replace dashboard
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Door lock failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors, doors may not stay closed
  * Solution: Replace door lock actuators
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Recalls:**

* None Found

**Summary**

The 1992 Ford F700 LPO is a generally reliable vehicle with a few known issues. The most significant issues include turbocharger failure, fuel injector failure, grinding gears, and alternator failure. The cost to fix these issues can vary depending on the severity of the problem and the labor rates in your area. Overall, the F700 LPO is a durable and capable workhorse with a loyal following among owners.
