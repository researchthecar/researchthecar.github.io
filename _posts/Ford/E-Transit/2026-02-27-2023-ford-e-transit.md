# 2023 Ford E-Transit Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Ford E-Transit brings all-electric capability to the commercial van market, offering businesses a zero-emission alternative for delivery and utility needs. As a relatively new and specialized electric platform, it combines familiar Transit utility with battery-electric propulsion. Prospective used buyers should evaluate charging infrastructure needs and inspect early production build quality.

---

## Engine

### Electric Motor

The E-Transit utilizes a single permanent magnet synchronous electric motor producing approximately 266 horsepower and 317 lb-ft of torque. This powertrain delivers smooth, instantaneous acceleration suited for stop-and-go commercial routes with minimal routine maintenance requirements.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive Transmission

Power is sent to the rear wheels via a single-speed direct-drive transmission designed specifically for commercial EV duty. The setup eliminates traditional multi-speed shifting for smooth, reliable power delivery.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V860000: Power Train:Axle Assembly:Axle Shaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=E-Transit&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash. In addition, a half shaft that is not fully engaged to the primary drive unit can result in a vehicle rollaway if the parking brake is not applied, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealer will inspect and replace the rear half shaft and power drive unit components as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Ford E-Transit is an intriguing option for commercial buyers looking to electrify their fleets without sacrificing cargo flexibility. Because widespread owner complaint data is sparse for this model year, buyers should ensure any remaining factory warranties transfer and thoroughly verify battery health before purchase.
