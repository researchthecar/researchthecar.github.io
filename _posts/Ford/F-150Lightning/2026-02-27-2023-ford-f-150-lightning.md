# 2023 Ford F-150 Lightning Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Ford F-150 Lightning brings electric truck capabilities to the mainstream, combining robust dual-motor power with traditional F-Series utility. As a relatively new EV platform, prospective buyers should carefully review available powertrain options and monitor real-world owner feedback regarding early software and electrical performance.

---

## Engine

### Standard-Range Dual-Motor Electric Powertrain

This setup features dual permanent magnet synchronous motors paired with a standard-range battery pack, delivering approximately 452 horsepower and 775 lb-ft of torque. It offers brisk acceleration and standard four-wheel drive, representing a solid entry point for daily driving and light-duty tasks.

### Extended-Range Dual-Motor Electric Powertrain

Upgrading to the extended-range battery increases output to roughly 580 horsepower while maintaining 775 lb-ft of torque via dual electric motors. This configuration provides superior driving range and robust towing capacity, making it the preferred choice for heavy-duty use and longer road trips.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Fixed Gear Transmission

The direct-drive single-speed transmission channels power seamlessly from the dual motors to all four wheels without traditional gear shifts. Its simple mechanical design minimizes moving parts, contributing to low maintenance overhead.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V168000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=F-150+Lightning&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A battery short-circuit increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high-voltage battery pack, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V688000: Visibility:Defroster/Defogger/Hvac System:Windshield:Electrical Heating Element](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=F-150+Lightning&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 103, "Windshield Defrosting and Defogging Systems." An inoperative windshield defrosting and defogging system may decrease the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the electronic cabin coolant heater, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Ford F-150 Lightning is an innovative and capable electric truck, though buyers should evaluate their specific towing and charging needs against the available powertrain configurations. Inspecting the vehicle's service history and verifying that all manufacturer updates have been completed will help ensure a dependable ownership experience.
