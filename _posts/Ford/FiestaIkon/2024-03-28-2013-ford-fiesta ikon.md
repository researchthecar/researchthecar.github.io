**Introduction:**
The 2013 Ford Fiesta Ikon is a subcompact car available with a 1.6-liter Ti-VCT engine and a 5-speed manual transmission. It delivers 120 horsepower and 112 lb-ft of torque. The Fiesta Ikon is known for its fuel efficiency, sporty handling, and user-friendly interior. However, it also has a few reported issues.

**Engine**

**1.6-liter Ti-VCT**
- **Intake camshaft failure:** Premature wear on the intake camshaft lobes can lead to reduced engine power, rough idling, and possible engine damage.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the affected camshaft and associated components.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,500+
- **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual**
- **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

- **Rear suspension noise:** Excessive noise or vibrations from the rear suspension can be caused by worn shock absorbers or faulty suspension components.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of the affected parts.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $800+

**Electrical**

- **Battery drain:** Premature battery discharge can be caused by a parasitic draw from electrical components or a failing battery.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of the electrical system, including replacement of the battery if necessary.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $150 - $400+
- **Recalls:** 14S07 (Battery Malfunction), 15C16 (Electrical System)

**Exterior**

- **Hood latch failure:** Malfunctioning hood latches can prevent the hood from closing properly or staying closed while driving.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the hood latch assembly.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
- **Recalls:** 14S33 (Hood Latch)

**Interior**

- **HVAC control issues:** Malfunctioning HVAC controls can affect temperature adjustments, fan speed, or airflow direction.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair of the HVAC system, including replacement of faulty components if necessary.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600+
- **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 2013 Ford Fiesta Ikon is prone to engine issues, electrical problems, and certain suspension and exterior issues. The severity and cost of repairs can vary, with some issues being more significant and expensive to resolve.
