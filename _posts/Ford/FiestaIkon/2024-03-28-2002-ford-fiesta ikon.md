The 2002 Ford Fiesta Ikon is a subcompact hatchback that was available with two engine options: a 1.3-liter Zetec-SE engine and a 1.6-liter Zetec-SE engine. Both engines were paired with a 5-speed manual transmission. The Fiesta Ikon was praised for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and nimble handling, but it was also criticized for its lack of power and its cramped interior.

**Engine**

* **1.3-liter Zetec-SE**
    * **Blown head gasket**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from the exhaust
        * Solution: Replace the head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing belt failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Sudden loss of power, engine will not start
        * Solution: Replace the timing belt
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **1.6-liter Zetec-SE**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **5-speed manual**
    * **Clutch failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears
        * Solution: Replace the clutch
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, flickering lights, dead battery
        * Solution: Replace the alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine will not start, grinding noise when starting
        * Solution: Replace the starter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots on body panels, especially around wheel wells and door frames
        * Solution: Remove rust and apply anti-rust treatment
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard cracking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the dashboard, especially around the vents and glove box
        * Solution: Replace the dashboard
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2002 Ford Fiesta Ikon is a generally reliable vehicle, but it does have some common issues. The most serious issues are the blown head gasket and timing belt failure, which can both lead to catastrophic engine damage. The Fiesta Ikon is also prone to rust, especially around the wheel wells and door frames. The cost of fixing these issues can vary depending on the severity of the problem, but it is typically in the range of $100 to $1000+.
