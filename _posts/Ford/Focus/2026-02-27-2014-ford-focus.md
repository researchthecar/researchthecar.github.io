# 2014 Ford Focus Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2014 Ford Focus can be a solid used-car candidate when it has been maintained well, but shoppers should go in with a clear inspection checklist. Owner complaints most often point toward Transmission Shudder, Transmission Clutch Failure, and Engine Stalling, so the best examples are the ones with documented repairs, normal test-drive behavior, and no unresolved warning lights or repeat symptoms.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V735000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Focus&modelYear=2014)**
- **Issue:** The excessive vaccuum may result in an engine stall while driving, without warning and without the ability to restart the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the Powertrain Control Module. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V515000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Focus&modelYear=2014)**
- **Issue:** The excessive vacuum may result in an engine stall while driving, without warning and without the ability to restart the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the Powertrain Control Module. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V813000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Focus&modelYear=2014)**
- **Issue:** The incorrect signals could cause the vehicle to hesitate or the engine to stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will replace the current crimped splices with new splices, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V643000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Focus&modelYear=2014)**
- **Issue:** A door that is not securely latched could open while the vehicle is in motion, increasing the risk of injury to a vehicle occupant.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will replace the door latches with an improved part, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V698000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Hatchback/Liftgate:Lock](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Focus&modelYear=2014)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 206, "Door Locks and Door Retention Components." With only a single press needed, the driver may accidently unlatch the hatchback, increasing the risk of injury to any unsecured passengers in the back.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the Body Control Module with updated software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 14V525000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Focus&modelYear=2014)**
- **Issue:** A reduction in fuel pressure may result in a vehicle stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will replace the FDM, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 14V495000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Focus&modelYear=2014)**
- **Issue:** The incorrect signals could cause the vehicle to hesitate or the engine to stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will replace the current crimped splices with new splices, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 14V514000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Focus&modelYear=2014)**
- **Issue:** The incorrectly manufactured steering gears may cause impaired steering, including the loss of steering control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will replace the steering gears, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2014 Ford Focus is not automatically a vehicle to avoid, but it rewards careful shopping. Prioritize examples with service records and a clean inspection, and pay close attention to Transmission Shudder, Transmission Clutch Failure, and Engine Stalling during the test drive and pre-purchase inspection. If those areas check out and any open recalls have been completed by VIN, the vehicle is easier to evaluate on its overall condition rather than on model-year reputation alone.
