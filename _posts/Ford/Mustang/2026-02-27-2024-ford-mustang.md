# 2024 Ford Mustang Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Ford Mustang represents a complete redesign for the iconic pony car, bringing modern digital interfaces and robust performance options. While drivers generally praise its driving dynamics and powerful engine choices, early model-year feedback highlights a variety of initial quality glitches. Buyers shopping in the used market should pay close attention to electrical stability, touchscreen functionality, and mechanical components during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.3L EcoBoost Turbocharged Inline-4

A 2.3-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing roughly 315 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque, serving as the efficient entry-level powerplant. It offers peppy daily driveability, though used buyers should verify proper maintenance history on turbocharged systems.

### 5.0L Coyote V8

A naturally aspirated 5.0-liter V8 engine pushing out between 480 and 500 horsepower depending on the trim, delivering traditional muscle car performance. It remains a fan favorite, though isolated electronic and motive power complaints have been reported by early owners.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences unexpected loss of power or hesitation during acceleration or while cruise control is engaged.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic evaluation by a certified technician to check powertrain control modules and throttle body wiring.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed SelectShift Automatic

A 10-speed automatic transmission available across multiple trims designed for quick gear changes and highway fuel efficiency. While generally smooth, some owners note unusual noises or shifting delays.

**Transmission Whining and Delayed Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual whining noises heard while driving or idling, clicking sounds when shifting between Park and Drive, and delayed gear engagement.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission fluid levels and condition, and consult a dealer for potential software updates or internal component checks.

### 6-Speed Manual (MT82)

A traditional 6-speed manual transmission paired with the V8 engine for an engaging, hands-on driving experience. Buyers should carefully inspect the clutch and shifter feel during a test drive.

**Clutch and Shifter Assembly Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Premature wear or major mechanical failure involving the clutch assembly, or decorative shift knob trim detaching from the lever.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged clutch components or secure/replace the shift knob assembly.

## Suspension

### Rear Suspension Knuckles and Tires

Provides ride comfort and handling stability. Early models have seen occasional complaints regarding rear suspension noises and component alignment.

**Suspension Knocking and Tire Rubbing**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual knocking noises from the rear suspension or unexpected jolts and tire rubbing while driving at moderate speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect rear suspension castings and alignment components for defects or wear.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake Pedal

Responsible for slowing and stopping the vehicle. Physical pedal feel issues and unusual noises have been noted by a small number of drivers.

**Pedal Squeak and Floorboard Travel**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal feels loose, produces squeaking noises when depressed, or occasionally sticks down near the floorboard.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake linkage, pedal return springs, and master cylinder assembly for mechanical binding.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Center Display

Manages the vehicle's digital instrument cluster and central infotainment screen. Glitches in this system are among the more frequent owner complaints for this model year.

**Screen Freezing and Flickering**
- **Symptoms:** Center touchscreen flickers, goes completely blank, or reboots randomly, rendering climate controls and the backup camera temporarily unavailable.
- **Solution:** Perform software updates or replace faulty display modules at an authorized service center.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Rear Glass

Covers exterior glass and bonded mirrors. Several owners have reported fragile windshield glass and mirror detachment issues.

**Windshield Cracking and Rearview Mirror Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** Rearview mirror snaps off the mounting bracket under normal adjustment, cracking the windshield glass, or windshields developing stress cracks spontaneously.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged glass and ensure rearview mirror mounts are securely re-anchored.
- **Cost:** $1000

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V800000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Pedals And Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Mustang&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loose brake pedal can result in a loss of braking function, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake pedal and bracket assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V220000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Mustang&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source such as hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire. In addition, a loss of fuel may result in an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the low-pressure fuel supply line, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V394000: Power Train:Clutch Assembly:Linkage:Hydraulic Hose/Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Mustang&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A melted clutch pressure line can result in a loss of drive power, increases the risk of a crash. A brake fluid leak increases the risk of fire in the presence of an ignition source.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the clutch pressure line and repair it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V496000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Brake Fluid Low Warning:Switch/Sensor/Float](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Mustang&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid level sensor may not be properly configured after a Body Control Module (BCM) service update, resulting in a constantly illuminated brake fluid level warning light that can conceal a low brake fluid condition. An undetected low brake fluid condition can lead to a loss of braking function, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the body control module (BCM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V593000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Mustang&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of vehicle handling and steering control increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rear suspension knuckle as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V634000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System:Injectors](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Mustang&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install new fuel injector O-rings and O-ring support discs, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V802000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Mustang&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The Instrument Panel Cluster (IPC) may not illuminate at startup or while driving. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 101, "Control and Displays" and 102, "Transmission shift lever sequence, starter interlock, and transmission braking effect." An instrument panel display that does.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument panel cluster software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V315000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Mustang&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera image to delay, freeze, or not display when the vehicle is in reverse. A frozen or missing rearview camera image can reduce the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Ford Mustang remains an exciting performance choice, but used buyers should approach early production models with moderate caution. While major mechanical failures are relatively uncommon, numerous minor electrical issues, infotainment glitches, and glass-related complaints have surfaced. Prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the electronics, transmission operation, and glass integrity before finalizing a purchase.
