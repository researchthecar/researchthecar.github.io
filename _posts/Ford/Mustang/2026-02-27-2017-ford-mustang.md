# 2017 Ford Mustang Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Ford Mustang combines classic muscle car styling with modern performance, available with either efficient four-cylinder turbo power, a traditional V6, or roaring V8 engines. While it remains a popular choice for enthusiasts, used car buyers should carefully evaluate common mechanical and body integrity trouble spots before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.3L EcoBoost Turbocharged I4

The 2.3L EcoBoost turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces around 310 horsepower, offering a blend of efficiency and brisk acceleration. Buyers should watch closely for signs of coolant intrusion and rough idling.

**Coolant Intrusion**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle upon startup, engine misfires while driving at various speeds, and coolant levels dropping steadily without external leaks.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential cylinder head or engine block replacement if coolant intrusion is confirmed.

### 3.7L V6

The naturally aspirated 3.7L V6 engine delivers roughly 300 horsepower as the base powerplant for earlier models. It is generally regarded as a durable, straightforward engine option.

### 5.0L Ti-VCT V8

The Coyote 5.0L V8 pushes out over 430 horsepower, providing iconic muscle car performance and exhaust notes. While robust, isolated reports of head gasket failures and coolant loss require vigilance during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Head Gasket Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Engine failing to start, white smoke, overheating, and unexplained loss of coolant.
- **Solution:** Replace the blown head gasket and inspect the engine block for warping or corrosion.

## Transmission

### Getrag MT82 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission pairs with a mechanical clutch pedal for an engaging driver experience. Prospective owners should test all gears thoroughly and check for clutch pedal stickiness.

**Clutch and Gear Engagement Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Clutch pedal sticking to the floor, squeaking noises, failure to engage higher gears like 5th and 6th, or starter interlock switch failure.
- **Solution:** Replace failing clutch components, master/slave cylinders, or repair the clutch pedal engagement sensor.

### SelectShift 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission offers smooth cruising capabilities across various trims. It generally displays dependable mechanical operation when routine fluid changes are maintained.

## Electrical

### Back Over Prevention

Safety equipment generally performs well, though rear backup camera electrical faults are a documented complaint area.

**Backup Camera Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Rearview camera displaying distorted images, a blank screen, or failing to activate entirely when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Inspect wiring harnesses and replace the backup camera or associated display modules as required.

## Exterior

### Door Latch and Structure

Cabin hardware is generally robust, though unexpected door unlatching poses a notable safety concern on select vehicles.

**Unexpected Door Opening**
- **Symptoms:** Driver or passenger side doors randomly unlatching and popping ajar while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty door latch assemblies or locking mechanisms.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V779000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System:Hoses/Lines/Piping/Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Mustang&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A sudden loss of engine oil may cause engine failure, increasing the risk of a crash. Additionally, an oil leak, in the presence of an ignition source, increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will replace the engine oil cooler tube assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V529000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Mustang&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The air bag inflator within the passenger frontal air bag module may rupture in the event of a crash. An inflator rupture may result in metal fragments striking the vehicle occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V168000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Mustang&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The return spring for the driver side interior door handle may come loose, allowing the driver's door to unlatch in a side impact crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 206, "Door Locks and Door Retention Components." If the door unlatches in a crash it can increase the.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the driver-side interior door handle and repair the return spring, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V082000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Mustang&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera that displays a blank or distorted image can reduce the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the decklid wiring harness and/or replace the rearview camera, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V572000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Mustang&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera that displays an inverted, distorted, or blank image can reduce the driver’s view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rearview camera as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V614000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Mustang&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A corroded cable can break, preventing the seat belt from properly restraining an occupant, and increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace both front seat belt anchor pretensioner assemblies, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Ford Mustang is an exciting and iconic performance car, but used buyers should perform a thorough mechanical inspection before sealing the deal. Pay close attention to potential EcoBoost coolant issues, manual clutch operation, and functioning door latches to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
