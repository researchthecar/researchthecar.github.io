# 2022 Ford Mustang

### Overview
The 2022 Ford Mustang, a popular choice for performance and style enthusiasts, generally presents a strong reliability record. Across its various engine and transmission configurations, the Mustang demonstrates robust engineering. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the documented problems for the 2022 model year are relatively minor and often isolated. Owners can expect a dependable driving experience, with most reported concerns being manageable and not indicative of widespread systemic failures. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and prospective buyers.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Ford Mustang generally exhibits good reliability, with its core mechanical components (engine and transmission) performing well across both the 2.3L EcoBoost and 5.0L V8 engines, and the 10-speed automatic and 6-speed manual transmissions. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, less commonly, intermittent battery drain. These are typically resolvable through software updates or targeted diagnostics. Suspension, exterior, and interior components have shown no widespread significant problems. Overall, the 2022 Mustang is a dependable vehicle, with potential buyers advised to be aware of the minor electrical concerns.
