The 2016 Ford Mustang is a popular sports car known for its iconic styling, powerful engine options, and engaging driving experience. Available with a range of engines, including a 3.7L V6, a 2.3L EcoBoost inline-4, and a 5.0L Coyote V8, it caters to diverse performance preferences. The 3.7L V6 produces 300 hp and 280 lb-ft of torque, providing a spirited driving experience. The car is praised for its handling, acceleration, and classic muscle car appeal. However, some owners have reported issues with the infotainment system, interior quality, and certain mechanical components. Transmission options included a 6-speed manual and a 6-speed automatic.

**6-Speed Automatic**

*   **Harsh Shifting/Jerking**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Reports of harsh shifting, jerking, or clunking noises during gear changes, particularly when the transmission is cold. May also experience delayed engagement or slipping between gears.
    *   Solution: In some cases, a software update to the transmission control module (TCM) can resolve the issue. In other cases, the transmission may require internal repairs or replacement, though this is less common.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Software Update $100-200; Transmission repair $1,500 - $3,500+; Transmission replacement $3,000 - $5,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The MyFord Touch infotainment system can freeze, become unresponsive, or crash. This can affect navigation, audio, and climate control functions.
    *   Solution: Software updates can sometimes resolve these issues. In some cases, the APIM (Accessory Protocol Interface Module) may need replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Software Update $0-$150; APIM replacement $500 - $1,200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Ford Mustang appears to be a relatively reliable vehicle. The most reported issues seem to be related to the 6-Speed Automatic Transmission and the MyFord Touch infotainment system. While less common than some other vehicles, these issues can range from minor inconveniences to more costly repairs. Potential buyers should pay close attention to the transmission's shifting behavior and test all infotainment functions during inspection. The potential costs can vary significantly based on the severity of the problem, ranging from software updates to full transmission replacement.
