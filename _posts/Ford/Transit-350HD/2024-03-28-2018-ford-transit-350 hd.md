**Introduction:**

The 2018 Ford Transit-350 HD is a full-size van known for its spacious interior, powerful engine, and strong towing capacity. It offers two engine and transmission options: a 3.5L EcoBoost V6 engine paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission and a 6.7L Power Stroke V8 diesel engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. While the Transit-350 HD is generally reliable, it has encountered some issues in various components.

**Engine**

**3.5L EcoBoost V6**

* **Turbo Lag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay in acceleration, especially when overtaking or climbing hills.
    * Solution: Software update, turbocharger replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, knocking or rattling noises.
    * Solution: Intake valve cleaning, software update
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**6.7L Power Stroke V8 Diesel**

* **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Clogging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, limp mode, warning lights.
    * Solution: Manual DPF regeneration, DPF replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust.
    * Solution: Turbocharger replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**10-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifts, slipping gears.
    * Solution: Software update, transmission rebuild
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **No Major Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Springs Breaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or grinding noises, sagging rear end.
    * Solution: Leaf spring replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, difficulty starting, battery warning light.
    * Solution: Alternator replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after sitting for a few days.
    * Solution: Diagnosing and isolating the source of the drain, such as faulty modules or parasitic draws.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Body Panels:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Visible corrosion and rust spots on the exterior, especially around the wheel wells and lower panels.
    * Solution: Rust removal, touch-up paint, or body panel replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning AC or heater, uneven temperature distribution.
    * Solution: AC compressor or heater component replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Dashboard Lights Flickering:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent flickering or dimming of dashboard lights.
    * Solution: Electrical connections diagnosis and repairs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

The 2018 Ford Transit-350 HD has some potential issues, particularly with the 3.5L EcoBoost V6 engine, suspension, and electrical system. While some issues can be relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others, such as turbocharger failure or transmission rebuilds, can be more costly. It is important to note that not all vehicles will experience these issues, and the severity and frequency of problems can vary depending on driving habits and maintenance schedules.
