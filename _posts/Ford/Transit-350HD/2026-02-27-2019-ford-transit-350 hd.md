# 2019 Ford Transit-350 HD

### Overview
The 2019 Ford Transit-350 HD is a robust and versatile commercial van designed for demanding work environments. Generally, it exhibits good reliability, particularly in its core mechanical components. Owners and fleet managers often praise its durability and capability for heavy-duty tasks. However, like any vehicle, specific components can experience issues over time, especially under strenuous use. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns, excluding routine maintenance and normal wear and tear, to provide a clear picture of potential problem areas for the 2019 model year across its various configurations.

---


## Engine

### 3.7L V6
**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, overheating, visible leaks around hoses or water pump.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, clamps, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### 3.5L EcoBoost V6
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger inspection and potential replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Intake Manifold Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, check engine light, potential vacuum leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold gasket or manifold.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


### 3.2L Power Stroke Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, DPF warning light.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration or DPF cleaning/replacement.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000

**EGR System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, black smoke, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of EGR valve and cooler.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (6R80)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from transmission pan or cooler lines, potential shifting issues.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals, lines, or transmission pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $250 - $600

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering, slipping, or delayed engagement during gear changes.
- **Solution:** Torque converter replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,000

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Ford Transit-350 HD generally offers good reliability for its class, with the powertrain components being the most common areas for reported technical issues. The gasoline engines, particularly the EcoBoost, can experience turbocharger or intake manifold problems, while the diesel variant may face DPF or EGR system concerns. Transmission issues are less common but can involve fluid leaks or torque converter problems. Electrical gremlins like battery drain and infotainment glitches are also noted. However, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are typically robust. Regular maintenance and addressing early signs of trouble are key to ensuring long-term dependability for this workhorse van.
