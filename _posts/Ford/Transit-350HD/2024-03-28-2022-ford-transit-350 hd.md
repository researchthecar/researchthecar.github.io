**Introduction:**

The 2022 Ford Transit-350 HD is a full-size commercial van designed for heavy-duty applications. It offers a choice of two engine options: a 3.5L V6 PowerBoost full hybrid and a 7.3L V8. Transmission options include a heavy-duty 10-speed SelectShift automatic transmission and a 6-speed automatic transmission. The Transit-350 HD is praised for its spacious cargo area, towing capacity of up to 10,500 lbs, and versatile configurations. However, some common issues have been reported among owners.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6 PowerBoost Full Hybrid**

* **Battery Cooling Fan Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Overheating battery, reduced power, warning lights
    * Solution: Replace battery cooling fan
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Harness Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions, including starting problems, loss of power, or flickering lights
    * Solution: Repair or replace electrical harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**7.3L V8**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per injector
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, turbocharger noises
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
    * Recalls: 22V-361, 22V-395

**Transmission**

**10-Speed SelectShift Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough shifting, delayed shifts, slipping gears
    * Solution: Software update or transmission repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
    * Recalls: 22V-341
* **Torque Converter Lockup Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or shuddering, reduced fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Failures:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging, excessive bouncing, abnormal handling
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, dead battery, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replace battery or diagnose electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Lock Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate opens unexpectedly, difficulty securing tailgate
    * Solution: Replace tailgate lock mechanism
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezes, audio issues, navigation errors
    * Solution: Software update or system replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2022 Ford Transit-350 HD offers impressive hauling capabilities and functionality, it has faced some issues that have impacted owners. Engine and transmission problems, such as battery cooling fan failures and fuel injector issues, can be costly to repair. Suspension concerns, like leaf spring failures, can affect vehicle stability and handling. Electrical and interior issues, though less severe, can be frustrating and inconvenient. The severity of the issues varies, and some can be addressed with relatively simple fixes, while others may require significant repairs.
