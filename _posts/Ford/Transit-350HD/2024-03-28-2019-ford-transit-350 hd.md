**Introduction**

The 2019 Ford Transit-350 HD is a full-size van offered with a variety of engine and transmission options. It features a powerful engine, comfortable interior, and a spacious cargo area. However, like any vehicle, the Transit-350 HD has faced some issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, reduced engine power, engine overheating
Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Power Valve Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Decreased engine power, black smoke from exhaust
Solution: Replace power valve
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
Recalls: None Found

**6.7L V8**

**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, whistling noise, blue smoke from exhaust
Solution: Replace turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: 20V629, 21V416

**Transmission**

**10-Speed Automatic**

**Shift Solenoid Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, transmission slipping, check engine light
Solution: Replace shift solenoid
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Valve Body Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, transmission slipping, no drive or reverse
Solution: Replace valve body
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension Control Arm Bushings**
Description/Symptoms: Squealing or clunking noises, vibration, uneven tire wear
Solution: Replace control arm bushings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
Recalls: None Found

**Rear Suspension Leaf Springs**
Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, reduced load capacity
Solution: Replace leaf springs
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim headlights, electrical system malfunctions
Solution: Replace alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
Recalls: None Found

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery dies quickly, even when vehicle is not in use
Solution: Identify and repair source of parasitic drain
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on source
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Door locks not working, doors not locking or unlocking
Solution: Replace door lock actuator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
Recalls: 19V653

**Interior**

**Infotainment System Freeze**
Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen unresponsive, system freezes, software glitches
Solution: Update software or replace infotainment system
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
Recalls: 19V467

**Summary**

Overall, the 2019 Ford Transit-350 HD has faced some issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and suspension. While some of these issues are relatively minor, others can be more severe and costly to repair. It's important to be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision and to keep up with regular maintenance to mitigate the risk of experiencing them.
