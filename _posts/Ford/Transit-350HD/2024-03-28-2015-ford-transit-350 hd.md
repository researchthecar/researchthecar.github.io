**Introduction**

The 2015 Ford Transit-350 HD is a heavy-duty cargo van that offers a range of engine and transmission options. The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces 280 horsepower and 265 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.5-liter EcoBoost V6 generates 310 horsepower and 400 lb-ft of torque. Transmission choices include a 6-speed automatic and a 10-speed automatic. The Transit-350 HD has received praise for its spacious cargo area, powerful engines, and comfortable cabin. However, it has also been criticized for its high price and some reliability issues.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6**
    * **Cylinder Head Cracking**
        * Description/Symptoms: The cylinder head may crack, causing coolant leaks, overheating, and loss of power.
        * Solution: Replace the cylinder head.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: NHTSA Recall Number 19V-505
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner may fail, causing the timing chain to skip or break. This can result in catastrophic engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain and tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.5L EcoBoost V6**
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger may fail, causing a loss of power and increased fuel consumption.
        * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
        * Recalls: NHTSA Recall Number 16V-717

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or slip when shifting gears.
        * Solution: Repair or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **10-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shuddering**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shudder or vibrate during acceleration or deceleration.
        * Solution: Update the transmission control module software.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free (under warranty)
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Springs**
    * **Sagging**
        * Description/Symptoms: The rear leaf springs may sag over time, reducing load capacity and ride height.
        * Solution: Replace the leaf springs.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, requiring frequent replacement.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair any electrical components that are drawing excessive power.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator may fail, causing the battery to discharge and the vehicle to lose power.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Hinge Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The tailgate hinges may fail, causing the tailgate to sag or become unhinged.
    * Solution: Replace the tailgate hinges.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, causing uneven temperatures or a lack of airflow.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair the climate control components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Belt Buckle Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: The seat belt buckles may stick or fail to latch properly.
    * Solution: Replace the seat belt buckles.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 per buckle
        * Recalls: NHTSA Recall Number 21V-768

**Summary**

The 2015 Ford Transit-350 HD has received mixed reviews regarding its reliability. Some owners have experienced severe issues with the engine and transmission, while others have had relatively few problems. However, the overall cost of potential repairs can be significant, especially for major component failures. Buyers should carefully consider the potential risks and costs before purchasing this vehicle.
