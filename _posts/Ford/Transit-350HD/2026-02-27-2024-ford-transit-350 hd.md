### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Ford Transit-350 HD demonstrates a strong initial reliability profile, consistent with the Transit line's established reputation for durability in commercial applications. While specific powertrain options like the 3.5L EcoBoost V6 and 2.0L EcoBlue Diesel, along with the 10-speed automatic transmission, show no significant widespread issues, vigilance is advised for the electrical system. Minor concerns such as intermittent infotainment screen unresponsiveness and potential battery drain due to parasitic draw have been noted, with solutions typically involving software updates or targeted electrical diagnosis. The suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally robust. Overall, the Transit-350 HD is a reliable choice for demanding commercial use, with most potential issues being minor and addressable.
