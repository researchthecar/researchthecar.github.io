**Introduction:**

The 2020 Ford Transit-350 HD is a heavy-duty commercial van with a spacious cargo area and a robust engine. It is available with two engine options: a 3.5L V6 EcoBoost gas engine (310 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque) and a 7.3L V8 gas engine (350 horsepower and 468 lb-ft of torque). The van comes with a 6-speed automatic transmission as standard.

People appreciate the Transit-350 HD for its powerful engines, comfortable ride, and high towing and payload capacities. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6 EcoBoost**

**Intake Manifold Valve Sticking:**
Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, check engine light illuminated.
Solution: Replacement of intake manifold valves.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+

**7.3L V8**

**No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Slipping:**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed response, check engine light illuminated.
Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V400000

**Suspension**

**Rear Leaf Springs Breaking:**
Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, excessive bouncing, clunking noises.
Solution: Replacement of rear leaf springs.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain:**
Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, difficulty starting.
Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system, potentially including battery replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+

**Starter Motor Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Engine fails to start, clicking noises.
Solution: Replacement of starter motor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+

**Exterior**

**Rust on Rear Doors:**
Description/Symptoms: Corrosion forming on rear doors, especially around the bottom edges.
Solution: Rust repair and preventive measures (e.g., undercoating).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Interior**

**Center Console Latch Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Center console lid latch breaks, causing difficulty opening or closing.
Solution: Replacement of center console latch.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+

**HVAC Control Module Malfunction:**
Description/Symptoms: Climate control not functioning properly, fan speed issues, temperature not regulating.
Solution: Replacement of HVAC control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Summary:**

The 2020 Ford Transit-350 HD is generally a reliable vehicle, but like any vehicle, it has some potential issues to be aware of. The most common issues reported by owners are related to the transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. The severity of the issues varies, with some being minor inconveniences and others requiring costly repairs. It is important to note that not all vehicles will experience these issues, and regular maintenance can help prevent or minimize their occurrence.
