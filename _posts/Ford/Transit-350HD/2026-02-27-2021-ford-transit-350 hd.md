**Intermittent Glitches**

- **Cost:** $0 - $150 (for potential software update labor)


### Battery Drain
**Accessory Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dying prematurely, especially when vehicle is off, potentially due to parasitic draw from accessories.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, repair or replacement of faulty accessory or module.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Ford Transit-350 HD demonstrates commendable reliability, with its powertrain and chassis components generally performing without significant widespread issues. The most commonly cited concerns tend to be minor electrical gremlins, such as occasional infotainment system glitches or potential battery drain issues related to accessories, which are often resolvable with software updates or targeted diagnostics. For fleet operators and individual owners, proactive checks of the electrical system and keeping software up-to-date are recommended. Overall, the Transit-350 HD is a dependable workhorse with a solid track record for its intended commercial applications.
