**Introduction**

The 2021 Ford Transit-350 HD is a heavy-duty cargo van that offers plenty of space and power for hauling large loads. It's available with multiple engine and transmission options, including a 3.5L V6, a 3.5L EcoBoost V6, and a 6.7L V8 diesel engine. The engines are paired with either a 6-speed automatic or a 10-speed automatic transmission.

The Transit-350 HD is a popular choice for businesses and contractors due to its versatility and reliability. It's also a solid option for families who need a spacious and powerful vehicle for hauling people and gear.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6 Engine**

**Leaking Intake Manifold Gaskets**
Description/Symptoms: Leaks coolant, causing overheating and loss of power
Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**3.5L EcoBoost V6 Engine**

**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, black smoke from exhaust, whistling noises
Solution: Replace turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000
Recalls: None Found

**6.7L V8 Diesel Engine**

**Fuel Injector Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, poor fuel economy, increased emissions
Solution: Replace fuel injectors
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Torque Converter Shudder**
Description/Symptoms: Vibrations when accelerating
Solution: Replace torque converter
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
Recalls: None Found

**10-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Shifting Problems**
Description/Symptoms: Hard or delayed shifting, slipping gears
Solution: Update transmission software, replace transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Rear Leaf Springs**
Description/Symptoms: Sagging or broken leaf springs
Solution: Replace leaf springs
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery dies quickly
Solution: Replace battery, check for parasitic draw
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Rust**
Description/Symptoms: Rust spots on body panels, frame
Solution: Repair or replace rusted parts
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies based on extent of damage
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**HVAC System Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating not working
Solution: Repair or replace HVAC components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Ford Transit-350 HD is a highly capable cargo van with a variety of engine and transmission options. However, it has been known to experience some issues, including leaking intake manifold gaskets, turbocharger failure, and fuel injector failure. These issues can be costly to fix, so it's important to factor them into the purchase price of the vehicle. Overall, the Transit-350 HD is a solid choice for businesses and families who need a powerful and versatile vehicle, but it's important to be aware of the potential issues it may experience.
