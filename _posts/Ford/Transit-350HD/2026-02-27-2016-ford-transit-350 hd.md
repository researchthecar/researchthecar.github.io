# 2016 Ford Transit-350 HD

### Overview
The 2016 Ford Transit-350 HD is a robust and capable heavy-duty van designed for commercial use. Its reliability is generally considered good, with a focus on durability for demanding tasks. Common areas of concern, while not widespread, tend to be related to specific powertrain components under heavy load or high mileage. Maintenance and timely repairs are crucial for maximizing the lifespan and performance of this vehicle. Owners should be aware of potential issues with certain engine and transmission configurations, particularly those subjected to rigorous daily operation. Overall, the Transit-350 HD offers a solid foundation for businesses requiring a dependable workhorse.

### 3.5L EcoBoost V6
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles, overheating, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace leaking hoses, gaskets, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


### 3.2L Power Stroke I5 Diesel
**DPF (Diesel Particulate Filter) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, failed emissions tests.
- **Solution:** DPF regeneration or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500

**EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, white smoke from exhaust, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the EGR cooler.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,000

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Ford Transit-350 HD demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly with its standard V6 gasoline engine and automatic transmission. The EcoBoost V6 and the 3.2L Power Stroke diesel, while offering performance advantages, have a few documented issues that owners should monitor, such as turbocharger problems and DPF/EGR concerns on the diesel. Electrical gremlins like battery drain and sensor failures are not uncommon but are generally repairable. Suspension, exterior, and interior components are typically robust for commercial duty. Regular maintenance and addressing potential issues promptly will ensure the longevity and dependability of the Transit-350 HD for demanding commercial applications.
