The 1996 Ford E-350 Econoline is a full-size van that was available with a variety of engine and transmission options. It was a popular choice for families and businesses alike, and it is still a common sight on the road today.

The E-350 Econoline was offered with a choice of three engines: a 4.9L inline-six, a 5.8L V8, and a 7.5L V8. The 4.9L inline-six produced 205 horsepower and 290 lb-ft of torque, while the 5.8L V8 produced 215 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque. The 7.5L V8 was the most powerful engine available, producing 245 horsepower and 380 lb-ft of torque.

The E-350 Econoline was also offered with a choice of three transmissions: a 4-speed automatic, a 5-speed manual, and a 6-speed manual. The 4-speed automatic was the most popular transmission choice, while the 5-speed manual was only available with the 4.9L inline-six engine. The 6-speed manual was only available with the 7.5L V8 engine.

Overall, the 1996 Ford E-350 Econoline is a reliable and versatile vehicle. It is a good choice for families and businesses alike, and it is still a common sight on the road today.

**Engine**

**4.9L Inline-Six**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant and oil leaks, overheating, engine misfire
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Head gasket failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant and oil leaks, overheating, engine misfire
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5.8L V8**

* **Spark plug blowout**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfire, loss of power, increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs, check for head gasket damage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake manifold gasket failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant and oil leaks, overheating, engine misfire
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**7.5L V8**

* **Head gasket failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant and oil leaks, overheating, engine misfire
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **EGR valve failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, engine stalling
    * Solution: Replace EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic (AOD)**

* **Overdrive failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: No power in overdrive gear, slipping or shuddering
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Manual (MT5OD)**

* **Clutch failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping or shuddering, difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Synchro failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace synchronizers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual (ZF S6-40)**

* **Overdrive failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: No power in overdrive gear, slipping or shuddering
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shift fork failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace shift fork
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

    * Recalls: None Found
* **Tie rod failure**

    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1996 Ford E-350 Econoline is a generally reliable vehicle, but it is not without its issues. The most common issues with the E-350 Econoline are related to the engine and transmission. Overall, the E-350 Econoline is a good choice for families and businesses alike, and it is still a common sight on the road today.
