**Introduction:**

The 1995 Ford E-350 Econoline is a full-size van known for its spaciousness, versatility, and towing capabilities. It was available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including:

* 4.9L V8 (205 hp, 300 lb-ft torque) paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission
* 5.8L V8 (235 hp, 335 lb-ft torque) paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission

**Engine**

**4.9L V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, reduced engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **EGR Valve Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, rough idling, decreased fuel efficiency
    * Solution: Replace EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay in shifting gears, loss of power
    * Solution: Repair or rebuild transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Ball Joint Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clicking or popping sounds when turning, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering lights, difficulty starting, battery warning light illuminated
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after extended periods of non-use
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening doors
    * Solution: Replace door handles
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on the dashboard
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Belt Retractor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat belt does not fully retract
    * Solution: Replace seat belt retractor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1995 Ford E-350 Econoline is generally considered a reliable vehicle, but it has some common issues that can be expensive to fix. Engine issues such as intake manifold gasket failure can lead to overheating and other problems. Transmission issues such as slipping can also be costly to repair. Suspension components like ball joints may wear out over time, and electrical issues such as alternator failure and battery drain can be annoying and potentially dangerous. However, interior issues are relatively minor and exterior issues are mostly cosmetic.
