**Introduction**

The 1993 Ford E-350 Econoline is a full-size van offered with a variety of engine and transmission options. Engine choices include the 4.9L inline-six, 5.0L V8, and 7.5L V8. Transmission options include a 4-speed automatic and a 5-speed manual. The E-350 has a towing capacity of up to 7,500 lbs. and is known for its spacious interior and versatility. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of common issues.

**Engine**

**4.9L Inline-Six**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve Cover Gasket Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from valve cover area
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or ticking noise from engine
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5.0L V8**

* **Head Bolt Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, loss of compression, overheating
    * Solution: Replace head bolts and head gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Spark Plug Blowout**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, decreased power
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs and inspect cylinder heads for damage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Exhaust Manifold Cracks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Exhaust leaks, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace exhaust manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**7.5L V8**

* **Fuel Pump Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace fuel pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Ignition Coil Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace ignition coil
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Water Pump Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks from water pump area
    * Solution: Replace water pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic (E4OD)**

* **Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping gears, difficulty shifting
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Overheating**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks, burning smell
    * Solution: Check for leaks and repair or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shift Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, transmission not shifting properly
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Whining or grinding noise from transmission
    * Solution: Inspect transmission and repair or replace as needed
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Syncro Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise
    * Solution: Replace synchronizers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaking Shocks or Struts**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor handling, bouncing or swaying
    * Solution: Replace shocks or struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Ball Joint Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise when turning or braking, excessive steering play
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sway Bar Link Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise when driving over bumps, instability
    * Solution: Replace sway bar links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery light on, flickering lights, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine won't start, grinding noise
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, battery warning light on
    * Solution: Inspect electrical system for parasitic draw, repair or replace faulty components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source of the drain
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: Body panels showing signs of corrosion
    * Solution: Inspect extent of rust, repair or replace body panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies widely depending on the location and extent of rust
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dent**
    * Description/Symptoms: Body panels have dents or creases
    * Solution: Professional body repair or DIY repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on size and location of dent
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Broken Taillights**
    * Description/Symptoms: Taillights not illuminating, cracked or missing lenses
    * Solution: Replace taillights
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Broken HVAC Controls**
    * Description/Symptoms: HVAC system not functioning properly, buttons not working
    * Solution: Replace HVAC control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cracked Dashboard**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard has cracks or warping
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Belt Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat belts not retracting properly, buckles not engaging
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace seat belt components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1993 Ford E-350 Econoline has a variety of common issues, including engine gasket leaks, transmission problems, suspension wear, and interior malfunctions. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific issue and the condition of the vehicle. While the E-350 is a popular and versatile vehicle, it is important to be aware of potential issues before purchasing one.
