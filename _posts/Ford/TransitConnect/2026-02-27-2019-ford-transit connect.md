# 2019 Ford Transit Connect

### Overview
The 2019 Ford Transit Connect is a versatile compact commercial van known for its practicality and fuel efficiency. As a workhorse vehicle, its reliability is a key factor for businesses. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, examining common problem areas across its various configurations. The Transit Connect generally offers a solid ownership experience, but like any vehicle, certain components and systems can be prone to specific failures. Understanding these potential issues can help owners and fleet managers anticipate and address them proactively, minimizing downtime and repair costs.

---


## Engine

### 1.5L EcoBoost (Gasoline)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant level warning light, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace leaking hoses, seals, or the water pump. Ensure proper coolant level and system pressure.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling or hissing sounds from the engine bay, increased fuel consumption, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and repair or replace the turbocharger, intercooler, or associated plumbing. Check for oil supply issues.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


### 1.5L EcoBlue Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, illuminated DPF warning light, rough idling.
- **Solution:** Perform a forced DPF regeneration. If regeneration fails, cleaning or replacement of the DPF may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200

**AdBlue System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** AdBlue warning light, reduced engine power, potential inability to start the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Check AdBlue fluid level and quality. Diagnose and repair or replace the AdBlue injector, pump, or sensor.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (6F15)
**Harsh Shifting / Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, delayed engagement, slipping between gears.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid level and condition. Software updates or internal transmission repairs may be required.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000


### 6-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Replace the clutch kit (disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or rattling noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replace worn suspension bushings in control arms, sway bars, or subframes.
- **Cost:** $250 - $600


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, dim lights, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently.
- **Solution:** Identify parasitic draw from faulty modules, accessories, or wiring. Test and replace the battery if necessary.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, Bluetooth connectivity issues, audio problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates for the infotainment system. In some cases, module replacement may be needed.
- **Cost:** $200 - $700


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Doors
**Sliding Door Mechanism Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty opening or closing sliding doors, sticking, or jamming.
- **Solution:** Lubricate and clean the door tracks and rollers. Replace damaged or worn components of the mechanism.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Ford Transit Connect demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly for its intended commercial use. The most frequently reported technical concerns revolve around the powertrain, specifically coolant leaks and turbocharger issues with the gasoline engine, and DPF/AdBlue system problems with the diesel variant. Transmission issues, while not pervasive, can include harsh shifting in automatics and clutch wear in manuals. Electrical gremlins like battery drain and infotainment glitches are also noted. Exterior issues are minor, often related to the sliding door mechanism. Interior components are generally robust. Proactive maintenance and addressing early signs of powertrain issues are recommended for optimal long-term reliability.
