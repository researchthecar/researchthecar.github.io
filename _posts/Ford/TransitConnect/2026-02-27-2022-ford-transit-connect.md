# 2022 Ford Transit Connect Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Ford Transit Connect is a versatile compact cargo and passenger van well-suited for urban commercial use and small businesses. Prospective buyers evaluating a used model will find a vehicle designed primarily for utility and maneuverability, though examining powertrain choices and standard vehicle systems remains an important part of the shopping process.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Duratec I4

The standard 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces around 162 horsepower and 144 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with front-wheel drive and offers efficient, straightforward operation for daily delivery routes.

### 1.5L EcoBoost Turbo I4

The available 1.5-liter turbocharged three-cylinder EcoBoost engine provides enhanced low-end torque and efficiency. It delivers balanced performance suited for stop-and-go city driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed SelectShift Automatic

This 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear changes and helps optimize fuel economy across varying payloads. Routine fluid maintenance supports long-term durability in commercial applications.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V442000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit+Connect&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to display a blank image, or the image may remain on the display after the backing event has ended. Both of these situations can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the rearview camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Ford Transit Connect provides a practical, efficient option for buyers needing compact commercial transport. Reviewing service records and inspecting the chosen powertrain will help ensure a reliable ownership experience.
