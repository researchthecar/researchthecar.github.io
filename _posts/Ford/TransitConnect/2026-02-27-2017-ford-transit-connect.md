# 2017 Ford Transit Connect Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Ford Transit Connect is a versatile compact van suited for small businesses and urban delivery needs. While it offers nimble handling and practical cargo space, used-car buyers should carefully review potential mechanical and electrical quirks. Reviewing maintenance records and performing a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help uncover persistent issues before buying.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Duratec I4

The naturally aspirated 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine produces 169 horsepower and 171 lb-ft of torque, serving as the standard powerplant for the lineup. It generally provides dependable daily service, though minor accessory component wear can occur over time.

### 1.6L EcoBoost Turbo I4

The turbocharged 1.6-liter EcoBoost four-cylinder engine generates 178 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque, offering stronger low-end acceleration. Buyers should ensure proper cooling system maintenance due to the higher thermal loads of turbocharged operation.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed SelectShift Automatic

A 6-speed automatic transmission handles power delivery across the lineup. While generally smooth, some owners have reported hesitation and slipping concerns that warrant a careful test drive.

**Transmission Slippage**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle hesitates to accelerate, feels like it is driving over rumble strips, or experiences intermittent slipping while driving at various speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified transmission specialist inspect the unit for internal wear or fluid pressure loss, and update or rebuild as necessary.

## Brakes

### Anti-lock Brake and Hydraulic System

The braking system provides adequate stopping power, but intermittent hydraulic and electronic ABS module failures have been reported by some owners.

**Brake Module and Component Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Brakes may fail to release properly due to ABS module communication issues, or front brakes can experience locking and rotation failure.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the ABS control module and brake hydraulics; replace faulty modules or stuck components and reprogram through a dealer if required.

## Steering

### Electronic Power Assist Steering

Electronic power steering provides light effort at low speeds, though rare electronic assist failures can lead to sudden loss of power steering.

**Power Steering Assist Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering assistance accompanied by heavy steering resistance, making the vehicle extremely difficult to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic power assist steering assembly and wiring harness; replace failed steering gear or control modules.

## Exterior

### Body Control and Door Electronics

electrical issues involving door latches, power windows, and accessory controls can affect convenience features and battery drain.

**Door Latch and Power Accessory Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Interior electronics fail to shut off when exiting the vehicle, door locks become inoperative, or power windows malfunction intermittently.
- **Solution:** Inspect door latch microswitches and body control modules, replacing faulty latches or relays that prevent proper power down.

### Glass and Roof Panels

Exterior panels and glass are generally durable, but panoramic sunroof-equipped models require careful inspection for wind noise and sealing integrity.

**Panoramic Roof Separation**
- **Symptoms:** Wind noise, water leaks, or complete detachment of the panoramic sunroof glass panel while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect roof glass seals and bonding frames for cracks or separation; reseal or replace damaged panoramic roof assemblies.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V305000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit+Connect&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The front driver and/or passenger seat belt pretensioner initiators could contain an improper generant mix ratio that may prevent the seat belt pretensioners from deploying in the event of a crash. In the event of a crash, the front driver and/or passenger seat belt pretensioners may not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace seat belt assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V260000: Visibility:Sun/Moon Roof Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit+Connect&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the panoramic roof panel separates from the vehicle while it is being driven, it may increase the risk of an injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will remove, clean, and reinstall the panoramic roof panel, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V376000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Gear Position Indication (Prndl)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit+Connect&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Either scenario increases the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the underhood shifter cable bushing, and add a protective cap, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V413000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Gear Position Indication (Prndl)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit+Connect&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Either scenario increases the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the under hood shift bushing and add a protective cap over the shift cable bushing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V294000: Visibility:Fixed Panaramic Roof/Sky Light Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit+Connect&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A roof panel that detaches while driving can create a road hazard and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will remove, clean, and reinstall the panoramic roof panel, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V572000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit+Connect&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera that displays an inverted, distorted, or blank image can reduce the driver’s view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rearview camera as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Ford Transit Connect is a practical and efficient option for work or passenger transport, but buyers should approach with informed caution. Pay close attention during a test drive to verify smooth transmission operation, responsive steering without electronic assist faults, and proper functioning of all doors and electrical accessories. Ensuring that all safety campaigns and recalls have been completed will greatly improve ownership peace of mind.
