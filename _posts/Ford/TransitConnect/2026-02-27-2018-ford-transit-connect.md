# 2018 Ford Transit Connect Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When evaluating a used 2018 Ford Transit Connect, prospective buyers will find a versatile compact van suited for commercial fleets or urban family hauling. However, owners have reported recurring mechanical and electrical concerns that require careful attention during a pre-purchase inspection. While the vehicle offers nimble handling and practical packaging, paying close attention to steering and braking components will help ensure a reliable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Duratec I4

The 2.5-liter naturally aspirated inline-four engine produces approximately 169 horsepower and 171 lb-ft of torque. Paired with a smooth-shifting automatic transmission, this standard engine provides adequate power for daily city driving and light commercial tasks with standard reliability.

### 1.6L EcoBoost Turbo I4

The optional 1.6-liter turbocharged EcoBoost inline-four delivers around 178 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. It offers stronger low-end acceleration and improved fuel economy, though buyers should verify regular maintenance history for optimal turbocharger longevity.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed SelectShift Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission delivers power to the front wheels across all trims. While generally capable, it requires attentive maintenance as certain owners have experienced torque converter shudder and unexpected downshifting issues.

**Torque Converter and Shudder Issues**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may exhibit shuddering during acceleration, unexpected downshifts, or complete torque converter failure without prior warning indicators.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection, replacement of the torque converter, or a complete transmission rebuild/replacement depending on internal wear.

## Brakes

### ABS and Hydraulic Brakes

The service brake system provides reliable stopping power under normal conditions, but intermittent ABS module failures can cause dangerous wheel lock-ups.

**Brake Locking and ABS Module Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Front brakes or individual wheels lock up unexpectedly while driving or fail to release properly, causing tire damage and driving hazards.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the braking system, replace faulty brake calipers, or replace the malfunctioning ABS hydraulic control module.

## Steering

### Power Steering Rack

The electronic power-assisted steering system provides light effort at low speeds, but the steering rack assembly is a known trouble spot that can fail prematurely.

**Power Steering Rack Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power steering assist fails completely without warning while driving, making the steering wheel very heavy and difficult to turn.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed power steering rack and pinion assembly.
- **Cost:** $2,700

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical accessories function adequately overall, though minor nagging issues like frequent headlight bulb failures can occur.

**Headlight Bulb Burnout**
- **Symptoms:** The driver's side front low beam headlight bulb burns out repeatedly in short succession.
- **Solution:** Inspect the headlight wiring harness and socket for corrosion or high resistance, then replace the bulb and socket if necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V376000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Gear Position Indication (Prndl)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit+Connect&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Either scenario increases the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the underhood shifter cable bushing, and add a protective cap, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V229000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit+Connect&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** When the back seat cushion is rotated from the stowed position to the upright position, the seat cushion may not remain locked into place when under load, such as in a crash or a sudden stop. In the event of a crash, if the seat back moves, the seat occupant has an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 22V413000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Gear Position Indication (Prndl)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit+Connect&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Either scenario increases the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the under hood shift bushing and add a protective cap over the shift cable bushing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V294000: Visibility:Fixed Panaramic Roof/Sky Light Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit+Connect&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A roof panel that detaches while driving can create a road hazard and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will remove, clean, and reinstall the panoramic roof panel, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V442000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit+Connect&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to display a blank image, or the image may remain on the display after the backing event has ended. Both of these situations can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the rearview camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V572000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit+Connect&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera that displays an inverted, distorted, or blank image can reduce the driver’s view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rearview camera as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Ford Transit Connect is a highly practical and configurable compact van, but used buyers should proceed with a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Particular attention should be paid to the steering rack, the ABS braking module, and transmission shift quality to avoid expensive out-of-pocket repairs after purchase.
