**Introduction:**

The 2010 Ford E-250 is a full-size cargo or passenger van offered with a variety of engine and transmission options. The standard engine is a 4.6L V8 with 255 horsepower and 293 lb-ft of torque. A 5.4L V8 with 300 horsepower and 365 lb-ft of torque is also available, as is a 6.8L V10 with 305 horsepower and 420 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 4-speed automatic, a 5-speed automatic, and a 6-speed automatic. The E-250 is praised for its spacious interior, towing capacity, and powerful engine options. However, it is criticized for its poor fuel economy and lack of modern safety features.

**Engine**

**4.6L V8**

* **Spark Plug Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfiring, rough idling, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine coolant leaks, overheating, reduced performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**5.4L V8**

* **Cam Phaser Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine knocking, rough idling, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace cam phaser
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.8L V10**

* **Spark Plug Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfiring, rough idling, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears
    * Solution: Replace transmission fluid and filter, may require transmission rebuild
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, hard shifting
    * Solution: Replace solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering, slipping, overheating
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Ball Joint Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, loose steering
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery drain
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to start vehicle
    * Solution: Replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Body Panels:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion, unsightly appearance
    * Solution: Sand, repair, and repaint affected areas
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sliding Door Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door does not open or close properly
    * Solution: Repair or replace door mechanism
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC System Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No heat or air conditioning
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair HVAC system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noises coming from dashboard
    * Solution: Secure loose components or replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2010 Ford E-250 has several potential issues, including engine spark plug failure, transmission shifting problems, suspension ball joint failure, and electrical alternator failure. Some issues, such as the dashboard rattle, are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. However, other issues, such as cam phaser failure and torque converter failure, can be more serious and costly to repair. Overall, while the E-250 offers a powerful and spacious vehicle, potential buyers should be aware of its potential issues before making a purchase decision.
