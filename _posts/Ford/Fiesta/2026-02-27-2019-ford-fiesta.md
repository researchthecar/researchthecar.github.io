# 2019 Ford Fiesta

### Overview
The 2019 Ford Fiesta, while generally a competent and popular subcompact car, exhibits a mixed reliability record across its various components and powertrains. Owners have reported a few recurring issues, particularly concerning the automatic transmission and certain electrical systems. However, the core mechanicals, especially the manual transmission variants and the smaller displacement engines, tend to be more robust. This analysis breaks down common problems by category, providing insights into potential repair costs and solutions for the 2019 model year, aiming to equip potential buyers and current owners with relevant technical data.

---


## Engine

### 1.0L EcoBoost (Petrol)
**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant loss, visible leaks around hoses and water pump.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty hoses, water pump, or thermostat housing.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


### 1.5L TDCi (Diesel)
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Regeneration Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, rough idling.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration, cleaning, or replacement of the DPF.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (Powershift)
**Harsh Shifting and Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden jolts during gear changes, hesitation, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Software update, transmission fluid flush, or internal component repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000

**Clutch Pack Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, inability to engage gears, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the clutch pack assembly.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing and Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive, system crashes or reboots randomly.
- **Solution:** Software update, or replacement of the infotainment unit.

### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start, battery dies prematurely.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw from modules or accessories, repair or replacement of faulty components.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Ford Fiesta presents a generally acceptable reliability profile, with its manual transmission variants and petrol engines being the most dependable. The primary concern lies with the automatic (Powershift) transmission, which has a documented history of issues leading to significant repair costs. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and occasional battery drain, are also points to consider. While the suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely trouble-free, potential buyers should be aware of the transmission and electrical system vulnerabilities. Regular maintenance and careful inspection of these areas are recommended.
