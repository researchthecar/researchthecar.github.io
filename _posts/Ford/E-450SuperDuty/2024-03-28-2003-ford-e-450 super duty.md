**Introduction:**

The 2003 Ford E-450 Super Duty is a heavy-duty van designed for commercial and personal use. It is available with a choice of engines and transmissions, including a 7.3L V8 diesel engine and a 5-speed automatic transmission. The E-450 Super Duty is known for its spacious cargo area, powerful engine, and robust construction. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of potential issues.

**Engine**

**7.3L V8 Diesel**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:** **Description/Symptoms:** Engine misfiring, poor fuel economy, reduced power. **Solution:** Replace faulty fuel injectors. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400-$800. **Recalls:** 04V112000
* **Turbocharger Failure:** **Description/Symptoms:** Lack of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust. **Solution:** Replace faulty turbocharger. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500-$3,000.
* **Oil Cooler Line Leak:** **Description/Symptoms:** Leaking oil, loss of oil pressure. **Solution:** Replace leaking oil cooler line. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200-$400. **Recalls:** 03V319000
* **Glow Plug Failure:** **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty starting in cold weather. **Solution:** Replace faulty glow plugs. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100-$200. **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:** **Description/Symptoms:** Inability to shift smoothly, loss of power. **Solution:** Repair or replace faulty transmission components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500-$3,000.
* **Shift Solenoid Failure:** **Description/Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed shifting. **Solution:** Replace faulty shift solenoid. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200-$400.
* **Torque Converter Lock-up Problems:** **Description/Symptoms:** Shuddering or vibration during acceleration. **Solution:** Repair or replace faulty torque converter. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500-$1,000.

**Suspension**

* **Control Arm Bushing Failure:** **Description/Symptoms:** Noises from the suspension, poor handling. **Solution:** Replace worn control arm bushings. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200-$400.
* **Ball Joint Failure:** **Description/Symptoms:** Clunking noises from the suspension, loose steering. **Solution:** Replace faulty ball joints. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100-$200 per joint.
* **Leaf Spring Breakage:** **Description/Symptoms:** Dropping rear of the vehicle, poor handling. **Solution:** Replace broken leaf springs. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300-$500 per spring.

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** **Description/Symptoms:** Battery draining quickly, even when the vehicle is turned off. **Solution:** Test and replace faulty electrical components (e.g., alternator, starter). **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200-$600.
* **Lighting Malfunctions:** **Description/Symptoms:** Headlights, taillights, or interior lights not working properly. **Solution:** Repair or replace faulty wiring or light bulbs. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100-$300.
* **Instrument Panel Failure:** **Description/Symptoms:** Gauges, odometer, or other displays not working properly. **Solution:** Repair or replace faulty instrument cluster. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300-$700.

**Exterior**

* **Rust:** **Description/Symptoms:** Formation of rust on body panels, especially near wheel wells and bumpers. **Solution:** Clean and treat rust spots, apply rust protection. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Dependent on severity.
* **Dent Resistance:** **Description/Symptoms:** Body panels can be easily dented. **Solution:** Repair or replace damaged panels. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500-$2,000 per panel.

**Interior**

* **Door Panel Separation:** **Description/Symptoms:** Door panels may separate from the door. **Solution:** Reattach door panels. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100-$200.
* **Climate Control Problems:** **Description/Symptoms:** A/C or heater not working properly. **Solution:** Repair or replace faulty climate control components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200-$600.

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2003 Ford E-450 Super Duty is a reliable vehicle with a few potential issues to be aware of. The engine and transmission components can be expensive to repair, but the suspension, electrical, and interior issues are typically less severe and costly. Regular maintenance and proper use can help prevent or mitigate some of these problems.
