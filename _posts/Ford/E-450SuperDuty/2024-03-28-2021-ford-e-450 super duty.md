**Introduction**

The 2021 Ford E-450 Super Duty is a heavy-duty commercial vehicle powered by a 7.3L V8 gasoline engine mated to a 6-speed automatic transmission. It offers a payload capacity of up to 7,850 pounds and a towing capacity of 22,500 pounds. The E-450 Super Duty is highly praised for its strong engine performance, durability, and spacious interior. However, it has some drawbacks such as its poor fuel economy and lack of advanced safety features.

**Engine**

**7.3L V8 Gasoline**

* **Cylinder Head Cracking:** Symptoms include excessive coolant loss, white smoke from exhaust, and overheating. Solution: Replace cylinder heads. Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
* **Fuel Injector Failure:** Symptoms include rough idling, engine stalling, and reduced fuel efficiency. Solution: Replace fuel injectors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:** Symptoms include delayed or inconsistent gear shifting, loss of power, and whining noises. Solution: Replace transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:** Symptoms include sagging or uneven ride height, clunking noises, and loss of control. Solution: Replace leaf springs. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
* **Shock Absorber Failure:** Symptoms include excessive bouncing, poor handling, and noise. Solution: Replace shock absorbers. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Symptoms include dimming lights, battery discharge, and difficulty starting. Solution: Replace alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Battery Drain:** Symptoms include difficulty starting or slow engine crank. Solution: Inspect and tighten battery terminals or replace battery. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Rusted Frame:** Symptoms include visible rust on the vehicle frame. Solution: Inspect and treat rust. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Symptoms include erratic temperature control, insufficient heating or cooling, and strange noises. Solution: Inspect and repair or replace climate control system components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Summary**

The 2021 Ford E-450 Super Duty has some notable issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and suspension. While some issues can be minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be severe and costly. It's important for owners to be aware of these potential problems and address them promptly to ensure safety and reliability.
