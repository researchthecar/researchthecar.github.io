**2019 Ford E-450 Super Duty**

The 2019 Ford E-450 Super Duty is a heavy-duty van offered with three engine and two transmission options. The standard engine is a 6.8L V10 gas engine producing 305 horsepower and 420 lb-ft of torque. Optional engines include a 7.3L V8 gas engine producing 350 horsepower and 468 lb-ft of torque, and a 6.7L Power Stroke V8 diesel engine producing 330 horsepower and 825 lb-ft of torque. The standard transmission is a 6-speed automatic, with an optional 10-speed automatic available on diesel models.

The E-450 Super Duty is praised for its impressive towing and payload capacities, as well as its comfortable interior and ample cargo space. However, it is criticized for its fuel economy, poor handling, and sluggish acceleration with the V10 engine.

**Engine**

**6.8L V10 Gas Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, engine overheating, rough idle, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Spark Plug Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, rough idle, decreased fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**7.3L V8 Gas Engine**

* No Issues Found

**6.7L Power Stroke V8 Diesel Engine**

* **EGR Valve Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor engine performance, reduced power, increased emissions
    * Solution: Replace EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, black smoke from exhaust, high-pitched whine
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping transmission, delayed shifting
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**10-Speed Automatic**

* No Issues Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, uneven tire wear, reduced ride comfort
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, dimming lights, premature battery failure
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system issue
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Frame:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on the vehicle frame, weakening the structure
    * Solution: Repair rusted areas and apply anti-corrosion treatment
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the dashboard while driving
    * Solution: Remove and reinstall dashboard components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Air Conditioning Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Lack of cold air, intermittent cooling, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace air conditioning compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Ford E-450 Super Duty has some notable issues, particularly with the 6.8L V10 gas engine and 6-speed automatic transmission. The most severe issue is the turbocharger failure on the 6.7L diesel engine, which can be costly to repair. The rust on frame issue is also a concern, as it can compromise the structural integrity of the vehicle. Overall, the E-450 Super Duty has some reliability issues that potential buyers should be aware of before making a purchase.
