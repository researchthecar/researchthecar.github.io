**Introduction:**

The 2009 Ford E-450 Super Duty is a heavy-duty van offering exceptional towing and payload capacities. It is available with a wide range of engine and transmission options, catering to diverse needs. Some of the popular engine choices include the 7.3L V8 Power Stroke diesel and the 6.8L V10 gas engine. Transmission options include a 5-speed automatic and a 6-speed manual. The E-450 Super Duty is appreciated for its robust performance, ample cargo space, and customizable configurations. However, like any vehicle, it has faced some common issues over the years.

**Engine**

**6.8L V10 Gas**
***Spark Plug Failure***
Description/Symptoms: Misfires, rough idling, poor fuel economy
Solution: Replace faulty spark plugs
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

***Intake Manifold Gasket Failure***
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating
Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+

**7.3L V8 Power Stroke Diesel**
***Fuel Injector Failure***
Description/Symptoms: Engine hesitation, reduced power, increased fuel consumption
Solution: Replace failed fuel injectors
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
Recalls: N/A

***Turbocharger Failure***
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke
Solution: Replace or rebuild turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+
Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**
***Shifting Problems***
Description/Symptoms: Hesitation, slipping, or delayed shifts
Solution: Repair transmission or replace valve body
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000+

**Suspension**

***Leaf Spring Failure***
Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, poor handling
Solution: Replace leaf springs
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+

***Shock Absorber Failure***
Description/Symptoms: Bouncy ride, poor handling
Solution: Replace shock absorbers
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Electrical**

***Alternator Failure***
Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, loss of battery power
Solution: Replace alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+

***Starter Motor Failure***
Description/Symptoms: Engine fails to crank
Solution: Replace starter motor
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

***Window Regulator Failure***
Description/Symptoms: Power windows do not operate
Solution: Replace window regulator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

***HVAC Blower Motor Failure***
Description/Symptoms: No air circulation
Solution: Replace blower motor
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Summary:**

The 2009 Ford E-450 Super Duty has faced some common issues, such as spark plug failure, intake manifold gasket failure, and transmission problems. While these issues can be costly to fix, they are not as severe compared to engine or transmission failures on other vehicles. Overall, the E-450 Super Duty remains a reliable and highly capable workhorse with a strong reputation in its segment.
