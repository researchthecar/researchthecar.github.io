**Introduction:**
The 2010 Ford E-450 Super Duty is a heavy-duty cargo van known for its spacious interior, powerful engine, and durable construction. It's available with various engine and transmission options, including a 6.8L V10 gasoline engine and a 6-speed automatic transmission. Despite its capabilities, the E-450 Super Duty has reported several common issues.

**Engine**

**6.8L V10 Gasoline Engine**
* **Spark Plug Failure:** Premature spark plug failure can cause misfires, rough idling, and reduced engine power. Replacement spark plugs costs approximately $100 - $200+.
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:** A faulty intake manifold gasket can lead to coolant and/or oil leaks. Repair involves replacing the gasket and costs around $300 - $600+.

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
* **Transmission Slipping:** Slipping gears or delayed engagement indicates transmission issues. Repairs may involve replacing solenoids or overhauling the transmission, costing $1500 - $3000+.
* **Torque Converter Shudder:** Vibration while accelerating may indicate a failing torque converter. Replacement costs range from $800 - $1200+.

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:** Excessive weight or overloading can cause leaf springs to break, affecting vehicle stability and handling. Replacing leaf springs costs approximately $400 - $800+.
* **Front-End Alignment Issues:** Misaligned tires can cause uneven wear and handling problems. Alignment costs vary depending on the severity, typically around $80 - $150.

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Electrical issues, such as aftermarket accessories or a malfunctioning alternator, can lead to rapid battery drain. Diagnosis and repairs vary based on the source of the issue.
* **Headlight Switch Failure:** The headlight switch may fail, causing intermittent or complete loss of headlights. Replacement costs around $200 - $300+.

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Malfunction:** Sticking or broken door handles can be a common issue, especially in cold weather. Replacing handles costs approximately $100 - $250+.
* **Rust on Frame and Undercarriage:** Exposure to harsh elements can lead to rust on the frame and undercarriage, compromising structural integrity. Rust removal and prevention measures can cost $500 - $1500+.

**Interior**

* **Window Regulator Failure:** Power window regulators may fail, causing windows to get stuck or fall off track. Replacing regulators costs around $300 - $500+.
* **AC/Heater Blowing Cold Air:** Malfunctioning AC or heater systems can result from faulty blend doors or a failed blower motor. Repairs can range from $200 - $800+.

**Summary:**
The 2010 Ford E-450 Super Duty offers impressive capabilities but has several common issues. Engine concerns include spark plug failure and intake manifold gasket leaks, while transmission problems involve slipping gears and torque converter shudder. Suspension issues include leaf spring breakage and alignment problems. Electrical issues range from battery drain to headlight switch failure. Exterior issues include door handle malfunctions and rust formation. Interior concerns include window regulator failures and AC/heater problems. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific problem.
