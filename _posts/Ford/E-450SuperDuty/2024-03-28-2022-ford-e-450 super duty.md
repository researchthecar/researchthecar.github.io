**Introduction:**

The 2022 Ford E-450 Super Duty is a heavy-duty commercial van powered by a 6.8L V8 engine paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission. It offers impressive capabilities with a payload capacity of up to 7,500 lbs and a towing capacity of up to 10,000 lbs. Drivers appreciate its spacious interior, reliable performance, and advanced safety features. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of issues.

**Engine**

* **6.8L V8**

**Issue:** Engine Overheating
* Description/Symptoms: Engine runs hot, coolant leaks, overheating warning light on
* Solution: Replace coolant pump, repair or replace radiator
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V571

**Transmission**

* **10-Speed Automatic**

**Issue:** Transmission Slipping
* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, delayed or no response from transmission, grinding noises
* Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
* Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, slow cranking, electrical components malfunction
* Solution: Replace or repair faulty alternator or battery
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
* Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside headlight housings
* Solution: Replace headlights
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle**
* Description/Symptoms: Rattling or buzzing sound from dashboard area
* Solution: Replace or tighten loose components, apply adhesive
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Ford E-450 Super Duty has some reported issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. While some issues can be relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others may be more severe and costly. It's important for owners to be aware of potential problems and address them promptly to ensure optimal performance and longevity of their vehicle.
