**Introduction to the 2023 Ford E-450 Super Duty**

The 2023 Ford E-450 Super Duty is a heavy-duty commercial van available with a 6.8L gasoline engine or a 7.3L gasoline engine. Both engines are paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission. The E-450 Super Duty is a capable workhorse with a spacious cargo area, impressive towing and payload capacities, and a host of convenient features. However, some issues have been reported with the vehicle.

**Interior**

**Dashboard**

**Loose or Rattling Dashboard Components:**
Description/Symptoms: Rattle or vibration from the dashboard, especially when driving on rough roads.
Solution: Tightening or replacing loose components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Climate Control System**

**Inoperative Climate Control Unit:**
Description/Symptoms: Climate control system does not respond or functions erratically.
Solution: Replacing the climate control unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Power Windows**

**Slow or Inoperative Power Windows:**
Description/Symptoms: Power windows operate slowly or do not function at all.
Solution: Repairing or replacing faulty switches, motors, or wiring.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Ford E-450 Super Duty has a few reported issues, mainly related to the interior. The cost of these repairs is moderate, with most issues being resolved for less than $500. Overall, the E-450 Super Duty is a reliable and capable workhorse, but potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase.
