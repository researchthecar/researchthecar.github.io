# 2017 Ford E-450 Super Duty

### Overview
The 2017 Ford E-450 Super Duty is a robust commercial vehicle designed for heavy-duty applications, often utilized as a cutaway chassis for various upfits like shuttle buses, RVs, and delivery vans. Its primary strengths lie in its sturdy construction and powerful engine options, which are generally reliable for demanding work. However, like any vehicle in its class, specific components can experience issues over time, particularly under strenuous use. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and fleet managers.

---


## Engine

### 6.8L Triton V10
**Intake Manifold Gasket Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, engine overheating, rough idle, potential misfires.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold gaskets.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Spark Plug Blow-Out**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, rough running, ticking noise from engine, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Requires specialized tools for repair or replacement of spark plug threads.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### 5.4L Triton V8
**Camshaft Position Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, rough idle, poor acceleration, stalling.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the camshaft position sensor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Transmission

### 5R110W Automatic
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping, shuddering, delayed engagement, overheating.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Solenoid Pack Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, transmission not shifting into certain gears, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the transmission solenoid pack.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Leaf Spring Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, uneven ride height, clunking noises over bumps.
- **Solution:** Replacement of broken leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per spring


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, electrical system malfunctions, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Wiring Harness Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical problems, non-functioning accessories, check engine lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of damaged wiring sections.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Trim
**Rust and Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Visible rust on body panels, frame, and undercarriage, especially in salt-belt regions.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment, panel replacement, or undercoating.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1000+ (depending on severity)


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents or weak airflow, regardless of fan speed setting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Ford E-450 Super Duty demonstrates a generally solid reliability profile for its class, with its powertrain components being the most frequently cited areas for potential issues. The 6.8L V10 engine, while powerful, can be prone to intake manifold gasket leaks and spark plug blow-out issues. The 5R110W transmission, though robust, may experience torque converter or solenoid pack problems. Suspension components, particularly leaf springs, can wear under heavy loads. Electrical issues like alternator failure and wiring harness problems are also noted. Rust can be a concern in corrosive environments. Overall, with diligent maintenance and addressing these specific potential issues, the E-450 remains a capable workhorse.
