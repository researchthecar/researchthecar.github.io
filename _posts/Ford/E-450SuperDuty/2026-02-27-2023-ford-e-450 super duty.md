## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Intermittent instrument cluster issues**
- **Symptoms:** Gauges flickering, warning lights illuminating without cause, or temporary loss of display.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of the instrument cluster or related wiring harness.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200

**Battery drain issues**
- **Symptoms:** Premature battery discharge, difficulty starting the vehicle after sitting.
- **Solution:** Tracing parasitic draws, checking alternator output, and potentially replacing the battery or faulty modules.
- **Cost:** $150 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Ford E-450 Super Duty demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its powertrain and chassis components, which are engineered for demanding commercial use. The 7.3L V8 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission are generally trouble-free. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically concerning intermittent instrument cluster malfunctions and potential battery drain problems. These are not typically indicative of fundamental design flaws but rather isolated component failures or installation issues common in specialized vehicle builds. For fleet operators, proactive electrical system checks and prompt attention to any warning lights are recommended to maintain optimal uptime and operational efficiency.
