**Introduction:**

The 2017 Ford E-450 Super Duty is a heavy-duty van offered with a range of engine and transmission options. It boasts a spacious cargo area and impressive towing capacity. Drivers appreciate its reliability and durability, while some concerns have been raised regarding certain components.

**Engine**

**7.3L V8 Gas**
* **Intake Manifold Runner Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, reduced engine power, and potential stalling.
    * Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold runners.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Spark Plug Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfiring, rough idling, and decreased fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Replacement of faulty spark plugs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.8L V10 Gas**
* **Oil Pump Drive Shaft Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of oil pressure, engine overheating, and potential seizure.
    * Solution: Replacement of the oil pump drive shaft.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**TorqShift 6-Speed Automatic**
* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid from the transmission pan or lines.
    * Solution: Tightening or replacing the leaking component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Harsh Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Abrupt or delayed gear shifts, accompanied by jerks or shuddering.
    * Solution: Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement. In severe cases, transmission rebuild or replacement may be needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Broken or sagging leaf springs, resulting in decreased stability and handling.
    * Solution: Replacement of the leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly or fails to hold a charge, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and replacement of faulty components, such as alternator, battery, or electrical wiring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 17V435000
* **Faulty HVAC System:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning heater, air conditioner, or ventilation system.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of faulty components within the HVAC system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Plastic Trim Panel Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling plastic trim panels, creating noise and reducing interior quality.
    * Solution: Tightening or replacement of the affected trim panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Windows failing to operate properly, either due to faulty switches, wiring, or motors.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and replacement of the defective components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlight assemblies, potentially impairing visibility.
    * Solution: Replacement of affected headlights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rust Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on body panels, especially in areas exposed to moisture and salt.
    * Solution: Rust treatment and touch-up paint. In severe cases, panel replacement may be necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Ford E-450 Super Duty has a relatively low frequency of reported issues, with the severity and cost of repairs varying depending on the specific problem. Some common issues include intake manifold runner problems, spark plug issues, transmission fluid leaks, and rear leaf spring failures. While these issues can be inconvenient and costly to address, they do not generally pose significant reliability concerns. Overall, the E-450 Super Duty remains a solid choice for heavy-duty van applications.
