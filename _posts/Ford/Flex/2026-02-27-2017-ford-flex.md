# 2017 Ford Flex Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Ford Flex offers a distinct, boxy wagon design with generous interior room and smooth road manners, making it a popular choice for families. However, prospective buyers should review certain known mechanical and electrical pain points—ranging from backup camera glitches to more serious powertrain maintenance items—before purchasing a used model.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L Duratec V6

The naturally aspirated 3.5-liter V6 engine produces around 287 horsepower and provides adequate daily motivation for the large crossover. While generally durable, buyers must monitor its internal water pump design, as failure can lead to catastrophic engine damage if coolant mixes with engine oil.

**Internal Water Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaking into the engine oil, engine overheating, or sudden engine shutdown while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the weep hole regularly for coolant traces and replace the internal water pump proactively if high mileage is reached.

### 3.5L EcoBoost V6

The twin-turbocharged 3.5-liter EcoBoost V6 generates a robust 365 horsepower, offering rapid acceleration and confident passing power. It pairs exclusively with all-wheel drive, though turbocharged plumbing and cooling systems require attentive maintenance.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed SelectShift Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly across front-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. Routine fluid changes help preserve shift quality over higher mileage.

**Gear Shifting Jolts and Popping Noises**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual popping noises and noticeable physical jolts or hesitation when the transmission changes gears.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the transmission control module, fluid condition, and mechanical linkages for wear.

## Suspension

### Rear Suspension and Toe Links

The suspension provides a compliant, comfortable ride quality suitable for road trips, but heavy towing or high mileage can accelerate wear on rear suspension joints.

**Suspension Wobble and Rear Toe Link Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle wandering while driving, unexpected lane changes, or a wobbling sensation from the rear end.
- **Solution:** Inspect rear suspension components and toe links for structural integrity and excessive play.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera and Infotainment

Electrical accessories generally function well, though touchscreen displays and backup camera linkages are prone to intermittent failure over time.

**Backup Camera Display Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rearview camera image appears distorted, goes completely blank, or shows a blue screen of death when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Check camera wiring connections or replace the tailgate-mounted backup camera assembly.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Standard safety features include multiple airbags and stability control systems, though owner reports highlight occasional latch malfunctions.

**Seat Belt Latch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Seat belts failing to lock properly or unbuckling unexpectedly while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt buckles and latch mechanisms for damage and replace faulty assemblies.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V072000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Flex&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A fractured rear toe link will cause a sudden change in vehicle handling and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear suspension toe links, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V925000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Flex&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak, in the presence of an ignition source, increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will inspect, and if necessary, replace the turbocharger oil supply tubes, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V695000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Flex&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera that fails to properly display an image can reduce the driver’s view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rearview camera as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V732000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Flex&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Door trim that detaches while driving can create a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair or replace the B-pillar trim as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Ford Flex remains an appealing and highly functional family cruiser thanks to its spacious cabin and unique styling. Used car shoppers should carefully inspect the vehicle's service history for coolant leaks, water pump maintenance, and rear suspension wear to ensure long-term reliability.
