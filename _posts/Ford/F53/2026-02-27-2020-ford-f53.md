### Standard Electrical System
**Intermittent Dash Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Various warning lights on the dashboard may illuminate or flicker without an apparent cause.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of the body control module (BCM) or related sensors. Software updates or component replacement may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

**Accessory Power Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Some accessories or auxiliary power outlets may not function intermittently or at all.
- **Solution:** Check fuses, relays, and wiring harnesses for the affected circuits. May require BCM diagnosis.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400
