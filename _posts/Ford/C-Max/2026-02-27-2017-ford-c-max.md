# 2017 Ford C-Max

### Overview
The 2017 Ford C-Max, a compact multi-activity vehicle, generally offers a solid ownership experience with a focus on practicality and fuel efficiency, particularly in its hybrid variants. While most owners report satisfaction, like any vehicle, certain areas have seen more attention than others. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of focus for owners and prospective buyers. We will examine the powertrain, suspension, electrical systems, and other key components to offer a comprehensive reliability picture.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Atkinson-Cycle I-4 (Hybrid)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant level warning light, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### 2.0L Atkinson-Cycle I-4 (Plug-in Hybrid)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant level warning light, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### 1.5L EcoBoost I-4 (Gasoline)
**Cooling System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Engine overheating, coolant loss, visible leaks from hoses or radiator.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty hoses, thermostat, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Ford C-Max demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly in its hybrid powertrains which are generally robust. The gasoline engine variants have seen some reports of cooling system issues and the automatic transmission can occasionally exhibit hesitation. Electrical gremlins, primarily related to the infotainment system and occasional battery drain, are the most commonly cited non-routine issues. Suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free from widespread problems. Owners should be mindful of potential coolant leaks and infotainment system glitches, but for the most part, the C-Max is a dependable choice, especially for those prioritizing fuel economy.
