# 2017 Ford C-Max Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2017 Ford C-Max can be an intriguing prospect for buyers seeking a practical, efficient hybrid or plug-in hybrid hatchback. While overall owner feedback is relatively limited, prospective owners should still carefully evaluate how the powertrain and supporting systems perform during a thorough test drive. Reviewing common owner reports helps establish a clear baseline of what to watch out for as these vehicles age.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L I-4 Hybrid / Energi

This 2.0-liter Atkinson-cycle four-cylinder engine is paired with an electric motor to generate a combined net horsepower of approximately 188. It generally offers solid efficiency and reliable daily operation, though proper diagnostic checks are recommended for peace of mind.

**Unexpected Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly lose power, stall at very low speeds, and fail to respond to accelerator pedal inputs without illuminating warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic evaluation by a qualified technician is required to identify potential fuel system, throttle body, or hybrid powertrain faults.

## Transmission

### Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (eCVT)

The eCVT smoothly blends gas and electric power to drive the front wheels. It is generally regarded as a robust and dependable unit in Ford's hybrid lineup.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V413000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Gear Position Indication (Prndl)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=C-Max&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Either scenario increases the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the under hood shift bushing and add a protective cap over the shift cable bushing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Ford C-Max presents a compelling option for efficiency-minded used car shoppers, provided the specific vehicle checks out mechanically. Be sure to perform a comprehensive test drive paying close attention to low-speed throttle response and overall powertrain smoothness before finalizing your purchase.
