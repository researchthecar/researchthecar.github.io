# 2016 Ford C-Max Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Ford C-Max is a practical hybrid hatchback offering strong fuel economy and a spacious interior for city commuters. While generally efficient, used car buyers should review owner-reported concerns regarding powertrain stability and electronics to ensure a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L I4 Atkinson-Cycle Hybrid

This 2.0-liter inline-four Atkinson-cycle engine pairs with an electric motor to produce a net 188 horsepower. It provides efficient daily driving performance, though owners should monitor for occasional throttle body concerns.

**Throttle Actuator Control**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience an intermittent inability to control vehicle speed accompanied by vibrations and diagnostic trouble codes such as P2112.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic throttle body for contamination or wear, and replace the throttle actuator assembly if necessary.

## Transmission

### eCVT Automatic Transmission

The electronically controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (eCVT) routes power smoothly from the hybrid powertrain to the front wheels. It is generally reliable, though owners should verify smooth transition between gas and electric modes during a test drive.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Issues with brake wear and corrosion have been noted by some owners in early-mileage driving conditions.

**Brake Rotor Rust and Premature Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle owners have reported premature rusting and degradation of brake components, requiring early service.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the brake pads and rotors for excessive corrosion or wear, and replace components as needed.
- **Cost:** $1000.00

## Electrical

### Infotainment Touchscreen

Electrical and infotainment glitches can affect the usability of the center console and associated features.

**Touchscreen and GPS Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The dashboard touchscreen may freeze, fail to display the rearview camera image, or cause GPS and audio system malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Perform a system reset, update the software, or replace the infotainment control module if hardware failure is diagnosed.

### Backup Camera System

Rear visibility equipment can experience operational glitches and is subject to active safety recalls.

**Rearview Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera display may fail to activate or intermittently show a blank screen when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Inspect the camera wiring harness and display module, and perform necessary manufacturer recall repairs when parts become available.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V052000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=C-Max&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Improperly welded apron joints may reduce the front-end structural integrity of the vehicle, increasing the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the apron joints, repairing the vehicle, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V413000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Gear Position Indication (Prndl)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=C-Max&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Either scenario increases the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the under hood shift bushing and add a protective cap over the shift cable bushing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V695000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=C-Max&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera that fails to properly display an image can reduce the driver’s view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rearview camera as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Ford C-Max is an appealing choice for buyers seeking hybrid efficiency, but thorough inspection is recommended. Pay close attention to the service history, braking components, and proper functioning of the electronics and rearview camera before finalizing a purchase.
