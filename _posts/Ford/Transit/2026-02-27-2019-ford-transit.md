# 2019 Ford Transit Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Ford Transit is a versatile and widely used commercial van, offering various configurations to suit different business and passenger transport needs. When shopping for a used 2019 model, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate both powertrain performance and potential owner-reported quirks. While commercial vehicles often experience heavy use, understanding common trouble spots can help secure a reliable workhorse.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L V6

The 3.7-liter naturally aspirated V6 produces around 275 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, serving as the standard baseline engine. It provides adequate power for lighter duty cycles and has a reputation for straightforward, dependable operation.

### 3.5L EcoBoost V6

The 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged EcoBoost V6 delivers approximately 310 horsepower and 400 lb-ft of torque, offering robust low-end pulling power. It is ideal for heavy loads and towing, though buyers should verify regular maintenance history on turbocharged plumbing.

### 3.2L Power Stroke I5 Diesel

The 3.2-liter Power Stroke inline-5 turbodiesel generates about 185 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque, designed for high-mileage commercial applications. It offers strong torque delivery and fuel efficiency, though complex emissions equipment requires careful inspection.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission pairs with the van's engine lineup to deliver smooth shifting under various loads. Regular fluid changes are recommended to maintain longevity in heavy commercial service.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V103000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the front driveshaft detaches from the vehicle, drive power will be lost, increasing the risk of a crash. The detached driveshaft may also create a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash for following traffic.
- **What to do:** XL will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front driveshaft with an updated one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V439000: Structure:Emergency:Escape/Egress/Exit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Temperature and vibration stresses may cause the tempered glass roof hatch to shatter. If the tempered glass roof hatch shatters, glass fragments may fall onto passengers, increasing the risk of an injury.
- **What to do:** LA West will notify owners, and dealers will replace the hatch lids, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V250000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The seat belt retractor block out zone may have not been set correctly, potentially resulting in a seat belt that may not properly secure the occupant. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 209, "Seat Belt Assemblies." - **What to do:** National Van Builders will notify owners, and dealers will replace the retractors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V631000: Parking Brake:Conventional:Mechanical:Linkage And Cable](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Parking brake failure can result in unintended vehicle movement, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger side parking brake cable with a cable that provides sufficient clearance to the damper shield, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V324000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fractured studs may allow the wheel to detach, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and if necessary, replace the rear axle shaft assembly, axle shaft mounting bolts, and rear wheel lug nuts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V222000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The seat belt retractor block out zone may have not been set correctly, potentially resulting in a seat belt that may not properly secure the occupant. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 209, "Seat Belt Assemblies." An unsecured occupant has an increased risk of injury in the.
- **What to do:** TransitWorks will notify owners, and dealers will replace the retractors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V229000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** When the back seat cushion is rotated from the stowed position to the upright position, the seat cushion may not remain locked into place when under load, such as in a crash or a sudden stop. In the event of a crash, if the seat back moves, the seat occupant has an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V572000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Transit&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera that displays an inverted, distorted, or blank image can reduce the driver’s view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rearview camera as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Ford Transit remains a strong contender in the full-size commercial van market due to its diverse engine choices and adaptable configurations. Prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the service history, particularly for units used in intensive fleet operations, to ensure long-term dependability.
