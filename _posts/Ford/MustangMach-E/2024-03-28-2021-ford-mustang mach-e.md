**Introduction**

The 2021 Ford Mustang Mach-E is an all-electric crossover SUV that has quickly become popular with consumers. It is praised for its sleek design, powerful performance, and advanced technology. However, like any vehicle, the Mustang Mach-E has experienced some issues since its release.

**Suspension**

**Squeaky Suspension**
Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or popping sounds coming from the suspension when going over bumps or uneven surfaces.
Solution: Replacement of suspension components or lubrication of suspension joints.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery drain even when the vehicle is not in use.
Solution: Software updates, replacement of battery cells, or replacement of electrical components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $5,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Infotainment System Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Blank or unresponsive infotainment screen, freezing or lagging software, or malfunctioning buttons.
Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment system.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Windshield Cracking**
Description/Symptoms: Cracks or chips in the windshield that can spread and impair visibility.
Solution: Replacement of the windshield.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
Recalls: Mustang Mach-E NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V319000

**Interior**

**Climate Control Malfunctions**
Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent or ineffective heating or cooling, or malfunctioning AC system.
Solution: Replacement of climate control components or software updates.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2021 Ford Mustang Mach-E is a well-received vehicle, it has experienced some issues, including battery drain, infotainment system problems, and windshield cracking. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, and some have resulted in recalls. Overall, the Mustang Mach-E remains a solid choice for those looking for an electric SUV, but potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues before making a purchase.
