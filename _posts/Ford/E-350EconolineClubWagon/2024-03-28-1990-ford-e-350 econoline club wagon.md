**Introduction**

The 1990 Ford E-350 Econoline Club Wagon is a full-size van that was available with a variety of engine and transmission options. It offered spacious seating for up to 15 passengers and ample cargo capacity. However, like any vehicle, it has faced some issues that owners and mechanics have reported.

**Engine**

**5.8L V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaks coolant, causes overheating, and affects engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **EGR Valve Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Causes rough idling, stalling, and poor fuel economy
    * Solution: Clean or replace EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Pump Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls or fails to start, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace fuel pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**7.5L V8**

* **Blown Head Gaskets**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, engine misfires
    * Solution: Replace head gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve Seat Recession**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of compression, poor performance, engine knocking
    * Solution: Rebuild cylinder heads
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slips**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, delayed engagement, loss of power
    * Solution: Overhaul or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping, shuddering, overheating
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, slipping, burning smell
    * Solution: Replace clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shifter Linkage Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, loose shifter
    * Solution: Adjust or replace shifter linkage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Ball Joint Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or popping noises, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, rough ride
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**

    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine cranks slowly or not at all
    * Solution: Replace starter solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery runs out of power quickly
    * Solution: Inspect and repair faulty electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Switch Failure**

**Interior**

    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1990 Ford E-350 Econoline Club Wagon has some common issues that owners may encounter.
