# 2024 Ford Bronco Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Ford Bronco Sport delivers a retro-styled, compact footprint with standard all-wheel drive, making it a popular choice for daily commuting and weekend adventures. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported concerns regarding powertrain electrical behaviors, fuel system notices, and minor cabin or exterior trim flaws. While many drivers enjoy its capability and features, checking service records and verifying completed safety updates is essential before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L EcoBoost Turbocharged Inline-3

The 1.5-liter turbocharged three-cylinder engine generates approximately 181 horsepower and 190 lb-ft of torque, serving as the standard power plant for most trims. It offers an efficient balance of fuel economy and pep, though buyers should monitor for reported fuel system and electrical stumbles.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report unexpected engine stalling, illuminated warning lights, and sudden loss of motive power during active driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect diagnostic trouble codes, powertrain control modules, and fuel delivery systems for potential faults.

**Fuel Injector Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have expressed apprehension regarding potential fuel injector leaks or fuel odors under the hood, posing safety concerns.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines, injectors, and any applicable manufacturer recall bulletins to ensure proper sealing and updated components.

### 2.0L EcoBoost Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces 250 horsepower and 277 lb-ft of torque, providing substantially quicker acceleration and stronger passing power for higher trims like the Badlands. It is generally praised for its pep, though routine maintenance is key.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission channels power to the standard four-wheel-drive system across all trims, offering smooth highway cruising and responsive gear selection.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Charging System

Manages cabin connectivity, displays, and electrical accessories; software updates often resolve minor glitches.

**Infotainment and Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note intermittent display freezing, Apple CarPlay or Android Auto connectivity failures, and occasional battery discharge issues.
- **Solution:** Update the vehicle software to the latest calibration and test the battery and charging system health.

## Exterior

### Body Glass and Trim

Exterior glass and trim elements require careful inspection for stress cracks or seal failures.

**Windshield and Quarter Glass Cracks**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported spontaneous windshield cracking or rear quarter panel glass separation due to factory adhesive issues.
- **Solution:** Inspect the windshield and bonded glass areas closely, and ensure any related recall or adhesion campaigns have been addressed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V267000: Electrical System:Body Control Module/Bcm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Bronco+Sport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An undetected low battery charge can result in a loss of electrical accessories such as hazard lights, or cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will recalibrate the body control module and power train control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V683000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Control Module (Tcm/Pcm/Tecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Bronco+Sport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An undetected low battery charge can result in a loss of electrical accessories such as hazard lights, or cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the body control module and powertrain control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V594000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer:Motor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Bronco+Sport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The front windshield wiper motor may become inoperative, causing the wipers to fail. Inoperative windshield wipers can reduce visibility in certain conditions, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the windshield wiper motor as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V158000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Control Module (Tcm/Pcm/Tecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Bronco+Sport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An undetected low battery charge can result in a loss of electrical accessories such as hazard lights, or cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will recalibrate the body control module and power train control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V442000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Bronco+Sport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to display a blank image, or the image may remain on the display after the backing event has ended. Both of these situations can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the rearview camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V467000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Bronco+Sport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source, such as hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the engine control software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V685000: Equipment:Electrical:Engine Block Heater](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Bronco+Sport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The engine block heater may crack and develop a coolant leak, causing it to short circuit when the block heater is plugged in. An electrical short circuit can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the block heater, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V340000: Suspension:Front:Control Arm:Lower Ball Joint](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Bronco+Sport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A detached control arm can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the front lower control arm ball joints as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Ford Bronco Sport is a versatile and stylish compact SUV, but used car buyers should perform a thorough inspection before finalizing a purchase. Pay close attention to electrical system functions, infotainment stability, and any open recall notices regarding fuel components or bonded glass. Choosing a well-maintained example with documented service history will help ensure a dependable ownership experience.
