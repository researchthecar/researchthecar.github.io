# 2023 Ford Bronco Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Ford Bronco Sport offers a rugged, compact SUV package infused with retro styling and capable off-road proportions. However, prospective buyers should review owner complaints regarding powertrain behavior and fuel system concerns. While it remains a popular choice for daily driving and light trail use, careful pre-purchase inspection is advised.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L EcoBoost 3-Cylinder

The standard 1.5-liter EcoBoost turbocharged three-cylinder engine generates approximately 181 horsepower and 190 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate pep for daily commutes while achieving respectable fuel economy.

**Fuel Injector Failure & Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may lose motive power, fail to accelerate, or unexpectedly stall while driving at low or high speeds.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnosis by a qualified technician and replacement of faulty fuel system components or injectors.

### 2.0L EcoBoost 4-Cylinder

The available 2.0-liter EcoBoost turbocharged four-cylinder engine ups performance to roughly 250 horsepower and 277 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration for highway passing and demanding trails.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission is paired across the Bronco Sport lineup, channeling power through standard four-wheel-drive systems.

**Transmission Jerking and Shifter Defects**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report violent jerking during gear shifts, delayed engagement, or the vehicle unexpectedly getting stuck in Park.
- **Solution:** Have the transmission control module and shift linkages inspected by a dealership for software updates or mechanical repairs.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Safety equipment includes standard modern airbag suites, though a handful of owners have flagged deployment and warning light concerns.

**Air Bag Deployment Failure**
- **Symptoms:** In frontal or rear-end collision scenarios, occupants report that airbags failed to deploy as expected.
- **Solution:** Inspect supplemental restraint system sensors and wiring harnesses for faults.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V686000: Exterior Lighting:Lighting Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Bronco+Sport&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Flickering parking lights can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the LED Driver module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V859000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Bronco+Sport&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source such as hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the engine control software to include fuel injector leak detection and install a drain tube, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V021000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Bronco+Sport&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A leak inside the ABS module can cause an unexpected increase in brake pedal travel or unexpected movement during an Auto Hold event, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the ABS Module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V223000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Bronco+Sport&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A loss of vehicle handling and control increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the tires, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V187000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Bronco+Sport&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source such as hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the engine control software to include fuel injector leak detection and install a drain tube, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V267000: Electrical System:Body Control Module/Bcm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Bronco+Sport&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An undetected low battery charge can result in a loss of electrical accessories such as hazard lights, or cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will recalibrate the body control module and power train control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V594000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer:Motor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Bronco+Sport&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The front windshield wiper motor may become inoperative, causing the wipers to fail. Inoperative windshield wipers can reduce visibility in certain conditions, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the windshield wiper motor as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V019000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Bronco+Sport&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Battery failure can result in a loss of electrical accessories, including the hazard lights, or cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and if necessary, replace the 12-volt battery, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Ford Bronco Sport blends retro charm and everyday utility, but used buyers should proceed with a degree of caution. Be sure to check service history for any unresolved fuel system or transmission complaints during your test drive.
