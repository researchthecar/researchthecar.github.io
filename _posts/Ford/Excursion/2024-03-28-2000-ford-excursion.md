**Introduction**

The 2000 Ford Excursion is a full-size SUV that offers robust performance and ample interior space. It is powered by a powerful 7.3L Power Stroke diesel engine, mated to a 4R100 TorqShift automatic transmission. The Excursion has a maximum towing capacity of 10,000 lbs and a payload capacity of 3,120 lbs.

While the Excursion is praised for its capabilities, it has several known issues that owners should be aware of before purchasing.

**Engine**

**7.3L Power Stroke Diesel**

* **Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: This issue occurs due to faulty injectors that fail to deliver fuel properly to the cylinders. Symptoms include poor engine performance, decreased fuel efficiency, and black smoke from the exhaust.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Valve Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A faulty EGR valve can cause engine performance problems, decreased fuel efficiency, and increased emissions. Symptoms include rough idling, hesitation during acceleration, and illuminated check engine light.
    * Solution: Replace the EGR valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**4R100 TorqShift Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping occurs when the transmission is unable to engage properly, resulting in loss of power and gear slippage. Symptoms include hesitation during shifting, slipping gears, and increased noise.
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Front Coil Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failed coil springs can result in decreased ride height, poor handling, and a rough ride. Symptoms include a sagging front end, uneven tire wear, and excessive body roll.
    * Solution: Replace the coil springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A persistent battery drain can leave the vehicle unable to start. Symptoms include a weak battery, frequent battery replacements, and dimmed headlights.
    * Solution: Diagnose the source of the battery drain and repair or replace the affected components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Body Panels:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on the body panels is a common issue with the Excursion. This can lead to holes and deterioration of the metal.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the affected body panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of damage
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, resulting in inadequate heating or cooling. Symptoms include uneven temperature distribution, excessive noise, and illuminated AC/heater warning lights.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the affected components of the climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

While the 2000 Ford Excursion offers impressive capabilities, it is not without its drawbacks. The potential issues can result in significant repair costs and affect the overall reliability of the vehicle. Owners should be aware of these issues before purchasing an Excursion and factor in the potential cost of repairs when considering their budget.
