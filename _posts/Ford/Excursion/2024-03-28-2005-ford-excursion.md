**Introduction:**

The 2005 Ford Excursion is a full-size SUV known for its spacious interior and powerful engine options. It came with two engine options: a 5.4L V8 and a 6.8L V10, paired with two transmission options: a 4-speed automatic and a 5-speed automatic. While praised for its towing and hauling capabilities, the Excursion has faced criticism for its fuel consumption and maneuverability.

**Engine**

**5.4L V8**

* **Spark Plug Blowout:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs and install updated cylinder heads
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Intake Manifold Runner Control Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light illuminates, reduced engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold runner control motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**6.8L V10**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, hard starting, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace fuel injector(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, loss of power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner and timing chain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Slipping Transmission:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips or hesitates when shifting
    * Solution: Replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fluid leak from transmission pan or lines
    * Solution: Replace transmission pan gasket or repair lines
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifts erratically or refuses to shift
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear axle drops, loss of load capacity
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor ride quality, excessive bouncing
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Determine source of battery drain and repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, warning light illuminates
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks malfunction, door cannot be opened or closed
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Running Board Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Running boards rust and deteriorate
    * Solution: Replace running boards
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard develops cracks or splits
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **HVAC System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heating or air conditioning does not work properly
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair HVAC system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

The 2005 Ford Excursion has some common issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior components. The most severe and expensive issues include spark plug blowout, timing chain tensioner failure, and transmission replacement. Some issues, such as door lock failure and HVAC system malfunctions, are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. Overall, the Excursion is a capable vehicle but potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before purchasing.
