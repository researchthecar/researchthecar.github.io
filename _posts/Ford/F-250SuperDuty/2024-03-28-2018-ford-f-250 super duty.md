**Introduction:**

The 2018 Ford F-250 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck known for its powerful engines, robust construction, and impressive towing capabilities. It offers a range of engine and transmission options, including the Power Stroke 6.7L V8 turbocharged diesel, Power Stroke 6.7L V8 turbocharged gasoline, and a 6.2L V8 gasoline engine. The F-250 is praised for its performance, off-road capabilities, and interior comfort, but it has also been subject to some specific issues.

**Engine**

**Power Stroke 6.7L V8 Turbocharged Diesel**

* **Diesel particulate filter (DPF) clogging:** DPF collects soot and particulate matter, and if it becomes clogged, it can restrict exhaust flow and trigger a warning light. Requires cleaning or replacement.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,500+
* **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number 19V818000

**Power Stroke 6.7L V8 Turbocharged Gasoline**

* **Intake manifold leaks:** Leaks can cause a loss of power, increased fuel consumption, and rough running. Requires replacement of the intake manifold.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**6.2L V8 Gasoline**

* No Issues Found

**Transmission**

* **Torqshift-G 6-speed automatic:**
    * **Torque converter shudder:** Vibrations while accelerating or decelerating due to a faulty torque converter. Requires torque converter replacement.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $3,000
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Torqshift-10R10 10-speed automatic:**
    * **Premature gear wear:** Gears can wear prematurely, causing noise and shifting issues. Requires transmission rebuild or replacement.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,500 - $5,000+
    * **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number 20V382000

**Suspension**

* **Front upper control arm bushings:** Bushings can fail and cause clunking or rattling, especially over bumps. Requires replacement of the control arms.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,200+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Battery can drain prematurely due to a faulty alternator or parasitic drain. Requires diagnosis and repair of the faulty component.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate lock failure:** Tailgate lock can fail to latch or open, leaving the tailgate insecure. Requires replacement of the tailgate lock.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Heater core leaks:** Leaks can cause excessive fogging or leaks in the cabin. Requires replacement of the heater core.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2018 Ford F-250 Super Duty has experienced some specific issues, including engine problems, transmission failures, suspension issues, and electrical malfunctions. The severity of the issues varies, with some being relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more costly and require significant repairs. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and monitor their vehicles for any signs of trouble. Regular maintenance and servicing can help prevent or detect problems early on, reducing the likelihood of costly repairs.
