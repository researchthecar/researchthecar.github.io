The 2001 Ford F-250 Super Duty is a robust workhorse appreciated for its towing capacity and overall durability. Engine options included the 5.4L Triton V8 (260 hp, 350 lb-ft torque), the 6.8L Triton V10 (310 hp, 425 lb-ft torque), and the 7.3L Power Stroke Turbo Diesel V8 (275 hp, 525 lb-ft torque). Transmission choices were primarily the 4R100 4-speed automatic and a ZF5 5-speed manual. Common praises include its solid build and capability, while criticisms often target its fuel economy and potential maintenance costs, particularly with the diesel engine.

**Engine**

**5.4L Triton V8**

*   **Spark Plug Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Spark plugs can seize in the cylinder heads, leading to breakage during removal. Ticking noise might be noticed.
    *   Solution: Special tools and techniques are required for extraction. In some cases, the cylinder head may need to be removed. Using anti-seize compound on new plugs is recommended.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+ depending on severity
    *   Recalls: None Found

**6.8L Triton V10**

*   **Exhaust Manifold Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Cracked or warped exhaust manifolds, leading to exhaust leaks. This often manifests as a ticking noise that is most noticeable when the engine is cold.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the exhaust manifold.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**7.3L Power Stroke Turbo Diesel V8**

*   **Cam Position Sensor (CPS) Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Stalling, rough running, or no-start condition.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the CPS.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Injector Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, misfires, white smoke from exhaust, and decreased fuel economy.
    *   Solution: Injector replacement or rebuild.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+ per injector
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Fuel Bowl Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Diesel fuel leak around the fuel bowl area. Can be a fire hazard.
    *   Solution: Replace fuel bowl seals or the entire fuel bowl assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4R100 4-Speed Automatic**

*   **Torque Converter Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Shuddering during acceleration, slipping between gears.
    *   Solution: Torque converter replacement and possible transmission flush.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $2000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Shift Solenoid Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh or erratic shifting, failure to shift into certain gears.
    *   Solution: Replacement of faulty shift solenoids.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
    *   Recalls: None Found

**ZF5 5-Speed Manual**

*   **Clutch Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clutch slippage, difficulty shifting, noise during clutch engagement.
    *   Solution: Clutch replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Leaf Spring Sagging/Cracking**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rear end sagging, poor ride quality, visible cracks in leaf springs.
    *   Solution: Leaf spring replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1000+ per spring
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Ball Joint Wear**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clicking or popping noises from the front suspension, uneven tire wear, loose steering.
    *   Solution: Ball joint replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+ per ball joint
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Alternator Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, slow starting, battery light illumination.
    *   Solution: Alternator replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Wipers stop working or only work on certain settings.
    *   Solution: Windshield wiper motor replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Rust on rocker panels and cab corners**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Visible rust forming on the rocker panels and cab corners.
    *   Solution: Repair or replacement of the affected panels.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Cracked Dashboard**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Cracking on the dashboard, especially in warmer climates.
    *   Solution: Replacement dashboard or dashboard cover.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Window not working or motor sounds weak.
    * Solution: Power window motor replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2001 Ford F-250 Super Duty, while a capable truck, has several common issues. The severity and cost of these issues range from relatively minor (CPS replacement) to major (torque converter failure or engine rebuild). Rust is a prevalent concern, especially in certain climates. Potential buyers should carefully inspect the vehicle for these issues and factor potential repair costs into their purchase decision.
