**Introduction:**

The 2000 Ford F-250 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck available with various engine and transmission options. The 7.3L Power Stroke diesel engine is known for its reliability and torque, producing 235 horsepower and 505 lb-ft of torque. The 6.8L V10 gasoline engine offers 310 horsepower and 425 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a five-speed manual and a four-speed automatic. The F-250 Super Duty is praised for its towing and hauling capabilities, but it has also faced some issues over the years.

**Engine**

**7.3L Power Stroke Diesel Engine**

**High-Pressure Oil Pump Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Loss of oil pressure, excessive engine noise, and engine damage
* **Solution:** Replace the high-pressure oil pump
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**6.8L V10 Gasoline Engine**

**Intake Manifold Runner Control Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Engine misfires, rough idling, and reduced power
* **Solution:** Replace the intake manifold runner control assembly
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual Transmission**

**Synchronizer Wear:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting gears, especially into higher gears
* **Solution:** Rebuild or replace the transmission
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $3,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Four-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Overdrive Band Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** No power in overdrive gear, slipping or shuddering while shifting
* **Solution:** Replace the overdrive band
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $600 - $1,200+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Ball Joint Wear:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Steering wobble, clunking or popping noises from the front suspension
* **Solution:** Replace the ball joints
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $800+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Cable Corrosion:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, dimming headlights, and electrical system malfunctions
* **Solution:** Replace the corroded battery cables
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

**Tailgate Hinge Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Tailgate will not open or close properly, rattling or drooping
* **Solution:** Replace the tailgate hinges
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $800+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

**Heater Core Leak:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Sweet smell in the vehicle, fogging or condensation on the windshield, and low coolant levels
* **Solution:** Replace the heater core
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $600 - $1,200+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 2000 Ford F-250 Super Duty has some common issues, including oil pump failure, intake manifold runner control failure, and overdrive band failure. While these issues can be costly to fix, they are not widespread and the F-250 Super Duty remains a reliable and capable heavy-duty pickup truck.
