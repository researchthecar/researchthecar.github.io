**2007 Ford F-250 Super Duty**

The 2007 Ford F-250 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck known for its power and durability. It comes with various engine and transmission options, including:

* **Engines:**
    * 5.4L Triton V8 (300 hp, 365 lb-ft)
    * 6.0L Power Stroke V8 Diesel (325 hp, 570 lb-ft)
    * 6.8L Triton V10 (362 hp, 457 lb-ft)
* **Transmissions:**
    * 5-speed automatic
    * 6-speed manual
    * 6-speed automatic

Drivers appreciate the F-250's towing and hauling capabilities but have reported various issues with specific components.

**Engine**

**5.4L Triton V8**

* **Spark Plug Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfires, rough idling, poor fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Stretch:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and related components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.0L Power Stroke V8 Diesel**

* **EGR Cooler Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, decreased engine power
    * Solution: Replace EGR cooler and gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: 06M09, 06T04
* **Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, decreased fuel efficiency
    * Solution: Replace injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per injector
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.8L Triton V10**

* **Intake Manifold Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine overheating, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Slipping Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips out of gear, difficulty shifting
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears
    * Solution: Replace clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front End Noises:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking, squeaking, rattling noises when driving over bumps
    * Solution: Inspect and replace worn suspension components (e.g., ball joints, bushings)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery light illuminates, electrical system malfunction
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Wiring Harness Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions, intermittent operation of components
    * Solution: Repair or replace wiring harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust and Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots on body panels, undercarriage
    * Solution: Rust prevention treatment or replacement of affected parts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system not functioning properly
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair HVAC components (e.g., blower motor, blend door actuator)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2007 Ford F-250 Super Duty is a capable pickup truck but has experienced various issues with specific components. Engine and transmission problems can be severe and expensive to repair, while suspension and electrical issues can impact performance and reliability. Interior and exterior issues are generally less serious. Before purchasing a used F-250 of this model year, potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
