**Introduction:**

The 2014 Ford F-250 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck available with various engine and transmission options. With its powerful engines and robust capabilities, it offers exceptional towing and hauling performance. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of issues reported by owners.

**Engine:**

- **6.7L Power Stroke Turbo Diesel V8**
    - **EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) System Failure**
        - Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, reduced engine power, rough idling
        - Solution: Replacement of EGR valve and cooler
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
        - Recalls: N/A
    - **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure**
        - Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, reduced fuel efficiency, difficulty starting
        - Solution: Replacement of high-pressure fuel pump
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $2,500
        - Recalls: N/A
- **6.2L V8 Gasoline**
    - **Cam Phaser Failure**
        - Description/Symptoms: Ticking sound from engine, reduced engine power, check engine light
        - Solution: Replacement of cam phasers
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000
        - Recalls: N/A

**Transmission:**

- **6-Speed TorqShift Automatic Transmission**
    - **Transmission Slipping**
        - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed acceleration, shuddering
        - Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500
        - Recalls: N/A
    - **Torque Converter Failure**
        - Description/Symptoms: Vehicle does not move or moves erratically, check engine light
        - Solution: Replacement of torque converter
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
        - Recalls: N/A
- **10-Speed TorqShift Automatic Transmission**
    - **No Significant Issues Found**

**Suspension:**

- **Front Suspension:**
    - **Ball Joint Wear**
        - Description/Symptoms: Loose steering, clunking noise during turns or bumps
        - Solution: Replacement of ball joints
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300 per ball joint
        - Recalls: N/A
- **Rear Suspension:**
    - **Leaf Spring Breakage**
        - Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, uneven ride height, noise while driving over bumps
        - Solution: Replacement of leaf springs
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500 for both sides
        - Recalls: N/A

**Electrical:**

- **Alternator Failure**
        - Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, reduced battery power, check engine light
        - Solution: Replacement of alternator
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
        - Recalls: N/A
- **Battery Drain**
        - Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly even when vehicle is not in use
        - Solution: Identification and repair of parasitic drain, such as faulty modules or wiring
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        - Recalls: N/A

**Exterior:**

- **Bed Liner Issues**
        - Description/Symptoms: Cracking, peeling, or bubbling of spray-on bed liner
        - Solution: Respraying or replacement of bed liner
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
        - Recalls: N/A

**Interior:**

- **Dashboard Trim Peeling**
        - Description/Symptoms: Vinyl or plastic trim on dashboard starts peeling or tearing
        - Solution: Replacement of dashboard trim
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        - Recalls: N/A
- **Seat Stitching Issues**
        - Description/Symptoms: Stitching on seats starts unraveling or breaking
        - Solution: Repair or replacement of seat covers
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per seat
        - Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

The 2014 Ford F-250 Super Duty is a capable heavy-duty truck, but it is not without its issues. Engine and transmission problems are the most significant concerns, with potential failures in EGR and fuel systems, as well as transmission slipping and torque converter issues. Suspension issues, such as ball joint wear and leaf spring breakage, can also affect ride quality and safety. Electrical problems, including alternator failure and battery drain, are also reported. Owners may also experience issues with the exterior bed liner and interior trim and seat stitching.

Overall, the severity and cost of the issues for the 2014 Ford F-250 Super Duty can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the repair required. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and consider extended warranties or regular maintenance to mitigate potential costs.
