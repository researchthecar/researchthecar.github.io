# 2007 Ford F-250 Super Duty

### Overview
The 2007 Ford F-250 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck known for its robust construction and towing capabilities. While generally reliable for its class, specific powertrain options and certain components have reported issues that owners should be aware of. This analysis focuses on common technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for the 2007 model year across its various configurations. Understanding these potential issues can help owners budget for repairs and make informed decisions regarding maintenance and longevity.

---


## Engine

### 5.4L Triton V8
**Spark Plug Blowout**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, rough idling, misfires, audible popping sound from engine.
- **Solution:** Requires specialized tools to extract broken spark plug and re-thread cylinder head, or use of inserts.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000

**Camshaft Synchronizer (Cam Sync) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially at idle or low RPMs.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the camshaft synchronizer and potentially the oil pump.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900


### 6.8L Triton V10
**Spark Plug Blowout**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, rough idling, misfires, audible popping sound from engine.
- **Solution:** Requires specialized tools to extract broken spark plug and re-thread cylinder head, or use of inserts.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000

**Camshaft Synchronizer (Cam Sync) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially at idle or low RPMs.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the camshaft synchronizer and potentially the oil pump.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900


### 6.0L Power Stroke V8 Diesel
**EGR Cooler Failure**
- **Symptoms:** White smoke from exhaust, coolant loss, overheating, loss of power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the EGR cooler, often with an upgraded unit.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200

**Oil Cooler Failure**
- **Symptoms:** High oil temperature, low oil pressure, potential engine damage if not addressed.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil cooler.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

**Head Gasket Failure / Head Studs**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, white smoke, overheating, loss of power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of head gaskets and often upgrading to ARP head studs.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000+


### 6.4L Power Stroke V8 Diesel
**DPF (Diesel Particulate Filter) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, warning lights on dashboard.
- **Solution:** Regeneration cycle, DPF cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000+

**EGR System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, rough running, reduced power.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of EGR components.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000


## Transmission

### 5R110W Automatic (Gas & Diesel)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping, shuddering, delayed engagement, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter and potentially transmission fluid flush.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Solenoid Pack Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, failure to shift into certain gears, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the transmission solenoid pack.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


### ZF6 Manual (Diesel)
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping, difficulty shifting, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch kit (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $700 - $1500

**Dual Mass Flywheel Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise, shuddering during engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the dual mass flywheel and clutch kit.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Leaf Spring Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, clunking noises, reduced load capacity.
- **Solution:** Replacement of broken or worn leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

**Ball Joint Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or popping noises when turning or going over bumps, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Instrument Cluster Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Gauges not working, warning lights staying on, intermittent power loss to cluster.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, starting issues, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Exterior

### Body and Trim
**Rust on Frame and Body**
- **Symptoms:** Visible rust, corrosion, potential structural weakening.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment, repair, or replacement of affected panels/frame sections.
- **Cost:** $200 - $2000+

**Tailgate Latch Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty opening or closing tailgate, latch not engaging properly.
- **Solution:** Lubrication or replacement of tailgate latch mechanism.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents, or air only coming from certain vents, inconsistent fan speed.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor or resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power locks not working on one or more doors, manual operation required.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty door lock actuator.
- **Cost:** $150 - $350

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Ford F-250 Super Duty, particularly models equipped with the 6.0L Power Stroke diesel, presents a higher likelihood of specific engine-related repairs such as EGR cooler and oil cooler failures, and head gasket issues. The gasoline V8 engines are prone to spark plug blowout and cam sync issues. Transmission reliability is generally good, though torque converter and solenoid issues can occur. Suspension components like leaf springs and ball joints may require attention over time. Electrical issues such as instrument cluster and alternator failures are not uncommon. Rust can be a concern on older models. Overall, while capable, potential buyers should be aware of these common issues and factor in potential repair costs, especially for the diesel variants.
