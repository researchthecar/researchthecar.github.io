**Introduction:**

The 2002 Ford E-150 Econoline Club Wagon is a versatile full-size van offering ample seating for up to 15 passengers. Its spacious cabin and reliable performance make it a popular choice for families and businesses alike. Powertrain options include a 5.4L V8 engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**5.4L V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, performance issues
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Spark Plug Blowout:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfires, rough idle, decreased power
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs and inspect for thread damage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration during acceleration or gear changes
    * Solution: Replace torque converter, transmission fluid filter, and fluid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $2000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Drooping suspension, uneven ride height, handling issues
    * Solution: Replace broken leaf spring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead quickly, especially after sitting overnight
    * Solution: Test and replace faulty components, such as relays, alternator, or battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Windows fail to operate or operate slowly
    * Solution: Replace window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Undercarriage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Visible corrosion on metal components beneath the vehicle
    * Solution: Preventative measures such as undercoating or rustproofing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Plastic Interior Trimming Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracking or breaking of plastic trim panels on the dashboard, door panels, or console
    * Solution: Replace trim panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No or weak airflow from vents
    * Solution: Replace blower motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2002 Ford E-150 Econoline Club Wagon generally has minor to moderate issues that are common for vehicles of this age. The most significant problems involve engine intake manifold gasket failures, transmission torque converter shudder, and suspension leaf spring breakage. However, the overall cost to repair these issues can vary significantly depending on the severity and the cost of labor.
