**Introduction**

The 2015 Ford F-450 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck that offers exceptional towing and hauling capabilities. It is powered by a choice of either a 6.7L Power Stroke V8 turbodiesel engine or a 6.2L gasoline V8 engine. Transmission options include a 6-speed TorqShift automatic and a 10-speed TorqShift automatic.

**Engine**

**6.7L Power Stroke V8 Turbodiesel**

**Issue:** Engine Overheating
Description/Symptoms: Engine temperature gauge rises to high levels, coolant leaks, and reduced engine performance.
Solution: Replace faulty radiator or thermostat.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
Recalls: 15V-457

The 2015 Ford F-450 Super Duty has a few reported issues, primarily related to engine overheating.
