**2019 Ford F-450 Super Duty**

The 2019 Ford F-450 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck that offers exceptional towing and hauling capabilities. It is available with three engine options: a 6.2L gasoline V8, a 6.7L Power Stroke V8 turbo diesel, and a 7.3L Godzilla V8 gasoline. The gasoline engines are paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission, while the diesel engine is paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission.

Despite its impressive features, the 2019 Ford F-450 Super Duty has faced some issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**6.2L Gasoline V8**

* **Intake Manifold Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracking or warping of the intake manifold, leading to coolant and oil leaks, decreased engine performance, and overheating.
    * Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.7L Power Stroke V8 Turbo Diesel**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the turbocharger, resulting in reduced power, increased exhaust emissions, and a whistling noise.
    * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**7.3L Godzilla V8 Gasoline**

* **Fuel Injector Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning fuel injectors, causing rough idling, misfires, and decreased fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replacement of the fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**10-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifts, or slipping gears.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of transmission components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering during acceleration, particularly in low gears.
    * Solution: Replacement of the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracking or breaking of the leaf springs, resulting in reduced ride quality, sagging, and potential loss of control.
    * Solution: Replacement of the leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid discharge of the battery, even when the vehicle is parked.
    * Solution: Inspection of the electrical system to identify and fix any parasitic drains.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Bed**: Premature rust formation on the bed, especially in areas where salt or other corrosive materials have been used.
* **Bumper Corrosion**: Corrosion and deterioration of the front and rear bumpers, particularly in areas prone to road salt and moisture.

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions:** Failure or intermittent functioning of the climate control system, causing insufficient heating or cooling.
* **Dashboard Cracks**: Cracking or warping of the dashboard, especially in areas exposed to sunlight or extreme temperatures.

**Summary**

The 2019 Ford F-450 Super Duty is a capable and powerful truck, but it has faced some reliability issues that have affected engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components. Some of these issues can be costly to repair, so it is important to be aware of them before purchasing a used vehicle.
