**Introduction:**

The 2021 Ford F-450 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck that offers impressive power and towing capabilities. It's available with multiple engine and transmission options, including a 6.7L Power Stroke V8 diesel engine with 475 horsepower and 1,050 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 10-speed TorqShift automatic transmission. Owners appreciate the truck's strength, towing capacity, and off-road performance, but some have reported issues in various areas.

**Engine**

**6.7L Power Stroke V8 Diesel**

**Turbocharger Wastegate Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive smoke, limp mode
Solution: Replace turbocharger or wastegate
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID Number: 22V577000

**EGR Valve Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Poor engine performance, increased emissions
Solution: Replace EGR valve
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**10-Speed TorqShift Automatic**

**Torque Converter Shudder**
Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shudder when shifting gears
Solution: Replace torque converter
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension Squeaking**
Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or grinding noise when turning or going over bumps
Solution: Lubricate or replace bushings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is off
Solution: Identify and fix parasitic current draw (e.g., faulty module, wiring issue)
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Headlight Condensation**
Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulates inside the headlight assemblies
Solution: Replace or seal headlight assemblies
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Instrument Cluster Flickering**
Description/Symptoms: Flickering or dimming of the instrument cluster
Solution: Replace instrument cluster
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 Ford F-450 Super Duty has some reported issues that range from minor annoyances to more costly repairs. While the truck's capabilities and durability are highly regarded, it's important to be aware of potential problems before making a purchase. The severity and cost of the issues vary, and it's always advisable to have any issues diagnosed and repaired by a qualified mechanic.
