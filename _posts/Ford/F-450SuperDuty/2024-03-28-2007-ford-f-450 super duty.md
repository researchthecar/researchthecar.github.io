**Introduction:**

The 2007 Ford F-450 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck available with various engine and transmission options. The 6.0L Power Stroke diesel engine produces 325 horsepower and 560 lb-ft of torque, while the 6.4L Power Stroke diesel engine generates 350 horsepower and 650 lb-ft of torque. The available transmissions include a 5-speed TorqShift automatic and a 6-speed manual. The F-450 is known for its impressive towing and payload capacities, but it has also been subject to several issues.

**Engine**

**6.0L Power Stroke**

* **EGR Valve Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive smoke, decreased engine power, and poor fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replacement of the EGR valve and cleaning of the intake system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: 07V439000
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased fuel consumption, and loud whistling noises.
    * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: 08V324000
* **Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, and white smoke from the exhaust.
    * Solution: Replacement of the head gaskets and resurfacing of the cylinder heads.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: 06V364000, 07V285000

**6.4L Power Stroke**

* **DPF Regeneration Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shortened intervals between regenerations, decreased fuel economy, and exhaust filter warning lights.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the DPF filter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: 08V235000

**Transmission**

**5-Speed TorqShift**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power, and slipping between gears.
    * Solution: Replacement of the transmission or repair of the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping, and chatter.
    * Solution: Replacement of the clutch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaking Shock Absorbers:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride quality, noise, and fluid leaks from the shocks.
    * Solution: Replacement of the shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per shock
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Worn Ball Joints:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, excessive play in the steering, and uneven tire wear.
    * Solution: Replacement of the ball joints.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per joint
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead quickly without warning.
    * Solution: Testing of the electrical system to identify the source of the drain and repair or replacement of the affected component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty Alternator:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical system malfunctions, and warning lights on the dashboard.
    * Solution: Replacement of the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Frame:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on the vehicle's frame, causing structural weakening.
    * Solution: Inspection, cleaning, and anti-corrosion treatment.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Trim Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracking or warping of the dashboard trim panels.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected trim panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2007 Ford F-450 Super Duty has experienced a range of issues, with varying levels of severity and cost. The most significant issues include engine failures, transmission slippage, and suspension problems. Some electrical and cosmetic issues have also been reported. Overall, while the F-450 is a capable workhorse, potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues and factor in the possible costs of repairs and maintenance.
