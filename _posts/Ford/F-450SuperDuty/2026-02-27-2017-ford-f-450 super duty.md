# 2017 Ford F-450 Super Duty

### Overview
The 2017 Ford F-450 Super Duty is engineered for heavy-duty work, offering robust performance and capability. Reliability data for this model year indicates a generally strong mechanical foundation, particularly in its powertrain options designed for demanding tasks. While most owners report satisfaction, like any heavy-duty vehicle, specific components can experience issues under strenuous use. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-related problems across key systems, providing insights into potential repair areas and their associated costs for the 2017 F-450.

---


## Engine

### 6.7L Power Stroke V8 Turbo Diesel
**EGR Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, white smoke from exhaust, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the EGR cooler assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noises.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger inspection, cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500


## Transmission

### 6R140 TorqShift Heavy-Duty Automatic
**Torque Converter Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping, shuddering, delayed engagement, overheating.
- **Solution:** Torque converter replacement or transmission fluid service.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Suspension

### Heavy-Duty Independent Front Suspension (IFS) / Solid Rear Axle
**Ball Joint Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, uneven tire wear, steering wander.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Leaf Spring Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, reduced load capacity, cracking or breaking.
- **Solution:** Replacement of damaged or fatigued leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Electrical Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Various warning lights, non-functional accessories (lights, wipers, power windows), starting issues.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


### Alternator
**Early Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming lights, battery warning light, difficulty starting, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Trim
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Cost:** N/A


## Interior

### Infotainment System
**Sync System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Freezing, unresponsiveness, Bluetooth connectivity issues, voice command errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600


### Seat Actuators
**Power Seat Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not moving forward/backward or reclining.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty seat motor/actuator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $500


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Ford F-450 Super Duty demonstrates solid reliability, especially concerning its core powertrain. The 6.7L Power Stroke diesel engine and the 6R140 transmission are generally robust, though specific issues like EGR cooler leaks and torque converter problems can arise, particularly under heavy use. Suspension components, such as ball joints and leaf springs, may require attention due to the vehicle's demanding nature. Electrical systems, particularly the BCM and alternator, have seen some reports of premature failure. Interior electronics, like the Sync system, can experience software-related glitches. Overall, the F-450 is a capable workhorse, but owners should be aware of potential repair costs associated with these specific components.
