**Introduction**

The 1999 Ford F-450 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck renowned for its ruggedness and towing capabilities. It offers several engine and transmission options, including:

* 7.3L Power Stroke V8 Diesel: 235 hp / 505 lb-ft
* 5.4L Triton V8 Gas: 260 hp / 350 lb-ft

People appreciate the F-450's powerful engines, sturdy construction, and towing prowess. However, it has received criticism for its dated interior, poor fuel economy, and some reliability concerns.

**Engine**

**7.3L Power Stroke V8 Diesel**

**Injector Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Engine misfiring, decreased power, excessive fuel consumption
* **Solution:** Replace injectors and injector cups
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Turbocharger Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noise, oil leaks
* **Solution:** Replace turbocharger
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,200 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**5.4L Triton V8 Gas**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Engine overheating, coolant leaks, loss of coolant
* **Solution:** Replace intake manifold gasket
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**4R100 Automatic**
* **Clutch Pack Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Slipping gears, delayed engagement, rough shifts
* **Solution:** Replace clutch packs
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,200 - $2,000
* **Recalls:** None Found

**5R110W Automatic**
* **Torque Converter Shudder**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Vibration or shuddering during acceleration
* **Solution:** Replace torque converter
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

**Leaf Spring Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Sagging suspension, uneven tire wear
* **Solution:** Replace leaf springs
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Battery not charging, electrical system failure
* **Solution:** Replace alternator
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Battery Drain**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Battery drains prematurely
* **Solution:** Diagnose and repair source of drain
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $50 - $500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Instrument Cluster Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Malfunctioning gauges, dimmed display
* **Solution:** Replace instrument cluster
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $800
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

While the 1999 Ford F-450 Super Duty is a capable workhorse, it has some reliability concerns that can be costly to fix. However, many owners appreciate its robust engine and towing capabilities. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues vary depending on the specific problem, but some of the more common issues can be significant expenses.
