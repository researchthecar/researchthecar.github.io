**Introduction:**

The 2016 Ford Transit-250 is a versatile full-size cargo van available with various engine and transmission options. With its powerful engine and spacious cargo area, it's a popular choice for commercial use. Despite its popularity, the Transit-250 has encountered several issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**3.7L V6**

* **Engine Misfire:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough engine idle, loss of power, illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace ignition coils and spark plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, engine stalling, reduced fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5.0L V8**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling noise, reduced power, illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioners
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2000+
    * Recalls: 19V-528

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration felt during acceleration or gear shifting
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $2000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Overhaul or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2500 - $4000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Suspension noise, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or knocking noise, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead after short periods of inactivity
    * Solution: Identify and disconnect parasitic draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Charging system warning light, dim headlights
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Hinge Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors sagging or not closing properly
    * Solution: Replace door hinges
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC/heater not working, temperature fluctuations
    * Solution: Replace climate control module or repair wiring harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Belt Sensor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat belt warning chime going off when seated
    * Solution: Replace seat belt buckle or sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Ford Transit-250 has a range of issues that can affect its drivability, reliability, and interior comfort. While some issues, such as intake manifold failure, can be severe and expensive to repair, others, like battery drain, are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix.
