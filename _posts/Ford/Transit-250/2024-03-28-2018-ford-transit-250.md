**Introduction**

The 2018 Ford Transit-250 is a versatile full-size van available in various configurations. It offers a robust 3.7L V6 engine that generates 275 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The Transit-250 is known for its spacious cargo area, comfortable interior, and solid towing capabilities. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of reported issues.

**Engine**

* **3.7L V6 Engine**

  * **Cam Phaser Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, decreased fuel efficiency, and Check Engine Light illumination
    * Solution: Replace cam phasers and related components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA 18V669
  * **Leaking Valve Cover Gaskets**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the valve covers, resulting in oil consumption, burning smell, and potential engine damage
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

  * **Torque Converter Shudder**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering during acceleration, especially at low speeds
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Springs**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or broken leaf springs, causing a rough ride and potential axle misalignment
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, especially when the vehicle is parked
    * Solution: Identify and fix electrical component causing the drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cruise Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cruise control does not engage or disengages randomly
    * Solution: Replace cruise control switch or reprogram module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: NHTSA 18V525

**Exterior**

* **Rear Door Lock Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear doors fail to lock or unlock, causing security concerns
    * Solution: Replace rear door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heating or cooling system does not work properly, causing discomfort
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair climate control components (e.g., heater core, blower motor, AC compressor)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard material cracks or splits, affecting aesthetics
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2018 Ford Transit-250 is a capable van, it has encountered some issues reported by owners. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some, such as cam phaser failure and torque converter shudder, can be significant. It's important to consider these potential issues when making a purchase decision and to consult with a qualified mechanic for regular maintenance and inspections to address problems early on.
