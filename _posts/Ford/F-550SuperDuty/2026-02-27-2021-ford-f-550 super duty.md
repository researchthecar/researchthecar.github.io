### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Ford F-550 Super Duty demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its robust diesel engine and heavy-duty transmission, which are designed for strenuous use. The most frequently cited, though still uncommon, issues tend to be minor electrical glitches, such as intermittent instrument cluster problems or occasional infotainment system software hiccups. Proactive maintenance and addressing minor electrical anomalies promptly will contribute to the longevity and dependable performance expected from this Super Duty model.
