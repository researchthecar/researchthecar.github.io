**Introduction**

The 2004 Ford F-550 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck that is powered by a 6.0L Power Stroke diesel engine that produces 325 horsepower and 560 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. The F-550 is known for its towing and hauling capabilities, as well as its spacious interior and rugged exterior. However, like all vehicles, it has its share of common issues that owners may encounter.

**Engine**

* **6.0L Power Stroke Diesel Engine**
    * **Sticking EGR Valve:** The EGR valve can become stuck open or closed, which can lead to a variety of problems, including poor engine performance, increased emissions, and decreased fuel economy.
        * Solution: Replace the EGR valve.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: 04V-357
    * **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger can fail due to a variety of reasons, including worn bearings, a faulty wastegate, or a cracked housing.
        * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Injector Failure:** The fuel injectors can fail, which can cause a variety of problems, including rough idling, poor acceleration, and increased emissions.
        * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Slipping Gears:** The transmission may slip gears, which can cause a loss of power and difficulty accelerating.
        * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Springs:** The rear leaf springs can break or sag, which can lead to a rough ride and decreased towing capacity.
        * Solution: Replace the leaf springs.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Front Ball Joints:** The front ball joints can wear out, which can lead to a clunking noise and decreased steering stability.
        * Solution: Replace the ball joints.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery can drain quickly, which can be caused by a variety of electrical problems, including a faulty alternator, a parasitic draw, or a damaged battery.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical problem.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Failure:** The headlights may fail due to a variety of reasons, including a faulty bulb, a damaged wiring harness, or a failed headlight switch.
        * Solution: Replace the faulty component.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Bed Rust:** The bed of the truck can rust, especially if it is not properly cared for.
        * Solution: Repair or replace the rusty areas.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Power Window Failure:** The power windows may fail due to a variety of reasons, including a faulty switch, a damaged wiring harness, or a failed window motor.
        * Solution: Replace the faulty component.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC Malfunction:** The HVAC system may malfunction, which can be caused by a variety of problems, including a faulty blower motor, a damaged blend door, or a failed temperature sensor.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair the HVAC problem.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2004 Ford F-550 Super Duty is a capable and reliable truck, but like all vehicles, it has its share of common issues that owners may encounter. Some of the more severe issues, such as engine and transmission problems, can be expensive to fix. However, many of the other issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to repair. Overall, the F-550 is a good choice for those who need a heavy-duty pickup truck that can handle tough jobs.
