**2007 Ford F-550 Super Duty**

The 2007 Ford F-550 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck offered with a variety of engine and transmission options. The standard engine is a 6.8L V10 gas engine producing 362 horsepower and 457 lb-ft of torque. Other available engines include a 6.4L Power Stroke diesel engine producing 350 horsepower and 650 lb-ft of torque, and a 6.0L Power Stroke diesel engine producing 325 horsepower and 570 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 6-speed automatic transmission and a 5-speed automatic transmission.

Owners of the 2007 Ford F-550 Super Duty generally praise its powerful engines, towing and hauling capabilities, and comfortable interior. However, some common issues have been reported with this vehicle.

**Engine**

* **6.0L Power Stroke Diesel Engine**
    * **EGR Cooler Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, white smoke from exhaust, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace EGR cooler
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: N/A
    * **Turbocharger Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whistling noise from turbocharger
        * Solution: Replace turbocharger
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: N/A
* **6.4L Power Stroke Diesel Engine**
    * **Oil Cooler Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating
        * Solution: Replace oil cooler
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: N/A
    * **Turbocharger Surge:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Whining or fluttering noise from turbocharger
        * Solution: Update software
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free
        * Recalls: N/A
* **6.8L V10 Gas Engine**
    * **Spark Plug Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Misfires, rough idle
        * Solution: Replace spark plugs
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifts
        * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
        * Recalls: N/A
* **5-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Torque Converter Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Slipping transmission, shuddering
        * Solution: Replace torque converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension:**
    * **Steering Rack Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty steering, loose steering wheel
        * Solution: Replace steering rack
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: N/A
* **Rear Suspension:**
    * **Leaf Spring Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, rough ride
        * Solution: Replace leaf springs
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle won't start, flickering lights
    * Solution: Replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, loss of electrical power
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust on body panels, frame
    * Solution: Rust repair or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC or heater not working properly
    * Solution: Repair or replace climate control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

The 2007 Ford F-550 Super Duty is a capable and powerful truck. However, it has its share of common issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the severity of the damage.
