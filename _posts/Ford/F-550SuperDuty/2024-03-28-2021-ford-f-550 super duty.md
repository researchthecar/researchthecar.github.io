**2021 Ford F-550 Super Duty**

The 2021 Ford F-550 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck that offers a wide range of engine and transmission options. The standard engine is a 6.7-liter Power Stroke turbodiesel V8, which produces 475 horsepower and 1,050 lb-ft of torque. A 7.3-liter gasoline V8 is also available, producing 430 horsepower and 475 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 10-speed automatic transmission, a 6-speed automatic transmission, and a 5-speed manual transmission.

Owners of the 2021 Ford F-550 Super Duty have reported a variety of issues with their vehicles, including engine problems, transmission problems, suspension problems, electrical problems, exterior problems, and interior problems.

**Engine**

* **6.7-liter Power Stroke Turbodiesel V8**
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger may fail, causing a loss of power and increased exhaust emissions.
        * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Injector Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The fuel injectors may fail, causing a loss of power and increased fuel consumption.
        * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **7.3-liter Gasoline V8**
