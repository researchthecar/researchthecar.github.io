**2020 Ford F-550 Super Duty**

The F-550 Super Duty is offered with several different engine and transmission options:

* 6.2L V8 Flex Fuel (385 horsepower, 430 lb.-ft. torque)
* 6.7L Power Stroke V8 Turbo Diesel (400 horsepower, 800 lb.-ft. torque)
* 6.7L Power Stroke V8 Turbo Diesel (450 horsepower, 1050 lb.-ft. torque)
* 6-speed automatic transmission
* 10-speed automatic transmission

The 2020 F-550 receives praise for its powerful engines, spacious interior, and rugged exterior. Some complaints include a hefty price tag, poor visibility, and uncomfortable seats.

**Engine**

**6.2L V8 Flex Fuel**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Burning oil, smoke from the exhaust, low oil levels
    * Solution: Replace the PCV valve and clean the oil passages
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Cooler Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking, overheating, decreased engine performance
    * Solution: Replace the oil cooler
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.7L Power Stroke V8 Turbo Diesel**

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, loud noises from the engine
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, poor fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace the injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Slipping Gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or hesitating, difficulty shifting, delayed engagement
    * Solution: Replace the transmission solenoids or valve body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $700 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**10-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed shifting, hard shifting, clunking noises from the transmission
    * Solution: Update the transmission control module software or replace the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Leaf Springs**

* **Premature Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaf springs breaking or sagging, causing uneven tire wear or a rough ride
    * Solution: Replace the leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Alternator**

* **Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunction, battery not charging, flickering lights
    * Solution: Replace the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Heated and Ventilated Seats**

* **Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seats not heating or cooling properly
    * Solution: Replace the heating/cooling elements
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Power Windows**

* **Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows not working properly, slow operation, or making unusual noises
    * Solution: Replace the window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2020 Ford F-550 Super Duty has various issues, ranging from engine and transmission problems to suspension and electrical malfunctions. While some fixes are relatively inexpensive, others can be costly, potentially driving up repair bills. While the F-550 is generally reliable, it's important to be aware of the potential issues before purchasing.
