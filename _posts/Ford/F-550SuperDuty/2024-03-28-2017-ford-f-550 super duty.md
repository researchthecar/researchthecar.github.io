## 2017 Ford F-550 Super Duty

The 2017 Ford F-550 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck designed for commercial and personal use. It comes equipped with a powerful V8 engine and a range of transmission options. The truck boasts impressive towing and payload capacities, making it an ideal choice for heavy-duty applications.

It features a 6.7L Power Stroke V8 Turbo Diesel engine that generates 440 horsepower and 925 lb-ft of torque. Other engine options include a 6.2L V8 gasoline engine and a 7.3L V8 gasoline engine.

Transmission options include a 6-speed automatic transmission, a 10-speed automatic transmission, and a 6-speed manual transmission.

## Engine

### **6.7L Power Stroke V8 Turbo Diesel**
- **Cracked Cylinder Head**
  - Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, loss of power
  - Solution: Replace cylinder head
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
  - Recalls: NHTSA ID Number: 19V273000
- **EGR Cooler Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, black smoke from exhaust
  - Solution: Replace EGR cooler
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
  - Recalls: None Found

### **6.2L V8 Gasoline**
- **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, loss of power
  - Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Spark Plug Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, reduced fuel economy
  - Solution: Replace spark plugs
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  - Recalls: None Found

### **7.3L V8 Gasoline**
- **No Issues Found**

## Transmission

### **6-Speed Automatic Transmission**
- **Torque Converter Shudder**
  - Description/Symptoms: Vibration while driving, especially at low speeds
  - Solution: Replace torque converter or transmission
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

### **10-Speed Automatic Transmission**
- **Shifting Issues**
  - Description/Symptoms: Hard shifts, delayed shifts, no shifts
  - Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

### **6-Speed Manual Transmission**
- **No Issues Found**

## Suspension

- **Leaf Spring Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Sagging or broken leaf springs
  - Solution: Replace leaf springs
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per side
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Shock Absorber Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Poor ride quality, bouncing or rattling
  - Solution: Replace shock absorbers
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per shock
  - Recalls: None Found

## Electrical

- **Battery Drain**
  - Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely
  - Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Alternator Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, charging system warning light
  - Solution: Replace alternator
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  - Recalls: None Found

## Exterior

- **Tailgate Latch Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Tailgate does not open or close properly
  - Solution: Replace tailgate latch
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Headlight Condensation**
  - Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside headlight assembly
  - Solution: Replace headlight assembly
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  - Recalls: None Found

## Interior

- **Climate Control Malfunction**
  - Description/Symptoms: AC or heater not working properly
  - Solution: Diagnose and repair climate control system
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Radio Malfunction**
  - Description/Symptoms: Radio does not turn on or play music
  - Solution: Replace radio
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

## Summary

The 2017 Ford F-550 Super Duty has several potential issues, including those related to the engine, transmission, and electrical system. Some issues, such as cracked cylinder heads and torque converter shudder, can be costly to repair. However, other issues, such as spark plug failures and alternator failures, are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. Overall, the F-550 has a mix of both severe and minor issues, with the severity and cost of repairs varying depending on the specific problem.
