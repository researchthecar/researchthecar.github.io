**Introduction**

The 2016 Ford F-550 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck that offers a range of engine and transmission options. It is known for its towing and hauling capabilities, along with its spacious cab and comfortable ride. Some popular engine options include the 6.7L Power Stroke V8 diesel engine and the 6.2L V8 gasoline engine. Transmission options range from a 6-speed TorqShift automatic to a 10-speed PowerShift automatic.

**Engine**

**6.7L Power Stroke V8 Diesel**

* **Engine Stalling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine stalls unexpectedly while driving, especially at low speeds.
    * Solution: Replacement of the fuel injector control module (FICM).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased exhaust smoke, and audible noise from the turbocharger.
    * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: 18V382, 20V570

**6.2L V8 Gasoline**

* **Valve Timing Solenoids Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, poor engine performance, and reduced fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replacement of the valve timing solenoids.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: 17S36

**Transmission**

**6-Speed TorqShift Automatic**

* **Transmission Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations and shuddering when shifting gears, especially between 3rd and 4th gear.
    * Solution: Replacement of the torque converter and transmission fluid flush.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,800 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: 17S33

**10-Speed PowerShift Automatic**

* **Transmission Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Whining or grinding noise coming from the transmission.
    * Solution: Replacement of the transmission output shaft bearing.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,400+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Squeaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or popping noise from the front suspension, especially over bumps.
    * Solution: Lubrication of the upper control arms.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Rear Suspension Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension sags, causing the truck to sit lower in the back.
    * Solution: Replacement of the leaf springs or addition of air bags.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, even when the truck is not in use.
    * Solution: Identification and repair of electrical system leaks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Depends on the source of the leak
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical system malfunctions, and difficulty starting the truck.
    * Solution: Replacement of the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $900+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door handle breaks or becomes loose, making it difficult to open the door.
    * Solution: Replacement of the door handle assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: 17S12

**Interior**

* **Seat Bolster Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The seat bolsters become worn or torn over time.
    * Solution: Replacement of the seat upholstery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system fails to adjust the temperature or airflow correctly.
    * Solution: Replacement of the HVAC control module or blend door actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

Overall, the 2016 Ford F-550 Super Duty is a reliable and capable heavy-duty pickup truck. However, it is not without its issues. The most severe and costly issue is the transmission shudder, which can lead to premature transmission failure. Other issues, such as engine stalling and electrical problems, are less severe but still require attention. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues before making a purchase and consider the estimated repair costs.
