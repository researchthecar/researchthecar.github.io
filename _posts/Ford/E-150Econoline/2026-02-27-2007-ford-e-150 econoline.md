# 2007 Ford E-150 Econoline

### Overview
The 2007 Ford E-150 Econoline is a workhorse van known for its utility and robust construction. While generally reliable for its intended purpose, like any vehicle, it can experience specific issues as it ages. This analysis focuses on documented common problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers. We will examine the primary mechanical systems, including the engine and transmission options available for this model year, as well as electrical, suspension, interior, and exterior components.

---


## Engine

### 4.6L Triton V8
**Spark Plug Blowout**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of engine power, rough idling, ticking noise from engine.
- **Solution:** Requires re-threading spark plug holes with a Heli-Coil or Time-Sert insert. Proper torque is crucial during reinstallation.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, engine overheating, rough idle, sweet smell from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold gaskets. Inspect manifold for cracks.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


### 5.4L Triton V8
**Spark Plug Blowout**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of engine power, rough idling, ticking noise from engine.
- **Solution:** Requires re-threading spark plug holes with a Heli-Coil or Time-Sert insert. Proper torque is crucial during reinstallation.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Camshaft Position Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check Engine light, rough idle, stalling, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the camshaft position sensor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**Exhaust Manifold Leak/Rupture**
- **Symptoms:** Ticking or puffing noise from engine, especially on startup; Check Engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the exhaust manifold or manifold studs. May require drilling out broken studs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

## Transmission

### 4R70E 4-Speed Automatic
**Torque Converter Clutch (TCC) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at highway speeds, slipping, delayed engagement, P0741 code.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter and potentially transmission fluid and filter.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200

**Solenoid Pack Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission slipping, P0750-P0760 codes.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the transmission solenoid pack.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Ball Joint Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, uneven tire wear, steering wander.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Leaf Spring Sagging**
- **Symptoms:** Rear end sag, reduced load capacity, poor handling when loaded.
- **Solution:** Replacement of sagging leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, weak or dead battery, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, window stuck in position, grinding noise when operating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Trim
**Rust on Body Panels**
- **Symptoms:** Visible rust spots, bubbling paint, corrosion, especially around wheel wells, rocker panels, and door bottoms.
- **Solution:** Rust repair, panel replacement, and repainting. Prevention through regular washing and undercoating is recommended.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500+

**Door Latch/Lock Actuator Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not locking or unlocking properly, difficulty opening or closing doors.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty door latch or lock actuator.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Interior

### Interior Trim and Upholstery
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents, or air only coming from certain vents, regardless of fan speed setting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor and resistor.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

**Dashboard Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Visible cracks on the dashboard surface, especially in areas exposed to sunlight.
- **Solution:** Dashboard replacement or repair (often cosmetic). Use of sunshades can help prevent further damage.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500+

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Ford E-150 Econoline is a generally robust vehicle, particularly the V8 engine options. Common issues tend to revolve around spark plug retention on the Triton V8s, and potential wear items like ball joints and leaf springs, which are expected for a vehicle of this type and age. Transmission issues, while not universal, can include torque converter and solenoid problems. Electrical components like the alternator and window regulators are also noted areas for potential repair. Exterior rust can be a concern depending on climate and maintenance. Overall, with diligent maintenance and addressing specific known issues, the E-150 can remain a reliable work vehicle.
