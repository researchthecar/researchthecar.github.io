**Introduction:**

The 2002 Ford Explorer Sport Trac is a mid-size pickup truck that was introduced as a replacement for the Ford Explorer Sport. It was offered with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 4.0L V6, a 4.6L V8, and a 5-speed manual or 5-speed automatic transmission. The Sport Trac was praised for its versatility, spacious interior, and powerful engines, while some criticized its handling and fuel economy.

**Engine**

**4.0L V6**

* **Misfire on acceleration**
      * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires and hesitates during acceleration, especially when under load.
      * Solution: Replace spark plugs and ignition coils.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400+
      * Recalls: None Found
* **Cylinder head gasket failure**
      * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks from the cylinder head, overheating, and white smoke from the exhaust.
      * Solution: Replace cylinder head gasket.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000-$1,500+
      * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing chain tensioner failure**
      * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or ticking noise from the engine, especially at startup.
      * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner and timing chain.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800-$1,200+
      * Recalls: None Found

**4.6L V8**

* **Spark plug blowouts**
      * Description/Symptoms: Sparks plugs can blow out of the engine, causing a loss of power and misfiring.
      * Solution: Repair cylinder heads and install stronger spark plugs.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000-$3,000+
      * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake manifold gasket failure**
      * Description/Symptoms: Vacuum leaks from the intake manifold, causing engine performance issues and check engine light.
      * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$800+
      * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch slipping**
      * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips when shifting gears, especially under load.
      * Solution: Replace clutch and pressure plate.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$800+
      * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Delayed or harsh shifting**
      * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifts late or with excessive force.
      * Solution: Flush transmission fluid and replace filters. If issue persists, may require transmission rebuild.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$2,000+
      * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission failure**
      * Description/Symptoms: Transmission may not shift gears or go into gear at all.
      * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000-$4,000+
      * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension clunking**
      * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise from the front suspension when driving over bumps.
      * Solution: Replace sway bar end links and bushings.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400+
      * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear suspension sagging**
      * Description/Symptoms: Rear of the vehicle sags due to worn-out leaf springs.
      * Solution: Replace leaf springs.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$800+
      * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
      * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly when the vehicle is turned off.
      * Solution: Test electrical system for parasitic draw and replace faulty components.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$500+
      * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator failure**
      * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system not charging properly, leading to battery depletion.
      * Solution: Replace alternator.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$800+
      * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door lock actuator failure**
      * Description/Symptoms: Doors do not lock or unlock with the key fob or door handles.
      * Solution: Replace door lock actuators.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400+
      * Recalls: None Found
* **Tailgate cable failure**
      * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate does not open or close properly due to a broken cable.
      * Solution: Replace tailgate cable.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$200+
      * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattles**
      * Description/Symptoms: Interior panels and dashboards rattle or creak while driving.
      * Solution: Tighten or replace loose interior trim.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$300+
      * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2002 Ford Explorer Sport Trac is a reliable vehicle that offers versatility and power. However, it is susceptible to several common issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. The severity of these issues ranges from minor annoyances to major repairs. The cost of repairs can be significant, especially for issues such as cylinder head gasket failure and transmission failure. Owners should be aware of these potential problems and budget accordingly for maintenance and repairs.
