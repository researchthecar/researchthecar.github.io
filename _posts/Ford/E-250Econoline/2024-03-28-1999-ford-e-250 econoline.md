**1999 Ford E-250 Econoline**

The 1999 Ford E-250 Econoline is a full-size van that was available in a variety of configurations, including cargo vans, passenger vans, and cutaway chassis cabs. It was offered with a choice of two engines: a 5.4L V8 or a 7.3L V8, and two transmissions: a 4-speed automatic or a 5-speed manual.

People like the E-250 for its spacious interior, powerful engines, and versatility. However, it has also been known to have some issues, which we will discuss in detail below.

**Engine**

**5.4L V8**

* **Intake Manifold Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Spark Plug Thread Stripping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty installing spark plugs, engine misfires
    * Solution: Repair or replace cylinder head
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**7.3L V8**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, rough idling, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **EGR Valve Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, reduced power, increased emissions
    * Solution: Replace the EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or vibration during acceleration, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping clutch
    * Solution: Replace the clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Transmission Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Grinding or whining noises when shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace the transmission gears or bearings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Front Ball Joint Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or popping noises when turning, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace the ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Rear Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, difficulty steering
    * Solution: Replace the leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies quickly, even when not in use
    * Solution: Check for parasitic draw and replace or repair faulty components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, electrical system problems
    * Solution: Replace the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on the body panels, frame, or undercarriage
    * Solution: Repair or replace rusted parts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of damage
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Door Hinge Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing the doors, sagging doors
    * Solution: Replace the door hinges
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to control the temperature or airflow
    * Solution: Replace the climate control module or actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks or splits in the dashboard
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

The 1999 Ford E-250 Econoline is a versatile and spacious van that has been praised for its powerful engines and ample interior space. However, it has also been known to have some issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and suspension components. The severity of these issues can vary depending on the specific model and year, but some can be costly to repair.
