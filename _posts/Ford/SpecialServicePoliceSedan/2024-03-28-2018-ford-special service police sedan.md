**Introduction**

The 2018 Ford Special Service Police Sedan is a purpose-built vehicle designed to meet the demanding needs of law enforcement agencies. It is available with two engine options: a 3.7L V6 producing 305 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, and a 5.0L V8 producing 400 horsepower and 400 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are mated to a 10-speed automatic transmission.

The Special Service Police Sedan has received generally positive reviews from law enforcement officers, who praise its performance, handling, and durability. However, some owners have reported a number of issues with the vehicle, including:

**Engine**

**3.7L V6**
* **Intake manifold gasket failure:** The intake manifold gasket can fail, causing coolant and oil leaks.
    * **Symptoms:** Coolant leak, oil leak, engine overheating
    * **Solution:** Replace the intake manifold gasket.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500-$700+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Camshaft position sensor failure:** The camshaft position sensor can fail, causing the engine to run rough or stall.
    * **Symptoms:** Engine misfire, rough idle, stalling
    * **Solution:** Replace the camshaft position sensor.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200-$300+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Knock sensor failure:** The knock sensor can fail, causing the engine to lose power.
    * **Symptoms:** Loss of power, engine knocking
    * **Solution:** Replace the knock sensor.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100-$200+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**5.0L V8**
* **Cylinder deactivation system failure:** The cylinder deactivation system can fail, causing the engine to run rough or misfire.
    * **Symptoms:** Engine misfire, rough idle, reduced fuel economy
    * **Solution:** Replace the cylinder deactivation solenoids.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500-$700+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Throttle body failure:** The throttle body can fail, causing the engine to stall or lose power.
    * **Symptoms:** Engine stalling, loss of power, check engine light
    * **Solution:** Replace the throttle body.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300-$500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**10-speed automatic**

* **Transmission shudder:** The transmission can shudder or vibrate when shifting gears.
    * **Symptoms:** Transmission shudder, vibration, harsh shifting
    * **Solution:** Update the transmission control module software or replace the transmission.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000-$2,000+
    * **Recalls:** 18T05 and 19C02
* **Transmission slipping:** The transmission can slip or fail to engage gears.
    * **Symptoms:** Transmission slipping, loss of power, check engine light
    * **Solution:** Replace the transmission.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,500-$4,000+
    * **Recalls:** 18T05 and 19C02

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery can drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * **Symptoms:** Battery drain, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * **Solution:** Inspect for parasitic drains and replace any faulty components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100-$500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Electrical shorts:** Electrical shorts can occur, causing various electrical components to malfunction.
    * **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning electrical components, blown fuses, check engine light
    * **Solution:** Inspect the electrical system for shorts and replace any damaged components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Varies depending on the location and severity of the short.
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate control system failure:** The climate control system can fail, causing the vehicle's interior temperature to become uncomfortable.
    * **Symptoms:** Inoperative climate control, hot or cold interior temperatures
    * **Solution:** Replace the climate control module or actuators.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500-$1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2018 Ford Special Service Police Sedan is a capable and reliable vehicle that has received generally positive reviews from law enforcement officers. However, some owners have reported a number of issues with the vehicle, including engine and transmission problems, electrical shorts, and climate control system failures. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some of them can be quite expensive to repair.
