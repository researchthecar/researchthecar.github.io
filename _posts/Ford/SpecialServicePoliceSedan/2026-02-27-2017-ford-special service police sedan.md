# 2017 Ford Special Service Police Sedan

### Overview
The 2017 Ford Special Service Police Sedan (S.S.P.S.), often based on the Ford Taurus platform, was engineered for demanding law enforcement duties. These vehicles are built with enhanced cooling, upgraded suspension, and robust electrical systems to withstand rigorous use. While generally reliable, the specialized nature of police vehicles means components are subjected to extreme conditions, leading to potential wear beyond typical civilian use. This analysis focuses on documented reliability concerns specific to the police package, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear. Understanding these potential issues is crucial for fleet managers and maintenance personnel.

---


## Engine

### 3.7L V6
**Cooling System Overload**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating during prolonged high-stress situations (e.g., high-speed pursuits, extended idling in extreme temperatures).
- **Solution:** Inspect and potentially upgrade radiator, fans, and coolant lines. Ensure proper coolant levels and condition.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200

**Carbon Buildup**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine performance, rough idling, potential misfires.
- **Solution:** Regular intake manifold cleaning and fuel system service.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### 3.5L EcoBoost V6
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Inspect turbocharger seals and lines for leaks or damage. Turbocharger replacement may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Direct Injection System Clogging**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idle, reduced fuel efficiency.
- **Solution:** Regular cleaning of fuel injectors and intake valves.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (6F50/6F55)
**Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, delayed shifting, transmission fluid smell.
- **Solution:** Ensure proper transmission fluid level and condition. Consider auxiliary transmission cooler installation.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, rough engagement.
- **Solution:** Torque converter replacement.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Suspension

### Police-Tuned Suspension
**Strut/Shock Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh ride, excessive bouncing, poor handling.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn struts and shocks.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200

**Bushings Deterioration**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, imprecise steering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn suspension bushings.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Electrical

### Upgraded Electrical System
**Accessory Power Draw**
- **Symptoms:** Battery drain, intermittent failure of police equipment (lights, radio, computers).
- **Solution:** Ensure auxiliary battery system is functioning correctly. Inspect wiring harnesses for damage or corrosion. Upgrade alternator if necessary.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Control Module Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Warning lights, erratic behavior of vehicle systems.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of affected control modules.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000


## Exterior

### Body Panels & Lighting
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Cost:** N/A


## Interior

### Cabin & Controls
**Wear on Driver's Seat**
- **Symptoms:** Tearing or excessive wear on the driver's seat upholstery due to frequent ingress/egress.
- **Solution:** Seat cover replacement or professional upholstery repair.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

**Wear on Steering Wheel/Controls**
- **Symptoms:** Rubbing or wear on steering wheel and dashboard buttons from frequent use.
- **Solution:** Replacement of steering wheel or individual buttons.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Ford Special Service Police Sedan is a robust platform designed for demanding law enforcement applications. While generally reliable, its specialized nature means certain components, particularly those related to the powertrain and suspension, can experience accelerated wear or specific failures due to extreme operational stress. Common issues include engine and transmission overheating under heavy load, accelerated wear on suspension components, and electrical system strain from auxiliary equipment. Proactive maintenance, regular inspections, and addressing potential issues like cooling system capacity and electrical load management are key to maintaining the reliability and operational readiness of these vehicles. The interior components, especially the driver's seat and controls, may show signs of heavy use.
