**Introduction**

The 2017 Ford Special Service Police Sedan is a high-performance vehicle designed for law enforcement applications. It is powered by a 3.5-liter EcoBoost V6 engine, producing 375 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

* **3.5-liter EcoBoost V6**

    * **Turbocharger Bearing Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whining or grinding noises from the engine
        * Solution: Replace turbocharger
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Manifold Runner Control Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Decreased engine performance, stalls, check engine light
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **10-speed Automatic**

    * **Shift Solenoid Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or rough shifts, slipping gears, check engine light
        * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension**

    * **Strut Assembly Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise, instability while driving
        * Solution: Replace strut assembly
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension**

    * **Coil Spring Breakage**
        * Description/Symptoms: Sagging in the rear, loss of ride height
        * Solution: Replace coil springs
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery**

    * **Battery Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle won't start, dimming headlights
        * Solution: Replace battery
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator**

    * **Alternator Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim headlights
        * Solution: Replace alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlights**

    * **Headlight Assembly Condensation**
        * Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside headlight assembly
        * Solution: Replace headlight assembly
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard**

    * **Instrument Cluster Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Gauges not working, flickering display
        * Solution: Replace instrument cluster
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
        * Recalls: 16V873

**Summary**

The 2017 Ford Special Service Police Sedan generally receives positive reviews for its performance and capabilities. However, it has experienced einige issues related to the engine, suspension, and electrical systems. The severity of these issues and their repair costs can vary widely. It's recommended to research and address these potential problems before purchasing the vehicle.
