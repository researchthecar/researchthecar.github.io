**Introduction:**

The 2016 Ford Special Service Police Sedan is a robust and capable law enforcement vehicle. It features a powerful 3.7-liter V6 engine that generates 300 horsepower and 278 lb-ft of torque. Power is sent to the rear wheels through a 6-speed automatic transmission. The Police Sedan also offers excellent handling and braking, thanks to its fully independent suspension and robust brake system.

While the Police Sedan is a reliable and well-made vehicle, it has experienced some issues since its release. The most common problems reported by owners include:

**Engine**

* **3.7L V6**
    * **Intake manifold gasket failure:** Intake manifold gasket can fail, causing a vacuum leak. Symptoms include rough idling, decreased engine power, and increased fuel consumption. Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000.
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure:** Timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to skip. Symptoms include a rattling noise from the engine, decreased engine power, and eventually engine failure. Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner and timing chain. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+ (requires engine disassembly).
* **Recalls:**
    * NHTSA Campaign Number: 17V370000   - Powertrain: Engine
    * NHTSA Campaign Number: 18V383000   - Engine and Engine Cooling

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission slipping:** Transmission may slip or shudder during gear changes. Symptoms include rough shifting, loss of power, and eventually transmission failure. Solution: Replace transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+ (requires transmission removal).
    * **Torque converter failure:** Torque converter may fail, causing the transmission to lose power. Symptoms include loss of acceleration, decreased engine power, and eventual transmission failure. Solution: Replace torque converter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000 (requires transmission removal).
* **Recalls:**
    * None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery failure:** Battery may fail prematurely. Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle, dimming lights, and electrical system malfunctions. Solution: Replace battery. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+ (depending on battery type).
* **Alternator failure:** Alternator may fail, preventing the battery from charging. Symptoms include a dead battery, flickering lights, and electrical system malfunctions. Solution: Replace alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ (requires electrical system diagnosis).
* **Recalls:**
    * None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Interior trim rattles:** Interior trim pieces may rattle or squeak over rough roads. Solution: Use sound deadening material or adjust trim pieces. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 (if professional installation is needed).
* **Climate control system failure:** Climate control system may fail, causing the heating or air conditioning to malfunction. Solution: Diagnose and repair climate control system. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ (depending on severity of issue).

**Summary Notes:**

The 2016 Ford Special Service Police Sedan is a generally reliable and capable vehicle, but some issues have been reported by owners. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific problem. While some issues are minor and can be fixed at a relatively low cost, others can be more serious and expensive to repair.
