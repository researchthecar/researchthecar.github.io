**Introduction:**

The 1992 Ford Topaz is a mid-size sedan available with a choice of two engine options and a 4-speed automatic transmission. The 2.3L inline-4 produces 110 horsepower and 135 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.0L V6 makes 140 horsepower and 165 lb-ft of torque. Owners appreciate the Topaz's comfortable ride and fuel efficiency, but some have reported issues with its reliability.

**Engine**

**2.3L Inline-4**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, engine misfires
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **EGR Valve Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Rough idle, stalling, poor fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L V6**

* **Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping between gears
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Struts/Shocks Wearing Out:**
    * Symptoms: Rough ride, clunking noises, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace struts/shocks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sway Bar Links Failing:**
    * Symptoms: Clunking noises, especially when going over bumps
    * Solution: Replace sway bar links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Battery not charging, dimming headlights, engine stalling
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Starter Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Engine cranks but won't start, clicking noise when starting
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust Prone:**
    * Symptoms: Rust forming on body panels, especially around wheel wells and lower body
    * Solution: Preventative measures (rustproofing, waxing) or body repairs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Symptoms: Cracks forming on dashboard
    * Solution: Replace dashboard or cover with dash mat
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Power Window Motor Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Power windows not working, grinding noises when operating
    * Solution: Replace power window motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+ per window
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1992 Ford Topaz is prone to several issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and suspension. While some issues are relatively minor and affordable to fix, others can be costly and require major repairs. The severity and cost of these issues can make the Topaz a less desirable choice for those seeking a reliable and affordable vehicle.
