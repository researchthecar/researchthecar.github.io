# 2013 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan

### Overview
The 2013 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan, built on the Ford Taurus platform, was engineered for demanding law enforcement duty. It offered robust performance and durability, with specific attention paid to components that would withstand high-stress environments. While generally reliable, like any vehicle subjected to rigorous use, certain areas have shown a propensity for issues that are beyond standard maintenance. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related problems reported by fleet operators and mechanics, providing insights into potential long-term reliability concerns for this specialized vehicle.

---


## Engine

### 3.7L V6
**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, overheating, visible leaks from hoses or radiator.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, radiator, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Intake Manifold Cracks**
- **Symptoms:** Vacuum leaks, rough idle, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


### 3.5L EcoBoost V6
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, oil leaks from turbo seals.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**Direct Injection System Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idle, carbon buildup on intake valves.
- **Solution:** Cleaning of intake valves, potential replacement of fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (6F50/6F55)
**Torque Converter Shudder**
- **Symptoms:** Vibration or shuddering during acceleration, especially at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, or torque converter replacement.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1500

**Transmission Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Delayed shifting, slipping gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair of cooling system, potential transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $4000


## Suspension

### Heavy-Duty Police Suspension
**Worn Ball Joints and Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, imprecise steering, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints and suspension bushings.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

**Strut/Shock Absorber Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, poor handling, visible leaks from shocks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of struts or shock absorbers.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming lights, battery warning light, starting issues.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Wiring Harness Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical component failures, short circuits.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of damaged wiring sections.
- **Cost:** $300 - $900


## Exterior

### Standard Body and Trim
**Door Latch Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not locking or unlocking properly, interior lights not functioning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty door latch actuators.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Interior

### Standard Interior Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents, or intermittent airflow.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2013 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan demonstrates a generally robust build, suitable for its intended purpose. However, the demanding nature of police work can expose weaknesses beyond typical consumer vehicle usage. The 3.7L V6 and 3.5L EcoBoost engines have reported issues with cooling systems, intake manifolds, and turbochargers, respectively. Transmission problems, particularly torque converter shudder and overheating, are also noted. Suspension components like ball joints and bushings are prone to wear due to constant stress. Electrical issues such as alternator failure and wiring harness problems can arise. Interior and exterior components, while less critical, can also experience failures like door latch actuators and HVAC blower motors. Regular, thorough maintenance and prompt attention to any developing issues are crucial for maximizing the longevity and reliability of these specialized vehicles.
