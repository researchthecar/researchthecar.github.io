# 2016 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan

### Overview
The 2016 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan, built on the robust platform of the Ford Taurus, was engineered for demanding law enforcement duty. It prioritized durability and performance, often incorporating heavy-duty components beyond standard civilian vehicles. While generally reliable, the unique operational stresses of police work can highlight specific areas of concern. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into the long-term reliability of its core systems, including the powertrain, suspension, and electrical components, as experienced in rigorous service.

---


## Engine

### 3.7L V6
**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, overheating, visible leaks around hoses and radiator.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, clamps, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Intake Manifold Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Vacuum leaks, rough idle, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


### 3.5L EcoBoost V6
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, excessive smoke.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of turbocharger(s).
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**Direct Injection System Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idle, reduced fuel efficiency.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of fuel injectors and related components.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (6F55)
**Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, delayed shifting, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, potential cooler upgrade.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering, slipping, rough engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


## Suspension

### Heavy-Duty Police Suspension
**Strut/Shock Absorber Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, poor handling, visible leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of struts and shocks.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

**Ball Joint and Bushing Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises, steering looseness, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints and suspension bushings.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming lights, battery warning light, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Wiring Harness Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical faults, non-functioning accessories, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair/replacement of damaged wiring.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its core powertrain and chassis components, designed for the rigors of law enforcement. Common issues tend to stem from the increased stress of police duty, such as suspension wear and potential overheating of the transmission under extreme conditions. Engine-specific concerns, especially with the EcoBoost variant, can involve turbocharger and direct injection systems. Electrical components like the alternator are also points to monitor. Overall, with diligent maintenance and addressing specific wear items promptly, the vehicle remains a capable and dependable choice for its intended purpose.
