**Introduction:**
The 2013 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan is a performance-oriented vehicle designed for law enforcement agencies. It is powered by a 3.5L V6 engine that produces 365 horsepower and 380 lb-ft of torque. The Police Interceptor Sedan is available with a 6-speed automatic transmission or a 10-speed automatic transmission. The vehicle is praised for its powerful engine, nimble handling, and spacious interior. However, it has also been reported to have some issues.

**Engine**
* **3.5L V6 Ti-VCT (365 HP/380 lb-ft)**
    * **Spark plug failure:** Premature failure of spark plugs can cause misfires, rough idling, and reduced performance.
        * **Solution:** Replace failed spark plugs.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
* **3.5L V6 EcoBoost (365 HP/420 lb-ft)**
    * **Turbocharger failure:** The turbocharger may fail due to overheating or excessive wear, resulting in reduced power and increased fuel consumption.
        * **Solution:** Replace failed turbocharger.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $3,000+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**
* **6-speed automatic transmission (non-optional)**
    * **Shift flare:** Hesitation or delayed gear shifts can occur, leading to rough driving.
        * **Solution:** Update transmission control module software or replace valve body.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
* **10-speed automatic transmission (optional)**
    * **Harsh shifting:** Transmission may shift abruptly, causing discomfort for occupants.
        * **Solution:** Update transmission control module software or replace transmission.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**
* **Front suspension:**
    * **Strut failure:** Premature failure of front struts can lead to poor handling, excessive tire wear, and noise.
        * **Solution:** Replace failed struts.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Rear suspension:**
    * **Lower control arm failure:** The lower control arm can develop cracks or rust, potentially causing handling issues or even a suspension collapse.
        * **Solution:** Replace failed lower control arm.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $700+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**
* **Battery drain:** The vehicle's battery may discharge prematurely, resulting in problems starting the car.
    * **Solution:** Inspect electrical system for parasitic drains and repair or replace faulty components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Headlight failure:** Headlights may experience premature burnout or failure.
    * **Solution:** Replace failed bulbs or repair electrical harness.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $50 - $200+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**
* **Clearcoat peeling:** The clearcoat on the vehicle's paint may peel or flake off, exposing the underlying primer or paint.
    * **Solution:** Repair or replace damaged clearcoat.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Rust:** Rust may develop on body panels, especially around the wheel wells and undercarriage.
    * **Solution:** Treat rust and apply corrosion protection.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**
* **Climate control malfunction:** The climate control system may fail to properly regulate temperature or airflow.
    * **Solution:** Inspect blend doors, actuators, and electrical connections for issues.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Infotainment system freezing:** The infotainment system may freeze or become unresponsive.
    * **Solution:** Perform a software reset or update, or replace faulty components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**
The 2013 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan is a reliable vehicle with powerful performance and a comfortable interior. However, it has been reported to have some issues, such as engine and transmission problems, suspension issues, electrical issues, and exterior concerns. The severity and cost of these issues vary widely, but some can be significant and require costly repairs.
