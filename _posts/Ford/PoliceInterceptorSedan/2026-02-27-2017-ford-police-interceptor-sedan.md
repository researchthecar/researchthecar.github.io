# 2017 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan, based on the Taurus platform, is built for heavy-duty fleet service with robust cooling and suspension upgrades. While intended to withstand severe operational demands, buyers looking at retired police cruisers should inspect them carefully for signs of extensive idle time and aggressive use. Powertrain options include both standard naturally aspirated V6 power and a high-performance EcoBoost V6, giving buyers a range of performance levels to choose from.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L Ti-VCT V6

This naturally aspirated 3.7-liter V6 engine produces around 305 horsepower and 279 lb-ft of torque, offering reliable baseline performance for fleet use. It generally proves durable in service, though high-mileage examples should be checked for standard maintenance upkeep.

### 3.5L EcoBoost Twin-Turbo V6

This twin-turbocharged 3.5-liter V6 generates approximately 365 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque, providing strong all-wheel-drive acceleration. Buyers should watch for carbon buildup and turbocharger health during pre-purchase inspections.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed SelectShift Automatic Transmission

This heavy-duty 6-speed automatic transmission pairs with all-wheel drive or front-wheel drive configurations to handle high-output police duties. Regular fluid changes are critical for longevity, especially given the heavy duty cycle of retired fleet vehicles.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V530000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Police+Interceptor+Sedan&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the heat shield detaches, the steering gear may overheat, increasing the steering effort required by the driver, especially at lower speeds, and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2017 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan, prioritize models with complete maintenance logs and verify engine and transmission health, as many units have endured rigorous service conditions. Thoroughly check the vehicle history for excessive idling hours and structural wear before purchase.
