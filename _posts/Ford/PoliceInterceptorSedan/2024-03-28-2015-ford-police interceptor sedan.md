**Introduction:**

The 2015 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan is a high-performance law enforcement vehicle based on the Ford Taurus. It is available with two engine options: a 3.7L V6 and a 3.5L EcoBoost V6. Both engines are paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The 3.7L V6 produces 305 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.5L EcoBoost V6 generates 365 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque. The Police Interceptor Sedan has received praise for its powerful engines, responsive handling, and comfortable interior. However, it has also been criticized for its high fuel consumption and some reported reliability issues.

**Engine**

**3.7L V6**
- **Intake Manifold Runner Control Valve Issues:**
    - Description: The intake manifold runner control valve can become stuck or fail, causing a loss of power or rough idling.
    - Solution: Replace the intake manifold runner control valve.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Timing Chain Tensioner Issues:**
    - Description: The timing chain tensioner can fail, leading to a loss of engine power or catastrophic engine failure.
    - Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $600-$1,000+
    - Recalls: 19S34

**3.5L EcoBoost V6**
- **Turbocharger Issues:**
    - Description: The turbochargers can fail prematurely, causing a loss of power or reduced fuel economy.
    - Solution: Replace the turbochargers.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500-$2,500+
    - Recalls: 22S43 and 22S44

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

- **Shift Solenoid Issues:**
    - Description: The shift solenoids can fail, causing harsh shifts or difficulty shifting gears.
    - Solution: Replace the shift solenoids.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$500+
    - Recalls: 19S33

**Suspension**

- **Front Control Arm Bushings:**
    - Description: The front control arm bushings can wear out prematurely, causing a clunking noise or vibration.
    - Solution: Replace the front control arm bushings.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Rear Shock Absorbers:**
    - Description: The rear shock absorbers can fail, causing a loss of ride comfort and handling.
    - Solution: Replace the rear shock absorbers.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Battery Drain Issues:**
    - Description: The vehicle's battery may drain prematurely, leaving drivers stranded.
    - Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the battery drain.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$600+
    - Recalls: 19S95
- **Electrical Harness Issues:**
    - Description: The electrical harness can develop shorts or loose connections, causing various electrical problems.
    - Solution: Repair or replace the affected section of the electrical harness.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400-$1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Rear Bumper Cover Issues:**
    - Description: The rear bumper cover can crack or break at the mounting points.
    - Solution: Replace or repair the rear bumper cover.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Center Console Lid Issues:**
    - Description: The center console lid may not latch properly or may break.
    - Solution: Replace the center console lid.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$300+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Overhead Console Issues:**
    - Description: The overhead console may rattle or become loose.
    - Solution: Tighten or replace the overhead console.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$200+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2015 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan offers strong performance and a well-equipped interior, it has been plagued by several reliability issues. Engine and transmission problems, including intake manifold and turbocharger failures, can be expensive to repair. Suspension and electrical issues further contribute to the vehicle's reliability concerns. These issues can not only impact the safety and performance of the vehicle but also result in significant downtime and repair costs for law enforcement agencies.
