# 2019 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan

### Overview
The 2019 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan, built on the Taurus platform, was engineered for demanding law enforcement duty. It offered robust performance and durability, with specific attention paid to powertrain and chassis components to withstand rigorous use. While generally reliable, like any vehicle subjected to extreme conditions, certain areas can experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for fleet managers and mechanics.

---


## Engine

### 3.7L V6
**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, overheating, visible coolant puddles under the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace leaking hoses, water pump, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Timing Chain Rattle**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup.
- **Solution:** Replace timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


### 3.5L EcoBoost V6
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace turbocharger assembly or related plumbing.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**Coolant Leaks (Intake Manifold)**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, potential misfires, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replace intake manifold gaskets or the manifold itself.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (6F55)
**Harsh Shifting / Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, delayed engagement, slipping between gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter service, solenoid replacement, or full rebuild.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3000

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping, potential stalling.
- **Solution:** Replace torque converter.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1500


## Suspension

### Police-Tuned Suspension
**Worn Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replace worn control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Premature Strut/Shock Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, reduced handling stability, visible leaks from struts/shocks.
- **Solution:** Replace struts and/or shocks.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, dead battery after sitting.
- **Solution:** Diagnose parasitic draw, check alternator and battery health.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

**Faulty Sensors (ABS/Wheel Speed)**
- **Symptoms:** ABS warning light, traction control light, inaccurate speedometer.
- **Solution:** Replace faulty wheel speed sensor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly in its core powertrain and chassis components designed for heavy-duty use. The most commonly reported issues tend to be related to the cooling system (leaks) and potential wear on suspension components like bushings and struts, which can be accelerated by demanding patrol conditions. Transmission issues, while not pervasive, can occur and may require significant repair. Electrical gremlins, such as battery drain or sensor failures, are also noted. Regular, diligent maintenance and prompt attention to early symptoms are crucial for maximizing the lifespan and minimizing downtime of these specialized vehicles.
