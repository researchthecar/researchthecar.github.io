## 2019 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan

The 2019 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan is a law enforcement vehicle based on the Ford Taurus. It is available with two engine options: a 3.3L Ti-VCT V6 and a 3.5L EcoBoost V6. Both engines are paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission. The Police Interceptor Sedan is praised for its powerful engines, impressive handling, and spacious interior. However, it has also been reported to have some issues, which are detailed below.

### Engine

**3.3L Ti-VCT V6**

* **Timing Chain Failure:** The timing chain may prematurely stretch or break, causing engine damage. Symptoms include a rattling noise from the engine, difficulty starting, and reduced engine power. The solution is to replace the timing chain and related components. Estimated cost to fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:** The intake manifold gasket may fail, causing a vacuum leak. Symptoms include rough idling, reduced engine power, and a hissing noise from the engine. The solution is to replace the intake manifold gasket. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000

**3.5L EcoBoost V6**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger may fail prematurely due to contamination or internal damage. Symptoms include a loss of engine power, blue or black smoke from the exhaust, and a rattling noise from the engine. The solution is to replace the turbocharger. Estimated cost to fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
* **Fuel Injector Failure:** The fuel injectors may fail, causing engine misfires or hesitation. Symptoms include rough idling, reduced engine power, and a check engine light. The solution is to replace the fuel injectors. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000 per injector

### Transmission

**10-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Issues:** The transmission may experience rough or delayed shifting, especially during downshifts. The solution is to reprogram the transmission control module or replace the transmission fluid. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $500
* **Transmission Fluid Leak:** The transmission may develop a leak, causing fluid loss and reduced transmission performance. The solution is to replace the leaking component. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $1,000

### Suspension

* **Front Suspension Noise:** The front suspension may make a clunking or rattling noise when driving over bumps or uneven surfaces. The solution is to replace the worn or damaged suspension components. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000+

### Electrical

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may prematurely discharge due to a parasitic electrical draw. The solution is to identify and repair the source of the electrical drain. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $500
* **Alternator Failure:** The alternator may fail, causing the battery to not charge properly. Symptoms include dim headlights, flickering interior lights, and a check engine light. The solution is to replace the alternator. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000

### Exterior

* **Tail Light Failure:** The tail lights may intermittently fail due to a faulty wiring harness. The solution is to replace the wiring harness. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $500

### Interior

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system may malfunction, causing difficulty adjusting the temperature or air flow. The solution is to replace the climate control module or other related components. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Power Seat Failure:** The power seat may fail to adjust or become stuck. The solution is to replace the power seat motor or switch. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $500

## Summary

The 2019 Ford Police Interceptor Sedan is a powerful and capable law enforcement vehicle. However, it has been reported to have some issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage. To ensure optimal performance and reliability, it is important to have the vehicle inspected and maintained regularly by a qualified mechanic.
