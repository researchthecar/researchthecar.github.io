# 2017 Ford Escape

### Overview
The 2017 Ford Escape is a compact SUV that offers a blend of practicality, comfort, and available all-wheel drive. It was generally well-received for its driving dynamics and versatile interior.  However, like many vehicles, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on common technical reliability concerns, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear, to provide a clear picture of potential mechanical and electrical challenges owners might encounter.  Understanding these potential issues can help in proactive maintenance and informed purchasing decisions.

---


## Engine

### 1.5L EcoBoost
**Coolant Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, overheating, visible coolant residue.
- **Solution:** Replacement of cracked cylinder head or faulty seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger inspection and potential replacement.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


### 1.6L EcoBoost
**Coolant Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, overheating, visible coolant residue.
- **Solution:** Replacement of cracked cylinder head or faulty seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Spark Plug Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, reduced fuel economy.
- **Solution:** Replacement of spark plugs and potentially ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### 2.0L EcoBoost
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger inspection and potential replacement.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000

**Coolant Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, overheating, visible coolant residue.
- **Solution:** Replacement of cracked cylinder head or faulty seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


## Transmission

### 6F35 Automatic Transmission
**Harsh Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** Jerking or rough shifts between gears, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush, solenoid replacement, or transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2500

**Transmission Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission warning light, burning smell, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission cooler inspection and repair/replacement, fluid service.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system reset, or replacement of the infotainment module.

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, dead battery, electrical components malfunctioning.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Ford Escape presents a mixed reliability profile. The EcoBoost engines, particularly the 1.5L and 1.6L variants, have shown a propensity for coolant leaks and turbocharger issues, which can be costly to repair. The automatic transmission can also exhibit problems like harsh shifting and overheating. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and battery drain, are also noted. However, the suspension, exterior, and interior components generally appear to be robust with no widespread reported issues. Prospective buyers should be aware of these potential engine and transmission concerns and factor in potential repair costs.
