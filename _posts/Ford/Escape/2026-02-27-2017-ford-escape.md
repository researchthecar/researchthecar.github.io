## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V617000: Visibility:Power Window Devices And Controls](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Escape&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 118, "Power-Operated Window Systems." If a window closes on a body part, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Ford has notified owners, and dealers will update the power window operating system software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V413000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Gear Position Indication (Prndl)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Escape&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Either scenario increases the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the under hood shift bushing and add a protective cap over the shift cable bushing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V011000: Equipment:Electrical:Engine Block Heater](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Escape&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The engine block heater may crack and develop a coolant leak, causing it to short circuit when the block heater is plugged in. An electrical short circuit can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the block heater, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V374000: Visibility:Power Window Devices And Controls](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Escape&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 118, "Power-Operated Window Systems." A closing window that does not reverse direction as expected can pinch a driver or passenger before retracting, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the power window operating system software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
