**1995 Ford LTS8000F**

The 1995 Ford LTS8000F is a heavy-duty tow truck offered with two engine and transmission options:

* **Engine:**
    * 5.8L V8 (360 cu. in.) OHV - 215 hp @ 3800 rpm, 315 lb-ft @ 3200 rpm
* **Transmission:**
    * 4-speed automatic transmission (E4OD)

**Engine**

**5.8L V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Visible coolant or oil leaks, poor engine performance, overheating
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Head Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: White smoke from exhaust, low coolant levels, overheating
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **EGR Valve Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, stalling, poor fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-speed Automatic Transmission (E4OD)**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Bushing Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises, uneven tire wear, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace worn bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Leaf Spring Breakage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear suspension, uneven tire wear, harsh ride
    * Solution: Replace broken leaf spring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, difficulty starting, battery warning light on
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies after sitting for a period of time
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Heater Core Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: No heat from heater, sweet smell when heater is on
    * Solution: Replace heater core
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to operate power windows
    * Solution: Replace power window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1995 Ford LTS8000F is a reliable and capable heavy-duty tow truck. However, like all vehicles, it is not without its potential issues. The most common problems reported by owners include engine gasket failures, suspension bushing wear, and electrical issues. While these issues can be expensive to fix, they are generally not severe and can be prevented with proper maintenance and care. Overall, the LTS8000F is a good choice for those who need a heavy-duty tow truck that is both reliable and affordable.
