**Introduction:**

The 1990 Ford FT900 was a heavy-duty pickup truck that offered various engine and transmission options. Popular for its powerful performance and durability, it also had some notable drawbacks.

**Engine**

**7.5L V8**
* **Blown Head Gaskets:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine overheating, coolant leaks, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace head gaskets and bolts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4.9L V8**
* **Timing Chain Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and related components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**
* **Slipping Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, hesitant acceleration
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,800 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Manual**
* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Axle Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Steering instability, excessive wear on tires
    * Solution: Replace axle or related components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Springs Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear end of truck sitting low
    * Solution: Replace rear leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim headlights
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Wiring Harness Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions, intermittent problems
    * Solution: Inspect and repair wiring harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on body panels, especially undercarriage
    * Solution: Rustproofing, repair or replacement of affected panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies significantly depending on extent of damage
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Tailgate Latch Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate difficult to open or close
    * Solution: Replace tailgate latch mechanism
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dash Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on dashboard surface
    * Solution: Replacement of dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating not working
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of climate control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 1990 Ford FT900 offered powerful performance, it had several common issues that could be costly to fix. The engine was prone to head gasket failures and timing chain problems, while the transmission could experience gear slippage. Suspension problems included front axle issues and rear springs sagging. Electrical problems involved alternator failures and wiring harness issues. Exterior issues were limited to rust and tailgate latch problems. The interior had issues with dashboard cracking and climate control malfunctions.
