# 2007 Ford Ikon

### Overview
The 2007 Ford Ikon, a subcompact car primarily sold in emerging markets, offered a practical and economical option for many buyers. ## Suspension

### Standard Independent Front / Torsion Beam Rear
**Worn Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn suspension bushings.
- **Cost:** $150 - $350


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light illuminated, difficulty starting the engine, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Faulty Window Regulators**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows failing to operate, slow or jerky window movement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250 per window


## Exterior

### Standard Body and Trim
**Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Rust appearing on wheel arches, door sills, and underbody, especially in areas with harsh winters or high humidity.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment and repair, rustproofing.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1000+

**Headlight Clouding**
- **Symptoms:** Yellowing or hazing of plastic headlight lenses, reducing light output and visibility.
- **Solution:** Headlight restoration kit or replacement of headlight units.
- **Cost:** $50 - $200


## Interior

### Standard Interior Components
**HVAC Fan Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Heating or air conditioning fan not working at any speed, or only working at certain speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor or fan motor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**Worn Seat Fabric**
- **Symptoms:** Tearing or excessive wear on seat upholstery, particularly on the driver's seat.
- **Solution:** Seat cover installation or professional upholstery repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Ford Ikon generally presents as a reliable vehicle for its class, with its powertrain (engine and transmission) showing few widespread technical issues. The most common concerns tend to be related to wear items in the suspension, such as bushings, and some electrical components like the alternator and window regulators. Exterior issues like corrosion and headlight hazing are also noted, particularly on older models or those exposed to harsh environments. Interior components like the HVAC fan motor and seat wear are also potential points of attention. Overall, with regular maintenance and addressing these specific potential issues, the 2007 Ikon can remain a dependable car.
