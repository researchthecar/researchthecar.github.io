# 2022 Ford Expedition Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Ford Expedition is a full-size SUV offering generous passenger room, strong towing capabilities, and modern technology. However, used car buyers should approach this model year with care due to a variety of owner-reported complaints concerning powertrain hesitations, electrical quirks, and body/glass integrity.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L EcoBoost V6

The standard 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged EcoBoost V6 engine delivers impressive horsepower and robust torque figures suited for heavy hauling. While generally powerful, this powerplant has generated owner complaints regarding fuel delivery issues and sudden stalls.

**Fuel Pump and Delivery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle suddenly loses acceleration, experiences power loss, and may shut down completely while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel system components and replace faulty fuel pumps or related parts under active safety campaigns.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Engine splutters, shakes, or unexpectedly stalls when idling, parking, or starting.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fuel pressure delivery issues and update or replace failing engine control modules.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 10-speed automatic transmission designed to optimize fuel economy across various driving conditions, it pairs with both rear-wheel and four-wheel-drive configurations. Some owners report harsh shifting behaviors and delays when cold.

**Erratic and Hard Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission shifts violently, hesitates, slams into gear, or experiences rough engagement when shifting into reverse or drive.
- **Solution:** Reprogram the transmission control module or inspect internal mechanical components for wear.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides adequate stopping power for a large SUV, though specific concerns involve annoying brake noise and trailer brake integration.

**Brake Squeal and Trailer Controller Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Loud squealing noises when coming to a stop, or failure of the trailer brake controller to engage automatically.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and trailer brake wiring modules for proper operation.

## Exterior

### Audio and Lighting Electronics

Electrical architecture supports advanced infotainment and driver aids, but owners have reported isolated issues with audio equipment failures and exterior lighting.

**Audio Amplifier Static and Headlight Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Loud popping or static noises from speakers causing severe driver distraction, or sudden failure of exterior headlights.
- **Solution:** Replace malfunctioning audio amplifiers or defective headlight assemblies.

### Glass and Roof Panels

The exterior features expansive glass options, though complaints highlight unexpected structural glass breakage on panoramic roofs.

**Spontaneous Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Panoramic sunroof or rear liftgate glass unexpectedly shattering while driving or parked.
- **Solution:** Replace shattered glass panels and check for frame alignment or manufacturing stress points.

## Interior

### Seating

Cabin comfort is generally high, though third-row seating materials may exhibit premature wear or design flaws.

**Third-Row Seat Cushion Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Internal metal seat frames poking through the third-row seat cushions, causing discomfort and wear.
- **Solution:** Replace seat cushions or padding assemblies.

## Safety

### Air Bag and Collision Systems

Safety equipment includes multiple airbags and active driver assists, but electronic sensor faults and warning lights have frustrated some owners.

**Warning Lights and Collision Avoidance Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Air bag warning lights illuminating, seat belt locking unexpectedly, or collision avoidance systems failing intermittently.
- **Solution:** Have diagnostic trouble codes scanned and faulty sensors or wiring harnesses replaced.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V193000: Electrical System: Integrated Trailer Brake Control](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Expedition&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Due to a software error, a towed trailer equipped with an electric or electric-over hydraulic brake system may not brake. Loss of trailer brake functionality could result in extended stopping distance, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the integrated trailer brake control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V420000: Equipment:Other:Owners/Service/Other Manual](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Expedition&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 202, "Head Restraints." Without instructions, the head restraints may be improperly adjusted, increasing the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V852000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Expedition&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirement Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Without a label that shows the proper restraint usage guidelines, the occupants may have an increased risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V132000: Electrical System: Integrated Trailer Brake Control](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Expedition&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Due to a software error, a towed trailer equipped with an electric or electric-over hydraulic brake system may not brake. Loss of trailer brake functionality can extend the distance required to stop, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the integrated trailer brake control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V314000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Expedition&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of brake function increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the front brake line and replace the brake line or air cleaner outlet pipe, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V315000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Expedition&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera image to delay, freeze, or not display when the vehicle is in reverse. A frozen or missing rearview camera image can reduce the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V455000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Expedition&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the low pressure fuel pump on super duty vehicles and update the powertrain control module (PCM) software on all other affected vehicles, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V729000: Visibility:Sun/Moon Roof Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=Expedition&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A detached wind deflector can become a road hazard and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the moonroof wind deflector as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2022 Ford Expedition, verify that all applicable safety recalls have been addressed by a certified dealer, especially regarding fuel delivery and visibility components. Pay close attention to transmission shifting smoothness and check for any history of electrical or glass defects during your test drive.
