# 2022 Ford Expedition

### Overview
The 2022 Ford Expedition is a full-size SUV known for its spacious interior, robust towing capabilities, and comfortable ride. As a significant refresh for the model year, it introduced updated styling, technology, and powertrain options. Reliability data for the 2022 model, being relatively new, is still accumulating. However, initial reports and the established track record of its core components suggest a generally solid performance. Owners often praise its family-friendly features and on-road manners. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the Expedition's engineering aims for durability and long-term usability, particularly with its well-regarded V6 engine and smooth-shifting transmissions.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Ford Expedition demonstrates strong reliability, particularly in its powertrain and core mechanical components. The 3.5L EcoBoost V6 and 10-speed automatic transmission are well-established and generally perform without significant issues. The independent rear suspension also contributes to a robust chassis. The most commonly reported, though not widespread, concerns tend to be minor electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches or occasional battery issues, which are not uncommon in modern vehicles with complex electronics. Overall, the 2022 Expedition is a dependable choice for a large SUV, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic flaws.
