# 2017 Ford Expedition Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Ford Expedition is a full-size SUV offering impressive cabin room, strong towing capacity, and a comfortable ride. However, prospective buyers should carefully review historical owner complaints and safety recalls. Key areas of concern involve brake performance, powertrain operation, and suspension hardware that require attention during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L EcoBoost V6

The 3.5L twin-turbocharged EcoBoost V6 produces approximately 365 horsepower and 420 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration and towing capability. While generally durable, early iterations can experience timing chain and cam phaser wear that requires attentive maintenance.

**Timing System and Cam Phaser Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report rattling noises upon cold startup, illuminated check engine lights, and potential loss of motive power on the highway.
- **Solution:** Inspect timing chain components and cam phasers; replace worn parts with updated components if rattle or fault codes are present.

### Master Cylinder and Hydraulic Brakes

The hydraulic brake system provides adequate stopping power for a large SUV, though master cylinder integrity is a critical inspection point due to reported fluid loss.

**Brake Fluid Leaks and Pedal Softness**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience a spongy or sinking brake pedal feel, reduced braking efficiency, and low brake fluid levels due to master cylinder leaks.
- **Solution:** Inspect the brake master cylinder for internal leaks and replace faulty assemblies under applicable recall guidelines or service procedures.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission is paired with rear-wheel drive or four-wheel drive systems. It delivers smooth shifts under normal conditions, though owners have reported occasional electronic or hydraulic control anomalies.

**Unexpected Harsh Downshifting**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly downshift into a lower gear while traveling at highway speeds, causing sudden deceleration, brief wheel slip, and erratic speedometer readings.
- **Solution:** Diagnose transmission control modules and valve bodies; update software or repair internal mechanical components as needed.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Lug Nuts

Suspension and wheel components are generally robust, though minor hardware annoyances like swollen lug nuts frequently affect routine maintenance.

**Swollen Factory Lug Nuts**
- **Symptoms:** Outer metal caps on factory lug nuts swell or become detached, preventing standard tire sockets from fitting and making roadside flat tire changes extremely difficult.
- **Solution:** Replace factory two-piece lug nuts with solid, single-piece aftermarket alternatives.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2017 Ford Expedition, verify that all safety recalls—particularly those concerning brake hydraulic components and rear-view camera systems—have been properly addressed by a dealer. Prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the 3.5L EcoBoost timing components, transmission shift behavior, and brake master cylinder condition to ensure long-term reliability.
