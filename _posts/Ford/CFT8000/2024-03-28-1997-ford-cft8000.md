**Introduction:**

The 1997 Ford CFT8000 is a heavy-duty commercial vehicle available with various engine and transmission configurations. The popular 7.3L Power Stroke diesel engine provides 275 horsepower and 525 lb-ft of torque. The vehicle is known for its high payload and towing capacity, but it has also encountered some issues.

**Engine:**

**7.3L Power Stroke Diesel**
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
- Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, high-pitched whistling noise.
- Solution: Replace turbocharger.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,500+
* **Injection Pump Failure:**
- Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, rough idle, decreased fuel economy.
- Solution: Replace injection pump.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Injector Failure:**
- Description/Symptoms: Misfiring, knocking, decreased power.
- Solution: Replace injectors.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+

**Transmission:**

**TorqShift 5-Speed Automatic**
* **Slippage:**
- Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifts erratically or fails to engage gears.
- Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Torque Converter Failure:**
- Description/Symptoms: Slipping, overheating, or shuddering during gear changes.
- Solution: Replace torque converter.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Suspension:**
* **Leaf Spring Failure:**
- Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, rough ride, broken leaves.
- Solution: Replace leaf springs.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical:**
* **Alternator Failure:**
- Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, flickering gauges, battery warning light.
- Solution: Replace alternator.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $750+
* **Starter Failure:**
- Description/Symptoms: Engine cranks but does not start.
- Solution: Replace starter.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
* **Wiring Harness Issues:**
- Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunctions, check engine light, blown fuses.
- Solution: Repair or replace wiring harness.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior:**
* **Headlight Malfunction:**
- Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering headlights, burnt-out bulbs.
- Solution: Replace headlight bulbs or assemblies.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* **Mirror Adjustment Failure:**
- Description/Symptoms: Mirrors fail to adjust electronically.
- Solution: Repair or replace mirror motors.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior:**
* **Heater/AC Failure:**
- Description/Symptoms: No heat or air conditioning, fan not working.
- Solution: Repair or replace blower motor, heater core, or AC compressor.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+

**Summary:**

The 1997 Ford CFT8000 can experience some significant issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. Repair costs can be high, ranging from $1,000 to over $4,000 for major components. These problems can affect the vehicle's reliability and overall performance, requiring regular maintenance and costly repairs.
