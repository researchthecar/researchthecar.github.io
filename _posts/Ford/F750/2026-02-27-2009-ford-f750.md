# 2009 Ford F750

### Overview
The 2009 Ford F-750 is a heavy-duty commercial truck designed for demanding vocational applications. Reliability for this model year is generally considered robust, particularly for its powertrain options which are built for durability and sustained performance under load. Common issues, when they arise, tend to be related to the specific operational demands placed on such vehicles rather than inherent design flaws. Maintenance and proactive checks are crucial for maximizing the lifespan and minimizing downtime of these workhorses. This analysis focuses on documented common failures and their associated repair considerations.

---


## Engine

### 6.7L Power Stroke V8 Diesel
**EGR Cooler Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, white smoke from exhaust, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the EGR cooler assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noise.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger inspection, cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500


### 7.2L Caterpillar C7 Diesel
**Injector Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, poor fuel economy, engine misfires, black smoke.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant level, visible leaks, overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair of hoses, gaskets, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


### 8.3L Cummins ISL Diesel
**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, engine stalling, loss of power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the fuel lift pump.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000

**Oil Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** External oil leaks, coolant contamination in oil.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil cooler seals or the unit itself.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Transmission

### Allison 2000 Series Automatic
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, shuddering, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


### Allison 3000 Series Automatic
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Low transmission fluid level, shifting problems, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair of seals, lines, or transmission pan.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### Eaton Fuller Manual (Various Models)
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the clutch assembly.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Suspension

### Heavy-Duty Leaf Spring Suspension
**Leaf Spring Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, uneven ride height, clunking noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of broken leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200 per axle


### Air Suspension (Optional)
**Air Bag Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking air bags or lines.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light illuminated, dimming lights, starting issues.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

**Wiring Harness Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical faults, non-functioning accessories, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair/replacement of damaged wiring.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Frame
**Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Rust on frame rails, body panels, and undercarriage, especially in salt-belt regions.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment, panel replacement, or frame repair.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3000+


### Lighting
**Headlight/Taillight Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture inside light housings.
- **Solution:** Sealing or replacement of light assemblies.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400 per unit


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Poor heating or cooling, unusual noises from vents.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of blower motor, blend doors, or refrigerant issues.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


### Seat Upholstery
**Wear and Tear**
- **Symptoms:** Rips, tears, and excessive wear on driver's seat from frequent use.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seat covers or the entire seat.
- **Cost:** $200 - $700


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2009 Ford F-750 is a capable work truck with a reputation for durability, especially concerning its diesel powertrains. While robust, like any heavy-duty vehicle, it is susceptible to issues stemming from high mileage and demanding operational environments. Common concerns include EGR cooler and turbocharger problems on the Power Stroke, injector and coolant leaks on the C7, and fuel pump issues on the Cummins. Transmission reliability is generally good, though torque converter and fluid leak issues can occur. Suspension components, particularly leaf springs, can experience wear and breakage under heavy use. Electrical issues like alternator failure and wiring problems are not uncommon. Exterior corrosion is a significant concern in certain climates. Interior issues are typically limited to HVAC and seat wear. Overall, with diligent maintenance and timely repairs, the F-750 can remain a reliable asset.
