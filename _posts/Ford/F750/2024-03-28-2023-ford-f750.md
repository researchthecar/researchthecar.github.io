**Introduction:**

The 2023 Ford F750 is a heavy-duty pickup truck known for its powerful engine options, robust towing and hauling capabilities, and spacious interior. It is available with a range of engines, including a 6.7L Power Stroke diesel V8 and a 7.3L gasoline V8. Transmission options include a 10-speed TorqShift automatic transmission and a 6-speed manual transmission.

Owners appreciate the F750's impressive performance and durability, along with its well-appointed interior and advanced features. However, there have been some reported issues with specific components.

**Engine**

**6.7L Power Stroke Diesel V8**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking fuel injector causing poor engine performance, rough idling, and reduced fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Replace faulty injector(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **EGR System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Exhaust Gas Recirculation system malfunctions can lead to increased emissions, reduced power, and check engine light illumination.
    * Solution: Repair or replace EGR valve and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Turbocharger Failures:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Turbocharger failure can cause reduced power, black smoke from exhaust, and whistling noises.
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**7.3L Gasoline V8**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**10-Speed TorqShift Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission may hesitate or jerk while shifting gears, especially during low speeds or under load.
    * Solution: Update transmission software or replace faulty solenoids.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission struggles to engage gears or may slip out of gear while driving.
    * Solution: Replace worn or damaged clutch packs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Failures:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaf springs may break or bend under heavy load, causing sagging and potential vehicle instability.
    * Solution: Reinforce or replace leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+ per side
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Shock Absorber Failures:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged shock absorbers can lead to excessive bouncing and poor handling.
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+ per pair
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Weak or discharged battery can cause slow cranking, electrical system malfunctions, and failure to start.
    * Solution: Replace battery or check charging system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator Failures:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning alternator can fail to charge the battery, leading to electrical system problems and stalling.
    * Solution: Replace alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate may fail to open or close properly due to damaged cables, hinges, or latches.
    * Solution: Repair or replace tailgate components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Bedliner Damage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Factory-installed bedliner may crack or peel under heavy use.
    * Solution: Replace bedliner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen may freeze, lag, or display error messages.
    * Solution: Software updates or system reset.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system may fail to regulate temperature effectively, resulting in overheating or freezing.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty sensors, actuators, or refrigerant leaks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Ford F750 has received mixed reviews from owners. While its powerful engines, excellent towing capabilities, and advanced features are praised, there have been some reported issues with specific components, especially in the engine and transmission categories. The severity and cost of these issues vary, and some may require professional repair or replacement. It is recommended that potential buyers carefully consider these issues and factor in potential repair costs when making their purchase decision.
