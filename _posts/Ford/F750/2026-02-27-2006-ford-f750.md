# 2006 Ford F750

### Overview
The 2006 Ford F-750 is a heavy-duty commercial truck designed for demanding vocational applications. Reliability for this model year is generally considered robust, particularly for its core powertrain components, reflecting its purpose-built nature. However, like any commercial vehicle, specific issues can arise depending on usage, maintenance, and the particular configuration of engine and transmission. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard wear and tear, providing insights into potential problem areas and their associated costs for owners and fleet managers.

---


## Engine

### 5.9L Cummins ISB (2006 Model Year)
**Injector Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, loss of power, increased exhaust smoke, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per injector, $2000 - $4000 for a set.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, engine stalling, loss of power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the fuel injection pump.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500


### 7.2L Caterpillar C7 (2006 Model Year)
**Oil Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant in oil, oil in coolant, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil cooler.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1500

**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noises.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the turbocharger.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


### 6.0L Power Stroke Diesel (2006 Model Year)
**EGR Cooler Failure**
- **Symptoms:** White smoke from exhaust, coolant loss, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the EGR cooler.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200

**Head Gasket Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, white smoke, overheating, coolant in oil.
- **Solution:** Replacement of head gaskets and related components.
- **Cost:** $2500 - $5000


## Transmission

### Allison 2000 Series (Automatic)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping, shuddering, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500


### Allison 3000 Series (Automatic)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible fluid leaks, low fluid level, potential transmission damage.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals and gaskets.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


### Eaton Fuller Manual Transmissions (e.g., FS-5406A, RTLO-8918B)
**Clutch Actuator Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting, clutch pedal issues.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the clutch actuator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Suspension

### Heavy-Duty Leaf Spring Suspension
**Leaf Spring Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging ride height, uneven tire wear, clunking noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of broken leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800 per spring set.


### Air Suspension (if equipped)
**Air Bag Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, loss of ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air bags.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per bag.


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light, dimming lights, starting problems.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Wiring Harness Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical faults, non-functioning accessories.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair/replacement of damaged wiring.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Frame
**Corrosion on Frame Rails**
- **Symptoms:** Rust and degradation of the frame, potential structural compromise.
- **Solution:** Frame treatment, repair, or replacement (in severe cases).
- **Cost:** $500 - $5000+


### Lighting Components
**Headlight/Taillight Moisture Intrusion**
- **Symptoms:** Fogging inside lenses, reduced light output.
- **Solution:** Replacement of affected light assemblies.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400 per assembly.


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Poor heating or cooling, unusual noises from the system.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of blower motor, evaporator, or condenser.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000


### Seat Mechanisms
**Seat Adjustment Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty adjusting or locking seats in position.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seat adjustment mechanisms.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2006 Ford F-750 demonstrates a generally solid reliability record, especially when equipped with the Cummins or Caterpillar engines, which are known for their durability in heavy-duty applications. The Allison transmissions also tend to be robust. However, potential owners should be aware of specific issues like injector and fuel pump problems on the Cummins, oil cooler and turbo issues on the Cat, and EGR/head gasket concerns on the 6.0L Power Stroke. Suspension components, particularly leaf springs, can experience wear and breakage under heavy use. Electrical issues like alternator failure and wiring problems are not uncommon in older heavy-duty vehicles. Regular and thorough maintenance is crucial to mitigate these potential problems and ensure the longevity of the F-750.
