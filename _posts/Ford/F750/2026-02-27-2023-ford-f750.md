### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Ford F-750 demonstrates strong reliability in its core powertrain components, with both the 7.3L V8 Gas and 6.7L Power Stroke Diesel engines, along with their associated TorqShift HD transmissions, showing no widespread reported issues. The chassis and suspension systems are also generally robust. Areas that may require attention include the electrical system, specifically potential issues with wiring harness connectivity and alternator longevity, which are common concerns in heavy-duty vehicles. Exterior and interior components appear to be holding up well. Overall, the F-750 is a dependable workhorse, but proactive electrical system checks and maintenance are advisable.
