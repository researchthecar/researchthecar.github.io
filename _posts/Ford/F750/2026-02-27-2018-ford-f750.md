# 2018 Ford F750

### Overview
The 2018 Ford F-750 is a heavy-duty commercial truck designed for demanding vocational applications. Reliability is a key consideration for fleet operators and business owners. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, examining common problem areas across its various configurations. While the F-750 is generally built for durability, specific components and engine/transmission pairings can exhibit certain tendencies. Understanding these potential issues can help in proactive maintenance and informed purchasing decisions for this robust workhorse.

---


## Engine

### 6.7L Power Stroke V8 Diesel
**EGR Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, white smoke from exhaust, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the EGR cooler assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Fuel Injector Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, loss of power, increased fuel consumption, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


### 6.8L Triton V10 Gas
**Spark Plug Blow-Out**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough running, loss of power, ticking noise from engine.
- **Solution:** Installation of inserts or replacement of cylinder heads.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500

**Intake Manifold Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vacuum leaks, rough idle, check engine light with lean codes.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Ford F-750 demonstrates solid reliability for its class, with the diesel Power Stroke engine and the TorqShift automatic transmission being the most common configurations. Potential issues, such as EGR cooler leaks and fuel injector problems in the diesel, and spark plug blow-out in the gas V10, are documented but not necessarily universal. Electrical system components like alternators and wiring harnesses can also require attention over time. Overall, with proper maintenance and addressing specific known issues, the F-750 is a dependable choice for commercial use.
