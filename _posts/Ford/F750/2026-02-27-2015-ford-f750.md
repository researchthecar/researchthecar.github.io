# 2015 Ford F750

### Overview
The 2015 Ford F-750 is a heavy-duty commercial truck designed for demanding vocational applications. Reliability is a key consideration for fleet operators and business owners. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, examining common problem areas across its powertrain, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior systems. The F-750 typically utilizes robust components to withstand heavy use, but like any vehicle, certain areas may experience specific challenges. Understanding these potential issues can help in proactive maintenance and long-term operational efficiency.

---


## Engine

### 6.7L Power Stroke V8 Diesel
**EGR Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, white smoke from exhaust, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the EGR cooler assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Fuel Injector Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, loss of power, increased fuel consumption, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500


### 6.8L Triton V10 Gas
**Spark Plug Blowout**
- **Symptoms:** Misfiring, rough running, loss of power, audible popping sound from engine.
- **Solution:** Installation of oversized spark plugs or thread inserts.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Intake Manifold Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vacuum leaks, rough idle, poor performance, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold gasket or manifold.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Ford F-750 demonstrates a generally solid reliability record for its class, particularly concerning its powertrain options. The 6.7L Power Stroke diesel and 6.8L Triton V10 gas engines, while robust, can experience specific issues like EGR cooler leaks or spark plug blowouts, respectively, which are not uncommon for these engine types in heavy-duty applications. Transmission options, especially the TorqShift automatic, are typically very dependable. Electrical systems, particularly the alternator and wiring harnesses, are the most frequently cited areas for potential problems, necessitating attention. Suspension, exterior, and interior components generally hold up well under typical vocational use. Proactive maintenance and addressing early signs of the noted engine and electrical issues can significantly contribute to the longevity and operational efficiency of the 2015 F-750.
