**Introduction:**

The 2017 Ford F650 is a heavy-duty pickup truck renowned for its robust diesel engine options, impressive towing and payload capacities, and comfortable interior. It offers two engine choices: a 6.7L Power Stroke V8 diesel and a 6.8L V10 gasoline engine. Engine power ranges from 330 to 450 horsepower, while torque outputs vary from 735 to 1,250 lb-ft. Boasting a sturdy frame and advanced suspension system, the F650 excels in handling rugged terrain and hauling heavy loads. The truck's comfortable interior features ample space, premium materials, and user-friendly technology.

**Engine**

**6.7L Power Stroke V8 Diesel**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace faulty fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Boost Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, whistling or hissing noises
    * Solution: Repair or replace leaking boost hose/pipe
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed TorqShift Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delay or difficulty engaging gears, slipping between gears
    * Solution: Replace or overhaul transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations when accelerating or decelerating
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Broken or cracked leaf spring, uneven ride height
    * Solution: Replace broken leaf spring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or leaky shock absorbers, reduced ride quality
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharge while parked or overnight
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical component causing drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative or flickering headlights
    * Solution: Repair or replace faulty headlight assemblies/wiring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust Prone:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on body panels, frame, or undercarriage
    * Solution: Regular maintenance, touch-up paint, rust prevention measures
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Paint Peeling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Peeling or flaking paint on body panels, especially on high-impact areas
    * Solution: Repaint affected areas
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative or weak heating or cooling system
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty HVAC components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Instrument Cluster Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inaccurate or malfunctioning gauges, warning lights
    * Solution: Replace instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Ford F650 offers robust engine options, impressive towing and payload capacities, and a comfortable interior. However, several issues can arise with this heavy-duty truck, including fuel injector failure, turbocharger boost leaks, transmission slipping, suspension component breakage, electrical battery drain, rust formation, paint peeling, HVAC malfunctions, and instrument cluster failures. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific part or component affected. Regular maintenance, timely repairs, and preventive measures can help mitigate some of these issues and extend the vehicle's lifespan.
