# 2004 Ford F650

### Overview
The 2004 Ford F650 is a heavy-duty commercial truck known for its robust construction and capability for various vocational applications. Reliability data for this model year indicates a generally solid performance, particularly in its core powertrain components. However, like many heavy-duty vehicles, specific issues can arise depending on usage, maintenance, and the chosen configurations. This analysis focuses on documented common problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and fleet managers.

---


## Engine

### 7.2L Caterpillar C7 Diesel
**Injector Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, loss of power, increased exhaust smoke, poor fuel economy.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per injector

**EGR Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** White smoke from exhaust, coolant loss, overheating, coolant in exhaust.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the EGR cooler assembly.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


### 5.9L Cummins ISB Diesel
**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Engine stalling, difficulty starting, loss of power, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the fuel lift pump.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noises, reduced fuel efficiency.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the turbocharger.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500


### 7.8L Ford Power Stroke Diesel
**Injector Harness Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough running, no-start condition, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the injector harness.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Oil Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant in oil, oil in coolant, overheating, milky oil.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil cooler.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900


## Transmission

### Allison 2000 Series Automatic
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping, shuddering, delayed engagement, overheating.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Solenoid Pack Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, transmission fault codes, inability to shift gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the transmission solenoid pack.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


### Eaton Fuller Manual Transmissions (e.g., RTLO series)
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the clutch assembly.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500

**Synchronizer Ring Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Grinding when shifting, difficulty engaging gears.
- **Solution:** Rebuild of the transmission with new synchronizer rings.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Suspension

### Heavy-Duty Leaf Spring Suspension
**Leaf Spring Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, uneven ride height, clunking noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of broken leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per spring

**Shackle Bushing Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, loose feel in steering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn shackle bushings.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming lights, battery warning light, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Wiring Harness Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical faults, non-functioning accessories, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair/replacement of corroded wiring harnesses.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Frame
**Frame Rust**
- **Symptoms:** Visible rust on frame rails, structural weakness, potential safety hazard.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment, repair, or frame replacement (if severe).
- **Cost:** $500 - $5000+

**Door Latch/Hinge Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not closing properly, sticking doors, squeaking hinges.
- **Solution:** Lubrication, adjustment, or replacement of latches and hinges.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from vents, weak airflow, unusual noises from dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Seat Upholstery Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Torn or worn seat covers, especially on driver's seat.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seat upholstery.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2004 Ford F650 generally exhibits good reliability, especially with its core powertrain options like the Cummins and Caterpillar engines, and Allison transmissions. Common issues tend to be related to specific component failures that are not uncommon in heavy-duty applications, such as injector problems, fuel pump issues, and torque converter wear. Suspension components, particularly leaf springs, can experience wear and breakage under heavy use. Electrical system issues like alternator failure and wiring corrosion are also noted. Interior wear, especially on seats, is typical for a work vehicle. Regular and thorough maintenance is crucial for mitigating many of these potential problems and ensuring the longevity of the F650.
