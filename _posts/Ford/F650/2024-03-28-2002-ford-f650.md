**Introduction**

The 2002 Ford F650 is a heavy-duty pickup truck with two available engine options. The first is a 7.3L Power Stroke diesel engine that produces 235 horsepower and 525 lb-ft of torque. The second is a 6.8L V10 gas engine that produces 305 horsepower and 420 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are mated to a 5-speed automatic transmission.

The F650 comes with a variety of features, including four-wheel drive, a tow package, and a heavy-duty suspension. It also has a spacious cab with seating for up to three people.

People who like the F650 praise its powerful engine, rugged construction, and spacious cab. However, they also complain about its poor fuel economy and rough ride.

**Engine**

* **7.3L Power Stroke Diesel Engine**
    * **Injector Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, rough idling, smoke from the exhaust
        * Solution: Replace the injectors
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of boost, black smoke from the exhaust
        * Solution: Replace the turbocharger
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **6.8L V10 Gas Engine**
    * **Spark Plug Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Misfiring, engine knocking
        * Solution: Replace the spark plugs
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found
