**2021 Ford F650**

The 2021 Ford F650 is a heavy-duty truck designed for commercial and industrial use. It is powered by a 6.7L Power Stroke diesel engine mated to a 6-speed automatic transmission. The F650 offers a variety of configurations, including a dump truck, flatbed, and utility body. It has a payload capacity of up to 12,000 pounds and a towing capacity of up to 33,000 pounds. The F650 is known for its ruggedness, reliability, and durability. However, like all vehicles, it has some reported issues.

**Engine**

* **6.7L Power Stroke Diesel Engine**

    * **Turbocharger Failure**
      * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, check engine light
      * Solution: Replace turbocharger
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
      * Recalls: None Found
    * **Injector Failure**
      * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, rough running, loss of power, black smoke
      * Solution: Replace injectors
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000 per injector
      * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

    * **Shifting Issues**
      * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifts, slipping
      * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
      * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension**
    * **Leaf Spring Failure**
      * Description/Symptoms: Sagging, uneven ride height, squeaking
      * Solution: Replace leaf springs
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per side
      * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension**
    * **Air Bag Leak**
      * Description/Symptoms: Loss of ride height, uneven ride height, warning light
      * Solution: Replace air bags
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per bag
      * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dimming lights, electrical system malfunction
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies quickly, headlights stay on after ignition off
    * Solution: Find and fix source of drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights dim or fail
    * Solution: Replace headlight bulbs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200 per bulb
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Storage Bin Hinge Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hinge breaks, causing storage bin to fall open
    * Solution: Replace hinge
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Steering Wheel Audio Controls Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Buttons stick or do not work
    * Solution: Replace steering wheel audio control unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Ford F650 is a generally reliable and capable heavy-duty truck. However, it has a few reported issues that can be costly to repair. The most severe issues include turbocharger failure, injector failure, and transmission shifting problems. These issues can impact the vehicle's performance, safety, and reliability. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and to have them addressed promptly by a qualified mechanic.
