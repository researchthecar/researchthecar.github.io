# 2016 Ford F650

### Overview
The 2016 Ford F-650 is a heavy-duty commercial truck designed for demanding vocational applications. Reliability for this model year is generally considered robust, particularly for its intended use in fleet operations and specialized services. Owners and operators often praise its durability and the availability of powerful powertrain options. However, like any heavy-duty vehicle, specific components can experience issues under strenuous conditions. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns, excluding routine maintenance and expected wear and tear, to provide a clear picture for potential buyers and fleet managers.

---


## Engine

### 6.7L Power Stroke V8 Turbo Diesel
**EGR Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, white smoke from exhaust, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the EGR cooler assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noises.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger inspection, cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500


### 7.2L Caterpillar C7 Diesel
**Injector Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, poor fuel economy, engine misfires, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200 per injector

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles, low coolant level, overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair of hoses, seals, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (e.g., Allison 2500/3000 Series)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping, shuddering, delayed engagement, overheating.
- **Solution:** Torque converter replacement or transmission service.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible fluid leaks, low fluid level, shifting problems.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals, lines, or transmission pan.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600


### 6-Speed Manual Transmission
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Suspension

### Heavy-Duty Leaf Spring Suspension
**Leaf Spring Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, uneven ride height, clunking noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of broken leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900 per axle


### Air Suspension (Optional)
**Air Bag Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air bags or air lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per bag


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light, dimming lights, starting problems.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

**Wiring Harness Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical faults, non-functioning accessories, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of damaged wiring.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Ford F-650 demonstrates solid reliability for its class, with the most common technical concerns revolving around the powertrain, particularly the EGR system and turbochargers on the Power Stroke diesel, and fuel injectors on the Caterpillar option. Transmission issues, while not pervasive, can include torque converter problems and clutch wear on manual variants. Suspension components like leaf springs can experience breakage under heavy loads. Electrical systems are generally robust, though alternator failures and wiring harness issues can occur. The exterior and interior components are typically durable for commercial use. Regular maintenance and addressing issues promptly are key to maximizing the longevity and performance of the F-650.
