**Introduction:**
The 2015 Ford F650 is a heavy-duty commercial truck available with a 6.7L Power Stroke V8 Diesel engine mated to a six-speed TorqShift HD automatic transmission or a 6.7L Power Stroke V8 Diesel engine mated to a 10-speed TorqShift HD automatic transmission. The F650 is known for its impressive towing and payload capacities, rugged construction, and versatility. However, it has also been reported to have some issues.

**Engine**

**6.7L Power Stroke V8 Diesel Engine with Six-Speed TorqShift HD Automatic Transmission**

* **Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rough idle, reduced power, black smoke
    * Solution: Replace injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Valve Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine power loss, decreased fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.7L Power Stroke V8 Diesel Engine with 10-Speed TorqShift HD Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed acceleration
    * Solution: Replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed TorqShift HD Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifter Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficult or impossible to shift gears
    * Solution: Replace shifter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**10-Speed TorqShift HD Automatic Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or broken leaf springs
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature battery discharge
    * Solution: Inspect and repair electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Hood Release Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to open the hood
    * Solution: Replace hood release latch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Overheating:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive heat emanating from center console
    * Solution: Replace center console
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2015 Ford F650 is a capable and versatile commercial truck but has been reported to have some issues. While the severity and cost of the issues can vary, the most common problems include engine injector failure, transmission slipping, and electrical issues. It is recommended that potential buyers research and address these issues before purchasing a used F650.
