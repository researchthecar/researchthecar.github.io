# 2019 Ford SSV Plug-In Hybrid

### Overview
The 2019 Ford SSV Plug-In Hybrid, while a capable and efficient vehicle, presents a mixed reliability profile. As a plug-in hybrid, it incorporates complex powertrain and electrical systems that can be sources of potential issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical failures beyond standard maintenance. Owners have reported specific concerns primarily related to the hybrid system's integration and certain powertrain components. While the core Ford engineering is generally robust, the advanced technology inherent in a PHEV can introduce unique challenges. This report aims to provide a clear, data-driven overview of common technical reliability concerns for this model year.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Atkinson Cycle Hybrid
**Hybrid Battery Cooling System**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating warnings, reduced electric range, system fault lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of coolant pump, hoses, or battery coolant.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Inverter/Converter Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, charging issues, dashboard warning lights related to hybrid system.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power inverter module.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


## Transmission

### eCVT (Electronic Continuously Variable Transmission)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic gear selection, hesitation during acceleration, occasional limp mode.
- **Solution:** Software update or recalibration of the transmission control module.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**Electric Motor/Generator Bearing Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Whining or grinding noises, especially at low speeds or during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn bearings or the affected motor/generator unit.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

## Electrical

### 12V Auxiliary Battery
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the gasoline engine, flickering dashboard lights, accessory malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V auxiliary battery.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


### High-Voltage Battery Pack
**Degradation**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced electric-only range, slower charging times, battery warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential module replacement or full pack replacement under warranty if applicable.
- **Cost:** $3000 - $8000+

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Ford SSV Plug-In Hybrid demonstrates average reliability for a vehicle in its class, with the primary concerns centering around its advanced hybrid powertrain components. Issues with the hybrid battery cooling system, inverter, and the eCVT's software are the most frequently cited technical problems. The high-voltage battery pack's longevity and potential degradation are also factors to consider. While the gasoline engine and conventional chassis components are generally robust, owners should be aware of the potential costs associated with hybrid system repairs, particularly outside of warranty. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are crucial for mitigating these issues.
