# 2015 Ford Ranger

### Overview
The 2015 Ford Ranger, a robust mid-size pickup truck, generally exhibits good reliability, making it a popular choice for both work and personal use. While specific model years and configurations can influence long-term durability, the 2015 model year benefits from Ford's extensive experience with this platform. Owners often praise its sturdy construction and capable performance. However, like any vehicle, certain components and systems may experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related problems reported by owners and technicians, providing insights into potential areas of concern and their associated repair costs.

### 3.2L Duratorq TDCi I5 Diesel
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, black smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**EGR Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, white smoke from exhaust, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the EGR cooler.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,000

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start, battery dies prematurely.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to find parasitic draw, repair or replace faulty component.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

**Faulty Alternator**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, electrical system malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Alternator replacement.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Ford Ranger is generally a reliable truck, particularly with the gasoline engine option. The 3.2L diesel engine, while powerful, has a few more documented issues, primarily related to the turbocharger and EGR cooler, which can be costly to repair. Automatic transmission issues, such as harsh shifting, are also noted and may require professional attention. Electrical problems like battery drain and alternator failure are not uncommon as vehicles age. Exterior issues are minor, with tailgate latch problems being the most frequently reported. Overall, the 2015 Ranger offers solid dependability, but potential buyers, especially of the diesel variant, should be aware of these specific potential repair areas.
