# 2017 Ford Ranger Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a 2017 Ford Ranger, prospective buyers should keep in mind that vehicle availability and specifications can vary by global market region, as this model year was not sold as a new light truck in the North American market. For those importing or evaluating international variants, reviewing maintenance history and verifying recall completions remain essential steps before purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.2L Duratorq TDCi 4-Cylinder Diesel

A 2.2-liter turbocharged inline-four diesel engine offering dependable torque output suited for utility and towing. It is generally regarded as robust with routine maintenance, though fuel system health should be verified.

### 3.2L Duratorq TDCi 5-Cylinder Diesel

A larger 3.2-liter five-cylinder turbocharged diesel engine providing higher horsepower and substantial pulling power. Buyers should inspect for standard diesel wear items such as EGR valve cleanliness and turbocharger operation.

### 2.5L Duratec 4-Cylinder Gasoline

A naturally aspirated 2.5-liter gasoline engine providing basic, straightforward power for fleet or lighter duty applications. It features a conventional design that typically presents few mechanical complications.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

A traditional six-speed manual gearbox offering direct driver control and simpler long-term maintenance. Buyers should check clutch engagement and shift smoothness during a test drive.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A six-speed automatic transmission designed to balance fuel economy and smooth power delivery across various driving conditions. Regular fluid changes are recommended to ensure long-term durability.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Reports highlight concerns regarding safety campaign coordination and parts availability for airbag-related campaigns.

**Takata Air Bag Recall Delays**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience delays or difficulties in scheduling recall service due to initial parts availability for airbag inflator replacements.
- **Solution:** Contact a local authorized dealership using the vehicle identification number (VIN) to verify if all applicable safety recalls have been completed free of charge.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Ford Ranger offers a range of durable diesel and gasoline powertrains paired with manual or automatic gearboxes. While major mechanical complaints are minimal in owner reports, buyers must prioritize checking safety recall histories—particularly regarding airbag components—to ensure complete peace of mind.
