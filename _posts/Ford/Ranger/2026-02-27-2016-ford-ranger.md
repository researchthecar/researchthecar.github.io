# 2016 Ford Ranger Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a 2016 Ford Ranger requires careful evaluation of available powertrain options and owner-reported history. While this model year sees limited direct owner complaint data in standard tracking, buyers should still thoroughly inspect vehicle condition and verify that any applicable recall services have been successfully performed.

---

## Engine

### 2.2L Duratorq TDCi I4 Diesel

The 2.2-liter four-cylinder turbo-diesel engine delivers around 150 horsepower and steady low-end torque for utility tasks. It is generally robust when maintained regularly, though buyers should check for proper service history.

### 3.2L Duratorq TDCi I5 Diesel

The 3.2-liter five-cylinder turbo-diesel engine produces roughly 197 horsepower and strong pulling power. It offers solid long-term reliability for heavy-duty use, provided routine maintenance intervals have been respected.

### 2.5L Duratec I4 Petrol

The 2.5-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder petrol engine generates about 164 horsepower for standard light-duty driving. It represents a simpler mechanical option with a dependable track record.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The six-speed manual transmission offers direct gear selection and straightforward mechanical durability. It is well-suited for buyers prioritizing simplicity and direct control.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The six-speed automatic transmission provides smooth shifting across various driving conditions. Regular fluid changes are recommended to ensure optimal longevity and shift quality.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Safety equipment relies heavily on proper component functionality and timely completion of manufacturer recall notices.

**Air Bag Component Availability**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience delays or difficulties in securing replacement parts for safety-related air bag recalls.
- **Solution:** Consult with an authorized dealership to check the status of open recalls and arrange for permanent remedy parts as soon as they become available.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Ford Ranger provides a solid foundation for a used midsize truck, featuring dependable diesel and petrol engine choices paired with manual or automatic gearboxes. Buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verify that all safety recalls have been addressed before finalizing a purchase.
