# 2004 Ford E-150 Club Wagon

### Overview
The 2004 Ford E-150 Club Wagon, a versatile full-size van, offered a range of powertrain options to suit various needs, from hauling cargo to transporting passengers. Reliability for this model year is generally considered average for its class and age. Common issues tend to be related to age and mileage, particularly with components like the transmission and certain electrical systems. However, the core mechanicals, especially the larger V8 engines, are known for their durability when properly maintained. Owners often report satisfaction with the vehicle's utility and robustness, though proactive maintenance is key to mitigating potential problems.

---


## Engine

### 4.2L V6
**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, radiator, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vacuum leaks, rough idle, potential coolant loss.
- **Solution:** Replacement of intake manifold gaskets.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


### 4.6L V8
**Spark Plug Blow-Out**
- **Symptoms:** Misfiring, loss of power, ticking noise from the engine.
- **Solution:** Installation of oversized spark plugs or thread repair kits.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Camshaft Position Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check Engine Light, stalling, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the camshaft position sensor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


### 5.4L V8
**Spark Plug Blow-Out**
- **Symptoms:** Misfiring, loss of power, ticking noise from the engine.
- **Solution:** Installation of oversized spark plugs or thread repair kits.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Exhaust Manifold Stud Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** Exhaust leak noise (ticking or puffing sound), reduced engine performance.
- **Solution:** Removal and replacement of broken studs, often requiring drilling.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Transmission

### 4R70W (4-Speed Automatic)
**Torque Converter Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, shuddering, difficulty shifting, whining noise.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter and potentially transmission fluid flush.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1500

**Solenoid Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of transmission solenoids.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


### 4R75W (4-Speed Automatic)
**Torque Converter Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, shuddering, difficulty shifting, whining noise.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter and potentially transmission fluid flush.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1500

**Overdrive Clutch Pack Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to engage overdrive (4th gear), slipping in higher gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission rebuild or replacement of internal clutch packs.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Ball Joint Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, uneven tire wear, steering wander.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Leaf Spring Sagging**
- **Symptoms:** Rear end sits low, reduced load capacity, rougher ride.
- **Solution:** Replacement of sagging leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow or jerky window movement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Exterior

### Body and Trim
**Rust Formation**
- **Symptoms:** Visible rust on body panels, wheel wells, and frame components, especially in areas with road salt.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment, panel replacement, or undercoating.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500+

**Door Latch/Lock Actuator Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not locking or unlocking properly, difficulty opening doors.
- **Solution:** Replacement of door latch or lock actuator.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents or weak airflow, regardless of fan speed setting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Dashboard Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Visible cracks on the dashboard surface, particularly on models exposed to significant sun.
- **Solution:** Dashboard cover installation or replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2004 Ford E-150 Club Wagon is a capable vehicle with generally decent reliability, especially for its age and class. The V8 engines are robust, but owners should be aware of potential issues like spark plug blow-out and exhaust manifold stud breakage. Transmission issues, particularly with the torque converter and solenoids, are not uncommon as mileage increases. Suspension components like ball joints and leaf springs may require attention due to the vehicle's load-carrying nature. Electrical gremlins, such as alternator failure and window regulators, can also arise. Regular maintenance and addressing minor issues promptly are crucial for maximizing the lifespan and reliability of this workhorse van.
