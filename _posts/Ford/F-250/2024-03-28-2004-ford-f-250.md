**Introduction:**

The 2004 Ford F-250 is a heavy-duty pickup truck known for its rugged durability and powerful engine options. It is available with a variety of gasoline and diesel engines, as well as four-wheel drive or rear-wheel drive. The F-250 is praised for its towing and hauling capabilities but has been criticized for its poor fuel economy.

**Engine**

**6.0L Power Stroke Diesel**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, black smoke from exhaust, increased oil consumption
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: 04V325000
* **Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, misfiring, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace injector
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per injector
    * Recalls: 04V321000

**5.4L Triton V8**

* **Spark Plug Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfire, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4R100 Automatic (Standard)**

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, shuddering during shifting
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**5R110 Automatic (Optional)**

* **Valve Body Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, slipping gears, delayed engagement
    * Solution: Replace valve body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per side
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, flickering electronics, battery failure
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rusted panels, frame, or components
    * Solution: Professional repair or replacement of affected parts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies widely depending on severity
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative heating or air conditioning, malfunctioning controls
    * Solution: Diagnostic testing and repair or replacement of components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative window motors or switches
    * Solution: Replace window motors or switches
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per window
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2004 Ford F-250 has a number of potential issues, particularly with the 6.0L Power Stroke diesel engine and 4R100 automatic transmission. These issues can be costly to repair, especially for the engine and transmission. However, the F-250 remains a popular choice for heavy-duty towing and hauling due to its ruggedness and power.
