### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Ford GT, due to its limited production and supercar nature, generally exhibits excellent reliability with no widespread common issues reported across its core mechanical and electrical systems. The rarity of the vehicle means that any potential issues, while not common, could be complex and costly to repair due to specialized parts and labor requirements. Overall, the GT is a testament to Ford's engineering prowess, offering a robust and reliable supercar experience for those fortunate enough to own one.
