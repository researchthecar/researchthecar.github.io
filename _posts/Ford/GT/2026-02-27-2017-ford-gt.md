# 2017 Ford GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Ford GT represents the modern revival of Ford's iconic supercar, featuring an advanced carbon-fiber chassis and a mid-mounted twin-turbocharged EcoBoost V6 engine. As a low-volume, high-performance exotic vehicle, it is built for track capability and collector status rather than daily commuting. While official owner-reported complaint data is sparse due to the vehicle's extreme exclusivity, prospective buyers should approach ownership expecting high specialized maintenance costs and meticulous care requirements.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The 2017 Ford GT is powered by a 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged EcoBoost V6 engine delivering approximately 647 horsepower and 550 lb-ft of torque. Derived from Ford's racing program, this high-output powerplant offers immense performance but requires strict adherence to maintenance schedules.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

Power is routed to the rear wheels via a Getrag 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission featuring paddle shifters. It provides lightning-fast gear changes suited for high-speed track performance and aggressive driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V707000: Structure](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=GT&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A hydraulic fluid leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will perform a software update to the fully integrated vehicle controller. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V443000: Structure](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=GT&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A hydraulic fluid leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the dynamics control module software and replace the rear wing check valve as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Ford GT is an engineering masterpiece designed primarily for performance enthusiasts and collectors. Because broad consumer reliability data is limited, buyers should rely on pre-purchase inspections by certified exotic specialists. Maintenance, insurance, and upkeep will demand a significant financial commitment commensurate with its supercar status.
