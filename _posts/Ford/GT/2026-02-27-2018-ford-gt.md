# 2018 Ford GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Ford GT represents a high-performance pinnacle for the brand, featuring ultra-exotic engineering, carbon fiber construction, and race-bred aerodynamics. Because these vehicles are produced in very limited numbers and driven primarily as collector or track-day cars, standard owner-reported complaint data is exceptionally sparse. Buyers looking at a pre-owned Ford GT should focus on specialized pre-purchase inspections by certified technicians, maintenance history, and verifying that all factory campaigns or recalls have been properly addressed.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L EcoBoost Twin-Turbocharged V6

This high-output 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged EcoBoost V6 produces approximately 647 horsepower and 550 lb-ft of torque. Derived from Ford's endurance racing program, it delivers blistering performance but requires meticulous maintenance and premium fuel.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Getrag Dual-Clutch Automatic

A lightning-fast 7-speed wet-clutch dual-clutch automatic transmission handles gear changes via paddle shifters. It is designed to withstand track abuse while offering quick shifts, though specialized servicing requires expert knowledge.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V707000: Structure](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ford&model=GT&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A hydraulic fluid leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will perform a software update to the fully integrated vehicle controller. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Ford GT is an elite supercar built for extreme performance rather than high-volume daily driving. Due to its ultra-low production volume and specialized mechanical layout, standard reliability metrics do not paint a complete picture. Prospective buyers must prioritize a comprehensive inspection by a specialized supercar technician and verify all service records before purchase.
