**Introduction:**

The 2021 Ford Police Interceptor Utility is a formidable law enforcement vehicle, powered by a standard 3.3L V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque. It also comes with an optional 3.0L EcoBoost V6 that generates a potent 400 horsepower and 415 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 10-speed automatic and a 6-speed automatic. The Police Interceptor Utility offers impressive handling and stability, making it a reliable choice for police pursuits. However, it has received some negative feedback regarding various issues.

**Engine**

**3.3L V6**

* **Cylinder Head Warping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive coolant loss, white smoke from the exhaust, rough idling.
    * Solution: Replacing the cylinder head.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L EcoBoost V6**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, illuminated check engine light.
    * Solution: Replacing the turbocharger and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**10-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Delay and Jerking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or abrupt shifting between gears.
    * Solution: Software updates or transmission replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises when driving over bumps, uneven tire wear.
    * Solution: Replacing the affected strut.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500 per strut
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sway Bar End Link Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clicking or rattling sounds while cornering.
    * Solution: Replacing the worn sway bar end links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Harness Shorting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning electrical components, battery drain.
    * Solution: Identifying and replacing the shorted harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Caps Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks and damage to the rear bumper caps.
    * Solution: Replacing the bumper caps.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 per cap
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Console Lid Binding:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening and closing the center console lid.
    * Solution: Adjusting or replacing the lid mechanism.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
    * Recalls: None Found

* **HVAC Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent temperature control, excessive noise from vents.
    * Solution: Replacing or repairing HVAC components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 Ford Police Interceptor Utility has faced some issues that may require attention. Engine problems, such as cylinder head warping and turbocharger failure, can compromise its performance. Transmission issues can lead to difficult driving experience. Suspension and electrical system issues can affect safety and reliability. Interior issues, though less severe, can cause discomfort. While the severity of these issues varies, they can lead to additional maintenance and repair costs.
