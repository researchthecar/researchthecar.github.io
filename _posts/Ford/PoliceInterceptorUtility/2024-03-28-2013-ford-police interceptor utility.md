**2013 Ford Police Interceptor Utility**

Equipped with a 3.7L V6 engine mated to a 6-speed automatic transmission, the 2013 Ford Police Interceptor Utility delivers 305 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. It offers a spacious interior, advanced technology, and a powerful engine, but owners have reported concerns with certain aspects of the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **3.7L V6**

    * **Timing Chain Failure**
      * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear and stretching of the timing chain, causing an engine misfire, loss of power, and rough idle.
      * Solution: Replacing the timing chain, tensioner, and gears.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
      * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic**

    * **Torque Converter Shudder**
      * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations or shuddering during acceleration, especially at low speeds.
      * Solution: Replacing the torque converter.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
      * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **PCM Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls, loss of power, and various electrical malfunctions.
  * Solution: Replacing the powertrain control module (PCM).
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
  * Recalls: 14S06 (2013-2014 Police Interceptor Utility, Taurus Police Interceptor, Explorer Police Interceptor)

* **Battery Drain**
  * Description/Symptoms: Premature battery discharge, even with the vehicle turned off.
  * Solution: Checking and replacing the affected component causing the battery drain.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Support Corrosion**
  * Description/Symptoms: Rust and corrosion on the rear bumper support, potentially causing the bumper to detach.
  * Solution: Replacing the rear bumper support.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: 15V287 (2013-2016 Police Interceptor Utility)

**Interior**

* **Seat Belt Buckle Malfunction**
  * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the seat belt buckle to latch properly, increasing the risk of injury in a crash.
  * Solution: Replacing the seat belt buckle.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  * Recalls: 14S07 (2013 Police Interceptor Utility, Taurus Interceptor, Explorer Interceptor)

* **Door Lock Malfunction**
  * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking the doors, leading to security concerns.
  * Solution: Replacing the door lock actuator or latch.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2013 Ford Police Interceptor Utility offers performance and utility, it has experienced some issues related to the engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior. The severity and cost of the issues vary, with some being more expensive and potentially safety-related. It is recommended to research these issues and consider their potential impact before purchasing or maintaining this vehicle.
