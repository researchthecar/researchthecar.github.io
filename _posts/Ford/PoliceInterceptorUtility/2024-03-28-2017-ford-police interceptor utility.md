**Introduction**
The 2017 Ford Police Interceptor Utility is a purpose-built law enforcement vehicle based on the Ford Explorer. It is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 380 horsepower and 430 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission. The Police Interceptor Utility is known for its performance, handling, and advanced technology features. However, it has also been associated with several issues.

**Engine**

* **3.5-liter V6**
    * **Excessive Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups or leading to engine seizures.
        * Solution: Ford has issued a technical service bulletin (TSB) recommending valve stem seal replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear or failure of the timing chain or tensioners can result in engine damage or stalling.
        * Solution: Replace the affected components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $7,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **10-speed Automatic**
    * **Delayed or Harsh Shifting**
        * Description/Symptoms: Owners may experience delayed or rough gear shifts, accompanied by clunking or grinding noises.
        * Solution: Reprogramming the transmission control module (TCM) or replacing the valve body.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Sagging**
        * Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension can prematurely sag, leading to poor handling and reduced stability.
        * Solution: Replacing the rear springs or installing a suspension reinforcement kit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery may discharge prematurely due to parasitic loss or electrical component malfunctions.
        * Solution: Troubleshooting and replacing the affected components, such as door switches or battery terminals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **BCM Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The Body Control Module (BCM) may malfunction, leading to electrical system issues such as non-functioning power windows, lights, or door locks.
        * Solution: Replacing the BCM.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Exterior door handles may become loose or break off, making it difficult to access the vehicle.
        * Solution: Replacing the door handle assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Dashboard Cracks**
        * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard material may develop cracks over time, especially in hotter climates.
        * Solution: Replacing the dashboard.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Ford Police Interceptor Utility has received mixed reviews, with owners praising its performance and capability, but also encountering a range of issues. Engine problems, particularly excessive oil consumption and timing chain failures, have been significant concerns. Transmission issues have also been reported, while electrical and suspension issues can affect the vehicle's reliability and functionality. While some issues are relatively minor and affordable to fix, others can be more costly and time-consuming to resolve. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues associated with the vehicle and consider the cost and availability of repairs before making a purchase.
