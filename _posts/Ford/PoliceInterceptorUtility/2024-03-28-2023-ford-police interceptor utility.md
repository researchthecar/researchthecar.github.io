**Introduction:**

The 2023 Ford Police Interceptor Utility is a purpose-built law enforcement vehicle based on the Ford Explorer SUV. It is available with a choice of two engines and two transmission options. The 3.3-liter V6 engine produces 294 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.0-liter EcoBoost V6 produces 400 horsepower and 415 lb-ft of torque. Both engines come standard with a 10-speed automatic transmission.

Owners generally appreciate the Police Interceptor Utility's spacious interior, capable performance, and advanced technology features. However, some issues have been reported with certain components.

**Engine**

**3.3-Liter V6**

* **Intake Manifold Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, poor performance, illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0-Liter EcoBoost V6**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced power, excessive smoke, whining or whistling noises
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**10-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation, hard shifts, slipping gears
    * Solution: Software updates, transmission fluid service, or transmission replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Repeated battery draining, electrical system failure
    * Solution: Inspect and repair faulty wiring or replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Latch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate won't latch properly, opens unexpectedly
    * Solution: Replace tailgate latch assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation Screen Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blank screen, frozen map, navigation errors
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of navigation unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2023 Ford Police Interceptor Utility generally receives positive reviews, there are some potential issues to be aware of. Engine problems, such as intake manifold failure or turbocharger failure, can be costly to repair. Transmission issues can also cause significant inconvenience and expense. Additionally, electrical and interior issues may affect the vehicle's functionality and usability. It is important for potential buyers to carefully consider these potential issues before purchasing a 2023 Ford Police Interceptor Utility.
