**2016 Ford Police Interceptor Utility**

The 2016 Ford Police Interceptor Utility is a purpose-built SUV designed for law enforcement agencies. It is based on the Ford Explorer platform but features a number of modifications and upgrades to enhance its performance in police work. The Police Interceptor Utility is available with two engine options: a 3.7L V6 and a 3.5L V6 EcoBoost. Transmission options include a 6-speed automatic and a 10-speed automatic.

**Overall:**
