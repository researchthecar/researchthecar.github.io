# 2016 Ford F-350

### Overview
The 2016 Ford F-350 Super Duty is a heavy-duty pickup truck known for its robust performance and towing capabilities. As a workhorse vehicle, its reliability is a key concern for owners. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond standard maintenance, examining common problem areas across its powertrain, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior systems. The goal is to provide a clear picture of potential reliability concerns for this model year, helping prospective buyers and current owners make informed decisions.

### 6.7L Power Stroke V8 Diesel
**EGR Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, white smoke from exhaust, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the EGR cooler.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noises.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger inspection and potential replacement.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500


## Transmission

### 6R140 TorqShift Heavy-Duty Automatic (Gas & Diesel)
**Torque Converter Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping, shuddering, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Torque converter replacement.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from seals or lines, low fluid level.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of seals or lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Ford F-350 generally exhibits good reliability, particularly with its gasoline engine and standard chassis components. The diesel engine, while powerful, has a few documented issues such as EGR cooler leaks and turbocharger problems that can be costly to repair. Transmission issues, specifically with the torque converter and fluid leaks, are also noted. Electrical system components like alternators and sensors can occasionally fail. Overall, the F-350 is a capable truck, but owners should be aware of potential diesel-specific engine and transmission concerns. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs are crucial for maintaining its long-term reliability.
