**Introduction:**

The 2015 Ford F-350 is a heavy-duty pickup truck available with a range of engine and transmission options. It is known for its powerful engines, rugged construction, and towing capabilities. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of common problems.

The F-350 is offered with the following engine options:

* 6.7L Power Stroke V8 Turbo Diesel: 440 horsepower, 925 lb-ft torque
* 6.2L V8: 385 horsepower, 430 lb-ft torque
* 6.8L V10: 362 horsepower, 457 lb-ft torque

Transmission options include:

* 6-speed TorqShift automatic (6R140)
* 6-speed manual
* 10-speed TorqShift automatic (10R140)

**Engine**

**6.7L Power Stroke V8 Turbo Diesel**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased engine noise, blue smoke from exhaust.
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 19V808000
* **EGR Cooler Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, decreased fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Replace EGR cooler.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.2L V8**

* **Valve Train Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tapping or ticking sound from engine.
    * Solution: Adjust or replace valve lifters.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cam Phasor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Stalling, rough idle, decreased fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Replace cam phasors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 16E047000

**6.8L V10**

* **Spark Plug Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfiring, engine knocking, decreased fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-speed TorqShift Automatic (6R140)**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shudder during gear changes.
    * Solution: Replace torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, difficulty shifting gears.
    * Solution: Overhaul or replace transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-speed Manual**

* **Grinding Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise.
    * Solution: Repair or replace transmission components (e.g., synchronizers).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**10-speed TorqShift Automatic (10R140)**

* **No Significant Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Rear Axle Vibration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations felt throughout the vehicle, especially at highway speeds.
    * Solution: Replace rear axle shafts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, especially after short trips.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical faults or replace battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust and corrosion on the tailgate, especially in areas where water collects.
    * Solution: Clean and treat affected areas, repaint if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the dashboard due to sun exposure or temperature fluctuations.
    * Solution: Replace dashboard or use a dashboard cover.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning heater or air conditioner, temperature control issues.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair HVAC system components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 Ford F-350 has a mix of minor and moderate issues that vary in severity and cost. The most common engine problems are turbocharger and EGR cooler failures, while the most common transmission issue is torque converter shudder. Suspension and electrical issues are less prevalent but can be costly to fix. Interior issues include dashboard cracking and HVAC malfunctions. Overall, the F-350 is a reliable workhorse, but potential buyers should be aware of these common problems before making a purchase.
