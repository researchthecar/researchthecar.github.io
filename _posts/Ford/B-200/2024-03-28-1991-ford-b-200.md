**Introduction:**

The 1991 Ford B-200 is a full-size van with a variety of engine and transmission options. The standard engine is a 4.9L V8 with 190 horsepower and 290 lb-ft of torque. Other engine options include a 5.0L V8 with 205 horsepower and 300 lb-ft of torque, a 5.8L V8 with 230 horsepower and 355 lb-ft of torque, and a 7.5L V8 with 245 horsepower and 380 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 4-speed automatic, a 5-speed manual, and a 6-speed manual.

People like the Ford B-200 for its spacious interior, powerful engine options, and towing and hauling capabilities. However, it is not without its issues.

**Engine**

**4.9L V8**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve cover gasket failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil, burning oil smell
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Water pump failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Overheating, coolant leaks, whining noise
    * Solution: Replace water pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5.0L V8**

* **Spark plug failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, loss of power, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Distributor cap and rotor failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, loss of power, hard starting
    * Solution: Replace distributor cap and rotor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5.8L V8**

* **Timing chain failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, loss of power, no start
    * Solution: Replace timing chain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**7.5L V8**

* **Fuel pump failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, loss of power, hard starting
    * Solution: Replace fuel pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
