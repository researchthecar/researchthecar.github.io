# 2004 Mercedes-Benz S430

### Overview
The 2004 Mercedes-Benz S430, part of the W220 generation, is a luxury sedan known for its comfort and advanced features for its time. While generally well-regarded for its sophisticated engineering, like any vehicle of this age, it can experience specific issues. This analysis focuses on common technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, highlighting areas that may require attention for owners. The S430 was equipped with a potent V8 engine and a refined automatic transmission, contributing to its smooth driving experience. Understanding these potential problem areas can help owners budget for and address necessary repairs, ensuring the continued enjoyment of this executive-class vehicle.

---


## Engine

### 4.3L V8 (M113)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replace faulty ignition coil(s) and spark plugs.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per coil

**Oil Cooler Seals Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks around the engine block, potential coolant contamination.
- **Solution:** Replace leaking oil cooler seals.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

**Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensor Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Hesitation during acceleration, poor fuel economy, rough idle, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Clean or replace the MAF sensor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission not engaging gears, limp mode.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Conductor Plate Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Replace the conductor plate and associated sensors.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


## Suspension

### ABC (Active Body Control) Suspension
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, 'ABC System Visit Workshop' warning message, stiff ride.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace leaking hydraulic lines, struts, or pump.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $3000+

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of hydraulic pressure, vehicle sagging, loud whining noise from pump.
- **Solution:** Replace the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2000


### Standard Suspension
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Cost:** No widespread issues reported.


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**SAM (Signal Acquisition Module) Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical failures (lights, wipers, windows), warning lights.
- **Solution:** Clean or replace the affected SAM module.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000

**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, window stuck in position.
- **Solution:** Replace the window regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per window

**Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** One or both headlights not illuminating, flickering lights.
- **Solution:** Replace the xenon headlight ballast.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800 per ballast

### Body and Trim
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No widespread issues reported.

## Interior

### Climate Control System
**Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** HVAC fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replace the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


### Seat Control Modules
**Seat Memory Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not moving correctly, memory settings not saving.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and repair or replace seat control module.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2004 Mercedes-Benz S430 is a luxurious vehicle with a generally solid powertrain, but its advanced systems, particularly the ABC suspension and complex electronics, are prone to issues as the car ages. Owners should be prepared for potential repairs related to hydraulic leaks in the ABC system, electrical gremlins stemming from SAM module corrosion, and transmission control module problems. While the V8 engine is robust, ignition coils and MAF sensors can require attention. Regular maintenance and prompt diagnosis of warning lights are crucial for mitigating costly repairs and maintaining the S430's comfort and performance.
