**Introduction:**

The 2005 Mercedes-Benz CL55 AMG is a high-performance luxury coupe powered by a 5.5-liter supercharged V8 engine. The CL55 AMG is available with a 5-speed automatic transmission, and it produces 493 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. It is known for its impressive performance, luxurious interior, and advanced technological features. However, like any vehicle, it has had its share of issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**5.5L Supercharged V8**

* **Intake Manifold Runner Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, reduced engine power, check engine light illumination.
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold runners.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Supercharger Intercooler Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Overheating engine, reduced performance, coolant leaks.
    * Solution: Replace intercooler pump.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Solenoid Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Harsh or delayed shifting, slipping gears.
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Airmatic Suspension Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging or riding low, reduced ride quality.
    * Solution: Replace air compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Check for parasitic battery drains, replace battery if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Trunk Lid Not Closing Properly:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Trunk lid does not latch securely, may open while driving.
    * Solution: Adjust trunk lid alignment or replace trunk latch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Harman Kardon Stereo System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Audio system not producing sound, intermittent sound issues, display problems.
    * Solution: Repair or replace stereo components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

The 2005 Mercedes-Benz CL55 AMG is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has experienced some common issues. The most severe and expensive problems are related to the engine, including the intake manifold runner malfunction and supercharger intercooler pump failure. Other issues, such as the shift solenoid malfunction and suspension compressor failure, are less common but can still be costly to repair. Overall, the CL55 AMG is a high-performance luxury vehicle that requires regular maintenance and timely repairs to ensure optimal performance and reliability.
