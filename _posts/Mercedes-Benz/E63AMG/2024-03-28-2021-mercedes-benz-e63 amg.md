**Introduction:**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG is a high-performance sedan that boasts exceptional power and handling. It is equipped with a powerful 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 603 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The E63 AMG has received praise for its thrilling acceleration, precise handling, and luxurious interior. However, some owners have reported specific issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**4.0-Liter Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Intake Valve Guide Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Replace intake valve guides
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced power, whining or whistling noises from the engine
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Hesitation and Jerking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitation or abrupt shifts, particularly at low speeds
    * Solution: Update transmission software
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Air Suspension**

* **Air Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Suspension lowering or losing stiffness, warning lights on the dashboard
    * Solution: Replace air compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharging quickly, especially when the vehicle is parked
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlight housing
    * Solution: Replace headlights or repair seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Freeze:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Infotainment screen becoming unresponsive
    * Solution: Reset or update the infotainment system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heating or cooling system not functioning properly
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty climate control components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2021 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG is a formidable performance sedan, it has been reported to encounter some specific issues. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some being relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, while others could be more significant and costly to repair. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and consider them when making their purchase decision.
