# 2016 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG delivers high-performance luxury, but used buyers should review owner complaints regarding steering assist malfunctions, fuel system odors, and exterior weather stripping issues. While powertrain components are generally robust when properly maintained, electrical and safety-related reports indicate that a thorough pre-purchase inspection is essential before bringing home a high-mileage German performance car.

---

## Engine

### 5.5L Biturbo V8

Equipped with a potent 5.5-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing high horsepower and torque figures, this powerhouse delivers blistering acceleration. Buyers must verify service history and check for any signs of fuel leaks or timing component wear.

**Fuel Odor and System Leak**
- **Symptoms:** A strong fuel odor is apparent while the vehicle is idling, suggesting potential leaks around fuel rails, pumps, or seals.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines, pumps, and injectors for leaks, and replace damaged seals or failed assembly units.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 7-Speed Automatic

The 7-speed multi-clutch automatic transmission routes power smoothly in daily driving while executing rapid gear changes under hard acceleration. Routine fluid changes are critical to preserving longevity and shift quality.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Issues related to electronic power assist failures can temporarily compromise maneuverability and require immediate professional attention.

**Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A power steering malfunction message appears on the dashboard, accompanied by a sudden loss of power steering assist, making the steering wheel very heavy.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the electronic power steering assembly and replace faulty modules, sensors, or wiring as required.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Seals

Body seals and glass fitment require periodic inspection to prevent moisture from entering the cabin and affecting electronic modules.

**Windshield Water Intrusion**
- **Symptoms:** Water drops form on the interior upper edge of the windshield during heavy rain, and the glass may exhibit movement when pushed.
- **Solution:** Remove and reinstall the windshield with fresh adhesive and new sealing weatherstripping.
