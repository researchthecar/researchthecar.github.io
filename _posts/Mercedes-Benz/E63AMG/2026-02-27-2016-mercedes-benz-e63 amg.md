# 2016 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG, a high-performance variant of the E-Class, is renowned for its potent V8 engine and sophisticated engineering. While generally considered a reliable performance sedan, like any complex vehicle, it can experience specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related problems reported by owners and technicians. The E63 AMG's advanced systems, particularly its powertrain and electronics, are areas where attention may be required over time. Owners should be aware of potential issues to ensure optimal performance and longevity of this luxury performance vehicle.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8 (M157)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, boost leaks, check engine light illumination, rattling noise from turbo area.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the wastegate actuator or the complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per actuator

**Oil Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil leaks around the oil cooler lines or the cooler itself, potential for oil in coolant.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking oil cooler lines or the oil cooler unit.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift MCT
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, rough shifting, shuddering during acceleration, potential for transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Rebuilding or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Mechatronic Unit Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting behavior, failure to engage gears, transmission fault codes.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $4000


## Suspension

### AMG Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, compressor running excessively, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200 per air spring, $500 - $900 for compressor


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, audio cutting out, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, or replacement of the COMAND head unit.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300 for software, $1500 - $3000 for replacement unit


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or circuits causing the drain.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG offers a compelling blend of luxury and extreme performance. While its core powertrain is robust, potential owners should be aware of specific issues that can arise, particularly concerning the turbocharger wastegate actuators, oil cooler lines, and the sophisticated AMG Speedshift MCT transmission's mechatronic unit and torque converter. The adaptive air suspension can also develop leaks. Electrical gremlins, such as COMAND system glitches and parasitic battery drain, are not uncommon. Despite these potential concerns, with diligent maintenance and prompt attention to any emerging problems, the E63 AMG can remain a thrilling and reliable performance machine.
