# 2021 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG, buyers gain access to a high-performance luxury vehicle that balances everyday usability with exhilarating twin-turbocharged power. While these executive super-sedans and wagons are engineered for thrilling performance, they also feature complex electronics, specialized powertrains, and advanced driver assistance systems that require thorough pre-purchase inspections. Owners have reported occasional mechanical and electrical hiccups, making it essential for prospective buyers to review service histories carefully.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 Engine

The hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers immense horsepower and torque, providing supercar-rivaling acceleration. Proper maintenance and timely servicing are critical to ensuring long-term reliability for this high-output performance motor.

**Cylinder Misfire and Valve Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of the check engine light, engine misfires, and hesitation leading to reduced engine power or limp mode.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis and potential cylinder head service or valve replacement as outlined by manufacturer guidance.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed multi-clutch automatic transmission is tuned for rapid, aggressive gear changes to match the V8's power delivery. While highly capable, owners should verify smooth shifting behavior during a test drive.

**Unexpected Transmission Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of motive power, failure to engage gears, or complete transmission breakdown while driving.
- **Solution:** Professional inspection by a qualified specialist; may require major transmission component repair or complete unit replacement.

## Brakes

### Electronic Parking Brake and Calipers

High-performance braking systems provide immense stopping power, but electronic actuators and sensors require proper calibration to avoid unexpected engagement issues.

**Parking Brake Seizing**
- **Symptoms:** Emergency brake unexpectedly engaging or locking up during parking maneuvers, preventing the vehicle from moving forward or backward.
- **Solution:** Inspection of parking brake actuators, software recalibration, or replacement of malfunctioning brake components.

## Electrical

### 48V Mild-Hybrid Electrical System

The sophisticated electrical architecture supports advanced luxury features and mild-hybrid performance assistance, though occasional battery and charging system faults have been noted by owners.

**Battery Malfunction and Electrical Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Instrument panel warning messages, sudden loss of motive power, or total vehicle inoperability requiring a tow.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic testing of the 48-volt system and replacement of faulty battery components or control modules.

### Surround View Camera and Parking Sensors

Advanced safety and driver-awareness systems enhance maneuvering convenience, though intermittent software or sensor glitches can occasionally impair camera functionality.

**Camera Inoperative Display**
- **Symptoms:** Rearview or surround-view camera failing to activate when reversing, accompanied by 'system inoperative' warning messages.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of faulty camera modules and wiring harnesses.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG is an extraordinary machine that blends luxury and ferocious performance, but prospective buyers should approach with a healthy respect for its complex engineering. While many owners enjoy trouble-free driving, reported issues ranging from electrical system glitches to sporadic powertrain and camera faults highlight the importance of a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection. Securing a detailed service record and verifying that all manufacturer updates have been performed will help ensure a more confident ownership experience.
