# 2015 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG

### Overview
The 2015 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG, a high-performance variant of the E-Class, is renowned for its potent V8 engine and luxurious appointments. As a performance-oriented vehicle, it generally exhibits strong build quality and engineering. However, like any complex machine, certain areas can be prone to specific issues, particularly those related to its high-output powertrain and advanced electronic systems. Owners should be aware of potential concerns, though widespread catastrophic failures are uncommon. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any developing problems are crucial for maintaining the E63 AMG's exceptional performance and longevity.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8 (M157)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, boost leaks, check engine light illumination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the wastegate actuator or the entire turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per actuator

**Coolant Leaks (Intercooler System)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, loss of coolant, visible coolant residue around intercooler pipes.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking intercooler hoses or seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift MCT
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, delayed engagement, unusual noises.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, failure to shift gears, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Suspension

### AMG Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the leaking air spring or air strut.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1500 per corner

**Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant compressor operation, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1300


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive to touch or freezes intermittently.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or components causing the drain.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG is a robust performance sedan with a generally good reliability record for its class. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the high-performance powertrain, specifically potential issues with turbocharger wastegates and torque converters. The advanced air suspension system can also present repair costs if leaks or compressor failures occur. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the COMAND infotainment system and parasitic battery drain, are not uncommon. While these issues can be costly to repair, they are often manageable with proper diagnosis and maintenance. Overall, the E63 AMG offers a thrilling driving experience with acceptable reliability for a vehicle of its caliber, provided owners are prepared for potential maintenance expenses.
