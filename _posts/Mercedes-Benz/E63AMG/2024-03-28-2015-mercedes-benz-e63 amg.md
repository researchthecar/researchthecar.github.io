**Introduction:**
The 2015 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG is a high-performance sedan powered by a 5.5L twin-turbocharged V8 engine paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The E63 AMG delivers 577 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, enabling it to accelerate from 0-60 mph in 3.6 seconds. It boasts an aggressive exterior, luxurious interior, and advanced technology. However, it also has some notable issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**5.5L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

**Intake Manifold Runners Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Intake manifold runners may stick open or closed, leading to reduced engine performance, poor fuel efficiency, and potentially engine damage.
* **Solution:** Replace faulty intake manifold runners.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

**Shift Solenoid Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Transmission may fail to shift gears smoothly or may get stuck in a specific gear.
* **Solution:** Replace faulty shift solenoid.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

**Air Suspension Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Air suspension system may malfunction, causing uneven ride height, reduced handling, and possible tire damage.
* **Solution:** Diagnose and repair or replace faulty air suspension components (e.g., air springs, compressor).
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $3,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Battery may discharge quickly or fail to hold a charge, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle or electrical system malfunctions.
* **Solution:** Replace faulty battery.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

**Sunroof Rattling:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Sunroof may rattle or squeak when open or closed, especially at low speeds.
* **Solution:** Inspect and adjust sunroof alignment or replace faulty sealing components.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,200
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

**Infotainment System Crash:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** COMAND infotainment system may crash or freeze intermittently, affecting navigation, audio, and other functions.
* **Solution:** Software update or hardware repair.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**
The 2015 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG is a performance-oriented sedan with impressive capabilities but also some known issues. The intake manifold runner failure and transmission shift solenoid failure are potentially serious issues that can affect drivability and safety. The air suspension system may also experience malfunctions, leading to compromised handling and costly repairs. Electrical and exterior issues such as battery failure and sunroof rattling are less severe but can still be annoying and inconvenient. Overall, the severity and cost of these issues should be considered when purchasing a used 2015 E63 AMG.
