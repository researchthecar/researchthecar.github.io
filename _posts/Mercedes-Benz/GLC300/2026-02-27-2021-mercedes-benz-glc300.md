# 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLC300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLC300, buyers will find a luxury compact SUV that blends refined styling with solid everyday performance. While powertrain configurations are well-established for this model year, prospective owners should review available service histories and inspect key mechanical and electrical systems during a pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Gas Engine

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration for daily driving while maintaining respectable efficiency, though routine maintenance and timely oil changes are critical for long-term durability.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission is paired with rear-wheel drive or 4MATIC all-wheel drive, offering smooth gear changes across various driving conditions. Regular fluid service helps ensure reliable long-term shifting performance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V230000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLC300&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A faulty electrical contact within the seat position switch may prevent the driver and passenger air bags and seat belt force limiters from properly activating during a crash. Air bags and seat belts that do not perform properly in a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the seat position switches, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V261000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLC300&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The electrical connection for the headlights may not be watertight, which can result in headlight failure. Headlight failure can decrease visibility for both the driver and surrounding traffic, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the headlight sealing plugs, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLC300 remains a comfortable and capable luxury crossover option on the used market. Buyers should prioritize examples with comprehensive maintenance records and perform a thorough inspection of all vehicle systems prior to purchase.
