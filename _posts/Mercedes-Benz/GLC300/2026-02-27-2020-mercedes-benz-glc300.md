# 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLC300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLC 300 offers a refined luxury crossover experience with polished driving dynamics and a feature-rich cabin. However, used-car buyers should review potential trouble spots, ranging from common electrical and headlight moisture concerns to powertrain reports such as oil pressure control faults and intermittent stalling.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M264)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine (M264) produces 255 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for daily driving. While generally smooth and responsive, buyers should watch out for reported oil pressure sensor issues and occasional drivability hiccups.

**Oil Pressure Control and Oil Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light, often displaying code P06DA for engine oil pressure control, or unexpected premature oil pump failure.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the oil pressure control sensor, solenoid, or oil pump assembly as necessary.
- **Cost:** $200 for sensor/solenoid parts, higher for pump replacement

**Engine Hesitation and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent engine hesitation when pressing the accelerator from a stop or during turns, or sudden loss of motive power.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic diagnose fuel delivery, throttle body, and engine management control modules.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9G-TRONIC 9-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes paired with rear-wheel drive or 4MATIC all-wheel drive. It is generally robust when maintained with regular fluid services.

## Electrical

### Back Over Prevention

Safety equipment generally performs well, though intermittent backup camera display failures have been reported by owners.

**Backup Camera Blank Screen**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment monitor displays a blank or inoperable screen when shifting into reverse, persisting intermittently.
- **Solution:** Diagnose camera wiring harnesses, software firmware, or replace the backup camera module if faulty.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical complaints center heavily on exterior lighting and moisture intrusion in headlamp assemblies.

**Headlight Moisture Ingress and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Water condensation accumulating inside the headlamp housing, leading to electrical shorting, warning messages, and complete headlight unit or module failure.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight housings for proper sealing and replace damaged modules or assemblies.

### Visibility / Sunroof

Exterior body complaints highlight rare instances of spontaneous panoramic glass roof failure.

**Panoramic Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The glass panoramic sunroof unexpectedly shatters or explodes while driving at highway or city speeds without apparent impact from outside debris.
- **Solution:** Inspect sunroof frame alignment and replace shattered glass panels under comprehensive insurance or warranty if applicable.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLC 300 is a sophisticated luxury compact SUV that blends comfort with strong performance. Prospective used-car buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's service history, check for headlight moisture accumulation, test all electronics including the backup camera, and listen closely for any engine or drivetrain noises during a pre-purchase inspection.
