- **Symptoms:** Battery may discharge prematurely, leading to starting issues. Often related to parasitic draw from specific modules.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLC300 demonstrates strong overall reliability, with its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission performing commendably. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are within the sophisticated electrical system, particularly with the MBUX infotainment interface experiencing occasional glitches and the potential for intermittent battery drain. These electrical concerns are not typically indicative of systemic failure but rather common challenges with advanced automotive technology. Beyond these, the exterior and interior components, as well as the suspension, are generally robust. Routine maintenance is key, but for specific technical issues, addressing software updates for the MBUX and diagnosing any parasitic battery draws are the most likely repairs.
