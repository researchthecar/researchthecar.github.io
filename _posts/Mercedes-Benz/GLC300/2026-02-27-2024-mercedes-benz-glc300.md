# 2024 Mercedes-Benz GLC300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz GLC300 continues to offer a refined luxury compact SUV experience, blending advanced technology, a comfortable ride, and a premium interior. As a relatively recent model year, buyers are largely looking at initial build quality and early-mileage owner reports to gauge potential trouble spots. While the driving dynamics and powertrain options deliver strong performance, prospective used-car buyers should thoroughly inspect modern electronics and powertrain components during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, equipped with a 48-volt mild-hybrid system. It offers strong, responsive acceleration for daily driving while maintaining competitive efficiency for a luxury SUV.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to either the rear wheels or 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system. It generally provides seamless gear changes tailored to maximize both fuel economy and performance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V741000: Visibility:Windshield](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLC300&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The front, rear, and fixed side windows may not be properly secured and can detach. In addition, the front air bags may not be properly supported by the windshield during deployment.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the front, rear, and fixed side windows, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz GLC300 remains an appealing luxury compact SUV with modern styling and advanced features. Because it is still early in its lifecycle, most available examples will have low mileage and benefit from remaining factory warranties. Used-car shoppers should prioritize comprehensive pre-purchase inspections, verifying that all advanced infotainment and driver-assistance features operate properly.
