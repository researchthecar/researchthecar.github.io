**Introduction:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Viale BRT is a heavy-duty bus designed for urban public transit. It is available with a single engine option: a 12.8-liter OM 471 in-line six-cylinder diesel engine producing 456 horsepower and 2,100 lb-ft of torque. The Viale BRT comes standard with a 6-speed automatic transmission.

Owners of the Viale BRT generally praise its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and powerful engine. However, there have been some reports of issues with various components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**12.8L OM 471 I6 Diesel**

* **EGR Valve Failure:** The EGR valve may fail, causing a loss of power and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Replace the EGR valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Control Unit Failure:** The transmission control unit may fail, causing erratic shifting or inability to shift gears.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission control unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Leaks:** The air suspension system may leak due to faulty seals or hoses.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty seals or hoses.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain prematurely due to faulty wiring or accessories.
    * Solution: Identify and repair the source of the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Malfunctioning Turn Signals:** The turn signals may malfunction due to faulty wiring or switches.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Body Panel Corrosion:** Body panels may develop corrosion over time, especially in areas exposed to moisture and road salt.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the affected panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system may malfunction, causing inadequate heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Adjustment Failure:** The power seat adjustments may fail, making it difficult to adjust the seat position.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty seat adjusters.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2019 Mercedes-Benz Viale BRT has received mixed reviews. While it offers a comfortable ride and powerful engine, there have been reports of issues with various components, including the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior. The severity and cost of these issues vary, and some owners have experienced major problems that have required extensive repairs. However, it is important to note that not all Viale BRTs will experience these issues.
