**Introduction:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz Viale BRT is a heavy-duty bus designed for urban transportation. It is available with multiple engine and transmission options. The OM936LA engine produces 354 horsepower and 1400 pound-feet of torque, while the OM471LA engine produces 428 horsepower and 1659 pound-feet of torque. Both engines are mated to a 6-speed Allison transmission.

People who like the Mercedes-Benz Viale BRT appreciate its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and powerful engine. Some common complaints include a lack of modern safety features, a high price tag, and poor fuel economy.

**Engine**

**OM936LA Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Failure:** The intake manifold can crack, causing loss of power and reduced fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2500 - $4000+
    * Recalls: SB-594072

**OM471LA Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger can fail, resulting in reduced power and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4000 - $6000+
    * Recalls: SB-594073

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Allison Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:** The transmission may experience difficulty shifting gears, resulting in rough or delayed shifts.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the transmission valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Spring Leaks:** The air springs can develop leaks, causing the bus to sag and reduce ride height.
    * Solution: Replace the air springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+ per air spring
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery can drain quickly, even when the bus is parked.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator Failure:** The alternator may fail to charge the battery, resulting in electrical system problems.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Panel Cracks:** The dashboard panel can crack around the air vents.
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard panel.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Overhead Compartment Failure:** The overhead compartments can become loose or detach, posing a safety hazard.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the overhead compartment.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2017 Mercedes-Benz Viale BRT has some reliability concerns in areas such as the engine, transmission, and electrical system. The estimated cost to fix these issues can range from hundreds to thousands of dollars per repair. While the bus is known for its comfort and performance, it may require more frequent maintenance and repairs than other comparable models.
