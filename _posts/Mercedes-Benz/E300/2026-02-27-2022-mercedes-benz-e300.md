### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz E300 demonstrates strong reliability, aligning with expectations for a premium vehicle. The powertrain, consisting of the 2.0L turbocharged engine and 9-speed automatic transmission, shows no significant widespread issues. The primary areas where owners might encounter costs beyond routine maintenance are related to the optional AIR BODY CONTROL suspension, which can develop leaks, and potential software glitches within the MBUX infotainment system. Premature 12V battery failure is also a possibility, though not unique to this model. Overall, the E300 is a dependable luxury sedan, with most reported issues being minor or related to optional features.
