# 2022 Mercedes-Benz E300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Mercedes-Benz, prospective buyers should carefully review owner-reported feedback regarding advanced driver assistance features, electrical accessories, and body components. While complaints represent individual owner experiences rather than guaranteed factory defects, monitoring these specific areas can help you conduct a more thorough pre-purchase inspection. Being aware of these potential trouble spots allows you to make a more informed purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine provides balanced power and efficiency for daily driving. Overall reliability is generally stable, though buyers should verify proper maintenance and check for internal engine concerns.

**Cylinder Head Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Premature wear at the valve seats can lead to engine misfires and a sudden loss of power.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the cylinder head and valve train for signs of wear or misfire codes during a pre-purchase evaluation.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear shifts across various driving conditions. It pairs well with the available drivetrains and requires regular fluid changes to maintain long-term durability.

## Electrical

### 48V Battery and Electrical System

Electrical complaints center around advanced mild-hybrid components and driver displays.

**48-Volt Battery Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** A dashboard warning light appears, followed shortly by a red warning message and potential vehicle deceleration or loss of power.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scans by a dealership or specialist are required to test the 48-volt battery system and associated components.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior trim and glass components require close visual checks during an inspection.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof may shatter or explode unexpectedly while driving without outside impact.
- **Solution:** Inspect the glass panel for pre-existing stress fractures and verify if any manufacturer coverage applies.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety suites offer helpful driver aids but can occasionally generate false alerts.

**Unintended Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly activate the brakes or report incorrect speed limits during normal traffic conditions.
- **Solution:** Ensure camera and radar sensors are properly calibrated and check for any applicable software updates.
