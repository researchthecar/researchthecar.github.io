**Introduction:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz E300 is a mid-size luxury sedan known for its advanced technology, refined interior, and potent engine. It offers two engine options paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The E300 is praised for its smooth ride, impressive handling, and spacious cabin. However, like any vehicle, it has faced some reported issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Inline-4 Turbocharged**
    * **Fuel Injector Issue:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, rough idle, reduced engine performance
        * Solution: Replace affected fuel injectors
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.0L V6 Bi-Turbocharged**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **9-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Control Module Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Erratic gear shifting, loss of power, warning lights
        * Solution: Replace or reprogram the transmission control module
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive Air Suspension**
    * **Air Spring Leak:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Uneven ride height, suspension noises
        * Solution: Replace leaking air springs
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+ per corner
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Conventional Suspension**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, battery drains quickly
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system (parasitic drain)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water droplets inside headlights
    * Solution: Replace affected headlights or reseal existing ones
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per headlight
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Mirror Adjustment Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Side mirrors fail to adjust or vibrate
    * Solution: Replace or repair affected mirror motors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per mirror
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Windshield Wiper Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Wipers stop working or malfunction
    * Solution: Replace wiper motor or linkage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Roof Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into cabin through panoramic roof
    * Solution: Replace or reseal roof drain tubes
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Bluetooth Connectivity Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Trouble pairing or maintaining Bluetooth connections
    * Solution: Update software or replace Bluetooth module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz E300 is generally a reliable and luxurious vehicle. However, it has faced some issues in areas such as the fuel system, transmission, suspension, and electrical components. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the repair required. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and consider regular maintenance to help prevent or address them.
