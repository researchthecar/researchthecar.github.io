### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz E300 demonstrates strong overall reliability, with its core mechanical components (engine and transmission) showing no significant widespread issues. These are not uncommon in modern vehicles with complex electronic systems. Exterior and interior components are generally robust. While repairs for electrical issues can be moderately expensive, they do not detract significantly from the vehicle's overall positive reliability profile. Routine maintenance is essential for longevity.
