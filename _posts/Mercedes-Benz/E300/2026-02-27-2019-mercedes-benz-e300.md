# 2019 Mercedes-Benz E300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz E300 delivers sophisticated luxury, advanced technology, and refined driving dynamics. While it offers a quiet cabin and robust performance options, prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints regarding electrical systems, camera displays, and select powertrain components.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 241 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth everyday acceleration. It is generally dependable, though owners should monitor for oil leaks during routine maintenance.

**Rear Main Seal Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leakage noticed during routine inspections or service appointments.
- **Solution:** Inspect the rear main seal and replace it if leakage is confirmed.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes across rear-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. It generally operates reliably with proper fluid maintenance.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

The suspension provides a compliant and controlled ride, though low-profile tires mounted on large alloy wheels can be vulnerable to road hazards.

**Cracked Rims and Tire Pressure Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Low tire pressure warning lights illuminating due to slow leaks from cracked alloy wheels or tire sidewall damage.
- **Solution:** Inspect alloy wheels for structural cracks and replace damaged wheels or tires.

## Electrical

### Back Over Prevention

The rearview camera and backup prevention systems assist with parking visibility, but a few owners have experienced intermittent feed failures.

**Backup Camera Display Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rearview camera displays distorted images, goes blank, or cuts out entirely while the vehicle is in reverse.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera wiring connections and replace the rearview camera or display module if faulty.

## Safety

### Seat Belt

Safety equipment generally performs as expected, though a subset of owners have reported seat belt and restraint sensor concerns.

**Seat Belt Retractor and Fastening Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty with seat belt retraction or warning messages regarding unfastened belts.
- **Solution:** Have the seat belt assembly inspected by a qualified technician and replace faulty retractors or sensors as needed.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz E300 is a luxurious and capable executive sedan, but used car shoppers should carefully inspect the vehicle's electrical systems, rearview camera functionality, and wheels. Verifying service history and ensuring all open safety campaigns have been addressed will help secure a dependable ownership experience.
