# 2017 Mercedes-Benz E300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury sedan, understanding common owner-reported concerns helps set realistic expectations for ownership and maintenance costs. While this generation delivers refined comfort and advanced technology, buyers should review past service records and thoroughly inspect potential vehicles for recurring electrical, steering, and fuel system complaints.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The standard 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine generates approximately 241 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, providing balanced performance and efficiency. While generally robust, buyers should watch for fuel system leaks and intermittent driveability concerns.

**Fuel Odor and Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report noticing a strong smell of gasoline inside or around the vehicle, occasionally linked to fuel line or pump pressure issues.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines, pump connections, and injectors for leaks or cracks, and replace damaged components.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly stall while driving or experience hesitation and failure to accelerate properly.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fuel delivery pressure and engine management sensors; replace faulty fuel pumps or injectors as necessary.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear changes and contributes to highway fuel economy. Maintenance history should confirm regular fluid changes to support long-term durability.

**Unintended Neutral Shift or Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may inadvertently shift into neutral while coming to a stop, or exhibit hesitation and rough shifting behavior.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic gear selector module and transmission control software; update modules or replace faulty shift components.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The electronic power steering system provides responsive handling. Intermittent assist failures can compromise maneuverability and require prompt diagnosis.

**Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience an unexpected loss of power steering functionality, accompanied by warning messages requiring increased physical effort to turn the wheel.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic power steering control module and wiring connections; replace failing steering assist components.

## Electrical

### Body Electrical and Infotainment

Advanced electronics govern most cabin and convenience features. Ensuring that sunroof drains remain unobstructed helps prevent costly moisture-related electrical faults.

**Water Intrusion and Electrical Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Water entering the cabin through compromised sunroof drains can pool on the floorboards and cause unexpected electrical battery drains.
- **Solution:** Clear blocked sunroof drain tubes and inspect cabin wiring harnesses for moisture damage or corrosion.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Occupant safety systems rely on sensitive electronic sensors and wiring. Illuminated warning lights require professional inspection to ensure proper deployment readiness.

**Air Bag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The SRS or airbag warning light illuminates on the dashboard, often pointing to seat cushion weight sensors or clock spring circuit faults.
- **Solution:** Scan diagnostic trouble codes to pinpoint faulty seat sensors, wiring harnesses, or clock springs, and replace as needed.

## Summary Verdict

This luxury sedan offers impressive comfort, advanced safety features, and refined driving dynamics. Prospective buyers should carefully verify vehicle history, check for any unresolved fuel or steering complaints, and ensure all electronic systems function properly before finalizing a purchase.
