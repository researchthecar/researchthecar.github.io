# 2017 Mercedes-Benz E300

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz E300, part of the W213 generation, is generally regarded as a reliable luxury sedan, blending advanced technology with a comfortable driving experience. It typically features a potent yet efficient turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with a smooth automatic transmission. While Mercedes-Benz vehicles are known for their sophisticated engineering, like any complex machine, some specific areas may experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas and their associated costs for owners.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M274)
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level warnings.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals, gaskets, or oil pan.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased exhaust smoke.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from the transmission pan or seals, rough shifting, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals, gaskets, or transmission pan.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting patterns, delayed engagement, 'limp mode' activation.
- **Solution:** Software update or reprogramming by a qualified technician.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, freezes, or restarts randomly.

### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, car won't start, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or circuits causing drain.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz E300 demonstrates good overall reliability, with the most commonly reported technical issues centering around the engine's oil seals and turbocharger, as well as occasional glitches with the infotainment system and potential battery drain. Transmission issues are less frequent but can involve fluid leaks or software quirks. The suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally robust. Proactive maintenance and addressing early signs of oil leaks or electrical anomalies can help mitigate potential repair costs and ensure a dependable ownership experience for this luxury sedan.
