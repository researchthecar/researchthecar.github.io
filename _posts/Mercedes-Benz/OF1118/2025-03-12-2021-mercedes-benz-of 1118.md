The 2021 Mercedes-Benz OF 1118 is a bus chassis known for its robust design and suitability for various transport applications. While horsepower and torque figures can vary based on regional configurations and specific engine options, it is generally equipped with a diesel engine in the 180-200 horsepower range. Common engine options include the Mercedes-Benz OM 934 LA. Users appreciate its reliability and durability, especially in demanding operational environments. Some typical complaints revolve around ride comfort and noise levels, which are characteristic of commercial vehicles in this class.

**Engine**

**OM 934 LA**

*   **Turbocharger Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, black smoke from the exhaust, whistling noise from the turbocharger.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Fuel Injector Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, misfires, decreased fuel efficiency, check engine light.
    *   Solution: Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz OF 1118 generally faces issues related to its engine components, specifically the turbocharger and fuel injectors, with repair costs ranging from moderate to high. The air suspension system can also experience leaks, resulting in ride quality issues, requiring repairs. Electrical problems, particularly with the multiplex wiring, can lead to intermittent failures, which can also result in moderate repair costs. These issues, while not universally reported, should be considered when evaluating the vehicle.
