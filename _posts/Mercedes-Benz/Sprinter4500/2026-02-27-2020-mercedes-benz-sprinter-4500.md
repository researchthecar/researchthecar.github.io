# 2020 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 4500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Evaluating a 2020 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 4500 involves looking closely at its heavy-duty capability, as this commercial platform is built for demanding work environments. While owner complaints in our data set are minimal, buyers should always verify the completion of open manufacturer safety recalls before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbo Inline-4 Diesel

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder diesel engine produces approximately 161 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It serves as an efficiency-focused option for lighter commercial duties within the lineup.

### 3.0L V6 Turbo Diesel

The 3.0-liter V6 turbodiesel generates around 188 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque, providing robust low-end power suited for heavy hauling and upfitted commercial bodies.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 7-speed automatic transmission pairs with select diesel configurations, offering smooth gear shifts and optimized highway cruising capability for commercial fleets.

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers wide gear coverage to help maximize fuel efficiency and power delivery under heavy loads.

## Safety

### Structure and Equipment

Exterior body and structural equipment items generally hold up well, though component availability can occasionally impact recall scheduling.

**Recall Part Availability Delay**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience delays in scheduling safety recall repairs due to initial parts availability constraints at dealerships.
- **Solution:** Contact an authorized Mercedes-Benz dealer to check part availability and schedule the necessary recall service.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V802000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Caliper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+4500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Air in the brake system can result in extended brake pedal travel, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will replace the rear brake calipers, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V631000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+4500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Over time, the hose could begin to leak brake fluid, affecting the braking performance of the vehicle, including the potential loss of directional stability when braking, thereby increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will check the condition of the brake hoses on the front and rear axles and replace them as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V055000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+4500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the software communication module for the automatic emergency call system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V410000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+4500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The wiring harnesses for the front seats may have been routed incorrectly and could become jammed and damaged in the swivel seat frames. Damage to the wiring harness can prevent the side air bag within the seat to deploy inadvertently or not deploy as designed, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will attach the mounting clip to the correct position, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V411000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+4500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The MBUX multimedia system software may cause the display to remain black, or reboot after startup. This could result in the rearview camera image not displaying properly.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 21V241000: Steering:Hydraulic Power Assist:Hose, Piping, And Connections](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+4500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A hydraulic fluid leak may cause a sudden loss of power steering assist, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will install a screw-type clamp, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V595000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+4500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will check the fuel hose clamp connections and tighten or replace the components as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V972000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+4500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Unintentional vehicle movement can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the electronic stability program control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 4500 is a capable heavy-duty commercial vehicle designed for intensive work applications. Prospective buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection and ensure that all open safety campaigns have been properly addressed by a certified dealer.
