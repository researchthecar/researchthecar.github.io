# 2023 Mercedes-Benz GLE53 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz GLE53 AMG delivers high-performance luxury with twin-turbocharged power and an upscale interior, but prospective buyers should review known owner complaints carefully. While offering thrilling driving dynamics and modern technology, this performance SUV has generated owner reports concerning unexpected electrical issues, advanced driver assistance glitches, and drivetrain behaviors.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged I6 Mild Hybrid

Equipped with an electrified 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine producing around 429 horsepower paired with an EQ Boost starter-generator, delivering robust acceleration and smooth power delivery.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Uses a 9-speed automatic transmission designed to handle high torque outputs while offering smooth shifts across various driving modes.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that the vehicle intermittently shifts or drops into neutral while driving or when coming to a stop, causing a temporary loss of motive power until the car is restarted.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic evaluation by a certified dealership is required to check transmission control modules and software updates.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Manages the vehicle's complex electronics, advanced infotainment displays, and 48-volt mild-hybrid architecture.

**Battery and Charging Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced sudden low charge warnings regarding the 48V and 12V battery systems, sometimes resulting in unexpected vehicle breakdowns and roadside warnings.
- **Solution:** Have the 48V battery system and charging modules inspected or replaced under warranty by an authorized service center.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Integrates cameras, radar, and collision-mitigation features designed to protect occupants and pedestrians.

**Phantom Braking and AEB Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Some drivers noted unexpected hard braking events triggered by cruise control or automatic emergency braking systems when no obstacles were present, while others experienced a lack of warnings during low-speed braking incidents.
- **Solution:** Check for manufacturer sensor recalibrations and software updates addressing driver assistance camera and radar alignment.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz GLE53 AMG is a compelling luxury performance vehicle, but used-car buyers should thoroughly inspect the electrical system and test-drive the vehicle to verify smooth transmission and driver-assistance operation. Ensuring that all relevant software updates and battery system checks have been performed can help mitigate potential ownership headaches.
