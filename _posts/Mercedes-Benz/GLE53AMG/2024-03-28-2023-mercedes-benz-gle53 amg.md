**Introduction:**

**Suspension:**

* Air Suspension

**Issue: Leaking Air Suspension Struts**
* Description/Symptoms: Loss of ride height, uneven ride, and excessive bouncing.
* Solution: Replacement of affected air suspension struts
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**Electrical:**

**Issue: Malfunctioning Brake Light Switch**
* Description/Symptoms: Brake lights not illuminating when brake pedal is depressed.
* Solution: Replacement of brake light switch
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

**Recalls:**

* NHTSA Campaign Number: 23V020000
* Description: Steering angle sensor may provide inaccurate data, potentially leading to loss of control.

**Exterior:**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior:**

* **Issue: Sunroof Malfunction**
* Description/Symptoms: Sunroof fails to open, close, or tilt properly.
* Solution: Resetting or replacement of sunroof motor
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Summary:**

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz GLE53 AMG generally has a good reliability record, but some owners have encountered issues with air suspension leaks and electrical problems. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost of repairs can be substantial. However, the vehicle's overall performance, luxury, and safety features make it a desirable choice for many buyers.
