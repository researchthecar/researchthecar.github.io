**2015 Mercedes-Benz GLA200**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz GLA200 is a compact luxury crossover SUV. It is available with a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission. The GLA200 has a sporty and stylish design, and it comes well-equipped with features such as a sunroof, leather seats, and a premium audio system. However, it is also known for its high price and some reliability issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder**

**Transmission**

* **Seven-speed dual-clutch**

**Summary**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz GLA200 is a stylish and well-equipped compact luxury crossover SUV. However, it is also known for its high price and some reliability issues, particularly with electrical and interior components. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be quite expensive to fix.
