**Introduction**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLA200 is a subcompact luxury crossover SUV. It is powered by a 2.0-liter inline four-cylinder engine that produces 221 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired to a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The GLA200 is available in front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive.

People like the GLA200 for its stylish exterior, comfortable interior, and powerful engine. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and suspension.

**Engine**

**2.0L Inline Four-Cylinder**

* **Turbocharger failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, check engine light
  * Solution: Replace the turbocharger
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel injector failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, decreased fuel efficiency
  * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

* **Transmission slipping**
  * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power, check engine light
  * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid and filter, or replace the transmission if necessary
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Mechatronic unit failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifting problems, loss of power, check engine light
  * Solution: Replace the mechatronic unit
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension**

* **Strut mount failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise when driving over bumps, poor handling
  * Solution: Replace the strut mounts
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
  * Recalls: None Found

**Rear Suspension**

* **Rear axle bearing failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Grinding noise from the rear of the vehicle when driving, vibration, poor handling
  * Solution: Replace the rear axle bearing
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
  * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle
  * Solution: Diagnose the cause of the battery drain and repair the problem
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical short**
  * Description/Symptoms: Blown fuses, flickering lights, smoke from the electrical system
  * Solution: Repair or replace the damaged electrical components
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof leak**
  * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin through the sunroof
  * Solution: Replace the sunroof seal or gasket
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system glitches**
  * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen unresponsive, freezing, error messages
  * Solution: Update the infotainment software or replace the infotainment system
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate control issues**
  * Description/Symptoms: AC or heater not working, inconsistent temperature control
  * Solution: Diagnose the cause of the problem and repair or replace the necessary components
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLA200 is a stylish and powerful luxury crossover SUV. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and suspension. The severity of the issues can vary depending on the specific problem, and the cost to fix can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars.
