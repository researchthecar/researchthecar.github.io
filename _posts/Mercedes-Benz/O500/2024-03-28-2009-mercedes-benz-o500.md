## 2009 Mercedes-Benz O500

The 2009 Mercedes-Benz O500 is a popular mid-size luxury sedan.  It is powered by a 5.5-liter V8 engine that produces 382 horsepower and 391 lb-ft of torque. The O500 is offered with either a seven-speed automatic transmission or a six-speed manual transmission.

People like the O500 for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and powerful engine. However, some people have complained about the O500's high price and its lack of fuel efficiency.

## Engine

**5.5-liter V8**

* **Intake Manifold Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold can crack, causing a loss of power and fuel efficiency.
Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: 12V024

## Transmission

**Seven-speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission fluid can leak from the transmission pan or cooler lines. This can lead to transmission damage if not repaired.
Solution: Repair the leak and replace the transmission fluid.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: 12V024

**Six-speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The clutch can wear out prematurely, causing difficulty shifting gears.
Solution: Replace the clutch.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

## Suspension

* **Air Suspension Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system can fail, causing the vehicle to ride low or unevenly.
Solution: Replace the air suspension components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: 11V329

## Electrical

* **Battery Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The battery can fail prematurely, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
Solution: Replace the battery.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
Recalls: 12V024

* **Electrical Harness Damage**
Description/Symptoms: The electrical harness can become damaged, causing electrical problems.
Solution: Repair or replace the electrical harness.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: 12V024

* **Windshield Wiper Failure**

## Summary

The 2009 Mercedes-Benz O500 is a powerful luxury sedan that is known for its spacious interior and comfortable ride. However, the O500 has been known to have some issues, including intake manifold failure, transmission fluid leaks, suspension problems, electrical problems, and interior issues. The severity of the issues varies, and the cost to fix them can be expensive.
