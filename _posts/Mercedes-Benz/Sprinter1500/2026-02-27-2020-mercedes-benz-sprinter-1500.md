# 2020 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 1500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2020 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 1500 requires a careful look at its powertrain offerings and overall platform reliability. As a versatile full-size van tailored for cargo and passenger duties, understanding how its engines and transmissions perform in real-world conditions helps prospective buyers navigate potential ownership costs.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Gasoline Engine

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder gasoline engine produces approximately 188 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering a smooth option for lighter duty cycles. Buyers should verify proper maintenance history to ensure longevity under commercial loads.

### 3.0L Turbo Diesel V6 Engine

The 3.0-liter turbo diesel V6 generates around 188 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque, providing robust low-end pulling power suited for heavy hauling. While capable, buyers should inspect the emissions and fuel systems closely for any signs of prior neglect.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with the gasoline engine, this 9-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes and optimized highway efficiency. Routine fluid changes are essential to maintain smooth operation over high mileage.

### 7-Speed G-Tronic Automatic Transmission

Matched with the diesel V6 configuration, this 7-speed automatic transmission is engineered for durability under heavy commercial demands. Proper upkeep ensures reliable power delivery across various driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V802000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Caliper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Air in the brake system can result in extended brake pedal travel, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will replace the rear brake calipers, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V631000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Over time, the hose could begin to leak brake fluid, affecting the braking performance of the vehicle, including the potential loss of directional stability when braking, thereby increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will check the condition of the brake hoses on the front and rear axles and replace them as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V055000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the software communication module for the automatic emergency call system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V411000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The MBUX multimedia system software may cause the display to remain black, or reboot after startup. This could result in the rearview camera image not displaying properly.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 21V410000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The wiring harnesses for the front seats may have been routed incorrectly and could become jammed and damaged in the swivel seat frames. Damage to the wiring harness can prevent the side air bag within the seat to deploy inadvertently or not deploy as designed, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will attach the mounting clip to the correct position, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V241000: Steering:Hydraulic Power Assist:Hose, Piping, And Connections](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A hydraulic fluid leak may cause a sudden loss of power steering assist, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will install a screw-type clamp, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V595000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will check the fuel hose clamp connections and tighten or replace the components as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V972000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Unintentional vehicle movement can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the electronic stability program control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
