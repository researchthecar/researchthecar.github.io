# 2021 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 1500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 1500 is a versatile full-size cargo and passenger van favored by commercial fleets, conversion upfitters, and adventurers alike. Offering refined driving dynamics and advanced technology for its class, it serves as a popular foundation for custom builds. Used buyers should evaluate potential powertrain options and any reported owner complaints carefully to ensure a reliable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Gas

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder gas engine produces approximately 188 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with a smooth-shifting automatic transmission. It offers a capable, responsive option for lighter-duty urban delivery and passenger configurations.

### 3.0L Turbo Diesel V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged diesel V6 generates around 188 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque, providing strong low-end pulling power. It is well-suited for heavy hauling and long-distance cruising, though ongoing maintenance costs can be higher.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission is paired with the gas engine option, delivering optimized gear ratios for fuel efficiency and smooth highway cruising. Routine fluid maintenance is essential for longevity.

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission works alongside the diesel V6 powerplant, offering robust durability under heavy loads. Regular servicing helps maintain crisp shifting behavior.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V513000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these assemblies fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V779000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** This may cause the starter-generator wiring harness to chafe against the transmission carrier, which could result in an electrical short-circuit. An electrical short can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engagement hooks on the affected cable duct with expanding rivets, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V410000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The wiring harnesses for the front seats may have been routed incorrectly and could become jammed and damaged in the swivel seat frames. Damage to the wiring harness can prevent the side air bag within the seat to deploy inadvertently or not deploy as designed, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will attach the mounting clip to the correct position, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V411000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The MBUX multimedia system software may cause the display to remain black, or reboot after startup. This could result in the rearview camera image not displaying properly.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 21V933000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A damaged brake hose may leak brake fluid, extending the distance needed to stop the vehicle and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the condition and clearance of the brake hose, modify the fender liner trim, and replace the brake hose as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V934000: Structure](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Loose bolt fasteners may allow the rear step to separate from the vehicle, becoming a road hazard and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will tighten the bolt fasteners and replace the console and attaching hardware, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V073000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 120, "Wheels and Rims-Other Than Passenger Cars." The operator may overload the vehicle, resulting in excessive tire wear and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace, as necessary, the tire and loading information label. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V131000: Suspension:Front:Springs:Torsion Bar](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An incorrectly assembled torsion bar may fail at the connection between the torsion bar and linkage, resulting in a loss of vehicle stability, and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will correct the position of the shim and replace the torsion bar linkages, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
