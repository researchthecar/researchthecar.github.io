# 2022 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 1500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 1500 is a versatile full-size van popular for commercial use and custom camper conversions alike. Equipped with capable powertrain options designed to handle heavy workloads, it offers modern technology and spacious configurations. Prospective used buyers should thoroughly review service histories and inspect the vehicle for any underlying mechanical or electrical concerns.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Gas Engine

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder gas engine produces approximately 188 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate power for lighter duty cycles and daily driving duties within the Sprinter lineup.

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Diesel Engine

The 2.0-liter four-cylinder turbo-diesel engine is offered in various output configurations, prioritizing low-end torque and fuel efficiency for commercial applications. It is paired with sophisticated emissions equipment that requires regular maintenance.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear shifts and helps optimize highway fuel economy for gas-powered models. Regular fluid changes are recommended to ensure long-term durability under heavy loads.

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission is paired with select diesel configurations, offering reliable power delivery across a wide operating range. It is well-suited for heavy-duty commercial demands when properly maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V796000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Internal:Park Pawl](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Vehicle rollaway increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software of the electronic stability, transmission control, direct select module, and electronic ignition switch control units, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V101000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The wiring harnesses for the swivel-seats may have been incorrectly installed and may become damaged, which can cause the front side air bags not to deploy as intended. An air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and reattach the wiring harnesses, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V204000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A blank instrument panel will not show critical information, such as vehicle speed, fuel gauge, warning messages, and warning lights, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument cluster software on the affected vehicles, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V444000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V806000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The topcoat paint formula may cause the windows and interior lashing rails to be insufficiently bonded to the vehicle. An insufficiently bonded window can detach during a crash, and objects secured to an insufficiently bonded interior lashing rail can detach, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the paint adhesion and repair it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V876000: Structure:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The partition wall may be missing bolts, allowing the wall to detach during a crash. A detached partition wall can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and secure the partition wall as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V930000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The instrument cluster illumination may not be adjustable and appear too bright in certain lighting conditions. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 101, "Controls and Displays." A display that is too bright can make it difficult for the driver to see critical safety.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument cluster software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 1500 brings strong capability and versatile configurations to the full-size van market. While powertrain options are generally robust, buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify overall mechanical and electronic health.
