# 2023 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 1500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 1500 is a versatile full-size van popular for commercial fleets, conversion projects, and passenger transport. Prospective buyers evaluating this model should weigh its modern powertrain capabilities against typical commercial vehicle maintenance considerations. While official owner-submitted complaint data for this model year is sparse, inspecting potential powertrain options and overall vehicle condition remains essential for a successful purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Diesel

This base diesel engine delivers reliable low-end torque well-suited for heavy hauling and utility applications. It is paired with efficient calibration to balance fuel economy with daily working demands.

### 2.0L High-Output Turbocharged Inline-4 Diesel

Offering increased horsepower and torque, the high-output diesel variant provides stronger acceleration under heavy loads. Buyers requiring frequent towing capacity often favor this configuration.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear shifts and optimizes engine power across varying speeds. It supports both rear-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations effectively.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V806000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The topcoat paint formula may cause the windows and interior lashing rails to be insufficiently bonded to the vehicle. An insufficiently bonded window can detach during a crash, and objects secured to an insufficiently bonded interior lashing rail can detach, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the paint adhesion and repair it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V849000: Suspension:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The front left suspension strut bolt may not be secured properly, which can result in a loss of steering control. A loss of steering control can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the front left suspension strut bolt connection and repair the body contact surface as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V876000: Structure:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The partition wall may be missing bolts, allowing the wall to detach during a crash. A detached partition wall can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and secure the partition wall as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V166000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The main wiring harness may chafe against the drive shaft and short circuit, which can result in a loss of drive power, power steering, and brake system control. A short circuit increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the routing of the main wiring harness and correct it, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V225000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A 12-Volt ground connection may not be secured properly, which can result in the ground connection overheating and a loss of drive power. An overheated ground connection can increase the risk of a fire, and a loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the 12-Volt ground connection, as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V517000: Suspension:Rear:Springs:Leaf Spring Assembly:U-Bolt, Leaf Spring To Axle](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+1500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An axle that moves out of position can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the spring U-bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
