**1999 Mercedes-Benz C230**

The 1999 Mercedes-Benz C230 is a luxury sedan that is powered by a 2.3-liter V6 engine that produces 185 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. It comes with a five-speed automatic transmission. The C230 is known for its comfortable ride, well-equipped interior, and excellent handling. However, it is also known to have some reliability issues.

**Engine**

* **M112 2.3L V6**
    * **Leaking Valve Cover Gaskets**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the top of the engine, causing a burning oil smell.
        * Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks from the intake manifold, causing overheating and reduced engine performance.
        * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **722.6 5-Speed Automatic**
    * **Torque Converter Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping during gear changes, overheating, or loss of power.
        * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushings**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn bushings cause excessive play and vibration in the front suspension.
    * Solution: Replace the lower control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Subframe Mounts**
    * Description/Symptoms: Broken mounts cause the rear subframe to move excessively, leading to misalignment and premature tire wear.
    * Solution: Replace the rear subframe mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Harness Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical problems, such as flickering lights, power windows not working, or the car not starting.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Central Locking System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The doors do not lock or unlock properly.
    * Solution: Replace the central locking module or actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof does not open or close properly.
    * Solution: Replace the sunroof motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1999 Mercedes-Benz C230 is a comfortable and well-equipped luxury sedan. However, it is known to have some reliability issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity of these issues can range from minor inconveniences to major repairs that can be costly. Buyers should be prepared for potential maintenance and repair expenses when considering this vehicle.
