# 2004 Mercedes-Benz C230

### Overview
The 2004 Mercedes-Benz C230, part of the W203 generation, offered a blend of luxury, performance, and German engineering. It was available with a potent supercharged inline-four engine, paired with either a manual or automatic transmission. While generally well-regarded for its driving dynamics and premium feel, like any vehicle of its age, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This data focuses on common mechanical and electrical problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential reliability concerns for prospective buyers or current owners.

---


## Engine

### 1.8L M271 Supercharged Inline-4
**Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain, tensioner, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Supercharger Belt Slippage**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whining noise from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the supercharger belt and potentially the tensioner.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gasket, Oil Cooler Seals)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the engine, burning oil smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of affected gaskets and seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual
**No widespread issues reported.**
- **Symptoms:** N/A
- **Solution:** N/A
- **Cost:** Varies


### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Harsh Shifting / Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, slipping gears, fluid leaks from the transmission pan or cooler lines.
- **Solution:** Fluid and filter change, potential valve body repair or transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $400 - $2,500+


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of front control arm bushings or entire control arms.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Sagging Springs**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sits lower than normal, poor ride quality.
- **Solution:** Replacement of front or rear coil springs.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows stop working or operate slowly.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensor Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, poor acceleration, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the MAF sensor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $350

**Corroded SAM Unit (Signal Acquisition Module)**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical gremlins, lights not working, power locks malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the SAM unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Exterior

### Body and Trim
**Rust on Wheel Arches and Sills**
- **Symptoms:** Visible rust formation, particularly in areas prone to road salt exposure.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment and body panel repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000+

**Headlight Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture buildup inside headlight assemblies.
- **Solution:** Sealing headlight units or replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400 per headlight


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**Headliner Sagging**
- **Symptoms:** Fabric lining of the roof begins to detach.
- **Solution:** Re-gluing or replacement of the headliner.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

**Pixelated Instrument Cluster Display**
- **Symptoms:** LCD display in the instrument cluster becomes unreadable or shows missing pixels.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2004 Mercedes-Benz C230 is a capable luxury sedan, but potential buyers should be aware of common issues. The supercharged engine's timing chain tensioner is a notable concern that requires attention to prevent catastrophic failure. Automatic transmission problems can also be costly. Electrical gremlins, particularly related to the SAM unit and window regulators, are not uncommon. Exterior rust can be an issue depending on the climate the car has lived in. While the interior can suffer from typical wear and tear, the pixelated instrument cluster is a specific electrical fault to watch for. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to early symptoms are crucial for maintaining the reliability of this model.
