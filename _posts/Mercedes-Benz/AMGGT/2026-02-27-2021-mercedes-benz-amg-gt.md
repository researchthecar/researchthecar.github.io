# 2021 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT is a high-performance sports car celebrated for its thrilling acceleration, striking design, and track-capable handling. As a high-end luxury vehicle, it combines exotic car performance with refined interior tech. Prospective used-car buyers should approach this model with standard high-performance expectations, keeping in mind that maintenance and potential repair costs for specialty AMG components can be substantial.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8

This hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers immense horsepower and torque, providing blistering straight-line performance. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though buyers should verify complete service history.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes suited for spirited driving. It operates smoothly in dynamic driving conditions, though low-speed urban maneuvering can occasionally feel assertive.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V042000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The wiring harness for the front wheel speed sensor may be the incorrect length, which could result in damage to the harness during certain driving conditions. A damaged speed sensor wiring harness can impair dynamic control functions, such as stability and traction control, and anti-lock brakes, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the wiring harness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V365000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A disabled eCall system would prevent a vehicle occupant from contacting the emergency services call center in an emergency, potentially delaying emergency responders and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT remains an exhilarating choice for enthusiasts seeking world-class performance and luxury. While available owner feedback reflects minimal widespread mechanical defects for this model year, buyers should always conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection and budget for premium maintenance costs associated with exotic performance vehicles.
