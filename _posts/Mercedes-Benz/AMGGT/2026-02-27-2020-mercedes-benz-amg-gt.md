# 2020 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT is an exotic high-performance sports car delivering incredible power and track-ready handling. For used car buyers, this luxury machine requires careful inspection, as maintenance and potential mechanical repairs associated with its hand-built engines and complex drivelines can be exceptionally costly.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces between 469 and 557 horsepower depending on the trim level. It offers blistering acceleration and robust overall reliability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes in a rear-transaxle layout. It is generally robust during spirited driving, though routine servicing is essential to ensure long-term durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V775000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamp, Reflective Devices and Associated Equipment." Incorrectly aimed headlights can reduce the visibility of oncoming traffic, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will check the headlight aim, and correct it, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V227000: Communication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The emergency call system (eCall) communication module may be missing the wiring harness ground line, which may cause the system to relay an inaccurate vehicle location, and restrict the ability to communicate verbally with the call center. An inaccurate vehicle location or the inability to communicate with the call center may delay emergency responders.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will repair the wiring harness of the communication module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 22V365000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A disabled eCall system would prevent a vehicle occupant from contacting the emergency services call center in an emergency, potentially delaying emergency responders and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.
