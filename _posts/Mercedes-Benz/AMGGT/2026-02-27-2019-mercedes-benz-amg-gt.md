# 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT is a high-performance sports car known for its aggressive styling, twin-turbo V8 power, and track-ready capabilities. While built to demanding engineering standards, prospective buyers should approach high-end luxury sports cars with careful inspection of mechanical and electrical systems, as specialized maintenance can be costly.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers substantial horsepower and torque across various AMG GT trims. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though buyers should expect premium servicing costs typical of hand-built high-performance engines.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides rapid gear shifts suited for both spirited driving and touring. It operates smoothly under normal conditions, but routine fluid changes and software health are critical for longevity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 22V365000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A disabled eCall system would prevent a vehicle occupant from contacting the emergency services call center in an emergency, potentially delaying emergency responders and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT offers thrilling performance paired with luxury grand touring manners. Buyers interested in a used model should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an experienced specialist to ensure all systems are in top condition.
