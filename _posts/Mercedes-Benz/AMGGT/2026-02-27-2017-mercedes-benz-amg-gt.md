# 2017 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT delivers breathtaking performance and exotic styling, anchored by a hand-built twin-turbocharged V8 engine. For prospective used-car buyers, this high-performance sports car generally maintains a low volume of reported owner complaints, though ownership costs for maintenance and specialty components remain high. As with any exotic vehicle, thorough inspection of mechanical and powertrain components is essential prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8

The 2017 AMG GT features a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, producing approximately 456 to 503 horsepower depending on the trim level. This hand-built powerplant offers exceptional throttle response and power delivery, though proper maintenance history is vital for longevity.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

Power is routed through a rear-mounted 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transaxle paired with a limited-slip differential. It provides lightning-fast gear changes suited for both track performance and spirited driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V457000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the driveshaft separates, there may be a loss of drive, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the carbon-fiber draft shaft, including the flange, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V787000: Communication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** For a full list of the affected models visit: https://static.nhtsa.gov/odi/rcl/2019/RMISC-19V787-4620.pdf An inaccurate vehicle location may delay emergency responders, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will also notify owners, and dealers will check software for a successful OTA or update the communication module software as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V478000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The bonding between the carbon-fiber driveshaft and the flange may be insufficient, which could result in driveshaft separation. A separated driveshaft may cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the carbon-fiber driveshaft, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 17V818000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The passenger occupant classification system (OCS) might not function properly, possibly causing the passenger frontal air bag to be incorrectly deactivated or incorrectly activated in the event of a crash. A passenger air bag that is incorrectly activated or deactivated in the event of a crash increases the risk of injury due to potential incorrect airbag.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the OCS software, and if necessary, replace the passenger seat cushion, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
