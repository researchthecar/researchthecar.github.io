# 2018 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT is a high-performance sports car celebrated for its thrilling dynamics, twin-turbo V8 power, and striking design. As a high-end luxury vehicle, prospective buyers should approach the pre-owned market with care, keeping in mind that maintenance and repair expenses for exotic platforms can be substantial.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces between 469 and 577 horsepower depending on the trim level. It delivers explosive acceleration and strong overall durability when properly maintained, though routine services can be costly.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast shifts via a rear-mounted transaxle layout. It performs well under aggressive driving conditions, though low-speed urban manners can occasionally feel abrupt.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V724000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Due to a software incompatibility, in the event of a low speed crash, both stages of the frontal air bags may deploy instead of only the first-stage deploying as intended. If the air bags do not deploy as intended, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the air bag control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V478000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The bonding between the carbon-fiber driveshaft and the flange may be insufficient, which could result in driveshaft separation. A separated driveshaft may cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the carbon-fiber driveshaft, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 18V208000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** In the event of an air bag deployment, the defective steel may cause the inflator to improperly inflate the driver-side air bag and potentially rupture. In the event of a crash involving driver air bag deployment, an improperly inflated air bag and/or an inflator rupture with sharp metal fragments might result in serious injury or death to the driver or to.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front driver air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V826000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the seat belt binds, slack may develop through occupant movement which may lead to increased personal injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will reconfigure the seat belt guides, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT remains an exhilarating and potent machine for enthusiasts. Buyers looking at the used market should prioritize vehicles with a documented service history and budget accordingly for premium upkeep and specialized care.
