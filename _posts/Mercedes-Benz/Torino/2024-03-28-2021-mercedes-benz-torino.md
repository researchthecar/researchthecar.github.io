**Introduction:**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz Torino is a luxury sedan powered by a 3.0L inline-6 turbo engine producing 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It comes with a standard 9-speed automatic transmission. The Torino is praised for its comfortable ride, stylish design, and advanced technology features, but some owners have reported issues in various areas.

### **Engine**

**3.0L Inline-6 Turbo**

* **Intake Manifold Issue**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, decreased engine performance
    * Solution: Replacement of intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive exhaust smoke
    * Solution: Replacement of turbocharger assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or jerky shifting, delayed gear engagement
    * Solution: Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, possible software update
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise, rough ride, misalignment
    * Solution: Replacement of strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Electrical**

* **Electrical Harness Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions, flickering lights, battery drain
    * Solution: Diagnosis and replacement of faulty harness components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery fails to hold a charge, vehicle does not start
    * Solution: Battery replacement, alternator testing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Exterior**

* **Door Lock Module Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks not functioning, keyless entry issues
    * Solution: Replacement of door lock module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezes, navigation errors, Bluetooth connectivity issues
    * Solution: System reset, software update
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC not cooling or heating properly, fan not working
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of AC compressor, fan motor, or other components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Summary**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz Torino has received positive reviews for its luxurious amenities and driving experience. However, some owners have encountered issues with its engine, transmission, and electrical systems. While the severity of these issues can vary, the estimated repair costs can be significant. Potential buyers should be aware of these reported problems and factor them into their decision-making process.
