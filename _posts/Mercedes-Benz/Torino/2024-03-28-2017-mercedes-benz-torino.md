**Introduction**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz Torino is a mid-size SUV that offers a luxurious blend of performance and comfort. It comes standard with a 3.5L V6 engine and a 9-speed automatic transmission, but a 2.0L turbocharged engine is also available. The Torino boasts a spacious interior with premium materials and the latest infotainment features. It is praised for its comfortable ride, powerful engine, and advanced safety technologies, but some owners have encountered issues with certain components.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6**
    * **Oil Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaks from the oil pan gasket or valve cover gaskets can occur, leading to visible oil stains under the vehicle.
        * Solution: Replace the faulty gasket(s).
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.0L Turbocharged**
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, loss of power, and blue smoke from the exhaust can indicate a problem with the turbocharger.
        * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **9-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rough shifting, delays, or gear slipping can occur due to faulty transmission solenoids or software issues.
        * Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module or replace the faulty solenoids.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension**
    * **Spring Sagging**
        * Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension may become excessively soft, causing the vehicle to sag and reduce ride quality.
        * Solution: Replace the sagging rear springs.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Starter**
    * **Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The starter may fail to engage the engine, resulting in a no-start condition.
        * Solution: Replace the starter.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlights**
    * **Condensation**
        * Description/Symptoms: Moisture can accumulate inside the headlight assemblies, causing them to appear cloudy or foggy.
        * Solution: Replace the affected headlights.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200 per headlight assembly
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System**
    * **Screen Flickering**
        * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment screen may flicker or display lines, making it difficult to use.
        * Solution: Replace the infotainment screen.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2017 Mercedes-Benz Torino generally receives positive reviews for its performance, comfort, and features, some owners have faced specific issues. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some being relatively minor and affordable to fix while others can be more significant and expensive. It is important to note that not all Torinos will experience these problems, and the overall reliability and satisfaction with the vehicle remain positive.
