# 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLE450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLE450, buyers can generally expect a luxurious driving experience backed by a smooth powertrain. However, owner complaints filed with federal regulators highlight several distinct areas requiring close inspection, particularly regarding engine operation, premature tire wear, and unexpected body glass incidents. Reviewing service records and carefully inspecting these specific components can help prospective buyers avoid costly surprises.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo Inline-6 (M256) with EQ Boost

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine utilizes mild-hybrid EQ Boost technology to deliver strong, refined performance with approximately 362 horsepower. It provides seamless power delivery for daily driving, though proper maintenance of its complex cooling and electronic systems is vital for long-term reliability.

**Engine Stalling and Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine hesitation, sputtering, and complete engine stalling while driving at various speeds, often accompanied by a check engine light.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnostic scanning to check for fuel delivery, sensor, or software faults.

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced sudden coolant leaks originating from the radiator area, potentially leading to overheating risks if unaddressed.
- **Solution:** Inspect cooling lines, radiator core, and connections for cracks or leaks; replace failed components.

### Fuel System

The fuel delivery system maintains fuel pressure and accurate tank level reporting to the central computer.

**Erratic Fuel Gauge and Pump Operation**
- **Symptoms:** Fuel range indicators and gauges drop rapidly or display inconsistent estimates despite adequate fuel levels.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel level sending units and pump modules for electrical or mechanical calibration issues.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to the 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system with well-spaced gear ratios for highway cruising efficiency and responsive acceleration. It is generally robust when routine fluid maintenance schedules are followed.

## Suspension

### Factory Tires and Wear

The suspension setup balances luxury ride comfort with capable handling, though factory-installed run-flat tires may experience accelerated wear under normal driving conditions.

**Premature Tire Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported rear or front tires wearing down to the cords or going bald unexpectedly within relatively low mileage intervals, such as 18,000 to 26,000 miles.
- **Solution:** Check tire tread depth, perform regular four-wheel alignment checks, and verify suspension geometry.
- **Cost:** $950

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof and Glass

Exterior trim and glass elements provide a modern aesthetic, though isolated reports of spontaneous roof glass failure warrant a careful pre-purchase inspection.

**Sudden Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof or surrounding roof glass panels can spontaneously fracture or explode outward while driving under normal conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect roof glass for stress fractures and replace damaged glass assemblies entirely.

## Interior

### Cabin Electronics and Climate Drains

Advanced electronic architecture powers the vehicle's convenience features and safety systems, though drainage maintenance is crucial to prevent moisture-related electrical faults.

**AC Condensation Water Intrusion**
- **Symptoms:** Blocked or improperly sealed air conditioning drain hoses can cause water to leak into the cabin, potentially affecting electronic modules.
- **Solution:** Clear AC drain lines and ensure proper factory routing or sealing fixes have been correctly applied.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V936000: Steering:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of steering control can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering coupling locking screw, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V178000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Detached trim bars may cause a road hazard and increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and secure the rear window trim bars, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V835000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A 12-Volt ground connection under the driver seat may not be secured properly, resulting in the ground connection overheating. An overheated ground connection can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and secure the 12-Volt ground connection as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLE450 offers a sophisticated blend of performance, luxury, and technology, making it an appealing choice on the used market. Prospective buyers should thoroughly evaluate the service history, check for any recurring engine hesitation or cooling system leaks, inspect tire wear patterns, and examine the condition of the panoramic glass roof before finalizing a purchase.
