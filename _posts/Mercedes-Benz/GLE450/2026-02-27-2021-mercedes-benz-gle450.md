### Overview
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLE450, a luxury mid-size SUV, generally presents a strong reliability profile, reflecting Mercedes-Benz's commitment to quality engineering. While most owners report a positive experience, like any complex vehicle, there are specific areas where minor concerns have been noted. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and prospective buyers. The GLE450 is equipped with a potent powertrain and sophisticated systems, contributing to its overall appeal in the competitive SUV segment.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLE450 demonstrates good overall reliability, with its core powertrain and transmission systems proving robust. The most commonly cited potential issues revolve around the optional Air Body Control suspension, which can develop leaks, and occasional software glitches within the advanced MBUX infotainment system. Premature 12V battery failure is also a possibility, though not unique to this model. For owners, regular software updates and prompt attention to any suspension warning lights are advisable. Despite these minor points, the GLE450 is considered a dependable luxury SUV, especially when equipped with the standard suspension.
