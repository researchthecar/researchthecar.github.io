# 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLE450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLE450 offers buyers a chance to experience modern luxury, advanced technology, and refined performance. As you evaluate your options on the pre-owned market, a careful review of powertrain configurations and owner-reported feedback will help guide you toward a well-maintained example.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (EQ Boost)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine utilizes a 48-volt mild-hybrid system (EQ Boost) to deliver an estimated 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It provides smooth power delivery and seamless stop-start functionality, though buyers should verify the health of both the traditional electrical components and the hybrid battery system during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 9-speed automatic transmission paired with standard 4MATIC all-wheel drive, this setup offers responsive gear changes and optimized highway cruising efficiency. Routine fluid maintenance is key to ensuring long-term durability and smooth operation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V671000: Seat Belts: Rear/Other:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** While buckling the seat belt, the middle seat belt buckle could be pushed down into the gap below the seat cushion. An unrestrained occupant has an increased risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will secure the middle seat belt buckle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V909000: Electrical System:Wiring:Interior/Under Dash](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Loose ground connection bolts on the electric heater may cause the wiring to overheat, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will check the electric heater ground connection and repair it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V936000: Steering:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A loss of steering control can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering coupling locking screw, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V835000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A 12-Volt ground connection under the driver seat may not be secured properly, resulting in the ground connection overheating. An overheated ground connection can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and secure the 12-Volt ground connection as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
