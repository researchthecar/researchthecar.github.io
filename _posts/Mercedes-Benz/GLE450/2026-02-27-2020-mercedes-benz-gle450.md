# 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLE450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLE450 delivers a sophisticated blend of luxury, advanced technology, and robust performance. However, used-car buyers should review potential ownership records closely, as early model-year complaints highlight various mechanical, electrical, and powertrain hiccups that can lead to expensive out-of-warranty repairs.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo Inline-6 (EQ Boost)

Equipped with a smooth 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine featuring EQ Boost mild-hybrid technology, generating around 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and refined cruising, though prospective buyers should verify service histories due to reported misfire and stalling complaints.

**Engine Misfire and Loss of Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report sudden engine misfires, illuminated check engine lights, and unexpected loss of motive power while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic inspection by a qualified technician to check ignition coils, spark plugs, or potential internal cylinder issues.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may stall unexpectedly in traffic or display coolant and electrical warning lights.
- **Solution:** Professional diagnosis of the electrical management and cooling systems.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly across all available driving modes. While generally praised for its seamless gear changes, occasional reports of electronic control module and shifting malfunctions warrant a thorough pre-purchase test drive.

**Transmission Shifting and Control Module Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may unexpectedly shift into neutral when selecting drive or reverse, or experience valve body and control module failures.
- **Solution:** Replacement or reprogramming of the Transmission Control Module (TCM) and valve body by a certified specialist.

## Suspension

### Rear Shock Absorbers

The advanced suspension setup provides exceptional ride comfort, but owners have reported premature rear shock absorber leaks and noises requiring replacement.

**Shock Absorber Leakage and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual rattling or knocking noises coming from the rear end of the vehicle when driving over bumps, often caused by fluid leaks.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed rear shock absorbers.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Lighting Electronics

Complex interior and exterior electronics manage everything from lighting to the MBUX infotainment interface, which can occasionally glitch or require module replacements.

**Infotainment and Headlamp Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Multimediascreens failing, loss of rearview camera function, and error messages related to headlamp control modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning, software updates, or replacement of faulty electronic control modules.

## Safety

### Air Bag and Seat Belts

Safety systems generally offer robust occupant protection, though sensor anomalies and rear seat belt mechanical wear have been noted by owners.

**Passenger Seat Weight Sensor and Seat Belt Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Air bag warning lights illuminating due to passenger weight sensor issues, or rear seat belts failing to retract or move past a certain point.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of faulty seat sensors, harnesses, or seat belt assemblies.
- **Cost:** $1,000 for out-of-pocket seat belt assembly repair.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V671000: Seat Belts: Rear/Other:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** While buckling the seat belt, the middle seat belt buckle could be pushed down into the gap below the seat cushion. An unrestrained occupant has an increased risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will secure the middle seat belt buckle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V648000: Equipment:Other:Owners/Service/Other Manual](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 202a, "Head Restraints." Improperly adjusted head restraints increase the risk of injury to the third row seat passengers in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 19V459000: Structure](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Water in the passenger compartment may affect the vehicle's electrical systems, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will seal the affected area, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V458000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash while the temperature in the passenger cabin is very high, the passenger frontal air bag deployment may be delayed or may not completely deploy. An air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V464000: Visibility:Windshield](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The windshield may not have been bonded correctly to the vehicle, possibly allowing it to separate from the vehicle and affect the full protective function of the passenger's frontal air bag in the event of a crash. A separated windshield may reduce the effectiveness of the passenger air bag restraint system and increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify the owner, and a dealer will inspect the windshield bonding and repair it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V226000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The wiring harness under the seat may become pinched during seat adjustment, possibly causing damage to the wires. Damaged wires may cause the rear side air bags to not deploy as intended.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and reroute the wiring harness as necessary, and repair any wire damage, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V867000: Seats:Mid/Rear Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The third-row seating headrests were manufactured with insufficient padding and a securing rod that may not hold the headrest in place, affecting the adjustment and operation of the headrest. The headrest may not properly protect the occupant in the event of a crash, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify dealers, and MBUSA will replace the third row headrests on the affected vehicle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V056000: Exterior Lighting:Back Up Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." If the reverse light remains illuminated in a gear other than reverse, it may cause confusion about the driver's intention to other motorists, and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the lighting control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLE450 offers luxury, performance, and advanced technology, but used-car shoppers should proceed with caution. Ensure you obtain a comprehensive vehicle history report, verify that all recalls and software updates have been completed, and secure a pre-purchase inspection from an independent mechanic to catch potential engine, transmission, and suspension issues early.
