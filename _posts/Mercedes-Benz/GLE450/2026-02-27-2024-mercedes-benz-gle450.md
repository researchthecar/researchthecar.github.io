# 2024 Mercedes-Benz GLE450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz GLE450 brings advanced luxury and modern technology to the midsize luxury SUV segment. As a relatively new model year, the vehicle benefits from contemporary engineering, though prospective buyers should evaluate both comfort features and underlying powertrain refinement before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (EQ Boost)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine utilizes a 48-volt mild-hybrid system, producing approximately 375 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It offers smooth power delivery and robust performance, serving as the core propulsion option for the GLE450.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly across standard all-wheel-drive configurations. It is designed to optimize both highway cruising efficiency and responsive acceleration.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V878000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An inadequate seal can allow fuel and oil to leak onto the road, creating a hazard for other road users and increasing the risk of a crash. Additionally, a fuel and oil leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high-pressure fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V117000: Vehicle Speed Control:Accelerator Pedal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE450&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A broken or detached accelerator pedal can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will drill through the spot welds and secure the bracket with rivets, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz GLE450 remains a sophisticated luxury choice with solid modern engineering. Used car buyers should conduct a thorough test drive to inspect powertrain operation and electronic systems, ensuring all features function as expected.
