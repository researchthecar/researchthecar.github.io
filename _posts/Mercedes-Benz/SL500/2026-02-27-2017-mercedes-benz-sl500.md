# 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a luxury roadster like the Mercedes-Benz SL500, buyers expect refined engineering and effortless performance. While owner-reported complaints are relatively sparse for this model year, reviewing real-world driver feedback reveals a few isolated electrical and mechanical glitches worth keeping on your radar during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 4.7L V8 Biturbo Engine

This potent 4.7-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers approximately 449 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, providing effortless acceleration and classic roadster performance. It generally proves robust with proper maintenance, though prospective buyers should ensure routine fluid and service intervals have been strictly followed.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with a rear-wheel-drive layout, this 9-speed automatic transmission offers smooth shifts and optimized highway cruising efficiency. While generally reliable, owners have occasionally reported unexpected gear engagement anomalies.

**Unexpected Shift to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly drop into neutral gear while pulling away from a stop, creating a hazardous loss of propulsion.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the transmission control module and diagnostic trouble codes to determine if a software update or mechanical repair is required.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides strong stopping power suited for a high-performance luxury convertible, though critical component checks are essential.

**Brake Engagement Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The brakes may fail to engage properly when approaching a stop sign, causing the vehicle to roll through intersections.
- **Solution:** Inspect the entire braking system, including hydraulic lines, brake booster, and electronic brake control modules, for signs of component failure.

## Steering

### Power Steering Column

Power tilt and telescopic steering functions enhance cabin comfort, though the motorized adjustment mechanism can occasionally experience position-locking issues.

**Steering Wheel Adjustment Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel may become stuck in the fully upward position and fail to return to the preset driving position upon vehicle entry.
- **Solution:** Disable the easy entry/exit feature as a temporary workaround and have the power steering column adjustment mechanism inspected or serviced.

## Safety

### Pre-Safe Safety System

Advanced driver assistance and safety electronics add sophisticated protection, though sensor calibration issues can occasionally lead to intrusive behavior.

**Unnecessary Seat Belt Tightening**
- **Symptoms:** The Pre-Safe system may trigger seat belt pretensioners unnecessarily in specific driving locations, unnecessarily restricting and distracting the driver.
- **Solution:** Have a dealership scan the vehicle's safety sensor modules and update software calibration parameters if excessive sensitivity is found.

## Summary Verdict

The Mercedes-Benz SL500 remains a compelling choice for enthusiasts seeking open-air luxury and impressive V8 performance. While owner complaints highlight occasional concerns ranging from electronic safety system sensitivity to rare transmission and brake anomalies, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable specialist will help ensure a confident buying experience.
