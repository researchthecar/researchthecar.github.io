**Introduction:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL500 is a luxury sports car that offers a thrilling driving experience and a comfortable interior. It is powered by a 4.7-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 449 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. The SL500 is available with either a 7-speed automatic transmission or a 9-speed automatic transmission. It comes standard with a variety of features, including a leather interior, heated and cooled seats, a navigation system, and a Burmester sound system.

**Engine**

Solution: Replace the camshaft adjuster.

**Summary:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL500 is a reliable and well-built vehicle, but it can experience some issues, including camshaft adjuster failure, valve body failure, alternator failure, audio system failure, convertible top issues, instrument panel failure, and heated seat failure. These issues can be expensive to fix, so it is important to be aware of them before purchasing a used SL500.
