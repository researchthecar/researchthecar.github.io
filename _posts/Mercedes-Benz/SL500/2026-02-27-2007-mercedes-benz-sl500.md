# 2007 Mercedes-Benz SL500

### Overview
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz SL500, part of the R230 generation, is a luxury grand tourer known for its performance and advanced features. Powered by a robust V8 engine and featuring a retractable hardtop, it offers a blend of sporty driving dynamics and comfortable cruising. While generally well-regarded for its build quality and engineering, like any vehicle of this age and complexity, certain areas can be prone to specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and enthusiasts.

---


## Engine

### 5.0L V8 (M113)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per coil

**Oil Cooler Seals Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks detected around the oil cooler area, potential for coolant contamination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of oil cooler seals.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

**Drive Belt Tensioner Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Squealing noises from the engine bay, potential for belt slippage or breakage.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the drive belt tensioner assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $500


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Transmission Conductor Plate Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, slipping gears, 'limp mode' engagement, speedometer issues.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the conductor plate and associated sensors.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from the transmission pan gasket or cooler lines.
- **Solution:** Replacement of transmission pan gasket, seals, or cooler lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, 'ABC System Malfunction' warning on the dashboard, loss of hydraulic pressure.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500

**ABC Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Fluid leaks from the struts, vehicle leaning to one side, harsh ride quality.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking ABC struts.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per strut

**Hydraulic Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible fluid leaks from hoses and connections within the ABC system.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses or fittings.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### Central Locking System
**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not locking or unlocking with remote or key, individual door malfunction.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty door lock actuator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $500 per door


### Infotainment System
**COMAND Head Unit Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, audio issues, navigation system errors, unresponsive buttons.
- **Solution:** Software updates or head unit repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000


### Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure
**Headlight Not Working**
- **Symptoms:** One or both xenon headlights failing to illuminate, flickering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight ballast unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per ballast


## Exterior

### Retractable Hardtop System
**Hydraulic Cylinder Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Slow or jerky operation of the roof, fluid leaks around roof mechanisms.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hydraulic cylinders or seals.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200 per cylinder

**Hydraulic Pump Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Roof failing to operate, unusual noises from the pump, low hydraulic fluid.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the roof hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000

**Microswitches Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Roof not closing completely, 'Roof Malfunction' warning, interlock issues.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty microswitches.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Interior

### Seat Adjustment Motors
**Power Seat Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not moving forward, backward, or reclining.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty seat adjustment motors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800 per seat


### Climate Control System
**Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only working on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz SL500 is a complex luxury vehicle where potential issues often stem from its advanced systems, particularly the Active Body Control (ABC) suspension and the retractable hardtop. While the 5.0L V8 engine is generally robust, ignition coil and oil cooler seal issues are not uncommon. Transmission reliability is decent, but conductor plate failures can be costly. Electrical gremlins, especially related to the COMAND system and door actuators, can also arise. Owners should be prepared for potentially high repair costs associated with the ABC system and the convertible top, which are the most significant reliability concerns. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are crucial for mitigating these issues.
