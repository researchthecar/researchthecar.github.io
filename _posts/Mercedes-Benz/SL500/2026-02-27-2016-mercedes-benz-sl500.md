# 2016 Mercedes-Benz SL500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz SL500 represents the pinnacle of luxury grand touring, offering effortless performance and refined craftsmanship. When shopping for a used model, understanding its powertrain options and minor owner-reported equipment quirks will help you select a well-maintained example.

---

## Engine

### 4.7L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This robust 4.7-liter biturbo V8 produces approximately 449 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, delivering swift and effortless acceleration. It is generally regarded as a solid performer, though routine maintenance and timely servicing are critical for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a smooth-shifting 9-speed automatic transmission, this setup sends power seamlessly to the rear wheels. It provides refined gear selection suited to relaxed cruising and spirited driving alike.

## Interior

### Tires

Factory-equipped run-flat tires provide convenience in the event of a puncture but can wear out or trigger monitoring warnings prematurely depending on road conditions and driving habits.

**Premature Tire Pressure Warning**
- **Symptoms:** An alert notification displays on the dashboard indicating low tire pressure in a rear run-flat tire while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the tire for punctures or slow leaks, and replace or repair as necessary according to manufacturer guidelines.
