**Vehicle Introduction:**

The 2007 Mercedes-Benz SLK200 is a stylish and sporty roadster that combines performance with luxury. Powered by a 1.8L inline-four gasoline engine, it produces 163 horsepower and 190 lb-ft of torque, mated to a 6-speed manual transmission. The SLK200 is known for its responsive handling, comfortable interior, and sleek exterior.

**Engine**

* **1.8L Inline-Four Gasoline Engine**

  * **Intake Manifold Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, loss of power, rough idle, decreased fuel efficiency
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or knocking noise from engine, engine performance issues
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 16V481000
**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual Transmission**

  * **Gearbox Grinding:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises during gear changes
    * Solution: Overhaul gearbox
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Electronic Throttle Control Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls or does not start, loss of power, check engine light illumination
    * Solution: Replace electronic throttle control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting vehicle
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system fault
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Minor Body Panel Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots appear on body panels, especially in areas prone to moisture
    * Solution: Repair and repaint affected areas
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Creaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Creaking or rattling noises from the dashboard
    * Solution: Apply sound deadening material or replace dashboard panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Steering Wheel Controls Malfunctioning:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Steering wheel controls do not respond or function intermittently
    * Solution: Replace steering wheel control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2007 Mercedes-Benz SLK200 has a few potential issues, but they are generally not severe or costly to repair. The most common engine problems include intake manifold malfunction and timing chain tensioner failure, while the most prevalent electrical issue is electronic throttle control module failure. Interior issues such as dashboard creaking and steering wheel control malfunction are also not uncommon. Overall, the SLK200 is a reliable and enjoyable vehicle, but it is important to be aware of potential maintenance costs.
