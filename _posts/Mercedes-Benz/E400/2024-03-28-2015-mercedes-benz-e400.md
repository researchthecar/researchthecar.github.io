**Introduction**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz E400 is a mid-size luxury sedan powered by a 3.0L V6 engine delivering 329 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque mated to a 7-speed automatic transmission. It offers a blend of elegance, performance, and technology. While praised for its refined handling, spacious interior, and impressive features, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **3.0L V6 Engine**
    * **Turbocharger Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive smoke, illuminated check engine light
        * Solution: Replace faulty turbocharger
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500
    * **Intake Valve Deposits:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, reduced engine performance, increased fuel consumption
        * Solution: Clean intake valves or replace if damaged
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Shifting Hesitation:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Delay or roughness when shifting gears
        * Solution: Software update, transmission fluid flush, or clutch replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle fails to adjust ride height, sagging or uneven suspension
        * Solution: Replace air suspension compressor
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering headlights, battery drain, illuminated electrical system warning light
        * Solution: Replace alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Battery Discharge:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle fails to start, battery light illuminates on dashboard
        * Solution: Replace battery, check for parasitic drain or charging system issues
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside headlight housing
        * Solution: Replace defective headlight assembly
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Malfunction:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Navigation screen freezes, provides incorrect directions
        * Solution: Software update, hardware replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000

**Summary**

While the 2015 Mercedes-Benz E400 offers a luxurious and technologically advanced driving experience, it has faced some reliability concerns. Issues range from moderate to severe, with costs varying depending on the problem. Notably, some owners have reported costly engine and suspension failures, while others have experienced less significant electrical and interior glitches. Overall, it's important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
