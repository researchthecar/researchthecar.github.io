### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz E400 is a well-built luxury vehicle with a strong reliability record. The powertrain (3.0L V6 engine and 9-speed automatic transmission) is robust and generally free from significant issues. Suspension components also tend to be durable. The most commonly reported, though not widespread, concerns revolve around the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, in some cases, premature battery drain. These issues are typically addressable through software updates or component replacement. Overall, the E400 offers a dependable ownership experience for a luxury sedan of its class.
