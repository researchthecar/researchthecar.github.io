**Introduction:**

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz E400 is a luxury sedan powered by a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine producing 329 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a 7-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive, with all-wheel drive available as an option. The E400 is praised for its refined performance, luxurious interior, and advanced technology. However, it has also faced some common problems.

**Engine**

* **3.0L Twin-Turbocharged V6**
    * **Cam Phaser Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, loss of power, check engine light
        * Solution: Replace cam phasers
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Oil Leak from Cylinder Head:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the rear of the engine
        * Solution: Replace cylinder head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Problems:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifting, transmission slipping
        * Solution: Replace transmission valve body or software update
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, especially in cold weather
    * Solution: Replace battery or alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Malfunctioning Steering Column:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power steering, steering wheel feels loose
    * Solution: Replace steering column
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Water in Headlights:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Condensation or water accumulation inside headlights
    * Solution: Replace headlights
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the sunroof
    * Solution: Adjust or replace sunroof tracks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Crash:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system freezes or crashes
    * Solution: Software update
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz E400 is a solid vehicle, but it has faced some common issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. While some problems can be minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more significant and costly. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues before purchasing this vehicle.
