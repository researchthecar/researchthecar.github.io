# 2016 Mercedes-Benz E400

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E400, part of the W212 generation's final years, is generally regarded as a reliable luxury sedan. It offers a blend of performance, comfort, and advanced technology. The E400 model, in particular, features a potent V6 biturbo engine paired with a smooth automatic transmission, contributing to a refined driving experience. While most owners report high satisfaction, like any complex vehicle, some specific areas have seen occasional reports of issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas and their associated costs for owners.

## Suspension

### Standard/Optional Air Suspension
**Air Suspension Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, especially after sitting, uneven ride height, compressor running frequently.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace leaking air springs, lines, or compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, navigation errors, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, reset procedures, or replacement of head unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dying prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to find the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E400 is a well-built luxury vehicle with a strong reliability record. The powertrain, consisting of the 3.0L Biturbo V6 and 7-speed automatic transmission, is robust and generally free from major defects. The most commonly reported technical issues tend to be related to the optional air suspension system, which can develop leaks over time, and occasional electrical gremlins within the COMAND infotainment system or parasitic battery drain. These issues, while not universal, are worth considering during pre-purchase inspection or for ongoing maintenance. Overall, the E400 offers a dependable ownership experience for those who stay on top of potential, albeit infrequent, repair needs.
