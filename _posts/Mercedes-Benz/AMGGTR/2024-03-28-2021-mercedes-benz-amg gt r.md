**Introduction**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R is a high-performance sports car that combines sleek design with impressive power. It features a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 577 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission, and the vehicle has a 0-60 mph time of 3.6 seconds. The AMG GT R is known for its sharp handling, responsive steering, and luxurious cabin. However, like any vehicle, there are some issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

* **4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8**
    * **Intake Valve Sticking**
        * Description/Symptoms: The intake valves become stuck open or closed, leading to a loss of power, rough idling, and increased emissions.
        * Solution: Replace the intake valves and associated components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Engine Overheating**
        * Description/Symptoms: The engine overheats during normal driving conditions, causing a loss of power, coolant leaks, and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the thermostat, water pump, or radiator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**
    * **Clutch Pack Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The clutch pack within the transmission fails, leading to slipping gears, difficulty shifting, and a loss of power.
        * Solution: Replace the clutch pack and associated components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $8,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The mechatronic unit, which controls the transmission's hydraulics and electronics, fails, resulting in a loss of power, difficulty shifting, and transmission overheating.
        * Solution: Replace the mechatronic unit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery drains prematurely, even when the vehicle is not in use.
        * Solution: Diagnose and replace the faulty component, such as the alternator, starter, or electrical wiring.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Headlight Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: The headlights flicker, dim, or fail to turn on altogether.
        * Solution: Replace the headlight assembly or electrical components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Trunk Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the trunk area during rainy conditions.
        * Solution: Replace or repair the trunk seals or weatherstripping.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Convertible Top Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: The convertible top fails to open or close properly, or leaks during rain.
        * Solution: Replace or repair the convertible top motor, hydraulic system, or seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Bugs**
        * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system experiences software glitches, crashes, or freezes.
        * Solution: Update the infotainment software or replace the head unit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Heated Seat Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: The heated seats fail to function properly or overheat.
        * Solution: Replace the heating elements or electrical components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R is a powerful and stylish sports car, but it has experienced some issues that owners should be aware of. The engine and transmission issues are the most serious and can be expensive to fix. The electrical, exterior, and interior issues are less severe but can still be frustrating for owners. Overall, it's important for potential buyers to be informed about these issues before making a purchasing decision.
