# 2018 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R is a high-performance track-focused sports car powered by a hand-built biturbo V8 engine. As an exotic, low-volume performance machine, prospective buyers should approach with careful inspection, keeping in mind that maintenance and repair costs for AMG GT models are significantly higher than standard passenger vehicles.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (577 hp)

This hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 produces 577 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, delivering explosive acceleration and track-ready performance. It is generally robust, though complex packaging and high operating temperatures require strict adherence to maintenance schedules.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The rear-mounted 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast shifts suited for aggressive driving. While durable under spirited use, proper fluid maintenance is critical to avoid costly clutch or actuator wear over time.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+R&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 17V826000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+R&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the seat belt binds, slack may develop through occupant movement which may lead to increased personal injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will reconfigure the seat belt guides, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R is an exhilarating supercar offering brutal track performance paired with exotic styling. Because complaint data is sparse due to its low production volume, buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a qualified specialist to ensure all systems are operating correctly.
