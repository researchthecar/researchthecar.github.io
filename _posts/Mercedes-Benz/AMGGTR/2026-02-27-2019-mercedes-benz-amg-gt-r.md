# 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R represents the pinnacle of track-focused performance for the nameplate, blending aggressive aerodynamics with a potent twin-turbocharged V8. As a high-performance exotic sports car, prospective buyers should evaluate both its formidable mechanical capability and the typical maintenance realities associated with low-volume German supercars. While complaint data for this specific low-production variant is sparse, thorough pre-purchase inspections remain essential to ensure proper mechanical and electronic health.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L V8 Biturbo (577 hp)

The hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 577 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, offering relentless acceleration and track-ready performance. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though buyers should watch for complex cooling system and turbo plumbing complexities typical of high-output forced-induction engines.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The rear-mounted 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes and pairs with a carbon-fiber driveshaft for optimal weight distribution. While highly capable on both street and circuit, software calibration and clutch health should be verified during any pre-purchase inspection.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+R&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 22V365000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+R&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A disabled eCall system would prevent a vehicle occupant from contacting the emergency services call center in an emergency, potentially delaying emergency responders and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R is an exhilarating, track-capable machine built for enthusiasts seeking raw performance and striking design. Because available owner complaint data is minimal, buyers are advised to prioritize a comprehensive inspection by an marque specialist to verify the condition of the powertrain, suspension, and electronics before finalizing a purchase.
