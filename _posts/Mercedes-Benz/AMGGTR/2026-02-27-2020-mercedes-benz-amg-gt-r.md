# 2020 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R is a high-performance track-focused supercar offering aggressive styling and exceptional handling. While it delivers thrilling power, prospective used-car buyers should carefully inspect specialized mechanical components and electronics, keeping in mind that maintenance and repair costs for hand-built exotic vehicles are exceptionally high.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R features a hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing around 577 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. It delivers explosive acceleration and track-ready performance, though prospective buyers should expect high ongoing maintenance and fuel costs typical of exotic high-performance powertrains.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

Power is routed to the rear wheels via a lightning-fast 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transaxle. The unit offers rapid, aggressive gear changes suited for high-speed driving, though smooth low-speed operation requires proper warming and occasional calibration.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+R&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 22V365000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+R&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A disabled eCall system would prevent a vehicle occupant from contacting the emergency services call center in an emergency, potentially delaying emergency responders and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R is an uncompromising performance machine designed for enthusiasts seeking raw track capability. Buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an exotic-car specialist to verify the health of the engine, transmission, and complex electronics before finalizing a purchase.
