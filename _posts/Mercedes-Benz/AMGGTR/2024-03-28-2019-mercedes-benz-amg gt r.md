## 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R is a high-performance sports car that has a twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V8 engine producing 577 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, paired to a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission, or a 160-hp electric motor paired to a 9-speed automatic transmission for the GT 63 S E Performance version. The GT R is known for its exceptional performance, handling, and luxurious interior. However, it is also known to have some issues that owners have experienced.

**Engine**
- 4.0L V8 engine

**Transmission**
- 7-speed dual-clutch transmission

**Suspension**
- No Issues Found

**Electrical**
- No Issues Found

**Exterior**
- No Issues Found

**Interior**
- Infotainment system software glitches: The infotainment system may experience software glitches that can cause it to freeze or crash. The solution is to update the software to the latest version. **Estimated Cost to Fix**: $0 - $100+
- Navigation system software errors: The navigation system may experience software errors that can cause it to malfunction or provide incorrect directions. The solution is to update the software to the latest version. **Estimated Cost to Fix**: $0 - $100+

**Recalls**
- NHTSA Campaign ID: 19V949000: The fuel pump may fail, causing the engine to stall. Mercedes-Benz will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump free of charge.
- NHTSA Campaign ID: 20V318000: The rear axle may fail, causing the vehicle to lose power. Mercedes-Benz will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear axle free of charge.

## Summary

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R is a high-performance sports car that is known for its exceptional performance, handling, and luxurious interior. However, it has been reported to have some issues, including software glitches in the infotainment and navigation systems. Overall, the issues with the AMG GT R are not severe and can be fixed at a reasonable cost.
