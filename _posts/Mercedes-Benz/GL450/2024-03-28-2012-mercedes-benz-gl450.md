**Introduction:**

The 2012 Mercedes-Benz GL450 is a luxury SUV known for its spacious cabin, robust performance, and advanced technology. It features a 4.6-liter V8 engine producing 362 horsepower and 382 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. Other engine and transmission options include a 3.0-liter V6 diesel producing 240 horsepower and 455 lb-ft of torque with a 7-speed automatic transmission and a 5.5-liter V8 producing 577 horsepower and 560 lb-ft of torque with a 7-speed automatic transmission. While the GL450 is known for its comfort and capability, it has faced some common issues over the years.

**Engine**

**4.6-liter V8**

* **Intake manifold failure:** The intake manifold can crack, causing a loss of power, increased emissions, and potential engine damage. It is an expensive repair that requires replacing the entire intake manifold.
* **Timing chain failure:** The timing chain can stretch or break, leading to catastrophic engine failure. It is a complex and costly repair that requires removing the engine from the vehicle.
* **Engine misfires:** Misfires can occur due to various issues, such as faulty spark plugs, ignition coils, or fuel injectors. They cause rough idling, hesitation during acceleration, and decreased fuel economy.

**3.0-liter V6 Diesel**

**No Issues Found**

**5.5-liter V8**

**No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**7-speed Automatic (4.6-liter V8 and 3.0-liter V6 Diesel)**

* **Transmission shudder:** The transmission may shudder when shifting gears, especially during low-speed driving. It can be caused by worn clutches or solenoids and requires transmission servicing or repair.
* **Transmission slipping:** The transmission may slip or hesitate during gear changes, leading to difficulty accelerating or shifting smoothly. It often requires a transmission rebuild or replacement.

**7-speed Automatic (5.5-liter V8)**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension failure:** The air suspension system can fail due to leaks in the air lines or struts, resulting in a rough ride and reduced ground clearance. It can be an expensive repair that involves replacing the affected components.
* **Rear suspension sagging:** The rear suspension can sag over time, causing the vehicle to lean to one side. It can be due to worn or broken springs or struts and requires replacement of the affected parts.

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery can drain excessively, preventing the vehicle from starting. It can be caused by faulty modules, electrical shorts, or a weak battery. Diagnosing and repairing the issue may require extensive electrical testing.
* **Navigation system failure:** The navigation system may malfunction, displaying incorrect information or freezing altogether. It can be caused by software glitches or hardware issues and may require a software update or replacement of the navigation unit.
* **Blind spot monitoring malfunction:** The blind spot monitoring system may fail to detect vehicles in blind spots, increasing the risk of accidents. It can be caused by faulty sensors or wiring and requires repair or replacement of the affected components.

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof leaks:** The sunroof may leak water into the cabin during rain or car washes. It can be due to clogged drains, damaged seals, or a faulty sunroof motor. Repairing the leaks involves locating the source of the problem and sealing or replacing the affected components.
* **Headlight condensation:** Moisture may accumulate inside the headlights, reducing visibility at night. It can be caused by faulty seals or ventilation issues and requires replacing the headlights or repairing the ventilation system.

**Interior**

* **Climate control malfunctions:** The climate control system may fail to regulate cabin temperature or airflow, causing discomfort for passengers. It can be caused by faulty sensors, actuators, or a malfunctioning control unit. Diagnosing and repairing the issue may require specialized equipment and expertise.

**Summary**

While the 2012 Mercedes-Benz GL450 offers a luxurious driving experience, it has faced some common issues that can be costly to repair. The severity of the issues varies, with some being minor inconveniences while others can lead to potential engine or transmission damage. Prospective buyers should be aware of these issues and factor in the potential repair costs when considering purchasing this vehicle.
