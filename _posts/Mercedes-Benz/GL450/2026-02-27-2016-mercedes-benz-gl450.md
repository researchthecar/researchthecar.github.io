# 2016 Mercedes-Benz GL450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury full-size SUV, the 2016 Mercedes-Benz GL450 offers impressive capability, a spacious cabin, and robust performance from its bi-turbo V6 engine. However, potential buyers should be aware of certain recurring owner complaints regarding electrical glitches, body structure issues like sunroof shattering, and powertrain quirks. Careful pre-purchase inspections are strongly recommended to uncover hidden electrical or fluid leaks before buying.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Bi-Turbo V6 (GL450)

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, providing swift acceleration for this large SUV. While generally robust, owners should watch for occasional cooling system hose failures and maintain strict service intervals.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 9-speed automatic transmission paired with 4MATIC all-wheel drive, this setup delivers smooth highway cruising. Most owners experience reliable shifting, though rare parking mechanism glitches have been reported.

**Spontaneous Parking Disengagement**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may spontaneously disengage from the park position, allowing the vehicle to roll backward unexpectedly when unoccupied.
- **Solution:** Have the gear selector, shift linkage, and parking pawl inspected immediately by a qualified technician.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Complex vehicle electronics govern most cabin and powertrain features. Owners have reported occasional water intrusion issues in rear compartments that can disrupt sensitive electronic modules.

**Water Intrusion and Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the spare tire and battery compartments, causing electrical failures affecting the starter and battery.
- **Solution:** Inspect weather seals around the trunk and rear glass, and clear clogged sunroof drain tubes to prevent fluid accumulation.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Windshield

Exterior glass components, particularly the panoramic sunroof and windshield, have been noted by owners as prone to sudden cracking or shattering under normal driving conditions.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass may spontaneously shatter while driving at highway speeds or while parked, sounding like a gunshot and raining glass inside the cabin.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for stress fractures and replace the assembly if compromised, ensuring no frame misalignment exists.

### Tailgate and Seating

The interior offers luxury amenities and seating for seven, though second-row headrests and electronic tailgate latches have drawn ergonomic and operational complaints from owners.

**Power Tailgate Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The tailgate may spontaneously open while driving or fail to latch properly when using the electronic closing buttons.
- **Solution:** Inspect the tailgate latch mechanism, electronic sensors, and wiring harnesses for faults.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GL450 combines refined performance and family-friendly utility, but it demands diligent maintenance. Buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the electrical system, panoramic glass condition, and transmission operation to avoid expensive surprises.
