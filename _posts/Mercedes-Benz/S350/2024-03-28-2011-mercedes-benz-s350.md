**2011 Mercedes-Benz S350**

The 2011 Mercedes-Benz S350 is a luxury sedan powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing 302 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. Paired with the 7-speed automatic transmission, the S350 offers a smooth and refined driving experience. Despite its luxurious features, the S350 has faced certain issues over the years.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6**
    * **Leaking oil pan gasket**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the oil pan gasket, visible as drips or puddles under the vehicle.
        * Solution: Replace the oil pan gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, reduced performance, and possible engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-speed automatic**
    * **Torque converter shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shudder felt during acceleration or gear changes.
        * Solution: Flush the transmission fluid and replace the torque converter.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, rough ride quality.
        * Solution: Replace the faulty air strut or compressor.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000 per corner
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery discharge**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains quickly, even when parked.
        * Solution: Test the electrical system for parasitic draw and replace any faulty components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Corrosion on grille**
        * Description/Symptoms: Chrome peeling or pitting on the front grille.
        * Solution: Replace the grille.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate control system failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating system not working properly.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair the faulty component (e.g., compressor, evaporator, etc.).
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Navigation system freezing**
        * Description/Symptoms: Navigation screen freezes or becomes unresponsive.
        * Solution: Update the software or replace the navigation module.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2011 Mercedes-Benz S350 offers a luxurious and comfortable ride, but it has faced some reliability issues over the years. Common problems include a leaking oil pan gasket, timing chain tensioner failure, and air suspension failure. These issues can be expensive to repair, ranging from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars.

While some of the electrical and interior issues may not be major, they can still be frustrating for owners. Overall, the 2011 Mercedes-Benz S350 is a reliable luxury sedan, but potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase.
