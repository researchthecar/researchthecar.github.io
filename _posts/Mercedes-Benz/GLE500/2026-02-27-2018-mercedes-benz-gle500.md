# 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE500, prospective buyers will find a luxury SUV offering robust performance and high-end features. However, reviewing owner-submitted complaints highlights several potential trouble spots, particularly regarding electrical glitches, powertrain shifting concerns, and accessory malfunctions. While these reports do not prove inherent factory defects, taking time to thoroughly inspect these systems can help you secure a reliable luxury vehicle.

---

## Engine

### 4.7L Bi-Turbo V8 Engine

The 4.7-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces robust horsepower and strong low-end torque for confident highway passing and towing. Regular maintenance is vital to ensure long-term reliability of its complex forced-induction and fuel delivery systems.

**Fuel System Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Hesitation during acceleration, unexpected engine stalling, and potential loss of power while navigating traffic.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the fuel delivery system and replace failing fuel pumps or filters as needed.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides smooth, refined gear changes designed to optimize both fuel efficiency and power delivery across various driving conditions. Routine fluid checks help maintain shift quality and prevent premature internal wear.

**Gear Shifter and Parking Defects**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to shift out of park or unexpected slipping into neutral while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic shifter module and transmission control unit for software glitches or mechanical wear.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

**Premature Brake Pad Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Metal-on-metal grinding noises from the wheels and a lack of adequate dashboard warning indicators for low pad life.
- **Solution:** Replace worn brake pads and rotors, and inspect the electronic wear sensors.

## Steering

### Steering System

**Random Swerving and Steering Lock**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected erratic steering movements or sudden resistance and locking of the steering wheel during operation.
- **Solution:** Check electronic power steering components, sensors, and steering column modules for electrical faults.

## Electrical

### Back Over Prevention

**Backup Camera Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The rear-view camera screen displays a black image or fails to initialize when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera wiring harnesses, software firmware, and replace the display module if necessary.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE500 delivers impressive V8 performance and a comfortable cabin, but buyers should approach used models with a careful eye toward potential electrical, braking, and transmission shifter concerns. Prioritizing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection that checks the fuel delivery system, backup camera functionality, and steering components will help ensure a safer and more dependable ownership experience.
