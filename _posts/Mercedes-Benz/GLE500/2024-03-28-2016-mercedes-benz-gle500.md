**Introduction:**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE500 is a mid-size luxury SUV that offers a blend of performance and comfort. It is powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.7-liter V8 engine paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The GLE500 generates 429 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque, allowing it to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 5.3 seconds. Other engine and transmission options include a 3.0-liter V6 diesel engine with a 7-speed automatic transmission and a 5.5-liter V8 engine with a 7-speed automatic transmission.

Owners of the GLE500 appreciate its spacious interior, luxurious amenities, and impressive driving dynamics. However, some issues have been reported by owners.

**Engine**

* **4.7-liter Twin-Turbocharged V8 Engine**
    * **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup:** Reduced engine performance, rough idling, and increased fuel consumption. Solution: Cleaning or replacing intake valves. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:** Engine misfires, poor acceleration, and increased emissions. Solution: Replacing fuel injectors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** Rattling noises from the engine at startup, reduced engine performance. Solution: Replacing timing chain tensioner. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * **Turbocharger Wastegate Failure:** Reduced engine power, increased exhaust noise. Solution: Replacing turbocharger wastegate. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Transmission**

* **9-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shifting Delays and Hesitations:** Slow or delayed gear changes, especially when under load. Solution: Software update or transmission fluid flush. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * **Slipping Transmission:** Loss of power or acceleration, erratic shifting. Solution: Overhauling or replacing transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure:** Transmission control issues, shifting problems, and limp mode. Solution: Replacing mechatronic unit. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:** Battery drains quickly, especially when parked. Solution: Testing and replacing faulty electrical components. Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the component
* **Electrical Control Unit Failure:** Various electrical malfunctions, such as inoperative lights, windows, or infotainment system. Solution: Replacing electrical control unit. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Alternator Failure:** Dimming headlights, electrical system malfunctions, battery discharge. Solution: Replacing alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Washer Fluid Leakage:** Leaking fluid reservoir or cracked nozzles. Solution: Replacing reservoir or nozzles. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
* **Panoramic Sunroof Leaks:** Water leaking into the cabin during heavy rain or car washes. Solution: Replacing sunroof seals or adjusting sunroof mechanism. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Lag and Glitches:** Slow or unresponsive touchscreen, software bugs. Solution: Software update or system reset. Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500+
* **Climate Control Malfunctions:** Inoperative air conditioning or heating, uneven temperature distribution. Solution: Inspecting and replacing faulty components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Seat Belt Warning Chime:** Persistent warning chime even when seat belts are fastened. Solution: Replacing seat belt buckle or sensor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

**Summary:**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE500 is a well-rounded SUV with impressive performance and luxury amenities. However, it has faced some reliability issues that can be costly to fix. Engine and transmission concerns are relatively severe and may require significant repairs. Electrical and interior issues, while less severe, can still be inconvenient and costly. Therefore, it is important to carefully consider these potential problems before purchasing a used GLE500.
