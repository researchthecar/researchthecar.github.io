# 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE500 offers strong performance and luxury amenities, but prospective buyers should review recurring owner complaints carefully. While owner reports to safety agencies highlight several noteworthy mechanical and electrical concerns, thorough pre-purchase inspections remain essential when evaluating luxury European SUVs of this age and mileage.

---

## Engine

### 4.7L Bi-Turbo V8 Engine

Powered by a 4.7-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing roughly 446 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration and towing capability. Routine maintenance is critical for long-term reliability due to the complex turbocharging and cooling systems.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Features a 9-speed automatic transmission paired with standard 4MATIC all-wheel drive, delivering smooth gear changes and optimized highway efficiency. Regular fluid changes help maintain shifting refinement and prevent premature wear.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake system feedback indicates sporadic concerns with holding functions and pedal response.

**Unexpected Brake or Hold Function Behavior**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles equipped with hold brake functions have occasionally moved forward unexpectedly from a complete stop, or experienced pedal hesitation.
- **Solution:** Have the brake system, master cylinder, and electronic parking/hold brake sensors inspected by a qualified technician.

## Steering

### Steering Assist and Electronic Power Steering

Issues with electronic power assist loss have been noted by owners, requiring prompt diagnosis of steering modules.

**Power Steering Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience sudden loss of power steering assistance accompanied by a dashboard warning message, making the steering wheel extremely heavy and difficult to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic scanning of the steering control module and potential replacement of the electric power steering motor or wiring harness.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Lighting complaints primarily center on diminished nighttime visibility and projector or housing adjustments.

**Headlight Failure and Adjustment Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights fail to illuminate adequately during nighttime and wet weather driving, or low beams fail to adjust properly.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight assemblies, bulbs, control modules, and leveling sensors for proper operation and alignment.

### Sunroof and Glass

Exterior glass reports occasionally highlight unexpected panoramic sunroof failures.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass can suddenly fracture or explode while driving at highway speeds, creating a loud noise and dropping glass into the cabin.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect surrounding frame and seals for wind-force damage or alignment issues.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE500 combines impressive V8 performance with high-end luxury features, but used-car buyers should approach with careful due diligence. Review service histories closely, and ensure a qualified mechanic inspects the electronic steering, lighting, and sunroof assemblies prior to purchase.
