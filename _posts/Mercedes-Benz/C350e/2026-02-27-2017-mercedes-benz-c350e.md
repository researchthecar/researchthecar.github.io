# 2017 Mercedes-Benz C350e

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz C350e, a plug-in hybrid variant of the C-Class, aimed to blend performance with fuel efficiency. It featured a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor, offering a combined output of 275 horsepower. While generally well-received for its technology and driving dynamics, like any vehicle, it has specific areas where owners have reported concerns. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability issues beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for this model year.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M274) + Electric Motor
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant warning light, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or the water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement or repair of the turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


## Transmission

### 7G-TRONIC PLUS (7-Speed Automatic) with Hybrid Integration
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from the transmission pan or seals, rough shifting, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals, gaskets, or transmission pan.
- **Cost:** $250 - $600

**Hybrid Drivetrain Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced electric-only range, 'Check Hybrid System' warning light, loss of power.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scan to identify faulty hybrid components (battery, inverter, electric motor) and repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $5,000+

## Electrical

### Hybrid Battery System
**Battery Degradation**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced electric-only driving range, slower charging times, 'Check Hybrid System' warning.
- **Solution:** Battery diagnostics and potential replacement (often covered under warranty for a period).
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $8,000+


### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or resets, potential replacement of the head unit.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz C350e offers a compelling blend of luxury, performance, and efficiency. While its core mechanical components are generally robust, potential owners should be aware of specific hybrid system-related concerns, such as battery degradation and associated warning lights, which can represent a significant repair cost outside of warranty. Engine-related issues like coolant leaks and turbocharger problems are not uncommon for turbocharged vehicles of this era. Electrical gremlins within the infotainment system can also arise. Regular maintenance, especially for the hybrid components, and thorough pre-purchase inspections are recommended to mitigate potential reliability concerns.
