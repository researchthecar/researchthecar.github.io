# 2017 Mercedes-Benz C350e Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz C350e combines luxury and plug-in hybrid efficiency, but prospective buyers should carefully review owner-reported issues across key systems. While offering strong performance and refined road manners, used models can present distinct mechanical and electrical challenges that warrant a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Plug-in Hybrid

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor delivers robust power and efficiency for daily driving. It provides spirited acceleration, though buyers should watch for common emissions and fuel system related faults as mileage climbs.

**Crankcase Ventilation Valve / P052E Fault**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light and stored diagnostic trouble code P052E indicating a positive crankcase ventilation malfunction.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnosis and replacement of the faulty crankcase ventilation valve assembly.

**Fuel Odor or Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Strong raw fuel smells noticeable inside the cabin or around the exterior of the vehicle during operation or while parked.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines, seals, and high-pressure components for leaks and replace damaged parts.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission works seamlessly with the hybrid powertrain to deliver smooth gear shifts. Routine fluid maintenance is essential to preserving long-term shift quality and reliability.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering

The electric power steering system provides light, responsive handling under normal conditions, though intermittent control module or assist failures have been reported by some owners.

**Power Steering Assist Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of electric power steering assist while driving, resulting in extremely heavy and difficult steering effort accompanied by dashboard warnings.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the electric power steering module and wiring harness, and replace faulty steering gear components if necessary.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Exterior Structure

Exterior body panels and trim are generally robust, though panoramic sunroof assemblies require careful inspection due to reports of spontaneous glass breakage or detachment.

**Panoramic Sunroof Shattering or Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** Loud popping noises followed by the glass sunroof panel spontaneously shattering or completely detaching from the vehicle while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the roof frame bonding and surrounding trim.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety equipment generally performs well in crash testing, but physical mechanisms like seat belt retractors can experience mechanical jams that require component replacement.

**Seat Belt Retractor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Driver or passenger seat belts retract fully and become stuck or jammed inside the B-pillar, rendering them completely inoperable.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat belt assembly and replace the jammed retractor mechanism.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V197000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C350e&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The front roof panel located between the windshield and the panoramic sunroof may not have been bonded correctly to the vehicle during a prior repair. The adhesive bonding of the front panel might deteriorate, possibly causing the front panel to detach from the vehicle and become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Mercedes-Benz dealer will remove the bonded panel and will install a new front panel by following the detailed repair instructions and using the approved primer, cleaner and adhesive, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C350e&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 17V627000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C350e&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The driver's air bag may unexpectedly deploy due to insufficient grounding of the steering components if an electrostatic discharge occurs and the air bag clockspring is broken. If the driver air bag inadvertently deploys, it can increase the risk of injury and a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will add sufficient grounding to the steering components, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V854000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C350e&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The front roof panel located between the windshield and the panoramic sunroof may not have been bonded correctly to the vehicle during a prior repair, allowing the front panel to detach from the vehicle. A detached roof panel can become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front panel, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz C350e offers an appealing mix of luxury and plug-in hybrid economy, but used car shoppers should approach with caution. Key areas to inspect include the crankcase ventilation system, fuel lines, seat belt retractors, and the panoramic sunroof. Prioritize vehicles with comprehensive service records and ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified European car specialist.
