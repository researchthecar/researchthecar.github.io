# 2016 Mercedes-Benz C350e

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz C350e, a plug-in hybrid variant of the C-Class, aimed to blend performance with fuel efficiency. It featured a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor, offering a combined output that provided brisk acceleration. As a luxury hybrid, it incorporated advanced technology and premium materials. Reliability for this model year, particularly for the hybrid components, is a key consideration for potential owners. While generally well-regarded for its driving dynamics and comfort, specific areas of concern can arise with any complex vehicle, especially those integrating sophisticated powertrain and electrical systems.

### Hybrid Battery System
**Reduced Electric Range**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable decrease in the distance the vehicle can travel on electric power alone, especially in extreme temperatures.
- **Solution:** Battery diagnostics and potential replacement. Software updates may also address some performance degradation.
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $8,000+


### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or errors with the COMAND infotainment system.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system reset. In rare cases, head unit replacement may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $150 - $1,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz C350e generally exhibits good reliability, particularly in its conventional powertrain and chassis components. The primary areas of potential concern are related to its advanced hybrid electrical system, specifically the battery pack, which can experience degradation over time leading to reduced electric range. Infotainment system glitches are also a possibility, though often resolvable with software updates. Owners should be mindful of the hybrid battery's lifespan and potential replacement costs. Overall, for a luxury plug-in hybrid of its era, the C350e offers a solid reliability record, provided regular maintenance and attention to its unique hybrid systems are given.
