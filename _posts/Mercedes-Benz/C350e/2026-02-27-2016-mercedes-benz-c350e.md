# 2016 Mercedes-Benz C350e Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz C350e combines a turbocharged four-cylinder engine with an electric motor to offer luxury and efficiency, but prospective buyers should approach with careful inspection. While many owners enjoy the upscale interior and driving dynamics, historical owner feedback highlights notable trouble spots ranging from fuel system leaks to electrical anomalies and panoramic sunroof issues. Knowing these common pain points can help you evaluate a used model and negotiate a fair price.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4 Plug-in Hybrid

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder plug-in hybrid powertrain pairs forced induction with electric assist to deliver strong combined output and respectable efficiency. While performance is smooth when operating properly, owners should watch out for fuel line wear and crankcase ventilation faults.

**Fuel Odor and Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** A strong smell of gasoline around the vehicle or visible fuel weeping near engine components and fuel lines.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines, high-pressure pumps, and fuel rails; replace damaged or degraded lines and seals.

**Crankcase Ventilation Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of the check engine light accompanied by diagnostic trouble codes such as P052E, rough idling, or engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) regulator valve and related hoses.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The traditional 7-speed automatic transmission works alongside the hybrid drive unit to channel power smoothly to the rear wheels. It generally operates reliably, provided routine fluid changes have been maintained by previous owners.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The regenerative and friction braking system provides solid stopping power, though occasional complaints cite uneven braking pull and pedal booster noise.

**Brake Pull and Hissing Noises**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle pulling to one side when the brake pedal is depressed, or a continuous hissing sound near the pedal.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake calipers, pads, rotors, and brake booster assembly for wear or vacuum leaks.

## Steering

### Electronic Power Steering Rack

The electronic power steering system provides light, adjustable feedback at low speeds and firmer weight on the highway, though total rack failures can be expensive to resolve.

**Steering Rack Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power steering assistance, heavy steering effort, or complete electrical steering failure while driving.
- **Solution:** Replace the electronic rack and pinion assembly.
- **Cost:** $5,840.14

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

The panoramic glass roof adds an airy feel to the cabin but has been a source of mechanical binding, track wear, and occasional spontaneous shattering reports.

**Sunroof Binding and Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual clicking, binding during opening or closing, glass sticking on one side, or spontaneous glass fracture.
- **Solution:** Lubricate and align tracks, or replace jammed mechanisms and shattered glass assemblies.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V787000: Communication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C350e&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** For a full list of the affected models visit: https://static.nhtsa.gov/odi/rcl/2019/RMISC-19V787-4620.pdf An inaccurate vehicle location may delay emergency responders, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will also notify owners, and dealers will check software for a successful OTA or update the communication module software as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V197000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C350e&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The front roof panel located between the windshield and the panoramic sunroof may not have been bonded correctly to the vehicle during a prior repair. The adhesive bonding of the front panel might deteriorate, possibly causing the front panel to detach from the vehicle and become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Mercedes-Benz dealer will remove the bonded panel and will install a new front panel by following the detailed repair instructions and using the approved primer, cleaner and adhesive, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V627000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C350e&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The driver's air bag may unexpectedly deploy due to insufficient grounding of the steering components if an electrostatic discharge occurs and the air bag clockspring is broken. If the driver air bag inadvertently deploys, it can increase the risk of injury and a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will add sufficient grounding to the steering components, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V114000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C350e&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the starting current limiter overheats, the surrounding components can melt, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will install an additional fuse in the electrical line to the starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V854000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C350e&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The front roof panel located between the windshield and the panoramic sunroof may not have been bonded correctly to the vehicle during a prior repair, allowing the front panel to detach from the vehicle. A detached roof panel can become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front panel, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz C350e offers an appealing mix of luxury and plug-in efficiency, but prospective buyers should exercise diligence. Prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection that checks for fuel odors, steering rack responsiveness, sunroof track integrity, and hybrid electrical health. Choosing a well-documented vehicle with a clean service history will significantly reduce your risk of encountering costly post-purchase repairs.
