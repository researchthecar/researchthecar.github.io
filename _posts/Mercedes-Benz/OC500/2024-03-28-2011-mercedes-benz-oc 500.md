**Introduction:**

The 2011 Mercedes-Benz OC 500 is a luxurious and powerful hatchback that combines performance with practicality. It features a 5.5-liter V8 engine that produces 382 horsepower and 391 lb-ft of torque. The OC 500 is available with a 7-speed automatic transmission that delivers smooth and responsive gear changes. It is praised for its comfortable interior, sleek exterior, and impressive performance, but some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **5.5L V8 Engine**
    * **Intake Manifold Runners Sticking:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold runners may become stuck, leading to reduced engine power, rough idling, and increased fuel consumption.
        * Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner may fail, resulting in a rattling noise from the engine, reduced power, and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and related components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Valve Body Issues:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The valve body may malfunction, leading to erratic shifting, delayed engagements, or difficulty shifting gears.
        * Solution: Repair or replace the valve body.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, resulting in difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the battery drain, such as a faulty electronic component or parasitic draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Chrome Trim Peeling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The chrome trim on the grille, headlights, and other exterior components may peel or flake off.
    * Solution: Replace or refinish the chrome trim.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may rattle or make noise when closed.
    * Solution: Tighten or adjust the sunroof mechanism.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Airbag Control Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The airbag control module may fail, disabling the airbags.
    * Solution: Replace the airbag control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: Yes (Number: 14V641000)

**Summary:**

The 2011 Mercedes-Benz OC 500 is a powerful and luxurious vehicle that has been praised for its performance and comfort. However, some owners have reported issues, including engine problems, transmission issues, and electrical issues. The cost of repairs can be significant, ranging from $100 to $2,500+ depending on the severity of the problem.
