**Introduction:**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz G500 is a rugged and luxurious SUV powered by a 4.0L V8 biturbo engine, producing 416 horsepower and 450 lb-ft of torque. It comes with a single transmission option: a 7-speed automatic transmission. The G500 offers a blend of off-road capability and high-end amenities, appealing to buyers seeking adventure and comfort. However, like any vehicle, it has experienced some issues.

**Engine**

**4.0L V8 Biturbo**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Decreased engine power, excessive oil consumption, louder turbocharger noises.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty turbocharger(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Filter Housing Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the top of the engine, near the oil filter.
    * Solution: Replace the oil filter housing gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations or shudders felt during acceleration or shifting.
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks from the transmission pan or lines.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the leaking component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, decreased handling, rear-end dipping.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty shock absorber(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly or fails to hold a charge.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace the faulty electrical component causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Control Unit Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical issues, such as flickering lights or dashboard malfunctions.
    * Solution: Reprogram or replace the affected ECU.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rack Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or whistling noises from the roof rack, especially at highway speeds.
    * Solution: Tighten or replace the roof rack mounting bolts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Creaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or rattling noises from the center console area.
    * Solution: Apply lubricant to the affected joints or replace the faulty component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz G500 faces a range of issues, including engine turbocharger failures, transmission torque converter problems, and suspension shock absorber failures. Electrical issues and interior creaks are also prevalent. The severity of these issues varies, with some being minor inconveniences and others potentially more costly and impactful. Repair costs can be significant, ranging from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their purchasing decision.
