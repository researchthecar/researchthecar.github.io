**Introduction:**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz G500 is a rugged luxury SUV known for its iconic boxy design and off-road capabilities. It features a 4.0L twin-turbocharged V8 engine, which produces 416 horsepower and 450 lb-ft of torque. The G500 comes exclusively with a 9-speed automatic transmission.

While the G500 is praised for its imposing presence, luxurious interior, and formidable performance, owners have reported some issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

**Injector Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, hesitation during acceleration, engine misfires.
Solution: Replacement of faulty injector(s).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic**

**Jerky Shifting:**
Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed gear changes,尤其是during low-speed maneuvers.
Solution: Software update or transmission fluid flush.
Estimated Cost to Fix: Free (software update) - $150 (fluid flush)

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Battery warning light illuminates, difficulty starting, electrical system malfunctions.
Solution: Replacement of alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Sunroof Leaks:**
Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin through the sunroof during rain or car washes.
Solution: Replacement of sunroof seals.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Infotainment System Malfunctions:**
Description/Symptoms: Screen freezes, touchscreen unresponsive, audio or navigation issues.
Solution: Software updates or replacement of infotainment module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: Free (software updates) - $2,000+ (module replacement)
Recalls: None Found

**Other**

**Brake Rotor Warping:**
Description/Symptoms: Pulsations or vibrations felt during braking, especially after driving in wet conditions.
Solution: Replacement of brake rotors.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+ per axle
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2021 Mercedes-Benz G500 is a well-built and capable vehicle, but it is not immune to issues. Some of the reported problems can be costly to repair, especially those involving the engine, transmission, and electrical system. However, these issues seem to be relatively rare, and most owners are satisfied with their G500s.
