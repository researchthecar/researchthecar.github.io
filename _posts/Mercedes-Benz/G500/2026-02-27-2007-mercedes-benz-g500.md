# 2007 Mercedes-Benz G500

### Overview
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz G500, a robust and iconic off-road luxury SUV, is known for its durable construction and powerful performance. While generally reliable, like any vehicle of its age and complexity, it can experience specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and enthusiasts. The G500's engineering emphasizes longevity, but certain components may require attention over time, particularly those related to its advanced systems and high-performance nature. Understanding these potential issues can help in proactive maintenance and informed ownership.

---


## Engine

### 5.0L V8 (M113)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light, reduced power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per coil

**Oil Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant in oil, oil in coolant, overheating, milky residue.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil cooler and potentially flushing the cooling system.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Mass Airflow Sensor (MAF) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Hesitation, poor acceleration, stalling, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the MAF sensor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, harsh shifts, transmission not engaging gears, limp mode.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**Conductor Plate Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shifting problems, no communication with TCM, error codes related to speed sensors.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the conductor plate.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


## Suspension

### Standard Independent Front Suspension / Solid Rear Axle
**Control Arm Bushing Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn control arm bushings.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Shock Absorber/Strut Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, poor handling, visible leaks from shocks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn shock absorbers or struts.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow window movement, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per window

**Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** One or both HID headlights not turning on or flickering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty ballast.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Instrument Cluster Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent gauge failures, warning lights staying on, dead pixels.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

## Interior

### Cabin Components
**Heated Seat Element Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Heated seats not working on one or both sides.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the heated seat element or associated wiring.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Climate Control Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Air not directing correctly (e.g., only blowing from defroster), unusual noises from dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty HVAC actuators.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz G500 is a highly capable and generally robust vehicle. While its core mechanical components, particularly the engine and transmission, are built for durability, owners should be aware of potential electrical gremlins and specific component failures common in vehicles of this era and complexity. Issues like ignition coil problems, transmission control module faults, and electrical component failures are the most frequently cited technical concerns. Proactive maintenance, especially fluid changes for the transmission and regular inspections, can mitigate many potential problems. Despite these points, the G500 remains a desirable and reliable choice for those seeking a premium off-road SUV, provided potential repair costs are factored into ownership.
