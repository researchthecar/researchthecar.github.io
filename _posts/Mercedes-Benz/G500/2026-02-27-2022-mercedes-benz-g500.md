# 2022 Mercedes-Benz G500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a luxury icon like the Mercedes-Benz G-Class, buyers expect uncompromising engineering and robust performance. While owner-reported complaints are relatively few, prospective purchasers should carefully evaluate fuel system components and dealer service communication when reviewing maintenance records.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L V8 Biturbo (416 hp / 450 lb-ft)

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces 416 horsepower and 450 lb-ft of torque, delivering immense power to this luxury SUV. It is generally robust, though buyers should verify that all fuel system service campaigns have been properly addressed.

**Fuel System Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported safety concerns and long delays regarding fuel system components and manufacturer recall campaigns.
- **Solution:** Have an authorized dealer verify and perform any pending manufacturer recall repairs related to the fuel system.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and responsive gear shifts tailored for both highway cruising and off-road capability. No recurring mechanical defects or major owner-reported complaints are currently highlighted for this gearbox.

## Summary Verdict

The Mercedes-Benz G-Class combines hand-built luxury with rugged performance, though prospective buyers should always perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Ensure that all safety recalls—particularly those concerning the fuel system—have been completed by a certified dealership before finalizing your purchase.
