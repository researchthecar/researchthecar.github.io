# 2021 Mercedes-Benz G500

### Overview
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz G500, a symbol of rugged luxury and off-road prowess, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting its premium engineering and robust construction. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the G500's core mechanical components, particularly its powertrain, have proven to be durable. Owners typically report satisfaction with its build quality and long-term performance. However, as with any high-end vehicle, potential owners should be aware of specific areas that may require attention, primarily related to its advanced electrical systems and the inherent complexity of its luxury features. Maintenance costs are expected to be higher than average due to the specialized nature of the vehicle and its components.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Infotainment Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional screen freezes, unresponsiveness, or connectivity issues with Apple CarPlay/Android Auto.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential replacement of infotainment module.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems (ADAS)
**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** False alerts or non-operation of features like adaptive cruise control, lane keeping assist, or parking sensors.
- **Solution:** Calibration or replacement of faulty sensors, wiring inspection.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz G500 demonstrates a high level of mechanical reliability, with its powerful 4.0L twin-turbo V8 engine and 9G-TRONIC transmission being particularly robust. The suspension system is also generally trouble-free. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the complex electrical systems, specifically the infotainment interface and advanced driver-assistance sensors, which can occasionally experience glitches or require recalibration. Interior components like the climate control system may also present occasional faults. Despite these minor electrical and climate control concerns, the G500 is considered a reliable luxury SUV, with its core engineering proving sound.
