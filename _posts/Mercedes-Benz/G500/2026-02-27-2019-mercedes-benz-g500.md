# 2019 Mercedes-Benz G500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Mercedes-Benz G500 (commonly marketed as the G550 in North America), buyers are investing in an iconic luxury SUV known for its heavy-duty capability and distinctive styling. While owner reports to safety regulators are relatively limited for this model year, prospective buyers should still inspect complex electronic systems, exterior glass durability, and cabin hardware during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8 Engine

This 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 416 horsepower and 450 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration for a heavy SUV. It is generally a powerful and refined powerplant, though routine maintenance of its complex turbocharging and cooling systems is essential.

## Transmission

### 9G-Tronic 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 9-speed automatic transmission paired with a permanent four-wheel-drive system and triple locking differentials, this setup provides smooth gear changes and exceptional off-road capability. Routine fluid changes are vital to maintaining long-term durability in this heavy-duty application.

### Differential Lock and Stability Control System

Issues related to advanced driver assistance and locking differential sensors have been noted by some owners, occasionally requiring multiple dealer visits to fully resolve.

**Differential Lock Malfunction Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported error messages on the display screen regarding the front/rear differential lock system, which can simultaneously deactivate the ABS and electronic stability control functions.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning is required to identify faulty sensors or modules, which may need to be replaced or reprogrammed by a qualified service center.

## Exterior

### Windshield Glass

The upright, flat windshield design makes the exterior glass particularly vulnerable to road debris impacts and stress cracks.

**Spontaneous or Rapidly Spreading Windshield Cracks**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced windshields cracking either spontaneously without apparent cause or spreading rapidly across the glass from minor impacts.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged windshield with OEM or high-quality aftermarket glass, ensuring proper calibration of any integrated ADAS camera sensors.

### Door Latches and Linkages

Cabin hardware generally offers high-end luxury, but rare mechanical issues with door latches can arise and require prompt physical inspection.

**Door Latch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The driver-side door may fail to securely close or stay latched when shut, creating a safety hazard while the vehicle is in motion.
- **Solution:** Inspect the door latch mechanism and actuator assembly, and replace faulty components as necessary to ensure secure closure.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz G500 delivers formidable performance and unmatched road presence, but its complex systems require thorough vetting before purchase. Buyers should carefully check all electronic differential features, inspect the windshield for prior repairs or stress cracks, and ensure all door mechanisms operate securely. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist is strongly recommended.
