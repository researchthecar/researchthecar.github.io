# 2016 Mercedes-Benz G500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a 2016 Mercedes-Benz G500, buyers are looking at an iconic, heavy-duty luxury SUV known for its rugged styling and commanding performance. While these vehicles are heavily over-engineered and built to last, used-car buyers should still carefully inspect auxiliary electronics, safety systems, and mechanical latches during a pre-purchase inspection to catch minor wear items early.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8

The 2016 G500 features a powerful 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing around 416 horsepower and 450 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant delivers rapid acceleration for a heavy SUV, though regular maintenance and premium fuel are essential for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 7-speed automatic transmission paired with a permanent four-wheel-drive system and triple locking differentials, this setup provides smooth highway cruising and extreme off-road capability. Routine fluid changes help maintain smooth gear shifts over high mileage.

## Exterior

### Door Latches and Locks

Cabin hardware and latches generally hold up well, though mechanical door latch wear can occasionally occur on heavily used vehicles.

**Door Latch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The front driver's side door fails to latch or close securely upon entering the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Inspect the door latch mechanism, striker, and locking linkages, and replace worn components as necessary.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Safety system complaints are sparse, but owners have reported occasional dashboard warning lights related to front passenger restraint systems.

**Air Bag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The front passenger side malfunction service required light and the main air bag warning light illuminate constantly on the instrument cluster.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the occupant classification sensor or wiring harness connections for fault codes.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz G500 offers a blend of handcrafted luxury, formidable V8 performance, and timeless utility.
