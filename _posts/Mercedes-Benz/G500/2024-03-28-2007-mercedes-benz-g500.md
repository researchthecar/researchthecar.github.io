**Introduction:**

The 2007 Mercedes-Benz G500 is a full-size luxury SUV renowned for its rugged off-road capabilities and iconic design. It comes standard with a 5.0L V8 engine producing 382 horsepower and 391 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**5.0L V8**

* **PCV Valve Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil, decreased engine performance
    * Solution: Replace PCV valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine oil or coolant leaks, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Lockup Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace torque converter lockup solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace worn bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Shock Absorber Mounts Deterioration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive rear-end bounce, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace shock absorber mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Heated Seat Element Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative heated seats
    * Solution: Replace heated seat element
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Brake Light Switch Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Non-illuminating brake lights, cruise control deactivating
    * Solution: Replace brake light switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $150
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Instrument Cluster Pixel Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Faded or missing pixels on instrument cluster display
    * Solution: Replace instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Navigation System DVD Drive Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system unable to read DVDs
    * Solution: Replace DVD drive
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Latch Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficult to open or close tailgate
    * Solution: Clean and lubricate latch, or replace if severely corroded
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sunroof Seal Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into interior through sunroof
    * Solution: Replace sunroof seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2007 Mercedes-Benz G500 is a reliable vehicle with relatively few major issues. The majority of problems reported involve minor electrical and suspension components that can be inexpensively repaired. However, some issues can be more costly to fix, such as intake manifold gasket leaks and instrument cluster failures.
