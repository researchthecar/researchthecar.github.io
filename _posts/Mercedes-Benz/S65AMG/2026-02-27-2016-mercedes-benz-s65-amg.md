# 2016 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG represents the pinnacle of luxury and ultra-high-performance engineering, propelled by a formidable hand-built V12 engine. While these flagship luxury sedans offer peerless comfort and prestige, potential buyers should approach used models with care, keeping in mind that high-end complex electronics, heavy wheel-and-tire setups, and luxury appointments can incur steep maintenance costs. Conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection is highly recommended.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L Biturbo V12 Engine

The 2016 S65 AMG is powered by a massive 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine producing around 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque. This handcrafted powerplant delivers effortless, turbine-like acceleration, though its immense thermal output places high demands on ancillary cooling and exhaust systems.

**Catalytic Converter Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine warning lights and potential emissions system inefficiency codes.
- **Solution:** Inspect exhaust components and replace failed catalytic converters if necessary.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift Plus Automatic Transmission

Managed by a 7-speed AMG Speedshift Plus automatic transmission routing power to the rear wheels, this unit is engineered to handle the staggering torque output of the V12. Routine fluid maintenance is critical to preserving shift quality and long-term mechanical durability.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

The heavy vehicle weight combined with large, low-profile performance tires makes the wheel and tire assemblies susceptible to road hazard damage.

**Wheel Cracks and Low-Profile Tire Blowouts**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden tire deflation, visible sidewall blowouts, or structural wheel cracks discovered during routine service.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged low-profile run-flat tires and inspect alloy wheels for structural fractures.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Steering concerns focus on rare electrical or assist interruptions reported by owners.

**Intermittent Steering Assist Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated power steering warning lights accompanied by sudden stiffening or momentary lock out of the steering wheel while driving.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic steering module, sensors, and power steering harness professionally inspected and repaired.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Glass Roof and Windshield

Exterior glass components, particularly large panoramic sunroof panels, can occasionally experience stress fractures.

**Spontaneous Glass Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Loud popping noises followed by unexpected stress cracks or shattering across the panoramic sunroof glass or front windshield.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect frame alignment to relieve body torque points.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Occupant safety restraints are generally durable, though occasional mechanical failures in buckles and retractors have been noted.

**Seat Belt Buckle and Retractor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Front passenger seat belt failing to latch securely into the buckle or rear seat belts becoming stuck and refusing to retract.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty seat belt buckles or retractor assemblies.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG is an engineering masterpiece offering supreme luxury and astonishing V12 performance. While its core powertrain is robust when properly maintained, prospective buyers should carefully inspect complex electronics, wheels, and glass components. Budgeting for specialized maintenance is essential when purchasing this flagship sedan.
