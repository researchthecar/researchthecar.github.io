# 2008 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG

### Overview
The 2008 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG, a pinnacle of luxury and performance, is equipped with a formidable twin-turbocharged V12 engine. While offering exhilarating power, its complexity and high-performance nature can lead to specific reliability concerns that differ from standard luxury sedans. Owners should be aware of potential issues related to the advanced powertrain, sophisticated electronics, and the specialized nature of AMG components. Regular and meticulous maintenance by qualified technicians is paramount to ensuring the longevity and optimal performance of this exceptional vehicle. Despite its inherent complexity, many owners report high satisfaction when issues are addressed proactively.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12 (M275)
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noises, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of turbochargers.
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $7,000

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hoses, seals, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, reduced engine power, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600 per coil


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (AMG Speedshift)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, harsh shifting, shuddering during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,500

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, suspension warning lights, bouncy ride.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic lines, pumps, or struts.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $4,000 per component

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of hydraulic pressure, vehicle sagging, suspension warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive buttons, audio issues, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,500


### Sensors
**Various Sensor Failures (e.g., ABS, Parking Assist)**
- **Symptoms:** Warning lights on the dashboard, malfunctioning driver assistance systems.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty sensors.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800 per sensor

### Summary Verdict
The 2008 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG is a high-performance luxury vehicle with a complex V12 engine and advanced systems. While it offers unparalleled driving dynamics, potential owners should anticipate higher maintenance and repair costs compared to less performance-oriented vehicles. Key areas to monitor include the twin-turbo V12 engine's components (turbos, ignition), the robust but complex transmission, and the sophisticated Active Body Control suspension system, which can be prone to hydraulic leaks. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system, can also arise. Proactive maintenance and using specialized AMG technicians are crucial for mitigating these potential issues and ensuring the long-term enjoyment of this exclusive automobile.
