## 2020 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG is the ultimate expression of luxury and performance. It is powered by a 6.0L twin-turbocharged V12 engine that produces 630 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to a 7-speed automatic transmission that sends power to all four wheels. The S65 AMG has a top speed of 186 mph and can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 4.1 seconds.

People who love the S65 AMG praise its powerful engine, luxurious interior, and advanced technology. However, some people find the car to be too expensive and its fuel economy to be poor.

### Engine
**6.0L Twin-Turbocharged V12**

**Valve Stem Seal Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the engine, blue smoke from the exhaust, rough idle, misfires
Solution: Replace the valve stem seals
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Suspension
**Air Suspension**

**Air Suspension Leak**
Description/Symptoms: The vehicle will sag on one or more corners, the compressor will run excessively, the ride quality will be rough
Solution: Replace the leaking component (air bag, strut, compressor, etc.)
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Interior
**Heated Steering Wheel Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The heated steering wheel does not heat up, the steering wheel buttons do not work
Solution: Replace the heated steering wheel module
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

### Summary

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG is a high-performance luxury sedan that offers a thrilling driving experience. However, it is not without its issues. The most common problems reported by owners include valve stem seal failure, air suspension leaks, and heated steering wheel malfunctions. The cost to fix these issues can be significant, so it is important to factor this into the cost of ownership.
