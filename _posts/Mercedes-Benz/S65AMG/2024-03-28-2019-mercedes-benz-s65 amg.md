**Introduction:**
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG is the pinnacle of Mercedes' luxury sedan lineup. It boasts a handcrafted 6.0L V12 twin-turbocharged engine producing 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque. The S65 AMG also features a 7-speed automatic transmission with paddle shifters and a rear-biased all-wheel drive system. While the S65 AMG is praised for its opulent interior, exceptional performance, and advanced technology, it has also been plagued by a number of issues.

**Engine**
**6.0L V12 Twin-Turbocharged**
- **Engine Misfires:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Rough engine idle, lack of power, and increased fuel consumption.
  - Solution: Replace spark plugs, coil packs, or fuel injectors.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Turbocharger Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, and high-pitched whining noise.
  - Solution: Replace faulty turbocharger.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**7-Speed Automatic**
- **Slipping Gears:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Transmission fails to engage gears smoothly, resulting in jolting or delayed shifting.
  - Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
**Air Suspension**
- **Defective Air Suspension Struts:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Uneven or sagging ride height, poor handling, and excessive noise.
  - Solution: Replace air suspension struts.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500 per strut
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
- **Electrical Harness Issues:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions, ranging from minor glitches to complete system failures.
  - Solution: Inspect and repair or replace damaged harness.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Battery Drain:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Battery loses charge quickly, even when the vehicle is parked.
  - Solution: Identify and fix the parasitic draw.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
- **Sunroof Leaks:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Water seeping into the cabin through the sunroof.
  - Solution: Replace sunroof seal or adjust sunroof alignment.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
- **Infotainment System Glitches:**
  - Description/Symptoms: System freezes, navigation errors, and unresponsive touchscreen.
  - Solution: Software update or hardware replacement.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1,000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
While the 2019 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG offers an exceptional driving experience, it is not without its issues. Engine misfires, turbocharger failure, slipping gears, suspension problems, and electrical glitches can be costly to repair. However, the severity and frequency of these issues vary, and not all vehicles will experience the same problems. Despite its known issues, the S65 AMG remains a highly desirable vehicle for those seeking unparalleled luxury and performance.
