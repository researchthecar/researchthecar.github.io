# 2017 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG represents the pinnacle of luxury and performance, blending handcrafted V12 power with an opulent cabin. Prospective buyers should approach with an awareness of high-end maintenance costs and potential component glitches, as ultra-luxury flagships like this require diligent upkeep to remain in peak condition.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L Biturbo V12 Engine

The 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless and monumental acceleration. While mechanically robust when properly serviced, supporting systems and cooling components require careful inspection due to intense engine bay heat.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission handles the immense torque output of the V12 engine with smooth, rapid gear changes. Buyers should verify proper electronic engagement of park and drive modes during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Automatic Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** When pressing the ignition button to turn off the vehicle while in drive, the transmission may unexpectedly shift into neutral instead of park, posing a rollaway risk.
- **Solution:** Have the gear selector module and software inspected by a qualified technician or dealership.

## Steering

### Steering Column

Power-adjustable steering columns offer personalized driving positions but rely on small electric motors that can occasionally fail.

**Power Steering Column Adjustment Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel adjustment motor fails in the vertical direction, trapping the steering wheel in the up position and complicating instrument cluster visibility.
- **Solution:** Replace the steering column adjustment motor or switch assembly.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Glass

Exterior glass and panoramic roof features provide an airy cabin feel but demand regular lubrication and track maintenance to prevent binding.

**Panoramic Sunroof Jamming**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported hearing a cracking sound while closing the panoramic sunroof, followed by it becoming stuck or failing to close properly.
- **Solution:** Lubricate, realign, or replace the sunroof track mechanism and motor components.

## Interior

### Dashboard Trim and Leather

High-end leather surfaces can degrade or shrink under intense sun exposure and heat, requiring costly cosmetic remedies.

**Dashboard Leather Shrinkage and Separation**
- **Symptoms:** The leather on the dashboard begins to pull away or shrink around the passenger and driver side airbag areas, exposing internal components.
- **Solution:** Leather re-adhesion or complete dashboard replacement may be required, which can incur significant out-of-pocket costs.
- **Cost:** $3,000 to $6,000

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S65+AMG&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 17V714000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S65+AMG&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A transistor within the power steering control unit may overheat resulting in the power steering system being disabled. If the power steering assist is unexpectedly disabled, it may increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the steering rack, replace it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V829000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S65+AMG&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The seat belt extenders on the affected vehicles may not retract as intended and may potentially break the event of a crash. In the event of a crash, if the seat belt extender does not retract and/or the extender breaks, the seat occupant may not be properly restrained, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V760000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S65+AMG&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The low beam headlights on the affected vehicles may not be properly adjusted. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." If the low-beam headlights are out of adjustment, the driver's visibility may be reduced, increasing.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and correct the headlight adjustment, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V816000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S65+AMG&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The rear passenger door on the driver side may indicate that it is locked, when it may not be. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 206, "Door Locks and Door Retention Components." If the door appears to be locked, the door may be unintentionally opened, increasing the risk of.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the left rear door lock, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG delivers breathtaking V12 performance and an exceptionally lavish interior experience. While major drivetrain mechanical failures are rare, buyers must budget for potential luxury-specific cosmetic upkeep, electronic quirks, and high component replacement costs. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an experienced specialist is essential.
