# 2007 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG

### Overview
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG, a pinnacle of luxury and performance, is equipped with a formidable twin-turbocharged V12 engine. While offering exhilarating power, its complexity and high-performance nature can lead to specific reliability concerns. This analysis focuses on documented issues beyond routine maintenance, highlighting areas that may require attention for owners of this exclusive vehicle. The S65 AMG represents a significant investment, and understanding its potential reliability quirks is crucial for maintaining its performance and value.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12 (M275)
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noises from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of turbocharger units.
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $7,000 per turbo

**Intercooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine performance, overheating, rough idling.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of intercooler hoses and components.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough running, check engine light illumination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500 per coil

**Coolant Leaks (ABC System)**
- **Symptoms:** Fluid leaks under the vehicle, suspension dropping, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hoses, pumps, or accumulators within the ABC system.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $4,000+


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission slipping, 'limp mode' engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement or reprogramming of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging suspension, fluid leaks, harsh ride quality, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic lines, struts, pumps, or accumulators.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $5,000+

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of hydraulic pressure, suspension dropping, loud whining noise.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, audio issues, navigation malfunctions, unresponsive controls.
- **Solution:** Software updates, component replacement (head unit, screen).
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,500


### Sensors (Parking, Rain, etc.)
**Malfunctioning Sensors**
- **Symptoms:** False alerts, non-functional systems (e.g., wipers not activating, parking assist errors).
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty sensors.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600 per sensor


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods, electrical system malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or circuits causing the drain.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000+

## Interior

### Climate Control System
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow or intermittent airflow from vents.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor or resistor.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


### Seat Actuators
**Malfunctioning Seat Adjustments**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not moving, stuck in position, memory function failure.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seat motors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,000 per seat

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG is a high-performance luxury sedan with a complex V12 engine and advanced systems like Active Body Control (ABC). While it offers an unparalleled driving experience, potential owners should be aware of specific reliability concerns. The engine, particularly its twin-turbochargers and intercoolers, along with the ABC suspension system, are the most common sources of significant repair costs. Electrical gremlins, especially related to the COMAND infotainment system and parasitic battery drain, can also arise. Transmission issues, though less frequent than engine or suspension problems, can also be costly to repair. Regular, specialized maintenance is paramount to mitigating these potential issues and ensuring the longevity of this exotic vehicle.
