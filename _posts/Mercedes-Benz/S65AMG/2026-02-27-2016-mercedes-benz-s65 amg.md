# 2016 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG, a pinnacle of luxury and performance, is equipped with a formidable V12 engine and a sophisticated transmission system. While generally engineered for high reliability, as with any high-performance vehicle, certain components may experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related problems reported by owners and technicians, providing insights into potential areas of concern and their associated repair costs. The S65 AMG's complex systems, particularly its advanced electronics and powertrain, require specialized attention for maintenance and repair to ensure continued optimal performance and longevity.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12 (M275)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, reduced engine power, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per coil

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased exhaust smoke, boost pressure issues.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of turbocharger(s) and associated plumbing.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $6,000 per turbocharger

**Coolant Leaks (Intercooler System)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant loss, reduced engine performance, visible coolant residue.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking intercooler hoses, pipes, or the intercooler itself.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (7G-TRONIC)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, failure to shift gears, limp mode engagement, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement or reprogramming of the Transmission Control Module.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping gears, delayed engagement, unusual noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC) System
**Hydraulic Line Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, uneven ride height, 'ABC System Malfunction' warning message, fluid leaks under the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hydraulic lines, seals, or suspension struts.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,500 per line/component

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of hydraulic pressure, vehicle sagging, 'ABC System Malfunction' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Screen Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Flickering screen, black screen, unresponsive touch controls, freezing.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the COMAND head unit.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $3,000


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or circuits causing excessive power draw.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG is a highly complex luxury performance vehicle. While its core powertrain is robust, potential issues can arise in areas such as the Active Body Control suspension system, the 7-speed transmission's control module, and the twin-turbo V12 engine's ignition and turbo components. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the COMAND infotainment system and parasitic battery drain, are also noted. Repairs for these issues can be substantial due to the specialized nature of the components and the vehicle's high-end engineering. Regular, specialized maintenance is crucial for mitigating these potential problems and ensuring the longevity of this exceptional automobile.
