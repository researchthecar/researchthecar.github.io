# 2009 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG

### Overview
The 2009 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG, a pinnacle of luxury and performance, is equipped with a formidable twin-turbocharged V12 engine. While offering exhilarating power, its complexity and high-performance nature can lead to specific reliability concerns. Owners should be aware of potential issues related to the advanced powertrain, sophisticated suspension system, and intricate electronics. Regular and meticulous maintenance is crucial for preserving the longevity and optimal performance of this high-end luxury sedan. Despite its performance pedigree, some components may require more attention than those found in less performance-oriented vehicles.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12 (M275)
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noises from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of turbochargers.
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $6,000

**Intercooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine performance, overheating, coolant smell.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of intercooler hoses and components.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500 per coil

**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers, Oil Cooler)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of gaskets and seals.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,200


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, shuddering during acceleration, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, inability to shift gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, suspension warning lights, fluid leaks under the car.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic lines, pumps, or struts.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $4,000 per component

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of hydraulic pressure, suspension dropping, loud whining noise.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, audio issues, navigation malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,500


### Sensors (Parking, Rain, etc.)
**Malfunctioning Sensors**
- **Symptoms:** False alerts, non-functional features (e.g., wipers not activating).
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty sensors.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600 per sensor


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods, electrical gremlins.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or circuits causing drain.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000

## Interior

### Climate Control System
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air conditioning or heat, intermittent fan operation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### Seat Actuators
**Malfunctioning Seat Adjustments**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not moving, stuck in position.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seat motors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,000

### Summary Verdict
The 2009 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG is a high-performance luxury vehicle with a complex V12 engine and advanced systems. While it offers unparalleled driving dynamics, potential owners should anticipate higher maintenance costs and the possibility of specific component failures, particularly within the powertrain, suspension, and electrical systems. Issues like turbocharger problems, ABC suspension leaks, and electronic glitches are not uncommon. Regular, specialized maintenance is paramount to mitigate these risks and ensure the continued reliability of this exotic sedan. It is a vehicle that demands significant investment in upkeep.
