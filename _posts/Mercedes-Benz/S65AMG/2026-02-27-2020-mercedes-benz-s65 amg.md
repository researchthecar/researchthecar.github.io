# 2020 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG

### Overview
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG, a pinnacle of luxury and performance, is equipped with a formidable handcrafted V12 engine. While generally robust, like any high-performance vehicle, it can encounter specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance. Owners can expect a generally dependable experience, but awareness of potential areas for attention can help maintain the vehicle's exceptional performance and longevity. The S65 AMG represents a significant investment, and understanding its reliability profile is crucial for prospective and current owners.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12 (M279)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, reduced engine power, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Oil Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil leaks, oil smell, potential for oil in coolant.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil cooler and associated seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle leaning to one side, suspension warning lights, fluid leaks under the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hydraulic lines, struts, or pump.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $4000+


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates or resets. In rare cases, module replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1000


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to find the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG is a highly exclusive and powerful vehicle. While its V12 engine and sophisticated systems are generally reliable, potential owners should be aware of specific issues such as ignition coil failures, oil cooler leaks, and potential hydraulic leaks within the Active Body Control suspension. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and battery drain, can also occur. These issues, while not universal, can be costly to repair due to the complexity and specialized nature of the components. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs are essential for maintaining the integrity and performance of this exceptional automobile.
