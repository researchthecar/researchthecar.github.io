# 2020 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a luxury flagship, understanding what current owners experience can help guide your purchase. While high-end luxury vehicles offer incredible performance and comfort, they also feature complex electronic and mechanical systems that require careful inspection before buying.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbocharged V12

Powered by a hand-built 6.0-liter biturbo V12 producing around 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless, immense acceleration. This flagship engine requires meticulous maintenance history to ensure long-term reliability and peak performance.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic

Paired with a responsive 7-speed dual-clutch or planetary automatic transmission designed to handle massive torque output smoothly. Regular fluid changes are vital for maintaining crisp shifting and overall drivetrain longevity.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Sunroof

Exterior glass components provide structural integrity and cabin visibility but require careful inspection for stress cracks.

**Glass Breakage and Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected windshield cracking originating from small impacts or spontaneous sunroof shattering while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect glass thoroughly before purchase and verify insurance coverage for comprehensive glass replacement.

## Summary Verdict

This high-performance luxury sedan delivers breathtaking power and unmatched road presence, but buyers should approach with a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to vehicle glass, electronic systems, and complete service records to ensure a satisfying ownership experience.
