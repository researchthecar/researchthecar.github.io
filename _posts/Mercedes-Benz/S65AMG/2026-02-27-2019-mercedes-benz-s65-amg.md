# 2019 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz S-Class represents the pinnacle of luxury, engineering, and advanced automotive technology. Prospective buyers exploring the used market should keep in mind that owner-reported grievances submitted to safety authorities highlight occasional electrical quirks, mechanical component glitches, and driver-assistance sensor anomalies. Reviewing service records and conducting a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection can help ensure a rewarding ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8 (S560)

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 463 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally smooth and effortless acceleration. This powerplant is generally robust, though proper maintenance history is essential for high-end luxury longevity.

### 6.0L Biturbo V12 (S65 AMG)

The hand-built 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine generates a massive 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque, offering peerless performance and supreme refinement. Buyers should anticipate higher routine maintenance expenses given the complex engineering of this flagship motor.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Plug-in Hybrid (S560e)

The plug-in hybrid powertrain pairs a turbocharged inline-six engine with an electric motor for seamless efficiency and refined electric-only driving capability. While innovative, buyers should verify the health of the high-voltage battery system during an inspection.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides smooth, nearly imperceptible gear changes tailored for supreme passenger comfort across various trims. Regular fluid changes are recommended to maintain shift quality and mechanical integrity.

### AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed AMG Speedshift multi-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes suited to high-performance V12 output. Proper software calibrations and timely servicing ensure optimal responsiveness and durability.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The sophisticated braking systems offer strong stopping power suited to a heavy luxury sedan, though electronic actuator issues require prompt attention.

**Rear Brake Actuator Seizure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported that the rear brake actuator can seize unexpectedly, rendering the parking or service brakes inoperable upon vehicle startup.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the brake actuators and replace any failed mechanical or electronic components as necessary.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Shifter

Power steering and column adjustments provide effortless maneuverability, though minor switchgear and adjustment track glitches can occur.

**Steering Column Adjustment Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** The motorized steering column adjustment mechanism can occasionally stick in the fully raised position and resist re-adjustment.
- **Solution:** Lubrication or replacement of the steering column adjustment motor and track assembly may be required by a specialist.

## Safety

### Seat Belts and Airbags

Advanced safety and pre-collision systems provide comprehensive protection, though complex sensor networks can occasionally trigger erroneous seat belt pretensioner activity.

**Automatic Pretensioner and Locking Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted instances where seat belts unexpectedly tighten or rear seat belt/airbag assemblies lock in place and fail to retract.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt sensors, pretensioner assemblies, and restraint control modules, replacing faulty components according to diagnostic codes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V839000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S65+AMG&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A correctly fastened seatbelt may be inaccurately detected as being unfastened, preventing the seat belt pre-tensioners from activating in the event of a crash. If the seat belt tensioning does not activate in the event of a crash, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the seat belt buckles, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz S-Class delivers an unmatched blend of prestige, comfort, and engineering excellence. While most owners enjoy trouble-free luxury cruising, potential buyers should carefully verify the proper operation of all electronic features, steering adjustments, and restraint systems during a thorough pre-purchase inspection to avoid unexpected repair costs.
