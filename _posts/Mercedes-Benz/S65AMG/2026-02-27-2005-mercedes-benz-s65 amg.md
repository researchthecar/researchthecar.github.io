# 2005 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG

### Overview
The 2005 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG, a pinnacle of luxury and performance, is equipped with a formidable twin-turbocharged V12 engine. While offering exhilarating power, its complexity and high-performance nature mean that certain components can be prone to issues, particularly as the vehicle ages. Owners should be aware of potential challenges related to the powertrain, electronics, and specialized systems. Regular and meticulous maintenance by qualified technicians is crucial for preserving the reliability and longevity of this high-performance luxury sedan. Despite its potential for costly repairs, the S65 AMG remains a desirable vehicle for enthusiasts who prioritize its unique driving experience and opulent features.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12 (M275)
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, black smoke from exhaust, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of turbochargers.
- **Cost:** $3000 - $6000 per turbo

**Intercooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine performance, overheating, coolant smell.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of intercooler hoses and components.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, reduced engine power, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500 per coil

**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers, Oil Pan)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil leaks, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of gaskets and seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2000


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (AMG Speedshift)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, shuddering during acceleration, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, inability to shift gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement or reprogramming of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2000


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, suspension warning lights, stiff ride, fluid leaks under the car.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic lines, struts, or pump.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $4000 per component

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of hydraulic pressure, vehicle sagging, suspension warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive buttons, audio issues, navigation problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates, module repair, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000


### Sensors (Parking, Rain, etc.)
**Malfunctioning Sensors**
- **Symptoms:** False alerts, non-functional features (e.g., wipers not activating), warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty sensors.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600 per sensor


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or wiring causing drain.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000


## Exterior

### Headlight Ballasts
**Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** One or both headlights not turning on, flickering headlights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight ballast.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per ballast


### Door Lock Actuators
**Malfunctioning Door Locks**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not locking or unlocking remotely or manually.
- **Solution:** Replacement of door lock actuators.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per actuator


## Interior

### Seat Adjustment Motors
**Faulty Seat Motors**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not moving or adjusting correctly.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seat adjustment motors.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800 per seat


### Climate Control System
**Blend Door Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to control temperature or airflow direction.
- **Solution:** Replacement of blend door actuators.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2005 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG is a complex and powerful vehicle. While it offers an exceptional driving experience, its high-performance nature and advanced technology can lead to significant repair costs. Key areas of concern include the V12 engine's twin-turbo system, the Active Body Control (ABC) suspension, and various electrical components. Owners should budget for potential maintenance and repairs, prioritizing specialized service from experienced technicians. Despite these considerations, for those who can manage the upkeep, the S65 AMG remains a remarkable blend of luxury, performance, and exclusivity.
