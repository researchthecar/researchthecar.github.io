**Introduction:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a twin-turbocharged 6.0-liter V12 engine paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. It produces an impressive 621 horsepower and 738 pound-feet of torque. The S65 AMG is lauded for its impressive acceleration, lavish interior, and advanced technology. However, like any vehicle, it is not without its potential issues.

**Engine:**

* **Cylinder Deactivation Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle may experience hesitation or rough idling due to a malfunctioning cylinder deactivation system.
    * Solution: Replacement of cylinder deactivation solenoids.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle may lose power or produce excessive smoke due to a failed turbocharger.
    * Solution: Replacement of turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+

**Transmission:**

* **7-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Shifting Irregularities:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or shift abruptly during acceleration or deceleration.
        * Solution: Software update or transmission fluid replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * **Transmission Control Module Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle may experience transmission issues such as lack of power or delayed shifting.
        * Solution: Replacement of transmission control module.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+

**Suspension:**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle may not maintain its ride height or experience a rough ride.
    * Solution: Replacement of air suspension compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Drainage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery drains prematurely, leading to difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Identification and repair of electrical draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Alternator Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's electrical system may not receive adequate power.
    * Solution: Replacement of alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+

**Exterior:**

* **Sunroof Mechanism Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may fail to open, close, or tilt properly.
    * Solution: Replacement of sunroof motor or control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+

**Interior:**

* **Infotainment System Lag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's infotainment system may experience delays or freezing.
    * Solution: Software update.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500
* **Ambient Lighting Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's ambient lighting may malfunction or fail completely.
    * Solution: Replacement of ambient lighting modules or wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Summary:**

While the 2017 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG is an impressive luxury sedan, it is not without potential issues. Engine and transmission problems are relatively rare but can be expensive to fix. Electrical and interior issues are more common and can range from minor annoyances to more serious problems. Overall, the S65 AMG is a reliable vehicle, but owners should be aware of the potential issues outlined above and budget accordingly.
