# 2012 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG

### Overview
The 2012 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG, a pinnacle of luxury and performance, is equipped with a formidable twin-turbocharged V12 engine. While offering exhilarating driving dynamics and a lavish interior, like any high-performance vehicle, it can present specific reliability concerns. This analysis focuses on documented issues beyond routine maintenance, highlighting potential areas for owners to monitor. The S65 AMG's complexity, particularly its advanced powertrain and sophisticated electronic systems, means that when issues do arise, they can be costly to repair. Understanding these potential weak points is crucial for maintaining this exceptional automobile.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12 (M275)
**Turbocharger Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, check engine light, inconsistent boost pressure.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty turbocharger wastegate actuators.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, reduced acceleration, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of individual or multiple ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per coil

**Coolant Leak (Intercooler System)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, loss of coolant, visible leaks around intercooler pipes.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking intercooler hoses and seals.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (AMG Speedshift)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, shuddering during acceleration, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, limp mode.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC) System
**Hydraulic Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, suspension warning lights, stiff ride, fluid leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $2500

**Strut/Cylinder Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Uneven ride height, suspension warning messages, fluid leaks from suspension components.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking ABC struts or cylinders.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, audio issues, navigation problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of COMAND head unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2500


### Sensors (Parking Assist, Blind Spot)
**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** False warnings, system deactivation, error messages.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty sensors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per sensor

## Interior

### Climate Control System
**Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


### Seat Adjustment Motors
**Malfunctioning Seat Adjustments**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not moving, stuck in position, unusual noises.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seat adjustment motors and switches.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800 per seat module

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG is a complex luxury performance sedan. While its V12 engine and advanced systems provide an unparalleled driving experience, potential owners should be aware of specific reliability concerns. The Active Body Control (ABC) suspension is a notable area where hydraulic component failures can occur, leading to significant repair costs. Engine-related issues, particularly with turbocharger actuators and ignition coils, are also documented. Electrical gremlins within the COMAND infotainment system and various sensors can arise. Transmission issues, though less common, can involve the torque converter or control module. Despite these potential challenges, with diligent maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs, the S65 AMG can remain a rewarding vehicle.
