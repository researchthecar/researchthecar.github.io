# 2018 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG

### Overview
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG, a pinnacle of luxury and performance, is equipped with a formidable handcrafted V12 engine and a sophisticated transmission system. While generally engineered for high reliability, as with any high-performance vehicle, specific components can experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, examining key areas such as the powertrain, suspension, electrical systems, and interior/exterior components. The S65 AMG's complexity means that while many systems are robust, potential issues can arise, particularly with advanced features and the high-output engine. Owners should be aware of these potential areas to ensure continued optimal performance and longevity of this exceptional vehicle.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12 (M279)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, reduced engine power, check engine light illumination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased exhaust smoke.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential repair or replacement of turbocharger(s) and associated plumbing.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000+

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant level warning, overheating.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of leaking hoses, seals, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (7G-TRONIC)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, failure to engage gears, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and reprogramming or replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping gears, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, suspension warning lights, fluid leaks under the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hydraulic lines, struts, or pump.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $4,000+

**Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of hydraulic pressure, suspension not responding, vehicle may sit low.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Flickering screen, unresponsive touch controls, system reboots.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the infotainment head unit.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $3,000


### Sensors (Parking, Blind Spot)
**Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** False warnings, system deactivation, error messages.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty sensor(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per sensor


## Exterior

### LED Lighting
**Headlight/Taillight Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent operation, flickering, or complete failure of LED units.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the affected LED module.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000 per module


## Interior

### Climate Control System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from vents, inconsistent fan speed.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the cabin blower motor.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


### Seat Adjustment Motors
**Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not moving or adjusting correctly.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty seat adjustment motor.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per motor


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG is a highly complex luxury performance sedan. While its V12 engine and advanced systems are generally robust, potential issues can arise, particularly with the sophisticated Active Body Control suspension, the high-output twin-turbocharged engine components (like ignition coils and turbochargers), and the intricate electronic systems. Transmission control module and torque converter issues are also noted. Electrical components, especially within the infotainment and sensor arrays, can require attention. Owners should budget for potential repairs, as the cost of parts and labor for these specialized systems can be significant. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining the reliability of this exceptional vehicle.
