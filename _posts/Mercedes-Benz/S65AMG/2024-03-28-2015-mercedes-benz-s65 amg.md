**Introduction:**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a twin-turbocharged V12 engine. It produces 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The S65 AMG is known for its opulent interior, advanced technology, and impressive performance. However, some owners have reported specific issues with their vehicles.

**Engine**

**M279 Twin-Turbocharged V12 Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, engine noise, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace turbochargers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Visible oil leaks from engine seals or gaskets
    * Solution: Replace or repair seals or gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Carbon Buildup:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced performance, heavy engine deposits
    * Solution: Chemical cleaning or intake manifold replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic Transmission:**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifts, transmission slipping
    * Solution: Replace or repair transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or more corners of the vehicle dropping or becoming unstable
    * Solution: Replace or repair air suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge when the vehicle is off
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Module Failures:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning electronics, error messages
    * Solution: Replace or repair electrical modules
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Chrome Trim Peeling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Chrome trim on the grille, door handles, or other areas peeling or fading
    * Solution: Replace or repair chrome trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Brake Rotor Warping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations or noise when braking
    * Solution: Replace warped brake rotors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Multi-Contour Seat Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat adjustments not working properly
    * Solution: Replace or repair multi-contour seat components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Command System Lag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slow or unresponsive touchscreen
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of Command system components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz S65 AMG is a highly capable vehicle, but it is not without its potential issues. The engine and transmission components can be prone to failures, while the electrical system and suspension can also experience problems. Some exterior and interior features may also require attention. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost of repairs can be substantial. Owners should be prepared for potential maintenance and repair expenses.
