**Introduction:**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter is a popular full-size van known for its spacious interior, powerful engine options, and advanced safety features. Engine options include a 2.1L inline-4 turbo diesel, a 3.0L V6 turbo diesel, and a 2.0L inline-4 turbocharged gasoline engine. Transmission options include a 6-speed manual, a 7-speed automatic, and a 9-speed automatic.

While the Sprinter has many positive attributes, it has also had its share of issues, particularly with its electrical system and engine components.

### **Engine**

**2.1L Inline-4 Turbo Diesel**

* **Fuel Injector Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, engine misfires, loss of power
    * Solution: Replacement of faulty fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **EGR Valve Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Increased emissions, decreased engine performance
    * Solution: Replacement of EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke
    * Solution: Replacement of turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L V6 Turbo Diesel**

* **Timing Chain Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or ticking sound from engine, loss of power
    * Solution: Replacement of timing chain and tensioners
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: Mercedes-Benz Recall #SB-210302
* **Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Stalling, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replacement of crankshaft position sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0L Inline-4 Turbocharged Gasoline**

* **Fuel Pump Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine hesitation, stalling
    * Solution: Replacement of fuel pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cylinder Head Warping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating
    * Solution: Replacement of cylinder head
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Shift Linkage Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Replacement of shift linkage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Clutch Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears
    * Solution: Replacement of clutch disc and pressure plate
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, transmission slippage
    * Solution: Replacement of torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: Mercedes-Benz Recall #SB-210401
* **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifts erratically, fails to engage gears
    * Solution: Replacement of mechatronic unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **Software Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitations, rough shifts
    * Solution: Software updates
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200
    * Recalls: Mercedes-Benz Recall #SB-210501

### **Suspension**

* **Front Stabilizer Bar Link Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise from front suspension, instability
    * Solution: Replacement of stabilizer bar links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, excessive vibration
    * Solution: Replacement of leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery repeatedly goes dead
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical system malfunctions
    * Solution: Replacement of alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: Mercedes-Benz Recall #SB-210601
* **Starter Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, grinding noise
    * Solution: Replacement of starter motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Exterior**

* **Tailgate Handle Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing tailgate
    * Solution: Replacement of tailgate handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Side Door Hinge Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing side door
    * Solution: Replacement of door hinges
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Interior**

* **Climate Control Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC or heat not working
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of climate control system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or more windows fail to operate
    * Solution: Replacement of power window motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2015 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter has a wide range of reported issues, particularly with its electrical system and engine components. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be major and costly. Owners should be aware of these potential problems and factor them into their purchase decision.
