**Introduction:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter is a full-size van that offers a wide range of configurations and options. It is powered by a 3.0L V6 diesel engine that produces 188 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque. The Sprinter is available with a 7-speed or 9-speed automatic transmission.

People like the Sprinter for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and powerful engine. However, there have been some reports of issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6 Diesel**

* **Diesel Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The fuel injector may fail, causing the engine to run poorly or lose power.
    * Solution: Replace the fuel injector.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger may fail, causing a loss of power and increased exhaust smoke.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may leak fluid, causing a loss of power and increased noise.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid and gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front suspension strut may fail, causing a clunking noise and a loss of ride quality.
    * Solution: Replace the suspension strut.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension Spring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension spring may fail, causing the vehicle to sag and lose stability.
    * Solution: Replace the suspension spring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may discharge prematurely, causing the vehicle to fail to start.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or diagnose and repair the cause of the discharge.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The electrical system may malfunction, causing various electrical issues such as flickering lights, power window failure, or dashboard warning lights.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door locks may fail to operate, causing the doors to lock or unlock unexpectedly.
    * Solution: Replace the door lock actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Liftgate Hinge Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The liftgate hinge may fail, causing the liftgate to sag or close improperly.
    * Solution: Replace the liftgate hinge.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may crack or warp due to excessive heat or sunlight.
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, causing the interior temperature to be uncomfortable.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter is a spacious and powerful van, but it has been reported to have some issues, including engine problems, transmission issues, and electrical malfunctions. Some of these issues can be expensive to fix, so it is important to consider these potential costs when purchasing a Sprinter.
