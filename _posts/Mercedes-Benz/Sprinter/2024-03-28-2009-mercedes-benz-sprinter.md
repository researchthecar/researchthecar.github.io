**Introduction:**

The 2009 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter is a versatile commercial van that offers impressive performance and a spacious cabin. It is available with a range of engine and transmission options, including:

* **Engine:**
    * 2.1L 4-cylinder diesel engine (154 hp, 280 lb-ft of torque)
    * 3.0L V6 diesel engine (188 hp, 325 lb-ft of torque)
* **Transmission:**
    * 5-speed manual transmission
    * 7-speed automatic transmission

**Engine**

**2.1L 4-Cylinder Diesel Engine**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, rough idling, black smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Injector replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced power, blue or black smoke from exhaust, whining or whistling sound
    * Solution: Turbocharger replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2500 - $4000+

**3.0L V6 Diesel Engine**

* **Emission Control Valve Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Illuminated check engine light, reduced fuel efficiency, poor acceleration
    * Solution: EGR valve replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
* **Diesel Particulate Filter Clogging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, reduced fuel efficiency, warning light on dashboard
    * Solution: DPF regeneration or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2500+
* **Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No start, intermittent stalling, rough idling
    * Solution: Crankshaft position sensor replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears, spongy clutch pedal
    * Solution: Clutch master cylinder replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
* **Synchronizer Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Grating or grinding noise when shifting gears, difficulty engaging specific gears
    * Solution: Synchronizer ring replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+

**7-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter Lockup Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering when accelerating or decelerating, loss of power
    * Solution: Torque converter replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $3000+
* **Transmission Control Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, difficulty shifting gears, illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: TCM repair or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+

**Suspension**

* **Front Shock Absorber Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride comfort, uneven tire wear, excessive bouncing
    * Solution: Shock absorber replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, poor handling, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Leaf spring replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
* **Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or squeaking noise when turning or going over bumps, reduced steering precision
    * Solution: Control arm bushing replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Battery replacement, diagnosis and repair of electrical draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, dead battery, charging system warning light on
    * Solution: Alternator replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
* **Wiring Harness Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunctions (e.g., lights flickering, power windows not working)
    * Solution: Wiring harness inspection and repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500+

**Exterior**

* **Body Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots on body panels, especially undercarriage and wheel wells
    * Solution: Rust repair, undercoating
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on extent of rust

**Interior**

* **HVAC Control Panel Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to adjust temperature, fan speed, or mode
    * Solution: HVAC control panel replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on the dashboard, especially in areas exposed to sunlight
    * Solution: Dashboard replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000+

**Summary**

The 2009 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter is a capable and spacious commercial van, but it comes with certain reliability concerns. The most common issues affect the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. These issues can vary in severity and cost to fix, ranging from relatively minor (e.g., battery discharge) to major (e.g., transmission failure). Overall, potential buyers should be aware of the potential maintenance costs associated with this vehicle and be prepared to address any issues that may arise.
