**Introduction:**

The 2007 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter is a versatile commercial vehicle offered in a range of configurations, including cargo vans, passenger vans, and chassis cabs. It comes with three engine options: NCV3 2.2L diesel, NCV3 3.0L V6 diesel, and OM642 6.0L V8 diesel. Transmission options include a 5-speed automatic, 6-speed manual, and 7-speed automatic. The Sprinter is known for its spacious interior, powerful engines, and reliable performance. However, certain model years have been plagued by various issues.

**Engine**

**NCV3 2.2L Diesel**

* **Leaking Injector Seals:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfire, rough idle, poor fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace injector seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noise
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**NCV3 3.0L V6 Diesel**

* **Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Valve Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine performance issues, decreased fuel efficiency
    * Solution: Clean or replace EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Clogging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Clean or replace DPF
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**OM642 6.0L V8 Diesel**

* **Timing Chain Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattle, loss of power, no start condition
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and related components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, shuddering, overheating
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Gearbox Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise while shifting or driving
    * Solution: Replace gears or bearings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Erratic shifting, transmission slipping, no power
    * Solution: Replace TCM
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings Worn:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise when turning or braking
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery warning light, electrical accessories not working
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine cranks but does not start
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior (excluding paint issues)**

* **Tailgate Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust or corrosion on the tailgate
    * Solution: Repair or replace tailgate
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior (excluding leather seat issues)**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on the dashboard surface
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC Fan Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No airflow from heating or air conditioning
    * Solution: Replace fan motor or resistor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2007 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter has been plagued by a range of issues affecting its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior. Some of these issues, particularly engine and transmission problems, can be severe and costly to repair. However, it's important to note that not all Sprinters experience these issues, and many owners report high levels of satisfaction with their vehicles. Potential buyers should carefully consider the potential risks and costs associated with these problems before purchasing a 2007 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter.
