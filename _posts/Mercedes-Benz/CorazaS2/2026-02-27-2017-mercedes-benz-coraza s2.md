# 2017 Mercedes-Benz Coraza S2

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S-Class (W222 generation) is renowned for its luxury, advanced technology, and overall comfort. As a flagship sedan, it generally exhibits strong build quality and reliability, particularly in its core mechanical components. However, like any complex luxury vehicle, it is not immune to potential issues, especially concerning its sophisticated electronic systems and specialized powertrain options. Owners can expect a premium ownership experience, but awareness of potential problem areas, particularly those related to advanced features and specific engine variants, is advisable for proactive maintenance and cost management.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Biturbo V6 (S550e Plug-in Hybrid)
**Hybrid System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced electric range, charging issues, warning lights related to hybrid battery or electric motor.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of hybrid battery pack, electric motor, or associated control modules.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $5,000+

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement or repair of turbocharger assembly and associated plumbing.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


### 4.7L Biturbo V8 (S550)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils and spark plugs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking gaskets (e.g., valve cover, oil pan).
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (7G-TRONIC)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from transmission pan or seals, rough shifting, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of transmission pan gasket, seals, or torque converter.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200

**Solenoid Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty transmission solenoids.

## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, bouncy ride, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air struts or air springs.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant compressor operation, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, displays errors, or freezes.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the infotainment head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, car won't start, warning lights on dash.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800


### LED Lighting
**Headlight/Taillight Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Individual LEDs failing, flickering, or entire units not working.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty LED modules or control units.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,000 per unit

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S-Class is a highly reliable luxury sedan, with most owners reporting a positive experience. The primary areas to monitor are the complex electrical systems, particularly the infotainment and advanced driver-assistance features, and the AIRMATIC air suspension, which can develop leaks over time. Powertrain reliability is generally strong, though specific issues like turbocharger problems on the V8 and hybrid system components on the S550e can arise. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are crucial for maintaining the S-Class's optimal performance and longevity.
