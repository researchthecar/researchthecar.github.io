## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or associated seals. Compressor may need replacement if overworked.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500 per corner

**AIRMATIC Control Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inconsistent ride height adjustment, warning lights on the dashboard, suspension becoming stiff or overly soft.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the AIRMATIC control module.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800
