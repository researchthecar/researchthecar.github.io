**Introduction**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz Coraza S2 is a high-performance sports car that offers a thrilling driving experience. It boasts a powerful engine and impressive handling, but it has also been reported to have some issues. Engine options include a 4.0L V8 biturbo with 603 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque and a 6.3L V12 biturbo with 1,200 horsepower and 1,180 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 7-speed automatic and a 9-speed automatic.

**Engine**

* **4.0L V8 biturbo**
    * **Turbocharger failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased exhaust smoke, abnormal engine noises
        * Solution: Replace turbocharger
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **6.3L V12 biturbo**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **7-speed automatic**
    * **Transmission shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shaking when shifting gears
        * Solution: Replace torque converter or transmission fluid flush
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **9-speed automatic**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery runs out prematurely
    * Solution: Replace battery or check for parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Cracked windshield**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks or chips in the windshield
    * Solution: Replace windshield
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezing, slow response, software bugs
    * Solution: Update software or replace infotainment unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate control malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating not working properly
    * Solution: Replace climate control module or check for refrigerant leaks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz Coraza S2 is a powerful and impressive sports car, but it does have some issues that can be costly to fix. The most common issues include turbocharger failure on the 4.0L V8 engine, transmission shudder on the 7-speed automatic transmission, and infotainment system glitches. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific part that fails.
