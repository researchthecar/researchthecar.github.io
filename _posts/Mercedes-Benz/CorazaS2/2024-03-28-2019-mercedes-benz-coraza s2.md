**Introduction**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Coraza S2 is a luxury sports coupe that offers a powerful engine and a sophisticated driving experience. Equipped with a 5.5-liter V8 engine paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission, it produces impressive horsepower and torque figures. Loved for its sleek styling, high-performance capabilities, and technologically advanced interior, the Coraza S2 has garnered a loyal following. Despite its exceptional qualities, the vehicle has faced some reported issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

**5.5-liter V8**

* **Valve Spring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear of valve springs can lead to engine misfires, rough idle, and reduced power.
    * Solution: Replacement of affected valve springs and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: A faulty timing chain tensioner can cause excessive chain slack, leading to abnormal engine noises, reduced performance, and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and possibly other related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Delays and Hesitations**
    * Description/Symptoms: Drivers may experience delayed or hesitant gear shifts, especially during acceleration or deceleration.
    * Solution: Software updates or reprogramming of the transmission control module. In some cases, hardware replacement may be necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 20V439000

**Suspension**

* **Rear Air Suspension Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: A leak in the rear air suspension system can lead to a loss of ride height, reduced handling, and a bumpy ride.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of the affected air suspension component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Front Control Arm Bushing Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn bushings in the front control arms can cause alignment issues, uneven tire wear, and reduced handling stability.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
* **Creaking Noises from Rear Suspension**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive creaking or groaning noises coming from the rear suspension may indicate worn or damaged components such as sway bar bushings or links.
    * Solution: Inspection and replacement of the affected suspension components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+

**Electrical**

* **Headlight Moisture Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlights can reduce visibility and increase the risk of bulb failure.
    * Solution: Replacement of affected headlights or repair of condensation entry points.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: A weak or prematurely discharged battery may prevent the vehicle from starting or lead to electrical system malfunctions.
    * Solution: Battery replacement and inspection for potential parasitic electrical drains.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Exterior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: A rattling noise coming from the panoramic sunroof while driving can be caused by loose components or misaligned tracks.
    * Solution: Inspection, adjustment, or replacement of the affected sunroof components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Cracked Windshield**
    * Description/Symptoms: The windshield may develop cracks due to impact from road debris or other external factors.
    * Solution: Replacement of the windshield.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may fail to regulate temperature or airflow, causing discomfort for passengers.
    * Solution: Inspection and repair or replacement of faulty components within the climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Infotainment System Lag**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience occasional lag or slow response times, affecting its functionality and usability.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of affected infotainment components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Summary**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Coraza S2 offers an exhilarating driving experience with its powerful engine and sophisticated features. However, it has faced some notable issues that owners should consider before purchasing. The potential severity and cost of these issues can vary, ranging from minor inconvenience to more significant repairs. While not all Coraza S2 models will experience these issues, it is important for prospective buyers to be aware of the reported problems to make an informed decision.
