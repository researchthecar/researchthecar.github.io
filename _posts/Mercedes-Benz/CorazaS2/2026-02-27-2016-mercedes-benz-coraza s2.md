# 2016 Mercedes-Benz Coraza S2

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S-Class (W222 generation) is renowned for its luxury, advanced technology, and overall comfort. As a flagship sedan, it generally exhibits strong build quality and reliability, especially considering its complex systems. However, like any high-end vehicle with sophisticated electronics and powerful powertrains, certain areas can experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for owners and prospective buyers of the 2016 model year. We will examine common issues reported across its various engine and transmission configurations, as well as other key vehicle systems.

---


## Engine

### 4.7L V8 Biturbo (S550)
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, unusual noises from the engine bay, exhaust smoke.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,500

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light illumination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### 6.0L V12 Biturbo (S65 AMG)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, overheating, sweet smell from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500

## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, 'Service Air Suspension' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air struts or air springs.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, 'Service Air Suspension' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Screen Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, displays incorrect information, or freezes.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,500


### Lighting Systems
**LED Headlight Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent or complete failure of LED headlights, dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty LED modules or control units.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,500 per headlight


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, difficulty starting the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or circuits causing excessive power draw.
- **Cost:** $300 - $900

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S-Class is a highly sophisticated luxury sedan that generally offers a reliable ownership experience. However, its complexity means that certain components, particularly within the advanced AIRMATIC suspension and the intricate electrical systems, can be prone to issues. Owners may encounter problems with air struts, compressors, infotainment glitches, and LED lighting. Engine and transmission reliability are generally strong, though specific issues like turbocharger problems on the V8 models and potential leaks on the V12 can arise. Proactive maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining the S-Class's optimal performance and longevity.
