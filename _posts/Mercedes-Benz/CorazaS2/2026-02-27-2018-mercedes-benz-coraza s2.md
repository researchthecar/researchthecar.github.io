## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software update or system reset. In some cases, head unit replacement may be required.
- **Cost:** $150 - $1200


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, battery warning lights, rapid battery discharge.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from modules or accessories, followed by repair or replacement of faulty component.
- **Cost:** $300 - $900

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz Coraza S2 demonstrates a commendable level of technical reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components (engines and transmissions), which are generally robust.
