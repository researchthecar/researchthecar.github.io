# 2004 Mercedes-Benz C32 AMG

### Overview
The 2004 Mercedes-Benz C32 AMG, a high-performance variant of the W203 C-Class, is known for its potent supercharged V6 engine and sporty driving dynamics. While generally well-regarded for its performance, like any vehicle of this age and performance level, certain areas warrant attention regarding long-term reliability. This analysis focuses on documented common issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential repair costs and areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers. The C32 AMG offers a compelling blend of luxury and power, but understanding its specific reliability profile is crucial for maintaining its performance and value.

---


## Engine

### 3.2L Supercharged V6 (M112 E32 ML)
**Supercharger Belt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, squealing noise from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the supercharger belt and potentially idler/tensioner pulleys.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

**Coolant Leaks (Intercooler System)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant leaks around the intercooler or associated hoses.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or the intercooler itself.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Burning oil smell, visible oil on the engine block, potential misfires if severe.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission not engaging gears, 'limp mode'.
- **Solution:** Replacement or repair of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Solenoid Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of transmission solenoids or a full transmission service.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200


## Suspension

### Standard AMG Sport Suspension
**Worn Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of front control arm bushings or complete control arms.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Leaking Struts/Shocks**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, reduced handling, visible fluid leaks from suspension components.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn struts or shocks.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1000


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, window stuck in position.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

**Instrument Cluster Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Flickering gauges, dead pixels, warning lights staying on.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000

**CAN Bus Communication Errors**
- **Symptoms:** Various warning lights, erratic behavior of multiple electronic systems.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or wiring.
- **Cost:** $300 - $900


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Trim
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Cost:** N/A


## Interior

### Cabin Electronics and Upholstery
**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250

**Seat Heater Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Seat heaters not working.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seat heating elements or switches.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2004 Mercedes-Benz C32 AMG is a capable performance sedan, but its advanced supercharged engine and complex electronics present potential reliability challenges typical for a vehicle of its age and performance focus. Key areas to monitor include the supercharger belt, intercooler system for leaks, and the 5-speed automatic transmission's solenoids and control module. Electrical gremlins, particularly with window regulators and the instrument cluster, are also noted. While the exterior and interior are generally robust, specific component failures can occur. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to early signs of trouble are essential for maintaining this AMG model's performance and longevity.
