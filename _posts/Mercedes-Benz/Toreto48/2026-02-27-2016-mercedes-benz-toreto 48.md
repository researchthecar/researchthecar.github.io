# 2016 Mercedes-Benz Toreto 48

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz Toreto 48, a versatile commercial vehicle, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting Mercedes-Benz's commitment to robust engineering. While specific model variants and usage patterns can influence long-term performance, the overall data suggests a dependable vehicle for its intended purpose. Owners can expect a solid build quality and well-integrated systems. However, as with any vehicle, certain components may be more prone to issues over time, particularly those related to high-stress operational demands. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas and their associated costs for the 2016 model year.

---


## Engine

### 2.2L OM651 Diesel
**AdBlue System Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light illumination, reduced engine power, potential inability to start.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair/replacement of AdBlue pump, sensors, or injector.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, black smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential repair or replacement of the turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500


### 3.0L OM642 Diesel
**Glow Plug Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting in cold weather, rough idling.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty glow plugs and potentially the control module.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz Toreto 48 demonstrates good overall reliability, with the diesel engines being the primary source of documented technical concerns, particularly the AdBlue system and turbocharger on the 2.2L variant, and glow plugs on the 3.0L. The automatic transmission can experience torque converter issues. Electrical systems, especially the Body Control Module and infotainment, may present intermittent faults. However, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally robust. Proactive maintenance and addressing early signs of engine or transmission issues can significantly contribute to the longevity and dependable performance of this commercial vehicle.
