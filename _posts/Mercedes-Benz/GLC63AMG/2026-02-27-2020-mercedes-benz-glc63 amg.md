# 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG

### Overview
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG, a high-performance variant of the popular GLC-Class SUV, combines potent AMG engineering with everyday practicality. Powered by a hand-built AMG 4.0L V8 biturbo engine, it delivers exhilarating acceleration and a dynamic driving experience. While generally well-regarded for its performance and luxury, like any performance vehicle, it can encounter specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### AMG 4.0L V8 Biturbo (M177)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, check engine light illumination, potential boost pressure issues.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty wastegate actuator or the complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Oil Pan)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level warnings.
- **Solution:** Replacement of gaskets and seals, or repair of the oil pan.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Transmission

### AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 9-Speed Transmission
**Harsh Shifting / Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable clunks or hesitations during gear changes, particularly at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Software update for transmission control module, or in more severe cases, internal transmission repairs or replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2500+

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from the transmission pan or seals, potential for slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals, gaskets, or transmission pan.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Suspension

### AMG Sport Suspension
**Air Suspension Leaks (if equipped)**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of air springs, lines, or compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1800


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, freezes, or restarts randomly.
- **Solution:** Software updates, or replacement of the head unit or related modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, warning lights related to battery or electrical system.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of battery, or faulty module.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG is a formidable performance SUV with a generally robust mechanical foundation. The primary areas of concern tend to revolve around the high-performance powertrain, specifically potential issues with turbocharger wastegates and occasional transmission shifting anomalies. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and battery drain, are also noted. While these issues can be costly to repair, they are not universally experienced. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining the reliability of this potent AMG model.
