# 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG delivers high-performance luxury, combining robust V8 twin-turbo power with sharp handling. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints regarding powertrain irregularities, electrical glitches, and body components before purchasing a used model.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8

The 4.0-liter biturbo V8 engine produces exceptional horsepower and torque, providing aggressive acceleration and dynamic performance. While generally robust, owners have occasionally reported unexpected stalling and electrical sensor faults.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report unexpected engine stalling while operating the vehicle, accompanied by warning lights and diagnostic trouble codes.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a qualified technician to check fault codes, inspect fuel and ignition systems, and replace failing sensors.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 9-Speed Automatic

The AMG Speedshift MCT 9-speed automatic transmission is paired with all-wheel drive, offering rapid gear changes suited for high-performance driving. Owners should verify smooth shifting behavior during a pre-purchase test drive.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly shift or get stuck in neutral while attempting to drive, interrupting power delivery to the wheels.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission control module software, shift linkages, and internal valve bodies for wear or electronic failure.

## Steering

### Steering and Front Axle

Provides precise steering feedback typical of AMG performance vehicles, though aggressive steering geometry can lead to low-speed tire skipping in cold conditions.

**Tire Skipping and Binding ('Crabbing')**
- **Symptoms:** Low-speed tight turns can cause a juddering, knocking, or skipping sensation from the front wheels as the tires drag across the pavement.
- **Solution:** Inspect front suspension geometry, steering components, and alignment specifications; adjusting tire compounds for colder weather may reduce the sensation.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Powers advanced infotainment, driver assistance, and performance systems, though occasional complex electrical glitches have been documented by owners.

**Sudden Loss of Power and Key Fob Errors**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may receive false 'Key Fob not Detected' warnings accompanied by a sudden loss of motive power while driving.
- **Solution:** Check key fob batteries, antenna modules, and powertrain control unit software for faults or corrosion.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Enhances cabin light and luxury appeal, though a subset of owners have reported issues with spontaneous glass breakage.

**Spontaneous Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass may suddenly crack, shatter, or separate from the vehicle frame while driving at highway speeds without prior impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the sunroof glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame seals for structural stress.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG is an exhilarating high-performance crossover, but its sophisticated powertrain and electronics require careful evaluation. Buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the transmission, front suspension geometry, and electrical systems to ensure trouble-free ownership.
