# 2024 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 offers luxury shoppers a refined midsize SUV experience equipped with modern technology and composed road manners. While newer model years generally benefit from ongoing refinement, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback and inspect vehicle condition thoroughly during a used car test drive.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily driving. It pairs with a 48-volt mild-hybrid system to smooth out start-stop operations.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to either the rear wheels or 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system. It generally delivers refined gear changes suited to luxury driving dynamics.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V878000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE350&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An inadequate seal can allow fuel and oil to leak onto the road, creating a hazard for other road users and increasing the risk of a crash. Additionally, a fuel and oil leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high-pressure fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V117000: Vehicle Speed Control:Accelerator Pedal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE350&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A broken or detached accelerator pedal can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will drill through the spot welds and secure the bracket with rivets, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 is a sophisticated luxury SUV featuring comfortable accommodations and modern powertrain options. Used-car buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verify service history to ensure optimal long-term reliability.
