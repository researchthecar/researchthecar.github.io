# 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 offers buyers a refined luxury midsize SUV experience with sophisticated interior tech and strong brand prestige. When shopping for a used model, understanding how its powertrain options and standard equipment perform over time is essential for a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate daily power and decent efficiency for the luxury SUV segment, though buyers should verify routine maintenance history.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission is paired with rear-wheel drive or 4MATIC all-wheel drive, offering smooth and responsive gear shifts. It generally delivers reliable performance when properly maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V671000: Seat Belts: Rear/Other:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE350&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** While buckling the seat belt, the middle seat belt buckle could be pushed down into the gap below the seat cushion. An unrestrained occupant has an increased risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will secure the middle seat belt buckle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V832000: Visibility:Glass, Side/Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE350&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rear window may be insufficiently bonded to the tailgate, which could allow the rear glass to detach. Detached rear glass can become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rear window, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V936000: Steering:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE350&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A loss of steering control can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering coupling locking screw, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V835000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE350&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A 12-Volt ground connection under the driver seat may not be secured properly, resulting in the ground connection overheating. An overheated ground connection can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and secure the 12-Volt ground connection as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 stands as a well-appointed luxury choice for used-car buyers seeking comfort and advanced features. Prospective owners should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verify complete service records to ensure long-term satisfaction.
