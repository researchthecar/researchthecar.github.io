# 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE350

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE350, a mid-size luxury SUV, generally offers a solid ownership experience with a focus on comfort and refinement. While it shares many components with its predecessor, the M-Class, it introduced some updates for the model year. Reliability for this model year is largely consistent with Mercedes-Benz's reputation for premium vehicles, though like any complex machine, certain areas can experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented common problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers. The GLE350 is known for its robust powertrain and comfortable ride, making it a popular choice in its segment.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (Naturally Aspirated)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant level warning, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or the water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of leaking gaskets (e.g., valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket) or seals.
- **Cost:** $250 - $600


## Transmission

### 7G-TRONIC PLUS (7-Speed Automatic)
**Transmission Shudder/Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Jerking or shuddering during gear changes, delayed engagement, rough shifting.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter change, software update, or potential internal transmission repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1500


## Suspension

### Standard Coil Spring Suspension
**Air Suspension Leaks (if equipped with AIRMATIC)**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, uneven ride height, 'airmatic suspension malfunction' warning.
- **Solution:** Leak detection and repair of air springs, lines, or compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1800


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, audio cutting out, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard reset, or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Discharge**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, battery warning light, electrical components failing intermittently.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty modules or battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 is a generally reliable luxury SUV, with its 3.5L V6 engine and 7-speed automatic transmission proving to be robust. The most commonly reported issues tend to be related to the electrical system, particularly infotainment glitches and potential battery drain, which can be addressed with software updates or component diagnosis. Some owners have reported coolant or oil leaks, which are typical for higher-mileage vehicles but usually manageable with timely repairs. If equipped with the AIRMATIC air suspension, leaks can occur, leading to ride height issues. Overall, the GLE350 offers a good balance of luxury and dependability, provided routine maintenance is performed and potential electrical gremlins are addressed promptly.
