# 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury midsize SUV, the 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 offers a comfortable cabin and refined road manners, but prospective buyers should review its mechanical and electrical reliability track record carefully. While it provides strong luxury appeal, owners have reported several recurring issues ranging from steering assistance failures to fuel system concerns and electronic glitches. Taking time to inspect service records and verify prior repairs can help avoid unexpected maintenance expenses down the road.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (M276)

The 3.5-liter naturally aspirated V6 engine produces roughly 302 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth daily performance. Overall, the powertrain is generally robust, though owners should monitor for potential fuel system and injector issues as the vehicle ages.

**Fuel Injector Leaks and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Fuel leaks around the intake manifold or fuel injectors can lead to engine stalling, loss of motive power, or hesitation while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines and injectors for leaks; replace faulty fuel injectors and seals as necessary.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (7G-Tronic Plus)

The 7-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes across rear-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. While mechanically durable, electronic shifting anomalies can occasionally surface.

**Unintended Neutral or Park Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly display gear selection warnings or independently shift into neutral or park while slowing or maneuvering.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scan of the electronic gear selector module and transmission control unit; software updates or hardware replacement may be required.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

**Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a sudden loss of power steering assistance accompanied by a dashboard warning message, making the steering wheel exceptionally heavy to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electric power steering system, associated wiring harnesses, and control modules for faults.

### Steering Box

The steering system provides responsive handling typical of a German luxury SUV, but power steering assist failures require prompt attention when they occur.

**Abnormal Steering Noises**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice unusual grinding or mechanical noises originating from the steering box area while turning at various speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering linkage, gear box, and mounting hardware for wear or damage.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical accessories and lighting systems generally perform well, though minor component failures and lighting complaints have been noted by some owners.

**Dim Headlights**
- **Symptoms:** Headlight output may seem inadequate or overly dim when driving on unlit roads at night, reducing forward visibility.
- **Solution:** Check headlight bulb condition and projector lens alignment; upgrade or replace worn lighting components if necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V955000: Fuel System, Diesel:Delivery:Fuel Pump:Control/Drive Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE350&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Water might accumulate in the spare tire wheel well and damage the fuel pump control unit, which could cause an engine stall while driving. An engine stall while driving increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a water drain plug, inspect for water intrusion, and replace the fuel pump control unit as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V787000: Communication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE350&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** For a full list of the affected models visit: https://static.nhtsa.gov/odi/rcl/2019/RMISC-19V787-4620.pdf An inaccurate vehicle location may delay emergency responders, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will also notify owners, and dealers will check software for a successful OTA or update the communication module software as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 is a well-appointed luxury SUV that balances comfort and everyday utility. While its engine and transmission offer refined performance, buyers should pay close attention to potential fuel system concerns, power steering assist failures, and electronic shifting glitches. Securing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and reviewing detailed service history can help ensure a more confident ownership experience.
