# 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLE-Class offers luxury, advanced technology, and refined styling, making it a compelling choice for luxury midsize SUV shoppers. However, prospective buyers should review owner complaints regarding various mechanical and electrical systems to understand potential ownership costs and common trouble areas.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (GLE 350)

A 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing around 255 horsepower, providing standard power for entry-level trims. It balances decent fuel economy with adequate acceleration, though buyers should watch for reported driveability glitches.

**Engine Stalling and Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report unexpected engine hesitation, loss of acceleration while driving, and eventual engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic testing by a qualified technician to check for fuel delivery, software, or ignition faults.

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant warnings and radiator leaks leading to overheating risks while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect radiator hoses and cooling components; replace damaged parts and refill coolant.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 / Mild Hybrid (GLE 450)

A 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 featuring EQ Boost mild-hybrid technology, generating roughly 362 horsepower for smooth, robust performance.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 9-speed automatic transmission paired across most models, designed to deliver seamless gear shifts and highway cruising efficiency.

## Suspension

### Tires and Wheels

Factory run-flat tires and suspension geometry have prompted owner complaints regarding accelerated wear.

**Rapid Tire Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rear or front tires wearing down prematurely or going bald within low mileage intervals.
- **Solution:** Perform regular wheel alignments, tire rotations, and replace worn tires as needed.
- **Cost:** $950

## Electrical

### Electrical and Climate System

Cabin electronics and climate control plumbing require inspection to prevent potential moisture-related issues.

**Water Intrusion from AC Vents**
- **Symptoms:** Cabin water leaks originating from air conditioning drain or vent lines.
- **Solution:** Clear and properly reseal AC condensation drain lines to prevent interior water accumulation.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior glass components, particularly large panoramic roofs, have experienced isolated incidents of spontaneous shattering.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof or windshield glass panel spontaneously cracks or shatters while driving under normal conditions.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the frame for structural stress.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V936000: Steering:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE350&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of steering control can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering coupling locking screw, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V178000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE350&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Detached trim bars may cause a road hazard and increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and secure the rear window trim bars, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V835000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE350&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A 12-Volt ground connection under the driver seat may not be secured properly, resulting in the ground connection overheating. An overheated ground connection can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and secure the 12-Volt ground connection as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLE-Class is a luxurious and capable midsize SUV, but used car buyers should inspect mechanical service histories closely. Pay special attention to tire wear patterns, engine performance, and cabin glass integrity during a pre-purchase inspection.
