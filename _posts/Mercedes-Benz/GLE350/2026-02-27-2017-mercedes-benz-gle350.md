# 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 combines luxury styling and comfortable daily driving, but used car buyers should carefully review owner-reported reliability trends. While mechanical foundations are generally solid, various electrical, steering, and body integrity issues have surfaced in owner feedback. Prospective buyers are encouraged to inspect critical systems thoroughly and verify service histories prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 302 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth and confident acceleration for everyday driving. Overall, this powertrain is regarded as durable, provided routine maintenance intervals are strictly followed.

**Engine Stalling and Fuel System Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected engine stalling, hesitation, or fuel delivery issues that can leave the vehicle immobilized.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the fuel pump, fuel system components, and diagnostic codes.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with the standard V6 engine, the 7-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear shifts across rear-wheel and 4MATIC all-wheel-drive configurations. Regular fluid service is recommended to maintain optimal shift quality and longevity.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake system feedback indicates occasional operational inconsistencies that require prompt professional inspection.

**Brake System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted unexpected vehicle movement while the hold brake feature is engaged or concerns regarding brake responsiveness.
- **Solution:** Have the braking system, brake booster, and associated electronic controls inspected by a professional technician.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering

Issues related to electronic power assist failure can compromise vehicle maneuverability without prior warning.

**Power Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a sudden loss of power steering assistance accompanied by a dashboard warning message, making the steering wheel extremely stiff and difficult to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning of the electronic steering module and electrical connections is required; faulty components or sensors may need replacement.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Exterior lighting complaints primarily center on illumination effectiveness and adjustment concerns during night driving.

**Headlight Performance and Adjustment Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report inadequate illumination from standard headlights during nighttime driving or difficulties with low beam headlight adjustment.
- **Solution:** Check bulb condition, alignment mechanisms, and headlight assemblies; upgraded bulbs or housing adjustments may improve visibility.

### Sunroof and Glass

Exterior body complaints highlight rare but severe incidents involving panoramic sunroof glass integrity.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass can suddenly fracture or explode while driving at highway speeds, creating a loud noise and scattering glass inside the cabin.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass and frame assembly for stress points; replacement of the glass panel is required if damaged.

## Safety

### Seat Belts and Restraints

Safety system grievances primarily involve sensor-related warnings within the occupant restraint monitoring network.

**Seat Belt Warning Light and Sensor Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated seat belt warning lights or alarm activations can occur intermittently due to faulty seat sensors.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the seat belt buckle sensor and replace faulty components or wiring harnesses as needed.
