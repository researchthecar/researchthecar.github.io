**Introduction:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 is a mid-size luxury SUV that offers a smooth and comfortable ride. It has a powerful V6 engine that produces 302 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The GLE350 is praised for its spacious interior, luxurious features, and advanced safety systems. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of issues.

**Engine**

* **V6 Engine:**
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, rough idling, reduced power output
        * Solution: Replace faulty fuel injectors
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Wastegate Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, black smoke from exhaust, turbocharger whining noise
    * Solution: Replace faulty wastegate
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **9-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Shift Hesitation or Jerking:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitation or jerking when shifting
        * Solution: Software update, transmission fluid replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension System Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Suspension becomes stiff or bouncy, uneven ride height
    * Solution: Replace faulty air compressor or struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when not in use
    * Solution: Identify and replace faulty electrical components (e.g., parasitic drain)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Control Unit (ECU) Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Various electrical system malfunctions (e.g., lighting, climate control)
    * Solution: Replace faulty ECU
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation or Water Ingress:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water or condensation inside the headlight assemblies
    * Solution: Replace or reseal headlight assemblies
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks through the sunroof during rainfall
    * Solution: Inspect and reseal sunroof seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Recliner Cable Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Driver or passenger seat recliner does not adjust smoothly or becomes inoperable
    * Solution: Replace faulty recliner cable
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 is a luxurious and capable SUV, it does have some potential issues. Engine problems, such as fuel injector failure and turbocharger issues, can be costly to fix. The air suspension system and electrical components can also experience problems that can lead to significant expenses. Overall, the severity and repair costs of the reported issues vary, but it is important for potential buyers to be aware of them before making a purchase decision.
