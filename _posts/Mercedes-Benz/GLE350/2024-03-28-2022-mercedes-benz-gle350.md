**Introduction:**

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 is a luxury midsize SUV powered by a turbocharged 2.0L inline-four engine that produces 255 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The GLE350 comes standard with all-wheel drive and offers a smooth and responsive ride, with a spacious and well-appointed interior. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of reported issues.

**Engine**

**2.0L Inline-Four**

* **Hesitation on acceleration:** Some owners report that the GLE350 hesitates or stumbles during acceleration, especially at low speeds.
* **Solution:** Replacing the spark plugs and ignition coils has been found to resolve this issue.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **Jerky or delayed shifts:** Owners have reported experiencing jerky or delayed shifts, particularly when shifting from Park to Drive or Reverse.
* **Solution:** Updating the transmission control module (TCM) software has been reported to improve shift performance.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $300+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Malfunctioning infotainment system:** Some owners have experienced issues with the infotainment system, including touchscreen freezes and audio glitches.
* **Solution:** Updating the software or replacing the infotainment unit may be required.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield cracking:** A few owners have reported their windshields cracking without any obvious impact or damage.
* **Solution:** Replacing the windshield is the only solution.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Passenger seat sensor malfunction:** The passenger seat sensor may fail to detect a passenger, resulting in the airbag warning light illuminating.
* **Solution:** Replacing the seat sensor is necessary to fix this issue.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 is a capable and refined SUV. However, like any vehicle, it has experienced some reported issues, mainly related to the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. While some of these issues can be expensive to repair, most are not particularly severe and can be addressed with proper maintenance and repairs. It's important to note that these issues do not necessarily affect every GLE350, and many owners have reported a positive ownership experience.
