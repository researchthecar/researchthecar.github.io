# 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 combines luxury and versatility, but prospective buyers should review known owner complaints regarding its powertrain and electrical systems. While it offers a comfortable ride and upscale cabin, keeping an eye on mechanical maintenance history is essential for a reliable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

Equipped with a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing around 302 horsepower, offering smooth acceleration for daily driving. It is generally robust, though fuel system components should be checked for wear.

**Fuel Pump Failure and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience hesitation during acceleration or unexpected engine stalling while driving, potentially leaving the driver stranded.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel delivery system and replace the malfunctioning fuel pump.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission providing refined gear changes across rear-wheel and all-wheel drive configurations. Regular fluid changes help maintain long-term shifting quality.

**Shifter and Gear Engagement Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience difficulty shifting out of park, or the transmission may slip into neutral unexpectedly while driving.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic shifter module and transmission control unit diagnosed and serviced by a qualified technician.

## Steering

### Steering System

**Unexpected Swerving**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may occasionally pull or swerve unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds, causing handling concerns.
- **Solution:** Check alignment, suspension components, and electronic steering assist modules for proper operation.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera

**Backup Camera Display Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The rear-view camera screen fails to activate when shifting into reverse, displaying a black screen instead.
- **Solution:** Inspect the camera wiring harness and replace the display module or camera unit if faulty.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V539000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE350&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The rear brake caliper pistons on these vehicles may not have been correctly coated, causing gas to become temporarily released into the rear brake hydraulic circuit, reducing braking performance. Reduced braking performance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will bleed the rear axle brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V123000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE350&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Over cured tires may develop a break in the sidewall, resulting in sudden air loss or belt edge separation which could lead to tread/belt loss. Either condition can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the tires, and replace them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V512000: Visibility:Sun/Moon Roof Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE350&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the front cover and/or rear fixed glass are not properly adhered, they may detach, creating a road hazard and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the panoramic sunroof, replace it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE350 is a sophisticated midsize luxury SUV with strong comfort credentials, but used buyers should verify the health of its fuel system, transmission electronics, and backup camera. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist is strongly recommended to catch any lingering electrical or mechanical issues.
