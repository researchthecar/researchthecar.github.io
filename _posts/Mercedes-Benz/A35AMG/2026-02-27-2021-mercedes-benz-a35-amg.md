# 2021 Mercedes-Benz A35 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz A35 AMG brings high-performance luxury to the compact segment, combining athletic handling with everyday usability. While owner feedback highlights a few electrical and driver-assistance glitches, overall mechanical performance remains largely consistent. Prospective buyers should carefully review service histories and test all onboard electronics during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M260)

This hand-built or AMG-tuned 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 302 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It delivers punchy acceleration and responsive power delivery, though proper maintenance with high-grade synthetic oil is essential for longevity.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT DCT Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission sends power to the 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system with rapid gear changes. It provides engaging performance on twisty roads, but buyers should check for smooth engagement at low speeds during a test drive.

## Electrical

### Back Over Prevention

Camera systems and driver aids generally enhance safety, though occasional software glitches can temporarily disable rearview visibility.

**Blank or Inoperative Backup Camera**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report that the rearview camera screen displays a blank image or states it is inoperative when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic checks by a dealer or specialist are required, as software updates or camera module replacements may be needed.

### Electrical System & Advanced Driver Assistance

The advanced electronics network powers numerous convenience and safety features, making software stability a key factor for trouble-free ownership.

**Unexpected Phantom Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle suddenly decelerates significantly while utilizing adaptive driving assistance features on the highway.
- **Solution:** Have the driver-assistance sensors, camera modules, and radar alignment inspected and recalibrated if necessary.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz A35 AMG is an exciting and sophisticated compact performance vehicle that appeals to enthusiasts seeking luxury and speed. While isolated owner reports point to minor electrical quirks and camera glitches, routine pre-purchase inspections can help uncover any lingering issues. Buyers who prioritize comprehensive service records and thoroughly test vehicle electronics will find this sporty model to be an engaging daily driver.
