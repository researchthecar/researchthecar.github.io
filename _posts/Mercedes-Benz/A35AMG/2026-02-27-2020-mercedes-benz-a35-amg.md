# 2020 Mercedes-Benz A35 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz A35 AMG delivers a potent blend of luxury and high-performance driving dynamics. As prospective buyers evaluate used models, reviewing owner-reported feedback helps highlight potential mechanical and electrical considerations.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Engine

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 302 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration. While generally robust, buyers should inspect service records for any history of major powertrain failures.

**Engine Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported a sudden, unexpected loss of engine power while driving at highway speeds without prior warning.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis and potential engine component replacement.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and pairs with the standard all-wheel-drive system. Smooth operation during test drives is essential to verify proper mechanical and electronic health.

**Transmission Communication Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The digital instrument panel may shut down with warning messages, preventing the vehicle from starting due to communication errors in the transmission software.
- **Solution:** Have a dealership inspect the transmission control module and update software as needed.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Assembly

Steering components generally offer sharp and responsive handling characteristic of the AMG brand.

**Steering Clicking and Popping**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel produces audible popping or clicking noises when turning, particularly noticeable when the vehicle is cold.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column and intermediate shaft components for wear or lack of lubrication.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Infotainment Systems

Advanced electrical architectures power the infotainment and safety suites, though occasional software glitches have been reported by owners.

**Backup Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera image blacks out intermittently when attempting to reverse, reducing visibility.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates or replace the camera module if internal failure is diagnosed.

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz A35 AMG is an engaging sport sedan that combines luxury with thrilling performance. Buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's electrical systems, steering responsiveness, and powertrain health before purchase.
