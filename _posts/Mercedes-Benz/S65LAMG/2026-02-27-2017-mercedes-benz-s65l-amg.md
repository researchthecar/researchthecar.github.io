# 2017 Mercedes-Benz S65L AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S65L AMG represents the pinnacle of luxury and performance, featuring a hand-built V12 engine and lavish interior appointments. While generally built to high standards, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback regarding occasional mechanical quirks, trim issues, and routine wear items. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified specialist is strongly advised to verify the condition of this complex flagship sedan.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L Biturbo V12

The 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces an immense 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless acceleration. It is a complex, high-performance powerplant that demands meticulous maintenance and can incur steep repair bills if neglected.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift Plus Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed AMG Speedshift Plus automatic transmission routes massive torque to the rear wheels with smooth execution. Proper fluid maintenance is vital to ensure long-term durability under high-output driving.

**Automatic Shifting to Neutral at Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** When pressing the ignition button to turn off the car while in drive with the foot on the brake, the vehicle shifts into neutral instead of park, potentially causing the car to roll if the door is not opened.
- **Solution:** Have the transmission control module and software inspected by a qualified dealer or technician.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Rims

Low-profile tires mounted on large alloy wheels enhance handling and stance, but leave the wheel rims more vulnerable to impact damage and cracking on rough road surfaces.

**Cracking AMG Wheels**
- **Symptoms:** Large diameter AMG rear alloy wheels develop cracks under normal driving conditions, leading to air loss or tire deflation.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged wheel rims and inspect tires for structural integrity.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Controls

Power-adjustable steering columns provide tailored ergonomics, but motorized adjustment components can occasionally fail.

**Power Steering Column Adjustment Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel moves inward and outward normally, but fails to adjust up and down, becoming stuck in the raised position and obstructing instrument panel visibility.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column adjustment motor and switch assembly for failure or binding.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Glass

Exterior glass and panoramic roof panels offer an open-air feel but require periodic lubrication and track maintenance.

**Panoramic Sunroof Binding**
- **Symptoms:** While closing the sunroof while driving at highway speeds, a cracking sound is heard, and the glass panel jams or fails to close flush.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof track mechanism, cables, and motor for alignment issues or obstructions.

## Interior

### Dashboard Trim and Airbags

The cabin is draped in premium leather and advanced electronics, though certain adhesive trim areas can degrade over time.

**Leather Dashboard Separation**
- **Symptoms:** The leather on the dashboard begins to pull away or separate near the passenger and driver side airbag deployment areas, potentially exposing internal components.
- **Solution:** Consult an upholstery specialist or dealership for dashboard repair or replacement options.
- **Cost:** $3,000 to $6,000
