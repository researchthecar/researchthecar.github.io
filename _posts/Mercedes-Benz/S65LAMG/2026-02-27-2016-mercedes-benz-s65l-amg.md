# 2016 Mercedes-Benz S65L AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S65L AMG represents the pinnacle of luxury and performance, featuring a hand-built twin-turbocharged V12 engine. While highly regarded for its opulence and effortless power, prospective used-car buyers should inspect these complex flagship sedans carefully. Owner reports highlight occasional concerns ranging from structural glass elements to minor electrical and safety system hiccups.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L Biturbo V12 Engine

The 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces a massive 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally smooth yet blistering acceleration. Maintenance and repair costs for this hand-built powerplant can be significantly higher than standard V8 models, requiring diligent upkeep.

**Catalytic Converter Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Warning lights illuminate on the dashboard, and inspection by service providers may indicate internal component failure.
- **Solution:** Have the exhaust system and catalytic converters professionally evaluated; replace failed components as needed.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift Plus Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission is paired exclusively with the rear-wheel-drive V12 powertrain, offering swift and refined gear changes. Routine fluid maintenance is critical to ensure long-term durability under the heavy torque output of the AMG engine.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Steering components provide precise maneuverability for a large luxury sedan, though occasional electronic assist glitches have been noted by owners.

**Power Steering Interruption**
- **Symptoms:** The power steering failure warning light illuminates on the dashboard, and the steering wheel may occasionally become stiff or difficult to turn.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic steering module, wiring harnesses, and fluid levels (if applicable) for faults or intermittent failures.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Glass Roof

Exterior glass and trim require careful examination, as stress fractures on large panoramic roofs have occasionally been reported by owners.

**Spontaneous Glass Fracture**
- **Symptoms:** A loud noise is heard while driving or parked, followed by a visible crack or shattering across the panoramic sunroof glass.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged panoramic glass panel and check the surrounding body frame and seals for proper alignment.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety restraint systems are crucial for occupant protection, and components should be thoroughly tested during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Seat Belt Buckle and Retractor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The seat belt may fail to latch securely into the front passenger buckle, or rear seat belts may become stuck and fail to retract properly.
- **Solution:** Inspect the affected seat belt assemblies and replace faulty buckles or retractor mechanisms.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S65L AMG remains an engineering masterpiece offering supreme comfort and unmatched V12 performance. Buyers should approach with an awareness that maintenance, component replacements, and specialized repairs can be exceptionally costly. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an experienced technician familiar with AMG models is strongly recommended.
