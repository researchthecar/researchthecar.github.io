# 2017 Mercedes-Benz S65L AMG

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S65L AMG, a pinnacle of luxury and performance, is equipped with a formidable handcrafted V12 engine and a sophisticated transmission system. While generally engineered for high reliability, as with any high-performance vehicle, certain components may experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and enthusiasts. The focus is on specific, non-wear-and-tear related failures that have been reported in the automotive community for this model year.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12 (M279)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, reduced engine power, check engine light illumination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per coil

**Turbocharger Actuator Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of boost pressure, sluggish acceleration, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the turbocharger wastegate actuator.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per actuator

**Coolant Leaks (Hoses/Pipes)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles, overheating, low coolant level warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking coolant hoses or pipes.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (7G-TRONIC)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, inability to select gears, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement or reprogramming of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping gears, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC) System
**Hydraulic Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, suspension warning light, fluid leaks under the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hydraulic lines, struts, or pump.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $4000+

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Complete loss of suspension height control, loud whining noise from pump.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System Glitches
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive or freezes, audio/visual issues.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, difficulty starting the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or components causing drain.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S65L AMG is a highly complex vehicle with a powerful V12 engine and advanced systems. While generally robust, owners should be aware of potential issues related to the engine's ignition and turbocharger systems, the transmission's control module and torque converter, and the sophisticated Active Body Control suspension. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and parasitic battery drain, can also occur. These issues, while not universal, represent the most commonly cited technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance for this ultra-luxury performance sedan. Proactive maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining optimal performance and longevity.
