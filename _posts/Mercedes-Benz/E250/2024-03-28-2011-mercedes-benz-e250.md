**Introduction:**

The 2011 Mercedes-Benz E250 is a mid-size luxury sedan powered by a 2.5-liter V6 engine producing 201 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The E250 is known for its comfortable ride, luxurious interior, and ample safety features. However, it has also been associated with several common issues.

**Engine**

* **2.5L V6 Engine (M272):**

  * **Intake Manifold Flaps Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of engine power, rough idling, and illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold flaps
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
  * **Balance Shaft Gear Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the front of the engine, loss of oil pressure, and possible engine damage
    * Solution: Replace the balance shaft gear
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic Transmission (722.9):**

  * **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or shuddering, difficulty shifting, and illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
  * **Valve Body Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Erratic shifting, delayed engagement, and illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace the valve body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Airmatic Suspension:**
  * **Air Spring Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle drops prematurely, uneven ride height, and reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace the air spring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+ per corner
    * Recalls: N/A
  * **Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Suspension malfunctions or vehicle fails to rise, illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace the compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle fails to start, loss of power, and flickering lights
  * Solution: Diagnose and replace faulty battery or electrical components
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlight assembly
  * Solution: Replace or repair the headlight assembly
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Rattle:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or squeaking noise from the panoramic sunroof
  * Solution: Adjust or replace the sunroof assembly
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: N/A

**Overall Summary:**
The 2011 Mercedes-Benz E250 can be a reliable luxury car, but several common issues have been reported. The most severe and costly issues involve the engine, transmission, and suspension, which can be expensive to repair. It's important to consider these potential problems when researching and purchasing this vehicle or to factor in the cost of possible repairs.
