**Introduction:**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E250 is a luxury sedan powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing 201 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque. It is available with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The E250 is known for its comfortable ride, luxurious interior, and advanced technology features. However, it has also been subject to various issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Timing Chain Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear or failure of the timing chain, leading to rough running, reduced power, and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leakage of oil from the engine, typically from seals or gaskets.
    * Solution: Inspection and replacement of the affected components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard or delayed shifting, slipping gears, or inability to engage gears.
    * Solution: Software updates, transmission fluid changes, or replacement of transmission components as needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure or malfunction of the air suspension system, resulting in suspension problems, uneven ride height, or decreased stability.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair or replacement of the affected components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature failure or discharge of the battery.
    * Solution: Inspection and replacement of the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside the headlight lenses.
    * Solution: Inspection and resealing or replacement of the headlights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leakage through the panoramic sunroof.
    * Solution: Inspection and replacement of affected seals or components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Software glitches, crashes, or connectivity problems with the infotainment system.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of components as needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E250 has experienced a range of issues, including engine timing chain failures, oil leaks, transmission shifting problems, and electrical issues. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be costly to repair.
