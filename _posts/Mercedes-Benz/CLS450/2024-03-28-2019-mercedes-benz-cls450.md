**Introduction**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLS450 is a sleek and stylish luxury sedan that offers a thrilling driving experience. It's powered by a turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-six engine that produces 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a nine-speed automatic transmission that delivers smooth and responsive shifts. The CLS450 also comes standard with all-wheel drive, which provides excellent traction in all weather conditions.

Despite its impressive performance and luxurious features, the 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLS450 has a number of reported issues that owners should be aware of before purchasing.

**Engine**
3.0L Inline-Six Engine

* **Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased oil consumption, check engine light
Solution: Replace turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, loss of power, check engine light
Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

9-Speed Automatic Transmission

* **Shifting Hesitation**
Description/Symptoms: Delay in shifting gears, rough shifts
Solution: Software update, transmission fluid change, transmission rebuild
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Torque Converter Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, slipping gears, check engine light
Solution: Replace torque converter
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Sunroof Leakage**
Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin through the sunroof
Solution: Replace sunroof seal
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

* **Air Conditioning Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning not blowing cold air, excessive noise
Solution: Recharge refrigerant, replace compressor
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLS450 is a well-rounded luxury sedan that offers a comfortable and thrilling driving experience. However, there are a number of reported issues that potential owners should be aware of before purchasing. Some of the issues, such as turbocharger failure and timing chain tensioner failure, can be costly to repair. While other issues, such as sunroof leakage and air conditioning malfunction, are less severe and less expensive to fix.
