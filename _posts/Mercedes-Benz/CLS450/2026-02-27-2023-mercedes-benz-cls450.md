# 2023 Mercedes-Benz CLS450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a 2023 Mercedes-Benz CLS 450 means exploring a sleek, luxury-focused four-door coupe that combines impressive performance with high-end style. While powertrain options generally run smoothly, used-car buyers should pay careful attention to reported wheel and tire durability issues stemming from low-profile setups on modern roads.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo Inline-6 Mild Hybrid (M256)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine features a 48-volt mild-hybrid system (EQ Boost), producing approximately 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It delivers seamless power delivery and strong overall reliability for luxury driving.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission pairs with the all-wheel-drive (4MATIC) system to offer quick, refined gear shifts and optimized highway efficiency. It is generally robust and well-matched to the inline-six engine.

## Suspension

### Factory Alloy Wheels & Run-Flat Tires

The suspension and wheel setup provides responsive handling and a composed ride, though large wheels paired with low-profile tires are vulnerable to impact damage.

**Wheel Cracking and Bending**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported recurring wheel bends, structural cracking, and tire sidewall bubbles when encountering normal road imperfections.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels thoroughly for structural integrity and consider replacing damaged wheels or switching to non-run-flat tires if roads are rough.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V100000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=CLS450&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The 48-volt ground cable connection located in the engine compartment may not be tightened properly and may result in the ground cable overheating. An overheated ground cable increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the 48-volt ground cable connection and repair it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz CLS 450 offers a compelling blend of luxury, technology, and robust powertrain performance. Prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the wheels and tires during a pre-purchase evaluation, as owners have noted susceptibility to damage from standard road hazards.
