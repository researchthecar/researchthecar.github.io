# 2023 Mercedes-Benz CLS450

### Overview
The 2023 Mercedes-Benz CLS450 is a luxury four-door coupe that blends performance, comfort, and advanced technology. As a premium vehicle, it generally exhibits strong build quality and sophisticated engineering. Reliability data for the initial model year is still emerging, but based on Mercedes-Benz's track record and early owner feedback, the CLS450 is expected to offer a solid ownership experience. Potential owners should be aware of the typical complexities associated with high-end vehicles, particularly concerning advanced electronic systems and powertrain components. However, widespread critical failures are not commonly reported for this model at this stage.

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Mercedes-Benz CLS450 demonstrates good initial reliability, with its primary powertrain components (3.0L inline-6 engine and 9G-TRONIC transmission) showing no widespread issues. The optional Air Body Control suspension is the most notable area for potential concern, with reports of air strut leaks. Electrical systems, particularly the infotainment and ADAS, can occasionally experience software glitches or sensor issues, which are common in complex modern vehicles. Exterior and interior components are generally robust. Overall, the CLS450 offers a reliable luxury experience, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic failures, though proactive maintenance and awareness of optional system complexities are advised.
