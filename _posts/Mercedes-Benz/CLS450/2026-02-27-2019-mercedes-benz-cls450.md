# 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLS450

### Overview
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLS450, a luxury four-door coupe, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, blending sophisticated design with robust engineering. It typically features a potent inline-six engine paired with a smooth automatic transmission. While offering a premium driving experience, like any complex automobile, it can encounter specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and prospective buyers. The data aims to highlight common, non-wear-and-tear related issues that have been reported, offering guidance on potential repairs and their associated costs.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (M256)
**EQ Boost System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced acceleration, warning lights, rough idling, potential stalling.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of the integrated starter-generator (ISG) or related electrical components.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $4,000

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level warnings.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking gaskets, seals, or oil cooler.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLS450 demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly with its powertrain. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the advanced EQ Boost system within the engine, which can exhibit electrical or mechanical faults, and potential issues with the optional Air Body Control suspension, such as strut leaks. Electrical gremlins, especially within the infotainment system, are also noted, though often resolvable with software updates. While the transmission is generally robust, proactive maintenance is always recommended. Owners should be aware of these potential, albeit not universal, issues and budget accordingly for repairs that fall outside of standard maintenance. Overall, the CLS450 offers a dependable luxury experience with a few specific technical vulnerabilities.
