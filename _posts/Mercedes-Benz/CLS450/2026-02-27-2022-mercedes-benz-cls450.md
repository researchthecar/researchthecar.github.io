### Overview
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLS450 is a luxury four-door coupe that blends performance, comfort, and advanced technology. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the CLS450 demonstrates a strong track record for its powertrain and core systems. Owners can expect a sophisticated driving experience with a focus on premium materials and cutting-edge features. ### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLS450 generally exhibits strong reliability, particularly concerning its core powertrain (3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 engine and 9G-TRONIC transmission). The most commonly cited potential issues tend to be related to the optional AIR BODY CONTROL suspension, where air spring leaks can occur, and occasional software glitches within the MBUX infotainment system, which are often resolvable with updates. Premature 12V battery failure has also been noted in some instances. Overall, for a luxury vehicle with advanced technology, the CLS450 presents a solid reliability profile, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic failures.
