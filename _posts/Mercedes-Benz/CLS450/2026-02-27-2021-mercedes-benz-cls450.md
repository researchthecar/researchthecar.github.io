# 2021 Mercedes-Benz CLS450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Mercedes-Benz CLS450, buyers can generally expect a luxurious grand tourer experience paired with modern powertrain technology. As owner-reported complaints are sparse for this model year, prospective buyers should focus on routine pre-purchase inspections and verifying vehicle history. While owner reports reflect isolated concerns, the overall mechanical footprint remains largely unblemished by widespread systemic failures in early data.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (EQ Boost)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine features a 48-volt mild-hybrid system (EQ Boost), producing approximately 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It provides smooth power delivery and responsive acceleration, though buyers should ensure the complex electrical and hybrid systems are thoroughly inspected.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly and efficiently to the vehicle's drivetrain options. It is generally robust, though regular fluid maintenance is recommended to maintain optimal shift quality over time.

## Suspension

### Wheels

Suspension and wheel components generally hold up well, though isolated structural wheel damage has been noted in rare owner reports.

**Rear Rim Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A through crack can develop on the wheel rim shoulder, resulting in a sudden loss of tire air pressure.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels for structural cracks and replace the damaged rim if failure occurs.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V483000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=CLS450&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect, and replace as necessary, the rear seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V230000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=CLS450&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A faulty electrical contact within the seat position switch may prevent the driver and passenger air bags and seat belt force limiters from properly activating during a crash. Air bags and seat belts that do not perform properly in a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the seat position switches, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V733000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=CLS450&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The 12-Volt battery in the trunk may not be properly secured, which, in the event of a crash, can cause electrical connection failure, disabling various safety features including, emergency call (eCall) system, electric seat adjustment, hazard warning lights, and automatic door locks. The inability to operate safety features (eCall system, hazard lights.
- **What to do:** Dealers will secure the 12V battery, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V100000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=CLS450&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The 48-volt ground cable connection located in the engine compartment may not be tightened properly and may result in the ground cable overheating. An overheated ground cable increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the 48-volt ground cable connection and repair it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz CLS450 presents itself as a stylish and capable luxury choice with very few widespread owner complaints on record. Buyers should conduct a standard pre-purchase inspection focusing on wheels, tires, and the advanced electrical systems. Overall, it remains a comfortable and sophisticated option for used car shoppers.
