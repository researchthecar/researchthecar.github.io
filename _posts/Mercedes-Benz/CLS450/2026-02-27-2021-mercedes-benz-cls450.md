# 2021 Mercedes-Benz CLS450

### Overview
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz CLS450 is a luxury four-door coupe that blends sophisticated styling with potent performance. It is equipped with a potent turbocharged inline-six engine paired with a mild-hybrid system, offering a smooth and responsive driving experience. While generally well-regarded for its build quality and advanced technology, like any vehicle, it can encounter specific issues. This report focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and prospective buyers. The CLS450 aims for a balance of luxury, comfort, and dynamic capability, and understanding its reliability profile is crucial for long-term ownership satisfaction.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 with EQ Boost (M256)
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level warnings.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of seals, gaskets, or oil cooler lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**EQ Boost System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced acceleration, warning lights on the dashboard, intermittent power loss.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of the electric auxiliary compressor or battery.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $4,000


## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC Automatic Transmission
**Harsh Shifting / Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, delayed engagement, or hesitation during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Software updates, transmission fluid flush and replacement, or internal component repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,200


## Suspension

### Air Body Control (Optional)
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, 'suspension too high' warning messages.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air struts or seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500 per corner

## Electrical

### Infotainment System (MBUX)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, Bluetooth connectivity issues, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.

### 12V Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently, battery warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V auxiliary battery.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz CLS450 generally exhibits good reliability, with its sophisticated powertrain and luxury features holding up well. The most commonly reported issues tend to be related to the optional Air Body Control suspension, potential oil leaks from the engine, and occasional software glitches within the MBUX infotainment system. The mild-hybrid EQ Boost system, while innovative, can also be a source of repair costs if it malfunctions. Electrical issues, particularly related to the 12V battery, are also noted. However, these are not typically widespread failures, and many CLS450 owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected problems. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning lights are recommended.
