# 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLB200 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLB200, buyers will find a stylish and versatile compact luxury SUV packed with modern technology and practical cargo space. While overall owner feedback is relatively sparse, prospective buyers should still carefully evaluate powertrain performance and electronics during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure everything operates smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 1.3L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 1.3-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 161 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commuting. It balances respectable fuel efficiency with responsive low-end power in standard driving conditions.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routes power smoothly in typical driving, though owners should verify proper shift quality during a test drive.

**Transmission Malfunction and Limp Mode**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission malfunction warning light may illuminate in red while driving at highway speeds, causing the vehicle to suddenly enter limp mode.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle diagnostic codes scanned by a qualified technician or dealership to isolate the underlying electrical or mechanical fault.
