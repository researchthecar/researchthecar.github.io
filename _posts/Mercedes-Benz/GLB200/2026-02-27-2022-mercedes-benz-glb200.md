### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLB200 demonstrates good overall reliability, with no significant widespread mechanical issues reported for its engine or transmission options. The primary area where minor concerns have been noted is within the electrical system, specifically occasional software glitches in the infotainment and connectivity features, which are typically resolvable with software updates. Other categories like suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally robust. As with any luxury vehicle, maintenance costs can be higher than average, and proactive servicing is recommended to ensure long-term dependability. For potential buyers, the GLB200 offers a compelling package with a solid reliability foundation, provided routine maintenance is adhered to.
