**Introduction**

The 2009 Mercedes-Benz Boxer OF is a medium-duty commercial van powered by a 6.0L V6 diesel engine mated to a 5-speed automatic transmission. It offers a spacious cargo area and various advanced features, such as electronic stability control and a rear-view camera. Owners appreciate its reliability, practicality, and comfortable cabin. However, some common issues have been reported in specific areas.

**Engine**

**6.0L V6 Diesel**

* **Turbocharger Actuator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased exhaust smoke, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, hesitation during acceleration
    * Solution: Overhaul or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Front Ball Joint Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or popping noises while turning, premature tire wear
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery warning light, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after extended periods of inactivity
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system or replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rusting on Exhaust System:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Visible rust on exhaust components
    * Solution: Replace rusted components or apply anti-rust coating
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to adjust temperature or fan speed
    * Solution: Replace climate control unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2009 Mercedes-Benz Boxer OF generally exhibits good reliability. However, certain issues, such as turbocharger actuator failure and transmission slipping, can be costly to repair. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any symptoms can help mitigate these issues and extend the vehicle's lifespan.
