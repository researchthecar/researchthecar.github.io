The 2021 Mercedes-Benz Boxer OF is a versatile van designed for commercial use. It is available with multiple engine and transmission options, providing customers with a range of capabilities to meet their specific needs. The Boxer OF is powered by either a 2.1-liter inline-4 turbodiesel engine producing 163 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque or a 3.0-liter V6 turbodiesel engine producing 190 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission.
