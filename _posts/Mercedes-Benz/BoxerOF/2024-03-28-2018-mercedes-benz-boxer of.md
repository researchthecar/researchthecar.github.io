**Introduction:**

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz Boxer OF is a robust commercial vehicle designed for heavy-duty applications. It boasts a reliable 4.2-liter V6 engine, producing 245 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. The Boxer OF comes standard with a 6-speed manual transmission and is also available with a 7-speed 7G-Tronic automatic transmission. The vehicle is praised for its muscular engine performance, spacious cabin, and impressive load capacity, but has been criticized for its dated infotainment system and some interior quality issues.

**Engine:**

* **4.2-liter V6**

  * **Leaking Oil Filter Housing:** Symptoms include oil leaks near the filter housing. The solution is to replace the o-ring and tighten the housing. Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
  * **Exhaust Manifold Cracked:** Symptoms include a rattling noise and loss of power. The solution is to replace the cracked manifold. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000

**Transmission:**

* **6-speed Manual**

  * **Difficulty Shifting into Gear:** Symptoms include grating noises or difficulty engaging gears. The solution is to adjust or replace the shifter linkage. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

* **7-speed 7G-Tronic Automatic**

  * **Shifting Hesitation:** Symptoms include delays or hesitation when shifting gears. The solution is to update the transmission software or replace the shift solenoid. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500

**Suspension:**

* **Front Coil Spring Breakage:** Symptoms include a sagging front end or uneven tire wear. The solution is to replace the broken spring. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $450

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Drain:** Symptoms include slow or no starting, especially after sitting for extended periods. The solution is to replace the battery or test for electrical faults. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Alternator Failure:** Symptoms include dimming lights, electrical system malfunctions, or a charging system warning light. The solution is to replace the alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

* **Tail Light Moisture:** Symptoms include condensation or water inside the tail light assembly. The solution is to replace the light assembly or check for leaks. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300

**Exterior:**

* **Door Handle Malfunction:** Symptoms include difficulty opening or closing the doors. The solution is to adjust or replace the door handle mechanism. Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200

**Interior:**

* **Climate Control Fan Failure:** Symptoms include a lack of airflow from the climate control system. The solution is to replace the fan motor or electrical components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
* **Seat Belt Buckle Faulty:** Symptoms include seat belt retracting slowly or not locking properly. The solution is to replace the buckle. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200

**Summary:**

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz Boxer OF is a robust commercial vehicle that generally performs well in terms of reliability. However, several reported issues can range from minor inconveniences to potentially costly repairs. The most common engine issues involve oil leaks and exhaust manifold cracks, while transmission problems often relate to shifting difficulties or hesitation. Other issues include suspension coil spring breakage, electrical battery drain or alternator failure, and various interior malfunctions. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific issue and the vehicle's condition and maintenance history.
