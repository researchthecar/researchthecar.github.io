**Introduction:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz Boxer OF is a medium-duty commercial van offered in a variety of configurations. It is powered by a turbocharged 3.0L V6 diesel engine paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The Boxer OF is known for its rugged construction, spacious interior, and advanced safety features. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of issues.

**Engine**

* **3.0L V6 Diesel**

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic**

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains over time, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty battery or alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty Wiring Harness**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical components malfunction due to faulty wiring.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the damaged wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Frame**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust forms on the metal frame of the vehicle, potentially leading to structural issues.
    * Solution: Sand and repaint the frame or apply a rust-resistant coating.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Squeaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard rattles or squeaks over rough terrain.
    * Solution: Identify the source of the noise and apply sound-dampening material.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The heating, ventilation, or air conditioning system may fail to function properly.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz Boxer OF is a reliable vehicle with a few common issues. Electrical problems are the most frequently reported, followed by interior and exterior issues. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some being minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more serious and costly. Overall, the Boxer OF is a solid choice for commercial applications, but it's important to be aware of potential issues before purchasing or leasing one.
