# 2019 Mercedes-Benz Boxer OF

### Overview
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Boxer OF, a robust commercial vehicle, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting Mercedes-Benz's commitment to durability in its van lineup. Designed for demanding professional use, it prioritizes longevity and operational efficiency. While specific model year data can be scarce for commercial vehicles compared to passenger cars, the Boxer OF has a reputation for solid engineering. Owners and fleet managers can expect a dependable platform, though like any vehicle, certain components may require attention over time. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related issues to provide a clear picture of potential reliability concerns.

---


## Engine

### 2.3L F1A Diesel
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, black smoke from exhaust, engine warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**EGR Valve Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, increased emissions, engine warning light, reduced engine performance.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### 3.0L F1C Diesel
**Injector Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Engine misfires, rough running, poor fuel economy, difficulty starting, black smoke.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**Glow Plug Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting in cold weather, engine warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty glow plugs.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell, shuddering during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch kit (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $900 - $1,800


### 6-Speed Automated Manual Transmission (AMT)
**Actuator Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning light, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of transmission actuators.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,000


## Suspension

### Standard Independent Front Suspension
**Ball Joint Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, uneven tire wear, steering looseness.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


### Leaf Spring Rear Suspension
**Leaf Spring Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, rattling noises, reduced load capacity.
- **Solution:** Replacement of broken leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,000


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**BCM Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent issues with lights, wipers, power windows, or dashboard warnings.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,200


### Alternator
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light, dimming headlights, electrical system malfunctions, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900


## Exterior

### Door Latches and Mechanisms
**Sticking Door Latches**
- **Symptoms:** Doors difficult to open or close, central locking system malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Lubrication or replacement of door latch mechanisms.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### Headlight/Taillight Seals
**Moisture Ingress**
- **Symptoms:** Condensation inside light housings, potential corrosion of electrical contacts.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals or entire light unit if damaged.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from vents, inconsistent fan speed.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor or resistor.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


### Seat Upholstery
**Premature Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Tearing or excessive wear on driver's seat bolsters and fabric.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seat covers/cushions.
- **Cost:** $250 - $700


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Boxer OF demonstrates a generally reliable performance profile for a commercial vehicle. The most commonly reported non-wear-and-tear issues tend to be concentrated in the engine (turbocharger, EGR valve, injectors) and transmission (clutch, actuators), particularly with higher mileage units. Electrical components like the BCM and alternator can also present issues. Exterior and interior components, while subject to wear, typically do not present widespread systemic failures beyond specific items like door latches or HVAC blower motors. Regular maintenance and addressing minor issues promptly are key to maximizing the longevity and operational uptime of the Boxer OF.
