## Introduction

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Gran Viale LE is a large luxury sedan. It is available with two engine options: a 3.0-liter V6 and a 4.0-liter V8. A 9-speed automatic transmission sends 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque to the rear wheels with the V6 engine. The V8 engine produces 469 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. The Gran Viale LE is known for its comfortable ride, luxurious interior, and powerful engine options. However, it has also been plagued by a number of mechanical issues.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6**

**Valve Cover Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the valve covers.
Solution: Replace the valve covers.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**4.0L V8**

**No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic**

**Shifting Issues**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shift harshly or refuse to go into certain gears.
Solution: Reprogram the transmission computer or replace the transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $4,000+
Recalls: 2019657, 2019741

**Suspension**

**Air Suspension Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system may fail, causing the vehicle to sag or ride rough.
Solution: Replace the air suspension struts or compressor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to struggle to start.
Solution: Replace the battery or alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Sunroof Leaks**
Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the vehicle through the sunroof.
Solution: Replace the sunroof seals or adjust the sunroof alignment.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control Issues**
Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may not work properly, resulting in uncomfortable temperatures inside the vehicle.
Solution: Replace the climate control module or wiring harness.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Gran Viale LE is a powerful and luxurious sedan, but it has also been plagued by a number of mechanical issues. The most common problems include engine oil leaks, transmission shifting issues, and air suspension malfunctions. These issues can be expensive to fix, so it is important to be aware of them before purchasing this vehicle.
