## Engine

### 2.2L CDI Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Regeneration Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated DPF warning light, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration, DPF cleaning, or replacement if severely clogged.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200

**Glow Plug Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting in cold weather, rough idling.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty glow plugs.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400


### 3.0L CDI V6 Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Regeneration Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated DPF warning light, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration, DPF cleaning, or replacement if severely clogged.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200

**EGR Valve Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, engine misfires, increased emissions, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $250 - $700
