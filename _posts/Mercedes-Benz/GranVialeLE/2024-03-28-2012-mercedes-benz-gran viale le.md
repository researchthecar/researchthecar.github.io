**Interior**

**Dashboard Rattling:**
Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may rattle or squeak when driving over uneven surfaces.
Solution: Replacing the dashboard clips or adjusting the dashboard mounting points.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Seat Control Malfunction:**
Description/Symptoms: The electric seat controls may become unresponsive or erratic, causing the seats to move unexpectedly or get stuck in an adjusted position.
Solution: Repairing or replacing the seat control module or wiring harness.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2012 Mercedes-Benz Gran Viale LE has a relatively low number of reported issues. The interior issues found are minor and can be addressed at a reasonable cost. The engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems are generally reliable.
