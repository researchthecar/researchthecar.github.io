## Engine

### 2.2L CDI Diesel
**Glow Plug Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting in cold weather, rough idling, check engine light illumination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty glow plugs.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**EGR Valve Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, increased emissions, rough idling, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


### 3.0L V6 CDI Diesel
**DPF Regeneration Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced power, increased fuel consumption, potential engine damage if ignored.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration or DPF replacement.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2500
