**Introduction:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz Viaggio 1050 is a full-size luxury coach bus with a 15.9-liter inline-6 engine producing 455 horsepower and 1,650 lb-ft of torque. It is offered with a 6-speed manual or a 12-speed automated manual transmission. Drivers appreciate its spacious interior, comfortable seating, and advanced safety features. However, some owners have encountered issues with various components.

## **Engine**

**15.9L Inline-6**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger can fail prematurely, leading to a loss of power and increased exhaust emissions.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Injector Issues:** Fuel injectors can become clogged or fail, causing rough idling, poor fuel economy, and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Clean or replace the fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+ per injector
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Wear:** The clutch can wear prematurely, resulting in slipping and difficulty shifting gears.
    * Solution: Replace the clutch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**12-Speed Automated Manual**

* **Shifting Delay:** The transmission can experience delays when shifting gears, leading to jerky acceleration and deceleration.
    * Solution: Update the transmission control module software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure:** The air suspension system can fail, causing the vehicle to lose height and ride rough.
    * Solution: Replace the air bags or compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Leaf Spring Breakage:** The leaf springs can break, resulting in decreased stability and handling.
    * Solution: Replace the leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per leaf spring
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery can drain quickly when the vehicle is parked, leaving it unable to start.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or identify and fix the source of the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Wiring Harness Issues:** The electrical wiring harness can experience shorts or breaks, causing various electrical problems.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the affected wiring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Exterior**

* **Door Seal Issues:** The door seals can fail, allowing water and wind to enter the interior.
    * Solution: Replace the door seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+ per door
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system can malfunction, resulting in inadequate heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the climate control unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Bugs:** The infotainment system can experience software bugs, causing it to crash or malfunction.
    * Solution: Update the system software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Summary**

While the 2017 Mercedes-Benz Viaggio 1050 is generally a reliable and comfortable vehicle, some owners have encountered issues with various components. The most common problems include turbocharger failure, fuel injector issues, and suspension problems. The severity of the issues can range from minor inconveniences to more serious breakdowns. The cost of repairs can also be significant, with some issues estimated to cost over $3,000 to fix.
