**Introduction:**

The 2004 Mercedes-Benz ML500 is a luxurious midsize SUV powered by a 5.0-liter V8 engine that produces 302 horsepower and 339 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The ML500 offers a spacious interior, comfortable ride, and strong performance. However, it has also been known to experience several issues.

**Engine**

**5.0L V8**

* **Intake Manifold Failure:**
    * **Description:** Cracks in the intake manifold lead to vacuum leaks, causing rough idling and decreased power.
    * **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold
    * **Estimated Cost:** $1,500 - $2,500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * **Description:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to jump, resulting in engine damage.
    * **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain and tensioner
    * **Estimated Cost:** $2,000 - $3,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * **Description:** The torque converter fails to transmit power effectively, causing shuddering, slipping, and loss of drive.
    * **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter
    * **Estimated Cost:** $2,500 - $3,500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Transmission Control Module Failure:**
    * **Description:** The TCM malfunctions, causing transmission shifting problems, such as hesitation, harsh shifting, and stuck gears.
    * **Solution:** Replacement of the TCM or transmission software update
    * **Estimated Cost:** $1,000 - $2,000
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure:**
    * **Description:** Leaking or malfunctioning air struts cause the suspension to sag or become uneven, affecting ride quality and handling.
    * **Solution:** Replacement of the air struts or air suspension compressor
    * **Estimated Cost:** $1,500 - $2,500+ for each strut
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * **Description:** The alternator fails to charge the battery, leading to electrical system issues, such as dim lights, difficulty starting, and loss of power.
    * **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator
    * **Estimated Cost:** $500 - $1,000
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Battery Failure:**
    * **Description:** Premature battery failure due to excessive electrical load or other electrical system issues.
    * **Solution:** Replacement of the battery
    * **Estimated Cost:** $200 - $500
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Tailgate Corrosion:**
    * **Description:** Corrosion forms around the rear tailgate, leading to rust and paint peeling.
    * **Solution:** Replacement or repair of the tailgate
    * **Estimated Cost:** $500 - $1,500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Creaking:**
    * **Description:** The center console creaks or rattles due to loose or worn components.
    * **Solution:** Tightening or replacing the affected components
    * **Estimated Cost:** $100 - $300
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Navigation System Malfunction:**
    * **Description:** The navigation system malfunctions or becomes unresponsive.
    * **Solution:** Update or replacement of the navigation unit
    * **Estimated Cost:** $500 - $1,500
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 2004 Mercedes-Benz ML500 has a range of potential issues, including engine and transmission failures, suspension problems, electrical malfunctions, exterior corrosion, and interior creaking and navigation system issues. The severity and cost of these problems vary, with some repairs being relatively inexpensive while others can be quite costly. It is important for owners and potential buyers to be aware of these issues and to factor in the potential cost of repairs when considering this vehicle.
