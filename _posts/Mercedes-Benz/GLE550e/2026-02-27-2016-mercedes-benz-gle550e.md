# 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE550e Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE550e offers a luxurious interior, advanced technology, and a plug-in hybrid powertrain that blends performance with efficiency. However, used car buyers should approach this model with awareness of potential electrical, steering, and powertrain complexities documented in owner complaints. While offering sophisticated luxury and robust performance, keeping an eye out for recurring mechanical and electronic glitches is recommended.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Turbocharged Plug-In Hybrid

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 hybrid engine pairs with an electric motor to deliver ample horsepower and smooth acceleration. It provides strong performance and efficiency for a luxury SUV, though owners should monitor the complex fuel and charging systems closely.

**Fuel System and Stalling Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences unexpected engine stalling, fuel leaks, or problems with fuel pumps and fuel injectors leading to a loss of motive power.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines, pump, and injectors for leaks or failure; replace faulty components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear changes tailored to the hybrid powertrain. While generally robust, electronic shift actuator anomalies have been reported by some owners.

**Unintended Neutral Shift or Parking Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly shift into neutral or park while driving or operating, or display erroneous warnings about gear selection.
- **Solution:** Check transmission software, linkage, and electronic control modules for faults or required updates.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The power steering system provides light, adjustable feedback typical of a luxury cruiser, but electronic assist failures can occasionally compromise maneuverability.

**Power Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering assistance accompanied by dashboard warning messages, making the steering wheel extremely heavy and difficult to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electric power steering motor, wiring harness, and control modules; replace failed assemblies as required.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Exterior lighting systems incorporate advanced illumination technology, though standard bulb degradation or housing issues can impact night-driving clarity.

**Dim Headlights**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights appear excessively dim during night driving, significantly reducing road visibility.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight assemblies, bulbs, and electrical connections for wear or degradation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V955000: Fuel System, Diesel:Delivery:Fuel Pump:Control/Drive Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE550e&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Water might accumulate in the spare tire wheel well and damage the fuel pump control unit, which could cause an engine stall while driving. An engine stall while driving increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a water drain plug, inspect for water intrusion, and replace the fuel pump control unit as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V787000: Communication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE550e&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** For a full list of the affected models visit: https://static.nhtsa.gov/odi/rcl/2019/RMISC-19V787-4620.pdf An inaccurate vehicle location may delay emergency responders, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will also notify owners, and dealers will check software for a successful OTA or update the communication module software as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE550e is an appealing choice for buyers seeking hybrid luxury and refined driving dynamics. However, prospective owners should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's electrical systems, steering assist components, and fuel delivery setup. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable technician is strongly advised to catch any hidden issues before buying.
