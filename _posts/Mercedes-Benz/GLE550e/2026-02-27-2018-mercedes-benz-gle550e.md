# 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE550e Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE550e offers a sophisticated plug-in hybrid luxury SUV experience, combining a robust powertrain with upscale features. While generally well-regarded for its comfort and performance, prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints concerning electrical issues, powertrain shifting anomalies, and accessory failures.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Turbocharged Plug-In Hybrid

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 paired with an electric motor delivers substantial combined horsepower and torque for responsive acceleration. It provides a refined driving dynamic typical of luxury hybrids, though fuel system components require attention if hesitation or stalling occurs.

**Fuel Pump Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle hesitation during acceleration and unexpected engine stalling while driving, which can create dangerous traffic situations.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel delivery system and replace the failing fuel pump.

## Transmission

### 7G-TRONIC Plus 7-Speed Automatic

The 7-speed automatic transmission is specifically engineered to integrate with the hybrid drive system for smooth power delivery. It generally offers refined shifts across various driving modes, though proper fluid maintenance is essential for long-term durability.

### Power Train and Shifter

Issues related to gear selection and unexpected shifting behavior have been reported by some owners, requiring diagnostic evaluation of the electronic shift controls.

**Shifter and Gear Engagement Defect**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to move the gear selector out of park, or unexpected slipping from drive into neutral while operating the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic shifter mechanism, transmission control module, and linkage inspected by a qualified technician.

## Electrical

### Back Over Prevention

Safety systems generally perform reliably, but auxiliary features like the rearview camera display can occasionally experience intermittent electronic failures.

**Backup Camera Display Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen fails to display the rearview image when shifting into reverse, showing a black screen instead.
- **Solution:** Check camera wiring connections, software firmware, or replace the backup camera unit.
