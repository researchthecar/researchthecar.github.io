# 2022 Mercedes-Benz A200 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz A200 brings luxury, advanced technology, and refined styling to the entry-level compact market. While owner feedback is generally limited for this model year, prospective buyers should review available powertrain options and monitor for reported component quirks during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 1.3L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 1.3-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine generates approximately 160 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque, providing an efficient yet punchy daily drive. While generally modern and responsive, isolated owner reports point to severe internal piston wear requiring major repairs.

**Premature Piston Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may stall, shut down unexpectedly, sputter, shake excessively, and experience a severe lack of power.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic testing and potential replacement of affected pistons or the entire engine assembly.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

A 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission channels power smoothly to the front wheels, offering crisp and rapid gear shifts. It balances everyday driving efficiency with sporty responsiveness when paired with the standard powertrain.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Issues related to exterior glass and body trim components are rare, though isolated incidents require careful inspection.

**Shattering Sunroof Glass**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass may spontaneously fracture or shatter while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for any prior stress fractures and replace the entire glass panel if damaged.
