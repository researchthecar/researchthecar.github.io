**Introduction:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz A200 is a compact luxury hatchback powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine that produces 188 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. It offers a comfortable ride, a luxurious interior, and impressive safety features. However, it has also been reported to have some potential issues.

- **Sunroof Rattle:**
    - Description/Symptoms: A rattling noise may be heard from the panoramic sunroof.

Overall, the 2017 Mercedes-Benz A200 has received positive reviews for its performance, comfort, and safety.
