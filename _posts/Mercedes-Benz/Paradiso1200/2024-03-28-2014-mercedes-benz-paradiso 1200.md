**2014 Mercedes-Benz Paradiso 1200**

The 2014 Mercedes-Benz Paradiso 1200 is a luxury coach bus manufactured by Mercedes-Benz do Brasil. It is available with a single engine and transmission option, a 12.8-liter OM 457 LA inline-6 diesel engine paired with a 6-speed Mercedes-Benz GO 250-6 manual transmission. The OM 457 LA produces 422 horsepower and 1,400 lb-ft of torque.

Owners of the 2014 Mercedes-Benz Paradiso 1200 generally praise its powerful engine, comfortable interior, and reliable performance. However, some owners have reported experiencing various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **OM 457 LA Inline-6 Diesel Engine**
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:** Description/Symptoms: The fuel injectors may fail, causing engine misfires, rough idling, and reduced power. Solution: Replace the faulty fuel injectors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+. Recalls: None Found
    * **Turbocharger Failure:** Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger may fail, resulting in reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, and excessive exhaust smoke. Solution: Replace the faulty turbocharger. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+. Recalls: None Found
    * **EGR Cooler Failure:** Description/Symptoms: The EGR cooler may fail, leading to overheating and reduced engine performance. Solution: Replace the faulty EGR cooler. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+. Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **GO 250-6 Manual Transmission**
    * **Synchronizer Failure:** Description/Symptoms: The synchronizers in the transmission may fail, causing difficulty shifting gears or grinding noises. Solution: Replace the faulty synchronizers. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000. Recalls: None Found
