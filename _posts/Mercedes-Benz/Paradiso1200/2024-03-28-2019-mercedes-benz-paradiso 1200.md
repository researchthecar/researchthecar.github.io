**2019 Mercedes-Benz Paradiso 1200**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Paradiso 1200 is a luxury coach with a powerful engine and a spacious interior. It comes with a single engine option, the OM 471 LA, which is a 12.8-liter inline-6 turbodiesel engine that produces 456 hp and 2,300 lb-ft of torque. The Paradiso 1200 comes with two transmission options: the G 360-12 Powershift 3 automated manual transmission and the GO 250-8 Powershift 8 automated manual transmission.

People who like the Paradiso 1200 praise its powerful engine, spacious interior, and comfortable ride. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical system.

**Engine**

* **OM 471 LA**
    * **Turbocharger failure:** The turbocharger can fail, causing a loss of power and reduced fuel efficiency.
        * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * **Injector failure:** The injectors can fail, causing rough idling, poor performance, and increased emissions.
        * Solution: Replace the injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**Transmission**

* **G 360-12 Powershift 3**
    * **Shifting problems:** The transmission may experience shifting problems, such as slipping gears or refusing to shift.
        * Solution: Repair or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
* **GO 250-8 Powershift 8**
    * **Mechatronic unit failure:** The mechatronic unit can fail, causing the transmission to lose power or shift erratically.
        * Solution: Replace the mechatronic unit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension:** The front suspension can develop worn or damaged components, leading to a rough ride and reduced handling.
    * Solution: Replace the worn or damaged components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Rear suspension:** The rear suspension can also develop worn or damaged components, leading to a bouncy ride and reduced stability.
    * Solution: Replace the worn or damaged components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery problems:** The battery can fail prematurely, causing the vehicle to lose power or become stranded.
    * Solution: Replace the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Alternator problems:** The alternator can fail, causing the vehicle to lose power or experience electrical problems.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Windshield wipers:** The windshield wipers can become worn or damaged, reducing visibility in inclement weather.
    * Solution: Replace the windshield wipers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150+
* **Headlights:** The headlights can develop condensation or other issues, reducing visibility at night.
    * Solution: Replace the headlights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Interior**

* **Climate control system:** The climate control system can develop issues, such as failing fans or malfunctioning temperature controls.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Dashboard:** The dashboard can develop cracks or other cosmetic issues.
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Summary**

Overall, the 2019 Mercedes-Benz Paradiso 1200 is a reliable vehicle with a powerful engine and a spacious interior. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical system. The cost of these issues can vary depending on the severity of the problem and the location of the repair.
