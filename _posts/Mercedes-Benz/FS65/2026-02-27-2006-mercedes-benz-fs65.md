### Air Suspension (Optional)
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, loss of ride height, audible hissing.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty air spring or associated air lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per corner


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light illuminated, dimming lights, engine stalling due to lack of charging.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

**Wiring Harness Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical faults, non-functioning lights or accessories, dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair/replacement of corroded sections of the wiring harness.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200

### Summary Verdict
The 2006 Mercedes-Benz FS65 demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its core powertrain and chassis components. The robust diesel engines and durable transmissions are designed for longevity. Potential issues are infrequent and tend to be isolated, such as occasional air spring leaks in models equipped with optional air suspension, or common electrical component failures like alternators and wiring harness issues that can arise with age and mileage. Exterior and interior components are generally well-built, with minor issues like headlight condensation being more cosmetic or seal-related. Proactive checks of the electrical system and air suspension, if applicable, are recommended for optimal performance.
