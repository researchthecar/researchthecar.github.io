**Introduction:**

The 2009 Mercedes-Benz LO 915 is a heavy-duty truck available with several engine and transmission options. It is powered by a 6-cylinder engine that generates 320 horsepower and 810 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 6-speed manual or a 6-speed automatic. The LO 915 is praised for its reliability, towing capacity, and comfortable ride. However, it also has some reported issues that vary depending on the configuration.

**Engine**

**6-Cylinder**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, black smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, rough idle, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise
    * Solution: Replace clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration while shifting or accelerating
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Spring Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging suspension, uneven ride height
    * Solution: Replace air springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, slow cranking
    * Solution: Replace battery, investigate parasitic power drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing doors
    * Solution: Replace door handles
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent temperature control
    * Solution: Repair or replace HVAC components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2009 Mercedes-Benz LO 915 is generally a reliable truck, but it has some potential issues to be aware of. The engine and transmission problems can be expensive to fix, while the suspension and electrical issues are usually more affordable. The interior and exterior issues are relatively minor. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage.
