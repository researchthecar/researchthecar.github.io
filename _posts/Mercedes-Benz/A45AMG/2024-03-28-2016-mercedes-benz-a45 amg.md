**Introduction to the 2016 Mercedes-Benz A45 AMG**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz A45 AMG is a high-performance hatchback that delivers blistering acceleration and agile handling. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 381 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission that sends power to all four wheels. The A45 AMG also boasts a sophisticated suspension system, providing excellent stability and cornering ability.

Despite its impressive performance, the A45 AMG has faced some issues that have been reported by owners. These issues range from minor annoyances to more serious mechanical problems.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

**Blown Head Gasket**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of coolant, white smoke from the exhaust, overheating
Solution: Replace head gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**AMG Speedshift DCT 7G**

**Transmission Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: Inability to engage gears, erratic shifting patterns
Solution: Repair or replace transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Faulty Ignition Coil**
Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling
Solution: Replace ignition coil
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Inability to adjust temperature, malfunctioning fan
Solution: Replace climate control module
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz A45 AMG is a high-performance hatchback that has been praised for its thrilling driving experience. However, it has also faced some issues that have been reported by owners. These issues include a blown head gasket, transmission slipping, faulty ignition coil, and climate control malfunction. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, so it's important to be aware of them before purchasing a used A45 AMG.
