# 2012 Mercedes-Benz ML550

### Overview
The 2012 Mercedes-Benz ML550, part of the W166 generation, is a luxury mid-size SUV known for its powerful performance and upscale interior. It typically came equipped with a robust 4.7L V8 engine paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. While generally well-regarded for its comfort and driving dynamics, like any vehicle, it has specific areas that have seen more attention from owners regarding reliability. This analysis focuses on documented issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for prospective buyers or current owners.

---


## Engine

### 4.7L V8 Biturbo (M278)
**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Oil Pan)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or oil pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Transmission

### 7G-Tronic (722.9) Automatic
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering or slipping during acceleration, rough shifting, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter assembly.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500

**Valve Body Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, transmission fault codes, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the transmission valve body.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000


## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, 'airmatic malfunction' warning on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air struts or air springs.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, 'airmatic malfunction' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,200

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Command System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen freezing, navigation issues, audio problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000

**Parking Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** False alerts, sensors not detecting obstacles, 'parking assist unavailable' message.
- **Solution:** Cleaning, recalibration, or replacement of faulty sensors.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Mercedes-Benz ML550 offers a compelling blend of luxury and performance. While its core powertrain is generally reliable, potential owners should be aware of specific issues that can arise, particularly with the optional AIRMATIC suspension and the 7G-Tronic transmission. Engine-related concerns like timing chain wear and oil leaks are not uncommon for high-mileage vehicles of this era. Electrical gremlins, especially with the infotainment system, can also occur. Despite these potential drawbacks, many ML550s remain dependable with proper maintenance. Addressing these known issues proactively can help ensure a more positive ownership experience.
