# 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLA35 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLA35 AMG delivers sharp styling and punchy performance for luxury crossover buyers. While it offers engaging driving dynamics, prospective buyers should review owner-reported feedback concerning various system quirks. As with any high-performance compact luxury vehicle, thorough pre-purchase inspections and attentive maintenance histories are recommended.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Engine (AMG-tuned)

This hand-assembled or performance-tuned 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 produces around 302 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration. It requires premium fuel and diligent maintenance to ensure long-term reliability in daily driving.

**Charcoal Canister Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light caused by fuel system components during routine refueling.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the EVAP system and replace the charcoal canister if saturated or damaged.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift DCT 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes paired with standard all-wheel drive. It offers smooth operation in comfort mode and crisp shifts in sport settings, though proper fluid maintenance is vital.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features provide added peace of mind, though sensor alignment and software calibration are critical for proper functioning.

**Unintended Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle's automatic emergency braking system engages unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership or certified service center to check for sensor calibration updates or software flashes.
