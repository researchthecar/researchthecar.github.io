**2021 Mercedes-Benz GLA35 AMG**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLA35 AMG is a compact luxury SUV that offers impressive performance and handling. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 302 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. The GLA35 AMG is available with either a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission or an eight-speed automatic transmission. It comes standard with all-wheel drive.

The GLA35 AMG is praised for its sporty driving dynamics, luxurious interior, and advanced technology features. However, it has also been reported to have some issues.

**Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, engine rattling, excessive smoke
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Dual-Clutch Transmission Shudder**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering during acceleration or gear changes
    * Solution: Software update or transmission replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Knock**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or rattling noise from front suspension
    * Solution: Replace front suspension struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Suspension Spring Sag**
    * Description/Symptoms: Uneven ride height, decreased handling performance
    * Solution: Replace rear suspension springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace battery or diagnose electrical system issue
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment System Freeze**
    * Description/Symptoms: Infotainment screen freezes or becomes unresponsive
    * Solution: Software update or hardware replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Center Console Squeak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noise from center console
    * Solution: Tighten or replace center console components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sunroof Seal Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the vehicle through the sunroof
    * Solution: Replace sunroof seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLA35 AMG is an impressive performance SUV with few reported issues. The most common problems involve the turbocharger, transmission, and suspension. While some of these issues can be expensive to fix, the overall severity of the problems is generally not severe.
