**Introduction:**

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLA35 AMG is a high-performance crossover powered by a potent 2.0L turbocharged inline-four engine. It boasts 302 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, coupled with an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission. The GLA35 AMG offers a thrilling driving experience, with sharp handling and ample power. However, it does have some reported issues that buyers should be aware of.

**Engine**

* **M260 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder**
   * **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
     * Symptoms: Low oil levels, blue smoke from exhaust
     * Solution: Replace piston rings and/or valve seals
     * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
     * Recalls: None Found
   * **Turbocharger Failure:**
     * Symptoms: Reduced power, whistling noise, check engine light
     * Solution: Replace turbocharger
     * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
     * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8G-DCT Dual-Clutch Transmission**
   * **Shifting Hesitations or Jerks:**
     * Symptoms: Rough shifts, delayed acceleration
     * Solution: Re-calibration, software update
     * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
     * Recalls: NHTSA ID: 22V-364

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Faulty Battery:**
     * Symptoms: Difficulty starting, electrical system malfunctions
     * Solution: Replace battery
     * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
     * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Shortages:**
     * Symptoms: Dimming lights, intermittent instrument cluster, fuses blowing
     * Solution: Inspect wiring harness, replace faulty components
     * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
     * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Creaks:**
     * Symptoms: Rattles, squeaks when roof is open or closed
     * Solution: Lubricate tracks, adjust roof alignment
     * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
     * Recalls: None Found
* **Center Console Trim Pieces Rattle:**
     * Symptoms: Loose or rattling trim around center console
     * Solution: Replace or tighten trim pieces
     * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
     * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLA35 AMG is a high-performance crossover with a powerful engine and thrilling driving dynamics. However, it has several potential issues related to the engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior trim. The severity of these issues varies from minor inconveniences to more costly repairs. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and consider the estimated repair costs before making a purchase decision.
