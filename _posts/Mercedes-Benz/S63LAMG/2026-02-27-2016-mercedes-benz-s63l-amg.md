# 2016 Mercedes-Benz S63L AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S63L AMG delivers breathtaking luxury paired with supercar-level performance from its hand-built biturbo V8. However, prospective buyers should approach this high-end flagship with eyes wide open regarding maintenance costs and complex electronics. While powertrain complaints are relatively sparse, owner reports highlight recurring concerns with exterior glass components, suspension wheels, and specific safety items.

---

## Engine

### 5.5L Biturbo V8 Engine

The 5.5-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces an immense 577 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, offering effortless acceleration. It is generally robust when maintained meticulously, though auxiliary emissions and exhaust components can occasionally prompt unexpected service visits.

**Catalytic Converter Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine warning lights illuminate on the dashboard, and a reputable service provider may determine the factory converters have failed prematurely.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the failed catalytic converter units.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 7-Speed Automatic

The 7-speed AMG Speedshift multi-clutch automatic transmission routes high torque to the rear wheels or 4MATIC system with rapid shifts. It is known to perform well dynamically, though routine fluid changes are critical for long-term health.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

Large staggered wheels and low-profile run-flat tires provide sharp handling but make the vehicle vulnerable to rough road impacts, occasionally leading to bent or cracked rims.

**Cracked Wheels and Tire Blowouts**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden tire air pressure loss, bulging sidewalls, or structural cracks discovered on factory alloy wheels during routine inspections.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged low-profile run-flat tires and have alloy wheels professionally repaired or replaced.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The electronic power steering setup provides precise control at high speeds, though rare intermittent failures have been reported by owners.

**Intermittent Steering Lockout**
- **Symptoms:** Power steering failure warning lights illuminate on the instrument cluster, and the steering wheel temporarily seizes or becomes extremely difficult to turn while driving.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic steering system and related control modules diagnosed immediately by an authorized technician.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof and Windshield

Extensive glass surfaces define the cabin's airy feel, but owners have experienced rare instances of spontaneous stress fractures.

**Spontaneous Glass Cracking and Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A loud bang is heard while driving or parked, followed by a sudden structural crack or complete shattering of the panoramic sunroof glass or windshield.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the vehicle frame for alignment or body torque issues.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety restraint systems are critical components that should be thoroughly tested during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure all latches and retractors function properly.

**Seat Belt Buckle and Retractor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Front passenger seat belts fail to latch securely into the buckle, or rear seat belts become stuck and refuse to retract properly.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the malfunctioning seat belt buckle assembly or retractor mechanism.
