The 2022 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500XD is a heavy-duty van popular for commercial use, conversions, and recreational vehicles. It's appreciated for its spaciousness, customizable configurations, and robust build quality. Some criticisms include its higher price point, potential for electronic issues, and ride quality when unladen. For the 2022 model year, the Sprinter 3500XD typically comes with either a 2.0L 4-cylinder turbo diesel engine producing 161 hp and 266 lb-ft of torque or a 3.0L V6 turbo diesel engine producing 188 hp and 325 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are commonly paired with a 9G-TRONIC 9-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**2.0L 4-Cylinder Turbo Diesel**

*   **Turbocharger Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive black smoke, unusual turbocharger noises (whining or screeching), check engine light.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**3.0L V6 Turbo Diesel**

*   **Oil Cooler Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks near the front of the engine, oil consumption increases, coolant contamination (in severe cases), and possible overheating.
    *   Solution: Replacing the oil cooler and associated seals/gaskets.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **EGR Valve Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, poor acceleration, increased emissions, check engine light.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the EGR valve.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic**

*   **Harsh Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Noticeable jolts during gear changes, delays in shifting, particularly between lower gears.
    *   Solution: Software updates, transmission fluid flush, or valve body replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $3,000+ depending on the severity
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Rear Suspension Sag (Under Load)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Excessive sagging in the rear suspension when carrying heavy loads.
    *   Solution: Upgrading to heavier-duty leaf springs, adding helper springs, or installing air suspension.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Sensor Malfunctions (Various)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Intermittent warning lights, inaccurate readings on gauges (e.g., temperature, fuel), and potential drivability issues.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis and replacement of faulty sensors (e.g., ABS sensor, crankshaft position sensor).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+ per sensor.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Infotainment System Glitches**
    *   Description/Symptoms: System freezes, unresponsive touchscreen, Bluetooth connectivity problems, navigation errors.
    *   Solution: Software updates, replacement of the infotainment unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Door Lock Actuator Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Doors not locking or unlocking, actuators making noise or not moving.
    *   Solution: Replacing the door lock actuator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Sliding Door Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing the sliding door, sticking, unusual noises, and latching problems.
    *   Solution: Adjusting the door alignment, lubricating the rollers and hinges, or replacing the latch mechanism.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **HVAC Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent airflow, temperature fluctuations, failure of the air conditioning or heating system, and unusual noises from the vents.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the HVAC system components, such as the blower motor, compressor, or control module.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500XD, while generally reliable, can experience some common issues. Engine problems such as turbocharger and oil cooler leaks on the different engine options can be relatively expensive to address. Electrical problems are common across many systems. Although there are no major recalls for the vehicle, issues with sensors and infotainment systems can become aggravating. It's important to note that the severity and cost of these issues can vary based on usage and maintenance history.
