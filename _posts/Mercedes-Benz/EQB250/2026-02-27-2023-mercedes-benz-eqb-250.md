# 2023 Mercedes-Benz EQB 250 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz EQB 250 offers an entry point into luxury all-electric driving, featuring a practical boxy layout and an available third-row seat. As a relatively new addition to the EV market, buyers looking at used models should carefully evaluate general electronics and high-voltage charging systems, though formal owner complaint patterns remain sparse.

---

## Engine

### Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor (Front-Wheel Drive)

The EQB 250 utilizes a single front-mounted permanent magnet synchronous electric motor producing around 188 horsepower. It delivers smooth, quiet acceleration suited for daily commuting with minimal moving parts compared to internal combustion alternatives.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Fixed Ratio Direct Drive

Power is routed to the front wheels via a single-speed automatic reduction gearbox. This direct-drive transmission offers seamless power delivery without traditional gear shifts and requires very little mechanical maintenance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V050000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=EQB+250&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The high voltage battery may fail internally and lead to a vehicle fire while parked or driving. A vehicle fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the battery management system software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz EQB 250 is an appealing option for used EV shoppers wanting luxury features and compact utility. While powertrain mechanics are generally robust, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's electrical systems, software versions, and charging capabilities before finalizing a purchase.
