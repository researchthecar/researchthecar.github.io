# 2024 Mercedes-Benz EQB 250 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz EQB 250 is an all-electric luxury compact SUV offering versatile seating and modern tech. As a relatively new entry in the luxury EV space, prospective used-car buyers should consider its electric powertrain options and overall build quality when shopping on the secondary market.

---

## Engine

### Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor (Front-Wheel Drive)

This single-motor electric drive system produces approximately 188 horsepower and delivers instant torque to the front wheels. It provides a smooth, quiet daily driving experience typical of modern luxury electric vehicles.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Fixed Gear Transmission

A direct-drive single-speed transmission channels power efficiently from the electric motor to the wheels. It eliminates traditional gear shift hesitation for seamless acceleration.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V050000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=EQB+250&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The high voltage battery may fail internally and lead to a vehicle fire while parked or driving. A vehicle fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the battery management system software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz EQB 250 presents a compelling option for buyers looking to enter the luxury electric vehicle market with practical space and refinement. As with any newer electric model, a thorough inspection of the high-voltage battery system and onboard electronics is recommended before purchase.
