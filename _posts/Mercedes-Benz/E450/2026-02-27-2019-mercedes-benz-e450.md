# 2019 Mercedes-Benz E450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Mercedes-Benz E450, buyers can generally expect a luxurious driving experience backed by robust powertrain engineering. However, owner reports highlight a few recurring electrical, safety, and chassis concerns that are worth noting during your pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering strong, smooth acceleration. It is generally regarded as a solid performer when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear shifts and optimizes fuel efficiency across highway and city driving. Overall, it operates reliably with routine fluid changes.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

Suspension components generally hold up well, though large wheels and low-profile tires are susceptible to road hazard damage.

**Cracked Rims and Tire Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Low tire pressure warning lights illuminate unexpectedly, or drivers experience sudden sidewall blowouts on low-profile run-flat tires.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels for structural cracks during a test drive and replace damaged wheels or compromised tires.

## Electrical

### Back Over Prevention

Driver assistance and safety features add considerable peace of mind, though certain electronic displays require verification.

**Backup Camera Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera display cuts out while the vehicle is in reverse, or shows a distorted image that obstructs visibility.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates or consult a dealer regarding applicable rear camera recalls and wiring harnesses.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Passive safety systems function reliably overall, but individual mechanical latch checks are recommended during a used car inspection.

**Seat Belt Latch and Retractor Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers or passengers report issues with seat belt fastening indicators or improper latching mechanics.
- **Solution:** Inspect all seat belt assemblies and buckles for proper engagement and warning sensor operation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V676000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=E450&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the spoiler detaches while driving, it can become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and correct the mounting of the spoiler, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V849000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=E450&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have a tailgate mounted spoiler that may not be properly attached and therefore could detach while driving. If the tailgate mounted spoiler detaches while driving, it can become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and correct the mounting of the spoiler, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V761000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=E450&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The Central Powertrain Controller (CPC) control unit could reset while driving, possibly causing an engine stall. An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the CPC control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V839000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=E450&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A correctly fastened seatbelt may be inaccurately detected as being unfastened, preventing the seat belt pre-tensioners from activating in the event of a crash. If the seat belt tensioning does not activate in the event of a crash, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the seat belt buckles, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V449000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=E450&modelYear=2019)**

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz E450 offers a compelling blend of luxury, technology, and strong performance. While most mechanical systems prove durable, prospective buyers should carefully inspect the wheels, backup camera system, and electrical components before finalizing a purchase.
