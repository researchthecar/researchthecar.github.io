# 2021 Mercedes-Benz E450

### Overview
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz E450, a mid-size luxury sedan and coupe, generally presents a strong profile for reliability, reflecting Mercedes-Benz's commitment to quality engineering. It typically offers a comfortable ride, advanced technology, and a refined driving experience. While individual ownership experiences can vary, the model is designed with robust components and sophisticated systems. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, aiming to provide a clear picture of potential concerns for owners and prospective buyers. The E450 is generally well-regarded for its powertrain and overall build quality, though like any complex vehicle, specific areas may warrant attention.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz E450 demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, particularly concerning its core powertrain components like the 3.0L turbocharged inline-6 engine and the 9G-TRONIC automatic transmission, for which no widespread issues are reported. The primary areas that may require attention are related to the optional Air Body Control suspension, which can experience leaks, and occasional electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches or premature 12V battery failure. These issues, while not universal, are the most commonly cited technical concerns beyond standard maintenance. Overall, the E450 is a solid choice for those seeking a luxurious and dependable vehicle, provided potential buyers are aware of and prepared for these specific, albeit infrequent, potential repair items.
