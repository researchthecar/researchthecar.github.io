# 2024 Mercedes-Benz E450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz E450 represents the pinnacle of modern luxury and technology, featuring advanced driver-assistance features, rich digital displays, and a robust powertrain. However, as a sophisticated luxury vehicle relying heavily on complex electronics and integrated software, owners have reported various electrical, safety, and powertrain hiccups. Prospective buyers should carefully review service histories and ensure all software updates have been applied.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (M256) with EQ Boost

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine utilizes a 48-volt mild-hybrid system (EQ Boost) to deliver smooth power and efficient performance, typically producing around 375 horsepower. While generally praised for its refined delivery, early owner reports highlight rare electrical or propulsion glitches requiring professional attention.

**Sudden Engine Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** Complete loss of drive power and unexpected engine shutdown while operating at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic evaluation by a certified technician to inspect electrical, fuel, and propulsion systems.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission comes paired with the E450's all-wheel-drive (4MATIC) system, offering seamless and quick gear shifts. No widespread recurring complaint patterns have emerged regarding this gearbox for this model year.

## Brakes

### Electronic Brake Control System

Advanced safety sensors integrate with the braking system to prevent collisions, though electronic interference can occasionally trigger unintended interventions.

**Unexpected Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle experiences unexpected automated braking events or slow-speed deceleration anomalies during routine parking maneuvers.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's sensor calibration and electrical control modules checked for updates or faults.

## Steering

### Electronic Steering Assist

Power steering and electronic assist modules tie heavily into the vehicle's overarching driver safety network.

**Active Emergency Assistance Faults**
- **Symptoms:** An 'Active Emergency Assistance Imperative' warning message displays on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Requires a software update performed by an authorized service center.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Instrument Cluster

The electrical architecture governs the extensive MBUX display panels and cabin systems, though software bugs can occasionally disrupt screen operations.

**Blank Screens and Infotainment Reboots**
- **Symptoms:** The digital instrument panel and infotainment screen suddenly go blank upon starting the vehicle, lacking normal warning or display indicators.
- **Solution:** Software updates or module resets performed by a dealership are typically required to restore proper functionality.

## Safety

### Driver Assistance and Collision Avoidance

Driver assistance suites provide cutting-edge safety features, but overly sensitive radar and camera components can result in false positive interventions.

**Phantom Emergency Braking and Lane-Keep Errors**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic emergency braking function or lane-keeping assist triggers unexpectedly without nearby hazards, or fails to disengage when commanded.
- **Solution:** Dealer software updates and sensor recalibration can help resolve persistent ADAS glitches.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V224000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=E450&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will check the transmission wiring harness routing and correct it, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz E450 delivers extraordinary comfort, strong inline-six performance, and state-of-the-art cabin technology. However, its heavy reliance on complex software leaves it vulnerable to electronic glitches, ranging from blank infotainment screens to unexpected driver-assistance interventions. Used-car shoppers should prioritize models with complete dealer service histories and verify that all software recalls and updates have been properly executed.
