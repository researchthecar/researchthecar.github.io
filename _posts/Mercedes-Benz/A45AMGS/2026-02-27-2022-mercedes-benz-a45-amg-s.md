# 2022 Mercedes-Benz A45 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When evaluating the 2022 Mercedes-Benz A45 AMG S, buyers are looking at a high-performance luxury hatchback boasting immense power from a compact turbocharged engine paired with an advanced all-wheel-drive system. While owner feedback is limited, prospective purchasers should remain attentive to potential mechanical and body hardware concerns during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M139)

The hand-built 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces roughly 416 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering performance. Buyers should ensure a thorough powertrain inspection is completed, as isolated owner reports indicate potential internal component failures.

**Premature Piston Failure and Engine Stall**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may die or shut down unexpectedly, sputter, shake excessively, and experience a loss of power.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnostic evaluation and potential replacement of pistons or the complete engine assembly.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and sends power to the 4MATIC+ all-wheel-drive system. It generally operates smoothly under spirited driving conditions, though routine fluid maintenance is essential for longevity.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Assembly

Exterior body hardware and glass components generally feature a modern finish, but buyers should carefully inspect panoramic roof panels for any signs of structural stress.

**Shattering Sunroof Glass**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass may shatter spontaneously while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for stress cracks or defects and replace the entire glass panel if compromised.

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz A45 AMG S offers extraordinary hot-hatch performance wrapped in a premium package. While reported issues are sparse, prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the high-output turbocharged engine and exterior glass integrity.
