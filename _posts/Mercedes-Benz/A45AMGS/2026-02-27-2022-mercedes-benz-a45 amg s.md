# 2022 Mercedes-Benz A45 AMG S

### Overview
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz A45 AMG S, a high-performance compact sedan, is engineered for exhilarating driving dynamics and luxury. While its potent powertrain and advanced technology are key selling points, like any performance vehicle, understanding its reliability is crucial for owners. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern and their typical resolutions. The A45 AMG S generally exhibits strong build quality, but specific components, particularly those under high stress, may require attention over time. This report aims to offer a clear, data-driven perspective on its technical reliability.

---


## Engine

### M139 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4
**Turbocharger Lag/Boost Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced acceleration, inconsistent power delivery, audible whistling from turbo.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of wastegate actuator, boost leaks, or turbocharger itself. Repair or replacement of faulty components.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2500

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant loss, engine overheating, sweet smell from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of hoses, seals, or radiator. Potential water pump replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $900


## Transmission

### AMG SPEEDSHIFT DCT 8-Speed
**Harsh Shifting/Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Abrupt gear changes, particularly at low speeds or during aggressive driving. Occasional hesitation.
- **Solution:** Software updates for transmission control module, fluid flush and replacement, or potential clutch pack inspection/replacement.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1500

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible fluid under the vehicle, burning smell, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Inspection and tightening of drain/fill plugs, replacement of seals or transmission cooler lines.
- **Cost:** $200 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz A45 AMG S demonstrates a commendable level of technical reliability for a vehicle in its performance class. The core powertrain components, the M139 engine and SPEEDSHIFT DCT transmission, are robust but can experience issues related to high-performance demands, such as turbocharger performance and transmission shifting characteristics. These are often addressable through software updates or component servicing. Electrical systems, particularly ADAS sensors, may require occasional attention. Overall, beyond expected maintenance for a performance car, the A45 AMG S is a well-built machine with no pervasive widespread defects reported, making it a reliable choice for enthusiasts prioritizing performance and luxury.
