# 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG, a high-performance luxury SUV, generally offers a robust and engaging driving experience. As a performance-oriented model, it combines potent V8 power with the comfort and refinement expected from Mercedes-Benz. While the GLE63 AMG is built with quality components, like any high-performance vehicle, certain areas may require more attention due to the demands placed upon them. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of focus for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential replacement of turbocharger seals, lines, or the entire unit.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,500

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, sweet smell, low coolant level.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair/replacement of hoses, radiator, water pump, or seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,200


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift Plus Automatic
**Transmission Shudder/Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Jerking or shuddering during gear changes, delayed engagement, rough shifting.
- **Solution:** Software updates, transmission fluid and filter service, or potential internal component repair/rebuild.
- **Cost:** $400 - $2,500


## Suspension

### Active Curve System (Optional)
**Hydraulic Leaks/Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Increased body roll, clunking noises, warning lights related to suspension.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic lines, actuators, or the hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $3,000


### Standard Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, compressor running excessively, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or seals.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, dead pixels, intermittent blackouts.
- **Solution:** Software updates, or replacement of the display unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dying unexpectedly, difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG is a powerful and luxurious SUV with a generally solid reliability record for its performance class. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to its high-performance components, such as the twin-turbo V8 engine's turbochargers and cooling system, and the advanced transmission. Optional suspension systems like the Active Curve System and air suspension can also present unique repair needs. Electrical issues, particularly with the infotainment system and potential battery drain, are also noted. While not immune to problems, most issues are addressable with proper diagnosis and repair. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs are crucial for maintaining the GLE63 AMG's performance and longevity.
