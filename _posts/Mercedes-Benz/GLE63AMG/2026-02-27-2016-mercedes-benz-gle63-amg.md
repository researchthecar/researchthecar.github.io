# 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury performance SUV, looking closely at owner feedback and potential mechanical pain points helps set realistic expectations. This model delivers commanding twin-turbo power and sophisticated cabin amenities, but prospective owners should carefully verify its mechanical and electrical health during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8 Engine

Equipped with a potent 5.5-liter bi-turbo V8 producing upwards of 550 to 577 horsepower, this high-performance engine delivers blistering acceleration. However, its complex under-hood packaging requires diligent maintenance and routine checks for fuel and cooling system integrity.

**Fuel Injector Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fuel leaks around the intake manifold or cracked fuel injectors spraying gasoline, potentially creating fire hazards.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines and injectors immediately; replace damaged fuel injectors and seals.

**Engine Stalling and Fuel Pump Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Engine stalling unexpectedly while driving, loss of motive power, or water accumulation affecting the trunk area and fuel pump.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel pump assembly, clear any blocked drainage areas, and replace failing fuel pumps.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift Automatic Transmission

Featuring a sporty 7-speed automatic transmission paired with an all-wheel-drive system, it offers crisp gear changes suited for high-output driving. Buyers should verify smooth operation across all drive modes during a test drive.

**Unintended Neutral or Park Shift Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle unexpectedly displays transmission warnings, shifts into neutral or park on its own while moving, or rolls unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic shifter module and transmission control unit diagnosed and updated by a qualified technician.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

**Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering assistance accompanied by dashboard warnings, making the steering wheel exceptionally heavy and difficult to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Inspect the power steering pump, electrical connections, and associated steering module for faults.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

**Dim Headlights**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights appear excessively dim during nighttime driving, significantly reducing forward visibility on unlit roads.
- **Solution:** Check bulb output, ballast integrity, and headlight lens clarity; upgrade or replace failing lighting components.
