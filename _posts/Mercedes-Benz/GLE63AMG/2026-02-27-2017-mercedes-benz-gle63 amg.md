# 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG, a high-performance luxury SUV, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting its premium engineering and robust construction. While its powerful AMG-tuned engine and sophisticated drivetrain are designed for demanding use, like any performance vehicle, specific components can experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related problems reported by owners and technicians, excluding routine maintenance. The GLE63 AMG aims to balance exhilarating performance with the practicality expected of an SUV, and its reliability data reflects this ambition, with most systems performing commendably.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8 (M157)
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased exhaust smoke.
- **Solution:** Replacement of turbocharger unit(s).
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant loss, sweet smell from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hoses, gaskets, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT PLUS 7G-TRONIC
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping gears, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,200


## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,200 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust ride height, vehicle stuck in a low position, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,300


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, reboots randomly, or displays errors.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or circuits causing drain.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG demonstrates a generally solid reliability record for a high-performance luxury SUV. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the advanced systems, such as the AIRMATIC air suspension and the twin-turbo V8 engine's ancillary components like turbochargers and cooling system. Transmission issues, particularly with the control module and torque converter, are also noted. Electrical gremlins, especially within the infotainment system and occasional battery drain, can occur. However, many owners report a positive ownership experience with proper maintenance. Addressing potential issues proactively can help maintain the vehicle's performance and longevity.
