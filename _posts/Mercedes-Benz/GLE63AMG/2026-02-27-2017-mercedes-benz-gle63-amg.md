# 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG offers thrilling performance and luxury, but prospective buyers should carefully weigh its complexity. While owners praise its exceptional power and refined cabin, federal safety and owner complaint databases highlight potential trouble spots ranging from electrical glitches to rare structural and steering concerns. Being a high-performance luxury SUV, routine maintenance and out-of-warranty repairs can demand a significant financial commitment.

---

## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8

The hand-built 5.5-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers immense horsepower and torque, providing supercar-like acceleration for this heavy luxury SUV. It requires meticulous maintenance history and premium fuel, and while robust when cared for, potential complexity out of warranty demands close inspection.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift Plus 7G-Tronic 7-Speed Automatic

The 7-speed automatic transmission features sport-tuned shift mapping designed to handle high torque output seamlessly. Fluid maintenance is vital to ensure longevity and smooth gear transitions throughout the vehicle's lifespan.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake system feedback points to occasional anomalies with automated hold features and stopping performance.

**Brake Hold and Stopping Irregularities**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted unexpected vehicle movement while the automatic hold brake function is engaged, or concerns regarding overall stopping responsiveness.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake fluid, master cylinder, brake booster, and electronic parking brake sensors for proper calibration.

## Steering

### Electronic Power Steering

Steering complaints center around rare electronic assist failures that suddenly increase steering effort.

**Power Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report a sudden warning message on the instrument cluster stating 'Power Steering Malfunction' accompanied by extremely stiff steering that makes the vehicle difficult to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic scanning of the power steering control module and potential replacement of the steering gear assembly or electrical components.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Lighting systems can occasionally suffer from illumination adequacy complaints or mechanical adjustment errors.

**Headlight Performance and Adjustment Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note inadequate illumination during nighttime and adverse weather driving, alongside low-beam headlights failing to adjust properly.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight housings, leveling sensors, and bulbs; replace faulty assemblies or control modules as needed.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety component complaints are minimal, occasionally involving sensitive seat belt warning indicators.

**Seat Belt Warning Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated seat belt warning lights on the dashboard even when occupants are properly buckled.
- **Solution:** Inspect buckle sensors and wiring harnesses; replace faulty seat belt receiver assemblies or sensors.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG is an exhilarating performance machine, but buyers should approach with a thorough pre-purchase inspection. While the twin-turbo V8 powertrain is generally sturdy when maintained, isolated owner reports point to potential electrical, steering, and braking issues that can be expensive to resolve. Securing a detailed service history and setting aside an adequate maintenance fund is highly recommended for prospective owners.
