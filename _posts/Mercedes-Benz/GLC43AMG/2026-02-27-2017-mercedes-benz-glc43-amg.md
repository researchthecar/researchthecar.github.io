# 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLC43 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLC43 AMG offers a compelling blend of luxury, high performance, and distinctive styling. However, prospective buyers should carefully review maintenance records and potential owner-reported mechanical concerns. While luxurious and powerful, used luxury performance vehicles require diligent upkeep to avoid unexpected repair expenses.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces roughly 362 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration and responsive performance. Buyers should monitor for potential fuel odors and crankcase ventilation faults as mileage accumulates.

**Fuel Leak or Odor**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report a strong smell of gasoline or raw fuel coming from the engine bay or around the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines and related components for leaks and replace faulty parts as required.

**Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light, diagnostic code P052E, and rough running or stalling.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty positive crankcase ventilation regular valve.
- **Cost:** $1,000+

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and rapid gear changes suited to the AMG performance profile. While generally robust, owners have occasionally noted internal control component failures that restrict gear selection.

**Valve Body Control Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission fails to shift beyond 5th gear, creating dangerous driving conditions on highways.
- **Solution:** Inspect the transmission valve body control unit and replace it if malfunctioning.

## Steering

### Power Steering

**Power Steering Assist Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Power Steering Malfunction warning light illuminates on the dashboard, leading to sudden and heavy steering effort.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the electrical power steering system and replace failing components.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Mirrors

**Panaromic Sunroof Off Track**
- **Symptoms:** Grinding noises when operating the roof, or the panoramic sunroof panel going off track and failing to close properly.
- **Solution:** Inspect sunroof tracks and cables, and realign or replace damaged hardware components.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

**Seat Belt Latch Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** Seat belts remain latched when parked and refuse to release smoothly upon pressing the button.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat belt latch mechanism and replace the buckle assembly if internal binding persists.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLC43 AMG is an exciting and luxurious performance crossover, but used buyers should approach with careful inspection habits.
