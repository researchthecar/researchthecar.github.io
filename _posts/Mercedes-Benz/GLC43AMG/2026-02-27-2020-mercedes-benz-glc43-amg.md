# 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLC43 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLC43 AMG delivers high-performance luxury with twin-turbo power, but prospective buyers should carefully review potential mechanical and electrical hiccups. While owner complaints cover a broad spectrum of minor and major issues, knowing what to look out for can help ensure a more confident purchasing experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine puts out roughly 385 horsepower, providing brisk acceleration and robust performance. While generally powerful and refined, owners have reported isolated issues concerning oil pressure sensors and pump performance as mileage builds.

**Oil Pressure Control Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of the check engine light accompanied by diagnostic trouble code P06DA.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty oil pressure control sensor or solenoid.
- **Cost:** $200

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 9-speed automatic transmission paired with AMG-tuned all-wheel drive, this unit offers quick, responsive shifts suited for sporty driving. Although no dominant recurring mechanical failures stand out in early owner feedback, regular fluid maintenance is vital.

## Electrical

### Back Over Prevention

Safety equipment generally performs well, though occasional intermittent glitches with the backup camera system have been noted by drivers.

**Backup Camera Blank Screen**
- **Symptoms:** The reverse camera display fails to activate or displays a blank screen when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Inspect the camera module, wiring harness, or software controller for faults.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical complaints often center on exterior lighting and moisture intrusion, which can temporarily disable forward illumination.

**Headlight Moisture Ingress and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Condensation building up inside the headlamp housing leading to electrical shorting, warning messages, and complete headlight failure.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the affected headlamp assembly and sealing components.

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior body components are mostly robust, though a notable subset of owners have experienced spontaneous panoramic sunroof glass failure.

**Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof unexpectedly shatters or explodes while driving under normal conditions without external impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered glass panel and inspect the frame assembly for alignment or pressure issues.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLC43 AMG blends exhilarating performance with upscale luxury, but buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to the condition of the exterior lighting for moisture, test all backup camera functions, and verify that the sunroof and engine sensors are operating properly to avoid unexpected repair bills.
