**Introduction:**
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz SLC180 is a compact luxury roadster powered by a 1.6L turbocharged inline-4 engine mated to a 7-speed automatic transmission, delivering 156 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. The SLC180 is praised for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and responsive handling, while common complaints include its limited rear-seat space and relatively high fuel consumption.
