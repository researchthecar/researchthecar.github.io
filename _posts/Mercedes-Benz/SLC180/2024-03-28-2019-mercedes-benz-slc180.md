**Introduction:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz SLC180 is a compact luxury roadster offered with a single engine option: a 1.6-liter turbocharged inline-four that produces 154 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a six-speed manual transmission, while a nine-speed automatic is available as an option. The SLC180 is praised for its stylish design, responsive handling, and comfortable interior. However, some owners have reported several issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **1.6L Turbocharged Inline-Four**
    * **Misfiring**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine hesitation, rough idle, reduced power output
        * Solution: Replace faulty spark plugs or ignition coils
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * **Oil Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil stains underneath the vehicle, low oil levels
        * Solution: Replace leaking gaskets or seals
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * **Timing Chain Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or ticking noises from the engine, reduced performance
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * **Recalls:**
        * **NHTSA Campaign Number:** 19V-748
        * **Description:** Fuel pump may fail, causing the engine to stall
        * **Fix:** Replace the fuel pump

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Manual**
    * **Grinding Gears**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grating noises
        * Solution: Replace worn clutch or gear components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Nine-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Delays**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delayed shifts, especially during acceleration
        * Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission valve body
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, difficulty starting the vehicle
        * Solution: Replace faulty battery or alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Electrical Faults**
        * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning dashboard displays, intermittent electrical problems
        * Solution: Inspect and repair electrical wiring or components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

* **Cracked Windshield**
        * Description/Symptoms: Visible cracks or damage to the windshield
        * Solution: Replace the windshield
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions**
        * Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent temperature, AC or heater not functioning properly
        * Solution: Repair or replace faulty climate control components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Summary:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz SLC180 has received mixed reviews from owners. While its stylish design and responsive handling are praised, several issues have been reported. Engine problems, particularly related to misfiring and oil leaks, are the most concerning. Transmission issues, such as grinding gears and shifting delays, have also been reported. Electrical faults and interior climate control malfunctions are less common but can still be frustrating. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the required repairs. It's recommended for owners to address these issues promptly to prevent further damage or inconvenience.
