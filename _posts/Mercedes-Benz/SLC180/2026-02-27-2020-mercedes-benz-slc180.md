# 2020 Mercedes-Benz SLC180 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a 2020 Mercedes-Benz SLC180, buyers are looking at a stylish luxury roadster that combines sporty handling with open-top cruising. Because this model represents the final year of the SLC-class production run, many buyers expect a refined and mature platform. Reviewing owner feedback and available vehicle data helps highlight what to expect from its powertrain options and overall condition during your used car search.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L Turbocharged Inline-4 Engine

The 1.6-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 154 horsepower and is paired with rear-wheel drive. It offers efficient performance for daily driving, though buyers should verify regular maintenance history.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and rapid gear changes tailored to optimize fuel economy and performance. It generally proves dependable when fluid changes are maintained according to schedule.
