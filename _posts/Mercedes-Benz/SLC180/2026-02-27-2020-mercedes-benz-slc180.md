### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz SLC180 demonstrates good overall reliability. The powertrain (engine and transmission) is robust, with no significant widespread issues reported. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, less commonly, premature battery failure. The retractable hardtop mechanism, while generally reliable, can occasionally experience sticking or slow operation. The suspension, exterior panels, and interior components are typically free from major defects. With regular maintenance and attention to potential minor electrical or top mechanism issues, the SLC180 should provide a dependable luxury driving experience.
