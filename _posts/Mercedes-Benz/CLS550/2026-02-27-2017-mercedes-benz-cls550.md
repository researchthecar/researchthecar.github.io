# 2017 Mercedes-Benz CLS550 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz CLS550 blends striking four-door coupe styling with robust V8 performance and a luxurious cabin. While general powertrain reliability is typically strong for this era, used car buyers should inspect the vehicle carefully for potential ownership complaints related to secondary components like factory wheels and tires.

---

## Engine

### 4.7L Bi-Turbo V8

This 4.7-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 402 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless acceleration. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though buyers should watch for oil leaks and complex cooling system maintenance as mileage climbs.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 9-speed automatic transmission, this setup provides smooth and efficient power delivery across various driving modes. Regular fluid changes are vital to preserving shift quality and long-term durability in this luxury application.

## Suspension

### Wheels

Handles ride quality and wheel mounting, though high-performance low-profile wheel configurations can leave rims susceptible to damage.

**Factory Wheel Cracking and Tire Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported sudden tire blowouts and unexpected cracking on factory alloy wheels without notable impacts from potholes or road hazards.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels thoroughly for structural hairline cracks during a pre-purchase inspection and replace damaged rims as necessary.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=CLS550&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz CLS550 offers high-end performance, comfort, and luxury features, but prospective buyers should perform a thorough mechanical and structural inspection. Pay close attention to factory wheel integrity and tire condition during your test drive to avoid unexpected out-of-pocket expenses.
