# 2017 Mercedes-Benz CLS550

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz CLS550, a luxury grand tourer, generally offers a high level of refinement and performance. While it excels in comfort and driving dynamics, like any complex vehicle, it can experience specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and prospective buyers. We will examine the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior systems to offer a comprehensive reliability picture.

---


## Engine

### 4.7L Twin-Turbo V8 (M278)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, whistling noise from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the affected wastegate actuator or turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Oil Leaks from Valve Covers or Oil Pan**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or oil pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Transmission

### 7G-TRONIC 7-Speed Automatic
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning light, limp mode.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the Transmission Control Module.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping gears, unusual noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air struts.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200 per corner

**Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle not raising to normal height, constant compressor running, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsive**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive to touch or buttons, system reboots randomly.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies frequently, car won't start after sitting for a short period.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or components causing the drain.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz CLS550 is a capable luxury vehicle, but potential owners should be aware of specific reliability concerns. The 4.7L twin-turbo V8 can experience issues with wastegate actuators and oil leaks. The 7G-TRONIC transmission may develop problems with its control module or torque converter. The AIRMATIC air suspension system is prone to leaks and compressor failures, which can be costly to repair. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the COMAND infotainment system and parasitic battery drain, are also noted. While many components are robust, addressing these potential issues proactively can help maintain the CLS550's luxury experience and value.
