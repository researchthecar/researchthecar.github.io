# 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE400 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE400 offers a refined luxury midsize SUV experience backed by robust powertrain options. However, prospective buyers should review known owner complaints concerning fuel system components, steering assist operation, and intermittent electrical or drivetrain glitches.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine delivers smooth power and strong acceleration, typical of Mercedes-Benz luxury performance. While generally robust, buyers should inspect for potential fuel injector or fuel pump concerns.

**Fuel Injector Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fuel leaks or spraying gasoline around the engine compartment, potentially leading to smoke and fire hazards.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines and injectors, and replace damaged or leaking fuel injectors.

**Engine Stalling and Fuel Pump Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle suddenly loses motive power, stalls while driving, or fails to start due to fuel delivery problems.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the fuel pump and related electrical circuits; replace failed fuel pumps or clear blocked drainage areas.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission provides smooth gear shifts suited to luxury cruising, though electronic shifter and sensor glitches occasionally require diagnostic attention.

**Unintended Neutral Shift and Parking Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle displays warning messages like 'Car Not in Park' or independently shifts into neutral or park while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission electronic control modules, gear selector sensors, and software calibrations.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The power steering system delivers responsive handling under normal conditions, but sudden assist failures have been reported by owners.

**Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering assistance accompanied by dashboard warning messages, making the steering wheel exceptionally heavy and difficult to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Inspect power steering components, electronic assist motors, and wiring harnesses for faults.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

The electrical system powers advanced comfort and safety features, but minor complaints involve exterior lighting performance.

**Headlight Dimness**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights appear excessively dim during nighttime driving, reducing road visibility.
- **Solution:** Check headlight bulb alignment, output strength, and lens clarity; upgrade or replace failing lighting assemblies.
