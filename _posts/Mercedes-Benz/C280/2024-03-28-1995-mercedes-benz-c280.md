**Introduction:**

The 1995 Mercedes-Benz C280 is a compact luxury sedan that combines performance and sophistication. It features a powerful 2.8-liter V6 engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. Praised for its comfortable ride, stylish exterior, and high-quality interior, the C280 has also been noted for some potential issues.

**Engine:**

**2.8L V6**

* **Vanos System Fault:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, rough idling, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace faulty Vanos unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from valve cover gasket
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Transmission:**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, slippage during acceleration
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, transmission may not shift into all gears
    * Solution: Replace faulty shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500

**Suspension:**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking, rattling, vibration while driving
    * Solution: Replace lower control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500

**Electrical:**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim headlights, flickering lights
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Check for parasitic drain, replace faulty battery or starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300

**Exterior:**

* **Headlight Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights not turning on or intermittent operation
    * Solution: Replace headlight switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300

**Interior:**

* **Sunroof Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof not opening or closing properly
    * Solution: Replace sunroof motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Summary:**

Overall, the 1995 Mercedes-Benz C280 is a reliable and well-built vehicle. However, like any car, it can experience some issues over time. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some being minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more significant and require more extensive repairs. It is important to note that this list is not exhaustive, and other issues may arise depending on the age and condition of the specific vehicle.
