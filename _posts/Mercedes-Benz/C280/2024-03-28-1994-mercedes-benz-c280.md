**Introduction:**

The 1994 Mercedes-Benz C280 is a luxurious mid-size sedan powered by a smooth and powerful V6 engine. It boasts a comfortable interior, refined handling, and a wide range of standard features. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of potential issues.

**Engine**

* **M104 2.8L V6**
    * **Camshaft Timing Chain Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or ticking noises from the engine, reduced power, and stalling.
        * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain, guides, and tensioners.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Manifold Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, decreased acceleration, and possible engine misfires.
        * Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **722.4 5-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Problems:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears or slipping gears when accelerating.
        * Solution: Repair or replacement of the transmission solenoid or valve body.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, dashboard warning lights, and reduced battery power.
        * Solution: Replacement of the alternator and battery.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the engine, grinding noises.
        * Solution: Replacement of the starter.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening the door from the outside.
        * Solution: Replacement of the door handle assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Heater Core Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Foggy windows, reduced heat output.
        * Solution: Replacement of the heater core.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Cruise Control Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Inability to engage or use cruise control.
        * Solution: Repair or replacement of the cruise control module or switch.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1994 Mercedes-Benz C280 has some potential issues, including camshaft timing chain failure, intake manifold failure, and electrical problems. These issues can be costly to fix, but overall, the C280 is a reliable vehicle with a comfortable driving experience.
