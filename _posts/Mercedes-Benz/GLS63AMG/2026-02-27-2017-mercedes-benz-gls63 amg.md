# 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG, a full-size luxury SUV, offers a potent blend of performance and opulence. Powered by a formidable V8 engine, it delivers exhilarating acceleration and a commanding presence on the road. While generally well-regarded for its luxury appointments and powerful drivetrain, like any high-performance vehicle, it can experience specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and prospective buyers. The GLS63 AMG aims to balance its performance capabilities with the comfort and practicality expected of a flagship SUV.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8 (M157)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, boost leaks, check engine light illumination, rattling noise from turbo area.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty wastegate actuator or complete turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Oil Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Oil in coolant reservoir, coolant loss, potential engine overheating, milky residue in oil.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the engine oil cooler.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, harsh shifting, shuddering during acceleration, potential transmission fluid leaks.
- **Solution:** Rebuilding or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting behavior, failure to engage gears, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1100


## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air struts.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust ride height, constant compressor operation, vehicle sagging.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1300

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG is a powerful and luxurious SUV that generally exhibits good reliability for its class. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the advanced AIRMATIC air suspension system, with potential for leaks or compressor issues. The twin-turbo V8 engine, while potent, can experience issues with wastegate actuators and oil coolers. Transmission-related problems, particularly with the torque converter and control module, are also documented. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment glitches and battery drain, can occur. Despite these potential issues, many owners report satisfaction with the vehicle's performance and comfort, provided regular maintenance is performed and potential problems are addressed promptly.
