# 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLS-Class delivers high-end luxury and commanding performance, but potential used-car buyers should carefully evaluate long-term maintenance and recurring component issues. Based on owner reports, certain systems—particularly the air suspension, braking, and panoramic glass—frequently demand attention and can lead to costly repairs. Thoroughly reviewing service histories and securing a pre-purchase inspection from a specialist can help mitigate unexpected ownership expenses.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Biturbo (GLS 450)

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces around 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth and capable power delivery for the GLS 450. Owners generally find this powerplant reliable, though standard German luxury maintenance schedules must be strictly followed.

### 4.7L V8 Biturbo (GLS 550)

The 4.7-liter twin-turbocharged V8 churns out roughly 449 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration for the GLS 550. While powerful, buyers should be mindful of potential complexities associated with high-output turbocharged engines as mileage climbs.

### 5.5L V8 Biturbo (AMG GLS 63)

Hand-built by AMG, the 5.5-liter twin-turbo V8 unleashes an impressive 577 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque for maximum performance. This high-performance engine requires meticulous servicing and premium fueling, with high potential upkeep costs typical of flagship AMG models.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic

Found on the GLS 450 and GLS 550, this 9-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes to maximize efficiency and smoothness. Routine fluid changes are essential to ensure long-term durability and crisp shifting.

### AMG Speedshift Plus 7G-TRONIC

Paired exclusively with the AMG GLS 63, this sport-tuned 7-speed automatic handles massive torque outputs with rapid gear changes. Performance-focused transmissions demand attentive fluid maintenance to prevent premature wear.

## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Suspension System

The adaptive air suspension system provides an exceptionally plush ride quality and adjustable ground clearance, though air springs and compressors are known wear items requiring eventual replacement.

**Air Suspension Compressor and Airbag Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report compressor cooling error messages, suspension lift malfunctions, and severe vehicle sinking or extremely bumpy rides making the vehicle unsafe to drive.
- **Solution:** Diagnose leaking air struts, faulty valve blocks, or failed compressors, and replace the defective components as necessary.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides strong stopping power for a heavy luxury SUV, but mechanical sticking and hydraulic complaints warrant close inspection during routine maintenance.

**Brake Pedal Feel and Caliper Binding**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers describe spongy brake pedals, abnormal noises, warning indicators, and in severe cases, stuck or frozen front brake calipers rendering the vehicle undrivable.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake lines, bleed the hydraulic system, and replace seized brake calipers or worn pads and rotors.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof and Glass

Exterior trim and glass components generally hold up well, though sporadic reports of unprovoked sunroof failures require careful visual checks.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The stationary glass panel of the panoramic sunroof can crack or shatter spontaneously while driving or sitting at a stoplight, producing a loud explosion sound.
- **Solution:** Clear glass shards and replace the damaged panoramic glass assembly with a new OEM panel.

## Safety

### Third-Row Seating

Cabin appointments are opulent and durable, though power-folding seat mechanisms can occasionally develop latching issues.

**Third-Row Seat Latch Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The third-row seats fail to lock securely into position, triggering continuous dashboard warning messages regarding passenger safety.
- **Solution:** Inspect the latch mechanism and electronic locking actuators, and repair or replace faulty seat hardware.

### Seatbelts

Occupant safety features are robustly integrated, though mechanical faults in restraint systems can occasionally surface.

**Seatbelt Retractor Locking**
- **Symptoms:** The seatbelt tightens unexpectedly during normal driving conditions and fails to release, trapping the occupant until movement ceases.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seatbelt pretensioner and retractor assembly, and replace malfunctioning units.
