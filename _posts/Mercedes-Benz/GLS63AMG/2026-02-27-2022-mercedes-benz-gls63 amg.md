# 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG

### Overview
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG, a high-performance luxury SUV, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting its premium engineering and build quality. Owners typically report a satisfying ownership experience with fewer unexpected issues compared to less premium vehicles. However, as with any high-performance machine, specific areas can occasionally present challenges, particularly those related to its potent powertrain and advanced technological features. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and prospective buyers.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Freezing screens, unresponsive touch controls, connectivity issues with Apple CarPlay/Android Auto, or navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates or resets. In some cases, replacement of the infotainment module may be required.
- **Cost:** $150 - $1200


### 12V Battery
**Premature battery drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently, or battery warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw or replacement of the 12V battery.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG demonstrates a commendable level of technical reliability for a high-performance luxury SUV. The core engine and transmission components are robust and have not shown widespread issues. Potential areas for attention are primarily concentrated in the advanced electronic systems, such as occasional infotainment glitches and rare instances of suspension sensor malfunctions. The 12V battery can also be a point of concern for some owners, potentially due to parasitic draws or its demanding electrical system. Overall, the GLS63 AMG offers a reliable ownership experience, provided owners are aware of and prepared for the potential, albeit infrequent, electronic and battery-related concerns.
