# 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG is a high-performance luxury SUV combining immense power with sophisticated comfort. While it offers impressive capability, prospective buyers should review owner-reported issues concerning electrical subsystems and structural components. Thorough pre-purchase inspections are strongly advised for luxury vehicles of this complexity.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8 Mild Hybrid (603 hp)

Equipped with a handcrafted 4.0-liter biturbo V8 engine paired with a 48V mild-hybrid system, producing approximately 603 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. This powertrain delivers blistering performance for a large SUV, though its advanced electrical integration requires attentive maintenance.

**Fuel Odor**
- **Symptoms:** An abnormally strong fuel odor can be detected inside the vehicle cabin without any active warning lights illuminated.
- **Solution:** Have the fuel system inspected by a qualified technician to locate and repair any potential leaks or vapor containment issues.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift TCT 9-Speed Automatic

Features a 9-speed automatic transmission designed to handle high torque output while providing smooth cruising and rapid gear changes. No widespread recurring transmission failure patterns are currently prominent in owner reports.

## Electrical

### 48V Battery and Charging System

The complex 48-volt electrical architecture powers advanced stabilization and mild-hybrid functions, but can occasionally trigger critical warning alerts and system shutdowns.

**48V Battery Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle suddenly displays a 48V battery error, limits speed, shuts off multimedia systems, or unexpectedly stalls while driving.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a dealership or certified specialist to diagnose battery module faults and perform required software updates or hardware replacements.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Glass

Exterior glass and panoramic roof panels add to the vehicle's upscale cabin feel, though isolated incidents of spontaneous glass breakage have been reported by owners.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass may shatter or explode unexpectedly while driving, producing a loud pop and falling glass debris.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof assembly for stress points and replace the glass panel under comprehensive insurance or warranty if applicable.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG delivers breathtaking performance and luxury, but prospective buyers must account for the high costs and complexity associated with its advanced powertrain and electronics. Paying close attention to the 48-volt electrical system and verifying all vehicle software updates during a pre-purchase inspection will help ensure a more confident ownership experience.
