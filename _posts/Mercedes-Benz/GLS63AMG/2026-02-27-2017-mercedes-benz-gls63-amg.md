# 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS-Class, buyers are greeted by a spacious, luxurious, and technologically advanced three-row SUV. However, used luxury vehicles of this caliber demand careful inspection, as owner-reported complaints highlight potential trouble spots ranging from complex electrical glitches and air suspension failures to drivetrain anomalies. Being fully aware of these common maintenance pain points can help prospective buyers negotiate a fair price or budget for future repairs.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Biturbo (GLS 450)

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces roughly 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration for the GLS 450. Overall, it is generally robust, though auxiliary electrical and fuel system components require routine monitoring as the vehicle ages.

### 4.7L V8 Biturbo (GLS 550)

The 4.7-liter twin-turbo V8 delivers an effortless 449 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque for the GLS 550. While powerful and smooth, the plumbing and fuel system components on this high-output engine need careful inspection for potential leaks.

**Fuel System and Fuel Rail Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Strong fuel odors inside or outside the vehicle and potential engine stalling while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines and rails for leaks, and ensure all relevant manufacturer safety campaigns have been completed.

### 5.5L V8 Biturbo (GLS 63 AMG)

Hand-built and tuned for maximum performance, this 5.5-liter twin-turbo V8 churns out an incredible 577 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque. It turns the heavy SUV into a high-speed cruiser, but high-performance maintenance costs and cooling system vigilance are essential for longevity.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic

Paired with select engines, the 9-speed automatic provides seamless highway cruising and optimized fuel economy. Owners should thoroughly test shift quality during a pre-purchase test drive to catch early signs of valve body wear.

**Erratic Shifting and Gear Engagement Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may fail to properly engage Park, shift abruptly without driver input, or exhibit erratic behavior.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning of the transmission control module and potential valve body inspection or software updates by a qualified technician.

### AMG Speedshift Plus 7G-TRONIC

Designed to handle the massive torque output of the AMG powerplant, this 7-speed automatic delivers rapid, aggressive gear changes. Regular fluid changes are vital to maintain crisp shifting and avoid premature internal wear.

## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension

The air suspension system delivers a plush, adjustable ride quality characteristic of flagship Mercedes-Benz SUVs. However, air springs, compressors, and pneumatic lines are known wear items that can lead to expensive repairs as the vehicle ages.

**Air Suspension Failure and Loss of Pressure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle drops in ride height, fails to lift, rides extremely bumpy, and can cause a temporary loss of vehicle control.
- **Solution:** Replace failed air struts, compressors, or leaking air lines.
- **Cost:** $3900

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The steering system provides light, maneuverable handling at low speeds and reassuring weight on the highway. Power assist failures, while infrequent, present severe safety risks and require immediate professional diagnosis.

**Sudden Power Steering Loss**
- **Symptoms:** A power steering malfunction warning message appears on the dash, followed by heavy or completely inoperable steering while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect power steering control modules, wiring harnesses, and fluid levels or electronic assist motors.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Advanced lighting packages offer great illumination when new, but degradation of projector components and bulbs can lead to compromised forward visibility over the years.

**Headlight Dimming and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Low beam headlights fade significantly over time or fail entirely, severely reducing nighttime road visibility.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight assemblies, bulbs, and ballasts; replace worn units as necessary.

## Interior

### Seats and Adjustments

The cabin features multi-contour, heated, and power-adjustable seating designed for maximum comfort on long trips. Owners occasionally report electrical faults within the seat heating elements and track motors.

**Seat Heater Malfunction and Power Adjustment Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Seat warmers become excessively hot and burn passengers, or power seat tracks fail to move forward and backward.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat heating elements, wiring harness under the seat, and adjustment motors for blockages or failure.

## Summary Verdict

The Mercedes-Benz GLS-Class blends executive-level luxury, family-friendly utility, and formidable performance. Prospective buyers should approach used models with a healthy awareness of potential upkeep costs, specifically regarding the air suspension, electrical accessories, and complex powertrain components.
