# 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG, buyers are looking at a high-performance luxury SUV powered by a potent biturbo engine. While these flagship models offer incredible comfort and speed, prospective owners should review owner-reported complaints regarding electrical systems and advanced driver-assistance features.

---

## Engine

### Handcrafted 4.0L V8 Biturbo with EQ Boost

This hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine features hybrid assist, producing around 603 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating performance for the large luxury SUV, though proper maintenance of its complex cooling and forced-induction systems is critical.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift TCT 9-Speed Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission is paired with all-wheel drive to manage the substantial power output of the AMG V8. It generally offers smooth operation and quick gear changes, though proper fluid maintenance is essential for long-term durability.

## Electrical

### 48V Electrical System

Manages the mild-hybrid components and advanced accessories, though complex electrical integration can occasionally prompt sudden warning lights.

**48V Battery Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported a 48V battery malfunction light illuminating, quickly followed by coolant temperature warnings and sudden loss of acceleration.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's 48-volt electrical architecture and battery modules inspected and replaced or reset by a qualified technician.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Large glass panoramic roofs provide an airy cabin feel but carry a minor risk of unprompted shattering reported across various modern luxury vehicles.

**Spontaneous Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A sudden loud boom can occur while driving, followed by the panoramic sunroof glass cracking and falling inward.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof assembly for stress fractures and replace the glass panel under comprehensive insurance or warranty if applicable.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety suites provide robust protection but rely heavily on clean sensors and stable software to prevent false activations.

**Phantom Braking and System Deactivation**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note that the vehicle may suddenly apply the brakes without obstacles ahead, or display active brake assist and blind-spot warnings.
- **Solution:** Check camera and radar sensors for obstructions, and have dealership software calibrations or sensor realignments performed if necessary.

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG combines ultra-luxury styling with supercar-level performance. While most mechanical aspects are robust, buyers should thoroughly inspect the electrical systems, 48-volt battery health, and driver-assistance sensors during a pre-purchase evaluation.
