**Introduction:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG is a high-performance SUV powered by a 4.0L twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 603 horsepower and 627 pound-feet of torque. It comes with a 9-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The GLS63 AMG is known for its luxurious interior, impressive performance, and advanced technology. However, owners have reported a number of issues, which are detailed below:

**Engine:**

* **Fuel Injectors (all)**
  * Description/Symptoms: Leaking or failing fuel injectors can cause rough idling, reduced power, and increased fuel consumption.
  * Solution: Replace the faulty fuel injectors.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V043000
* **Turbochargers (all)**
  * Description/Symptoms: Faulty turbochargers can lead to loss of power, increased exhaust smoke, and whistling noises.
  * Solution: Replace the defective turbochargers.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

* **9-Speed Automatic (all)**
  * Description/Symptoms: Transmission problems may include slipping gears, delayed shifts, or complete failure.
  * Solution: Repair or replace the faulty transmission components, such as solenoids, clutches, or the torque converter.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $6,000+
  * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V069000

**Suspension:**

* **Air Suspension (all)**
  * Description/Symptoms: Issues with the air suspension may cause uneven ride height, reduced handling, or compressor failure.
  * Solution: Replace the faulty air springs, struts, or compressor.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Battery (all)**
  * Description/Symptoms: Faulty batteries can lead to difficulty starting the vehicle, loss of power, or alternator problems.
  * Solution: Replace the defective battery.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Panoramic Sunroof (all)**
  * Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported issues with the panoramic sunroof, including leaks and rattling noises.
  * Solution: Repair or replace the sunroof assembly.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Climate Control (all)**
  * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control systems may result in inadequate heating or cooling, or erratic fan operation.
  * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace the defective components, such as the climate control module or actuators.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLS63 AMG is a high-performance SUV with impressive features. However, it has been plagued by a number of issues, including engine and transmission problems, suspension failures, electrical malfunctions, exterior leaks, and interior climate control issues. The severity and cost of the repairs vary depending on the issue encountered and the necessary parts.
