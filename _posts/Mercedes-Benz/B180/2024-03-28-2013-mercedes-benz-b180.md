**Introduction to the 2013 Mercedes-Benz B180**

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz B180 is a compact luxury MPV featuring a 1.6L inline-four turbocharged engine producing 122 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. It is offered with a standard six-speed manual transmission or an optional seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The B180 is praised for its spacious cabin, comfortable ride, and fuel efficiency, while criticisms include its lack of power and premium pricing.

**Engine**

* **1.6L Inline-Four Turbocharged M270 Engine**

    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, turbocharger noises, smoke from the exhaust
        * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Valve Sticking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, engine misfires, reduced power
        * Solution: Cleaning or replacement of the intake valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Manual Transmission**

    * **Shifter Bushing Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loose shifter
        * Solution: Replacement of the shifter bushing
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

* **Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**

    * **Mechatronic Unit Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shuddering, gear slipping, difficulty shifting
        * Solution: Replacement of the mechatronic unit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Struts**
    * **Strut Mount Bearing Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise from the front suspension, vibration in the steering wheel
        * Solution: Replacement of the strut mount bearings
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharge, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replacement of the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlight lenses
    * Solution: Replacement of the headlight unit or resealing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: MB040173

**Interior**

* **Air Conditioning Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to cool the cabin, strange noises
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the air conditioning system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue

**Summary**

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz B180 has several notable issues, particularly with its turbocharger, intake valves, and mechatronic unit. These issues can be costly to repair, and some have resulted in recalls. While the compact MPV offers a premium driving experience and a comfortable cabin, the potential for severe and expensive mechanical issues should be considered before purchasing.
