**Introduction**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz C250 is a luxury compact sedan powered by a 2.0L inline-4 turbocharged engine mated to a 7-speed automatic transmission. It produces 211 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. The C250 is praised for its sleek design, comfortable interior, and advanced technology. However, it has also been subject to various issues, as detailed below.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Inline-4 Turbocharged Engine**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise on startup, reduced engine power, and potential engine damage.
    Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner and related components.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaking from the pan or other seals, causing fluid loss and potential transmission damage.
    Solution: Replacement of leaking seals and fluid top-up.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Control Arm Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged control arms causing rattling or knocking noises while driving, as well as impaired handling.
    Solution: Replacement of affected control arm.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    Description/Symptoms: Premature battery discharge, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
    Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system, replacement of battery if necessary.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Glitches**
    Description/Symptoms: Intermittent freezing, crashing, or other issues with the infotainment system.
    Solution: Software updates or replacement of affected components.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Drain Plugs Clogging**
    Description/Symptoms: Water buildup in sunroof channels, potentially causing leaks into the vehicle interior.
    Solution: Cleaning or replacement of sunroof drain plugs.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Errors**
    Description/Symptoms: Incorrect map directions, freezing or crashing of navigation system.
    Solution: Software updates or replacement of navigation unit.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz C250 has experienced a range of issues affecting various components. The most severe and costly issues involve the engine timing chain tensioner failure and transmission fluid leak. Other common issues include suspension control arm failure, electrical glitches, and sunroof drain plug clogging. While these issues can be inconvenient and expensive to fix, it's important to note that not all C250 models will necessarily experience these problems.
