**Introduction:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLS450 is a luxurious and capable full-size SUV powered by a 3.0L twin-turbocharged V6 engine that produces 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a 9-speed automatic transmission and 4MATIC all-wheel drive. The GLS450 is known for its spacious and well-appointed interior, advanced technology, and impressive off-road capabilities. However, it is not without its share of issues.

**Engine**

* **3.0L Twin-Turbocharged V6**

Issue: **Fuel Injector Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, loss of power, rough idle
Solution: Replace faulty fuel injectors
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

Issue: **Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from exhaust
Solution: Replace faulty turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+

**Transmission**

* **9-Speed Automatic**

Issue: **Transmission Slippage**
Description/Symptoms: Jerking or slipping during gear changes
Solution: Replace faulty transmission components (e.g., clutches, solenoids)
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+

Issue: **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, inability to shift gears
Solution: Replace faulty mechatronic unit
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension**

Issue: **Air Spring Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Uneven ride height, loss of air pressure
Solution: Replace faulty air springs
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

Issue: **Air Compressor Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Suspension lowers while driving, excessive noise
Solution: Replace faulty air compressor
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery**

Issue: **Battery Discharge**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, loss of power
Solution: Replace faulty battery
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

Issue: **Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, loss of electrical power
Solution: Replace faulty alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+

**Exterior**

* **Body Panels**

Issue: **Rust**
Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on body panels, especially undercarriage
Solution: Repair or replace rusted panels
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System**

Issue: **Touchscreen Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Unresponsive or glitchy touchscreen
Solution: Software update or replacement of touchscreen
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

* **Leather Seat Stitching**

Issue: **Premature Wear**
Description/Symptoms: Stiching on leather seats comes loose prematurely
Solution: Repair or replace damaged stitching
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Summary:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLS450 is a capable and luxurious SUV, but it has been plagued by several issues, including fuel injector failure, turbocharger failure, transmission slippage, mechatronic unit failure, air spring leaks, and battery discharge. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be quite expensive to repair.
