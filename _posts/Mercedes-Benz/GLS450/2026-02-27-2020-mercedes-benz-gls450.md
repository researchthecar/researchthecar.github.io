# 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLS450

### Overview
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLS450, a full-size luxury SUV, aims to blend opulent comfort with robust performance. As the first model year of its generation, it introduced updated styling, a more advanced interior, and a new powertrain. While generally well-received for its refinement and technology, like any first-year model, there can be specific areas to monitor for potential reliability concerns. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into common problems reported by owners and technicians.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (M256)
**EQ Boost System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent loss of power, rough idling, warning lights on the dashboard, reduced acceleration.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of the EQ Boost starter-generator or related control modules.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,500

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level warnings.
- **Solution:** Inspection and tightening or replacement of seals, gaskets, or oil pan.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC Automatic Transmission
**Hesitation or Jerking During Shifts**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable delays or abruptness when the transmission shifts gears, particularly at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Software updates for the transmission control module, or in more severe cases, internal transmission repairs or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $4,000


## Suspension

### Air Body Control (Optional)
**Air Suspension Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively, warning lights related to suspension.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking air springs, lines, or the air compressor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,500

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**MBUX Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, Bluetooth connectivity issues, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000


### 12V Battery
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently, battery warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V battery.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLS450, while offering a luxurious and technologically advanced experience, exhibits some common first-year model concerns. The primary areas to watch are the EQ Boost system within the engine, potential transmission hesitation, and issues with the optional Air Body Control suspension. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the MBUX infotainment system, and premature 12V battery failures have also been noted. While the exterior and interior generally hold up well, owners should be aware of these potential technical vulnerabilities. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining the reliability of this premium SUV.
