# 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLS450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The Mercedes-Benz GLS450 offers a luxurious ride, advanced technology, and ample space for families. However, prospective buyers should carefully review owner-reported mechanical and electrical concerns before purchasing a used model. While luxury European SUVs often command strong interest, maintenance costs and complex electrical systems require careful inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (GLS450)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine utilizes a 48-volt mild-hybrid system (EQ Boost) to deliver smooth power and efficient performance. Routine maintenance is critical, as complex under-hood components can experience heat-related wear over time.

**Wiring Harness Insulation Deterioration**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent check engine lights, electrical fault codes, and brittle wiring harness insulation near ignition coil connectors due to heat exposure.
- **Solution:** Inspect the engine wiring harness for degradation and replace damaged sections or connectors.

**Fuel Rail Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Fuel odor or visible fuel leaks stemming from the passenger side fuel rail system.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the fuel rail assembly and replace leaking components.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected engine stalling while driving, accompanied by warning lights and abrupt loss of propulsion.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scans are required to identify fault codes related to fuel delivery, electrical sensors, or powertrain management.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission is paired with standard all-wheel drive, providing responsive gear changes and refined highway cruising. Software calibrations and regular fluid changes are essential to maintain smooth operation.

**Delayed Downshifting and Shifting Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission fails to downshift sequentially when slowing down, resulting in sudden drops to lower gears and hesitation during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Perform a transmission control module software update or inspect internal valve body components.

## Electrical

### 48V Battery and Infotainment System

The cabin features extensive digital displays and advanced electrical architecture that can occasionally experience glitches or require battery service.

**48-Volt Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start, and the 48-volt battery warning light illuminates on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed 48-volt battery component.
- **Cost:** $3000

**Intermittent Dashboard Screen Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** The digital dashboard screen freezes or stops working randomly while driving.
- **Solution:** Perform software resets or replace faulty display modules if issues persist.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features provide comprehensive driver assistance, though sensor calibration is vital to prevent false activation.

**Phantom Braking and Collision Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle's automated emergency braking system engages unexpectedly without obstacles present on the road.
- **Solution:** Calibrate the driver assistance sensors, cameras, and radar systems at an authorized service center.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V709000: Structure:Body:Door:Hinge And Attachments](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLS450&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The detached trim can become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and fasten the rear door window trim, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V089000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLS450&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 101, "Control and Displays." If the interior switches are not illuminated, they may be more difficult to find and operate, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the instrument cluster software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The used Mercedes-Benz GLS450 offers a high-end luxury driving experience, but buyers should approach with a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to the vehicle's electrical systems, mild-hybrid battery health, and powertrain operation to avoid unexpected repair bills.
