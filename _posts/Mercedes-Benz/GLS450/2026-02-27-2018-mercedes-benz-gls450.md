# 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLS450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLS450 is a luxurious and spacious full-size SUV offering impressive performance and family-friendly versatility. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints carefully, as certain recurring problem areas—particularly concerning suspension and braking systems—frequently require attention.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Biturbo V6 Engine

Equipped with a 3.0-liter biturbo V6 engine producing approximately 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque paired with a smooth automatic transmission, providing robust acceleration for a large luxury SUV.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Utilizes a 9-speed automatic transmission paired with 4MATIC all-wheel drive, designed to maximize fuel efficiency and deliver smooth gear changes under varied driving conditions.

## Suspension

### Air Suspension System

Owner reports indicate that the air suspension system can experience recurring component wear and compressor malfunctions leading to rough rides.

**Air Suspension and Compressor Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report compressor cooling warnings, malfunction notifications, and vehicle lowering or riding extremely bumpy and unsafely.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnosis and replacement of faulty air struts, air compressors, or valve blocks.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake system complaints highlight potential concerns with pedal responsiveness and component wear requiring proactive maintenance.

**Brake Pedal Feel and Caliper Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note spongy brake pedal resistance, abnormal noises, brake warning indicators, and stuck or frozen front calipers.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and calipers; replace seized brake components or flush brake fluid as needed.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

The exterior glass components should be inspected closely, as multiple owners experienced sudden shattering of the panoramic roof.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The stationary glass panel of the panoramic sunroof has been reported to spontaneously explode or shatter while driving or stationary without impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered glass panel and clear out any remaining glass debris.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V539000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLS450&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The rear brake caliper pistons on these vehicles may not have been correctly coated, causing gas to become temporarily released into the rear brake hydraulic circuit, reducing braking performance. Reduced braking performance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will bleed the rear axle brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLS450, a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable technician is strongly advised. Pay close attention to the health of the air suspension system and brake components to avoid costly surprises down the road.
