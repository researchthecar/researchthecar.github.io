# 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS450

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS450 is a full-size luxury SUV that offers a comfortable and capable driving experience, blending the brand's signature refinement with robust utility. As a first-year model for the GLS designation (previously the GL-Class), it inherited a well-established platform. While generally considered reliable, like any vehicle, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for prospective and current owners. The GLS450 is known for its powerful V6 engine and smooth automatic transmission, contributing to its appeal as a family hauler or a capable long-distance cruiser.

## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one or more corners, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**

### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after sitting for a short period, requiring jump starts.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from modules or accessories, repair or replacement of faulty components.
- **Cost:** $200 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS450 generally exhibits good reliability, particularly with its powertrain. The most commonly cited issues tend to revolve around the optional AIRMATIC air suspension system, which can develop leaks or compressor problems, leading to potentially significant repair costs. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment glitches and intermittent battery drain, are also reported, though often resolvable with software updates or diagnosis of parasitic draws. Beyond these specific points, the GLS450 is a robust vehicle, and owners who maintain it diligently and address any emerging issues promptly can expect a dependable luxury SUV experience.
