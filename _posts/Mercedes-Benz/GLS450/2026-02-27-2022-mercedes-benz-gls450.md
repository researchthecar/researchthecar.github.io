# 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLS450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a luxury full-size SUV like the 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLS450 brings impressive comfort, advanced technology, and refined performance. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported issues concerning electrical systems, driver assistance features, and sudden powertrain behaviors. While owner complaints represent individual experiences rather than guaranteed factory defects, keeping these potential trouble spots in mind can help you evaluate a prospective vehicle thoroughly during a test drive and pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 MHEV

The 2022 GLS450 is powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine equipped with a 48-volt mild-hybrid system (EQ Boost), generating approximately 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant delivers smooth, robust acceleration suited for a large luxury SUV. Overall, the engine architecture is generally solid, though isolated owner reports mention electrical and charging interactions tied to the mild-hybrid system.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The GLS450 utilizes a 9-speed automatic transmission paired with standard 4MATIC all-wheel drive, offering seamless gear changes tailored for highway cruising and city driving alike. While generally dependable, occasional owner reports note unexpected shifting behavior that requires professional diagnostic attention.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle unexpectedly shifts into neutral while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose transmission control module software and internal mechanical components.

## Electrical

### 48V Battery and Infotainment System

The vehicle relies heavily on complex electrical architectures, including a 48-volt mild-hybrid battery and dual screen displays. Electrical anomalies can occasionally trigger sudden system warnings and powertrain shutdowns.

**48V Battery Malfunction and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** The dashboard displays a 48V battery malfunction warning, followed by coolant temperature warnings and sudden loss of acceleration or complete vehicle shutdown.
- **Solution:** Inspect the 48-volt battery system, charging components, and related software modules for faults.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

The exterior features expansive glass options like the panoramic sunroof, which has on rare occasions experienced spontaneous glass breakage.

**Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass shatters or explodes loudly without any external impact or visible road debris.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the sunroof frame for structural stress or installation flaws.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance and Lane Assist

Advanced safety suites provide helpful driver aids but can occasionally experience sensitivity issues leading to unexpected braking events or temporary sensor deactivations.

**Phantom Braking and Sensor Deactivation**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle suddenly applies the brakes without an obstruction ahead, or collision avoidance and lane-keeping systems report reduced visibility and shut down unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Check camera and radar sensor alignments, clear windshield obstructions, and update driver-assistance software.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V936000: Steering:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLS450&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of steering control can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering coupling locking screw, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V178000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLS450&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Detached trim bars may cause a road hazard and increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and secure the rear window trim bars, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLS450 is a sophisticated and capable luxury cruiser, but prospective buyers should approach with a healthy awareness of potential electrical and driver-assistance glitches. When shopping for a used model, ensure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection is performed, verifying the health of the 48-volt mild-hybrid system, checking for smooth transmission operation, and confirming that all safety sensors and electronics function properly.
