**Introduction**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Boxer 40 is a luxury SUV that offers a powerful engine and a spacious interior. It is powered by a 3.0-liter V6 engine that produces 362 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque. Other engine and transmission options include a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine that produces 241 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission, and a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine that produces 429 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The Boxer 40 has a comfortable ride and a well-equipped interior. However, it is not as fuel-efficient as some of its competitors.

**Engine**

**3.0-liter V6**

* **Oil Leaks:** Oil leaks can develop around the valve covers or oil pan gasket. Symptoms include a burning oil smell, low oil levels, and smoke from the engine. The solution is to replace the leaking gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
* **Timing Chain Issues:** The timing chain can become stretched or damaged over time. Symptoms include a rattling noise from the engine, reduced performance, and increased fuel consumption. The solution is to replace the timing chain and related components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Turbocharger Problems:** The turbocharger can fail due to a variety of issues, such as a faulty wastegate or bearing failure. Symptoms include reduced performance, excessive smoke from the exhaust, and noises from the turbocharger. The solution is to replace the turbocharger. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:** The transmission may experience shifting problems, such as delayed or rough shifts. Symptoms include hard shifting, slipping gears, and a lack of response from the transmission. The solution is to reprogram the transmission control module or replace the transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:** Transmission fluid leaks can develop around the transmission pan gasket or seals. Symptoms include low transmission fluid levels and slipping gears. The solution is to replace the leaking gasket or seal. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Malfunctions:** The air suspension system can malfunction due to a variety of issues, such as a faulty compressor or air leaks. Symptoms include a low ride height, uneven ride height, and a lack of response from the suspension. The solution is to diagnose and repair the faulty component. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery can drain prematurely due to a variety of issues, such as a faulty alternator or parasitic drain. Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle and a low battery warning light. The solution is to diagnose and repair the faulty component. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
* **Infotainment System Issues:** The infotainment system may experience various issues, such as a black screen, frozen screen, or unresponsive buttons. The solution is to update the software or replace the infotainment unit. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Undercarriage:** Rust can develop on the undercarriage of the vehicle due to exposure to moisture and road salt. Symptoms include visible rust spots and a weakened undercarriage. The solution is to clean and treat the rust, and apply undercoating to prevent further corrosion. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions:** The climate control system may malfunction due to a variety of issues, such as a faulty blend door actuator or refrigerant leak. Symptoms include inconsistent temperatures, poor air flow, and a lack of response from the climate control system. The solution is to diagnose and repair the faulty component. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Recalls:**

* NHTSA Campaign Number: 20V552000: Fuel Pump Failure

**Summary**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Boxer 40 is a well-equipped and powerful SUV. However, it has been known to experience a number of issues, including engine oil leaks, timing chain problems, and transmission shifting problems. The cost to fix these issues can vary depending on the severity of the problem. Overall, the Boxer 40 is a reliable vehicle, but it is important to be aware of potential issues before purchasing one.
