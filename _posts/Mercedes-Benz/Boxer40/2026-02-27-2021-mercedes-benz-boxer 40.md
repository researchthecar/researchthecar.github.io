### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz Boxer 40 demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. Exterior and interior components are generally durable, suitable for the demanding commercial environment. Overall, the Boxer 40 is a dependable choice for businesses seeking a reliable work vehicle with minimal unexpected repair costs.
