The 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG, a high-performance luxury convertible, generally exhibits strong reliability, befitting its premium status. While specific widespread issues are uncommon, as with any high-performance vehicle, diligent maintenance is crucial. ## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle leaning to one side, 'ABC system temporarily unavailable' warning message, stiff ride.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or pump. Fluid flush and refill.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,500

**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic ride height adjustments, 'ABC fault' warning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty ride height sensors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive or freezes intermittently.
- **Solution:** Software update or reset of the COMAND system. In rare cases, head unit replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1,500


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, difficulty starting the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from modules (e.g., telematics, amplifier). Replacement of faulty module or repair of wiring.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000


## Exterior

### Retractable Hardtop
**Actuator/Motor Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Top fails to retract or deploy, unusual noises during operation, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair or replacement of roof actuators, motors, or hydraulic lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG is a highly capable and generally reliable performance luxury car. The powerful 5.5L twin-turbo V8 engine and 7-speed transmission are robust. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are the sophisticated Active Body Control (ABC) suspension, which can develop leaks or sensor problems, and the complex retractable hardtop mechanism, which may require actuator or motor repairs. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches or intermittent battery drain, are also possibilities, though not widespread. Overall, with regular professional maintenance and addressing any early warning signs promptly, the SL63 AMG offers a dependable and exhilarating ownership experience.
