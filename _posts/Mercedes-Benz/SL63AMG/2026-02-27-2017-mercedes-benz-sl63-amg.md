# 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG delivers high-performance luxury with a twin-turbo V8 engine and refined grand-touring capabilities. Used-car shoppers should thoroughly inspect these complex performance machines, as owner reports point to occasional electronic, brake, and drivetrain concerns.

---

## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8

The 5.5-liter bi-turbo V8 engine produces 577 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, offering blistering acceleration and robust overall performance. It is a sophisticated powerplant that requires meticulous maintenance to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 7-Speed Automatic

The AMG Speedshift MCT 7-speed automatic transmission utilizes a wet clutch pack in place of a traditional torque converter for lightning-fast gear changes. It provides crisp shifts suited for spirited driving, though overall system complexity warrants a careful pre-purchase inspection.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly shift into neutral gear while attempting to accelerate from a stop, posing a safety hazard.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the transmission control module, fluid levels, and internal linkages for faults.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

High-performance braking system designed to haul down speed rapidly.

**Brake System Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Brakes may fail to engage properly when approaching a stop, causing the vehicle to roll through intersections.
- **Solution:** Inspect the brake hydraulic system, electronic brake controls, and fluid pressure immediately upon experiencing any pedal inconsistency.

## Steering

### Steering Column

Power-adjustable steering column with memory features.

**Power Steering Adjustment Stuck**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel may become stuck in the fully upward position and fail to return to the preset driving position.
- **Solution:** Disable the easy entry/exit feature or service the steering column adjustment motor and mechanism.

## Safety

### Pre-Safe Safety System

Advanced electronic safety and driver assistance features.

**Unnecessary Seatbelt Tightening**
- **Symptoms:** The pre-safe system may unexpectedly tighten seatbelts restrictively in non-emergency situations, distracting the driver.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's safety sensors and pre-safe control modules diagnosed and calibrated by a specialist.

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG is an exhilarating luxury roadster, but its high-tech powertrain and electronics require attentive care. Prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the transmission, braking systems, and onboard electronics.
