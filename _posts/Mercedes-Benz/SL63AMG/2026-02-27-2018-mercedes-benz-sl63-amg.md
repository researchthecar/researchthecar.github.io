# 2018 Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG represents the pinnacle of luxury performance roadsters, combining a hand-built twin-turbo V8 with sophisticated high-end engineering. While owner complaint volume is remarkably low for this model year, prospective buyers should always perform a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection given the complex electronics and high-performance mechanical systems typical of AMG flagships.

---

## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 5.5-liter bi-turbo V8 engine delivers immense power and torque, offering breathtaking straight-line acceleration. Proper maintenance and timely servicing are essential to keep this high-performance hand-built motor running reliably.

## Transmission

### AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 7-Speed Automatic

The 7-speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT multi-clutch transmission provides rapid gear changes and responsive manual control via paddle shifters. It is robustly built to handle high torque output, though regular fluid changes are recommended for longevity.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG is an exhilarating luxury cruiser backed by minimal owner complaints in public databases.
