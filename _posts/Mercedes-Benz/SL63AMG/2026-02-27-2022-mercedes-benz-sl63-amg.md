# 2022 Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2022 Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG, buyers are looking at a high-performance luxury roadster packed with advanced technology and formidable power. While owner reports to safety regulators are relatively limited for this flagship convertible, early adopters have noted potential issues involving electrical stability, keyless entry recognition, and occasional powertrain anomalies. Prospective buyers should carefully verify that all electronic control modules and software updates are up to date before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8

The 4.0-liter hand-built biturbo V8 delivers robust horsepower and torque, offering breathtaking acceleration suited for a grand tourer. Overall reliability for this high-output powerplant is generally tied to strict maintenance adherence and complex engine management systems.

**Unexpected Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that the vehicle can experience unintended deceleration and sudden engine stalls while driving at highway speeds without prior warning lights.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic testing by a qualified technician to inspect powertrain control modules, fuel delivery, and software calibrations.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 9-Speed Automatic

The AMG Speedshift MCT 9-speed automatic transmission utilizes a wet clutch start-up to deliver rapid, performance-oriented gear changes. Buyers should evaluate shift smoothness across all drive modes during a test drive.

**Abrupt Gear Reduction and Shifting Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may occasionally get stuck in a lower gear during highway driving, causing sudden speed drops and unresponsive throttle input while revs climb.
- **Solution:** Have a dealership or specialist check for transmission control unit software updates or internal hydraulic faults.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Battery System

The electrical architecture governs virtually every comfort, convenience, and performance feature in the roadster.

**Rapid Battery Drain and Inoperable Safety Features**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles may experience severe battery drain when left sitting idle for short periods, leading to critical battery warnings and temporarily inoperable safety systems upon startup.
- **Solution:** Inspect the dual-battery charging system, check for parasitic draws, and verify if any applicable manufacturer service campaigns apply to the vehicle.

**Keyless Entry and Starting Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced instances where the vehicle fails to detect the smart key upon entry, preventing the car from starting and showing no warning indicators.
- **Solution:** Reprogram or replace key fobs, and inspect the vehicle's keyless go control modules and interior antennas.

### MBUX Infotainment and Driver Assistance

Advanced digital displays and touch-sensitive controls integrate deeply into the daily operation of the vehicle.

**Infotainment System Blackout**
- **Symptoms:** The central MBUX display may fail to power on, rendering climate control, convertible top operation, navigation, and active safety warnings like blind-spot monitoring inoperable.
- **Solution:** Perform a system hard reset or update/replace the infotainment head unit and associated software modules.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG is an engineering marvel that blends open-top luxury with supercar-level performance. However, because of its heavy reliance on complex digital electronics and high-strung powertrains, used buyers should prioritize models with comprehensive service histories and remaining warranty coverage. A thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the electrical charging system and MBUX functionality is highly recommended.
