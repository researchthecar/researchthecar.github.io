# 2016 Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG is a high-performance luxury roadster known for its exceptional power and sophisticated styling. While overall complaint volume is very low for this model year, prospective buyers should still carefully inspect core mechanical systems and tires, as ownership costs for high-end AMG models can be substantial.

---

## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8 Engine

The 5.5-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces roughly 577 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration. It is a complex, high-performance powerplant that requires meticulous maintenance and premium fuel.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT automatic transmission uses a wet clutch pack instead of a traditional torque converter for rapid shifts. It offers quick responsiveness suited to the car's athletic character, though proper fluid changes are critical for long-term health.

## Suspension

### Tires

Factory run-flat tires provide convenience in the event of a puncture but can wear quickly and are costly to replace.

**Premature Tire Pressure Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Alert notifications display low tire pressure on extended-wear run-flat tires while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect tires for punctures or slow leaks, and replace or repair as necessary.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG is a thrilling and luxurious roadster with limited reported owner complaints. Buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an AMG specialist to verify the health of the twin-turbo engine, complex electronics, and suspension components.
