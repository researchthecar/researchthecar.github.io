# 2023 Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a modern high-performance luxury roadster like the 2023 Mercedes-Benz SL63 AMG means prioritizing blistering twin-turbo V8 power and advanced technology. While early owner feedback points to isolated electronic and safety system glitches, overall real-world data remains limited for this model year. Buyers should carefully evaluate complex driver-assistance features during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8

This hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces an estimated 577 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, delivering immense straight-line performance. It pairs sophisticated forced induction with robust engineering, though buyers should maintain strict service intervals to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 9-Speed Automatic

The 9-speed multi-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, aggressive gear changes suited to the AMG personality while remaining smooth in comfort mode. Routine fluid maintenance is critical for preserving shift quality and clutch longevity over time.

## Safety

### Electrical System and Safety Modules

Complex onboard electronics govern everything from powertrain management to active safety equipment, occasionally resulting in software-related glitches reported by owners.

**Warning Light and Safety System Disruption**
- **Symptoms:** Multiple warning lights illuminate unexpectedly on the dashboard, potentially disabling auxiliary safety features and adaptive cruise control functions while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan the vehicle computer for diagnostic trouble codes and perform necessary software updates or sensor recalibrations.
