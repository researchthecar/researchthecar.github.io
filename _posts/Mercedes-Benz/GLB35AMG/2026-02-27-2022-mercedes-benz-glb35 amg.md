# 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLB35 AMG

### Overview
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLB35 AMG is a performance-oriented compact SUV that blends practicality with AMG's signature driving dynamics. As a relatively new model, particularly in its AMG variant, long-term reliability data is still emerging. However, initial reports suggest a solid build quality, characteristic of Mercedes-Benz. Owners generally praise its potent powertrain, agile handling, and versatile interior. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the GLB35 AMG appears to be holding up well, with most reported concerns being minor or related to specific component failures rather than widespread design flaws. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring its continued performance and longevity.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLB35 AMG demonstrates good initial reliability, with its core mechanical components (engine and transmission) showing no significant widespread issues. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and potential sensor malfunctions for driver assistance features. These are often resolvable with software updates or recalibration. Exterior and interior components appear robust. Overall, the GLB35 AMG is considered a reliable performance SUV, provided routine maintenance is performed and any minor electrical gremlins are addressed promptly.
