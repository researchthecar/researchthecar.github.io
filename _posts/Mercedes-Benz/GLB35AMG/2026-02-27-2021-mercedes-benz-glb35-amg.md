# 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLB35 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLB35 AMG offers a sporty and versatile package for buyers seeking a compact luxury crossover with strong performance credentials. As with any high-performance vehicle, prospective owners should review powertrain options and monitor electronic systems closely during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (AMG-tuned)

This hand-assembled 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 302 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It delivers robust acceleration and lively performance, though buyers should ensure regular maintenance intervals were strictly followed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed AMG Speedshift Dual-Clutch Automatic

Equipped with an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission paired with AMG Performance 4MATIC all-wheel drive, this unit provides rapid gear shifts. While generally responsive, owners should test drive the vehicle thoroughly to verify smooth engagement across all gears.

**Transmission Limp Mode**
- **Symptoms:** A red transmission malfunction warning light illuminates while driving at highway speeds, causing the vehicle to suddenly enter limp mode and restrict power.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a qualified technician or dealership to diagnose diagnostic trouble codes within the transmission control module.
