### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Mercedes-Benz GLB35 AMG demonstrates a strong initial reliability profile, particularly concerning its core powertrain components. The 2.0L turbocharged engine and 8-speed dual-clutch transmission are robust and have proven dependable in other Mercedes-Benz applications. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the advanced MBUX infotainment system, typically resolved with software updates, and the potential for standard 12V battery degradation over time, a common concern across many modern vehicles. Suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally holding up well, with no widespread defects reported. Overall, the GLB35 AMG is positioned as a reliable performance SUV, provided routine maintenance is adhered to and potential minor electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
