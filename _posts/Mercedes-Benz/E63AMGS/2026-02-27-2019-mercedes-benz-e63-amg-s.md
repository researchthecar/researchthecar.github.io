# 2019 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz E-Class delivers luxury, cutting-edge technology, and robust performance, but prospective buyers should carefully review potential owner complaints regarding its electrical systems, driver assistance features, and rearview cameras. While many of these issues are minor or addressed via manufacturer campaigns, keeping an eye on historical maintenance and open notices is key to a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (E300)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 241 horsepower, offering efficient daily driving power for the base E-Class models.

**Crankcase Ventilation Valve**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light accompanied by diagnostic trouble code P052E71.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty crankcase ventilation valve.

**Rear Main Seal Oil Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil seepage or leaking detected during routine vehicle inspections.
- **Solution:** Replace the rear main seal to prevent further oil loss.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6 (E450)

The 3.0-liter twin-turbo V6 engine generates around 362 horsepower, providing smooth acceleration and refined performance across touring trims.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (AMG E63 S)

The handcrafted 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine unleashes a massive 603 horsepower, delivering track-capable performance and rapid acceleration for high-end enthusiasts.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp, smooth gear shifts across rear-wheel and 4MATIC all-wheel-drive configurations.

### AMG Speedshift MCT 9-Speed Transmission

The AMG-tuned 9-speed multi-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes designed to handle high torque outputs seamlessly.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Owners have reported occasional electrical glitches, ranging from sensitive sensor responses to display malfunctions.

**Instrument Cluster Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The digital instrument cluster fails or goes dark following water intrusion from heavy rain.
- **Solution:** Inspect and seal potential water entry points, and repair or replace affected instrument cluster modules.

### Back Over Prevention

Driver assistance systems and backup cameras are critical for daily safety, though some owners have experienced intermittent feed failures.

**Backup Camera Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera image freezes, distorts, or fails to display entirely while the vehicle is placed in reverse.
- **Solution:** Have the backup camera system diagnosed; software updates or hardware replacements may be required.

## Safety

### Seat Belt

Restraint systems generally perform reliably, though occasional sensor or latch complaints have been registered by owners.

**Seat Belt Warning and Latch Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Driver or passenger seat belt warning lights or chimes activate incorrectly, or occupants report fastening difficulties.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt buckles and wiring sensors, replacing faulty components as needed.
