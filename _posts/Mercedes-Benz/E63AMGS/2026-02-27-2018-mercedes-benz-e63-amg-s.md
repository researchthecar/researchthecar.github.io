# 2018 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury vehicle, understanding common trouble spots can save you from unexpected repair bills. This overview outlines owner-reported concerns for the vehicle, providing a balanced perspective on potential mechanical, electrical, and structural issues to help guide your purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 Engine

The 4.0-liter biturbo V8 engine produces robust horsepower and high torque, delivering exceptional performance. Proper maintenance and timely servicing are crucial for long-term reliability in high-output AMG applications.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences unexpected engine stalling while operating, leading to potential loss of power on the road.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel delivery systems, throttle body, and sensor connections; perform diagnostic testing for fault codes.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed multi-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear changes suited for high-performance driving. Routine fluid changes at recommended intervals are vital to maintain smooth operation.

**Shifting and Valve Body Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission may unexpectedly shift into neutral, experience harsh engagement between drive and reverse, or enter limp mode.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid condition, inspect the electronic valve body, and update or replace transmission control modules as needed.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

Performance-tuned suspensions and low-profile wheel packages enhance handling precision but remain vulnerable to road hazards and cracking.

**Wheel Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Low-profile alloy wheels develop structural cracks under normal driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels thoroughly for air leaks or fractures, and replace damaged alloy rims as necessary.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera and Display Systems

Onboard electronics manage critical safety displays, infotainment, and driver assists, though auxiliary camera modules can occasionally exhibit intermittent bugs.

**Intermittent Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The backup camera display goes black, shows an error icon with a slash mark, or fails to open properly when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera wiring harnesses, software firmware, and display modules for loose connections or intermittent faults.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Advanced safety systems provide comprehensive occupant protection, though individual motorized restraint components can experience mechanical wear.

**Seat Belt Retractor Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Seat belt fails to fully retract, leaving dangerous slack across the driver's chest and lap.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty seat belt retractor motor and assembly through an authorized service center.

This model offers thrilling performance and high-end luxury, but prospective buyers should carefully inspect the transmission, electronics, and alloy wheels. Pre-purchase inspections by a qualified marque specialist are strongly recommended to identify any hidden wear before finalizing a purchase.
