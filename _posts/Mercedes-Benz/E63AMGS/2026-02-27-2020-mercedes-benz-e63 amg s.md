# 2020 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S, a high-performance variant of the E-Class, is engineered for exhilarating driving dynamics and luxurious comfort. Powered by a potent handcrafted AMG engine and paired with a sophisticated transmission, it offers exceptional power delivery. While generally robust, like any complex performance vehicle, it can encounter specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and enthusiasts.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (M177)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, 'check engine' light illumination, potential whistling noise from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty wastegate actuator or the complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Coolant Leaks (Intercooler Hoses)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, engine overheating, loss of coolant level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of damaged intercooler hoses and clamps.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 9-Speed Automatic
**Harsh Shifting / Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, hesitation when shifting into drive or reverse, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Software update for transmission control module, or in severe cases, internal transmission repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2500+


## Suspension

### AMG RIDE CONTROL+ Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, 'check suspension' warning message, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the leaking air spring or associated air lines.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND/MBUX)
**Software Glitches / Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, intermittent audio issues, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500+

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its exterior and interior components, which show no widespread issues. The primary areas of concern revolve around the high-performance powertrain and advanced systems. Potential buyers should be aware of possible issues with the turbocharger wastegate actuators and intercooler hoses on the 4.0L V8 engine, as well as occasional harsh shifting or software glitches within the AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT transmission. The air suspension system can also develop leaks over time. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and battery drain, are also noted. While these issues can be costly to repair, they are not universally reported and often stem from the demanding nature of a performance vehicle. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining the E63 AMG S's reliability.
