# 2020 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S, buyers are looking at a high-performance luxury machine blending everyday usability with immense twin-turbo V8 power. While owner feedback in federal databases touches on various vehicle systems, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate vehicle history, powertrain health, and electronics during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (M177)

The 2020 E63 AMG S is powered by a handcrafted 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing around 603 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. This potent powerplant delivers blistering performance, though maintenance can be complex and costly given its high-strung nature.

**Oil Pump Control and Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported intermittent engine warning messages related to oil pump control valves, as well as oil migration into electrical harnesses from sensors.
- **Solution:** Inspect the wiring harness for oil contamination and have a qualified technician diagnose any fault codes related to the oil pump valve.
- **Cost:** $4,000+

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 9-Speed Automatic

The vehicle utilizes a multi-clutch 9-speed automatic transmission paired with an AMG Performance 4MATIC+ all-wheel-drive system. It offers rapid gear changes suited for high-performance driving, though routine fluid changes are critical for long-term health.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera System

**Intermittent Rearview Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note that the rearview camera display may freeze, go completely black, or display an inoperable message when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera software, wiring connections, and replace faulty camera modules if necessary.

## Exterior

### Roof and Windows

**Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced sudden, unexpected shattering of the panoramic sunroof glass while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the surrounding frame for alignment or stress points.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S offers world-class performance and luxury, but prospective used-car buyers must approach with eyes wide open regarding potential repair costs. Ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection is performed by an independent specialist familiar with AMG models to check for oil leaks, electrical integrity, and mechanical condition.
