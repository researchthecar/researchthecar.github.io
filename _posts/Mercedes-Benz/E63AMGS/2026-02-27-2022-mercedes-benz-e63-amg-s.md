# 2022 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S, buyers are looking at an exceptionally powerful luxury performance vehicle. While owner reports highlight a range of sophisticated electronic and mechanical concerns—ranging from advanced driver assistance quirks to electrical and body hardware issues—these luxury models demand careful inspection and a thorough pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L V8 Biturbo Engine

The hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers immense horsepower and torque, providing supercar-level performance. Buyers should ensure regular maintenance intervals have been strictly followed to maintain high-performance reliability.

## Transmission

### AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed multi-clutch automatic transmission routes power smoothly through the performance-oriented all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid changes are essential to ensure long-term durability under high-output driving conditions.

## Steering

### Steering and Front Suspension Components

Front-end hardware and low-profile tire setups require careful attention to avoid curb or underbody clearance issues.

**Underbody Shield Interference**
- **Symptoms:** Plastic aerodynamic shields or blocks near the front bumper can make contact with the tires or low-profile rims during tight low-speed turns.
- **Solution:** Inspect front underbody shields for proper fastening, sagging, or damage from road debris.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Battery System

Complex onboard electronics and mild-hybrid architectures require thorough diagnostic checks during a pre-purchase inspection.

**48V Battery Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** A 48V battery malfunction warning appears on the dashboard, followed by a red warning light and sudden vehicle deceleration or loss of power.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's electrical charging and 48-volt battery system inspected by a qualified technician or dealer.

**Backup Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The backup camera displays a completely blank screen when the vehicle is shifted into reverse.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera wiring harnesses, software configurations, and display modules for faults.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Large glass roof panels offer an airy cabin feel but should be thoroughly inspected for any signs of structural stress.

**Sunroof Glass Fracture**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof panel may shatter or explode unexpectedly without warning while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for stress points or prior damage, and consult a dealer regarding available warranty or service bulletins.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety sensors and driver assistance suites can occasionally misinterpret road conditions, creating unexpected braking events.

**Phantom Braking and False Speed Limits**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may incorrectly read speed limit signs or abruptly activate emergency brakes without an actual obstruction on the road.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates relating to the driver assistance systems and clean sensor lenses.
