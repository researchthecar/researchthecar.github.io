# 2018 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S is a high-performance luxury sedan renowned for its potent powertrain and sophisticated engineering. As a flagship model, it generally exhibits strong build quality and reliability, reflecting Mercedes-Benz's commitment to premium automotive standards. However, like any complex vehicle, particularly one with a performance focus, certain areas may require attention over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear, to provide a clear picture of potential concerns for owners and enthusiasts of this exceptional machine.

## Suspension

### AMG RIDE CONTROL+ Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner


## Electrical

**Software Glitches**

### 12V Battery

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S is a highly reliable performance sedan, with its core powertrain components demonstrating excellent durability. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are related to the sophisticated air suspension system, which can develop leaks over time, and occasional electrical gremlins within the infotainment system or premature 12V battery failure. These issues, while not pervasive, are the most commonly cited technical concerns. Overall, the E63 AMG S offers a robust ownership experience, provided these potential, albeit manageable, issues are addressed promptly.
