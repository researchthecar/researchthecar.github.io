# 2014 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2014 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S is a high-performance luxury sedan renowned for its potent V8 engine and sophisticated engineering. As a flagship model, it generally exhibits strong build quality and reliability, especially considering its performance capabilities. However, like any complex vehicle, certain areas may require attention over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for owners and prospective buyers. The AMG S variant, with its enhanced powertrain, is built to a high standard, but understanding specific component vulnerabilities is key to long-term ownership satisfaction.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8 (M157)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, check engine light, turbocharger underboost codes.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the wastegate actuator or the complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per actuator

**Coolant Leaks (Intercooler System)**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant level, engine overheating, visible coolant residue around intercooler hoses.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking intercooler hoses or seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT
**Torque Converter Seal Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission fluid leaks, potential for low fluid levels, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter seal.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2000

**Mechatronic Unit Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, harsh gear changes, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $2500 - $4000

## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Battery Drain Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, dead battery after short periods, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of parasitic draw, potential battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800 (including battery)

**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen freezing, navigation issues, audio problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the COMAND head unit.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S is a robust performance sedan with a generally good reliability record for its class. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the high-performance powertrain, specifically potential issues with turbocharger wastegate actuators and torque converter seals. Electrical gremlins, such as battery drain and COMAND system glitches, can also occur. While these issues can be costly to repair, they are not universally reported across all vehicles. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs are crucial for maintaining the E63 AMG S's performance and longevity.
