# 2021 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S, a high-performance variant of the E-Class, is renowned for its potent V8 engine, sophisticated all-wheel-drive system, and luxurious interior. As a halo model, it generally benefits from rigorous engineering and quality control. However, like any complex performance vehicle, it is not immune to potential issues. This data focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance, aiming to provide a clear picture for owners and prospective buyers. The focus is on specific components and their reported problems, offering insights into potential repair needs and associated costs.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (M177)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, check engine light, whistling noises from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty wastegate actuator or the complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Coolant Leaks (Intercooler System)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, loss of coolant, visible coolant residue around intercooler pipes.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or intercooler components.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Transmission

### AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 9-Speed Automatic
**Harsh Shifting / Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, hesitation when shifting into drive or reverse, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Software update, transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, or potential internal transmission repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,500


## Suspension

### AMG RIDE CONTROL+ Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the leaking air spring or associated air lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000 per corner

**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Incorrect ride height, suspension warning light, stiff or overly soft ride.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty suspension height sensors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND/MBUX)
**Software Glitches / Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, intermittent audio issues, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1,000


### 12V Battery Drain
_No major issues reported for this configuration._

## Exterior

### LED Headlights
**Adaptive Lighting Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights not turning or adjusting with steering, warning light on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty headlight control module or adaptive lighting motor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,500


## Interior

### Seat Heating/Ventilation Elements
**Malfunctioning Seat Climate Control**
- **Symptoms:** Seat heating or ventilation not working on one or both seats, intermittent operation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the heating/ventilation element or seat control module.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S is a highly capable performance sedan with a generally strong reliability record, especially considering its complex engineering. The most commonly reported issues tend to be related to the advanced systems, such as the air suspension, turbocharger wastegates, and the sophisticated infotainment. While these can lead to costly repairs, they are not indicative of widespread systemic failures. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are crucial for mitigating potential problems. For owners, budgeting for potential repairs related to these specific components is advisable, but overall, the E63 AMG S offers a compelling blend of performance and luxury with reasonable long-term reliability when properly cared for.
