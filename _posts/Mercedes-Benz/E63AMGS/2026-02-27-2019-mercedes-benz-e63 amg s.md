# 2019 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S is a high-performance luxury sedan renowned for its potent powertrain and sophisticated engineering. As a halo model within the E-Class lineup, it generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting Mercedes-Benz's commitment to quality and advanced technology. However, like any complex performance vehicle, specific areas can experience issues, particularly those related to its high-output engine and advanced drivetrain systems. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related reliability concerns for the 2019 model year, providing insights into potential repair costs and common problems.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (M177)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, boost pressure issues, rattling noise from turbo area.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty wastegate actuator or the complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Coolant Leaks (Intercooler System)**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant level warning, visible coolant residue, engine overheating, reduced performance.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or intercooler components.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC (9-Speed Automatic)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning lights, limp mode activation.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,800

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping gears, unusual noises from the transmission.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,000


## Suspension

### AIR BODY CONTROL (Adaptive Air Suspension)
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively, 'Visit Workshop' message.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust ride height, constant 'Visit Workshop' message, vehicle stuck at low height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, freezes, or restarts intermittently.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, car won't start, warning lights on dash.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S is a highly capable performance sedan with a generally strong reliability record for its class. The primary areas where owners may encounter non-routine issues are related to the advanced powertrain, particularly the turbochargers and transmission control modules, and the sophisticated AIR BODY CONTROL suspension system, which can be prone to leaks or compressor failures. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches or parasitic battery drain, are also noted but are less common. While these potential issues can be costly to repair, they do not detract significantly from the overall positive reliability perception of this luxury performance vehicle. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs are crucial for ensuring long-term dependability.
