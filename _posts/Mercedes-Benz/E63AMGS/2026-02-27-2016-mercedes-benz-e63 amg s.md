# 2016 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S, a high-performance variant of the E-Class, is renowned for its potent V8 engine and sophisticated engineering. As a flagship AMG model, it generally offers a high level of build quality and performance. However, like any complex performance vehicle, certain areas may require attention over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential issues and their associated costs for owners.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8 (M157)
**Turbocharger Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, check engine light, wastegate rattle.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty turbocharger actuator or the entire turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant loss, overheating, sweet smell from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or the intercooler.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, rough shifting, shuddering during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Rebuilding or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,500

**Mechatronics Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the transmission mechatronics unit.
- **Cost:** $1,800 - $3,500


## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air compressor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500 per corner

**Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle not raising, constant compressor noise, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,200


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, battery warning light, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty modules or battery.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S is a robust performance sedan, but its complexity means potential issues can arise, particularly with the high-performance powertrain and advanced air suspension. Owners should be prepared for potential costs related to turbocharger components, transmission mechatronics, and air suspension parts. While the electrical system can experience minor glitches, and the infotainment system may require updates, these are generally less critical than powertrain or suspension concerns. Overall, with diligent maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs, the E63 AMG S can remain a reliable and exhilarating vehicle.
