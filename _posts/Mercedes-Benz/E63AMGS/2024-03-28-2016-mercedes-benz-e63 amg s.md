**Introduction:** The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S is a high-performance sedan powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine making 577 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. It comes with a 7-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive. The E63 AMG S is praised for its blistering acceleration, precise handling, and luxurious interior. However, it faces some common issues like other performance vehicles.

**Engine**
**4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noises
    * Solution: Replace turbochargers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cam phaser issues:**
    * Symptoms: Rattling sounds, reduced engine power, and rough idling 
    * Solution: Replace the cam phasers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
    * Symptoms: Reduced fluid levels, slipping gears, transmission shuddering
    * Solution: Replace transmission pan gasket or transmission filter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Jerking or hesitation during gear changes
    * Solution: Replace mechatronic unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Symptoms: Battery dies after extended periods of non-use
    * Solution: Update software or replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Trim Pieces Fading:**
    * Symptoms: Chrome or black exterior trim fading and peeling
    * Solution: Replace or paint trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per piece
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside headlights
    * Solution: Replace headlight assembly or repair seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per headlight
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Panoramic Roof Malfunction:**
    * Symptoms: Roof not opening or closing properly, rattling noises
    * Solution: Replace or repair roof motor or tracks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Lag:**
    * Symptoms: Touchscreen delays or freezes
    * Solution: Software update or hardware replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S is a powerful and luxurious performance sedan. However, it faces some issues such as turbocharger failures, transmission fluid leaks, battery drain, and interior malfunctions. While the severity of these issues can vary, many can be costly to repair. Owners should be aware of these potential problems before purchasing or servicing a 2016 Mercedes-Benz E63 AMG S.
