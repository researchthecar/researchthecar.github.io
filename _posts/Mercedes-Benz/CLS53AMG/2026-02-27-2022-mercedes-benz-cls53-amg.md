# 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLS53 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLS53 AMG means looking at a stylish luxury performance sedan equipped with potent mild-hybrid powertrains and high-end technology. While owner experiences vary, prospective buyers should review available complaints regarding electrical systems and wheel durability during pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo Inline-6 Mild Hybrid (AMG Enhanced)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine utilizes an electric auxiliary compressor and a 48-volt mild-hybrid system (EQ Boost), generating approximately 429 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque. It delivers rapid acceleration paired with sophisticated performance dynamics, though buyers should verify the health of the complex 48V electrical architecture.

### 48-Volt Charging System

Advanced electrical architectures support heavy accessory loads and mild-hybrid functions, making professional diagnostic scans critical during vehicle evaluation.

**48V Charging System Warning and Power Reduction**
- **Symptoms:** A warning regarding the 48-volt charging system may appear on the dash, accompanied by climate control failure and a reduction in engine power to a crawl speed.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the 48V battery, starter-generator, and associated control modules for fault codes.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed AMG Speedshift TCT Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission features quick shift times and power delivery tailored to the AMG performance profile. Regular fluid maintenance is recommended to keep shifts crisp across all driving modes.

## Suspension

### Wheels

Suspension and wheel components are designed to balance sport handling with luxury ride comfort, but large low-profile wheels can be vulnerable to road impacts.

**Cracked Wheel Rim**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience unexpected tire pressure loss or sudden blowouts caused by structural cracking in the alloy wheel rims.
- **Solution:** Inspect all wheels closely for inner barrel cracks during a pre-purchase inspection and replace damaged rims as necessary.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

The passive safety suite includes comprehensive airbag protection, though owners should always ensure sensor systems are functioning properly.

**Failure of Air Bags to Deploy in a Collision**
- **Symptoms:** In certain frontal impact scenarios, owner reports indicate that front air bags may fail to deploy as expected.
- **Solution:** Verify that the vehicle has a clean history free of structural accident repairs and check for any pending safety recalls or sensor faults.
