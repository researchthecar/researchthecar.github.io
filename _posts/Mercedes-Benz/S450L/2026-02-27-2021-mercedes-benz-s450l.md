# 2021 Mercedes-Benz S450L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz S-Class represents the pinnacle of luxury, technology, and engineering for the brand. However, as an exceptionally complex flagship sedan, prospective buyers should review owner-reported issues concerning powertrain components, advanced driver assistance systems, and large wheel setups. While owner feedback highlights cutting-edge comfort and performance, mechanical and electronic issues can be expensive to diagnose and resolve outside of warranty coverage.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (S450)

A smooth and efficient 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine featuring mild-hybrid EQ Boost technology, producing around 362 horsepower. It delivers seamless power delivery, though its intricate electronic and turbo systems require diligent maintenance.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (S580)

A robust 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine pushing approximately 496 horsepower, offering effortless acceleration. Owners should monitor for potential valvetrain or actuator concerns as mileage accumulates.

**Camtronic Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle loses acceleration capability and illuminates the check engine light.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle diagnosed by a qualified specialist or dealership to replace the failed actuator components.
- **Cost:** $9000

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 9-speed conventional automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel drive or 4MATIC all-wheel drive. It is generally praised for smooth gear changes and optimized highway cruising efficiency.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

Suspension components provide an exceptionally plush ride, but vehicles equipped with large 21-inch AMG wheels are prone to rim cracking and frequent tire damage on rough road surfaces.

**Rim Cracking and Tire Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Low tire pressure warnings, sidewall bulges, and air loss due to cracked 21-inch alloy wheels.
- **Solution:** Inspect large-diameter wheels for structural cracks and replace damaged rims or low-profile tires as needed.

## Electrical

### Driver Assistance Cameras

Extensive electronics manage everything from cabin climate to autonomous driving features, occasionally triggering spurious warning messages when camera or sensor visibility is compromised.

**Camera System Unavailable Messages**
- **Symptoms:** Instrument panel displays warnings that the camera system is dirty or currently unavailable.
- **Solution:** Clean sensor areas or have the dealership inspect camera modules and associated software calibrations.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety suites offer robust collision mitigation, though owner reports indicate occasional false positives where collision avoidance systems activate abruptly without immediate hazard.

**Inadvertent Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle suddenly and unexpectedly engages the emergency brakes while driving at normal speeds.
- **Solution:** Check for relevant manufacturer software updates or service campaigns addressing driver assistance calibration.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz S-Class delivers unmatched luxury and technological sophistication, but potential buyers should proceed with care. Due to the high complexity of its onboard electronics, powertrain components, and potential wheel durability issues with larger rim packages, securing an extended warranty or comprehensive pre-purchase inspection is strongly advised.
