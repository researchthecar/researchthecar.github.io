## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, audible hissing from suspension components.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air strut or air spring.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Air Compressor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust ride height, suspension not inflating, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (MBUX)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, intermittent audio issues, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500 (for updates/diagnostics), $1000+ (for module replacement)


### 12V Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, battery warning lights, electrical components malfunctioning due to low voltage.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from modules or accessories, battery replacement if necessary.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz S450L demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The 3.0L inline-six engine and 9-speed automatic transmission are robust and generally free from significant issues. Potential areas of concern, though not widespread, tend to be related to the advanced AIRMATIC air suspension system, which can develop leaks or compressor faults, and the complex electrical system, where infotainment glitches or battery drain can occur. Interior components like seat actuators may also require attention over time. Overall, the S450L offers a reliable luxury experience, but owners should be aware of the potential costs associated with maintaining its sophisticated suspension and electronic systems.
