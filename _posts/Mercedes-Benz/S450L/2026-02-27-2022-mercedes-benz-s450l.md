# 2022 Mercedes-Benz S450L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz S-Class represents the pinnacle of luxury, technology, and engineering, but buyers in the used market should be aware of a few complex electronic, powertrain, and wheel-related issues reported by owners. While flagship sedans offer supreme comfort and advanced driver-assistance features, their heavy reliance on intricate computer architecture and high-performance low-profile tire packages can lead to notable maintenance considerations. Thoroughly inspecting service records and verifying proper mechanical and software updates will help ensure a smoother ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo Inline-6 (S500 / S450 Equivalent)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine features mild-hybrid EQ Boost technology, producing around 429 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exceptionally smooth power delivery and strong efficiency for a large luxury sedan, though prospective buyers should ensure all software campaigns are up to date.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (S580)

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine generates approximately 496 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, offering effortless acceleration. While robust, owners have occasionally noted complex electrical and mechanical fault codes requiring professional diagnostic attention.

**Engine Stalling and Camshaft Wear**
- **Symptoms:** The check engine warning light illuminates, followed by unexpected engine stalling while driving.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a certified dealership or mechanic for diagnostic trouble code scanning and camshaft inspection or replacement.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the rear wheels or 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system. Most drivers praise its seamless shifting, though rare software or hardware glitches can manifest as harsh gear engagement.

**Drivetrain Jolt During Gear Shifts**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle experiences a severe or violent jolt and push when transitioning between gears, particularly during highway cruising.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician check transmission control module software adaptations and inspect internal drivetrain components.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Low-Profile Tires

The air suspension and wheel packages provide an ultra-plush ride quality, but large factory wheel options paired with low-profile tires make the vehicle vulnerable to pothole damage.

**Frequent Tire Blowouts and Rim Damage**
- **Symptoms:** Repeated tire blowouts, sidewall blisters, and bent alloy wheels when driving over normal road imperfections on large 21-inch wheel setups.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels and low-profile tires for damage; consider switching to higher profile tires or smaller diameter wheels if road conditions are poor.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides immense stopping power appropriate for a heavy luxury vehicle, though minor environmental sticking can occasionally occur.

**Brake Seizing and Loud Release Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Brakes seize after exposure to rain or car washes, requiring extra accelerator pressure to get moving, accompanied by a loud screeching sound upon release.
- **Solution:** Have the brake calipers, pads, and rotors inspected for moisture sensitivity, corrosion, or mechanical binding.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Electrical Architecture

Advanced digital displays and high-voltage electrical systems power the cabin amenities but can occasionally experience software glitches or module failures.

**Dashboard Display and Head Unit Blackout**
- **Symptoms:** The central dashboard display, instrument cluster, and head unit go completely blank, causing a loss of driver gauges, climate controls, and backup camera functionality.
- **Solution:** Perform a system reset or have the dealer update software and inspect electrical connections.

## Summary Verdict

Purchasing a pre-owned 2022 flagship luxury sedan brings incredible comfort and advanced technology, but buyers should approach with careful inspection. Pay close attention to the health of the 21-inch wheel and tire assemblies, ensure all complex infotainment software has been updated, and verify that the powertrain shifts cleanly without hesitation. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist is strongly recommended to catch any lingering electrical or mechanical anomalies.
