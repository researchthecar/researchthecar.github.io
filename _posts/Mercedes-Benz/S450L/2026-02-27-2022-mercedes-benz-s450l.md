## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, loss of ride height, audible hissing from suspension components.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust ride height, constant running of compressor, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (MBUX)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, intermittent audio issues, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates or resets, potential module replacement in rare cases.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500 (for software updates/diagnostics)


### 12V Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, battery warning lights, electrical components malfunctioning due to low voltage.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from modules or accessories, battery replacement if necessary.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 (for battery and diagnostics)

**Intermittent Seat Adjustment Malfunction**

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz S450L demonstrates a strong overall reliability profile, consistent with its luxury segment expectations. The powertrain, including the 3.0L inline-six turbocharged engine and the 9G-TRONIC transmission, shows no significant widespread issues. Potential concerns are primarily concentrated in the sophisticated AIRMATIC air suspension system, where leaks or compressor failures can occur, and in the advanced electrical systems, particularly the MBUX infotainment with occasional software glitches and the possibility of 12V battery drain issues. Interior components like seat adjustment motors can also present minor issues. While these are not deal-breakers, owners should be aware of these potential, albeit infrequent, repair costs. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining the S450L's renowned dependability and luxury experience.
