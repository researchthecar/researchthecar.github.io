# 2016 Mercedes-Benz SLK350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz SLK350 offers an engaging open-air driving experience backed by refined luxury and strong performance. As shoppers evaluate this retractable hardtop roadster on the used market, understanding potential owner-reported concerns can help guide a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

Equipped with a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 302 horsepower, this naturally aspirated power plant delivers brisk acceleration and smooth delivery. It is generally regarded as a robust option, though proper maintenance history is essential for long-term reliability.

### Vapor Canister

Electrical and emissions hardware are generally dependable, but underbody mounts and sensor connections should be checked during routine inspections.

**Vapor Canister Mounting Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted that the EVAP vapor canister can detach from the underside of the vehicle at high speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the underbody mounting brackets and hardware during routine oil changes or services to ensure components are securely fastened.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Featuring a 7-speed automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel drive, this gearbox provides quick and seamless gear changes. Routine fluid changes help maintain smooth operation and prevent premature wear.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Panel

Exterior body components and panoramic roof options generally hold up well, though periodic inspection of seals and trim is recommended.

**Glass Panel Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported that the glass roof panel can unexpectedly loosen or detach while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the panoramic or sunroof roof assembly for secure bonding, loose trim, or structural wear, and have a qualified technician repair or replace compromised seals.

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz SLK350 combines luxury roadster performance with solid engineering. Buyers should perform a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on underbody mounting hardware, exterior trim, and roof panel integrity to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
