# 2021 Mercedes-Benz CLA35 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Mercedes-Benz CLA35 AMG, buyers are treated to a stylish, high-performance compact sedan that pairs everyday luxury with athletic handling. While owner-submitted reports to federal safety regulators offer valuable insight into potential trouble spots, it is important to remember these notes reflect individual experiences rather than widespread manufacturer defects. Taking a careful look at powertrain options and reported areas like body hardware or suspension can help you make a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (AMG-enhanced M260)

This hand-assembled 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 302 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating performance for the CLA35 AMG, though buyers should verify smooth power delivery during a test drive.

**Loss of Acceleration Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected losses of power while driving at highway speeds, limiting the ability to accelerate safely.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the fuel/propulsion system and powertrain control modules for fault codes or pending software updates.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift DCT 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission sends power to the 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system with lightning-fast gear changes. Routine fluid maintenance is key to keeping this performance gearbox shifting smoothly.

## Suspension

### Front Control Arm and Ball Joint

The sport-tuned suspension provides sharp cornering agility, though high-stress components should be thoroughly checked during a used car inspection.

**Front Ball Joint Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A sudden failure of the front suspension ball joint can cause handling instability or detachment of the wheel at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect front suspension components, control arms, and ball joints for excessive wear, play, or structural damage during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Electrical

### Tail Lamp Assembly

Electrical accessories and exterior lighting should be fully tested to ensure proper operation and weather resistance.

**Water Intrusion in Rear Light**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture and water precipitation can build up inside the rear lamp housing due to an imperfect seal.
- **Solution:** Inspect rear light housings for condensation and replace faulty gaskets or the entire lamp assembly if moisture penetration is present.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Assembly

Exterior body components and glass features require careful evaluation to ensure proper sealing and structural integrity.

**Panoramic Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced the glass sunroof panel spontaneously shattering or exploding while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Examine the panoramic glass roof for any preexisting stress cracks, chips, or improper alignment before finalizing your purchase.

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz CLA35 AMG is an engaging, high-performance luxury sedan that offers plenty of thrills for the enthusiast driver.
