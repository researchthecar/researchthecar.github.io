# 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLA35 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLA 35 AMG blends sharp styling and high-performance luxury into a compact four-door coupe layout. While driver reports are generally limited for this model year, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback regarding visibility accessories and foundational systems during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Engine

This hand-assembled or AMG-enhanced 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 302 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It delivers punchy acceleration and responsive power delivery, though proper maintenance and premium fuel are essential for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routes power through an AMG Performance 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system. It offers rapid gear changes suited for spirited driving, though prospective buyers should evaluate shift smoothness during a test drive.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Complaints concerning the braking system are sparse, but proper pad and rotor inspection remains vital for performance models.

**Brake Responsiveness Concern**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported that the vehicle did not slow down as expected when applying the brakes during highway traffic conditions.
- **Solution:** Have the braking system inspected by a qualified technician to verify pad life, rotor condition, and hydraulic pressure.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior complaints primarily highlight rare instances of spontaneous glass failure in the panoramic roof assembly.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof panel may suddenly fracture or explode into small pieces while driving at highway speeds without apparent impact.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for stress fractures and ensure warranty coverage or insurance applies if replacement is necessary.

### Visibility and Roof Assembly

Interior visibility and roof components generally offer modern luxury appointments, though overhead glass integrity should be verified.

**Overhead Glass Fracture**
- **Symptoms:** Occupants may experience a loud bang followed by sudden breakage of the panoramic sunroof glass while operating the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Check the overhead glass panel and sunshade for structural integrity and consult a dealership for evaluation.
