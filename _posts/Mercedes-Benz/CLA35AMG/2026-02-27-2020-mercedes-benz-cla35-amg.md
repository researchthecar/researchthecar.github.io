# 2020 Mercedes-Benz CLA35 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz CLA35 AMG offers sporty performance and luxury styling, but prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints carefully. While major powertrain options deliver strong performance, owner feedback highlights recurring concerns regarding exterior glass, electrical accessories, and select safety system components.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (AMG Enhanced)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 302 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration for an entry-level luxury performance model. Routine maintenance is critical, and buyers should verify that all software updates and recalls have been properly addressed.

**Engine Hesitation and Stuttering**
- **Symptoms:** The check engine light illuminates, and the vehicle may experience stuttering or jerking during acceleration or while reversing.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by an authorized service center to diagnose sensor readings, fuel delivery, or powertrain control module faults.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear changes paired with an AMG Performance 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system. It provides responsive engagement under spirited driving conditions, though proper fluid maintenance is essential for long-term durability.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera and Trunk Mechanism

Advanced safety features enhance daily driving confidence, but mechanical actuators for backup cameras and trunk latches can experience operational failures.

**Stuck Backup Camera Housing**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera housing emblem on the trunk remains permanently stuck open, or the camera display disappears shortly after shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Have the camera assembly and trunk latch mechanism inspected and replaced or lubricated by a qualified technician.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior glass and visibility components generally function well, though isolated reports indicate vulnerability to sudden glass breakage.

**Unexpected Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report a sudden loud pop or explosion sound followed by the panoramic glass sunroof shattering unexpectedly while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for pre-existing stress fractures and consult a dealership or insurance provider if replacement is necessary.
