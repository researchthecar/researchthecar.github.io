**Introduction:**

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz CLA35 AMG is a performance-oriented compact sedan powered by a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine producing 302 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. The CLA35 AMG is available with either a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission (7G-DCT) or an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission (8G-DCT). The vehicle received high praise for its sporty handling, aggressive styling, and luxurious interior but was criticized for its firm ride quality and limited rear-seat space.

**Engine:**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

- **Turbocharger Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from exhaust, unusual noises from engine bay.
    - Solution: Replacement of turbocharger and associated components.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Fuel Injector Malfunction:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, reduced fuel economy.
    - Solution: Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**7G-DCT:**

- **Shifting Issues:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Delayed or jerky gear changes, transmission slipping, inability to engage gears.
    - Solution: Software updates, clutch pack replacement, transmission rebuild.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    - Recalls: MB20-06, MB20-10

**8G-DCT:**

- **No Issues Found**

**Suspension:**

- **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from front suspension, vibration in steering wheel.
    - Solution: Replacement of strut mount bearings.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Rear Shock Absorber Leaks:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Loss of damping, reduced ride comfort, oil leaks from rear shocks.
    - Solution: Replacement of rear shock absorbers.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

- **Battery Discharge:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, loss of power, warning lights on dashboard.
    - Solution: Replacement of battery and/or alternator.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Infotainment System Malfunctions:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen unresponsive, system crashes, audio issues.
    - Solution: Software updates, component replacements.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    - Recalls: MB20-43

**Exterior:**

- **Sunroof Panel Leaks:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into cabin through sunroof, water stains on roof liner.
    - Solution: Replacement of sunroof seal or drainage tubes.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: MB20-54

**Interior:**

- **Headliner Sagging:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Ceiling fabric becomes loose or detached from roof.
    - Solution: Reattachment or replacement of headliner.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Trim Panel Buzzing:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise or vibrations from interior trim panels.
    - Solution: Tightening or replacement of trim panels.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2020 Mercedes-Benz CLA35 AMG has a moderate level of potential issues, with some concerns in the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The cost to fix these issues can vary widely depending on the severity of the problem. It is important to note that not all vehicles will experience these issues, and many owners may have a trouble-free ownership experience.
