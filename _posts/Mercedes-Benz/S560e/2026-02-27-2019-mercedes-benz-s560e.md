# 2019 Mercedes-Benz S560e Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz S560e represents the pinnacle of luxury, combining a plug-in hybrid powertrain with the supreme comfort of a flagship sedan. While ownership experiences are generally sophisticated and serene, used car buyers should remain mindful that early driver reports indicate occasional electronic quirks, intermittent powertrain behavior, and auxiliary system complaints.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6 Plug-In Hybrid

The 2019 S560e features a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 paired with an electric motor, delivering robust performance and impressive efficiency for a large luxury car. This plug-in hybrid layout offers smooth power delivery, though its advanced electronics require proper maintenance history verification.

**Abnormal Vibration and Service Messages**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected engine vibrations upon startup, alongside premature service reminder notifications shortly after routine maintenance.
- **Solution:** Have an independent mechanic or authorized service center perform a thorough electronic diagnostic scan and inspect engine mounts.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Managed by a 9-speed automatic transmission integrated with the hybrid drive unit, gear changes are designed to be seamless. Buyers should test all transmission and selector functions during a pre-purchase test drive.

**Shift Lever and Electronic Control Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The steering column shifter may intermittently fail to engage park, reverse, or neutral, occasionally misdirecting inputs to the navigation map cursor instead.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic shifter module and update vehicle software to the latest manufacturer calibration.

## Steering

### Steering Column Control Module

Steering and column-mounted controls offer effortless maneuverability, but rely heavily on stable software integration.

**Column Shifter Interference**
- **Symptoms:** The electronic steering column shifter fails to select drive or park modes correctly, operating secondary display functions instead.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the steering column module and update or replace faulty control units.

## Interior

### Keyless Entry and Cabin Electronics

Advanced cabin electronics and comfort features provide a high-tech user experience, though complex wiring demands attention to electrical health.

**Keyless Entry Auto-Lock Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly lock itself from the inside while the key fob is left inside the cabin, risking accidental lockouts.
- **Solution:** Check key fob programming, battery health, and body control module software for potential updates.

**Power Seat Electrical Odor**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced a burning-leather smell and minor smoke originating from underneath the driver's seat components.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat wiring harnesses, power adjustment motors, and related fuses for shorts or overheating.

## Safety

### Seat Belts and Airbag Systems

Comprehensive safety equipment protects occupants, though mechanical seat belt mechanisms should be physically verified during an inspection.

**Seat Belt Locking and Pretensioner Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Rear seat belts can lock up in place and refuse to retract, or safety pretensioners may overly tighten during highway driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt assemblies, buckle sensors, and restraint control modules, replacing jammed components as necessary.

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz S560e is a sophisticated luxury hybrid that rewards prospective buyers with unmatched comfort and technology.
