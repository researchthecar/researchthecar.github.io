# 2017 Mercedes-Benz CLS63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz CLS63 AMG S is a high-performance luxury sedan known for its potent powertrain and sophisticated design. As a performance-oriented model, it generally exhibits strong build quality, but like any vehicle, it can experience specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and prospective buyers. The data aims to offer a clear picture of the CLS63 AMG S's long-term dependability, particularly concerning its complex mechanical and electrical systems.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8 (M157)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, boost leaks, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty wastegate actuator or turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**PCV System Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks, rough idle, potential for oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) valve or system components.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, harsh shifting, shuddering during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Suspension

### AMG Sport Suspension
**Air Suspension Leaks (if equipped)**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of air springs, lines, or compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, audio issues, navigation problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates, or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or circuits causing the drain.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz CLS63 AMG S is a robust performance vehicle, but its complexity means potential for specific, albeit not universal, issues. The most commonly cited concerns revolve around the powertrain, particularly the turbocharger wastegate actuators and transmission torque converter or control module. Electrical gremlins, such as COMAND system glitches and parasitic battery drain, are also noted. While the suspension and exterior/interior components are generally reliable, owners should be aware of potential air suspension issues if equipped. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining this high-performance luxury sedan.
