# 2018 Mercedes-Benz CLS63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz CLS63 AMG S, a high-performance luxury sedan, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting its premium engineering and build quality. While owners can expect a robust driving experience, like any performance vehicle, certain components may require attention over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers. The CLS63 AMG S is known for its potent powertrain and sophisticated chassis, which are typically well-engineered but can be sources of specific, albeit infrequent, technical challenges.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8 (M157)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, boost pressure issues, check engine light illumination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the affected wastegate actuator or the complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Coolant Leaks (Intercooler Hoses)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant leaks, potential engine overheating, reduced intercooler efficiency.
- **Solution:** Replacement of cracked or degraded intercooler hoses.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT
**Torque Converter Seal Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission fluid leaks, potential for low fluid levels affecting shifting performance.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter seal.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200

**Mechatronics Unit Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the transmission mechatronics unit.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


## Suspension

### AMG RIDE CONTROL Sport Suspension
**Air Spring Bladder Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, uneven ride height, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the failed air spring bladder or the complete air strut assembly.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1100 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System Glitches
**COMAND System Freezing/Rebooting**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, system randomly restarts, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the COMAND head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, difficulty starting the vehicle, frequent battery replacement.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or components causing excessive power draw.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz CLS63 AMG S is a highly capable performance vehicle with a generally strong reliability record. The primary areas where owners might encounter technical issues beyond routine maintenance are related to the complex AMG powertrain, specifically potential wastegate actuator problems or intercooler hose leaks on the 5.5L V8 engine. The transmission, while robust, can occasionally experience torque converter seal leaks or mechatronics unit issues. The sophisticated AMG suspension, particularly the air springs, can also be a source of repair costs. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and parasitic battery drain, are also noted. Despite these potential concerns, the CLS63 AMG S remains a desirable performance sedan, with most issues being addressable by qualified technicians.
