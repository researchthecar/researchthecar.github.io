# 2018 Mercedes-Benz S450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz S450 represents the pinnacle of luxury, delivering advanced technology, exceptional comfort, and refined engineering. Prospective buyers should carefully inspect powertrains, electrical systems, and routine wear components during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (S450)

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk and smooth acceleration. It provides a more efficient entry point to the lineup while maintaining the vault-like quietness expected of a flagship luxury sedan.

**Engine Misfire and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional engine misfires during acceleration, accompanied by engine shaking, a check engine light, and temporary loss of power.
- **Solution:** Inspect ignition coils, spark plugs, and compression levels; replace faulty components as necessary.

**Oil and Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant levels or oil accumulation near the rear engine seals.
- **Solution:** Inspect gaskets and seals for premature wear, and replace leaking seals or hoses.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the rear wheels or 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system. It is generally robust, though occasional electronic selector glitches have been reported.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The gear shifter independently shifts into neutral while driving, requiring the driver to hold the shifter down to reset the system.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic gear selector module and update or replace faulty transmission control software/hardware.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking components provide strong stopping power when operating correctly, but any reports of reduced responsiveness require immediate professional inspection.

**Brake Deceleration Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal depression fails to slow or stop the vehicle adequately, or brakes fail to respond without prior warning.
- **Solution:** Thoroughly inspect the master cylinder, brake booster, and hydraulic lines for pressure loss or mechanical failure.

## Steering

### Electronic Steering System

The power-assisted steering system offers light and precise handling at low speeds while firming up on the highway, though rare electronic assist faults can occur.

**Steering Malfunction and Stiffness**
- **Symptoms:** Steering wheel becomes extremely difficult to turn accompanied by a dashboard warning message to increase physical effort.
- **Solution:** Check power steering electronic modules, wiring harnesses, and steering rack motor assemblies.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Communication System

The vehicle relies heavily on complex electrical architectures to power its luxury features, demanding healthy battery voltage and up-to-date software modules.

**Communication Module Software Errors**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle communication systems fail or trigger open recall notices regarding software errors.
- **Solution:** Perform authorized dealer software updates and module resets.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V407000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S450&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The electrical power bars inside the pre-fuse box in the trunk may not have been secured properly. If the power bars are not properly secured, it can cause higher electrical resistance increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the pre-fuse box, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V910000: Lane Departure: Lane Keep: Automatic Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S450&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the system remains active despite the driver not having their hands on the wheel, the driver's inattention may increase the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Mercedes has notified owners, and dealers will update the software of the steering control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V908000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S450&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the system remains active despite the driver not having their hands on the wheel, the driver's inattention may increase the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA has notified owners, and dealers will inspect the vehicles and correct the steering boot installation, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V915000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Pads](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S450&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The incorrect size brake components may lead to the wrong replacement components fitted to the vehicle during brake repairs, possibly affecting braking performance and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V816000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S450&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The rear passenger door on the driver side may indicate that it is locked, when it may not be. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 206, "Door Locks and Door Retention Components." If the door appears to be locked, the door may be unintentionally opened, increasing the risk of.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the left rear door lock, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz S450 offers supreme comfort and advanced luxury amenities, but its high-tech systems require careful scrutiny. Buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the powertrain, electronics, and brake systems to ensure trouble-free ownership.
