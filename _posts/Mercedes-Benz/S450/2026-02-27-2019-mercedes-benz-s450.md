# 2019 Mercedes-Benz S450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz S-Class represents the pinnacle of luxury, comfort, and advanced automotive technology. When shopping for a pre-owned model, understanding common owner-reported concerns regarding powertrain options and vehicle electronics can help guide your inspection and ensure a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo V6 (S450)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth and effortless acceleration. It is generally regarded as a refined powerplant well-suited for luxury cruising.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (S560)

The 4.0-liter biturbo V8 engine generates around 463 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque for robust performance. It offers strong reliability in standard operation, though proper maintenance history is essential.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes across rear-wheel drive and 4MATIC all-wheel drive configurations. It pairs effectively with available engines to maximize highway efficiency and responsiveness.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking components provide strong stopping power, but occasional actuator failures have been noted by owners.

**Rear Brake Actuator Seizure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported that the rear brake actuator can seize up, rendering the braking system inoperable upon starting the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle towed to a qualified facility or dealership to inspect and replace the failed brake actuator.

## Steering

### Steering Column

Steering precision is excellent, though power adjustment mechanisms can occasionally experience mechanical sticking.

**Steering Column Adjustment Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** The motorized up-and-down adjustment mechanism for the steering column can stick in the fully raised position and resist re-adjustment.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column adjustment motor and switch assembly, and lubricate or replace components as necessary.

## Electrical

### Keyless Entry and Locking

Advanced cabin electronics offer exceptional convenience features, though occasional sensor or locking glitches may arise.

**Unintended Door Locking**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced scenarios where the vehicle automatically locks itself from the inside while the key fob is inside the cabin.
- **Solution:** Check key fob programming and door module software for updates or faults.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V584000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S450&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The A, B, and C pillar covers may have been improperly installed, possibly affecting the deployment of the side curtain air bags and allowing the covers to detach in the event of a crash. If a side curtain air bag does not deploy as intended, or the pillar covers detach during a crash, there is an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify the owner, and a dealer will inspect the pillar covers, reinstalling them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V910000: Lane Departure: Lane Keep: Automatic Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S450&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the system remains active despite the driver not having their hands on the wheel, the driver's inattention may increase the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Mercedes has notified owners, and dealers will update the software of the steering control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V839000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S450&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A correctly fastened seatbelt may be inaccurately detected as being unfastened, preventing the seat belt pre-tensioners from activating in the event of a crash. If the seat belt tensioning does not activate in the event of a crash, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the seat belt buckles, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V449000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S450&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The software in the replaced instrument cluster may cause the seat belt warning system to not operate properly, only displaying a solid warning light if either the driver or passenger seat belt is unfastened, instead of a blinking light with an audible tone.
