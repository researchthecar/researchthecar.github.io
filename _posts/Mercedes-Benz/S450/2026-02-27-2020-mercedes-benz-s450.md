# 2020 Mercedes-Benz S450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Mercedes-Benz S450, buyers can generally expect an exceptionally luxurious flagship sedan experience with refined performance. While these luxury cars offer high-end comfort and advanced technology, prospective owners should review owner-reported feedback concerning various vehicle systems to ensure a well-maintained example.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged I6

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine provides smooth, robust power delivery suited for a flagship luxury sedan. Overall reliability is generally solid, though owners should monitor auxiliary engine systems during regular maintenance.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may suddenly stall while driving at highway speeds without prior warning, making it difficult to restart.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the fuel delivery system and replace the faulty fuel pump if necessary.

**Unintended Speed Increase**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent surges where vehicle speed increases unexpectedly by up to 35 mph.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership or specialized technician to check throttle body sensors and engine control module calibrations.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes that complement the smooth power output of the powertrain. It typically performs reliably when routine fluid maintenance intervals are observed.

## Exterior

### Glass and Sunroof

Exterior glass and panoramic roof panels require careful inspection for stress cracks or prior damage during a pre-purchase evaluation.

**Sudden Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Sunroof or windshield glass may crack spontaneously or shatter unexpectedly while driving or parked, creating loud noises and entering the cabin.
- **Solution:** Inspect glass components for prior impacts and replace damaged windshields or panoramic sunroof assemblies with OEM parts.
