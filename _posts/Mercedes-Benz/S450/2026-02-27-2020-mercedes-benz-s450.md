## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, loss of ride height, 'Service Air Suspension' warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air strut or air spring assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to maintain ride height, constant compressor running, 'Service Air Suspension' warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND/MBUX)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, navigation errors, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500


### 12V Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, battery warning lights, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from modules or accessories, battery replacement if necessary.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz S450 generally demonstrates good reliability, consistent with its luxury segment positioning. The powertrain (3.0L V6 engine and 9-speed automatic transmission) is robust and typically free from major concerns. Potential areas for attention include the AIRMATIC air suspension, where leaks or compressor issues can arise, and the complex electrical system, which may experience occasional software glitches or battery drain issues. These are not necessarily widespread failures but are common points of concern for vehicles with advanced technology. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are recommended to ensure long-term dependability.
