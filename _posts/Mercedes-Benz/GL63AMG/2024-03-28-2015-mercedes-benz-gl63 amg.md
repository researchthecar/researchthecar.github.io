**Introduction:**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz GL63 AMG is a luxury SUV renowned for its high performance and lavish amenities. It features a potent 5.5-liter V8 biturbo engine that generates 550 horsepower and 560 lb-ft of torque. The GL63 AMG is equipped with a 7-speed automatic transmission and comes standard with all-wheel drive. While praised for its exhilarating driving experience, the vehicle has encountered some issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

**5.5L V8 Biturbo**

* **Valve Cover Leaks:**
Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the valve covers, causing visible oil spots under the vehicle.
Solution: Replacing the faulty valve covers.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, and rattling noises.
Solution: Repairing or replacing the faulty turbocharger.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Delayed Gear Shifts:**
Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay when shifting gears.
Solution: Software update or transmission repairs.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Leaks:**
Description/Symptoms: Inability to maintain proper ride height, resulting in a sagging suspension.
Solution: Replacing faulty air suspension components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Strut Mount Wear:**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise and vibrations during driving.
Solution: Replacing the worn strut mounts.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
Description/Symptoms: Unexplained battery drain, leading to vehicle not starting.
Solution: Diagnosing and fixing the electrical fault causing the discharge.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

* **Headlight Malfunctions:**
Description/Symptoms: Flickering or failure of LED headlights.
Solution: Replacing the faulty headlight components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID: 19V246000

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rack Leaks:**
Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the vehicle through the panoramic sunroof when closed.
Solution: Replacing or resealing the faulty roof rack components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Tailgate Corrosion:**
Description/Symptoms: Rust or pitting on the tailgate.
Solution: Replacing the corroded tailgate or repainting.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattle:**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive rattling or squeaking noises coming from the panoramic sunroof.
Solution: Adjusting or replacing sunroof components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Dashboard Creaks:**
Description/Symptoms: Irregular noises and creaks from the dashboard.
Solution: Replacing or repairing dashboard components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz GL63 AMG exhibits a range of issues, some of which can be costly to repair. The severity of the issues varies, with some, such as occasional dashboard creaks, being relatively minor, while others, like turbocharger failure, can significantly impact the vehicle's performance and reliability. Owners should be aware of these potential problems and factor in the potential repair costs when considering purchasing or maintaining this vehicle.
