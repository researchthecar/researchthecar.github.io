# 2016 Mercedes-Benz GL63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury full-size SUV, the 2016 Mercedes-Benz GL63 AMG offers commanding performance, high-end comfort, and utility. However, prospective buyers should carefully weigh its sophistication against potential ownership costs and reported issues ranging from electrical issues to body and interior quirks. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable specialist is strongly recommended.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Turbo Diesel (GL350 BlueTEC)

A 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 diesel engine producing around 240 horsepower and 455 lb-ft of torque. It delivers strong low-end pulling power and high fuel efficiency for highway cruising, though diesel emissions and fuel system components require careful maintenance.

### 3.0L V6 Twin-Turbo (GL450)

A 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 gasoline engine pushing approximately 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It offers smooth, punchy acceleration and serves as a balanced mid-tier option between the base diesel and high-performance V8s.

### 4.7L V8 Twin-Turbo (GL550)

A potent 4.7-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine generating about 429 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. It provides effortless power delivery for towing and passing, though prospective buyers should monitor for standard twin-turbo plumbing and cooling wear.

### 5.5L V8 Twin-Turbo (GL63 AMG)

A hand-built 5.5-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine outputting a formidable 550 horsepower and 560 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating performance for a large SUV, but demands meticulous maintenance and can incur high repair costs.

**Engine Compartment Fire Hazard**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle can unexpectedly catch fire in the engine bay shortly after being parked.
- **Solution:** Inspect the engine bay thoroughly for any signs of fuel, oil, or electrical leaks; address worn lines immediately.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 7-speed automatic transmission paired across various engine trims, offering smooth gear shifts and refined highway cruising. Regular fluid changes are vital to maintain long-term reliability and shift quality.

**Unexpected Park Disengagement**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may spontaneously disengage from the Park position, causing the unattended vehicle to roll backward.
- **Solution:** Check for related software updates or mechanical shift linkage wear, and always engage the electronic parking brake on inclines.

## Electrical

### Battery and Drainage

Manages power distribution, charging, and numerous cabin electronics. Cabin water leaks can compromise critical electrical modules located in lower compartments.

**Water Intrusion in Battery Compartment**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the spare tire and battery well compartments, disrupting the battery and starter functionality.
- **Solution:** Inspect weather stripping, sunroof drains, and rear seals for clogs or gaps, and clear any standing moisture immediately.

## Exterior

### Tailgate and Seating

Cabin features and utility hardware that generally offer high comfort, though electronic tailgate latches and rear headrest positioning can occasionally frustrate users.

**Tailgate Self-Opening**
- **Symptoms:** The tailgate may unexpectedly unlatch and open while driving, refusing to close properly via the electronic door button.
- **Solution:** Inspect the tailgate latch mechanism, wiring harness, and electronic switch for failures or corrosion.

**Active Headrest Positioning**
- **Symptoms:** Second-row headrests may push passenger heads too far forward or sit at uncomfortable heights for smaller occupants and children.
- **Solution:** Adjust headrest settings where possible or utilize compatible child safety seats that accommodate the factory contours.

### Sunroof and Glass

Body panels and expansive glass elements designed for style and visibility, though large panoramic glass panels carry a known risk of spontaneous breakage reported by some owners.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof panels may suddenly shatter or explode while driving or parked, raining glass into the cabin.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for pre-existing micro-fractures, and consider applying a protective safety film if appropriate.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GL63 AMG is a high-performance luxury cruiser that delivers tremendous power and road presence, but it demands diligent upkeep. Buyers should inspect the vehicle thoroughly for potential water intrusion in electrical compartments, sunroof glass integrity, and powertrain health. Working with an independent mechanic who specializes in AMG models can help avoid costly surprises after purchase.
