# 2016 Mercedes-Benz GL63 AMG

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GL63 AMG, a high-performance luxury SUV, generally exhibits strong reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. However, as with any high-performance vehicle, there are specific areas that warrant attention. Owners often report satisfaction with the robust engine and transmission, which are engineered for power and durability. The primary concerns tend to revolve around the complex electronic systems and the specialized nature of AMG components, which can sometimes lead to more costly repairs if issues do arise. Regular maintenance is crucial to ensure the longevity of this performance-oriented machine.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift Plus Automatic
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid, rough shifting, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals, transmission pan, or cooler lines.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,200

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, delayed engagement, slipping.
- **Solution:** Torque converter replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Suspension

### Active Curve System (Optional)
**Hydraulic Leaks/Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Increased body roll, warning lights on the dashboard, unusual noises from suspension.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic pump, lines, or actuators.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,500


### Standard Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, compressor running excessively, harsh ride.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or compressor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch, display errors.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000


### Sensors and Modules
**Parking Sensor Failures**
- **Symptoms:** False alerts, sensors not detecting obstacles, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty sensors or control module.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GL63 AMG is a powerful and generally reliable vehicle, especially considering its performance focus. The 5.5L twin-turbo V8 engine and 7-speed AMG transmission are robust, though potential issues like turbocharger problems and transmission fluid leaks can occur and require specialized attention. The advanced suspension systems, particularly the optional Active Curve System, can be a source of more significant repair costs if hydraulic components fail. Electrical gremlins, especially with the infotainment and sensor systems, are also noted. While not immune to issues, proactive maintenance and addressing problems promptly can help maintain the GL63 AMG's performance and longevity.
