# 2018 Mercedes-Benz G550 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a luxury heavy-duty SUV like the 2018 Mercedes-Benz G550 offers a blend of iconic old-school styling and robust modern performance. Because this model sits at the crossroads of rugged engineering and high-end luxury, prospective used-car buyers should approach with a clear understanding of its specialized mechanical foundation. While official owner-reported complaint data for this specific model year is remarkably sparse, evaluating the powertrain options and overall build quality remains essential for a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers approximately 416 horsepower and 450 lb-ft of torque, offering immense power for both highway and off-road driving. This powerplant is generally robust, though proper maintenance and regular oil service are critical to ensure long-term turbocharger health.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission channels power seamlessly to the permanent four-wheel-drive system with triple locking differentials. It provides refined shifts suited to the vehicle's heavy-duty character, though routine fluid changes are recommended to maintain optimal performance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=G550&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz G550 stands out as a formidable and prestigious choice for luxury SUV buyers, backed by a potent V8 engine and a stout powertrain architecture. With minimal owner-reported issues on file, standard used-car inspection diligence—focusing on service history, undercarriage wear, and electronics—will serve buyers well in finding a dependable example.
