# 2021 Mercedes-Benz G550 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz G550 combines iconic luxury styling with robust modern performance, making it a highly desirable high-end SUV. While owner reports to safety authorities highlight a few unusual electrical and braking incidents, overall mechanical data remains limited. Prospective buyers should carefully inspect electrical systems and powertrain operation during a pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L V8 Biturbo

This 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces 416 horsepower and 450 lb-ft of torque, offering strong acceleration and formidable capability. It is generally robust, though proper maintenance is critical for long-term luxury vehicle reliability.

### Engine Electrical and Ignition

Complex onboard electronics govern major vehicle functions, meaning occasional software glitches or sensor faults can impact drivability.

**Unexpected Engine Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles have occasionally stalled or shut down completely while idling at a red light or traveling at low speeds, accompanied by dashboard safety warnings and a temporary failure to restart.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan the vehicle computer for diagnostic trouble codes and inspect the electrical harness and ignition system modules.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and responsive power delivery across various driving conditions. It pairs seamlessly with the standard four-wheel-drive system and requires regular fluid changes to maintain optimal shifting.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The heavy-duty braking system provides strong stopping power, but electronic control module glitches can occasionally trigger abnormal behavior.

**Brake Lockup**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the service brakes unexpectedly locked up while driving, creating hazardous traffic situations.
- **Solution:** Inspect the anti-lock braking system (ABS) module, brake calipers, and related electronic stability control sensors for faults or software updates.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V483000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=G550&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect, and replace as necessary, the rear seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V230000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=G550&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A faulty electrical contact within the seat position switch may prevent the driver and passenger air bags and seat belt force limiters from properly activating during a crash. Air bags and seat belts that do not perform properly in a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the seat position switches, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V097000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=G550&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A loss of anti-lock brakes or electronic stability control can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and either install a protective sleeve or replace the front axle wiring harness, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz G550 is a powerful and luxurious machine, but its complex electrical architecture requires careful evaluation. While major mechanical failures are infrequent in owner feedback, isolated reports of sudden engine stalling and brake lockups underscore the importance of a thorough professional inspection. Buyers should prioritize examples with verified service history and clear diagnostic scans before purchasing.
