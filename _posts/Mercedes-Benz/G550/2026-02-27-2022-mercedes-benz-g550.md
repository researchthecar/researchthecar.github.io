# 2022 Mercedes-Benz G550 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Mercedes-Benz G550, buyers can generally expect a powerful luxury SUV built for rugged capability and iconic style. While comprehensive owner feedback is limited, reviewing specific powertrain specifications and remaining vigilant during pre-purchase inspections will help ensure a confident buying experience.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L V8 Biturbo

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 416 horsepower and 450 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust performance. It offers strong reliability overall, though buyers should verify proper maintenance history.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9G-Tronic 9-speed automatic transmission comes paired with standard all-wheel drive, providing smooth shifting and refined power delivery. It is a durable unit well-suited to the vehicle's heavy-duty demands.
