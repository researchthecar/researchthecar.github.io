# 2020 Mercedes-Benz G550 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used Mercedes-Benz G550 offers a blend of iconic luxury and robust performance, but buyers should approach with careful consideration regarding maintenance and known owner-reported issues. As with any high-end luxury vehicle, keeping up with preventative care is essential to avoiding costly surprises.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L V8 Biturbo

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers approximately 416 horsepower and 450 lb-ft of torque, providing exceptional power for both on-road and off-road driving. It generally offers solid performance, though routine maintenance is critical due to the complex nature of its turbocharged system.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides smooth power delivery and aids in highway efficiency. It pairs well with the V8 engine, though owners should ensure regular fluid changes to maintain long-term reliability.

## Interior

### Interior Trim and Accessories

Cabin features premium materials and advanced electronics, though specific trim components can occasionally face logistical delays for recall service.

**Recall Parts Delay and Availability**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience delays in scheduling safety recall repairs due to backordered replacement parts.
- **Solution:** Check with an authorized dealership to verify if open recalls have been completed before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V090000: Power Train:Driveline:Differential Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=G550&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Deactivation of the vehicle stability control system or anti-lock brakes may increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the differential locking modules and replace them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V247000: Latches/Locks/Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=G550&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** If an unrestrained child unexpectedly opens a door, they may fall from the vehicle increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will install a label on the rear doors with the correct operating direction, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V097000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=G550&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A loss of anti-lock brakes or electronic stability control can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and either install a protective sleeve or replace the front axle wiring harness, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The Mercedes-Benz G550 remains a commanding and capable luxury SUV, but prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect service records and verify the completion of any open safety recalls. While major mechanical failures are not overwhelmingly common in owner reports, maintenance costs for luxury components can be high.
