# 2017 Mercedes-Benz G550 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury SUV, the Mercedes-Benz G550 stands out with its iconic styling and robust off-road heritage. Prospective buyers evaluating this model should carefully inspect electronic systems, stability controls, and driver-assistance features during a pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8

Equipped with a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing approximately 416 horsepower and 450 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant delivers brisk acceleration and strong towing capability, though buyers should verify regular maintenance history for optimal longevity.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Utilizes a 7-speed automatic transmission paired with a permanent four-wheel-drive system and triple locking differentials. It offers smooth gear shifts and refined highway cruising, making it well-suited for both daily driving and rugged conditions.

## Brakes

### Anti-lock Braking and Stability Control

Manages stopping power and vehicle stability, requiring careful functional checks during used car evaluation.

**ABS and Electronic Stability Control Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported dashboard warning lights and complete or intermittent failure of anti-lock braking and dynamic handling systems.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the ABS module, wheel speed sensors, and stability control software for fault codes.

## Electrical

### Traction Control Module

Controls core electronic safety systems and vehicle stability programming.

**Traction Control Software and Module Errors**
- **Symptoms:** Triggered error codes and software glitches can render the vehicle temporarily undrivable, requiring specialized module replacement.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a certified service center is required to identify faulty control units and update or replace affected modules.

### Backup Camera and Parking Sensors

Assists with driver visibility and low-speed maneuvering.

**Intermittent Rear Camera and Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rearview cameras and proximity sensors may intermittently fail to display or function properly, potentially creating reversing hazards.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera wiring harnesses, lens cleanliness, and sensor modules; replace faulty hardware as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=G550&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 17V713000: Forward Collision Avoidance: Adaptive Cruise Control](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=G550&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A malfunction of the Electronic Stability Program (ESP), while Distronic Plus is engaged, may cause the engine to not reduce power, regardless of the vehicle's speed, driving situation or brake application. Increased braking effort and/or shifting to neutral would be needed to stop the vehicle, lengthening the distance needed to stop the vehicle and.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the ESP control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V817000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Power Assist:Vacuum:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=G550&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Increased braking effort may increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the vacuum hose, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

Overall, the Mercedes-Benz G550 offers strong performance and a distinctive luxury experience, but prospective buyers should conduct a thorough inspection. Pay close attention to electronic stability controls, sensor operations, and general diagnostic health to minimize unexpected repair outlays.
