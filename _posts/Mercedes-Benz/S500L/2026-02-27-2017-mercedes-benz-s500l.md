# 2017 Mercedes-Benz S500L

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S500L, a flagship luxury sedan, is generally recognized for its high level of engineering and comfort. While it offers a sophisticated driving experience, like any complex vehicle, it can encounter specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance. Owners can expect a premium ownership experience, but awareness of potential problem areas can help in proactive maintenance and cost management. The S500L combines potent performance with advanced technology, making its reliability a key factor for discerning buyers.

---


## Engine

### 4.7L Twin-Turbo V8 (M278)
**Timing Chain Tensioner Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Oil Pan)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or oil pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200

## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, 'airmatic system malfunction' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air struts or air springs.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,300


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Software Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, intermittent audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system reset; potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dying unexpectedly, frequent need for jump starts, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or circuits causing drain.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000+

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S500L is a highly reliable luxury vehicle, with its core powertrain components generally performing well. The most commonly cited issues tend to be related to the advanced AIRMATIC air suspension system, which can develop leaks or compressor problems, and occasional electrical gremlins within the sophisticated infotainment and battery management systems. Engine-specific concerns, such as timing chain tensioner wear and minor oil leaks, are also documented but are not considered widespread failures. Overall, the S500L offers a robust ownership experience, with potential repair costs leaning towards the higher end due to the complexity and premium nature of the vehicle.
