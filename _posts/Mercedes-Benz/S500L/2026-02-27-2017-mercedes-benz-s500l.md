# 2017 Mercedes-Benz S500L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S-Class delivers flagship luxury, advanced technology, and effortless performance, making it an appealing choice on the used market. However, prospective buyers should approach with awareness that maintaining a high-end European luxury vehicle can involve significant costs. While major mechanical failures are relatively uncommon, owners have reported isolated concerns ranging from interior trim separation to electrical and sunroof issues.

---

## Engine

### 4.7L Biturbo V8

Powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.7-liter V8 engine producing around 449 horsepower, this powertrain offers swift, ultra-smooth acceleration. Routine maintenance is critical for long-term reliability and avoiding costly upkeep.

**Fuel System and Evaporative Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light on the instrument panel or strong fuel odors.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the fuel tank, lines, and EVAP system, and replace damaged or leaking components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes that complement the luxury cruiser character. Overall durability is generally solid when properly serviced.

**Automatic Shift to Neutral Upon Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle shifts into neutral instead of park when the ignition button is pressed while still in drive.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership or specialist to check for software updates or selector module behavior.

## Steering

### Steering Column

The power-adjustable steering column provides personalized comfort, though occasional electrical motor or mechanism faults can restrict movement.

**Power Steering Wheel Adjustment Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Steering wheel moves in and out normally, but fails to adjust up and down, becoming stuck in place.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column adjustment motor and switch mechanism, and replace faulty parts.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Glass

Exterior styling is timeless, though panoramic glass panels and windshields require careful inspection for chips, cracks, or track alignment issues.

**Panoramic Sunroof Binding**
- **Symptoms:** A cracking sound heard while operating the sunroof, followed by failure to open or close properly.
- **Solution:** Inspect the tracks, rails, and mechanical linkages, and replace jammed or broken sunroof components.

## Interior

### Dashboard Trim

Cabin materials are exceptionally plush, though sun exposure and heat can occasionally cause adhesive failure on leather dash components.

**Dashboard Leather Delamination**
- **Symptoms:** Leather covering on the dashboard begins to pull away or warp around the passenger and driver airbag deployment areas.
- **Solution:** Replacement or professional reupholstering of the dashboard assembly.
- **Cost:** $3,000 to $6,000

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S-Class remains an engineering benchmark for comfort and technological sophistication. Used buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection paying close attention to dashboard trim condition, electronics, and mechanical systems. Budgeting for higher-than-average maintenance costs is essential to keep this flagship sedan running at its best.
