# 2018 Mercedes-Benz S500L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz S-Class delivers an ultra-luxury driving experience with advanced technology, plush comfort, and powerful engine options. While generally sophisticated and refined, prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints regarding powertrain performance, electronic glitches, and brake responsiveness. Diligent maintenance history and a pre-purchase inspection are strongly advised for this flagship luxury sedan.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L V8 Bi-Turbo (S560)

The 4.0L twin-turbocharged V8 engine provides robust power and smooth acceleration, serving as a staple powerplant for high-end luxury models. It offers strong performance, though complex mechanical systems demand rigorous maintenance to prevent leaks and ignition issues.

**Engine Misfire and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional engine misfires during acceleration accompanied by shaking, loss of power, and an illuminated check engine light.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose ignition components, fuel delivery, and cylinder compression, then replace faulty parts as needed.

**Engine Oil and Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Unexplained fluid spots or low fluid levels, such as engine oil leaking from the rear main seal or a slow coolant leak.
- **Solution:** Inspect engine seals and gaskets, and replace worn seals or hoses to prevent low fluid levels.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes tailored to maximize efficiency and smooth power delivery. While generally refined, owners should monitor for intermittent electronic gear selection faults.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The gear shifter independently shifts into neutral while driving, requiring the driver to hold the shifter for an extended period to reset.
- **Solution:** Have the transmission control module and linkage inspected by a specialist for software updates or hardware repairs.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides strong stopping power when operating correctly, but critical failures have been reported by some owners.

**Brake Pedal Response Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal depression fails to slow or stop the vehicle adequately, resulting in sudden loss of deceleration capability.
- **Solution:** Inspect the entire hydraulic brake system, master cylinder, and brake booster immediately, replacing failed components.

## Steering

### Electronic Steering System

The power steering system provides effortless maneuverability at low speeds and reassuring weight on the highway, though electronic glitches can occasionally trigger manual effort warnings.

**Sudden Steering Stiffness**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel becomes extremely difficult to turn while driving, accompanied by a steering malfunction warning message.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the electronic power steering module and wiring harness, and replace faulty steering components as necessary.

## Interior

### Dashboard Trim

The interior cabin features exquisite materials and top-tier craftsmanship, though prolonged environmental exposure can occasionally cause trim degradation.

**Dashboard Leather Peeling**
- **Symptoms:** The leather covering on the passenger side of the dashboard begins to peel up or warp.
- **Solution:** Consult an interior trim specialist for re-gluing or replacing the affected leather dashboard panels.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz S-Class remains a pinnacle of engineering and luxury, but prospective buyers should approach with careful inspection habits.
