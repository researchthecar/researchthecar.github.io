**Introduction**

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLB250 is a compact luxury SUV that features a powerful 2.0-liter turbocharged engine and an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission. It generates 221 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The GLB is praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and impressive safety features. However, it also has some notable issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Engine**
    * **Fuel Injector Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, rough idling, reduced power
        * Solution: Replace faulty fuel injectors
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Turbocharger Wastegate Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced boost pressure, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace turbocharger
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**
    * **Clutch Pack Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears
        * Solution: Replace clutch packs
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Random warning lights, electrical component failures
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the components involved

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin during rain or car wash
    * Solution: Replace or repair panoramic sunroof seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: NHTSA Recall Number: 20V-552 - Sunroof May Detach from Vehicle

**Summary**

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLB250 is a generally reliable vehicle, but it has experienced some notable issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. The severity of these issues can range from minor annoyances to major repairs. The estimated costs to fix can be significant, and it is essential to consider the potential expenses when purchasing or owning this vehicle.
