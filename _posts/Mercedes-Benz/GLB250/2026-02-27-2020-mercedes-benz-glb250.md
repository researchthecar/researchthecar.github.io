### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLB250 demonstrates good overall reliability, with its core mechanical components (engine and transmission) showing no significant widespread issues. Routine maintenance and addressing any electrical anomalies promptly are recommended for optimal long-term ownership.
