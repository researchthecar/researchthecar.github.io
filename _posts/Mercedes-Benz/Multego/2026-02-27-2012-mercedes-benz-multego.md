# 2012 Mercedes-Benz Multego

### Overview
The 2012 Mercedes-Benz Multego, a model primarily known for its commercial applications, generally exhibits robust engineering, reflecting Mercedes-Benz's reputation for durability. While specific data for this exact model year and variant can be scarce in consumer-focused reliability databases, its underlying components often share similarities with other Mercedes-Benz commercial vehicles. Preventive maintenance and timely servicing are crucial for maximizing the longevity and minimizing unexpected downtime of these vehicles.

## Engine

### OM934 5.1L Inline-4 Diesel
**EGR System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light illumination, reduced engine power, rough idling, increased emissions.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of EGR valve and cooler, potential software updates.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200

**Turbocharger Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased exhaust smoke.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger inspection, repair, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500


### OM936 7.7L Inline-6 Diesel
**Fuel Injector Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, misfires, poor fuel economy, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles, overheating, low coolant level, sweet smell from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair/replacement of hoses, water pump, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Transmission

### Mercedes-Benz G 85-6 Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch disc, pressure plate, and release bearing.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1400


### Allison 3000 Automatic
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Low transmission fluid level, shifting problems, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair of seals, gaskets, or cooler lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2000


## Suspension

### Standard Leaf Spring & Air Suspension
**Air Suspension Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Leak detection and repair of air springs, lines, or compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Worn Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn suspension bushings.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600
