**Introduction:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Alliado is a luxury sedan that comes with two engine options: a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder and a 3.0L turbocharged V6. Both engines are paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The 2.0L engine produces 255 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.0L engine produces 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. The Alliado has been praised for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and advanced technology features. However, it has also been criticized for its high price and recurring mechanical issues.


**Engine**
**2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder**

**Fuel Injector Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, rough idling, reduced engine power, illuminated check engine light
Solution: Replacement of faulty fuel injectors
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 20V066000

**3.0L Turbocharged V6**

**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive smoke, whining or whistling noise, illuminated check engine light
Solution: Replacement of faulty turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**9-Speed Automatic**

**Shifting Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Jerky or delayed shifting, transmission slipping, inability to engage gears, illuminated check engine light
Solution: Repair or replacement of faulty transmission components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
**No Issues Found**

**Interior**
**Dashboard Rattle**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise and vibration from the dashboard, particularly over rough roads
Solution: Tightening or replacement of loose components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Alliado is a feature-rich luxury sedan, but it is not without its issues. The engine and transmission problems can be particularly costly to repair, especially for the V6 engine. The electrical system and interior components also have some reported issues. The severity and cost of these issues can be a major concern for potential buyers.
