**Introduction**

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz Alliado is a luxury mid-size SUV that was available with one available engine and a single transmission option. The 3.5L V6 produces 302 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque and is mated to a 7-speed automatic transmission. The Alliado received praise for its spacious and comfortable interior, but it was criticized for its uninspired driving dynamics and high price.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6**

    * **Intake Valve Camshaft Adjustment Issue**
        * Description/Symptoms: The intake valve camshaft adjuster can fail, causing a rough idle and a loss of power.
        * Solution: Replace the intake valve camshaft adjuster.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Oil Leak from Valve Cover Gasket**
        * Description/Symptoms: The valve cover gasket can fail, causing an oil leak.
        * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic**

    * **Jerking or Lurching When Shifting**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may jerk or lurch when shifting gears.
        * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission fluid.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator can fail,导致电池放电。
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery can drain prematurely.
    * Solution: Test the battery and charging system to identify the source of the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Corrosion**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door handles can corrode over time.
    * Solution: Replace the door handles.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Water Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof can leak water into the cabin.
    * Solution: Replace the sunroof seal or adjust the sunroof alignment.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Panel Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard panel can crack over time.
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard panel.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz Alliado has a number of potential issues, including engine, transmission, electrical, exterior, and interior problems. The severity of these issues varies, but many can be expensive to repair. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor them into their purchase decision.
