# 2024 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG E Performance Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG E Performance represents the pinnacle of luxury and plug-in hybrid performance, blending a hand-built AMG engine with advanced electric propulsion. Because this is a high-tech flagship vehicle, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback regarding complex electronics and powertrain integration. As with any sophisticated modern luxury sedan, a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified specialist is recommended.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 Plug-In Hybrid

This high-performance powertrain pairs a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine with an electric motor to deliver immense horsepower and torque. It offers blistering acceleration while incorporating complex hybrid management systems that require proper software maintenance.

**Control Unit and Instrument Cluster Resets**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle control units can trigger increased system resets as part of fallback behavior, causing the digital instrument cluster display to temporarily cut out or go completely blank.
- **Solution:** Have a certified dealership check for software updates or diagnostic trouble codes related to the vehicle control modules.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed AMG Speedshift MCT Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed multi-clutch automatic transmission is engineered to handle massive torque outputs while providing rapid gear changes. Smooth operation depends heavily on sophisticated electronic controls and regular software updates.

**Harsh Shifting and Power Train Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported harsh or abnormal shifts during normal acceleration, particularly between 3rd and 4th gear, as well as rare instances of unexpected power loss at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership to inspect transmission software calibration, fluid levels, and related powertrain control modules.

## Suspension

### Tires and Wheels

The suspension and wheel setup prioritizes exceptional handling and ride comfort, though low-profile tires remain vulnerable to road hazards.

**Low-Profile Tire Bumps and Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Low-profile performance tires on large wheels can develop unexpected bubbles or bumps on the sidewall, potentially leading to rapid air loss.
- **Solution:** Inspect tires regularly for sidewall damage and replace damaged low-profile tires promptly.

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG E Performance is an engineering masterpiece offering supreme luxury and staggering hybrid performance.
