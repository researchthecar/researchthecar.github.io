## Engine

### OM 936 7.7L Inline-6 Diesel
**DPF Regeneration Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced power, increased fuel consumption, rough idling.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration, DPF cleaning, or replacement. Ensure proper driving cycles for regeneration.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**AdBlue System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** AdBlue warning light, reduced engine power, emissions system faults.
- **Solution:** Check AdBlue fluid level and quality, inspect sensors and pumps, potential software updates or component replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000


## Transmission

### Mercedes-Benz G 280-6 Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Clutch replacement.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1800


### Mercedes-Benz GO 210 Automatic
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, delayed gear engagement, slipping.
- **Solution:** Torque converter repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500


## Suspension

### Air Suspension System
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Leak detection and repair, replacement of damaged air springs or lines.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust ride height, constant vehicle sag, no air pressure.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200
