# 2017 Mercedes-Benz S550 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S550 offers exceptional luxury, advanced technology, and effortless performance, making it a tempting flagship sedan on the used market. However, prospective buyers should approach with caution regarding high-end maintenance costs and occasional reports of interior trim separation, exterior glass issues, and complex electrical or powertrain quirks.

---

## Engine

### 4.7L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.7-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 449 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, delivering smooth and authoritative acceleration. Routine maintenance can be expensive, and buyers should verify service history and inspect for fluid leaks.

**Fuel System and Check Engine Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of the check engine symbol on the instrument panel immediately after refueling or normal operation.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning at a qualified service center to inspect fuel tank integrity, EVAP system components, and clear fault codes.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly through rear-wheel drive or 4MATIC all-wheel-drive configurations. While generally robust, electronic shift logic issues can occasionally occur.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle shifts into neutral instead of park when the ignition button is pressed while still in drive.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership for software updates or inspect the electronic gear selector module and door switch sensors.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Rims

Air suspension provides a plush ride quality, but larger low-profile wheel options can be susceptible to damage from road imperfections.

**Cracking AMG Wheels**
- **Symptoms:** Rear alloy wheels developing structural cracks under normal daily driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged wheels with reinforced or standard non-AMG wheel setups if recurring damage persists.

## Steering

### Power Steering Adjustment

Steering feel is precise and properly weighted for a luxury cruiser, though power adjustment mechanisms can occasionally experience mechanical failure.

**Stuck Power Steering Column**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel moves inward and outward normally, but fails to adjust upward and downward, sticking in the up position.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column adjustment motor and switch assembly for mechanical binding or electrical failure.

## Exterior

### Glass and Sunroof

Exterior glass and panoramic roof features add an airy feel to the cabin but require careful inspection for alignment and stress cracks.

**Windshield and Sunroof Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Cracking sounds from the panoramic sunroof during operation or repeated, spontaneous windshield cracks under normal driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect sunroof tracks for binding and replace damaged windshields or sunroof assemblies with OEM glass.

## Interior

### Dashboard Trim

Cabin materials are generally world-class, though high-heat environments can cause cosmetic adhesive failures around the upper dashboard.

**Leather Dashboard Separation**
- **Symptoms:** The leather wrapping on the dashboard begins to pull away or detach near the passenger and driver side airbag deployment areas.
- **Solution:** Professional re-gluing or complete dashboard replacement, which can involve significant out-of-pocket costs.
- **Cost:** $3,000 to $6,000

**[Recall 21V197000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S550&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The front roof panel located between the windshield and the panoramic sunroof may not have been bonded correctly to the vehicle during a prior repair. The adhesive bonding of the front panel might deteriorate, possibly causing the front panel to detach from the vehicle and become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Mercedes-Benz dealer will remove the bonded panel and will install a new front panel by following the detailed repair instructions and using the approved primer, cleaner and adhesive, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V072000: Communication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S550&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The communication module might have been inadvertently deactivated during a service visit, which could have disabled the Emergency Call (eCall) function. A disabled eCall system would prevent a vehicle occupant from contacting emergency services through the call center in an emergency, potentially delaying emergency responders and increasing the risk of.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the communication module and activate it, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S550&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 17V714000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S550&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A transistor within the power steering control unit may overheat resulting in the power steering system being disabled. If the power steering assist is unexpectedly disabled, it may increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the steering rack, replace it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V829000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S550&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The seat belt extenders on the affected vehicles may not retract as intended and may potentially break the event of a crash. In the event of a crash, if the seat belt extender does not retract and/or the extender breaks, the seat occupant may not be properly restrained, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S550 remains a pinnacle of engineering and comfort, but used buyers should budget carefully for potential high-cost repairs. Prioritize models with comprehensive service records and have a trusted specialist inspect the electronics, dashboard upholstery, and suspension components before purchasing.
