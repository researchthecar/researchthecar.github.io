# 2009 Mercedes-Benz S550

### Overview
The 2009 Mercedes-Benz S550, representing the W221 generation, is a luxury sedan known for its advanced technology and comfortable ride. While generally well-regarded for its performance and features, like any complex vehicle, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for this model year. Understanding these common issues can help prospective buyers and current owners anticipate and address potential repair needs, ensuring the continued smooth operation of this sophisticated vehicle.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L V8 (M273)
**Timing Chain Tensioner Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain tensioners, guides, and potentially the chain itself.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Oil Cooler)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or oil cooler seals.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (722.9 '7G-Tronic')
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, slipping gears, inability to shift, 'limp mode'.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the transmission control module.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, rough engagement, delayed acceleration.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,800


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC) System
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, 'ABC System Malfunction' warning on the dashboard, fluid leaks under the car.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hoses, struts, or pump.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $4,000+

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of ride height control, loud whining noise from the pump, 'ABC System Malfunction' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,800


### Standard Air Suspension (if equipped)
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, 'ABC System Malfunction' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air struts.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,500 per strut


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Screen Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Flickering screen, dead pixels, unresponsive touch controls, system freezing.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000


### Keyless Go System
**Door Handle Sensors**
- **Symptoms:** Keyless entry not working, car not recognizing key fob proximity.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty door handle sensors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### Battery Drain
_No major issues reported for this configuration._

## Exterior

### Headlight Ballasts
**Xenon Headlight Failure**
- **Symptoms:** One or both headlights not turning on, flickering headlights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight ballast (igniter).
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per ballast


## Interior

### Seat Actuators
**Power Seat Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not moving forward/backward, up/down, or reclining.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty seat motors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900 per seat


### Climate Control System
**Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2009 Mercedes-Benz S550 offers a luxurious experience but comes with potential reliability concerns, particularly in its advanced systems. The engine's timing chain tensioners and potential oil leaks are notable, as are transmission issues like TCM failures and torque converter problems. The complex Active Body Control (ABC) suspension is prone to hydraulic leaks and pump failures, which can be costly to repair. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and battery drain, are also reported. While not all owners will experience these issues, they represent common failure points for this model. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining the S550's performance and longevity.
