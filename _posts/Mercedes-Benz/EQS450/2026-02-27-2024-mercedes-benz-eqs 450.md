### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Mercedes-Benz EQS 450 demonstrates a high level of initial reliability, particularly in its electric powertrain and core structural components. The most frequently cited potential issues revolve around the adaptive air suspension, where leaks can occur, and occasional software glitches within the advanced MBUX infotainment system. The 12V auxiliary battery is also noted as a component that may require replacement sooner than expected in some instances. Overall, the EQS 450 is built with robust engineering, and most owners are likely to experience a trouble-free ownership experience, with potential issues being manageable and within the expected range for a luxury electric vehicle of this complexity.
