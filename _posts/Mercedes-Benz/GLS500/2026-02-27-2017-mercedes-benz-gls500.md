# 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS-Class delivers high-end luxury and robust powertrain options, but used-car buyers should carefully evaluate long-term maintenance histories. While driver complaints are typical for a luxury vehicle of this age, specific recurring trouble spots involving air suspension reliability and electrical accessories require thorough pre-purchase inspections to avoid costly repairs.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (GLS 450)

A 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine producing around 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration paired with smooth power delivery for everyday driving.

**Fuel and Battery System Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report illuminated check engine lights, electrical charging faults, or fuel system smells stemming from degraded battery resistance or fuel component wear.
- **Solution:** Inspect the battery health and fuel rails for leaks, replacing worn components as needed.

### 4.7L Twin-Turbo V8 (GLS 500 / GLS 550)

A potent 4.7-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine pushing out roughly 449 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally robust performance with a smooth, effortless cruising character.

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8 (AMG GLS 63)

A hand-built 5.5-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine generating a massive 577 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque, providing ultra-high performance for enthusiasts prioritizing immense acceleration.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 9-speed automatic transmission paired with standard 4MATIC all-wheel drive, designed to maximize highway efficiency and keep engine revs low.

**Shifting and Parking Engagement Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience erratic gear shifting, hesitation, or intermittent failure of the transmission to properly engage the Park position.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning for valve body faults or software updates, with potential mechanical replacement of internal control modules if failure persists.

## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension

The adaptive air suspension system provides a plush, highly customizable ride quality but can incur significant out-of-pocket expenses when air springs or compressors eventually degrade.

**Air Suspension Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of air pressure while driving, causing the vehicle to drop, ride extremely rough, and become difficult to control.
- **Solution:** Inspect air struts, compressors, and control lines for leaks, and replace failed air spring components.
- **Cost:** $3900

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Provides speed-sensitive power steering assist, though rare electrical glitches can temporarily interrupt steering responsiveness.

**Power Steering Loss**
- **Symptoms:** A power steering malfunction warning message appears on the dash, followed by a complete loss of power steering assist while traveling at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect electronic power steering modules, wiring harnesses, and fluid levels for faults.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Handles exterior illumination and advanced lighting features, though some owners report premature degradation of headlight output.

**Headlight Dimming and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Low beam headlights fade significantly over time or emit humming noises, causing poor forward visibility during night driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight assemblies, bulbs, and ballasts, and replace worn lighting units.

## Interior

### Seating and Climate Adjustments

Governs cabin comfort features including power adjustments and heating elements, which can occasionally experience localized electrical shorts.

**Seat Heater Malfunction and Power Adjustments**
- **Symptoms:** Seat warmers overheat dangerously to the point of burning occupants, or power seat tracks fail to slide forward and backward.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat heating elements, wiring switches, and under-seat adjustment motors for internal shorts or obstructions.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS-Class offers luxury, performance, and ample passenger space, but prospective buyers must budget for potential high-end maintenance. Key areas requiring close inspection include the air suspension system, transmission control behavior, and electrical accessories. Securing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from a specialist mechanic is strongly advised before finalizing a purchase.
