# 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG blends high-performance turbocharged power with luxury subcompact crossover styling. While it offers engaging driving dynamics, prospective buyers should review known owner complaints concerning unexpected transmission behavior and engine fuel delivery components. Researching maintenance records and performing a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help uncover any lingering powertrain or electrical issues.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (AMG)

A hand-built 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing roughly 375 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating performance for the class, though premium maintenance and attentive upkeep are critical for long-term reliability.

**Fuel Injector Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Gasoline odor and potential engine performance issues or warning lights triggered by faulty fuel delivery components.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel system lines and replace failed fuel injectors as necessary.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift Dual-Clutch Automatic

A 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission engineered for rapid gear changes. While performance-oriented, some owners have reported unexpected shifts into neutral requiring a vehicle restart.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle unexpectedly shifts into neutral while driving, causing a temporary loss of drive power and sometimes displaying reverse inoperative error messages.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scan by a qualified technician to check for transmission software updates or mechanical selector faults.

## Interior

### Dashboard and Warning Systems

Manages vehicle telemetry, lighting, and advanced driver assistance systems. Electrical glitches can occasionally trigger false warnings or compromise convenience features.

**Instrument Cluster and Safety System Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic behavior from dashboard gauges, non-functioning turn signals, or unexpected activation of the emergency braking and airbag warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect electrical harnesses, ground connections, and module software for faults.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG is an exciting and fast luxury crossover, but buyers should approach with careful due diligence. Pay close attention to powertrain operation during test drives—specifically checking for unexpected gear shifts or hesitation—and ensure all electrical features function properly. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from an independent specialist is strongly advised.
