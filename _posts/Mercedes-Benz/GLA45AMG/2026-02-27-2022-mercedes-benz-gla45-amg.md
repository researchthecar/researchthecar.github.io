# 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG offers buyers high-performance thrills wrapped in a compact luxury package, but it is important to keep expectations grounded regarding everyday reliability. As owner reports highlight a few isolated electronic and system quirks, performing a thorough pre-purchase inspection is recommended before bringing home this sporty crossover.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M139)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine (M139) produces an impressive 382 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration for its class. Routine maintenance is critical for this high-output hand-built powerplant to ensure long-term durability.

### Fuel System

Fuel delivery components designed to manage emissions and vapor recovery.

**Charcoal Canister Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The check engine light may illuminate unexpectedly due to issues originating from the EVAP and charcoal canister system.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the EVAP system and replace the charcoal canister if component failure is verified.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

Power is managed by an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission paired with AMG Performance 4MATIC+ all-wheel drive. It offers rapid gear shifts suited for spirited driving, though prospective buyers should test-drive to ensure smooth operation at low speeds.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced driver assistance sensors designed to detect obstacles and mitigate frontal collisions.

**Unintended Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle's automated emergency braking system may unexpectedly engage while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Report incidents to an authorized dealer to check for sensor misalignment, software updates, or radar calibration issues.
