# 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG blends punchy turbocharged performance and nimble handling into a compact luxury crossover package. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported issues concerning powertrain electronic behavior, electrical accessories, and select body components before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The hand-built 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces high-strung output near 375 horsepower, offering exceptional acceleration. Proper maintenance and prompt attention to minor emissions components are key to long-term reliability.

**Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) Regulator Valve**
- **Symptoms:** Triggers a check engine light with diagnostic code P052E due to a failing positive crankcase ventilation regulator valve.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect and replace the PCV regulator valve assembly.

**Fuel Odor or Leak**
- **Symptoms:** A strong raw fuel smell noticeable around or inside the vehicle during operation.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines, seals, and connections for seepage and replace faulty components.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, aggressive gear changes tailored for performance driving. While generally robust, owners have reported sporadic electronic or hydraulic shifts into neutral.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle unexpectedly shifts into neutral while driving at speed or coming to a stop, accompanied by warning messages and a sudden loss of drive power.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a dealer or specialist is required to check for transmission control module updates or internal mechanical faults.

## Electrical

### Body Electrical and Infotainment

**Intermittent Instrument Cluster and Accessory Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Gauges like the speedometer and tachometer drop to zero or oscillate erratically, and backup cameras or infotainment screens go blank.
- **Solution:** Inspect power supplies, ground points, and module connections; replace failing display or communication modules as needed.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

**Sunroof Jamming or Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof panel misaligns, sticks, fails to close properly, or in rare instances, shatters unexpectedly while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect sunroof tracks and seals for bonding failures, and replace damaged glass or mechanism guides.

## Safety

### Air Bag and Restraints

**Air Bag Warning Light Illumination**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning light illuminates on the dashboard, often linked by diagnosis to a fractured or failed steering column clockspring.
- **Solution:** Replace the steering column clockspring assembly to restore proper electrical continuity to the airbag system.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG provides thrilling performance and distinctive styling, but it requires diligent maintenance. Buyers should thoroughly test all electronics, verify transmission responsiveness, and check for any history of PCV or sunroof issues prior to purchase.
