**2016 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG is a high-performance crossover powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-4 engine that produces 375 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission and all-wheel drive.

The GLA45 AMG is praised for its powerful engine, agile handling, and upscale interior. However, some owners have reported issues with various components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Timing Chain Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain in the engine can fail prematurely, causing the engine to stall or lose power.
    * Solution: The timing chain and related components need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold gasket can leak, causing a loss of power and illuminated check engine light.
    * Solution: The intake manifold gasket needs to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

* **Transmission Control Module Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission control module can fail, causing the transmission to malfunction or fail to shift properly.
    * Solution: The transmission control module needs to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front strut mounts can fail, causing excessive noise and vibration.
    * Solution: The strut mounts need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear shock absorbers can fail, causing a rough ride and loss of stability.
    * Solution: The rear shock absorbers need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain excessively over time.
    * Solution: The battery or related components need to be tested and replaced if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
* **Park Assist Control Module Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The park assist control module may fail, causing the parking sensors to malfunction.
    * Solution: The park assist control module needs to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture can accumulate inside the headlights.
    * Solution: The headlights need to be resealed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof motor may fail, preventing the sunroof from opening or closing.
    * Solution: The sunroof motor needs to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Navigation System Glitch**
    * Description/Symptoms: The navigation system may experience glitches or freeze up.
    * Solution: The navigation system software needs to be updated.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Overall Summary**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG is a generally reliable performance crossover. However, some owners have experienced issues with the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior. The severity of the issues can vary, and the cost to fix them can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars.
