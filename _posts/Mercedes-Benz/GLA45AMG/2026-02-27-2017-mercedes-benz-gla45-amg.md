# 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG, buyers are looking at a high-performance luxury subcompact crossover that packs a serious punch under the hood. While its handcrafted turbocharged engine and agile handling offer an engaging driving experience, prospective owners should pay close attention to potential issues ranging from electrical glitches to fuel odors and sunroof mechanical failures. Keeping up with maintenance and checking service history can help mitigate some of these common ownership hurdles.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M133)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine (M133) produces an impressive output of around 375 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering performance for its size. While highly capable and robustly engineered by AMG, buyers should inspect for minor fuel system odors and PCV line wear.

**Fuel Odor or Leak**
- **Symptoms:** A strong smell of gasoline inside and around the vehicle, particularly noticeable when starting the car or driving with windows closed.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel system components, lines, and connections for leaks or vapor escaping, and replace faulty seals or hoses as necessary.

**Crankcase Ventilator Line Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of the check engine light accompanied by rough running or PCV system failure symptoms.
- **Solution:** Replace the malfunctioning crankcase ventilation line with an updated component.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes tailored to the sporty nature of the AMG lineup. Buyers should test-drive the vehicle thoroughly to ensure smooth engagement and verify that no erratic shifting or unexpected neutral drops occur.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may spontaneously shift into neutral while driving, sometimes accompanied by dashboard light flickering or error messages.
- **Solution:** Diagnose transmission control module software and mechanical shift linkages; update software or replace failing sensors/modules as needed.

## Electrical

### Electrical System & Warning Lights

The electrical architecture supports advanced infotainment and driver assistance features, though occasional software anomalies and warning light illuminations have been reported by owners.

**Random Warning Light Illumination**
- **Symptoms:** Multiple unknown warning lights suddenly illuminate on the instrument cluster while driving and may randomly turn off.
- **Solution:** Check for software glitches and update vehicle computer software or replace faulty sensors at a qualified service center.

## Interior

### Sunroof and Moonroof Assembly

Exterior trim and glass assemblies generally hold up well, but the optional panoramic sunroof mechanism requires careful inspection for track binding and water leaks.

**Sunroof Mechanism Jamming or Leaking**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof fails to open or close properly due to broken plastic rail guides, or water leaks into the cabin during wet weather.
- **Solution:** Replace broken plastic rail sliders and guides, and clear or repair clogged or leaking drainage channels.

## Safety

### Air Bag and Clock Spring

Safety systems include advanced multi-stage airbags, though wear on internal steering column components like the clock spring can trigger warning indicators.

**Air Bag Warning Light & Clock Spring Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The driver's side air bag service light remains constantly illuminated, sometimes accompanied by clicking noises and stiffness when turning the steering wheel.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column and replace the broken clock spring and associated airbag components.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG offers exhilarating hot-hatch performance wrapped in a crossover body style, making it a thrilling choice for enthusiast buyers.
