**Introduction:**

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG is a high-performance compact SUV featuring a 382-horsepower 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine paired with an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. Despite its impressive specs, the GLA45 AMG has faced some reported issues across various components.

**Engine**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission Issue**
    * Description/Symptoms: Jerking or hesitation during gear changes, especially at low speeds.
    * Solution: Software update or transmission fluid replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Radio Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Radio becomes unresponsive or displays error codes.
    * Solution: Radio replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
* **Rearview Camera Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rearview camera becomes blurry or displays a black screen.
    * Solution: Rearview camera replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V-443

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Center Console Armrest Squeaking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Armrest covering starts to squeak over time.
    * Solution: Armrest replacement or lubrication
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
* **Navigation Screen Lag**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation screen becomes slow or unresponsive.
    * Solution: Software update or screen replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary:**

While the 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG offers impressive performance and features, it has faced some issues in areas such as the transmission, electrical system, and interior. The severity of these issues varies, and the estimated repair costs can range from moderate to high. However, it's important to note that these issues may not affect all vehicles and that individual experiences may differ.
