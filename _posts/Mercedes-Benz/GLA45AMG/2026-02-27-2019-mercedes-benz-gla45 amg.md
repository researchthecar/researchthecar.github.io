# 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG

### Overview
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG, a compact performance SUV, generally offers a robust and engaging driving experience. It is powered by a potent turbocharged engine known for its exhilarating acceleration. While the vehicle is built with Mercedes-Benz's signature quality, like any high-performance machine, specific areas can experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related reliability concerns reported by owners and technicians, providing insights into potential problem areas and their associated repair costs for the specific engine and transmission configurations available.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M133)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, 'check engine' light illumination, potential boost leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the wastegate actuator or the entire turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**PCV Valve Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil leaks, potential for increased oil consumption, 'check engine' light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### AMG SPEEDSHIFT DCT 7-Speed
**Mechatronics Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed gear engagement, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the transmission mechatronics unit.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $4000

**Clutch Pack Wear (Aggressive Driving)**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, rough engagement, especially under hard acceleration or during spirited driving.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn clutch packs.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLA45 AMG is a performance-oriented vehicle that, while generally reliable, can present specific issues related to its high-output powertrain. The engine's turbocharger and PCV system are potential areas for concern, as is the sophisticated AMG SPEEDSHIFT dual-clutch transmission, particularly its mechatronics unit and clutch packs, especially if subjected to aggressive driving. Other systems like suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components show good reliability with no widespread reported issues. Owners should be aware of these potential powertrain-related repairs, which can be costly, and ensure regular maintenance is performed to mitigate risks.
