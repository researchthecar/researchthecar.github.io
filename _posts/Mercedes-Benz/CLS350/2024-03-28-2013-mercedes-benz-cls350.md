**2013 Mercedes-Benz CLS350**

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz CLS350 is a luxury four-door coupe that combines style and performance. It features a 3.5-liter V6 engine with 302 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The CLS350 is known for its sleek design, comfortable interior, and impressive handling. However, it has been reported to have some issues.

**Engine**

3.5-liter V6

* **Timing Chain Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, loss of power, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Leak from Oil Filter Housing:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak near engine, low oil level
    * Solution: Replace oil filter housing gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

7-Speed Automatic

* **Transmission Control Unit (TCU) Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, no shifting, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace TCU
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve Body Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed shifting, hard shifting, transmission slipping
    * Solution: Replace valve body solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

No Issues Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead after sitting for a few days, electrical systems not working
    * Solution: Replace battery or identify and fix electrical drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **SRS (Airbag) Light Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Airbag light staying on, airbag deployment issues
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair airbag system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Paint Peeling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Paint peeling or chipping on rear bumper
    * Solution: Repaint rear bumper
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No air flow from HVAC vents, blower motor noise
    * Solution: Replace blower motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Multifunction Steering Wheel Buttons Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Steering wheel buttons not working, intermittent operation
    * Solution: Replace steering wheel buttons or associated wiring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz CLS350 has some potential issues, particularly in the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity of the issues can vary, with some being more costly to repair than others. While these issues are not widespread, it is important to be aware of them when considering this vehicle.
