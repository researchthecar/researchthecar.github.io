# 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLS350

### Overview
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLS350, part of the C257 generation, generally presents a strong reliability profile, reflecting Mercedes-Benz's commitment to quality engineering. This model typically features a potent yet refined powertrain, designed for both performance and comfort. While specific widespread mechanical failures are uncommon for this model year, like any luxury vehicle, potential owners should be aware of the typical areas where issues might arise, primarily concerning advanced electronic systems and specific component wear under high mileage. The CLS350 aims to blend sporty aesthetics with executive-class luxury, and its reliability data suggests it largely succeeds in delivering a dependable ownership experience, provided regular maintenance is adhered to.

**Sensor Malfunctions**

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLS350 demonstrates good overall reliability, with its core powertrain components (engine and transmission) showing no significant widespread issues. The primary areas to monitor are the optional Air Body Control suspension, which can develop leaks over time, and the sophisticated electrical systems, particularly the MBUX infotainment and ADAS features, which may occasionally encounter software glitches or sensor issues. These electrical problems are often resolvable with software updates or recalibration. Exterior and interior components are generally robust. For potential buyers, ensuring the vehicle has a comprehensive service history and addressing any specific electrical or suspension concerns during a pre-purchase inspection is advisable for a smooth ownership experience.
