# 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLS350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLS350, buyers will find a stylish luxury four-door coupe that blends high-end interior appointments with refined performance. While comprehensive owner feedback is still developing, early reports highlight a few electronic and mechanical areas worth monitoring during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 M264

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 295 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, often paired with a mild-hybrid 48-volt system. It provides responsive acceleration for daily driving while maintaining respectable fuel efficiency for a luxury vehicle.

### 48V Charging System

Electrical complaints center around mild-hybrid components, which require specialized diagnostic equipment to service properly.

**48-Volt Battery and Charging Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience warning messages regarding the 48-volt charging system, followed by a loss of air conditioning and reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the 48-volt battery, integrated starter-generator, and associated charging modules for faults or software updates.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and rapid gear changes across various driving conditions. It is well-integrated with the powertrain options to optimize both highway cruising efficiency and responsive low-end power delivery.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Rims

Suspension and wheel components are generally robust, though low-profile tires mounted on large alloy wheels remain susceptible to road hazard damage.

**Wheel Rim Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden tire pressure loss or blowouts due to structural cracks forming in the alloy wheel rims.
- **Solution:** Inspect all alloy wheels for hidden cracks or structural damage, especially after driving over severe potholes or road debris, and replace compromised wheels.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Safety system complaints highlight rare structural or sensor-related incidents that require thorough professional inspection following any collision history.

**Failure to Deploy in Frontal Impact**
- **Symptoms:** Frontal airbags may fail to deploy during a collision, increasing the risk of occupant injury.
- **Solution:** Ensure that the vehicle has no active airbag warning lights on the dashboard and verify that all safety restraint systems have not been previously compromised or improperly repaired.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLS350 remains an appealing luxury choice with strong powertrain options and advanced features. Prospective buyers should prioritize a detailed pre-purchase inspection focusing on the 48-volt electrical system and alloy wheel integrity to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
