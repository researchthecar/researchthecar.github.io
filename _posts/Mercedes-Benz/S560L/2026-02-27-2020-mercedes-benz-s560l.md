## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, 'airmatic system malfunction' warning on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air strut or air spring assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, intermittent audio issues, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500


### 12V Battery
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, electrical system warnings, random electronic component failures.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V auxiliary battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Malfunctioning Adjustment**

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz S560L demonstrates strong overall reliability, with its powertrain (engine and transmission) being particularly robust and free from widespread reported issues. The primary areas where owners might encounter problems are related to the advanced AIRMATIC air suspension system, specifically potential air strut leaks, and occasional electrical gremlins within the sophisticated infotainment system or premature 12V battery failure. Interior components like seat actuators can also experience malfunctions. Despite these potential issues, the S560L remains a dependable luxury vehicle when properly maintained, with most reported problems being repairable, albeit at a premium cost reflective of its luxury status.
