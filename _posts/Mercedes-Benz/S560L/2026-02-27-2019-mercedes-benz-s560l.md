# 2019 Mercedes-Benz S560L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz S560L represents the pinnacle of luxury, comfort, and advanced engineering in the flagship sedan segment. Prospective buyers looking at a pre-owned model will find an exceptionally sophisticated vehicle, though its complex technological and mechanical systems require careful inspection. While major mechanical failures are rare, owner reports highlight occasional electronic glitches, actuator concerns, and isolated driver-assistance system quirks that are worth keeping in mind during your shopping journey.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Bi-Turbo V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 463 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless and smooth acceleration. It is generally robust, though proper maintenance is critical to longevity.

**Abnormal Engine Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle vibrates abnormally upon startup without illuminating any dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect engine mounts, ignition components, and fuel delivery systems to isolate the source of the vibration.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6 (S450 / Hybrid Variants)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged engine options provide refined power delivery and improved fuel efficiency for base or hybrid variants. They offer balanced performance for daily driving duties.

## Transmission

### 9G-Tronic 9-Speed Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides seamless gear changes and optimizes highway efficiency. It pairs smoothly with the available engine lineups.

## Brakes

### Brake Actuator

The braking system provides strong stopping power for a heavy luxury sedan, but owners should verify hydraulic integrity and actuator response during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Rear Brake Actuator Seizure**
- **Symptoms:** The rear brake actuator seizes and becomes completely inoperable upon starting the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle towed to a qualified service center or dealership to replace the failed rear brake actuator assembly.

**Firm Pedal Feel / Double Pumping**
- **Symptoms:** Brakes feel inconsistent, requiring a double pump to successfully bring the vehicle to a complete stop.
- **Solution:** Inspect the brake master cylinder, brake booster, and hydraulic lines for leaks or pressure loss.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Controls

Steering precision is excellent, though power-adjustment mechanisms and electronic column shifters can occasionally develop operational glitches.

**Steering Column Adjustment Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** The power-adjustable steering column regularly sticks in the full upward position and resists easy readjustment.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column adjustment motor and track mechanism, lubricating or replacing parts as needed.

**Column Shifter Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The gear selector fails to shift into Park, Neutral, or Reverse, instead erroneously operating the navigation map cursor.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the electronic shifter module and column control unit for software glitches or hardware faults.

## Interior

### Cabin Electronics and Keyless Entry

The complex electrical architecture powers endless luxury conveniences, but isolated software bugs and sensor anomalies can occasionally cause interior accessory quirks.

**Keyless Entry Auto-Locking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle automatically locks itself from the inside with the key fob left inside, potentially stranding occupants or pets.
- **Solution:** Check key fob proximity sensors and body control module software settings for anomalies.

## Safety

### Seat Belts and Pre-Safe Systems

Advanced safety features offer exceptional occupant protection, though sensitive collision-preparation sensors can occasionally trigger false positives.

**Unexpected Seat Belt Tightening**
- **Symptoms:** Seat belts suddenly tighten autonomously while driving under normal conditions as if preparing for an imminent collision.
- **Solution:** Have the Pre-Safe collision avoidance sensors and seat belt tensioner modules diagnosed for calibration errors.

**Stuck Rear Seat Belt**
- **Symptoms:** Rear seat belt and airbag combination locks rigidly in place and refuses to extend or retract.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat belt retractor mechanism and replace the assembly if the locking mechanism fails.
