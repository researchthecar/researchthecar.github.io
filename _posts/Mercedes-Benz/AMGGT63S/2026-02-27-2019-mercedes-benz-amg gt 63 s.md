# 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT 63 S

### Overview
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT 63 S is a high-performance luxury grand tourer known for its potent V8 engine and sophisticated engineering. As a halo model within the AMG lineup, it generally exhibits strong build quality and performance. However, like any complex, high-strung vehicle, it can experience specific issues, particularly related to its advanced powertrain and electronics. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, aiming to provide a clear picture for owners and potential buyers.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (M177)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Boost pressure loss, 'check engine' light, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty wastegate actuator or complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Coolant Leaks (Intercooler System)**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant level, overheating, visible coolant residue around intercooler pipes.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, clamps, or intercooler seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Transmission

### 9-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT
**Harsh Shifting / Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, hesitation when shifting into drive or reverse, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Software update for transmission control module, or in severe cases, internal transmission repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000+


## Suspension

### AMG RIDE CONTROL+ Suspension
**Air Spring Bladder Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, 'check suspension' warning message.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the affected air spring assembly.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing / Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, freezes, or restarts intermittently.
- **Solution:** Software update, or potential replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, difficulty starting the vehicle, warning lights related to battery voltage.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from modules or accessories, followed by repair or replacement of faulty component.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT 63 S is a formidable performance machine with generally good reliability for its class. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the complex powertrain, specifically the turbocharger wastegate actuators and potential issues with the advanced transmission's shifting behavior. Suspension components, particularly the air springs, can also require attention. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and intermittent battery drain, are not uncommon in high-tech vehicles of this era. While not immune to problems, most issues are repairable, though costs can be significant due to the specialized nature of AMG components. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining this high-performance vehicle.
