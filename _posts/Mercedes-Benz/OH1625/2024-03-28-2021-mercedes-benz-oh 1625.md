## 2021 Mercedes-Benz OH 1625

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz OH 1625 is a heavy-duty truck that is powered by a 7.7-liter OM 936 LA inline-6 turbodiesel engine that produces 249 horsepower and 1200 Nm of torque. It is mated to a 6-speed manual transmission. The OH 1625 is available in a variety of configurations, including a 4x2 rigid chassis, a 6x2 rigid chassis, and a 6x4 rigid chassis.

People who like the OH 1625 praise its powerful engine, comfortable cabin, and rugged construction. However, some people have reported problems with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and other components.

**Engine**

* **OM 936 LA Inline-6 Turbodiesel Engine**
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger may fail, causing a loss of power and increased emissions.
        * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Injector Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The injectors may fail, causing a loss of power and increased emissions.
        * Solution: Replace the injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Clutch Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The clutch may fail, causing difficulty shifting gears or a loss of power.
        * Solution: Replace the clutch.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description: The alternator may fail, causing a loss of power and eventually a dead battery.
    * Symptoms: Dimming headlights, difficulty starting the engine, or warning lights on the dashboard.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Buttons Not Working**
    * Description/Symptoms: The buttons on the dashboard may not work, making it difficult to control the vehicle's features.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty buttons.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

## Summary

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz OH 1625 is a heavy-duty truck that is generally reliable and well-built. However, some people have reported problems with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and other components. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage.
