# 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLA250

### Overview
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLA250, a compact luxury sedan, generally offers a good balance of performance and premium features. As with many vehicles in its class, reliability can vary, but common concerns tend to focus on specific electronic components and, in some cases, the powertrain. Owners often praise its sporty handling and stylish design. However, potential buyers should be aware of reported issues that, while not universal, have been noted by a segment of owners. This data aims to provide a technical overview of potential reliability concerns, excluding routine maintenance and normal wear and tear, to help prospective owners make informed decisions.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M270/M274)
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, sweet smell from the engine.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic (7G-DCT)
**Transmission Shudder/Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky shifts, hesitation during acceleration, rough engagement of gears.
- **Solution:** Software update, transmission fluid flush, or internal component repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,500

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, freezes, or displays errors.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of battery or faulty module.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 presents a generally reliable package, with its powertrain and chassis components showing good durability. The primary areas of concern tend to be within the electrical system, particularly the infotainment unit and potential battery drain issues, which can lead to unexpected costs. Engine-related issues like turbocharger problems and coolant leaks, while not epidemic, have been reported and require attention. Transmission issues, such as shuddering, are also a possibility. Despite these points, for a luxury compact vehicle, the CLA250 offers a solid ownership experience when these potential issues are addressed proactively. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs are recommended.
