# 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLA250, prospective buyers will find a stylish and technologically advanced compact luxury car. However, reviewing owner-submitted complaints reveals certain mechanical and electrical pain points that warrant careful inspection prior to purchase. Being aware of these recurring problem areas can help you negotiate a fair price or steer clear of a problematic example.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration and respectable efficiency for daily driving. Overall, it is a robust powerplant, though owners have reported occasional fuel system component failures requiring professional service.

**Fuel Injector and Seal Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fuel leaks, strong gasoline odors in or around the vehicle, and engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Replace failing fuel injectors and worn injector seals or o-rings.
- **Cost:** $4,154.67

**Fuel Rail Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Fuel leaking from the fuel rail resulting in sudden engine shutdown and a hazardous driving condition.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel rail assembly and replace leaking components.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers quick, sporty gear shifts paired with front-wheel drive or 4MATIC all-wheel drive. While generally smooth, some drivers have experienced disconcerting electronic or mechanical shifts into neutral.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission spontaneously shifts into neutral while driving at varying speeds, causing a sudden loss of motive power and a 'Reverse Inoperative' error message.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic evaluation by a dealer or specialist; software updates or internal component repairs may be required.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Rims

The suspension balances sporty handling with a firm ride quality, but low-profile tires paired with large alloy wheels leave the vehicle vulnerable to pothole damage.

**Cracking Rims and Sidewall Bulges**
- **Symptoms:** Factory alloy rims developing cracks under moderate low-speed driving, leading to recurrent flat tires and tire sidewall bulges.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged alloy wheels and inspect low-profile tires for structural integrity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V219000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=CLA250&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The right side door locks may not have been properly manufactured. As a result, the right-side doors may not be able to be opened from outside of the vehicle.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the door locks, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 combines luxury appeal with engaging performance, but used-car buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay special attention to service history regarding fuel system components and test-drive the vehicle carefully to verify proper transmission operation and wheel integrity.
