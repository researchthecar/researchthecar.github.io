# 2020 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2020 Mercedes-Benz CLA250, buyers will find a stylish, tech-forward compact luxury sedan that balances sporty handling with a refined interior. However, prospective owners should review common owner-reported complaints regarding electronics, visibility, and body components before purchasing. Being aware of these specific trouble spots can help you inspect a used model more thoroughly.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (CLA250)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 221 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration for daily driving. It pairs modern direct-injection technology with strong power delivery, though proper maintenance is critical to avoid drivability quirks.

**Engine Hesitation and Stuttering**
- **Symptoms:** The check engine light illuminates, followed by engine stuttering or jerking while reversing or driving at lower speeds on surface streets.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic run diagnostic codes to check fuel delivery, ignition components, and sensor readings.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers quick, seamless gear shifts and comes mated to either front-wheel drive or 4MATIC all-wheel drive. While generally responsive, buyers should test drive the vehicle to ensure smooth engagement across all gears.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera and Trunk Badge Mechanism

Safety equipment generally performs well, but mechanical issues with articulating parking camera housings can cause visibility issues when backing up.

**Stuck Backup Camera Housing**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera housing emblem on the trunk remains permanently open, or the camera feed appears briefly before cutting out to the home screen when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the trunk-mounted camera actuator mechanism or associated wiring harness.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof and Windshield Wipers

Exterior components are mostly robust, though isolated reports highlight issues with panoramic glass durability and multifunction switch behavior.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof shatters unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds without any prior impact or visible debris.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged panoramic glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame for alignment or stress points.

**Erratic Windshield Wipers**
- **Symptoms:** Front windshield wipers activate randomly on dry roads and fail to respond properly to the manual control stalk.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the wiper switch assembly or rain sensor module and replace faulty electrical components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V989000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=CLA250&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A sudden loss of power steering assist can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** A dealer will inspect and repair the power supply connection, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V732000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Pads](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=CLA250&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The vehicles may not be equipped with an inspection gauge to check the wear of the rear brake pads. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 135, "Light Vehicle Brake Systems." Driving with worn brake pads can reduce braking ability, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install an inspection gauge and provide supplemental owner's manual information, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 offers compelling luxury and performance, but buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to the functionality of the backup camera mechanism, panoramic sunroof glass integrity, and smooth engine operation during your test drive.
