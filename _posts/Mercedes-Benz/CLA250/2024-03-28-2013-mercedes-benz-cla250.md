**Introduction**

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 is a compact luxury sedan powered by a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine producing 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It is available with a 6-speed manual or 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The CLA250 is known for its stylish design, well-appointed interior, and nimble handling characteristics. However, it has been reported to have several issues that drivers should be aware of.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**
    * **Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups.
        * Solution: Replace piston rings and valve stem seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: MB Campaign 2015090001
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise at engine start-up, reduced engine power, and check engine light illumination.
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual**
    * **Faulty Clutch Release Bearing**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears, grinding noises, and clutch pedal pulsation.
        * Solution: Replace clutch release bearing.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**
    * **Transmission Jerking/Harsh Shifting**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rough gear changes, hesitation during acceleration, and juddering.
        * Solution: Software updates or transmission replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Malfunctioning Windshield Wipers**
        * Description/Symptoms: Wipers may not operate properly or may stop working intermittently.
        * Solution: Replace wiper motor or control module.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Faulty Door Handles**
        * Description/Symptoms: Door handles may become loose or unresponsive, making it difficult to open the doors.
        * Solution: Replace door handle assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattle**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise coming from the sunroof, especially at higher speeds.
        * Solution: Adjust or replace sunroof mechanism.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Sticking Power Window Switches**
        * Description/Symptoms: Power window switches may become sticky or unresponsive, making it difficult to operate the windows.
        * Solution: Clean or replace power window switches.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty Climate Control System**
        * Description/Symptoms: Climate control may not regulate temperature properly, resulting in inconsistent cabin temperatures.
        * Solution: Replace climate control actuator or sensor.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 has been reported to encounter several issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical components. While some issues are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and require substantial repairs. Owners should be aware of these potential problems and factor them into their purchase decision and maintenance costs.
