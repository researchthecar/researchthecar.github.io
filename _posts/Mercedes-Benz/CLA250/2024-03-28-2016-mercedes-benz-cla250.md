**Introduction**
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 is a compact luxury sedan that boasts a sleek and sporty design. It comes standard with a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine that produces 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. Other engine options include a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 with 221 horsepower, and a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 with 375 horsepower for the CLA45 AMG model. Drivers appreciate the CLA250's stylish exterior, comfortable interior, and responsive handling. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**
**2.0-liter Turbocharged Inline-4 (208 horsepower)**
* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, and loud noises from the engine.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Injector Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting, and poor fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Inline-4 (221 horsepower)**
* **Engine Oil Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the engine block or gaskets, visible as dark spots under the vehicle.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the faulty gasket or seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Inline-4 (375 horsepower)**
* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**
**7-speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**
* **Shifting Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifts, slipping gears, or a lack of response when shifting.
    * Solution: Replace or reprogram the transmission control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
**Front Suspension**
* **Strut Mount Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises from the suspension, excessive vibration, and reduced handling stability.
    * Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges excessively when the vehicle is parked, causing difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the battery drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Warning lights on the dashboard, flickering lights, or loss of electrical power.
    * Solution: Scan for diagnostic trouble codes and repair the faulty component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varied depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Sunroof Rattling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or buzzing sound coming from the sunroof when closed.
    * Solution: Lubricate or adjust the sunroof rails.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the dashboard, particularly when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: Reposition or secure the dashboard components that are rubbing or rattling.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating not working properly, incorrect temperature settings.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the faulty climate control module or sensors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 has received mixed reviews from owners regarding reliability. Some have experienced frequent and costly repairs, particularly with the engine and transmission components. However, others have had positive experiences with their vehicles and have not encountered significant issues. The severity and cost of the issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the affected part. It is recommended that potential buyers research potential issues and consider extended warranty options to protect against unexpected expenses.
