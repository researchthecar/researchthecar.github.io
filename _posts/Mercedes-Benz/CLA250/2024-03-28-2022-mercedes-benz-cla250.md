**Introduction**
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 is a compact luxury sedan powered by a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine producing 221 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The CLA250 is praised for its stylish design, premium interior, and nimble handling, but criticized for its cramped rear seat and limited trunk space.

**Electrical**
* **Power Window Motors**
Description/Symptoms: The power window motors may fail, causing the windows to become inoperable.
Solution: Replace the faulty motors.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **Fuses**
Description/Symptoms: Blown fuses in the fuse box may cause various electrical components to malfunction.
Solution: Replace the blown fuses.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $20 - $100+
* **Electrical Harness**
Description/Symptoms: Faulty wiring or connectors in the electrical harness can lead to electrical issues, such as flickering lights, power loss, or engine problems.
Solution: Inspect and repair the damaged wiring or connectors.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**
* **Headlights**
Description/Symptoms: Condensation or moisture may accumulate inside the headlights.
Solution: Replace the affected headlights.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
* **Windshield Wipers**
Description/Symptoms: The windshield wipers may fail to operate correctly or make excessive noise.
Solution: Inspect and replace or adjust the worn or damaged wiper blades or arms.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+

**Interior**
* **Trim Panels**
Description/Symptoms: The trim panels on the dashboard or door panels may become loose or rattle.
Solution: Reattach or replace the loose trim panels.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* **Infotainment System**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience software glitches, freezing, or connectivity issues.
Solution: Update the system software or reset the system.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $100+

**Summary**
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 is generally a reliable vehicle with few major issues. The electrical system and exterior components may experience some minor problems, but most issues can be resolved at a reasonable cost. The severity of the issues is typically low to moderate, and most can be addressed without major expenses.
