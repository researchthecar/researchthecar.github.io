# 2014 Mercedes-Benz CLA250

### Overview
The 2014 Mercedes-Benz CLA250, while offering a stylish and sporty entry into the luxury compact sedan segment, presents a mixed reliability profile. It shares many components with other Mercedes-Benz models, which can be both a benefit for parts availability and a drawback if those shared components have known issues. Owners generally appreciate the vehicle's design and performance, but some recurring technical concerns have been noted, particularly concerning the powertrain and certain electronic systems. Regular maintenance is crucial for mitigating potential problems, and addressing early signs of trouble can prevent more significant and costly repairs down the line. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related issues.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M270)
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic (7G-DCT)
**Harsh Shifting / Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Rough gear changes, hesitation, jerky acceleration, especially at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, software update, or transmission control module (TCM) repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,500

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from transmission pan or seals, potential for slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking seals or transmission pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, audio cutting out, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, rebooting the system, or replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1,500


### Power Steering Control Module
**Power Steering Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power steering assist, heavy steering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power steering control module.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 is a stylish compact luxury car with a generally acceptable reliability record for its class, though not without its specific concerns. The most frequently reported issues revolve around the 7-speed dual-clutch transmission, which can exhibit harsh shifting, and potential problems with the turbocharger and coolant system in the 2.0L engine. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the COMAND infotainment system and power steering module, also appear on occasion. While the suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely robust, owners should be aware of these potential powertrain and electrical vulnerabilities. Proactive maintenance and prompt attention to early symptoms are recommended to ensure long-term satisfaction and avoid more significant repair costs.
