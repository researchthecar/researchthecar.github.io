**Introduction:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 is a compact luxury sedan that offers a sleek design, comfortable interior, and responsive handling. It comes with a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-4 engine, producing 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. Other engine options include a larger 3.0-liter V6 for the CLA350 and a 2.1-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder diesel for the CLA250d.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **No Issues Found**

* **3.0L V6**
    * **Intake Manifold Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the intake manifold can cause rough idling, loss of power, and increased fuel consumption.
        * Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

* **Clutch Pack Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wear or failure of the clutch packs can cause problems with shifting, shuddering, and slipping.
    * Solution: Replace the clutch pack.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged strut mount bearings can result in a clunking or rattling noise when turning or going over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace the strut mount bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $450
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature battery discharge can cause difficulties starting the vehicle or system malfunctions.
    * Solution: Inspect and replace the battery or identify electrical system drain sources.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattles**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or poorly fitting interior panels can cause rattling or squeaking when driving over rough roads.
    * Solution: Tighten or replace the affected panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has some common issues to watch out for. The most significant issues involve potential intake manifold failure on the V6 engine, clutch pack failure in the transmission, and strut mount bearing problems. While these issues can be expensive to fix, they are not widespread and most CLA250 owners report a positive experience with the vehicle.
