# 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLA250, buyers will find a stylish and technologically advanced compact luxury sedan. While owner reports to federal regulators are relatively sparse for this model year, prospective buyers should still inspect foundational systems carefully during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 221 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration for daily driving while maintaining reasonable efficiency, though proper maintenance with premium fuel is essential for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid and crisp gear shifts. Buyers should test-drive the vehicle thoroughly to ensure smooth engagement across all driving conditions, as dual-clutch systems can sometimes feel firm at low speeds.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking system complaints involve isolated reports of delayed or reduced stopping response under sudden braking conditions.

**Reduced Braking Response**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced instances where the vehicle did not slow down as expected immediately upon pressing the brake pedal during highway traffic slowdowns.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the brake pads, rotors, fluid, and anti-lock braking system modules during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Owner reports highlight isolated incidents involving exterior glass components, specifically the panoramic sunroof.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported hearing a loud bang while driving on the highway, followed by the panoramic glass roof shattering unexpectedly without any apparent impact from outside objects.
- **Solution:** Inspect the panoramic glass thoroughly for micro-cracks or stress points before purchase, and consult a dealer or glass specialist if replacement is necessary.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 generally presents as a low-complaint luxury option, though isolated owner reports regarding panoramic glass durability and brake response warrant a careful hands-on inspection. Buyers should prioritize a comprehensive test drive and a professional pre-purchase evaluation to ensure all systems operate properly.
