# 2016 Mercedes-Benz CLA250

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz CLA250, a compact luxury sedan, generally offers a good balance of performance and premium features. As with many vehicles in its class, particularly those with turbocharged engines and advanced transmissions, some specific areas have seen more attention from owners regarding reliability. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for owners and prospective buyers. The data aims to highlight common problem areas and their associated repair considerations, offering a clearer picture of the vehicle's long-term dependability.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M270)
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic (7G-DCT)
**Harsh Shifting / Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Rough gear changes, hesitation, jerky acceleration, especially at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, software update, or internal transmission repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,500

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission warning lights, inability to shift gears, limp mode.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,800

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system reset, or replacement of infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1,000

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, dead battery, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty module or battery.
- **Cost:** $300 - $900

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 presents a generally reliable package, with its core powertrain components showing decent durability. However, potential owners should be aware of specific issues that have been reported, particularly concerning the turbocharger and the dual-clutch transmission, which can sometimes exhibit harsh shifting or require module attention. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and battery drain, are also noted concerns. While suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free from widespread problems, proactive maintenance and awareness of these potential technical issues are recommended for a smoother ownership experience.
