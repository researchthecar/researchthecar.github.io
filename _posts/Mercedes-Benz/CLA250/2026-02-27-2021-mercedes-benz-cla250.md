# 2021 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 combines sleek, coupe-like styling with modern luxury and advanced technology. As a used luxury purchase, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback regarding powertrain response, electrical accessories, and body hardware to ensure a well-maintained vehicle.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces 221 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering lively performance for daily driving. Overall, it provides a balance of efficiency and power, though buyers should verify smooth throttle response during a test drive.

**Acceleration Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences sudden loss of power or hesitation while attempting to accelerate on highways.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician or dealership inspect the vehicle computer and propulsion sensors for fault codes and software updates.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and pairs with either front-wheel drive or 4MATIC all-wheel drive. It is generally responsive, but buyers should test for smoothness during low-speed maneuvering.

## Suspension

### Front Suspension Ball Joint

Suspension components generally provide a sporty ride quality, though individual component wear should always be checked during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Ball Joint Separation**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden front suspension failure or wheel detachment while driving at low to moderate speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect front suspension components, control arms, and ball joints for wear or structural damage.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting Assembly

The vehicle features modern LED exterior lighting and advanced cabin electronics, requiring a thorough check of all electrical accessories.

**Tail Lamp Moisture Accumulation**
- **Symptoms:** Water droplets or condensation visible inside the rear light housing due to improper sealing.
- **Solution:** Inspect the tail light seal and replace the lamp assembly or gasket if moisture intrusion persists.

### Sunroof Glass

Exterior panels and glass features define the sleek profile of the vehicle, making a close inspection of window seals and panoramic glass important.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Panoramic sunroof glass unexpectedly shatters or explodes while driving without external impact.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass assembly and frame alignment, and replace the glass panel if structural integrity is compromised.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V034000: Child Seat](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=CLA250&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** If the anchoring bar is missing from the ISOFIX bracket, the child restraint cannot be properly secured to the seat using the ISOFIX securing system, increasing the risk of injury for a child in that seat.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the right-rear ISOFIX bracket and replace the bracket, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz CLA250 offers an engaging driving experience and premium interior appointments suited for luxury shoppers. While owner complaints highlight isolated issues regarding acceleration performance, suspension components, exterior lighting seals, and glass integrity, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection can help mitigate potential risks.
