# 2021 Mercedes-Benz E350e Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Mercedes-Benz E-Class, buyers are treated to a blend of luxury, advanced technology, and refined performance. However, evaluating owner complaints reveals specific areas to watch out for, particularly concerning powertrain mechanical performance, electrical system stability, and auxiliary components. While these luxury vehicles offer an upscale driving experience, being aware of reported issues with engine valvetrain wear, transmission operation, and 48-volt electrical components will help prospective buyers perform thorough pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M264)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering responsive performance for daily driving. It provides solid fuel efficiency for a luxury sedan, though buyers should watch for reported valvetrain concerns.

**Cylinder Misfire and Valve Seat Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light, engine misfires, and potential sudden loss of power or transition into limp mode while driving.
- **Solution:** Professional diagnosis by a qualified technician; affected vehicles may require cylinder head service or valve replacement as outlined by manufacturer service documentation.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and seamless gear shifts across various driving conditions. While generally refined, isolated owner reports highlight severe mechanical failures requiring extensive repairs.

**Premature Transmission Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of motive power while driving on the highway, inability to shift gears, or complete mechanical breakdown.
- **Solution:** Towing to a specialized repair facility or dealership for comprehensive transmission inspection, fluid analysis, or component replacement.

## Brakes

### Parking Brake and Service Brakes

The braking system provides strong stopping power suited for a luxury sedan. However, electronic parking brake glitches can occasionally cause unexpected locking issues.

**Brake Seizure**
- **Symptoms:** Emergency brake independently seizes or locks up after sitting for multiple days, causing the vehicle to abruptly stop or resist movement.
- **Solution:** Inspection of the electronic parking brake actuators, brake calipers, and associated control wiring.

## Electrical

### 48V Hybrid Electrical System

The advanced electrical architecture supports auxiliary hybrid features and onboard systems. Owners have reported occasional failures within the 48-volt system that can render the vehicle entirely unresponsive.

**48-Volt Battery Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle becomes completely inoperable, warning messages on the instrument panel, and sudden loss of motive power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 48-volt battery component and resetting of the vehicle's electrical control modules.

### Back-Over Prevention Cameras

Driver assistance and safety cameras enhance everyday maneuverability and parking safety. Intermittent software glitches can occasionally disable the backup camera display.

**Inoperative Rearview Camera**
- **Symptoms:** Rearview and 360-degree camera display fails to activate when shifting into reverse, accompanied by 'system inoperative' warnings.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of faulty camera modules and display wiring harnesses.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz E-Class delivers impressive luxury, comfort, and advanced features, but prospective buyers should approach with careful due diligence.
