# 2021 Mercedes-Benz E350e

### Overview
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz E350e, a plug-in hybrid variant of the E-Class sedan, aims to blend performance with fuel efficiency. It typically features a turbocharged four-cylinder gasoline engine paired with an electric motor and a battery pack. This powertrain configuration is designed to offer both electric-only driving for shorter commutes and combined power for longer journeys. Reliability data for this specific model year and powertrain combination suggests a generally robust performance, with most reported issues being minor or related to the hybrid system's integration rather than fundamental mechanical flaws. Owners generally appreciate the luxury, technology, and driving dynamics, with the hybrid system contributing to a refined and quiet driving experience.

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (Gasoline) + Electric Motor
**Hybrid System Malfunction Indicator**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light illuminated, reduced electric-only range, occasional loss of power.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of hybrid control module, battery management system, or electric motor components. May require software updates or replacement of faulty modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1500

**Coolant Leaks (Minor)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant loss, sweet smell, engine overheating warnings.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz E350e demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly for its powertrain and core mechanical components. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the sophisticated hybrid system, with occasional electronic glitches or hybrid system warnings being the most frequently cited issues. These are often resolvable with software updates or minor component replacements. The battery pack's longevity is a long-term consideration inherent to all hybrid vehicles. For the most part, the E350e offers a dependable luxury hybrid experience, aligning with Mercedes-Benz's reputation for quality, though owners should be prepared for potential costs associated with advanced hybrid technology.
