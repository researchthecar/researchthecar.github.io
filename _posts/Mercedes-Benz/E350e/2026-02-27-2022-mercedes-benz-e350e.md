# 2022 Mercedes-Benz E350e Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury vehicle, understanding common owner complaints can help you navigate potential pitfalls. The Mercedes-Benz E-Class brings sophisticated styling and advanced technology to the table, but owners have reported various mechanical and electrical quirks. Reviewing these owner-reported areas provides valuable insight into what to watch out for during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M264)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine delivers responsive power and efficient performance for daily driving. While generally capable, buyers should be aware of potential owner-reported cylinder head wear issues.

**Cylinder Head Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported premature wear at valve seats, leading to engine misfires and a sudden loss of power while driving.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis and potential cylinder head service or replacement by a qualified technician.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Plug-In Hybrid (E350e Powertrain)

This plug-in hybrid powertrain pairs a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine with an electric motor to balance efficiency and electric-only driving range. It offers smooth power delivery, though proper maintenance of both hybrid and conventional systems is essential.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and efficient gear shifts across various driving conditions. It is generally robust, though regular fluid changes are recommended to maintain longevity.

## Electrical

### 48V Battery and Charging System

The electrical architecture incorporates advanced mild-hybrid components, which occasionally present charging or battery-related warnings according to owner reports.

**48V Battery Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** A 48V battery malfunction warning appears on the dashboard followed by a red warning light and sudden loss of motive power, causing the vehicle to decelerate.
- **Solution:** Have the 48V battery and related electrical charging components inspected and replaced or reset by a dealership or specialist.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

The panoramic glass roof adds an open-air feel to the cabin, though rare instances of spontaneous glass failure have been noted by owners.

**Sudden Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass can unexpectedly shatter or explode while driving without any external impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the frame for alignment or stress points.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance System

Advanced driver assistance features enhance safety on the road, but sensors can occasionally misinterpret road conditions or stationary objects.

**Phantom Braking and False Speed Limits**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may suddenly activate the emergency brakes without an obstruction ahead or incorrectly read and adjust to false speed limit drops.
- **Solution:** Have camera and radar sensors recalibrated or checked for software updates by a certified service center.
