# 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE350d Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE-Class offers a luxurious cabin, refined road manners, and a variety of powerful engine options. However, prospective buyers should approach the used market with a degree of caution. Owner complaints highlight several recurring areas of concern, including potential fuel system issues, steering assist faults, and electrical or transmission glitches that warrant a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Turbo Diesel (GLE350d)

A 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 diesel engine providing strong low-end torque and impressive highway fuel economy. It pairs with an automatic transmission for smooth cruising, though maintenance history is crucial for diesel longevity.

### 3.5L V6 Gasoline (GLE350)

A naturally aspirated 3.5-liter V6 gasoline engine producing around 302 horsepower, offering brisk acceleration for daily driving. While generally durable, early production units can exhibit fuel system and injector complaints.

**Fuel System Stalling and Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may experience unexpected engine stalling while driving, difficulty starting, or fuel leaks around the intake and fuel injectors.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the fuel pump, lines, and fuel injectors for leaks or failure, and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 7-speed automatic transmission designed to deliver seamless gear changes across various driving modes. Buyers should test all gear selections and check for software updates during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Unintended Neutral Shift and Parking Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly display 'Car Not in Park' warnings, shift into neutral or park on its own while operating, or roll unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Check transmission software calibration and inspect electronic shifter modules or internal mechanical linkages at a certified service center.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The power steering system provides speed-sensitive assistance for easy low-speed parking and stable highway handling. Steering assist failures are a noted pain point among owner complaints.

**Power Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a sudden loss of power steering assistance accompanied by a warning message on the instrument cluster, making the steering wheel exceptionally heavy and difficult to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic power steering rack, wiring harnesses, and associated control modules for faults or recall applicability.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Manages exterior illumination, including optional advanced LED lighting systems. Proper adjustment and bulb health are essential for safe night driving.

**Dim Headlights**
- **Symptoms:** Headlight output may appear excessively dim during nighttime driving, significantly reducing forward visibility on unlit roads.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight assemblies, bulbs, and projector lenses for clouding or electrical voltage drops, and upgrade or replace components as needed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V903000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE350d&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the front passenger air bag does not deploy as necessary in the event of a crash, the front passenger has an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the OCS software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V241000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE350d&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If water enters the power steering control unit, the power steering assist may be deactivated, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the connector and inspect the electric power steering rack, replacing the rack as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE-Class combines upscale luxury and capable powertrains, but used car shoppers should perform a rigorous inspection. Pay close attention to potential fuel system concerns, steering assist operation, and electronic gear selector behavior. Securing a comprehensive service history and having a Mercedes specialist evaluate the vehicle can help avoid costly surprises.
