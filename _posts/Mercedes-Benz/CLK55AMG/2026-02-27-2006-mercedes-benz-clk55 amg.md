# 2006 Mercedes-Benz CLK55 AMG

### Overview
The 2006 Mercedes-Benz CLK55 AMG, a high-performance variant of the CLK-Class, is known for its potent V8 engine and engaging driving dynamics. As a luxury performance coupe, it offers a blend of comfort and sportiness. While generally well-built, like any vehicle of this age and performance level, certain areas may require attention. This analysis focuses on documented common issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential reliability concerns for owners and enthusiasts. The CLK55 AMG utilizes a robust powertrain, but specific components can be prone to wear or failure over time, impacting its overall dependability.

---


## Engine

### 5.4L V8 (M113)
**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers, Oil Cooler)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets, oil cooler seals, or associated hoses.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Cooling System Leaks (Hoses, Radiator)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant puddles, steam from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of coolant hoses, radiator, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

**Drive Belt Tensioner Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Squealing noise from the engine bay, potential loss of power steering or alternator function.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the drive belt tensioner assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, 'limp mode', transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement or repair of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Conductor Plate Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission fault codes.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the conductor plate and associated sensors.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Front Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Sway Bar Links**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling or clunking noises, especially when turning or going over uneven surfaces.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn sway bar links.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow or jerky window movement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

**Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Xenon headlights not turning on or flickering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight ballast unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Instrument Cluster Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Flickering gauges, dead pixels on display, warning lights staying on.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200

## Interior

### Cabin Components
**Seat Heater Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Heated seats not working or working intermittently.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seat heating elements or switches.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

**Sunroof Mechanism Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Sunroof not opening/closing properly, noisy operation.
- **Solution:** Lubrication, adjustment, or replacement of sunroof motor/tracks.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2006 Mercedes-Benz CLK55 AMG is a desirable performance coupe with a strong V8 engine. While it offers an exhilarating driving experience, potential owners should be aware of common issues that can arise with age and mileage. The engine, particularly its oil and cooling systems, and the 5-speed automatic transmission are areas where repairs may be necessary. Electrical components, such as window regulators and headlight ballasts, can also be a source of concern. Suspension components like control arm bushings are also subject to wear. Despite these potential issues, with diligent maintenance and timely repairs, the CLK55 AMG can remain a reliable and enjoyable vehicle.
