# 2019 Mercedes-Benz S560 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz S560 represents the pinnacle of luxury, advanced technology, and refined performance. As a high-end flagship sedan, it offers sophisticated engineering designed to deliver an exceptionally smooth ride. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints regarding electrical anomalies, braking components, and powertrain functions to ensure thorough pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8

The 4.0-liter biturbo V8 engine produces approximately 463 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, providing effortless acceleration and refined power. While generally robust, buyers should inspect for smooth operation and look out for rare reports of abnormal vibrations.

**Abnormal Engine Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected engine vibrations upon starting the vehicle, occasionally without illuminated warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect engine mounts, ignition components, and fuel delivery systems.

### 3.0L Turbo Inline-6 (Hybrid S560e)

The plug-in hybrid variant pairs a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 with an electric motor for efficient luxury cruising. It offers seamless power delivery, though hybrid systems require specialized diagnostic knowledge.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers swift, barely perceptible gear shifts suited to the flagship sedan. It is generally reliable when properly maintained with scheduled fluid services.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking components provide strong stopping power for a heavy luxury vehicle, but occasional owner complaints cite actuator and pedal-feel concerns.

**Rear Brake Actuator Seizure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced sudden seizure and inoperability of the rear brake actuator, requiring towing and professional service.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic parking brake and rear actuator assembly for proper engagement and release.

**Soft Pedal Feel**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners noted that brakes did not hold well initially, occasionally requiring double-pumping to achieve adequate stopping power.
- **Solution:** Check brake fluid condition, bleed the brake lines, and inspect master cylinder and booster operation.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Shifter

The steering system offers light, precise control, though power adjustment mechanisms inside the column can occasionally malfunction.

**Steering Column Adjustment Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** The power steering column adjustment mechanism can occasionally stick in the fully raised position and resist re-adjustment.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column telescoping motor and switch assembly; lubrication or component replacement may be required.

## Interior

### Cabin Electronics and Keyless Entry

Electrical architecture is extensive and feature-rich, though complex cabin electronics occasionally present isolated sensor or wiring anomalies.

**Keyless Entry Auto-Lock Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Owners reported instances where leaving keys inside the vehicle resulted in unexpected automatic door locking.
- **Solution:** Verify software calibration of the keyless entry and locking modules at a dealership.

**Seat Wiring Burning Odor**
- **Symptoms:** Rare reports indicate burning odors or smoke originating near power seat wiring beneath the driver's seat.
- **Solution:** Inspect power seat wiring harnesses, fuses, and heating elements for signs of chafing or short circuits.

## Safety

### Seat Belts and Pre-Safe Systems

Advanced safety systems provide robust protection but can occasionally trigger sensitive driver-assist warnings or belt tensioning behaviors.

**Pre-Safe Seat Belt Tightening and Handling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted unexpected seat belt tightening and momentary side-to-side vehicle sway associated with safety system activation.
- **Solution:** Have dealer technicians run diagnostic scans on the Pre-Safe sensor network and active safety modules.

**Seat Belt Retractor Jam**
- **Symptoms:** Rear seat belts or combined buckle assemblies can occasionally lock in place and refuse to retract.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace jammed seat belt retractor assemblies as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V173000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S560&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Two engine oil plugs may not withstand high engine loads while driving, possibly resulting in an engine oil leak. Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will reinforce the engine oil plugs by adding a retaining plate, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V540000: Forward Collision Avoidance: Sensing System: Radar](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S560&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An impaired Active Brake Assist may not automatically apply partial or full braking as expected, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the radar sensor control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V910000: Lane Departure: Lane Keep: Automatic Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S560&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the system remains active despite the driver not having their hands on the wheel, the driver's inattention may increase the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Mercedes has notified owners, and dealers will update the software of the steering control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V839000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S560&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A correctly fastened seatbelt may be inaccurately detected as being unfastened, preventing the seat belt pre-tensioners from activating in the event of a crash. If the seat belt tensioning does not activate in the event of a crash, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the seat belt buckles, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V821000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S560&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A detached idler gear can damage the engine, resulting in a stall and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the engine, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
