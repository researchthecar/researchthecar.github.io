## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, loss of ride height, 'airmatic system malfunction' warning on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air strut or air spring assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, 'airmatic system malfunction' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, intermittent audio issues, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dying prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle, warning lights related to battery voltage.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the parasitic draw (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz S560 is a highly reliable luxury vehicle, with its core powertrain components (engine and transmission) demonstrating excellent durability. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are related to the sophisticated AIRMATIC air suspension system, particularly air strut leaks and compressor failures, and occasional electrical gremlins such as infotainment software glitches or parasitic battery drain. These issues, while not universal, are the most commonly reported technical concerns. Proactive maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs can help mitigate these potential problems and ensure the continued enjoyment of this exceptional sedan.
