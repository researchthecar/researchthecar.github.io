## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, loss of ride height, 'Service Suspension' warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air strut or air spring.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, 'Service Suspension' warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, navigation system errors, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500


### 12V Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, electrical systems malfunctioning or failing intermittently, 'Auxiliary Battery Malfunction' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V auxiliary battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz S560 is a highly reliable luxury sedan, with its powertrain and core mechanicals demonstrating strong durability. The most commonly reported technical concerns tend to be related to the advanced AIRMATIC air suspension system, specifically air strut leaks and compressor failures, which can be costly to repair. Additionally, the sophisticated electrical system, particularly the infotainment software, can occasionally exhibit glitches requiring updates or resets. While the 4.0L twin-turbo V8 engine and 9G-TRONIC transmission are robust, owners should be mindful of potential 12V battery issues. Overall, the S560 offers excellent reliability for its class, provided that potential suspension and electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
