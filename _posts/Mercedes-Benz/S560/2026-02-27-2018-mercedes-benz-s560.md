The 2018 Mercedes-Benz S560 represents the pinnacle of luxury and engineering, offering a sophisticated driving experience. However, like any complex luxury vehicle, certain areas may require attention over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers. The S560 is equipped with advanced technology, which, while enhancing the driving experience, can also be a source of specific electronic or mechanical challenges.

## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air strut or air springs.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, Bluetooth connectivity issues, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or component replacement if necessary.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500


### 12V Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently, battery warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V auxiliary battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz S560 is a highly reliable luxury sedan, with its powertrain generally free from significant issues. The most commonly reported technical concerns tend to be related to the advanced AIRMATIC air suspension system, particularly air strut leaks and compressor failures, which can be costly to repair. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and occasional 12V battery drain, are also noted but are often resolvable with software updates or battery replacement. Interior components and exterior bodywork are typically robust. Overall, while minor issues can arise due to the vehicle's complexity, the S560 offers a dependable ownership experience for a luxury vehicle of its caliber.
