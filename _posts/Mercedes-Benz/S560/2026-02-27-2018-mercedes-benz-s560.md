# 2018 Mercedes-Benz S560 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz S560 represents the pinnacle of luxury, delivering effortless performance, advanced technology, and vault-like ride quality. While these flagship sedans are generally engineered to a high standard, used-car buyers should approach with mindfulness regarding potential complexity-driven issues. Maintenance costs on high-end German flagships can be substantial, making a thorough pre-purchase inspection and service history review essential.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8 Engine

Powered by a potent 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 producing around 463 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, this engine delivers exceptionally smooth and rapid acceleration. Reliability is generally solid with proper routine maintenance, though its high-complexity systems require diligent upkeep to avoid costly out-of-warranty repairs.

**Engine Misfire and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional engine misfires during acceleration, accompanied by engine shaking, a loss of power, and an illuminated check engine light.
- **Solution:** Diagnose ignition components, fuel injectors, or compression levels and replace faulty parts as needed.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides seamless gear changes that complement the luxury sedan's relaxed cruising character. It is generally robust, though occasional electronic or software anomalies can arise.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The gear shifter independently shifts into neutral while driving, requiring the driver to hold the shifter down for an extended period to reset the system.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the electronic shifter module and transmission control unit for software glitches or hardware faults.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides strong stopping power suited for a heavy luxury vehicle, but routine inspections of pads, rotors, and hydraulic actuators are critical for safety.

**Brake Responsiveness Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where depressing the brake pedal failed to adequately slow or decelerate the vehicle as expected.
- **Solution:** Inspect the brake hydraulic system, booster, pads, and rotors thoroughly for wear or mechanical failure.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Steering components provide light and responsive handling at low speeds while firming up on the highway, though electronic assist failures can occasionally occur.

**Stiff Steering and Malfunction Warning**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel becomes extremely difficult to turn accompanied by a dashboard warning message indicating a steering malfunction.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic power steering rack, wiring connections, and control modules for failures.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V407000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S560&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The electrical power bars inside the pre-fuse box in the trunk may not have been secured properly. If the power bars are not properly secured, it can cause higher electrical resistance increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the pre-fuse box, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V173000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S560&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Two engine oil plugs may not withstand high engine loads while driving, possibly resulting in an engine oil leak. Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will reinforce the engine oil plugs by adding a retaining plate, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V910000: Lane Departure: Lane Keep: Automatic Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S560&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the system remains active despite the driver not having their hands on the wheel, the driver's inattention may increase the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Mercedes has notified owners, and dealers will update the software of the steering control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V908000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S560&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the system remains active despite the driver not having their hands on the wheel, the driver's inattention may increase the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA has notified owners, and dealers will inspect the vehicles and correct the steering boot installation, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V821000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S560&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A detached idler gear can damage the engine, resulting in a stall and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the engine, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V449000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S560&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The software in the replaced instrument cluster may cause the seat belt warning system to not operate properly, only displaying a solid warning light if either the driver or passenger seat belt is unfastened, instead of a blinking light with an audible tone. **[Recall 21V197000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S560&modelYear=2018)**

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz S560 remains an extraordinary luxury cruiser offering unmatched comfort and performance. While major mechanical failures are relatively uncommon, the vehicle's dense array of advanced electronics and high-performance powertrain components mean that repairs can be expensive when things go wrong. Prospective buyers should prioritize models with a documented service history and budget accordingly for future maintenance.
