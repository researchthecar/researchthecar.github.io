**Introduction:**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz G65 AMG is an ultra-luxurious off-roader powered by a twin-turbocharged 6.0L V12 engine. It produces an impressive 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque. Despite its impressive performance, the G65 AMG has been known to experience issues that can affect its reliability.

**Engine:**

**6.0L V12 Biturbo**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** Premature failure of the turbochargers, leading to reduced power and engine performance. Solution: Replacement of failed turbochargers. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
* **Fuel Injector Issues:** Malfunctioning fuel injectors can cause rough idling, poor engine performance, and increased fuel consumption. Solution: Replacement of faulty fuel injectors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Transmission:**

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Harsh Shifting:** Rough or delayed gear changes, especially at low speeds. Solution: Transmission software update or replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,200+

**Suspension:**

* **Air Suspension Malfunctions:** Failure of the air suspension system can cause uneven ride height, suspension noises, and reduced stability. Solution: Repair or replacement of faulty components within the air suspension system (e.g., air springs, compressor, sensors). Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+

**Electrical:**

* **Electrical System Failure:** Intermittent electrical issues, such as dashboard malfunctions, loss of power, or electrical component failures. Solution: Diagnosis and repair of faulty electrical components or wiring harness. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Battery Discharge:** Premature battery discharge due to parasitic drain or faulty components. Solution: Battery replacement or diagnosis and repair of faulty components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+

**Exterior:**

* **Body Panel Misalignment:** Improper alignment of body panels, resulting in gaps or uneven surfaces. Solution: Adjustment or replacement of misaligned panels. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Interior:**

* **Dashboard Squeaking:** Noises or creaking from the dashboard, especially when driving over rough surfaces. Solution: Removal of dashboard and application of lubricating material. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Climate Control Issues:** Malfunctioning climate control system, leading to ineffective heating or cooling. Solution: Diagnosis and repair of faulty components within the climate control system. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2015 Mercedes-Benz G65 AMG is a powerful and luxurious off-roader, but it is not immune to reliability issues. While some issues are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems and factor them into their decision-making process.
