# 2018 Mercedes-Benz G65 AMG

### Overview
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz G65 AMG, a pinnacle of luxury and performance, is renowned for its robust construction and potent V12 engine. While generally considered a reliable vehicle, especially given its high-performance nature and exclusive market, certain components can experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and enthusiasts. The G65 AMG's unique blend of off-road capability and on-road presence means its components are subjected to diverse stresses, which can influence long-term durability.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased exhaust smoke.
- **Solution:** Replacement of affected turbocharger(s).
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $6,000 per turbocharger

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hoses, gaskets, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (7G-TRONIC)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, failure to engage gears, 'limp mode'.
- **Solution:** Reprogramming or replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500


## Suspension

### Solid Axle Suspension
**Worn Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, imprecise handling, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn suspension bushings.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Display issues, unresponsive touch screen, system reboots.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

### Climate Control System
**Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Airflow not directing correctly (e.g., stuck on defrost), unusual noises from dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty HVAC actuators.

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz G65 AMG is a highly exclusive and powerful SUV. While its core mechanical components, particularly the engine and transmission, are built to handle immense power, they are not immune to issues. Turbocharger failures and coolant leaks are the most notable engine concerns. The transmission can sometimes suffer from TCM malfunctions. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and battery drain, are also reported. Suspension bushings can wear over time, leading to minor handling and noise issues. Overall, the G65 AMG offers a robust ownership experience, but potential buyers should be aware of the high cost associated with repairs for its specialized components.
