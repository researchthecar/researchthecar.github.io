# 2016 Mercedes-Benz G65 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a 2016 Mercedes-Benz G65 AMG, buyers are looking at a hand-built, ultra-exclusive luxury SUV powered by a twin-turbo V12 engine. While these vehicles offer immense presence and handcrafted luxury, their complex mechanical and electrical systems require thorough inspection before purchase. Prospective owners should review available service records carefully to ensure all maintenance has been properly managed.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L Biturbo V12

This hand-built 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque, delivering immense straight-line performance. It requires rigorous, specialized maintenance, and buyers should have the cooling and ignition systems thoroughly inspected.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift Plus 7G-Tronic 7-Speed Automatic

The 7-speed automatic transmission handles the massive torque output of the V12 engine while delivering smooth gear changes. Regular fluid changes are critical to ensure long-term durability and prevent hesitation or harsh shifting.

## Exterior

### Door Latches and Locks

Cabin appointments and power accessories are built to high standards, though mechanical door hardware should be checked for proper alignment and operation.

**Door Latch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The front driver's side door fails to latch or close securely when entering the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Inspect the door latch mechanism, striker, and locking components for mechanical wear or internal failure, and replace the affected latch assembly if necessary.

## Safety

### Air Bag

Occupant restraint and airbag sensors generally operate reliably, though occasional warning light activations can occur due to sensor faults.

**Air Bag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The air bag warning light and front passenger side malfunction service indicator illuminate constantly or intermittently on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) using specialized diagnostic tools to identify faulty seat sensors, wiring harnesses, or modules.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz G65 AMG is an exotic, heavy-duty luxury SUV built for performance and prestige rather than traditional economy. Buyers must approach used models with diligence, prioritizing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist to verify the condition of the powertrain, electronics, and safety systems.
