# 2014 Mercedes-Benz G65 AMG

### Overview
The 2014 Mercedes-Benz G65 AMG, a pinnacle of luxury and performance, is known for its robust construction and powerful V12 engine. While generally well-regarded for its unique presence and off-road capability, like any high-performance vehicle, it can experience specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and prospective buyers. The G65 AMG's complex systems, particularly its powertrain and electronics, are areas where specific attention may be warranted.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12 (M275)
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced power, whistling noises, boost leaks, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $7,000

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, reduced engine performance, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant loss, sweet smell from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (7G-TRONIC)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from transmission pan or lines, rough shifting, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals, gaskets, or lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $900

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Torque converter replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Mercedes-Benz G65 AMG is a formidable vehicle with a powerful V12 engine. While its core mechanical components are generally robust, potential owners should be aware of specific issues that can arise, particularly concerning the twin-turbocharged engine's turbochargers and ignition system, as well as potential leaks and torque converter problems in the 7-speed automatic transmission. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment glitches and sensor failures, are also noted. The suspension, exterior, and interior systems are relatively free of widespread reported issues. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any emerging symptoms are crucial for maintaining the reliability of this high-performance luxury SUV.
