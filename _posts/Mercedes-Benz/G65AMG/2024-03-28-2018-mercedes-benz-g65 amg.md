**Introduction**
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz G65 AMG is a high-performance luxury SUV powered by a twin-turbocharged 6.0-liter V12 engine producing 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque. It comes with a seven-speed automatic transmission and a full-time four-wheel drive system. The G65 AMG is known for its impressive off-road capabilities, luxurious interior, and powerful engine. However, some owners have reported issues with certain components of the vehicle.

**Engine**
**6.0L V12 Engine**

**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, whistling sound from the engine bay
Solution: Replace faulty turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**Seven-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Shifting Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Difficult or delayed gear changes, slipping gears, grinding noises
Solution: Update transmission software, replace faulty components (e.g., valve body, torque converter)
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
**Air Suspension System**

**Air Bag Leaks**
Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sitting unevenly, reduced ride height, warning message on dashboard
Solution: Replace faulty air bags, inspect and seal leaks in the suspension system
Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
**Battery**

**Premature Battery Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical system malfunctions
Solution: Replace faulty battery
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
**Body Panels**

**Rust Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on body panels, especially around the wheel arches
Solution: Repair or replace affected body panels, apply anti-rust coatings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
**Dashboard**

**Cracked Dashboard**
Description/Symptoms: Sunken or cracked areas on the dashboard
Solution: Replace faulty dashboard
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
While the 2018 Mercedes-Benz G65 AMG is a capable and luxurious SUV, it has experienced some issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. The severity of these issues varies, but some of them can be costly to repair. It's important for potential owners to be aware of these potential issues and to consider them when making a purchase decision.
