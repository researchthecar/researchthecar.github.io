# 2017 Mercedes-Benz G65 AMG

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz G65 AMG, a pinnacle of luxury and performance, is known for its robust construction and powerful V12 engine. While generally reliable, its high-performance nature and complex systems can lead to specific, albeit infrequent, issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and enthusiasts. The G65 AMG's exclusivity means that repairs can be costly, emphasizing the importance of proactive maintenance and addressing any emerging issues promptly to preserve its legendary status and performance.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential replacement of turbocharger seals or the entire unit.
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $7,000

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of leaking hoses, gaskets, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (7G-TRONIC)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, rough shifting, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of transmission pan gasket, seals, or torque converter.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz G65 AMG is a highly exclusive and powerful vehicle. While its core mechanical components are generally robust, owners should be aware of potential issues related to its high-performance twin-turbo V12 engine, particularly turbocharger longevity and coolant system integrity. The transmission, while generally reliable, can experience fluid leaks. Electrical gremlins, especially within the infotainment system and potential battery drain, are also noted. Given the vehicle's complexity and luxury status, repair costs can be substantial. Regular, specialized maintenance is crucial for mitigating these potential issues and ensuring the continued performance and reliability of this iconic SUV.
