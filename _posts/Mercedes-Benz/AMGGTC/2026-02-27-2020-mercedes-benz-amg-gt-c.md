# 2020 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT C Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT C represents the pinnacle of high-performance luxury, offering aggressive styling and track-capable handling paired with hand-built AMG engineering. As an exotic sports car, maintenance and ownership costs reflect its high-end pedigree, making a thorough pre-purchase inspection essential for prospective used-car buyers.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

Equipped with a hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 producing approximately 550 horsepower and 502 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant delivers blistering performance, though buyers should budget for specialized maintenance.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

Utilizes a rear-mounted 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transaxle designed for rapid gear changes and optimized weight distribution. It provides crisp shifts suited for both spirited driving and cruising.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+C&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 22V365000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+C&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A disabled eCall system would prevent a vehicle occupant from contacting the emergency services call center in an emergency, potentially delaying emergency responders and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT C is an exhilarating high-performance machine that requires careful evaluation before purchase. Because reported owner complaints are minimal and heavily reliant on individual driving conditions, buyers should prioritize a comprehensive inspection by a qualified specialist to ensure optimal mechanical health.
