# 2021 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT C Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT C represents the pinnacle of high-performance luxury, combining aggressive styling with track-capable dynamics. Powered by a hand-built biturbo V8, it offers exhilarating acceleration and precise handling that appeals directly to enthusiast drivers. As a low-volume exotic sports car, prospective buyers should focus heavily on verifying routine maintenance history and inspecting high-performance wear items before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8

This hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 550 horsepower and 502 lb-ft of torque. It delivers blistering performance and responsive power delivery, though proper maintenance and warm-up cycles are essential for longevity.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The rear-mounted 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transaxle provides lightning-fast gear changes and contributes to the car's balanced weight distribution. It performs exceptionally well under spirited driving conditions while remaining refined in everyday traffic.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V365000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+C&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A disabled eCall system would prevent a vehicle occupant from contacting the emergency services call center in an emergency, potentially delaying emergency responders and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT C is an extraordinary performance machine built for drivers seeking exotic thrills and refined grand-touring capability. With no major recurring complaint patterns reported, standard due diligence—such as a professional pre-purchase inspection and service record verification—will help ensure a rewarding ownership experience.
