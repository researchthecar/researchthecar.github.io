# 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT C Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT C represents an exhilarating high-performance sports car offering aggressive styling and track-capable dynamics. Because this low-volume luxury performance vehicle generates few owner complaints in standard reporting databases, prospective buyers should focus primarily on rigorous pre-purchase inspections and verifying proper maintenance history.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Engine

This hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 550 horsepower and 502 lb-ft of torque. It delivers blistering acceleration paired with exotic exhaust notes, though routine maintenance costs on forced-induction AMG powerplants are predictably high.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transaxle

The rear-mounted 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transaxle provides lightning-fast gear changes and optimizes weight distribution for handling. Buyers should test-drive thoroughly to ensure smooth engagement across all modes, as complex transaxle repairs can be expensive.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+C&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 22V365000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+C&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A disabled eCall system would prevent a vehicle occupant from contacting the emergency services call center in an emergency, potentially delaying emergency responders and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT C is an engineering masterpiece built for pure driving excitement rather than commuter practicality. While formal complaint trends are virtually nonexistent, maintenance, insurance, and potential component replacement expenses demand a healthy upkeep budget.
