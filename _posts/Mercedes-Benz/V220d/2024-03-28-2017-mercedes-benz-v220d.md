**Introduction:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz V220d is a versatile luxury MPV that combines practicality with premium amenities. It offers a spacious cabin, advanced safety features, and a powerful 2.1-liter diesel engine paired with a 7G-TRONIC PLUS automatic transmission. The V220d delivers 163 horsepower and 380 lb-ft of torque, providing ample performance for daily driving and long journeys. However, despite its many strengths, the V220d has faced some specific issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**2.1L Diesel Engine**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, rough idling, reduced power output, illuminated engine warning light
    * Solution: Replace faulty fuel injector(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive smoke from exhaust, whining or grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace or repair turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7G-TRONIC PLUS Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or jerking during gear changes, delayed or missed shifts
    * Solution: Reprogram or replace transmission control module, replace valve body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to engage or hold gears, loss of power while driving
    * Solution: Replace transmission fluid, inspect and repair internal components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Abnormal ride height, reduced damping, warning light on dashboard
    * Solution: Replace faulty air compressor, strut, or sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Axle Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise from rear axle area, vibrations while driving
    * Solution: Replace faulty bearing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle fails to start even with a charged battery
    * Solution: Diagnose electrical system for parasitic draw, inspect alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Harness Faults:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical issues such as flickering lights, power window failure, or instrument cluster problems
    * Solution: Inspect and repair faulty wiring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises or vibrations from dashboard area
    * Solution: Install damping pads, tighten loose components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Multimedia System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Lagging or freezing, navigation malfunctions, audio glitches
    * Solution: Update software, replace faulty components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**External**

* **Rust on Undercarriage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive corrosion on metal parts beneath the vehicle
    * Solution: Inspect and apply rust mitigation measures
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Exterior Trim Loose or Falling Off:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or missing molding, chrome trim, or badges
    * Solution: Reinstall or replace faulty trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz V220d faces several issues, ranging from minor nuisances to more serious and expensive repairs. Engine and transmission problems can be costly, while electrical issues may lead to intermittent or even severe malfunctions. Suspension and exterior issues are less common but can still impact the vehicle's safety and reliability. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but owners should be aware of the potential problems before purchasing or owning a V220d.
