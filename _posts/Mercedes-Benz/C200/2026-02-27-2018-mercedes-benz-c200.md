# 2018 Mercedes-Benz C200 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz C-Class offers a refined cabin, advanced technology, and poised driving dynamics that make it a compelling choice on the used luxury car market. However, prospective buyers should approach with careful inspection, as owner reports highlight potential mechanical and electrical trouble spots. Being aware of these frequent grievances can help shoppers negotiate a better price or budget for future upkeep.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

A 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing around 241 horsepower, providing responsive acceleration and everyday efficiency. While generally robust, owners have reported intermittent sensor and crankcase ventilation faults.

**Check Engine Light and Code P052E**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light accompanied by diagnostic trouble code P052E related to crankcase ventilation.
- **Solution:** Requires replacing the faulty crankcase ventilation component with an updated manufacturer-revised part.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected engine stalling while driving at various speeds, increasing the risk of accidents.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic evaluation by a qualified technician to identify underlying fuel or emissions control failures.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 9-speed automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel or all-wheel drive systems, offering smooth and rapid gear changes. This gearbox generally operates reliably when maintained with regular fluid changes.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and Power Assist

Issues related to steering responsiveness and potential mechanical wear in the steering rack require careful checking during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Steering Rack Locknut Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Potential internal material cracks in the steering rack locknut that could affect steering integrity.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering components for wear and verify whether the vehicle was serviced under relevant manufacturer service campaigns.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety restraints must be fully functional; check all seat belt mechanisms for smooth retraction during a test drive.

**Failure to Retract**
- **Symptoms:** Front driver or passenger seat belts fail to retract smoothly into their stowed positions after use.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt assemblies and replace retractors or related hardware if binding occurs.
