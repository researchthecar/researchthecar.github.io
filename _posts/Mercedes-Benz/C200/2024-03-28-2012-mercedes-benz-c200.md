Introduction: The 2012 Mercedes-Benz C200 is a compact executive sedan that offers a comfortable and luxurious driving experience. It is powered by a 1.8-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine that produces 184 horsepower and 206 pound-feet of torque. The C200 comes standard with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The vehicle is praised for its stylish design, high-quality interior, and advanced technology features. However, it also has some known issues that potential buyers should be aware of.

**Engine**
- **1.8-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine**
    - **Turbo failure**. Symptoms include a loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, and a check engine light. Solution: Replace the turbocharger. Estimated cost to fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    - **Intake manifold failure**. Symptoms include rough idling, stalling, and difficulty starting. Solution: Replace the intake manifold. Estimated cost to fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    - **Coolant leak**. Symptoms include a low coolant level, overheating, and a check engine light. Solution: Replace the coolant hose or other components as needed. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $1,000+
    - **Oil leak**. Symptoms include a low oil level, oil spots under the vehicle, and a check engine light. Solution: Replace the oil pan gasket or other components as needed. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Transmission**
- **7-speed automatic transmission**
    - **Shifting problems**. Symptoms include delayed shifts, harsh shifts, and a check engine light. Solution: Replace the transmission fluid or other components as needed. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Suspension**
- **Front suspension**. Symptoms include excessive noise, vibration, or instability while driving. Solution: Replace the suspension components as needed. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $2,000+
- **Rear suspension**. Symptoms include excessive noise, vibration, or instability while driving. Solution: Replace the suspension components as needed. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Electrical**
- **Battery failure**. Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle, dim headlights, and a check battery light. Solution: Replace the battery. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $500+
- **Alternator failure**. Symptoms include dim headlights, flickering lights, and a check alternator light. Solution: Replace the alternator. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000+
- **Starter failure**. Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle or a grinding noise when starting. Solution: Replace the starter. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000+
