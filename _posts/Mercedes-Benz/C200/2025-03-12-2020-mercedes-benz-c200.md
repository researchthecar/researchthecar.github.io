The 2020 Mercedes-Benz C200 is a popular luxury sedan known for its stylish design, comfortable ride, and advanced technology. The C200 comes equipped with a 1.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing 181 horsepower and 207 lb-ft of torque. Other engine options include the C300 with a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder and AMG C43 and C63 models with more powerful engines. The C200 is generally praised for its fuel efficiency, elegant interior, and refined driving experience. Some common complaints include the high cost of maintenance and repairs, and some find the rear seat space to be a bit cramped.

**Engine**

**1.5L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M264)**

*   **Faulty Auxiliary Coolant Pump**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Engine overheating, coolant leaks, "Check Engine" light illuminated. The auxiliary coolant pump may fail prematurely.
    *   Solution: Replace the faulty auxiliary coolant pump.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting, rough idle, and a noticeable loss of power.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the high-pressure fuel pump and potentially associated fuel lines.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800-$1200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic (9G-Tronic 725.0)**

*   **Rough or Harsh Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Noticeable harshness or jerkiness during gear changes, especially between lower gears. Can sometimes be more pronounced when the transmission is cold.
    *   Solution: Some cases can be resolved with a software update to the transmission control module (TCM). More severe cases may require valve body replacement or, in rare instances, a full transmission replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Software update: $150 - $300. Valve body replacement: $1500 - $3000+. Full transmission replacement: $5000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Malfunctioning MBUX System**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Freezing, crashing, lagging, or unresponsive touchscreen. Connectivity issues with Bluetooth or Apple CarPlay/Android Auto.
    *   Solution: Software updates, system resets, or replacement of the MBUX control unit in severe cases.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Software update: $0 - $200. MBUX control unit replacement: $1000 - $2500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Faulty Steering Column Lock**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The steering wheel may not unlock, preventing the car from starting. "Check Engine" light may illuminate with a steering column lock fault code.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the steering column lock module.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof not opening or closing properly, getting stuck, or making excessive noise. Water leaks around the sunroof seal.
    *   Solution: Cleaning and lubricating the sunroof tracks, replacing the sunroof motor, or resealing the sunroof.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Cleaning/lubrication: $100 - $200. Sunroof motor replacement: $400 - $800+. Resealing sunroof: $300 - $600+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Automatic High Beam Assist Malfunction**
    *   Description/Symptoms: High beams not automatically dimming when oncoming traffic is detected, or failing to activate in dark conditions.
    *   Solution: Calibration or replacement of the camera sensor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Summary**

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz C200, while a desirable vehicle, can experience a range of issues. The electrical system, particularly the MBUX and steering column lock, can be problematic. Engine issues, such as auxiliary coolant pump failure, can lead to costly repairs. While the transmission is generally reliable, some owners report rough shifting that may require expensive repairs. While some issues are minor, others can be quite costly, making a thorough pre-purchase inspection essential.
