**Introduction:**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz C200 is a compact luxury sedan equipped with a 2.0L inline-4 turbocharged engine producing 184 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. It received praise for its stylish design, luxurious interior, and agile handling but faced criticism for its firm ride and limited rear legroom.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Inline-4 Turbocharged Engine**

    * **Turbocharger Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, high-pitched whining noise
        * Solution: Replace the turbocharger
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Valve Deposits:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, rough idle
        * Solution: Clean or replace the intake valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic Transmission**

    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Gear shifting problems, loss of power, check engine light
        * Solution: Replace the mechatronic unit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Torque Converter Shudder:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering during acceleration
        * Solution: Replace the torque converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Starter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clicking noise when attempting to start the vehicle, no engine crank
    * Solution: Replace the starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Replace the battery or diagnose electrical drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlight assemblies
    * Solution: Replace the headlight assemblies
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or squeaking noise from the sunroof
    * Solution: Adjust or replace the sunroof panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Central Console Button Sticking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Buttons on the central console become stuck or unresponsive
    * Solution: Replace the button assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz C200 has been known to experience a range of issues, with the most common problems affecting the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. While some issues are relatively minor, others can be costly to repair. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues experienced by this vehicle vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage.
