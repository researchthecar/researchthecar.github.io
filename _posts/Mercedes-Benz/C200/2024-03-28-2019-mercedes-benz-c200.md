**Introduction:**
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz C200 is a luxury sedan powered by a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine producing 255 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a 9-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive. The C200 is praised for its comfortable ride, responsive handling, and stylish interior. However, it has received some complaints about its reliability and certain features.

### **Engine**
- **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Engine**
  - **Intake Valve Deposits:**
    - **Description/Symptoms:** Excessive carbon buildup on intake valves can lead to decreased engine performance, hesitation during acceleration, and potential stalling.
    - **Solution:** Professional cleaning of the intake valves and related components.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
    - **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V469000

### **Transmission**
- **9-Speed Automatic Transmission**
  - **Shifting Hesitations:**
    - **Description/Symptoms:** Occasional delays or hesitancy during gear changes, particularly between 1st and 2nd gears.
    - **Solution:** Software updates or transmission control module (TCM) replacement.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
    - **Recalls:** None Found

### **Suspension**
  - **Rear Air Suspension Leakage:**
    - **Description/Symptoms:** Airbag leaks can cause the rear of the vehicle to sag or bounce excessively.
    - **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty airbag.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500+
    - **Recalls:** None Found

### **Electrical**
  - **Battery Discharge:**
    - **Description/Symptoms:** Sudden battery discharge, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
    - **Solution:** Diagnosis of electrical system for parasitic drain or battery replacement.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $800+
    - **Recalls:** None Found

### **Exterior**
  - **Headlight Condensation:**
    - **Description/Symptoms:** Moisture can build up inside the headlight assemblies, fogging them up.
    - **Solution:** Replacement of the affected headlight.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,200+
    - **Recalls:** None Found

### **Interior**
  - **Infotainment System Lag:**
    - **Description/Symptoms:** Delays and freezes when using the infotainment system, including navigation, audio, and climate controls.
    - **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the infotainment unit.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000+
    - **Recalls:** None Found

### **Summary**
Overall, the 2019 Mercedes-Benz C200 has received mixed reviews regarding reliability. While some owners have experienced significant issues, others have had positive experiences. The most common problems reported include intake valve deposits, shifting hesitations, suspension leaks, battery discharge, and infotainment system lag. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific cause and the extent of the damage.
