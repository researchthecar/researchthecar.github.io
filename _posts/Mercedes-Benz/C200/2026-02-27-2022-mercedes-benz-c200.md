# 2022 Mercedes-Benz C200 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Mercedes-Benz C200, buyers will find a refined luxury sedan offering modern tech and efficient performance. However, like any sophisticated modern luxury vehicle, owner reports highlight a few areas of concern ranging from fuel system issues to electronic glitches. Reviewing these owner-reported patterns can help prospective buyers inspect potential trouble spots before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M254)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine (M254) pairs mild-hybrid technology to deliver smooth, efficient power suited for daily driving. It offers responsive acceleration, though buyers should verify that all fuel system updates and checks have been performed.

**Fuel Pump and Delivery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected loss of engine power, stalling, or the vehicle failing to accelerate properly while attempting to merge or drive safely in traffic.
- **Solution:** Inspection of the fuel pump and related delivery modules by a qualified technician, with replacement of faulty components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9G-TRONIC 9-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes and optimizes highway efficiency. It generally operates reliably when properly maintained, with no widespread systemic mechanical failures highlighted in owner complaints.

## Brakes

### Active Brake Assist

Advanced safety features provide an extra layer of protection, though sensor-based systems require careful diagnosis if warning lights or assistance faults occur.

**Forward Collision Avoidance and Brake Assist Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced unexpected behavior or failure warnings from the active safety systems, including concerns regarding the automated braking assistance during frontal incidents.
- **Solution:** Have a certified service center run diagnostics on the vehicle's radar sensors, camera modules, and safety software calibration.

### Service Brakes

Braking components provide solid stopping power, but any abnormal noises or pedal feedback should be inspected promptly.

**Brake Pedal Thumping and Clunking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers notice an unusual clunking noise and a physical thumping sensation transmitted through the brake pedal when coming to a stop.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and caliper mounting hardware for proper seating and potential manufacturing defects.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Display Screen

Cabin electronics manage the majority of the vehicle's convenience and safety controls, making software stability vital.

**Blank Display Screen**
- **Symptoms:** The central infotainment screen intermittently goes completely blank, temporarily disabling backup camera functionality and climate controls.
- **Solution:** Software updates or module resets performed by a dealership technician can resolve intermittent screen blackouts.

## Summary Verdict

The Mercedes-Benz C200 provides a luxurious driving experience with a high-tech cabin and efficient powertrain options. While most mechanical systems prove solid, used-car buyers should thoroughly check the service history for any fuel system or electronic software updates. A pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable technician is strongly recommended to ensure all safety systems and electronics function as intended.
