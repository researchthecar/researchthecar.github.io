### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz C200 demonstrates strong technical reliability, with no significant widespread issues reported across its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission. The primary areas of concern are minor electrical gremlins, particularly related to the advanced infotainment system's software, and a potential for premature 12V battery failure, which is not uncommon in modern vehicles with high electrical demands. These issues are generally manageable and do not detract significantly from the vehicle's overall dependability. Owners can expect a robust and well-built automobile, provided regular maintenance is performed and any minor electrical glitches are addressed promptly.
