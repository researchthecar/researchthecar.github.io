**Introduction:**

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz C200 is a luxury compact sedan powered by a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. It delivers 255 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. The C200 is praised for its refined interior, advanced technology, and comfortable driving experience. However, some owners have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine**

* **Oil Leak from Oil Filter Housing:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Leaks oil around the oil filter housing.
  * Solution: Replace oil filter housing unit.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
* **Timing Chain Noise:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise during engine operation, especially during startups and accelerations.
  * Solution: Replace timing chain or tensioner.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Issues:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifting, especially during low-speed maneuvers.
  * Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission components.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID 22V-088

**Suspension**

* **Excessive Road Noise:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise transmitted into the cabin from the tires.
  * Solution: Replace or adjust suspension components.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly or struggles to hold a charge.
  * Solution: Diagnose and fix underlying issue, such as faulty alternator or electrical components.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system fails to function properly, leading to temperature discomfort.
  * Solution: Update software or replace faulty components.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
* **Squeaky Headliner:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Headliner makes squeaking noises during driving.
  * Solution: Tighten headliner or replace damaged insulation.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Summary:**

While the 2022 Mercedes-Benz C200 offers a luxurious and comfortable driving experience, it has encountered some issues related to the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity of these issues can vary, but some can be costly to repair. Owners should be aware of potential problems and factor in the potential repair costs when considering this vehicle.
