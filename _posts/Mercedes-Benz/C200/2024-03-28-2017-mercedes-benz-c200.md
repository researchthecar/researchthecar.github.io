**Introduction**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz C200 is a compact luxury sedan that offers a comfortable and sophisticated driving experience. It comes equipped with a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine that produces 241 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. The C200 is available with either a 6-speed manual transmission or a 7-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

* **2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4**

    * **Intake valve deposits**
        * Description/Symptoms: Carbon buildup on the intake valves can cause rough idling, reduced power, and increased fuel consumption.
        * Solution: Remove and clean the intake valves.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

* **Fuel injector failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Faulty fuel injectors can cause difficulty starting, rough idling, and reduced power.
        * Solution: Replace the affected fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-speed manual**
    * **Clutch failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged clutch plates can cause slipping, difficulty shifting gears, or a complete loss of power.
        * Solution: Replace the clutch assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

* **7-speed automatic**
    * **Torque converter shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations or shuddering during acceleration or shifting can be caused by a faulty torque converter.
        * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, causing difficulty starting the vehicle.
        * Solution: Test and replace the affected electrical components, such as the alternator, starter, or battery.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Sunroof rattle**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noises coming from the sunroof when driving over rough roads or at high speeds.
        * Solution: Adjust or replace the sunroof tracks and seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

* **Navigation system freezing**
        * Description/Symptoms: The navigation system freezes or becomes unresponsive.
        * Solution: Update the navigation system software or replace the head unit if necessary.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2017 Mercedes-Benz C200 has a relatively low number of reported issues. The most common problems are related to the engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity of these issues ranges from minor inconveniences to more serious mechanical failures that can be expensive to repair. However, the C200 has received positive reviews for its performance, luxury features, and overall driving experience.
