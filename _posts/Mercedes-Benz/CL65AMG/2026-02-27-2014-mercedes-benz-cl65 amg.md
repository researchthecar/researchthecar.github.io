# 2014 Mercedes-Benz CL65 AMG

### Overview
The 2014 Mercedes-Benz CL65 AMG, a pinnacle of luxury grand touring, is equipped with a formidable twin-turbocharged V12 engine. While offering exhilarating performance, its complexity and high-strung nature can lead to specific reliability concerns. Owners should be aware of potential issues related to the powertrain, particularly the advanced engine management and cooling systems, as well as the robust but complex transmission. Electrical systems, given the car's extensive features, can also present challenges. However, with diligent maintenance and prompt attention to any developing problems, the CL65 AMG can remain a rewarding ownership experience, though maintenance costs are inherently high.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12 (M275)
**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, overheating, visible leaks from hoses or radiators.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced power, unusual noises from the engine bay, boost leaks.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential replacement of turbochargers or associated plumbing.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $5000+

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping, shuddering, delayed engagement, or harsh shifting.
- **Solution:** Rebuilding or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, 'limp mode', transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1800


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, 'ABC System Fault' warning on the dashboard, fluid leaks.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hydraulic lines, struts, or pump.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $4000+

**Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of suspension height control, loud whining noise from the pump.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, audio issues, navigation problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the COMAND head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000


### Sensor Failures
**Various Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine lights, ABS/ESP warnings, incorrect readings from various systems.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty sensors (e.g., O2 sensors, ABS sensors, crank/cam sensors).
- **Cost:** $200 - $600 per sensor

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Mercedes-Benz CL65 AMG is a high-performance luxury coupe with a complex V12 powertrain and advanced systems. While generally well-built, its sophisticated nature means potential for costly repairs, particularly concerning the engine's cooling and turbocharging, the robust transmission, and the Active Body Control suspension. Electrical gremlins, especially within the COMAND system, can also arise. Owners should budget for premium maintenance and be prepared for specialized repairs. Despite these potential issues, the CL65 AMG offers an unparalleled driving experience for those who prioritize performance and luxury, provided they are committed to its upkeep.
