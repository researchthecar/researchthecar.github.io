**Introduction:**

The 2012 Mercedes-Benz CL65 AMG is a high-performance luxury coupe powered by a twin-turbocharged 6.0L V12 engine. It produces 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. The CL65 AMG is known for its impressive acceleration, luxurious interior, and sophisticated appearance. However, some owners have encountered various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**6.0L V12**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** Symptoms include loss of power, blue or black smoke from the exhaust, and excessive oil consumption. Solution: Replace the faulty turbocharger or rebuild the engine. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $10,000+
* **Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure:** Symptoms include stalling, rough idling, and poor engine performance. Solution: Replace the crankshaft position sensor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:** Symptoms include difficulty shifting gears, loss of power, or shuddering during acceleration. Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
* **Torque Converter Failure:** Symptoms include shuddering, stalling, or slipping. Solution: Replace the torque converter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500

**Suspension**

* **Air Spring Leaks:** Symptoms include uneven ride height, reduced ground clearance, or a grinding noise. Solution: Replace the faulty air springs. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per air spring

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Symptoms include a dead battery, difficulty starting the vehicle, or flickering lights. Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Electrical Module Failures:** Various electrical modules, such as the body control module or navigation system, can malfunction, causing a range of issues. Solution: Replace or repair the faulty module. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

* **Panel Gaps:** Poor panel alignment can occur, leading to uneven or misaligned body panels. Solution: Adjust or replace the affected panels. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Failure:** The navigation system may freeze, crash, or display incorrect information. Solution: Update or replace the navigation software or unit. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
* **Control Panel Buttons Sticking or Malfunctioning:** Buttons on the control panel may become unresponsive or sticky. Solution: Replace the faulty buttons or control panel. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2012 Mercedes-Benz CL65 AMG is a highly capable and impressive vehicle. However, it is important to be aware of the potential issues that some owners have experienced. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, but they should be taken into consideration when making a purchase decision.
