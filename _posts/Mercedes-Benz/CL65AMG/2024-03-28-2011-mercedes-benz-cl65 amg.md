## 2011 Mercedes-Benz CL65 AMG

The 2011 Mercedes-Benz CL65 AMG is a high-performance luxury coupe that offers a thrilling driving experience. It comes with a powerful 6.0-liter V12 engine that produces 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. The CL65 AMG also features a number of advanced features, including an air suspension, active body control, and a panoramic sunroof.

While the CL65 AMG is a highly capable vehicle, it is not without its problems. Here are some of the most common issues that owners have reported:


### Engine

**6.0L V12 Engine**
- **Intake manifold runner flap failure:** The intake manifold runner flaps can fail, causing a loss of power and a rough idle. The flaps can be replaced for approximately $1,000 to $2,000.
- **Camshaft adjuster failure:** The camshaft adjusters can fail, causing a loss of power and a rough idle. The camshaft adjusters can be replaced for approximately $2,000 to $3,000.

### Transmission

- **5-speed automatic transmission:** The 5-speed automatic transmission can fail, causing a loss of power and a rough shift. The transmission can be replaced for approximately $3,000 to $5,000.

### Suspension

- **Air suspension failure:** The air suspension can fail, causing the vehicle to ride lower than normal. The air suspension can be replaced for approximately $2,000 to $3,000.
- **Active body control failure:** The active body control system can fail, causing the vehicle to handle poorly. The active body control system can be replaced for approximately $3,000 to $5,000.

### Electrical

- **Battery failure:** The battery can fail, causing the vehicle to lose power. The battery can be replaced for approximately $300 to $500.
- **Alternator failure:** The alternator can fail, causing the vehicle to lose power. The alternator can be replaced for approximately $500 to $800.

### Exterior

- **Sunroof leaks:** The panoramic sunroof can leak, causing water damage to the interior. The sunroof can be replaced for approximately $1,000 to $2,000.

### Interior

- **Infotainment system failure:** The infotainment system can fail, causing the loss of audio and navigation functions. The infotainment system can be replaced for approximately $1,000 to $2,000.
- **Heated seat failure:** The heated seats can fail, causing the seats to be cold. The heated seats can be replaced for approximately $500 to $800.

Overall, the 2011 Mercedes-Benz CL65 AMG is a reliable vehicle, but it is not without its problems. The most common issues include engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior problems. The severity of these issues varies, but they can all be costly to repair.
