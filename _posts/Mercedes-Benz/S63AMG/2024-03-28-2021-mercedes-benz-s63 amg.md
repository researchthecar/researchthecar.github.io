**2021 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG is a high-performance luxury sedan that offers a combination of power, comfort, and technology. It is powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V8 engine that produces 603 horsepower and 631 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The S63 AMG comes standard with all-wheel drive and a host of features, including a panoramic sunroof, leather upholstery, heated and ventilated front seats, a Burmester sound system, and a 12.8-inch touchscreen infotainment system.

**Engine**

* **4.0L V8 Biturbo**
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive exhaust smoke, rattling noises
    Solution: Replacement of turbocharger
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
    Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **9-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Issues**
    Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifts, slipping gears
    Solution: Software update, transmission fluid change, or transmission rebuild
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,500+
    Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG is a well-engineered and reliable vehicle, but it is not without its issues. The most common issues reported by owners include turbocharger failure, shifting issues, battery drain, and infotainment system malfunctions. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the repair. Overall, the S63 AMG is a solid choice for those seeking a high-performance luxury sedan, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues before making a purchase.
