# 2020 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG represents the pinnacle of luxury and high-performance engineering, pairing lavish comfort with immense twin-turbo V8 power. While owner reports to NHTSA are generally sparse for this flagship performance sedan, prospective buyers should still inspect complex electronic, fuel, and glass systems carefully. As with any high-end luxury vehicle, conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection is essential to ensure long-term mechanical and electrical health.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8 Engine

The S63 AMG is powered by a hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing roughly 603 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque. It delivers breathtaking acceleration and robust performance, though buyers should expect higher-than-average maintenance and premium fuel requirements.

### Fuel Pump

Delivers fuel from the tank to the high-pressure injection system.

**Unexpected Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may suddenly lose power and cut off while driving on the highway without prior warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the fuel system and replace the failing fuel pump if pressure irregularities are detected.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Power is routed through an AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 9-speed automatic transmission featuring rear-wheel drive or 4MATIC+ all-wheel drive configurations. This gearbox provides lightning-fast shifts under aggressive driving while maintaining smooth highway cruising refinement.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Glass

Exterior glass panels providing visibility and cabin weather protection.

**Spontaneous Glass Cracking or Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Windshield or panoramic sunroof glass may crack rapidly from minor impacts or shatter unexpectedly while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect all glass surfaces for existing chips or stress fractures and replace damaged windshields or sunroof assemblies with OEM glass.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG is an extraordinary performance flagship that combines blistering V8 speed with world-class luxury appointments. While isolated owner complaints point to potential fuel pump and glass durability concerns, major mechanical failures remain uncommon in available data. Prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an experienced Mercedes-Benz specialist to verify overall vehicle condition.
