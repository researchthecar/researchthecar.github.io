# 2010 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG

### Overview
The 2010 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG, a high-performance luxury sedan, offers a potent blend of power and refinement. Powered by a formidable 6.2-liter V8 engine, it delivers exhilarating acceleration and a commanding road presence. While generally well-regarded for its performance and luxury appointments, like any complex, high-performance vehicle, it can experience specific issues. Owners should be aware of potential concerns primarily related to its advanced powertrain and sophisticated electronic systems. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any developing problems are crucial for maintaining the S63 AMG's exceptional driving experience and long-term reliability.

---


## Engine

### 6.2L V8 (M156)
**Camshaft Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, misfires, check engine light, reduced power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of camshafts and associated components.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $4,000

**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Oil Cooler)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil drips, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or oil cooler seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift MCT
**Harsh Shifting/Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, delayed engagement, slipping between gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter service, potential valve body or torque converter repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $3,500


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Leaks/Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, suspension warning lights, bouncy ride, loss of hydraulic fluid.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic lines, struts, or ABC pump.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $5,000+


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, audio issues, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, module reset, or replacement of COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000


### Sensors (Parking/Ambient)
**Malfunctioning Sensors**
- **Symptoms:** False warnings from parking assist, incorrect climate control operation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty sensor(s).
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2010 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG is a powerful and luxurious vehicle, but its complexity means potential owners should be prepared for higher maintenance costs and specific repair needs. The engine's performance can come with risks like camshaft wear, and the advanced ABC suspension system is prone to hydraulic issues. Transmission problems, though not universal, can also be costly to address. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the COMAND system, are not uncommon. While the exterior and interior are generally robust, the powertrain and electronic systems require diligent attention and a significant budget for upkeep. Overall, it's a car that rewards passionate drivers but demands a commitment to its specialized care.
