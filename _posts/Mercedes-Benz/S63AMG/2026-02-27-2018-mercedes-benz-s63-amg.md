# 2018 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG represents the pinnacle of luxury performance sedan engineering, delivering breathtaking power from its handcrafted biturbo V8 engine. While these flagship luxury vehicles are generally built to high standards, prospective buyers should review known owner complaints concerning occasional powertrain hiccups, electrical items, and braking concerns. Careful pre-purchase inspection can help uncover latent issues before committing to a high-end luxury purchase.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8 Engine

The 4.0-liter handcrafted biturbo V8 engine pushes roughly 603 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, offering immense performance. Proper maintenance history and timely fluid changes are critical to keeping this complex powerplant running reliably.

**Engine Misfire and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional engine misfires during acceleration accompanied by shaking, loss of power, and an illuminated check engine light.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning, ignition coil or spark plug replacement, or addressing compression anomalies as needed.

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Presence of engine oil accumulation near the rear main seal or other gasket locations.
- **Solution:** Inspect sealing surfaces and replace worn gaskets or seals as required.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a responsive multi-speed automatic transmission designed to handle high torque output seamlessly. Regular fluid maintenance ensures smooth power delivery and longevity.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The gear shifter occasionally shifts into neutral independently while driving, requiring manual input to reset.
- **Solution:** Inspect electronic gear selector modules, software calibration, and transmission control units.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

High-performance braking systems provide strong stopping power but require attentive servicing of pads and rotors to maintain optimal feel.

**Brake Response Delay**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where depressing the brake pedal failed to adequately slow or stop the vehicle as expected.
- **Solution:** Inspect hydraulic brake lines, master cylinder operation, and brake booster components.

**Rotor Vibration and Dust Retention**
- **Symptoms:** Brake dust accumulation on the interior side of front rotors leading to surface coarseness and steering vibrations.
- **Solution:** Inspect, clean, or resurface/replace brake rotors and pads.

## Steering

### Electronic Steering System

The power-assisted steering system offers effortless maneuverability at low speeds and precise weight at high speeds, though electronic assist faults can occasionally arise.

**Stiff Steering and Warning Messages**
- **Symptoms:** Steering wheel becomes extremely difficult to turn accompanied by a dashboard warning message to increase physical effort.
- **Solution:** Diagnose power steering control modules, wiring connections, and steering rack actuators.

## Interior

### Dashboard Trim and Materials

The opulent cabin features premium leather and advanced trim finishes that require care to prevent environmental wear and degradation over time.

**Dashboard Leather Peeling**
- **Symptoms:** The leather covering on the dashboard begins to pull away or peel up on the passenger side.
- **Solution:** Re-adhere or replace affected dashboard leather trim panels through a specialized upholstery shop.
