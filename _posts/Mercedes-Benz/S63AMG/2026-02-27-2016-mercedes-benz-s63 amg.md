# 2016 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG, a high-performance luxury sedan, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting its premium engineering and build quality. While owners can expect a sophisticated driving experience, like any complex vehicle, certain areas may require attention over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for owners and prospective buyers. The S63 AMG is equipped with a potent V8 engine and advanced drivetrain, which are typically robust but can be sources of specific, albeit infrequent, technical challenges. Attention to these areas can help maintain the vehicle's peak performance and longevity.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8 (M157)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, check engine light, whistling noises from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty wastegate actuator or the complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Oil Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Oil in coolant reservoir, coolant loss, potential overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the engine oil cooler and potentially flushing the cooling system.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, rough shifting, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Rebuilding or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,000

**Mechatronics Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the transmission mechatronics unit.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000


## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air struts.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,800 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant compressor operation, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, audio issues, navigation problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates, or in severe cases, replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to find the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG is a highly capable luxury performance sedan with a generally good reliability record for its class. The primary areas that may require attention are the advanced AIRMATIC air suspension system, particularly air spring leaks and compressor issues, and potential electronic glitches within the COMAND infotainment system. The powerful 5.5L twin-turbo V8 engine and its associated transmission are robust, but specific components like wastegate actuators and torque converters can occasionally present issues. Owners should be prepared for the higher cost of repairs associated with high-performance luxury vehicles. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs are crucial for maintaining the S63 AMG's optimal performance and longevity.
