**Introduction:**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG is a high-performance luxury sedan that combines a powerful engine with a luxurious interior and advanced technology. It is powered by a twin-turbocharged 5.5-liter V8 engine that produces 577 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 7-speed SPEEDSHIFT MCT automatic transmission. The S63 AMG is praised for its exhilarating performance, opulent cabin, and advanced driver assistance systems. However, it also has its fair share of potential problems.

**Engine:**

**5.5L V8 Biturbo**

- **Turbocharger Failure:**
  - Symptoms: Loss of power, reduced acceleration, increased engine noise
  - Solution: Turbocharger replacement
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Intake Manifold Runner Issues:**
  - Symptoms: Rough idling, stalling, reduced engine power
  - Solution: Intake manifold replacement
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**7-Speed SPEEDSHIFT MCT**

- **Clutch Pack Failure:**
  - Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping during gear changes, loss of power
  - Solution: Clutch pack replacement
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

- **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
  - Symptoms: Suspension sagging, uneven ride height
  - Solution: Compressor replacement
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Strut Mount Failure:**
  - Symptoms: Excessive noise, vibration, or rattling over bumps
  - Solution: Strut mount replacement
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per strut
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

- **Battery Drain:**
  - Symptoms: Difficulty starting, dead battery
  - Solution: Battery replacement, electrical system inspection
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Electrical Module Malfunction:**
  - Symptoms: Intermittent or malfunctioning electrical components (e.g., lights, windows, seats)
  - Solution: Module replacement
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

No Issues Found

**Interior:**

- **Infotainment System Issues:**
  - Symptoms: Freezing, crashing, or unresponsive touchscreen, loss of audio
  - Solution: Software update, hardware replacement
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Climate Control Malfunction:**
  - Symptoms: Inconsistent temperature, excessive noise, failure to regulate temperature
  - Solution: Climate control unit replacement
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG is a highly capable and luxurious sedan, but it is not without its potential issues. Engine problems, specifically turbocharger and intake manifold issues, can be costly to repair. Transmission clutch pack failures can also lead to expensive repairs. The car's complex electrical system and advanced interior features can also experience problems that can be difficult to diagnose and fix. Suspension issues and exterior problems are relatively minor compared to the potential engine, transmission, electrical, and interior concerns. Overall, the S63 AMG is a high-performance car that requires proper maintenance and care to avoid costly repairs.
