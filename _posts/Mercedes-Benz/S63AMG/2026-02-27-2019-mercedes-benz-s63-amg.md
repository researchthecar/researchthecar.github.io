# 2019 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG represents the pinnacle of luxury, performance, and high-tech engineering. As a high-end flagship sedan, buyers looking at the used market should carefully evaluate potential mechanical, electrical, and driver-assistance complexities, as owner reports to safety regulators highlight occasional isolated glitches.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Bi-Turbo V8

This hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces an impressive 603 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering performance. While generally robust when properly maintained, prospective owners should budget for premium upkeep associated with high-performance AMG powerplants.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed AMG Speedshift MCT Automatic

The 9-speed multi-clutch automatic transmission routes power smoothly and rapidly to the rear or all four wheels depending on configuration. It is designed to handle high torque loads, though proper fluid service intervals are critical for long-term reliability.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

High-performance braking systems provide immense stopping power but require diligent inspection of actuators and pads.

**Rear Brake Actuator Seizure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances where the rear brake actuator unexpectedly seized, rendering the braking system temporarily inoperable upon vehicle startup.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle towed to a qualified facility or dealership for replacement of the failed brake actuator.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Column Shifter

Power-adjustable steering columns add convenience, though electrical adjustment mechanisms can occasionally experience positioning faults.

**Steering Column Adjustment Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** The motorized up-and-down adjustment mechanism for the steering column may stick in the fully raised position.
- **Solution:** Consult a technician to inspect the steering column adjustment motor and track mechanism.

## Interior

### Keyless Entry and Cabin Electronics

Advanced cabin electronics and comfort features provide incredible luxury, though complex software architectures can occasionally produce unpredictable behavior.

**Unexpected Door Locking**
- **Symptoms:** The keyless entry system may inadvertently lock the doors from the inside while the key fob remains inside the vehicle cabin.
- **Solution:** Ensure spare keys are kept accessible and have the vehicle's keyless entry module scanned for diagnostic software updates.

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG is an extraordinary luxury performance sedan offering world-class comfort and staggering power.
