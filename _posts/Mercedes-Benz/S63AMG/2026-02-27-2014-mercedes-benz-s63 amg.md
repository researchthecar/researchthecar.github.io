# 2014 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG

### Overview
The 2014 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG, a high-performance luxury sedan, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, especially considering its complex engineering and powerful twin-turbocharged V8 engine. Owners typically praise its robust build quality and advanced technology. However, as with any high-performance vehicle of this caliber, certain components can be prone to issues, particularly those related to the advanced powertrain and sophisticated electronic systems. Maintenance costs are expected to be higher than average due to the specialized nature of the AMG components and the luxury vehicle segment it occupies. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related issues.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (M157)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, boost leaks, check engine light illumination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the wastegate actuator or the complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per actuator

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, reduced engine performance, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300 per coil

**Fuel Injector Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, misfires, poor fuel economy, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per injector


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift MCT
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, harsh shifting, shuddering during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Rebuilding or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Mechatronics Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronics unit.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $4000


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC) Suspension
**Hydraulic Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, suspension warning lights, stiff ride.
- **Solution:** Repair of leaking lines, seals, or replacement of ABC pump/struts.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $3000 per component

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of hydraulic pressure, vehicle sagging, suspension warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Screen Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Flickering screen, unresponsive touch controls, blank display.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500


### Parking Sensor Malfunctions
**False Alerts or Non-Functionality**
- **Symptoms:** Audible warnings when no obstacle is present, or no warnings when an obstacle is near.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty parking sensors or wiring repair.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500 per sensor

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG offers a compelling blend of luxury and performance. While its core powertrain and chassis are generally robust, potential owners should be aware of the higher likelihood of issues with its complex Active Body Control suspension system and advanced electronic components, particularly the COMAND infotainment. The twin-turbo V8 engine, while powerful, can experience issues with wastegate actuators, ignition coils, and fuel injectors. Transmission issues, though less common, can be costly to repair. Regular, specialized maintenance is crucial for mitigating these potential problems and ensuring the longevity of this high-performance luxury vehicle.
