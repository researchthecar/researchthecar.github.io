# 2021 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a flagship luxury sedan, evaluating potential trouble spots helps ensure a smoother ownership experience. This reliability overview highlights recurring owner-reported concerns drawn from available vehicle feedback, offering practical insights into powertrain options and known system quirks.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8 Hybrid

A potent 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine paired with hybrid assist, producing substantial horsepower and torque for effortless performance. Buyers should verify maintenance records and watch for complex electronic or valvetrain service requirements.

**Camtronic Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle loses acceleration capability and illuminates the check engine light.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified dealership or mechanic inspect the valvetrain components and replace the failed actuator as needed.
- **Cost:** $9000

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A smooth-shifting 9-speed automatic transmission designed to manage high torque outputs while maintaining highway efficiency. Regular fluid changes are vital to preserving long-term shift quality.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

The large alloy wheels and low-profile performance tires offer sharp handling but are prone to impact damage on rough road surfaces.

**Wheel Cracks and Premature Tire Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Low tire pressure warnings, air leaks, visible sidewall bubbling, and structural cracks on large factory alloy rims.
- **Solution:** Inspect large-diameter alloy wheels for structural damage during routine tire rotations and replace compromised tires or wheels promptly.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting and Cameras

Advanced driver aids and multi-beam lighting systems rely heavily on sensitive onboard sensors and cameras that can occasionally register false error codes.

**Adaptive Headlight and Camera Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic high-beam operation or dashboard warning messages indicating that camera systems are dirty or currently unavailable.
- **Solution:** Clean sensor areas around the windshield and have optical or lighting control modules diagnosed by a qualified technician.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Active safety suites provide critical driver protection, though sensor calibration issues can occasionally lead to unexpected system interventions.

**Inadvertent Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle unexpectedly triggers the forward collision warning and applies harsh braking force while traveling at cruising speeds.
- **Solution:** Ensure camera and radar lenses are unobstructed, and check with a dealer for any applicable software updates or sensor recalibrations.

Overall, this flagship luxury performance sedan delivers exceptional comfort and power, but prospective buyers should carefully inspect complex electronic systems, driver-assistance sensors, and low-profile wheel setups. Conducting a pre-purchase inspection with a marque specialist will help uncover potential hidden repair costs before finalizing your purchase.
