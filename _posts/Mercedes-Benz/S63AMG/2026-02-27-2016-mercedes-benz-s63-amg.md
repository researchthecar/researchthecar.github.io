# 2016 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG delivers breathtaking luxury and high-performance engineering, but prospective buyers should approach with a clear understanding of its complexity. While owners praise its exceptional power and interior refinement, owner feedback highlights occasional mechanical and electrical vulnerabilities common to high-end European flagships. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by an experienced specialist is essential before bringing one home.

---

## Engine

### 5.5L Biturbo V8 Engine

Equipped with a hand-built 5.5-liter twin-turbocharged V8 producing approximately 577 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, this engine offers immense acceleration and sophisticated performance. Proper maintenance and timely servicing are critical to prevent costly longevity issues.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift Automatic Transmission

Managed by a 7-speed AMG Speedshift multi-clutch automatic transmission paired with 4MATIC all-wheel drive, it delivers rapid gear changes and confident power delivery. Smooth operation depends heavily on consistent fluid changes at recommended intervals.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

The heavy performance setup places significant stress on low-profile tires and large alloy wheels, making them susceptible to damage from road imperfections.

**Factory Wheel Cracks and Tire Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced unexpected tire blowouts or discovered cracked factory alloy wheels during routine service visits.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels for structural fractures and ensure tires are properly inflated; replace damaged rims or compromised low-profile tires promptly.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The power steering system provides precise handling for a large sedan, though rare electronic or mechanical faults can occasionally cause temporary loss of assist.

**Power Steering Interruption**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported warning lights illuminating on the dash followed by a sudden loss or locking of power steering assistance while driving.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection of the steering assembly, electronic modules, and wiring harness to identify and replace failing components.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Glass Roof

The large panoramic roof enhances cabin light but can occasionally experience stress-related thermal or structural cracking.

**Spontaneous Glass Fractures**
- **Symptoms:** A loud bang can be heard while driving or parked, followed by a visible stress crack or shattering of the panoramic sunroof glass.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the surrounding frame for alignment or body torque issues.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Restraint system components generally operate dependably, though occasional mechanical failures in buckles and retractors have been documented.

**Seat Belt Buckle and Retractor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Occupants may find that the seat belt fails to latch securely into the receiver buckle, or rear belts may become stuck and fail to retract.
- **Solution:** Have the affected seat belt assembly or buckle mechanism inspected and replaced by a qualified service center.
