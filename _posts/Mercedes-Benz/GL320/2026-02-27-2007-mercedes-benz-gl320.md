# 2007 Mercedes-Benz GL320

### Overview
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz GL320 is a luxury SUV that offers a comfortable ride and robust performance, particularly with its diesel engine options. While generally well-regarded for its build quality and features, like any vehicle of this age, it has specific areas that have seen more frequent attention from owners. This analysis focuses on common mechanical and electrical issues reported by owners, excluding routine maintenance and normal wear and tear. Understanding these potential problem areas can help prospective buyers and current owners budget for and address necessary repairs, ensuring the continued reliability and enjoyment of this capable vehicle.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L OM642 Turbo Diesel
**Intake Manifold Runner Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, rough idle, loss of power, rattling noise from intake area.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold assembly or individual runner levers.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200

**EGR System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, rough idle, black smoke, reduced engine performance.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve and cooler.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

**Turbocharger Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noise, excessive smoke, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


## Transmission

### 722.9 7G-Tronic Automatic
**Valve Body Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, slipping gears, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the transmission valve body.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping, rough engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Suspension

### AirMatic Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or seals.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle not raising, constant compressor noise, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1100

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz GL320, particularly with the 3.0L diesel engine, presents a mixed reliability profile. While the core powertrain is generally robust, specific components like the intake manifold runners, EGR system, and turbocharger are known to require attention over time. The optional AirMatic suspension can also be a source of costly repairs due to leaks or compressor failures. Electrical gremlins, often related to sensors, can also arise. Despite these potential issues, many owners find the GL320 to be a comfortable and capable luxury SUV when properly maintained. Prospective buyers should be aware of these common problems and factor in potential repair costs, especially for the diesel engine and air suspension systems.
