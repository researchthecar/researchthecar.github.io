**Introduction**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE43 AMG is a mid-size luxury SUV powered by a 3.0L V6 biturbo engine paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The engine produces 362 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque. The GLE43 AMG is known for its impressive performance and luxurious interior, but it has also been plagued by several issues.

## **Engine**
- **3.0L V6 Biturbo**
  - **Leaking Valve Cover Gaskets**
    - Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the valve cover gaskets, causing smoke and engine performance issues.
    - Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    - Recalls: None Found

## **Transmission**
- **9-Speed Automatic**
  - **Rough Shifting and Driveline Vibrations**
    - Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifts roughly, especially when cold, and the driveshaft vibrates at low speeds.
    - Solution: Software updates, replacing the driveshaft, or rebuilding the transmission.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $4,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

## **Suspension**
- **No Issues Found**

## **Electrical**
- **Battery Discharge**
    - Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, even when the vehicle is parked.
    - Solution: Replace the battery or alternator.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

## **Exterior**
- **Leaking Tail Lights**
    - Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the tail lights, causing condensation and electrical issues.
    - Solution: Replace the tail lights.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found

## **Interior**
- **Infotainment System Bugs and Lag**
    - Description/Symptoms: Infotainment system freezes, lags, or experiences other software glitches.
    - Solution: Software updates or replacing the infotainment unit.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+
    - Recalls: None Found

## **Summary**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE43 AMG is a popular luxury SUV with impressive performance and a luxurious interior. However, it has faced several issues, including leaking valve cover gaskets, rough shifting and driveline vibrations, battery discharge, leaking tail lights, and infotainment system bugs. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some being minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more serious and costly.
