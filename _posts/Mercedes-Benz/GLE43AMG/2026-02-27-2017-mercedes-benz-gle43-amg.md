# 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE43 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE43 AMG, buyers are generally drawn to its blend of luxury, utility, and sporty performance. However, evaluating its reliability requires a close look at owner-reported concerns ranging from electrical issues to mechanical and structural hiccups. While online complaints reflect specific owner experiences rather than guaranteed factory defects, they offer valuable insights into potential trouble spots to check during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 362 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration suited to the AMG badge. It generally offers robust performance, though regular maintenance and monitoring of cooling and fuel systems are critical for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with the AMG-tuned drivetrain, the 9-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and rapid gear changes. While overall operation is refined, prospective buyers should test-drive thoroughly to ensure crisp shifting and absence of hesitation.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake system feedback points to occasional unexpected movement while the hold feature is engaged and general stopping concerns.

**Brake Hold and Stopping Irregularities**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles equipped with the brake hold feature may occasionally creep forward unexpectedly from a complete stop, or drivers may experience stopping hesitation.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the brake booster, master cylinder, and brake software calibration.

## Steering

### Steering Assist and Power Steering

Issues related to electronic power steering assist can temporarily compromise vehicle maneuverability.

**Power Steering Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a sudden loss of electronic power steering accompanied by an instrument cluster warning message, rendering the steering wheel extremely stiff and difficult to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic scanning of the power steering control module and potential replacement of faulty electrical components or steering gear assemblies.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Lighting system concerns primarily involve nighttime visibility and headlamp adjustment mechanisms.

**Headlight Performance and Adjustment Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report inadequate illumination during nighttime and rainy driving conditions, alongside low-beam headlights failing to adjust properly.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight assemblies, bulbs, and automatic leveling sensors; replace degraded components or upgrade housings as needed.

### Sunroof and Glass

Structural exterior complaints focus on unexpected sunroof glass failures.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A sudden loud pop or explosion sound heard while driving, followed by the panoramic sunroof glass shattering and falling into the cabin.
- **Solution:** Inspect the panoramic glass assembly for stress cracks or manufacturing flaws and replace the shattered glass panel along with cleaning the interior debris.
