# 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE43 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE43 AMG delivers strong performance and luxury features, but used-car buyers should carefully inspect powertrain operation and electronics. While owner-reported complaints for this performance model are relatively limited, issues surrounding electrical glitches, intermittent backup camera failures, and transmission or shifting anomalies have been noted in driver feedback.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Bi-Turbo V6 Engine

The 3.0-liter bi-turbo V6 engine produces approximately 385 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration and sporty performance. Overall, this AMG-tuned powerplant is generally robust, though routine maintenance of cooling and accessory components is vital to prevent unexpected failures.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission is paired with AMG-enhanced all-wheel drive for smooth power delivery and quick gear changes. While generally responsive, prospective buyers should verify crisp engagement across all gears during a test drive.

**Shifter and Gear Engagement Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience difficulty shifting out of park or intermittent slipping into neutral while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the electronic shifter module, gear selector linkage, and transmission control software.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The high-performance braking system provides strong stopping power suited for an AMG vehicle, but heavy curb weight can accelerate component wear.

**Premature Brake Pad Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rear brake pads wearing down quickly to metal-on-metal without timely dashboard warning indicators.
- **Solution:** Manually inspect brake pad thickness and rotor condition during routine maintenance rather than relying solely on dashboard sensors.

## Steering

### Steering System

The responsive sport-tuned steering delivers precise handling characteristics, though proper alignment and suspension integrity are critical.

**Unexpected Wandering or Swerving**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may occasionally exhibit unexpected directional wandering or difficulty maintaining a straight path.
- **Solution:** Check wheel alignment, steering angle sensor calibration, and suspension component bushings for wear or play.

## Electrical

### Back Over Prevention

Active and passive safety systems offer advanced protection, though electronic display components require verification during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Backup Camera Display Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen fails to switch to the rearview camera display when shifting into reverse, showing a black screen instead.
- **Solution:** Inspect the camera wiring harness, software version, and display module connections; replacement of the camera unit may be necessary.

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE43 AMG is an appealing choice for buyers seeking luxury performance, but due diligence is essential. Be sure to thoroughly test all electronic features, verify transmission shift quality, and check brake wear items before finalizing a purchase.
