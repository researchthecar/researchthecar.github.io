# 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLE43 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLE43 AMG offers a blend of luxury and performance, but prospective buyers should review owner-reported issues carefully before purchasing. While it delivers strong performance from its AMG-tuned powertrain, common owner concerns involve exterior trim components, electrical systems, and safety features. Being informed about these potential trouble spots helps ensure a smoother ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces roughly 385 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for this performance luxury SUV. Overall, the engine is generally robust, though occasional electrical and sensor-related glitches have been noted by owners.

**Oil Pump Solenoid Code**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light accompanied by a P06DA00 diagnostic trouble code.
- **Solution:** Inspect the oil pump solenoid and associated wiring harness, replacing damaged components as needed.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to the 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system, balancing responsive gear changes with highway cruising efficiency. It maintains a solid reputation for durability when properly maintained.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Communication System

Cabin electronics generally function well, though intermittent communication and smartphone pairing glitches have been reported by some owners.

**Connectivity and Infotainment Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Failure of the SIM card or smartphone integration features like Apple CarPlay to establish a steady connection.
- **Solution:** Perform software updates or reset the infotainment and communication modules according to manufacturer guidelines.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof and Trim

Owner reports highlight vulnerabilities in the panoramic sunroof assembly and surrounding exterior bonding, which can deteriorate over time.

**Detaching Roof Trim and Glass Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Abnormal banging or whistling noises from the roof area while driving at highway speeds, followed by loose exterior trim pieces or glass shattering.
- **Solution:** Have the sunroof bonding adhesive, exterior seals, and rail system inspected by a qualified technician to prevent component detachment.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety restraint electronics can occasionally log faults in the front seat belt pretensioners, triggering dashboard warning lights.

**Seat Belt Pretensioner Fault**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated seat belt warning light on the dashboard despite occupants being securely buckled.
- **Solution:** Scan the vehicle's CAN bus system for hard fault codes and replace malfunctioning seat belt powered pretensioners.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLE43 AMG is a compelling luxury performance vehicle, but used car shoppers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to the condition of the panoramic sunroof bonding, check for any active engine diagnostic codes, and verify that all safety restraint systems and infotainment features operate correctly.
