**Introduction:**

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLE43 AMG is a mid-size luxury SUV powered by a turbocharged 3.0L V6 engine producing 385 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a 9-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The GLE43 AMG is praised for its powerful engine, spacious interior, and advanced technology features. However, there have been some reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**3.0L Turbo V6**

* **Valve Stem Seals Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Replace valve stem seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Hesitation and Jerking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, sudden jolts or jerks
    * Solution: Software updates, transmission fluid replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging on one or more corners
    * Solution: Inspect and replace faulty air suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+ per corner
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Keyless Entry Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Key fob not detected, unable to unlock or start the vehicle
    * Solution: Battery replacement, software updates
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Entertainment System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Display not functioning, no audio output
    * Solution: Replace entertainment system module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the sunroof area when driving
    * Solution: Adjust or replace sunroof weatherstripping
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Headliner Detachment**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fabric lining of the roof separating from the frame
    * Solution: Reattach or replace headliner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Other**

* **Brake Rotor Warping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Pulsation or vibration when braking
    * Solution: Replace brake rotors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+ per axle
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLE43 AMG has received positive reviews for its performance and features. However, there have been some issues reported with certain components, particularly the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. While some of these issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more costly and may affect the reliability of the vehicle. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and consider them when making a purchasing decision.
