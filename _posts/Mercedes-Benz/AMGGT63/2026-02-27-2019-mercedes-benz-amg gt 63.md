# 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT 63

### Overview
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT 63 is a high-performance grand tourer that blends luxury with exhilarating driving dynamics. As a flagship model from AMG, it is engineered with advanced technology and powerful V8 engines. While generally well-regarded for its performance and build quality, like any complex vehicle, it can experience specific issues. This report focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and enthusiasts.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (M177)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, whistling noise from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty wastegate actuator or complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Oil Pan)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level warnings.
- **Solution:** Replacement of gaskets or seals; tightening of fasteners.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Transmission

### 9-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT
**Harsh Shifting / Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, hesitation when shifting into drive or reverse, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Software update, transmission fluid flush and replacement, or internal transmission repair/rebuild.
- **Cost:** $800 - $3,500


## Suspension

### AMG RIDE CONTROL+ Suspension
**Air Suspension Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, 'Service Air Suspension' warning message.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs, lines, or compressor.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, glitches, or freezes during operation.
- **Solution:** Software update, system reset, or replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical components malfunctioning, battery warning light.
- **Solution:** Testing and replacement of the battery, diagnosis of parasitic draw.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT 63 is a highly capable performance vehicle with a generally strong reliability record for its class. The most commonly cited technical issues tend to revolve around the complex powertrain and advanced suspension systems, particularly wastegate actuator failures in the twin-turbo V8, occasional harshness in the 9-speed transmission, and potential leaks in the air suspension. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and battery drain, are also noted. While these issues can be costly to repair, they are not indicative of systemic failure across the board. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining the optimal performance and longevity of this luxury sports car.
