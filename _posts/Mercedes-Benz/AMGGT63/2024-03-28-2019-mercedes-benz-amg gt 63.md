**Introduction:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT 63 is a high-performance sports car that combines luxury and exhilarating driving dynamics. It is available with two engine options: a twin-turbocharged 4.0L V8 producing 577 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque, and a twin-turbocharged 4.0L V8 producing 630 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The AMG GT 63 is praised for its aggressive styling, powerful performance, and advanced technology, but it has also been met with some criticism for its stiff ride and high price.

**Engine**

**4.0L V8 (577 hp)**

* **Cylinder deactivation issues**
  * Description/Symptoms: The engine may experience misfires or rough idling due to problems with the cylinder deactivation system.
  * Solution: Recoding the ECU and updating software.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
  * Recalls: None Found

**4.0L V8 (630 hp)**

* **Turbocharger boost issues**
  * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle may experience reduced power and poor acceleration due to problems with the turbocharger boost system.
  * Solution: Replacing or adjusting the turbocharger actuators and wastegates.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift hesitation**
  * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may exhibit hesitation or jerkiness during gear changes.
  * Solution: Recoding the transmission control unit (TCU) or replacing the hydraulic control unit.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment system glitches**
  * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience crashes, glitches, or connectivity issues.
  * Solution: Updating the software or replacing the head unit.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Ventilated seat malfunctions**
  * Description/Symptoms: The ventilated seats may not function properly or make excessive noise.
  * Solution: Replacing the seat ventilation system actuators.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT 63 is a powerful and technologically advanced sports car, but it has been met with some issues in the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. These issues can range from minor inconveniences to more significant problems that require expensive repairs. It is important to consider these potential issues when making a decision about purchasing or owning this vehicle.
