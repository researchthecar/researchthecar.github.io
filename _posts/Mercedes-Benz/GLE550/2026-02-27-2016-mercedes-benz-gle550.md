# 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE550 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE-Class offers a luxurious cabin, advanced technology, and robust powertrain options. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported issues involving fuel system failures, power steering malfunctions, and unexpected electronic glitches.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (GLE400 / Plug-in Hybrid)

A smooth and efficient 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine producing approximately 329 horsepower, offering a balanced blend of performance and fuel economy for everyday driving.

### 3.5L V6 (GLE350)

A naturally aspirated 3.5-liter V6 engine delivering around 302 horsepower, serving as the standard reliable powerplant for base models with widespread availability.

**Fuel Injector Failure and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle stalls unexpectedly, loses motive power, or experiences gasoline leaks from cracked fuel injectors.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines and injectors for damage or leaks; replace faulty injectors and repair fuel delivery components as needed.

### 4.7L Twin-Turbo V8 (GLE550)

A powerful 4.7-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine generating approximately 429 horsepower, providing robust towing capacity and effortless high-speed acceleration.

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8 (AMG GLE 63)

A high-performance hand-built 5.5-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine outputting up to 577 horsepower, designed for drivers seeking exceptional sports-car performance in an SUV package.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

A responsive 7-speed automatic transmission paired with select V6 variants, known for smooth gear changes and reliable long-term highway operation.

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A modern 9-speed automatic transmission designed to optimize fuel efficiency and smoothness, standard across most mainstream GLE trims.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle unexpectedly shifts into neutral or displays warnings indicating the car is not in park while driving.
- **Solution:** Have the transmission control module diagnosed and updated or repaired by a qualified technician.

## Steering

### Power Steering Assist

Electronic power steering system providing speed-sensitive steering feedback and maneuverability.

**Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering assistance, making the steering wheel extremely heavy and difficult to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Inspect power steering components, electronic control modules, and associated wiring harnesses for faults.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Exterior lighting systems including advanced LED headlights and tail assemblies.

**Headlight Dimness and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights provide insufficient illumination during night driving, creating poor visibility conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight assemblies, bulbs, and electrical connections; replace worn or degraded lighting components.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE-Class delivers a refined driving experience and plenty of luxury features, but buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to potential fuel system concerns, steering assist functionality, and transmission electronic operation to avoid unexpected repair bills.
