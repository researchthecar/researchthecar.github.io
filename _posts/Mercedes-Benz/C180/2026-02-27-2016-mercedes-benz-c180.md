# 2016 Mercedes-Benz C180 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a 2016 Mercedes-Benz C-Class, buyers find a stylish and feature-rich luxury sedan. While it offers a comfortable ride and upscale interior, used-car shoppers should carefully review maintenance records and inspect common problem areas like the powertrain, electrical systems, and body hardware before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M274)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine delivers around 241 horsepower, balancing punchy performance with everyday efficiency. Buyers should watch for fuel system odors and crankcase ventilation faults during inspection.

**Fuel Leak or Odor**
- **Symptoms:** A strong smell of gasoline around the vehicle or visible fuel weeping near engine lines.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines, pumps, and injectors, and replace any compromised components.

**Check Engine Light and Crankcase Ventilation**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light often accompanied by error codes related to the positive crankcase ventilation valve.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and replace faulty crankcase ventilation valves or related hoses.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission (7G-Tronic Plus)

The 7-speed automatic transmission provides smooth gear changes across various driving modes. Routine fluid maintenance is key to ensuring long-term durability.

## Steering

### Electronic Power Steering Rack

The electronic power steering system provides responsive handling, though complete rack failures can occur unexpectedly.

**Steering Rack Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Complete loss of power steering assistance or abnormal noises when turning the wheel.
- **Solution:** Replace the electronic rack and pinion assembly.
- **Cost:** $5,840.14

## Electrical

### Electrical System and Lighting

Complex onboard electronics power everything from lighting to engine management, but electrical issues can be expensive to resolve.

**Sudden Engine Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle stalls or shuts down unexpectedly while driving in traffic without prior warning.
- **Solution:** Inspect electrical harness, fuel delivery, and engine management modules.

**Headlight Assembly Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Warning message indicating a failed headlight, requiring full LED assembly replacement.
- **Solution:** Replace the entire headlight assembly unit.
- **Cost:** $3,500

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

The panoramic sunroof adds an open-air feel to the cabin but is prone to mechanical binding and rare glass fracture incidents.

**Sunroof Binding and Glass Shivering**
- **Symptoms:** Sunroof binds, fails to close evenly, or shatters unexpectedly while driving.
- **Solution:** Lubricate or repair track mechanisms, or replace glass and track assemblies as necessary.
