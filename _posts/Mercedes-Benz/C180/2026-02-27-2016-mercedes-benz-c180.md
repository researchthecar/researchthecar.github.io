### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz C180 demonstrates strong overall reliability, with no major widespread mechanical failures reported for its engine or transmission options. The primary areas of concern tend to be electrical, specifically occasional infotainment system glitches and, less commonly, intermittent battery drain issues. These are often resolvable with software updates or diagnosis of parasitic draws. Exterior and interior components generally hold up well, reflecting the premium build quality expected from Mercedes-Benz. Routine maintenance is crucial for longevity, but beyond that, owners can expect a dependable driving experience with a low probability of significant mechanical issues.
