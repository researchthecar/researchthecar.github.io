**Introduction**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz C180 is a compact luxury sedan that offers a blend of style, performance, and comfort. It comes with a 1.6L inline-4 turbocharged engine that produces 156 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The C180 is praised for its impressive fuel efficiency, responsive handling, and luxurious interior. However, it has also been reported to have certain issues affecting various components.

**Engine**

* **1.6L Inline-4 Turbocharged Engine**

**Issue: Excessive Oil Consumption**
Description/Symptoms: The engine consumes excessive amounts of oil, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
Solution: Replace the defective piston rings or oil pump.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Issue: Timing Chain Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The timing chain, which synchronizes the movement of the camshaft and crankshaft, prematurely fails, resulting in incorrect valve timing and engine damage.
Solution: Replace the timing chain, tensioner, and related components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Issue: Rough Shifting**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission experiences rough or delayed shifting, particularly during gear changes.
Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Issue: Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery discharges prematurely due to a faulty component or electrical leak.
Solution: Inspect and replace the defective component or repair the electrical leak.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Issue: Headlight Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The headlights experience flickering, dimming, or failure due to a faulty ballast or wiring harness.
Solution: Replace the ballast or wiring harness.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Issue: Sunroof Rattling**
Description/Symptoms: The panoramic sunroof rattles while driving, causing excessive noise and vibration inside the cabin.
Solution: Adjust the sunroof alignment or replace the sunroof seals.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
Recalls: None Found

**Issue: Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The climate control system fails to regulate the cabin temperature effectively, resulting in discomfort for passengers.
Solution: Repair or replace the faulty climate control module or sensors.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz C180 has been reported to have some issues, including excessive oil consumption, timing chain failure, rough shifting, battery drain, and headlight malfunction. While these issues can be inconvenient and costly to repair, they are not widespread and do not affect all C180 models. The severity and cost of the repairs vary depending on the specific issue and the extent of the damage. Overall, the C180 remains a reliable and enjoyable vehicle for most owners.
