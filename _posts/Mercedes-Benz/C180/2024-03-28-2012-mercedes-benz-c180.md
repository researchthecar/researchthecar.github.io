**2012 Mercedes-Benz C180**

The 2012 Mercedes-Benz C180 is a luxury compact sedan powered by a 1.8L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. It offers a comfortable and stylish interior, numerous safety features, and responsive handling. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of reported issues.

**Engine**

* **1.8L M271 Turbocharged 4-Cylinder**

**Fuel Injector Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, engine misfires, rough idle, reduced power, illuminated check engine light
Solution: Replace faulty fuel injectors
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Intake Manifold Leakage**
Description/Symptoms: Vacuum leaks, engine running rough, loss of power, illuminated check engine light
Solution: Replace the intake manifold
Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed 7G-Tronic Automatic**

**Transmission Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, delayed shifts, shuddering or jerking
Solution: Replace or repair transmission components (e.g., clutches, valve body)
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim headlights, electrical accessories not functioning, illuminated battery light
Solution: Replace or repair the alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Squeaking**
Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noises from the dashboard while driving
Solution: Apply lubricant to dashboard components
Estimated Cost to Fix: DIY if possible, otherwise $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

* **Window Regulator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Inability to open or close windows, loud noises while operating windows
Solution: Replace the window regulator motor or mechanism
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Summary**

The 2012 Mercedes-Benz C180 has a few reported issues, primarily related to the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity of the issues varies, with some being minor annoyances and others requiring significant repairs. The estimated costs of repair are generally moderate to high, as expected for a luxury vehicle. It's important to note that not all vehicles will experience these issues, and proper maintenance can help extend the vehicle's lifespan and prevent potential problems.
