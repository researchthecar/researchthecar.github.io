**Introduction:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz C180 is a compact luxury sedan powered by a 1.6-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. It produces 156 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. The C180 is appreciated for its refined interior, impressive fuel economy, and responsive handling. However, it has received criticism for its limited rear legroom and somewhat firm ride quality.

**Engine**

**1.6L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent hesitation, stalling, or rough idling due to inadequate fuel delivery.
    * Solution: Replace faulty fuel injector(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Hesitation or Delay:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission may hesitate or delay when shifting gears, especially during acceleration.
    * Solution: Update transmission software or replace solenoids within the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Shock Absorber Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Visible leaks from the front shock absorbers, resulting in reduced damping and a rougher ride.
    * Solution: Replace leaking shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature battery discharge due to a faulty alternator or parasitic drain.
    * Solution: Replace alternator or diagnose and repair electrical system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical Harness Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions such as flickering lights, non-functioning accessories, or intermittent starting problems due to faulty wiring.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace damaged electrical harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fogging or condensation inside headlights, reducing visibility.
    * Solution: Reseal or replace headlight assemblies.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Trim Panel Discoloration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Black trim panels on the exterior may fade or discolor over time, especially in sunny climates.
    * Solution: Replace discolored trim panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noises from the sunroof during driving, especially when open.
    * Solution: Adjust or replace sunroof components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Navigation System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system may malfunction, freeze, or provide inaccurate directions.
    * Solution: Update navigation software or replace navigation unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2017 Mercedes-Benz C180 is a well-rounded luxury sedan with a few common issues. Most problems are minor and can be addressed with relatively affordable repairs. However, some more significant electrical or transmission issues may come with more substantial costs.
