**Introduction**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz C180 is a luxury compact sedan powered by a 1.6-liter turbocharged inline-four engine producing 156 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. Other engine and transmission options include:

* 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine producing 184 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission
* 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine producing 241 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission

Owners appreciate the C180's comfortable ride, spacious cabin, and advanced technology. However, it has also received some criticism for its high price and limited rear legroom.

**Engine**

**1.6-liter Turbocharged Inline-Four Engine**

**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, rattling noises, smoke from the engine bay
Solution: Replace turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Inline-Four Engine (184 hp)**

**Fuel Injector Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, rough idling, reduced engine performance
Solution: Replace fuel injectors
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Inline-Four Engine (241 hp)**

**No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Transmission Shudder**
Description/Symptoms: Vibration felt during acceleration or deceleration
Solution: Reprogram transmission control module or replace transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery dies after prolonged parking
Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system, replace battery if necessary
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: Recall #16V779, Recall #18V726

**Exterior**

**Headlight Condensation**
Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside headlight assembly
Solution: Replace headlight assembly
Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000 per headlight
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Sunroof Rattle**
Description/Symptoms: Noise from sunroof when closed
Solution: Adjust or replace sunroof components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz C180 has a few reported issues that can be costly to repair, such as turbocharger failure and transmission shudder. However, many of the issues are relatively minor and can be fixed for less than $1,000. Overall, the vehicle receives positive reviews for its comfort, technology, and performance.
