# 2022 Mercedes-Benz SL55 AMG

### Overview
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz SL55 AMG, a high-performance roadster, generally exhibits strong build quality and engineering, reflecting its premium segment positioning. As a first-year model for this generation, some minor teething issues are not uncommon in automotive launches. However, widespread critical failures have not been extensively documented. Owners can expect a robust driving experience, with the primary focus on performance and luxury. Reliability data suggests that while individual component issues can arise, they are not indicative of systemic problems across the model range. Regular maintenance and adherence to service schedules are crucial for optimal long-term performance and durability.

## Suspension

### Active Suspension System (ABC)
**Minor sensor calibration drift**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional dashboard warning lights related to suspension height or stiffness, potentially leading to slightly uneven ride quality.
- **Solution:** Recalibration of suspension sensors by a qualified technician.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (MBUX)
**Software glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional unresponsiveness of touchscreen, freezing of the system, or minor connectivity issues with Apple CarPlay/Android Auto.
- **Solution:** Software updates and system resets. In rare cases, a full system reinstallation may be required.
- **Cost:** $0 - $200


### Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems (ADAS)
**Sensor sensitivity adjustments**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent false alerts from parking sensors or adaptive cruise control, particularly in adverse weather conditions.
- **Solution:** Cleaning of sensor housings and potential recalibration of sensor sensitivity by a dealership.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz SL55 AMG demonstrates a commendable level of reliability for a performance-oriented, first-generation model. While minor electrical glitches, particularly with the MBUX infotainment system, and occasional suspension sensor recalibrations have been noted, these are not indicative of major systemic flaws. The powertrain components, including the potent V8 engine and 9-speed automatic transmission, are performing robustly. Exterior and interior components generally meet Mercedes-Benz's high standards. With proactive maintenance and addressing minor issues promptly, owners can expect a dependable and exhilarating ownership experience.
