# 2007 Mercedes-Benz SL55 AMG

### Overview
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz SL55 AMG, a high-performance luxury roadster, is known for its potent supercharged V8 engine and sophisticated engineering. While generally well-built, like any complex performance vehicle, it can experience specific issues as it ages. This data focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related problems that owners might encounter, providing insights into potential repair areas and associated costs. The SL55 AMG combines exhilarating performance with a premium experience, but understanding its potential reliability quirks is crucial for prospective and current owners.

---


## Engine

### 5.4L Supercharged V8 (M113)
**Supercharger Clutch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping noise from the supercharger, reduced power, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the supercharger clutch assembly.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200

**Coolant Leaks (Intercooler System)**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant level, overheating, visible coolant residue around intercooler hoses and pump.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or intercooler pump.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

**Drive Belt Tensioner Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Squealing noise from the engine bay, potential for belt slippage or breakage.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the serpentine belt tensioner.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning light, limp mode.
- **Solution:** Replacement or repair of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Conductor Plate Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission errors.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the conductor plate.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1100


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**ABC Pump Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, fluid leaks under the car, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000

**ABC Strut/Cylinder Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle leaning to one side, suspension warning messages, bouncy ride.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking ABC struts or cylinders.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

## Exterior

### Retractable Hardtop Mechanism
**Hydraulic Leaks/Pump Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slow or failed operation of the roof, fluid leaks in the trunk area.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic lines, cylinders, or pump.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Roof not closing or opening completely, error messages.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty roof sensors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz SL55 AMG is a powerful and luxurious vehicle, but its complexity means potential for costly repairs, particularly in the Active Body Control (ABC) suspension system and the retractable hardtop mechanism. The supercharged V8 engine, while robust, can also present issues like supercharger clutch failure and coolant leaks. Transmission problems, often related to the conductor plate or TCM, are also noted. Electrical components, especially related to comfort features and lighting, can require attention. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are essential to mitigate these potential issues and maintain the vehicle's performance and value.
