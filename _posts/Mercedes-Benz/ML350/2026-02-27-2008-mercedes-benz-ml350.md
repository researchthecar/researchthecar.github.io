# 2008 Mercedes-Benz ML350

### Overview
The 2008 Mercedes-Benz ML350, part of the W164 generation, is a luxury mid-size SUV known for its comfort and performance. While generally well-regarded, like any vehicle of its age, it has specific areas that have shown a propensity for issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into common problems experienced by owners. Understanding these potential weak points can help prospective buyers and current owners make informed decisions regarding maintenance and potential repairs.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (M272)
**Intake Manifold Runner Flap Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, rough idle, loss of power, P2004/P2005/P2006 error codes.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold assembly or repair kit.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200

**Balance Shaft Failure (M272/M273)**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, rough idle, misfires, P0016/P0017 codes (crankshaft/camshaft position correlation).
- **Solution:** Replacement of the balance shaft and timing components. This is a labor-intensive repair.
- **Cost:** $2500 - $4500


## Transmission

### 7G-TRONIC (722.9)
**Valve Body Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, slipping gears, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the transmission valve body.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Torque Converter Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping, delayed acceleration.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500


## Suspension

### Standard Air Suspension (Optional)
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, compressor running excessively, 'Service Suspension' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air struts.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle not leveling, compressor not engaging, 'Service Suspension' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1500

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**SAM (Signal Acquisition Module) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical faults, malfunctioning lights, power windows, or other accessories.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential repair or replacement of the SAM unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Keyless Go System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, door handle sensors not working.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of the Keyless Go module or related sensors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2008 Mercedes-Benz ML350 offers a premium experience but comes with potential reliability concerns, particularly in its powertrain and optional air suspension. The M272 engine's balance shaft and intake manifold issues, along with the 7G-TRONIC transmission's valve body and torque converter problems, are significant repair considerations. Owners of vehicles with the optional air suspension should be aware of potential leaks and compressor failures. While the electrical system can have its quirks, especially with the SAM module, the exterior and interior generally hold up well. Regular and thorough maintenance, along with a pre-purchase inspection focusing on these known problem areas, is crucial for managing ownership costs and ensuring long-term reliability.
