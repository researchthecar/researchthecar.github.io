**Introduction:**

The 2009 Mercedes-Benz ML350 is a midsize luxury SUV known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and luxurious amenities. It is available with a 3.5L V6 engine and a 7-speed automatic transmission, producing 268 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. Drivers appreciate the ML350's refined cabin and responsive handling, while some dislike its high fuel consumption and expensive maintenance.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Intake Valve Deposits:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, decreased power
    * Solution: Clean or replace intake valves
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise on engine startup, reduced engine performance
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration felt while accelerating
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Transmission Control Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Erratic gear shifting, loss of gears
    * Solution: Replace transmission control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging suspension, reduced ride height
    * Solution: Replace air suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, battery discharge
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Frequent battery replacement, difficulty starting vehicle
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair cause of battery drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Fog Light Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fog lights fill with water condensation
    * Solution: Replace or repair fog light housing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Drain Clogs:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into cabin
    * Solution: Clean or replace sunroof drain tubes
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2009 Mercedes-Benz ML350 has several reported issues, with the most common being engine intake valve deposits, transmission torque converter shudder, and air suspension failure. While some issues are relatively minor, others can be costly to repair. It is important to factor in these potential expenses when considering purchasing an ML350.
