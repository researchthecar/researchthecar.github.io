# 2004 Mercedes-Benz C320

### Overview
The 2004 Mercedes-Benz C320, part of the W203 generation, is generally considered a solid vehicle, offering a blend of luxury, performance, and Mercedes-Benz's renowned build quality. It was available with a capable 3.2L V6 engine and a smooth automatic transmission. While many owners report high satisfaction, like any vehicle of this age, certain components can experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented common problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective buyers or current owners.

---


## Engine

### 3.2L V6 (M112)
**Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, potential misfires, rough idle.
- **Solution:** Replacement of intake manifold gaskets.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Oil Cooler Seals**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant in oil, oil in coolant, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of oil cooler seals.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**PCV System Clogging**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil leaks, potential engine sludge.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of PCV valve and associated hoses.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Conductor Plate Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, transmission slipping, 'limp mode', check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the conductor plate and transmission fluid service.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, failure to engage gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Front Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel.
- **Solution:** Replacement of front control arm bushings or complete control arms.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Sway Bar Links**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling or clunking noises, especially when turning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of sway bar links.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not working, window stuck in position.
- **Solution:** Replacement of window regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $250 - $450 per window

**Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensor Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, poor acceleration, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the MAF sensor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** One or both xenon headlights not illuminating or flickering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight ballast.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per side


## Exterior

### Body and Trim
**Rust on Wheel Arches and Rocker Panels**
- **Symptoms:** Visible rust or bubbling paint, particularly in areas with harsh winters.
- **Solution:** Rust repair and repainting.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800+

**Sunroof Drain Clogs**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, musty smell.
- **Solution:** Clearing of sunroof drain tubes.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**Pixelated Instrument Cluster Display**
- **Symptoms:** Faded or missing pixels on the odometer and other display segments.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** HVAC fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2004 Mercedes-Benz C320 (W203) is a well-built luxury sedan with a generally good reliability record for its age. The 3.2L V6 engine is robust, but common issues like intake manifold gasket leaks and oil cooler seal failures can occur. The 5-speed automatic transmission is generally reliable, though conductor plate issues can arise. Electrical gremlins, particularly with window regulators and instrument cluster displays, are not uncommon. Exterior rust can be a concern in certain climates. Overall, with diligent maintenance and addressing these known potential issues, the C320 can remain a comfortable and enjoyable vehicle.
