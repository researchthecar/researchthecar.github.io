**Introduction:**

The 2003 Mercedes-Benz C320 is a luxury sedan powered by a 3.2-liter V6 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. It produces 215 horsepower and 229 lb-ft of torque. The C320 offers a comfortable and refined ride, but has been known to experience some issues.

**Engine**

**3.2L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vacuum leaks, rough idle, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace affected components (e.g., valve seals, piston rings)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears under load
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear end of vehicle sits lower than front
    * Solution: Replace rear suspension springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Front Control Arm Bushings:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises over bumps
    * Solution: Replace bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Window Regulator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows do not roll up or down
    * Solution: Replace window regulator motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Central Locking Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors do not lock or unlock properly
    * Solution: Replace central locking module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Drain Clogs:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into cabin during rain
    * Solution: Clean or unclog sunroof drains
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Fade:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard surface becomes dull or discolored
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Belt Tensioner Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat belt does not retract properly
    * Solution: Replace seat belt tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $400
    * Recalls: 03V261000

**Summary:**

The 2003 Mercedes-Benz C320 is a reliable vehicle, but it has been known to experience some issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. The severity of the issues varies, but they can be costly to repair. It is important to factor these potential expenses into the overall cost of ownership when considering this vehicle.
