**Vehicle Introduction:**

The 2001 Mercedes-Benz C320 is a luxurious sedan powered by a 3.2L V6 engine and available with a 5-speed automatic transmission. Known for its elegant design, spacious interior, and performance-oriented driving dynamics, it offers 215 horsepower and 229 lb-ft of torque. While praised for its refined ride and comfortable interior, the vehicle has faced some reported issues over time.

**Engine**

* **M112 3.2L V6 Engine with 5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

    * **Head Gasket Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace head gasket, resurface cylinder head
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: N/A
    * **Intake Manifold Flap Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, rough idle, engine warning light
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

* **722.6 5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

    * **Transmission Slippage:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed gear engagement, loss of power
        * Solution: Repair or replace transmission components, software update
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise when driving over bumps, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after short periods of non-use
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system, replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Central Locking Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to lock or unlock doors, trunk, or windows
    * Solution: Replace central locking module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Rear Subframe:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on rear subframe, visible rust
    * Solution: Replace or repair affected subframe component
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of climate control system, inconsistent temperature
    * Solution: Repair or replace climate control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Door Lock Actuator Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors from inside or outside
    * Solution: Replace affected door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

The 2001 Mercedes-Benz C320 has received positive feedback for its performance and luxury features, but it has not been without some issues. The most common problems include head gasket failure, transmission slippage, suspension component wear, electrical faults, and interior malfunctions. While some issues can be relatively minor and affordable to fix, others can be more severe and costly. It's recommended for owners to stay informed about potential issues and have regular maintenance performed to prevent or mitigate problems.
