**Introduction:**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E500 is a luxury sedan powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.7-liter V8 engine paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. It produces 402 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. The E500 is known for its comfortable ride, refined interior, and ample power. However, it has been subject to several issues affecting various components.

**Engine**

**Twin-Turbocharged 4.7L V8**

**Turbocharger Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whining noise, smoke from the engine bay
Solution: Replace turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Camshaft Adjustment Actuator Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, poor acceleration, increased fuel consumption
Solution: Replace camshaft adjustment actuator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

**Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifts erratically, vehicle may fail to move
Solution: Replace mechatronic unit
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Air Suspension System Malfunction:**
Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sags on one side, uneven ride height, reduced handling
Solution: Repair or replace air suspension components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain:**
Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, difficulty starting the vehicle
Solution: Check for parasitic electrical draws, replace battery if necessary
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control System Malfunction:**
Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heater not working properly
Solution: Repair or replace climate control system components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Summary:**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E500 is a luxurious sedan with powerful performance. However, it has been affected by several issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. The severity of the issues can range from minor inconveniences to major expenses. Potential owners should be aware of these issues and consider the associated repair costs before purchasing or owning the vehicle.
