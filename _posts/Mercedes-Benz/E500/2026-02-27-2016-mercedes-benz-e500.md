# 2016 Mercedes-Benz E500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E-Class delivers a luxury driving experience defined by sophisticated engineering, comfortable ride quality, and refined performance. However, prospective buyers should approach used models with attention to potential maintenance costs and known component wear. While standard reliability remains typical for a high-end European luxury vehicle, certain electrical, steering, and body integrity issues have been reported by owners.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Biturbo (E400/E500 Spec)

This twin-turbocharged engine option offers robust horsepower and strong torque delivery for effortless highway cruising. Maintenance requires diligent adherence to fluid change intervals and close monitoring of auxiliary engine systems.

**Fuel Odor and Leak**
- **Symptoms:** A noticeable fuel odor is apparent while the vehicle is idling, raising concerns about fuel system integrity.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines, injectors, and rails for leaks and replace compromised seals or components.

### 4.6L V8 Biturbo

A high-output V8 engine providing smooth, authoritative acceleration and refined power delivery. Regular professional servicing is recommended to ensure long-term reliability of its complex turbocharging and cooling systems.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

A smooth-shifting automatic transmission designed to balance performance and fuel efficiency across various driving conditions. Routine fluid services are essential to maintain shift quality and prevent premature internal wear.

## Brakes

### Parking Brake

The braking system delivers confident stopping power, though mechanical wear in parking brake cables and surrounding linkage components can occur over time.

**Parking Brake Cable Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctions within the electronic parking brake system trigger warning lights or failure codes.
- **Solution:** Inspect the parking brake cables and related actuator components, replacing worn assemblies.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The electronic power steering setup provides precise handling at highway speeds and light effort during low-speed maneuvers, though occasional electrical assist failures have been documented.

**Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A power steering malfunction warning message appears on the dashboard accompanied by a sudden loss of power steering assist, making the vehicle difficult to steer.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the electric power steering assembly or associated control modules and replace failing components as necessary.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Glass Seals

Exterior body seals and glass installations generally maintain good weather protection, though aging adhesive seals can occasionally allow water intrusion.

**Windshield Seal Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Water drips form along the top of the front windshield interior during heavy rain, and the glass may exhibit slight movement when pushed.
- **Solution:** Remove and reseal or replace the windshield glass to restore proper cabin weatherproofing.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Comprehensive safety equipment provides robust occupant protection, though sensitive electronic sensors within seats and restraint systems require careful troubleshooting if warnings arise.

**Passenger Seat Sensor Fault**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger seat occupancy sensor fails, preventing the passenger-side airbag from activating and illuminating warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the seat sensor mat and replace faulty components or wiring harnesses.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E-Class remains a compelling choice for buyers seeking executive comfort, sophisticated styling, and strong performance. Prospective owners should carefully inspect prospective vehicles for service history records, paying particular attention to steering assist functionality, windshield weatherstripping integrity, and electrical sensor operation. Budgeting for potential premium maintenance costs will help ensure a satisfying ownership experience.
