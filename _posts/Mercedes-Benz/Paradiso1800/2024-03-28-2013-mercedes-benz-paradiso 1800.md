## 2013 Mercedes-Benz Paradiso 1800

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz Paradiso 1800 is a luxury coach bus with a powerful engine and a comfortable interior. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 12.8-liter OM 457 LA engine with 471 horsepower and 1,800 lb-ft of torque. The Paradiso 1800 is also available with a 12-speed Mercedes-Benz PowerShift automated manual transmission.

People who like the Paradiso 1800 praise its powerful engine, comfortable interior, and stylish exterior. However, some people have reported problems with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior.

### Engine

**12.8-liter OM 457 LA**

* **Engine Failure:** The engine may fail due to a faulty crankshaft. Symptoms include a loss of power, a knocking noise, and a check engine light. The engine will need to be replaced, which can cost $10,000 to $15,000.
* **Oil Leaks:** The engine may develop oil leaks due to a faulty oil pan gasket. Symptoms include a loss of oil, a burning smell, and a check engine light. The oil pan gasket will need to be replaced, which can cost $500 to $1,000.

### Transmission

**12-speed Mercedes-Benz PowerShift**

* **Transmission Slipping:** The transmission may slip gears due to a faulty solenoid. Symptoms include a loss of power, a grinding noise, and a check engine light. The transmission will need to be repaired or replaced, which can cost $2,000 to $5,000.
* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:** The transmission may develop fluid leaks due to a faulty seal. Symptoms include a loss of transmission fluid, a burning smell, and a check engine light. The seal will need to be replaced, which can cost $500 to $1,000.

### Suspension

* **Air Suspension Failure:** The air suspension system may fail due to a faulty air compressor. Symptoms include a loss of ride height, a rough ride, and a check engine light. The air compressor will need to be replaced, which can cost $2,000 to $3,000.
* **Suspension Noises:** The suspension may develop noises due to worn bushings or struts. Symptoms include a clunking noise, a rattling noise, or a squeaking noise. The bushings or struts will need to be replaced, which can cost $500 to $1,000.

### Electrical

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain due to a faulty alternator. Symptoms include a dead battery, a slow start, and a check engine light. The alternator will need to be replaced, which can cost $500 to $1,000.
* **Electrical Shorts:** The electrical system may develop short circuits due to faulty wiring. Symptoms include blown fuses, a loss of power, and a check engine light. The wiring will need to be repaired or replaced, which can cost $500 to $1,000.

### Exterior

* **Rust:** The body may develop rust due to a lack of proper protection. Symptoms include unsightly brown spots, bubbling paint, and holes in the body. The body will need to be repaired or replaced, which can cost $2,000 to $5,000.
* **Wind Noise:** The exterior may develop wind noise due to a faulty seal around the windows or doors. Symptoms include a loud whistling noise or a rattling noise. The seal will need to be replaced, which can cost $500 to $1,000.

### Interior

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system may malfunction due to a faulty sensor or actuator. Symptoms include a lack of heat or air conditioning, a noisy blower motor, or a check engine light. The sensor or actuator will need to be replaced, which can cost $500 to $1,000.
* **Seat Malfunction:** The seats may malfunction due to a faulty motor or switch. Symptoms include a seat that does not move, a seat that moves erratically, or a check engine light. The motor or switch will need to be replaced, which can cost $500 to $1,000.

### Summary

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz Paradiso 1800 is a luxury coach bus with a powerful engine and a comfortable interior. However, some people have reported problems with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior. The severity of these issues varies, but some of them can be expensive to repair.
