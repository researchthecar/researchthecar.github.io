# 2016 Mercedes-Benz S400L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S400L represents a pinnacle of luxury and technological sophistication, but used car buyers must approach luxury flagships with an eye toward potential maintenance and electrical complexities. While powertrain options are generally robust when properly maintained, owners have reported sporadic complaints concerning exterior glass components, steering systems, and seat belt hardware. Thorough pre-purchase inspections are strongly advised to verify the condition of high-complexity systems.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Biturbo

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine delivers smooth and responsive power suitable for a luxury long-wheelbase sedan. It is generally reliable when routine fluid maintenance schedules are strictly followed.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic

The 7-speed automatic transmission provides seamless gear changes tailored to maximize ride comfort in the S-Class. Regular fluid and filter changes are key to ensuring longevity.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

Staggered wheel setups and low-profile run-flat tires are vulnerable to damage from road imperfections.

**Wheel Cracks and Tire Blowouts**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden tire deflation, visible sidewall blowouts, or fractured alloy wheels discovered during routine service checks.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged low-profile tires and inspect alloy wheels for structural cracks.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Steering components generally offer responsive handling, though electronic assist failures have been reported by some owners.

**Power Steering Lockout**
- **Symptoms:** Power steering failure warning lights illuminate, and the steering wheel may seize or experience intermittent lockout while driving.
- **Solution:** Have the steering module, electronic sensors, and related wiring inspected and replaced by a qualified technician.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Glass Roof

Exterior glass and sunroof components can occasionally experience unexpected stress fractures.

**Spontaneous Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A loud bang is heard while driving or parked, followed by a visible crack or shattering of the panoramic sunroof or windshield glass.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the frame for body torque or alignment issues.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Occupant restraint systems generally function securely, but mechanical wear in buckles and retractors can occur.

**Seat Belt Buckle and Retractor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Seat belt fails to latch securely into the buckle or the seat belt webbing remains stuck and refuses to retract.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty seat belt buckles or retractor assemblies.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S400L delivers supreme comfort and sophisticated engineering, but prospective buyers should carefully inspect electronic steering components, wheels, and glass assemblies. While major mechanical failures are uncommon, out-of-warranty luxury repairs can be expensive. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist is essential before finalizing any purchase.
