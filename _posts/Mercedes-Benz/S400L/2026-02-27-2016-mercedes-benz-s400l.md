# 2016 Mercedes-Benz S400L

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S400L, a luxury sedan known for its advanced technology and comfort, generally exhibits strong reliability. As a flagship model, it incorporates sophisticated systems that, while contributing to its premium experience, can occasionally present unique challenges. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers. The S400L is equipped with a hybrid powertrain, combining a V6 gasoline engine with an electric motor, which is a key area to examine for specific reliability trends. Overall, the vehicle is built to high standards, but understanding potential technical quirks is essential for maintaining its optimal performance and value.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 Hybrid (276.950)
**Hybrid Battery Degradation**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced electric-only range, decreased overall power output, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the hybrid battery pack.
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $8,000

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, overheating, sweet smell from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $900

## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, 'Service Required' message.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air struts or air springs.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, audio cutting out, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates or reset, potential replacement of head unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1,500


### 12V Auxiliary Battery
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical systems malfunctioning (e.g., power windows, lights), 'Auxiliary Battery Malfunction' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V auxiliary battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S400L is a highly sophisticated luxury vehicle that, for the most part, offers a reliable ownership experience. The primary areas that may require attention beyond standard maintenance are related to its advanced hybrid system, specifically the hybrid battery's lifespan and potential coolant leaks from the V6 engine. The AIRMATIC air suspension, while providing exceptional comfort, can be a source of costly repairs if air struts or the compressor fail. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the COMAND infotainment system and the auxiliary battery, are also noted. However, many components, including the transmission and exterior elements, demonstrate robust reliability. Proactive maintenance and addressing early warning signs are key to mitigating potential repair costs and ensuring the longevity of this premium sedan.
