# 2017 Mercedes-Benz S400L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S400L represents the pinnacle of luxury and technological sophistication, offering a remarkably smooth and quiet ride for discerning drivers. While generally well-engineered, used-car buyers should pay close attention to interior trim durability, electrical quirks, and specific mechanical items reported by owners. Conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection will help ensure that high-end features and mechanical systems are in top condition before purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 329 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque, delivering swift and effortless acceleration. This powerplant is generally robust, though regular maintenance and cooling system checks remain essential for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides seamless gear changes and optimizes highway fuel efficiency. Overall reliability is solid, though electronic gear selector behavior warrants verification during a test drive.

**Automatic Shift to Neutral Upon Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** When attempting to turn off the vehicle while in drive, the transmission may shift into neutral instead of park, posing a potential roll-away risk if the driver is not attentive.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic shifter module and transmission software inspected by a qualified technician or dealership to ensure proper parking engagement.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Rims

Air suspension delivers a remarkably cushioned ride quality, though heavy vehicle weight places high stress on wheels and tires.

**Wheel Rim Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Alloy wheel rims, particularly larger optional or AMG variants, may develop structural cracks when encountering regular road imperfections.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged wheel rims and inspect tires for proper sidewall profile and inflation pressures to absorb impacts better.

## Steering

### Steering Column

Power steering assist feels precise and well-weighted, though motorized convenience adjustments inside the column can occasionally experience mechanical binding.

**Power Steering Column Adjustment Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel adjusts inward and outward normally, but fails to move vertically, becoming stuck in the uppermost position.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column adjustment motor and switch mechanism, and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Exterior

### Headlamp Assemblies

Advanced electrical architectures control everything from cabin ambient lighting to driver assists, requiring a healthy battery and clean module connections.

**Headlight Moisture Accumulation**
- **Symptoms:** Condensation or moisture builds up inside the exterior headlight housings after heavy rain or car washes.
- **Solution:** Install updated moisture barrier packets or replace the headlight housing seals if moisture persistence threatens electronic components.

## Interior

### Dashboard Trim and Leather

Cabin materials are exceptionally plush, but certain leather trim pieces can experience adhesive failure in hot climates.

**Leather Dashboard Separation**
- **Symptoms:** The leather upholstery on the dashboard begins to peel or separate near the passenger-side airbag deployment area, potentially exposing internal components.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the affected dashboard assembly through an upholstery specialist or dealership, which can involve significant out-of-pocket costs.
- **Cost:** $3,000 to $6,000

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S400L delivers an exceptionally refined driving experience backed by a smooth powertrain and advanced luxury amenities. While major mechanical failures are infrequent, used-car buyers should carefully inspect interior dashboard leather condition, steering column adjustments, and wheel integrity. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist is strongly recommended to catch any hidden electronic or trim-related issues before finalizing a purchase.
