# 2007 Mercedes-Benz ML320

### Overview
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz ML320, part of the W164 generation, is a luxury mid-size SUV known for its comfort and capability. It was offered with a potent diesel engine, providing a good balance of performance and fuel efficiency. While generally well-regarded, like any vehicle of its age, it has specific areas that have seen more frequent attention from owners. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, highlighting common issues and their potential resolutions to assist prospective buyers or current owners in understanding the vehicle's long-term ownership profile.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Turbo Diesel (OM642)
**Injectors Leaking**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, loss of power, fuel smell, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty injector(s) and seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per injector

**EGR Valve Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, black smoke from exhaust, rough idling, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noise from the engine, excessive smoke, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Oil Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, milky oil, overheating, potential for coolant contamination in the oil.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil cooler and potentially flushing the cooling system.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Transmission

### 722.6 5-Speed Automatic
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, slipping gears, failure to shift, 'limp mode'.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Suspension

### Standard Air Suspension (Optional)
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, compressor running excessively, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000 per corner for springs, $700 - $1500 for compressor

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow operation, grinding noise.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per window

**Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** One or both HID headlights not working, flickering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight ballast.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500 per ballast

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz ML320, particularly with its diesel engine, can be a reliable luxury SUV if well-maintained. Common concerns revolve around the diesel engine's emissions system (EGR, DPF), fuel injectors, and turbocharger, as well as potential issues with the optional air suspension and the automatic transmission's control module or torque converter. Electrical components like window regulators and headlight ballasts can also require attention. While not immune to age-related problems, addressing these specific potential failures proactively can contribute to a more dependable ownership experience. Regular servicing and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for mitigating costly repairs.
