# 2002 Mercedes-Benz CLK320

### Overview
The 2002 Mercedes-Benz CLK320, part of the W208 generation, is generally considered a reliable luxury coupe and convertible. It offers a blend of performance, comfort, and distinctive styling. While it shares many components with other Mercedes-Benz models of its era, specific attention should be paid to certain areas as the vehicle ages. The M112 V6 engine is known for its robustness, and the transmission is typically dependable. However, like many vehicles from this period, electrical gremlins and aging rubber components in the suspension can emerge. Overall, with proper maintenance, the CLK320 can be a rewarding ownership experience, though potential buyers should be aware of common, albeit not universal, issues.

---


## Engine

### 3.2L V6 (M112)
**Coolant Leaks (Intake Manifold)**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant smell, visible coolant residue around the intake manifold, potential overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold gaskets or the manifold itself.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Oil smell, visible oil on the engine block, potential smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, 'limp mode', failure to engage gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Conductor Plate Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the conductor plate and associated sensors.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Sagging Springs/Worn Shocks**
- **Symptoms:** Poor ride quality, excessive body roll, nose-diving under braking.
- **Solution:** Replacement of springs and/or shock absorbers.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, window stuck in position.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensor Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check Engine Light, rough idle, poor acceleration, stalling.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the MAF sensor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $350

**Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** One or both xenon headlights not working, flickering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight ballast unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Exterior

### Body and Trim
**Clear Coat Peeling**
- **Symptoms:** Fading paint, peeling clear coat, especially on roof and hood.
- **Solution:** Repainting affected areas.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000+

**Sunroof Drain Clogs**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, musty smell, potential headliner damage.
- **Solution:** Clearing of sunroof drain tubes.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**Pixelated Instrument Cluster Display**
- **Symptoms:** Missing or garbled segments on the LCD display (odometer, fuel gauge).
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $200


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2002 Mercedes-Benz CLK320 (W208) is a capable and stylish vehicle with a generally good reliability record for its age. The V6 engine and automatic transmission are robust, but common issues tend to arise in areas like electrical components (window regulators, MAF sensors), suspension wear (bushings, shocks), and age-related exterior problems such as clear coat failure. Interior electronics, like the instrument cluster display, can also develop faults. Regular maintenance and proactive attention to potential leaks (coolant, oil) and electrical gremlins are key to ensuring long-term dependability. While not immune to problems, many issues are well-documented and repairable, making it a viable option for those seeking a classic German luxury coupe or convertible.
