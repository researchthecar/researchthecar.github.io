# 2016 Mercedes-Benz C300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz C300 offers a compelling blend of luxury, refined interior styling, and engaging driving dynamics. However, prospective buyers should approach used models with care, as owner-reported complaints highlight several recurring mechanical and electrical concerns. Paying close attention to documented issues regarding fuel system odors, steering components, and sunroof hardware can help you spot a well-maintained example and avoid costly repairs.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 241 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration and respectable efficiency. While generally robust, routine maintenance inspections are vital due to reported issues with fuel lines and crankcase ventilation components.

**Fuel Leaks and Odors**
- **Symptoms:** A strong smell of gasoline around or inside the vehicle, often linked to fuel lines or fuel rail components.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines and injectors thoroughly; replace damaged lines or leaking seals.

**Crankcase Ventilation and Check Engine Light (P052E)**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light accompanied by diagnostic trouble codes such as P052E relating to the positive crankcase ventilation valve.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty crankcase ventilation regulator valve or associated hoses.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth, quick gear shifts tailored to various driving modes. Paired with either rear-wheel drive or 4MATIC all-wheel drive, it generally operates reliably when fluid changes are performed at recommended service intervals.

## Steering

### Electronic Power Steering Rack

Issues involving the electronic steering rack can lead to expensive repairs if power assist fails entirely.

**Steering Rack Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering assistance or mechanical failure within the electronic rack and pinion assembly.
- **Solution:** Replace the electronic power steering assembly.
- **Cost:** $5,840.14

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

The panoramic sunroof is a popular luxury feature, but owners have reported mechanical binding and occasional spontaneous glass breakage.

**Sunroof Binding and Glass Shuttering**
- **Symptoms:** Panoramic sunroof binds during operation, fails to close evenly, or experiences spontaneous glass fracture.
- **Solution:** Clean and lubricate tracks, replace damaged plastic guide tracks, or replace the glass assembly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V787000: Communication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C300&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** For a full list of the affected models visit: https://static.nhtsa.gov/odi/rcl/2019/RMISC-19V787-4620.pdf An inaccurate vehicle location may delay emergency responders, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will also notify owners, and dealers will check software for a successful OTA or update the communication module software as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V850000: Steering:Rack And Pinion:Rack](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C300&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A car that cannot be properly steered has an increased risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the steering rack, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V197000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C300&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The front roof panel located between the windshield and the panoramic sunroof may not have been bonded correctly to the vehicle during a prior repair. The adhesive bonding of the front panel might deteriorate, possibly causing the front panel to detach from the vehicle and become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Mercedes-Benz dealer will remove the bonded panel and will install a new front panel by following the detailed repair instructions and using the approved primer, cleaner and adhesive, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C300&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 21V961000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System:Fuel Rail](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C300&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source, such as hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel rail and fuel injectors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V627000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C300&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The driver's air bag may unexpectedly deploy due to insufficient grounding of the steering components if an electrostatic discharge occurs and the air bag clockspring is broken. If the driver air bag inadvertently deploys, it can increase the risk of injury and a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will add sufficient grounding to the steering components, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V114000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C300&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the starting current limiter overheats, the surrounding components can melt, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will install an additional fuse in the electrical line to the starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V177000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C300&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Various control units on these vehicles may have been updated with incorrect software, potentially affecting the correct deployment of the air bags in the event of a crash. If the air bags do not deploy as intended in the event of a crash, there would be an increased risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update software in the affected control units, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz C300 delivers rewarding luxury and performance, but prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Particular attention should be paid to the condition of the fuel system, electronic steering components, and sunroof hardware to ensure trouble-free ownership.
