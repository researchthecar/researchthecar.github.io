# 2022 Mercedes-Benz C300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury sedan, understanding potential problem areas can help you make an informed decision. This reliability summary explores owner-reported concerns and powertrain configurations for the vehicle, providing a balanced look at what to watch out for during your test drive and inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M254)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine (M254) produces approximately 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, often paired with a 48-volt mild-hybrid system. It offers strong performance and efficiency, though buyers should watch out for fuel delivery and electrical software updates.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected vehicle stalling, loss of engine power, or an inability to accelerate properly in traffic.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the fuel delivery system and verify that all relevant software updates or component replacements have been completed.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and efficient gear shifts across rear-wheel and all-wheel drive configurations. It generally operates reliably, though proper fluid maintenance is essential for long-term durability.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake components should be thoroughly checked during a pre-purchase inspection to rule out unusual mechanical noises or excessive wear.

**Clunking Noise and Pedal Thumping**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice an unusual clunking noise or physical thumping sensation through the brake pedal when coming to a stop.
- **Solution:** Have the brake pads, rotors, and caliper mounting hardware inspected for premature wear or improper seating.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Assist

Advanced safety features generally enhance driver confidence, but sensor alignment and software stability are critical.

**Active Brake Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have noted unexpected warnings or failures of the active brake assist and collision prevention systems during critical driving situations.
- **Solution:** Check diagnostic trouble codes related to driver assistance systems and ensure camera and radar sensors are clean and properly calibrated.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V189000: Steering: Steering Wheel/Handle Bar](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C300&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Deactivated hands-off detection will prevent DISTRONIC system features from functioning, including warnings to grab the steering wheel and calling first responders as part of the Active Emergency Stop Assist, which can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the hand detection control unit software for the DISTRONIC system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V678000: Electrical System:Wiring: Rear Compartment/Trunk](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C300&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Moisture accumulation in the trunk may leak into the signal acquisition module (SAM), causing an electrical short circuit. An electrical short circuit in the SAM could lead to various malfunctions, such as rear exterior lighting failure, unintentional rear seat folding, and rearview camera failure, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a cover for the SAM, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V462000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=C300&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the transmission wiring harness routing, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

Overall, this luxury sedan delivers engaging performance, high-tech features, and refined comfort. While owner complaints highlight specific areas of concern such as fuel pump operation and driver-assistance electronics, a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verification of service history will help ensure a dependable ownership experience.
