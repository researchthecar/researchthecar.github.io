The 2022 Mercedes-Benz C300 is a luxury sedan that offers a comfortable and refined driving experience. It comes with a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine that produces 255 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. Other engine and transmission options include:

* 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine with a 48-volt mild-hybrid system (255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque) paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission
* 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine with a 48-volt mild-hybrid system and plug-in hybrid capability (312 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque) paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission

People who like the 2022 Mercedes-Benz C300 praise it for its comfortable ride, luxurious interior, and powerful engine. However, some people have also reported issues with the vehicle, including:

**Engine**

* **2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine (non-hybrid)**
    * **Fuel injector failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The fuel injectors may fail, causing the engine to run rough or stall.
        * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine with a 48-volt mild-hybrid system**
    * **Starter-generator failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The starter-generator may fail, causing the engine to be difficult to start or run.
        * Solution: Replace the starter-generator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **9-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Shifting problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shift gears roughly or hesitate to shift.
        * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or check for electrical leaks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Air conditioning failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The air conditioning may not blow cold air.
    * Solution: Recharge the air conditioning system or replace the compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary of Issues:**
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz C300 has been reported to have some issues, including fuel injector failure and starter-generator failure. These issues can be costly to fix, but they are not widespread. Overall, the C300 is a reliable and well-built vehicle.
