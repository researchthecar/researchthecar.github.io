**Introduction:**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz C300 is a mid-size luxury sedan known for its sophisticated design, comfortable interior, and impressive performance. It is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine that generates 255 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The C300 is praised for its responsive handling, high-quality materials, and advanced technology features. However, several issues have been reported by owners.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Engine**

* **Oil Leaks:** Leakage can occur from the engine oil pan gasket or valve cover gasket. Symptoms include a noticeable drop in oil level and visible oil stains under the vehicle. Requires replacing the affected gaskets. $300 - $600+
* **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger may fail due to excessive wear or oil starvation. Symptoms include reduced engine power, increased noise, and black smoke from the exhaust. Turbocharger replacement is necessary. $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Fuel Injector Failure:** The fuel injectors may malfunction, causing engine misfires and reduced fuel economy. Injectors need to be replaced. $500 - $1,000+

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:** The transmission may experience rough or delayed shifts, especially during acceleration or gear changes. The issue could be due to a faulty torque converter or transmission control module. Requires transmission repair or replacement. $2,000 - $4,000+

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may discharge quickly, especially when the vehicle is not in use. Various electrical components can cause the drain, such as a faulty alternator or parasitic draw. Requires diagnosing and repairing or replacing the affected component. $500 - $1,500+
* **Infotainment System Glitches:** The infotainment system may experience occasional glitches or malfunctions. Updating the software or replacing the head unit may resolve the issue. $500 - $2,000+

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattling:** The sunroof may rattle at certain speeds or when opening/closing. Tightening bolts or replacing the sunroof assembly can fix the issue. $500 - $1,000+
* **Dashboard Squeaks:** The dashboard may develop squeaks or rattles over time. Identifying and addressing the source of the noise, such as loose trim or fittings, can eliminate the issue. $200 - $500+

**Summary:**

While the 2021 Mercedes-Benz C300 offers a luxurious and enjoyable driving experience, some potential issues should be considered. The engine can experience oil leaks, turbocharger failures, or fuel injector malfunctions. The transmission may have shifting problems, and the electrical system can suffer from battery drain or infotainment glitches. The interior may develop sunroof rattles or dashboard squeaks. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but it is advisable to be aware of them before purchasing or owning the vehicle.
