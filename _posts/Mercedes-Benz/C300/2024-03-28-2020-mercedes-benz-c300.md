**Introduction**

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz C300 is a compact luxury sedan that offers a refined driving experience and a host of advanced features. It is available in three trim levels: Premium, Exclusive, and Pinnacle. The C300 is powered by a 2.0-liter inline-4 engine that produces 255 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a 9-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive. All-wheel drive is available as an option.

People like the C300 for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and powerful engine. However, some have complained about its high price and limited cargo space.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Inline-4**
    * **Turbocharger Wastegate Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger wastegate may fail, causing the engine to lose power and produce excessive smoke.
        * Solution: Replace the turbocharger wastegate.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner may fail, causing the timing chain to skip and damage the engine.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and timing chain.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: 2020 Mercedes-Benz C-Class (NHTSA Campaign ID: 20V174000)

**Transmission**

* **9-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shift Solenoid Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The shift solenoid may fail, causing the transmission to shift erratically or not shift at all.
        * Solution: Replace the shift solenoid.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain excessively, causing the vehicle to fail to start.
        * Solution: Replace the battery or repair the electrical system drain.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Screen Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system screen may fail, causing it to become unresponsive or display errors.
        * Solution: Replace the infotainment system screen.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The power windows may fail to operate, causing them to become stuck in the open or closed position.
        * Solution: Replace the power window motor or switch.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz C300 is a reliable vehicle overall. However, there have been some reported issues with the engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior components. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but most are relatively minor and can be fixed for under $1,000.
