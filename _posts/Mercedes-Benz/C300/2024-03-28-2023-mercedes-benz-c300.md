**Introduction**

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz C300 is a luxury sedan known for its sleek design, powerful engine, and advanced technology. It is powered by a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine producing 255 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The C300 has received praise for its comfortable ride, spacious cabin, and impressive fuel economy. However, some owners have reported experiencing issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder**

* **Oil Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil from the engine, visible underneath the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the oil pan gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: A rattling noise coming from the engine, especially during start-up or acceleration.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Hesitations**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay when shifting gears, especially during low-speed maneuvers.
    * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is parked.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or check for parasitic electrical draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment Screen Blackouts**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment screen goes blank or freezes, requiring a restart.
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the screen unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Rattling**
    * Description/Symptoms: A rattling or vibration noise from the panoramic sunroof, especially on rough roads.
    * Solution: Adjust or lubricate the sunroof rails.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2023 Mercedes-Benz C300 has a mix of reported issues, ranging from minor electrical glitches to more significant engine problems. While some issues may be covered under warranty, others can be costly to repair. Potential buyers should carefully consider the potential costs associated with these issues before making a purchase decision.
