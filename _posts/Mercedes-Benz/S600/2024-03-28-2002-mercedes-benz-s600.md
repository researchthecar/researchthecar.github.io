**Introduction:**

The 2002 Mercedes-Benz S600 is a full-size luxury sedan known for its opulent interior, powerful performance, and advanced technology. It is equipped with a 5.8-liter V12 engine that produces 493 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. While the S600 offers an exceptional driving experience, it has also been prone to certain issues.

**Engine**

**5.8L V12**

- **Valve Stem Seal Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Blue smoke from exhaust, excessive oil consumption.
Solution: Replacement of valve stem seals.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

- **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, delayed or no engagement of gears.
Solution: Replacement of shift solenoids.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Airmatic Suspension Leak:**
Description/Symptoms: Uneven ride height, deflated suspension bags.
Solution: Repair or replacement of airmatic components (e.g., air bags, compressor, valves).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Battery Discharge:**
Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery drain, difficulty starting the vehicle.
Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical components (e.g., alternator, battery, wiring).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

- **Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering xenon headlights.
Solution: Replacement of xenon headlight ballast(s).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Clear Coat Peeling:**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of clear coat on various exterior panels.
Solution: Repainting affected panels.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Navigation System Malfunction:**
Description/Symptoms: Screen freeze or errors, incorrect navigation data.
Solution: Software update or replacement of navigation unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2002 Mercedes-Benz S600 offers an extraordinary luxury experience, it has been known to experience various issues. The most severe and expensive issues include airmatic suspension leaks, transmission problems, and exterior clear coat peeling. It is important to factor in the potential costs of repairs and maintenance when considering the ownership of this vehicle.
