# 2016 Mercedes-Benz S600 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S600 represents the pinnacle of luxury, powered by an exceptionally smooth and robust V12 engine. While the core mechanical and powertrain foundations are generally robust when properly maintained, prospective buyers should review ownership history and inspect auxiliary systems closely for potential out-of-warranty repair expenses.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L Biturbo V12 Engine

The 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces approximately 523 horsepower and 612 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless, vault-quiet acceleration. It is a complex, high-performance powerplant requiring diligent fluid maintenance and specialized servicing to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 7-speed automatic transmission designed to handle the immense torque output of the V12 engine smoothly and efficiently. Regular fluid changes are vital to maintaining shift quality and preventing premature wear.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The power steering system provides effortless maneuverability, but rare electronic glitches can result in intermittent assist warnings.

**Intermittent Power Steering Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Power steering failure warning lights may illuminate, accompanied by a temporary heavy or locked-out steering feel while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning is required to check for electronic power steering fault codes and software updates or hardware replacements as indicated.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Occupant safety systems generally perform well, though isolated mechanical failures in seat belt buckles and retractors have been reported by owners.

**Seat Belt Latch and Retractor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Front passenger seat belt buckles may fail to latch securely, or rear seat belts can become stuck and fail to retract properly.
- **Solution:** Have the affected seat belt buckle or retractor assembly inspected by a qualified technician and replaced if mechanical failure is confirmed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V787000: Communication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S600&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** For a full list of the affected models visit: https://static.nhtsa.gov/odi/rcl/2019/RMISC-19V787-4620.pdf An inaccurate vehicle location may delay emergency responders, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will also notify owners, and dealers will check software for a successful OTA or update the communication module software as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V197000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S600&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The front roof panel located between the windshield and the panoramic sunroof may not have been bonded correctly to the vehicle during a prior repair. The adhesive bonding of the front panel might deteriorate, possibly causing the front panel to detach from the vehicle and become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Mercedes-Benz dealer will remove the bonded panel and will install a new front panel by following the detailed repair instructions and using the approved primer, cleaner and adhesive, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V899000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S600&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the control unit pins lose connection, there would be a loss of electric power steering assist, increasing the risk of a crash. The increase in pin contact resistance can also increase the risk of a fire, even while the vehicle is parked and ignition is off.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the electric power steering control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V854000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S600&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The front roof panel located between the windshield and the panoramic sunroof may not have been bonded correctly to the vehicle during a prior repair, allowing the front panel to detach from the vehicle. A detached roof panel can become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front panel, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S600 offers supreme comfort and extraordinary V12 performance, making it a compelling pre-owned luxury buy for enthusiasts who budget for specialized maintenance. Prospective owners should perform a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics, steering assistance, and structural glass integrity.
