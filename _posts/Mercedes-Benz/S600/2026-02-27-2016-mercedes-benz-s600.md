# 2016 Mercedes-Benz S600

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S600 is a pinnacle of luxury and engineering, representing the flagship model of the S-Class lineup. It is renowned for its opulent interior, advanced technology, and powerful V12 engine. As a high-performance luxury sedan, its reliability is generally strong, reflecting Mercedes-Benz's commitment to quality. However, like any complex vehicle, certain components can experience issues over time, particularly those related to its sophisticated systems. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L V12 Biturbo (M279)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light illumination, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per coil

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil on engine components, burning oil smell, oil level dropping.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, 'airmatic system malfunction' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air strut(s).
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500 per strut

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of compressor, 'airmatic system malfunction' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, audio issues, navigation problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates or resets; potential module replacement if persistent.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500 (for updates/diagnostics), $1,000+ (for module replacement)


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dying unexpectedly, difficulty starting the vehicle, frequent battery replacement.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800 (depending on the cause)

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S600, with its potent V12 engine and luxurious appointments, generally offers a high level of reliability befitting its flagship status. The primary areas that may require attention are related to the sophisticated AIRMATIC air suspension system, which can develop leaks or compressor issues, and potential electrical gremlins within the advanced infotainment system or parasitic battery drain. The V12 engine itself is robust, but components like ignition coils can require replacement. While not immune to issues, most problems are not indicative of systemic failure but rather the complexities inherent in a high-end, feature-rich vehicle. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining its optimal performance and longevity.
