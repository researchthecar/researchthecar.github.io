# 2015 Mercedes-Benz S600

### Overview
The 2015 Mercedes-Benz S600 is a pinnacle of luxury and engineering, representing the flagship model of the S-Class lineup. It is renowned for its potent V12 engine, opulent interior, and advanced technology. As a high-performance luxury sedan, its reliability is generally strong, reflecting Mercedes-Benz's commitment to quality. However, like any complex vehicle, particularly one with a sophisticated V12 powertrain and advanced electronic systems, certain areas may require more attention over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L V12 Twin-Turbocharged (M275)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light illumination, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per coil

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil residue around the top of the engine, burning oil smell, potential for oil level drop.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Coolant Leaks (Hoses/Pipes)**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant level, overheating, visible coolant residue, sweet smell of coolant.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking coolant hoses or pipes.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC) / AIRMATIC
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, 'ABC System Malfunction' warning on the dashboard, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hydraulic lines, struts, or pump.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $4000+

**Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Similar to hydraulic leaks, but often accompanied by a whining noise from the pump, loss of suspension height control.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent freezing, unresponsiveness, navigation issues, audio problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates or resets. In severe cases, head unit replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500


### Sensors (Parking Assist, Blind Spot)
**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** False warnings, system deactivation, 'Parking Assist Unavailable' messages.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of faulty sensors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per sensor

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Mercedes-Benz S600 is a highly sophisticated luxury vehicle. While its core powertrain and general build quality are robust, its complexity introduces potential for specific issues, particularly in the advanced suspension systems (ABC/AIRMATIC) and the intricate electrical and electronic components. Owners should be prepared for potentially high repair costs associated with these systems, especially if hydraulic leaks or electronic module failures occur. The V12 engine, while powerful, can also present maintenance needs like ignition coil replacements and potential oil leaks. Despite these points, for a vehicle of its caliber and age, the S600 offers a generally reliable ownership experience when properly maintained, with no widespread critical failures reported across the board.
