# 2007 Mercedes-Benz S600

### Overview
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz S600, a flagship luxury sedan, is renowned for its opulent interior, advanced technology for its era, and powerful V12 engine. As a high-performance vehicle from this period, it represents a significant investment in engineering and comfort. However, like many complex luxury cars, it can present specific reliability challenges, particularly related to its sophisticated powertrain and electronic systems. Owners should be aware of potential issues that may arise, often stemming from the intricate nature of its components and the demands placed upon them. Regular and specialized maintenance is crucial for preserving the integrity and performance of this exceptional automobile.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L V12 Twin-Turbo
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per coil

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of turbocharger(s).
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $5,000 per turbocharger

**Coolant Leaks (ABC System)**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid under the car, suspension dropping, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hoses, pumps, or accumulators within the Active Body Control (ABC) system.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $3,000+


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission slipping, 'limp mode' engagement, no gear engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement or reprogramming of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**Conductor Plate Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the conductor plate and associated sensors.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,200


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Suspension sag, fluid leaks under the vehicle, stiff ride, warning messages.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, struts, or the ABC pump.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $4,000+

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of hydraulic pressure, suspension not adjusting, loud whining noise from the pump.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive buttons, audio issues, navigation problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates, or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000+


### Sensors and Modules
**Various Sensor Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated warning lights (e.g., ABS, ESP, Check Engine), erratic system behavior.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty sensors (e.g., wheel speed sensors, O2 sensors).
- **Cost:** $200 - $800 per sensor

## Interior

### Climate Control System
**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


### Seat Actuators
**Malfunctioning Seat Adjustments**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not moving or adjusting correctly, memory seat function failing.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty seat motors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per seat

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz S600 is a pinnacle of automotive luxury and engineering, but its complexity translates to potential reliability concerns, particularly with its V12 twin-turbo engine and advanced Active Body Control (ABC) suspension. Owners can expect to encounter issues related to ignition coils, turbochargers, and hydraulic leaks within the ABC system, which can be costly to repair. The transmission, while generally robust, can suffer from TCM or conductor plate failures. Electrical gremlins, especially within the COMAND infotainment system and various sensors, are also noted. While the exterior and interior are generally well-built, specific components like HVAC resistors and seat actuators can fail. Proactive and specialized maintenance is paramount for mitigating these potential issues and ensuring the long-term enjoyment of this high-performance luxury sedan.
