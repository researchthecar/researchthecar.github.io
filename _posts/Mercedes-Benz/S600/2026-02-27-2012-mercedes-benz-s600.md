# 2012 Mercedes-Benz S600

### Overview
The 2012 Mercedes-Benz S600 is a flagship luxury sedan renowned for its opulent interior, powerful performance, and advanced technology. As a top-tier model, it features a potent V12 engine and a sophisticated automatic transmission, aiming for a refined driving experience. While generally well-regarded for its comfort and features, like any high-end vehicle, it can present specific reliability concerns, particularly related to its complex systems and high-performance components. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L V12 Twin-Turbo
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light illumination, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per coil

**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the engine, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or other seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Coolant Leaks (Hoses/Radiator)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles, sweet smell from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, radiator, or associated components.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, failure to shift gears, limp mode engagement, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Transmission Control Module.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping gears, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $2500


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, suspension warning light, fluid leaks under the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hydraulic lines, struts, or pump.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $4000+

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of suspension height control, loud whining noise from the pump, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive buttons, audio issues, navigation malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the COMAND head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2500


### Keyless Go System
**Door Handle Sensors**
- **Symptoms:** Keyless entry/start not functioning intermittently or completely.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty door handle sensors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per handle


### Headlight Control Module
**Malfunctioning Xenon Headlights**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights not turning on, flickering, or self-leveling issues.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight control module or ballast.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Exterior

### Door Seals
**Deterioration**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, wind noise at higher speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn door seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Interior

### Seat Massagers/Climate Control
**Malfunctioning Seat Functions**
- **Symptoms:** Massagers not working, heating/cooling elements failing, seat adjustment issues.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair/replacement of seat modules, motors, or climate control components.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500+


### Headliner Fabric
**Sagging**
- **Symptoms:** Headliner fabric detaching from the backing, especially around sunroof.
- **Solution:** Re-gluing or replacement of the headliner.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Mercedes-Benz S600, while a pinnacle of luxury and engineering, presents a mixed reliability profile. Its complex V12 engine and advanced ABC suspension system are prone to costly repairs, particularly hydraulic leaks and component failures. Electrical systems, including the COMAND infotainment and various comfort features, can also develop glitches. Owners should anticipate higher maintenance and repair costs compared to less complex vehicles. Regular, specialized maintenance is crucial to mitigate potential issues. Despite these potential drawbacks, for those who can afford the upkeep, the S600 offers an unparalleled luxury experience.
