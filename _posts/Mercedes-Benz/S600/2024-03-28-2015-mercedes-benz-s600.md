**Introduction:**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz S600 is a luxury sedan powered by a 6.0L V12 biturbo engine producing 523 horsepower and 612 lb-ft of torque. It comes with a 7-speed automatic transmission and an all-wheel drive system. The S600 is known for its high level of luxury and performance, but it has also been criticized for its high price and complex infotainment system.

**Engine**

* 6.0L V12 biturbo

  * **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, black smoke from the exhaust, increased oil consumption
    * Solution: Replace the turbochargers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 15V540000
  * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, loss of power, engine failure
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioners and timing chain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* 7-speed automatic

  * **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifting, slipping gears
    * Solution: Replace or repair the transmission valve body or solenoid pack
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* No Issues Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, difficulty starting the car
    * Solution: Replace the defective battery or diagnose electrical system for parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the sunroof area
    * Solution: Adjust or replace the sunroof seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent or ineffective temperature control
    * Solution: Replace the climate control module or blend door actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz S600 is a highly luxurious and powerful sedan, but it has also been known to experience some significant issues. The most serious issues include turbocharger failure, timing chain tensioner failure, and shifting problems. These issues can be costly to repair and may impact the vehicle's performance and reliability. However, it's important to note that these issues are not universal to all S600 models, and many owners have had positive experiences with their vehicles.
