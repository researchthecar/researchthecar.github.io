# 2017 Mercedes-Benz S600

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S600 is a pinnacle of luxury and engineering, representing the flagship S-Class sedan. It is equipped with a potent V12 engine, offering unparalleled performance and refinement. As expected from a high-end luxury vehicle, the S600 is built with premium materials and advanced technology. Reliability for such a complex vehicle is generally good, with owners reporting satisfaction with its long-term durability. However, due to the sophisticated nature of its components, potential issues, while not widespread, can be costly to repair. Regular maintenance and adherence to service schedules are crucial for maintaining the optimal performance and longevity of this exceptional automobile.

## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, reduced engine power, check engine light illumination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per coil

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased fuel consumption, exhaust smoke.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential repair or replacement of turbocharger(s) and associated plumbing.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $5,000+

## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, 'airmatic system malfunction' warning message.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or associated seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, 'airmatic system malfunction' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, audio cutting out, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates or potential replacement of the COMAND head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3,000+


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1,000+

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S600 is a highly luxurious and powerful vehicle. While generally reliable, its complex V12 engine and advanced AIRMATIC suspension system are the primary areas where potential issues may arise. These issues, though not common, can be expensive to rectify due to the specialized nature of the components and the labor involved. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system, can also occur. Owners should prioritize diligent maintenance and be prepared for potentially high repair costs associated with this flagship model. Overall, for those who can afford the upkeep, the S600 offers an exceptional ownership experience.
