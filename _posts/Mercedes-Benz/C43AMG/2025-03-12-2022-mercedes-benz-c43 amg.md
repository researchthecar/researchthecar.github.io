The 2022 Mercedes-Benz C43 AMG is a performance sedan that blends luxury and sporty driving dynamics. It's powered by a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine producing 385 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. Some owners appreciate the C43's responsive engine, agile handling, and luxurious interior. However, common complaints include a firm ride quality, some infotainment system complexity, and higher running costs compared to non-AMG models. The C-Class is also available with a turbocharged 2.0L inline-4 engine making 255 horsepower.

**Engine**

**3.0L Twin-Turbocharged V6**

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Whining noise from the engine, reduced power, check engine light. Can occur at relatively low mileage.
    * Solution: Replacement of the failed turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Coolant Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Low coolant level warning, visible coolant leak under the vehicle, engine overheating.
    * Solution: Identify and repair the source of the coolant leak (e.g., hoses, water pump, thermostat housing).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+ depending on the source and repair.
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Oil Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil spots under the car, burning oil smell, low oil level warning.
    * Solution: Locate and repair the source of the leak, replacing gaskets or seals as needed. Common leak points include valve cover gaskets and oil pan gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+ depending on location and complexity.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9G-Tronic 9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Rough Shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: Harsh or jerky gear changes, especially during low-speed acceleration or deceleration.
    * Solution: Software update, transmission flush with genuine Mercedes-Benz fluid. In severe cases, valve body replacement may be necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 (fluid change) - $2,000+ (valve body replacement).
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Delayed Engagement**
    * Description/Symptoms: A noticeable delay between selecting a gear (e.g., Drive or Reverse) and the transmission actually engaging.
    * Solution: Transmission fluid flush, software update. In some cases, internal transmission damage may require more extensive repairs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 (fluid change) - $3,000+ (transmission repair/replacement).
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Transmission Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine revs increase without a corresponding increase in vehicle speed, especially during acceleration.
    * Solution: Transmission fluid flush and filter change. If the problem persists, internal transmission damage is likely and requires repair or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 (fluid change) - $4,000+ (transmission repair/replacement).
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Issues (if equipped)**
    * Description/Symptoms: Uneven ride height, sagging suspension, "Suspension Malfunction" warning light, air compressor noise.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace the faulty air struts, air compressor, or related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $2,500+ per strut, $500 - $1,500+ for the compressor.
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Noisy Suspension Components**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the suspension, especially when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: Inspect and replace worn sway bar links, bushings, ball joints, or other suspension components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+ per component.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    * Description/Symptoms: The MBUX infotainment system freezes, crashes, or becomes unresponsive.
    * Solution: Software update, system reset. In rare cases, hardware replacement may be needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Software updates are typically covered under warranty. Hardware replacement can be expensive.
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sensor Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Faulty readings from sensors related to systems like blind spot monitoring, lane keeping assist, or parking sensors.
    * Solution: Diagnose and replace the faulty sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+ depending on the sensor.
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Car battery drains quickly, especially when the vehicle is not driven frequently.
    * Solution: Diagnose and address any parasitic drain on the battery. This could involve replacing a faulty module or repairing wiring issues.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+ depending on the cause.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: LED headlight flickering, dimming, or complete failure.
    * Solution: Headlight replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+ per headlight.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Heater Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat heaters not working or overheating.
    * Solution: Replacement of the seat heater element or module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sunroof Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof not opening or closing properly, unusual noises, or leaks.
    * Solution: Inspection and repair of the sunroof mechanism, motor, or seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz C43 AMG, while offering a thrilling driving experience, has some reported issues that prospective buyers should be aware of. Engine issues such as turbocharger failures and coolant/oil leaks can be costly to repair. Transmission problems like rough shifting and delayed engagement are also reported. Suspension issues are prevalent on vehicles with air suspension, while electrical issues mostly pertain to the infotainment system and sensors. Although the severity and cost of repairs can vary, addressing these potential problems proactively can help ensure long-term reliability. The severity of these issues range from minor inconveniences to major repairs, and the cost can vary widely.
