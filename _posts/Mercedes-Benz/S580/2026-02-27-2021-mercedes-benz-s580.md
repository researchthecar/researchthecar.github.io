# 2021 Mercedes-Benz S580 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz S580 delivers flag-ship luxury, advanced technology, and effortless performance, but prospective buyers should carefully review owner reports regarding complex electronics, driver-assistance sensors, and wheel durability. While most driving experiences highlight the exceptional comfort and refinement of this executive sedan, owner complaints point to specific recurring electronic issues and mechanical quirks that require attention.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8 Engine

The S580 is powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing around 496 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 48-volt mild-hybrid system. It offers effortless, authoritative acceleration, though its high-tech components can be expensive to diagnose and repair out of warranty.

**Camtronic Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may lose acceleration unexpectedly while driving, accompanied by an illuminated check engine light.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic or dealership inspect the valvetrain components and replace the failed actuator as needed.
- **Cost:** $9000

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Power is routed through a 9-speed automatic transmission designed to deliver seamless gear changes across rear-wheel-drive and 4MATIC all-wheel-drive configurations. Overall mechanical durability is generally solid, with few widespread complaint patterns reported by owners.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

The optional large AMG wheel and tire packages on this luxury sedan provide sharp styling and handling, but they suffer from high vulnerability to pothole damage and premature wear.

**Cracked Rims and Tire Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Large 21-inch low-profile wheels are prone to sidewall bubbling, air loss, and rim cracking when encountering road imperfections.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels and tires thoroughly for structural damage, and consider switching to smaller wheel diameters or more robust tire sidewalls if road conditions are poor.

## Electrical

### Camera and Lighting Systems

Sophisticated exterior lighting and camera arrays support the vehicle's advanced automation, but sensor sensitivity can cause nuisance warning messages.

**Camera System Errors and Erratic High Beams**
- **Symptoms:** Instrument clusters display frequent 'Camera System Unavailable' or 'Camera Dirty' warnings, and adaptive high-beam assist may switch erratically.
- **Solution:** Clean sensor areas around the windshield and cameras, or visit a dealership to update software and recalibrate driver assistance hardware.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Driver assistance features offer cutting-edge safety protection, though occasional sensor calibration issues can result in erratic warnings or unexpected braking inputs.

**Inadvertent Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The forward collision avoidance system may trigger false positive warnings and apply the brakes harshly during normal driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Have dealer technicians check sensor calibration and ensure all relevant software updates are applied.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V704000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S580&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace as necessary, the fuel tank including the suction jet pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V843000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm):Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S580&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An engine control unit software error may cause a sudden engine stall, without the ability to restart the vehicle. An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the engine control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V789000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S580&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated passenger air bag increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the air bag control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V931000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S580&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Driver distraction can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** For vehicles that were not able to be updated remotely, dealers will provide the software update, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V124000: Air Bags:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S580&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The air bag control unit may be improperly secured and may result in an unintentional air bag or seat belt deployment, or an improper deployment in the event of a crash. Unintentional or improper deployment of air bags and/or seat belts can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the air bag control unit fastening nuts, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V167000: Exterior Lighting:Back Up Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S580&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Disabled rear turn signals, hazard lights, and reverse lights can reduce the vehicle's visibility to other driver's, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the rear signal software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V189000: Steering: Steering Wheel/Handle Bar](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S580&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Deactivated hands-off detection will prevent DISTRONIC system features from functioning, including warnings to grab the steering wheel and calling first responders as part of the Active Emergency Stop Assist, which can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the hand detection control unit software for the DISTRONIC system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V214000: Steering:Column](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S580&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The electrical connector to the steering column module may not be secured properly, which can disable gear shifts and/or safety features including the electronic stability program, the driver's air bag, and turn signal indicators. Disabled safety features or the inability to shift gears can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and reinstall the cover for the electrical connector, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz S580 remains an engineering marvel of comfort and performance, but its heavy reliance on advanced electronics and large low-profile wheels introduces specific maintenance considerations. Buyers should thoroughly inspect the service history, check for outstanding software updates or campaign repairs, and carefully evaluate the condition of the wheels and tires before purchase.
