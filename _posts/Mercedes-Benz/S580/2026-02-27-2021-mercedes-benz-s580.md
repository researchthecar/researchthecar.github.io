## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (MBUX)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, intermittent system reboots, connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or module replacement if necessary.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500 (for updates/diagnostics), $1000+ (for module replacement)


### 12V Battery
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical systems malfunctioning, difficulty starting, warning lights related to battery or charging system.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V auxiliary battery.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz S580 is a highly sophisticated luxury sedan with a strong emphasis on reliability, particularly in its powertrain. The 4.0L V8 engine and 9-speed automatic transmission have shown no significant widespread issues. However, potential owners should be aware of the complexity of the AIRMATIC air suspension system, which can be prone to leaks or compressor failures, representing the most common reported technical concern. Electrical systems, especially the advanced MBUX infotainment, may occasionally encounter software glitches requiring updates. The 12V auxiliary battery has also been noted for potential premature failure. Interior components like seat actuators can also experience malfunctions. Overall, while the S580 offers a premium and generally dependable experience, proactive maintenance and awareness of these specific potential issues are recommended.
