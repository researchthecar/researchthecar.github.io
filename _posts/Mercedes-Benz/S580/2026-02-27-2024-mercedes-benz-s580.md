# 2024 Mercedes-Benz S580 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz S580 represents the pinnacle of luxury, advanced technology, and engineering for the flagship sedan lineup. As a relatively recent model year, owner reports are sparse, but prospective buyers should still remain mindful of early-production electronic quirks and powertrain complaints reported to safety authorities.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (S580)

This potent 4.0-liter biturbo V8 produces approximately 496 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, offering effortless acceleration and refined power delivery. It pairs luxury performance with smooth everyday drivability, though long-term maintenance costs for high-end German performance engines remain substantial.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Plug-In Hybrid (S580e)

The plug-in hybrid powertrain combines a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 with an electric motor to deliver efficiency alongside strong system output. It provides an advanced electrified driving experience, though complex hybrid electronics require careful diagnostic oversight.

**Instrument Cluster Display Cutout**
- **Symptoms:** The digital instrument cluster temporarily goes completely blank due to repeated system resets triggered by control unit fallback behavior.
- **Solution:** Have an authorized dealer inspect the control unit software and perform any necessary updates or module replacements.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 9-speed automatic transmission is paired with standard 4MATIC all-wheel drive, delivering seamless gear changes suited to a luxury flagship. While generally smooth, early owner complaints highlight occasional shifting harshness and powertrain electronic faults.

**Harsh Shifting and Power Train Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience harsh, abnormal gear shifts between 3rd and 4th gear at lower speeds, or sudden power loss at higher highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Consult a certified technician for transmission control module diagnostics, software recalibration, or mechanical inspection.

## Suspension

### Tires and Wheels

The suspension and wheel setup prioritizes plush ride quality, but low-profile tires on large wheels remain vulnerable to road hazards.

**Premature Tire Bulges and Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected bumps or bubbles form on low-profile tires, potentially leading to rapid air loss or run-flat conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect low-profile tires regularly for structural damage and replace compromised tires promptly.

## Exterior

### Body and Trim

Exterior panels and aerodynamics are tightly engineered, though rare wind-noise or trim anomalies can occasionally occur at highway velocities.

**Unexplained Exterior Noises**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden explosive or loud snapping sounds can emanate from the vehicle exterior at high speeds without warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a professional technician inspect body seals, trim fasteners, and structural glass integrity.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz S580 delivers world-class comfort and advanced powertrain options, but used buyers should thoroughly inspect vehicle history and electronics functionality. Given the complexity of its hybrid systems, digital displays, and 9-speed automatic transmission, securing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and remaining aware of owner-reported electronic quirks is highly recommended.
