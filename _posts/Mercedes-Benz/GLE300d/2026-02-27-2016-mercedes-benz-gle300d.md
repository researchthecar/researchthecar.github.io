# 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE300d Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE300d, buyers should carefully review potential powertrain operation and electrical complaints reported by owners. While diesel variants offer strong torque and highway efficiency, prospective owners should inspect vehicle history records closely and verify proper maintenance intervals.

---

## Engine

### 2.1L Inline-4 Diesel (OM651)

The 2.1-liter turbocharged inline-4 diesel engine produces approximately 200 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering robust low-end pulling power and high fuel economy for highway cruising.

**Fuel System and Stalling Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances of engine stalling, fuel leaks, or unexpected loss of motive power.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines, fuel pumps, and injectors for leaks or failures; replace faulty components as needed.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission (7G-Tronic Plus)

The 7-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear changes across various drive modes, though electronic shift glitches have been highlighted in owner feedback.

**Unintended Shifting or Powertrain Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted unexpected shifts into neutral or park, alongside messages indicating the vehicle is not securely in park.
- **Solution:** Diagnose electronic gear selector modules and transmission control units for software errors or hardware wear.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Issues related to steering assist reliability require prompt attention to ensure safe maneuverability.

**Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience sudden loss of power steering assistance, generating dashboard warnings and making the steering wheel heavy to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electric power steering system, associated wiring harnesses, and related control modules for faults.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical accessories and lighting components should be fully tested during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Headlight Dimness**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced forward illumination and limited visibility when driving at night in unlit areas.
- **Solution:** Check headlight bulb output, lens clarity, and projector alignment.

**[Recall 16V759000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE300d&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The low beam headlights on the affected vehicles may not be properly adjusted. Equipment." If the low-beam headlights are out of adjustment, the driver's visibility may be reduced, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and correct the headlight adjustment, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V177000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE300d&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Various control units on these vehicles may have been updated with incorrect software, potentially affecting the correct deployment of the air bags in the event of a crash. If the air bags do not deploy as intended in the event of a crash, there would be an increased risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update software in the affected control units, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V655000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE300d&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles may have excess bulkhead insulation extending into the lower windshield area, possibly affecting the windshield bonding and the full protective function of the passenger's frontal air bag in the event of a crash. If the passenger's frontal air bag does not function as designed in the event of a crash, the front seat passenger has an increased.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will remove any excess bulkhead insulation and rework the windshield bonding, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V903000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE300d&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the front passenger air bag does not deploy as necessary in the event of a crash, the front passenger has an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the OCS software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V241000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE300d&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If water enters the power steering control unit, the power steering assist may be deactivated, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the connector and inspect the electric power steering rack, replacing the rack as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V955000: Fuel System, Diesel:Delivery:Fuel Pump:Control/Drive Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE300d&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Water might accumulate in the spare tire wheel well and damage the fuel pump control unit, which could cause an engine stall while driving. An engine stall while driving increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a water drain plug, inspect for water intrusion, and replace the fuel pump control unit as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE300d combines diesel efficiency and luxury utility, but buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to fuel system components, steering assist operation, and transmission shifting behavior to avoid unexpected repair expenses.
