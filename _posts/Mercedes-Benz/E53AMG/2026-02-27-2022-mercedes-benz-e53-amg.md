# 2022 Mercedes-Benz E53 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Mercedes-Benz E53 AMG, buyers can generally expect a thrilling luxury performance experience paired with advanced technology. However, owner reports submitted to federal safety regulators highlight a few recurring electrical, powertrain, and driver-assistance quirks to keep on your radar during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 with EQ Boost

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz E53 AMG features a responsive 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine paired with an EQ Boost 48-volt mild-hybrid system, producing approximately 429 horsepower. While generally praised for its smooth power delivery and performance, some owners have reported complex electrical system and battery-related concerns.

**48V Battery Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** A 48-volt battery malfunction warning appears on the dashboard, followed by a red warning light and a rapid loss of motive power leading to sudden vehicle deceleration.
- **Solution:** Have the 48V electrical system and related battery components diagnosed and replaced or updated by a qualified service center.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift TCT 9-Speed Automatic

Power is routed through an AMG Speedshift TCT 9-speed automatic transmission featuring a wet start-off clutch for quick gear changes. This transmission is well-regarded for offering both relaxed cruising and aggressive track-ready shifts.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera

Cabin electronics and infotainment displays offer extensive customization and connectivity, though sporadic glitches have been noted by drivers.

**Blank Backup Camera Screen**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment display shows a completely blank screen when the vehicle is shifted into reverse, disabling the rearview camera view.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates or have the camera module and wiring inspected for faults.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior styling is highlighted by aggressive AMG bodywork and an expansive panoramic roof, though rare glass durability issues have been reported.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass sunroof unexpectedly shatters or explodes while driving without any external impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the frame for proper structural alignment.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced driver-assistance systems provide cutting-retge safety features, but sensors can occasionally misinterpret road conditions, leading to unexpected alerts or braking.

**Phantom Braking and False Speed Limit Alerts**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle suddenly engages the automatic emergency brakes without any obstruction ahead or incorrectly displays and adjusts to false speed limits.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera and radar sensor alignment, and ensure the vehicle software is running the latest manufacturer calibrations.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz E53 AMG delivers a compelling mix of luxury and high-performance engineering. Buyers should carefully verify the health of the 48-volt mild-hybrid system, test all driver-assistance and infotainment features, and inspect the exterior glass and body panels during a comprehensive pre-purchase evaluation.
