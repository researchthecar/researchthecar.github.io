### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz Metris demonstrates strong reliability, particularly in its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission, where no significant widespread issues are reported. The electrical system shows some minor vulnerabilities, with occasional infotainment glitches and potential battery drain issues being the most common concerns, though these are generally manageable with software updates or component diagnosis. Exterior and interior components are largely free from significant defects. Overall, the Metris is a dependable commercial vehicle with a good reliability record for its class, suitable for demanding work environments.
