# 2020 Mercedes-Benz Metris Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz Metris is a versatile mid-size van designed to bridge the gap between compact cargo haulers and full-sized commercial vehicles. Buyers looking at a used Metris will find a capable daily workhorse backed by Mercedes-Benz engineering, though checking service history and prior commercial usage is always recommended before purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering a solid balance of utility and efficiency for commercial tasks. It provides adequate power for hauling and city driving, though routine maintenance is essential to ensure long-term durability.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the rear wheels while helping optimize highway fuel economy. Shift quality is generally responsive under load, making it well-suited for stop-and-go delivery duties.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V055000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the software communication module for the automatic emergency call system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V715000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The integrated backup battery compartment located underneath the front passenger's seat may not have its own cover, potentially exposing the battery when the seat is moved forward. If conductive material, such as metal, unintentionally drops into the opening and contacts the terminals on the battery, an electrical short can occur, increasing the risk of a.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and Mercedes-Benz dealers will install covers over the battery compartment area, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V729000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The screwed fittings between the rigid steel brake lines routed through the underbody of the vehicle and the brake hoses in the area of the front and rear axles may not have been correctly installed. The connection between the brake line and the brake hose may leak, causing a sudden loss of brake fluids, impacting braking performance and increasing the risk.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will check the affected fittings at the connection points of the brake lines to the brake hoses, correcting them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V066000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Fluid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A detached brake fluid reservoir can leak brake fluid, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the brake fluid reservoir mounting, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V134000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the fuel hose connections, and replace the clamps and fuel hose, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V360000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A disabled eCall system would prevent a vehicle occupant from contacting the emergency services call center in an emergency, potentially delaying emergency responders and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V463000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The electrical connections for the infotainment system may not be properly secured, which can cause the rearview camera image not to display. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the infotainment system connection, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V070000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Water may accumulate on the air bag control unit, which can cause the air bags not to deploy as intended. An air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a cover on the air bag control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz Metris presents a practical option for used van shoppers needing a maneuverable yet spacious commercial vehicle. While formal owner complaints are limited, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's mechanical condition and maintenance records to ensure reliable future operation.
