# 2018 Mercedes-Benz Metris Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2018 Mercedes-Benz Metris, buyers will find a capable mid-size utility van designed to bridge the gap between compact cargo haulers and full-size commercial vans. While owner feedback highlights solid utility, prospective purchasers should carefully evaluate known mechanical pain points and recurring electronic or sensor-related grievances reported by drivers.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing ample pep for urban deliveries and hauling duties. Overall, it serves as a willing power plant, though isolated component wear like PCV valve sticking has been reported by owners.

**Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The check engine light illuminates, and diagnostic scans may return code P052E due to a sticking PCV valve.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic or dealer inspect and replace the faulty PCV valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $2100

### Instrument Cluster and Fuel Display

electrical issues in the instrument cluster can occasionally display erratic warnings or inaccurate driving metrics, so verify all gauge readings during a test drive.

**Inaccurate Fuel Range Display**
- **Symptoms:** The digital dashboard range indicator may show significantly more remaining driving distance than the fuel level actually supports, leading to unexpected low-fuel situations.
- **Solution:** Consult a technician to check for software updates or faulty fuel sending units if the digital range calculation drifts.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission sends power smoothly to the rear wheels, offering balanced gear ratios suited for both highway cruising and stop-and-go city driving.

## Brakes

### Wheel Speed Sensors

Steering and stability systems rely heavily on wheel speed feedback, making sensor longevity an important maintenance item to monitor during your pre-purchase inspection.

**Wheel Speed Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent failure of wheel speed sensors can disable major safety features including the anti-lock braking system (ABS), blind-spot monitoring, and stability control.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the faulty sensor using an OBD-II scanner and replace the defective wheel speed sensor unit.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V497000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the bolts loosen and fall out, the driveshaft may separate causing a loss of drive, thereby increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the transmission flange, securing bolts, and clean the shaft seal, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V693000: Unknown Or Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Failure to identify loose safety related parts can increase the risk of crash and injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 21V055000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the software communication module for the automatic emergency call system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V222000: Unknown Or Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Failure to identify loose safety related parts can increase the risk of crash and injury.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will complete the final quality inspection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V715000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The integrated backup battery compartment located underneath the front passenger's seat may not have its own cover, potentially exposing the battery when the seat is moved forward. If conductive material, such as metal, unintentionally drops into the opening and contacts the terminals on the battery, an electrical short can occur, increasing the risk of a.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and Mercedes-Benz dealers will install covers over the battery compartment area, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V489000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** An unexpected loss of power steering assist can increase the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and Metris dealers will replace the steering unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V066000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Fluid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A detached brake fluid reservoir can leak brake fluid, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the brake fluid reservoir mounting, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V099000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A defective memory chip in the rearview camera may cause the display image to freeze. A frozen rearview image does not show the live view of what is currently behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
