### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz Metris demonstrates strong reliability, especially concerning its core powertrain components. The 2.0L turbocharged engine and 7-speed automatic transmission are generally trouble-free. Areas that have seen some owner-reported issues include the infotainment system, which can experience glitches, and occasional reports of premature battery drain, potentially due to parasitic electrical draws. These electrical issues, while not universal, are the most common technical concerns. Overall, the Metris is a dependable work vehicle, with most potential problems being manageable and not indicative of systemic failures across the model.
