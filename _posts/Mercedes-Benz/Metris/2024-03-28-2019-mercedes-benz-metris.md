**Introduction**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Metris is a commercial van that offers versatility and functionality. It is available with a choice of two engines: a 2.0L gasoline engine and a 3.0L turbodiesel engine. Engine options are paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The Metris has a spacious interior and a variety of safety features. Customers appreciate the van's fuel efficiency, spaciousness, and ease of handling, while common complaints include limited cargo space and a lack of some desired features.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Gasoline Engine**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks around the intake manifold, causing engine overheating and possible engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, poor fuel economy, and rough idling.
        * Solution: Clean the intake valves manually.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **3.0L Turbodiesel Engine**
    * **Variable Valve Timing Solenoid Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, reduced engine power, and increased fuel consumption.
        * Solution: Replace the variable valve timing solenoid.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Valve Sticking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, black smoke from the exhaust, and increased fuel consumption.
        * Solution: Clean or replace the EGR valve.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks or drops in fluid level, causing transmission slipping and damage.
        * Solution: Identify and repair the source of the leak, such as faulty seals or gaskets.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Shift Solenoid Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, harsh shifting, or difficulty engaging gears.
        * Solution: Replace the faulty shift solenoid.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Struts**
    * **Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the front suspension, especially during turns.
        * Solution: Replace the strut mount bearing.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Suspension Leaf Springs**
    * **Leaf Spring Breakage**
        * Description/Symptoms: Broken leaf springs can cause the rear of the vehicle to sag, affecting handling and ride quality.
        * Solution: Replace the broken leaf spring.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * **Parasitic Draw**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly even when the vehicle is turned off, causing difficulty starting.
        * Solution: Identify and eliminate the source of the parasitic draw.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure**
    * **Alternator Diode Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunction, dim headlights, and difficulty starting the vehicle.
        * Solution: Replace the alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tail Light Assembly Malfunction**
    * **Condensation Buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulates inside the tail light assembly, causing it to malfunction and potentially leak.
        * Solution: Replace the tail light assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control System**
    * **Heater Core Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks from the heater core, causing a sweet smell in the cabin and potentially fogging up the windshield.
        * Solution: Replace the heater core.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **Instrument Cluster**
    * **Display Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: The instrument cluster display may become blank or show errors, affecting visibility of vehicle information.
        * Solution: Replace or repair the instrument cluster.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Metris has several potential issues that owners should be aware of. While some issues, such as intake manifold gasket failure and carbon buildup, can be relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others like transmission fluid leaks and alternator failure can be more serious. Overall, the severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix them can range from a few hundred dollars to over a thousand. However, with proper maintenance and repairs, the Metris can provide reliable service.
