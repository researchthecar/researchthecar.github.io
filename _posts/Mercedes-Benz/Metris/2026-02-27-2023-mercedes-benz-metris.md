# 2023 Mercedes-Benz Metris Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a 2023 Mercedes-Benz Metris requires weighing its capable utility against limited complaint data from early owners. While official complaint logs are sparse, prospective buyers should still inspect critical steering and safety systems during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Gas

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing reliable power for commercial and passenger hauling duties. It generally offers solid day-to-day operation, though proper maintenance of the turbo system and cooling components is essential.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission driving the rear wheels, this setup delivers smooth gear changes and optimized highway efficiency. Routine fluid changes are recommended to ensure long-term mechanical durability under heavy commercial loads.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The power steering system utilizes electronic assist, which can occasionally experience intermittent faults based on owner reports.

**Random Power Steering Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that the power steering can occasionally fail while driving, but it may return to normal operation after restarting the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the electronic steering control module, wiring harness, and software calibrations if steering assist cuts out.

## Safety

### Steering and Advanced Safety Systems

Safety electronics oversee various collision avoidance and stability features, though rare owner reports suggest unexpected steering behavior in emergency maneuvers.

**Unintended Steering Intervention**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported that the vehicle's automated steering system appeared to take over unexpectedly during a multi-car collision avoidance situation.
- **Solution:** Ensure that all driver-assistance camera and sensor modules are properly calibrated and check for any relevant software updates at a dealership.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V444000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V881000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V463000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The electrical connections for the infotainment system may not be properly secured, which can cause the rearview camera image not to display. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the infotainment system connection, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V070000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Water may accumulate on the air bag control unit, which can cause the air bags not to deploy as intended. An air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a cover on the air bag control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz Metris presents as a practical and capable mid-size van with standard powertrain options. Because owner-reported data is limited, buyers should perform a thorough test drive paying close attention to steering assist responsiveness and advanced safety features.
