# 2019 Mercedes-Benz Metris Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Metris is a versatile mid-size van designed to bridge the gap between compact cargo haulers and full-size commercial vans. For prospective used-car buyers, it offers practical utility and European driving dynamics, though shoppers should carefully evaluate the condition of its powertrain and electronics before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate power for hauling and city driving, though buyers should ensure consistent maintenance histories.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 7-speed automatic transmission driving the rear wheels, this setup is designed for smooth highway cruising and load management. Routine fluid changes are vital for long-term durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V693000: Unknown Or Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Failure to identify loose safety related parts can increase the risk of crash and injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 21V055000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the software communication module for the automatic emergency call system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V545000: Equipment:Other:Owners/Service/Other Manual](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The incorrect manual may not describe the proper operation of the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the information package including the printed version of the operator's manual, maintenance booklet and information sheets, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V222000: Unknown Or Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Failure to identify loose safety related parts can increase the risk of crash and injury.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will complete the final quality inspection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V715000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The integrated backup battery compartment located underneath the front passenger's seat may not have its own cover, potentially exposing the battery when the seat is moved forward. If conductive material, such as metal, unintentionally drops into the opening and contacts the terminals on the battery, an electrical short can occur, increasing the risk of a.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and Mercedes-Benz dealers will install covers over the battery compartment area, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V666000: Seats:Front Assembly:Head Restraint](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The incorrectly mounted or missing spring pins can cause the upholstery carrier to come loose from the head restraint housing, which increases the risk of an injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and Mercedes-Benz dealers will replace the head restraints, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V729000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The screwed fittings between the rigid steel brake lines routed through the underbody of the vehicle and the brake hoses in the area of the front and rear axles may not have been correctly installed. The connection between the brake line and the brake hose may leak, causing a sudden loss of brake fluids, impacting braking performance and increasing the risk.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will check the affected fittings at the connection points of the brake lines to the brake hoses, correcting them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V340000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The driveshaft may not be attached to the intermediate bearing correctly, causing excessive wear to the driveshaft joint and excessive vibrations. Damage to the driveshaft joint could result in a loss of drive, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners and dealers will inspect the drive shaft, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Metris is a capable commercial or passenger van option for buyers needing a smaller footprint than a full-size Sprinter. While major complaint trends are minimal, thorough pre-purchase inspections of the engine and transmission are always recommended.
