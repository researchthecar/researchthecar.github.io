# 2016 Mercedes-Benz Metris Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz Metris bridges the gap between midsize and full-size commercial vans, offering strong utility and car-like handling for business or passenger transport. However, prospective buyers should inspect used models carefully for recurring mechanical complaints, fuel system odors, and electrical anomalies.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

Powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing approximately 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing capable pulling power for a midsize van. While generally robust, buyers should monitor for fuel line leaks and associated odors.

**Fuel Leaks and Odors**
- **Symptoms:** A strong smell of fuel inside or outside the vehicle, often accompanied by visible fuel weeping or leaking from lower engine hoses.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines and lower hoses, and replace damaged or worn components as specified in applicable service bulletins and recalls.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 7-speed automatic transmission paired with a rear-wheel-drive layout, offering smooth highway cruising and responsive gear selection for commercial applications.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The electric power steering system provides light, maneuverable handling in tight spaces, though owners have reported unexpected losses of power assist due to hardware failure.

**Power Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering functionality while driving, making the steering wheel extremely heavy and difficult to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electric power steering motor mounting bolts, wiring, and steering rack assembly, and replace faulty hardware or steering units.

## Safety

### Seat Belts and Airbag Sensors

Occupant safety systems include standard airbags and pretensioners, but owners should check for warning lights indicating wiring harness wear.

**Seatbelt Pretensioner Wiring Chafing**
- **Symptoms:** Airbag warning light illuminates on the dashboard due to a short circuit in the seatbelt pretensioner wiring harness.
- **Solution:** Inspect factory wiring paths for chafing or wear, repair damaged wires, and secure the harness to prevent future friction.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V342000: Power Train:Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The weld may fail and result in a loss of power to the wheels, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear differential gears, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V055000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the software communication module for the automatic emergency call system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V715000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The integrated backup battery compartment located underneath the front passenger's seat may not have its own cover, potentially exposing the battery when the seat is moved forward. If conductive material, such as metal, unintentionally drops into the opening and contacts the terminals on the battery, an electrical short can occur, increasing the risk of a.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and Mercedes-Benz dealers will install covers over the battery compartment area, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V290000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Loss of power steering assist would require a higher steering effort, especially at lower speeds, which may increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the aluminum bolts with steels bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V837000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the affected hose and clamp, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V335000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The transmission support may not have been properly tightened to the vehicle body, potentially resulting in damage to the driveshaft. Damage to the driveshaft could cause a loss of power to the wheels, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the transmission support mounting bolts, replacing and tightening them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V595000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The improperly welded pins may lose contact, deactivating the power steering assist and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the electric power steering rack, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V272000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The improper software may cause problems with the electronic stability control system, the air bag control unit or the engine management software. These problems can result in an increased risk of a crash or an increased risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and Mercedes-Benz and Freightliner dealers will update the affected control units on the vehicles, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz Metris is a versatile and capable van for work fleets or large families, but prospective used-car buyers should perform a thorough mechanical inspection. Pay close attention to the engine compartment for fuel odors, verify that power steering hardware is secure, and check that all safety recalls have been completed by a certified dealer.
