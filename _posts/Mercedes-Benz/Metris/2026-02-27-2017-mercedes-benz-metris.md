# 2017 Mercedes-Benz Metris Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Mercedes-Benz Metris, buyers will find a capable mid-size van designed for commercial utility or family transport. While it offers unique packaging and strong hauling capabilities, prospective owners should review owner-reported reliability trends carefully. Certain mechanical and electrical areas, particularly concerning steering and engine support systems, have surfaced in owner feedback and warrant close inspection during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing balanced power for cargo and passenger hauling. It is generally robust, though auxiliary emission and ventilation components require monitoring for premature wear.

**Crankcase Ventilation Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light with diagnostic trouble code P052E, indicating issues with emissions and oil vapor control components.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed crankcase ventilation assembly, including associated tubing and valves.
- **Cost:** $1000 plus labor

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth power delivery to the rear wheels, tailored for commercial and everyday driving duties. It maintains a solid reputation when properly serviced with regular fluid changes.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering System

Owner reports indicate potential vulnerabilities in the electric power steering rack and control modules, which can lead to a sudden loss of steering assist.

**Steering Rack and Control Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering assist, making the vehicle difficult to maneuver and control, often accompanied by warning messages.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electric power steering control unit and replace the steering rack assembly if internal failure is confirmed.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera System

The safety category includes visibility and driver-assistance features, where intermittent backup camera performance has been noted by owners.

**Backup Camera Display Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Rearview camera displays a blank, frozen, or intermittent image instead of a live feed while reversing.
- **Solution:** Diagnose camera wiring, display module, or software connections and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V497000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the bolts loosen and fall out, the driveshaft may separate causing a loss of drive, thereby increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the transmission flange, securing bolts, and clean the shaft seal, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V055000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the software communication module for the automatic emergency call system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V715000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The integrated backup battery compartment located underneath the front passenger's seat may not have its own cover, potentially exposing the battery when the seat is moved forward. If conductive material, such as metal, unintentionally drops into the opening and contacts the terminals on the battery, an electrical short can occur, increasing the risk of a.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and Mercedes-Benz dealers will install covers over the battery compartment area, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V290000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Loss of power steering assist would require a higher steering effort, especially at lower speeds, which may increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the aluminum bolts with steels bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V837000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the affected hose and clamp, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V335000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The transmission support may not have been properly tightened to the vehicle body, potentially resulting in damage to the driveshaft. Damage to the driveshaft could cause a loss of power to the wheels, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the transmission support mounting bolts, replacing and tightening them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V595000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The improperly welded pins may lose contact, deactivating the power steering assist and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the electric power steering rack, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V935000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As a result, in the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, the air bag may not vent properly. An improperly vented air bag may not protect the front passenger as expected, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and authorized Mercedes-Benz Sprinter dealers will replace the front passenger air bag, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz Metris is a versatile option for buyers needing a mid-size van, but due diligence is essential. Prioritize vehicles with a documented service history, paying close attention to steering functionality and engine auxiliary systems. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified technician can help uncover potential electronic or mechanical concerns before purchase.
