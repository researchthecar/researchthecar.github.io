# 2021 Mercedes-Benz Metris Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz Metris is a mid-size commercial and passenger van designed to bridge the gap between compact vans and full-size cargo haulers. Buyers looking at the used market will find a capable daily worker with strong towing and payload capabilities, though overall owner complaint data is sparse for this model year. As with any modern European commercial vehicle, a thorough pre-purchase inspection is recommended to ensure all systems are functioning properly before buying.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for hauling and city driving. It offers a balance of utility and efficiency, though regular maintenance is key to long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides smooth gear changes and helps optimize fuel efficiency on the highway. It is paired exclusively with the rear-wheel-drive layout on this model year.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V513000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these assemblies fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V106000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat frame base may not have been welded properly. An improperly welded seat frame base may have reduced stability, which can increase the risk of injury to the occupant in a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the seat frame base, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V066000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Fluid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A detached brake fluid reservoir can leak brake fluid, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the brake fluid reservoir mounting, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V134000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the fuel hose connections, and replace the clamps and fuel hose, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V360000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A disabled eCall system would prevent a vehicle occupant from contacting the emergency services call center in an emergency, potentially delaying emergency responders and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V444000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V463000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Metris&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The electrical connections for the infotainment system may not be properly secured, which can cause the rearview camera image not to display. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the infotainment system connection, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz Metris presents a solid option for commercial buyers and families needing a spacious van with car-like maneuverability. Because available owner complaint data is minimal for this model year, prospective buyers should focus on verifying service history and checking for any outstanding recall work at a local dealer.
