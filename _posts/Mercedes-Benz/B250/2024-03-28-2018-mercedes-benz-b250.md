**Introduction to the 2018 Mercedes-Benz B250**

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz B250 is a compact luxury hatchback that offers a comfortable and refined driving experience. It comes standard with a 2.0-liter inline-four engine paired with a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission, producing 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The B250 is also available with a 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system.

People appreciate the B250 for its luxurious interior, spacious cabin, and advanced safety features. However, some users have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**2.0-Liter Inline-Four Engine**

* **Excessive Engine Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loud rattling or knocking noises coming from the engine during acceleration or idle.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Visible oil leaks from the engine gaskets or seals.
    * Solution: Replace the leaking gaskets or seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**

* **Delayed Gear Shifts:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay when shifting gears, especially between first and second gear.
    * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty battery or check for electrical system malfunctions.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning or freezing of the infotainment system.
    * Solution: Update the infotainment software or replace the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the windshield wiper motor, resulting in inoperative wipers.
    * Solution: Replace the wiper motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Panel Misalignment:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misalignment of the panoramic sunroof panels, leading to wind noise or water leaks.
    * Solution: Realign the panels or replace the sunroof assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2018 Mercedes-Benz B250 is a reliable vehicle with a comfortable interior and advanced features. However, a few potential issues have been reported, such as engine noise, oil leaks, delayed gear shifts, and battery drain. The cost of repairs can range from minor expenses to more expensive fixes, depending on the severity of the issue.
