**Introduction:**

The 2014 Mercedes-Benz B250 is a compact luxury hatchback powered by a turbocharged 2.0L inline-four engine (M270) paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission (7G-DCT). It offers a blend of performance, comfort, and practicality. Owners appreciate its spacious cabin, responsive engine, and advanced safety features. However, like any vehicle, it has experienced various issues.

**Engine**
**M270 2.0L Inline-Four + 7G-DCT**

* **Excessive oil consumption:** Engine burns excessive oil, leading to low oil levels and potentially catastrophic failure.
  * Solution: Replacing piston rings or the entire engine.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $8,000+
  * Recalls: 2014060001

**Transmission**
**7G-DCT**

* **Shifting hesitation or jerkiness:** Transmission lags or engages with a hard shift, causing discomfort and uneven acceleration.
  * Solution: Software update or rebuilding the transmission.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension strut failure:** Worn strut mountings or faulty shock absorbers, resulting in a rattling or knocking sound and poor ride quality.
  * Solution: Replacing struts and mounts.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,200

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Premature battery discharge, leading to starting issues and component malfunctions.
  * Solution: Identifying and fixing electrical drain sources.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical module failures:** Malfunctioning electronic modules, such as the control unit for the transmission or engine, cause intermittent problems and error messages.
  * Solution: Software updates or replacing the affected module.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Exterior**

* **LED headlight condensation:** Moisture buildup inside the headlight housing, causing poor visibility and potential short circuits.
  * Solution: Replacing or repairing the headlight assembly.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200

**Interior**

* **Windshield wiper motor noise:** Excessive noise or vibration from the windshield wiper motor, especially at low speeds.
  * Solution: Replacing the wiper motor.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

* **Center console rattle:** Loose or poorly fitted components in the center console, causing rattling or distracting noises.
  * Solution: Tightening or replacing affected parts.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400

**Summary:**

While the 2014 Mercedes-Benz B250 offers many positive attributes, it has also experienced several issues ranging from minor inconveniences to serious mechanical problems. The most significant issues include excessive oil consumption, transmission shifting issues, and suspension strut failures. The cost of repairs can vary widely depending on the severity of the problem and the parts required. Overall, owners should be aware of potential issues and factor in the cost of repairs when considering purchasing or owning this vehicle.
