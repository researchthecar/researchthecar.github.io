## Introduction to the 2019 Mercedes-Benz B250

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz B250 is a compact luxury hatchback that slots below the A-Class and CLA-Class sedans. It's offered with a turbocharged 2.0L inline-4 engine that produces 221 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission.

The B250 has received praise for its spacious and well-equipped interior, comfortable ride, and strong performance. However, it has also been criticized for its high price and lack of some features found in competing models.

## Engine

* **2.0L Inline-4**

**Exhaust Manifold Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Exhaust smell, loss of power, reduced fuel efficiency
Solution: Replace exhaust manifold
Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200

## Transmission

* **8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

**Transmission Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power
Solution: Replace transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 19V922000

**Clutch Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
Solution: Replace clutch
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000

## Suspension

* **Front Suspension**

**Strut Wear**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, poor handling
Solution: Replace struts
Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000

**Control Arm Bushing Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Vibration, clunking noises
Solution: Replace control arm bushings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600

## Electrical

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely
Solution: Replace battery or identify and fix electrical drain
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical system malfunction
Solution: Replace alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200

## Exterior

**Sunroof Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into cabin through sunroof
Solution: Repair or replace sunroof seal
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

## Interior

**Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: AC not working, heat not working
Solution: Repair or replace climate control unit
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Seat Belt Sensor Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Seat belt warning light stays on
Solution: Replace seat belt sensor
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500

## Summary

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz B250 is a well-rounded compact luxury hatchback, but it has its share of potential issues. The most severe and expensive problem is transmission slipping, which can lead to a complete transmission failure. Other issues, such as exhaust manifold leaks and sunroof leaks, are less serious but can still be costly to fix. Overall, the B250 has a moderate reliability rating, and potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues before purchasing.
