# 2016 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG, buyers are looking at a high-performance compact luxury sedan powered by a potent hand-built turbocharged engine. While offering exhilarating driving dynamics and aggressive styling, these performance-oriented luxury vehicles require diligent maintenance and careful inspection. Owners have reported a mix of electronic glitches, powertrain quirks, and exterior body concerns that prospective buyers should keep in mind during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (AMG)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces an impressive 355 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering performance for its size. High-output forced-induction engines demand strict adherence to maintenance schedules and premium fuel to ensure long-term durability.

**Check Engine Light & Stalling Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine warnings, rough idling, or unexpected engine stalling during operation.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan for fault codes (such as thermostat or emissions-related codes) and inspect engine sensors and crankcase systems.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routes power through an AMG Performance 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system for rapid gear changes. While performance is sharp, owners should verify smooth shifting behavior during a test drive.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle unexpectedly drops out of gear into neutral while driving, displaying a warning message instructing the driver not to shift into gear and to visit a dealer.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning of the transmission control module and software updates or hardware inspections by an authorized specialist are recommended.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Low-Profile Tires

Firm sport-tuned suspension setups provide exceptional handling responsiveness but leave low-profile tires and large alloy wheels more vulnerable to pothole damage.

**Side Wall Bubbles and Blowouts**
- **Symptoms:** Frequent tire sidewall bubbling, sudden deflation, or blowout when encountering rough road surfaces due to low-profile tires.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged low-profile run-flat tires and inspect alloy wheels for bending or structural damage.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Dashboard Displays

Advanced onboard electronics control major convenience and driver information features, making a thorough electrical check essential during your used car inspection.

**Console and Screen Blackout**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard screens, radio consoles, or infotainment displays randomly turning off, losing power, or failing to respond to controls.
- **Solution:** Check battery health, wiring harnesses, and software firmware versions; software resets or module replacements may be required.

## Exterior

### Sunroof / Moonroof Assembly

Exterior panels and glass components generally maintain a sleek aesthetic, though panoramic sunroof assemblies warrant careful inspection for structural integrity.

**Spontaneous Glass Fracture**
- **Symptoms:** Loud popping or explosive sound accompanied by the sunroof glass cracking or shattering completely while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the panoramic roof panel for any micro-cracks, and replace damaged or compromised glass assemblies.
