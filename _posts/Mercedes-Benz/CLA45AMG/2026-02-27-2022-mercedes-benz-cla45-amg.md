# 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG, buyers are looking at a high-performance luxury compact sedan known for its aggressive styling and potent turbocharged four-cylinder engine. While these sporty models offer engaging driving dynamics and upscale cabin appointments, prospective owners should review available owner feedback regarding occasional unexpected exterior and body issues.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M139)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces an impressive 382 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque. It delivers thrilling acceleration and robust performance for enthusiastic drivers, though proper maintenance is crucial.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routes power through an AMG Performance 4MATIC+ all-wheel-drive system. It offers lightning-fast gear changes suited to spirited driving conditions.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The high-performance braking system provides strong stopping power, though routine inspections are vital given the vehicle's sporty mission.

**Perceived Brake Responsiveness Delay**
- **Symptoms:** An owner noted a situation during sudden highway traffic slowdowns where applying the brakes felt temporarily insufficient or unresponsive.
- **Solution:** Have the brake pads, rotors, and hydraulic system professionally inspected for wear or potential fluid issues during a pre-purchase test drive.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior complaints center primarily around glass durability concerning the optional panoramic roof assembly.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that the panoramic glass roof can suddenly shatter or explode into pieces while traveling at highway speeds without any apparent impact from outside objects.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass thoroughly for any pre-existing stress fractures before purchase, and consult a dealer regarding potential warranty coverage if replacement is necessary.

## Interior

### Sunroof Interior Trim

Cabin components remain largely trouble-free outside of rare structural glass incidents associated with overhead components.

**Sudden Glass Breakage Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Occupants may hear a loud, unexpected bang from above while driving under bridges or on open highways as the roof panel breaks.
- **Solution:** Examine the interior sunshade and surrounding headliner area for signs of glass debris or structural stress.

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG delivers exceptional performance and luxury in a compact footprint, making it an exciting choice for enthusiasts.
