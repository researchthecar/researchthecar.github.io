**Introduction:**

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG is a compact luxury sedan powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-4 engine that produces 382 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission. The AMG Dynamic Plus Package adds a limited-slip front differential, adjustable suspension, active exhaust, and increased top speed.

**Engine**

**2.0L Inline-4 Turbo**

* **Crankshaft Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive engine noise, vibration, or loss of power
    * Solution: Replace crankshaft
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, decreased fuel economy, abnormal exhaust smoke
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**

* **Clutch Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, transmission slipping out of gear, shuddering during acceleration
    * Solution: Replace clutch pack and potentially other transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises when turning or going over bumps
    * Solution: Replace or lubricate front suspension components (e.g., sway bar bushings, control arms)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead after short periods of parking
    * Solution: Troubleshoot electrical system for parasitic draw and repair/replace faulty components (e.g., alternator, battery, starter)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, navigation issues, loss of audio
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of infotainment unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Loose Exterior Trim:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Trims or badges falling off or rattling
    * Solution: Reattach or replace trim pieces
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into cabin through sunroof
    * Solution: Replace or repair sunroof seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cracked Dashboard:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks forming on dashboard surface
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG has some reported issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. Engine issues can be severe and costly, while transmission issues can also be significant. The vehicle also has some electrical, interior, and exterior issues. Overall, the severity and cost of these issues vary, but potential owners should be aware of them before purchasing or owning one of these vehicles.
