# 2020 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG delivers high-performance luxury with aggressive styling and a potent turbocharged engine. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported issues concerning electrical components, body glass, and powertrain performance when evaluating a used model.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M139)

Powered by a hand-built 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing an impressive 382 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque, this high-output powerplant offers thrilling acceleration. Buyers should inspect service history carefully, as high-stress forced induction requires meticulous maintenance.

**Engine Stutter and Performance Issues**
- **Symptoms:** The check engine light illuminates, followed by engine stuttering or jerking while reversing or driving at low speeds on surface streets.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic or dealership diagnose the ignition, fuel, or sensor systems to isolate the root cause of the stutter.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission paired with performance 4MATIC+ all-wheel drive, it delivers rapid, aggressive gear shifts tailored for spirited driving. Overall mechanical durability is generally solid, though smooth low-speed operation relies heavily on software calibration and clutch health.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Service Brakes

Handles stopping power, ABS modules, and hydraulic brake lines.

**Brake System Recall Delays**
- **Symptoms:** Owners experience difficulties securing prompt remedy parts for open manufacturer safety recall campaigns related to hydraulic brake components.
- **Solution:** Contact an authorized dealership to check the status of open recall repairs and schedule service as parts become available.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera and Trunk Mechanism

Covers driver assistance systems, visibility aids, and associated trunk-lid mechanics.

**Stuck Backup Camera and Trunk Hatch Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera housing emblem on the trunk remains permanently open, and the camera feed disappears from the infotainment screen after a few seconds when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the trunk-mounted camera actuator mechanism or associated wiring harness components.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof and Windshield Wipers

Encompasses exterior glass, weather sealing, and wiper system functionality.

**Unexpected Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof shatters unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds with no prior damage or external impact observed.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered glass panel and inspect the frame for structural stress or alignment issues.

**Random Windshield Wiper Activation**
- **Symptoms:** The front windshield wipers activate randomly by themselves without manual input and fail to turn off using the control stalk.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the wiper switch stalk or the front SAM (Signal Acquisition and Actuation Module) control unit.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG offers exceptional performance and luxury, but used-car buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle prior to purchase. Pay close attention to electrical features such as the backup camera and wipers, examine glass panels for any stress cracks, and verify that all open safety recalls have been properly addressed by a certified dealer.
