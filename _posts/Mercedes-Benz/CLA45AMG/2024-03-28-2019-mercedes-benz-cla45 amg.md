**Introduction:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG is a high-performance compact luxury sedan known for its aggressive styling, powerful engine, and advanced technology. It boasts a 2.0L inline-4 turbocharged engine that produces 382 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. However, some owners have encountered specific issues with the vehicle.

**Engine:**

**2.0L Inline-4 Turbocharged Engine:**

**Issue:** **Engine Stalling**
* Description/Symptoms: The engine suddenly shuts off while driving or idling, causing loss of power.
* Solution: Replace the fuel pump control module.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission:**

**Issue:** **Transmission Slipping**
* Description/Symptoms: The transmission experiences sudden loss of power while shifting gears, accompanied by jerking or shuddering sensations.
* Solution: Repair or replace the clutch packs and/or valve body.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

**Issue:** **Infotainment System Glitch**
* Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience occasional freezing, glitches, or unresponsive touchscreens.
* Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment unit.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG is a well-regarded vehicle for its performance and luxury features. However, some owners have encountered issues related to the engine's fuel system, transmission slipping, and infotainment system glitches. While these issues may not affect all CLA45 AMG models, it is important to be aware of them when considering purchasing or maintaining the vehicle.
