# 2018 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG

### Overview
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG, a high-performance compact luxury sedan, generally offers a robust and engaging driving experience. Built on the A-Class platform, it combines AMG's performance tuning with the practicality of a four-door coupe. While its potent turbocharged engine and sophisticated all-wheel-drive system are highlights, like any performance vehicle, it can encounter specific issues. This data focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related reliability concerns reported by owners and technicians, providing insights into potential problem areas and their associated costs for the 2018 model year.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M133)
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**PCV System Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil leaks, potential engine misfires.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve or system.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT DCT
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, failure to engage gears, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Reprogramming or replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500

**Clutch Pack Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, slipping gears, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch packs.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,000

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the COMAND unit.

### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of parasitic draw, or battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG is a performance-oriented vehicle with a generally good reliability record for its class. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to its high-output powertrain, specifically potential issues with the turbocharger, transmission control module, and clutch packs, which can be costly to repair. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and intermittent battery drain, are also noted. However, many owners report satisfaction with the vehicle's overall dependability, provided regular maintenance is performed. Addressing potential issues proactively can help mitigate larger repair bills.
