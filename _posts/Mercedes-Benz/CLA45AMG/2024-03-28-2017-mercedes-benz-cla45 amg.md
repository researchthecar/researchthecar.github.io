**Introduction:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG is a high-performance compact sedan that boasts cutting-edge technology and a thrilling driving experience. It is powered by a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine producing 375 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque, paired exclusively with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The CLA45 AMG offers impressive acceleration and handling, with a 0-60 mph time of 4.2 seconds. It features a sleek and stylish exterior, complemented by a well-equipped and premium interior.

However, some owners have reported specific issues with the 2017 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Engine**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Engine hesitation, stalling, difficulty starting
Solution: Replace failed fuel injectors
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

* **Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive boost pressure, reduced engine power, check engine light
Solution: Replace faulty wastegate actuator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Gear Slippage:**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power, check engine light
Solution: Replace faulty transmission clutches or solenoids
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise, vibration, poor handling
Solution: Replace worn or damaged strut mounts
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
Description/Symptoms: Battery fails to hold charge, difficulty starting the vehicle
Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system faults or replace faulty components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment Screen Black Out:**
Description/Symptoms: Central infotainment display goes black, unresponsive to inputs
Solution: Software update or replacement of infotainment unit
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Dimming or Flickering:**
Description/Symptoms: Headlights dim or flicker, reduced visibility at night
Solution: Replace faulty ballast or bulbs
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
Description/Symptoms: Climate control system fails to regulate temperature, excessive noise from vents
Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty climate control components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG has received mostly positive feedback for its performance and styling. However, there are some known issues with the engine, transmission, electrical, exterior, and interior components that owners should be aware of. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost to repair can range from minor to significant. It is recommended to have the vehicle serviced by a qualified mechanic if any issues arise.
