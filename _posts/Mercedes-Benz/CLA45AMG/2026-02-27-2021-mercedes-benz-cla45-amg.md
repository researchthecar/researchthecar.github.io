# 2021 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG, buyers are looking at a high-performance luxury compact that pairs aggressive styling with turbocharged thrills. While owner feedback is generally limited, prospective buyers should review available complaint data carefully to understand potential ownership hurdles. Keeping up with routine inspections on high-performance powertrains and complex vehicle systems remains essential for long-term enjoyment.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M139)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 (M139) engine produces an impressive 382 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating performance for this luxury sport sedan, though buyers should ensure proper maintenance records are available.

**Loss of Acceleration Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the vehicle suddenly loses power and fails to accelerate properly while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician or dealership inspect the powertrain management system, throttle body, and associated sensors for fault codes or required software updates.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts paired with the performance-oriented 4MATIC+ all-wheel-drive system. It offers smooth operation under normal driving conditions, though regular fluid changes are vital for longevity.

## Suspension

### Front Suspension Ball Joint

Suspension components handle significant cornering loads in high-performance trims, making periodic mechanical inspections crucial.

**Ball Joint Failure**
- **Symptoms:** An unexpected failure of the front suspension ball joint can cause handling instability or separation of the wheel assembly at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect front suspension components, control arms, and ball joints regularly for wear, play, or structural damage, and replace compromised parts immediately.

## Electrical

### Tail Lamp Assembly

Electrical systems control lighting, infotainment, and driver assists; minor weather-sealing issues can occasionally affect exterior lamps.

**Moisture Accumulation**
- **Symptoms:** Water precipitation or condensation forms inside the rear tail light housing due to an imperfect seal.
- **Solution:** Inspect the tail light housing seals and replace the gasket or the entire lamp assembly if moisture intrusion persists.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Glass

Exterior trim and glass components require a close visual inspection during a used car pre-purchase evaluation to check for stress cracks or improper sealing.

**Sudden Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass may unexpectedly fracture or explode while driving down the highway without external impact.
- **Solution:** Have the glass panel replaced and inspect the sunroof frame alignment and wind deflector for stress points.
