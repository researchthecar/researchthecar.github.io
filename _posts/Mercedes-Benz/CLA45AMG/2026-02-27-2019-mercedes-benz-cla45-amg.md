# 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG offers high-performance luxury, but prospective buyers should review common owner-reported trouble spots before purchasing. While powertrain and fuel system concerns have surfaced in related compact Mercedes models, careful pre-purchase inspections can help uncover hidden wear and potential mechanical issues.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine delivers high-output performance characteristic of AMG models, pushing substantial horsepower and torque. While thrilling to drive, owners should monitor the fuel delivery and injection components for potential leaks and seal failures.

**Fuel Injector Failure and Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported fuel odors, engine shut-down, and fuel leaks around the fuel rail and injector seals, sometimes resulting in expensive out-of-pocket repair bills.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel injector seals, O-rings, and fuel injectors for leaks or degradation; replace failed components as necessary.
- **Cost:** $4,154.67

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes tailored to high-performance driving. Buyers should test drive the vehicle thoroughly to ensure smooth shifting behavior and check for any unexpected gear engagement anomalies.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience the transmission unexpectedly shifting into neutral while driving at various speeds, resulting in a sudden loss of drive power and illumination of warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect transmission control modules, software calibrations, and shift linkage components.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

The suspension and wheel setup is tuned for aggressive handling and a firm ride quality. Due to low-profile tires and large alloy wheels, components are susceptible to damage from road hazards and potholes.

**Wheel Cracks and Tire Sidewall Bulges**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted rims cracking during moderate low-speed driving and recurring tire sidewall bulges, leading to unexpected flat tires.
- **Solution:** Inspect factory wheels for structural fractures and check tires regularly for bubbles, bulges, or premature wear.
