**2021 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG is a high-performance compact sedan with a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-four engine producing 382 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission and all-wheel drive. The CLA45 AMG is praised for its thrilling acceleration, sharp handling, and luxurious cabin, but it is also criticized for its firm ride and limited rear-seat space.

### **Engine**

**2.0-Liter Turbocharged Inline-Four**

- **Loss of Power Under Acceleration:** The engine may experience a sudden loss of power during acceleration. This is caused by a faulty wastegate actuator, which controls the turbocharger boost.
  - **Solution:** Replace the wastegate actuator.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500
- **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger may fail prematurely, leading to a loss of power and excessive smoke from the exhaust. This is caused by excessive heat and stress on the turbocharger.
  - **Solution:** Replace the turbocharger.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $5,000

### **Transmission**

**8-Speed Dual-Clutch**

- **Delayed Gear Shifts:** The transmission may exhibit delayed gear shifts, especially when upshifting or downshifting. This is caused by a faulty transmission control module or a problem with the clutch packs.
  - **Solution:** Reprogram the transmission control module or replace the clutch packs.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000
- **Transmission Shutter:** The transmission may shudder or vibrate while driving, particularly at low speeds. This is caused by worn or damaged clutch discs.
  - **Solution:** Replace the clutch discs.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+

### **Suspension**

- **No Issues Found**

### **Electrical**

- **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain prematurely, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle. This is caused by a faulty module or parasitic drain.
  - **Solution:** Find and repair the faulty module or identify and eliminate the parasitic drain.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+
- **Infotainment System Malfunction:** The infotainment system may malfunction, including issues with navigation, radio, or Bluetooth connectivity. This is caused by a software glitch or a faulty component.
  - **Solution:** Update the software or replace the faulty component.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $1,000+

### **Exterior**

- **No Issues Found**

### **Interior**

- **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system may malfunction, including issues with temperature control, fan speed, or air flow. This is caused by a faulty control module or a problem with the sensors.
  - **Solution:** Replace the control module or sensors.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
- **Panoramic Sunroof Leaks:** The panoramic sunroof may leak water into the cabin during heavy rain. This is caused by a faulty seal or drain system.
  - **Solution:** Replace the seal or repair the drain system.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+

### **Summary**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz CLA45 AMG has several potential issues that owners should be aware of, particularly with the engine and transmission. While some issues can be resolved for a relatively low cost, others can be quite expensive to repair. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues should be considered when evaluating the vehicle's overall value and reliability.
