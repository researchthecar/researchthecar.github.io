### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Mercedes-Benz GLK300 is generally considered a reliable luxury compact SUV. Its powertrain, suspension, and core structural components have proven durable with no widespread critical failures reported. The most commonly cited issues tend to be related to the infotainment system, which can experience glitches or connectivity problems, and occasional battery drain issues, which are not uncommon in vehicles with complex electrical systems. These electrical gremlins are typically manageable with diagnostics and software updates or component replacement. Overall, for its age and luxury segment, the GLK300 offers a dependable ownership experience, provided routine maintenance is followed and potential electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
