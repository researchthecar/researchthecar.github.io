# 2002 Mercedes-Benz E320

### Overview
The 2002 Mercedes-Benz E320, part of the W211 generation, is generally regarded as a solid luxury sedan with a focus on comfort and performance. It was available with a robust V6 engine and a smooth automatic transmission. While many owners report high satisfaction, like any vehicle of this age, certain components can experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented common problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective buyers or current owners looking to maintain their vehicle. The E320 offers a blend of German engineering and luxury amenities, making it a desirable option in the used market.

---


## Engine

### 3.2L M112 V6
**Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, sweet smell of coolant, engine misfires, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold gaskets.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Oil Cooler Seal Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil in coolant reservoir, coolant in oil, potential for engine damage if not addressed.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil cooler seals.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Camshaft Position Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, rough idling, difficulty starting, stalling.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty camshaft position sensor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Transmission

### 5G-Tronic (722.6) Automatic
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, slipping gears, 'limp mode', transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement or repair of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Conductor Plate Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, no gear engagement, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the conductor plate.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Front Control Arm Bushings Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Air Suspension (Optional - ABC System)**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, hydraulic leaks, 'ABC Malfunction' warning.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking struts, pumps, or hoses.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $3000+


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow window movement, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Xenon headlights not turning on or flickering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight ballast.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Keyless Go System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Key fob not recognized, difficulty starting the car.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of keyless go module or antennas.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Exterior

### Body and Trim
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Cost:** N/A


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**Pixelated Instrument Cluster Display**
- **Symptoms:** Faded or missing pixels on the odometer and other displays.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Heated Seat Element Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Heated seats not working.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the heating element or seat module.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2002 Mercedes-Benz E320 is a well-built vehicle with a generally good reliability record for its era. The primary areas to be mindful of are potential leaks from the engine's oil cooler seals and intake manifold gaskets, and common transmission issues like TCM or conductor plate failures, which can be costly to repair. Electrical gremlins, particularly with window regulators and the instrument cluster display, are also noted. Owners with the optional Active Body Control (ABC) suspension should be prepared for potentially significant repair costs. Despite these potential issues, with diligent maintenance and addressing problems promptly, the E320 can remain a comfortable and reliable luxury sedan.
