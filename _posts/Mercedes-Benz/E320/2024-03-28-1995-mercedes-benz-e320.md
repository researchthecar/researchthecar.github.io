**Introduction:**

The 1995 Mercedes-Benz E320 is a luxury sedan powered by a 3.2L V6 engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. It produces 217 horsepower and 229 lb-ft of torque. The E320 was known for its comfortable ride, spacious cabin, and refined driving experience but had some reported reliability issues.

**Engine**

**3.2L V6**

* Valve cover gasket leaks: Oil leaks from the valve cover gaskets.
    * Replace valve cover gaskets.
    * $500 - $1,000+
    * Recall: MB96117
* Head gasket leaks: Coolant leaks from the head gaskets.
    * Replace head gaskets.
    * $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recall: None Found
* Timing chain tensioner failure: Timing chain tensioner fails, leading to timing chain slipping.
    * Replace timing chain tensioner and timing chain.
    * $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recall: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic (722.5)**

* Harsh shifting: Transmission shifts harshly between gears.
    * Replace transmission fluid and filter.
    * $200 - $500
    * Recall: None Found
* Slipping gears: Transmission slips out of gear.
    * Overhaul or replace transmission.
    * $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recall: None Found

**Suspension**

* Strut mounts worn: Worn strut mounts cause clunking noises and excessive vibration.
    * Replace strut mounts.
    * $500 - $800+
    * Recall: None Found
* Control arms bushings worn: Worn control arms bushings cause uneven tire wear and poor handling.
    * Replace control arms bushings.
    * $300 - $600+
    * Recall: None Found

**Electrical**

* Alternator failure: Alternator fails, leading to battery drain.
    * Replace alternator.
    * $500 - $800+
    * Recall: None Found
* Window switch malfunctions: Window switch fails, preventing windows from operating.
    * Replace window switch.
    * $100 - $200+
    * Recall: None Found

**Exterior**

* Door handle breakage: Door handle breaks, making it difficult to open the door.
    * Replace door handle.
    * $200 - $400+
    * Recall: None Found
* Tail light lenses cracking: Tail light lenses crack, allowing moisture to enter.
    * Replace tail light lenses.
    * $200 - $400+
    * Recall: None Found

**Interior**

* Sunroof leaks: Sunroof leaks water into the cabin.
    * Reseat sunroof or replace seals.
    * $500 - $1,000+
    * Recall: None Found
* Dashboard fading: Dashboard fades or cracks from sun exposure.
    * Replace dashboard.
    * $500 - $1,000+
    * Recall: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1995 Mercedes-Benz E320 is a luxurious and comfortable sedan but has some common reliability concerns. Engine issues, such as valve cover and head gasket leaks, can be expensive to repair. Transmission issues are also prevalent, with harsh shifting and slipping gears being reported. The suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components can also have issues. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific issue and the mileage of the vehicle.
