# 2007 Mercedes-Benz E320

### Overview
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz E320, part of the W211 generation, is generally regarded as a solid luxury sedan, particularly known for its comfortable ride and refined interior. This model year often features the robust 3.2L V6 diesel engine (OM642) paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. While Mercedes-Benz vehicles are known for their advanced technology and premium feel, like any complex machine, they can encounter specific issues as they age. This analysis focuses on documented common problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers of the 2007 E320.

---


## Engine

### 3.2L V6 Diesel (OM642)
**Intake Manifold Runner Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, rough idle, loss of power, rattling noise from the intake area.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold assembly or individual runner levers.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noises, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**EGR Valve Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, rough idle, reduced engine performance, increased emissions.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Transmission

### 722.9 7-Speed Automatic
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, slipping gears, failure to engage gears, limp mode, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping, delayed engagement, unusual noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Air Suspension (Optional)**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, loss of ride height, compressor running constantly, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of air springs, compressor, or lines.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500 per corner/component

**Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn control arm bushings.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**SAM (Signal Acquisition Module) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical problems, malfunctioning lights, power window issues, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential repair or replacement of the SAM unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000

**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not working, slow operation, or falling into the door.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Xenon headlights not turning on or flickering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight ballast.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Exterior

### Body and Trim
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Cost:** N/A


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**Sunroof Drain Clogs**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, musty smell, potential damage to electronics.
- **Solution:** Clearing of sunroof drains.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300

**Seat Control Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not adjusting correctly or at all.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair/replacement of seat control modules or motors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz E320 (W211) offers a compelling blend of luxury and performance, particularly with its diesel engine. While generally reliable, potential owners should be aware of common issues such as intake manifold runner problems, EGR valve faults, and transmission control module failures. The optional air suspension can also be a source of costly repairs. Electrical gremlins, particularly related to the SAM module and window regulators, are not uncommon. Despite these potential drawbacks, many of these issues are well-documented and repairable, allowing for continued enjoyment of this capable luxury sedan if properly maintained and addressed proactively.
