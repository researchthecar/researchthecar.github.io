### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz OH 1624 demonstrates a high level of technical reliability across its core powertrain and structural components. Documented issues are minimal, with the most notable potential concern being air spring leaks in vehicles equipped with the optional rear air suspension. The OM 924 LA engine and the manual transmission options are generally robust and do not present widespread failure patterns. Electrical and exterior components also appear to be well-engineered for the demands of commercial use. Overall, the OH 1624 is a dependable platform, with maintenance efforts primarily focused on routine servicing and addressing isolated incidents like air suspension issues, which are relatively straightforward to repair.
