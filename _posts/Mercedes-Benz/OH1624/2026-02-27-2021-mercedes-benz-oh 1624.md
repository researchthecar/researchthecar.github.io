### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz OH 1624 demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its core powertrain and chassis components. The OM 924 LA engine and the manual transmission options are robust and rarely present significant issues beyond standard maintenance. The primary area where minor concerns have been noted is with the optional rear air suspension, where air spring leaks can occur, leading to potential sagging and requiring component replacement. Electrical, exterior, and interior components generally perform as expected without widespread fault reports. Fleet operators can anticipate a dependable vehicle with a focus on durability, with proactive checks on the air suspension system being a minor consideration for models equipped with it.
