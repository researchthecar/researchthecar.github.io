# 2017 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT S is a high-performance sports car delivering exhilarating power and striking design. While built for the track and spirited driving, prospective used buyers should note that complex high-performance powertrains and advanced electronics demand careful inspection and diligent maintenance.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 503 horsepower and 479 lb-ft of torque. It offers blistering acceleration and responsive power delivery, though maintenance and servicing costs associated with high-performance AMG engines are notably high.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transaxle is mounted at the rear for optimal weight distribution and shifts rapidly under hard driving. Buyers should verify proper clutch engagement and smooth operation across all driving modes during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V787000: Communication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+S&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** For a full list of the affected models visit: https://static.nhtsa.gov/odi/rcl/2019/RMISC-19V787-4620.pdf An inaccurate vehicle location may delay emergency responders, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will also notify owners, and dealers will check software for a successful OTA or update the communication module software as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+S&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT S is an exceptional machine for enthusiasts seeking exotic performance and luxury. Buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection by an experienced technician to verify the health of the high-performance powertrain and complex electrical systems before purchasing.
