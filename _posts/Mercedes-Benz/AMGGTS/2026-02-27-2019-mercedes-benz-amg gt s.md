# 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT S

### Overview
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT S is a high-performance sports car renowned for its exhilarating driving dynamics and potent powertrain. As a halo model within the AMG lineup, it generally exhibits strong build quality and engineering. However, like any complex performance vehicle, specific areas can experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and enthusiasts. The AMG GT S is built with precision, but understanding its potential weak points can aid in proactive maintenance and informed ownership.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (M178)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, whistling noise from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty wastegate actuator or complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Oil Pan)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of gaskets or seals.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT DCT
**Mechatronics Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, transmission warning light, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronics unit.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000

**Clutch Pack Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, rough engagement, delayed shifts.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch packs.
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $6,000


## Suspension

### AMG RIDE CONTROL Sport Suspension
**Electronic Dampers Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh ride, clunking noises, suspension warning light, inconsistent damping.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty adaptive dampers.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, audio issues, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty module or circuit causing drain.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT S is a highly capable performance machine with a generally robust mechanical foundation. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the complex powertrain and electronic systems inherent in a vehicle of this caliber. Issues with the turbocharger wastegate actuators, transmission mechatronics, and electronic dampers have been noted. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and parasitic battery drain, can also occur. While these issues can be costly to repair, they are not universally reported across all vehicles. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are crucial for maintaining the reliability of this exotic sports car.
