# 2016 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a pre-owned 2016 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT S offers high-performance thrills and striking styling, but prospective buyers should carefully evaluate potential mechanical and electronic quirks. While owner reports to federal safety regulators provide valuable insight into real-world ownership experiences, they represent individual incidents rather than universal defects. Reviewing specific powertrain options and common trouble areas helps ensure you choose a well-maintained example.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 503 horsepower and 479 lb-ft of torque in the GT S trim. This hand-built powerplant delivers blistering acceleration and robust overall reliability when properly maintained with timely fluid services.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The rear-mounted 7-speed AMG Speedshift dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes tailored to spirited driving. While generally durable, smooth operation depends heavily on adhering to strict factory transmission fluid and filter change intervals.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

High-performance braking systems provide immense stopping power but can incur significant replacement costs when routine maintenance comes due.

**Premature Brake Noise and Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported noticing persistent squealing or grinding noises shortly after purchasing used vehicles, indicating worn friction materials.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads and rotors for wear, and replace components with quality OEM or compatible aftermarket replacements.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering

Power steering issues, while relatively uncommon, require immediate diagnostic attention if electronic error messages or stiffness occur.

**Steering Wheel Lockup**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden steering wheel lockup or electronic power steering malfunction messages while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the steering control module and update or reflash software as needed.

## Exterior

### Panoptic Sunroof

Exterior body and glass components should be thoroughly checked during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure proper fit and weather sealing.

**Sunroof Binding or Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof assembly may fail to close completely or show signs of loosening during high-speed driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof tracks for alignment issues and clear any obstructions, or replace damaged mechanical guide rails.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V787000: Communication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+S&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** For a full list of the affected models visit: https://static.nhtsa.gov/odi/rcl/2019/RMISC-19V787-4620.pdf An inaccurate vehicle location may delay emergency responders, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will also notify owners, and dealers will check software for a successful OTA or update the communication module software as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V308000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+S&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the adhesive bond fails and results in a loss of torque to the rear wheels, the vehicle operator may be unable to move the vehicle, or may experience deceleration if the vehicle is in motion, increasing the risk of a vehicle crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the carbon-fiber driveshaft, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V177000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+S&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Various control units on these vehicles may have been updated with incorrect software, potentially affecting the correct deployment of the air bags in the event of a crash. If the air bags do not deploy as intended in the event of a crash, there would be an increased risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update software in the affected control units, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

Budgeting for premium upkeep will help ensure a rewarding and reliable ownership experience.
