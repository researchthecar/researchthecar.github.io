# 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT S is a high-performance sports car delivering exotic styling and formidable twin-turbo V8 power. As a specialized high-end luxury vehicle, shoppers should approach used models with an awareness of potential maintenance and ownership costs associated with exotic European engineering.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Engine

This hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 produces approximately 515 horsepower and 494 lb-ft of torque, offering blistering acceleration and track-capable performance. Overall, the powertrain is robust when properly maintained, though high-performance operating conditions demand diligent fluid and service intervals.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

A rear-mounted 7-speed dual-clutch transaxle delivers lightning-fast gear changes and aids in achieving near-ideal weight distribution. The transmission is generally responsive and durable, though low-speed parking lot jerks can occasionally occur as characteristic of dual-clutch designs.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+S&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 22V365000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+S&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A disabled eCall system would prevent a vehicle occupant from contacting the emergency services call center in an emergency, potentially delaying emergency responders and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT S offers world-class performance and luxury, with powertrain options that are generally stout. Prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an exotic car specialist to verify mechanical health and ensure prior maintenance was meticulously performed.
