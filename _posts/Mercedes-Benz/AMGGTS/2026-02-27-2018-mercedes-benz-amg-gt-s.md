# 2018 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT S is a high-performance luxury sports car delivering exhilarating power and striking design. As a specialized high-end performance machine, prospective buyers should carefully review maintenance history and mechanical health to ensure a rewarding ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter biturbo V8 engine produces approximately 515 horsepower and 494 lb-ft of torque, offering blistering acceleration and responsive power delivery. This handcrafted performance powerplant requires meticulous adherence to service intervals to maintain long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The rear-mounted 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes and enhances overall vehicle weight distribution. It provides crisp shifts suited for spirited driving, though routine fluid maintenance is essential for optimal operation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+S&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 17V826000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+S&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the seat belt binds, slack may develop through occupant movement which may lead to increased personal injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will reconfigure the seat belt guides, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V818000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=AMG+GT+S&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The passenger occupant classification system (OCS) might not function properly, possibly causing the passenger frontal air bag to be incorrectly deactivated or incorrectly activated in the event of a crash. A passenger air bag that is incorrectly activated or deactivated in the event of a crash increases the risk of injury due to potential incorrect airbag.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the OCS software, and if necessary, replace the passenger seat cushion, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
