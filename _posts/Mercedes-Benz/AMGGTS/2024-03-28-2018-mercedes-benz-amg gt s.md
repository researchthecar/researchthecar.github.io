Introduction: The 2018 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT S is a high-performance sports car powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V8 engine that produces 515 horsepower and 494 lb-ft of torque, which is connected to a rear-mounted 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The AMG GT S is praised for its thrilling driving experience, luxurious interior, and impressive performance. However, like any vehicle, it is not immune to issues.

**Engine**

**4.0L V8**

* **Check Engine Light**
  * Description/Symptoms: The check engine light illuminates on the dashboard, indicating a potential issue with the engine.
  * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the underlying engine problem, which may vary depending on the specific cause.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Injector Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: The fuel injectors become clogged or fail, causing engine misfiring, rough idling, and reduced power.
  * Solution: Replacement of the faulty fuel injector.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping**
  * Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips while shifting gears, causing a loss of power or a rough shift.
  * Solution: Replacement of the faulty clutch packs or other internal transmission components.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Squeaky Suspension**
  * Description/Symptoms: The suspension produces a squeaking sound over bumps or uneven roads.
  * Solution: Lubrication or replacement of the suspension components, such as bushings or ball joints.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
  * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains excessively when the car is parked, leading to starting issues.
  * Solution: Diagnosis and replacement of the faulty electrical component, such as a parasitic draw or alternator.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Malfunctions**
  * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system experiences random crashes, glitches, or connectivity issues.
  * Solution: Software update or replacement of the faulty infotainment unit.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Paint Defect**
  * Description/Symptoms: The paint on certain areas of the vehicle, such as the hood or trunk, may peel or chip prematurely.
  * Solution: Repainting of the affected area.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues**
  * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may not maintain the desired temperature or experience malfunctions.
  * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the faulty climate control component, such as the compressor or temperature sensor.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT S is a high-performance vehicle that offers an exhilarating driving experience and luxurious interior. However, it is not without its issues. Some of the common problems reported by owners include engine check light illumination, fuel injector failure, transmission slipping, squeaky suspension, electrical issues, paint defects, and climate control problems. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific cause and your location. It is advisable to have the vehicle inspected by a certified mechanic to identify any potential problems and address them promptly to ensure a reliable and enjoyable ownership experience.
