# 2016 Mercedes-Benz S550e

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S550e, a plug-in hybrid variant of the flagship S-Class, aimed to blend luxury, performance, and efficiency. It featured a sophisticated powertrain combining a V6 gasoline engine with an electric motor, offering substantial power and the ability to travel on electric power alone for a limited range. As a high-end luxury vehicle, the S550e was equipped with advanced technology and premium materials. Reliability data for this specific model year and trim indicates a generally strong performance, with most reported issues being minor or related to the complex hybrid system's integration rather than fundamental design flaws. Owners generally reported a satisfying ownership experience, appreciating the refined ride and cutting-edge features.

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 Hybrid
**Hybrid Battery Cooling System**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating warnings, reduced electric-only range, reduced overall power output.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of faulty cooling pump, hoses, or coolant.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Electric Motor/Generator Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Whining noises, loss of regenerative braking, reduced acceleration, "Check Hybrid System" warning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential repair or replacement of the electric motor/generator unit or associated control modules.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $6000+

## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, rougher ride, "Visit Workshop" warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air struts or air springs.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant "Visit Workshop" message.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, navigation system errors, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard resets, or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500


### 12V Auxiliary Battery
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical systems malfunctioning, warning lights, difficulty starting (even with hybrid system active).
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V auxiliary battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S550e presents a generally reliable profile for a luxury plug-in hybrid. The primary areas of concern revolve around the complexity of the hybrid powertrain, specifically the battery cooling system and potential electric motor issues, which can be costly to repair. The AIRMATIC suspension, while providing excellent comfort, is also a known area where components like struts and compressors can require attention over time. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the advanced infotainment system and the auxiliary battery, are also noted. However, the gasoline engine and transmission are typically robust. For owners, proactive maintenance and addressing hybrid system warnings promptly are key to maintaining long-term reliability and avoiding significant expenses.
