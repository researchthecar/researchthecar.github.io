# 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLC 63 AMG S offers high-performance luxury, but used buyers should approach with an understanding of potential reliability hiccups. While its robust AMG powerplant delivers thrilling acceleration, owner reports highlight recurring concerns ranging from electrical and lighting glitches to occasional drivetrain and engine management symptoms. Careful inspection of maintenance records and prior warranty work is recommended before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8 Engine

Equipped with a hand-built 4.0-liter biturbo V8 engine producing immense horsepower and torque, this AMG powerplant delivers exceptional performance. Buyers should ensure consistent oil service intervals have been maintained to safeguard complex internal components.

**Engine Oil Pressure Control**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light accompanied by diagnostic trouble codes related to oil pressure regulation.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty oil pressure control sensors or solenoids as necessary.

**Throttle Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent lag or hesitation when pressing the accelerator pedal, particularly during turns or when pulling out into traffic.
- **Solution:** Perform a diagnostic scan of the powertrain control module and update software or service throttle components if required.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed multi-clutch automatic transmission is tailored for rapid shifts in performance driving environments. Regular fluid changes are vital for maintaining smooth operation over high mileage.

**Unexpected Stalling or Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle occasionally hesitates or drops into neutral-like behavior while maneuvering at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Check transmission control module software and inspect mechanical clutch packs for abnormal wear.

## Electrical

### Back Over Prevention

Driver assistance and visibility systems provide essential safety features, though intermittent display glitches have been reported by owners.

**Backup Camera Blank Screen**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment display fails to show the rearview camera image when shifting into reverse, occasionally remaining inoperable until the vehicle is restarted.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera wiring harnesses, software firmware, and infotainment display modules for faults.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Exterior lighting systems can suffer from moisture intrusion, which compromises sensitive electrical control units within the lamp assemblies.

**Headlight Moisture Ingress and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Condensation accumulation inside the headlamp housing leading to electrical shorting, warning messages, and complete headlight assembly failure.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight seals and replace damaged headlight modules, control units, or housings.

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior body structure and glass components require careful inspection for stress cracks or wind noise indicating seal degradation.

**Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof panel unexpectedly shatters or separates while driving at highway speeds without prior impact or warning.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the frame assembly for alignment issues.

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLC 63 AMG S is an exhilarating performance luxury SUV, but prospective buyers should inspect it thoroughly.
