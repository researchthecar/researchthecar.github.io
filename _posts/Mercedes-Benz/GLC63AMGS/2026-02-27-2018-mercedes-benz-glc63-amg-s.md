# 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury performance SUV, the 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG S offers formidable power and upscale styling. However, prospective buyers should review potential owner-reported trouble areas involving the powertrain, electrical accessories, and body hardware before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8

The handcrafted 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers immense output, typically generating around 503 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque in AMG S spec. While robustly engineered, buyers should verify regular maintenance intervals and watch for potential auxiliary system leaks or emissions-related ventilation faults.

**Crankcase Ventilation and PCV Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine lights accompanied by fault codes such as P052E.
- **Solution:** Inspect the crankcase ventilation system and replace faulty PCV valves or related solenoid components as needed.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 9-Speed Automatic

Paired with a wet clutch start-up configuration, the AMG Speedshift 9-speed automatic transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes tailored for performance driving. Routine fluid changes and software health are key to keeping this complex gearbox shifting smoothly.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle unexpectedly drops into neutral while driving or fails to engage the correct drive gear upon acceleration.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the electronic gear selector module and transmission control unit for software updates or mechanical wear.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Audio Systems

Electrical systems on high-performance luxury models manage numerous advanced convenience and entertainment features, requiring thorough pre-purchase checks of all cabin tech.

**Audio Amplifier Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Complete loss of audio volume for the radio, GPS navigation, and Bluetooth-connected devices.
- **Solution:** Test the factory amplifier unit and replace it if internal hardware failure is confirmed.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Exterior Trim

Exterior body and glass components require careful physical inspection, particularly around panoramic roof seals and exterior body moldings, to prevent wind noise or detachment risks.

**Panoramic Sunroof Glass and Trim Disbonding**
- **Symptoms:** Exterior roof trim panels detaching at highway speeds or panoramic glass panels cracking spontaneously.
- **Solution:** Inspect exterior roof molding adhesion and ensure sunroof glass seals are securely bonded.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Occupant protection systems should be rigorously verified during inspection to ensure all seat belts and airbags function without electronic warnings.

**Rear Seat Belt Latch Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty latching rear seat belts or securing buckle plates properly into assembly housings.
- **Solution:** Inspect rear seat belt latches for foreign debris or mechanical jamming, and replace faulty buckle assemblies.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG S is an exhilarating performance SUV, but its high-complexity powertrain and luxury electronics demand careful scrutiny. Buyers should commission a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the V8 ventilation system, transmission shift behavior, and exterior trim integrity to ensure a reliable ownership experience.
