# 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG S is a high-performance luxury SUV that blends potent AMG engineering with the practicality of the GLC platform. While generally well-regarded for its exhilarating driving dynamics and premium interior, like any performance vehicle, it can experience specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and prospective buyers. We will examine the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical systems, exterior, and interior components to offer a comprehensive reliability picture.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (M177)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, boost pressure issues, rattling noise from turbo area.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty wastegate actuator or the complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Coolant Leaks (Intercooler Hoses)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant leaks, engine overheating, loss of coolant level, sweet smell from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of damaged intercooler hoses and potentially associated seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 9-Speed Transmission
**Harsh Shifting / Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable clunks or jerks during gear changes, especially at lower speeds or during aggressive acceleration.
- **Solution:** Software update for transmission control module, or in more severe cases, internal transmission component inspection and repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200 (Software update/Diagnosis), $2000+ (Internal Repair)

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible fluid leaks from the transmission pan or seals, burning smell, low transmission fluid level.
- **Solution:** Tightening or replacement of transmission pan gasket, or replacement of faulty seals.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500


## Suspension

### AMG RIDE CONTROL+ Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, 'air suspension malfunction' warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the leaking air spring or associated air lines.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1500 per corner

**Control Arm Bushing Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800 per axle


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, freezes, or requires a system reboot.
- **Solution:** Software update for the infotainment system, or in rare cases, replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600 (Software/Diagnosis), $1500+ (Head Unit Replacement)


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, requires frequent jump starts, warning lights related to electrical system.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the parasitic draw (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair or replacement of the faulty component.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG S demonstrates a generally solid reliability record for a high-performance vehicle, with most reported issues stemming from its potent powertrain and advanced suspension systems. Common concerns include potential wastegate actuator issues and coolant leaks within the engine, as well as occasional harsh shifting or leaks from the AMG SPEEDSHIFT transmission. The air suspension can be prone to leaks, and electrical gremlins like infotainment unresponsiveness or battery drain are not unheard of. However, many of these issues are addressable with proper diagnosis and repair. The exterior and interior components, barring typical wear, are largely free from widespread defects. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining the reliability and performance of this AMG model.
