# 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG S is a high-performance luxury SUV that blends potent V8 power with everyday practicality. As a performance-oriented model, it generally exhibits strong build quality and engineering. However, like any complex vehicle, particularly one with a focus on performance, certain areas may be more prone to specific issues than standard models. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, aiming to provide a clear picture for owners and potential buyers.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (M177)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, loss of power, whistling noise from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty wastegate actuator or potentially the entire turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant loss, sweet smell, engine overheating, white smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Transmission

### AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 9-Speed Transmission
**Harsh Shifting / Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable clunks or jerks during gear changes, especially at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Software update for transmission control module, or in more severe cases, internal transmission repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2500+


## Suspension

### AMG RIDE CONTROL+ Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing / Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, displays errors, or restarts randomly.
- **Solution:** Software update or potential replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, electrical components malfunctioning, battery warning light.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of battery if necessary.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG S demonstrates a generally robust mechanical and electrical foundation, befitting its premium performance segment. The primary areas of concern tend to revolve around the specialized AMG components, such as the turbocharger wastegate actuators and the performance transmission, which can experience issues like harsh shifting. The air suspension system, while offering superior comfort and adjustability, can be susceptible to leaks over time. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and occasional battery drain, are also noted. While not indicative of widespread failure, these potential issues highlight the importance of diligent maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs to ensure the longevity and optimal performance of this potent SUV.
