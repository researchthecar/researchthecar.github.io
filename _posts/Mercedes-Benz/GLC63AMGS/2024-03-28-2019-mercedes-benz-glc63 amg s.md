**Introduction**
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG S is a high-performance SUV that combines luxury and practicality. It is powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V8 engine that generates 503 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, mated to a nine-speed automatic transmission. The GLC63 AMG S also features a sport-tuned suspension, high-performance brakes, and a host of advanced driver assistance systems.

While the GLC63 AMG S is praised for its impressive performance, spacious cabin, and luxurious appointments, it has also been met with some criticism for its high price and stiff ride quality.

**Engine**
**4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8**
* **Intake Manifold Cracking:** The intake manifold can crack, causing a loss of power and a rough idle. The solution is to replace the intake manifold, which costs an estimated $2,000 - $3,000.
* **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbochargers can fail, resulting in a loss of power and a loud whistling noise. Replacing the turbochargers is a major repair that can cost upwards of $5,000.
* **Valve Train Noise:** Some vehicles may experience excessive valve train noise, which can be caused by a faulty timing chain or tensioner. The solution is to replace the affected components, which costs an estimated $1,000 - $2,000.
* **Recalls:**
    * NHTSA Recall ID: 19V695 - Airbag Control Unit Failure
    * NHTSA Recall ID: 19V703 - Incorrect Engine Control Software

**Transmission**
**9-Speed Automatic**
* **Shifting Problems:** Some vehicles may experience shifting problems, such as harsh shifts, delayed shifts, or a lack of response. The solution is to update the transmission software or replace the transmission control module, which costs an estimated $1,000 - $2,000.
* **Transmission Leak:** The transmission can develop a leak, causing fluid loss and potentially leading to transmission failure. The solution is to reseal the transmission, which costs an estimated $500 - $1,000.
* **Recalls:**
    * None Found

**Suspension**
* **Strut Mount Failure:** The strut mounts can fail, causing a clunking noise and a rough ride. The solution is to replace the strut mounts, which costs an estimated $300 - $500.
* **Air Suspension Malfunction:** The air suspension system can malfunction, causing the vehicle to sit at an uneven height or lose its ability to adjust. The solution is to repair or replace the air suspension components, which costs an estimated $1,000 - $2,000.
* **Recalls:**
    * None Found

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain:** Some vehicles may experience a battery drain, which can prevent the vehicle from starting. The solution is to diagnose and repair the electrical system, which costs an estimated $200 - $500.
* **Infotainment System Issues:** The infotainment system can experience glitches, such as freezing or losing connection. The solution is to update the infotainment software or replace the infotainment module, which costs an estimated $500 - $1,000.
* **Recalls:**
    * NHTSA Recall ID: 19V712 - Rearview Camera Failure

**Exterior**
* **Mirror Cap Falling Off:** The mirror caps can fall off due to faulty clips. The solution is to replace the mirror caps, which costs an estimated $100 - $200.
* **Recalls:**
    * None Found

**Interior**
* **Panoramic Sunroof Rattle:** Some vehicles may experience a rattling noise from the panoramic sunroof. The solution is to adjust or replace the sunroof, which costs an estimated $500 - $1,000.
* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system can malfunction, causing the air conditioning or heating to not work properly. The solution is to repair or replace the climate control module, which costs an estimated $500 - $1,000.
* **Recalls:**
    * None Found

**Summary**
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG S is a high-performance SUV that offers impressive performance and luxury. However, like any vehicle, it is not without its issues. Some common issues include intake manifold cracking, turbocharger failure, shifting problems, and suspension malfunctions. The cost of repairs can vary depending on the severity of the issue, but it is important to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing a GLC63 AMG S.
