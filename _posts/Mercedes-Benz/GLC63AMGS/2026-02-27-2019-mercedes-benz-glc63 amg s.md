# 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG S is a high-performance luxury SUV that blends potent V8 power with everyday practicality. As a performance-oriented model, it generally exhibits strong build quality and engineering. However, like any complex vehicle, particularly one with a focus on performance, certain areas may require more attention than others. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance, aiming to provide a clear picture for owners and prospective buyers. We will examine the powertrain, suspension, electrical systems, and other key areas to identify any prevalent issues.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (M177)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, boost pressure issues, rattling noise from turbo area.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty wastegate actuator or the complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil on engine components, burning oil smell, potential for oil to drip onto exhaust manifold.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Transmission

### AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 9-Speed Automatic
**Harsh Shifting / Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable jolts or hesitations during gear changes, particularly at lower speeds or during aggressive acceleration.
- **Solution:** Software update for transmission control module, or in some cases, internal transmission service/repair.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1000 (software update to potential internal repair)


## Suspension

### AMG RIDE CONTROL+ Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively, warning lights on the dash.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated air lines.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1500 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND/NTG 5.5)
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive to touch inputs, system reboots unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment control unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLC63 AMG S is a robust performance SUV with a generally good reliability record for its class. The primary areas that have seen documented issues are related to the performance-oriented components, such as the turbocharger wastegate actuators and potential for oil leaks from valve cover gaskets on the potent 4.0L V8 engine. The AMG SPEEDSHIFT transmission can occasionally exhibit harsh shifting, often rectifiable with software updates. The air suspension system, while providing excellent ride quality, can be prone to air spring leaks over time. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and occasional battery drain, are also noted. Despite these points, the GLC63 AMG S remains a desirable and capable vehicle, with most issues being addressable through specialized repair.
