**Introduction:**

The 2002 Mercedes-Benz CL230 is a luxury coupe powered by a 2.3-liter V6 engine. It offers a smooth and comfortable ride, along with a spacious cabin and elegant styling. However, like any vehicle, it has its fair share of issues.

**Engine**

* **2.3L V6 M112**
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to jump or break. This can lead to catastrophic engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and adjust the timing.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake manifold gasket leak:** The intake manifold gasket can develop a leak, causing a loss of vacuum and a rough idle.
        * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Valve cover gasket leak:** The valve cover gasket can leak oil, causing a burning smell and smoke from the engine.
        * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-speed Automatic Transmission (722.6)**
    * **Transmission slipping or shuddering:** The transmission may slip or shudder during gear changes.
        * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid filter, solenoid pack, and possibly the transmission itself.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Torque converter failure:** The torque converter can fail, causing a loss of power and difficulty shifting gears.
        * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension strut failure:** The front suspension struts can fail, causing a rough ride and poor handling.
        * Solution: Replace the front suspension struts.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+ per axle
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Headlight switch failure:** The headlight switch can fail, causing the headlights to turn off or flicker.
        * Solution: Replace the headlight switch.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on undercarriage:** The undercarriage of the vehicle can develop rust over time, especially if it is exposed to moisture and salt.
        * Solution: Treat the rust with rust inhibitor or have the undercarriage professionally repaired.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the severity of the rust
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door lock actuators failure:** The door lock actuators can fail, causing the doors to become stuck or difficult to open.
        * Solution: Replace the door lock actuators.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per door
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2002 Mercedes-Benz CL230 is a reliable vehicle with a few known issues. The engine and transmission problems can be serious and expensive to repair, but the suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior issues are typically less severe and less costly.
