**Introduction:**

The 2004 Mercedes-Benz CL230 is a luxury coupe powered by a 3.0-liter V6 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. Known for its elegant design, plush interior, and powerful engine, the CL230 also has its share of issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

**3.0-liter V6 Engine**

* **Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of coolant, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or erratic shifting, slipping gears
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Airmatic Suspension Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of air pressure, sagging suspension, uneven ride
    * Solution: Repair or replace air bags, compressor, or hoses
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace electrical components, such as alternator or starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights flicker or fail to illuminate
    * Solution: Replace ballast
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per headlight
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Panel Binding:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof panel sticks or binds when opening or closing
    * Solution: Lubricate or replace sunroof tracks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Overall Summary:**

The 2004 Mercedes-Benz CL230 has potential issues that range from minor inconveniences to major repairs. While some issues, such as headlight ballast failure, are relatively common and affordable to fix, others, such as head gasket failure, can be very expensive to resolve. However, the CL230's overall reliability is considered average, and many owners enjoy the comfort, performance, and prestige it offers.
