## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Suspension Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace leaking air springs, lines, or compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Inconsistent ride height adjustment, harshness or excessive softness in ride quality, suspension warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and replace faulty ride height sensors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Mercedes-Benz EQS 580 demonstrates a high level of reliability, particularly concerning its electric powertrain and core components. Overall, the EQS 580 offers a robust and dependable ownership experience for a luxury electric vehicle.
