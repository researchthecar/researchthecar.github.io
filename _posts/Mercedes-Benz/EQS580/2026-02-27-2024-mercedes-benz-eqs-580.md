# 2024 Mercedes-Benz EQS 580 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz EQS 580 represents the pinnacle of electric luxury, offering advanced technology and smooth performance. As a relatively new luxury EV model, prospective used-car buyers should evaluate individual vehicle histories, particularly regarding cutting-edge electronics and integrated software systems.

---

## Engine

### Dual Permanently Excited Synchronous Electric Motors

The EQS 580 is powered by dual permanently excited synchronous electric motors producing an impressive 536 horsepower and 633 lb-ft of torque, providing standard 4MATIC all-wheel drive. These electric motors deliver swift, seamless acceleration and are generally trouble-free in daily operation.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Fixed Gear Automatic Transmission

Power is routed to the wheels via a responsive single-speed fixed-gear automatic transmission optimized for electric vehicle efficiency and quiet operation. This direct-drive system involves fewer mechanical moving parts than traditional multi-speed gearboxes, supporting long-term drivetrain reliability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V372000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery:Management System/Energy Control Module (Bms/Becm):Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=EQS+580&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The battery management system software may cause the high-voltage battery to shut down, which can result in a sudden loss of drive power. A sudden loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the battery management system software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V115000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=EQS+580&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An 80-Amp fuse that fails can result in a sudden loss of drive power or a loss of critical safety functions such as the restraint systems or instrument cluster, increasing the risk of a crash or injury. Additionally, there could be an increased risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the main fuse boxes, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz EQS 580 is an ultra-luxury electric vehicle backed by a robust powertrain, though buyers should remain mindful of complexities inherent to high-tech luxury vehicles. Thoroughly inspecting software versions, charging functionality, and remaining factory warranties will ensure a confident pre-owned purchase.
