# 2022 Mercedes-Benz EQS 580 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz EQS 580 represents the brand's flagship all-electric luxury sedan, offering cutting-edge technology and high-end performance. As an early model year for this advanced electric platform, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate both powertrain capabilities and initial vehicle electronics during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### Dual Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motors

Equipped with dual permanent magnet synchronous electric motors generating approximately 516 horsepower and 631 lb-ft of torque with all-wheel drive. This powertrain delivers effortless acceleration and smooth, silent power delivery with no traditional internal combustion maintenance concerns.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Fixed Gear Transmission

Features a single-speed automatic reduction gear paired with the dual-motor all-wheel-drive system. The direct-drive layout provides seamless power application without traditional gear shifting.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V074000: Visibility:Rearview Mirrors/Devices:Interior](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=EQS+580&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The multi-purpose camera and the rearview mirror may be insufficiently attached to the windshield and may detach, impairing front passenger air bag deployment and various assistance systems, including Adaptive Cruise Control (DISTRONIC) with active steering assist, Brake Assists with pedestrian detection, Lane Keeping Assists, Traffic Sign Assist, Augmented.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the windshield and reinstall the multi-purpose camera and rearview mirror as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz EQS 580 delivers immense luxury and performance, though as an early production model of an advanced electric vehicle, buyers should ensure all software updates and dealer campaigns have been properly applied.
