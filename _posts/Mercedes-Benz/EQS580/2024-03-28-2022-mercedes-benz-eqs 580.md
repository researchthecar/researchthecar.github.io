The 2022 Mercedes-Benz EQS 580 is an all-electric luxury sedan powered by a single electric motor that produces 516 horsepower and 631 pound-feet of torque. It comes with a 107.8 kWh battery pack that provides an estimated 350 miles of range on a single charge. The EQS 580 is praised for its sleek design, spacious cabin, and advanced technology, but it has also received some criticism for its high price and limited availability.

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain Issue**
Description/Symptoms: Several owners have reported that their EQS 580s experience significant battery drain when parked.
Solution: Mercedes-Benz has released a software update to address this issue.
Estimated Cost to Fix: Free of charge
Recalls: None Found

**Infotainment System Glitches**
Description/Symptoms: Some users have encountered occasional glitches with the infotainment system, such as freezing or unresponsive screens.
Solution: These glitches can usually be resolved by restarting the system.
Estimated Cost to Fix: None
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Window Trim Issue**
Description/Symptoms: A small number of owners have reported that the window trim on their EQS 580s has become loose or misaligned.
Solution: Mercedes-Benz dealers can reinstall or replace the affected trim pieces.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Roof Noise Issue**
Description/Symptoms: A few owners have complained of a rattling noise coming from the panoramic sunroof.
Solution: Mercedes-Benz has issued a recall (NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V664000) to inspect and repair the affected vehicles.
Estimated Cost to Fix: Free of charge
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V664000

**Summary**

While the 2022 Mercedes-Benz EQS 580 has received positive reviews overall, there have been some reported issues with the vehicle's battery, infotainment system, exterior trim, and interior roof. Most of these issues appear to be relatively minor and can be resolved through software updates or dealer repairs. However, the battery drain issue and the roof noise issue could be more serious and may require more extensive repairs.
