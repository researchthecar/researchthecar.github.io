**2015 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 2500**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 2500 is a versatile and reliable cargo van known for its spacious interior, robust engine, and advanced safety features. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including:

* **Engine:**
    * 2.1L 4-cylinder turbo diesel (161 hp, 266 lb-ft)
    * 3.0L V6 turbo diesel (188 hp, 325 lb-ft)
* **Transmission:**
    * 7-speed automatic
    * 5-speed manual (only available with 2.1L engine)

**Engine**

**2.1L 4-cylinder Turbo Diesel**

* **No Issues Found**

**3.0L V6 Turbo Diesel**

* **Fuel Pump Pressure Sensor Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light illumination, loss of power, stalling.
    * Solution: Replace fuel pump pressure sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, shifting problems, check engine light illumination.
    * Solution: Replace TCM.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, warning light illumination, electrical system malfunction.
    * Solution: Replace alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Starter Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine not starting, clicking or grinding noise.
    * Solution: Replace starter motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Side Mirror Signal Light Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Turn signal not functioning in side mirror.
    * Solution: Replace turn signal bulb or wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sunroof Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into cabin through sunroof.
    * Solution: Inspect and reseal sunroof.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Lid Latch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Center console lid not staying closed.
    * Solution: Replace latch mechanism.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Power Window Switch Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power window not functioning.
    * Solution: Replace power window switch or wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2015 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 2500 has some common issues that are generally not severe or costly to fix. The most concerning issues are the transmission control module failure and alternator failure, which can be expensive to repair.
