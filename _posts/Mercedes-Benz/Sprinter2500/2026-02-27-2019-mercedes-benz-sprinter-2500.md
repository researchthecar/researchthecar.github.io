# 2019 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 2500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2019 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 2500 requires careful attention to its complex engineering and commercial-grade heritage. While these vans offer robust capability, versatile configurations, and advanced diesel powertrains, buyers should inspect the vehicle thoroughly for any prior commercial wear and tear. Reviewing maintenance records and performing a pre-purchase inspection can help uncover hidden issues before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Gas Engine

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 gas engine produces approximately 188 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. It offers a smooth driving experience well-suited for lighter delivery duties and passenger configurations.

### 3.0L Turbo Diesel V6 Engine

The 3.0-liter turbo diesel V6 generates roughly 188 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque, delivering strong low-end pulling power for heavy hauling and conversions. It is a durable highway cruiser, though maintenance costs can run higher than gas alternatives.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 9-speed automatic transmission pairs with the gasoline engine to optimize gear ratios for fuel efficiency and responsiveness. Routine fluid maintenance is key to ensuring long-term durability in commercial applications.

### 7G-TRONIC 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission works alongside the V6 diesel engine to handle heavy towing and payload demands smoothly. Regular servicing helps prevent harsh shifting and premature wear under heavy loads.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V309000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights:High/Low Beam Dimmer Switch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Being unable to turn off the high beams may result in oncoming traffic being blinded by the vehicle's lights, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and Mercedes-Benz or Freightliner Sprinter dealers will update the control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V800000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the weld seam may break and cause the seat cushion to shift forward, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the tilt adjuster for the front seats, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V908000: Equipment](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The lashing rails on the side walls may not have been properly installed, possibly resulting in the lashing rails detaching. If the lashing rails disconnect, the cargo could become unsecured, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will correctly affix the lashing rails to the side walls, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V798000: Fuel System, Diesel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The bayonet lock between the fuel pump and the fuel tank may not have been closed and locked during manufacturing. As a result, a leak between the fuel pump and the fuel tank could occur.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the bayonet lock on the fuel pump, locking it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V693000: Unknown Or Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Failure to identify loose safety related parts can increase the risk of crash and injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 19V695000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The airbag systems may not have been activated after vehicle production. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An inactive airbag system can increase the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the vehicles to determine if the airbag systems have been activated, correcting them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V507000: Visibility:Glass, Side/Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a side impact crash or vehicle rollover, the fitted side windows may not adequately protect the rear occupants from being ejected, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and Mercedes-Benz or Freightliner dealers will replace the single-pane safety glass with laminated safety glass., free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V078000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Exhaust System:Emission Control:Gas Recirculation Valve (Egr Valve)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The connecting screws on the exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) pipe may not have been correctly tightened. If the connecting screws are not properly tightened, hot exhaust gases may leak from the EGR pipe, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and Mercedes-Benz or Freightliner Sprinter dealers will renew the screw connection and the flange seals on the exhaust gas recirculation pipe, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 2500 remains a versatile and capable choice for commercial fleets and camper van conversions alike. Prospective buyers should prioritize models with documented service histories and verify the condition of the specific powertrain option chosen. A thorough pre-purchase inspection remains the best safeguard against unexpected repair bills.
