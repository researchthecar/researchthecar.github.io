# 2024 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 2500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 2500 is a highly capable full-size van popular for commercial fleets and custom camper conversions alike. Buyers looking at the used market will find sophisticated engineering, strong diesel powertrain options, and modern cabin technology. Because this vehicle is still relatively new, long-term reliability trends are still emerging, but prospective owners should thoroughly evaluate powertrain health and electrical accessories during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Turbo Diesel (Standard Output)

A 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder diesel engine producing around 170 horsepower, designed to offer balanced fuel efficiency for daily delivery and utility duty. Buyers should expect dependable baseline performance under normal commercial operation.

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Twin-Turbo Diesel (High Output)

A high-output 2.0-liter twin-turbocharged diesel engine generating approximately 211 horsepower, tailored for heavier payload capacities and towing needs. This option provides robust power delivery but relies on complex multi-stage turbocharging.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A smooth-shifting 9-speed automatic transmission paired with the diesel engine lineup, engineered to optimize fuel economy across varying loads. It generally delivers refined operation, though proper fluid maintenance is essential for longevity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V517000: Suspension:Rear:Springs:Leaf Spring Assembly:U-Bolt, Leaf Spring To Axle](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An axle that moves out of position can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the spring U-bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V135000: Parking Brake](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 105, "Hydraulic and Electric Brake Systems." Parking brake failure can result in a vehicle rollaway, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the parking brake cable bracket as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V609000: Structure:Frame And Members](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The longitudinal frame members may not be reinforced properly. Frame members with inadequate strength may reduce the vehicle's performance in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the frame, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V725000: Structure:Frame And Members](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The right longitudinal frame member may not be reinforced properly, decreasing the structural integrity of the third-row seats. Third-row seats with inadequate strength may fail to protect occupants, increasing the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the frame, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 2500 remains a premier choice in the full-size van segment, offering versatile powertrain configurations and refined driving dynamics. While major mechanical trouble spots are not yet established for this model year, used buyers should prioritize vehicles with documented service histories and test all onboard electronics before finalizing a purchase.
