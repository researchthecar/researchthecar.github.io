# 2023 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 2500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 2500 is a versatile full-size van popular for commercial fleets and custom camper conversions alike. While it offers advanced technology and robust capability, used-car buyers should carefully evaluate its complex powertrain options and overall condition. Reviewing maintenance history and checking for any lingering owner-reported issues can help ensure a dependable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Diesel (Standard Output)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 diesel engine delivers around 168 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, prioritizing fuel efficiency for lighter commercial duties. It represents a modern addition to the lineup, though buyers should verify routine maintenance adherence.

### 2.0L Twin-Turbocharged Inline-4 Diesel (High Output)

Equipped with a high-output twin-turbo setup, this 2.0-liter diesel produces approximately 211 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque. It provides stronger pulling power for heavy loads and camper upfits, balancing performance with modern emissions equipment.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission pairs with the diesel engine options to optimize power delivery and highway efficiency. It offers smooth gear changes across various load conditions, though prospective buyers should test-shift thoroughly during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V806000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The topcoat paint formula may cause the windows and interior lashing rails to be insufficiently bonded to the vehicle. An insufficiently bonded window can detach during a crash, and objects secured to an insufficiently bonded interior lashing rail can detach, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the paint adhesion and repair it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V849000: Suspension:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The front left suspension strut bolt may not be secured properly, which can result in a loss of steering control. A loss of steering control can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the front left suspension strut bolt connection and repair the body contact surface as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V876000: Structure:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The partition wall may be missing bolts, allowing the wall to detach during a crash. A detached partition wall can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and secure the partition wall as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V166000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The main wiring harness may chafe against the drive shaft and short circuit, which can result in a loss of drive power, power steering, and brake system control. A short circuit increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the routing of the main wiring harness and correct it, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V225000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A 12-Volt ground connection may not be secured properly, which can result in the ground connection overheating and a loss of drive power. An overheated ground connection can increase the risk of a fire, and a loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the 12-Volt ground connection, as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V517000: Suspension:Rear:Springs:Leaf Spring Assembly:U-Bolt, Leaf Spring To Axle](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An axle that moves out of position can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the spring U-bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V725000: Structure:Frame And Members](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+2500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The right longitudinal frame member may not be reinforced properly, decreasing the structural integrity of the third-row seats. Third-row seats with inadequate strength may fail to protect occupants, increasing the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the frame, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 2500 is a sophisticated and highly capable van platform, but its complex engineering requires diligent maintenance tracking. Prospective buyers should prioritize models with documented service records and conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify mechanical and electrical health.
