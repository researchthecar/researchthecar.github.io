**Introduction**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG is a high-performance sedan that is powered by a handcrafted 4.0L twin-turbo V8 engine. It produces 469 horsepower and 479 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 9-speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT transmission.

People who like the C63 AMG appreciate its powerful engine and thrilling driving experience. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems.

**Engine**

* **4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 Engine**
    * **Oil Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported oil leaks coming from the engine's oil pan, valve covers, and rear main seal.
        * Solution: The leaking components need to be replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported turbocharger failures, resulting in a loss of power and excessive smoke.
        * Solution: The turbocharger needs to be replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **9-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT Transmission**
    * **Transmission Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported the transmission slipping out of gear, especially during acceleration or deceleration.
        * Solution: The transmission may need to be repaired or replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported issues with the air suspension system, resulting in a rough ride and uneven suspension height.
    * Solution: The air suspension components, such as the air springs or compressor, need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Faults**
    * Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported various electrical system faults, including flickering headlights, malfunctioning instrument cluster, and battery drain.
    * Solution: The faulty electrical components need to be repaired or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Center Console Creaking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported excessive creaking noises coming from the center console area.
    * Solution: The center console components may need to be adjusted or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG is a powerful and thrilling car, it has been reported to have some issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, but some of them can be significant and require expensive repairs.
