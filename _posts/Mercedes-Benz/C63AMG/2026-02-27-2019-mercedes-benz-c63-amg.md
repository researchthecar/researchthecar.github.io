# 2019 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG delivers high-performance luxury, but prospective buyers should carefully weigh its complex mechanical and electronic systems. While owner feedback highlights thrilling driving dynamics, various reliability concerns have been reported regarding powertrain components, electrical subsystems, and safety equipment. Prioritizing a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help uncover hidden wear or pending faults before finalizing a used vehicle purchase.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

Powered by a hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 producing substantial horsepower and torque, offering breathtaking acceleration. Buyers should maintain rigorous service intervals to ensure long-term durability of the forced-induction setup.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine hesitation, rough vibrations during startup, or sudden loss of motive power while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician perform a full diagnostic scan for stored fault codes and inspect sensors, throttle components, and oil control valves.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated warning lights accompanied by diagnostic trouble codes pointing toward fuel delivery or pump failure.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel pump and associated electrical circuits, replacing failed modules as necessary.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 9-Speed Automatic

Features a wet-clutch 9-speed automatic transmission designed for rapid gear changes and rear-wheel-drive performance. Routine fluid changes are critical to preserving shift quality under high-load conditions.

**Powertrain Engagement Delay**
- **Symptoms:** Momentary unresponsiveness or failure to move forward when pressing the accelerator pedal after coming to a stop.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid levels, update control module software, or inspect internal clutch packs for wear.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and Column

**Steering Binding or Locking**
- **Symptoms:** Abnormal noises or complete steering wheel lockup while executing turns at low or moderate speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column, electronic power steering assist motor, and associated linkages for mechanical binding.

### Tilt Adjustment

Power-adjustable steering column mechanisms can occasionally experience positioning failures.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety restraints require regular checks to ensure emergency locking retractors operate smoothly.

**Failure to Retract**
- **Symptoms:** Front seat belts fail to retract fully into their stowed positions, leaving loose webbing that can catch on exiting passengers.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat belt assemblies and replace retractors or guides as needed.
