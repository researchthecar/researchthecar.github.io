# 2023 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG represents a high-performance evolution for the luxury compact segment, transitioning to an advanced plug-in hybrid powertrain. While early-model owner feedback is limited, prospective buyers should review initial reports carefully to understand potential teething issues common to redesigned luxury vehicles.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Plug-In Hybrid

Features a hand-built 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 paired with an electric motor and rear-mounted battery, generating an impressive output exceeding 600 horsepower. This complex powertrain delivers immense performance but relies heavily on sophisticated electronics.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 9-Speed Automatic

Utilizes a wet clutch 9-speed automatic transmission designed to handle high torque outputs while offering rapid gear changes. It provides seamless power delivery suited for both track performance and daily driving.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

**Spontaneous Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced the panoramic glass roof shattering unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds without any external impact.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the glass assembly under warranty or insurance coverage.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

**Unexpected Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the forward collision avoidance system or active brake assist abruptly applied the brakes or brought the vehicle to a sudden stop without an apparent obstruction.
- **Solution:** Have authorized service personnel inspect and recalibrate the radar and camera sensors, and ensure vehicle software is updated.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG offers staggering performance and cutting-edge hybrid technology, but prospective buyers should inspect advanced driver-assistance features and glass components thoroughly. Because this model year introduces a complete redesign, maintaining a comprehensive warranty is strongly recommended.
