**Introduction**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG is a high-performance sedan that slots above the standard C-Class lineup. It is powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 451 horsepower and 384 pound-feet of torque. The C63 AMG comes standard with a seven-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive.

The C63 AMG is praised for its muscular engine, responsive handling, and comfortable interior. However, it has also been criticized for its high price and limited fuel economy.

**Engine**

**4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Intake Valve Deposits**
    * Description/Symptoms: Carbon buildup on the intake valves can cause reduced engine performance, fuel economy, and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Remove the intake manifold and clean the intake valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger wastegate actuator may fail, causing the turbocharger to overboost. This can lead to engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger wastegate actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Seven-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Clutch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The torque converter clutch may fail, causing slipping and shuddering during gear changes.
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear air suspension compressor may fail, causing the rear suspension to sag.
    * Solution: Replace the rear air suspension compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is parked.
    * Solution: Test and replace any faulty electrical components that are draining the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience glitches, such as freezing or lagging.
    * Solution: Update the infotainment system software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG is a high-performance vehicle with a number of potential issues. The most severe issues include the torque converter clutch failure and turbocharger wastegate actuator failure, which can lead to engine damage. Other issues, such as battery drain and suspension problems, are less serious but can still be costly to fix. Overall, the C63 AMG is a reliable car, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues before purchasing one.
