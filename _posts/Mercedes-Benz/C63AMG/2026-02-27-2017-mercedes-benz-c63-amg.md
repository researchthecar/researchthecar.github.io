# 2017 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG delivers thrilling performance and luxury, but prospective buyers should research common owner-reported grievances. While powertrain options offer exceptional power, online complaints highlight specific areas of concern including electrical issues, exterior body structure issues, and safety system anomalies.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Bi-Turbo V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine generates formidable horsepower and torque, offering exhilarating acceleration. Regular maintenance and attentive monitoring of fuel and ventilation systems are recommended for long-term reliability.

**Fuel Odor or Leak**
- **Symptoms:** A strong smell of gasoline inside or outside the cabin, particularly after driving or while parked.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines, high-pressure pumps, and connections for leaks; replace faulty seals or lines as necessary.

**Check Engine Light / P052E Code**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of the check engine light often accompanied by code P052E relating to the positive crankcase ventilation valve.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the crankcase ventilation system and replace the malfunctioning valve or associated wiring.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected engine stalling while coming to a stop or during low-speed operation, accompanied by warning lights.
- **Solution:** Check electronic engine management software, fuel delivery, and sensors; consult a specialist for diagnostic troubleshooting.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 7-Speed Automatic

The 7-speed multi-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts suited to high-performance driving. Routine fluid changes are essential to maintain smooth operation.

## Steering

### Power Steering

Steering responsiveness is sharp, but electric power assist failures can occasionally occur and require immediate professional diagnosis.

**Electric Power Steering Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering assistance accompanied by a warning message on the dashboard, making the steering wheel very heavy and difficult to turn.
- **Solution:** Scan the electronic power steering module for fault codes and replace faulty steering racks or electrical control units if necessary.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Roof Structure

Exterior body components and panoramic glass roofs require periodic checks to ensure proper alignment and seal integrity.

**Sunroof Shattering or Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** Loud banging noises followed by spontaneous shattering of the glass sunroof panel or complete detachment while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect sunroof glass and mounting seals for stress or defects; replace damaged roof panel assemblies.

## Safety

### Seat Belt

Safety equipment generally performs well, though physical seat belt retraction mechanisms have experienced isolated failures.

**Seat Belt Retraction Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The driver or passenger seat belt retracts fully into the B-pillar or housing and becomes stuck, rendering it unusable.
- **Solution:** Inspect the retractor mechanism and replace the seat belt assembly if the locking or retraction mechanism fails.
