# 2021 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG, buyers gain access to a high-performance luxury vehicle built for thrilling power and refined comfort. While owner reports to safety authorities are generally limited, prospective buyers should review specific real-world owner feedback regarding engine drivability, auxiliary systems, and cooling components. Keeping up with routine maintenance and pre-purchase inspections can help ensure the vehicle remains in peak condition.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Bi-Turbo V8 (M177)

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers immense horsepower and torque, providing exhilarating performance. Buyers should monitor cooling lines and engine management systems during routine upkeep.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the vehicle experienced unexpected engine stalling, lost power while driving, or struggled to exceed low speeds alongside an illuminated check engine light.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan for diagnostic trouble codes and inspect the fuel injection and ignition systems.

**Coolant Hose Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced sudden coolant leaks resulting from hose failures in the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Inspect cooling system hoses for wear, cracking, or loose connections, and replace damaged lines immediately.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 9-Speed Automatic

The 9-speed multi-clutch automatic transmission offers rapid gear shifts and crisp power delivery suited for high-performance driving. It generally operates reliably when properly maintained with scheduled fluid services.

## Electrical

### Panoramic Sunroof

Cabin electrical and convenience features offer high-tech luxury, though occasional mechanism issues can arise.

**Sunroof Mechanism Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof may fail to open or close properly, sometimes producing grinding or popping noises during operation.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof tracks and motor assembly for obstructions or mechanical failure, and lubricate or replace components as needed.
