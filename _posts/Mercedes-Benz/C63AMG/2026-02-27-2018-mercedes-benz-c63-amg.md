# 2018 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG delivers exhilarating performance paired with luxurious appointments, but prospective buyers should carefully weigh its complex mechanical and electronic systems. While ownership can be deeply rewarding for enthusiasts, these high-performance models require diligent maintenance and can incur steep repair bills if issues arise.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L V8 Bi-Turbo Engine

Hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing immense horsepower and torque, delivering supercar-rivaling performance. Buyers should verify complete service history and watch for early indicators of crankcase or internal component wear.

**Check Engine Light and Code P052E**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light accompanied by diagnostic trouble code P052E relating to crankcase ventilation.
- **Solution:** Replace the defective crankcase ventilation valve component with an updated manufacturer part.

**Premature Engine Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected engine shutdown while driving at highway speeds with potential loss of power.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic inspection required; may necessitate extensive engine repair or total replacement.
- **Cost:** 16500.00

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 9-Speed Automatic

Multi-clutch 9-speed automatic transmission designed for rapid, aggressive gear changes and robust power delivery. Regular fluid services are critical to maintaining shift quality and longevity.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and Assistance

Electronic power steering system tailored for sharp handling feedback, though rare failures can lead to sudden loss of assist.

**Power Steering Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering assistance accompanied by a dashboard warning message.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering assembly and replace faulty electrical or mechanical steering components as needed.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof and Roof Panel

Exterior glass and body trim components require periodic inspection to ensure weatherstripping and adhesive bonds remain secure.

**Detachment of Roof Glass Panel**
- **Symptoms:** A loud popping noise followed by the front glass roof panel partially or completely detaching while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect roof bonding agents and have the panel re-secured or replaced using updated manufacturer adhesive specifications.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Standard passive safety restraint systems that occasionally experience mechanical retractor binding or failure.

**Failure to Retract**
- **Symptoms:** Front driver and passenger seat belts fail to retract smoothly back into the stowed position after use.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt retractors and replace faulty mechanical assemblies if necessary.
