**Introduction:**

The 2012 Mercedes-Benz GLK350 is a compact luxury SUV known for its stylish exterior, premium interior, and agile handling. It offers a 3.5-liter V6 engine that delivers 268 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The GLK350 is praised for its comfortable ride, impressive fuel efficiency, and spacious cabin. However, it has faced some reliability concerns over the years.

**Engine**

**3.5-liter V6**

- **Intake Manifold Failure:**
   - Symptoms: Rough idling, engine misfires, loss of power
   - Solution: Replace intake manifold and gaskets
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,600+
   - Recalls: SB-2013050003

- **Valve Stem Seal Failure:**
   - Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, thick blue smoke from exhaust
   - Solution: Replace valve stem seals
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+

**Transmission**

**7-speed Automatic**

- **Transmission Slippage:**
   - Symptoms: Loss of acceleration, delayed gear shifts
   - Solution: Replace or overhaul transmission
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

- **Battery Drain:**
   - Symptoms: Difficulty starting, electrical system malfunction
   - Solution: Replace battery or diagnose electrical system
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300

- **Alternator Failure:**
   - Symptoms: Dimming headlights, electrical system malfunction
   - Solution: Replace alternator
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $700 - $1,200

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

- **Door Lock Malfunction:**
   - Symptoms: Doors not locking/unlocking, door open indicator light
   - Solution: Replace faulty lock actuator or door lock module
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600

**Summary:**

The 2012 Mercedes-Benz GLK350 has faced some reliability issues, primarily with its engine and transmission. The intake manifold and valve stem seal failures with the 3.5-liter V6 engine can be costly to repair. The 7-speed automatic transmission may also experience issues with slippage. However, other components such as the suspension and exterior have not reported any significant problems. Overall, the severity and frequency of these issues vary, and not all GLK350s will experience them. Regular maintenance and timely repairs can help mitigate these concerns and ensure a reliable driving experience.
