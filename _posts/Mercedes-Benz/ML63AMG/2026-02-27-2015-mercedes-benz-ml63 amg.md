# 2015 Mercedes-Benz ML63 AMG

### Overview
The 2015 Mercedes-Benz ML63 AMG, part of the W166 generation, is a high-performance luxury SUV that blends potent AMG engineering with the practicality of an SUV. While generally well-regarded for its exhilarating driving dynamics and premium interior, like any performance vehicle, it can present specific reliability concerns. Owners often praise its powerful V8 engine and sophisticated all-wheel-drive system. However, the complexity of its AMG components means that certain areas, particularly related to the powertrain and electronics, may require more attention and potentially higher maintenance costs compared to standard ML models. This analysis focuses on documented common issues beyond routine maintenance.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8 (M157)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, boost leaks, check engine light illumination, rattling noise from turbo area.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty wastegate actuator or the entire turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per actuator

**Coolant Leaks (Intercooler System)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, loss of coolant, visible coolant residue around intercooler pipes and connections.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, clamps, or intercooler core.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift Plus (7G-TRONIC)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping gears, delayed engagement, unusual noises.
- **Solution:** Rebuilding or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, failure to shift, limp mode activation, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and reprogramming or replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200


## Suspension

### Active Curve System / AirMatic
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or associated seals.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000 per corner

**Active Curve System Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from sway bar actuators, reduced body roll control, clunking noises.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hydraulic lines or actuators.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Screen Freezing or Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** COMAND screen becomes unresponsive, freezes, or displays errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2500


### Parking Sensor Failures
**Faulty Parking Sensors**
- **Symptoms:** False alerts, non-functional parking assist system, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty parking sensors.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500 per sensor

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Mercedes-Benz ML63 AMG offers a compelling blend of performance and luxury. While its core mechanicals are robust, the high-performance nature and advanced systems introduce potential reliability concerns, particularly with the twin-turbo V8 engine's wastegates and intercooler system, the AMG transmission's torque converter and TCM, and the sophisticated suspension components like the Active Curve System and AirMatic. Electrical gremlins, especially within the COMAND infotainment and parking sensor systems, can also arise. Owners should budget for potentially higher maintenance and repair costs associated with these specialized AMG features. Regular servicing by qualified technicians is crucial for maintaining its performance and longevity.
