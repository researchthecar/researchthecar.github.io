# 2023 Mercedes-Benz S500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz S500 delivers flagship luxury, cutting-edge technology, and refined performance. Prospective buyers should review owner-reported feedback regarding advanced driver assistance features, suspension and tire durability, and body components when evaluating a used model.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (M256) with EQ Boost

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine utilizes a 48-volt mild-hybrid system, producing approximately 429 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque. It provides smooth, effortless acceleration and strong overall reliability for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 9G-Tronic 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly through the standard 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system. It generally operates seamlessly with minimal owner complaints regarding mechanical failure.

## Suspension

### Tires and Wheels

Owner reports indicate occasional vulnerability to low-profile tire damage on rough road surfaces.

**Sidewall Bubbles and Tire Blowouts**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported premature sidewall blistering, bubbling, and sudden tire blowouts on low-profile tires under normal driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect tires closely for structural damage and replace compromised low-profile tires promptly with appropriately rated replacements.

## Brakes

### Adaptive Cruise Control and Driver Assistance

Advanced driver assistance features offer high-tech convenience but rely heavily on sensor calibration and software updates.

**Unexpected Deceleration and Speed Fluctuation**
- **Symptoms:** The adaptive cruise control system may occasionally brake abruptly or misread speed limit signs, leading to unintended deceleration.
- **Solution:** Have a certified dealer check for software updates or sensor recalibrations if driver assistance features behave erratically.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Body Structure

Exterior body components require careful clearance checks in parking environments and occasional monitoring of glass integrity.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The glass sunroof panel may shatter unexpectedly while driving without any apparent external impact.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for pre-existing stress fractures and work with insurance or warranty providers for panel replacement.

**Front Bumper Clearance**
- **Symptoms:** The low front fascia design can catch on parking bumpers, causing partial detachment when backing out of spaces.
- **Solution:** Exercise caution when parking near concrete blocks or curbs to avoid contacting the lower bumper valence.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz S500 remains an exceptional luxury sedan with robust powertrain options. Used-car shoppers should thoroughly inspect low-profile tires and wheels, verify that driver assistance software is up to date, and check exterior body panels during a pre-purchase inspection.
