# 2022 Mercedes-Benz S500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz S-Class represents the pinnacle of luxury, technology, and engineering, offering exquisite comfort and high-tech cabin features. However, as a highly complex flagship luxury sedan, prospective used-car buyers should carefully inspect electrical systems, suspension setups, and specific powertrain performance. While many owners enjoy a trouble-free flagship experience, owner complaints point to potential issues ranging from high-tech screen blackouts to wheel and tire durability concerns on larger factory setups.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (S 500)

The S 500 features a smooth 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine equipped with EQ Boost mild-hybrid technology, producing approximately 429 horsepower. It delivers seamless power and impressive efficiency for a large luxury sedan, though complex electronic engine controls require careful diagnostic oversight.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The check engine warning light illuminates and the engine unexpectedly stalls while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician diagnose fault codes; potential component replacement such as camshafts or associated sensors may be required.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (S 580)

The S 580 is powered by a robust 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine generating 496 horsepower, offering effortless acceleration and refined high-speed cruising. Like its inline-6 counterpart, it relies heavily on advanced vehicle architecture that can occasionally generate complex diagnostic challenges.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Severe electrical glitches or harsh mechanical jolts occur during gear transitions, impacting vehicle momentum.
- **Solution:** Inspect powertrain control modules, transmission software calibrations, and electrical connections at a specialized service center.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system standard on US models. It generally provides seamless gear changes, though isolated owner reports note occasional shift harshness requiring software updates.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Low-Profile Tires

Air suspension systems provide a cloud-like ride quality, but models equipped with optional 21-inch wheels and low-profile tires are particularly susceptible to pothole damage and premature tire failure.

**Frequent Tire Blowouts and Rim Damage**
- **Symptoms:** Repeated tire sidewall blisters, sudden blowouts, and bent rims when driving over normal road imperfections on large diameter wheels.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels and tires for structural damage, and consider swapping to higher-profile tires or standard wheels if frequent road hazards are a concern.

## Brakes

### Brake System

The advanced braking systems provide strong stopping power suited for a heavy flagship sedan, though specific environmental conditions can occasionally cause mechanical binding or noise.

**Wet Brake Seizing and Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Brakes seize or stick after exposure to rain or car washes, requiring abnormally high pedal pressure to move and producing a loud screeching sound upon release.
- **Solution:** Have the brake calipers, pads, and rotors inspected for corrosion, moisture retention, or abnormal wear.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and High-Voltage Electricals

The cabin is heavily digitized with multiple high-resolution screens and advanced driver-assistance networks. While technologically impressive, complex electrical architectures can occasionally experience software glitches or component fatigue.

**Dashboard Display and Infotainment Blackout**
- **Symptoms:** Center touchscreens and digital instrument clusters completely go blank, causing a loss of vital driving readouts, climate controls, and backup camera functionality.
- **Solution:** Perform software resets, check battery health, or replace faulty control modules and head units under warranty when applicable.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V931000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Driver distraction can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** For vehicles that were not able to be updated remotely, dealers will provide the software update, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V194000: Suspension:Subframe/Mounting Bracket](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A detached thrust arm can cause a sudden loss of vehicle control, or cause the vehicle to pull to one side during emergency braking, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the sub frame weld on the rear axle carrier and replace the rear axle carrier as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V189000: Steering: Steering Wheel/Handle Bar](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Deactivated hands-off detection will prevent DISTRONIC system features from functioning, including warnings to grab the steering wheel and calling first responders as part of the Active Emergency Stop Assist, which can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the hand detection control unit software for the DISTRONIC system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V935000: Structure:Interior Panels:Dashboard](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=S500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The dashboard may be insufficiently attached, which can result in an improper front passenger air bag deployment in the event of a crash. Improper deployment of an air bag can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace a portion of the dashboard, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz S500 delivers unmatched luxury, advanced safety tech, and serene highway comfort, making it a compelling choice for used-car buyers seeking an executive sedan. However, potential buyers should perform a thorough inspection of the vehicle's complex electrical systems, verify software updates, and carefully check the condition of the wheels and low-profile tires. Seeking out a certified pre-owned model or securing an extended warranty is highly recommended to protect against out-of-pocket costs on high-tech electronic repairs.
