**2021 Mercedes-Benz S500**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz S500 is a luxurious sedan that offers a powerful engine, a sophisticated interior, and advanced technology. It is powered by a 4.0-liter V8 biturbo engine that produces 496 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. Other engine and transmission options include a 3.0-liter inline-6 turbo engine with a 48-volt mild hybrid system, and a 4.0-liter V8 biturbo engine with a plug-in hybrid system.

The S500 is known for its spacious and well-appointed cabin, featuring high-quality materials and advanced infotainment features. However, it has also been criticized for its complex technology, which can be difficult to use for some drivers.

**Engine**

**4.0-liter V8 Biturbo**

* **Leaking oil pan gasket:** Oil leaking from the oil pan due to a faulty gasket.
    * Solution: Replace the oil pan gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Faulty fuel pump:** Engine stalling or loss of power due to a malfunctioning fuel pump.
    * Solution: Replace the fuel pump.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting problems:** Difficulty shifting gears or delayed shifts due to a faulty transmission control module (TCM).
    * Solution: Replace the TCM.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension leaks:** Loss of air pressure in the air suspension system, causing the vehicle to ride low.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the leaking air suspension components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery discharge:** Battery draining prematurely due to a faulty parasitic drain.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the parasitic drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment system glitches:** Malfunctions in the infotainment system, such as freezing or unresponsive screens.
    * Solution: Update the infotainment software or replace the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof leaks:** Water leaking into the cabin from a faulty sunroof seal.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the sunroof seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Door lock issues:** Doors not locking or unlocking properly due to a faulty door lock actuator.
    * Solution: Replace the door lock actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate control malfunctions:** Climate control system not functioning properly, causing uneven temperatures or excessive noise.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the faulty climate control components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Steering wheel vibration:** Vibration felt in the steering wheel while driving at certain speeds.
    * Solution: Balance the tires or diagnose and repair any suspension issues.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2021 Mercedes-Benz S500 is a luxurious and high-performance sedan, it has been reported to experience some issues. These issues range in severity and cost from minor electrical glitches and interior problems to more serious engine and transmission issues. Potential owners should be aware of these potential issues before purchasing a vehicle.
