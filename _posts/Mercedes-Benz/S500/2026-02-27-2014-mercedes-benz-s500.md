# 2014 Mercedes-Benz S500

### Overview
The 2014 Mercedes-Benz S500, representing the W222 generation, is a flagship luxury sedan known for its advanced technology and opulent comfort. While generally well-regarded for its build quality and sophisticated engineering, like any complex vehicle, it has specific areas that have seen more attention from owners regarding reliability. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for owners and prospective buyers. The S500 in this model year typically features a powerful V8 engine and a refined automatic transmission, contributing to its smooth driving experience but also presenting potential points of failure if not properly maintained or if manufacturing defects arise.

---


## Engine

### 4.7L Twin-Turbo V8 (M278)
**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Oil Pan)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or oil pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Transmission

### 7G-TRONIC PLUS (7-Speed Automatic)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, harsh gear changes, transmission warning light, limp mode.
- **Solution:** Replacement or repair of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping gears, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, 'chassis too low' warning message, bouncy ride.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air struts or air springs.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, 'chassis too low' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,300


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Screen Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Flickering screen, black screen, unresponsive touch controls.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $3,000

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Mercedes-Benz S500 is a luxurious and technologically advanced vehicle, but its complexity means certain components can be prone to issues. The most commonly reported technical concerns revolve around the AIRMATIC air suspension system, particularly leaks in the air struts and compressor failures. Engine-related issues, such as timing chain wear and oil leaks, can also arise, requiring significant repair costs. The 7-speed automatic transmission may experience problems with its control module or torque converter. Electrical gremlins, especially within the COMAND infotainment system and battery drain issues, are also noted. While the exterior and interior generally hold up well, owners should be prepared for potential maintenance costs associated with the advanced systems, particularly the air suspension and powertrain.
