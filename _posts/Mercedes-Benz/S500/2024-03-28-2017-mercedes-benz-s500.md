**Introduction:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S500 is a full-size luxury sedan known for its opulent interior, advanced technology, and powerful performance. It is powered by a 4.7-liter V8 engine paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. Other engine and transmission options include:

* 3.0L Inline-6 Diesel Engine with 9-speed automatic transmission
* 4.0L V8 Biturbo Engine with 9-speed automatic transmission

**Engine**

**4.7L V8 Engine with 9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced power, black smoke from exhaust, unusual noises from engine bay
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 18V711000
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, reduced power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L Inline-6 Diesel Engine with 9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Clogging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced power, increased fuel consumption, black smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Clean or replace DPF
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **EGR Valve Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rough idling, reduced power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4.0L V8 Biturbo Engine with 9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, hard shifting, loss of gears
    * Solution: Replace mechatronic unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shuddering, overheating, slipping
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging, uneven ride height, warning light
    * Solution: Replace compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Air Suspension Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle leaning to one side, rough ride, warning light
    * Solution: Replace air strut
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, trouble starting vehicle
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair source of drain, replace battery if necessary
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Ignition Coil Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, reduced power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace ignition coil
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, unresponsive, software glitches
    * Solution: Software update, replace infotainment unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Navigation System Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Maps not loading, inaccurate directions, system crashes
    * Solution: Software update, replace navigation unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2017 Mercedes-Benz S500 is known for its luxury and performance, but it has some potential issues that can be costly to repair. Common issues include turbocharger failure, timing chain tensioner failure, and suspension issues. The electrical and interior systems can also experience some problems, but these are typically less severe.
