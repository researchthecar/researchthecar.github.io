## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust ride height, constant compressor noise, suspension not inflating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (MBUX)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, intermittent connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system reset. In rare cases, module replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500 (for updates/diagnostics), $1000+ (for module replacement)


### 12V Battery System
**Premature Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, electrical systems malfunctioning, warning lights related to battery.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of battery if faulty.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Mercedes-Benz S500 is engineered for high reliability, with its primary powertrain components (engine and transmission) showing no widespread reported issues. Overall, the S500 offers a dependable luxury experience, with potential issues primarily related to complex suspension and electronic components rather than core mechanicals.
