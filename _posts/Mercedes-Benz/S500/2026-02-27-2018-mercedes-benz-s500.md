# 2018 Mercedes-Benz S500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz S-Class represents the pinnacle of luxury, comfort, and advanced automotive engineering. While generally robust, used-car buyers should review potential trouble spots regarding powertrain components, electronics, and occasional mechanical glitches typical of high-complexity flagship sedans.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (S450)

A smooth and efficient base 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine producing around 362 horsepower, offering refined performance and respectable efficiency for daily driving.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (S560 / S500)

A potent 4.0-liter bi-turbo V8 engine delivering approximately 463 horsepower, known for effortless acceleration and sophisticated operation, though complex auxiliary systems require attentive maintenance.

**Engine Misfire and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional engine misfires during acceleration accompanied by engine shaking, loss of power, and an illuminated check engine light.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose ignition coils, spark plugs, and cylinder compression, and replace faulty components as needed.

**Oil and Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Unexplained fluid spots under the vehicle, low coolant warnings, or oil seepage from rear engine seals.
- **Solution:** Inspect engine seals, gaskets, and coolant hoses for wear, and replace degraded components to prevent overheating or major fluid loss.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A sophisticated 9-speed automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel or 4MATIC all-wheel-drive configurations, designed for seamless gear transitions and optimized highway efficiency.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The gear shifter independently shifts into neutral while driving, requiring the driver to hold the shifter down for an extended period to reset the system.
- **Solution:** Have the transmission control module and gear selector assembly inspected by a dealer or specialist for software updates or hardware faults.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Advanced hydraulic braking systems providing strong stopping power suited for a heavy luxury sedan, though regular component checks are essential.

**Brake Responsiveness Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal depression fails to adequately slow or stop the vehicle, resulting in severely reduced deceleration without prior warning.
- **Solution:** Immediately inspect the master cylinder, brake booster, fluid lines, and electronic brake actuators for hydraulic or mechanical failure.

## Steering

### Electronic Steering System

Power-assisted steering system offering responsive handling characteristics, though electronic assist failures can occasionally occur.

**Steering Stiffness Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel becomes extremely difficult to turn while driving, accompanied by a dashboard warning message indicating increased physical effort is required.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic power steering motor, wiring harnesses, and control modules evaluated for faults or software glitches.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz S-Class delivers exceptional luxury, cutting-edge technology, and robust performance options.
