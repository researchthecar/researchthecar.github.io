# 2018 Mercedes-Benz S500

### Overview
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz S500 is a flagship luxury sedan renowned for its advanced technology, opulent interior, and powerful performance. As a premium vehicle, it generally exhibits strong build quality and reliability, reflecting Mercedes-Benz's commitment to engineering excellence. However, like any complex automobile, certain components can experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear, to provide a clear picture of potential areas for attention for owners and prospective buyers of this model year.

---


## Engine

### 4.7L Twin-Turbo V8 (M278)
**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Oil Pan)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of gaskets and seals.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Transmission

### 7G-TRONIC PLUS (7-Speed Automatic)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, rough shifting, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, limp mode.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,500


## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, 'airmatic system malfunction' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air struts or air springs.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,800 per strut

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of compressor, 'airmatic system malfunction' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Screen Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Display flickering, unresponsive touch screen, system reboots.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, frequent need for jump starts, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to find the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, short circuit) and repair.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz S500 is a highly sophisticated luxury vehicle with a generally strong reliability record. The most commonly reported technical issues tend to be related to the advanced AIRMATIC air suspension system, particularly air strut leaks and compressor failures, which can be costly to repair. The 4.7L twin-turbo V8 engine can experience timing chain wear and oil leaks, while the 7-speed automatic transmission may develop torque converter issues or TCM malfunctions. Electrical gremlins, especially within the COMAND infotainment system and occasional battery drain problems, are also noted. Despite these potential concerns, the S500 offers a premium ownership experience, and addressing these issues proactively can help maintain its performance and comfort.
