# 2017 Mercedes-Benz S500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S-Class represents the pinnacle of luxury, comfort, and advanced engineering, though prospective buyers should anticipate higher-than-average maintenance and repair costs typical of a flagship German luxury sedan. While major mechanical breakdowns are relatively uncommon compared to cosmetic and electronic nuances, any issues that do arise can be quite expensive to resolve. Reviewing past maintenance records and having a pre-purchase inspection performed by a specialist can help mitigate unexpected ownership expenses.

---

## Engine

### 4.7L Biturbo V8

The 4.7-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers robust power with approximately 449 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, offering exceptionally smooth and effortless acceleration. It is generally a robust powerplant, though routine maintenance must be strictly maintained to prevent ancillary fluid leaks.

**Fuel Tank Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Fuel odors around the vehicle, visible gas leaks under the rear, or a persistent check engine light after refueling.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel tank assembly and replace or repair damaged components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides seamless gear changes and helps optimize fuel efficiency on the highway. It pairs smoothly with the available drivetrain options, maintaining high standards of luxury refinement.

**Automatic Shift to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle shifts into neutral rather than park when the ignition button is pressed while the foot is on the brake.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic shifter module and transmission software evaluated by a qualified technician.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Rims

Suspension components are sophisticated and provide an exceptionally plush ride, though large low-profile AMG wheels are susceptible to road impact damage.

**Cracking AMG Rims**
- **Symptoms:** Low-profile wheels develop structural cracks under normal driving conditions, leading to slow air leaks.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged wheel rims and consider standardizing wheel and tire profiles if recurring.

## Steering

### Steering Column

Power steering and adjustment features offer high levels of customization, though power-adjustable columns can occasionally experience motor binding.

**Steering Column Adjustment Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel moves in and out normally, but fails to adjust upward or downward, remaining stuck in place.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the steering column adjustment motor or associated switch gear.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Sunroof

Exterior glass and panoramic roof assemblies require periodic inspection to ensure proper alignment and seal integrity.

**Sunroof and Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof jams or makes a cracking sound during operation, or the windshield develops recurring cracks.
- **Solution:** Lubricate or repair the sunroof tracks and mechanism, or replace damaged glass as needed.

## Interior

### Dashboard Trim

Cabin materials are exceptionally high quality, though certain leather dash components can warp or separate under prolonged sun exposure.

**Leather Dashboard Separation**
- **Symptoms:** The leather wrapping on the dashboard begins to pull away or lift near the passenger-side airbag deployment zone.
- **Solution:** Re-secure or replace the dashboard assembly through an authorized dealer or interior specialist.
- **Cost:** $3,000

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S-Class delivers unmatched comfort, advanced technology, and prestigious styling, making it an appealing pre-owned luxury buy. However, prospective buyers should budget for higher maintenance costs and verify that comfort features, interior trim, and electrical systems operate without flaw before purchasing.
