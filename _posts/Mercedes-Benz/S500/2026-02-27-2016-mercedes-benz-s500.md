# 2016 Mercedes-Benz S500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S-Class represents the pinnacle of luxury, engineering, and advanced automotive technology. While it offers a supremely comfortable ride and powerful engine options, used car buyers should approach high-end luxury flagships with diligence. Maintenance and repair costs can be substantial, and periodic owner complaints highlight concerns ranging from electrical anomalies to structural glass issues.

---

## Engine

### 4.7L Bi-Turbo V8

The 4.7-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers robust power (approximately 449 horsepower) and effortless acceleration. It is generally robust, but complex emissions and exhaust components require careful inspection on high-mileage examples.

**Catalytic Converter Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Warning lights illuminate on the dashboard due to factory defects in the catalytic converters before 80,000 miles.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the faulty catalytic converter assemblies.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission provides smooth, refined gear shifts tailored to luxury cruising. No widespread or recurring mechanical complaint patterns are highlighted for this unit.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Run-Flat Tires

Large wheels paired with stiff run-flat tires are vulnerable to road imperfections, increasing the likelihood of rim damage and tire failures.

**Wheel Cracks and Tire Blowouts**
- **Symptoms:** Staggered low-profile run-flat tires experience sudden sidewall blowouts, and factory wheels are prone to developing structural cracks.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged wheels and inspect low-profile tires regularly for structural integrity.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Issues involving intermittent power steering loss can pose severe safety risks and demand immediate professional evaluation.

**Power Steering Lockout**
- **Symptoms:** The power steering failure warning light illuminates and the steering wheel unexpectedly locks out or deactivates while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the electronic steering components and replace failing modules or sensors.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Glass Roof and Windshield

Large glass surfaces enhance the cabin feel but can occasionally suffer from sudden stress fractures or adhesive failures.

**Spontaneous Glass Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Panoramic sunroof glass or front windshield cracks or shatters spontaneously while driving or while parked with no evidence of impact from debris.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the surrounding frame and body alignment for stress points.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Ensuring occupant safety restraints are fully functional is critical, as buckle and retractor failures have been reported by owners.

**Seat Belt Buckle and Retractor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Front passenger seat belt fails to latch securely into the buckle, or rear seat belts become stuck and fail to retract.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace malfunctioning seat belt buckles or retractor mechanisms.

## Summary Verdict

Purchasing a pre-owned 2016 Mercedes-Benz S-Class requires a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable specialist. While the car delivers unmatched luxury and performance, prospective buyers should budget for potentially high upkeep costs and verify the condition of complex electrical systems, suspension components, and glass panels.
