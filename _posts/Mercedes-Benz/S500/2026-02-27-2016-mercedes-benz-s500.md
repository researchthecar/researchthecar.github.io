# 2016 Mercedes-Benz S500

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S500 is a flagship luxury sedan renowned for its advanced technology, opulent interior, and powerful performance. As a top-tier model, it generally exhibits strong build quality and reliability, reflecting its premium positioning. However, like any complex vehicle, certain areas may experience issues over time, particularly those related to its sophisticated electronic systems and high-performance powertrain. This analysis focuses on documented, non-routine reliability concerns, excluding standard maintenance and wear-and-tear items, to provide a clear picture of potential technical challenges owners might encounter.

## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air strut(s).
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, audio cutting out, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or replacement of control unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500


### Sensors (Parking, Blind Spot)
**Malfunctioning Sensors**
- **Symptoms:** False warnings, system deactivation, error messages related to parking assist or blind spot monitoring.
- **Solution:** Cleaning, recalibration, or replacement of faulty sensor(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $800 per sensor/module

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S500 is a highly reliable luxury vehicle, with its powertrain and core mechanicals demonstrating excellent durability. The primary areas where owners might encounter non-routine issues are related to the sophisticated AIRMATIC air suspension system, particularly air strut leaks and compressor failures, and the complex electrical systems, including infotainment glitches and sensor malfunctions. While these issues can be costly to repair, they are not indicative of systemic failure across the model. With proper maintenance and attention to these specific components, the S500 should provide a dependable and luxurious ownership experience.
