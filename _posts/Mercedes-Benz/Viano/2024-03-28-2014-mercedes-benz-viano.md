**Introduction**

The 2014 Mercedes-Benz Viano is a luxury minivan that offers versatility, comfort, and performance. It is available with a range of engine and transmission options, including a 3.0L V6 diesel engine and a 7-speed automatic transmission. The Viano is known for its spacious interior, smooth ride, and powerful engine, but it has also experienced some issues over the years.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6 Diesel**

* **Turbocharger failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased engine noise, blue or black smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **EGR valve failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Decreased fuel economy, rough idling, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed gear engagement, slipping between gears
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque converter failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering, vibration, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height
    * Solution: Replace air suspension compressor or struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, car struggles to start
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlamp failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlamps flicker or stop working entirely
    * Solution: Replace headlamp bulbs or modules
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear window wiper failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Wiper does not move or functions intermittently
    * Solution: Replace rear window wiper motor or linkage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate control failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC or heater does not blow cold or hot air
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair HVAC system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door lock failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power door locks do not function properly
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 Mercedes-Benz Viano is a reliable vehicle overall, but it has experienced some issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. The severity of these issues can vary, but most can be repaired for a reasonable cost. If you are considering purchasing a Viano, be sure to have it inspected by a qualified mechanic to identify any potential issues before they become major problems.
