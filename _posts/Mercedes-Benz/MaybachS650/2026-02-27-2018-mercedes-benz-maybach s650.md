# 2018 Mercedes-Benz Maybach S650

### Overview
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz Maybach S650 represents the pinnacle of luxury and performance, combining a potent V12 engine with unparalleled comfort and advanced technology. As a flagship model, its reliability is generally high, reflecting meticulous engineering and premium componentry. However, like any complex vehicle, certain areas may experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear, to provide a clear picture for owners and potential buyers. The Maybach S650 is built to a high standard, and its reliability data reflects this, with most reported issues being isolated or related to the advanced electronic systems characteristic of modern ultra-luxury vehicles.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12 (M279)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light illumination, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Turbocharger Actuator Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of boost pressure, sluggish acceleration, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the turbocharger wastegate actuator.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or associated seals.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, suspension warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, navigation system errors, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1000


### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS)
**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** False warnings from adaptive cruise control, lane keeping assist, or parking sensors; system deactivation.
- **Solution:** Calibration or replacement of faulty sensors (e.g., radar, camera).
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000

- **Solution:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Cost:** No widespread issues reported.


## Interior

### Climate Control System
**Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inconsistent temperature distribution, inability to control airflow to specific vents, unusual noises from the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty HVAC blend door actuators.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### Seat Adjustment Motors
**Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust seat position, slow or jerky seat movement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the electric seat adjustment motor.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz Maybach S650 demonstrates strong overall reliability, befitting its ultra-luxury status. The V12 engine, while powerful, has a few documented issues like ignition coil and turbo actuator problems, which are generally repairable. The AIRMATIC suspension, a key comfort feature, can be prone to air spring leaks or compressor issues, representing a significant repair cost. Electrical systems, particularly the advanced infotainment and ADAS, may experience software glitches or sensor malfunctions, common in high-tech vehicles. Interior components like seat motors and climate control actuators can also fail. Despite these points, the Maybach S650 is a robust vehicle, and with diligent maintenance and prompt attention to any emerging issues, it offers a dependable and luxurious ownership experience.
