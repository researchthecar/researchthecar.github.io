# 2007 Mercedes-Benz CLK500

### Overview
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz CLK500, part of the W209 generation, is a luxury grand tourer known for its blend of performance and comfort. Powered by a robust V8 engine and typically paired with a smooth automatic transmission, it offers a refined driving experience. While generally well-regarded for its build quality and driving dynamics, like any vehicle of its age, it can exhibit specific reliability concerns. This analysis focuses on common issues reported by owners and technicians, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear, to provide a clear picture of its technical reliability.

---


## Engine

### 5.0L M113 V8
**Oil Cooler Seals Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil in coolant reservoir, coolant in oil, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of oil cooler seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Vacuum leaks, rough idle, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of intake manifold gaskets.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Transmission

### 5G-Tronic (722.6) 5-Speed Automatic
**Conductor Plate Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, transmission slipping, 'limp mode', P0705 code.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the conductor plate and associated sensors.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, no gear engagement, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**SAM Unit (Signal Acquisition Module) Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical failures, malfunctioning lights, power windows, wipers.
- **Solution:** Cleaning and sealing of SAM unit, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not working, window falling into door.
- **Solution:** Replacement of window regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

## Interior

### Cabin Components
**Headliner Sagging**
- **Symptoms:** Fabric detaching from the headliner board.
- **Solution:** Re-gluing or replacement of headliner.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Pixelated Instrument Cluster Display**
- **Symptoms:** Missing or garbled information on the dashboard display.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz CLK500 offers a compelling package for those seeking a luxurious and sporty coupe. While its V8 engine and automatic transmission are generally robust, potential owners should be aware of specific common issues. Leaking oil cooler seals and intake manifold gaskets are notable engine concerns. The 5G-Tronic transmission can experience problems with the conductor plate or TCM. Electrical gremlins, particularly related to the SAM unit and window regulators, are also reported. Interior issues like sagging headliners and pixelated displays are cosmetic or functional annoyances. Despite these potential pitfalls, with diligent maintenance and addressing these known issues, the CLK500 can remain a reliable and enjoyable vehicle.
