# 2014 Mercedes-Benz S400

### Overview
The 2014 Mercedes-Benz S400, part of the W222 generation, is a luxury sedan renowned for its advanced technology, opulent interior, and refined driving experience. While generally considered a reliable vehicle, particularly for its class, like any complex luxury car, it can encounter specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, examining common problem areas reported by owners and technicians. The S400 typically features a hybrid powertrain, combining a V6 gasoline engine with an electric motor, which introduces unique considerations for its long-term durability and repair costs.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid (276.950) + Electric Motor
**Hybrid Battery Degradation**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced electric-only range, decreased overall power output, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Hybrid battery pack replacement.
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $7,000

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant level warnings, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace leaking hoses, water pump, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,200

## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, 'Visit Workshop' message.
- **Solution:** Replace leaking air struts or air springs.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, loss of air pressure.
- **Solution:** Replace the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, navigation system errors, audio cutting out.
- **Solution:** Software updates, control unit reset, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1,000


### 12V Auxiliary Battery
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical systems malfunctioning, warning lights, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Replace the 12V auxiliary battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Mercedes-Benz S400 is a sophisticated luxury vehicle with a generally good reliability record for its segment. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to its advanced hybrid system, specifically the longevity of the hybrid battery pack and potential coolant leaks within the powertrain. The AIRMATIC air suspension system, while providing excellent comfort, can be a source of costly repairs if air struts or the compressor fail. Electrical issues, particularly with the COMAND infotainment system and the auxiliary battery, are also noted. Despite these potential issues, many owners find the S400 to be a dependable and luxurious car when properly maintained, with repair costs reflecting its premium nature.
