# 2021 Mercedes-Benz A220 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used compact luxury car, the 2021 Mercedes-Benz A220 offers sophisticated styling and modern cabin technology. However, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback regarding electrical issues and driver assistance quirks to ensure a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M260)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 188 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for daily driving. It generally offers solid performance, though owners have occasionally reported unexpected electrical shutdowns.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and pairs with front-wheel drive or 4MATIC all-wheel drive. It is engineered for efficiency and responsiveness in diverse driving conditions.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Owner reports highlight occasional electrical system concerns, including power interruptions and component delays.

**Unexpected Vehicle Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may completely shut down while in motion without prior warning, posing a significant hazard in traffic.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician or dealership inspect the electrical architecture and ensure all relevant campaigns or software updates are applied.

### Backup Camera and Driver Assistance

Safety equipment generally functions well, though occasional reports note camera display failures and driver assist system anomalies.

**Blank Backup Camera Display**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera screen displays blank or reports being inoperative when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera wiring connections and consult a dealer to determine if software reprogramming or module replacement is required.

**Phantom Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may suddenly decelerate while using cruise control or advanced driving assistance features past off-ramps.
- **Solution:** Keep driver assistance software updated and remain alert to take manual control when traffic sign or speed adoption misreads roadway layouts.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V034000: Child Seat](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=A220&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** If the anchoring bar is missing from the ISOFIX bracket, the child restraint cannot be properly secured to the seat using the ISOFIX securing system, increasing the risk of injury for a child in that seat.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the right-rear ISOFIX bracket and replace the bracket, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz A220 brings advanced luxury features to the entry-level market, but used car buyers should verify that all electronic systems and software updates are fully functioning. Conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help catch potential electrical or sensor glitches before finalizing the purchase.
