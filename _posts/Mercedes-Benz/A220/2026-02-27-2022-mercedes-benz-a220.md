# 2022 Mercedes-Benz A220 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a 2022 Mercedes-Benz A220, buyers are looking at a sleek luxury compact sedan that offers advanced tech and agile handling. While overall owner feedback is limited, prospective purchasers should remain aware of isolated owner-reported concerns regarding powertrain stability and exterior glass durability.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 188 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for the compact sedan. While generally responsive, isolated owner reports highlight severe internal component failures requiring major mechanical intervention.

**Premature Piston Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The engine may die or shut down unexpectedly, sputter, rock, shake excessively, and lack power.
- **Solution:** Requires extensive diagnostic testing and a full engine replacement or replacement of all four pistons.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and pairs with either front-wheel drive or 4MATIC all-wheel drive. It generally offers smooth operation, though buyers should test drive thoroughly to ensure crisp engagement across all gears.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior body components and glass panels generally maintain a modern aesthetic, but owners should inspect panoramic roof glass carefully for structural integrity.

**Shattering Sunroof Glass**
- **Symptoms:** The glass panel of the sunroof may spontaneously shatter while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for any stress fractures and replace the entire glass assembly if damage is found.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V662000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=A220&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Incorrectly adjusted headlights can reduce the visibility of oncoming traffic, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and correct the headlight adjustment, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz A220 presents an attractive option for used car buyers seeking entry-level luxury, modern infotainment, and sporty performance. Because owner complaint data points to isolated yet severe incidents involving internal engine components and exterior glass, buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection and review maintenance records before finalizing a purchase.
