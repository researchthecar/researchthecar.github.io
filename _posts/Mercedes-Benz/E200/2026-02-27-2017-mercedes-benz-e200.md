# 2017 Mercedes-Benz E200

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz E200, part of the W213 generation, is generally regarded as a reliable executive sedan, blending luxury with advanced technology. It typically features a potent yet efficient turbocharged gasoline engine and a smooth automatic transmission. Owners often praise its refined driving experience, high-quality interior, and comprehensive safety features. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the E200 from this model year has demonstrated a solid track record, with most reported concerns being minor or related to specific component failures rather than widespread design flaws. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring its long-term performance and reliability.

### Standard/Optional Air Body Control
**Air Suspension Leaks (Air Body Control)**

**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dying prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle, warning lights related to battery.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz E200 is a strong contender in the luxury sedan market, offering a high level of refinement and technology. Reliability is generally good, with the powertrain being particularly robust. The most common areas for potential concern involve the optional Air Body Control suspension system, which can develop leaks, and occasional electrical gremlins such as infotainment glitches or battery drain issues. These are not typically indicative of systemic failures but rather isolated component problems that can be addressed. With diligent maintenance and prompt attention to any emerging issues, the E200 should provide a dependable and luxurious ownership experience.
