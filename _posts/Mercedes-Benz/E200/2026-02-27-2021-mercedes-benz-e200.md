# 2021 Mercedes-Benz E200

### Overview
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz E200, part of the W213 generation, generally presents a strong reliability profile, reflecting Mercedes-Benz's commitment to quality engineering. This model year benefits from mature technology and refined components. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the E200 typically avoids widespread, recurring problems. Owners can expect a premium driving experience with a focus on comfort and performance. Common areas of attention, as with many modern luxury vehicles, tend to be in the sophisticated electronic systems and specific powertrain elements, though significant failures are not prevalent. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring the longevity and optimal performance of this sophisticated sedan.

**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated seals.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (MBUX)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, connectivity issues (Bluetooth/Apple CarPlay/Android Auto).

### 12V Battery
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Electrical system malfunctions, slow cranking, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V battery.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz E200 demonstrates good overall reliability, with the powertrain (engine and transmission) being particularly robust. Potential concerns are minor and primarily related to the sophisticated electronic systems, such as infotainment glitches, and specific components like air suspension springs in equipped models. The 12V battery is a common replacement item across many modern vehicles and can be a point of failure if not maintained. For the most part, owners can expect a dependable luxury vehicle, provided routine maintenance is performed. Addressing software updates promptly and being aware of potential air suspension issues can help mitigate minor inconveniences.
