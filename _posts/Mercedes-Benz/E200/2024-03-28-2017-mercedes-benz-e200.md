**Introduction**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz E200 is a luxury sedan powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 184 horsepower and 207 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a nine-speed automatic transmission. The E200 is known for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and advanced safety features. However, it has also received some criticism for its high price and lack of performance compared to some rivals.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**
    * **Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and engine damage
        * Solution: Replace piston rings and valve stem seals
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive smoke, and loud noises from the turbocharger
        * Solution: Replace the turbocharger
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Nine-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shifting Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed shifts, and rough shifting
        * Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission control module
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension**
    * **Leakage**
        * Description/Symptoms: Air leaks from the suspension system, leading to reduced ride height and handling issues
        * Solution: Replace air bags or seals
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is parked
        * Solution: Replace faulty batteries or electrical components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen malfunctions, software glitches, and connectivity problems
        * Solution: Update software or replace infotainment system
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulates inside the headlights, reducing visibility
        * Solution: Replace headlights or seal gaps
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating not working properly, or excessive noise from the climate control system
        * Solution: Replace faulty parts or update software
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz E200 has received positive feedback for its comfort, space, and safety features. However, it has also experienced some reliability issues with its engine, transmission, electrical system, and suspension. The severity of these issues varies, with some being minor inconveniences and others being more significant and expensive to repair.
