**Introduction:**

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz E200 is a luxurious midsize sedan that offers a refined and comfortable driving experience. Powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-four engine, it produces 197 horsepower and 280 pound-feet of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The E200 is praised for its sleek design, spacious interior, and advanced technology features. However, some owners have reported various issues with different components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **M264 2.0L Inline-Four Engine**

**No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **9G-Tronic 9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Insufficient Fluid Pressure**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission may slip or become unresponsive.
Solution: Replace fluid pressure sensor or transmission control unit (TCU).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension**

**Air Compressor Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Suspension may become stiff or unresponsive.
Solution: Replace air compressor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drainage**
Description/Symptoms: Battery may drain quickly, leading to starting issues.
Solution: Update software, replace faulty battery, or check for parasitic current draw.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment System Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Infotainment system may freeze, crash, or fail to respond.
Solution: Update software or replace infotainment unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Exterior Trim Corrosion**
Description/Symptoms: Exterior trim, such as door handles or chrome accents, may show signs of corrosion.
Solution: Replace affected trim pieces.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Creaking**
Description/Symptoms: Sunroof may creak or rattle when opened or closed.
Solution: Lubricate sunroof rails or replace sunroof motor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,200+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz E200 has been reported to have some issues, particularly with its transmission and suspension systems. Electrical issues and certain interior concerns have also been noted. While these issues can be costly to fix, it's important to keep in mind that each vehicle's experience may vary. Regular maintenance and addressing any problems promptly can help ensure the longevity and reliability of the E200.
