# 2016 Mercedes-Benz SL400 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2016 Mercedes-Benz SL400 means looking at a luxury grand tourer built for performance and comfort. While limited owner-reported data is available, buyers should carefully evaluate the vehicle's condition, especially regarding wear-and-tear items like tires and complex onboard electronics. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified specialist remains essential.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz SL400 is powered by a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine producing approximately 329 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant delivers smooth, responsive acceleration while serving as the entry-level engine for the roadster lineup.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Power is routed to the rear wheels via a 7-speed automatic transmission designed for quick, refined gear shifts. Regular fluid maintenance is recommended to ensure long-term durability and smooth operation.

## Interior

### Tires

Factory run-flat tires provide mobility during pressure loss but can be susceptible to wear or road hazard sensitivity.

**Premature Tire Pressure Warning**
- **Symptoms:** An alert notification displays on the dashboard indicating low pressure on a specific tire while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the tire for punctures or slow leaks, and replace or repair as necessary according to manufacturer specifications.
