# 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS550

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS550, a full-size luxury SUV, generally offers a robust and reliable ownership experience, reflecting Mercedes-Benz's commitment to quality engineering. It is equipped with a powerful twin-turbocharged V8 engine and a sophisticated 9-speed automatic transmission, designed for both performance and comfort. While the vehicle excels in many areas, like most complex luxury vehicles, certain components may experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### 4.7L Twin-Turbo V8
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential replacement of turbocharger seals, wastegates, or the entire turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,500

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level warnings.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking gaskets, seals, or oil cooler.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic
**Harsh Shifting / Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, delayed engagement, or hesitation during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Software updates, transmission fluid flush and replacement, or potential internal component repair/rebuild.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,200


## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,500 per corner

**Suspension Control Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning suspension height adjustment, warning lights on the dashboard, stiff or overly soft ride.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of the suspension control module.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing / Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, displays errors, or restarts randomly.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the head unit/display.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, vehicle struggles to start, electrical components malfunction.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from modules or accessories, followed by repair or replacement of faulty component.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS550 is a generally reliable luxury SUV, particularly in its core powertrain components. The 4.7L V8 engine and 9-speed transmission are robust, though potential issues like turbocharger problems and occasional transmission shifting anomalies can arise, often requiring significant repair costs. The AIRMATIC air suspension system, while providing excellent comfort, is a notable area where owners may encounter expensive repairs related to leaks or module failures. Electrical gremlins, especially within the infotainment system and potential battery drain issues, are also documented concerns. Despite these potential drawbacks, with diligent maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs, the GLS550 can offer a dependable and luxurious driving experience.
