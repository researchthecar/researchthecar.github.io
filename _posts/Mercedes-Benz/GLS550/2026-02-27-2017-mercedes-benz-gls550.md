# 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS550 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS550 offers luxury, ample seating, and robust performance, but buyers looking at the pre-owned market should approach with care. While owner reports to federal safety regulators cover a range of component categories, powertrain and suspension complexity demand close inspection. As a high-end luxury European SUV, keeping up with maintenance is essential to avoid steep unexpected repair bills.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (GLS 450)

Though not the standard GLS550 powerplant, this 3.0-liter biturbo V6 generates around 362 horsepower and offers respectable punch for everyday driving. It serves as the baseline engine for the broader GLS lineup, balancing decent fuel economy with traditional Mercedes performance.

### 4.7L Twin-Turbo V8 (GLS 550)

The 4.7-liter biturbo V8 churns out an impressive 449 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless acceleration and substantial towing capability. Buyers should verify complete service history and check for any history of fuel or oil leaks around the engine bay.

**Fuel System and Smell**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle occupants may notice strong gasoline odors or sudden engine stalling while driving due to fuel rail or delivery component degradation.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines and rails for leaks, and verify that all relevant manufacturer service campaigns or recalls have been performed.

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8 (AMG GLS 63)

Reserved for the high-performance AMG variant, this hand-built 5.5-liter V8 pushes out a staggering 577 horsepower for thrilling straight-line performance. It requires meticulous maintenance and premium upkeep to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with select engines, this 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and seamless gear shifts on the highway. Routine fluid changes are critical to maintaining shift quality and longevity.

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 7-speed automatic transmission routes high torque to the 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system, providing confident power delivery. Periodic fluid services are necessary to prevent premature internal wear.

**Shifting and Parking Engagement Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may fail to properly engage Park, or can exhibit erratic shifting behavior and unexpected gear changes without driver input.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the valve body, electronic control modules, and transmission software for faults or wear.

## Suspension

### Airmatic Suspension

The adjustable air suspension system provides a plush, cloud-like ride quality and adjustable ground clearance, but out-of-warranty repairs can become quite expensive as components age.

**Air Suspension Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may fail to inflate the air springs, resulting in a harsh, bumpy ride, sagging corners, or complete loss of ride-height control and vehicle handling stability.
- **Solution:** Replace failing air struts, compressor pumps, or leaking air lines as needed.
- **Cost:** $3900

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Steering feel is generally well-weighted and responsive for a large luxury SUV, though electronic assist faults can occasionally disable power assistance.

**Power Steering Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** A power steering malfunction warning message may appear on the dash, followed by a sudden loss of power steering assist while driving.
- **Solution:** Check electronic steering modules, wiring harnesses, and fluid levels or pump components for failure.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical architecture governs numerous convenience and safety systems, making thorough pre-purchase checks vital.

**Headlight Output Degradation**
- **Symptoms:** Low beam headlights can dim or fade significantly over time, creating poor forward visibility when driving at night or in inclement weather.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight assemblies, bulbs, and ballasts, and replace worn components or housings as necessary.

## Interior

### Seats

Cabin appointments are upscale and feature-rich, though motorized adjustments and seat electronics require verification during a test drive.

**Seat Heater Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Seat warmers may overheat abnormally, causing extreme discomfort or burning sensations for occupants.
- **Solution:** Disconnect the affected seat heater element immediately and have the wiring and heating elements inspected or replaced by a technician.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLS550 delivers immense power, advanced luxury features, and spacious three-row accommodations, but prospective buyers should proceed with a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Suspension components, complex electrical accessories, and high-performance powertrain parts can incur substantial out-of-pocket repair expenses if deferred. Securing a detailed service history and prioritizing a well-maintained example will greatly improve your ownership experience.
