**Introduction:**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GL350 is a mid-size luxury SUV known for its combination of performance, comfort, and practicality. It features a 3.5L V6 engine with 302 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. Praised for its spacious cabin, premium interior, and advanced technology, some owners have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**Engine:** 3.5L V6

* **Intake Manifold Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vacuum leak, rough idle, decreased fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from engine, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Transmission:** 7-Speed Automatic

* **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, delayed shifting, transmission slipping
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Control Module (TCM) Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Incorrect gear selection, limp mode
    * Solution: Update or replace TCM
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Suspension failure, uneven vehicle ride height
    * Solution: Replace air suspension compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise over bumps, unstable handling
    * Solution: Replace strut mount bearing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, slow engine cranking
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system, replace battery if necessary
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Command System Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system errors, audio system issues, Bluetooth connectivity problems
    * Solution: Software update or replace Command unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside headlight assembly
    * Solution: Replace headlight assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Ineffective climate control, A/C not blowing cold air
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair climate control system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into cabin through sunroof
    * Solution: Adjust or replace sunroof seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GL350 has several potential issues to consider, including engine intake manifold leaks, timing chain tensioner failure, transmission problems, suspension issues, and electrical malfunctions. Although these issues can be expensive to repair, the cost varies depending on the severity of the problem and the specific parts involved. Overall, it's important to weigh the potential costs against the GL350's overall reliability and performance before making a purchase decision.
