# 2016 Mercedes-Benz GL350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury full-size SUV, the 2016 Mercedes-Benz GL350 offers impressive capability, space, and refinement. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints regarding electrical anomalies, body and glass integrity, and powertrain quirks to ensure proper maintenance and inspection before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo Diesel V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged diesel V6 engine produces approximately 240 horsepower and 455 lb-ft of torque, providing strong low-end pulling power and respectable highway efficiency for towing and heavy hauling. Regular maintenance of the complex emissions and fuel systems is critical for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear shifts paired with standard 4MATIC all-wheel drive, though routine fluid changes are vital to prevent premature wear.

**Unexpected Gear Disengagement**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may spontaneously disengage from park, allowing the vehicle to roll unintentionally while unattended.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic shifter module, software calibrations, and parking pawl mechanism for proper operation.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

The vehicle relies heavily on complex wiring harnesses and control modules that can be sensitive to moisture intrusion and battery draw issues.

**Water Intrusion in Trunk Compartment**
- **Symptoms:** Water accumulates in the spare tire well and battery compartment, potentially damaging the starter and battery systems.
- **Solution:** Check and clear rear sunroof drains, tailgate seals, and taillight gaskets to prevent moisture ingress.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Windshield Glass

Exterior glass and body seals require careful inspection during a used car purchase to verify structural integrity and weatherproofing.

**Spontaneous Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof or windshield may crack or spontaneously shatter while driving or parked, creating a loud noise and raining glass into the cabin.
- **Solution:** Inspect glass for prior stress cracks or stone chips, and replace damaged panels with OEM glass components.

### Tailgate and Locks

Cabin electronics, seating adjustments, and convenience features generally perform well but can experience intermittent latch or sensor faults.

**Power Tailgate Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The tailgate may occasionally open while driving or fail to latch properly when closing via the electronic buttons.
- **Solution:** Inspect the tailgate latch actuator, wiring harness in the hinge area, and door control module.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety systems generally provide robust protection, but owners should verify that all seat belts and restraint mechanisms operate smoothly.

**Seat Belt Retractor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Front passenger seat belts may fail to retract properly after use, remaining loose or slack.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat belt assembly and retractor mechanism, and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GL350 provides high-end luxury and capability, but used-car buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the panoramic glass, rear trunk compartment for water intrusion, and all electronic features. Verifying service history and ensuring open recalls have been addressed will help secure a more dependable ownership experience.
