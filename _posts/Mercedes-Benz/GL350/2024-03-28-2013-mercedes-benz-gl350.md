**Introduction:**

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz GL350 is a luxury SUV powered by a 3.5L V6 engine producing 302 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It comes with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The GL350 is known for its spacious and luxurious interior, as well as its off-road capabilities. However, it has also been noted for certain issues in various components.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

**Oil Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Leak from the rear main seal or oil filter housing gasket, causing oil loss and a visible leak.
Solution: Replace the faulty seal or gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

**Hesitation/Jerking**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or jerks during gear changes, especially when shifting from 2nd to 3rd gear.
Solution: Reprogramming of transmission control module or replacement of valve body.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, especially after the vehicle has been parked overnight.
Solution: Diagnose and repair any parasitic drain sources, such as malfunctioning modules or relays.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Sunroof Rust**
Description/Symptoms: Rust develops along the edges of the panoramic sunroof, leading to leaks and discoloration.
Solution: Replace the sunroof assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Power Seat Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Power seat fails to move in one or more directions, or makes unusual noises while adjusting.
Solution: Replace the faulty power seat motor or module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz GL350 has a few notable issues, primarily in the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. While the severity of the issues varies, some, such as the battery drain and sunroof rust, can be costly to repair. Overall, the vehicle's issues do not appear to be widespread or debilitating, but potential buyers should be aware of them before purchasing.
