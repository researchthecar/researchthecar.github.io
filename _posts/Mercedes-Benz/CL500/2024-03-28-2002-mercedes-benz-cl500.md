**Introduction**

The 2002 Mercedes-Benz CL500 is a luxury coupe known for its performance and comfort. It features a 5.0-liter V8 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. The engine produces 302 horsepower and 339 lb-ft of torque. The CL500 is praised for its smooth and powerful performance, luxurious interior, and elegant styling. However, it has also been reported to have some common issues.

**Engine**

**5.0L V8**

* **Valve stem seal failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust
  * Solution: Replace valve stem seals
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing chain tensioner failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, decreased engine performance
  * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission slipping or shuddering**
  * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping during acceleration, shuddering while driving
  * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging, reduced ride height, uneven tire wear
  * Solution: Replace air suspension struts or compressor
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, warning light on dash, dimming or flickering lights
  * Solution: Replace alternator
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Engine won't start, clicking noise when trying to start
  * Solution: Replace starter
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Clearcoat peeling**
  * Description/Symptoms: Clearcoat layer peeling or bubbling, dull appearance
  * Solution: Repaint affected panels
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard cracking**
  * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on the dashboard surface, especially near the windshield
  * Solution: Replace dashboard or repair with epoxy
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Wood trim fading**
  * Description/Symptoms: Wood trim on the dash or doors fading or discoloring
  * Solution: Refinish or replace wood trim
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2002 Mercedes-Benz CL500 is a capable and stylish luxury coupe. However, it is not without its issues. Engine problems, such as valve stem seal failure and timing chain tensioner failure, can be expensive to repair. Transmission issues, including slipping or shuddering, are also common. Suspension problems, such as air suspension failure, can affect the ride quality and safety. Electrical issues, such as alternator and starter failure, can leave the vehicle stranded. Lastly, exterior and interior issues, such as clearcoat peeling, dashboard cracking, and wood trim fading, can affect the overall appearance and comfort of the vehicle. While some of these issues are relatively minor, others can be significant and require costly repairs.
