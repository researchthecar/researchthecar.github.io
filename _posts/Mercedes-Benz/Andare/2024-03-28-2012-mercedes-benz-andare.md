**Introduction**

The 2012 Mercedes-Benz Andare is a luxury sedan that offers a powerful engine and a sophisticated interior. It is available with a 3.5L V6 engine that produces 302 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, or a 4.6L V8 engine that produces 402 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6**
    * **Intake Vaccum Leak**
      * Description/Symptoms: Decreased engine power and fuel efficiency, rough idle, check engine light illumination
      * Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500
      * Recalls: None Found
* **4.6L V8**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slipping**
      * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed engagement, shuddering during acceleration
      * Solution: Replace transmission clutch packs or torque converter
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
      * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
      * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains quickly, even when parked
      * Solution: Replace faulty battery or charging system components (alternator, voltage regulator)
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
      * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Malfunction**
      * Description/Symptoms: Headlights flicker or fail to illuminate
      * Solution: Replace headlight bulbs, wiring, or control module
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500
      * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Trunk Lid Alignment Issues**
      * Description/Symptoms: Trunk lid misaligned, causing it to open or close unevenly
      * Solution: Adjust trunk lid hinges or replace latch assembly
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
      * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Malfunction**
      * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system freezes, displays incorrect information, or fails to load
      * Solution: Update software or replace navigation unit
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
      * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof Rattling**
      * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof makes rattling noises while driving
      * Solution: Tighten sunroof bolts or replace sunroof tracks
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
      * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2012 Mercedes-Benz Andare is a reliable vehicle with a few common issues. While some issues, such as battery drain and headlight malfunctions, are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others, such as transmission slipping and navigation system malfunction, can be more costly and severe. Overall, the Andare offers a comfortable and luxurious driving experience with a powerful engine and well-appointed interior, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues before purchasing.
