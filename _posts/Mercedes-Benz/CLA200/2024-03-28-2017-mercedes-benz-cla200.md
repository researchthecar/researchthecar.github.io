**Introduction:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz CLA200 is a compact luxury sedan that delivers a sporty driving experience. It is powered by a 2.0L inline-4 turbocharged engine that produces 183 horsepower and 206 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. The CLA200 offers an impressive array of features and technologies, including an infotainment system, navigation, and a host of driver-assist systems. While the CLA200 has received praise for its styling and performance, it has also been subject to a number of reported issues.

**Engine**

**2.0L Inline-4 Turbocharged Engine**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the engine, rough idle, decreased power output.
    * **Solution:** Replace faulty fuel injectors.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Malfunction:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, loud noises from the engine, illuminated check engine light.
    * **Solution:** Replace faulty wastegate actuator.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,200 - $1,800+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Loss of power during acceleration, shuddering or hesitation when shifting.
    * **Solution:** Reprogram transmission control module, replace clutch packs.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,800 - $3,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Clunking or rattling noises from the front of the vehicle when driving over bumps or uneven surfaces.
    * **Solution:** Replace worn or damaged sway bar links or control arm bushings.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $800+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Battery discharges quickly, difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical system malfunctions.
    * **Solution:** Diagnose and repair electrical system components causing the drain.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Varies depending on the cause
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Headlight Malfunction:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Headlights flicker or fail to turn on, error messages on the dash.
    * **Solution:** Replace faulty headlight bulbs, repair or replace electrical wiring.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $350 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Malfunction:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Doors fail to lock or unlock using the remote or interior buttons.
    * **Solution:** Repair or replace faulty door lock actuators.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Sunroof Rattling:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Noises from the sunroof when driving at high speeds or over bumps.
    * **Solution:** Adjust or tighten sunroof components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $400+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Noise:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Rattling or squeaking noises from the center console area.
    * **Solution:** Tighten loose bolts or components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $300+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Climate control system fails to regulate temperature, blower motor makes loud noises.
    * **Solution:** Repair or replace malfunctioning climate control components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz CLA200 has been praised for its styling and performance, but it has also been subject to a number of issues. While some of these issues are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of these reported issues before purchasing a CLA200.
