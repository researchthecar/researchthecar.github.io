**Introduction:**

The Mercedes-Benz CLA200 is a luxury compact sedan known for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and advanced technology features. It is available with a 1.3-liter M282 inline-4 turbocharged engine paired with a 7G-DCT 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. Standard features include a 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system, LED headlights, and blind-spot monitoring. Despite its premium nature, the CLA200 has received mixed reviews, with some owners reporting issues affecting various components.

**Engine**

**1.3L M282 Inline-4 Turbocharged**

* **Engine Misfiring:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, rough idling, check engine light illumination
    * Solution: Replacement of spark plugs and/or ignition coils
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, decreased fuel economy, engine stalling
    * Solution: Replacement of fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Leak from Turbocharger Seal:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil smell in the engine compartment, visible oil leak under the vehicle
    * Solution: Replacement of turbocharger seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7G-DCT 7-Speed Dual-Clutch**

* **Transmission Shuddering:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering felt during acceleration or gear changes
    * Solution: Software update, clutch pack replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Hesitation or Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delay in gear engagement, poor acceleration
    * Solution: Transmission fluid flush, clutch pack replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2022 Mercedes-Benz CLA200 has received mixed reviews regarding its reliability. While it offers a refined driving experience, several issues have been reported, including engine misfiring, fuel injector failure, transmission shuddering, infotainment system malfunctions, and battery discharge. The severity of these issues can vary, and some may require costly repairs. It is important for prospective buyers to consider these potential problems before making a purchase decision.
