# 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE500e Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE500e combines luxury plugin-hybrid efficiency with traditional upscale styling. Used-car shoppers should review maintenance records carefully, as owner reports point to occasional electrical glitches and powertrain anomalies that warrant a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 Plug-In Hybrid

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 paired with an electric motor produces around 436 total system horsepower, offering robust acceleration. Overall reliability is generally solid, but proper upkeep of both the combustion and hybrid electrical systems is essential.

**Fuel Pump and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle hesitation during acceleration, unexpected engine stalling while driving, or potential fuel system failures leaving the driver stranded.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel delivery system and replace failing fuel pumps or related electrical components as needed.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear shifts tailored for hybrid driving dynamics. Routine fluid changes and software updates help maintain long-term dependability.

**Shifter and Gear Engagement Defect**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to move the gear selector out of park, or unexpected slipping into neutral while driving or stopped.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the electronic shifter module and transmission control units for faults, and replace worn linkage or internal components if necessary.

## Electrical

### Back Over Prevention

Standard safety features offer modern protection, though owners should verify that all electronic driver aids and cameras function properly during a test drive.

**Backup Camera Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The rear-view camera screen fails to activate when reverse is engaged, displaying a black image or remaining unresponsive.
- **Solution:** Inspect the camera wiring harness, display unit, and related software modules, replacing faulty hardware as required.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE500e delivers an appealing mix of plug-in efficiency and luxury comfort. While most systems perform reliably, prospective buyers should verify the proper operation of the electronic shifter, fuel delivery components, and rearview camera system before finalizing a purchase.
