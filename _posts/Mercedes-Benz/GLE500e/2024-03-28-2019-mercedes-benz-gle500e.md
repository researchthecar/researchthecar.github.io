## 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLE500e

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLE500e is a plug-in hybrid luxury SUV that combines a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine with an electric motor for a total system output of 443 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. It comes with a 9G-TRONIC 9-speed automatic transmission and 4MATIC all-wheel drive. The GLE500e has a spacious and well-equipped interior, and it offers impressive fuel economy for its size. However, it can be expensive to maintain and repair.

## Engine
### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

**Leaking Valve Cover Gasket**
Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the top of the engine, visible as a wet or oily area around the valve cover.
Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
Recalls: None Found

**Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Rattling or ticking noise from the engine, reduced engine power.
Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

## Transmission
### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic

**Shifting Problems**

## Summary

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLE500e is a reliable and well-equipped SUV, but it can be expensive to maintain and repair.
