# 2021 Mercedes-Benz Maybach S580 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz Maybach S580 represents the pinnacle of luxury, combining ultra-plush comfort with advanced technology and effortless performance. As an ultra-luxury flagship, prospective used-car buyers should anticipate high maintenance and ownership costs commensurate with its tier. While powertrain options are robust and generally refined, shoppers should carefully inspect complex electrical and driver-assistance systems during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8 (M178/M176)

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 496 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally smooth and swift acceleration. This powerplant is equipped with a 48-volt mild-hybrid system (EQ Boost) and generally offers solid mechanical reliability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 9G-Tronic 9-Speed Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power seamlessly to the 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system, offering imperceptible gear shifts suited to chauffeured or executive driving. Regular fluid changes are recommended to maintain long-term smoothness and longevity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V493000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Maybach+S580&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera may not display the rearview image due to a software error in the MBUX multimedia system. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the MBUX multimedia system software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz Maybach S580 is an engineering masterpiece built for supreme comfort and effortless cruising, though its advanced technology requires thorough evaluation before purchase. Buyers should ensure all electronic features and powertrain systems operate correctly, as post-warranty repairs can be exceptionally costly. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a specialized technician is strongly recommended.
