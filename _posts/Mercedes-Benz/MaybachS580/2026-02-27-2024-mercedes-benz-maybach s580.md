## Electrical

### Infotainment System (MBUX)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or unexpected reboots of the central display and control unit.

**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent or persistent warnings for systems like adaptive cruise control, lane keeping assist, or parking sensors; system deactivation.
- **Solution:** Cleaning of sensors, recalibration, or replacement of faulty sensor modules.

### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Mercedes-Benz Maybach S580 is engineered for exceptional reliability, leveraging advanced technology and premium materials.
