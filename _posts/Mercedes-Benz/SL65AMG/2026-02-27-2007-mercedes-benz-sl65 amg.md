# 2007 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG

### Overview
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG, a high-performance roadster, is known for its potent twin-turbocharged V12 engine and luxurious features. While offering exhilarating performance, like many complex, high-performance vehicles of its era, it can present specific reliability challenges. Owners should be aware of potential issues related to its advanced powertrain, sophisticated electronics, and the specialized nature of its components. Regular and meticulous maintenance is crucial for preserving the performance and longevity of this exotic vehicle. Despite its performance pedigree, some areas require vigilant attention to ensure optimal operation and avoid costly repairs.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbocharged V12 (M275)
**Intercooler System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, overheating, coolant smell.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace leaking intercooler hoses, seals, or the intercooler unit itself.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of boost, unusual noises (whining, grinding), smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace failing turbochargers and associated plumbing.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000 per turbo

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replace faulty ignition coils and spark plugs.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300 per coil


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission slipping, 'limp mode'.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and replace the TCM.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,000

**Valve Body Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission fluid leaks.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace the transmission valve body.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, suspension warning lights, fluid leaks under the car.
- **Solution:** Inspect and repair or replace leaking ABC struts, hoses, or pump.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $4,000 per strut

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of hydraulic pressure, suspension not functioning, loud pump noise.
- **Solution:** Replace the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, audio issues, navigation problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000


### Sensors and Modules
**Various Sensor Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine lights, ABS/ESP warnings, cruise control malfunction.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and replace faulty sensors (e.g., ABS sensors, camshaft position sensors).
- **Cost:** $200 - $600 per sensor


## Exterior

### Retractable Hardtop Mechanism
**Hydraulic Pump/Cylinder Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Top not operating smoothly, leaks, failure to open/close.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace hydraulic components of the roof mechanism.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $3,000

**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Top not latching, warning lights related to roof operation.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and replace faulty roof sensors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Interior

### Climate Control System
**Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only working on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replace the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250


### Seat Actuators
**Power Seat Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Seats not moving, stuck in position.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace faulty seat motors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG is a pinnacle of automotive engineering, offering immense power and luxury. However, its complexity, particularly the V12 twin-turbo engine and Active Body Control suspension, means potential for significant repair costs. Owners should budget for specialized maintenance and be prepared for issues related to the intercooler, turbochargers, ABC system hydraulics, and the retractable hardtop mechanism. While the core powertrain is robust when maintained, the ancillary systems are where most common issues arise. Despite these potential drawbacks, for those who can afford the upkeep, the SL65 AMG remains a desirable and thrilling grand tourer.
