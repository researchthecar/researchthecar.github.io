**Introduction**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG is a high-performance luxury sports car that offers exhilarating performance with its twin-turbocharged V12 engine. It boasts an impressive 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque. Despite its impressive capabilities, there are some reported issues that owners may encounter.

**Engine**

* **Twin-Turbocharged 6.0L V12 M279 Biturbo**

    * **Leaking Head Gaskets**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from the exhaust
        * Solution: Replace head gaskets
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased exhaust smoke, whistling sounds
        * Solution: Replace turbocharger(s)
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $7,000+

**Transmission**

* **AMG Speedshift MCT 7-Speed**

    * **Transmission Control Module Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Gear shifting issues, transmission slipping, error messages
        * Solution: Replace transmission control module
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+

**Suspension**

* **AMG Ride Control Sport Suspension**

    * **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Inability to adjust ride height, suspension sagging
        * Solution: Replace air suspension compressor
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Diagnose and replace faulty electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Leakage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin when it rains
    * Solution: Replace or repair seals around the sunroof
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: Freezing, slow response, display issues
    * Solution: Update software or replace infotainment unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+

**Summary**

The 2015 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG is a powerful and luxurious sports car, but it is not without its potential issues. Some owners may experience problems with the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior components. The severity and cost of repairs can vary, but some issues, such as leaking head gaskets and turbocharger failure, can be significant. As with any high-performance vehicle, regular maintenance and inspections are crucial for maintaining the longevity and reliability of the SL65 AMG.
