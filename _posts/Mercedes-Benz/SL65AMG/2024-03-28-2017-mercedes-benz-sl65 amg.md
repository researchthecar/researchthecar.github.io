**Introduction:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG is a high-performance grand tourer powered by a monstrous 6.0L V12 engine producing 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque. It features a luxurious interior with advanced technology and a stunning exterior design. The SL65 AMG is renowned for its blistering acceleration, refined handling, and exclusivity.

**Engine**

* **6.0L V12 Biturbo (M279)**
    * **Turbocharger failure:** Symptoms include loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, and illuminated check engine light. Solution: Replacement of failed turbochargers. Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Automatic (722.6)**
    * **Torque converter shudder:** Symptoms include shuddering or vibration during gear changes. Solution: Replacement of torque converter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive Air Suspension:**
    * **Air spring leaks:** Symptoms include sagging suspension, uneven ride height, and warning lights on the dashboard. Solution: Replacement of ruptured air springs. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500 per corner
    * **Air compressor failure:** Symptoms include inoperative suspension system and suspension stuck in one position. Solution: Replacement of air compressor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Symptoms include dead battery after prolonged parking. Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system, such as faulty components or wiring issues. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Electrical gremlins:** Symptoms include intermittent electrical malfunctions affecting lights, windows, audio system, etc. Solution: Comprehensive electrical system diagnosis and repair. Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause

**Exterior**

* **Convertible Top Issues:**
    * **Roof leaks:** Symptoms include water leaking into the cabin during heavy rain. Solution: Replacement of worn seals or repair of damaged convertible top mechanism. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * **Mechanism failure:** Symptoms include difficulty opening or closing the convertible top, or complete failure of the mechanism. Solution: Repair or replacement of faulty components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+

**Interior**

* **Cracked Dashboard:** Symptoms include cracks appearing on the dashboard surface. Solution: Replacement of dashboard or repair using specialized adhesives. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Symptoms include ineffective heating or cooling, or intermittent fan operation. Solution: Diagnosis and repair of climate control components, such as sensors, actuators, or the AC unit. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Summary:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG is a powerful and luxurious grand tourer, but it is not without its issues. The reported problems range from minor inconveniences to potentially costly repairs, with the most prevalent being turbocharger failures, air suspension issues, and various electrical gremlins. The cost of repairs can be significant, especially for engine and suspension components. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
