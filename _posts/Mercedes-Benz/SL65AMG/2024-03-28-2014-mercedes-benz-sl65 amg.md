**Introduction:**

The 2014 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG is a high-performance luxury roadster powered by a hand-built 6.0L twin-turbocharged V12 engine that generates 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque. It features a 7-speed automatic transmission and is available with a variety of amenities and features. While the SL65 AMG offers exceptional performance and luxury, it has faced some reliability concerns.

**Engine:**

**6.0L Twin-Turbocharged V12**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, abnormal noises.
    * Solution: Replace turbochargers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5000 - $8000+
    * Recalls: #14V214000
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling or knocking, reduced power, check engine light.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioners.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3000 - $5000+
    * Recalls: #16V402000

**Transmission:**

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering, slipping, or harsh shifts.
    * Solution: Replace torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2500 - $4000+

**Suspension:**

* **Air Suspension Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Uneven ride height, sagging or bouncing, reduced handling.
    * Solution: Replace air suspension components (struts, compressor, etc.).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $6000+
* **Control Arm Bushings Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive play or knocking in the suspension.
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, reduced electrical functioning.
    * Solution: Diagnose and fix parasitic draw, replace battery if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
* **Wiring Harness Faults:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions (e.g., lights, gauges, etc.).
    * Solution: Repair or replace wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $3000+

**Exterior:**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Wipers malfunction or fail to work.
    * Solution: Replace wiper motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
* **LED Taillight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation in LED taillights.
    * Solution: Replace affected taillights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+

**Interior:**

* **Navigation System Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system errors, loss of functionality.
    * Solution: Software update, hardware replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
* **Climate Control System Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC or heater malfunctions, reduced functionality.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace affected components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $3000+

**Summary:**

The 2014 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG is a powerful and luxurious roadster with a number of known reliability issues. The most severe issue is the turbocharger failure, which can lead to significant engine damage. Other common problems include timing chain tensioner failure, torque converter clutch failure, and battery discharge. The estimated cost of repairs can be significant, especially for complex issues. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before considering purchasing this vehicle.
