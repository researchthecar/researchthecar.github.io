# 2018 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG represents the pinnacle of high-performance luxury grand touring, powered by a hand-built V12 engine. Because this ultra-low-volume exotic is primarily collector-driven, available owner feedback in public databases is extremely sparse. Buyers looking at this high-end roadster should focus on thorough pre-purchase inspections, verifying service history, and budgeting for the specialized maintenance required for a high-output AMG flagship.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L Biturbo V12

This hand-built 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 produces an immense 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless high-speed acceleration. It is an exceptionally complex powerplant requiring meticulous maintenance and specialized servicing.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT Automatic

The 7-speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT Multi-Clutch Technology automatic transmission routes massive torque to the rear wheels with rapid gear shifts. It offers smooth operation for grand touring alongside aggressive shifting in manual modes.
