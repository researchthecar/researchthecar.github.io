# 2016 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a high-performance luxury roadster like the 2016 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG, understanding its mechanical baseline is essential for a confident purchase. Known for its hand-built V12 power and sophisticated grand touring capabilities, this flagship convertible combines extreme performance with high-end luxury features. Prospective buyers should inspect the vehicle thoroughly, keeping in mind that maintenance and upkeep for high-end AMG models can be demanding.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L Biturbo V12

The 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque, delivering relentless acceleration. While exceptionally powerful, maintaining and servicing this hand-built AMG powerplant requires careful attention and can involve substantial specialized costs.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT PLUS 7G-TRONIC Automatic

This 7-speed automatic transmission is paired with the rear-wheel-drive V12 setup to manage massive torque output while offering smooth highway cruising and responsive manual paddle shifting. Routine fluid changes are vital to ensure long-term durability under high-performance use.

## Interior

### Tires

The suspension is tuned for a balance of sharp handling and comfortable grand touring, working alongside specialized high-performance tires.

**Run-Flat Tire Pressure and Durability**
- **Symptoms:** An alert notification displays on the dashboard indicating low pressure on a specific tire while driving at highway speeds. Because these are extended-mobility run-flat tires, the vehicle has no spare tire on board.
- **Solution:** Inspect tires regularly for wear and monitor tire pressure sensors closely; replace tires as needed when tread depth diminishes or damage occurs.
