# 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG, buyers are looking at a high-performance luxury roadster built for power and prestige. While owner reports to NHTSA are relatively sparse for this exclusive model, reviewing potential problem areas in electronics, steering, and powertrain can help prospective owners inspect vehicles thoroughly before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L Biturbo V12 (621 hp)

Equipped with a handcrafted 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine producing approximately 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque. This powerhouse offers immense straight-line acceleration, though buyers should anticipate high maintenance and specialized servicing costs typical of ultra-high-performance V12 powertrains.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift Plus 7G-Tronic 7-Speed Automatic

Features a 7-speed automatic transmission designed to handle the massive torque output of the V12 engine. While generally robust, owners should verify smooth engagement and responsive shifting during a test drive.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience the vehicle unexpectedly dropping into neutral gear while attempting to accelerate from a stop, resulting in a temporary loss of propulsion.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the transmission control module, shift linkages, and diagnostic trouble codes to pinpoint electronic or mechanical engagement failures.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

High-performance braking systems require attentive maintenance and regular checks of both friction materials and hydraulic assists.

**Brake System Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the brakes failed to engage properly when approaching a stop sign, leading to extended stopping distances or a complete failure to hold.
- **Solution:** Immediately have the hydraulic and electronic brake systems inspected by a specialist familiar with Mercedes-Benz high-performance braking components.

## Steering

### Power Steering Column Adjustment

Power adjustable steering columns add convenience but rely on motorized actuators that can occasionally bind or fail.

**Stuck Steering Wheel Adjuster**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel may become stuck in the fully upward position and fail to return to its preset driving position upon vehicle entry.
- **Solution:** Disabling the easy entry/exit feature in the vehicle settings can prevent recurrence, though mechanical column components may require professional inspection if jamming persists.

## Safety

### PRE-SAFE Safety Systems

Advanced driver assistance and safety electronics provide high levels of protection but can occasionally trigger false warnings or interventions.

**Unnecessary Seatbelt Tightening**
- **Symptoms:** The PRE-SAFE system may inappropriately tighten the seatbelts while driving in normal traffic conditions, causing driver distraction and discomfort.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership or specialized technician to check for software updates or sensor calibrations related to the restraint and safety systems.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG is an engineering marvel focused on luxury and staggering V12 performance. While owner-reported complaints are few due to its low production numbers, potential buyers should thoroughly inspect the complex electronics, braking system, and transmission operation prior to purchase to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
