# 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG, a high-performance luxury roadster, is generally known for its robust engineering and powerful V12 engine. As a halo model, it typically benefits from meticulous build quality and premium components. However, like any complex vehicle, especially one with a high-output engine and advanced systems, certain areas can experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and enthusiasts.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12 (M275)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, reduced engine power, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per coil

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential repair or replacement of turbochargers and associated plumbing.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $6,000 per turbocharger

**Coolant Leaks (Intercooler System)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant leaks, reduced intercooler efficiency.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of intercooler hoses, pumps, or the intercooler itself.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (7G-Tronic)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, inability to shift gears, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, shuddering during acceleration, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, suspension warning lights, stiff ride, fluid leaks under the car.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking ABC struts, hoses, or pump.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $3,000 per component

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of hydraulic pressure, vehicle sagging, suspension warning messages.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND)
**Screen Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Flickering screen, unresponsive touch controls, system freezes.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $3,000


### Sensor Failures
**Various Sensor Failures (e.g., ABS, Parking Assist)**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated warning lights on the dashboard, malfunctioning driver assistance features.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty sensors.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800 per sensor


## Exterior

### Retractable Hardtop Mechanism
**Actuator/Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Top not opening or closing properly, unusual noises during operation, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic actuators, motors, or control modules for the roof.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $3,000


## Interior

### Climate Control System
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from vents, inconsistent fan speed.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor or the blower motor itself.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG is a pinnacle of automotive engineering, offering exhilarating performance. While generally reliable, its complexity means potential issues can arise, particularly with the high-output V12 engine, advanced Active Body Control suspension, and the intricate retractable hardtop mechanism. Owners should be prepared for potentially high repair costs associated with these specialized systems. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are crucial for preserving the longevity and performance of this exotic roadster. Most reported issues are specific to the advanced technology rather than fundamental design flaws.
