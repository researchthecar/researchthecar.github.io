# 2012 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG

### Overview
The 2012 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG, a high-performance luxury roadster, is known for its potent V12 engine and sophisticated engineering. While generally well-regarded for its performance and comfort, like any complex vehicle, it can experience specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and enthusiasts. The SL65 AMG's advanced systems, particularly its powertrain and electronics, are areas where attention may be required over time, though many owners report a satisfying ownership experience when these are addressed.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L Twin-Turbo V12 (M275)
**Turbocharger Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, check engine light, inconsistent boost pressure.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty turbocharger actuator or associated control module.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Intercooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, rough idling, increased engine temperature, visible coolant leaks.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of intercooler hoses and/or the intercooler unit.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough running engine, check engine light, reduced performance.
- **Solution:** Replacement of individual faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping, shuddering, delayed engagement, unusual noises during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, failure to shift gears, limp mode activation, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, suspension warning lights, fluid leaks under the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking ABC lines, pumps, or struts.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $3000+

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of hydraulic pressure, vehicle dropping, suspension warning messages.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive buttons, audio issues, navigation problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the COMAND head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Convertible Top Hydraulics
**Hydraulic Cylinder Leaks/Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Slow or non-functional convertible top operation, fluid leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty hydraulic cylinders or seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1800

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG is a powerful and luxurious vehicle, but its complexity, particularly the V12 engine and Active Body Control suspension, can lead to significant repair costs if issues arise. Common concerns include turbocharger components, transmission control modules, and ABC system leaks. While the core engine and transmission are robust, specific ancillary systems and electronics can be prone to failure. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are crucial for maintaining this high-performance machine. Owners should budget for potential repairs, especially for the specialized systems that contribute to its unique driving experience.
