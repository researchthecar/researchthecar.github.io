**Introduction:**

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG is a high-performance grand tourer equipped with a twin-turbocharged 6.0L V12 engine producing 621 horsepower and 738 lb-ft of torque. It is only available with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The SL65 AMG is praised for its exhilarating performance, luxurious interior, and advanced technology. However, it also has some notable issues.

**Engine**

**6.0L V12 BiTurbo**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, whining or whistling noises from the engine
    * Solution: Replace the faulty turbocharger(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $8,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, poor fuel economy, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replace the faulty fuel injector(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Engine Oil Cooler Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks, low oil pressure warnings
    * Solution: Replace the faulty oil cooler
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping during gear shifts, delayed engagement
    * Solution: Replace the faulty torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaks from the transmission, low fluid levels
    * Solution: Replace the faulty gaskets or seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Suspension sagging, difficulty adjusting ride height, reduced stability
    * Solution: Replace the faulty compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive bouncing, poor ride quality, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace the faulty shock absorber(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per shock
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replace the faulty battery or identify and fix the electrical drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin
    * Solution: Replace the faulty seals or reseal the roof
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Lag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slow response time, freezing
    * Solution: Update the navigation system software or replace the unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Heated and Ventilated Seat Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seats do not heat or cool properly
    * Solution: Replace the faulty heating or cooling elements
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per seat
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz SL65 AMG is a powerful and luxurious vehicle, but it does have some notable issues. These issues can be costly to repair, and owners should be prepared for potential expenses. The severity of the issues varies, with some being minor inconveniences and others potentially affecting the safety and reliability of the vehicle. It is important to consider these potential problems when evaluating this vehicle.
