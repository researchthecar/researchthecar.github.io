# 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLE580 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLE580 represents the pinnacle of luxury performance in the midsize SUV segment, pairing high-end cabin tech with formidable powertrain options. Used-car buyers looking at this flagship luxury cruiser will find robust performance, though it is important to inspect complex electrical systems and advanced powertrain components thoroughly prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8 with EQ Boost (48V Mild Hybrid)

This twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V8 produces approximately 483 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, augmented by a 48-volt mild-hybrid system for seamless power delivery. It offers blistering acceleration and refined cruising manners, though prospective buyers should ensure all hybrid system software updates have been properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the standard 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system across varying driving conditions. It delivers rapid yet polished gear changes, contributing heavily to both highway efficiency and confident power delivery.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V832000: Visibility:Glass, Side/Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLE580&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rear window may be insufficiently bonded to the tailgate, which could allow the rear glass to detach. Detached rear glass can become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rear window, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLE580 offers an exceptionally smooth and powerful driving experience defined by its V8 mild-hybrid powertrain and luxurious interior appointments. While formal owner complaints are minimal, buyers should still conduct a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the advanced electrical and powertrain features to ensure long-term peace of mind.
