**Introduction:**

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG is a high-performance SUV with a luxurious interior. It is powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V8 engine that produces 577 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. The G63 AMG comes with a nine-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive.

People like the G63 AMG for its powerful engine, luxurious interior, and off-road capabilities. However, it has been criticized for its high price, poor fuel economy, and cramped rear seats.

**Engine:**
- **4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8**
 - **Turbocharger Failure:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noise
   - Solution: Replace turbochargers
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
   - Recalls: None Found
 - **Intake Manifold Cracking:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, poor performance, engine misfires
   - Solution: Replace intake manifold
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**
- **9-Speed Automatic**
 - **Transmission Slipping:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power
   - Solution: Replace transmission or transmission components
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**
 - **Rear Axle Failure:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Axle vibration, difficulty steering, loss of control
   - Solution: Replace rear axle
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**
 - **Battery Drain:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead unexpectedly, slow engine cranking
   - Solution: Replace battery, repair or replace electrical components
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
   - Recalls: None Found
 - **Infotainment System Malfunctions:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Screen freeze, system crashes, navigation errors
   - Solution: Software updates, hardware replacement
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**
 - **Rust on Undercarriage:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on metal components, paint bubbling
   - Solution: Rust removal and preventive measures
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**
 - **Sunroof Glass Shattering:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Panoramic sunroof glass spontaneously cracks or shatters
   - Solution: Replace sunroof glass
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
   - Recalls: Recall number 22V-486
 - **Door Lock Failure:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Doors cannot be locked or unlocked, key fob malfunction
   - Solution: Repair or replace door lock actuators
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG has some potential issues that can be costly to repair. The engine and transmission problems are particularly concerning. However, the severity and cost of the issues vary depending on the specific problem and part affected.
