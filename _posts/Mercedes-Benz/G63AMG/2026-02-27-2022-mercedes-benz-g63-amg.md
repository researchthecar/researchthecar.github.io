# 2022 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a 2022 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG, buyers are looking at an iconic luxury SUV powered by a hand-built biturbo V8 engine. While these vehicles offer immense performance and road presence, prospective owners should review owner feedback regarding fuel and propulsion systems before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8 Engine

This 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers an impressive 577 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, providing rapid acceleration for the heavy SUV. Overall, this hand-built powerplant is robust, though buyers should monitor owner-reported fuel system concerns.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a responsive 9-speed automatic transmission featuring steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters, this drivetrain routes power smoothly to the permanent all-wheel-drive system. It generally operates reliably under high torque loads.
