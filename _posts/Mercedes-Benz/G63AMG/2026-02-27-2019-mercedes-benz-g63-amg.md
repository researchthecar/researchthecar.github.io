# 2019 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2019 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG offers a blend of iconic styling, immense power, and rugged capability. While these luxury SUVs are built to a high standard, used car buyers should remain aware that owner reports indicate potential quirks in electrical and body systems. A thorough pre-purchase inspection is recommended to ensure all systems operate smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L V8 Biturbo (AMG M178/M177)

This hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces an impressive 577 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating performance for the heavy luxury SUV while generally maintaining solid mechanical standing.

## Transmission

### 9G-Tronic 9-Speed Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and rapid shifts tailored to the high-output V8 powerplant. It pairs standard four-wheel drive with robust gearing well-suited for both highway cruising and off-road excursions.

### Differential Lock and Stability Control System

Electrical and stability systems manage complex off-road features, though occasional sensor or module faults have been noted by owners.

**Differential Lock Malfunction Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience error messages on the instrument cluster regarding the lock differential system, frequently accompanied by inactive ABS and ESP warnings.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician or dealership diagnose the sensor inputs and replace any failing control modules or switches.

## Exterior

### Windshield Glass

The exterior features iconic styling and upright glass, though the vertical windshield geometry leaves it vulnerable to stone strikes.

**Spontaneous or Severe Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** The upright, flat windshield profile makes it susceptible to severe cracking from minor road debris, sometimes spreading rapidly from top to bottom.
- **Solution:** Inspect the windshield thoroughly for existing chips and consider professional glass coverage or protective film options.

### Door Latches

Interior cabin amenities and heavy-duty hardware are generally robust, but individual latch components can experience mechanical wear.

**Door Latch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The driver-side door may fail to securely close or stay latched when shut firmly, creating a safety hazard while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect door latch mechanisms and replace worn or malfunctioning lock assemblies as needed.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG remains a highly desirable high-performance luxury SUV with a commanding presence and robust powertrain. While owner-reported issues tend to center around isolated electrical alerts, glass vulnerability, and latch hardware, overall mechanical durability is strong. Prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive inspection focusing on electronics, body seals, and service history.
