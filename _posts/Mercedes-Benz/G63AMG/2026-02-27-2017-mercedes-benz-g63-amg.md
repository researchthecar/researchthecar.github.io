# 2017 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a luxury high-performance SUV like the 2017 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG, buyers are drawn to its iconic styling and hand-built twin-turbo V8 engine. While these rugged vehicles command a strong presence and impressive capability, prospective owners should carefully inspect individual vehicle history and electronic systems before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (M157)

The 5.5-liter bi-turbo V8 engine produces 563 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque, delivering immense acceleration for this heavy luxury SUV. It requires meticulous maintenance history and regular fluid checks to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift Plus Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission features steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters and transfers power smoothly through a permanent all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid services are essential to maintain crisp shifts and overall durability.

## Brakes

### ABS and Electronic Stability Control

Braking components provide strong stopping power for the heavy G63 platform, though electronic modules require proper calibration.

**ABS and Stability Control System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported warning lights or erratic behavior related to the anti-lock braking and electronic stability control systems.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan the vehicle computer for diagnostic trouble codes and inspect wheel speed sensors or module connections.

## Electrical

### Traction Control and Stability Modules

The vehicle relies heavily on complex electronic architectures to manage traction, stability, and convenience features.

**Traction Control Software Errors**
- **Symptoms:** Software glitches can trigger error codes and render the vehicle inoperable, requiring specialized module replacement.
- **Solution:** Software updates or module replacement by an authorized service center may be necessary to clear persistent fault codes.

### Backup Camera and Parking Sensors

Advanced safety systems assist with maneuvering this large SUV, though sensor reliability should be verified during pre-purchase inspections.

**Intermittent Rear Camera and Sensor Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Rearview cameras and proximity sensors may intermittently fail to display images or alert the driver during reversing maneuvers.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera wiring harnesses, power supplies, and sensor lenses for damage, or replace faulty display components.

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG offers unmatched road presence and formidable V8 performance, but its complex electronics and heavy mechanical systems demand thorough pre-purchase evaluations.
