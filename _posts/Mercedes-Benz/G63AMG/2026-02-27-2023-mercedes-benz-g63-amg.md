# 2023 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a 2023 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG, buyers are looking at an iconic, ultra-luxury high-performance SUV powered by a handcrafted biturbo V8. While owner reports to safety regulators are relatively few for this model year, prospective buyers should still carefully review vehicle history records and ensure all open manufacturer campaigns have been addressed by an authorized dealer.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8 (577 hp)

The 4.0-liter handcrafted biturbo V8 produces approximately 577 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering performance for this heavy luxury SUV. It generally provides robust power, though buyers should watch for electronic or sensor-related anomalies reported in isolated owner feedback.

**Unexpected Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience the vehicle shutting down unexpectedly while driving, accompanied by warning lights and a 'see manual' message on the instrument cluster.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the electrical or fuel management system for fault codes and software updates.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift TCT 9-Speed Automatic

The AMG Speedshift TCT 9-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, responsive shifts tailored to the high-performance nature of the G63 AMG. It pairs standard all-wheel drive with robust gearing, and no widespread mechanical failure trends have been highlighted by current owner reports.
