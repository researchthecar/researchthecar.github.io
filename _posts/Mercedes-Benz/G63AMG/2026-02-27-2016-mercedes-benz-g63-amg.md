# 2016 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG, buyers are looking at a hand-built, high-performance icon blending rugged utility with luxury. While these vehicles are built to an exceptionally high standard, prospective owners should still review potential owner-reported concerns regarding interior hardware and safety systems to ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 5.5L Biturbo V8

The 2016 G63 AMG features a hand-built 5.5-liter twin-turbocharged V8 producing approximately 563 horsepower and 560 lb-ft of torque. This robust powerplant delivers immense acceleration and capability, though routine maintenance and premium fluid changes are critical for long-term health.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift Plus 7G-Tronic 7-Speed Automatic

Paired with the V8 engine is a 7-speed AMG Speedshift Plus automatic transmission featuring steering-wheel paddle shifters. It is designed to handle heavy torque loads smoothly, though buyers should verify proper shifting performance during a test drive.

## Exterior

### Door Latches and Locks

Cabin hardware generally holds up well, though isolated mechanical latch issues have been reported.

**Door Latch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The front driver's side door fails to latch or close securely upon entry.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the door latch mechanism or associated locking linkages.

## Safety

### Air Bag

Owner reports indicate occasional concerns with supplemental restraint system warning indicators illuminating.

**Air Bag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The air bag warning light and front passenger malfunction service required indicator illuminate constantly or intermittently.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) sensors, wiring harnesses, or passenger seat occupancy sensor modules.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG remains a commanding and desirable luxury SUV. While owner-reported complaints are relatively sparse, buyers should carefully test all interior latches and verify that all electronic safety warnings function properly before completing a purchase.
