# 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE450 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE450 AMG offers a compelling blend of luxury, advanced technology, and high-performance engineering. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported feedback regarding specific mechanical and electrical components to make an informed purchasing decision. While luxury European SUVs provide a refined driving experience, keeping a healthy maintenance budget can help mitigate unexpected repair surprises.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (Bi-Turbo)

Equipped with a 3.0-liter biturbo V6 engine producing around 362 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration and sporty performance. Overall, this powertrain option delivers strong daily drivability, though routine maintenance is essential for longevity.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission (9G-TRONIC)

Paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission featuring all-wheel drive, offering smooth gear changes designed to optimize fuel efficiency and power delivery.

**Unexpected Shifting or Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported occasional unexpected gear shifts, such as sudden downshifting or hesitation when depressing the accelerator pedal.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect transmission control modules and software calibration.

## Steering

### Electronic Power Steering System

The electronic power steering system provides speed-sensitive steering assistance suited for a luxury performance SUV.

**Power Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A 'Power Steering Malfunction' warning message may appear on the instrument cluster, causing the steering wheel to become stiff and extremely difficult to turn.
- **Solution:** Inspect electronic power steering components, wiring harnesses, and steering motor modules for faults.

## Electrical

### Exterior Lighting and Infotainment

Electrical architecture supports advanced cabin displays, safety features, and exterior lighting systems.

**Headlight and Display Performance Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have noted inadequate illumination from factory headlights during nighttime or rainy driving conditions, alongside intermittent center digital screen failures.
- **Solution:** Check bulb conditions, headlight alignment mechanisms, and infotainment software or connections.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Glass Panels

Exterior body elements include a panoramic sunroof option designed to enhance cabin openness and natural light.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A loud boom or explosion sound followed by the sudden shattering of the panoramic sunroof glass while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect sunroof assembly and replace damaged glass panels under comprehensive insurance or warranty if applicable.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE450 AMG is an engaging, high-performance luxury SUV that impresses with its powerful V6 engine and upscale interior. However, buyers should thoroughly inspect prospective vehicles for historical electronic power steering warnings, sunroof integrity, and transmission shift behavior. Conducting a pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable technician can help ensure a rewarding and trouble-free ownership experience.
