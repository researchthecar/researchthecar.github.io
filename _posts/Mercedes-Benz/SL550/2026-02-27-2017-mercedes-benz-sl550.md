# 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL550 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL550 combines luxury and high-performance engineering into a sophisticated roadster. Used car buyers looking at this model should review known owner reports regarding electronic safety features, steering adjustments, and powertrain behavior to ensure a well-maintained driving experience.

---

## Engine

### 4.7L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This potent 4.7-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers robust horsepower and strong torque for effortless acceleration. It is a sophisticated powerplant that requires diligent maintenance to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and efficient gear shifts tailored to various driving modes. It generally operates reliably when properly maintained, supporting the vehicle's dynamic performance.

## Steering

### Steering Column Adjustment

Steering components generally offer precise handling, though occasional power adjustment mechanism concerns have been reported by owners.

**Power Steering Wheel Adjustment Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel may remain stuck in the raised position and fail to return to its preset position upon entry.
- **Solution:** Owners have temporarily bypassed the issue by disabling the easy entry/exit feature or manually adjusting the column.

## Safety

### Pre-Safe Safety System

Advanced electrical and safety systems provide high-tech convenience, but complex electronic modules can occasionally exhibit erratic behavior.

**Unnecessary Seatbelt Tightening**
- **Symptoms:** The pre-safe safety belts may unexpectedly tighten and restrict movement, potentially distracting the driver.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's restraint control module and sensors inspected by a qualified technician.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=SL550&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL550 is a luxurious and capable cruiser, but prospective buyers should carefully inspect all electrical features, steering adjustments, and powertrain functions before purchasing.
