**Introduction**

The 2012 Mercedes-Benz SL550 is a grand tourer coupe convertible that offers both luxury and performance. It is powered by a 4.7L twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 429 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. The SL550 comes standard with a 7-speed automatic transmission with manual shifting mode.
