# 2007 Mercedes-Benz SL550

### Overview
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz SL550, part of the R230 generation, is a luxury grand tourer known for its performance and comfort. Powered by a robust 5.5-liter V8 engine, it offers a compelling driving experience. While generally well-regarded for its engineering, like any vehicle of this age and complexity, it can experience specific issues. This data focuses on common, documented problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers. Understanding these potential reliability points can help in maintaining the vehicle's optimal performance and value.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L V8 (M273)
**Intake Manifold Runner Flap Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, rough idle, loss of power, rattling noise from intake area.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold runner flap actuator or the entire intake manifold assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Oil Cooler Seals Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks detected around the oil cooler, potential for coolant contamination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil cooler seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### 7G-TRONIC (7-Speed Automatic)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears, limp mode.
- **Solution:** Replacement or repair of the Transmission Control Module.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Valve Body Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the transmission valve body.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, suspension warning lights, fluid leaks under the car.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking ABC hoses, struts, or pump.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $3000+

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of hydraulic pressure, vehicle sagging, suspension warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**SAM (Signal Acquisition Module) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical gremlins, malfunctioning lights, power windows, or other accessories.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential repair or replacement of the SAM unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200

**Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Xenon headlights not turning on or flickering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight ballast.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Exterior

### Retractable Hardtop System
**Hydraulic Cylinder Leaks/Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Slow or failed operation of the retractable hardtop, fluid leaks.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hydraulic cylinders or lines.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Hydraulic Pump Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to operate the hardtop, pump noise.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the hardtop hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


## Interior

### Seat Control Modules
**Malfunctioning Seat Adjustments**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not moving, memory functions failing.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of seat control modules or wiring.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### COMAND Navigation System
**Screen Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Flickering screen, unresponsive touch, or display issues.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the COMAND unit or screen.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz SL550 is a luxurious and powerful convertible, but its advanced systems, particularly the Active Body Control (ABC) suspension and the retractable hardtop, are known to be potential sources of costly repairs. Engine and transmission components, while generally robust, can also develop issues like intake manifold problems or TCM failures. Electrical gremlins, often related to the SAM module, are not uncommon. Owners should be prepared for higher maintenance costs compared to less complex vehicles, with a strong emphasis on regular inspections of the hydraulic systems and electrical components to mitigate potential failures.
