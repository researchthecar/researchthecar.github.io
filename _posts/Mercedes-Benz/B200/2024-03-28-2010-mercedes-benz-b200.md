**Introduction:**

The 2010 Mercedes-Benz B200 is a compact luxury hatchback powered by a 2.0L inline-four engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). It produces 134 horsepower and 136 lb-ft of torque. The B200 is praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and premium features. However, it has also received criticism for its sluggish acceleration and lack of handling excitement.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Inline-Four**

**No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

**Delayed Acceleration and Jerking**
Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delayed acceleration when starting from a standstill, followed by sudden jerking or jolting.
Solution: Software update or replacement of CVT unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Premature Strut Wear**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the front suspension, uneven tire wear.
Solution: Replacement of worn struts.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Draining**
Description/Symptoms: Battery drains overnight or prematurely, requiring jumpstarts.
Solution: Replacement of faulty battery or alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Hatch Seal Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the rear cargo area or trunk.
Solution: Replacement of faulty hatch seal.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: MB21V189003

**Interior**

* **Faulty HVAC Blower Motor**
Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent airflow or fan speed, including no airflow.
Solution: Replacement of faulty blower motor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Other**

**No Issues Found**

**Summary:**

The 2010 Mercedes-Benz B200 has a relatively low number of reported issues. The most common problems involve transmission issues, specifically delayed acceleration with the CVT. The cost of repairs can range from several hundred dollars to over $2,500. Overall, the B200 is a reliable vehicle with minor issues that do not significantly impact its overall ownership experience.
