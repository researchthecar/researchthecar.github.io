**Introduction:**

The 2009 Mercedes-Benz B200 is a premium compact hatchback that offers a comfortable and luxurious driving experience. It is powered by a 2.0L inline-4 engine producing 134 horsepower and 155 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed manual or a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The B200 is praised for its spacious interior, high-quality materials, and responsive handling, but it has also been met with some complaints regarding reliability.

**Engine**

**2.0L Inline-4**

**Intake Manifold Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Cracking or warping of the intake manifold, leading to vacuum leaks, rough idling, and decreased engine performance.
* **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $600 - $1,200+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

**No Issues Found**

**CVT**

**Clutch Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Slipping or shuddering of the transmission, difficulty shifting gears, and loss of power.
* **Solution:** Replacement of the clutch.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

**Strut Bearing Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Worn or damaged strut bearings, causing rattling or clunking noises from the suspension, especially over bumps or uneven surfaces.
* **Solution:** Replacement of the strut bearings.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $250 - $600+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Discharge**
* **Description/Symptoms:** The battery drains prematurely, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle or a complete loss of electrical power.
* **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of the faulty component (e.g., alternator, battery, or electrical wiring).
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

**Headlight Condensation**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Moisture buildup inside the headlight assemblies, causing fogging or reduced visibility during night driving.
* **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight assemblies or repair of the seals.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $800+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

**Seat Belt Buckle Malfunction**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty inserting or releasing the seat belt buckle, potentially leading to safety concerns.
* **Solution:** Replacement of the seat belt buckle assembly.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $150 - $400+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2009 Mercedes-Benz B200 has several reported issues that can affect its reliability. The most common problems include intake manifold failure, transmission clutch failure, suspension strut bearing failure, battery discharge, headlight condensation, and seat belt buckle malfunctions. While some of these issues can be relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more costly and impact the vehicle's performance and safety. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and to have their vehicle regularly inspected and serviced to prevent or minimize problems.
