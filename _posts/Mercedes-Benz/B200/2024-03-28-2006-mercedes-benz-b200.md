**Introduction:**

The 2006 Mercedes-Benz B200 is a compact luxury hatchback that offers a comfortable ride, ample interior space, and impressive fuel economy. It is powered by a 2.0-liter naturally aspirated inline-four engine paired with a Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT).

**Engine**

* **2.0L Inline-Four Engine**
    * **Intake Manifold Leak**
      * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, check engine light, overheating
      * Solution: Replace intake manifold
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
      * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
      * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, loss of power, check engine light
      * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
      * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**
    * **Shuddering or Slipping**
      * Description/Symptoms: Vibration, loss of power, hesitation
      * Solution: Replace CVT fluid, replace CVT belt, or replace entire CVT
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $5,000+
      * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings Worn**
  * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise, uneven tire wear
  * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
  * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely
  * Solution: Replace battery, diagnose and repair electrical draw
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Paint Peeling**
  * Description/Symptoms: Peeling paint on the rear bumper
  * Solution: Repaint rear bumper
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Electric Seat Adjustment Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Seat does not adjust or moves erratically
  * Solution: Replace seat motor or control module
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,000+

**Summary:**

The 2006 Mercedes-Benz B200 is a reliable vehicle overall, but it may experience some common issues. The most severe and costly issues are related to the engine and transmission, while other issues are relatively minor and less expensive to fix. Potential owners should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their purchasing decision.
