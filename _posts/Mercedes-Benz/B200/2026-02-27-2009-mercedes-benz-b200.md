# 2009 Mercedes-Benz B200

### Overview
The 2009 Mercedes-Benz B200, part of the W245 generation, is a compact multi-purpose vehicle (MPV) known for its practicality and premium feel. While generally considered a reliable vehicle, like any car, it has specific areas that have seen more attention from owners and technicians. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability issues beyond routine maintenance. The B200 was offered with various engine and transmission configurations, and understanding their specific reliability profiles is crucial for potential buyers and current owners.

---


## Engine

### 1.8L M266 Petrol
**Camshaft Adjuster Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, check engine light, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the camshaft adjuster unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gasket)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil around the top of the engine, burning oil smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the valve cover gasket.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.7)
**Harsh Shifting / Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, delayed engagement, slipping between gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter change, solenoid replacement, or full rebuild.
- **Cost:** $400 - $2000+

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, 'limp mode', transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


### 6-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $700 - $1300


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Popping or creaking noise when turning the steering wheel, especially at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the strut mount bearings.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, window stuck in position.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

**Instrument Cluster Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent gauge failures, dead pixels on display, warning lights staying on.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000

## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250

**Sunroof Drain Clog**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, especially after rain or washing.
- **Solution:** Clearing of the sunroof drain tubes.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

### Summary Verdict
The 2009 Mercedes-Benz B200 offers a blend of practicality and Mercedes-Benz refinement. While it avoids major widespread catastrophic failures, owners should be aware of potential issues with the camshaft adjusters and valve cover gaskets on petrol engines, and transmission control module or harsh shifting on the automatic gearbox. Suspension components like control arm bushings can wear over time, and electrical gremlins such as window regulators and instrument cluster problems are not uncommon. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to early symptoms can help mitigate repair costs and ensure continued reliability for this versatile compact MPV.
