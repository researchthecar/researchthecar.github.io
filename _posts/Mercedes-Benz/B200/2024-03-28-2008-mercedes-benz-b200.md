**2008 Mercedes-Benz B200**

The 2008 Mercedes-Benz B200 is a compact luxury hatchback offered with a single engine and transmission option. It features a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 134 horsepower and 155 lb-ft of torque, paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). Critics praised the B200 for its comfortable interior, spacious cargo area, and fuel efficiency, but lamented its lack of driving excitement and uninspiring exterior design.

**Engine**
- **2.0L Four-Cylinder**

  - **Premature Cam Adjuster Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Grinding noise from the engine, reduced engine power, and potential engine damage
    - Solution: Replace the cam adjuster(s)
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
- **CVT**

  - **Hesitation and Jerking**
    - Description/Symptoms: Intermittent hesitation, jerking, or slipping while shifting
    - Solution: Software update, transmission fluid change, or transmission replacement
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $5,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
- **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**
- **Electrical System Malfunctions**
    - Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical issues, such as flickering lights, power window failures, or radio malfunctions
    - Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty electrical components or wiring
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
- **No Issues Found**

**Interior**
- **Climate Control Malfunctions**
    - Description/Symptoms: Failure of the climate control system to operate properly, resulting in inadequate heating or cooling
    - Solution: Replace or repair faulty climate control module or actuators
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 2008 Mercedes-Benz B200 is a comfortable and fuel-efficient hatchback. However, it is not without its issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. These problems can be costly to repair, with some estimates reaching into the thousands of dollars. It is important to weigh the potential costs against the benefits before purchasing this vehicle.
