### AirMatic Air Suspension
**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Gradual loss of ride height, particularly when parked; bouncy ride.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking air struts.

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Safety Bus demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, particularly in its core powertrain and structural components.
