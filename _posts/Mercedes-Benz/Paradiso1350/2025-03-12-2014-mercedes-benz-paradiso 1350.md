The 2014 Mercedes-Benz Paradiso 1350 is a popular touring bus known for its comfort and reliability, typically powered by Mercedes-Benz diesel engines paired with automatic transmissions. While specific engine and transmission options may vary depending on the region and configuration, common engines include the Mercedes-Benz OM 457 LA series. This engine delivers approximately 360 horsepower and a torque of 1600 Nm. Owners generally appreciate the Paradiso 1350 for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and robust build quality. Some common dislikes include the high initial cost and potential for expensive repairs due to the complex systems.

**Engine**

**Mercedes-Benz OM 457 LA (Diesel)**

*   **Coolant Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks may occur from hoses, radiator, or water pump. Overheating can result.
    *   Solution: Replace faulty hoses, repair or replace the radiator, or replace the water pump.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Turbocharger Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, and unusual noises from the turbocharger.
    *   Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $3000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Mercedes-Benz GO 240-8 (Automatic)**

*   **Gear Slippage**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips between gears, causing a loss of power and jerky acceleration.
    *   Solution: Replace or rebuild the transmission.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $6000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Delayed Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The transmission hesitates or delays when shifting gears, especially when cold.
    *   Solution: Replace the transmission valve body or rebuild the transmission.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $4000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system loses pressure, causing the bus to sag on one side or overall. This can result in a bumpy ride and reduced stability.
    *   Solution: Replace faulty air springs, air lines, or the air compressor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Multiplexing System Failures**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Issues with the multiplexing system can lead to malfunctions in various electrical components, such as lights, climate control, and entertainment systems.
    *   Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace the affected modules or wiring harnesses.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $2000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Delamination of Windows**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The laminated windows can experience delamination, causing clouding or separation of the layers.
    *   Solution: Replace the affected windows.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3000+ per window
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **HVAC Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent cooling or heating, unusual noises from the system, or complete failure of the HVAC system.
    *   Solution: Repair or replace the compressor, condenser, evaporator, or control module.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $2000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 Mercedes-Benz Paradiso 1350, while generally reliable, can experience a range of issues, from engine and transmission problems to suspension and electrical malfunctions. The most severe and costly issues tend to be related to the transmission, turbocharger, and air suspension systems. Electrical issues related to the multiplexing system can also be complex and expensive to resolve.
