# 2017 Mercedes-Benz Paradiso 1350

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz Paradiso 1350, a premium motorhome, generally exhibits strong build quality and reliability, reflecting Mercedes-Benz's engineering standards. Owners often praise its comfortable ride and robust chassis. However, like any complex vehicle, specific components can experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for the 1.9L and 2.4L JTDm diesel engines, as well as common transmission and electrical systems. The focus is on providing actionable data for owners and potential buyers.

---


## Engine

### 1.9L JTDm Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated DPF warning light, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration cycle, DPF cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200

**EGR Valve Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, black smoke from exhaust, engine warning light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $250 - $600


### 2.4L JTDm Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated DPF warning light, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration cycle, DPF cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200

**Turbocharger Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine, increased smoke.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger inspection, repair, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2500


## Transmission

### Manual Transmission
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $700 - $1500


### Automatic Transmission
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, delayed engagement, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Torque converter inspection and potential replacement.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Air Suspension Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle leaning to one side, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Leak detection and repair of air lines, seals, or air springs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning lights, power windows, central locking, or dashboard indicators.
- **Solution:** BCM diagnostics and reprogramming or replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive, system reboots unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software update or head unit replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz Paradiso 1350 demonstrates good overall reliability, with the primary technical concerns centering around the diesel particulate filter (DPF) and EGR valve for both engine variants, and potential turbocharger wear on the 2.4L. Transmission issues, while not endemic, can involve clutch wear on manuals and torque converter problems on automatics. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the Body Control Module and infotainment system, are also noted. Suspension components like air springs can develop leaks. Exterior and interior elements are generally robust. Proactive maintenance, especially concerning the DPF system, is recommended to mitigate potential costs and ensure optimal performance.
