## Engine

### 3.0L V6 BlueTEC Diesel
**DPF (Diesel Particulate Filter) Regeneration Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration, DPF cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**AdBlue System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** AdBlue warning lights, reduced engine performance, potential inability to start.
- **Solution:** Check AdBlue fluid level and quality, inspect sensors and pump, refill or replace components.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000

## Suspension

### Air Suspension System
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, uneven ride height, compressor running frequently.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace leaking air springs or lines.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant compressor running.
- **Solution:** Replace the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1500


## Electrical

### House Electrical System
**Inverter/Converter Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of AC power, battery charging issues, flickering lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and repair or replace the inverter/converter unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000

**Solar Panel Controller Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Inadequate battery charging from solar, error codes on the controller.
- **Solution:** Inspect wiring, reset controller, or replace faulty unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600
