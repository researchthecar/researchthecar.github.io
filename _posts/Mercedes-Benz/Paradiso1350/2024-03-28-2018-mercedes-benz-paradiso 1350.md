**Introduction**

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz Paradiso 1350 is a luxurious coach bus powered by a Mercedes-Benz OM 471 engine and Mercedes-Benz GO 250-8 transmission. The OM 471 engine produces 456 horsepower and 1,550 lb-ft of torque. The GO 250-8 transmission is an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Paradiso 1350 offers a smooth and comfortable ride, along with a variety of amenities for passengers. However, the vehicle has experienced some issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

**OM 471**

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger fails, causing a loss of power and black smoke from the exhaust.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The fuel injectors fail, causing a rough idle, misfires, and decreased fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**GO 250-8**

* **Transmission Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips gears, causing a loss of power and difficulty accelerating.
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension fails, causing the vehicle to ride low and lose stability.
    * Solution: Replace the air suspension components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator fails, preventing the battery from charging.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The windshield wiper motor fails, preventing the wipers from working properly.
    * Solution: Replace the windshield wiper motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Belt Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The seat belts do not retract properly or lock into place.
    * Solution: Replace the seat belt components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2018 Mercedes-Benz Paradiso 1350 is a reliable and comfortable coach bus. However, it has experienced some issues with its engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they should be considered before purchasing or leasing a Paradiso 1350.
