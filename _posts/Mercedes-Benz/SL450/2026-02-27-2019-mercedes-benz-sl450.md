# 2019 Mercedes-Benz SL450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz SL450 offers a blend of luxury, open-air cruising, and twin-turbocharged performance. For used-car buyers considering this high-end roadster, understanding its powertrain options and monitoring potential mechanical or electronic quirks will help ensure a satisfying ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The 3.0-liter biturbo V6 engine generates approximately 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration. This powerplant is generally robust, though regular maintenance is vital for long-term reliability in high-end luxury applications.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and quick gear shifts tailored for both relaxed cruising and spirited driving. It pairs well with the V6 engine, maintaining refined performance across various driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V768000: Power Train:Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=SL450&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Unexpected changes in the vehicle's handling can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the front-left mounting point of the rear axle carrier. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V571000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=SL450&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger air bag may have been improperly sewn, allowing the seam to tear during deployment. A tear in the air bag may decrease performance and increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger air bag, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz SL450 is a sophisticated and capable luxury roadster with strong powertrain foundations. Prospective buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify the health of its mechanical and electronic systems.
