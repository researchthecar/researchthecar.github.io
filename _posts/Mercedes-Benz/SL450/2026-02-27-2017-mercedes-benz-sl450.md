# 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL450 offers a blend of luxury and open-air performance, powered by a sophisticated turbocharged V6 engine. While these grand tourers are generally built to high standards, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback regarding occasional electronic, steering, and powertrain behavior. A thorough pre-purchase inspection is recommended to ensure all complex systems and electronics operate as intended.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration. It is generally regarded as a robust powerhouse, though routine maintenance and cooling system checks are essential for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission, this drivetrain delivers smooth gear changes and optimized highway efficiency. While generally reliable, owners should verify proper shift behavior during a test drive.

**Unexpected Shift to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly drop into neutral gear while attempting to pull away from a stop, resulting in a temporary loss of propulsion.
- **Solution:** Have the transmission control module and mechanical shift linkages inspected by a qualified technician.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake System

The braking system provides strong stopping power, but any reports of pedal travel issues or delayed response require immediate professional diagnosis.

**Brake Pedal Failure to Engage**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a sudden failure of the brakes to engage properly when approaching a stop sign, significantly increasing stopping distance.
- **Solution:** Immediately inspect the master cylinder, brake booster, and hydraulic fluid pressure components.

## Steering

### Power Tilt Steering Column

Power adjustment features add convenience but can occasionally encounter mechanical or software calibration snags.

**Steering Wheel Stuck in Up Position**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel fails to return to its preset driving position upon vehicle entry, remaining stuck in the fully upward position.
- **Solution:** Disable the easy entry/exit feature in the vehicle settings or inspect the steering column adjustment motor and mechanism.

## Safety

### Pre-Safe Safety Systems

Advanced safety electronics govern many aspects of the cabin and driving experience, making diagnostic software scans a vital part of evaluating a used model.

**Unnecessary Seatbelt Tensioning**
- **Symptoms:** The Pre-Safe system may unnecessarily tighten the seatbelts in certain locations, causing driver distraction and discomfort.
- **Solution:** Have the dealership check for software updates or sensor calibration adjustments for the Pre-Safe collision avoidance system.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V058000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=SL450&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz SL450 is a sophisticated luxury cruiser that rewards careful shopping. While major mechanical failures are infrequent, potential buyers should thoroughly check the electronics, steering column adjustments, and transmission operation during a test drive. Prioritizing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist will help uncover any hidden issues before purchase.
