# 2018 Mercedes-Benz SL450 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2018 Mercedes-Benz SL450 offers a blend of luxury and open-top performance, powered by a refined twin-turbo V6 engine. While formal complaint volume for this specific model year is very light, buyers should carefully inspect all mechanical and electrical systems during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure everything operates smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine delivers robust horsepower and smooth torque delivery typical of Mercedes-Benz luxury roadsters. This powertrain option generally offers solid performance, though routine maintenance and timely servicing are critical for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic

The 9G-TRONIC 9-speed automatic transmission provides quick and seamless gear changes tailored to maximize both efficiency and performance. It is well-regarded for highway cruising, but buyers should test-drive the vehicle thoroughly to verify smooth shifting across all gears.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V571000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=SL450&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger air bag may have been improperly sewn, allowing the seam to tear during deployment. A tear in the air bag may decrease performance and increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger air bag, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz SL450 is a sophisticated luxury cruiser backed by minimal owner complaints in public datasets. Prospective buyers should still prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection, verify that all open recalls have been addressed by a certified dealer, and review service history.
