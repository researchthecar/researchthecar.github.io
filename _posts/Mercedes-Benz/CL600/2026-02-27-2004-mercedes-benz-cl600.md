# 2004 Mercedes-Benz CL600

### Overview
The 2004 Mercedes-Benz CL600, a flagship luxury coupe, is renowned for its potent V12 engine and opulent features. While offering a sophisticated driving experience, its complexity, particularly with the twin-turbocharged V12 and advanced electronics, can lead to specific reliability concerns as the vehicle ages. Owners often report issues related to the ABC (Active Body Control) suspension system, ignition coils, and various electronic modules. Maintenance and repair costs can be substantial due to the specialized nature of the components and the high labor rates associated with these high-performance vehicles. Despite these potential drawbacks, many owners find the CL600's blend of performance and luxury to be worth the investment in upkeep.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L V12 Twin-Turbo (M275)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light, reduced power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils and potentially spark plugs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800 per coil, $1000 - $2000 for a set.

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets, Oil Cooler Seals)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil on engine components, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking gaskets and seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, excessive smoke.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of turbochargers.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $5000 per turbocharger.


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, slipping gears, inability to shift, 'limp mode'.
- **Solution:** Replacement or repair of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering, slipping, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, 'ABC System Malfunction' warning light, bouncy ride.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hoses, struts, or pumps.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $4000 per component, $5000 - $10000+ for full system overhaul.

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of hydraulic pressure, vehicle sagging, loud whining noise.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Electrical

### Various Electronic Modules
**SAM Unit (Signal Acquisition Module) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning lights, wipers, power windows, dashboard warnings.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the SAM unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Keyless Go System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not locking/unlocking, engine not starting, intermittent operation.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair/replacement of related sensors and control modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000

**Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** One or both headlights not working, flickering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight ballast.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per ballast.


## Exterior

### Standard Chassis
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Cost:** No widespread issues reported.


## Interior

### Standard Interior
**Seat Control Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not adjusting, memory functions failing.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair/replacement of seat control modules or motors.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000

**Climate Control System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Uneven cooling/heating, blower motor problems, control panel errors.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of climate control modules, sensors, or blower motor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200

### Summary Verdict
The 2004 Mercedes-Benz CL600 is a complex luxury vehicle with a powerful V12 engine. While offering exceptional performance and comfort, its advanced systems, particularly the ABC suspension and intricate electronics, are prone to issues as the car ages. Owners should be prepared for potentially high maintenance and repair costs, especially for components like ignition coils, ABC hydraulics, and electronic modules. Regular, specialized maintenance is crucial for mitigating these issues and preserving the vehicle's performance and luxury. Despite these potential reliability concerns, the CL600 remains a desirable car for those who appreciate its unique blend of power and refinement and are willing to invest in its upkeep.
