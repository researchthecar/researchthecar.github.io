## 2016 Mercedes-Benz Viaggio 900

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz Viaggio 900 is a luxury coach bus manufactured by Daimler Buses. It is powered by a 12.8-liter inline-6 diesel engine that produces 428 horsepower and 1,450 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 6-speed manual transmission. The Viaggio 900 has a spacious interior with seating for up to 57 passengers. It also features a variety of amenities, including air conditioning, a DVD player, and a refrigerator.

People like the Viaggio 900 for its comfortable ride, powerful engine, and spacious interior. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and electrical system.

**Engine**

* **12.8L Inline-6 Diesel Engine**
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, decreased fuel economy
        * Solution: Replace turbocharger
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
 * **Fuel Injector Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, rough idling, decreased fuel economy, black smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
 * **EGR Valve Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, increased emissions, engine knocking
        * Solution: Clean or replace EGR valve
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
 * **Head Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Clutch Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, shuddering
        * Solution: Replace clutch
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
 * **Transmission Fluid Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid, difficulty shifting gears
        * Solution: Repair or replace leaking seal
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
 * **Synchronizer Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
        * Solution: Replace synchronizer
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Uneven ride, sagging suspension, decreased handling
        * Solution: Repair or replace air suspension components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000

**Electrical**

* **Battery Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, electrical system malfunctions
        * Solution: Replace battery
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
 * **Alternator Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Overcharging or undercharging battery, difficulty starting
        * Solution: Replace alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
 * **Wiring Harness Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunctions, intermittent problems
        * Solution: Repair or replace wiring harness
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500

**Exterior**

* **Cracked Windshield**
        * Description/Symptoms: Large crack in windshield
        * Solution: Replace windshield
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Door Lock Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors
        * Solution: Repair or replace door lock
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Cracks in dashboard material
        * Solution: Replace dashboard
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
 * **Seat Belt Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Seat belt does not retract or latch properly
        * Solution: Replace seat belt
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
 * **Air Conditioning System Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: No cold air, AC leaks, compressor noise
        * Solution: Repair or replace AC components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500

**Summary**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz Viaggio 900 is a capable and luxurious coach bus. However, it has been reported to have some issues with its engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost to fix them can be significant. Overall, the Viaggio 900 is a good choice for those who need a reliable and comfortable coach bus. However, it is important to be aware of the potential issues that it may have.
