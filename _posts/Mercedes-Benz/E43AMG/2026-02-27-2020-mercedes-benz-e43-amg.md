# 2020 Mercedes-Benz E43 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Mercedes-Benz, reviewing owner-submitted feedback can help highlight potential areas to inspect during a pre-purchase evaluation. While luxury performance vehicles offer impressive engineering and technology, prospective buyers should carefully assess powertrain operation, electronics, and overall vehicle condition to ensure long-term satisfaction.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine generates robust horsepower and torque, delivering brisk acceleration typical of AMG-tuned models. Buyers should conduct a thorough mechanical inspection to check for fluid leaks and electrical harness condition.

**Oil Pump Valve and Harness Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported intermittent engine warning messages related to oil pump valve operation, as well as oil leaks from sensors reaching the wiring harness.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the engine wiring harness and oil control valves for leaks or fault codes.
- **Cost:** $4,000+

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission channels power smoothly through all-wheel drive, offering refined gear changes for daily driving and spirited runs. Routine fluid maintenance is key to preserving shifting performance.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera

Safety and driver-assistance electronics generally function well, though screen and camera glitches have been documented by some vehicle owners.

**Rearview Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced freezing, blank screens, or complete inoperability when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Inspect software integration and camera wiring connections; software updates or hardware replacement may be necessary.

## Exterior

### Glass and Roof

Exterior panels and trim maintain a sleek appearance, but buyers should closely inspect glass integrity and weatherstripping.

**Sunroof and Trim Delamination**
- **Symptoms:** Reports indicate rare instances of panoramic sunroof glass shattering or convertible top window sections separating prematurely.
- **Solution:** Carefully inspect all exterior glass and window seals for signs of stress, cracking, or edge separation.

## Summary Verdict

A used Mercedes-Benz performance vehicle can be a thrilling and sophisticated daily driver when properly maintained. Prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist to verify engine health, electrical system integrity, and electronics functionality.
