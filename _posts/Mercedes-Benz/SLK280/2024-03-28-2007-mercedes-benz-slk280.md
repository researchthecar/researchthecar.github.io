**Introduction**

The 2007 Mercedes-Benz SLK280 is a luxurious and sporty roadster that offers a thrilling driving experience. It is powered by a 3.0L V6 engine that produces 228 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque. The SLK280 is available with a 6-speed manual transmission or a 5-speed automatic transmission. It features a retractable hardtop, which provides both open-air motoring and the convenience of a closed roof. The SLK280 has received positive reviews for its performance, handling, and design. However, it has also been noted for some reliability issues.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6 Engine**

* **Timing chain failure:** The timing chain may fail, causing the engine to stop running. Symptoms include a rattling noise from the engine, a loss of power, and an illuminated check engine light. The solution is to replace the timing chain. Estimated cost to fix: $2000 - $3000
* **Valve cover gasket leak:** The valve cover gasket may leak, causing oil to leak onto the engine. Symptoms include a visible oil leak on the engine, a decrease in oil level, and an illuminated check engine light. The solution is to replace the valve cover gasket. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1000
* **Exhaust manifold crack:** The exhaust manifold may crack, causing a loss of power and an illuminated check engine light.
