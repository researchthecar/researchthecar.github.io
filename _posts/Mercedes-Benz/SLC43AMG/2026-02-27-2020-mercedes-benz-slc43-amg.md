# 2020 Mercedes-Benz SLC43 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a 2020 Mercedes-Benz SLC43 AMG means looking at a high-performance luxury roadster with sharp handling and potent twin-turbo power. While official complaint data for this model year is very minimal, buyers should still approach a used luxury sports car with careful attention to service history, complex electronics, and mechanical condition.

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Bi-Turbo Engine

This model features a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine producing approximately 385 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque. It delivers thrilling acceleration and robust performance, though regular maintenance and premium fuel are required to keep it running smoothly.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Power is sent to the rear wheels via a 9-speed automatic transmission designed for quick shifts and highway efficiency. It complements the sporty character of the roadster, though prospective buyers should test all gear changes during a pre-purchase inspection.
