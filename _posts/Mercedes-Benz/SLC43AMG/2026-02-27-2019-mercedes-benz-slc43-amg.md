# 2019 Mercedes-Benz SLC43 AMG Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Mercedes-Benz SLC43 AMG, buyers can generally expect high-performance thrills paired with upscale luxury. As with any high-output luxury roadster, reviewing powertrain health and keeping an eye out for isolated owner-reported engine management glitches is essential during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Biturbo

The 3.0-liter V6 biturbo engine produces approximately 385 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration for the roadster. Overall, it is a robust performance motor, though buyers should verify proper maintenance history and check for any stored diagnostic codes.

**Engine Hesitation and Exhaust Smoke**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience hesitation during acceleration and notice excessive smoke coming from the exhaust pipe, often accompanied by an illuminated check engine light.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose stored fault codes (such as code P052E71) and inspect the emissions or positive crankcase ventilation systems.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic

The 9G-TRONIC 9-speed automatic transmission provides rapid and smooth gear changes tailored to the AMG performance profile. It generally operates reliably when routine fluid service intervals are maintained.
