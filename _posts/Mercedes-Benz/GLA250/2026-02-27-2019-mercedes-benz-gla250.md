# 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLA250

### Overview
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLA250, a compact luxury SUV, generally presents a solid reliability record, reflecting Mercedes-Benz's commitment to quality engineering. It offers a blend of premium features, agile handling, and a comfortable ride, making it a popular choice in its segment. While most owners report a positive ownership experience, like any vehicle, there are specific areas where potential issues have been noted. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into common problems, their symptoms, potential solutions, and associated costs for the primary engine and transmission configurations.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M270/M274)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant warning light, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased oil consumption, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic (7G-DCT)
**Harsh Shifting / Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable jolts or hesitations during gear changes, particularly at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Software update, transmission fluid flush and replacement, or internal transmission repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,500

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting behavior, inability to select gears, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,800

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLA250 demonstrates good overall reliability, with the 2.0L turbocharged engine and 7-speed dual-clutch transmission being the primary areas where owners might encounter technical issues. Coolant leaks and turbocharger problems are the most commonly cited engine concerns, while the transmission can sometimes exhibit harsh shifting or module failures. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and battery drain, are also worth noting. However, these issues are not universally experienced, and many GLA250 models perform without significant trouble. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any emerging symptoms are recommended to ensure long-term dependability.
