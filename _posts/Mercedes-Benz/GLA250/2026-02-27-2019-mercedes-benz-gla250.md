# 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLA250 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Mercedes-Benz GLA250, buyers will find a stylish and agile luxury subcompact crossover. However, reviewing owner-submitted complaints reveals a few recurring mechanical and electrical concerns that prospective owners should inspect closely during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine produces approximately 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration for daily driving. It generally proves mechanically solid, though buyers should monitor for standard turbocharged maintenance needs.

### Fuel Injector

Fuel delivery components warrant careful attention to prevent unexpected leaks or combustion issues.

**Fuel Injector Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Gasoline odors around the vehicle and illumination of warning lights, sometimes accompanied by rough running.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel injectors for leaks and replace faulty units as needed.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers quick shifts but is associated with notable owner complaints regarding unexpected shifting behavior.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle unexpectedly drops out of gear into neutral while driving at various speeds, causing a temporary loss of motive power accompanied by warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a qualified technician; software updates or transmission control module service may be required.

## Electrical

### Body Electrical and Sensors

electrical issues can occasionally trigger false warnings or disrupt cabin accessories.

**Dashboard Warnings and Inoperative Signals**
- **Symptoms:** Multiple erratic dashboard warnings, non-functional turn signals, and gauge cluster failure.
- **Solution:** Inspect wiring harnesses, control modules, and check for water intrusion near pillars.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V585000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLA250&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The seatbelt for a passenger seated in the rear middle seating position might not be properly secured, which could increase the risk of an injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the left anchor plate of the rear middle seatbelt, correcting its installation as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V672000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLA250&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A loss of engine operation, power steering assist, ESP system, or Active Brake Assist can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the engine compartment fusebox mounting and correct it, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V132000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLA250&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An incorrect rear seat back covering carpet could have been installed on the split-rear seat back. As such, these vehicles fail to comply to Federal Motor Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 225, "Child Restraint Anchorage Systems." If the upper anchorage point is covered, the child seat will not be able to be secured as intended in the center position of the.
- **What to do:** Mercedes will notify owners, and dealers will check the rear seat back covering carpet on the affected vehicles and replace it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V912000: Visibility:Sun/Moon Roof Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLA250&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Water leaking into the vehicle from around the sunroof may affect the electrical contacts of the window air bag, potentially causing the window air bag to not deploy as intended, increasing the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Mercedes has notified owners, and dealers will inspect the bonding of the panorama sliding sunroof and the electrical contacts of the window air bag on the affected vehicles, replacing the components as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V033000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Control Module (Tcm/Pcm/Tecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLA250&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the transmission shifts into neutral while driving, there would be loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the transmission control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The Mercedes-Benz GLA250 offers luxury and performance in a compact footprint, but prospective buyers should carefully check for transmission shifting anomalies and electrical glitches during a pre-purchase inspection.
