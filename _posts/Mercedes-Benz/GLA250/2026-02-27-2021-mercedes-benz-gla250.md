# 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLA250 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLA250, buyers will find a compact luxury SUV that offers nimble handling, a refined interior, and efficient turbocharged performance. While it provides an engaging daily drive, prospective owners should review owner-reported feedback regarding engine performance, electrical drainage issues, and body component functionality. Familiarizing yourself with these common owner grievances can help ensure a more confident and informed purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Engine

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces 221 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, delivering lively acceleration suited for urban and highway driving. Overall, it is a robust powerplant, though buyers should watch closely for cooling and ignition anomalies reported by some owners.

**Rough Idling and Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected rough idling accompanied by a blinking check engine light, engine misfires, and a sudden loss of motive power while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the ignition system, fuel injectors, and diagnostic trouble codes to pinpoint and replace failing components.

**Coolant and Oil Mixing**
- **Symptoms:** Engine oil may appear milky due to coolant mixing into the lubrication system, potentially pointing to internal gasket or cooler failures.
- **Solution:** Perform a comprehensive cooling and lubrication system pressure test to identify the source of contamination, such as a failing oil cooler or head gasket.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers quick, seamless gear changes and pairs smoothly with front-wheel or 4MATIC all-wheel-drive configurations. It generally operates reliably when properly maintained, with few major systemic mechanical complaints noted by owners.

## Interior

### Air Conditioning and Drainage Circuitry

Electrical architecture supports modern infotainment and convenience features, but owners should be mindful of environmental debris or condensation clogging drainage pathways near sensitive wiring.

**AC Condensation Backup and Short Circuit**
- **Symptoms:** Clogged AC drainage tubing can trap condensation, causing water to back up and short-circuit the vehicle's circuit board or electrical components.
- **Solution:** Clear any blockages from the HVAC drainage tubes and inspect adjacent electrical harnesses for moisture intrusion or corrosion.

## Safety

### Power Liftgate

Exterior styling and utility features function well overall, though mechanical power liftgate operations should be checked during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Unexpected Liftgate Closure**
- **Symptoms:** The rear power liftgate may close unexpectedly while loading items, presenting a physical safety hazard to individuals standing underneath.
- **Solution:** Inspect liftgate strut mechanisms, sensors, and software calibration to ensure proper holding force and obstacle detection.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V102000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLA250&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The ground connection for the front passenger air bag may not be mounted properly, which could result in an inadvertent deployment. Inadvertent air bag deployment can increase the risk of injury for an occupant in the front passenger seat, and increase the possibility of a vehicle crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the ground connection for the passenger air bag and correct it, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V125000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Caliper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLA250&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Reduced brake performance can increase the risk of a crash. In addition, detached brake components can create a road hazard, and increase the risk of a crash for other drivers.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the fasteners on the rear brake caliper housings, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V732000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Pads](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLA250&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The vehicles may not be equipped with an inspection gauge to check the wear of the rear brake pads. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 135, "Light Vehicle Brake Systems." Driving with worn brake pads can reduce braking ability, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install an inspection gauge and provide supplemental owner's manual information, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLA250 combines luxury appeal with spirited performance, making it an attractive pre-owned option if inspected carefully. Buyers should thoroughly verify engine health, check for any signs of fluid contamination, and ensure that drainage pathways and power accessories operate correctly. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a specialist can mitigate most hidden risks before finalizing your purchase.
