**Interior**

* **Faulty Climate Control System**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, causing the temperature to fluctuate or the fan to stop working.
    * Solution: Replacement of the climate control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    
* **Glitchy Infotainment System**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience glitches, such as freezing, unresponsive touchscreens, or connectivity issues.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Recalls**

* **NHTSA Recall Number:** 22V-139
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear axle drive shaft may fracture, potentially causing a loss of power and increasing the risk of a crash.
    * Solution: Replacement of the rear axle drive shaft
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: N/A
* **NHTSA Recall Number:** 22V-206
    * Description/Symptoms: The fuel pump may fail, resulting in a loss of engine power and the inability to restart the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replacement of the fuel pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: N/A

**Summary**

Overall, the 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLA250 has a relatively low number of known issues, with most being minor and easily resolved. The most commonly reported issues are related to the climate control system and infotainment system, but these issues are not widespread. With regular maintenance and timely repairs, the GLA250 can provide a reliable and enjoyable driving experience.
