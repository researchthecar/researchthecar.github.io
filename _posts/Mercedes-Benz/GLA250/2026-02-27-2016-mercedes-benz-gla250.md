# 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLA250

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLA250 is a compact luxury SUV that offers a blend of premium features and a sporty driving experience. While generally considered a reliable vehicle within its class, like any automotive model, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for prospective buyers or current owners. The GLA250 is known for its turbocharged engine and available all-wheel-drive system, contributing to its appeal in the premium compact SUV segment. Understanding these common issues can help in proactive maintenance and informed decision-making.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger inspection, repair, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, sweet smell from the engine.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair/replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic (7G-DCT)
**Transmission Shudder/Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky shifts, hesitation during acceleration, rough engagement.
- **Solution:** Software updates, transmission fluid flush, or internal component repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLA250 demonstrates a generally solid reliability record, with most reported issues concentrated in the powertrain and electrical systems. Common concerns include potential turbocharger problems and coolant leaks, as well as occasional transmission shudder. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment screen and battery drain, are also noted. However, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free from widespread defects. Addressing potential issues early, especially those related to the engine and transmission, can help maintain the vehicle's performance and longevity. Overall, it offers a premium experience with a few predictable areas for attention.
