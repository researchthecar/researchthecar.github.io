# 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLA250 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Mercedes-Benz GLA250, buyers find a stylish and agile compact luxury crossover that balances daily drivability with brand prestige. While overall owner satisfaction can be high, prospective purchasers should carefully evaluate mechanical and electrical histories. Reviewing specific components like the fuel system, electronics, and transmission operation helps ensure a reliable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M274)

Powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing approximately 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, this powertrain delivers brisk acceleration and reasonable fuel economy. Buyers should verify proper maintenance records, particularly concerning common wear items and crankcase ventilation.

**Fuel Odor and Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report a strong, persistent smell of gasoline inside the cabin upon starting or operating the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines, connections, and high-pressure pumps for leaks, and replace damaged components as necessary.

**Crankcase Ventilator Line Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Triggering of the check engine light accompanied by a failing crankcase ventilation hose.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty crankcase ventilator line with an updated component.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

Equipped with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission paired with front-wheel drive or 4MATIC all-wheel drive, offering quick gear changes. Prospective owners should test drive thoroughly to ensure smooth engagement and prompt gear selection.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may intermittently shift into neutral on its own while driving, accompanied by erratic dashboard behavior or transmission error messages.
- **Solution:** Diagnose transmission control module software and internal actuators; update or replace failing control units.

## Electrical

### Electrical System and Infotainment

Manages vehicle instrumentation, lighting, and infotainment features, though software glitches can occasionally trigger false warnings or display failures.

**Spurious Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Various unknown warning lights illuminate on the instrument cluster randomly while driving and may cycle on and off.
- **Solution:** Update vehicle computer software or replace faulty sensors and control modules as diagnosed by a technician.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Glass

Handles exterior visibility and optional panoramic roofing; buyers should check for signs of water intrusion around the headliner.

**Sunroof Rail and Water Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Plastic rail guides and sliders break, preventing the sunroof from closing properly and allowing water to leak into the cabin.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged sunroof track mechanisms, clips, or seals to restore proper weatherproofing.

## Safety

### Air Bag and Steering Column

Includes supplemental restraint systems and steering wheel controls designed for occupant safety, requiring immediate attention if warning indicators appear.

**Air Bag Warning and Clockspring Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Constant illumination of the air bag service light, often accompanied by a clicking noise from the steering wheel and stiff steering operation.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column clockspring and replace faulty air bag or steering components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V229000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLA250&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The steering shaft bore hole for the universal joint bearing may allow excessive movement that, over time, could result in the separation of the bearing and universal joint. A separated universal joint bearing can cause a loss of steering control and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect, and as necessary, replace the steering shaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V080000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLA250&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The pelvis air bag or the front passenger air bag inflator initiator may fail to ignite in the event of a crash. If the pelvis airbag or the front passenger airbag does not deploy as intended in the event of a crash, the occupants have an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the affected air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V114000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=GLA250&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the starting current limiter overheats, the surrounding components can melt, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will install an additional fuse in the electrical line to the starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The GLA250 offers a compelling blend of luxury, efficiency, and nimble driving dynamics for used-car buyers. However, prospective owners should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on potential fuel system odors, sunroof mechanism integrity, and transmission shift behavior. Addressing these key areas helps secure a dependable and enjoyable luxury compact crossover.
