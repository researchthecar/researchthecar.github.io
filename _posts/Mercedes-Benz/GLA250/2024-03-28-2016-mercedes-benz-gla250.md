**Introduction**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLA250 is a compact SUV that offers a blend of luxury and practicality. It comes with a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 208 horsepower and 258 pound-feet of torque, paired with a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission. Other engine and transmission options available include:

* 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine with 221 horsepower and 258 pound-feet of torque (GLA250 4MATIC)
* 2.1-liter turbocharged four-cylinder diesel engine with 136 horsepower and 221 pound-feet of torque (GLA220 CDI)
* 2.1-liter turbocharged four-cylinder diesel engine with 177 horsepower and 350 pound-feet of torque (GLA220 CDI 4MATIC)

The GLA250 is known for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and capable performance, but it's not immune to certain issues.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine with 208 Horsepower**

* **Cylinder head defect**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear of the camshafts and rocker arms, leading to engine noise, reduced performance, and potential engine failure
    * Solution: Replace the affected cylinder head
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Intake manifold issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Faulty intake manifold runner flaps can cause hesitation or stalling during acceleration
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine with 221 Horsepower**

* **No Issues Found**

**2.1-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Diesel Engine with 136 Horsepower**

* **Fuel injector problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clogged or faulty fuel injectors can lead to poor engine performance, increased emissions, and reduced fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace the affected fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.1-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Diesel Engine with 177 Horsepower**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**

* **Transmission shudder or hesitation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed gear shifts, especially during low-speed maneuvers
    * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission clutch pack
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature battery discharge, even when the vehicle is parked
    * Solution: Investigate and fix any electrical component that is drawing excessive power when the vehicle is off
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source of the problem
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical component failures**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning sensors, switches, or other electrical components can cause various issues, depending on the affected part
    * Solution: Replace the affected electrical component
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the part and labor involved
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin through the sunroof
    * Solution: Reseal the sunroof or replace the sunroof assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard delamination**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard material peeling, cracking, or separating
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Door panel rattles**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or misaligned door panels vibrating or making noise
    * Solution: Adjust or replace the door panel clips
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLA250 is an overall reliable vehicle, but it does have some potential issues that owners should be aware of. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some being minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more serious and expensive. It's important to properly maintain the vehicle and address any issues promptly to ensure its longevity and safety.
