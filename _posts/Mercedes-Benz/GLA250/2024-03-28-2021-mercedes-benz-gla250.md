**Introduction**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLA250 is a compact luxury crossover. It comes with a turbocharged 2.0L inline-4 engine that produces 221 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission. The GLA250 has a spacious interior and a well-equipped infotainment system. However, it has some drawbacks, such as its firm ride and lack of off-road capability.

**Engine**

**2.0L Inline-4**

* No Issues Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Dual-Clutch**

* **Hesitation and Jerking during Gear Shifts**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or jerk when shifting gears. This can be especially noticeable during low-speed maneuvers, such as when driving in stop-and-go traffic.
    * Solution: The transmission may need to be reprogrammed or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Premature Wear of Clutch Packs**
    * Description/Symptoms: The clutch packs in the transmission may wear out prematurely, leading to slipping and a loss of power. This can be accompanied by a burning smell or grinding noise.
    * Solution: The clutch packs may need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Malfunctioning Infotainment System**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze, reboot, or display error messages. It may also lose connectivity to Bluetooth devices or the internet.
    * Solution: The infotainment system may need to be updated or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use. This can be caused by a faulty component or a parasitic draw.
    * Solution: The electrical system may need to be inspected and repaired.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Squeaky Dashboard**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may squeak or rattle over bumps or during acceleration.
    * Solution: The dashboard may need to be disassembled and lubricated.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty Climate Control**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may not blow air at the desired temperature or may produce excessive noise.
    * Solution: The climate control module or related components may need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLA250 has a few reported issues, primarily with the transmission and electrical system. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be costly to repair. Overall, the GLA250 is a well-made vehicle, but it is important to be aware of potential problems before purchasing one.
