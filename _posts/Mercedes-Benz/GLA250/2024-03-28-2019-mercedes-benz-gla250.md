**Introduction:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLA250 is a compact SUV that combines luxury, performance, and practicality. It is powered by a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine that produces 221 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The GLA250 has a spacious and well-equipped interior, and it offers a comfortable and engaging driving experience. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of reported issues.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

**Check Engine Light Issue**
Description/Symptoms: The Check Engine Light illuminates due to an issue with the camshaft position sensors. This can cause rough idling, reduced engine power, and increased fuel consumption.
Solution: Replace the camshaft position sensors.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

**Transmission Slipping Issue**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting gears, especially under acceleration. This can be accompanied by a loss of power or a shuddering sensation.
Solution: Update the transmission control module software or replace the transmission clutch packs.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Discharge Issue**
Description/Symptoms: The battery may discharge prematurely, leaving the vehicle unable to start. This can be caused by a faulty battery or a parasitic drain from electrical components.
Solution: Test and replace the battery or identify and fix the source of the parasitic drain.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500

**Exterior**

**Panoramic Sunroof Leakage Issue**
Description/Symptoms: Water may leak into the cabin through the panoramic sunroof during heavy rain or car washes.
Solution: Replace the sunroof seals or adjust the sunroof alignment.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Suspension**

**Rear Suspension Noise Issue**
Description/Symptoms: A knocking or rattling noise may be heard from the rear suspension when driving over bumps or uneven surfaces.
Solution: Replace or lubricate the rear suspension components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800

**Interior**

**Infotainment System Malfunction Issue**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may malfunction, displaying a blank screen or freezing on certain applications.
Solution: Update or reset the infotainment system software.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300

**Summary:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLA250 is a solid choice for those seeking a compact luxury SUV. While it has a few reported issues, such as the check engine light issue and transmission slipping, most of these issues are relatively minor and can be resolved with moderate cost. The vehicle's overall reliability is generally acceptable, and it offers a comfortable and enjoyable driving experience.
