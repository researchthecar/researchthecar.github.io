## 2019 Mercedes-Benz Gran Viale RF

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Gran Viale RF is a luxury compact sedan with a spacious interior and plenty of power. It is powered by a 2.0-liter inline-4 engine that produces 255 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The Gran Viale RF is available in both rear-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations.

Drivers have praised the Gran Viale RF for its comfortable ride, responsive handling, and impressive fuel economy. However, there have been some reports of issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior.

### **Engine**

**2.0L Inline-4 Engine**

- **Issue:** Excessive oil consumption
- **Description/Symptoms:** The engine may consume more than 1 quart of oil every 1,000 miles. This can lead to low oil levels, which can damage the engine.
- **Solution:** The engine may need to be rebuilt or replaced.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $3,000 - $5,000+
- **Recalls:** None Found

### **Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

- **Issue:** Shifting problems
- **Description/Symptoms:** The transmission may shift roughly or hesitate to shift gears. This can be caused by a variety of issues, including faulty solenoids, sensors, or clutches.
- **Solution:** The transmission may need to be repaired or replaced.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+
- **Recalls:** None Found

### **Suspension**

- **Issue:** No Issues Found

### **Electrical**

- **Issue:** Battery drain
- **Description/Symptoms:** The battery may drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is not in use. This can be caused by a variety of issues, including faulty electrical components or a parasitic draw.
- **Solution:** The electrical system needs to be inspected to identify and fix the source of the battery drain.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+
- **Recalls:** None Found

### **Exterior**

- **Issue:** Cracked tail lights
- **Description/Symptoms:** The tail lights may crack or become damaged due to exposure to heat or impact.
- **Solution:** The cracked tail lights may need to be repaired or replaced.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
- **Recalls:** None Found

### **Interior**

- **Issue:** Climate control system not cooling properly
- **Description/Symptoms:** The climate control system may not cool the vehicle properly, even when set to the lowest temperature. This can be caused by a variety of issues, including faulty sensors, actuators, or refrigerant leaks.
- **Solution:** The climate control system needs to be inspected and repaired or replaced as needed.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
- **Recalls:** None Found

### **Summary**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Gran Viale RF is a luxurious and powerful compact sedan. However, there have been some reports of issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, electrical system, exterior, and interior. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem.
