## Engine

### 2.2L OM651 Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Regeneration Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration, DPF cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1500

**EGR Valve Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, poor acceleration, increased emissions, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $250 - $700


### 3.0L OM642 Diesel
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, black smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger inspection, repair, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**Glow Plug Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting in cold weather, rough idle.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty glow plugs and potentially the control module.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Transmission

### 7G-TRONIC PLUS (7-Speed Automatic)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, delayed shifting, low transmission fluid level warning.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair of leaks, fluid flush and refill.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

**Torque Converter Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, rough gear changes, slipping.
- **Solution:** Torque converter replacement.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500


## Suspension

### Standard Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or seals.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust ride height, constant vehicle sag.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Navigation issues, audio control problems, general system lag.

### Sensor Failures (ABS, Parking Assist)
