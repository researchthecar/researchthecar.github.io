**Introduction**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S is a performance-oriented sedan that combines luxury and power. Under the hood lies a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine paired with a 9-speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 9G transmission. This powertrain delivers an impressive 469 horsepower and 479 lb-ft of torque. The C63 AMG S offers a thrilling and engaging driving experience with precise handling and impressive acceleration. While it receives praise for its exceptional performance, some owners have reported specific issues.

**Engine**

* **4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Engine**

**Fuel Pump Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Engine stalling, difficulty starting, loss of power.
* **Solution:** Replace the failed fuel pump.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $800 - $1,200+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Intake Manifold Issues:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Reduced engine performance, check engine light.
* **Solution:** Replace the affected intake manifold.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $800+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

* **9-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 9G Transmission**

**TCC Lock-up Clutch Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Transmission slipping, shuddering, delayed gear engagement.
* **Solution:** Replace the TCC lock-up clutch assembly.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive Suspension**

**Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Loss of suspension height, rough ride.
* **Solution:** Replace the failed air suspension compressor.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electronic Stability Program (ESP)**

**ESP Malfunction:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Loss of vehicle stability, warning lights.
* **Solution:** Diagnose and repair the faulty ESP system.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,200+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlamps**

**Headlamp Condensation:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Accumulation of moisture inside the headlamps.
* **Solution:** Replace or repair the affected headlamps.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $800 - $1,500+
* **Recalls:** MB-2018090001

**Interior**

* **Climate Control**

**Climate Control System Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Malfunctioning climate control, inadequate cooling or heating.
* **Solution:** Diagnose and repair the climate control system.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

While the 2019 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S offers exceptional performance, owners have reported specific issues. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some requiring major repairs. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before purchasing this vehicle. It is recommended to carefully inspect any used C63 AMG S and consider a comprehensive warranty to cover potential repairs.
