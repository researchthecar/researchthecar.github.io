# 2015 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2015 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S, a high-performance variant of the W205 C-Class, is renowned for its potent handcrafted V8 engine and dynamic driving capabilities. As a performance-oriented vehicle, its reliability is generally considered good for its class, though specific components may require more attention than a standard sedan. Owners often praise its exhilarating performance and luxurious interior. However, like any complex performance car, potential buyers should be aware of certain areas that may experience issues, particularly those related to the powertrain and advanced electronic systems. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any developing problems are crucial for maintaining its peak performance and longevity.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (M177)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, whistling noise from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty wastegate actuator or complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Oil Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Oil smell, visible oil leaks around the engine, potential for oil in coolant.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil cooler and associated seals.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900


## Transmission

### AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 7-speed Automatic
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, delayed gear engagement, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, limp mode.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, audio cutting out.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or components causing the drain.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S offers a compelling blend of performance and luxury. While its core mechanical components, particularly the engine and transmission, are robust for a high-performance vehicle, potential owners should be aware of specific issues like wastegate actuator failures, oil cooler leaks, and torque converter problems. Electrical gremlins, especially with the COMAND infotainment system and occasional battery drain, can also arise. Despite these potential concerns, with diligent maintenance and addressing issues promptly, the C63 AMG S remains a desirable and capable performance sedan. Its reliability is generally on par with other vehicles in its segment, but the cost of repairs can be higher due to specialized AMG components.
