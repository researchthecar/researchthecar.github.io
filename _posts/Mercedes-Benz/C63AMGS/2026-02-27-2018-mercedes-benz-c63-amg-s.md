# 2018 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S offers high-performance luxury, but used buyers should review common owner-reported complaints closely. While luxury features and powerful engineering define the driving experience, certain recurring electrical, body, and powertrain concerns have been documented by owners. Prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect vehicle history records and ensure all necessary manufacturer service campaigns have been completed.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8

Powered by a hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing around 503 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, the C63 AMG S delivers blistering performance. Buyers should check for proper maintenance and watch for potential crankcase or emissions-related sensor faults.

**Check Engine Light and PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light accompanied by diagnostic trouble codes such as P052E pointing to crankcase ventilation issues.
- **Solution:** Replace the defective crankcase ventilation valve component with an updated design part.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected engine shutdown while driving, posing safety risks in traffic or on highways.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic inspection of the engine management and fuel systems to isolate and replace failing components.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 9-Speed Automatic

The AMG Speedshift MCT 9-speed multi-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear changes suited for high-performance driving. Routine fluid services are vital to maintaining its responsiveness and longevity.

## Steering

### Steering Rack

The steering system provides sharp and precise feedback, though early production models experienced recall oversight regarding steering rack locknut integrity.

**Power Steering Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering assistance accompanied by dashboard warning messages.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering components and replace the steering rack assembly if internal locknut or mechanical failure is confirmed.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior styling is aggressive and premium, but structural bonding issues around the panoramic glass roof require buyer vigilance.

**Roof Panel Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** The front glass roof panel between the windshield and panoramic sunroof comes loose or detaches entirely while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the bonding agent and re-seal or replace the roof glass panel according to updated manufacturer procedures.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety equipment generally performs well, though some owners report seat belt retraction issues.

**Failure to Retract**
- **Symptoms:** Front driver and passenger seat belts fail to retract smoothly or return to their stowed positions.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt assemblies and replace retractors if mechanical binding or wear is found.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S is an exhilarating performance car that blends luxury with immense V8 power. However, used buyers should carefully review service records, verify that all steering and roof-related safety campaigns have been addressed, and budget for potential high-end maintenance costs common in high-performance German vehicles.
