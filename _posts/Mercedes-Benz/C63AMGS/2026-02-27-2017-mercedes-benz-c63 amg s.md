# 2017 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S, a high-performance variant of the C-Class, is renowned for its potent V8 engine and dynamic driving capabilities. Built with AMG's signature engineering prowess, it generally offers a robust and thrilling driving experience. However, like any performance vehicle, specific areas can be prone to issues under demanding use. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and enthusiasts.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (M177)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, whistling noises from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty wastegate actuator or the complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Oil Pan)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level warnings.
- **Solution:** Replacement of gaskets and seals; tightening of oil pan bolts.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Transmission

### AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 7-speed
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, rough gear changes, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Rebuilding or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, failure to engage gears.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200


## Suspension

### AMG RIDE CONTROL Sport Suspension
**Adaptive Damping System Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh ride, clunking noises over bumps, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of faulty sensors, actuators, or dampers.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $3000+


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard resets, or potential replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1000

**Battery Drain Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, dead battery after short periods, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty modules or battery.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S is a formidable performance sedan with a generally strong reliability record for its class. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the high-performance powertrain and its associated systems. Potential issues with the turbocharger wastegates, torque converter, and adaptive suspension components are the most frequently cited technical problems. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and battery drain, can also occur. While these issues can be costly to repair, they are not universally present and often depend on driving style and maintenance history. Overall, for a vehicle of this caliber, the reliability is respectable, but owners should be prepared for potential maintenance costs associated with its performance-oriented nature.
