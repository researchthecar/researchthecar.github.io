# 2019 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S offers exceptional luxury and high-performance engineering, making it an exciting pre-owned purchase for driving enthusiasts. However, prospective buyers should approach with a mindful eye toward potential mechanical and electrical hiccups. While many owners enjoy trouble-free performance, reports filed with federal safety regulators highlight select recurring complaints involving powertrain hesitation, electrical sensors, and interior components.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8 Engine

The hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers immense horsepower and torque, providing thrilling acceleration. Routine maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are essential for long-term reliability.

**Loss of Motive Power / Throttle Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may suddenly shut off or experience a loss of motive power while driving, accompanied by stored fault codes related to throttle position sensors.
- **Solution:** Inspect throttle body and accelerator pedal position sensors; replace faulty electronic sensors or assemblies as necessary.

**Oil Control Valve Defect**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of the check engine light and potential engine lock-up while driving due to a defective oil control valve.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician perform diagnostic testing to inspect oil control valves and replace malfunctioning components.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed multi-clutch automatic transmission is tailored for rapid gear shifts and dynamic driving dynamics. Proper fluid maintenance is vital to preserving shift quality and overall drivetrain health.

**Unexpected Acceleration Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle momentarily fails to respond when attempting to accelerate from a stop or low-speed turn.
- **Solution:** Check for transmission control module software updates or consult a specialist for diagnostic inspection.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Rims

Performance-tuned suspensions paired with large wheels and low-profile tires offer incredible grip but make the vehicle more vulnerable to rough road impacts.

**Wheel Rim Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Factory alloy wheels develop structural cracks, often accelerated by rough road conditions and low-profile tires.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels for structural integrity and replace cracked rims with durable aftermarket or OEM replacements.

## Steering

### Steering Assembly

The power steering system provides sharp and responsive handling characteristic of AMG performance models, though any reports of binding or lockup require immediate professional inspection.

**Steering Wheel Lockup**
- **Symptoms:** Steering wheel becomes difficult to turn or locks completely while executing low-speed turns, creating severe control hazards.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column and electronic power steering components immediately for mechanical failure or electrical faults.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Occupant safety systems are generally robust, but physical wear on components like seat belt retractors should be checked during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Failure to Retract**
- **Symptoms:** Front driver and passenger seat belts fail to fully retract into their stowed positions, creating trip hazards and hindering rear passenger exit.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt assemblies and replace retractor mechanisms if mechanical failure is confirmed.
