# 2018 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S is a high-performance luxury sedan renowned for its potent V8 engine and dynamic driving experience. As a performance-oriented vehicle, it generally exhibits strong build quality and engineering. However, like any complex machine, certain areas may require attention over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential issues that owners might encounter. The data aims to offer a balanced perspective on the C63 AMG S's long-term dependability, considering its powerful drivetrain and sophisticated systems.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (M177)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, boost pressure issues, unusual noises from the turbo area.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty wastegate actuator or the complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil residue around the top of the engine, burning oil smell, potential for oil to drip onto exhaust components.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the valve cover gaskets.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Transmission

### AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 9-Speed Automatic
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from the transmission pan or cooler lines, potential for shifting issues, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking seals, lines, or the transmission pan.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting behavior, delayed engagement, or harsh shifts. Often resolved with software updates.
- **Solution:** Reprogramming or updating the transmission control module (TCM) software.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, Bluetooth connectivity issues, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard resets, or potential replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1,200


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently, battery warning light.
- **Solution:** Testing and replacement of the battery, investigation into parasitic draw.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S is a robust performance vehicle with a generally good reliability record for its class. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to its high-performance components, such as potential wastegate actuator issues on the potent 4.0L V8 engine and occasional software glitches or leaks within the 9-speed automatic transmission. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the COMAND infotainment system and battery drain, are also noted. However, many owners report a satisfying ownership experience with diligent maintenance. Addressing potential issues proactively, especially those related to the powertrain and electronics, is recommended for maintaining optimal performance and longevity.
