# 2016 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S delivers exhilarating performance paired with luxurious appointments, but prospective used-car buyers should navigate its complexities with caution. While federal safety complaints and owner reports highlight a range of potential mechanical and electrical hiccups, informed buyers who prioritize thorough pre-purchase inspections can find a thrilling luxury ride. Understanding both the powertrain options and common trouble spots helps ensure you enjoy the AMG experience without unexpected financial strain.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

The hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine pumps out approximately 503 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque in the AMG S variant. It provides breathtaking acceleration and responsive power delivery, though maintenance and repair costs reflect its high-performance pedigree.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift MCT Automatic

The 7-speed multi-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, aggressive gear changes suited for spirited driving. Regular fluid changes are vital to maintain shift quality and longevity under high-output conditions.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and EPS

Reports indicate occasional electronic power steering failures that can compromise vehicle control and require expensive rack replacements.

**Electric Power Steering Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power steering assistance while driving, making the steering wheel extremely heavy and difficult to turn, creating dangerous traffic situations.
- **Solution:** Replace the defective electronic power steering rack and pinion assembly.
- **Cost:** $5,840.14

## Exterior

### Lighting and Electronics

Advanced electrical systems and complex LED lighting assemblies can be expensive to repair or replace when individual components fail.

**Headlight Assembly Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Warning messages indicating headlight malfunction, requiring the replacement of sealed LED lighting assemblies.
- **Solution:** Replace the entire LCG headlight assembly unit.
- **Cost:** $3,500

### Panoramic Sunroof

The panoramic sunroof assembly is prone to mechanical binding, track wear, and rare instances of spontaneous glass failure.

**Sunroof Binding and Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Panoramic glass panels binding on tracks, failing to close evenly, making loud crunching noises, or spontaneously shattering while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect sunroof tracks, lubricate mechanisms, or replace broken plastic guides and damaged glass assemblies.
