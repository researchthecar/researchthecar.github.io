# 2020 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury performance car, the 2020 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S offers thrilling twin-turbo V8 power wrapped in a stylish compact package. However, prospective buyers should carefully review owner-reported issues spanning electrical glitches, powertrain quirks, and body hardware anomalies. While its performance pedigree is undeniable, keeping up with maintenance and thorough pre-purchase inspections are essential for a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (M177)

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers immense horsepower and torque, providing exceptional straight-line performance. While generally robust, owners have occasionally reported sudden power losses and mechanical hesitation requiring expert service.

**Loss of Motive Power / Cylinder Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience sudden loss of engine power, hesitation from a stop, or internal component wear leading to compromised performance.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnostic evaluation; major internal engine failures may necessitate extensive component or full engine replacement.
- **Cost:** $4,100.43

## Transmission

### 9-Speed AMG Speedshift MCT Automatic

The 9-speed AMG Speedshift multi-clutch automatic transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes tailored for aggressive driving. Smooth everyday operation is typical, though rare electronic or mechanical hiccups can occur.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly drop out of gear while driving at highway speeds, requiring the driver to coast and restart the engine.
- **Solution:** Have transmission control modules and software checked by a qualified technician for updates or diagnostic trouble codes.

## Steering

### Steering System

The responsive steering system provides sharp handling characteristics suited for sports car enthusiasts. Proper wheel alignment and routine checks are vital given the high-performance tire configurations.

**Steering Grinding and Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice abnormal grinding noises when turning the steering wheel or unusual tire wear patterns along the inner shoulders.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering components, alignment settings, and tires regularly to prevent premature wear and safety concerns.

## Electrical

### Sunroof and Trunk Mechanism

The luxury cabin features high-end materials and advanced tech conveniences. Owners should verify that motorized convenience features like power trunks and sunroofs operate smoothly without erratic behavior.

**Abrupt Trunk Closure and Sunroof Glass Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic trunk lid may close unexpectedly without input, and panoramic sunroof glass can occasionally shatter spontaneously.
- **Solution:** Test trunk sensor operations and inspect panoramic glass panels for stress fractures or alignment flaws.

## Exterior

### Roof Panel and Exterior Trim

Exterior styling is aggressive and refined, though certain body panels and trim pieces require attention to ensure they remain securely fastened.

**Detaching Roof Panels**
- **Symptoms:** Exterior glass panels or trim pieces near the roof and windshield may loosen, rattle, or detach entirely while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect exterior roof bonding and trim integrity, and seek dealer remediation if looseness or peeling is detected.

## Safety

### Driver Assistance and Airbag Systems

Advanced safety systems provide peace of mind, though sensitive electronic sensors can occasionally trigger warning lights or require calibration.

**Active Brake Assist Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Warning messages regarding limited active brake assist functions may illuminate on the dashboard during normal driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Clean sensor areas, check camera calibrations, and have diagnostic fault codes scanned by an authorized service center.
