# 2017 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S offers exhilarating performance backed by a hand-built twin-turbo V8 engine, making it a thrilling choice for luxury sport sedan enthusiasts. However, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate potential maintenance costs and known component quirks. While ownership brings exceptional driving dynamics, staying informed about recurring owner complaints regarding structural trim, electrical glitches, and powertrain management can help ensure a more satisfying ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (M177)

Powered by a hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing around 503 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque in S specification, delivering immense performance. This high-output powerplant requires meticulous maintenance and premium fluids, with owners occasionally reporting emissions-related fault codes and fuel system odors.

**Check Engine Light / P052E Fault Code**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of the check engine light, often related to positive crankcase ventilation error codes like P052E, which may recur even after temporary clears.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of affected crankcase ventilation components or associated valves by a qualified technician.

**Fuel Odor or Leak**
- **Symptoms:** A strong gasoline smell inside the cabin or around the exterior of the vehicle during operation or after parking.
- **Solution:** Thorough inspection of fuel lines, connections, and high-pressure components for leaks or loose seals.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift MCT 7-Speed Automatic

Features a wet-clutch 7-speed multi-clutch automatic transmission tailored for rapid gear changes and aggressive power delivery in rear-wheel-drive configurations. While generally robust, buyers should test all shift modes thoroughly during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering

The electric power steering system provides responsive handling but can occasionally experience electronic assist loss, requiring immediate professional evaluation.

**Power Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering assistance accompanied by warning messages on the instrument cluster, making the steering wheel exceptionally heavy and difficult to turn.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scan for electric power steering fault codes and replacement of faulty electronic control modules or steering gear units if necessary.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Exterior Structure

Exterior structural reports focus primarily on panoramic sunroof anomalies, including spontaneous glass breakage or wind-induced detachment.

**Panoramic Sunroof Glass Detachment or Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Loud banging noises at highway speeds followed by the sunroof glass panel detaching or spontaneous shattering of the glass panel without warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the sunroof assembly and inspection of surrounding roof panel seals and bonding.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Issues involving seat belt retractors becoming stuck or failing to pull out properly have been reported by owners, representing a critical safety concern.

**Retractor Jam / Stuck Seat Belt**
- **Symptoms:** Driver or passenger seat belts retract completely and become stuck inside the B-pillar, rendering them completely inoperable.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of the seat belt assembly and retractor mechanism.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz C63 AMG S is an extraordinary performance machine that delivers world-class power and luxury, but it demands careful vetting prior to purchase. Buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the high-performance V8 engine, fuel system integrity, electronic steering assists, and panoramic sunroof condition. Budgeting for specialized maintenance and potential out-of-pocket repairs is essential to fully enjoy this high-end sport sedan.
