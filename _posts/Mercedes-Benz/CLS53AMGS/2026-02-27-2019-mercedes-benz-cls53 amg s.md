# 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLS53 AMG S

### Overview
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLS53 AMG S is a high-performance luxury sedan that generally offers a strong level of reliability, reflecting its premium engineering and build quality. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the CLS53 AMG S has demonstrated a commendable track record for its powertrain and core systems. Owners typically report satisfaction with its robust engine and advanced transmission. However, as with any complex performance vehicle, there are specific areas where potential concerns have been noted, primarily related to the sophisticated electronic systems and some aspects of the exhaust and emissions control, which are common in modern performance cars. Overall, the CLS53 AMG S is considered a reliable choice within its segment, provided regular maintenance is performed.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Inline-6 Turbocharged (M256)
**EQ Boost System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced power assist, intermittent warning lights, rough idling.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of the integrated starter-generator (ISG) or related control modules.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,500

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level warnings.
- **Solution:** Inspection and tightening or replacement of seals and gaskets, such as valve cover or oil pan gaskets.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC Automatic (9-Speed)
**Harsh Shifting / Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, hesitation when shifting into drive or reverse.
- **Solution:** Software updates for the transmission control module, or in more severe cases, internal transmission repairs or fluid service.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1,200


## Suspension

### AIR BODY CONTROL (Adaptive Air Suspension)
**Air Suspension Leaks / Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, constant compressor running, suspension warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking air struts, lines, or the air compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,500 per corner/component


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (COMAND/MBUX)
**Screen Freezing / Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen unresponsive, display errors, system reboots.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard resets, or potential replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLS53 AMG S is a compelling performance luxury sedan with a generally positive reliability outlook. The sophisticated 3.0L inline-six engine and 9-speed automatic transmission are robust, though potential issues with the EQ Boost system and occasional transmission shifting anomalies have been noted. The AIR BODY CONTROL suspension, while providing excellent ride comfort, can be a source of costly repairs if leaks or compressor failures occur. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and battery drain, are not uncommon in vehicles with such advanced technology. Despite these points, the CLS53 AMG S offers a reliable ownership experience for those who prioritize performance and luxury, with most issues being manageable through proper maintenance and timely repairs.
