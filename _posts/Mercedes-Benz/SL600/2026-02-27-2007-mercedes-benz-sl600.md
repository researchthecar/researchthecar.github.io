# 2007 Mercedes-Benz SL600

### Overview
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz SL600, a high-performance luxury roadster, is equipped with a potent twin-turbocharged V12 engine. While offering exhilarating performance, its complexity and advanced technology can lead to specific reliability concerns. Owners often report issues related to the active suspension system, the complex electronic systems, and the powerful V12 engine's ancillary components. Maintenance and repair costs can be substantial due to the specialized nature of the vehicle and the high cost of parts. Despite these potential drawbacks, many owners find the driving experience and luxury appointments justify the investment in upkeep.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V12 (M275)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light, reduced power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800 per coil, plus labor

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of turbochargers and associated lines.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000 per turbo, plus labor

**Coolant Leaks (ABC System)**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant level, visible leaks, engine overheating, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hoses, seals, or the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $3,000


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (722.6)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, failure to shift gears, 'limp mode'.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**Conductor Plate Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, transmission slipping, error codes related to gear selection.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the conductor plate and associated sensors.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,200


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, 'ABC Malfunction' warning on the dashboard, stiff ride.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic lines, struts, or the ABC pump.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $4,000 per component, depending on severity

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of hydraulic pressure, vehicle sagging, loud whining noise from the pump.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**COMAND System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive buttons, navigation issues, audio problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the COMAND unit.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $3,000


### Sensors
**Various Sensor Failures (e.g., ABS, Parking Assist)**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated warning lights (ABS, ESP, Parking Assist), system malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty sensors.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600 per sensor, plus labor


## Exterior

### Retractable Hardtop Mechanism
**Hydraulic Pump/Cylinder Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Slow or failed operation of the hardtop, fluid leaks in the trunk area.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic components for the roof.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000

**Microswitches and Sensors**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent or complete failure of the hardtop to operate, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty microswitches or sensors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Interior

### Seat Control Modules
**Malfunctioning Seat Adjustments**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not moving, memory functions failing, heating/cooling issues.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of seat control modules or motors.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


### Climate Control System
**Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Mercedes-Benz SL600 is a complex luxury vehicle where reliability is often tied to meticulous maintenance and the potential for costly repairs, particularly concerning its V12 engine, sophisticated ABC suspension, and intricate retractable hardtop. While the core powertrain is robust, ancillary systems and electronics are prone to issues that require specialized attention. Owners should budget for higher-than-average maintenance and repair costs. Despite these challenges, for those who prioritize performance and luxury, the SL600 can still be a rewarding ownership experience if properly cared for.
