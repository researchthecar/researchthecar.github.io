**Introduction**

The 2005 Mercedes-Benz SL600 is a grand tourer that offers a luxurious driving experience and impressive performance. It is powered by a 5.5-liter V12 engine that produces 493 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. The SL600 is known for its spacious interior, advanced technology features, and smooth handling. However, like most vehicles, it has its fair share of issues that potential buyers should be aware of.

**Engine**

**5.5L V12**

* **Leaking valve cover gaskets**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the top of the engine
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake manifold runner control failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Decreased engine power, rough idling
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold runner control actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, loss of electrical power
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Replace or charge battery, check for parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Window regulator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power window does not work
    * Solution: Replace window regulator motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Convertible top leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin when the top is closed
    * Solution: Replace or repair convertible top seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation system freezing**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation screen becomes unresponsive or freezes
    * Solution: Update or replace navigation software
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cruise control malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cruise control does not work or engages randomly
    * Solution: Replace cruise control switch or module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2005 Mercedes-Benz SL600 is a luxurious and powerful vehicle, but it is not without its issues. Engine and transmission problems can be costly to fix, and electrical and interior issues can be frustrating. However, with proper maintenance and repairs, the SL600 can provide many years of enjoyable driving.
