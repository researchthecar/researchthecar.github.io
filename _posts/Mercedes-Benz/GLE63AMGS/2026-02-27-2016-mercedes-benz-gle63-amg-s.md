# 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S delivers high-performance luxury with robust power output, but prospective buyers should carefully weigh potential maintenance and reliability concerns. Owner feedback highlights occasional mechanical and electrical issues, emphasizing the importance of a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 5.5L Bi-Turbo V8 Engine

Equipped with a potent 5.5-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing around 577 horsepower, delivering exhilarating acceleration. While powerful, high-performance engines demand rigorous maintenance and can incur steep repair bills if components like fuel injectors or pumps fail.

**Fuel System and Stalling Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may experience unexpected engine stalling, fuel leaks, or starting failures.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines, pump, and injectors for leaks or failure; replace faulty components.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift Automatic Transmission

Features a responsive 7-speed automatic transmission tailored for all-wheel-drive performance. Proper fluid changes and software updates are vital for longevity, as shifting anomalies can occasionally surface.

**Unexpected Shifting and Powertrain Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may unexpectedly shift into neutral or park while operating, or exhibit jerky lurches and hesitation.
- **Solution:** Diagnose transmission control module and software calibration; inspect mechanical linkages and output bearings.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

**Power Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering assistance accompanied by dashboard warning messages, making the steering wheel exceptionally heavy and difficult to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering gear, electronic power assist modules, and associated wiring harnesses for faults.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

**Dim Headlights**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced nighttime visibility due to dim headlight beam projection.
- **Solution:** Check headlight bulbs, projector housings, and alignment settings.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S offers exceptional performance and luxury, but its complex engineering requires careful attention.
