# 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S is a high-performance luxury SUV that blends potent V8 power with advanced technology and premium comfort. As a top-tier AMG model, it is engineered for exhilarating driving dynamics and sophisticated refinement. Reliability for such a performance-oriented vehicle often hinges on proper maintenance and the inherent complexity of its systems. While generally well-regarded for its build quality and performance, like any complex machine, it can experience specific issues, particularly related to its high-output powertrain and advanced electronic features. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (M177)
**Turbocharger Lag/Boost Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced acceleration, intermittent power loss, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential turbocharger repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $6,000

**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant smell, visible coolant leaks.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair/replacement of hoses, radiator, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


## Transmission

### AMG SPEEDSHIFT TCT 9-Speed Automatic
**Harsh Shifting/Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, difficulty engaging gears, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter change, software update, or internal transmission repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $2,000


## Suspension

### AMG ACTIVE RIDE CONTROL
**Air Suspension Leaks/Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, uneven ride height, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Leak detection and repair of air springs, lines, or compressor replacement.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**MBUX Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Screen unresponsive, system crashes, connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system reset, or replacement of control unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1,000


### 12V Battery
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, electrical system malfunctions, battery warning light.
- **Solution:** Battery testing and replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S demonstrates strong performance and luxury, with most reported issues concentrated in its high-performance powertrain components and advanced electronic systems. Potential concerns include turbocharger performance, cooling system integrity, and occasional glitches with the MBUX infotainment. The transmission and suspension systems, while complex, can also present issues like harsh shifting or air suspension leaks. Electrical components, particularly the 12V battery, may require attention sooner than expected. Overall, while not immune to problems, the GLE63 AMG S offers a generally reliable experience when meticulously maintained, with repair costs reflecting its premium and performance-oriented nature.
