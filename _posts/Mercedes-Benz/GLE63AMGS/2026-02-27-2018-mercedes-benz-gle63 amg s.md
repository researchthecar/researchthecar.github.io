# 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S is a high-performance luxury SUV that combines potent V8 power with the practicality of an SUV. While generally well-regarded for its performance and premium features, like any complex vehicle, it can experience specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and prospective buyers. The focus is on components that have shown a propensity for failure or require significant attention.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased exhaust smoke, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of affected turbocharger(s).
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, sweet smell from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hoses, gaskets, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT PLUS 7G-TRONIC
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning light, limp mode.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and reprogramming or replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping gears, rough engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Suspension

### Active Curve System (Optional)
**Hydraulic Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Increased body roll, warning lights related to suspension, unusual noises from the suspension.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the hydraulic pump and potentially associated lines.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or seals.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** COMAND display freezes, touch functions do not work, system reboots randomly.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000


### Battery Drain
_No major issues reported for this configuration._

## Exterior

### LED Lighting
**Headlight Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture or fogging inside headlight assemblies.
- **Solution:** Sealing of headlight housing or replacement of assembly if severe.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S offers exhilarating performance, but potential owners should be aware of specific reliability concerns. The 5.5L twin-turbo V8, while powerful, can experience issues with turbochargers and coolant leaks. The AMG SPEEDSHIFT transmission may encounter problems with its control module or torque converter. For models equipped with optional advanced suspension systems like the Active Curve System or AIRMATIC, hydraulic pump failures and air spring leaks are documented concerns. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and battery drain, can also arise. While the interior and exterior generally hold up well, minor issues like headlight condensation can occur. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining this high-performance SUV.
