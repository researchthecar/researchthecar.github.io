# 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S represents the pinnacle of luxury performance SUVs, offering immense power paired with high-end cabin appointments and advanced technology. While buyers primarily seek this vehicle for its thrilling driving dynamics and prestige, prospective owners should review common owner-reported concerns ranging from engine drivability and electrical glitches to unexpected exterior glass failures. As with many high-performance luxury vehicles, thorough inspection records and careful vetting are recommended prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8

The hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces an impressive 603 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration and robust capability. It features sophisticated internal engineering designed for track-ready performance while serving daily driving duties.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift TCT 9-Speed Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission is specifically tuned by AMG for lightning-fast gear changes and smooth power delivery across all driving modes. It manages the substantial output of the V8 engine efficiently while providing relaxed highway cruising.

## Suspension

### Tires and Wheels

The performance-oriented suspension and wide, low-profile tires provide exceptional grip but can be prone to accelerated wear depending on driving habits and road conditions.

**Premature Tire Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rear or front tires wearing down to the cords or bald unusually quickly within low mileage intervals.
- **Solution:** Inspect tire tread depth regularly and replace tires with high-quality replacements; ensure proper wheel alignment is maintained.
- **Cost:** $950

## Electrical

### Electrical and Climate Systems

Advanced onboard electronics and convenience features generally operate smoothly, though minor cabin drainage and locking quirks have been documented by owners.

**Water Intrusion and Drainage Clogs**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaking into the cabin from air conditioning vent hoses or roof drains, potentially causing electrical system faults.
- **Solution:** Check AC condensate lines and sunroof drains for blockages or improper past repairs.

**Inadvertent Door Locking**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle doors automatically lock with the key fob left inside the cabin, risking child or pet lockouts.
- **Solution:** Exercise caution when placing keys inside the vehicle and keep spare keys accessible.

### Driver Assistance and Visibility

Active safety systems offer high-tech protection but rely heavily on clean sensors and up-to-date software calibrations.

**Unintended Deceleration**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences sudden unexpected slowing or braking while cruise control and lane assist features are active on the highway.
- **Solution:** Have dealer software calibrations and radar/camera sensors inspected for proper alignment and updates.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior glass components, particularly the large panoramic sunroof panels, require careful inspection for pre-existing stress or damage.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof panel unexpectedly explodes or shatters outward while driving under normal road conditions, producing a loud noise resembling a gunshot.
- **Solution:** Have the glass assembly inspected for stress fractures, and replace the sunroof glass if compromised.
