**Introduction:**

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S is a high-performance SUV powered by a twin-turbocharged 5.5-liter V8 engine that produces 577 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a 9-speed automatic transmission and 4MATIC all-wheel drive. The GLE63 AMG S offers a luxurious interior with advanced technology features, as well as exceptional handling and performance. However, some owners have reported experiencing certain issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure (All Engines)**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbochargers may fail, leading to a loss of power, increased fuel consumption, and excessive smoke from the exhaust.
    * Solution: Replace the affected turbochargers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Valve Issues (All Engines)**
    * Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold valves may become clogged or stuck, causing rough idling, decreased engine performance, and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Clean or replace the intake manifold valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **9-Speed Automatic Transmission Gearbox Problems (All Transmissions)**
    * Description/Symptoms: The 9-speed automatic transmission may experience shifting issues, hesitations, or shuddering during acceleration.
    * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the gearbox.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure (All Suspensions)**
    * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension compressor may fail, leading to a loss of ride height, uneven suspension levels, and reduced handling.
    * Solution: Replace the air suspension compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge (All Models)**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may discharge excessively, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle or complete electrical failure.
    * Solution: Diagnose and fix the underlying electrical drain, which may involve replacing faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation (All Models)**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture may accumulate inside the headlights, causing fogging or condensation.
    * Solution: Replace the affected headlights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Issues (All Models)**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience lag, freezing, or display errors.
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof Rattling (All Models)**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may rattle or make noises when opened or closed.
    * Solution: Adjust the sunroof alignment or replace the sunroof motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2018 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S is a powerful and luxurious SUV. However, some owners have reported encountering certain issues, including turbocharger failure, intake manifold valve issues, transmission gearbox problems, air suspension compressor failure, battery discharge, headlight condensation, infotainment system issues, and sunroof rattling. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, and it's important to have them addressed promptly to ensure the optimal performance and reliability of the vehicle.
