# 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S

### Overview
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S is a high-performance luxury SUV that combines potent V8 power with the practicality of an SUV. While generally well-regarded for its performance and premium features, like any complex vehicle, it can experience specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and prospective buyers. The focus is on components that have shown a tendency for premature failure or significant repair needs.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased exhaust smoke, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of affected turbocharger(s).
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, sweet smell from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hoses, gaskets, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift Plus Automatic
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Transmission Control Module.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,000

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping gears, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC) System
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, bouncy ride, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hydraulic lines, struts, or pump.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $4,000+


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen unresponsive, system reboots randomly, display errors.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment head unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or circuits causing the drain.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S, while a formidable performance SUV, presents potential reliability concerns primarily in its complex powertrain and advanced suspension systems. Owners may encounter issues with the twin-turbo V8 engine's turbochargers and cooling system, as well as the automatic transmission's control module and torque converter. The Active Body Control suspension is also a notable area for potential hydraulic leaks. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and parasitic battery drain, can also arise. While the exterior and interior generally hold up well, the mechanical and electrical complexities necessitate diligent maintenance and budgeting for potential repairs, especially as the vehicle ages.
