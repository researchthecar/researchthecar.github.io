# 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE 63 AMG S delivers formidable performance backed by a hand-built twin-turbocharged V8 engine and sophisticated luxury appointments. However, prospective buyers should approach used models with attention to potential electrical, steering, and body integrity issues reported by owners. While high-performance luxury SUVs offer an engaging driving experience, mechanical and electronic complexity means maintenance and repair costs can be substantial.

---

## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Engine (AMG)

The GLE 63 AMG S is powered by a robust 5.5-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine generating over 550 horsepower, paired with AMG-tuned performance parts. It provides relentless acceleration but requires diligent maintenance and premium fuel to ensure long-term reliability.

**Engine Stalling and Fuel System Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected engine stalling and hesitation, occasionally requiring towing or fuel pump inspection.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic evaluation of the fuel delivery system and replacement of faulty fuel pumps or sensors if required.

## Transmission

### AMG Speedshift Plus 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 7-speed AMG Speedshift automatic transmission featuring steering wheel paddle shifters and multiple drive modes. It handles high torque loads effectively, though smooth operation depends heavily on timely fluid changes.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

While high-performance brakes provide immense stopping power, proper pad and rotor maintenance is critical for safety.

**Brake System Hesitation and Stopping Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal hesitation, unintended vehicle movement while the hold brake feature is engaged, or reduced stopping confidence.
- **Solution:** Thoroughly inspect brake boosters, calipers, pads, and ABS control modules.

## Steering

### Electronic Power Steering

Issues related to electronic power assist can occasionally disable steering feedback without prior warning, posing safety concerns.

**Power Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience sudden loss of power steering accompanied by an instrument cluster warning message, making the steering wheel extremely stiff and difficult to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Inspect electronic power steering modules, wiring harnesses, and steering rack components for failure.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical system concerns primarily center around exterior lighting performance and digital display reliability.

**Headlight Failure and Low Beam Adjustment**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced road illumination during night driving and rain, or low beam headlights failing to adjust properly.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight assemblies, bulbs, leveling sensors, and control modules.

### Sunroof and Glass

Body and exterior complaints highlight rare instances of spontaneous panoramic sunroof glass breakage.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Loud explosive sound followed by the panoramic glass roof shattering unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect surrounding tracks for structural stress or alignment defects.
