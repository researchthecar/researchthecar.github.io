# 2023 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a luxury performance SUV, prospective buyers of this model will find impressive power paired with advanced technology. However, reviewing owner feedback highlights a few notable electronic, powertrain, and driver-assist quirks that are worth keeping in mind during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0L twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers immense horsepower and torque, providing exceptional acceleration for a luxury performance vehicle. Overall, the core engine architecture is robust, though owners should maintain a strict service schedule to keep the high-output turbochargers running smoothly.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission is engineered to handle high torque outputs while offering smooth shifts across various driving modes. While generally dependable, electronic communication glitches can occasionally lead to unexpected shifting behavior.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may intermittently drop into neutral while stopped or driving, failing to accelerate when the driver presses the gas pedal until restarted.
- **Solution:** Software updates or transmission control module inspection by a certified technician.

## Electrical

### Electrical System & Battery

Complex onboard electronics and hybrid auxiliary systems require thorough diagnostic checks during routine maintenance.

**Battery Charge Low Warnings and Electrical Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may receive sudden low charge warnings for the 12V or hybrid battery systems, occasionally leading to instrument cluster blackouts or vehicle stalling.
- **Solution:** Inspect the 48V and 12V battery charging systems, update software, or replace failing modules.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Glass

Exterior glass and trim require careful inspection for stress cracks or sealing issues.

**Glass Cracking and Roof Panel Shivering**
- **Symptoms:** Windshields or panoramic roof glass panels may experience unexpected cracking or shattering without direct impact from road debris.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged glass panels and inspect mounting frames for structural stress points.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced driver-assistance systems add an extra layer of protection, but sensor sensitivity can occasionally cause erratic behaviors.

**Automatic Emergency Braking Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic emergency braking system may experience unexpected activation or fail to provide warnings and braking assistance when approaching obstacles.
- **Solution:** Have dealership personnel check sensor calibration, software versions, and radar alignment.
