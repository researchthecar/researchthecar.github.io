**Introduction:**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S is a high-performance SUV powered by a twin-turbocharged 5.5-liter V8 engine that generates 577 horsepower and 560 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a 7-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The GLE63 AMG S offers a luxurious and spacious interior, along with impressive agility and handling for its size.

Despite its impressive performance and features, the GLE63 AMG S has been plagued by a number of issues, some of which can be costly to repair.

**Engine**

**5.5-Liter V8**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, decreased performance
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, reduced power, Check Engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shudder during acceleration, especially at low speeds
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Control Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, gear hunting, Check Engine light
    * Solution: Replace transmission control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, reduced ride height
    * Solution: Replace leaking air suspension strut or hose
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+ per corner
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Identify and repair or replace faulty electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Navigation System Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system freezing, inaccurate directions
    * Solution: Update or replace navigation software
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside the headlight lenses
    * Solution: Replace headlight lenses
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per headlight
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or noise coming from the sunroof area
    * Solution: Adjust or replace sunroof components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Bugs:**
    * Description/Symptoms: System freezing, unresponsive touch screen, Bluetooth connectivity issues
    * Solution: Update or replace software, hardware repairs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S is a capable and luxurious SUV, but it has been plagued by a number of issues that can be expensive to repair. The most serious problems affect the engine, transmission, and suspension. However, electrical, exterior, and interior issues can also be costly.

Overall, the severity of the issues and their potential costs should be considered when evaluating this vehicle.
