# 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S offers formidable performance paired with luxury and advanced technology. While its powertrain delivers high-output performance, prospective buyers should review ownership records carefully for recurring electrical, body, and safety complaints noted by drivers.

---

## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Engine

The 5.5-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 577 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally rapid acceleration. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning indicators are essential for long-term reliability.

**Check Engine Warning & Oil Pump Solenoid Codes**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported intermittent check engine warning lights illuminating on the dashboard, often accompanied by diagnostic trouble codes such as P06DA00 relating to the oil pump solenoid and associated wiring harnesses.
- **Solution:** Inspect the oil pump solenoid and engine wiring harness, and replace damaged electrical components as needed.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG Speedshift Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, responsive shifts tailored to high-performance driving. It generally operates reliably when fluid changes are performed on schedule.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof and Trim

Exterior body components and trim require routine inspection, particularly around large glass roof assemblies.

**Sunroof Panel Detachment and Glass Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced loud banging noises while driving at highway speeds, followed by exterior trim pieces or roof panels detaching, or in severe cases, the panoramic glass shattering unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof bonding adhesive and rail mechanism, and have damaged glass panels or failing adhesive replaced immediately.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety restraint systems are critical for occupant protection, and any warning lights should be diagnosed promptly.

**Seat Belt Pretensioner Fault Codes**
- **Symptoms:** The seat belt warning light illuminates on the dashboard even when occupants are securely buckled, and diagnostic scans reveal hard fault codes set by the front seat belt powered pretensioners.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan the vehicle's computer system and replace faulty seat belt pretensioner assemblies or wiring connectors.

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S is an immensely powerful luxury performance SUV that demands careful vetting before purchase. Buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle for proper operation of the panoramic sunroof, check for active diagnostic trouble codes related to the engine and wiring, and verify that all safety systems function correctly.
