**Introduction to the 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S is a high-performance SUV powered by a 5.5-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 577 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque. Paired with a seven-speed automatic transmission, it offers a thrilling driving experience with impressive acceleration and handling. Owners appreciate its luxurious interior, comfortable ride, and aggressive styling. However, it has received some criticism for its high fuel consumption and expensive maintenance costs.

**Engine**

**5.5L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Underboost**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power and sluggish acceleration due to insufficient boost pressure.
    * Solution: Inspect and replace faulty boost solenoids or wastegate actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Flap Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Stalling, rough idling, and reduced engine performance due to faulty intake manifold flaps.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Seven-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Hesitations**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or hesitant shifting, especially during acceleration or downshifts.
    * Solution: Reprogram the transmission control unit (TCU) or replace faulty solenoid valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Shudder**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations felt during acceleration or low-speed driving due to a faulty torque converter.
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Uneven ride height, excessive body roll, and loss of stability due to faulty air suspension components.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace faulty air springs, struts, or compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge when the vehicle is parked due to parasitic current loss.
    * Solution: Identify and eliminate the source of the current draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical failures affecting various systems, such as lighting, sound, and dashboard controls.
    * Solution: Troubleshoot and repair faulty wiring, connections, or modules.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Rattling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises or vibrations coming from the panoramic sunroof while driving.
    * Solution: Adjust or replace sunroof alignment or hardware.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inaccurate navigation data, freezing, or unresponsive touchscreen.
    * Solution: Update software or replace navigation head unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning heating or air conditioning system, leading to discomfort or inconsistent temperatures.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty components, such as the compressor, thermostat, or actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz GLE63 AMG S is a high-performance SUV with thrilling dynamics and a luxurious interior. However, it has experienced some issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. These issues can be costly to repair, ranging from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before purchasing this vehicle.
