**Introduction**

The 2014 Mercedes-Benz CL63 AMG is a high-performance grand tourer powered by a twin-turbocharged 5.5-liter V8 engine. It produces 557 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque and is paired with a seven-speed automatic transmission. The CL63 AMG is praised for its thrilling performance, luxurious interior, and advanced technology features. However, like any vehicle, it has some issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: The turbochargers can fail, causing a loss of power and whistling noises.
  * Solution: Replace the turbochargers.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Transmission Jerking**
  * Description/Symptoms: The transmission can jerk or hesitate during shifts.
  * Solution: Update the transmission software.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free under warranty
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Malfunction**
  * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system can fail, causing the vehicle to sag or have uneven ride height.
  * Solution: Replace air struts or air compressor.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: The strut mount bearings can wear out, causing clunking noises when turning or hitting bumps.
  * Solution: Replace strut mount bearings.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
  * Description/Symptoms: The battery can discharge quickly, leading to starting problems.
  * Solution: Test battery and electrical system; replace battery if necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical Short**
  * Description/Symptoms: A short in the electrical system can cause various electrical failures, such as malfunctioning lights or power windows.
  * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical short.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Front Bumper Clips Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: The plastic clips that hold the front bumper in place can break, causing the bumper to come loose.
  * Solution: Replace front bumper clips.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Door Handle Malfunction**
  * Description/Symptoms: The door handles can break or malfunction, making it difficult to open the doors.
  * Solution: Replace door handles.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
  * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system can malfunction, causing the AC or heat to not work properly.
  * Solution: Diagnose and repair climate control system.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment System Screen Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system screen can go black or become unresponsive.
  * Solution: Replace infotainment system screen.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2014 Mercedes-Benz CL63 AMG is a highly capable vehicle, but it is not without its issues. The most common problems reported by owners are related to the engine, transmission, and suspension. These issues can be costly to fix, so it's important to factor in potential repair costs when considering purchasing this vehicle.
