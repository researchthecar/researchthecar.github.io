# 2013 Mercedes-Benz CL63 AMG

### Overview
The 2013 Mercedes-Benz CL63 AMG, a high-performance grand tourer, is known for its potent V8 engine and luxurious appointments. While generally well-regarded for its performance and comfort, like any complex luxury vehicle, it can experience specific issues as it ages. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and enthusiasts. The CL63 AMG's advanced systems, while offering exceptional driving dynamics, can sometimes present unique repair challenges.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L Twin-Turbo V8 (M157)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, boost pressure issues, rattling noise from turbo area.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the wastegate actuator or the complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500 per actuator

**Coolant Leaks (Intercooler System)**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant level, overheating, visible coolant residue around intercooler pipes.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking intercooler hoses, seals, or the intercooler itself.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Transmission

### 7-Speed AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, rough shifting, slipping gears, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, failure to engage gears.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and reprogramming or replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Suspension

### Active Body Control (ABC) Suspension
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, 'ABC Malfunction' warning on the dashboard, fluid leaks under the car.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking ABC struts, hoses, or pump.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $3,000 per component

**ABC Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of hydraulic pressure, vehicle sagging, loud whining noise from the pump.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ABC hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


## Electrical

### Command System (Infotainment)
**Navigation System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Freezing screen, unresponsive controls, inaccurate GPS.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,000


### Keyless Go System
**Door Handle Sensor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to unlock or lock doors by touching the handle.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty door handle sensors.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500 per handle

### Summary Verdict
The 2013 Mercedes-Benz CL63 AMG offers exhilarating performance but comes with potential reliability concerns, particularly in its advanced systems. The twin-turbo V8 engine can experience wastegate actuator failures and intercooler leaks. The AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT transmission may develop torque converter issues or TCM malfunctions. The sophisticated Active Body Control (ABC) suspension is prone to hydraulic leaks and pump failures, which can be costly to repair. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and keyless go sensor issues, are also documented. While exterior and interior components are generally robust, owners should be prepared for significant maintenance costs associated with these high-performance and complex systems.
