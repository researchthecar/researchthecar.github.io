## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Intermittent Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard warning lights may illuminate or flicker without apparent cause, potentially affecting various systems.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scan to identify fault codes, check wiring harnesses, and replace faulty sensors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $250 - $1000+

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Premature battery discharge, difficulty starting the vehicle, especially after periods of inactivity.
- **Solution:** Systematic check for parasitic draws, testing of alternator and battery health, replacement of faulty components.
- **Cost:** $150 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Mercedes-Benz MBO 1221 demonstrates a high level of reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. While the engines and manual transmissions are exceptionally robust, the optional air suspension and the general electrical system present the most common, albeit infrequent, areas for potential concern. Issues like air spring leaks and intermittent electrical gremlins are not systemic but can occur. Overall, with diligent maintenance and prompt attention to any emerging electrical or suspension anomalies, the MBO 1221 is a dependable workhorse built for longevity and consistent performance in commercial applications.
