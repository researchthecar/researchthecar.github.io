# 2017 Mercedes-Benz S600L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S600L represents the pinnacle of luxury, combining stately road presence with an ultra-smooth V12 powerplant. While flagship luxury sedans of this caliber offer peerless comfort and advanced technology, prospective buyers should approach with an awareness that maintenance, electronic complexity, and specialized repairs can command significant out-of-pocket costs.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L Biturbo V12

Equipped with a 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine producing around 523 horsepower and 612 lb-ft of torque, this flagship powerplant delivers effortless acceleration. Routine maintenance is critical, and upkeep costs tend to be notably higher than standard V8 models.

## Transmission

### 7G-Tronic Plus 7-Speed Automatic

Paired exclusively with rear-wheel drive, the 7-speed automatic transmission is designed for seamless, whisper-quiet gear shifts. Regular fluid changes are essential to maintain long-term reliability in high-torque applications.

**Automatic Shift to Neutral Upon Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** When pressing the ignition button to turn off the vehicle while in drive with the foot on the brake, the transmission may shift into neutral instead of park, potentially leading to vehicle roll if the door is not opened.
- **Solution:** Have the gear selector module and transmission software evaluated by a qualified technician or dealership.

## Steering

### Steering Column Adjustment

Power-adjustable steering columns offer refined convenience but rely on small electric actuators that can occasionally experience mechanical binding.

**Power Steering Column Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel adjusts in and out normally, but fails to move up and down, becoming stuck in the uppermost position and obstructing the view of the instrument cluster.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the steering column adjustment motor or tilt mechanism as needed.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Glass

Large glass panoramic roofs provide an airy cabin feel but demand periodic track cleaning and lubrication to prevent alignment issues.

**Panoramic Sunroof Binding**
- **Symptoms:** Occupants may hear a cracking sound while closing the sunroof, followed by the glass panel becoming stuck or failing to close properly.
- **Solution:** Lubricate track mechanisms, or replace broken guide rails and sunroof cables.

## Safety

### Dashboard Trim and Airbag Surround

Exquisite leather and wood trim define the cabin, though premium materials require careful climate management to prevent adhesive failure over time.

**Leather Dashboard Separation**
- **Symptoms:** The leather wrapping on the dashboard begins to pull away or separate near the passenger-side airbag deployment zone, potentially exposing internal components.
- **Solution:** Requires professional re-gluing or complete dashboard replacement, which can involve substantial out-of-pocket expense.
- **Cost:** $3,000 to $6,000

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz S600L offers extraordinary luxury, but prospective owners should inspect interior trim adhesion, steering column adjustments, and sunroof operation closely prior to purchase. Setting aside a robust maintenance reserve is strongly recommended to handle specialized repairs on this flagship sedan.
