# 2020 Mercedes-Benz S600L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a luxury flagship like the 2020 Mercedes-Benz S600L, buyers expect supreme engineering, effortless performance, and refined comfort. While official owner complaint volume remains relatively low for this niche model, prospective buyers should review available vehicle history and inspect powertrain and exterior glass elements carefully during a pre-purchase inspection. Note that NHTSA complaints represent individual owner experiences rather than inherent manufacturing defects.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L V12 Biturbo

The 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine delivers immense horsepower and torque, offering exceptionally smooth and effortless acceleration. It is a highly complex engineering marvel that requires meticulous maintenance history to ensure long-term reliability.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may suddenly stall or cut off completely on the road without prior warning, making it difficult to restart.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the fuel pump and electrical delivery system, replacing faulty pump components as necessary.

**Check Engine Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of the check engine light on the dashboard, which may recur shortly after being cleared by a diagnostic scanner.
- **Solution:** Perform a comprehensive diagnostic scan to identify underlying sensor or powertrain fault codes and replace malfunctioning components.

### Vehicle Speed Control

Issues related to throttle and speed control are infrequent but require immediate diagnostic attention if experienced.

**Unintended Speed Increase**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent, unexpected acceleration of the vehicle by up to 35 mph without driver input.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic throttle body, accelerator pedal sensor, and engine management software for faults.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides seamless gear changes tailored to luxury cruising and power delivery. It generally operates reliably when serviced according to factory maintenance schedules.

## Exterior

### Glass and Sunroof

Exterior glass components, including large panoramic sunroofs and acoustic windshields, should be thoroughly inspected for chips or stress fractures prior to purchase.

**Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Small initial impacts or stress points can rapidly develop into long cracks stretching across the windshield.
- **Solution:** Inspect the windshield for minor pitting or chips and replace the glass promptly if structural cracks form.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Loud popping sounds followed by the sudden shattering or explosion of the sunroof glass while driving or parked.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass assembly and frame alignment for any signs of stress or damage, and replace shattered glass.
