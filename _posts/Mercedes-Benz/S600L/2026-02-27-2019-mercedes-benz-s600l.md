# 2019 Mercedes-Benz S600L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz S-Class represents the pinnacle of luxury, but prospective buyers should review owner-reported feedback carefully. While powertrain options generally deliver sophisticated performance befitting a flagship sedan, isolated complaints highlight various electrical, brake, and steering concerns.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Biturbo V8

This twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces robust horsepower and torque, offering exceptionally smooth power delivery. It is generally robust, though proper maintenance is critical for long-term luxury reliability.

### 6.0L Biturbo V12

The ultra-smooth V12 engine delivers immense power and effortless acceleration suited for top-tier executive cruising. Maintenance can be complex and costly given its intricate plumbing and cylinder count.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic

This nine-speed automatic transmission provides seamless gear changes and optimizes highway efficiency. It pairs smoothly with available engines, though buyers should verify smooth shifting during test drives.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes and Actuator

Braking components provide strong stopping power for a heavy luxury vehicle, but individual hydraulic or actuator failures require prompt attention.

**Rear Brake Actuator Seizure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances where the rear brake actuator seized and became completely inoperable, requiring towing and professional service.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the faulty brake actuator assembly as diagnosed by a qualified technician.

**Soft Brake Pedal Feel**
- **Symptoms:** Some drivers experience brakes that do not hold well initially, occasionally requiring a double pump to successfully stop the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Bleed the brake system and check for hydraulic leaks or master cylinder wear.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Shifter

Steering systems offer precise control, though mechanical adjustments and column-mounted selectors can experience occasional glitches.

**Steering Column Adjustment Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** The steering column adjustment mechanism can stick in the full upright position, preventing easy readjustment.
- **Solution:** Lubricate or replace the steering column adjustment motor and track components.

## Interior

### Cabin Electronics and Keyless Entry

Extensive electronics drive the S-Class experience, making software updates and thorough pre-purchase electrical checks essential.

**Keyless Entry Auto-Locking Issue**
- **Symptoms:** Keys left inside the vehicle can prompt the system to unexpectedly lock the doors from the inside, potentially trapping occupants or pets.
- **Solution:** Reprogram or update vehicle keyless entry software modules through an authorized service center.

## Safety

### Seat Belts and Airbags

Advanced safety systems offer high-tech protection, but complex sensor integration can occasionally lead to erratic seat belt or airbag behavior.

**Seat Belt Locking and Pretensioner Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted seat belts unexpectedly tightening during normal driving or rear seat belts locking in place and failing to retract.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt sensors, pretensioners, and retractors, replacing faulty assemblies as needed.
