# 2016 Mercedes-Benz S600L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S600L represents the pinnacle of luxury, performance, and flagship engineering. While these executive sedans offer an exceptionally smooth ride and immense V12 power, used-car buyers should approach ownership with an understanding of complex electronics, high-end suspension upkeep, and potential body or safety component quirks.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L V12 Biturbo Engine

Equipped with a robust 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine producing approximately 523 horsepower and 612 lb-ft of torque, this flagship powerplant delivers effortless acceleration. Routine maintenance is critical, as specialized servicing and component replacement costs can be substantial.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Mated to the V12 powerplant is a smooth-shifting 7-speed automatic transmission designed to deliver seamless power delivery to the rear wheels. Regular fluid changes are essential to maintain shift quality and long-term mechanical durability.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The power steering system provides effortless maneuvering at low speeds and firm feedback on the highway, though occasional electronic or assist failures have been reported.

**Power Steering Failure and Lockout**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a sudden illumination of the power steering warning light accompanied by heavy steering resistance or temporary steering lockout while driving.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic steering system and related control modules inspected by a qualified technician to diagnose root electrical or mechanical faults.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Glass Roof

Exterior glass and panoramic roof components provide an airy cabin feel, though rare instances of spontaneous thermal or stress cracking have been documented.

**Spontaneous Panoramic Sunroof Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof or sunroof panel may develop sudden cracks or shatter while driving or while parked, accompanied by a loud pop.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof frame for alignment or body torque issues, and replace the shattered glass panel with OEM components.

### Trunk Latch Mechanism

Interior convenience features and power amenities are exceptionally advanced, though occasional sensor or actuator glitches can arise in cold climates.

**Trunk Lid Rebound in Cold Weather**
- **Symptoms:** In cold temperatures below 40 degrees, the power trunk lid may close when commanded by the button only to immediately reopen on its own.
- **Solution:** Inspect the trunk latch actuator and electronic sensors, and recalibrate or replace the latch mechanism if it fails to lock securely.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Occupant safety systems are generally durable, but isolated failures involving belt buckles and retractors require immediate attention.

**Seat Belt Buckle and Retractor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Front passenger seat belt buckles may fail to latch securely, or rear seat belts can become stuck and fail to retract properly.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty seat belt buckle assemblies or malfunctioning retractors as necessary.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz S600L offers unmatched luxury, prestige, and effortless V12 performance. While its mechanical core is robust, prospective buyers should carefully inspect advanced electronics, steering assist systems, panoramic glass panels, and cabin convenience features. Due to the high cost of specialized repairs, obtaining a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from an experienced Mercedes-Benz specialist is strongly recommended.
