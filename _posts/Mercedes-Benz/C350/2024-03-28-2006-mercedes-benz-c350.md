**2006 Mercedes-Benz C350**

The 2006 Mercedes-Benz C350 is a luxury sedan that offers a comfortable and refined driving experience. It is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 268 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The C350 comes standard with a 7-speed automatic transmission.

Some of the things that people like about the C350 include its stylish design, comfortable interior, and powerful engine. However, some people have reported experiencing issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating engine
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Variable Valve Timing Actuator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace variable valve timing actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed gear engagement
    * Solution: Replace or repair transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or knocking noise from front of vehicle
    * Solution: Replace strut mount
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Short**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blown fuses, flickering lights
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2006 Mercedes-Benz C350 is a generally reliable vehicle, but some owners have reported experiencing issues with the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost to repair them can be significant. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
