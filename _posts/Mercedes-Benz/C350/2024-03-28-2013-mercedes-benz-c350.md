**Introduction:**

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz C350 is a luxury sedan that has a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 302 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a 7-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive. The C350 is known for its comfortable ride, luxurious interior, and strong performance.

**Engine:**

* **M276 3.5L V6:**
    * **Oil cooler line failure:** Oil leaks from the oil cooler lines, causing low oil levels and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the oil cooler lines.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake manifold runner flap issues:** The intake manifold runner flaps can stick or fail, causing reduced engine performance and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

* **7-Speed Automatic (7G-TRONIC PLUS):**
    * **Transmission slipping or shuddering:** The transmission may slip or shudder while shifting gears, causing rough shifts and potential drivetrain damage.
        * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid and filter or rebuild the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

* **Battery discharge:** The battery may discharge prematurely, causing difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Inspect and replace the battery, alternator, or other electrical components as needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight condensation:** Moisture may accumulate inside the headlights, causing poor visibility.
    * Solution: Replace the headlights or reseal them.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Sunroof drain tube clogging:** The sunroof drain tubes can become clogged, causing water to leak into the cabin.
    * Solution: Clean the drain tubes or replace them if damaged.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **COMAND system freezing:** The COMAND infotainment system may freeze or malfunction, causing loss of functionality and safety features.
    * Solution: Update the system software or replace the COMAND unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2013 Mercedes-Benz C350 is a reliable vehicle overall, but it has some potential issues with its engine, transmission, and electrical systems. These issues can be costly to fix, but most are relatively rare.
