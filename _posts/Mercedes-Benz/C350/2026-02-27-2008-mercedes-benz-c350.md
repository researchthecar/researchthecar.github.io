# 2008 Mercedes-Benz C350

### Overview
The 2008 Mercedes-Benz C350, part of the W204 generation, is generally considered a solid luxury sedan with a focus on comfort and performance. It offers a refined driving experience and a well-appointed interior. While most owners report a positive ownership experience, like any vehicle, it has specific areas that have seen more attention from repair shops. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for prospective buyers or current owners.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (M272)
**Balance Shaft Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, rough idle, poor performance, potential stalling.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the balance shaft and associated components. This is a significant repair.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000+

**Intake Manifold Runner Flap Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light (P2004, P2005, P2006 codes), reduced engine power, rough idle.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold assembly or repair of the flap mechanism.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets, Oil Cooler Seals)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil leaks, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or oil cooler seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Transmission

### 7G-TRONIC (7-Speed Automatic)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, transmission not engaging gears, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Transmission Control Module.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,000

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, window getting stuck.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** One or both Xenon headlights not working, flickering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight ballast unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2008 Mercedes-Benz C350 (W204) is a capable luxury sedan, but potential buyers should be aware of specific mechanical vulnerabilities. The 3.5L V6 engine, particularly the M272 variant, is prone to balance shaft and intake manifold issues, which can be costly to repair. The 7-speed automatic transmission can also experience problems with its control module or torque converter. While the suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior systems are generally robust, minor electrical issues like window regulators and headlight ballasts can occur. Addressing these potential engine and transmission concerns proactively through thorough pre-purchase inspections and maintenance can significantly improve the long-term ownership experience.
