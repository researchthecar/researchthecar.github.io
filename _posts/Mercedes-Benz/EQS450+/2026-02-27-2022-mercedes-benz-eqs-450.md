# 2022 Mercedes-Benz EQS 450+ Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz EQS 450+ represents the brand's flagship luxury electric vehicle journey, offering exceptional range and cutting-edge technology. As an early model year for this bespoke EV platform, buyers should approach the used market with a balanced view of high-tech innovation and potential growing pains associated with complex software and early production runs.

---

## Engine

### Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor (Rear-Wheel Drive)

This single-motor setup delivers approximately 329 horsepower and 417 lb-ft of torque to the rear wheels. It provides smooth, silent acceleration and robust efficiency, serving as the base powertrain for the EQS lineup.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Fixed Gear Transmission

A dedicated single-speed automatic reduction gearbox manages power delivery seamlessly without traditional shift interruptions. It is integrated directly with the rear electric motor assembly for optimal efficiency and minimal mechanical complexity.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz EQS 450+ delivers unmatched luxury and impressive electric driving range, but prospective buyers should carefully evaluate its complex digital interfaces and early-production electronics.
