# 2023 Mercedes-Benz EQS 450+ Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz EQS 450+ represents the brand's flagship luxury electric vehicle experience, offering advanced technology, exceptional range, and a serene cabin. As a relatively new luxury EV on the used market, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate electronics, software performance, and overall build quality alongside standard EV pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### Permanently Excited Synchronous Motor (Rear-Wheel Drive)

This single rear-mounted electric motor produces approximately 329 horsepower and 417 lb-ft of torque. It delivers smooth, effortless acceleration with strong efficiency and typically requires minimal mechanical maintenance.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Fixed Ratio Transmission

A single-speed direct-drive transmission manages power delivery to the rear wheels. It provides seamless power with no gear-shifting interruption and features exceptional mechanical reliability.
