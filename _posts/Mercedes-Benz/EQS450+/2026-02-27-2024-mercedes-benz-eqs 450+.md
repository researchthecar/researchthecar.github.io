### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Mercedes-Benz EQS 450+ demonstrates strong initial reliability, particularly in its core electric powertrain and battery systems, which show no widespread issues. The primary areas of concern identified are related to the sophisticated electrical systems, specifically occasional MBUX software glitches and a potential for premature 12V auxiliary battery failure. The adaptive air suspension, while advanced, has seen some reports of compressor malfunctions. Overall, the EQS 450+ is a technologically advanced vehicle where most issues are software-related or specific component failures that are not indicative of systemic powertrain weaknesses. Early adopters should be aware of potential infotainment quirks and ensure proper maintenance of the 12V battery.
