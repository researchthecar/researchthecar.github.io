## Suspension

### AIRMATIC Air Suspension
**Air Suspension Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of leaking air springs, lines, or compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000


## Electrical

### High-Voltage Battery System
**Battery Management System (BMS) Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced range, charging issues, error messages related to battery performance.
- **Solution:** Software updates or recalibration of the BMS by a qualified technician.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500


### Infotainment System
**MBUX System Freezes/Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen unresponsive, system reboots unexpectedly, navigation or audio malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard reset of the system, or potential hardware replacement if persistent.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1000


### 12V Auxiliary Battery
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical systems malfunctioning, warning lights, difficulty starting (though less critical in EVs).
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V auxiliary battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Mercedes-Benz EQS 450+ demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its core electric powertrain components. The electric drive unit and single-speed transmission have shown no widespread issues, aligning with the expected robustness of modern EV technology. However, potential owners should be aware of isolated reports concerning the AIRMATIC air suspension, specifically air leaks, which can lead to ride height issues and require professional attention. Electrical systems, while generally reliable, can occasionally present minor glitches with the MBUX infotainment and battery management systems, typically resolvable with software updates. The 12V auxiliary battery is also a component to monitor, as with many modern vehicles. Exterior and interior components have largely been free of significant reported defects. Overall, the EQS 450+ offers a dependable luxury electric vehicle experience, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic flaws.
