**Introduction:**

The 1991 Mercedes-Benz LP1419, a mid-sized, three-axle commercial vehicle, was powered by a naturally aspirated 4.2 L OM366 inline-6 diesel engine mated to a four-speed manual transmission. The LP1419 offered reliable performance and durability, but there have been some reported issues with its various components.

**Engine**

* **OM366 4.2 L Diesel Inline-6**
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:** Symptoms include rough idling, decreased power, and poor fuel economy. Requires replacement of faulty injectors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:** Symptoms include loss of coolant, overheating, and rough idling. Involves replacing the intake manifold gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * **Water Pump Failure:** Symptoms include overheating and coolant leaks. Requires replacement of the water pump. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Transmission**

* **Four-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Gearshift Linkage Malfunction:** Symptoms include difficulty shifting or grinding noises when shifting. Needs adjustment or replacement of worn-out linkage components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure:** Symptoms include spongy clutch pedal or difficulty disengaging the clutch. Involves replacing the clutch master cylinder. Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension:** No Issues Found
* **Rear Suspension:** No Issues Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Symptoms include dim headlights, battery not charging, and electrical system malfunctions. Requires replacement of the alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
* **Starter Motor Failure:** Symptoms include difficulty starting the engine or failure to start. Involves replacing the starter motor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Body Rust:** Symptoms include visible rust spots or corrosion on the body panels. Requires repair or replacement of affected panels. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+ per panel
* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:** Symptoms include difficulty locking or unlocking the doors from the inside or outside the vehicle. Replacement of the door lock actuators is necessary. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+ per door

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:** Symptoms include cracks or warping on the dashboard. Replacement of the dashboard is required. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
* **Seat Belt Buckle Failure:** Symptoms include failure to latch or release the seat belt buckle. Replacement of the buckle is necessary. Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+ per buckle

**Summary:**

Overall, the 1991 Mercedes-Benz LP1419 is a reliable commercial vehicle, but it has some common issues like fuel injector failure, intake manifold gasket leak, and electrical system malfunctions that can be costly to repair. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the extent of the damage or failure. Regular maintenance and addressing issues promptly can help extend the lifespan of the LP1419 and mitigate potential expenses.
