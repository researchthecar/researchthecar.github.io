# 2016 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500 is a heavy-duty commercial van and RV chassis widely trusted for its capability, heavy payload capacity, and spacious platform. However, used-car buyers should carefully inspect prospective vehicles for recurring electrical and sensor-related glitches, particularly involving wheel speed sensors that can trigger limp mode or stability control warnings. Routine maintenance history and thorough pre-purchase inspections are essential when considering this hardworking platform.

---

## Engine

### 2.1L 4-Cylinder Diesel (OM651)

A 2.1-liter twin-turbocharged inline-4 diesel engine generating approximately 161 horsepower and 265 lb-ft of torque. It offers higher fuel efficiency for lighter commercial duties, though proper emissions maintenance is crucial.

### 3.0L V6 Diesel (OM642)

A 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 diesel engine producing around 188 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque. Known for strong low-end pulling power, making it a popular choice for heavy conversion vans and motorhome builds.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (7G-Tronic)

A 7-speed automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel or dual-rear-wheel drive configurations. It provides smooth highway cruising and optimizes power delivery, though fluid changes must be kept up to date.

## Suspension

### Rear Leaf Springs

Suspension setup is engineered to bear heavy loads, though constant maximum weight capacities can accelerate wear on leaf springs and tires.

**Leaf Spring Sagging**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle rear end sits noticeably lower while parked or loaded, indicating diminished load support from factory springs.
- **Solution:** Inspect rear suspension leaf springs for bending or fatigue and replace or reinforce them with upgraded suspension components.

## Brakes

### Wheel Speed Sensors

Braking components provide strong stopping power for heavy payloads, but wheel speed sensors are a known trouble spot that can disable active safety features.

**Premature Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated ABS, traction control, and stability control warning lights, sometimes accompanied by unexpected transmission limp mode or erratic gear selection.
- **Solution:** Diagnose faulty wheel speed sensors using an OBD-II scanner and replace the defective front or rear sensor units.
- **Cost:** $760

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting and Harnesses

Electrical architecture supports numerous commercial upfits and safety systems, but moisture intrusion in exterior lighting requires vigilance.

**Water Intrusion in Tail Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Tail light assemblies fill with water or moisture, leading to short circuits, bulb failures, and intermittent warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect tail light housings for proper sealing, drain accumulated water, and replace damaged wiring harnesses or lamp assemblies.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V004000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V003000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the driver frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front driver air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V042000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc):Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The Electronic Stability Control (ESC) unit may become disabled on these vehicles. If ESC becomes disabled, vehicle driving stability may be impacted under certain driving conditions, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear-wheel speed sensor with a more robust sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V663000: Steering:Hydraulic Power Assist:Hose, Piping, And Connections](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The sudden loss of power steering assist and hydraulic steering fluid leaking onto the roadway may increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will check the return line connections of the power steering system, correcting it as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V592000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A loose driveshaft may strike and puncture the gas tank, resulting in a fuel leak, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will install the missing catch strap for the driveshaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V093000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As a result, the air bag control unit may not remain functional after the initial impact, possibly preventing additional air bags from deploying in response to a secondary impact. If additional air bags do not deploy as intended, the occupants are at an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will install a reinforcement plate and protective bracket around the air bag control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V670000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The bolts connecting the lower seat belt connection to the rear bench seat may not have been properly tightened. If the bolts were not properly tightened, the seat belt may fail to secure the seat occupant in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and Sprinter dealers will inspect the connecting bolts, tightening them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V113000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A vehicle that exceeds its maximum weight has an increased risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the UVW label and inspect the vehicle to make sure that it is not overloaded, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500 remains a capable choice for commercial fleets and camper van conversions, provided it has been properly maintained. Buyers should prioritize checking the service history for wheel speed sensor replacements, electrical system integrity, and suspension wear. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a technician familiar with Mercedes-Benz diesel platforms is strongly recommended before finalizing a purchase.
