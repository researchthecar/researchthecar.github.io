# 2024 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500 is a heavy-duty commercial van built for demanding utility and transport roles. Featuring robust diesel powertrain options, it is designed for high-mileage commercial duties and heavy payloads. While brand-new models benefit from modern engineering and warranty coverage, buyers should carefully evaluate maintenance histories and configuration choices when purchasing used commercial vehicles.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbo Diesel 4-Cylinder

A 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder diesel engine available in varying power outputs, providing balanced efficiency for commercial delivery fleets. It offers reliable performance when properly maintained with scheduled diesel service intervals.

### 2.0L Twin-Turbo Diesel High-Output 4-Cylinder

A high-output 2.0-liter twin-turbocharged diesel engine delivering increased horsepower and torque for heavy towing and payload requirements. This modern powerplant relies on complex emissions systems that require diligent upkeep.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 9-speed conventional automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive configurations. It provides smooth shifting and optimizes fuel economy across varied commercial operating conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V517000: Suspension:Rear:Springs:Leaf Spring Assembly:U-Bolt, Leaf Spring To Axle](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An axle that moves out of position can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the spring U-bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V135000: Parking Brake](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 105, "Hydraulic and Electric Brake Systems." Parking brake failure can result in a vehicle rollaway, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the parking brake cable bracket as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V609000: Structure:Frame And Members](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The longitudinal frame members may not be reinforced properly. Frame members with inadequate strength may reduce the vehicle's performance in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the frame, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
