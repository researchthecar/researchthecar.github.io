**Introduction:**
The 2011 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500 is a full-size van available with the following powertrain options:

* **Engine:**
    * 3.0L V6 Diesel Engine (188 hp / 325 lb-ft torque)
    * 3.0L V6 Gasoline Engine (190 hp / 258 lb-ft torque)

* **Transmission:**
    * 5-speed Manual Transmission
    * 5-speed Automatic Transmission

The Sprinter 3500 is known for its spacious cabin, versatile cargo space, and high towing capacity. However, several issues have been reported by owners.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6 Diesel Engine**

* **Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, poor fuel efficiency, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace faulty injector
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L V6 Gasoline Engine**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from engine, reduced power, engine stalling
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual Transmission**

* **Difficult Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace or repair clutch assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Slipping Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping out of gear, hesitation when shifting
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Axle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Wobbling or vibration at high speeds, decreased stability
    * Solution: Replace rear axle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim lights, electrical system malfunctions
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after sitting for extended periods
    * Solution: Diagnose and fix source of battery drain (could be faulty module, parasitic draw, etc.)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Lock Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate unable to be opened or closed securely
    * Solution: Replace tailgate lock
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Door Handle Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handle breaks off, making it difficult to open the door
    * Solution: Replace door handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise coming from dashboard area
    * Solution: Apply soundproofing or tighten loose components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Heater Fan Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heater fan not blowing air or blowing intermittently
    * Solution: Replace heater fan or resistor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
While the Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500 offers a spacious and versatile interior, it has been plagued by a range of issues. Engine problems, particularly with the diesel engine, can be costly to repair. Transmission issues may require major work or replacement. Suspension and electrical faults can also impact performance and comfort. Interior issues, though less severe, can be annoying and costly to fix. The severity and cost of these issues can vary significantly, making it important for potential buyers to consider these factors before purchasing a 2011 Sprinter 3500.
