# 2017 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2017 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500, buyers will find a heavy-duty commercial van widely favored for conversion projects, fleet use, and heavy hauling. While it offers robust utility and capable diesel performance, prospective owners should review common owner-reported grievances regarding emissions equipment, braking system sensors, and wheel or tire durability.

---

## Engine

### 2.1L 4-Cylinder Diesel Engine

The 2.1-liter OM651 4-cylinder twin-turbo diesel engine produces roughly 161 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. It offers improved fuel economy for lighter commercial duties, though owners should monitor emissions equipment closely.

**Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF) System Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden warnings regarding low DEF fluid or system faults, occasionally forcing the vehicle into 'limp mode' with severely restricted speeds.
- **Solution:** Have the diesel exhaust fluid sensors, injectors, and control module inspected and serviced or replaced by a qualified technician.

### 3.0L V6 Diesel Engine

The 3.0-liter OM642 V6 turbodiesel engine generates approximately 188 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque, mated to a 5-speed or 7-speed automatic transmission. It provides strong towing and hauling capabilities well-suited for heavy upfits, though complex auxiliary systems require vigilant maintenance.

**Vehicle Speed Control and Brake Sensor Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected illumination of ABS, Electronic Stability Program (ESP), and traction control warning lights, sometimes accompanied by brief brake pedal chattering or cruise control disengagement.
- **Solution:** Diagnose wheel speed sensors, brake light switches, or stability control modules and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 7G-Tronic 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission distributes power smoothly to the rear wheels across varying loads. Regular fluid and filter changes are vital to maintain long-term shifting reliability.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

Dually rear wheel setups on the 3500 series carry substantial weight, making periodic inspection of wheels, valve stems, and tire wear essential for safety.

**Wheel Cracking and Rapid Air Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported premature cracking on factory wheels leading to persistent flat tires, or wheel covers shifting and interfering with tire valve stems.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels thoroughly for structural cracks during routine tire rotations and replace damaged rims immediately.

## Safety

### Supplemental Restraint System (SRS)

Safety systems include standard stability controls and airbags, though aftermarket or factory swivel seat configurations require careful clearance checks to protect internal wiring.

**Swivel Seat Wiring Damage**
- **Symptoms:** Upfitted vans featuring swivel driver seats can inadvertently pinch or sever internal wiring harnesses routed through the seat base, triggering airbag warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat base wiring harnesses for proper routing and clearance, repairing or shielding damaged wires to restore SRS continuity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V003000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the driver frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front driver air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V004000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V042000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc):Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The Electronic Stability Control (ESC) unit may become disabled on these vehicles. If ESC becomes disabled, vehicle driving stability may be impacted under certain driving conditions, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear-wheel speed sensor with a more robust sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V288000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles may be missing spot welds on the front side wall, connecting the door portal to the hinge reinforcements in the A-pillar area, possibly affecting vehicle strength in a crash. If the missing welds affect body strength, there may be an increased risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the vehicles and add any missing spot welds, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V663000: Steering:Hydraulic Power Assist:Hose, Piping, And Connections](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The sudden loss of power steering assist and hydraulic steering fluid leaking onto the roadway may increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will check the return line connections of the power steering system, correcting it as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V113000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A vehicle that exceeds its maximum weight has an increased risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the UVW label and inspect the vehicle to make sure that it is not overloaded, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V804000: Lane Departure: Assist](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If Crosswind Assist system does not activate the automatic braking system as expected in the event of a strong crosswind, there would be an increased risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the ESP control unit with corrected software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V786000: Equipment](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are equipped with Kidde Plastic-Handle or Push Button 'Pindicator' fire extinguishers that may become clogged, preventing the extinguisher from discharging as expected or excessive force may be needed to activate the extinguisher. Additionally, in certain models, the nozzle may detach from the valve assembly with enough force that it could.
- **What to do:** Midwest will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fire extinguisher with a different brand, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500 is a capable and versatile platform for heavy commercial tasks or custom camper conversions, but it demands careful pre-purchase inspection. Buyers should verify the health of the diesel emissions and DEF systems, inspect wheels for stress cracking, and check that auxiliary features like swivel seats have not compromised internal wiring harnesses.
