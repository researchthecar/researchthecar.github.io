**Introduction:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500 is a versatile and capable full-size van that provides ample space and performance for commercial and recreational purposes. It offers various engine and transmission options, with a powerful 3.0L V6 turbo diesel engine delivering 188 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque. The Sprinter 3500 is praised for its spacious interior, robust capabilities, and refined driving experience. However, like all vehicles, it has some reported issues that potential buyers should be aware of.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6 Turbo Diesel**

**Fuel Injector Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Engine misfiring, reduced power, excessive smoke
* **Solution:** Replace faulty fuel injectors
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,200 - $1,800+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Solenoid Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, delayed or no engagement, check engine light
* **Solution:** Replace defective solenoid
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $800 - $1,200
* **Recalls:** None Found

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Battery fails to hold a charge, difficulty starting
* **Solution:** Inspect and repair or replace faulty components, such as the alternator or battery
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $600+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Dashboard Panel Cracks:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Dashboard panel develops cracks over time
* **Solution:** Replace or repair cracked panel
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500 is generally a reliable and capable vehicle. However, it has some reported issues that buyers should consider before purchasing. The most significant issue is the potential for fuel injector failure in the 3.0L V6 turbo diesel engine, which can be costly to repair. Other reported issues include transmission solenoid failure, battery drain, and dashboard panel cracks. Overall, the severity and cost of these issues vary, and potential buyers should research and consider their individual needs and budget before making a decision.
