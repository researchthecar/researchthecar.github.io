# 2020 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500 is a heavy-duty commercial van and chassis platform widely utilized for large cargo operations and motorhome conversions. While offering strong capability and advanced tech features, used-car buyers should review owner-reported electronic and driver-assistance system glitches carefully.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbo Inline-4 Diesel

A 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder diesel engine producing approximately 161 horsepower, paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. This powertrain serves as a lighter-duty baseline option for the platform.

### 3.0L Turbo V6 Diesel

A 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 diesel engine generating roughly 188 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque, mated to a 7G-TRONIC 7-speed automatic transmission. It provides robust pulling power suited for heavy commercial upfits and RV builds.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

A standard multi-speed automatic transmission designed to manage high torque loads in commercial applications. It generally operates smoothly when properly maintained.

## Brakes

### Brake Assist and Cruise Control

The braking and driver safety systems rely heavily on sensors that can occasionally trigger temporary fault warnings.

**Intermittent Brake Assist Inoperable Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Warning messages regarding brake assist appear on the dash, and safety features like cruise control temporarily suspend operation.
- **Solution:** Check wheel speed sensors, brake software calibrations, and system error codes at a specialized service center.

## Interior

### Dashboard Display and Electronics

Owner reports highlight occasional anomalies within the complex digital cabin electronics and control modules.

**Instrument Cluster Flickering**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard display begins flickering on and off, accompanied by multiple false warning messages while windshield wipers operate uncontrollably.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the body control module, wiring harnesses, and software for faults.

### Seat Mounts

Cab ergonomics are generally functional, though certain physical components require cautious handling.

**Exposed Seat Track Edges**
- **Symptoms:** Front and rear seat mounting tracks feature sharp metal edges that can cut legs while entering or exiting the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Install protective trim covers over the exposed track ends to prevent accidental contact.

## Safety

### Crosswind Assist and Collision Avoidance

Advanced driver assistance systems provide added stability for large box vehicles but can occasionally misbehave.

**Inadvertent Braking Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Crosswind assist or collision avoidance features activate unexpectedly at highway speeds, causing sudden unintended braking.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership to ensure all ADAS software updates and sensor calibrations are fully up to date.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V802000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Caliper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Air in the brake system can result in extended brake pedal travel, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will replace the rear brake calipers, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V631000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Over time, the hose could begin to leak brake fluid, affecting the braking performance of the vehicle, including the potential loss of directional stability when braking, thereby increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will check the condition of the brake hoses on the front and rear axles and replace them as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V055000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The software design of the communication module may fail to communicate the correct vehicle location for the emergency call system (eCall) in the event of a crash. Failure of the eCall system may result in emergency responders being dispatched to the wrong location, increasing the risk of injury following a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update the software communication module for the automatic emergency call system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V410000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The wiring harnesses for the front seats may have been routed incorrectly and could become jammed and damaged in the swivel seat frames. Damage to the wiring harness can prevent the side air bag within the seat to deploy inadvertently or not deploy as designed, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will attach the mounting clip to the correct position, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V411000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The MBUX multimedia system software may cause the display to remain black, or reboot after startup. This could result in the rearview camera image not displaying properly.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 21V241000: Steering:Hydraulic Power Assist:Hose, Piping, And Connections](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A hydraulic fluid leak may cause a sudden loss of power steering assist, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will install a screw-type clamp, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V595000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** DVUSA will notify owners, and dealers will check the fuel hose clamp connections and tighten or replace the components as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V972000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=Sprinter+3500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Unintentional vehicle movement can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the electronic stability program control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500 is a capable workhorse, but potential buyers should thoroughly check the electronics, driver-assistance features, and software update history. Verifying that all open service campaigns have been completed will help ensure a more dependable ownership experience.
