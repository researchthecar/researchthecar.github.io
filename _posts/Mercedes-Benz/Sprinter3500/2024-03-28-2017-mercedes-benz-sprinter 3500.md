**Introduction:**

The 2017 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500 is a versatile commercial van offering exceptional performance and advanced features. It comes equipped with a 3.0L V6 turbocharged diesel engine, generating 188 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque. The standard transmission is a 7-speed automatic, while a 5-speed automatic and a 4x4 drivetrain are available options. Owners appreciate its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and advanced safety features, but common complaints include limited fuel efficiency and occasionally unresponsive touchscreen controls.

**Engine**

* **3.0L V6 Turbocharged Diesel**

  * **Fuel Injector Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Erratic engine idle, decreased power, excessive smoke
    * Solution: Replacement of faulty fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic**

  * **Shifting Hesitations:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or rough gear changes
    * Solution: Software updates, transmission fluid flush, replacement of transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **5-Speed Automatic**

  * **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced power transfer, difficulty changing gears
    * Solution: Transmission overhaul or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature battery discharge, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Diagnosis and replacement of faulty electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Hinge Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rusted door hinges, difficulty opening/closing doors
    * Solution: Replacement of door hinges
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning AC or heater, excessive noise
    * Solution: Replace climate control module, repair electrical connections
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Annoying noises from the dashboard
    * Solution: Apply anti-rattle tape or foam insulation
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2017 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 3500 is a capable and well-built vehicle. While it has some common issues, such as fuel injector malfunctions and transmission hesitation, most of these can be resolved relatively inexpensively. The severity and cost of the issues are generally moderate, not significantly impacting the vehicle's overall reliability or ownership costs.
