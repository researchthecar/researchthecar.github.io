## 2013 Mercedes-Benz R350

The Mercedes-Benz R350 is a luxury crossover SUV that was produced from 2006 to 2013. It was offered with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 3.5-liter V6 engine with a 6-speed automatic transmission, a 5.0-liter V8 engine with a 6-speed automatic transmission, and a 3.0-liter diesel V6 engine with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The R350 was praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and luxurious appointments. However, it was also criticized for its high price, poor fuel economy, and dated exterior design.

### Engine
**3.5-Liter V6**
**Intake Manifold Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold can crack, causing a loss of engine power and fuel efficiency.
Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 13V377000

**5.0-Liter V8**
**No Issues Found**

**3.0-Liter Diesel V6**
**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger can fail, causing a loss of engine power and increased exhaust emissions.
Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Transmission
**6-Speed Automatic**
**Transmission Slippage**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip gears, causing a loss of power and acceleration.
Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

**7-Speed Automatic**
**No Issues Found**

### Suspension
**Air Suspension Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system may fail, causing the vehicle to sag and ride rough.
Solution: Replace the air suspension system.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 13V128000

### Electrical
**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain quickly, causing the vehicle to fail to start.
Solution: Replace the battery or alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

### Exterior
**No Issues Found**

### Interior
**Navigation System Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The navigation system may fail to function, causing the vehicle to lose its navigation capabilities.
Solution: Update or replace the navigation system software.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Summary
The 2013 Mercedes-Benz R350 is a luxury crossover SUV that has a number of potential issues. The most common issues include intake manifold failure, turbocharger failure, transmission slippage, air suspension failure, battery drain, and navigation system failure. The severity of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem, but they can all be expensive to fix. Overall, the R350 is a reliable vehicle, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues before purchasing one.
