**Introduction:**

The 2011 Mercedes-Benz R350 is a versatile luxury crossover SUV offered with a single engine option: a 3.5L V6 producing 268 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It comes paired exclusively with a 7-speed automatic transmission. Praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and powerful engine, some drivers have experienced various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6**

    * **Timing chain tensioner failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling or ticking, check engine light, and reduced engine power.
        * Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner and related components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-speed automatic**

    * **Shifting problems:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, or delayed gear engagement.
        * Solution: Re-programming transmission control module, fluid replacement, or transmission rebuild.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

    * **Front control arm bushings premature wear:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or clunking noises when turning or braking, uneven tire wear.
        * Solution: Replacement of worn bushings.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

    * **Alternator failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, difficulty starting, battery warning light illuminated.
        * Solution: Replacement of alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

    * **Tailgate seal failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cargo area.
        * Solution: Replacement of tailgate seal.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

    * **Climate control system malfunction:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent temperature control, malfunctioning vents, or blower not working.
        * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of specific components, such as control module, sensors, or actuators.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2011 Mercedes-Benz R350 offers a comfortable and reliable driving experience, it is susceptible to certain issues, particularly with its engine timing chain tensioner and transmission. The cost of repairs for these problems can vary significantly, ranging from minor to major expenses. However, overall, the R350 remains a capable and well-regarded choice among luxury SUV buyers.
