# 2020 Mercedes-Benz SLC300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a 2020 Mercedes-Benz SLC300, buyers will find a luxury retractable-hardtop roadster that blends sporting style with refined cruising. Because available owner data is minimal, prospective purchasers should focus their evaluation on routine pre-purchase inspections and verifying that all manufacturer recalls have been properly addressed by a qualified service center.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 241 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration for the roadster. It provides a balanced mix of daily efficiency and engaging performance, though routine maintenance is essential for long-term turbo reliability.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9G-Tronic 9-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the rear wheels while maximizing highway efficiency. It generally delivers refined gear changes suited to both relaxed touring and spirited driving.
