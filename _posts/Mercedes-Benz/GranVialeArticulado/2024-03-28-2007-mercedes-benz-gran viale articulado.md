**Introduction to the 2007 Mercedes-Benz Gran Viale Articulado**

The 2007 Mercedes-Benz Gran Viale Articulado is a luxurious articulated bus with exceptional performance and comfort. It offers a spacious interior with plush seating and advanced infotainment features. The Viale is powered by a 12-liter OM 471 inline-6 engine delivering 476 horsepower and 2,200 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed Mercedes PowerShift automated transmission. Owners appreciate its reliability, smooth ride, and impressive fuel efficiency. However, there are some specific issues to be aware of.

**Engine**

**OM 471 Inline-6**

* **Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Valve Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light illumination, loss of power, rough idle
    * Solution: Replacement of EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, engine misfires, black smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replacement of injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Mercedes PowerShift Automated**

* **Transmission Control Module (TCM) Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or jerking, delayed gear changes
    * Solution: Reprogramming or replacement of TCM
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, shuddering, overheating
    * Solution: Replacement of torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging, reduced ride height
    * Solution: Replacement of compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replacement of alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery repeatedly goes dead
    * Solution: Diagnosis of electrical system to identify parasitic draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside headlight assemblies
    * Solution: Resealing or replacement of headlights
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rust on Undercarriage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on metal components underneath the vehicle
    * Solution: Rust removal and protective coating application
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Fan Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No air flow from HVAC system
    * Solution: Replacement of fan motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Seat Belt Pretensioner Deployment:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat belt tightens without occupant in seat
    * Solution: Replacement of seat belt pretensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2007 Mercedes-Benz Gran Viale Articulado is a reliable and enjoyable vehicle with known issues typical of high-mileage diesel engines and complex luxury vehicles. However, the potential cost for major repairs should be considered. Regular maintenance and attention to potential issues can help preserve the vehicle's performance and value.
