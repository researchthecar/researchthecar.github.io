## Suspension

### Air Suspension System
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, uneven ride height, audible hissing from suspension components.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty air spring or associated air lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800 per corner

**Compressor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Failure to maintain ride height, persistent compressor running, warning lights on dashboard.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200
