## Suspension

### Air Suspension System
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one side, uneven ride height, audible hissing from suspension components.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty air spring or associated air lines.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to maintain ride height, constant running of compressor, warning lights on dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2000


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic behavior of lights, wipers, door locks, or dashboard indicators; system resets.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1800


### Infotainment/Display Unit
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
