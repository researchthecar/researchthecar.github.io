**Faulty Seatbelt Latch**
Description/Symptoms: The seatbelt latch may become stuck or difficult to release, causing the seatbelt to become inoperable.

**Malfunctioning Climate Control System**
Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may fail to adjust temperature or airflow, resulting in discomfort for passengers.
Solution: Repair or replace the climate control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Faulty Window Switches**
Description/Symptoms: The window switches may become intermittent or unresponsive, causing windows to become stuck open or closed.
Solution: Replace the affected window switches.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2015 Mercedes-Benz Gran Viale Articulado has a good reliability record, with minimal reported issues. While these issues can be costly to repair, they do not represent a major threat to the vehicle's safety or performance.
