**Introduction**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz Gran Viale Articulado is a rear-engine, rear-wheel-drive bus manufactured in Brazil. It is powered by a 10.3-liter inline-six diesel engine producing 354 horsepower and 1600 Nm of torque. The Gran Viale Articulado is available with a 6-speed manual transmission or an optional 8-speed automatic transmission. Despite its positive reception for its fuel efficiency and comfort, several issues have been reported.

**Engine**

**10.3-liter Inline-Six Diesel Engine**

***Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure***
Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls, difficulty starting.
Solution: Replace the crankshaft position sensor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
Recalls: None Found

***High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure***
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, engine knocking.
Solution: Replace the high-pressure fuel pump.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000
Recalls: None Found

***EGR Valve Issues***
Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, increased emissions.
Solution: Clean or replace the EGR valve.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-speed Manual Transmission**

***Gear Shift Difficulty***
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises.
Solution: Replace worn or damaged parts (shifter linkage, shift forks, synchronizers).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

***Clutch Wear***
Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears.
Solution: Replace the clutch assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
Recalls: None Found

**8-speed Automatic Transmission**

***Transmission Fluid Leaks***
Description/Symptoms: Fluid leaking from transmission housing.
Solution: Replace the leaking component (gasket, seal, torque converter).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

***Shift Solenoid Failure***
Description/Symptoms: Erratic shifting, delayed or no shifting.
Solution: Replace the faulty solenoid.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

***Battery Discharge***
Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly.
Solution: Check for parasitic drain, replace battery if necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
Recalls: None Found

***Alternator Failure***
Description/Symptoms: Electrical system not receiving power.
Solution: Replace the alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

***Rear-view Mirror Issues***
Description/Symptoms: Rear-view mirror looses adjustment, cracks.
Solution: Replace the rear-view mirror.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

***Dashboard Panel Fitment Issues***
Description/Symptoms: Dashboard panels not fitting properly, gaps and misalignments.
Solution: Realign and secure dashboard panels.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
Recalls: None Found

***Steering Wheel Trim Peeling***
Description/Symptoms: Steering wheel trim peeling or disintegrating.
Solution: Replace the steering wheel trim.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2016 Mercedes-Benz Gran Viale Articulado offers impressive fuel efficiency and comfort, it has been plagued by several issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. The most significant concerns include crankshaft position sensor failure, high-pressure fuel pump failure, transmission fluid leaks, and shift solenoid failures. Repair costs can range from minor to substantial, potentially affecting the overall reliability and cost of ownership.
