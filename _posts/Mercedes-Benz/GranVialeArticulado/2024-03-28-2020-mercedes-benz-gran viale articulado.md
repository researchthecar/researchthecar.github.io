**Introduction to the 2020 Mercedes-Benz Gran Viale Articulado**

The Mercedes-Benz Gran Viale Articulado is a luxury coach bus designed for charter and tour operators. It is powered by a single OM 471 LA six-cylinder inline engine with 428 horsepower and 1,638 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to a GO 190-6 Powershift 6-speed automated manual transmission. Passengers will enjoy the comfortable interior with high-end amenities, while drivers appreciate its impressive performance and fuel efficiency.
