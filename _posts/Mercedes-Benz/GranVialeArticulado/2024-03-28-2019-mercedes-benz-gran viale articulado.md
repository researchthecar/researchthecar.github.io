**Introduction**
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Gran Viale Articulado is a luxury bus that has a 12.8L in-line six-cylinder diesel engine that produces 428 horsepower and 1,450 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The Gran Viale Articulado is known for its spacious interior, comfortable seating, and advanced safety features. However, some owners have reported experiencing various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**
* 12.8L in-line six-cylinder diesel engine

**Transmission**
* 6-speed automatic

**Suspension**
* No Issues Found

**Electrical**
**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
Solution: Replace the battery or alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000

**Exterior**
* No Issues Found

**Interior**
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The climate control system does not work properly, causing the interior to be too hot or too cold.
Solution: Replace the climate control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
* **Infotainment System Glitch**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system freezes or crashes, making it difficult to use.
Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800

**Summary**
The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Gran Viale Articulado is a comfortable and spacious bus, but it has been reported to have some electrical and interior issues. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix them can range from $200 to $1,500.
