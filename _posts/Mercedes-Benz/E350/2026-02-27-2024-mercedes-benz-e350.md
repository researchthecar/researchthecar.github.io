# 2024 Mercedes-Benz E350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz E350 brings sophisticated luxury and advanced technology to the executive sedan market, but early owner reports highlight a few notable electronic, powertrain, and driver-assistance quirks. While many owners enjoy its comfort and refined ride, prospective buyers should review potential software glitches and electrical system feedback to ensure a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M254) with Mild Hybrid

Equipped with a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine featuring a 48-volt mild-hybrid system, generating around 255 horsepower. This modern powertrain balances responsive acceleration with efficiency, though early production models have logged isolated electrical and starting concerns.

**Unexpected Engine Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** Complete loss of drive power and sudden engine shutdown while operating at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Requires immediate dealer diagnosis of the powertrain control module, fuel system, or electrical architecture.

**Starting Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Recurring starting failures where the vehicle refuses to crank or start.
- **Solution:** Have the starting system, battery integration, and starter components inspected by a certified technician.

## Transmission

### 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Uses a 9-speed automatic transmission paired with either rear-wheel drive or 4MATIC all-wheel drive, designed for seamless gear shifts. It generally operates smoothly with no prominent recurring mechanical failure clusters reported by owners.

## Brakes

### Electrical Brake Integration

Integrates computerized assistance with traditional braking, though sensor misinterpretations can occasionally trigger unwanted slowdowns.

**Unexpected Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle experiences unexpected braking interventions or jerking during low-speed parking maneuvers.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates relating to driver-assistance and collision-avoidance sensor calibration.

## Steering

### Driver Assistance Systems

Comprehensive safety and active driver-assist suites offer high-tech protection but can occasionally generate false positives or stubborn overrides.

**Phantom Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic emergency braking function triggers unexpectedly during normal driving without obstacles, causing severe sudden jerking.
- **Solution:** Inspect and recalibrate the forward-facing radar and camera sensors at an authorized service center.

**Lane-Keeping Assist Override Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The lane-keeping assist function fails to disengage when commanded by the driver, continuing to apply steering inputs.
- **Solution:** Requires a software update or module diagnostic check to restore proper toggle responsiveness.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Instrument Cluster

Manages the advanced cabin displays and convenience electronics, though occasional software glitches can temporarily blank the screens.

**Screen Blanking and Reboot**
- **Symptoms:** The digital instrument panel and infotainment screens become completely blank upon starting the vehicle, losing warning indicators and feedback.
- **Solution:** Software updates or module resets performed by a dealership can often resolve display blanking anomalies.

## Exterior

### Glass and Roof Panels

Exterior glass features wide panoramic views, though rare incidents of thermal or structural stress shattering have been documented.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass sunroof unexpectedly shatters or explodes while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the glass assembly for stress fractures and replace under warranty or comprehensive insurance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V224000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=E350&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will check the transmission wiring harness routing and correct it, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz E350 offers an impressive blend of luxury, technology, and performance, but prospective buyers should inspect it thoroughly for electronic and driver-assistance quirks. Prioritize models with complete dealer service records and ensure all relevant software updates have been applied to mitigate infotainment and sensor-related glitches.
