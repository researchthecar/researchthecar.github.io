### Optional AIR BODY CONTROL (Air Suspension)
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, loss of ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated seals. Compressor may need inspection/replacement if overworked.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (MBUX)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional unresponsiveness, freezing, or unexpected reboots of the infotainment screen.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system resets. In rare cases, module replacement may be required.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500


### 12V Battery
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, electrical system malfunctions, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V battery.

### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Mercedes-Benz E350 demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, particularly concerning its core powertrain components. The 2.0L turbocharged engine and 9-speed automatic transmission are robust and have a solid track record. Potential areas for attention, though not widespread, include the optional AIR BODY CONTROL suspension, which can develop leaks over time, and occasional software glitches within the advanced MBUX infotainment system. The 12V battery is a common wear item across many modern vehicles and may require replacement within its expected lifespan. Overall, the E350 is a well-engineered luxury sedan with a strong outlook for reliability when properly maintained.
