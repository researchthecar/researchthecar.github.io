**Introduction:**

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz E350 is a midsize luxury sedan powered by a 2.0L inline-4 engine producing 255 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. Paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission, the E350 offers a refined driving experience. It features a spacious and well-appointed cabin, along with advanced safety and technology features. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of reported issues.

**Engine**

* **Engine Misfire/Stalling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent engine misfires, stalling, or rough idling
    * Solution: Software updates, fuel injector replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning air suspension system, causing uneven ride height or reduced handling
    * Solution: Air suspension compressor or strut replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **MBUX Infotainment System Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: System freezes, unresponsive touch screen, or Bluetooth connectivity problems
    * Solution: Software updates, hardware replacement if necessary
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA ID: 20V607000

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, requiring jumpstarts or replacement
    * Solution: Software updates, parasitic battery drain investigation
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to adjust temperature or airflow, excessive noise from blower motor
    * Solution: Climate control module replacement, blower motor repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Ambient Lighting Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning ambient lighting, flickering or unresponsive
    * Solution: Ambient lighting module replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2020 Mercedes-Benz E350 offers a premium driving experience, it is not without its reported issues. The most common complaints involve the electrical system, suspension, and interior. Some issues can be addressed with software updates or minor repairs, while others may require more expensive component replacements. Overall, the severity and frequency of the reported issues vary, and the cost of repairs can be substantial.
