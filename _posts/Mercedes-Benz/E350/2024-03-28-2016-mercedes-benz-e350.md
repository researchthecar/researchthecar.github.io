**Introduction:**

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E350 is a luxury sedan renowned for its comfort, performance, and technology. It features a 3.5L V6 engine and a 7-speed automatic transmission, delivering 302 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. While the E350 is generally well-received, it has experienced some reported issues affecting various components.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

**Intake Manifold Air Flap Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning air flap in the intake manifold, leading to reduced engine power and potential stalling.
* Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Control Module Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Faulty transmission control module, resulting in transmission slipping, delayed shifts, or failure to shift gears.
* Solution: Replacement of the transmission control module.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Failed air suspension compressor, leading to loss of air pressure and suspension malfunction.
* Solution: Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
* Description/Symptoms: Excessive battery discharge, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
* Solution: Diagnosis of the cause of the battery drain and repair of the faulty component.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Headlight Condensation**
* Description/Symptoms: Buildup of condensation inside the headlight assemblies, affecting visibility.
* Solution: Replacement of the faulty headlight assemblies.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per headlight
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Command System Screen Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning COMAND system screen, causing display issues, touch sensitivity problems, or complete failure.
* Solution: Replacement of the COMAND system screen.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2016 Mercedes-Benz E350 is a solid choice in the luxury sedan segment, it has experienced some reported issues in certain areas. The engine and transmission issues can be costly to repair, while the suspension and electrical problems can lead to inconvenience and safety concerns. The interior issues are less severe but can affect the overall user experience. Overall, it is important to factor these potential issues into the decision-making process when considering the purchase of an E350.
