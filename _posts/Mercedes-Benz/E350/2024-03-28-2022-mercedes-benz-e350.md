## 2022 Mercedes-Benz E350

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz E350 is a midsize luxury sedan that offers a balance of comfort, performance, and technology. It is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine that produces 255 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission and comes standard with rear-wheel drive. All-wheel drive is available as an option.

The E350 boasts a spacious interior with high-quality materials, advanced safety features, and intuitive infotainment system. However, like any vehicle, it has some known issues that potential buyers should be aware of.

### Engine

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four**

**Fuel Injector Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Engine misfires, rough idling, reduced power output, increased fuel consumption.
* **Solution:** Replace faulty fuel injectors.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800+
* **Recalls:** None Found

### Transmission

**9-Speed Automatic**

**Shift Hesitations**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Delays during gear changes, especially when shifting from lower to higher gears.
* **Solution:** Software update, transmission fluid replacement, or transmission component replacement.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $1,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

### Suspension

**Air Suspension**

**Air Spring Leaks**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Loss of ride height, uneven ride quality, suspension noise.
* **Solution:** Replace leaking air springs.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+ per corner
* **Recalls:** None Found

### Electrical

**Battery Discharge**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is parked.
* **Solution:** Check for parasitic battery drain, replace faulty battery or alternator.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

### Exterior

**Windshield Wiper Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Wiper blades fail to clear water or snow from windshield.
* **Solution:** Replace wiper motor or blades.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $300
* **Recalls:** None Found

### Interior

**Climate Control Malfunction**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Incorrect temperature settings, insufficient airflow, AC compressor failure.
* **Solution:** Repair or replace AC compressor, blower motor, or control module.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz E350 is a generally reliable vehicle, but like any other car, it has some known issues that can affect its performance and ownership experience. The most common issues include fuel injector failure in the engine, shift hesitations in the transmission, air spring leaks in the suspension, battery discharge in the electrical system, and windshield wiper failure. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but potential buyers should be aware of them before making a purchase decision.
