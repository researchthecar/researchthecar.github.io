# 2016 Mercedes-Benz E350

### Overview
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E350, part of the W212 generation's facelift, is generally regarded as a reliable luxury sedan. It offers a blend of comfort, performance, and advanced technology. While most owners report a positive ownership experience, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen more attention than others. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for prospective buyers or current owners. The E350 typically came with a robust V6 engine and a smooth automatic transmission, contributing to its overall dependability.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (M276)
**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets, Oil Cooler Seals)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of affected gaskets or seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Coolant Leaks (Thermostat Housing)**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant level dropping, overheating, white residue around the housing.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the thermostat housing.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, potential module reset or replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $600

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, battery warning light, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty module or battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E350 demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its powertrain. The 3.5L V6 engine and 7-speed automatic transmission are generally robust, with only minor oil and coolant leaks reported as occasional issues. The electrical system is the most common area for minor concerns, with infotainment glitches and battery drain being the primary complaints, often resolvable with software updates or targeted diagnostics. Suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free from widespread problems. With diligent maintenance and attention to potential minor leaks or electrical quirks, the E350 should provide a dependable and luxurious driving experience.
