# 2016 Mercedes-Benz E350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E350 blends luxury, comfort, and performance, but prospective buyers should carefully weigh potential repair histories. While owner complaints span various systems from steering assists to windshield seals, a thorough pre-purchase inspection can catch many common aging issues before they become expensive surprises.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (E350)

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 302 horsepower and offers smooth, reliable performance for daily driving. It is generally robust, though owners should monitor for minor fuel system or accessory leaks as the vehicle ages.

**Fuel Odor or Leak**
- **Symptoms:** A noticeable fuel odor inside or around the vehicle while idling, raising concerns about potential leaks in the fuel system.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines, seals, and pump assemblies for leaks, and replace damaged components.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission delivers refined gear shifts suited to rear-wheel or 4MATIC all-wheel-drive layouts. Regular fluid maintenance is recommended to ensure long-term durability and smooth operation.

## Brakes

### Parking and Service Brakes

The braking system provides strong stopping power, but electronic parking brake components should be inspected during routine service.

**Electronic Parking Brake Cable Binding**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctions or failures within the electronic parking brake system, often requiring cable replacement.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic parking brake cables and actuators, replacing worn or binding parts.

## Steering

### Power Steering Assist

Steering components generally feel precise and responsive, though occasional electronic assist failures have been reported by owners.

**Power Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A 'steering wheel malfunction' warning message appears on the dashboard, accompanied by a sudden loss of power steering assist, making the vehicle hard to steer.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the electronic power steering module or wiring harness, and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Driver Assist Systems

Electrical architecture is complex, supporting advanced safety and convenience features that require proper battery health and occasional sensor recalibration.

**Collision Prevention Warning Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The collision prevention or adaptive cruise control systems may occasionally trigger unexpected braking alerts or interventions.
- **Solution:** Have wheel sensors, radar modules, and software calibrations inspected by a qualified technician.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Seals

Exterior body panels and trim hold up well, though window and windshield weatherstripping should be checked for signs of aging or moisture leaks.

**Windshield Seal Water Intrusion**
- **Symptoms:** Water drops or leaks forming on the interior headliner or along the top of the front windshield after rain, sometimes accompanied by slight glass movement when pushed.
- **Solution:** Re-seal or replace the windshield glass to restore weatherproofing.

## Safety

### Air Bag and Occupant Sensors

Safety equipment is comprehensive, featuring a full suite of airbags and active restraints, though sensitive occupancy sensors can occasionally trigger warning lights.

**Passenger Seat Sensor Fault**
- **Symptoms:** An airbag warning light illuminates, or the vehicle indicates that the passenger-side airbag will not activate due to a seat sensor fault.
- **Solution:** Scan diagnostic codes and replace the faulty seat occupancy sensor mat or module.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V787000: Communication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=E350&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** For a full list of the affected models visit: https://static.nhtsa.gov/odi/rcl/2019/RMISC-19V787-4620.pdf An inaccurate vehicle location may delay emergency responders, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will also notify owners, and dealers will check software for a successful OTA or update the communication module software as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V627000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=E350&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The driver's air bag may unexpectedly deploy due to insufficient grounding of the steering components if an electrostatic discharge occurs and the air bag clockspring is broken. If the driver air bag inadvertently deploys, it can increase the risk of injury and a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will add sufficient grounding to the steering components, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V177000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=E350&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Various control units on these vehicles may have been updated with incorrect software, potentially affecting the correct deployment of the air bags in the event of a crash. If the air bags do not deploy as intended in the event of a crash, there would be an increased risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will update software in the affected control units, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V899000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=E350&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the control unit pins lose connection, there would be a loss of electric power steering assist, increasing the risk of a crash. The increase in pin contact resistance can also increase the risk of a fire, even while the vehicle is parked and ignition is off.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the electric power steering control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V505000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=E350&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the rubber seal falls into the engine compartment, it may contact parts of the engine or exhaust system, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rubber seal, in the engine compartment, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz E350 remains a compelling choice for luxury sedan shoppers thanks to its refined powertrain and upscale cabin. Prospective buyers should perform a detailed pre-purchase inspection focusing on the electronics, steering assist modules, and window/windshield seals to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
