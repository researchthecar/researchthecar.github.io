# 2022 Mercedes-Benz E350

### Overview
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz E350 is a luxury mid-size sedan known for its refined driving experience, advanced technology, and comfortable interior. As part of the E-Class lineup, it aims to balance performance with everyday usability. Reliability data for the 2022 model year, while generally positive, can be influenced by the specific powertrain and optional features. Owners often praise its build quality and sophisticated features, but like any complex vehicle, certain areas may warrant attention. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective and current owners.

## Suspension

### Standard/Optional Air Body Control
**Air Suspension Leaks (Optional Air Body Control)**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of leaking air springs, lines, or compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**MBUX Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or unexpected reboots of the infotainment screen and controls.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard resets, or potential replacement of control modules.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500


### 12V Battery
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Electrical system malfunctions, slow cranking, failure to start, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mercedes-Benz E350 demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly concerning its core powertrain components (2.0L turbo engine and 9G-TRONIC transmission), which show no widespread reported issues. The primary areas that may require attention are the optional Air Body Control suspension, which can be prone to leaks, and occasional glitches within the MBUX infotainment system, often resolvable with software updates. Premature 12V battery failure is also a possibility, though not unique to this model. Exterior and interior components are generally robust. For owners, ensuring proper maintenance and being aware of potential issues with the air suspension and electronics will contribute to a positive ownership experience.
