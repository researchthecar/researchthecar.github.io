# 2022 Mercedes-Benz E350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Mercedes-Benz E350, buyers are looking at a luxury sedan known for sophisticated styling and refined road manners. While generally well-regarded, owner reports highlight certain areas that require close inspection, particularly regarding advanced driver assistance features, electrical systems, and occasional body component issues. Prospective owners should review these reported concerns during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M264)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces around 255 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for daily driving. It balances efficiency with responsive power delivery, though proper maintenance is vital for longevity.

**Cylinder Head Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners report premature wear at the valve seats, which can lead to engine misfires and a sudden loss of power.
- **Solution:** Inspection by a qualified technician is required; repairs may involve professional cylinder head servicing or replacement.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes across rear-wheel-drive and 4MATIC all-wheel-drive configurations. It generally operates reliably, contributing to both highway fuel economy and refined power delivery.

## Electrical

### 48V Battery and Electrical System

Electrical concerns primarily center on advanced charging systems and infotainment display modules.

**48V Battery Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a 48V battery malfunction warning on the dashboard, followed by a red warning light and sudden power deceleration.
- **Solution:** Have the 48V electrical system, battery, and associated components tested and serviced by a dealership or specialist.

**Backup Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The backup camera display may occasionally show a completely blank screen when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera wiring connections and software modules; software updates or hardware replacement may be necessary.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior body glass and trim require careful examination during a used car inspection.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances where the panoramic glass sunroof spontaneously shatters or explodes while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for any stress fractures or defects, and replace the glass assembly if compromised.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance and Speed Assist

Advanced safety systems enhance protection but can occasionally exhibit calibration sensitivity.

**False Braking and Speed Limit Errors**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may occasionally misread speed limit signs or unexpectedly activate emergency braking in traffic without a clear obstruction.
- **Solution:** Check for available manufacturer software updates for the sensor modules and recalibrate the forward-facing cameras and radar.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V189000: Steering: Steering Wheel/Handle Bar](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mercedes-Benz&model=E350&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Deactivated hands-off detection will prevent DISTRONIC system features from functioning, including warnings to grab the steering wheel and calling first responders as part of the Active Emergency Stop Assist, which can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the hand detection control unit software for the DISTRONIC system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The Mercedes-Benz E350 offers an upscale driving experience with advanced technology, but prospective buyers should carefully verify the condition of its electrical architecture, safety sensors, and engine components. Conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help identify potential issues before finalizing your purchase.
