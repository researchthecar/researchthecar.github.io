**2019 Mercedes-Benz Boxer 60**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Boxer 60 is powered by a 4.0L V8 engine that produces 450 horsepower and 450 lb-ft of torque. It is available with a 7-speed automatic transmission or a 9-speed automatic transmission. The Boxer 60 has all-wheel drive and a luxurious interior with a high-end sound system and a panoramic sunroof.

Owners of the Boxer 60 have praised it for its powerful engine, comfortable interior, and impressive off-road capabilities. However, some have also reported experiencing various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**4.0L V8 Engine**

* **Timing Chain Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: A stretched or broken timing chain can cause the engine to lose power or stall. In some cases, it can also lead to catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: The timing chain must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: A faulty fuel injector can cause the engine to run rough, misfire, or lose power.
    * Solution: The failed fuel injector must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve Spring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Broken valve springs can cause the engine to make a ticking noise and lose power. In severe cases, it can also lead to catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: The valve springs must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may have difficulty shifting gears or may slip out of gear.
    * Solution: The transmission fluid may need to be changed or the transmission may need to be repaired or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Delays**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may have a delay when shifting gears.
    * Solution: The transmission software may need to be updated.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: A worn or damaged front suspension strut can cause the vehicle to bounce or sway excessively.
    * Solution: The front suspension strut must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension Coil Spring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: A broken or weak rear suspension coil spring can cause the vehicle to sag in the rear.
    * Solution: The rear suspension coil springs must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: A parasitic draw on the battery can cause the battery to drain quickly.
    * Solution: The faulty component that is causing the battery drain must be identified and replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Short Circuit**
    * Description/Symptoms: An electrical short circuit can cause a variety of issues, including blown fuses, damaged components, or electrical fires.
    * Solution: The electrical short circuit must be located and repaired.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture can build up inside the headlight housing causing condensation to form.
    * Solution: The headlight assembly must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Tailgate Paint Peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: The paint on the tailgate can peel or chip.
    * Solution: The tailgate must be repainted.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may not function properly, causing the vehicle to be too hot or too cold.
    * Solution: The climate control module may need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Navigation System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The navigation system may not be able to find destinations or may give incorrect directions.
    * Solution: The navigation system software may need to be updated or the navigation system may need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz Boxer 60 is a powerful and luxurious SUV. However, it has been known to experience a variety of issues, including engine problems, transmission problems, and electrical problems. The severity of these issues can vary from minor to severe, and the cost to fix them can be substantial.
