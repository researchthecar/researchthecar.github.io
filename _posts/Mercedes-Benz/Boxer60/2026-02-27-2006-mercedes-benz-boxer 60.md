# 2006 Mercedes-Benz Boxer 60

### Overview
The 2006 Mercedes-Benz Boxer 60, a commercial van known for its utility and robust build, generally presents a solid reliability record. While specific model year data can be scarce for commercial vehicles, common areas of attention for this era often revolve around powertrain components and electrical systems, particularly under heavy usage. Owners and fleet managers should be aware of potential issues that can arise from prolonged service, though widespread critical failures are not a dominant theme. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring the longevity and operational efficiency of the Boxer 60.

---


## Engine

### 2.8L TDI Diesel
**Glow Plug Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting in cold weather, rough idling.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty glow plugs.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, black smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the turbocharger.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch kit (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $600 - $1000


### 6-Speed Manual
**Synchronizer Ring Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Grinding noise when shifting gears, difficulty engaging gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of synchronizer rings and related components.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Leaf Spring Fatigue**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, increased road noise, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn or broken leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### Wiring Harness
**Corrosion and Shorts**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical failures, warning lights, non-functional accessories.
- **Solution:** Inspection, cleaning, and repair or replacement of damaged wiring.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600


### Alternator
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $500


## Exterior

### Body Panels
**Rust and Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Visible rust spots, particularly around wheel arches, door sills, and undercarriage.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment, panel repair, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from vents, inconsistent fan speed.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor or motor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2006 Mercedes-Benz Boxer 60 is a capable commercial vehicle with a generally acceptable reliability profile for its class and age. The most common concerns tend to be related to the diesel engine's ancillary components like glow plugs and turbochargers, as well as wear on manual transmission clutches and synchronizers. Suspension components, particularly leaf springs, can also require attention due to the nature of commercial use. Electrical issues, such as wiring harness problems and alternator failures, are not uncommon. Exterior rust can be a concern depending on climate and maintenance. Overall, with diligent maintenance and prompt attention to emerging issues, the Boxer 60 can remain a dependable workhorse.
