# 2024 Rivian R1S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Rivian R1S is an advanced, high-performance electric SUV that offers immense capability and luxury. However, as an early production luxury EV from a newer automaker, buyers should carefully research owner-reported glitches and mechanical quirks. While many owners praise its performance and utility, looking into real-world reliability reports helps set realistic expectations for used car ownership.

---

## Engine

### Dual-Motor AWD Electric Powertrain

The Dual-Motor configuration uses dual electric motors to deliver strong everyday power and all-wheel-drive capability. It serves as an efficient entry point to the R1S lineup with solid baseline reliability.

### Tri-Motor AWD Electric Powertrain

The Tri-Motor setup adds a secondary rear motor for exceptional torque vectoring and rapid acceleration. This configuration offers high performance while maintaining complex electronic management systems.

## Transmission

### Quad-Motor AWD Electric Powertrain

The Quad-Motor setup features an independent motor for each wheel, generating massive horsepower and unmatched off-road control. It represents the pinnacle of Rivian's performance engineering, though high-output configurations require thorough software oversight.

**Unintended Acceleration and Surging**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report instances where the vehicle unexpectedly surges forward or accelerates from a complete stop despite applying the brakes.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a qualified service center for software calibration and pedal sensor diagnostics.

### Single-Speed Direct-Drive Gearbox

This direct-drive single-speed transmission channels power smoothly to the wheels without traditional gear shifting. It is mechanically simple and generally robust, contributing to low routine maintenance.

## Suspension

### Air Suspension System

The adaptive air suspension provides adjustable ride height and smooth dampening across various terrains.

**Front Suspension Clunking and Noises**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note a noticeable clunking or thumping noise coming from the front end when driving over speed bumps or rough surfaces.
- **Solution:** Inspect suspension bushings, fasteners, and air struts for premature wear or looseness.

## Steering

### Steering and Front Hub Assembly

Electronic power steering delivers responsive handling, though proper fastener maintenance is critical.

**Front Fastener and Hub Bolt Loosening**
- **Symptoms:** Front suspension fasteners and wheel hub bolts can back out over time, leading to steering vibration and uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Check and retorque front hub bolts and suspension fasteners to manufacturer specifications during routine service.

## Electrical

### High-Voltage Electrical System

The high-voltage electrical architecture powers all vehicle functions, requiring robust software oversight.

**Sudden Power Loss and Immobilization**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly shut down while driving despite having adequate battery range, locking the rear wheels and requiring a tow.
- **Solution:** Have diagnostic codes scanned by a service center to check for software glitches or battery management module faults.

## Exterior

### Glass and Windshield

Exterior glass panels contribute to the vehicle's structural aesthetic and panoramic visibility.

**Spontaneous Rear Window Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The rear windshield has been reported to crack or shatter suddenly while parked without any direct external impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered glass panel and inspect the surrounding window frame for stress points.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V319000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rivian&model=R1S&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Without the dashboard label, operators may be unaware of the increased risks that an air bag poses to an infant or child in the front seat, increasing the risk of injury to occupants.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V367000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rivian&model=R1S&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamp, Reflective Devices and Associated Equipment." Improperly aimed headlights can result in insufficient illumination of the road, decreasing the driver's visibility and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Rivian will inspect and adjust the headlight alignment as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V408000: Air Bags:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rivian&model=R1S&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Damaged pillar trim panel clips can result in improper side curtain air bag deployment, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Rivian will replace the pillar trim panels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V370000: Seat Belts:Front:Anchorage](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rivian&model=R1S&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The D-ring bolts for one or both front seat belt anchorage assemblies may not have been properly installed. An improperly secured seat belt may not restrain an occupant as intended, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Rivian will inspect and properly secure the seat belt anchorage assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V003000: Suspension:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rivian&model=R1S&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The toe link joint may separate, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Rivian will replace the rear toe link bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V009000: Seat Belts:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rivian&model=R1S&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An improperly secured seat belt retractor may not adequately restrain the seat occupant, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Rivian will inspect and tighten the bolts as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2024 Rivian R1S is a groundbreaking electric adventure vehicle, but prospective buyers should inspect used models thoroughly. Pay close attention to suspension noises, steering component integrity, and software stability. Consulting service records and confirming that any early-production adjustments have been performed will help ensure a smoother ownership experience.
