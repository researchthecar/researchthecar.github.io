**Introduction:**

The 2023 Rivian R1S is a groundbreaking electric SUV that combines rugged capability with luxurious amenities. It is available with two electric motor options, with the Standard Motor producing 415 horsepower and 513 lb-ft of torque, and the Adventure Motor delivering 835 horsepower and 908 lb-ft of torque. The R1S is praised for its impressive off-road abilities, spacious and well-appointed interior, and advanced driver-assist features. However, it has also faced some issues.

**Electric Motor**

**Standard Motor**

- **Stator Overheating:**
   - **Description/Symptoms:** Excessive heat buildup in the stator, leading to loss of power and potentially a vehicle fire.
   - **Solution:** Replacement of the stator.
   - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $5,000 - $8,000+
   - **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V-959

- **Inverter Failure:**
   - **Description/Symptoms:** Malfunction of the inverter that converts DC power to AC power for the motor, resulting in a loss of propulsion.
   - **Solution:** Replacement of the inverter.
   - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,500 - $3,500+

**Adventure Motor**

**No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

**All Models**

- **Front Shock Absorber Failure:**
   - **Description/Symptoms:** Leaking or worn shock absorbers, causing a rough ride and potential instability.
   - **Solution:** Replacement of the shock absorbers.
   - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+ per shock absorber

- **Rear Leaf Spring Breakage:**
   - **Description/Symptoms:** Fracture of the leaf springs, resulting in a lowered ride height and reduced handling.
   - **Solution:** Replacement of the leaf springs.
   - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+ per leaf spring

**Electrical**

**All Models**

- **Battery Capacity Degradation:**
   - **Description/Symptoms:** Gradual loss of battery capacity over time, reducing driving range.
   - **Solution:** Battery replacement in severe cases.
   - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $12,000 - $18,000+

- **Charging Port Malfunction:**
   - **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty or failure to charge the vehicle's battery.
   - **Solution:** Replacement of the charging port.
   - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

- **Body Panel Misalignment:**
   - **Description/Symptoms:** Gaps or unevenness between body panels.
   - **Solution:** Adjustment or replacement of body panels.
   - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+

**Interior**

- **Center Console Cracked:**
   - **Description/Symptoms:** Cracks in the plastic center console, affecting aesthetics and storage space.
   - **Solution:** Replacement of the center console.
   - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+

- **Climate Control Malfunction:**
   - **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty maintaining desired cabin temperature, including issues with heating or cooling.
   - **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of the climate control system.
   - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $1,000+

**Summary:**

The Rivian R1S is an impressive vehicle with potential for significant impact. However, it has faced some issues, particularly with its electric motors, suspension, and electrical system. The severity of these issues varies, and some can be expensive to fix. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential problems and to seek repairs if necessary.
