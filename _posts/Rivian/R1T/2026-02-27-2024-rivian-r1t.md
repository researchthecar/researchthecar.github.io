## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Compressor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag, loss of adjustable ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200

**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Uneven vehicle stance, persistent air loss, "suspension fault" warnings.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking air struts.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000 per strut

### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Rivian R1T demonstrates a promising start in terms of reliability, with its electric powertrain and core components showing minimal widespread issues. The primary areas of concern identified are related to the adaptive air suspension system, which can experience compressor malfunctions or strut leaks, and occasional software glitches within the infotainment system. The 12V auxiliary battery is also a component to monitor for potential premature failure. Overall, the R1T appears to be a robust electric truck, but owners should be aware of potential suspension and electrical system nuances. Regular software updates and prompt attention to suspension warnings are recommended.
