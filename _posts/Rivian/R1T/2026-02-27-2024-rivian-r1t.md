# 2024 Rivian R1T Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Rivian R1T is an innovative, high-performance electric truck that offers impressive capabilities and modern technology. However, as an advanced EV from a relatively young manufacturer, buyers should review early owner reports concerning software stability, steering geometry, and suspension hardware fasteners. Ensuring any open service campaigns or field actions have been completed is a vital step for prospective shoppers.

---

## Engine

### Dual-Motor AWD Electric Powertrain

Features a dual-motor electric propulsion setup delivering strong acceleration and everyday efficiency. Overall reliability has been generally solid, though isolated owner reports mention unexpected propulsion loss.

### Quad-Motor AWD Electric Powertrain

Employs a high-output quad-motor layout providing exceptional horsepower and torque vectoring capabilities. It represents the pinnacle of R1T performance, though complex electronic management requires attentive firmware maintenance.

### Tri-Motor AWD Electric Powertrain

Utilizes a tri-motor configuration balancing robust performance and range. This newer powertrain option provides brisk acceleration with refined electronic controls.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct-Drive Gearbox

Employs a single-speed direct-drive reduction unit paired with each motor for seamless power delivery without traditional gear shifts. This simplified mechanical design typically offers long-term durability and minimal maintenance.

## Suspension

### Hubs and Knuckles

The adaptive air suspension and wheel hub assemblies provide versatile ride height adjustment, though structural fastener torque requires careful verification.

**Loose Hub-to-Knuckle Fasteners**
- **Symptoms:** Wheel hubs may develop loose fasteners, potentially leading to grinding noises against the brakes, stability concerns, or a full system shutdown.
- **Solution:** Verify that all relevant factory field service actions regarding hub and knuckle fastener torque specifications have been properly performed.

## Steering

### Steering Assembly

The electric power steering system delivers responsive handling, though a few owners have experienced vibration or alignment concerns.

**Steering Wheel Shaking and Alignment**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported steering wheel vibrations at speeds over 20 mph or persistent vehicle pulling that resists standard alignment adjustments.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician or service center inspect the front-end geometry, wheel balance, and suspension mounting points.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Instrument Displays

Advanced digital displays control most vehicle functions, though occasional software lags and reboots have been documented by early drivers.

**Screen Stutter and System Reboots**
- **Symptoms:** The central infotainment and gauge cluster displays may slow down, stutter, and occasionally undergo a full system reboot while driving, temporarily freezing camera views.
- **Solution:** Perform a soft reboot of the vehicle computer systems or install the latest over-the-air software updates provided by the manufacturer.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V319000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rivian&model=R1T&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Without the dashboard label, operators may be unaware of the increased risks that an air bag poses to an infant or child in the front seat, increasing the risk of injury to occupants.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V367000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rivian&model=R1T&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamp, Reflective Devices and Associated Equipment." Improperly aimed headlights can result in insufficient illumination of the road, decreasing the driver's visibility and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Rivian will inspect and adjust the headlight alignment as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V408000: Air Bags:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rivian&model=R1T&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Damaged pillar trim panel clips can result in improper side curtain air bag deployment, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Rivian will replace the pillar trim panels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V370000: Seat Belts:Front:Anchorage](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rivian&model=R1T&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The D-ring bolts for one or both front seat belt anchorage assemblies may not have been properly installed. An improperly secured seat belt may not restrain an occupant as intended, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Rivian will inspect and properly secure the seat belt anchorage assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V003000: Suspension:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rivian&model=R1T&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The toe link joint may separate, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Rivian will replace the rear toe link bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V009000: Seat Belts:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rivian&model=R1T&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An improperly secured seat belt retractor may not adequately restrain the seat occupant, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Rivian will inspect and tighten the bolts as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Rivian R1T is a technologically advanced and capable electric truck, but used-car buyers should perform a thorough inspection before purchasing. Verify that all factory service campaigns—particularly those addressing suspension fasteners and software stability—have been fully completed by an authorized service center.
