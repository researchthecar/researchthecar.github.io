# 2022 Rivian R1T Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The Rivian R1T is an innovative, high-performance all-electric truck known for its impressive capability and advanced tech. However, as an early-production electric vehicle, used-car buyers should anticipate a range of growing pains. Owners frequently report software glitches, air suspension and half-shaft issues, and various electrical or trim issues that often require service center visits.

---

## Engine

### Quad-Motor Electric Propulsion System

Features a quad-motor electric setup delivering approximately 835 horsepower and instant torque to all four wheels. While exceptionally powerful, early-production software and electrical integration have occasionally triggered system shutdowns and warnings.

### Dual-Motor Electric Propulsion System

A dual-motor electric setup providing standard all-wheel-drive capability with strong efficiency and robust torque output. This configuration represents a simpler powertrain architecture for the platform.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct-Drive Transmission

Uses a single-speed direct-drive gearbox paired with each electric motor unit to transfer power smoothly without traditional gear shifts. This system is mechanically simple, though related half-shafts have shown premature wear and noise in service.

## Suspension

### Air Suspension System

The complex adaptive air suspension provides adjustable ride height and handling, but early models have seen recurring issues with leaking dampers, hydraulic pump failures, and total system deflation.

**Air Suspension Collapse and Dampers**
- **Symptoms:** The air suspension system can fail or leak, causing the vehicle to drop unevenly or collapse onto its bump stops while driving.
- **Solution:** Have the air struts, hydraulic pumps, and dampers inspected, and replace failed components or seals through authorized service channels.

## Electrical

### 12-Volt Battery and Electrical System

Electrical architecture relies heavily on integrated software and secondary batteries, which have occasionally caused unexpected shutdowns and premature battery replacements.

**12-Volt Battery Drain and Electrical Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may display unexpected electrical service warnings, experience burnt electrical odors, or suffer from premature 12-volt battery failure.
- **Solution:** Test and replace failing 12-volt batteries and check wiring harnesses or software modules for persistent parasitic draws.

## Exterior

### Door Handles and Glass

Exterior styling incorporates flush electronic handles and expansive glass roofs, both of which have been prone to occasional mechanical sticking or glass durability complaints.

**Flush Door Handle Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Electronic door handles fail to present or stay open, potentially locking occupants inside or preventing entry from the outside.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic actuators and door mechanisms, and replace faulty latch assemblies or software controllers.

## Safety

### Forward Collision and Driver Assist

Advanced safety features and camera suites offer robust driver aids, but can occasionally misinterpret road conditions, resulting in erratic automated braking behaviors.

**Unexpected Phantom Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The collision avoidance system or driver assistance features may trigger sudden, unintended braking events while traveling at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Ensure driver assistance software and camera calibrations are up to date, and report persistent sensor anomalies to a service center.

## Summary Verdict

The Rivian R1T is an extraordinarily capable and powerful electric truck, but used-car buyers should approach early model years with careful preparation. Prospective owners should thoroughly check the service history for air suspension repairs, electrical updates, and half-shaft integrity, and ensure all open recalls have been properly addressed.
