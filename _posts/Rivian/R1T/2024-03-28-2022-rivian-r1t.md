**Electrical**

* **Faulty Battery Management System:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, charging issues, battery overheating
    * Solution: Software update or battery replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Random reboots, freezing, unresponsive touchscreen
    * Solution: Software updates or infotainment module replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical Harness Damage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions, warning lights, loss of functionality
    * Solution: Harness repair or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Bed Lighting Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperable or flickering bed lights
    * Solution: Light replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Bonnet Misalignment:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Uneven gaps between the bonnet and fenders
    * Solution: Panel adjustment
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free (under warranty)
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water intrusion from the sunroof during rainfall
    * Solution: Seal replacement or sunroof adjustment
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Power Seat Position Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seats not moving to the desired position or returning to memory settings
    * Solution: Software updates or seat motor replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Rivian R1T is an impressive vehicle with some known issues that primarily affect the electrical system and a few other components. The cost to fix these issues varies widely, with some being covered under warranty and others requiring significant repairs. Overall, the severity of the issues is moderate, and most owners have been satisfied with Rivian's responsiveness in addressing and resolving them.
