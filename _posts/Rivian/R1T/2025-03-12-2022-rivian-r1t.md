The 2022 Rivian R1T is an all-electric pickup truck that boasts impressive performance and a luxurious interior. All 2022 R1T models come standard with a dual-motor all-wheel-drive system, producing over 600 horsepower and 600 lb-ft of torque. Some models also feature a quad-motor configuration for even greater performance. Drivers appreciate the R1T's off-road capabilities, stylish design, and innovative storage solutions, such as the gear tunnel. However, some owners have reported issues with build quality, software glitches, and long wait times for service.

**Electric Motor**

**Dual-Motor AWD**

*   **Drive Motor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Complete failure of one or both drive motors, leading to loss of power and inability to drive. Symptoms may include error messages on the vehicle's display and unusual noises.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the faulty drive motor(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $8,000 - $12,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Quad-Motor AWD**

*   **Torque Sleep**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Delay in acceleration on the 2022 R1T model in Conserve mode. This issue is specific to the 2022 model and not present in later years.
    *   Solution: No repair. Issue has been resolved through over-the-air software update in 2023.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Battery/Charging System**

*   **12V Battery Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Frequent dead 12V battery. Can cause the vehicle to be completely unresponsive.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the 12V battery. Upgrading to an aftermarket lithium battery is also possible.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Charging Port Door Malfunction**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The charging port door fails to open or close properly.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the charging port door actuator or entire door assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The air suspension compressor fails, causing the vehicle to sag or become unable to adjust ride height. Error messages related to the air suspension system may appear.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Software Glitches and Infotainment Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system freezes, crashes, or exhibits various software glitches. Common issues include navigation problems, unresponsive touch screen, and Bluetooth connectivity issues.
    *   Solution: Software updates, system resets, or replacement of the infotainment system.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (Software Updates) to $2,000+ (Replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Sensor Malfunctions (Parking Sensors, Driver Assist)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Parking sensors malfunction, providing false alerts or failing to detect objects. Driver-assist features, such as lane keep assist and adaptive cruise control, may also experience issues.
    *   Solution: Replacement of faulty sensors or recalibration of the driver-assist system.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+ per sensor.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Key Fob Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Key fob fails to unlock/lock doors, start the vehicle, or operate remotely.
    * Solution: Battery replacement, reprogramming, or complete key fob replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5-$400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Powered Tonneau Cover Problems**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The powered tonneau cover malfunctions, failing to open, close, or retract properly. This can be caused by motor failure, sensor issues, or physical obstructions.
    *   Solution: Repair or replacement of the tonneau cover motor, sensors, or entire assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Seat Adjustment Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The power seat adjustment mechanism fails, preventing the seat from moving forward, backward, up, or down.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the seat motor or adjustment mechanism.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **HVAC System Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Issues with the heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) system. Common problems include inconsistent temperature control, failure to blow air, or unusual noises.
    *   Solution: Repair or replacement of the HVAC blower motor, control module, or other components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Rivian R1T, while an impressive electric truck, has its share of reported issues. Electrical and software glitches, along with suspension system concerns, appear to be the most common problems. While some issues are minor inconveniences, others, such as drive motor failure, can be costly to repair. The severity and cost of these issues vary, so prospective buyers should be aware of these potential problems and carefully inspect the vehicle before purchase.
