**Introduction:**

The 1996 INFINITI J30 is a luxury sedan known for its refined ride, spacious interior, and powerful engine. It offered a choice of two V6 engines paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The 3.0-liter V6 produces 190 horsepower and 200 pound-feet of torque, while the 3.2-liter V6 generates 210 horsepower and 217 pound-feet of torque. The J30 was praised for its smooth performance, well-appointed cabin, and advanced safety features. However, it also had some notable issues affecting certain components.

**Engine**

**3.0-liter V6**

* **Oil Leaks from Valve Cover Gaskets:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil from the top of the engine, visible on the valve covers.
  * Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  * Recalls: None Found

**3.2-liter V6**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slips or Jerks:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or slips when shifting gears, especially during acceleration or deceleration.
  * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Sagging:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Front of the vehicle appears lower than the rear, uneven tire wear.
  * Solution: Replace the front struts or springs.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Suspension Clunking:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Noises (clunking or rattling) coming from the rear of the vehicle when driving over bumps or rough roads.
  * Solution: Inspect and replace faulty rear suspension components, such as bushings, shocks, or control arms.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Faulty Ignition Coils:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, decreased fuel economy.
  * Solution: Replace the ignition coils.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunctions, warning lights on the dashboard, difficulty starting the vehicle.
  * Solution: Replace the alternator.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Faulty Climate Control Unit:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system malfunctions, improper temperature regulation, buttons become unresponsive or inoperable.
  * Solution: Replace the climate control unit.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Seat Belt Buckle Failures:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Seat belt buckles may not engage or release properly, causing difficulty fastening seat belts.
  * Solution: Replace the affected seat belt buckles.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
  * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID: 96V227000

**Summary:**

The 1996 INFINITI J30 is a reliable and comfortable vehicle, but it is not without its issues. The most common problems include oil leaks, transmission issues, and suspension wear. Electrical and interior components can also fail, but these occur less frequently. The severity and cost of the issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the severity of the damage. Regular maintenance and inspections can help prevent or identify potential problems before they become more severe and expensive to fix.
