# 2012 INFINITI G25

### Overview
The 2012 INFINITI G25, a luxury sport sedan, offered a blend of performance and refinement. It was generally considered a reliable vehicle, particularly for its segment. The G25 was powered by a 3.7-liter V6 engine, paired with either a 7-speed automatic or a 6-speed manual transmission. While most owners reported a positive experience, like any vehicle, some common areas of concern have been noted over time. This analysis focuses on specific components and potential issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into the long-term durability of the 2012 G25.

---


## Engine

### 3.7L V6 (VQ37VHR)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Increased oil level checks required, potential blue smoke from exhaust under heavy acceleration.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels regularly. In severe cases, engine internal inspection and potential repair or replacement may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $5,000+

**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup or at idle.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (JR710E)
**Harsh Shifting / Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, hesitation when shifting into drive or reverse.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter change, solenoid replacement, or in severe cases, transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3,000+

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 INFINITI G25 is generally a reliable luxury sedan, with its VQ37VHR engine being a strong point. However, potential owners should be aware of possible oil consumption issues and timing chain wear, especially on higher mileage examples. The 7-speed automatic transmission can sometimes exhibit shifting problems. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and occasional battery drain, are also noted. Exterior issues like headlight condensation and interior climate control blend door failures are less common but can occur. Overall, with diligent maintenance and awareness of these potential issues, the G25 can provide a satisfying ownership experience.
