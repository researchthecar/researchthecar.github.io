**Introduction:**

The 2007 INFINITI QX56 is a full-size luxury SUV powered by a standard 5.6L V8 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. Known for its spacious and luxurious interior, the QX56 also offers features such as all-wheel drive and a rear-seat entertainment system.

**Engine:**

**5.6L V8**

* **Valve Body Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or erratic shifting, delayed engagement, shuddering or jerking during gear changes
    * Solution: Replace the valve body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cam Position Sensor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine performance issues, rough idle, stalling, illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace the cam position sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine, rattling or clicking sounds, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Control Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission not shifting properly, limp mode, no response from transmission
    * Solution: Replace the transmission control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Lock-Up Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations, shuddering, or loss of power during acceleration
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Suspension lowering, uneven ride height, reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace the air suspension compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Front Suspension Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises when driving over bumps, excessive tire wear
    * Solution: Replace the strut mount
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per side
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Drain Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery draining prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical system drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies widely depending on the source of the drain
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery warning light illuminated, charging system failure
    * Solution: Replace the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Rust Issues on Lower Body Panels:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust appearing on the lower body panels, especially around the wheel wells and rocker panels
    * Solution: Rust repair or replacement of affected panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies widely depending on the extent of rust
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin from the sunroof
    * Solution: Replace or repair the sunroof seals or drainage system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2007 INFINITI QX56 has been reported to have a range of issues, some of which can be costly to repair. The most common problems include valve body issues in the transmission and various electrical issues. The severity and cost of the issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage. Overall, it's essential for owners to stay up-to-date on maintenance and repairs to minimize potential issues and maintain the vehicle's reliability.
