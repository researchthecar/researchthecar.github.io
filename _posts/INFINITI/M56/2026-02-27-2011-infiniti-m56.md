## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Navigation System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or incorrect map data.
- **Solution:** Software update or system reset. In some cases, replacement of the head unit may be required.
- **Cost:** $150 - $1200


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Premature battery failure or dead battery upon startup, often linked to parasitic draw from modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory). Repair or replacement of the faulty component.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from vents or intermittent fan speed control.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor resistor or the blower motor itself.

### Summary Verdict
The 2011 INFINITI M56 demonstrates commendable reliability, particularly in its powertrain. The robust 5.6L V8 engine and the 7-speed automatic transmission are generally trouble-free. The chassis and suspension components also hold up well. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are within the electrical system, specifically with the infotainment and navigation units experiencing occasional glitches, and a possibility of intermittent battery drain. The interior and exterior components are largely free from widespread defects. Overall, the M56 is a dependable luxury sedan, with most potential issues being relatively minor and manageable.
