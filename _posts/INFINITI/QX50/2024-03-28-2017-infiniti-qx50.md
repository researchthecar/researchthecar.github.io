**Introduction:**

The 2017 INFINITI QX50 is a compact luxury SUV powered by a 3.7-liter V6 engine paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission, producing 325 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque. It is praised for its stylish exterior, comfortable interior, and powerful engine, but has been criticized for its outdated infotainment system and limited cargo space.

**Engine**

* **3.7-liter V6**

**Transmission**

* **7-speed automatic**

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
  * Description/Symptoms: Excessive battery drain due to faulty alternator or parasitic draw
  * Solution: Replace alternator or diagnose and repair parasitic draw
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Alternator Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Premature alternator failure due to overheating or electrical issues
  * Solution: Replace alternator
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Starter Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Starter will not engage or engine fails to start due to faulty starter
  * Solution: Replace starter
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Latch Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate latch becomes inoperable, causing tailgate to open unexpectedly or not latch properly
  * Solution: Replace tailgate latch assembly
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Lag**
  * Description/Symptoms: Infotainment system experiences frequent lag, freezing, or crashes
  * Solution: Software updates or replacement of infotainment module
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1,000+
* **Climate Control Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system malfunctions, resulting in improper temperature or airflow
  * Solution: Diagnose and replace faulty components in HVAC system
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
* **Seat Heater Malfunction**
  * Description/Symptoms: Seat heater fails to heat properly or overheats
  * Solution: Replace faulty seat heater element
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Summary:**

The 2017 INFINITI QX50 is a reliable vehicle with few major issues. However, there have been some reports of battery drain, alternator failure, and infotainment system lag. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix them can range from minimal to significant. Overall, the QX50's issues do not appear to be widespread or severe enough to significantly impact its overall reliability.
