# 2020 INFINITI QX50 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Infiniti QX50, buyers will find a stylish luxury compact SUV featuring an innovative variable-compression engine. While many owners enjoy its comfortable cabin and distinct design, prospective buyers should review owner-reported feedback concerning powertrain behavior, electrical system quirks, and safety alerts to make an informed purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L VC-Turbo 4-Cylinder

The 2020 Infiniti QX50 is powered by a 2.0-liter variable-compression turbocharged (VC-Turbo) four-cylinder engine producing approximately 268 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. This technically advanced powerplant balances performance and efficiency, though buyers should verify service history and watch for any engine-related complaints.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected losses of vehicle propulsion while operating on the road, creating hazardous driving situations.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a qualified technician is required to identify underlying mechanical or electronic faults in the variable-compression system.

**Electrical Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Various dashboard warning lights illuminate unexpectedly, alerting the driver to potential system faults.
- **Solution:** Scan the vehicle's computer for diagnostic trouble codes and inspect associated sensor harnesses.

## Transmission

### Exttronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Power is routed through a continuously variable transmission (CVT) paired with either front-wheel drive or intelligent all-wheel drive. Regular fluid maintenance is vital for longevity, as smooth operation is expected during daily driving.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Electrical System

Electrical complaints center primarily around auxiliary screen resets and occasional electronic system glitches.

**Screen Blanking and Sudden Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced infotainment screens going black and rebooting while driving, alongside rare instances of abrupt vehicle stalling.
- **Solution:** Check for available software updates or inspect the charging and ignition systems for loose connections.

## Safety

### Driver Assistance and Collision Avoidance

Driver-assistance suites like ProPILOT Assist enhance highway commuting, but sensor calibration issues can occasionally lead to unexpected system interventions.

**Unintended Braking or Speed Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note that advanced driver-assistance features or automated systems have occasionally triggered unexpected slowdowns or unusual noises while cruising at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Have dealership personnel recalibrate radar sensors, cameras, and associated driver-assist modules if erratic behavior persists.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V025000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX50&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Illegible tire size or loading capacity information can lead to the incorrect sized tire being installed or its overloading, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** INFINITI will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the legibility of and, as necessary, replaced the placard, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V437000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX50&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Engine failure increases the risk of a crash. A bearing failure may cause a breach in the engine block, allowing hot oil to be discharged, increasing the risk of an engine fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the engine control module (ECM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Infiniti QX50 offers compelling styling, a high-tech engine design, and a comfortable interior package. While most examples provide a pleasant driving experience, used-car buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's electronic systems, review service records for any past engine or powertrain concerns, and perform a comprehensive test drive prior to purchase.
