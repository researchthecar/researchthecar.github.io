**Introduction:** The 2016 INFINITI QX50 is a compact luxury SUV that offers a comfortable and stylish driving experience. It is powered by a 3.7L V6 engine that produces 325 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque. The QX50 comes with a 7-speed automatic transmission. It is well-liked for its spacious interior, luxurious features, and powerful engine, but some owners have reported issues with various components.

**Engine:** 3.7L V6

* **Timing Chain Issue**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature stretching or failure of the timing chain, leading to engine noise, reduced performance, and potential catastrophic engine failure.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain, tensioner, and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: #16V576000

* **Oil Consumption Issue**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent oil top-ups.
    * Solution: Varies depending on the cause, such as worn piston rings or valve seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+

**Transmission:** 7-Speed Automatic

* **Shifting Hesitations/Jerking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifting, resulting in hesitation or jerking during gear changes.
    * Solution: Software updates, transmission fluid replacement, or in severe cases, transmission rebuild.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Suspension:**

* **Strut Mount Issue**
    * Description/Symptoms: Wear or damage to the strut mounts, leading to knocking or clunking noises, especially over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacement of the strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Drain Issue**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, especially when parked.
    * Solution: Inspection for parasitic drain and replacement of faulty components, such as the alternator or electrical modules.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Exterior:**

* **Roof Trim Panel Issue**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loosening or detaching of the roof trim panels at highway speeds.
    * Solution: Reattachment or replacement of the trim panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior:**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure or malfunction of the automatic climate control system, resulting in improper temperature regulation.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair or replacement of faulty components, such as the climate control module or actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Summary:** The 2016 INFINITI QX50 is a comfortable and luxurious SUV, but it has been plagued by several known issues. Engine and transmission problems can be serious and expensive to fix, and electrical and suspension issues can also be costly. Some exterior and interior issues are less severe, but they can still be annoying and detract from the overall experience. Overall, the severity and cost of these issues may be a concern for potential buyers or current owners.
