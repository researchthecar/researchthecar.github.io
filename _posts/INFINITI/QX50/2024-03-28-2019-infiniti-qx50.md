**Introduction:**

The 2019 INFINITI QX50 is a mid-size SUV powered by a 2.0L VC-Turbo I4 engine paired with a CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission). It offers a smooth and refined driving experience, with a spacious interior and advanced technology features. However, the vehicle has also faced some reported issues by owners.

**Engine**

**2.0L VC-Turbo I4**

* **Turbocharger Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Decreased engine performance, audible whining or whistling noises.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Variable Compression System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Irregular engine noise, reduced fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Update or replace the variable compression system components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **Hesitation or Jerking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitation or jerking during gear changes.
    * Solution: Update transmission software or replace the transmission belt and pulleys.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V-203

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Identify and fix the underlying cause of the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Squeak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noises coming from the rear axle area.
    * Solution: Lubricate or replace suspension components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Fitment:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misaligned or uneven gaps between the rear bumper and the body.
    * Solution: Adjust or replace the bumper.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Temperature control issues, excessive noise or vibrations from the climate control system.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace climate control components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V-111

**Summary:**

The 2019 INFINITI QX50 has faced several issues related to its engine, transmission, electrical system, and suspension. While some issues are minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential problems and address them promptly to avoid further damage or inconvenience.
