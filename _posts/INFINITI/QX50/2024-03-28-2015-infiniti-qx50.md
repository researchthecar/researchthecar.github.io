**Introduction:**
The 2015 INFINITI QX50 is a luxury compact SUV powered by a single engine option: a 3.7L V6 producing 325 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque. It comes with a single transmission option: a 7-speed automatic. The QX50 is known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and premium features. However, it has also been plagued by several issues.

**Engine**

* **3.7L V6 Engine Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sudden loss of power, knocking sounds from the engine, thick blue exhaust smoke.
    * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall ID: #15V416000

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic Transmission Jerking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation and jerking during gear changes, especially when shifting from Park to Drive or Reverse.
    * Solution: Software update or transmission replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy suspension, excessive bouncing, uneven tire wear, leaking struts.
    * Solution: Replacement of struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500 per strut
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery losing charge even with a new battery, various electrical components failing.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of faulty electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the component(s) involved
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Shattering**
    * Description/Symptoms: Spontaneous shattering of the panoramic sunroof glass.
    * Solution: Replacement of sunroof glass.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control system, inconsistent temperature regulation, excessive noise.
    * Solution: Replacement of climate control unit or its components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2015 INFINITI QX50 is a vehicle with known issues that can be severe and expensive to fix. The most serious issue is the engine failure, which can lead to a complete loss of power. Other issues, such as transmission jerking, suspension failure, and electrical problems, can also be costly to resolve. While the QX50 has its strengths, potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before making a purchase decision.
