# 2022 INFINITI QX50 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Infiniti QX50, prospective buyers will find a stylish luxury crossover featuring an innovative variable-compression turbocharged engine. While the vehicle offers modern tech and distinctive styling, a handful of owner-reported concerns touch upon electrical systems, steering, and body glass. Reviewing service history and verifying that all manufacturer safety campaigns have been addressed are smart steps for anyone considering this model.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L VC-Turbo 4-Cylinder

The 2.0-liter variable-compression turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 268 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, utilizing advanced technology to balance efficiency and power. Owners have reported notices regarding engine inspection campaigns, so verifying service records is recommended.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The continuously variable transmission transfers power smoothly to front-wheel or all-wheel drive configurations. It provides efficient highway cruising, though buyers should test-drive the vehicle thoroughly to ensure responsive operation.

## Steering

### Steering Mechanism

Steering performance is generally precise, but isolated reports of heavy steering feel warrant careful inspection during a test drive.

**Steering Wheel Binding**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected steering wheel stiffness or locking while traveling at highway speeds, making vehicle control difficult.
- **Solution:** Have the steering column, electronic power steering components, and related software inspected by a qualified technician.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Display System

The electrical architecture supports numerous convenience and driver-assistance features, though some owners have experienced screen blackouts and infotainment glitches.

**Infotainment Glitches and Screen Blackout**
- **Symptoms:** Display screens and infotainment centers may experience intermittent time zone resets, and the dashboard screen can occasionally go completely blank.
- **Solution:** Software updates or module resets performed by a dealership can sometimes resolve display instability.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior panels and trim hold up well overall, though panoramic glass components require careful checks for any signs of stress.

**Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass may spontaneously fracture or explode while driving at highway speeds, allowing glass fragments to enter the cabin.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the sunroof frame for alignment or stress points.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V187000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Lock](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX50&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An unlocked door may unlatch and open during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front right-side passenger door lock assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V437000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX50&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Engine failure increases the risk of a crash. A bearing failure may cause a breach in the engine block, allowing hot oil to be discharged, increasing the risk of an engine fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the engine control module (ECM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Infiniti QX50 offers an upscale cabin and a clever turbocharged powertrain, making it an attractive option in the used luxury crossover market. Buyers should perform a thorough inspection, pay close attention to infotainment and steering operation during a test drive, and confirm that all open manufacturer service campaigns have been completed.
