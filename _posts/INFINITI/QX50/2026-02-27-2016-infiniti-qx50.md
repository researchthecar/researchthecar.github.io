# 2016 INFINITI QX50 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2016 Infiniti QX50, prospective buyers will find a luxury crossover that offers engaging rear-wheel-drive or all-wheel-drive dynamics. However, owner-reported complaints indicate potential trouble spots ranging from unexpected engine and powertrain concerns to electrical glitches. Reviewing these owner experiences can help shoppers identify what to look for during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L V6 Engine

The 2016 Infiniti QX50 is powered by a 3.7-liter V6 engine producing approximately 325 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque. Known for delivering spirited acceleration, this naturally aspirated engine generally provides reliable performance but requires careful maintenance history verification.

**Ignition Coil and Powertrain Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported severe engine performance issues, unexpected surging or high RPMs, and component failures requiring expensive replacements such as ignition coils or major engine overhauls.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the ignition system, engine components, and powertrain computer during a pre-purchase inspection to rule out failing coils or internal damage.
- **Cost:** $1,000 for ignition coil replacement up to $12,000 for major engine and transmission repairs

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 7-speed automatic transmission paired with either rear-wheel drive or intelligent all-wheel drive, this gearbox offers smooth highway cruising. While widespread failures are not dominant in standard owner logs, isolated high-cost powertrain repairs have been noted by owners.

## Electrical

### Infotainment Display

The electrical architecture supports advanced convenience features, though intermittent issues involving infotainment displays and auxiliary controls can occasionally surface.

**Intermittent Display Screen**
- **Symptoms:** The dashboard display or navigation screen fails to light up immediately upon vehicle startup, occasionally taking a prolonged period to function.
- **Solution:** Inspect display power connections, fuses, and software firmware; failing head units may require professional servicing or replacement.

## Exterior

### Door Latch and Sunroof

Exterior body hardware and glass components are generally durable, but isolated incidents involving jammed door latches and vulnerable sunroof glass warrant a close physical check.

**Jammed Door Locks and Glass Fractures**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note instances where a driver-side door fails to unlock or open independently from the rest of the vehicle, or sunroof glass spontaneously shatters while driving.
- **Solution:** Lubricate or replace faulty door lock actuators and inspect sunroof glass assemblies for stress points or prior damage.

### Child Seat LATCH Anchors

Cabin ergonomics and seating are generally comfortable, though specific design limitations regarding child seat anchor accessibility have frustrated owners.

**Inaccessible LATCH Anchors**
- **Symptoms:** Child seat lower anchors are positioned deeply underneath the seat cushions, creating significant difficulty and potential injury risks when attaching child safety seats.
- **Solution:** Utilize seatbelt installation methods if lower anchors prove too difficult to access safely, or consult a certified child passenger safety technician.

## Safety

### Air Bag and Restraint System

Safety equipment generally functions as intended, though occasional warning light activations and electrical component anomalies have been documented by vehicle owners.

**Air Bag Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced unexpected illumination of the 'Air Bags Deactivated' and 'Seat Belt Detached' warning lights while driving or parked.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a certified repair facility is required to pinpoint sensor faults, wiring harness issues, or seat belt tensioner problems.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Infiniti QX50 delivers luxury performance and strong V6 power, making it an appealing used crossover option for driving enthusiasts. However, buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection that prioritizes checking the engine ignition components, electrical accessories, and door locks. Verifying a complete service history can help mitigate potential high-cost surprise repairs down the road.
