# 2023 INFINITI QX50 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2023 Infiniti QX50, buyers will find a stylish luxury crossover featuring modern technology and unique engine design. While owner reports to federal safety regulators remain relatively limited, early feedback highlights a few notable mechanical and structural concerns worth noting during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Variable Compression Turbocharged (VC-Turbo) I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine utilizes variable compression technology to balance performance and efficiency, producing approximately 268 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. It is the sole engine option available for this model year, paired exclusively with a continuous variable transmission.

**Engine Stalling and Starting Difficulties**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine stalling while driving on the road, as well as trouble starting the vehicle after completing long trips.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the fuel delivery system, ignition components, and electronic engine control modules for fault codes.

**Engine Sputtering and Failure to Move**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note that pressing the start button can cause the vehicle to sputter, followed by a failure of the vehicle to move when attempting to drive.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the fuel system and throttle body assembly to ensure proper fuel pressure and throttle response.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The continuously variable transmission directs power to either the front wheels or the intelligent all-wheel-drive system depending on the chosen trim level. Buyers should test drive the vehicle to ensure smooth acceleration and prompt engagement without unusual hesitation.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior components generally hold up well, though glass and trim pieces require careful examination.

**Sudden Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced the panoramic sunroof glass suddenly shattering while traveling at normal highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for any prior stress fractures and verify that the replacement glass is installed according to manufacturer specifications.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V154000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX50&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Due to a manufacturing issue, the driver's air bag inflator may absorb moisture, and cause the air bag to deploy improperly. An air bag that deploys improperly may not protect the driver as intended during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front driver's air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Infiniti QX50 offers compelling luxury and innovative engineering, but used car buyers should remain vigilant during their evaluations. Given isolated reports involving engine starting behavior, stalling, and unexpected sunroof glass breakage, a thorough mechanical inspection and test drive are strongly recommended before finalizing a purchase.
