# 2017 INFINITI QX50 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2017 Infiniti QX50, buyers will find a luxury crossover known for its sporty rear-wheel-drive platform and strong V6 performance. While many owners enjoy its engaging driving dynamics, prospective purchasers should carefully evaluate potential owner-reported trouble spots regarding electrical systems, structural components, and safety sensors before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L V6 Engine

Equipped with a 3.7-liter V6 engine producing approximately 325 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration. This engine is generally well-regarded mechanically, though isolated owner reports note unexpected stalling incidents.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Utilizes a 7-speed automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel drive or intelligent all-wheel drive. The transmission typically delivers smooth shifting, though rare owner complaints have noted unexpected shifting into neutral or speed sensor issues.

## Steering

### Steering Alignment

Steering complaints occasionally note tracking difficulties or a loose feel during high-speed highway driving.

**Highway Wandering and Drifting**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle pulls or drifts across highway lanes at normal speeds, requiring constant steering correction by the driver.
- **Solution:** Perform a thorough wheel alignment check and inspect steering and suspension components for wear.

## Exterior

### Body Control Module

Electrical complaints center on module performance and sensitivity to moisture intrusion from exterior drainage issues.

**Water Intrusion and BCM Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Electrical accessories malfunction or fail repeatedly due to water leaking onto the body control module from clogged or misdirected sunroof/drainage paths.
- **Solution:** Inspect and clear drain paths, and replace the body control module if water damage has caused permanent electronic failure.

### Moonroof Glass

A small number of owner reports indicate rare instances of spontaneous glass breakage on the vehicle's moonroof.

**Spontaneous Moonroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The moonroof glass shatters unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds without any apparent impact from outside objects.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame for stress or alignment issues.

## Safety

### Air Bag Sensors

Owner reports highlight occasional issues with the supplemental restraint system sensors, particularly within the passenger seat electronics.

**Passenger Airbag Sensor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning light illuminates unexpectedly on the dashboard and remains lit, indicating a fault in the passenger seat electronics.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnosis by a qualified technician, which may involve replacing the passenger front seat bottom assembly.

The 2017 Infiniti QX50 provides a rewarding driving experience with strong V6 performance, but used car buyers should perform a thorough inspection prior to purchase.
