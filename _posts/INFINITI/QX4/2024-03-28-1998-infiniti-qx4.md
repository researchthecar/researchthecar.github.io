**Introduction:**

The 1998 INFINITI QX4 is a mid-size luxury SUV powered by a 3.3L V6 engine, producing 210 horsepower and 235 lb-ft of torque. It features a 4-speed automatic transmission and comes in two trim levels: Base and Luxury. The QX4 offers a comfortable and spacious interior, a strong engine, and a reliable drivetrain. However, it faces criticisms for its poor fuel economy and outdated design.

**Engine**

**3.3L V6**

- **Oil Leak:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the valve covers, oil pan gasket, or rear main seal.
  - Solution: Replace faulty gaskets or seals.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Timing Belt Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The timing belt can break, leading to catastrophic engine damage.
  - Solution: Follow the recommended maintenance schedule to replace the timing belt and components at 60,000 miles.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,000+
  - Recall: #03V182000

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

- **Transmission Slipping:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips or hesitates when shifting gears.
  - Solution: Replace faulty transmission components, such as the torque converter or solenoid pack.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Front Strut Mount Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The strut mounts can fail, causing the front end to clunk or rattle.
  - Solution: Replace the failed strut mounts.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Rear Leaf Spring Sagging:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The rear leaf springs can sag, leading to a lowered rear end and reduced handling.
  - Solution: Replace the sagging leaf springs.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Power Window Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The power windows may fail to operate or become inoperative.
  - Solution: Replace the faulty window motor or switch.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Alternator Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The alternator may fail, resulting in a loss of electrical power and a dead battery.
  - Solution: Replace the failed alternator.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Exterior Trim Peeling:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The exterior trim, including the chrome accents and door handles, may peel or discolor over time.
  - Solution: Touch up or repaint the affected trim pieces.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Dashboard Cracking:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may develop cracks over time, especially in areas exposed to sunlight.
  - Solution: Replace the cracked dashboard.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1998 INFINITI QX4 is a reliable SUV with some common issues that are mostly minor and easy to fix. The engine and transmission are strong, but the suspension and electrical components may require occasional attention. The interior and exterior issues are primarily cosmetic and do not affect the vehicle's performance or safety. Overall, the QX4 provides a comfortable and dependable driving experience despite the potential for occasional repairs.
