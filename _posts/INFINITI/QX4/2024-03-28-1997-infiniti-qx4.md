**1997 INFINITI QX4**

The 1997 INFINITI QX4 is a mid-size SUV that was offered with a 3.3-liter V6 engine and a 4-speed automatic transmission. It has a horsepower of 170 hp and a torque of 225 lb-ft. The QX4 is known for its comfortable interior, spacious cargo capacity, and reliable performance. However, like any vehicle, it has some common issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

* **3.3L V6**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:** Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaking from the intake manifold. Decreased engine performance, overheating. Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+ Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slippage:** Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or shuddering during gear changes. Difficulty shifting. Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+ Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Sagging:** Description/Symptoms: Rear of the vehicle sagging or drooping. Difficulty handling and braking. Solution: Replace the rear suspension components (e.g., springs, shocks, struts). Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging properly. Dimming headlights. Electrical accessories not working. Solution: Replace the alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+ Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Vehicle not starting. Grinding noise when starting. Solution: Replace the starter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+ Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Body Panels:** Description/Symptoms: Rust spots or corrosion on the exterior body panels, particularly around the wheel wells, door frames, and tailgate. Solution: Repair or replace the affected body panels. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+ Recalls: None Found
* **Tailgate Lock Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Tailgate not opening or closing properly. Solution: Replace the tailgate lock. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+ Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues:** Description/Symptoms: Climate control not blowing air or not blowing cold air. Solution: Replace the climate control module or actuator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ Recalls: None Found
* **Power Seat Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Power seat not moving properly or not adjusting. Solution: Replace the power seat motor or switch. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+ Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 1997 INFINITI QX4 is a reliable and comfortable SUV. However, it has some common issues that can be costly to fix, such as transmission slippage and suspension sagging. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor in the potential cost of repairs when considering purchasing a used QX4.
