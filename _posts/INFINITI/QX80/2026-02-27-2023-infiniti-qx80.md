# 2023 INFINITI QX80 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Infiniti QX80 serves as a traditional, heavy-duty luxury SUV powered by a muscular V8 engine. While early ownership reports are limited, buyers looking at the pre-owned market should still evaluate standard luxury vehicle maintenance costs and inspect tech features carefully.

---

## Engine

### 5.6L V8 Engine

The 5.6-liter V8 engine produces roughly 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque, providing robust towing capacity and traditional performance. This powertrain is generally sturdy and well-established, though fuel economy is notably low.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to either the rear wheels or an available four-wheel-drive system. It operates reliably under normal driving conditions when properly maintained.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Display System

Cabin electronics manage infotainment, driver aids, and vehicle settings.

**Display Flickering and Audio Interruption**
- **Symptoms:** The forward collision warning indicator stays illuminated for extended periods while the infotainment display blinks on and off, disrupting audio playback and smartphone integration.
- **Solution:** Have a dealership inspect the head unit software and wiring harnesses for potential updates or loose connections.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior glass and trim components require careful physical inspection during a used vehicle purchase.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The glass panel on the sunroof may fracture or explode suddenly with a loud noise without any prior impact or overhead debris.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame for structural stress or defects.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V455000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX80&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An overloaded vehicle can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install new labels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Infiniti QX80 offers strong luxury presence and traditional V8 capability, though buyers should thoroughly check electronic infotainment functions and exterior glass condition during a pre-purchase inspection.
