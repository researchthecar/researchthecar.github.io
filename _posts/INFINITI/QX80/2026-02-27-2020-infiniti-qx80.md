# 2020 INFINITI QX80 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Infiniti QX80, buyers will find a traditional, heavy-duty luxury full-size SUV powered by a brawny V8 engine. While its towing capacity and opulent cabin appeal to many families, prospective owners should review available owner feedback regarding occasional electronics behavior and advanced driver assistance quirks.

---

## Engine

### 5.6L V8 Engine

The 5.6-liter V8 engine produces 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque, offering robust acceleration and strong towing capability for this full-size luxury SUV. It is generally a sturdy, traditional powerplant, though some owners have reported unusual behavior when aggressive cruise control resume commands prompt sudden high-RPM downshifts [nhtsa-complaint-11337507].

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to either the rear wheels or the available four-wheel-drive system. It generally operates reliably, working in tandem with the V8 engine to manage highway cruising and heavy loads.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior glass components are generally durable, though isolated incidents of spontaneous sunroof failure have been reported by owners.

**Shattering Sunroof Glass**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass may spontaneously shatter while driving without prior warning or external impact [nhtsa-complaint-11718855].
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the frame assembly for any structural stress points.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

The forward collision avoidance system provides safety features, but select driver reports indicate unexpected automated braking activations.

**Phantom Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may automatically and aggressively engage the emergency brakes without cause while driving at city or highway speeds, sometimes triggered by passing under bridges [nhtsa-complaint-11582054, nhtsa-complaint-11463241].
- **Solution:** Have a dealer or qualified technician inspect the forward-facing radar and camera sensors for proper calibration, alignment, or software updates.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V373000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump:Control/Drive Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX80&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V455000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX80&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An overloaded vehicle can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install new labels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Infiniti QX80 delivers classic V8 performance, generous interior space, and substantial capability for buyers seeking a large luxury SUV. While major mechanical failures are uncommon, used car shoppers should thoroughly test the driver-assistance technologies and inspect glass and electronic features during a pre-purchase test drive.
