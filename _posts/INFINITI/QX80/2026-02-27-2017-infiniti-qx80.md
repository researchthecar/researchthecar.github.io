# 2017 INFINITI QX80

### Overview
The 2017 INFINITI QX80 is a full-size luxury SUV known for its powerful V8 engine and spacious interior, designed for comfortable long-distance cruising and towing. As a large, body-on-frame SUV, it prioritizes robust construction and a commanding presence. While generally considered reliable, like any vehicle, it can experience specific issues as it ages. This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers. The focus is on components that have shown a tendency for premature failure or widespread complaints.

## Suspension

### Hydraulic Body-Based Suspension System
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle leaning to one side, "Service Vehicle Soon" warning light, loss of self-leveling capability.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace leaking hydraulic lines, pump, or actuators.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive, system freezes, navigation glitches.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2000


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, requiring jump starts, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Diagnose parasitic draw from modules or accessories, replace faulty battery.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 INFINITI QX80 demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly with its powertrain. The 5.6L V8 engine and 7-speed automatic transmission are robust and rarely present significant issues beyond normal wear. The primary areas where owners might encounter problems are related to the advanced Hydraulic Body-Based Suspension System, which can develop leaks, and certain electrical components, such as the infotainment screen and potential battery drain issues. Interior climate control actuators can also be a point of failure. While these issues are not universal, they represent the most commonly reported technical concerns for this model year. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are recommended.
