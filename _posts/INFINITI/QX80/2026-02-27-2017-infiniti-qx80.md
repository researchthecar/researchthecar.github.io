# 2017 INFINITI QX80 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Infiniti QX80 is a large, luxury full-size SUV powered by a robust V8 engine. While offering strong towing capabilities and a spacious cabin, used car buyers should carefully inspect electrical systems and safety features, as owner reports highlight occasional unexpected glitches.

---

## Engine

### 5.6L V8 Engine

The 2017 Infiniti QX80 comes equipped with a 5.6-liter V8 engine producing roughly 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant generally delivers strong performance and towing capacity, though routine electrical upkeep is vital to prevent unexpected stalls.

**Unexpected Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly shut off while driving or turning in traffic, accompanied by a sudden loss of electrical power and a dead battery.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the electrical charging system, battery terminals, and alternator for faults.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission comes paired with rear-wheel drive or four-wheel drive systems. It offers smooth shifting characteristics and robust durability in normal driving conditions.

## Electrical

### Electrical Network

The advanced electronics architecture on the QX80 supports numerous luxury and driver-assist features, making comprehensive pre-purchase scans essential.

**Network Communication Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience various electronic glitches, disabled safety systems, or diagnostic trouble codes indicating communication network jams.
- **Solution:** Inspect software modules, telematics control units, and wiring harnesses for proper configuration and connectivity.

## Safety

### Airbags and SRS

Safety equipment generally performs well, but sporadic reports point to sensitive occupant detection and airbag warning system faults.

**Airbag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning light may illuminate unexpectedly or flash continuously on the dashboard without any prior collision.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a dealership or certified specialist to pull diagnostic codes and inspect the seat modules or control units.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V455000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX80&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An overloaded vehicle can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install new labels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Infiniti QX80 remains a comfortable and capable luxury cruiser, but prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to the electrical systems, battery health, and electronic safety aids to ensure trouble-free ownership.
