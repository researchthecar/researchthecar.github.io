The 2022 INFINITI QX80 is a full-size luxury SUV. It's available with a 5.6L V8 engine and a 7-speed automatic transmission. The QX80 is praised for its powerful engine, spacious interior, and comfortable ride. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle, including:

**Engine**

* **5.6L V8**
    * **Excessive oil consumption:** Some owners have reported that their QX80s consume excessive amounts of oil, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage. The issue can be caused by worn piston rings or valve seals.
    * **Solution:** Replacing the worn components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+
    * **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V607000
* **5.6L V8 Hybrid**
    * **Hybrid battery failure:** Some owners have reported that the hybrid battery in their QX80s has failed, resulting in a loss of power and reduced fuel economy. The issue can be caused by a faulty battery module or a malfunctioning battery control unit.
    * **Solution:** Replacing the failed battery module or battery control unit.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $5,000 - $10,000+
    * **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V383000

**Transmission**

* **7-speed automatic**
    * **Shifting problems:** Some owners have reported that their QX80s experience shifting problems, such as slipping gears or delayed shifts. The issue can be caused by a faulty transmission control module or a worn transmission solenoid.
    * **Solution:** Replacing the faulty transmission control module or transmission solenoid.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Some owners have reported that their QX80s experience battery drain, even when the vehicle is not in use. The issue can be caused by a faulty electrical component or a parasitic drain.
    * **Solution:** Identifying and replacing the faulty electrical component or repairing the parasitic drain.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system issues:** Some owners have reported that their QX80s experience infotainment system issues, such as freezing, crashing, or losing connectivity. The issue can be caused by a software bug or a faulty infotainment module.
    * **Solution:** Updating the infotainment software or replacing the faulty infotainment module.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 INFINITI QX80 is a reliable vehicle with a powerful engine and spacious interior. However, some owners have reported issues with the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity and cost of the issues vary depending on the specific problem.
