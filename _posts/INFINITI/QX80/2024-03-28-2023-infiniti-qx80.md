**Introduction:**

The 2023 INFINITI QX80 is a full-size luxury SUV powered by a 5.6L V8 engine paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. It offers a spacious interior, a powerful engine, and advanced safety features. However, some owners have reported specific issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**5.6L V8**

* **Timing Chain Guide Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear or failure of the timing chain guide can lead to a loss of engine power, rough idling, and engine noise.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain guide and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Valve Deposits:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Carbon buildup on intake valves can cause reduced engine performance, fuel economy, and rough idling.
    * Solution: Cleaning or replacement of intake valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip or hesitate during gear changes, especially when accelerating.
    * Solution: Replacement or repair of the transmission components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear or failure of the front strut mount can cause noise, vibration, and decreased ride comfort.
    * Solution: Replacement of the strut mount.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear air suspension compressor may fail, causing the vehicle to sag or have an uneven ride height.
    * Solution: Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery may drain prematurely, especially when parked.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical components, such as alternator or parasitic draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rack Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Wind noise or whistling noises may occur when driving with the roof rack installed.
    * Solution: Re-tightening or replacing affected components on the roof rack.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Heater/AC Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The heater or air conditioning system may not function properly, resulting in insufficient heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the HVAC system, including components such as the blower motor or refrigerant leak.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Navigation System Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system, including the navigation feature, may malfunction or freeze.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of affected components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the INFINITI QX80 offers a luxurious and powerful experience, some owners have encountered specific issues with various components. The most common issues seem to be related to the engine, transmission, and suspension, which can be costly to repair. However, it is important to note that these issues are not widespread and may not affect every vehicle.
