# 2021 INFINITI QX80

### Overview
The 2021 INFINITI QX80, a full-size luxury SUV, generally presents a solid reliability record, particularly concerning its powertrain. Built on a robust platform, it aims to provide a comfortable and capable driving experience for families and those needing substantial towing or hauling capacity. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with few major mechanical concerns, like any vehicle, there are isolated reports of specific issues that, while not widespread, are worth noting for potential buyers. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, offering insights into potential areas of concern and their typical resolutions.

**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**

### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery may drain unexpectedly, leading to starting issues.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 INFINITI QX80 demonstrates strong reliability, especially with its proven 5.6L V8 engine and 7-speed automatic transmission, which are generally trouble-free. The primary areas where minor issues have been reported are the advanced Hydraulic Body-based Suspension System (HBMC), which can occasionally develop fluid leaks, and the infotainment system, which may experience occasional freezing or unresponsiveness. Battery drain issues have also been noted by a small number of owners. Overall, the QX80 is a dependable choice in the large luxury SUV segment, with most potential concerns being relatively minor and manageable, rather than indicative of systemic powertrain weaknesses.
