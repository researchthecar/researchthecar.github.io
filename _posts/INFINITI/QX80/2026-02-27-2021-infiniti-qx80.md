# 2021 INFINITI QX80 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Infiniti QX80 is a large luxury SUV that offers robust capability and a traditional V8 driving experience. While many owners enjoy its comfort and towing power, used car buyers should review owner-reported concerns regarding engine performance, body components, and visibility. Prospective purchasers are encouraged to thoroughly inspect powertrain operation and vehicle history before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 5.6L V8 Engine

Powered by a 5.6-liter V8 engine producing around 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque, providing strong towing capacity. Overall reliability is generally solid, though owners have reported occasional fuel system and acceleration concerns.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected engine stalling while driving and occasional failure to accelerate properly.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a qualified technician is required to inspect the fuel delivery system and related powertrain components.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission available in rear-wheel drive or four-wheel drive configurations. It delivers smooth shifting characteristics, with no prominent recurring mechanical failure trends reported by owners.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Body Trim

Exterior complaints center around rare glass breakage and cosmetic paint or trim deterioration.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced instances where the sunroof glass shattered unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds without prior impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame for structural stress or alignment issues.

**Paint Peeling and Trim Bubbling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle paint has been observed peeling on exterior surfaces like the rear bumper, alongside bubbling on the front grille trim.
- **Solution:** Consult a professional auto body shop for repainting or trim replacement options.

### Exterior Lighting

Lighting complaints are primarily design-oriented rather than indicative of electrical component failures.

**Rear Turn Signal Placement**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have expressed safety concerns regarding the low mounting position of the rear turn signals, potentially reducing visibility to trailing drivers.
- **Solution:** No standard mechanical fix exists as this relates to factory design specifications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V373000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump:Control/Drive Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX80&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V599000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX80&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rear-view camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V455000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX80&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An overloaded vehicle can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install new labels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Infiniti QX80 is a capable luxury cruiser with a powerful V8 engine, but used car shoppers should remain vigilant. Reviewing vehicle service records for any history of stalling, inspecting exterior glass, and verifying overall cosmetic condition will help ensure a satisfying ownership experience.
