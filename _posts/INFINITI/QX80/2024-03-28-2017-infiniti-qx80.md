## 2017 INFINITI QX80

The 2017 INFINITI QX80 is a full-size luxury SUV with seating for seven. It is offered with one engine option, a 5.6L V8 engine that produces 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque, paired with a seven-speed automatic transmission. The QX80 is known for its spacious interior, powerful engine, and luxurious features. However, it has also been known to have some issues.

## Engine
**5.6L V8**

**Valve Body Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, hard shifting, or complete loss of gears.
Solution: Replace the valve body.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+

**Timing Chain Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, loss of power, or complete engine failure.
Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $7,000+

**Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 21V392000

## Transmission
**7-Speed Automatic**

**Torque Converter Shudder**
Description/Symptoms: Vibrations or shuddering during acceleration or shifting.
Solution: Replace the torque converter.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+

**Recalls:** None Found

## Interior
**Cruise Control Switch Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Cruise control not engaging or disengaging, or intermittent operation.
Solution: Replace the cruise control switch.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Rear Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Rear climate control not working properly, blowing hot or cold air intermittently.
Solution: Replace the rear climate control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+

## Summary

The 2017 INFINITI QX80 is a generally reliable vehicle, but it has been known to have some issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage. It is important to have any issues addressed promptly to prevent further damage or safety concerns.
