# 2019 INFINITI QX80 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Infiniti QX80, buyers will find a commanding, luxury full-size SUV powered by a robust V8 engine. While luxury, comfort, and towing capacity are strong suits, prospective owners should review owner-reported grievances concerning electrical drains, unexpected electronic braking interventions, and visibility issues.

---

## Engine

### 5.6L V8 Engine

The 2019 Infiniti QX80 is powered by a 5.6-liter V8 engine producing roughly 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque. This traditional naturally aspirated powerplant offers strong towing capabilities and smooth performance, though fuel economy is expectedly low.

**Erratic Fuel Gauge**
- **Symptoms:** The fuel gauge provides inaccurate or erratic readings, alternating between full and empty without warning.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel level sensors and replace components if faulty.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Power is routed through a 7-speed automatic transmission available in rear-wheel drive or four-wheel drive configurations. It generally provides smooth gear changes suited for a large luxury cruiser.

## Electrical

### Battery and Charging System

Electrical complaints center on recurring battery drains that leave owners stranded.

**Premature Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle repeatedly fails to start due to a completely drained battery, requiring frequent jumps or premature replacements.
- **Solution:** Perform a parasitic draw test to isolate electrical accessories that may remain powered when the vehicle is off.

### Backup Camera

Safety and visibility concerns mainly focus on low-light performance of driver assistance cameras.

**Poor Nighttime Visibility**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera display appears extremely dark, blurry, and grainy when operating in low-light or nighttime conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera lens cleanliness or consult a dealer for potential display adjustments.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior body components are generally durable, though rare instances of glass breakage have been reported.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The glass sunroof panel suddenly explodes or shatters while driving under normal conditions without external impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the frame for structural stress.
- **Cost:** $2500+

## Safety

### Forward Emergency Braking

Braking system feedback highlights occasional unexpected interventions from driver-assist safety tech.

**Phantom Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The forward emergency braking system unexpectedly triggers audible warnings and applies the brakes hard without an actual obstruction.
- **Solution:** Have the dealer check for software updates or sensor calibration issues for the collision avoidance system.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V654000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX80&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 26V455000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX80&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An overloaded vehicle can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install new labels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Infiniti QX80 delivers strong V8 performance and a plush cabin, making it an appealing option for luxury SUV buyers. However, used car shoppers should thoroughly check the electrical system for battery drain issues, verify the behavior of the automated emergency braking system, and inspect the backup camera during a test drive.
