# 2022 INFINITI QX80 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Infiniti QX80 is a large, luxury body-on-frame SUV powered by a robust V8 engine paired with a traditional automatic transmission. As buyers shop the used market for this flagship vehicle, understanding its baseline powertrain options and minor reported electronic quirks can help guide a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 5.6L V8 Engine

The 5.6-liter naturally aspirated V8 produces 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque, delivering strong towing capacity and traditional power delivery. It is generally a durable powerplant, though buyers should verify regular maintenance history.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 7-speed automatic transmission comes paired with either rear-wheel drive or four-wheel drive. It offers smooth highway cruising shifts and dependable long-term operation under normal driving conditions.

## Electrical

### Center Stack Display

Infotainment and display electronics should be thoroughly tested during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure all features function without unexpected resets.

**Center Display System Fault**
- **Symptoms:** The center stack display turns off completely or continuously resets, affecting the backup camera, audio, and navigation systems.
- **Solution:** Have a dealership or qualified technician inspect the infotainment software and wiring harness connections; a module update or replacement may be required.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V455000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX80&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An overloaded vehicle can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install new labels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Infiniti QX80 combines stout V8 performance with a well-appointed luxury cabin, making it an appealing choice for buyers seeking a large utility vehicle. While initial owner reports note potential center display glitches, overall mechanical reliability remains tied to standard routine maintenance checks during your used car search.
