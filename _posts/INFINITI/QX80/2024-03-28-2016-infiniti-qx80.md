**Introduction to the 2016 INFINITI QX80:**

The 2016 INFINITI QX80 is a full-size luxury SUV known for its powerful performance, spacious interior, and advanced technology. It is available with a single engine option, a 5.6-liter V8 producing 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. People appreciate the QX80 for its luxurious cabin, comfortable ride, and powerful engine, while some dislikes include its high fuel consumption and outdated infotainment system.

**Engine**
**5.6L V8**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
  * Description/Symptoms: The engine consumes excessive amounts of oil, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
  * Solution: Replace piston rings.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Delayed Shifting**
  * Description/Symptoms: The transmission hesitates or delays when shifting gears, especially during acceleration or upshifting.
  * Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module (TCM) or replace the valve body.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: The front strut mount wears out prematurely, causing a clunking noise over bumps and reduced handling.
  * Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Battery Discharge**
  * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains excessively when the vehicle is parked, especially in cold weather.
  * Solution: Replace the battery or check for electrical components draining the power.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
  * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulates inside the headlight assemblies, causing fogging or reduced visibility.
  * Solution: Replace the headlights.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Sunroof Drainage Issues**
  * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof drains clog, causing water to leak into the cabin.
  * Solution: Unclog the drains and replace any damaged components as needed.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Navigation System Lag**
  * Description/Symptoms: The navigation system responds slowly and experiences delays when inputting information or navigating.
  * Solution: Update the software or replace the navigation unit.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 INFINITI QX80 has some reported issues that owners should be aware of. The excessive oil consumption issue with the V8 engine is a serious concern that could lead to costly engine damage if not addressed promptly. The transmission issues, suspension issues, and electrical issues can also be inconvenient and expensive to fix. However, it is important to note that these issues do not affect all QX80 owners, and many have had positive experiences with the vehicle.
