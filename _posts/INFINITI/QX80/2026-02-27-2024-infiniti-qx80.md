### Summary Verdict
The 2024 INFINITI QX80 demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components, the 5.6L V8 engine and 7-speed automatic transmission, which show no widespread issues. The robust suspension system also appears to be holding up well. However, potential owners should be aware of some reported electrical gremlins, specifically concerning the infotainment system's responsiveness and intermittent battery drain issues. These electrical problems, while not affecting all vehicles, can be a source of frustration and incur moderate repair costs. Exterior and interior components generally perform as expected without significant reported defects. With proactive attention to potential electrical concerns, the QX80 is poised to be a dependable luxury SUV.
