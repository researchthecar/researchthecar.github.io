# 2024 INFINITI QX80 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Infiniti QX80 represents the final year of the model's rugged, V8-powered generation, offering immense towing capacity and three rows of luxurious seating. While overall volume from owner reports is exceptionally low, prospective buyers should review available electrical and body glass feedback during their pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 5.6L V8 Engine

Equipped with a 5.6-liter naturally aspirated V8 engine producing around 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque. This traditional powerplant is generally robust and well-proven, though fuel economy is heavy by modern standards.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Utilizes a 7-speed automatic transmission paired with either rear-wheel drive or Infiniti's intelligent four-wheel-drive system. It delivers smooth highway cruising power with minimal reported mechanical hesitation.

## Electrical

### Charging System

Electrical accessories and vehicle charging systems generally function well, though isolated battery drain concerns have been reported by owners.

**Battery Drain / Low Voltage**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences recurring weak or no-start conditions even after replacing the battery, as the charging system may struggle to maintain proper voltage during normal driving patterns.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician test the alternator, variable voltage control system (VVCS), and parasitic draw to isolate the root cause.

## Exterior

### Rear Windshield

Body panels and exterior trim are solidly built, though rare reports of spontaneous glass breakage warrant a close look at rear window condition.

**Spontaneous Rear Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The rear window shatters suddenly while the vehicle is parked, without any signs of external impact or vandalism.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered rear liftgate glass and inspect the window frame and defroster grid connections for defects.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V455000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX80&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An overloaded vehicle can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install new labels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Infiniti QX80 is a capable and luxurious large SUV backed by very few early owner complaints. Buyers should still conduct a thorough inspection of the electrical charging system and verify that exterior glass is free of stress fractures before finalizing a purchase.
