# 2016 INFINITI QX80 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Infiniti QX80 is a large, luxury full-size SUV offering powerful performance and a spacious three-row cabin. While generally robust, used shoppers should review known owner complaints regarding electrical quirks, body accessories, and occasional mechanical or braking concerns before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 5.6L V8 (VK56VD)

The 2016 Infiniti QX80 is powered by a 5.6-liter V8 engine (VK56VD) producing 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque. This naturally aspirated powerplant delivers strong towing capacity and smooth acceleration, though isolated owner reports note minor fuel system and electrical sensor anomalies.

**Fuel Odor or Gauge Inaccuracies**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported strong fuel odors around the vehicle or erratic gas gauge readings dropping unexpectedly after filling up.
- **Solution:** Have the fuel system, filler neck, and sending units inspected by a qualified technician.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission available in rear-wheel or four-wheel drive configurations, this gearbox generally offers smooth shifting. However, rare valve body failures have been reported by owners experiencing gear limitation.

**Transmission Sticking in Lower Gears**
- **Symptoms:** Some drivers experience the vehicle refusing to shift past third gear due to internal valve body issues.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a dealership or transmission specialist may require replacement or repair of the valve body assembly.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake performance is generally strong for a heavy luxury SUV, though owners have noted rare mechanical hardware failures such as dislodged caliper pins.

**Brake Caliper Pin Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal travels all the way to the floor and calipers make contact with the inside of the wheel rim.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake hardware, slide pins, and calipers immediately, replacing any compromised components.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior panels and trim hold up well, though isolated complaints highlight unexpected sunroof glass breakage under wind pressure.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** An abnormal booming noise occurs while driving at highway speeds, followed by the sunroof glass suddenly shattering.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged sunroof glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame for alignment or stress points.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Safety systems function well overall, but electrical voltage fluctuations and sensitive passenger occupancy classification sensors can occasionally trigger warning lights.

**Airbag Warning Light and Occupant Sensor**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning light illuminates intermittently due to low voltage or passenger seat occupancy sensors failing to properly recognize passengers.
- **Solution:** Check battery connections, voltage levels, and have dealership diagnostic tools recalibrate or replace faulty seat sensor components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V455000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX80&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An overloaded vehicle can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install new labels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Infiniti QX80 is a capable and luxurious family cruiser with a stout V8 engine. While most owners enjoy trouble-free driving, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle for rare issues such as transmission valve body operation, brake hardware integrity, electrical sensor performance, and sunroof condition during a pre-purchase inspection.
