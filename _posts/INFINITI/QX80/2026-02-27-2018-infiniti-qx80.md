# 2018 INFINITI QX80 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2018 Infiniti QX80, buyers will find a large, luxury full-size SUV powered by a robust V8 engine. While it offers premium comfort and strong towing capabilities, prospective owners should review owner-reported areas of concern regarding electronics and body components to ensure a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 5.6L V8 Engine

The 5.6-liter V8 engine produces approximately 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration and towing capacity for this large SUV. It is generally known as a durable powerplant, though routine maintenance is essential for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 7-speed automatic transmission available in rear-wheel or four-wheel drive configurations, this gearbox delivers smooth power delivery. It maintains a solid reputation for durability under normal driving conditions.

## Steering

### Steering Wheel Trim

Some owners have noted physical cosmetic wear and cracking on steering wheel trim pieces.

**Steering Wheel Trim Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** The colored plastic trim on the steering wheel cracks and may lift when turning the wheel.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering wheel trim and replace damaged plastic components to prevent potential interference while driving.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster Lighting

Owner reports indicate potential dimming or failure of specific segments within the instrument cluster lighting.

**Instrument Cluster Illumination Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard bulbs or instrument cluster lighting begin to dim or go out completely, particularly around the high end of the RPM gauge and low end of the speedometer.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the instrument cluster and replace faulty bulbs or the cluster assembly as needed.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Isolated complaints mention unexpected sunroof glass failure during normal operation.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A loud bang is heard and the sunroof glass shatters outward spontaneously without any external impact.
- **Solution:** Clear broken glass, inspect the sunroof frame for structural damage, and replace the glass assembly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V654000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX80&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 26V455000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX80&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An overloaded vehicle can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install new labels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Infiniti QX80 is a capable and luxurious full-size SUV backed by a dependable V8 powertrain. While most mechanical systems prove sturdy, buyers should thoroughly test interior electronics, inspect instrument lighting, and check exterior glass and trim during a pre-purchase inspection.
