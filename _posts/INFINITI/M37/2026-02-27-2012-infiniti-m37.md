### Summary Verdict
The 2012 INFINITI M37 is a reliable luxury sedan with a strong mechanical foundation. The 3.7L V6 engine and 7-speed automatic transmission are generally trouble-free. The most commonly reported issues tend to be in the electrical system, specifically related to infotainment glitches and occasional battery drain, which are often resolvable with software updates or component diagnosis. Other areas like suspension, exterior, and interior components show good durability. Overall, the M37 offers a dependable ownership experience for those seeking a blend of performance and luxury, provided potential electrical quirks are addressed.
