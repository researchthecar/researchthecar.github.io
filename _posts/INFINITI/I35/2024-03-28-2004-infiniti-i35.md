**Introduction**

The 2004 INFINITI I35 is a midsize luxury sedan known for its comfortable interior, stylish design, and powerful engine. It is available with a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with either a 5-speed automatic transmission or a 6-speed manual transmission.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6 (5-speed automatic transmission)**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
 * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating, rough idle
 * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
 * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
 * Recalls: N/A
* **Timing Chain Guide Wear**
 * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, reduced power, engine failure
 * Solution: Replace timing chain and guides
 * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
 * Recalls: N/A

**3.5L V6 (6-speed manual transmission)**

* **Rear Main Seal Leak**
 * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the rear of the engine
 * Solution: Replace rear main seal
 * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
 * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**5-speed automatic transmission**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
 * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leak, slipping gears
 * Solution: Replace transmission fluid seal or filter
 * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
 * Recalls: N/A

**6-speed manual transmission**

* **Clutch Failure**
 * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping clutch
 * Solution: Replace clutch
 * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
 * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure**
 * Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, reduced handling
 * Solution: Replace rear shock absorbers
 * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
 * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
 * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting
 * Solution: Replace battery or check for electrical system issues
 * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
 * Recalls: N/A
* **Alternator Failure**
 * Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, dashboard warning lights, electrical system issues
 * Solution: Replace alternator
 * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
 * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leak**
 * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin through the sunroof
 * Solution: Replace sunroof seal or drain tubes
 * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
 * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Center Console Trim Cracking**
 * Description/Symptoms: Cracking or peeling trim on the center console
 * Solution: Replace center console trim
 * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
 * Recalls: N/A
* **Window Switch Failure**
 * Description/Symptoms: Windows not operating properly, stuck windows
 * Solution: Replace window switch or window motor
 * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
 * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

The 2004 INFINITI I35 generally has a good reputation for reliability, but it is not immune to some common issues. Engine and transmission problems can be costly to repair, ranging from $500 to over $3,000. Suspension and electrical issues are also worth noting, although they are typically less expensive to fix. Overall, the I35 is a solid choice for those seeking a comfortable and stylish sedan, but potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues associated with this vehicle.
