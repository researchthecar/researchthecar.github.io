# 2012 INFINITI EX35

### Overview
The 2012 INFINITI EX35, a luxury crossover, generally presents a solid reliability record, particularly for its powertrain. Owners often praise its smooth ride and premium interior. However, like any vehicle, it's not entirely immune to potential issues. Common areas of concern, though not universally reported, tend to be concentrated in specific electronic components and, less frequently, some aspects of the suspension. The VQ37VHR engine, known for its robust performance, is a strong point. Overall, the EX35 offers a good balance of luxury and dependability, with most reported problems being manageable and not indicative of systemic failures across the model range. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring its continued reliability.

## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Front Suspension Noises**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or rattling sounds from the front end, especially over bumps.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn suspension components such as control arms, ball joints, or bushings.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Navigation System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, or inaccurate GPS positioning.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Keyless Entry/Start System
**Intermittent Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle not recognizing the key fob, difficulty starting the engine.
- **Solution:** Reprogramming of key fobs, replacement of key fob battery, or potential issues with the receiver module.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 INFINITI EX35 demonstrates good overall reliability, with its 3.7L V6 engine and automatic transmission being particularly dependable. The most commonly cited issues are related to the electrical system, specifically the infotainment and keyless entry/start systems, which can experience glitches or intermittent malfunctions. Some owners have also reported noises from the front suspension, typically requiring replacement of worn components. Exterior and interior elements are generally robust. With proactive maintenance and attention to potential electrical quirks, the EX35 should continue to provide a reliable and enjoyable ownership experience.
