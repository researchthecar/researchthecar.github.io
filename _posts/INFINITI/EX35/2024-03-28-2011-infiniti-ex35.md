## 2011 INFINITI EX35

The 2011 INFINITI EX35 is a luxury crossover SUV that combines performance, comfort, and style. It is equipped with a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 297 horsepower and 253 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. The EX35 offers a well-appointed interior with premium materials and advanced technology features. It has received praise for its refined driving experience, spacious interior, and impressive safety ratings. However, some owners have reported certain issues with their vehicles.

### Engine

* **V6 3.5L**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating engine, rough idle
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, white smoke from exhaust, overheating engine
        * Solution: Replace cylinder head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

* **5-Speed Automatic**
    * **Harsh Shifting**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rough gear changes, delayed or hesitant shifting
        * Solution: Fluid flush, transmission control module reprogramming, or transmission rebuild
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Transmission Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Inability to engage gears, loss of power while driving
        * Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

* **Front Control Arm Bushings Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the front of the vehicle, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace front control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorbers Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking shocks, poor ride quality, excessive body roll
    * Solution: Replace rear shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Electrical

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharge, warning light illumination, dimmed lights
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, battery light illumination
        * Solution: Identify and fix the source of the battery drain, such as a faulty module or parasitic draw
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

* **Headlight Corrosion**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hazy or yellowed headlights, reduced light output
    * Solution: Replace headlights or restore them with a headlight restoration kit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Hatch Shocks Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to hold the rear hatch open, hatch sagging or falling
    * Solution: Replace rear hatch shocks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Interior

* **Dashboard Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks or splits in the dashboard material
    * Solution: Replace dashboard or repair cracks with adhesive
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to adjust temperature or airflow, AC unit not cooling or heating properly
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty sensors, actuators, or control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Other

* **Fuel Pump Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting, reduced fuel efficiency
    * Solution: Replace fuel pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Brake Pad and Rotor Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squealing or grinding noises when braking, reduced braking effectiveness
    * Solution: Replace brake pads and rotors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

## Summary

Overall, the 2011 INFINITI EX35 is generally a reliable vehicle. However, some owners have reported issues with the intake manifold gasket, cylinder head gasket, transmission, and suspension components. The severity and cost of repairs can vary depending on the specific issue and the extent of the damage. Regular maintenance and timely repairs can help prevent or mitigate some of these problems.
