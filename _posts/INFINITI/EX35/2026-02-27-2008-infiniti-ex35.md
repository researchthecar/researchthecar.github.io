# 2008 INFINITI EX35

### Overview
The 2008 INFINITI EX35 is a luxury crossover that aimed to blend sporty driving dynamics with practicality. Powered by a robust V6 engine and typically paired with an automatic transmission, it offered a refined driving experience. Reliability for the EX35 is generally considered good, with owners reporting fewer significant mechanical issues compared to some competitors in its class. However, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen more attention from owners and mechanics. This analysis focuses on documented common issues, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear, to provide a clear picture of its long-term dependability.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L VQ35HR V6
**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil on engine components, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or other seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Coolant Leaks (Hoses/Radiator)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant leaks under the vehicle, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses or radiator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

## Electrical

### Standard System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, navigation issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Keyless Entry/Start Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, key fob not recognized.
- **Solution:** Battery replacement in key fob, or potential issues with the intelligent key system receiver.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2008 INFINITI EX35 demonstrates a generally solid reliability record. The powertrain, particularly the 3.5L V6 engine and the 5-speed automatic transmission, is known for its durability, though minor oil and coolant leaks can occur over time. Electrical components, such as the infotainment system and keyless entry, are the most frequently cited areas for potential issues, but these are often resolvable with updates or minor repairs. Suspension and exterior/interior components have not shown widespread problems, contributing to an overall positive reliability outlook for this model. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring its continued dependable performance.
