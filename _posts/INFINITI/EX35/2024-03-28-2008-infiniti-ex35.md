**Introduction:**

The 2008 INFINITI EX35 is a compact luxury crossover SUV powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine delivering 297 horsepower and 253 lb-ft of torque. It is available with either a 5-speed automatic or a 6-speed manual transmission. Drivers praise the EX35 for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and strong performance. However, there are some known issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6 Engine**

* **Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Engine overhaul or piston ring replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy rattling or ticking from the engine, indicating a failing timing chain tensioner.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain and tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission:**

* **Transmission Slippage or Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or shuddering when shifting gears, indicating clutch pack or torque converter issues.
    * Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual Transmission:**

* **No Known Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise and vibration from the suspension, particularly over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace the strut mount bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Sway Bar End Link Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from the rear suspension when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace the worn sway bar end links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, leading to a dead battery and difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Identify and fix the electrical component causing the drain (possibly a faulty alternator, parasitic draw, or damaged wiring).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Headlight Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights or taillights may not work intermittently or may stay on constantly.
    * Solution: Replace the headlight switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Significant Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Knobs Sticking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Temperature or fan speed control knobs become stuck or difficult to move.
    * Solution: Replace the climate control panel.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Seat Latch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear seat backrest latch fails, preventing the seat from being folded down.
    * Solution: Replace the latch mechanism.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2008 INFINITI EX35 is a generally reliable vehicle, but some common issues can be a concern. The most significant problems are the engine oil consumption and timing chain tensioner failure, which can be costly to repair. Other issues, such as suspension wear, electrical problems, and interior glitches, are less severe but still worth considering before purchasing this vehicle.
