**Introduction:**

The 2010 INFINITI EX35 is a luxury SUV known for its comfortable and stylish interior, powerful engine, and advanced features. It is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 297 horsepower and 253 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. Critics praise the EX35 for its smooth ride, responsive handling, and luxurious cabin. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of common issues that owners have encountered.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6**
    * **Excess Oil Consumption:** Some owners have reported issues with excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Description/Symptoms: Burning smell, blue smoke from exhaust, low oil level warnings
    * Solution: Replacing piston rings or installing oil consumption reduction kits
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slipping:** Owners have experienced instances of transmission slipping, causing hesitation, rough shifting, or failure to engage gears.
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or hesitant shifting, slipping gears, check engine light
    * Solution: Replacing or rebuilding the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure:** The control arm bushings in the front suspension can deteriorate prematurely, leading to knocking or clunking noises when driving over bumps.
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking sounds over rough surfaces, vibrations in steering wheel
    * Solution: Replacing the control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Some owners have encountered issues with excessive battery drain, especially after leaving the vehicle parked for extended periods.
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, slow engine crank, flickering lights
    * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing electrical system issues, such as faulty modules or parasitic drains
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:** The door lock actuators on the EX35 can malfunction, preventing doors from locking or unlocking properly.
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors not locking or unlocking, warning lights on instrument panel
    * Solution: Replacing the door lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system in some EX35s can experience issues, such as inconsistent temperature control, fan malfunctions, or complete failure.
    * Description/Symptoms: Blower not functioning, incorrect temperature regulation, strange noises
    * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing faulty components, such as sensors, actuators, or the control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2010 INFINITI EX35 is a reliable vehicle with some common issues to be aware of. The engine can experience excessive oil consumption, while the transmission may exhibit slipping or failure. The suspension can have prematurely failing control arm bushings, and electrical system issues can lead to battery drain. External issues include door lock actuator failure, while the interior can experience climate control malfunctions. While some repairs can be costly, most issues are manageable and do not affect the overall reliability of the EX35.
