### Summary Verdict
The 2009 INFINITI EX35 is generally considered a reliable vehicle, with its VQ35HR V6 engine and 5-speed automatic transmission being particularly robust and rarely reporting significant issues. Owners can expect dependable performance from the core mechanical components. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically related to infotainment system glitches and occasional battery drain issues. These are not typically catastrophic failures but can be inconvenient and require professional diagnosis. Overall, the EX35 offers a good balance of luxury and reliability, making it a solid choice in its segment, provided these minor electrical quirks are addressed.
