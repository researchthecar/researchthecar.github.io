**Introduction:**

The 2012 INFINITI EX35 is a compact luxury SUV powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine mated to a 7-speed automatic transmission. It produces 297 horsepower and 253 lb-ft of torque. The EX35 is praised for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and responsive handling. However, it has received criticism for its limited cargo space and lack of certain advanced safety features.

**Engine:**

* **3.5L V6:**

    * **VVEL Timing System Failure:**
      * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, reduced power, check engine light
      * Solution: Replacement of VVEL solenoids and timing chain
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
      * Recalls: 13V128000, 13V371000, 14V110000
    * **Oil Consumption:**
      * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
      * Solution: Piston ring replacement or engine overhaul
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
      * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

* **7-Speed Automatic:**

    * **Transmission Solenoid Failure:**
      * Description/Symptoms: Rough shifting, delayed engagement, check engine light
      * Solution: Replacement of transmission solenoids
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
      * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings:**
    * **Wear and Tear:**
      * Description/Symptoms: Squealing noise, poor handling, uneven tire wear
      * Solution: Replacement of control arm bushings
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * **Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Navigation System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: GPS not working, touchscreen unresponsive, screen freezing
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of navigation unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering headlights, moisture inside housing
    * Solution: Replacement of headlight module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Roof Rack Crossbars:**
    * **Corrosion and Rust:**
      * Description/Symptoms: Rust and corrosion on crossbars
      * Solution: Replacement of crossbars
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Sunroof Drain Clogs:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into cabin, sunroof not opening properly
    * Solution: Cleaning or replacement of sunroof drains
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Central Console Trim Peeling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Trim on central console peeling or cracking
    * Solution: Replacement of central console trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2012 INFINITI EX35 has some potential issues, primarily related to the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. While some of these issues can be costly to repair, many are relatively minor and affordable to fix. The EX35 remains a generally reliable vehicle, but potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before making a purchase.
