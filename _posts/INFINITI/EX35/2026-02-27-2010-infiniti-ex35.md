# 2010 INFINITI EX35

### Overview
The 2010 INFINITI EX35, a luxury crossover, generally presents a solid reliability record, particularly concerning its powertrain. It is equipped with a robust V6 engine known for its smooth performance and durability. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, there are a few areas that have seen occasional reports of issues. These tend to be minor and often related to specific components rather than systemic failures. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring the longevity of this vehicle, but major mechanical concerns are not a widespread characteristic of the 2010 EX35.

### Summary Verdict
The 2010 INFINITI EX35 is a reliable vehicle with a strong powertrain. The 3.5L V6 engine and 5-speed automatic transmission are generally trouble-free. Most reported issues are minor and related to the electrical system, such as infotainment glitches or occasional battery drain. These are not typically indicative of major mechanical flaws. With diligent maintenance, the EX35 should provide dependable service. Owners can expect a positive ownership experience with a low probability of significant repair costs outside of routine maintenance and the few noted electrical concerns.
