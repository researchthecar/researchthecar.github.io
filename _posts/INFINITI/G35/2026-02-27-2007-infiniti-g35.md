# 2007 INFINITI G35

### Overview
The 2007 INFINITI G35, a popular luxury sport sedan, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, particularly for its performance-oriented nature. It offered a potent V6 engine and a choice between a smooth automatic or engaging manual transmission. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with few major mechanical woes, like any vehicle, some common areas have seen more attention than others. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential issues owners might encounter with specific components.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L VQ35HR V6
**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil on engine components, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or other related seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Engine stalling, difficulty starting, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the crankshaft position sensor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow window operation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power window motor and regulator.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

**BCM (Body Control Module) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical gremlins, issues with lights, locks, or other accessories.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000+

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 INFINITI G35 is a solid choice for those seeking a blend of performance and luxury, with a generally good reliability record. The VQ35HR engine is robust, though owners should be aware of potential oil leaks from valve covers and the possibility of crankshaft position sensor failures. Transmissions, both automatic and manual, have proven to be durable. Electrical gremlins, such as window motor issues or BCM problems, are the most commonly cited non-routine repairs. Overall, with diligent maintenance and attention to these specific potential issues, the G35 can offer a rewarding ownership experience.
