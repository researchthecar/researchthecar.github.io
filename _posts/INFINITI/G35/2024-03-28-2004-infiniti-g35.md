**Introduction**

The 2004 INFINITI G35 is a luxury sports sedan powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine. It is available with either a 6-speed manual or 5-speed automatic transmission. The G35 is praised for its sporty handling, powerful engine, and comfortable interior. However, it has also received complaints regarding some specific issues.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, rough idling, reduced engine power.
* Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual**

**Synchronizer Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises during shifts.
* Solution: Replace the transmission synchronizers.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* Recalls: None Found

* **5-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Control Unit (TCU) Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, erratic shifting, check engine light.
* Solution: Replace the transmission control unit.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the front suspension, vibration.
* Solution: Replace the front lower control arm bushings.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Window Switch Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Windows not operating properly, intermittent function.
* Solution: Replace the window switch.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Panel Cracking**
* Description/Symptoms: Cracks appearing on the dashboard panel.
* Solution: Replace the dashboard panel.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2004 INFINITI G35 is a reliable and sporty luxury sedan with some potential issues. While the engine, transmission, and interior issues can be expensive to fix, the exterior and suspension issues are generally less costly. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues are relatively minor compared to other vehicles in its class.
