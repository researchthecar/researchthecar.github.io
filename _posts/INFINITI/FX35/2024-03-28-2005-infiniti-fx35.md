**2005 INFINITI FX35**

The 2005 INFINITI FX35 is a mid-size luxury crossover SUV that debuted in 2003. It was offered with a single engine option, a 3.5-liter V6, and two transmission options: a 5-speed automatic and a 6-speed manual. The V6 produced 280 horsepower and 275 lb-ft of torque. Despite its positive reviews for its powerful engine, stylish design, and comfortable interior, the FX35 has also been known to experience several issues.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Intake camshaft position sensor failure:**
    * Symptoms: Engine stalling, rough idling, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace the intake camshaft position sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Variable valve timing (VVT) control solenoid failure:**
    * Symptoms: Engine knocking, reduced power, illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace the VVT control solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Exhaust camshaft sensor failure:**
    * Symptoms: Engine stalling, rough idling, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace the exhaust camshaft sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque converter failure:**
    * Symptoms: Transmission slipping, shuddering, overheating
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shift solenoid failure:**
    * Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, transmission slipping, illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace the shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch master cylinder failure:**
    * Symptoms: Leaking fluid, difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace the clutch master cylinder
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front strut mount failure:**
    * Symptoms: Noises while driving over bumps, reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace the strut mount
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+ per side
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear sway bar link failure:**
    * Symptoms: Clunking noises while driving over bumps
    * Solution: Replace the sway bar link
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+ per side
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure:**
    * Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery not charging
    * Solution: Replace the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery failure:**
    * Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Replace the battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Scratching on the clear coat:**
    * Symptoms: Superficial damage to the clear coat
    * Solution: Can be repaired through detailing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* **Door handle failure:**
    * Symptoms: Difficulty opening the door
    * Solution: Replace the door handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per door
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 05V333000

**Interior**

* **Center console lid failure:**
    * Symptoms: Lid breaking, not closing properly
    * Solution: Replace the center console lid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Plastic trim cracking:**
    * Symptoms: Cracking on the plastic trim
    * Solution: Replace the trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2005 INFINITI FX35 is a reliable and enjoyable vehicle. However, it has been known to experience several issues, most notably with the engine and transmission. While the severity of these issues varies, it is important for potential buyers to be aware of them before purchasing.
