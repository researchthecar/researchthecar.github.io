The 2010 INFINITI FX35 is a luxury crossover SUV that offers a blend of sporty performance and upscale features. The FX35 is powered by a 3.5-liter VQ35HR V6 engine producing 303 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque. A 7-speed automatic transmission is the only transmission option available. Some owners appreciate the FX35's stylish design, comfortable ride, and strong engine. Others find the rear passenger and cargo space limited, and fuel economy to be below average. A less common engine option for the 2010 INFINITI FX is a 5.0-liter V8 producing 390 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6 (VQ35HR)**

*   **Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported higher than expected oil consumption, requiring frequent top-offs between oil changes. This can manifest as a burning oil smell, low oil level warning lights, or visible exhaust smoke.
    *   Solution: Regular monitoring of oil levels and using the recommended oil type can help mitigate the issue. In severe cases, repair may involve replacing valve stem seals or piston rings.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $4,000+ (depending on the extent of the repair)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

*   **Torque Converter Shudder**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners report a shudder or vibration during light acceleration, typically at lower speeds. This can be due to wear or degradation of the torque converter.
    *   Solution: A transmission fluid flush can sometimes alleviate the issue temporarily. In more severe cases, the torque converter may need to be replaced.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 (fluid flush) - $1,500+ (torque converter replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Front Strut Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Premature failure of the front struts, leading to a bouncy ride, poor handling, and clunking noises when driving over bumps.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the front struts.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800 (for both struts)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Navigation System/Infotainment Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Freezing, slow response times, or complete failure of the navigation system or infotainment screen.
    *   Solution: Software updates may resolve minor issues. In more severe cases, the navigation unit may need to be replaced.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (for software updates) - $1,000+ (for unit replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Automatic Headlight Sensor Malfunction**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Automatic headlights failing to activate or deactivate properly, even in appropriate lighting conditions.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the automatic headlight sensor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$300
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof becoming stuck, making unusual noises when opening or closing, or leaking water.
    *   Solution: Lubricating the sunroof tracks may resolve minor issues. In more severe cases, the sunroof motor or complete sunroof assembly may need to be replaced.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 (for lubrication) - $1,000+ (for motor/assembly replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2010 INFINITI FX35 has some common issues, with engine oil consumption and transmission torque converter shudder being the most prevalent. Suspension issues are common as well. Electrical and sunroof problems are less frequent but can be costly to repair. The severity of these issues varies, and diligent maintenance and regular inspections can help to mitigate some of them. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues and budget accordingly for possible repairs.
