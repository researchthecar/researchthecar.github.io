**Introduction:**

The 2012 INFINITI FX35 is a luxury crossover SUV that combines sporty handling with premium amenities. It features a 3.5-liter V6 engine with 303 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The FX35 offers a spacious cabin, sharp exterior styling, and a comfortable ride. However, it has received some negative feedback regarding its fuel economy and dated interior design.

**Interior:**

* **Faulty Navigation System**

Description/Symptoms: The navigation system may freeze, display incorrect information, or fail to respond to user inputs.
Solution: Software update or replacement of the navigation unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

* **Squeaky Interior Panels**

Description/Symptoms: Plastic panels in the interior may rattle or squeak, especially when driving over rough roads.
Solution: Applying lubrication or replacing affected panels.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2012 INFINITI FX35 has relatively few reported issues. The main concerns revolve around the navigation system and occasional interior squeaks. While the issues can be inconvenient, they are not typically severe or expensive to fix.
