**Introduction:**

The 2003 INFINITI FX35 is a mid-size luxury crossover SUV. It is powered by a 3.5L V6 engine that produces 280 horsepower and 275 lb-ft of torque. The FX35 is available with either a 5-speed automatic transmission or a 6-speed manual transmission.

The FX35 is known for its stylish design, luxurious interior, and powerful performance. However, it has also been known to have some issues.

**Engine**

* **V6 3.5L Engine**
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: A rattling noise coming from the engine, especially at start-up and during acceleration
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: N/A
    * **Oil leaks from the valve covers**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the top of the engine, visible as dark stains on the engine or around the spark plugs
        * Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Transmission slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission slipping out of gear, especially during shifting or under load
        * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: N/A
* **6-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Grinding or difficulty shifting**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission grinding or becoming difficult to shift into gear
        * Solution: Replace the clutch or transmission fluid
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical problems such as dim lights, starting difficulties, or a battery that won't hold a charge
    * Solution: Replace the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery draining quickly, even when the car is not in use
    * Solution: Diagnose and fix the electrical system issue causing the drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Door lock actuators failing**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door locks not working properly, either not unlocking or not locking
    * Solution: Replace the door lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per door
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Climate control malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system not working properly, blowing warm air when it should be cold, or vice versa
    * Solution: Replace the climate control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Power window failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The power windows not working properly, not going up or down
    * Solution: Replace the power window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per window
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

The 2003 INFINITI FX35 is a reliable and enjoyable vehicle, but it has been known to have some issues. The most severe and expensive issues are related to the transmission and engine, but there are also some minor issues with the electrical system and interior components. Overall, the cost of repairs for these issues can range from minor to moderate, depending on the severity of the issue.
