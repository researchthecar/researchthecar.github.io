# 2003 INFINITI FX35

### Overview
The 2003 INFINITI FX35, a luxury crossover SUV, offered a blend of performance and utility. Powered by a robust V6 engine and available with all-wheel drive, it aimed to provide a sporty driving experience. While generally considered a reliable vehicle for its era, like any automotive model, it has certain areas that have seen more attention from owners regarding potential issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into common problems and their potential resolutions for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L VQ35DE V6
**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil on the engine, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or other related seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Camshaft Position Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check Engine light, rough idling, stalling, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the camshaft position sensor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (RE5R05A)
**Harsh Shifting / Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, hesitation when shifting into Drive or Reverse.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter change, solenoid replacement, or transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $400 - $2500+


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow window movement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator and motor assembly.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500 per window

**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** HVAC fan only works on the highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250

### Summary Verdict
The 2003 INFINITI FX35 is a generally dependable luxury SUV, with its VQ35DE engine being a strong point. However, owners should be aware of potential issues such as oil leaks from valve covers and camshaft position sensor failures, which are relatively common for this engine. The automatic transmission can sometimes exhibit harsh shifting, potentially requiring fluid services or component replacements. Suspension components, particularly control arm bushings, may wear over time, leading to noise and handling issues. Electrical gremlins like failing window motors and HVAC resistor issues are also noted. Despite these points, with proper maintenance and addressing issues promptly, the FX35 can remain a reliable vehicle.
