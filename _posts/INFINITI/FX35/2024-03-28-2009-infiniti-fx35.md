**Introduction**

The 2009 INFINITI FX35 is a mid-size luxury crossover SUV that offers a combination of performance, style, and comfort. It is available with a 3.5L V6 engine that produces 303 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. The FX35 can be paired with either a 5-speed automatic transmission or a 7-speed automatic transmission.

Owners generally appreciate the FX35's powerful engine, stylish design, and spacious interior. However, it has received some criticism for its firm ride quality and limited cargo space.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine consumes excessive amounts of oil, requiring frequent top-ups. This can lead to engine damage if not addressed.
    * Solution: Replacing the piston rings and valve stem seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Guide Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain guide can fail, causing the timing chain to jump or break. This can lead to catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacing the timing chain guide and timing chain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips out of gear or hesitates during shifting.
    * Solution: Replacing the transmission fluid and filter or rebuilding the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**7-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Rear Air Suspension Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear air suspension system fails, causing the rear of the vehicle to sag.
    * Solution: Replacing the air suspension struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front lower control arm bushings wear out, causing a clunking noise and reduced handling.
    * Solution: Replacing the control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator fails, causing the battery to drain and the vehicle to lose power. Alternator (FD0603A0) is most commonly affected.
    * Solution: Replacing the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Inspecting and replacing any faulty electrical components or modules.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Condensation forms inside the headlight assemblies.
    * Solution: Replacing the headlights or resealing them.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Bumper Paint Peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: The paint on the rear bumper peels or fades.
    * Solution: Refinishing or replacing the rear bumper.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof motor fails, causing the sunroof to become inoperable.
    * Solution: Replacing the sunroof motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard rattles or makes noise when driving on rough roads.
    * Solution: Identifying and addressing the source of the rattle, usually by tightening or replacing loose components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2009 INFINITI FX35 is a generally reliable vehicle, but it has some potential issues that owners should be aware of. The most serious problems are the engine's excessive oil consumption and timing chain guide failure, which can lead to costly repairs. The suspension and electrical components also have some known issues, but these are typically less expensive to fix. Overall, the FX35 is a solid choice for those seeking a luxury crossover SUV, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues before purchasing one.
