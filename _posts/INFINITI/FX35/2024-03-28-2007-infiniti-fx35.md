**Introduction:**

The 2007 INFINITI FX35 is a luxury crossover SUV renowned for its sporty handling, stylish design, and premium interior. It came with a standard 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission, producing 285 horsepower and 275 lb-ft of torque.

**Engine**

**3.5-liter V6**

* **Intake Camshaft Sensor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idling, engine misfires, illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace the intake camshaft sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Cooler Line Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil pooling under the vehicle, low oil levels, engine overheating
    * Solution: Replace the leaking oil cooler line
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Slipping Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips out of gear, hesitation when shifting, difficulty accelerating
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Bushings Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive road noise, poor handling, premature tire wear
    * Solution: Replace the worn bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Front Strut Mount Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or grinding noises when turning, reduced steering response, vibration in the steering wheel
    * Solution: Replace the strut mount bearing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, electrical systems malfunction, dashboard lights dim
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the drain, such as a faulty alternator or parasitic draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Short:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blown fuses, erratic electrical behavior, burnt wiring, potential fire risk
    * Solution: Locate and repair the short circuit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof won't open or close, makes grinding noises, leaks water
    * Solution: Repair or replace the sunroof motor, tracks, or seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2007 INFINITI FX35 is a reliable and enjoyable vehicle. The most common issues tend to be minor and relatively inexpensive to fix. However, some more serious issues, such as transmission slipping or electrical shorts, can be more costly to repair. It's important to have your vehicle regularly serviced and inspected by a qualified mechanic to address any potential problems early on.
