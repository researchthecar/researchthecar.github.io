# 2011 INFINITI FX35

### Overview
The 2011 INFINITI FX35, a luxury crossover SUV, generally offers a solid ownership experience with a focus on performance and premium features. Powered by a robust V6 engine and paired with a smooth automatic transmission, it delivers an engaging driving dynamic. While most owners report satisfaction, like any vehicle, certain areas have seen more attention than others. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas for inspection or concern for prospective buyers or current owners.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L VQ35HR V6
**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil residue around the engine, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or other seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Coolant Leaks (Hoses/Radiator)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses or radiator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (RE5R05A)
**Harsh Shifting/Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, hesitation when shifting into drive or reverse.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, potential valve body or solenoid repair.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of control arms or bushings.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, navigation issues.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200

**Power Window/Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Windows not rolling up/down, doors not locking/unlocking.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty actuator motors.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2011 INFINITI FX35 is a generally reliable luxury SUV, particularly noted for its potent V6 engine. The most common reported technical concerns tend to be related to the transmission's automatic shifting behavior and potential oil or coolant leaks from the engine, which are not uncommon for vehicles of this age and mileage. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and power accessory failures, can also arise. Suspension components like control arm bushings may require attention over time. However, the exterior and interior generally hold up well. With proactive maintenance and addressing these specific potential issues, the FX35 can remain a dependable and enjoyable vehicle.
