# 2013 INFINITI FX50

### Overview
The 2013 INFINITI FX50, a luxury performance SUV, is generally known for its robust engineering and powerful V8 engine. While it offers a compelling driving experience, like any vehicle, it has specific areas that have seen more attention from owners regarding reliability. This analysis focuses on documented issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for owners and prospective buyers. The FX50's performance-oriented nature means some components are stressed more than a standard vehicle, which can influence long-term reliability trends. Understanding these specific points can help in proactive maintenance and informed decision-making.

---


## Engine

### 5.0L V8 (VK50VE)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Increased oil level checks required, potential blue smoke from exhaust under certain conditions.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels regularly, ensure proper PCV system function, and in some cases, piston ring replacement may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (JR710E/JR711E)
**Harsh Shifting / Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, hesitation when shifting into drive or reverse.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter service, potential valve body or solenoid replacement.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,200


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Popping or clunking noise when turning the steering wheel, especially at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of front strut mount bearings.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Glitches / Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Navigation screen freezing, audio system becoming unresponsive, backup camera issues.
- **Solution:** Software update, potential head unit replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2013 INFINITI FX50 is a capable luxury SUV with a powerful engine. While generally reliable, owners should be aware of potential issues such as oil consumption and timing chain wear on the 5.0L V8 engine. The transmission can sometimes exhibit harsh shifting. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and occasional battery drain, are also noted. Suspension components like strut mounts can require attention. However, the exterior and interior generally hold up well. Proactive maintenance, especially concerning the engine and transmission, is recommended to ensure long-term dependability.
