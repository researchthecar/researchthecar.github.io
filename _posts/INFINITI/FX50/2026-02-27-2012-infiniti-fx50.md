# 2012 INFINITI FX50

### Overview
The 2012 INFINITI FX50 is a luxury performance SUV known for its potent V8 engine and engaging driving dynamics. As a premium vehicle, its reliability is generally considered good, but like any complex machine, it has specific areas that have seen more attention from owners and technicians. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for owners and prospective buyers. The FX50's robust construction and advanced features contribute to its overall appeal, though understanding common problem areas can aid in proactive maintenance and repair.

---


## Engine

### 5.0L V8 (VK50VE)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Excessive oil burning, potentially leading to low oil levels and engine damage if not monitored.
- **Solution:** Regular oil level checks and top-offs. In some cases, piston ring replacement may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,000


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (JR710E/JR711E)
**Harsh Shifting / Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, delayed engagement, or hesitation during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement. Solenoid or valve body repair/replacement may be required.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Strut Mounts**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or popping noises over bumps, especially at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of front strut mounts.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Glitches / Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Navigation screen freezing, unresponsiveness, or display errors.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 INFINITI FX50 offers a compelling blend of performance and luxury. While generally reliable, potential owners should be aware of a few key areas. The 5.0L V8 engine can experience oil consumption and timing chain wear, requiring diligent monitoring and potentially costly repairs. The 7-speed automatic transmission may develop shifting issues. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and battery drain, are also noted. Suspension components like strut mounts can wear out. Despite these points, the FX50 remains a capable and enjoyable vehicle, with most issues being addressable through informed maintenance and timely repairs.
