# 2017 INFINITI Q70L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Infiniti Q70L is a long-wheelbase luxury sedan that offers spacious rear legroom and refined performance. When shopping for a used model, understanding the available powertrain options and potential owner-reported areas of concern will help you make an informed decision.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L V6

This 3.7-liter V6 engine produces approximately 330 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque. It is generally regarded as a durable powerplant, though buyers should verify regular maintenance history.

### 5.6L V8

The 5.6-liter V8 engine delivers strong performance with around 420 horsepower and 417 lb-ft of torque. It provides effortless acceleration for the larger sedan body style.

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid

This hybrid powertrain pairs a 3.5-liter V6 with an electric motor to generate a net 360 horsepower. It offers improved fuel efficiency alongside traditional luxury sedan driving dynamics.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 7-speed automatic transmission is paired across the lineup with rear-wheel or all-wheel drive configurations. It generally operates smoothly when properly maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V470000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q70L&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Both scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driveshaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Infiniti Q70L provides a comfortable, spacious ride with robust engine choices. While owner complaints for this model year remain minimal, prospective buyers should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verify service records before buying.
