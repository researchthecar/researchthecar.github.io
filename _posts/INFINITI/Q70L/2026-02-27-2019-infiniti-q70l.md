# 2019 INFINITI Q70L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Infiniti Q70L offers a stretched wheelbase and executive-class rear legroom, pairing traditional luxury sedan styling with robust powertrain choices. Buyers looking at the used market will find a refined cruiser equipped with either a responsive V6 or a powerful V8 engine. While overall owner complaint volume is relatively low for this model year, prospective purchasers should thoroughly inspect vehicle history reports and perform standard pre-purchase checks to ensure proper operating condition.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L V6 Engine

This naturally aspirated 3.7-liter V6 engine produces approximately 330 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque, delivering smooth and reliable daily performance. It is a well-established powerplant for the brand with generally solid long-term mechanical durability.

### 5.6L V8 Engine

The 5.6-liter V8 engine generates roughly 420 horsepower and 417 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration and effortless highway passing. This engine is durable, though buyers should expect higher fuel consumption compared to the V6 option.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission pairs with both engine options and features adaptive shifting and manual shift modes. It generally offers smooth operation, though regular fluid maintenance is recommended to preserve long-term shift quality.

## Steering

### Heated Steering Wheel

Steering systems on this luxury sedan generally operate reliably, though isolated comfort-related complaints can occur.

**Excessive Steering Wheel Heating**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported that the heated steering wheel becomes excessively hot, particularly around the connection points.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the steering wheel heating element and control module if abnormal temperatures occur.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V654000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q70L&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V470000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q70L&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Both scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driveshaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Infiniti Q70L is a comfortable and spacious luxury sedan that provides strong performance through its V6 and V8 powertrain options. Given the limited number of reported complaints, it remains a viable choice for used-car buyers seeking executive styling and comfort. Always conduct a thorough test drive and professional inspection prior to purchase.
