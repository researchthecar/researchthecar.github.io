# 2016 INFINITI Q70L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Infiniti Q70L offers extended rear legroom and a refined executive ride, pairing luxury amenities with stout powertrain options. While owner feedback for this model year is relatively sparse, prospective buyers should still inspect standard mechanical components and electronics during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L V6

This 3.7-liter naturally aspirated V6 engine produces approximately 330 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque. It is a well-known powerplant that generally offers dependable everyday performance when properly maintained.

### 5.6L V8

This 5.6-liter V8 engine generates roughly 420 horsepower and 417 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration for the long-wheelbase sedan. It provides robust capability but tends to consume more fuel than the V6 variant.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic

The 7-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the rear wheels or available all-wheel-drive system. It is generally durable, though proper fluid maintenance is key to long-term shifting smoothness.

## Steering

### Steering Wheel Heater

Steering controls and heating elements generally function well, though minor ergonomic complaints exist among some owners.

**Excessive Heat and Switch Location**
- **Symptoms:** The heated steering wheel can feel uncomfortably hot, and the activation switch is placed low on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Turn off the steering wheel heater manually when the grip becomes too warm.

## Exterior

### Windshield Wipers

Owner reports indicate occasional concerns regarding exterior visibility equipment failure during adverse weather.

**Wiper Motor Seizure**
- **Symptoms:** Windshield wipers may suddenly seize or fail to operate during heavy weather conditions like snowstorms.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the wiper motor or linkage mechanism if failure occurs.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Safety systems generally maintain high standards, though occasional sensor warnings require professional diagnostic evaluation.

**Air Bag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The supplemental restraint system (SRS) warning indicator may illuminate unexpectedly on the instrument cluster.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan the diagnostic trouble codes to pinpoint and resolve the underlying sensor or wiring fault.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V470000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q70L&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Both scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driveshaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Infiniti Q70L is a comfortable luxury cruiser backed by generally dependable powertrain options. Buyers should perform a thorough inspection of the electronics, wiper systems, and safety warning indicators to ensure everything operates correctly.
