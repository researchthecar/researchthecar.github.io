# 2018 INFINITI Q70L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Infiniti Q70L offers a blend of luxury, spacious rear seating, and robust performance from its available engine options. While generally a solid pre-owned luxury sedan, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback regarding interior electronics and safety systems.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L V6

This naturally aspirated 3.7-liter V6 engine produces approximately 330 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque. It provides smooth acceleration and has a generally favorable reputation for mechanical durability in daily driving.

### 5.6L V8

The powerful 5.6-liter V8 engine delivers around 420 horsepower and 417 lb-ft of torque for strong performance. It requires more frequent fuel stops but rewards drivers with robust power delivery and longevity.

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid

This hybrid powertrain pairs a 3.5-liter V6 with an electric motor to generate a net output of around 360 horsepower. It offers improved fuel economy while maintaining spirited performance characteristics.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission comes paired with rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive configurations. It shifts smoothly under normal conditions and requires routine fluid maintenance to ensure long-term reliability.

## Safety

### Air Bags / Occupant Classification System

The safety category focuses primarily on occupant restraint sensors, where isolated owner reports indicate potential sensitivity to moisture and premature component wear.

**Passenger Seat Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger occupant classification system may trigger airbag warning codes like B00A0-02 or fail to detect a seated passenger, potentially disabling the passenger airbag.
- **Solution:** Owners report that dealerships may need to replace the affected seat bottom assembly, including internal electronics and sensors, to resolve persistent codes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V470000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q70L&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Both scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driveshaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Infiniti Q70L remains a comfortable and powerful luxury cruiser for buyers seeking rear-seat legroom and strong powertrain choices. Prospective owners should thoroughly inspect the front passenger seat sensors and occupant classification systems during a pre-purchase test drive.
