### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected battery drain leading to starting issues, often linked to parasitic draw from electronic modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw and repair of faulty modules or wiring. Battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 INFINITI Q70L demonstrates a strong reliability record, particularly concerning its core powertrain components (3.7L V6 and 5.6L V8 engines, and the 7-speed automatic transmission), which are generally robust and free from widespread issues. The suspension and exterior/interior components also hold up well with typical usage. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are within the electrical system, specifically related to infotainment glitches and occasional battery drain problems. These electrical concerns are not uncommon in modern luxury vehicles with complex electronic systems. Overall, the Q70L is a dependable choice, with most potential problems being manageable and not indicative of systemic failures.
