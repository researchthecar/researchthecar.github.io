# 2015 INFINITI Q70L

### Overview
The 2015 INFINITI Q70L, a long-wheelbase variant of the Q70 sedan, aimed to provide a more spacious and luxurious experience compared to its standard counterpart. It was offered with a choice of potent powertrains, including a V6 and a V8 engine, paired with a smooth automatic transmission. The Q70L generally presented a strong showing in terms of reliability, with owners reporting satisfaction with its build quality and performance. However, like any vehicle, some specific areas have seen more attention than others. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for owners and prospective buyers.

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 INFINITI Q70L demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, particularly in its powertrain and core mechanical components. The 3.7L V6 and 5.6L V8 engines, along with the 7-speed automatic transmission, are generally robust and do not present widespread issues. Similarly, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free from significant reported problems. The primary areas that have seen some attention are electrical in nature, specifically occasional glitches or freezing of the infotainment screen and premature battery drain. These issues, while not pervasive, are worth noting. Overall, the Q70L offers a reliable luxury sedan experience, with most potential concerns being manageable and not indicative of systemic flaws.
