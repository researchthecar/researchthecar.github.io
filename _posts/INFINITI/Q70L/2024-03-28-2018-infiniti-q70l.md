**Introduction**

The 2018 INFINITI Q70L is a full-size luxury sedan powered by a naturally aspirated 3.7L V6 engine or a turbocharged 3.0L V6 engine. Both engines are mated to a 7-speed automatic transmission.

Owners commonly praise the Q70L for its spacious interior, refined ride quality, and powerful engines. However, they often criticize its dated appearance, lack of advanced safety features, and high operating costs.

**Engine**

**3.7L V6**

**Timing Chain Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Metallic rattling noise, reduced engine power, illuminated check engine light
Solution: Replace timing chain and related components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Slippage or Harsh Shifting**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping between gears, delayed or harsh gear changes, illuminated check engine light
Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after extended periods of inactivity, parasitic electrical draw
Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Leaking Headlights**
Description/Symptoms: Water condensation or moisture inside headlights
Solution: Replace headlight assembly
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Rear Seat Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Inability to adjust or regulate rear seat temperature
Solution: Replace rear climate control module
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2018 INFINITI Q70L has a few significant issues that can be costly to repair, such as timing chain failure and transmission slippage. Other issues are less severe but can still impact convenience and functionality, such as rear seat climate control malfunction. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
