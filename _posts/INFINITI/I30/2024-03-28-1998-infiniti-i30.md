**Introduction:**

The 1998 INFINITI I30 is a luxury sedan known for its elegant styling and comfortable interior. It was available with two engine options: a 3.0-liter V6 and a 3.5-liter V6. Transmission choices included a 4-speed automatic and a 5-speed automatic. The V6 engines produce 190 horsepower and 205 lb-ft of torque, respectively. Drivers appreciate the I30's smooth ride, responsive handling, and well-appointed cabin. However, it has some reliability concerns that potential buyers should be aware of.

**Engine:**

**3.0-liter V6**
- **Oil Leakage from Valve Cover Gasket:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil from the valve cover gasket, causing a burning smell and reduced oil levels.
    - Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant from the intake manifold gasket, leading to overheating and reduced engine performance.
    - Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**3.5-liter V6**
- **No Issues Found**

**Transmission:**

**4-speed Automatic**
- **Transmission Slipping:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears or slipping while driving, causing a loss of power.
    - Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**5-speed Automatic**
- **No Issues Found**

**Suspension:**

- **Strut Mounts Deterioration:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Worn-out strut mounts causing a noisy and uncomfortable ride, especially over bumps.
    - Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per side
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Sway Bar Link Wear:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Loose or broken sway bar links leading to excessive body roll and poor handling.
    - Solution: Replace the sway bar links.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+ per link
    - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

- **Alternator Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, reduced battery power, and electrical system problems.
    - Solution: Replace the alternator.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Battery Drain:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Battery quickly discharging, causing difficulty starting the vehicle.
    - Solution: Test and replace any faulty electrical components, such as the alternator or battery.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

- **Sunroof Panel Alignment:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Misaligned sunroof panel, causing wind noise and potential water leaks.
    - Solution: Adjust or replace the sunroof panel.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

- **Dashboard Cracking:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Dashboard developing cracks and peeling, especially near the windshield.
    - Solution: Replace the dashboard.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Climate Control Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control system, causing inconsistent or ineffective heating or cooling.
    - Solution: Repair or replace the climate control module.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1998 INFINITI I30 offers a comfortable and refined driving experience, but it has some reliability issues that can be costly to repair. The most common issues include oil leaks from the engine, transmission problems, and suspension deterioration. The interior also has some durability concerns, such as dashboard cracking and climate control failure. Overall, these issues are moderate in severity and cost, but potential buyers should be aware of them before purchasing a used I30.
