**Introduction:**

The 1999 INFINITI I30 was a luxury sedan known for its elegant design, spacious interior, and responsive handling. It came equipped with a 3.0L V6 engine and a 4-speed automatic transmission. While it received praise for its comfort and refinement, it also faced some common issues that drivers should be aware of.

**Engine**

* **V6 3.0L**
    * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:** A common issue causing oil leaks. Oil starts to seep through the valve cover gasket and can accumulate on the engine. The leak can cause misfiring or an illuminated Check Engine Light.
    * **Fix:** Replacing the valve cover gasket and taking care to clean the surfaces before installing the new gasket.
    * **Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+

**Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Failure:** Premature transmission failure can occur, leading to various issues such as slipping gears, grinding noises, or complete loss of function.
    * **Fix:** Replacing the faulty transmission with a new or rebuilt one.
    * **Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Symptoms can include dim headlights, slow power window operation, or a discharged battery.
    * **Fix:** Replacing the alternator.
    * **Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Starter Failure:** The starter motor may fail to engage the flywheel, preventing the engine from starting.
    * **Fix:** Replacing the starter motor.
    * **Cost to Fix:** $400 - $800+
* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain excessively when the vehicle is off, causing it to die prematurely.
    * **Fix:** Troubleshooting the electrical system to identify and eliminate the cause of the drain.
    * **Cost to Fix:** Varies depending on the diagnosis.

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leak:** The sunroof seal may fail, allowing water to leak into the interior.
    * **Fix:** Replacing the sunroof seal.
    * **Cost to Fix:** $300 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Failure:** The climate control system may stop functioning properly, resulting in incorrect temperature settings or poor airflow.
    * **Fix:** Troubleshooting the system to identify and replace faulty components.
    * **Cost to Fix:** Varies depending on the diagnosis.
* **Cruise Control Switch Failure:** The cruise control switch may malfunction, preventing the driver from setting or adjusting the cruise control speed.
    * **Fix:** Replacing the cruise control switch.
    * **Cost to Fix:** $200 - $300+

**Summary:**

While the 1999 INFINITI I30 is known for its comfort and style, it has experienced some common issues that can be costly to repair. Transmission failure, electrical problems, and sunroof leaks are the most significant concerns. It's essential for owners to be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their maintenance and repair plans.
