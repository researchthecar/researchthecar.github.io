**Introduction:**

The 2000 INFINITI I30 is a luxury sedan powered by a 3.0-liter V6 engine and mated to a 5-speed automatic transmission. It was praised for its smooth ride, comfortable interior, and powerful engine, but was criticized for its lack of modern features and unreliability.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** Intake manifold gaskets wear out and leak coolant, causing engine overheating and coolant loss. Replacing the gaskets and intake manifold is necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Power Window Regulator Failure:** Window regulators can fail, causing the windows to become stuck or inoperable. Replacing the regulator is necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
* **Fuel Pump Failure:** Fuel pumps can fail, leading to engine stalling or difficulty starting. Replacing the fuel pump is necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage or Shuddering:** The transmission may slip or shudder during gear changes. Replacing the transmission filter and fluid, or rebuilding the transmission may be necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
* **Solenoid Failure:** Transmission solenoids can fail, leading to erratic shifting or failure to shift gears. Replacing the solenoids is necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Suspension**

* **Strut and Shock Absorber Wear:** Struts and shock absorbers can wear out, leading to a rough ride and poor handling. Replacing the struts and shock absorbers is necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $2,000+

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Alternators can fail, causing battery drain or electrical system malfunction. Replacing the alternator is necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Battery Drain:** The battery can drain prematurely due to faulty components or parasitic draw. Investigating and repairing the cause is necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Radio Malfunction:** The radio may experience malfunctions, such as losing presets or not powering on. Repairing or replacing the radio is necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Climate Control Failure:** The climate control system may fail to regulate temperature or airflow. Replacing the climate control unit or actuators is necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Housing Fogging:** Headlight housings may become foggy or cloudy, reducing light output. Replacing the headlight housings is necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary:**

The 2000 INFINITI I30 has numerous mechanical and electrical issues that can be costly to repair. Owners may experience engine problems, transmission problems, and suspension wear. Interior and exterior components can also malfunction, affecting comfort and functionality. The severity and cost of these issues make the I30 a risky choice for used car buyers.
