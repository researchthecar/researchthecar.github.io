**Introduction:**

The 1995 INFINITI Q45 is a luxury sedan equipped with a standard 4.5-liter V8 engine, delivering 278 horsepower and 303 lb-ft of torque. It comes with a standard 4-speed automatic transmission. Critics praise the Q45 for its smooth ride, lavish interior, and potent V8 engine. However, it is criticized for its outdated styling, lack of advanced features, and subpar fuel efficiency.

**Engine**

**4.5L V8**

* **Coolant Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaks in the coolant hoses or gaskets, resulting in overheating and loss of coolant
    * Solution: Replace the faulty hoses or gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaks from the valve covers, oil pan, or timing cover, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage
    * Solution: Replace or reseal the leaking components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the intake manifold gasket, causing vacuum leaks and a rough idle
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Slipping Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips or hesitates when shifting, especially under load
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Harsh Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifts abruptly or with clunks
    * Solution: Repair or replace the shift solenoids or the transmission control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear of the control arm bushings, causing clunking noises, misalignment, and reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace the worn bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator fails to charge the battery, leading to a dead battery and loss of power
    * Solution: Replace the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The starter motor fails to engage or crank the engine
    * Solution: Replace the starter motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Undercarriage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on the undercarriage of the vehicle, particularly in areas exposed to road salt and moisture
    * Solution: Inspect and treat the rusted areas
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Faulty Climate Control Unit:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control unit malfunctions, resulting in incorrect temperature or airflow
    * Solution: Replace or repair the climate control unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Seat Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The power seat switches become unresponsive or malfunction, preventing adjustment of the seat
    * Solution: Replace the faulty switches
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1995 INFINITI Q45 exhibits several issues, ranging from minor electrical problems to more costly engine and transmission repairs. While some components have proven reliable, others require careful inspection and maintenance. Owners should be prepared for potential repair expenses, as some issues can be quite costly to address.
