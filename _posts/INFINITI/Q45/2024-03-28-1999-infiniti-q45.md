**Introduction**

The 1999 INFINITI Q45 is a full-size luxury sedan known for its spacious interior, powerful engine, and advanced technology. It was available with two engine options: a 300 horsepower 4.1L V8 and a 340 horsepower 4.5L V8. Both engines were paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Q45 was praised for its smooth ride, quiet cabin, and luxurious amenities. However, it also had some reported issues.

**Engine**

**4.1L V8**

- **Intake Manifold Gaskets Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks, engine overheating, reduced performance
Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID: 97V132014, INFINITI Campaign ID: 97C

- **Camshaft Position Sensor Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, engine misfires, rough idle
Solution: Replace camshaft position sensor
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**4.5L V8**

- **Oil Consumption**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
Solution: Replace piston rings or engine rebuild
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $5,000+
Recalls: None Found

- **Secondary Air Injection System Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, reduced performance, increased emissions
Solution: Replace air pump, check valve, or relay
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

- **Transmission Slips or Hesitates**
Description/Symptoms: Delayed shifting, slipping gears, hard shifts
Solution: Flush transmission fluid, replace filters and solenoids
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

- **Transmission Fluid Leaks**
Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid, slipping gears, overheating
Solution: Replace leaking seals or gaskets
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Strut/Shock Absorber Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Worn struts/shocks, bumpy ride, reduced handling
Solution: Replace struts/shock absorbers
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

- **Control Arm Bushings Worn**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises, unstable handling, uneven tire wear
Solution: Replace control arm bushings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after sitting, parasitic draw
Solution: Find and fix source of parasitic draw
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on source of issue
Recalls: None Found

- **Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical accessories malfunctioning, dead battery
Solution: Replace alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Headlight Lenses Fogging**
Description/Symptoms: Foggy or cloudy headlight lenses, reduced visibility
Solution: Polish or replace headlight lenses
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

- **Windshield Washer Pump Failure**
Description/Symptoms: No windshield washer fluid spraying
Solution: Replace windshield washer pump
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: AC or heater not working, temperature fluctuations
Solution: Replace actuator, blend door, or climate control unit
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

- **Power Seat Motor Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Power seats not moving or moving intermittently
Solution: Replace power seat motor
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1999 INFINITI Q45 is a well-regarded luxury sedan, but it does have some potential issues. The most common engine problems are intake manifold gasket leaks and camshaft position sensor failure. The transmission may experience slipping or hesitation and fluid leaks. Suspension components can wear out, leading to a bumpy ride and reduced handling. Electrical issues can include battery drain and alternator failure. Exterior concerns include fogged headlight lenses and a faulty windshield washer pump. Interior problems may arise with climate control malfunction and power seat motor failure. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be significant and may require professional repairs.
