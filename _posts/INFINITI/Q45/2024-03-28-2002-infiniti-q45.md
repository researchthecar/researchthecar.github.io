## 2002 Infiniti Q45

The 2002 Infiniti Q45 is a full-size luxury sedan known for its spacious interior, powerful V8 engine, and smooth ride. It comes equipped with a standard 4.5-liter V8 engine that produces 340 horsepower and 333 lb-ft of torque. A 5-speed automatic transmission is the only transmission option.

Despite its luxurious features, the 2002 Infiniti Q45 has encountered several issues reported by owners.

### Engine

- **V8 Engine**
    - **Camshaft Position Sensor Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Check Engine Light illuminates, engine stalls or hesitates during acceleration
    - Solution: Replace camshaft position sensor
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    - Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

- **5-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    - **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    - Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks from pan or lines, low transmission fluid level
    - Solution: Replace worn seals or lines, refill transmission fluid
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    - Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

- **No Issues Found**

### Electrical

- **Battery Drain**
    - Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when the car is turned off
    - Solution: Disconnect battery or replace alternator
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    - Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

- **Broken Headlight Lens**
    - Description/Symptoms: Headlight lens cracks or breaks, allowing moisture to enter
    - Solution: Replace headlight assembly
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    - Recalls: None Found

### Interior

- **Instrument Panel Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Gauges malfunction or do not display information correctly
    - Solution: Replace instrument panel cluster
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    - Recalls: None Found

## Summary

Overall, the 2002 Infiniti Q45 has several potential issues, including engine problems, transmission fluid leaks, battery drains, broken headlight lenses, and instrument panel failures. While these issues can be costly to repair, they are not widespread and should not deter potential buyers from considering this vehicle. In general, the Q45 is a reliable and comfortable sedan with impressive performance and features.
