**Introduction**

The 1998 INFINITI Q45 is a full-size luxury sedan noted for its spacious interior, smooth ride, and powerful engine. It came equipped with a 4.1L V8 engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission, delivering 278 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque. While it boasts impressive performance and comfort, the Q45 has been plagued by several common issues affecting various components.

**Engine**

**4.1L V8**

- **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
  Description/Symptoms: Leaks coolant and oil, causing performance loss and overheating.
  Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  Recalls: None Found

- **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
  Description/Symptoms: Loud rattling noises during start-up and acceleration, performance problems.
  Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and related components.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
  Recalls: None Found

- **Camshaft Sensor Malfunction**
  Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, rough idle, engine performance issues.
  Solution: Replace the camshaft sensor.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
  Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

- **Transmission Fluid Leakage**
  Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks, causing low fluid levels and performance problems.
  Solution: Repair or replace the transmission pan, seals, or gaskets to stop the leak.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  Recalls: None Found

- **Delayed or Rough Shifting**
  Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or shifts roughly, especially during upshifts.
  Solution: Replace the transmission solenoid or valve body.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
  Description/Symptoms: Loud clunking or grinding noises from the suspension during turns or over bumps.
  Solution: Replace the strut mount bearing.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per axle
  Recalls: None Found

- **Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear**
  Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling steering, uneven tire wear.
  Solution: Replace the lower control arm bushings.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+ per axle
  Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Alternator Failure**
  Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, electrical system problems, dimming headlights.
  Solution: Replace the alternator.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
  Recalls: None Found

- **Power Window Motor Failure**
  Description/Symptoms: Power windows inoperable or slow-moving.
  Solution: Replace the power window motor.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+ per window
  Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Headlight Condensation**
  Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulates inside the headlight assemblies.
  Solution: Replace the headlight assemblies or seal them to prevent condensation.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per assembly
  Recalls: None Found

- **Chrome Trim Peeling**
  Description/Symptoms: Chrome trim on the grille, window surrounds, or wheels becomes dull or peeling.
  Solution: Re-chrome or replace the affected trim pieces.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+ depending on the amount of trim affected
  Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Sunroof Switch Malfunction**
  Description/Symptoms: Sunroof does not open or close properly.
  Solution: Replace the sunroof switch or control module.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
  Recalls: None Found

- **Heated Seat Element Failure**
  Description/Symptoms: Heated seats do not function or only heat unevenly.
  Solution: Replace the heated seat element or switch.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+ per seat
  Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 1998 INFINITI Q45 offers a comfortable and powerful driving experience, it is not without its drawbacks. The engine, transmission, and suspension have known issues that can be expensive to repair. Electrical problems, such as alternator failure, can also be inconvenient and costly. However, some interior and exterior issues, such as sunroof switch malfunction and chrome trim peeling, are less severe and may not require immediate attention. Nonetheless, potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their purchasing decision.
