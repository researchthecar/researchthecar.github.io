### 2000 INFINITI Q45

The 2000 INFINITI Q45 is a luxury sedan that offers a comfortable and refined driving experience. It is powered by a 4.1-liter V8 engine that produces 260 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque. The Q45 is available with either a 4-speed automatic transmission or a 5-speed automatic transmission.

People who like the 2000 INFINITI Q45 praise its spacious interior, smooth ride, and powerful engine. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior.

**Engine**

* **4.1L V8 Engine**
    * **Oil leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the engine can cause a loss of oil pressure and engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the leaking gaskets or seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing chain failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain can stretch or break, causing the engine to lose power or stall.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Shifting problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip, hesitate, or refuse to shift gears.
        * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid and filter. If the problem persists, the transmission may need to be rebuilt or replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **5-speed automatic transmission**
    * **No issues found**

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension**
    * **Strut mounts**
        * Description/Symptoms: The strut mounts can wear out, causing the suspension to make noise or the vehicle to handle poorly.
        * Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear suspension**
    * **No issues found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery**
    * **Battery drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain quickly, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
        * Solution: Replace the battery.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator**
    * **Alternator failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The alternator may fail, causing the battery to drain and the vehicle to lose power.
        * Solution: Replace the alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlights**
    * **Headlight condensation**
        * Description/Symptoms: Condensation may build up inside the headlights, reducing visibility.
        * Solution: Replace the headlights.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Body panels**
    * **Rust**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rust may form on the body panels, reducing the vehicle's appearance and value.
        * Solution: Repair the rust or replace the affected body panels.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard**
    * **Dashboard cracking**
        * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may crack over time, reducing the vehicle's appearance and value.
        * Solution: Replace the dashboard.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate control**
    * **A/C compressor failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The A/C compressor may fail, causing the vehicle to lose cooling.
        * Solution: Replace the A/C compressor.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2000 INFINITI Q45 is a reliable and comfortable luxury sedan. However, it is not without its issues. Some of the most common problems reported by owners include engine oil leaks, timing chain failure, shifting problems, strut mount wear, and battery drain. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost to fix them can range from minor to major. Overall, the 2000 INFINITI Q45 is a good choice for those who are looking for a spacious and refined sedan. However, it is important to be aware of the potential issues that can arise before making a purchase.
