**Introduction:**

The 1996 INFINITI Q45 is a full-size luxury sedan renowned for its powerful engine, comfortable interior, and advanced features. It was available with two engine options and a single transmission.

**Engine**

* **VH41DE 4.1L V8**

  * **Intake Valve Guide Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature engine wear, excessive oil consumption, and misfires.
    * Solution: Replace intake valve guides.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **VH45DE 4.5L V8**

  * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loud ticking or rattling noise from the engine, engine performance loss.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner and primary timing chain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **RE5R05A 5-Speed Automatic**

  * **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping when shifting, loss of power.
    * Solution: Replace torque converter and rebuild transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery light illumination, electrical system failure.
    * Solution: Replace alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Ignition Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the car, intermittent stalling.
    * Solution: Replace ignition switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Trunk Lid Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots on the rear trunk lid, especially around the license plate area.
    * Solution: Replace or repair trunk lid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent or malfunctioning climate control system.
    * Solution: Replace climate control module or related sensors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 1996 INFINITI Q45 offers a luxurious driving experience, it is not immune to certain issues. The most severe problems are related to the engine and transmission, which can lead to costly repairs. The electrical and exterior components also have known issues, but they are generally less severe and less expensive to fix. Overall, the reliability of the Q45 is average compared to other luxury sedans of its era.
