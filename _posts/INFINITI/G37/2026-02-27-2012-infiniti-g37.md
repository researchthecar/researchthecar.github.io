- **Cost:** $100 - $1500

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Premature battery failure or unexplained battery drain, potentially linked to parasitic draw from modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty modules or battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 INFINITI G37 stands out as a highly reliable luxury vehicle. Its core mechanical components, including the robust 3.7L V6 engine and the 7-speed automatic transmission, are exceptionally durable and rarely present significant issues. The suspension and chassis are also well-engineered for longevity. The primary areas where owners might encounter minor concerns are within the electrical system, specifically related to infotainment system glitches and occasional battery drain issues. These are generally manageable and do not detract from the overall strong reliability of the G37. With proper maintenance, the G37 is expected to provide many years of dependable service.
