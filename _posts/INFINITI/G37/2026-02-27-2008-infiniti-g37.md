# 2008 INFINITI G37

### Overview
The 2008 INFINITI G37, a popular luxury sport sedan, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, particularly for its powertrain. It was equipped with a potent V6 engine and offered in both rear-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, certain components can experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related problems that may require attention, providing insights into potential repair costs and solutions for owners.

---


## Engine

### 3.7L VQ37VHR V6
**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil on the engine block, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (RE5R05A)
**Harsh Shifting / Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, difficulty engaging gears, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, solenoid replacement, or transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3,500

## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Electrical Gremlins**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning power windows, interior lights, door locks, or dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


### Infotainment System
**Screen Glitches / Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Navigation screen freezing, audio system unresponsive, display issues.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2008 INFINITI G37 is a solid choice for reliability, especially concerning its robust 3.7L V6 engine and available transmissions. The most common non-wear-related issues tend to be minor oil leaks from valve covers and potential problems with the automatic transmission's solenoids or control module, which can manifest as rough shifting. Electrical gremlins related to the BCM or infotainment system can occur, but are not pervasive. Overall, with regular maintenance, the G37 offers a dependable luxury driving experience, with repair costs generally aligning with its luxury segment.
