### Overview
The 2013 INFINITI G37, a popular luxury sport sedan, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, particularly for its powertrain. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, certain components can occasionally present issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and prospective buyers. The G37's strong engineering contributes to its reputation for durability, making it a solid choice in its segment.

### Summary Verdict
The 2013 INFINITI G37 demonstrates strong overall reliability, with its VQ37VHR engine and 7-speed automatic transmission being particularly robust and rarely reporting significant issues. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and key fob battery drain. These are generally manageable and do not detract significantly from the vehicle's dependable nature. Suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free from widespread problems. With proper maintenance, the G37 is expected to provide a reliable and enjoyable ownership experience.
