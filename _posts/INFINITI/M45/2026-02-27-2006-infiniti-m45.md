# 2006 INFINITI M45

### Overview
The 2006 INFINITI M45, a luxury sedan, offered a compelling blend of performance and comfort. Powered by a robust V8 engine, it generally provided a solid ownership experience. While not immune to the occasional issue, common problems were relatively infrequent for its age and class. Owners often praised its smooth ride, powerful acceleration, and upscale interior. However, like many vehicles of this era, certain components could experience wear over time, particularly in the electrical and suspension systems. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring the longevity and reliability of this sophisticated sedan.

## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Front Suspension Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, uneven tire wear, vague steering feel.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn bushings.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Rear Shock Absorbers**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, reduced handling stability, visible leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of rear shock absorbers.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Electrical Gremlins**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning power windows, door locks, interior lights, or dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200


### Alternator
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light illuminated, difficulty starting the engine.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

## Interior

### Infotainment System
**Navigation System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, inaccurate GPS location, unresponsiveness.
- **Solution:** Software update or potential head unit replacement.

### Climate Control
**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

### Summary Verdict
The 2006 INFINITI M45 is a generally reliable luxury sedan, particularly in its powertrain. The 4.5L V8 engine and 5-speed automatic transmission are known for their durability. Common issues tend to be concentrated in areas like suspension components (bushings, shocks) and certain electrical modules (BCM, alternator), which are not uncommon for vehicles of this age. Interior electronics, such as the navigation system and HVAC controls, can also present occasional problems. With diligent maintenance and prompt attention to any developing issues, the M45 can remain a dependable and enjoyable vehicle.
