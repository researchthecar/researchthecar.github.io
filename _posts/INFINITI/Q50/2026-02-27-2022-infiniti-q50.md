### Summary Verdict
The 2022 INFINITI Q50 demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The 3.0L twin-turbo V6 engine and 7-speed automatic transmission are well-regarded for their durability. Potential minor concerns are primarily concentrated in the electrical system, specifically with occasional infotainment glitches and, in rare cases, premature battery failure. These issues are generally manageable and do not detract significantly from the vehicle's dependable nature. Suspension, exterior, and interior components show no widespread reliability concerns, suggesting a well-built vehicle for its class.
