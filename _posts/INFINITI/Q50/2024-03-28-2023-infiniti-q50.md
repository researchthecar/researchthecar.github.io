**Introduction**

The 2023 INFINITI Q50 is a luxury sports sedan that offers a blend of performance and refinement. It comes with a single engine option, a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6, that produces 300 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. The Q50 is available with either a 7-speed automatic transmission or a 6-speed manual transmission. The Q50 has received critical acclaim for its sharp handling, powerful engine, and luxurious interior. However, there have been some reported issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **3.0L Twin-Turbocharged V6**
    * **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: Over time, carbon deposits can accumulate on the intake valves, reducing airflow and engine performance. Symptoms may include reduced power, rough idling, and increased fuel consumption.
        * Solution: The intake valves need to be cleaned or replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * **Turbocharger Wastegate Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger wastegate may fail, leading to decreased boost pressure and engine power. Symptoms may include reduced acceleration, increased exhaust smoke, and illuminated check engine light.
        * Solution: The turbocharger wastegate needs to be replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 17V308000

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may develop a leak, leading to fluid loss and diminished performance. Symptoms may include slipping gears, delayed engagement, and whining or grinding noises.
        * Solution: The transmission fluid leak needs to be repaired.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **6-Speed Manual**
    * **Grinding Gears**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may grind when shifting gears, indicating worn or damaged synchronizers. Symptoms may include difficulty engaging gears, especially at higher speeds.
        * Solution: The transmission needs to be rebuilt or replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Malfunctioning Headlight Control Module**
    * Description/Symptoms: The headlight control module may malfunction, causing the headlights to dim, flicker, or turn off unexpectedly. Symptoms may also include illuminated dashboard warning lights.
    * Solution: The headlight control module needs to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
        * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V762000

**Exterior**

* **Wiper Fluid Spray Nozzles Clogging**
    * Description/Symptoms: The wiper fluid spray nozzles may become clogged, preventing the washer fluid from effectively cleaning the windshield.
    * Solution: The wiper fluid spray nozzles need to be cleaned or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150

**Interior**

* **Recurring Infotainment System Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience occasional glitches, such as freezing, rebooting, or displaying error messages.
    * Solution: Software updates or component replacements may be necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary**

Overall, the 2023 INFINITI Q50 is a reliable and capable luxury sedan. However, some potential issues should be considered. The engine and transmission problems can be costly to repair, while the electrical and interior issues are generally less severe.
