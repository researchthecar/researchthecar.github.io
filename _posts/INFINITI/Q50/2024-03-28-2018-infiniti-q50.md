## 2018 INFINITI Q50

The 2018 INFINITI Q50 is a luxury sports sedan that offers a powerful engine, a comfortable interior, and a variety of features. It is available with a choice of two engine options: a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine or a 3.5-liter naturally aspirated V6 engine. Both engines are paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission.

The Q50 has received praise for its powerful engine, responsive handling, and comfortable interior. However, it has also been criticized for its high price, limited cargo space, and lack of a manual transmission option.

## Engine
**3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine**

**Turbocharger failure**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased oil consumption, excessive smoke from the exhaust pipe
Solution: Replace the turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+

**Fuel injector failure**
Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, rough idle, loss of power
