**Introduction**

The 2017 INFINITI Q50 is a compact luxury sedan known for its sporty handling, luxurious interior, and advanced technology. It is available with a range of engine and transmission options, including a twin-turbocharged V6, a naturally aspirated V6, and a turbocharged 4-cylinder.

The twin-turbo V6 produces 400 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The naturally aspirated V6 produces 300 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, mated to either a 7-speed automatic transmission or a 6-speed manual transmission. The turbocharged 4-cylinder produces 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission.

Owners of the 2017 INFINITI Q50 generally appreciate its performance, comfort, and stylish design. However, the vehicle has also been reported to have some issues in various areas.

**Engine**

* **Twin-Turbo V6**
    * **Oil Pump Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear of the oil pump gears can lead to loss of oil pressure, causing engine damage. Symptoms may include engine knocking, rattling, or seizing.
        * Solution: Replacement of the oil pump and related components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Valve Timing Solenoid Issues:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Faulty valve timing solenoids can disrupt the timing of the engine valves, leading to reduced performance, poor fuel economy, and engine misfires.
        * Solution: Replacement of the affected solenoids and reprogramming of the engine control unit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $700 - $1,200+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Naturally Aspirated V6**
    * **Timing Chain Rattle:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Insufficient lubrication can cause the timing chain to stretch and rattle, especially during cold starts.
        * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain and related components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,800 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharged 4-Cylinder**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Torque Converter Shudder:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering sensation while accelerating from a stop or at low speeds.
        * Solution: Replacement of the torque converter.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid can cause low fluid levels, leading to transmission problems. Symptoms may include slipping gears or difficulty shifting.
        * Solution: Repair or replacement of the leaking component, such as seals or gaskets.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **6-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Clutch Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clutch slipping or chatter while shifting, difficulty engaging gears, or grinding noises.
        * Solution: Replacement of the clutch assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn control arm bushings can cause clunking or rattling noises, especially over bumps or rough roads.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive battery drain can lead to a dead battery, especially when the vehicle is parked for extended periods.
    * Solution: Diagnosis of the electrical system to identify the source of the drain, such as a parasitic draw or faulty component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience freezing, unresponsive touchscreens, or other malfunctions.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperable or intermittent door locks, usually accompanied by a clicking noise when trying to lock or unlock.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected door lock actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Adaptive Cruise Control Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning adaptive cruise control switch, causing the system to not engage or operate properly.
    * Solution: Replacement of the adaptive cruise control switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2017 INFINITI Q50 has received positive feedback for its performance, styling, and features. However, it has also experienced some issues in various areas. While the severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage, it is important for owners to be aware of potential concerns and to seek professional attention if any symptoms arise.
