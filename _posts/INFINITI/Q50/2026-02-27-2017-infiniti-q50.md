# 2017 INFINITI Q50 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Infiniti Q50 delivers athletic styling and engaging performance, making it an appealing option on the used luxury sedan market. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported reliability trends across various mechanical and electrical systems before purchasing. Paying close attention to powertrain performance, suspension wear, and safety electronics will help ensure a dependable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine produces around 208 horsepower, offering an entry-level balance of efficiency and responsiveness for daily commuting.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The engine may unexpectedly lose power or stall while driving, sometimes accompanied by concerns regarding the fuel pump control module.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel system and consult a qualified technician to determine if software updates or component replacements are necessary.

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 is available in higher trims—delivering up to 300 or 400 horsepower—providing robust acceleration and strong passing power.

**Coolant Leaks and Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine shutdown, coolant loss, and radiator fan assembly failures leading to elevated operating temperatures.
- **Solution:** Inspect the cooling system, radiator fan module, and hoses regularly for signs of leakage or wear.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the rear wheels or intelligent all-wheel-drive system, with no major recurring mechanical defect trends heavily highlighted in owner complaints.

## Suspension

### Control Arms and Bushings

The suspension setup balances firm handling with highway comfort, though front control arm bushings require periodic inspection.

**Leaking Lower Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Hydraulic bushings on the lower control arms may develop fluid leaks over time, leading to compromised ride quality and handling stability.
- **Solution:** Replace the affected control arm assemblies or worn hydraulic bushings.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides adequate stopping power for this performance-oriented sedan, though routine inspection of rotors and pads is recommended.

**Brake Pedal Vibration and Shudder**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may shudder or shake abnormally when the brake pedal is depressed, or exhibit a vehicle pull while stopping.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake rotors for warping and check brake pads and suspension components for uneven wear.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Depending on the trim, the vehicle may feature conventional hydraulic steering or Infiniti's Direct Adaptive Steering electronic system.

**Power Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a sudden loss of power steering assist or unexpected steering pull, making the vehicle difficult to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Have the steering rack, electronic control modules, and fluid levels professionally inspected.

## Safety

### Occupant Detection System

Safety systems generally offer comprehensive protection, but electronic seat sensors require attention if warning lights appear on the dashboard.

**Passenger Airbag Sensor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger seatbelt or airbag warning light may illuminate, indicating a faulty occupant classification sensor that could disable the airbag during a collision.
- **Solution:** Have the dealer diagnose the diagnostic trouble codes and recalibrate or replace the seat sensor module.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V571000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q50&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the driver's frontal air bag, the inflator may crack and release the gas needed to properly deploy the air bag, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will replace the driver side air bag inflator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V244000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q50&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As a result, the passenger frontal air bag may be turned off and not deploy in the event of a crash. If the passenger frontal air bag does not deploy as intended in the event of a crash, the passenger is at an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the Air Bag Control Unit (ACU) and OCS Electronic Control Unit (ECU) in Altima, Maxima, Murano, Rogue, and Sentra vehicles, and replace the OCS ECU in LEAF, NV200, NV200 Taxi, Pathfinder, Infiniti Q50, JX35, and QX60. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V476000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q50&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and Infiniti dealers will reprogram the Fuel Pump Control Module (FPCM) with corrected software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V470000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q50&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Both scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driveshaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Infiniti Q50 offers an attractive mix of luxury features and potent engine choices, but used car buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Particular attention should be paid to potential steering assist failures, front suspension bushing wear, and electronic safety sensor status. Addressing these items early helps secure a more reliable and enjoyable ownership experience.
