**Introduction:**

The 2015 INFINITI Q50 is a compact luxury sedan powered by either a 3.7L V6 or a 3.5L V6 hybrid engine. The V6 engine produces 328 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, while the hybrid engine produces a combined 360 horsepower and 299 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The Q50 is praised for its stylish design, powerful engines, and sharp handling. However, it is criticized for its cramped rear seats and lack of advanced safety features.

**Engine**

* **3.7L V6**
    * **Engine Knock:**
        * Description/Symptoms: A consistent knocking or rattling noise coming from the engine during acceleration.
        * Solution: Replacing the engine with the updated version (VR30DDTT)
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $10,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.5L V6 Hybrid**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Transmission Shudder:**
        * Description/Symptoms: A vibration or shudder felt when shifting gears or accelerating.
        * Solution: Replacing or rebuilding the transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: NTB20-030

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **In-Car Entertainment System:**
    * **Infotainment Screen Lag:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Delayed response or freezing of the infotainment screen.
        * Solution: Software updates
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the update
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Remote Keyless Entry System:**
    * **Key Fob Not Detected:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The car's key fob is not recognized by the vehicle, preventing entry.
        * Solution: Replacing the key fob battery or the receiver module
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate Control System:**
    * **AC Vents Not Blowing Air:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning vents do not circulate air properly.
        * Solution: Replacing the blower motor or actuator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Seat Controls:**
    * **Seat Memory Not Saving:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The power seat memory function does not save or recall seat positions.
        * Solution: Software updates or replacing the seat control module
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the solution
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2015 INFINITI Q50 is a reliable vehicle, but it has some common issues that can be costly to fix. Engine knock and transmission shudder are the most severe issues, while other problems, such as electrical glitches and interior malfunctions, are less concerning. Overall, the Q50 is a good choice for luxury car buyers who prioritize performance and style but are willing to accept potential reliability issues.
