**2019 INFINITI Q50**

The 2019 INFINITI Q50 is a luxury sports sedan that offers a compelling combination of performance, technology, and style. It is available with two engine options: a 300-horsepower 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 and a 400-horsepower 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6. Both engines are paired with a seven-speed automatic transmission. The Q50 has received praise for its sharp handling, responsive steering, and comfortable interior. However, it has also been criticized for its high price tag and some reliability concerns.

**Engine**

* **3.0L Twin-Turbocharged V6**

* **3.0L Twin-Turbocharged V6 (400hp)**

> **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic**

> **Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
> Description/Symptoms: The TCM may fail, causing the transmission to shift erratically or not engage gears. Symptoms include difficulty shifting, slipping gears, and illuminated check engine light.
> Solution: Replace the TCM.
> Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
> Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

> **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

> **Battery Drain**
> Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely due to a parasitic draw on the electrical system. Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle, flickering lights, and dead battery.
> Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the parasitic draw.
> Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
> Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

> **Headlight Condensation**
> Description/Symptoms: Condensation may form inside the headlight assemblies, causing fogging or water droplets.
> Solution: Replace the headlight assemblies.
> Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
> Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

> **Infotainment System Freezes**
> Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze or become unresponsive. Symptoms include black screen, loss of audio, and inability to control vehicle functions.
> Solution: Update the infotainment system software.
> Estimated Cost to Fix: Free - $500+
> Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 INFINITI Q50 is a capable and stylish luxury sports sedan, but it has faced some reliability issues. The severity of these issues varies from minor inconveniences to potential engine damage. The cost to fix these issues can be significant, but many are covered under warranty. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these issues before purchasing a Q50.
