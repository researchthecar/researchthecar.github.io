# 2019 INFINITI Q50

### Overview
The 2019 INFINITI Q50, a luxury sport sedan, generally presents a solid reliability record, particularly for its powertrain. Owners often praise its engaging driving dynamics and premium interior. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unscheduled repairs, like any vehicle, there are a few areas that have seen more attention than others. These tend to be concentrated in specific electronic components and, to a lesser extent, some aspects of the suspension. The available powertrains, especially the V6 options, are generally robust. However, it's important to be aware of potential issues to ensure proactive maintenance and address any concerns promptly.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 INFINITI Q50 is generally a reliable vehicle, with its V6 engines and automatic transmission being particularly strong points. The most commonly reported issues are related to the infotainment system's responsiveness and occasional battery drain, which can often be resolved with software updates or diagnosis of parasitic draws. Some owners have noted minor suspension component wear, such as bushings, leading to noise. The 2.0L turbo engine has seen some reports of oil leaks. Overall, the Q50 offers a good balance of performance and dependability, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of widespread systemic failures.
