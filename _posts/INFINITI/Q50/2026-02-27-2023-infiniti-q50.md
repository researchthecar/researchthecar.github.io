# 2023 INFINITI Q50 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Infiniti Q50 delivers sporty luxury and twin-turbo performance, making it an appealing option on the used market. While overall powertrain configurations are generally well-established, prospective buyers should review owner-reported feedback regarding handling, electrical systems, and occasional accessory glitches.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine generates roughly 300 horsepower in standard trims and up to 400 horsepower in the Red Sport 400. It offers strong acceleration and smooth power delivery, though proper maintenance of the turbochargers and cooling system is vital for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with the V6 engine across rear-wheel and all-wheel drive layouts, this 7-speed automatic transmission provides smooth gear changes and highway efficiency. Used buyers should test drive the vehicle thoroughly to ensure crisp, hesitation-free shifting.

## Suspension

### Wheel Knuckle and Ball Joint

Suspension components require routine inspection to ensure structural integrity and safe handling characteristics.

**Wheel and Suspension Separation**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden separation of a wheel from the vehicle near the knuckle or ball joint area, leading to an immediate loss of control.
- **Solution:** Inspect front and rear suspension components, ball joints, and wheel knuckles for wear, damage, or loose hardware during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Steering

### Steering System

Steering components generally function well, though individual owner reports note occasional vagueness in road feel.

**Loose or Unresponsive Steering Feel**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that the steering can feel loose, jumpy, or disconnected from the road, making it difficult to keep the car tracking straight.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the steering linkage, alignment settings, and electronic steering components for wear or calibration errors.

## Safety

### Occupant Detection Sensor

Electrical and sensor systems manage modern safety features, but minor glitches can occasionally trigger false warnings.

**False Passenger Seatbelt Warning**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger seatbelt warning chime or light activates even when the front passenger seat is completely unoccupied.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a dealership or specialist to inspect the seat occupancy sensor and recalibrate or replace the seatbelt warning module.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Infiniti Q50 remains a stylish and capable luxury sedan for used car shoppers. While most mechanical systems prove durable, prospective buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the steering, suspension joints, and electronic sensors to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
