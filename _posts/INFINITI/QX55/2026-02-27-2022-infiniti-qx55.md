# 2022 INFINITI QX55

### Overview
The 2022 INFINITI QX55, a luxury crossover coupe, generally presents a strong reliability profile, reflecting INFINITI's commitment to quality and driver satisfaction. Built on a platform shared with the QX50, it benefits from proven engineering. The powertrain, a 2.0-liter variable compression turbo engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT), is designed for a balance of performance and efficiency. While most owners report a positive experience, like any vehicle, there can be isolated incidents. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, aiming to provide a clear picture of potential concerns for prospective and current owners.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 INFINITI QX55 demonstrates good overall reliability, with its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission showing no significant widespread issues. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, in some instances, premature 12V battery failure. These are not typically indicative of systemic design flaws but rather isolated incidents that can occur in modern vehicles. Owners should be aware of these potential, though infrequent, issues and ensure timely software updates and battery checks. For the most part, the QX55 offers a dependable luxury driving experience.
