# 2022 INFINITI QX55 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury crossover, the Infiniti QX55 stands out with its coupe-like styling and distinctive presence. While owner feedback is relatively limited, prospective buyers should review initial reports regarding powertrain behavior and minor interior or safety system quirks. A thorough pre-purchase inspection can help verify that all mechanical and electronic systems are operating as expected.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L VC-Turbo 4-Cylinder

This 2.0-liter variable-compression turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 268 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, delivering a balance of efficiency and performance. Buyers should ensure proper maintenance records and watch for any reported hesitation or stalling behaviors during a test drive.

**Engine Stalling and Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine stalling while underway, accompanied by warning lights and occasional hesitation or abnormal noises when applying the brakes.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a qualified technician is recommended to check for ECM fault codes and address underlying powertrain or sensor anomalies.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Paired standard with all-wheel drive, this continuously variable transmission is designed to optimize fuel economy and smooth acceleration. Regular fluid maintenance is recommended to help ensure long-term durability.

## Interior

### Seats

Cabin comfort and seating adjustability are central to the luxury experience, making physical seat stability important to check during a test drive.

**Shifting Driver Seat**
- **Symptoms:** The driver's seat may exhibit noticeable movement or shifting when cornering, navigating on-ramps, or turning into driveways.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat track mounting bolts and frame components for looseness; adjust or replace hardware under warranty if applicable.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features enhance peace of mind, though sensor calibration is critical for proper operation.

**Unintentional Automatic Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience unexpected activation of the automatic emergency braking system, often accompanied by a high-pitched warning chime when shadows or minor obstructions are misidentified.
- **Solution:** Have dealership technicians inspect sensor alignment, calibration, and software updates to minimize false collision warnings.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V437000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX55&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Engine failure increases the risk of a crash. A bearing failure may cause a breach in the engine block, allowing hot oil to be discharged, increasing the risk of an engine fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the engine control module (ECM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The Infiniti QX55 offers striking styling and a modern luxury cabin, but used car buyers should remain vigilant during evaluations. Pay close attention to powertrain responsiveness, driver assistance sensor behavior, and interior fixtures during your test drive to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
