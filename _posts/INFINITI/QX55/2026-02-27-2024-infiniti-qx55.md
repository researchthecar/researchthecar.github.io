# 2024 INFINITI QX55

### Overview
The 2024 INFINITI QX55, a luxury crossover coupe, aims to blend sporty styling with premium comfort. Built on the QX50 platform, it shares much of its mechanical underpinnings, including its powertrain. Early data suggests a generally reliable vehicle, with most reported issues falling within expected parameters for a new model year. Owners generally praise its distinctive design, comfortable interior, and smooth ride. However, as with any vehicle, there are specific areas that warrant attention, particularly concerning the infotainment system and some minor electrical glitches. The VC-Turbo engine, while innovative, has a track record from previous models that indicates good, but not flawless, performance. Overall, the QX55 presents a compelling option for those seeking a stylish and comfortable luxury SUV, with a reliability profile that is competitive within its segment.

## Engine

### VC-Turbo (2.0L Variable Compression Turbocharged Inline-4)
**Oil Consumption Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Slightly higher than average oil consumption, especially under heavy load or during spirited driving. May require more frequent oil level checks.
- **Solution:** Regular oil level monitoring and top-offs. Ensure adherence to recommended oil type and change intervals. Consult dealer if consumption is excessive.
- **Cost:** $50 - $150 (for oil and filter change)

**Turbocharger Lag**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional slight delay in power delivery from a standstill or at low RPMs.
- **Solution:** This is a characteristic of some variable compression turbo engines. Software updates or driving style adjustments may mitigate.
- **Cost:** No widespread issues reported.


## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Infrequent reports of slight hesitation or minor jerking during gear shifts or at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Software updates for the transmission control module. Regular fluid checks and changes are crucial.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300 (for fluid service)

### Summary Verdict
The 2024 INFINITI QX55 demonstrates a solid reliability profile for a luxury crossover coupe, particularly given its relatively new status. The primary areas of concern are minor and largely related to the infotainment system's software and the inherent characteristics of the VC-Turbo engine, such as oil consumption. The CVT transmission, while generally smooth, has seen isolated reports of minor hesitations. However, these issues are not widespread and often addressable with software updates or routine maintenance. The suspension, exterior, and interior components appear to be holding up well. For potential buyers, regular maintenance and staying updated on any manufacturer service bulletins are recommended to ensure optimal performance and longevity.
