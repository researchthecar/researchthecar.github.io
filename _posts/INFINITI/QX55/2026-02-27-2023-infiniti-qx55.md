# 2023 INFINITI QX55 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Infiniti QX55 brings coupe-like styling and luxury appointments to the crossover market, but used-car buyers should review its mechanical and electrical history carefully. While owner reports to federal regulators remain limited, powertrain and interior concerns have surfaced among early adopters. Evaluating specific service history and verifying proper operation of electronics and seating components will help ensure a dependable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Variable Compression Turbo (VC-Turbo) Inline-4

The QX55 is powered by a 2.0-liter variable compression turbocharged inline-4 engine producing approximately 268 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. While offering innovative engineering and responsive performance, buyers should thoroughly inspect service records for any signs of internal mechanical distress.

**Sudden Engine Failure and Knocking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine knocking, illuminated oil pressure warnings, and sudden loss of acceleration culminating in complete engine failure.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis and potential engine replacement depending on internal component damage.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Power is routed through a continuously variable transmission paired with standard all-wheel drive. The transmission is designed to maximize fuel efficiency, though routine fluid maintenance is essential for longevity.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Connectivity

In-cabin technology provides seamless connectivity features, though occasional software glitches can affect phone integration.

**Bluetooth and Apple CarPlay Disconnections**
- **Symptoms:** Smartphones connected via Bluetooth or Apple CarPlay may suddenly disconnect after several minutes of normal operation while driving.
- **Solution:** Check for system software updates or reset infotainment and phone pairing connections.

## Interior

### Rear Seats

The cabin features modern luxury amenities and seating configurations, though comfort systems should be tested during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Rear Seat Heater Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced rear seat heaters malfunctioning and overheating, which can scorch cushion materials and create potential burn or fire hazards.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat heating elements and consult a dealer to address faulty wiring or switch components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V187000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Lock](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX55&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An unlocked door may unlatch and open during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front right-side passenger door lock assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Infiniti QX55 offers striking design and advanced tech features, but prospective buyers should proceed with due diligence. Given reported concerns regarding engine reliability and interior accessories, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verification of warranty coverage are strongly recommended before finalizing a purchase.
