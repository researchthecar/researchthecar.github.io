# 2017 INFINITI Q60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Infiniti Q60 is a striking luxury coupe that offers sharp styling and potent powertrain options. While many owners enjoy its performance, prospective buyers should review certain recurring complaints concerning powertrain hesitations, steering tracking issues, and expensive seat sensor failures. Keeping a close eye on maintenance history and checking all electronic systems before purchase can help avoid unexpected repair costs.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This entry-level 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate daily driving power. Buyers should ensure proper turbo maintenance, as rare catastrophic failures have been reported at moderate mileages.

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (VR30DDTT)

This twin-turbocharged 3.0-liter V6 is available in 300-hp or 400-hp (Red Sport) configurations, delivering robust performance. While generally powerful, buyers should monitor for occasional throttle or ventilation-related fault codes.

**Loss of Motive Power and Fault Codes**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience unexpected hesitation, failure to accelerate when the pedal is depressed, and illuminated check engine lights accompanied by fault codes such as P052E.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the specific fault code, inspect the crankcase ventilation or throttle systems, and update or replace faulty components as needed.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

A traditional 7-speed automatic transmission handles power delivery across rear-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. It generally offers smooth operation, though routine fluid checks are recommended to maintain longevity.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Routine brake inspections are vital to catch component wear or loose hardware before safety is compromised.

**Brake Pad Cracking and Caliper Separation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may hear a continuous grinding, tapping, or metallic noise from the wheels, caused by damaged brake pads or loose caliper bolts.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and caliper mounting bolts during regular service intervals, and replace worn or damaged hardware immediately.

## Steering

### Steering System

Issues related to alignment sensitivity and tracking can make the vehicle feel unsettled on certain road surfaces.

**Vehicle Wandering and Pulling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may drift or pull to the left or right while driving at various speeds, requiring constant steering correction to stay in the lane.
- **Solution:** Check wheel alignment, suspension components, and steering calibration. Some owners note that sensitive alignment settings or electronic steering features require careful tuning.

## Safety

### Occupant Classification Sensor

Airbag sensor malfunctions can disable crucial safety systems and involve expensive out-of-pocket repairs if component replacement is required.

**Passenger Seat Weight Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning light illuminates on the dashboard, indicating a fault within the passenger occupant detection system.
- **Solution:** Dealerships typically diagnose the failed sensor, but replacement may require purchasing an entire seat frame assembly, with out-of-pocket costs cited up to $8,200.
- **Cost:** $8200.00

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V476000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q60&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and Infiniti dealers will reprogram the Fuel Pump Control Module (FPCM) with corrected software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Infiniti Q60 rewards drivers with sharp styling and engaging performance, particularly with the twin-turbo V6 engine options. However, buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle for steering wander, brake hardware integrity, and functional airbag sensors. Verifying maintenance history and ensuring all electronic safety systems operate correctly will help secure a reliable ownership experience.
