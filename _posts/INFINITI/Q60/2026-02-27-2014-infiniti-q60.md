# 2014 INFINITI Q60

### Overview
The 2014 INFINITI Q60, a luxury sports coupe, generally offers a solid ownership experience with a focus on performance and premium features. While it shares its platform with the Nissan 370Z, INFINITI's refinements aim for a more comfortable and sophisticated ride. Reliability for this model year is largely positive, with most owners reporting satisfaction. However, as with any performance-oriented vehicle, certain components may require more attention over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective buyers and current owners.

### Infotainment System
**Infotainment Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system reset. In rare cases, unit replacement may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1500 (Software update to unit replacement)


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dying unexpectedly, requiring jump starts or replacement sooner than expected.
- **Solution:** Diagnose parasitic draw or faulty charging system components. Replace battery if necessary.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 INFINITI Q60 is generally a reliable luxury sports coupe. The powertrain, including the robust 3.7L V6 engine and the 7-speed automatic transmission, demonstrates strong durability with no widespread issues reported. Similarly, the suspension and exterior components are typically trouble-free. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are within the electrical system, specifically concerning infotainment glitches and occasional battery drain, and the interior, with potential HVAC blower motor failures. These issues are not pervasive but are worth noting. Overall, the Q60 offers a good balance of performance and dependability for its class, provided routine maintenance is performed and potential electrical/HVAC concerns are addressed promptly.
