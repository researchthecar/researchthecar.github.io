**2019 INFINITI Q60**

The 2019 INFINITI Q60 is a luxury sports coupe that offers a range of engine and transmission options. The base engine is a turbocharged 3.0-liter V6 that produces 300 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. A more powerful version of the V6, known as the Red Sport 400, is available and produces 400 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a seven-speed automatic transmission.

Drivers appreciate the Q60's stylish design, powerful engines, and responsive handling, while some have expressed concerns about its firm ride and limited visibility.

**Engine**

**3.0-liter V6**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** Symptoms include reduced engine power, rattling noises, and check engine light illumination. Solution: Replace the turbocharger. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** Symptoms include rattling noises from the engine and potential engine damage if not addressed promptly. Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**3.0-liter V6 Red Sport 400**

* **High-pressure Fuel Pump Failure:** Symptoms include stalling, difficulty starting, and reduced engine power. Solution: Replace the high-pressure fuel pump. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
* **Camshaft Actuator Failure:** Symptoms include reduced engine power, rough idling, and check engine light illumination. Solution: Replace the camshaft actuator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+

**Transmission**

**Seven-Speed Automatic**

* **Hesitation or Delay:** Symptoms include a noticeable delay in gear changes or hesitation when shifting. Solution: Software update or transmission fluid replacement may resolve the issue. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+
* **Shifting Problems:** Symptoms include difficulty shifting gears, getting stuck in a particular gear, or grinding noises. Solution: May require replacing the transmission control module or other components within the transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:** Symptoms include difficulty starting, electrical system malfunctions, or a dead battery. Solution: Replace the battery or diagnose electrical components for potential parasitic drain. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Infotainment System Software Glitches:** Symptoms include freezing, crashing, or unresponsive touchscreen. Solution: Software update or potentially replacing the infotainment unit. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:** Symptoms include inoperable windshield wipers or intermittent operation. Solution: Replace the wiper motor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **Window Regulator Failure:** Symptoms include windows that don't roll up or down properly, or become stuck. Solution: Replace the window regulator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions:** Symptoms include inconsistent temperature control, inoperative air conditioning or heating, or strange noises from the vents. Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty components within the climate control system. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
* **Power Seat Malfunctions:** Symptoms include seats that do not move, get stuck, or move erratically. Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty motors or electrical connections within the seat system. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+

**Summary**

The 2019 INFINITI Q60 is a well-regarded sports coupe, but it does have some potential issues that buyers should be aware of. The most significant issues are related to the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be costly to repair. Overall, the Q60 is a reliable vehicle, but potential buyers should be prepared for the possibility of some repairs.
