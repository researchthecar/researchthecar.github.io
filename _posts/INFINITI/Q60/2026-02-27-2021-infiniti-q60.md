# 2021 INFINITI Q60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Infiniti Q60, buyers will find a stylish luxury coupe that delivers strong turbocharged performance and striking road presence. While owner-reported complaints are relatively limited, prospective buyers should review available powertrain options and check for electrical or structural warnings during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (300 hp)

The base 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 produces 300 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk everyday acceleration. This engine provides a solid balance of luxury cruising capability and turbocharged performance.

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (400 hp - Red Sport 400)

The high-output 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 generates 400 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque for the Red Sport 400 trim. Buyers prioritizing aggressive performance will appreciate this potent engine, though maintenance history remains vital.

### Engine Electrical

Monitors engine electrical controls and wiring harnesses that manage powertrain sensors and dashboard warnings.

**Electrical Warning Lights and Sudden Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that multiple warning lights suddenly illuminate on the dash, followed by a loss of engine power or the vehicle entering limp mode.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan the vehicle computer for stored diagnostic trouble codes and inspect the electrical charging and sensor systems.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to either the rear wheels or intelligent all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid inspections help ensure long-term shifting reliability.

## Safety

### Structure and Brackets

Covers structural framework, braces, and collision avoidance mounting hardware.

**Front Structural Bracket Fracture**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of malfunction warning lights accompanied by structural component fatigue or a fractured aluminum bracket located between front assemblies.
- **Solution:** Inspect structural support brackets for cracks or physical damage and replace any compromised components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V599000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q60&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rear-view camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V234000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm):Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q60&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A lack of fuel may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** INFINITI will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the ECM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Infiniti Q60 offers compelling styling and potent twin-turbo V6 performance, making it an appealing choice for coupe buyers. While overall complaint volume is low, buyers should perform a thorough test drive and pre-purchase inspection to verify electrical stability and structural integrity.
