**2017 INFINITI Q60**

The 2017 INFINITI Q60 is a luxury sports coupe that offers a thrilling driving experience with its powerful engines and responsive handling. It comes with two engine options: a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 producing 300 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 with 400 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The Q60 has received praise for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and advanced technology features. However, like any vehicle, it has also had some issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 (300 horsepower)**
* **Intake Valve Issues:** Sticking or damaged intake valves can cause rough idling, reduced power, and increased emissions.
   * Solution: Repair or replace the intake valves.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Exhaust System Failure:** Leakage or corrosion in the exhaust system can lead to reduced power, poor fuel economy, and loud exhaust noise.
   * Solution: Repair or replace the affected exhaust components.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
   * Recalls: None Found

**3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 (400 horsepower)**
* **Timing Chain Noise:** A faulty timing chain or tensioner can produce a rattling or grinding noise from the engine.
   * Solution: Replace the timing chain and tensioner.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:** A malfunctioning turbocharger can cause reduced power, increased oil consumption, and smoke coming from the exhaust.
   * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-speed Automatic Transmission**
* **Shifting Problems:** The transmission may experience delayed or rough shifting, hesitation, or slipping.
   * Solution: Repair or replace the transmission components.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Fluid Leaks:** Leaking transmission fluid can cause reduced performance and damage to transmission components.
   * Solution: Repair the source of the leak and replace the transmission fluid.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Shock Absorber Failure:** Worn or damaged shock absorbers can lead to poor ride quality, excessive bouncing, and decreased handling.
   * Solution: Replace the shock absorbers.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+ per axle
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Strut Mount Failure:** Defective strut mounts can cause clunking or squeaking noises from the suspension.
   * Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+ per axle
   * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** A faulty battery or charging system can lead to the battery draining quickly.
   * Solution: Replace the battery or repair the charging system.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Issues:** Various electrical problems like malfunctioning lights, windows, or infotainment system have been reported.
   * Solution: Diagnose and repair the specific electrical issue.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
   * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Moisture buildup inside the headlights can affect visibility.
   * Solution: Replace the headlights.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+ per headlight
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Panel Gaps:** Uneven gaps between body panels can affect the vehicle's aesthetics.
   * Solution: Adjust or replace the affected panels.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:** The infotainment system may experience software bugs, slow performance, or connectivity issues.
   * Solution: Update the software or repair the system.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunctions:** The climate control system may blow hot or cold air improperly.
   * Solution: Repair or replace the climate control components.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,500+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 INFINITI Q60 is a stylish and powerful sports coupe that has received positive reviews for its performance and features. However, it has also experienced some issues that can be costly to repair. Engine and transmission problems seem to be the most severe, with potential repair costs exceeding $2,000. Electrical and interior issues, while less severe, can still affect the overall ownership experience.
