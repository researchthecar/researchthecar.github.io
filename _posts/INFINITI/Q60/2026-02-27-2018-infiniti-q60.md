# 2018 INFINITI Q60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Infiniti Q60 offers striking coupe styling and spirited performance, making it an attractive option on the used luxury market. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported reliability trends carefully, particularly concerning electrical systems, safety components, and powertrain maintenance history.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The entry-level 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces around 208 horsepower and offers modest fuel economy for daily driving. Buyers should inspect belt conditions and service history regularly.

**Accessory Belt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The accessory drive belt may fracture unexpectedly without major prior warning signs, making the vehicle difficult to control and posing a safety hazard.
- **Solution:** Inspect the accessory belt periodically for wear and replace it immediately if cracking or fraying is observed.

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (300 hp)

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 delivers a smooth 300 horsepower, providing a balanced blend of responsive acceleration and everyday drivability for luxury coupe owners.

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (400 hp - Red Sport 400)

The high-output 3.0-liter twin-turbo V6 pushes 400 horsepower for aggressive performance enthusiasts. Regular maintenance and careful pre-purchase inspections are recommended due to the higher stress on forced-induction components.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

A traditional 7-speed automatic transmission manages power delivery across the Q60 lineup, offering smooth shifts when properly maintained.

## Brakes

### Anti-Lock Brake System

Brake performance is strong under normal conditions, but older or high-mileage hydraulic components should be thoroughly checked during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Spongy Brake Pedal and ABS Actuator Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The brake pedal may feel spongy or travel excessively toward the floor before engaging stopping power adequately.
- **Solution:** Inspect the braking system hydraulics and ABS actuator assembly, and bleed or replace components as necessary.

## Steering

### Direct Adaptive Steering

The available drive-by-wire steering system provides adjustable feel but relies heavily on complex electronics that can occasionally require software updates or sensor service.

**Steering Stiffness and Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the steering wheel becomes extremely heavy and difficult to turn, accompanied by a steering warning indicator on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Restarting the vehicle can temporarily clear electronic glitches, but persistent issues require a dealer or specialist diagnosis of the electronic steering actuators and modules.

## Safety

### Air Bag Occupancy System

Safety equipment generally performs well, though occasional sensor faults in the occupant detection system can trigger warning lights.

**Airbag Warning Light and Sensor Faults**
- **Symptoms:** The dashboard airbag or SRS warning light illuminates persistently, often indicating a fault within the passenger occupancy classification system or seat components.
- **Solution:** Have the diagnostic trouble codes scanned by a qualified technician to determine if the OCS unit, sensor, or seat frame requires repair or replacement.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V654000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q60&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Infiniti Q60 remains a stylish and engaging used coupe choice, provided buyers verify its mechanical and electronic health. Pay close attention to service records, steering responsiveness, and safety warning lights during your test drive to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
