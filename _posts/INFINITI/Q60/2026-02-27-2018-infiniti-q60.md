# 2018 INFINITI Q60

### Overview
The 2018 INFINITI Q60 is a luxury sports coupe that generally offers a good balance of performance and comfort. While it presents a stylish design and engaging driving dynamics, like any vehicle, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, examining common problems reported by owners and repair data. The Q60 is available with different powertrain configurations, and reliability can vary slightly between them. Understanding these potential issues can help prospective buyers and current owners make informed decisions regarding maintenance and repairs.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (VR30DDTT)
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, whistling noises from the engine bay, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil on the engine or under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of gaskets, seals, or oil cooler lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (JR710E/JR711E)
**Harsh Shifting / Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable clunks or jerks during gear changes, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and refill, software update, or transmission repair/rebuild.
- **Cost:** $400 - $2,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 INFINITI Q60, particularly with the potent 3.0L twin-turbo V6 engine, is generally a reliable luxury coupe. However, potential owners should be aware of reported issues concerning turbocharger reliability and occasional oil leaks, which can be costly to repair. The 7-speed automatic transmission can sometimes exhibit harsh shifting. Electrical gremlins, primarily with the infotainment system, and premature battery drain are also noted concerns. While the suspension, exterior, and interior components show good reliability, proactive maintenance and awareness of these specific potential issues are recommended for long-term ownership satisfaction.
