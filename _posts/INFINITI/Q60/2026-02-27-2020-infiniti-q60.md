# 2020 INFINITI Q60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Infiniti Q60, buyers will find a stylish luxury sport coupe that pairs striking design with turbocharged performance. While formal owner complaints and reported issues are relatively minimal for this model year, prospective owners should still carefully evaluate individual vehicle condition, maintenance history, and electronics functionality during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (300 hp)

This entry-level 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 delivers 300 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, offering strong acceleration for daily driving. It provides a balanced blend of everyday drivability and sporty performance for the luxury coupe segment.

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (400 hp - Red Sport 400)

Reserved for the Red Sport 400 trim, this high-output 3.0-liter twin-turbo V6 pushes out 400 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque. It provides exhilarating performance, though buyers should verify powertrain health and electronic throttle response during a test drive.

**Unintended Acceleration Incident**
- **Symptoms:** A driver experienced sudden, unexpected acceleration while traveling at highway speeds during a rainstorm.
- **Solution:** Have the throttle body, accelerator pedal sensor, and engine control module inspected by a qualified technician.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic

The 7-speed automatic transmission comes paired with rear-wheel drive or intelligent all-wheel drive across the lineup. Routine fluid maintenance helps ensure smooth shifting and long-term durability.

## Safety

### Passenger Airbag Sensor

Safety systems generally perform reliably, though occasional sensor glitches can require dealer diagnosis.

**Intermittent Passenger Airbag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning light intermittently turns on and off, sometimes fluctuating even after a passenger occupies the seat.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat occupancy sensor mat, wiring harness, and airbag control module connections for faults.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V145000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q60&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The seat belt webbing sensor locking mechanism may not lock as intended. In the event of a crash involving multiple impacts, the seat belt may not properly restrain the occupant, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rear seat belt assembly production dates, and replace the assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Infiniti Q60 remains an appealing option for used luxury coupe buyers seeking distinctive styling and potent twin-turbo power. Because owner complaint data is sparse, prospective buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection to check electronics, safety sensors, and powertrain responsiveness before finalizing a purchase.
