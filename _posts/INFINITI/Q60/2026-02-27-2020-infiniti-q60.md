# 2020 INFINITI Q60

### Overview
The 2020 INFINITI Q60 is a luxury sports coupe that offers a blend of performance and style. While generally considered a reliable vehicle, like any car, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential weak points. The Q60 is known for its potent V6 engine options and engaging driving dynamics. Understanding these common concerns can help prospective buyers and current owners make informed decisions regarding maintenance and potential repairs.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (VR30DDTT)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Rattle**
- **Symptoms:** Audible rattling noise from the engine bay, particularly at low RPMs or during deceleration.
- **Solution:** Wastegate actuator replacement or adjustment.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking gaskets or seals (e.g., valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket).
- **Cost:** $200 - $600


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (JR710E/JR711E)
**Harsh Shifting / Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, hesitation when shifting into drive or reverse.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and replacement, software update, or internal transmission repair.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1500

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 INFINITI Q60 presents a generally solid reliability record, with its powerful 3.0L twin-turbo V6 engine being the highlight. The most commonly cited issues revolve around minor engine-related concerns like turbocharger wastegate rattle and occasional oil leaks, which are typically manageable. The transmission, while robust, can sometimes exhibit harsh shifting, often resolvable with software updates or fluid services. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system's responsiveness, are also noted. Suspension, exterior, and interior components have shown good durability. Overall, the Q60 is a reliable choice for a luxury sports coupe, provided owners are aware of and address these specific potential issues proactively.
