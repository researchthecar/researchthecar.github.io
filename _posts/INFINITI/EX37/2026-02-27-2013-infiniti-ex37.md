# 2013 INFINITI EX37

### Overview
The 2013 INFINITI EX37, a luxury crossover known for its sporty handling and premium interior, generally exhibits good reliability. While no vehicle is entirely without potential issues, the EX37 has a solid reputation for its powertrain and core components. Owners often praise its comfortable ride and advanced features for its model year. However, like many vehicles of this age, some owners may encounter specific, though not widespread, concerns related to certain electronic systems or minor interior wear. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for prospective buyers or current owners.

### Summary Verdict
The 2013 INFINITI EX37 is a generally reliable luxury crossover. Its 3.7L V6 engine and 5-speed automatic transmission are robust and have few documented widespread issues. The suspension system also holds up well. Potential areas of concern are primarily within the electrical system, such as infotainment glitches and power liftgate malfunctions, and some interior wear on seats. The HVAC blower motor is another component that may require attention. Overall, the EX37 offers a dependable ownership experience, with most issues being minor and repairable, especially when compared to some competitors of the same era. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring its continued reliability.
