# 2019 INFINITI QX30 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Infiniti QX30 brings distinctive styling and a Mercedes-Benz-derived platform to the compact luxury crossover segment. While overall owner complaint volume remains relatively low, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate powertrain performance and available safety technology during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. Developed in cooperation with Mercedes-Benz, it delivers punchy low-end acceleration but requires diligent adherence to maintenance schedules for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routes power to front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive configurations. It offers quick gear shifts, though buyers should test drive to ensure smooth operation at low speeds.

## Electrical

### Accelerator Position Sensor

Electrical and sensor complaints are infrequent, but intermittent wiring or sensor faults can occasionally impact drivability.

**Loss of Power During Acceleration**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may suddenly lose power while driving at highway speeds, creating a dangerous hesitation or failure to accelerate properly.
- **Solution:** Inspect the accelerator position sensor and associated wiring harness, and replace faulty electrical components as necessary.

## Safety

### Forward Emergency Braking

Advanced safety features enhance protection, though owners should verify that driver-assist sensors and collision avoidance systems are fully operational.

**Forward Collision Avoidance Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected failures or improper activation of the forward emergency braking and collision avoidance systems.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the radar sensors, camera alignment, and system control modules for diagnostic trouble codes.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V654000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX30&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Infiniti QX30 is an intriguing option for shoppers seeking European luxury flair in a compact footprint. Buyers should prioritize a thorough test drive and professional inspection focusing on electrical sensors and driver-assist features to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
