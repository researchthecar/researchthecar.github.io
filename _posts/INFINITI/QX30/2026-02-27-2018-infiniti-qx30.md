# 2018 INFINITI QX30 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2018 Infiniti QX30, buyers should note that this luxury subcompact SUV—developed in partnership with Mercedes-Benz—offers a distinct European flavor. While owner complaints cover a variety of electrical, engine, and interior issues, approaching this vehicle with a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help you spot potential trouble spots before sealing the deal.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, sourced from Mercedes-Benz. It offers brisk acceleration for city driving, though buyers should watch for occasional sensor and diagnostic trouble codes as reported in owner feedback.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers power to either the front wheels or an all-wheel-drive system. Prospective owners should pay close attention to shift quality during a test drive to ensure smooth operation.

**Unexpected Shifting into Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly slip into neutral while driving at highway speeds accompanied by a warning message on the dash.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician or dealer inspect the transmission control module and software calibration.

## Electrical

### Battery and Audio System

Electrical architecture relies heavily on integrated harnesses and modules, making thorough diagnostic testing wise for used models.

**Battery Drain from Radio**
- **Symptoms:** The radio fails to turn off when the vehicle is powered down, resulting in a completely dead battery within hours.
- **Solution:** Inspect the radio module, wiring harnesses, and ignition switch logic to isolate the parasitic draw.

## Interior

### Headliner

Cabin appointments generally offer a luxury feel, but trim and headliner durability warrant careful inspection during a used car test.

**Sagging Rear Headliner**
- **Symptoms:** The rear portion of the headliner can detach and sag downward, potentially obstructing rear window visibility.
- **Solution:** Re-securing or replacing the headliner assembly through an upholstery specialist or dealer.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V355000: Seat Belts:Front:Anchorage](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX30&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** This can result in the lower seat belt anchorage detaching in a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with certain requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages", number 209, "Seat Belt Assemblies, and number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." - **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will weld in a new retaining plate and secure the seat belt anchorage with a new anchor bolt, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V654000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX30&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 17V691000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX30&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The driver's air bag may unexpectedly deploy due to insufficient grounding of the steering components if an electrostatic discharge occurs and the air bag clockspring is broken. If the driver air bag inadvertently deploys, it can increase the risk of injury and a crash.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will add sufficient grounding to the steering components, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Infiniti QX30 presents a stylish and nimble option for used car buyers seeking luxury proportions, but it requires mindful evaluation. Be sure to check the transmission shift behavior, verify proper electrical accessory shutoff, and inspect the cabin for any interior trim or headliner wear before purchasing.
