# 2017 INFINITI QX30 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Infiniti QX30 brings distinctive styling and Mercedes-Benz-derived mechanical underpinnings to the luxury compact crossover segment. While offering an engaging drive, used car buyers should note that this model has accumulated notable owner complaints concerning electrical issues, steering concerns, and interior trim durability. Thoroughly inspecting service records and verifying completed safety recalls is strongly recommended before purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

Powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing approximately 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, sourced from Mercedes-Benz. It provides punchy acceleration for daily driving, though proper maintenance of its turbo system and cooling components is essential for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

Pairs with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission available in front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive configurations. While gear changes are generally crisp, owners should test-drive thoroughly to ensure smooth engagement from a stop and during low-speed maneuvering.

## Steering

### Steering Assembly

Steering complaints center around power assist failures and mechanical binding, requiring careful verification of recall completion.

**Steering Seizure or Loss of Power Assist**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported warning messages such as ABS inoperable, followed by sudden losses of power steering assist or intermittent steering wheel stiffness.
- **Solution:** Check for uncompleted steering recalls, inspect the lower steering shaft, and have the electronic power steering module and sensors diagnosed by a qualified technician.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Backup Camera

Electrical system concerns generally involve minor screen blackouts and accessory faults rather than total system failures.

**Intermittent Blackout Screen**
- **Symptoms:** The navigation and infotainment display screen intermittently goes completely black when shifting into reverse, eliminating the backup camera view.
- **Solution:** Inspect software firmware versions, display wiring harnesses, and the rear camera module connections.

## Interior

### Headliner and Sunshade

Interior fit and finish complaints are notable for premature wear on overhead trim components.

**Sagging Headliner and Panoramic Sunshade Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The rear headliner fabric detaches and sags significantly, while the panoramic sunroof sunshade material can easily rip or tear, obstructing rear visibility.
- **Solution:** Replace or professionally re-glue the sagging headliner assembly and install updated sunshade components.
- **Cost:** Varies widely depending on professional upholstery labor rates.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Safety component complaints primarily focus on intermittent warning lights requiring sensor or clock spring replacement.

**Air Bag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The supplemental restraint system (SRS) warning light illuminates unexpectedly on the instrument cluster, indicating a fault within the steering wheel clock spring module.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the SRS trouble codes and replace the failed clock spring module or associated wiring harness.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V143000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX30&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have a driver-side curtain air bag inflator initiator that may fail to ignite during a crash, preventing the air bag from deploying. If the air bag fails to deploy in the event of a crash, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will replace the driver-side curtain air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V691000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX30&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The driver's air bag may unexpectedly deploy due to insufficient grounding of the steering components if an electrostatic discharge occurs and the air bag clockspring is broken. If the driver air bag inadvertently deploys, it can increase the risk of injury and a crash.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will add sufficient grounding to the steering components, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V939000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX30&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have a passenger air bag inflator initiator that may fail to ignite during a crash, preventing the air bag from deploying. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." - **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V419000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX30&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The steering shaft bore hole for the universal joint bearing may allow excessive movement that, over time, could result in the separation of the bearing and universal joint. A separated universal joint bearing can cause a loss of steering control and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lower steering shaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Infiniti QX30 offers distinctive styling and German mechanical roots, but prospective buyers should inspect the vehicle thoroughly. Pay close attention to interior trim condition, electrical accessories like the backup camera, and ensure all safety recalls have been properly addressed by a dealership before finalizing a purchase.
