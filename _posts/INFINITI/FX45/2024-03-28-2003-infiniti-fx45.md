**Introduction:**

The 2003 INFINITI FX45 is a mid-size SUV powered by a powerful 4.5-liter V8 engine mated to a 5-speed automatic transmission. It delivers an impressive 315 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque. The FX45 is known for its sporty handling, luxurious interior, and ample cargo space. However, various issues have emerged over the years, affecting different components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **4.5L V8 Engine**
    * **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup:** Carbon buildup on the intake valves can cause misfiring, reduced engine power, and poor fuel economy.
    * **Solution:** Intake valve cleaning and decarbonization.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
```

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Slipping Gears:** Transmission slipping can result in difficulty shifting gears or loss of power during acceleration.
    * **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and replacement, or transmission rebuild in severe cases.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $3,500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings:** Worn or damaged control arm bushings can cause excessive noise, vibration, and poor handling.
* **Solution:** Replacement of control arm bushings.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $700+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **HID Headlight Ballast Failure:** The HID headlight ballasts can fail, resulting in inoperative headlights.
* **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty ballast.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+ per side
* **Recalls:** NHTSA Recall ID: 07V187000 (Headlight Ballast)

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Wind Deflector Issues:** The sunroof wind deflector may become loose or damaged, causing wind noise and buffeting.
* **Solution:** Replacement of the wind deflector.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control System Malfunctions:** The HVAC system may experience issues with temperature control, fan speeds, or airflow direction.
* **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of the faulty components, such as the blower motor, control module, or actuators.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 2003 INFINITI FX45 is a vehicle that offers impressive performance and luxurious amenities. However, it has experienced various issues over the years, ranging from minor to more severe. Some of the most common problems include engine intake valve carbon buildup, transmission slipping, suspension bushing wear, electrical issues with the headlight ballasts, and sunroof wind deflector problems. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, but they should be addressed promptly to ensure the可靠性 and safety of the vehicle. Regular maintenance and timely repairs can help to minimize the occurrence of these issues and preserve the overall value of the FX45.
