**Introduction**

The 2016 INFINITI QX70 is a mid-size luxury SUV that comes equipped with a 3.7-liter V6 engine or a 5.0-liter V8 engine, each paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The QX70 is known for its stylish design, advanced technology, and comfortable interior. However, like any vehicle, it has been reported to have certain issues.

**Engine**

* **3.7-liter V6**
    * **Timing Chain Rattle:** A rattling noise coming from the engine, caused by a faulty timing chain tensioner.
        * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and timing chain.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **5.0-liter V8**
    * **Engine Tick:** A ticking noise heard from the engine, caused by worn-out valve lifters.
        * Solution: Replacement of the valve lifters.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Hesitation:** Delay or hesitation when shifting gears, caused by faulty transmission solenoids.
        * Solution: Replacement of the transmission solenoids.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Failure of the alternator, causing electrical system issues and battery drain.
        * Solution: Replacement of the alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Harness Issues:** Short circuits or loose connections in the electrical harness, leading to various electrical problems.
        * Solution: Inspection and repair or replacement of the affected wiring.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:** Water leaking from the sunroof due to a faulty drain or seal.
        * Solution: Repair or replacement of the sunroof drain or seal.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:** A rattling noise coming from the dashboard, caused by loose screws or brackets.
        * Solution: Tightening of loose screws or replacement of brackets.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Failure of the climate control system, causing issues with heating, cooling, or airflow.
        * Solution: Inspection and repair or replacement of the affected components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 INFINITI QX70 has been reported to have a few specific issues, mainly related to the engine, transmission, and electrical components. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost of repairs can range from a few hundred dollars to over $1,500. Overall, the QX70 is a reliable vehicle, but it's important to be aware of the potential issues before making a purchase.
