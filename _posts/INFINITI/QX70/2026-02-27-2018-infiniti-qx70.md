# 2018 INFINITI QX70

### Overview
The 2018 INFINITI QX70 is a luxury mid-size SUV known for its distinctive styling and engaging driving dynamics. While generally considered a reliable vehicle, like any automotive model, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for prospective buyers and current owners. The QX70, particularly in its later years, benefits from INFINITI's established engineering, but understanding common failure points can aid in proactive maintenance and informed decision-making regarding repairs.

---


## Engine

### 3.7L V6 (VQ37VHR)
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, low oil level warnings, burning oil smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking gaskets (e.g., valve cover, oil pan) or seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles, sweet smell from engine bay, white smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Replacement of radiator, hoses, water pump, or head gasket.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic (JR710E/JR711E)
**Harsh Shifting / Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, difficulty engaging drive or reverse, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, solenoid replacement, or transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3000


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or rattling noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn suspension bushings.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing / Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive, system freezes or reboots randomly.
- **Solution:** Software update, or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical components malfunctioning, battery warning light.
- **Solution:** Testing and replacement of the battery, diagnosis of parasitic draw.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 INFINITI QX70 generally presents a solid reliability record, with its robust 3.7L V6 engine and 7-speed automatic transmission being the core of its dependable performance. However, owners should be aware of potential issues such as oil and coolant leaks from the engine, and occasional harsh shifting from the transmission, which may require attention. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and battery drain, are also noted. Suspension components like bushings can wear over time, leading to noise and handling degradation. Exterior and interior components are largely free from widespread defects. Overall, the QX70 offers a reliable luxury SUV experience, provided owners are mindful of these specific potential concerns and address them promptly.
