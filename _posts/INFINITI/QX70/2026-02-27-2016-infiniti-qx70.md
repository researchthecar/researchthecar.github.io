# 2016 INFINITI QX70 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Infiniti QX70 blends sports-car styling with crossover utility, featuring a distinct design and performance-oriented driving dynamics. For prospective used-car buyers, researching owner feedback and maintaining thorough pre-purchase inspections are essential steps to ensure long-term satisfaction.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L V6 Engine

This 3.7-liter naturally aspirated V6 engine produces approximately 325 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque. It is generally regarded as a robust and durable powerplant for this luxury crossover.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission features manual shift modes and downshift rev matching. It delivers responsive gear changes and pairs well with both rear-wheel and all-wheel drive configurations.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Safety equipment owner feedback highlights potential concerns that warrant careful pre-purchase inspection.

**Air Bag Deployment Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Following a front-end collision, the vehicle's frontal airbags failed to deploy as expected.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a certified professional or authorized dealer to diagnose and repair any SRS system faults or sensor issues.
