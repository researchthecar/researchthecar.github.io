# 2015 INFINITI QX70

### Overview
The 2015 INFINITI QX70, a luxury crossover SUV, generally presents a solid reliability record, particularly concerning its powertrain. Owners often praise its robust V6 engine and smooth transmission performance. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the QX70 tends to avoid widespread, recurring problems that significantly impact its day-to-day usability or long-term value. Common areas of attention, as with many vehicles of this age and class, might involve certain electronic features or suspension components under high mileage, but these are typically manageable and not indicative of systemic flaws. Overall, the 2015 QX70 is considered a dependable choice within its segment.

## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or popping noises when turning the steering wheel, especially at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the strut mount bearings.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Worn Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Uneven tire wear, vague steering feel, clunking noises over bumps.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen may freeze, become unresponsive to touch, or reboot randomly.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, often after the vehicle has been sitting for a period. May be related to parasitic draw from certain modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of parasitic draw, potentially involving module replacement or software reflash.
- **Cost:** $200 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 INFINITI QX70 demonstrates commendable reliability, with its core powertrain components being particularly robust.
