# 2017 INFINITI QX70 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Infiniti QX70, buyers get a performance-oriented luxury crossover known for its distinct styling and potent powertrain options. While owner reports to safety agencies highlight a few isolated electrical, body, and powertrain concerns, prospective owners should evaluate each vehicle carefully during a test drive and pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L V6 Engine

The 2017 Infiniti QX70 features a 3.7-liter V6 engine producing approximately 325 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and generally solid mechanical durability, though individual electrical and drainage issues have been reported.

**Fuel Gauge Inaccuracy**
- **Symptoms:** The fuel gauge may display an incorrect reading, such as showing half a tank when the fuel level is actually depleted, potentially leading to unexpected stalling.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the fuel sending unit or level sensor and replace faulty components as necessary.

**Water Intrusion and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Water may leak into the cabin from the sunroof drains during heavy rain, potentially reaching under-dash electronics and causing starting issues or engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Clear or repair clogged or defective sunroof drain tubes and inspect dashboard wiring for moisture damage.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 7-speed automatic transmission featuring manual shift modes, this drivetrain routes power smoothly to the rear wheels or intelligent all-wheel-drive system. Most units operate reliably, though occasional shifting irregularities have been noted by owners.

**Hard Shifting and Gear Selection Delay**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience harsh shifting behavior or temporary failure of the transmission to engage the proper gear while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid condition, update control modules if applicable, or have a professional diagnostician inspect internal components.

## Brakes

### Brake and Traction Control Sensors

The braking system provides strong stopping power, but electronic sensor sensitivity can occasionally trigger unwarranted dashboard warnings.

**Spurious Traction Control and Brake Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** The traction control and brake warning lights may illuminate intermittently during normal driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheel speed sensors, wiring harnesses, and brake fluid levels for faults or corrosion.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Glass

The exterior styling is sleek and aerodynamic, though owners have reported rare instances of structural glass vulnerability on the panoramic or standard sunroof.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass may fracture or shatter unexpectedly while driving without impact from external objects.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged sunroof glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame for alignment or stress points.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Occupant safety is supported by a comprehensive airbag system, though sensor malfunctions can occasionally trigger warning lights requiring component replacement.

**Passenger Airbag Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Airbag warning lights may illuminate due to failures in the passenger-side occupancy sensor matrix.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace malfunctioning passenger seat sensor components as required by diagnostic trouble codes.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Infiniti QX70 remains an engaging choice for used luxury crossover buyers who prioritize sporty driving dynamics and distinctive styling. While owners have logged isolated complaints concerning sunroof glass durability, electrical issues, and occasional transmission shifting roughness, a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help ensure you select a well-maintained model.
