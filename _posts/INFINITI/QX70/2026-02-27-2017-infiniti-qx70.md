# 2017 INFINITI QX70

### Overview
The 2017 INFINITI QX70 is a luxury mid-size SUV known for its sporty handling and distinctive styling. It offers a potent V6 engine and a capable all-wheel-drive system, making it a compelling choice for those seeking performance in an SUV package. Reliability for the 2017 model year is generally considered good, with owners reporting satisfaction with its powertrain and overall build quality. However, like any vehicle, there are specific areas where potential issues have been noted, primarily related to the infotainment system and some minor electrical components. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring the longevity and optimal performance of the QX70.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen may freeze, become unresponsive to touch, or reboot unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Bluetooth Connectivity Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty pairing devices or intermittent loss of Bluetooth connection.
- **Solution:** Software updates, device reset, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500


### Sensors
**ABS/Traction Control Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of ABS or traction control warning lights, potentially due to faulty wheel speed sensors.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty wheel speed sensor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 INFINITI QX70 demonstrates solid mechanical reliability, particularly with its robust 3.7L V6 engine and 7-speed automatic transmission, which rarely present widespread issues. Suspension and exterior components are also generally dependable. The primary areas of concern are within the electrical system, specifically the infotainment unit's tendency to freeze or become unresponsive, and occasional Bluetooth connectivity problems. Minor electrical faults like ABS/traction control warning lights due to sensor issues have also been reported. Overall, the QX70 is a reliable luxury SUV, but potential buyers should be aware of and budget for possible infotainment system repairs.
