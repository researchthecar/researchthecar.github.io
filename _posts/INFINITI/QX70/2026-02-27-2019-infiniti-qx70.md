# 2019 INFINITI QX70

### Overview
The 2019 INFINITI QX70, a luxury mid-size SUV, generally presents a solid reliability record, reflecting INFINITI's commitment to quality engineering. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the QX70 for this model year demonstrates good performance in key areas. Owners can expect a durable powertrain and a well-built interior. However, as with any vehicle, specific components may experience isolated problems, particularly those related to electronics and suspension over time. Regular maintenance is crucial for ensuring the longevity and optimal performance of the QX70. This analysis focuses on documented common issues beyond standard wear and tear.

## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or popping noises when turning the steering wheel, especially at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the strut mount bearings.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen may freeze, become unresponsive to touch, or reboot unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery drains faster than expected, leading to starting issues. Can be related to parasitic draw from electronic modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw and repair of faulty module or wiring; battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Climate Control
**Blend Door Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to control temperature or direct airflow properly; clicking noises from the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blend door actuator.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 INFINITI QX70 is a generally reliable luxury SUV. Its V6 engine and 7-speed automatic transmission are robust and have few documented widespread issues. The primary areas where owners might encounter problems are within the electrical system, specifically the infotainment unit and potential battery drain issues, and some suspension components like strut mount bearings. Interior climate control can also be affected by blend door actuator failures. These issues are not pervasive but are worth noting for potential buyers. Overall, with diligent maintenance and attention to these specific potential weak points, the QX70 should provide a dependable ownership experience.
