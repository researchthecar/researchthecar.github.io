# 2018 INFINITI QX60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Infiniti QX60 offers a comfortable, spacious cabin and smooth three-row luxury hauling, but prospective used-car buyers should carefully inspect its powertrain and electronics history. While many owners enjoy its everyday refinement, owner complaints highlight potential trouble spots ranging from transmission vibrations to intermittent electrical and structural concerns.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

Powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 295 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for the midsize luxury crossover. While generally capable, isolated owner reports point to major internal mechanical failures and smoke issues requiring close inspection.

**Internal Engine Failure and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported sudden losses of motive power accompanied by engine knocking sounds, starting failures, or thick white smoke from the exhaust.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnostic evaluation by a certified technician; severe cases may necessitate major engine repair or complete engine replacement.

### Center Console and Wipers

Manages comfort accessories, lighting, and driver conveniences, though a few owners experienced frustrating electronic glitches.

**Console Blackout and Wiper Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The center console and radio may completely power off while climate controls run uncontrollably, and windshield wipers can intermittently stall during heavy rain.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuses, relays, and the body control module; malfunctioning infotainment displays or wiper motors may require component replacement.

## Transmission

### CVT Automatic Transmission

Uses a Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) paired with front-wheel drive or intelligent all-wheel drive. Buyers should thoroughly test-drive the vehicle to check for hesitation, shuddering, or slipping.

**Transmission Shudder and Slippage**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience noticeable shudders, jerking during acceleration, or a sensation that the transmission is slipping.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid condition and differential couplings; persistent mechanical shudder may require component rebuilding or transmission replacement.

## Steering

### Power Steering

Steering provides light and easy maneuverability for a large crossover, but intermittent assist failures pose a notable safety concern.

**Steering Lockup**
- **Symptoms:** The power steering system may temporarily stiffen or lock up when making turns from a complete stop.
- **Solution:** Inspect power steering components, fluid levels, and electronic assist modules for faults.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior panels and glass features provide an upscale look, though sunroof durability warrants a careful physical inspection.

**Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers hear a loud, gunshot-like noise while driving followed by the sudden shattering of the sunroof glass panel.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass assembly and inspect the frame for proper alignment and stress concentration.

## Safety

### Airbags and Seat Belts

Standard safety equipment is comprehensive, but sensor malfunctions can trigger persistent warning indicators on the dashboard.

**Airbag Light and Sensor Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Airbag and seatbelt warning lights remain illuminated or flash continuously due to passenger seat assembly sensor failures.
- **Solution:** Diagnose occupant detection sensors; repairs often require replacing faulty seat sensor wiring harnesses or assembly components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V807000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX60&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A brake fluid leak on the circuit board may result in an electrical short, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will replace the ABS actuator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Infiniti QX60 remains an appealing option for families seeking a comfortable and feature-rich luxury crossover, but it requires a diligent pre-purchase inspection. Buyers should pay close attention to the operation of the CVT transmission, test all electrical accessories, and ensure any open brake safety recalls have been completed by a dealer.
