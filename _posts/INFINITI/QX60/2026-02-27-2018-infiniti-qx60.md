# 2018 INFINITI QX60

### Overview
The 2018 INFINITI QX60 is a luxury three-row SUV that generally offers a comfortable ride and a spacious interior. From a reliability standpoint, it performs reasonably well, with many owners reporting satisfaction. However, like most vehicles, it has a few areas that have seen more attention than others. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for owners and prospective buyers. The QX60 is known for its smooth powertrain and family-friendly features, making it a popular choice in its segment. Understanding its reliability profile can help in making informed decisions regarding maintenance and potential repairs.

## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional hesitation during acceleration or a slight jerk when shifting between drive and reverse.
- **Solution:** Software updates, transmission fluid flush, or in severe cases, transmission replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800 for fluid service, $3000 - $6000 for replacement

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen may occasionally freeze, become unresponsive to touch, or reboot unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software updates or a system reset. In some cases, the unit may need to be replaced.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300 for software updates/diagnostics, $1000 - $2000 for replacement


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have reported batteries failing sooner than expected, leading to starting issues.
- **Solution:** Battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 INFINITI QX60 presents a generally reliable profile for a luxury SUV. The powertrain, particularly the 3.5L V6 engine, is robust with no significant widespread issues. The primary areas of concern are the CVT transmission, which can occasionally exhibit hesitation or jerking, and the electrical system, with some reports of infotainment screen glitches and premature battery drain. These issues, while not universal, are the most commonly cited technical problems. Most repairs for these components are manageable, though transmission replacement can be a significant expense. Overall, the QX60 offers a good balance of luxury and dependability, with most owners experiencing satisfaction.
