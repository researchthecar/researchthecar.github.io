# 2020 INFINITI QX60

### Overview
The 2020 INFINITI QX60 is a luxury three-row SUV that generally offers a comfortable and refined driving experience. As with most vehicles, it has seen some reports of specific issues, though widespread problems are not a dominant theme. Reliability is generally considered good, with owners appreciating its smooth powertrain and upscale interior. However, like any complex machine, certain components can experience failures over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers.

## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional hesitation during acceleration or a slight jerk when shifting between drive and reverse.
- **Solution:** Software update or transmission fluid flush. In severe cases, transmission replacement may be required.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500 for fluid service; $3,000 - $6,000 for replacement.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Glitching**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen may become unresponsive, freeze, or display graphical glitches.
- **Solution:** Software reset or update. Dealer diagnosis may be needed for persistent issues.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300 for software update/reset.


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reports of the battery draining unexpectedly, leading to starting issues.
- **Solution:** Check for parasitic draws, test battery health, and replace if necessary.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400 for battery replacement.

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 INFINITI QX60 demonstrates a generally solid reliability record, with its 3.5L V6 engine and standard suspension systems proving to be robust. The most frequently cited technical concerns revolve around the CVT transmission, which can occasionally exhibit hesitation or jerking, often resolvable with software updates or fluid services, though more serious issues may necessitate replacement. Electrical components, particularly the infotainment system's responsiveness and occasional battery drain, are also areas to monitor. Overall, the QX60 offers a dependable luxury SUV experience, provided potential buyers are aware of and prepared for these specific, albeit not universal, issues.
