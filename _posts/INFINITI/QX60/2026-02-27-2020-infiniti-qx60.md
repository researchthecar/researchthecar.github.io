# 2020 INFINITI QX60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Infiniti QX60, buyers will find a comfortable, three-row luxury crossover equipped with a standard V6 engine and a continuous variable transmission. While it offers a smooth ride and a well-appointed cabin, owner feedback highlights a few notable areas of concern. Prospective buyers should pay close attention to powertrain performance, electronics, and body hardware during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 2020 Infiniti QX60 comes standard with a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 295 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque. This engine provides smooth acceleration for everyday driving, though owners occasionally report hesitation or unexpected power loss.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine stalling, sudden loss of power while driving at highway speeds, or hesitation and rough idling.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a qualified mechanic is required to check for fault codes related to fuel delivery, sensors, or throttle control.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Power is routed through an Xtronic continuously variable transmission available in front-wheel drive or intelligent all-wheel drive configurations. The CVT is designed for fuel efficiency, but used buyers should thoroughly test the transmission for smooth operation.

**Transmission Failure and Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced sudden transmission failure resulting in a loss of forward drive gears, intermittent jerking, or hesitation during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection of the transmission system; complete CVT replacement may be necessary in severe cases of mechanical failure.

## Electrical

### Body Electrical and Power Windows

The vehicle relies heavily on onboard electronics for comfort and convenience features, making a complete electrical accessory check vital during evaluation.

**Window Malfunction and Electrical Odors**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows may fail to roll up properly accompanied by clicking noises, or an electrical burning smell may emanate from the heating and ventilation system.
- **Solution:** Inspect power window regulators, switches, and HVAC wiring harnesses to isolate and repair faulty components.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Glass

Exterior panels and trim are generally robust, but the panoramic glass roof has been cited by owners for spontaneous breakage.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report hearing a sudden loud blast or explosion-like sound while driving at highway speeds, followed by the sunroof glass shattering without any external impact.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the shattered glass panel and thorough cleaning of the interior cabin to remove glass shards.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features aim to protect occupants, though sensor calibration issues can occasionally lead to false collision alerts.

**Unintended Automatic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle's collision avoidance system may occasionally misinterpret the driving environment and automatically engage the brakes without an actual obstacle present.
- **Solution:** Have dealership technicians inspect and recalibrate the radar sensors and forward-facing camera systems.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V402000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Spindle](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX60&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The steering knuckle or rear axle could deform upon impact, such as from hitting a curb, resulting in a loss of steering control, or possible wheel separation. Both of these conditions can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the front steering knuckle and rear axle housing, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Infiniti QX60 offers luxury family transport with a strong V6 engine, but used buyers should exercise diligence. Particular attention should be paid to CVT transmission operation, engine power delivery, and checking all electrical accessories and the sunroof glass during a pre-purchase inspection.
