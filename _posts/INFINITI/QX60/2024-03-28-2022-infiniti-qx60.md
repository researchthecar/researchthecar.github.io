**2022 INFINITI QX60**

The 2022 INFINITI QX60 is a luxury SUV that comes with several engine and transmission options. The base engine is a 3.5-liter V6 that produces 295 horsepower and 275 lb-ft of torque. A more powerful 3.5-liter V6 is also available, producing 355 horsepower and 340 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission.

The QX60 has received praise for its comfortable interior, powerful engine, and advanced safety features. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems.

**Engine**

**3.5-liter V6 (295 horsepower)**

* **VVT solenoid failure:** The variable valve timing (VVT) solenoid can fail, causing the engine to run poorly and lose power. The solution is to replace the VVT solenoid. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $200-$400.
* **Intake manifold gasket leak:** The intake manifold gasket can develop a leak, allowing air to enter the engine. This can cause the engine to run rough and lose power. The solution is to replace the intake manifold gasket. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $300-$500.

**3.5-liter V6 (355 horsepower)**

* **None Found**

**Transmission**

**9-speed automatic transmission**

* **Shifting problems:** Some owners have reported problems with the transmission shifting gears. The solution is to have the transmission serviced or replaced. The estimated cost to fix this issue can range from $1,000-$3,000 or more.

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension noise:** Some owners have reported a noise coming from the front suspension. The solution is to have the suspension inspected and repaired. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $200-$400.
* **Rear suspension squeak:** Some owners have reported a squeak coming from the rear suspension. The solution is to have the suspension inspected and lubricated. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $100-$200.

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Some owners have reported that the battery is draining prematurely. The solution is to have the battery and charging system inspected and repaired. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $200-$400.
* **Electrical glitches:** Some owners have reported electrical glitches, such as flickering lights or power loss. The solution is to have the electrical system inspected and repaired. The estimated cost to fix this issue can vary depending on the severity of the problem.

**Exterior**

* **None Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system issues:** Some owners have reported problems with the infotainment system, such as freezing or crashing. The solution is to update the software or replace the infotainment system. The estimated cost to fix this issue can vary depending on the severity of the problem.
* **Climate control problems:** Some owners have reported problems with the climate control system, such as not blowing air or not heating or cooling properly. The solution is to have the climate control system inspected and repaired. The estimated cost to fix this issue can vary depending on the severity of the problem.

**Summary**

Overall, the 2022 INFINITI QX60 is a reliable SUV, but it has had some reported issues. The most common issues are with the engine, transmission, and suspension. The severity of the issues can vary, and the cost to fix them can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars.
