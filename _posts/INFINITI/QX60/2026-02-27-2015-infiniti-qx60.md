# 2015 INFINITI QX60

### Overview
The 2015 INFINITI QX60 is a luxury three-row SUV that offers a comfortable ride and a spacious interior, making it a popular choice for families. It is generally considered a reliable vehicle, particularly in its powertrain. However, like most vehicles, it has had some reported issues that owners should be aware of. This analysis focuses on common mechanical and electrical concerns that have been documented, excluding routine maintenance and normal wear and tear. The QX60's primary powertrain is a V6 engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT), which, while generally smooth, has seen some specific complaints.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (VQ35DD)
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking gaskets or seals (e.g., valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket).
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, sweet smell from engine bay, visible coolant puddles, low coolant level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of hoses, radiator, water pump, or head gasket depending on the source of the leak.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1500+


## Transmission

### CVT (JR710E/JR711E)
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle hesitates during acceleration, sudden jerks or lurches, rough shifting feel.
- **Solution:** Software update for the transmission control module, or in severe cases, transmission fluid flush and replacement, or transmission rebuild/replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3500+

**Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission warning light illuminated, reduced engine power, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Check and top off transmission fluid, inspect transmission cooler for blockages or leaks, or replace transmission cooler.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsive**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, freezes, or restarts randomly.
- **Solution:** Software update for the infotainment system, or potential replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1200


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start, battery warning light, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnose parasitic draw, replace faulty battery, or repair faulty module causing drain.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

## Interior

### Climate Control System
**AC/Heater Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Air conditioning blows warm air, heater does not produce heat, inconsistent temperature.
- **Solution:** Check refrigerant levels, inspect blower motor, or replace blend door actuators.
- **Cost:** $300 - $900


### Seat Actuators
**Power Seat Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats fail to move forward, backward, or adjust height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty seat motor or control module.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 INFINITI QX60 offers a generally reliable ownership experience, with its V6 engine being a strong point. The most frequently cited concerns revolve around the CVT transmission, which can exhibit hesitation or overheating issues, and some electrical gremlins like infotainment screen unresponsiveness and battery drain. Interior components like the climate control and power seats have also seen occasional failures. While these issues can be costly to repair, they are not universally present across all vehicles. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs can help mitigate potential problems and ensure a smoother ownership experience for this luxury SUV.
