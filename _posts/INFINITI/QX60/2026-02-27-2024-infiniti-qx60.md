# 2024 INFINITI QX60

### Overview
The 2024 INFINITI QX60 presents itself as a strong contender in the luxury three-row SUV segment, aiming to blend upscale comfort with practical utility. INFINITI has generally focused on delivering a refined driving experience and a well-appointed interior. For the 2024 model year, the QX60 continues with its established powertrain and chassis, building upon the foundation of previous iterations. Reliability is a key consideration for buyers in this class, and early data suggests a generally positive outlook for the QX60, with most reported issues being minor or isolated. ### Summary Verdict
The 2024 INFINITI QX60 demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, particularly in its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission. The 3.5L V6 engine and 9-speed automatic transmission have proven to be robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, in some instances, premature 12V battery failure. These are not typically indicative of systemic powertrain or chassis weaknesses. Overall, the QX60 is positioned as a reliable choice for luxury SUV buyers, with most potential issues being manageable and not significantly impacting the vehicle's core functionality or long-term durability.
