# 2024 INFINITI QX60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2024 INFINITI QX60, buyers will find a stylish and technologically advanced three-row luxury crossover. While modern owner feedback highlights a comfortable cabin and strong road presence, some early adopters have reported isolated electrical quirks and powertrain-related hiccups. Reviewing available maintenance histories and conducting a thorough test drive can help ensure any potential mechanical or electrical issues have been addressed.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 2024 INFINITI QX60 comes equipped with a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 295 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque. This naturally aspirated powerplant delivers smooth acceleration for daily driving and family hauling duties. Overall, it provides dependable performance, though prospective buyers should verify that all powertrain control software is up to date.

**Unexpected Power Loss and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the vehicle experienced a sudden loss of power, engine stumbles, or unexpected engine shut down while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the fuel system, electrical connections, and powertrain control modules for fault codes or loose harnesses.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Power is routed through a 9-speed automatic transmission paired with either front-wheel drive or intelligent all-wheel drive. This multi-speed unit replaces older continuously variable transmissions, offering a more traditional and engaging shift feel. Routine maintenance and software checks are recommended to ensure longevity.

**Intermittent Driveline Jolts**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience unexpected vehicle jolting or abrupt shifts, particularly noticeable when decelerating to a stop.
- **Solution:** Consult an authorized dealer to check for transmission control module (TCM) software updates or calibration resets.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Instrument Cluster

The electrical architecture supports numerous advanced driver assists and dual displays, though complex systems can occasionally exhibit software-related bugs.

**Screen Reboots and Display Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen and instrument panel may intermittently turn off and restart independently while driving.
- **Solution:** Software updates performed by a dealership can often stabilize the digital displays and resolve intermittent communication errors.

## Exterior

### Windshield Wipers and Glass

Exterior panels and glass components generally offer good fit and finish, though owners should carefully inspect large glass surfaces like the panoramic roof before finalizing a purchase.

**Wiper Water Deflection and Panoramic Glass Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Windshield wipers can push accumulated water across the driver's side window during heavy rain, obscuring mirror visibility. Additionally, rare reports note spontaneous panoramic sunroof glass fracturing.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for any stress micro-fractures prior to purchase, and consider aftermarket wiper blade adjustments if water deflection becomes bothersome.

## Safety

### Seat Belts and Occupant Restraints

Safety systems are comprehensive, featuring multiple airbags and advanced collision avoidance technologies designed to protect all three seating rows.

**Inflatable Lap/Shoulder Restraint Locking**
- **Symptoms:** Rear seat occupants, particularly children, may experience the seat belt automatic locking retractor engaging tightly around the waist if the shoulder belt is misplaced.
- **Solution:** Educate passengers on proper seat belt positioning and ensure retractors release smoothly by feeding slack back into the mechanism.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V814000: Exterior Lighting:Lighting Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX60&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Incorrectly adjusted headlights can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reconfigure the Intelligent Power Distribution Module (IPDM) settings, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V154000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX60&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Due to a manufacturing issue, the driver's air bag inflator may absorb moisture, and cause the air bag to deploy improperly. An air bag that deploys improperly may not protect the driver as intended during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front driver's air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V176000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX60&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 208 "Occupant Crash Protection," 209 "Seat Belt Assemblies," and 210 "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages." An unsecured lap belt pretensioner will not properly restrain an occupant during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the left-side front lap seat belt pretensioner assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 INFINITI QX60 offers a refined luxury experience with strong V6 performance and a capable 9-speed automatic transmission. While most owner-reported issues center around minor software glitches, intermittent electrical behavior, and isolated powertrain concerns, the vehicle remains a competitive choice in the midsize luxury SUV segment. Buyers are advised to review service records and test all infotainment and driver-assist features thoroughly before purchase.
