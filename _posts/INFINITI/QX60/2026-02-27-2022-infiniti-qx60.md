# 2022 INFINITI QX60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Infiniti QX60 offers a refined three-row cabin, handsome styling, and a transition away from older CVT transmissions to a more conventional nine-speed automatic. While many owners enjoy its comfort and road manners, used-car buyers should review potential trouble spots. Early-model owner complaints highlight recurring electrical glitches, unexpected sunroof glass shattering, and rapid brake or tire wear.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

Powered by a 3.5-liter V6 producing approximately 295 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth everyday performance. Overall reliability for this engine is generally decent, though isolated oil leak concerns have been reported.

**Valve Seal and Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks and premature component wear reported at relatively low mileage.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the engine bay for leaks and replace affected seals if necessary.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Pairs the 3.5L V6 with a conventional 9-speed automatic transmission, replacing the controversial CVTs of previous generations. It generally delivers refined power delivery across front-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake system complaints center around fast rear pad wear and low-speed pedal feedback.

**Premature Rear Brake Wear and Jerkiness**
- **Symptoms:** Rapid wear on rear brake pads requiring annual replacement, accompanied by a jerking sensation just before coming to a complete stop.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and calipers regularly; replace worn components and check for caliper binding.

## Electrical

### Battery and Charging System

Electrical system concerns include occasional parasitic battery drains and infotainment glitches.

**Battery Drain and Starting Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start after being parked overnight, requiring a jump-start which triggers multiple dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Test the battery and charging system; replacement of the battery or software updates may be required.

### Body Electrical and Accessories

**Random Alarm and Liftgate Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle security alarm triggers randomly, or the power liftgate opens and closes unexpectedly when walking near the rear.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership or technician to diagnose sensor faults or reprogram the body control module.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior body components are generally solid, though panoramic sunroof shattering incidents have been reported.

**Sudden Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof suddenly explodes or shatters while driving at highway speeds without external impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered glass panel and inspect the frame for alignment issues or stress points.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V111000: Seats:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX60&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages." A missing mounting bolt may allow the seat track to move during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the second-row seat track and install a new mounting bolt as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V814000: Exterior Lighting:Lighting Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX60&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Incorrectly adjusted headlights can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reconfigure the Intelligent Power Distribution Module (IPDM) settings, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Infiniti QX60 is a comfortable and well-equipped luxury crossover, but used-car buyers should perform a thorough inspection prior to purchase. Pay close attention to the health of the electrical system, test all convenience features like the power liftgate and alarm, and verify the condition of the brakes and sunroof. A pre-purchase inspection by an independent mechanic can help catch these potential issues early.
