# 2022 INFINITI QX60

### Overview
The 2022 INFINITI QX60 is a luxury three-row SUV that aims to blend comfort, technology, and practicality. As a relatively new generation for the QX60, initial reliability data is still emerging, but early indicators suggest a solid performance for its class. The vehicle is equipped with a refined powertrain designed for smooth operation and adequate power for family duties. Infotainment and driver-assistance features are a strong suit, contributing to the overall user experience. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the QX60 appears to be holding up well in its first model year on the market, with owners generally reporting a positive ownership experience regarding its mechanical and electrical systems.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 INFINITI QX60 demonstrates good initial reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components (3.5L V6 engine and 9-speed automatic transmission) and chassis systems. The most commonly noted, albeit minor, issues tend to be related to the infotainment system, such as occasional software glitches or connectivity hiccups, which are often resolvable with software updates. Other areas like the exterior and interior have shown no significant widespread problems. Overall, the QX60 appears to be a dependable choice within the luxury SUV segment for its debut model year, with potential owners likely to experience a smooth and trouble-free ownership experience, barring minor electronic quirks.
