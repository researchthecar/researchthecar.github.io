**Introduction to the 2014 INFINITI QX60**

The 2014 INFINITI QX60 is a mid-size, three-row luxury SUV known for its opulent interior, smooth ride, and impressive safety features. It is available with either a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT), producing 265 horsepower and 248 lb-ft of torque.

Despite its many positive attributes, the 2014 INFINITI QX60 has encountered some specific issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Intake manifold failure:** The intake manifold can crack or warp over time, allowing unmetered air into the engine.
Symptoms: Rough idle, reduced power, check engine light
Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **Slipping or hesitation during shifting:** The CVT may experience issues with slipping or hesitating during gear changes.
Symptoms: Delay or jerks in shifting, reduced performance
Solution: CVT fluid flush and/or replacement of the CVT transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Some 2014 QX60s experience excessive battery drain, leading to premature battery depletion.
Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, loss of electrical power
Solution: Diagnosis and replacement of faulty components, such as the battery or alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Panoramic sunroof rattling:** The large panoramic sunroof may vibrate or rattle at high speeds.
Symptoms: Excessive noise from the sunroof
Solution: Adjustment or replacement of the sunroof seals or tracks
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation system freezing:** The infotainment system may experience freezing or crashing issues.
Symptoms: Inability to use navigation or other features
Solution: Software update or replacement of the infotainment module
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2014 INFINITI QX60 offers a luxurious and comfortable driving experience, some reported issues do exist. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they should be considered before making a purchase decision. Engine and transmission problems may be the most concerning, as they can be costly to repair. Electrical and exterior issues are less severe but can still be frustrating for owners.
