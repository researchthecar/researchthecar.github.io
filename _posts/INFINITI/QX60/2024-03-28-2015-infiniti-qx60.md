**2015 INFINITI QX60**

The 2015 INFINITI QX60 is a three-row luxury SUV that offers a refined and comfortable driving experience. It is powered by a standard 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 265 horsepower and 248 lb-ft of torque, paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). Other engine and transmission options are not available for this model year.

**Engine**

**3.5-liter V6**

- **Excessive Oil Consumption:** Some owners have reported excessive oil consumption, resulting in low oil levels and potential engine damage. The issue can be caused by worn piston rings or valve seals.
- **Description/Symptoms:** Burning oil smell, low oil level warning light, reduced engine performance
- **Solution:** Replace piston rings or valve seals
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,500+
- **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

- **Hesitation or Shuddering:** Some owners have experienced hesitation or shuddering during acceleration or gear changes, indicating potential transmission issues.
- **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty accelerating, vibration or shaking during gear shifts
- **Solution:** Replace CVT fluid and filter, or replace CVT assembly if necessary
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $4,000+
- **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

- **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

- **Battery Drain:** Some owners have reported excessive battery drain, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
- **Description/Symptoms:** Dead battery, slow cranking
- **Solution:** Replace battery or investigate electrical system for parasitic drain
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500
- **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

- **Chrome Trim Peeling:** The chrome trim on the grille and other exterior parts has been known to peel or discolor over time.
- **Description/Symptoms:** Dull or missing chrome finish
- **Solution:** Replace chrome trim
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
- **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

- **Sunroof Rattling:** Some owners have reported a rattling noise coming from the panoramic sunroof.
- **Description/Symptoms:** Rattling noise while driving or over bumps
- **Solution:** Adjust sunroof tracks or replace sunroof panel
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500
- **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 INFINITI QX60 has a few reported issues that vary in severity and cost to fix. The most common issues include excessive oil consumption, transmission hesitation, and chrome trim peeling. While these issues can be inconvenient, they are not widespread and can be addressed by a qualified mechanic. Overall, the QX60 remains a reliable and comfortable SUV for daily use.
