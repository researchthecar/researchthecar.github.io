# 2023 INFINITI QX60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Infiniti QX60 offers a stylish, three-row luxury crossover experience featuring a revised powertrain and modern cabin technology. While many owners enjoy its comfort and features, early model-year owner complaints highlight a range of electronic, safety-system, and intermittent mechanical quirks that prospective buyers should inspect closely.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces 295 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth acceleration for daily driving. It is generally robust, though isolated owner reports note fuel pump failures and stalling issues.

**Fuel Pump Failure / Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may lose acceleration power while driving or stall unexpectedly, mimicking an empty fuel tank and leaving the car stranded in traffic.
- **Solution:** Have the fuel pump inspected and replaced by a qualified service center if failure occurs.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission replaces the older CVT units found in previous generations, delivering more responsive and conventional gear shifts. It pairs with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, though buyers should verify smooth operation during test drives.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking performance is generally secure, though routine inspections are recommended to monitor pad life and ensure parking mechanisms operate correctly.

**Premature Rear Brake Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rear brake pads may wear out faster than expected, sometimes requiring replacement at relatively low mileage milestones.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pad thickness and rotor condition during routine maintenance visits.

## Electrical

### Electrical System & Accessories

The QX60 features extensive cabin technology and electrical architecture, though owners have reported scattered glitches ranging from infotainment connectivity drops to door lock and charging pad sensitivities.

**Door Lock Actuator & Moisture Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Door handles may fail to disengage locks during rain, leaving occupants locked out, or door lock actuators may malfunction intermittently.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty door lock actuators or affected door handle sensor modules.

**Wireless Charging Pad and USB Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** The wireless charging pad fails to charge devices while causing connected phones to overheat, a symptom shared by certain USB ports.
- **Solution:** Consult dealership service bulletins or avoid using malfunctioning charging pads until updated modules are installed.

## Safety

### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

Safety equipment includes comprehensive driver-assist suites, but sensor calibration issues can occasionally trigger false warnings or unexpected braking interventions.

**Phantom Forward Collision Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may incorrectly sense a front-end collision while driving at highway speeds and forcefully apply the emergency brakes without an obstacle present.
- **Solution:** Have dealership technicians check radar and camera calibration or software updates for the collision avoidance system.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V108000: Seats:Mid/Rear Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A seat that becomes stuck or fails to lock in a secured position may not properly restrain the occupant during a crash, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will remove and replace the second-row seat, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V268000: Seats:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The driver's powered seat may not be fully secured to the seat frame, causing the seat frame welds to detach from the rear inboard seat bracket. An unsecured seat with broken welds may not properly restrain an occupant during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the driver's seat rail clearance, and if necessary, replace the driver's seat cushion frame, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V814000: Exterior Lighting:Lighting Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Incorrectly adjusted headlights can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reconfigure the Intelligent Power Distribution Module (IPDM) settings, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Infiniti QX60 represents a substantial upgrade over older CVT-equipped models, offering refined styling and a dependable V6 powertrain. While most owners experience trouble-free driving, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's electrical accessories, verify proper driver-assist sensor calibration, and ensure all recall and service updates have been completed.
