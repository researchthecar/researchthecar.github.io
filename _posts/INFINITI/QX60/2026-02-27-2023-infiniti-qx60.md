# 2023 INFINITI QX60

### Overview
The 2023 INFINITI QX60, a luxury three-row SUV, generally presents a strong reliability profile, building on INFINITI's reputation for refined interiors and comfortable driving dynamics. Owners and automotive reviews suggest that common concerns are minimal, with most feedback centering on the expected performance of its powertrain and the quality of its cabin appointments. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear, to provide a clear picture of the vehicle's long-term reliability.

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 INFINITI QX60 demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its powertrain (3.5L V6 engine and 9-speed automatic transmission) and core chassis components. The most frequently noted, albeit minor, issues tend to be related to the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches that are often resolved with software updates, and a few reports of premature 12V battery failure. These are not widespread critical failures but rather minor inconveniences. Overall, the QX60 is positioned as a dependable luxury SUV for families, with a low incidence of significant mechanical or structural problems reported for this model year.
