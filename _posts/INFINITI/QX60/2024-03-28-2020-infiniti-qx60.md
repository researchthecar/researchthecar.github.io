**Introduction:**

The 2020 INFINITI QX60 is a luxury mid-size SUV known for its spacious cabin, refined ride, and advanced safety features. It is available with a single engine and transmission option:

* 3.5L V6 engine with 295 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque
* CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)

**Engine**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported their vehicles consuming excessive amounts of oil, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage. Symptoms include low oil level indicators, blue smoke from the exhaust, and a burning oil smell.
    * Solution: Replace piston rings, valve seals, or the entire engine.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **CVT Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have experienced problems with the CVT transmission, such as slipping, shuddering, or hesitation during acceleration.
    * Solution: Replace the CVT transmission or specific components within it.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Air Suspension Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear air suspension system can malfunction, causing the vehicle to sag or experience uneven ride height.
    * Solution: Replace the rear air suspension bags or compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported their vehicles experiencing battery drain, leading to dead batteries and difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair any electrical system faults or replace the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Condensation can build up inside the headlights, affecting visibility during rain or humid conditions.
    * Solution: Replace the headlights with updated units.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The panoramic sunroof can rattle or squeak over uneven surfaces.
    * Solution: Adjust or lubricate the sunroof sealing mechanisms.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Dashboard Creaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard can creak or rattle, especially over uneven surfaces or in hot weather.
    * Solution: Install sound-dampening materials or adjust interior panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2020 INFINITI QX60 is a generally reliable vehicle with some notable issues. The most severe problems include excessive oil consumption, CVT transmission issues, and rear air suspension malfunctions. These issues can be costly to repair, but some solutions are available through recalls or extended warranties. Overall, the severity of the issues is moderate, but the cost of repairs can be significant.
