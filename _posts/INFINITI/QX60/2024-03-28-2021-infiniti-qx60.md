**Introduction:**

The 2021 INFINITI QX60 is a mid-size luxury SUV known for its spacious cabin, comfortable ride, and advanced technology features. It's powered by a standard 3.5L V6 engine with 295 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6**

  * **Fuel Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace fuel pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 21V624000
  * **Power Steering Pump Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking fluid, whining or growling noises from steering wheel
    * Solution: Replace power steering pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **9-Speed Automatic**

  * **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, slipping gears, delayed response
    * Solution: Software update, transmission rebuild
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Struts:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or rattling noises, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace front struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting vehicle
    * Solution: Replace battery or diagnose and repair electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Handles don't open or close properly
    * Solution: Replace or repair door handles
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Rear Entertainment System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: System doesn't work, glitches, or freezes
    * Solution: Software update or replace rear entertainment system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2021 INFINITI QX60 has a few reported issues in various categories. The most common engine problems are related to the fuel pump and power steering pump, while the transmission can experience shifting problems. Suspension issues involve front strut failure. Electrical problems include battery drain. Exterior issues may arise with door handle malfunctions. Interior concerns include rear entertainment system issues. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but most can be resolved for a few hundred to a few thousand dollars.
