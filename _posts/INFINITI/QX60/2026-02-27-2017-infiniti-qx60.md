# 2017 INFINITI QX60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Infiniti QX60 offers a spacious three-row cabin, comfortable ride quality, and a luxury-focused interior that appeals to families. However, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate its mechanical history, particularly regarding powertrain durability and electrical stability. Reviewing maintenance records and performing a thorough pre-purchase inspection are strongly advised.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (VQ35DD)

The 2017 QX60 features a 3.5-liter direct-injection V6 engine producing approximately 295 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant delivers smooth acceleration for daily driving, though owners have occasionally reported electrical and oil pressure warnings.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Paired with the V6 engine, the continuously variable transmission handles power delivery but is a known point of concern. Buyers should test-drive thoroughly and verify any history of transmission service or replacement.

**Transmission Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of motive power, vehicle stopping in traffic, or complete transmission failure without prior warning.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis and potentially a complete CVT replacement or rebuild by a qualified transmission specialist.

### Power Distribution and Alternator

The electrical architecture supports numerous comfort and safety features, though intermittent stalls and charging system complaints have been documented by owners.

**Unexpected Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle suddenly loses motive power while driving, accompanied by flickering warning lights or electrical system shutdowns.
- **Solution:** Inspect the alternator, battery connections, and powertrain control module (PCM) for faults.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes and ABS

The braking system provides adequate stopping power for this midsize luxury crossover, though sporadic mechanical and anti-lock brake sensor issues can arise.

**Brake Pedal Pulsation and Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal bounces or vibrates during application accompanied by loud noises, making it difficult to stop smoothly at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and ABS module components for wear or malfunction.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Glass

Exterior panels and trim are generally durable, but a recurring pattern of spontaneous sunroof glass breakage has been reported by a subset of owners.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic or standard sunroof glass suddenly explodes while driving on the highway without external impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the frame for alignment issues or stress points.

## Safety

### Airbag and Occupant Detection

Safety equipment includes a comprehensive suite of airbags and active driver aids, though sensor and wiring faults can trigger warning lights.

**Airbag Warning Light and Sensor Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Airbag indicator light flashes continuously, often indicating faults with side airbag modules or occupant classification sensors.
- **Solution:** Inspect wiring harnesses, connectors, and sensor modules; replace faulty components as diagnosed by a dealer.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V807000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX60&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A brake fluid leak on the circuit board may result in an electrical short, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will replace the ABS actuator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V601000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX60&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A brake fluid leak on the circuit board may result in an electrical short, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the serial number on the ABS actuator, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Infiniti QX60 offers strong appeal for buyers seeking a comfortable, feature-rich family crossover. However, prospective owners must approach with caution due to documented complaints concerning CVT transmission longevity, electrical power interruptions, and spontaneous sunroof breakage. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the transmission and electrical systems is essential before finalizing any purchase.
