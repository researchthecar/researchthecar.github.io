# 2017 INFINITI QX60

### Overview
The 2017 INFINITI QX60 is a luxury three-row SUV that generally offers a comfortable ride and a spacious interior. From a reliability standpoint, it performs reasonably well, with most owners reporting satisfaction. However, like any vehicle, it has a few areas that have seen more attention than others. The powertrain, particularly the continuously variable transmission (CVT), is a focal point for some reported concerns. Electrical systems and interior components are generally robust, but occasional glitches can occur. The exterior and suspension systems tend to be solid, contributing to the overall positive ownership experience for many.

## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)
**Hesitation or Shuddering**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may hesitate during acceleration or exhibit a shuddering sensation, particularly at lower speeds or when coming to a stop.
- **Solution:** Software updates, transmission fluid flush and replacement, or in severe cases, transmission replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1500 (for fluid service/software) up to $4000+ (for replacement)

**Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission warning light may illuminate, accompanied by a burning smell or reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid level and condition, inspect cooling system for leaks or blockages, and potentially replace transmission cooler.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen may freeze, become unresponsive to touch, or reboot unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software updates for the infotainment system, or in some cases, replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500 (for software/diagnostics) up to $1500+ (for replacement)


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery may die prematurely, leading to starting issues. Can sometimes be related to parasitic draw from electrical components.
- **Solution:** Test battery and charging system, identify and repair parasitic draw if present.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 INFINITI QX60 is a generally reliable luxury SUV, with its 3.5L V6 engine proving to be robust. The primary area of concern for this model year is the CVT transmission, which can experience issues like hesitation, shuddering, or overheating, potentially leading to significant repair costs. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and occasional battery drain, are also noted. However, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are typically solid. Regular maintenance, especially for the transmission fluid, is recommended to mitigate potential problems and ensure longevity.
