# 2019 INFINITI QX60

### Overview
The 2019 INFINITI QX60 is a luxury three-row SUV that generally offers a comfortable ride and a spacious interior. From a reliability standpoint, it performs reasonably well, with most owners reporting satisfaction. However, like any vehicle, it has a few areas that have seen more attention than others. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for owners and prospective buyers. The QX60's powertrain and core systems are largely dependable, but specific components have occasionally presented issues that warrant consideration.

## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may exhibit hesitation during acceleration or feel jerky when shifting between gears.
- **Solution:** Software updates, transmission fluid flush, or in severe cases, transmission replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500 for fluid service, $3,000 - $7,000 for replacement

**Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission warning light may illuminate, accompanied by a burning smell or reduced performance.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid level and condition, inspect cooling system, and potentially replace transmission cooler.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400 for fluid and inspection, $500 - $1,500 for cooler replacement

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsive**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen may freeze, become unresponsive to touch, or reboot unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system reset, or replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300 for software updates/reset, $1,000 - $2,500 for head unit replacement


### Battery
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, dimming lights, or electrical system malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Battery testing and replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 INFINITI QX60 demonstrates a generally solid reliability record, particularly in its engine and suspension components, which are largely trouble-free. The primary areas that have seen some owner-reported concerns are the CVT transmission, with occasional issues like hesitation or overheating, and the electrical system, specifically the infotainment screen and battery longevity. While these issues are not universal, they represent the most common technical deviations from expected performance. Addressing these potential problems proactively, such as through regular maintenance and software checks, can help ensure a more reliable ownership experience. Overall, the QX60 is a dependable choice for a luxury family SUV, with a manageable set of potential technical considerations.
