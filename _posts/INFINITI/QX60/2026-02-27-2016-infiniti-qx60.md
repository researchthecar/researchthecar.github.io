# 2016 INFINITI QX60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Infiniti QX60 offers a spacious three-row cabin, comfortable ride, and refined styling, making it an appealing option for families searching for a used luxury crossover. However, prospective buyers should approach with careful inspection, as owner reports highlight some recurring mechanical and safety-related concerns. Reviewing vehicle service records and checking for completed safety updates will help ensure a more dependable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

Equipped with a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 265 horsepower and 248 lb-ft of torque, this powerplant delivers smooth acceleration for daily driving. Overall mechanical performance is generally reliable, though owners have occasionally noted auxiliary issues like cooling fan noises.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Utilizes a Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) paired with front-wheel drive or intelligent all-wheel drive, designed to optimize fuel efficiency. Buyers should thoroughly test-drive this powertrain component to check for smooth operation and responsiveness.

**CVT Limp Mode**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may suddenly enter limp mode, severely limiting vehicle speed to around 10 MPH and restricting engine revs until the vehicle is restarted.
- **Solution:** Diagnose transmission health, check for software updates, or consult a specialist for potential transmission repair or replacement.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Display System

Electrical systems drive many of the vehicle's luxury features, though intermittent software glitches in the infotainment and display interfaces have been reported.

**Infotainment Display Resetting**
- **Symptoms:** The center display screen and audio/video control system continuously reset themselves, restricting access to climate controls, audio, and the backup camera.
- **Solution:** Perform a system software update or replace faulty head unit modules as diagnosed by a technician.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Assembly

Exterior styling and lighting are durable, but glass components like the optional sunroof require careful inspection for any pre-existing stress cracks.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass can unexpectedly fracture or shatter outward while driving at highway speeds, accompanied by a loud popping noise.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the surrounding frame for structural stress or alignment issues.

## Safety

### Occupant Classification System (OCS)

Safety equipment generally performs well in crash testing, though electronic sensors controlling supplemental restraint systems warrant careful pre-purchase verification.

**Passenger Airbag Sensor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger airbag indicator light flickers or stays on, failing to properly detect an adult occupant seated in the front passenger seat.
- **Solution:** Have a certified repair center inspect the OCS sensor mat, wiring harnesses, or control module for calibration or replacement.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V244000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=QX60&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As a result, the passenger frontal air bag may be turned off and not deploy in the event of a crash. If the passenger frontal air bag does not deploy as intended in the event of a crash, the passenger is at an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the Air Bag Control Unit (ACU) and OCS Electronic Control Unit (ECU) in Altima, Maxima, Murano, Rogue, and Sentra vehicles, and replace the OCS ECU in LEAF, NV200, NV200 Taxi, Pathfinder, Infiniti Q50, JX35, and QX60. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Infiniti QX60 remains a comfortable and feature-rich luxury crossover choice for families on a budget, provided it passes a thorough mechanical inspection. Buyers should pay close attention to the CVT transmission's responsiveness, verify that all safety sensor recalls and updates have been completed, and inspect the panoramic sunroof and electrical systems before finalizing a purchase.
