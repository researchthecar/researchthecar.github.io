# 2010 INFINITI M35

### Overview
The 2010 INFINITI M35, a luxury sedan known for its blend of performance and comfort, generally exhibits good reliability. Powered by a robust V6 engine, it offers a refined driving experience. While most owners report satisfaction, like any vehicle, certain components can experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers. The M35's design prioritizes a premium feel, and its reliability record reflects a solid engineering foundation, though specific attention to certain systems can help maintain its optimal performance.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (VQ35HR)
**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or other seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Coolant Leaks (Hoses/Radiator)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses or radiator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (RE5R05A)
**Harsh Shifting/Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, hesitation when shifting into gear, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, potential valve body or solenoid repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000+


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software update, potential replacement of head unit or related modules.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500+

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, dead battery after sitting.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty modules or battery.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

## Interior

### Cabin Features
**Sunroof Drain Clogs**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, musty smell, headliner staining.
- **Solution:** Clearing of sunroof drain tubes.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250

### Summary Verdict
The 2010 INFINITI M35 is a generally reliable luxury sedan. The powertrain, particularly the 3.5L V6 engine, is robust, though common age-related issues like oil and coolant leaks can occur. The automatic transmission is mostly dependable, but some instances of harsh shifting have been noted, potentially requiring fluid service or more involved repairs. Suspension components, such as control arm bushings, may wear over time, leading to noise and handling degradation. Electrical gremlins, especially with the infotainment system, are not uncommon in vehicles of this age. Interior issues are minor, with sunroof drain clogs being the most frequently cited problem. Overall, the M35 offers good value with reasonable reliability, provided routine maintenance is performed and potential issues are addressed promptly.
