**Introduction:**

The 2008 INFINITI M35 is a mid-size luxury sedan renowned for its sleek styling, comfortable interior, and refined performance. It offers a range of engine and transmission options, including a 3.5L V6 and a 5-speed automatic transmission. The M35 has received praise for its handling, acceleration, and luxurious amenities. However, like all vehicles, it has reported issues in various components.

**Engine:**

* **3.5L V6**

    * **Intake Camshaft Adjustment Solenoid Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, poor performance, and illuminated check engine light (P0011, P0014)
        * Solution: Replace intake camshaft adjustment solenoid
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $900+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

* **5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

    * **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks from the transmission pan or seals, leading to low transmission fluid levels and potential transmission damage
        * Solution: Replace transmission pan gasket or seals
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive vibration, noise, and reduced handling performance when driving over uneven surfaces
    * Solution: Replace front lower control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains quickly, even when the car is turned off
    * Solution: Diagnose and fix the cause of the battery drain, such as a faulty alternator, parasitic draw, or malfunctioning electronic device
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Sunroof Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof fails to open or close, or makes excessive noise during operation
    * Solution: Replace sunroof motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Navigation System Glitch:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system freezes, displays incorrect information, or fails to respond to commands
    * Solution: Update navigation software or replace navigation unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2008 INFINITI M35 has reported issues in several components, ranging from minor inconveniences to more severe problems. The most common issues include intake camshaft adjustment solenoid failure, transmission fluid leak, front lower control arm bushing failure, battery drain, sunroof motor failure, and navigation system glitches. While some issues may be costly to fix, many are relatively inexpensive and can be addressed by a qualified mechanic.
