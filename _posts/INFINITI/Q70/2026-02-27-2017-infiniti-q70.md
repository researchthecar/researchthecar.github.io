# 2017 INFINITI Q70

### Overview
The 2017 INFINITI Q70 is a luxury sedan known for its comfortable ride and potent performance, particularly with its V6 and V8 engine options. Reliability for this model year is generally strong, reflecting INFINITI's commitment to quality. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unscheduled repairs, like any vehicle, certain components can occasionally present issues. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related problems that may arise, providing insights into potential repair areas and their associated costs. The Q70 aims to blend performance with dependability, and for the most part, it succeeds.

**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Premature battery discharge, requiring jump starts or battery replacement sooner than expected.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw from electrical components, repair or replacement of faulty module.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 INFINITI Q70 demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, with its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission performing robustly. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are within the electrical system, specifically related to the infotainment unit and potential battery drain. These are not universal problems but have been noted by a segment of owners. Overall, the Q70 is a dependable luxury sedan, and proactive maintenance, along with awareness of potential electrical quirks, will contribute to a satisfying ownership experience.
