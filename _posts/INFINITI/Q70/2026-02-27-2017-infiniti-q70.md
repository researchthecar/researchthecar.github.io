# 2017 INFINITI Q70 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Infiniti Q70 is a luxury sedan offering a blend of strong performance and traditional executive styling. While overall owner complaint volumes are relatively low, prospective buyers should review available feedback on niche mechanical and exterior areas before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L V6

The 3.7-liter V6 engine produces approximately 330 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque, offering responsive performance for daily driving. It is generally regarded as a durable powertrain option when maintained properly.

### 5.6L V8

The 5.6-liter V8 engine generates roughly 420 horsepower and 417 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration and passing power. Buyers looking for maximum performance should expect higher fuel consumption.

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid

The 3.5-liter V6 hybrid powertrain pairs an electric motor with a V6 engine to produce a net 360 horsepower. It provides an efficient option within the lineup, though prospective buyers should consider potential long-term hybrid battery maintenance.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 7-speed automatic transmission is paired with both rear-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations across the Q70 lineup. It generally shifts smoothly and offers reliable operation in standard driving conditions.

## Steering

### Steering System

Steering feedback is generally well-regarded, though isolated owner reports indicate tracking issues.

**Unsteady Steering Response**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners report that the vehicle feels dangerously unsteady, reacting to road surfaces and pulling to the right or left without warning.
- **Solution:** Have the steering components, alignment, and suspension inspected by a qualified professional to check for wear or out-of-spec adjustments.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Glass

Exterior trim and body panels are generally durable, but isolated incidents involving glass components have been noted by owners.

**Unexpected Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass may fracture or shatter suddenly without apparent external impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged sunroof assembly and inspect the surrounding frame for stress or alignment issues.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V470000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q70&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Both scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driveshaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Infiniti Q70 provides a comfortable and powerful luxury driving experience with established engine and transmission choices. While limited owner complaints suggest overall solid build quality, buyers should carefully inspect the steering feel and exterior glass during a pre-purchase evaluation.
