**Introduction**

The 2016 INFINITI Q70 is a mid-size luxury sedan that offers a blend of performance and sophistication. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 3.7-liter V6, a 5.6-liter V8, and a hybrid option. The Q70 is praised for its comfortable interior, sleek exterior design, and responsive handling. However, there have been some reported issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **3.7-liter V6**

    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** The intake manifold gasket may fail, causing a vacuum leak and rough engine idle.
        * **Solution:** Replace the intake manifold gasket.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $250 - $400
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner may fail, leading to stretched timing chains and potentially catastrophic engine damage.
        * **Solution:** Replace the timing chain tensioner and timing chains.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500
* **5.6-liter V8**

    * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:** The valve cover gaskets may leak oil, causing a loss of oil and a burning oil smell.
        * **Solution:** Replace the valve cover gaskets.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $700

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic Transmission**

    * **Jerking or Rough Shifting:** The transmission may experience jerky shifting or become rough when shifting gears.
        * **Solution:** Update the transmission fluid and torque converter.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800
* **Hybrid Transmission**

    * **Battery Failure:** The hybrid battery may fail, leading to a loss of power and reduced fuel efficiency.
        * **Solution:** Replace the hybrid battery pack.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The vehicle's battery may drain prematurely due to a parasitic draw.
    * **Solution:** Inspect the electrical system for a faulty component and repair or replace as necessary.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $300+
* **Infotainment System Malfunction:** The infotainment system may experience glitches, freezing, or become unresponsive.
    * **Solution:** Update the system software or replace the infotainment unit if necessary.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Switch Failure:** The sunroof switch may fail, preventing the sunroof from opening or closing properly.
    * **Solution:** Replace the sunroof switch.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $300
* **Carpet Staining:** The carpet in the footwells or trunk may stain or discolor easily.
    * **Solution:** Professional cleaning or replacement of the carpet.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $800+

**Summary**

The 2016 INFINITI Q70 offers a premium driving experience, but it has experienced some issues. The engine and transmission issues can be serious and costly to repair. Other issues, such as electrical malfunctions and interior problems, are less severe but can still be frustrating for owners. Overall, the Q70's cost of ownership may be higher than expected due to these potential issues.
