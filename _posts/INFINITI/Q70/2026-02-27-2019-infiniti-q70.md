# 2019 INFINITI Q70 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Infiniti Q70 is a mid-size luxury sedan offering a blend of traditional styling and potent performance. Used car buyers looking at this model will find a comfortable cabin and a choice of robust powertrains, though it is wise to thoroughly evaluate vehicle history and conduct a pre-purchase inspection to catch any potential drivetrain or electrical anomalies reported by some owners.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L V6

The 3.7-liter V6 engine produces approximately 330 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration for daily driving. It is generally regarded as a durable powerplant, though some owners have reported initial hesitation during morning warm-ups.

### 5.6L V8

The 5.6-liter V8 engine generates roughly 420 horsepower and 417 lb-ft of torque, delivering swift, effortless passing power. This robust option provides strong overall reliability but consumes more fuel than its V6 counterpart.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic

The 7-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the rear wheels or available all-wheel-drive system. It operates reliably under normal driving conditions, matching well with both available engine choices.

### Vehicle Speed Control

Owner reports indicate occasional electronic throttle or speed control hesitation during initial morning operation.

**Acceleration Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle hesitates and unexpectedly slows down to about 5 mph when attempting to accelerate, particularly during morning drives.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the throttle body, powertrain control module, and related electronic sensors for faults.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V654000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q70&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V470000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=INFINITI&model=Q70&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Both scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driveshaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Infiniti Q70 provides a comfortable and powerful luxury driving experience, backed by traditionally solid mechanical foundations. While isolated owner reports note early-morning acceleration hesitation, the vehicle remains a compelling choice for used-car shoppers who prioritize spaciousness and strong engine performance.
