# 2018 INFINITI Q70

### Overview
The 2018 INFINITI Q70 is a luxury sedan known for its comfortable ride and potent performance, particularly with its V6 engine options. Reliability for this model year is generally strong, reflecting INFINITI's reputation for building durable vehicles. Owners typically report satisfaction with the powertrain and overall build quality. However, like any vehicle, there are specific areas where minor issues can arise, primarily related to the infotainment system and occasional sensor malfunctions. Routine maintenance is key to ensuring the longevity of this sophisticated sedan.

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 INFINITI Q70 demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, with its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission performing exceptionally well. The V6 and V8 engines are robust, and the 7-speed automatic transmission is generally trouble-free. Areas that may require attention are primarily within the electrical system, specifically the infotainment screen which can experience glitches, and occasional issues with parking sensors. Exterior and interior components are typically durable. Overall, the Q70 is a solid choice for a pre-owned luxury sedan, provided minor electrical quirks are understood and addressed.
