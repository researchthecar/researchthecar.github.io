# 2018 INFINITI Q70 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Infiniti Q70 is a luxury sedan offering a blend of performance and comfort, anchored by established V6 and V8 engine options paired with automatic transmissions. When shopping for a used Q70, buyers should review service history and inspect onboard systems carefully, as owner reports point to occasional electronic and safety monitoring glitches.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L V6

The 3.7-liter V6 engine produces around 330 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration. It is generally regarded as a durable powertrain option for daily driving.

### 5.6L V8

The 5.6-liter V8 engine generates roughly 420 horsepower and 417 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust passing power. This option provides strong performance but typically yields lower fuel economy than the V6.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic

The 7-speed automatic transmission comes paired with both rear-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations. It provides smooth gear shifts and dependable long-term operation when properly maintained.

## Safety

### Seat Belts and Air Bags

Owner reports note occasional warning light activations related to occupant detection systems.

**Warning Light Illumination**
- **Symptoms:** The seat belt and air bag warning lights illuminate on the dashboard intermittently or continuously.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician or dealership inspect the passenger seat cushion sensor wiring and modules for faults.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Infiniti Q70 provides a comfortable and powerful luxury driving experience backed by generally solid powertrain choices. Used car buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify that all safety systems and electronics are operating properly.
