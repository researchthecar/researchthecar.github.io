### Summary Verdict
The 2016 INFINITI Q70 generally presents a reliable ownership experience, particularly concerning its core powertrain components like the V6 and V8 engines and the 7-speed automatic transmission. These systems are robust and do not show widespread failure patterns. However, potential owners should be aware of some electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and occasional battery drain issues, which may require attention. The interior climate control system, specifically the blower motor, has also been noted as a potential point of failure. Overall, the Q70 is a solid choice for a luxury sedan, with most issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic design flaws.
