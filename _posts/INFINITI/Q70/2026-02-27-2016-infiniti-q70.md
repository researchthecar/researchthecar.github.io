# 2016 INFINITI Q70 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Infiniti Q70 is a midsize luxury sedan known for its spacious cabin, refined ride, and strong available engine lineup. Used car buyers looking at this model will find a comfortable cruiser with a distinct executive style. While overall owner complaint volume remains relatively low, prospective buyers should review available vehicle history reports and inspect standard mechanical and electrical systems before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L V6

This 3.7-liter V6 engine produces approximately 330 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration for daily driving. It is a generally dependable powerplant known for traditional naturally aspirated performance.

### 5.6L V8

The larger 5.6-liter V8 engine generates roughly 420 horsepower and 417 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration and smooth power delivery. It offers strong performance, though fuel economy is lower compared to the V6 option.

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid

This hybrid powertrain combines a 3.5-liter V6 with an electric motor to produce a net 360 horsepower while prioritizing fuel efficiency. Buyers considering the hybrid should verify battery health and overall system performance during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission is paired with either rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive across the model lineup. It generally provides smooth gear shifts, though buyers should test drive the vehicle thoroughly to ensure seamless operation.

## Steering

### Steering Wheel Heater Switch

Interior electrical switches and comfort controls generally function well, though specific switch placements and heating intensity have drawn occasional driver feedback.

**Heated Steering Wheel Switch Location and Temperature**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report that the heated steering wheel can become too hot to hold comfortably, requiring the driver to turn it off using a switch located in a low bank on the left side.
- **Solution:** Exercise caution when operating the heated steering wheel and use the dashboard switch to manage temperature levels as needed.

## Exterior

### Windshield Wipers

Exterior wiper mechanisms generally operate reliably, but severe weather can occasionally expose mechanical wear or linkage binding.

**Windshield Wiper Motor or Linkage Seizure**
- **Symptoms:** Windshield wipers may suddenly seize in the downward position during heavy winter weather or heavy rainstorms, impairing forward visibility.
- **Solution:** Inspect the wiper motor and linkage assembly for binding or failure, and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Safety systems are designed to protect occupants effectively, but warning lights require professional diagnostic evaluation to ensure proper system readiness.

**Air Bag Warning Indicator Illumination**
- **Symptoms:** The SRS air bag warning light on the instrument cluster may illuminate unexpectedly, indicating a fault within the supplemental restraint system.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified service center scan the vehicle's diagnostic computer to identify the specific trouble code and replace malfunctioning sensors or wiring.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Infiniti Q70 offers a compelling blend of luxury, comfort, and performance for used car shoppers. While isolated owner complaints highlight minor issues with wiper operation, switch ergonomics, and air bag warning lights, the vehicle does not suffer from widespread powertrain defects. Buyers should perform a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verify all electronic and safety systems before finalizing their purchase.
