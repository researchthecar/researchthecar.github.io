**Introduction:**

The 2019 INFINITI Q70 is a full-size luxury sedan known for its sleek design, spacious interior, and powerful performance. It is available with a choice of three engine options and two transmission options. The naturally aspirated 3.7-liter V6 produces 330 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque, while the twin-turbocharged 3.0-liter V6 delivers 300 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. The top-of-the-line 5.6-liter V8 generates an impressive 416 horsepower and 414 lb-ft of torque. A 7-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive are standard, while all-wheel drive is optional.

**Engine**

**3.7-liter V6**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine, reduced power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Valve Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, reduced power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace faulty valve springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0-liter Twin-Turbo V6**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced power, hissing sounds, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace faulty turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5.6-liter V8**

* **Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Low oil level, smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace piston rings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-speed Automatic**

* **Shift Flare:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed gear shifts
    * Solution: Update transmission software
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises, reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace faulty strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead quickly, electrical faults
    * Solution: Test and replace faulty electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezes, audio issues, navigation problems
    * Solution: Update software, replace faulty components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin
    * Solution: Replace sunroof seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC or heater not working properly
    * Solution: Replace faulty climate control components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2019 INFINITI Q70 has a few issues that can be costly to repair, but it offers a comfortable and powerful driving experience. The severity of the issues varies from minor electrical glitches to major engine problems. The cost of repairs can range from affordable to expensive, depending on the issue.
