**Introduction:**

The 2012 Fiat Strada is a compact pickup truck offered with a single engine and transmission option. It has a 1.4-liter Fire engine that produces 85 horsepower and 90 lb-ft of torque, paired with a five-speed manual transmission. The Strada is known for its versatility, affordability, and fuel efficiency. However, it has received some complaints regarding reliability and certain component issues.

**Engine**

**1.4-liter Fire**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Leakage of coolant or oil from the intake manifold, causing engine overheating, poor performance, and possible internal damage.
Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

**Shifting Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging or shifting gears, grinding noises during shifting, or popping out of gear.
Solution: Inspection and repair of the transmission, which may include replacement of worn gears, synchronizers, or the clutch.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension Noise**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking or banging noises from the front suspension when driving over bumps or rough terrain.
Solution: Replacement of worn or damaged suspension components, such as control arms, ball joints, or bushings.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Electrical Harness Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Faulty wiring or loose connections can lead to various electrical problems, such as flickering lights, malfunctioning gauges, or starting issues.
Solution: Inspection and repair of the electrical harness, which may include replacing damaged wires or connectors.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**HVAC System Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Blowing hot air when set to cold or vice versa, poor air flow, or failure of the climate control system.
Solution: Inspection and repair of the HVAC system, which may include replacing faulty components, such as the blower motor or temperature blend door actuator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2012 Fiat Strada has some reported issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. While some of these issues are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix, others can be costly and affect the overall reliability of the vehicle. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential problems and address them promptly to maintain the vehicle's performance and longevity.
