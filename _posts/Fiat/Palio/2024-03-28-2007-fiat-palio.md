**Introduction**

The 2007 Fiat Palio, a compact hatchback, is powered by a 1.2-liter engine that produces 65 horsepower and 73 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 5-speed manual transmission. The Palio is well-regarded for its fuel efficiency, affordability, and agile handling, while criticisms include its lack of power and basic interior.

**Engine**

1.2L

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
Description/Symptoms: The engine burns excessive amounts of oil, leading to decreased oil levels and potential engine damage.
Solution: Replace the piston rings and valve stem seals.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

5-Speed Manual

**Transmission Fluid Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Fluid leaks from the transmission, resulting in reduced lubrication and potential transmission failure.
Solution: Replace the transmission seals.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The alternator fails to charge the battery, leading to electrical system malfunctions and potential engine shutdown.
Solution: Replace the alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Windshield Wiper Motor Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The windshield wiper motor fails, preventing the wipers from operating.
Solution: Replace the wiper motor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Airbag Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The airbag light illuminates on the dashboard, indicating a malfunction in the airbag system.
Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace the airbag system components as needed.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2007 Fiat Palio faces several issues, primarily in the engine and transmission systems. The excessive oil consumption issue can be a significant concern, while the transmission fluid leak and alternator failure require timely attention to prevent further damage. Additionally, the airbag malfunction presents a safety hazard that should be addressed immediately. Overall, the Palio's issues are moderate in severity, with repair costs ranging from $200 to $1,000+ depending on the issue and the extent of repairs required.
