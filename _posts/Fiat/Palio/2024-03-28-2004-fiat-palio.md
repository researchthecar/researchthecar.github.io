## 2004 Fiat Palio

The 2004 Fiat Palio is a subcompact car that was available with a variety of engine and transmission options. The 1.2-liter FIRE engine produced 60 horsepower and 72 lb-ft of torque, while the 1.4-liter FIRE engine produced 70 horsepower and 80 lb-ft of torque. Both engines were available with a 5-speed manual transmission or a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

The Palio was praised for its fuel efficiency, affordability, and maneuverability, but it was criticized for its cramped interior and lack of features.

**Engine**

* **1.2-liter FIRE**
    * **Blown head gasket**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking externally, white smoke from exhaust, overheating
        * Solution: Replace head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * **Timing belt failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine will not start, loud noise from engine
        * Solution: Replace timing belt
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: 06V323000
* **1.4-liter FIRE**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **5-speed Manual**
    * **Difficulty shifting gears**
        * Description/Symptoms: Gears grind when shifting, difficulty engaging gears
        * Solution: Replace shift linkage or transmission fluid
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **CVT**
    * **Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or slips when accelerating
        * Solution: Replace CVT fluid or CVT transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim headlights, electrical system malfunctions
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: 06V547000
* **Starter failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine will not crank, clicking noise when turning key
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: 06V399000

**Exterior**

* **Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots on body panels, especially around wheel wells and rocker panels
    * Solution: Rust repair or replacement of affected panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity
* **Headlight bulb failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights not working or dim
    * Solution: Replace headlight bulb
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $20 - $50+

**Interior**

* **Seat belt retraction failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat belt does not retract properly
    * Solution: Replace seat belt retractor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: 06V292000
* **Air conditioning failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC not blowing cold air, compressor not engaging
    * Solution: Recharge AC system or replace compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+

**Summary**

The 2004 Fiat Palio is a budget-friendly car with good fuel efficiency and maneuverability. However, it is prone to several issues, including blown head gaskets, timing belt failure, transmission problems, and electrical failures. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, but some can be quite expensive to repair. Overall, the Palio may not be the most reliable choice in its class.
