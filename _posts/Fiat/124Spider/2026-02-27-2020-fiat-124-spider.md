# 2020 Fiat 124 Spider Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Fiat 124 Spider combines Italian styling with reliable Mazda-derived roadster underpinnings, offering buyers an engaging and relatively straightforward open-top driving experience. While owner complaints in our dataset are sparse, shoppers should remain mindful of broader industry recalls and standard mechanical checks for a used sports car of this vintage.

---

## Engine

### 1.4L MultiAir Turbo Inline-4

This 1.4-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 160 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque (or up to 164 hp and 184 lb-ft in Abarth trims). It provides spirited acceleration for the lightweight roadster and generally requires diligent oil maintenance to keep the MultiAir valve actuation system running smoothly.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual gearbox features short throws and a direct mechanical feel, making it a favorite for driving enthusiasts. It has a solid reputation for durability when paired with the 1.4L turbo engine.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The optional 6-speed traditional automatic transmission offers smooth, predictable shifts for everyday cruising. It is well-matched to the powerband of the turbocharged engine.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Air bag system complaints are primarily centered around manufacturer safety campaign logistics and part availability rather than unexpected physical deployments.

**Recall Part Availability Delays**
- **Symptoms:** Owners receiving safety recall notices for air bags have reported difficulties scheduling repairs due to temporary shortages of replacement parts or equipment at local dealerships.
- **Solution:** Contact an authorized dealership to check part availability and schedule the free recall repair as soon as components arrive.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V694000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Fiat&model=124+Spider&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A software error in the air bag control module may cause the front air bags to deploy with excessive force during a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An air bag that deploys with excessive force can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the air bag control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Fiat 124 Spider is an appealing weekend cruiser and lightweight sports car option for used buyers, benefiting from shared Mazda MX-5 mechanical roots. Prospective buyers should verify that all open safety recalls—particularly concerning air bags—have been successfully resolved by an authorized dealer prior to purchase.
