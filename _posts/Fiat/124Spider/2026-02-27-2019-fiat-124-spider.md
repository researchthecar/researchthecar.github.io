# 2019 Fiat 124 Spider Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Fiat 124 Spider, buyers will find a sporty, light roadster sharing much of its mechanical foundation with the Mazda MX-5 Miata while utilizing its own distinct Italian-tuned powertrain. While generally celebrated for its engaging driving dynamics and distinctive styling, prospective owners should review known owner-reported concerns regarding interior reflections and specific component wear.

---

## Engine

### 1.4L MultiAir Turbo Inline-4

This 1.4-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 160 horsepower (or 164 hp in Abarth trims) and 184 lb-ft of torque. It provides spirited acceleration for the lightweight roadster, though routine maintenance on the turbocharging system and oil changes are essential for longevity.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

A traditional 6-speed manual gearbox offering crisp, short throws and direct driver engagement. It is generally robust when maintained properly with standard fluid changes.

### 6-Speed Aisin Automatic Transmission

A conventional 6-speed automatic transmission featuring steering wheel-mounted paddle shifters on select trims. It delivers smooth daily drivability while complementing the turbo engine's power band.

## Exterior

### Dashboard Trim and Windshield

Interior visibility can occasionally be impacted by reflective dashboard materials under specific lighting conditions.

**Dashboard Reflection**
- **Symptoms:** Bright sunlight reflects off the dashboard and silver trim plates onto the windshield, potentially compromising forward visibility at certain angles.
- **Solution:** Using a dash cover or polarized sunglasses can help reduce glare and reflections.

## Safety

### Seat Belt Retractors

Seat belt mechanisms are critical safety items, and owners have noted potential issues with retraction hardware.

**Seat Belt Failure to Retract**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report that the driver or passenger seat belts fail to retract smoothly, leaving slack in the belt during use.
- **Solution:** The seat belt assemblies or retractors must be inspected and replaced by a service center.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V879000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Fiat&model=124+Spider&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V694000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Fiat&model=124+Spider&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A software error in the air bag control module may cause the front air bags to deploy with excessive force during a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An air bag that deploys with excessive force can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the air bag control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Fiat 124 Spider is an enjoyable weekend cruiser with engaging handling and distinctive road presence. Used car buyers should verify that all open recalls have been completed and carefully inspect interior visibility factors as well as standard safety hardware like seat belts during a pre-purchase test drive.
