**Introduction:**

The 2020 Fiat 124 Spider is a stylish Italian sports car that shares its platform with the Mazda MX-5 Miata. With its 1.4-liter turbocharged engine producing 164 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque, the 124 Spider offers an exhilarating driving experience. Despite its sporty nature, the 124 Spider also features a comfortable interior and an infotainment system.

While the 124 Spider is generally praised for its handling and performance, it does have some drawbacks. Common complaints include its limited trunk space and lack of modern safety features.

**Engine**

* **1.4L Turbocharged**

    * **Turbocharger Failure:** **Symptoms:** Loss of power, increased oil consumption **Solution:** Replace turbocharger **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+ **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual**

    * **Shifting Difficulties:** **Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting gears **Solution:** Adjust or replace shift linkage **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+ **Recalls:** None Found

* **6-Speed Automatic**

    * **Transmission Slippage:** **Symptoms:** Loss of power during acceleration **Solution:** Replace transmission **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $3,000 - $5,000+ **Recalls:** NHTSA ID: 20V579000

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise:** **Symptoms:** Squeaking or rattling sounds from the rear of the vehicle **Solution:** Replace rear suspension bushings **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $700+ **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Failure:** **Symptoms:** Loss of power, no response from engine or lights **Solution:** Replace electrical components or repair wiring **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+ **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Paint Peeling:** **Symptoms:** Paint chipping or peeling off the body **Solution:** Repaint affected areas **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+ **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:** **Symptoms:** Rattling sounds coming from the dashboard **Solution:** Replace or tighten dashboard components **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+ **Recalls:** None Found
* **HVAC Malfunction:** **Symptoms:** AC or heater not working properly **Solution:** Replace HVAC components **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+ **Recalls:** NHTSA ID: 20V347000

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2020 Fiat 124 Spider is a reliable and fun-to-drive sports car. While it does have some issues, most of them are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix. The main issues to look out for are related to the turbocharger, transmission, and suspension.
