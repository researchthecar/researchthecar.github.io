# 2018 Fiat 124 Spider Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2018 Fiat 124 Spider, buyers will find a sporty, Mazda-MX-5-derived roadster offering distinct Italian styling and turbocharged performance. While generally praised for its engaging driving dynamics, prospective owners should review owner-reported feedback regarding powertrain starting concerns and interior safety assemblies. As with any used sports car, a thorough pre-purchase inspection is recommended to verify overall mechanical and electrical health.

---

## Engine

### 1.4L MultiAir Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

The 1.4-liter MultiAir turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 160 horsepower (and up to 164 hp in Abarth trims) with 184 lb-ft of torque. It delivers punchy low-end power suited to the roadster's lightweight chassis, though routine maintenance is essential for longevity.

**Intermittent Starting and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may experience intermittent failure to start or unexpected stalling without illuminating warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the ignition, fuel system, starter, and battery connections.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

A traditional 6-speed manual gearbox featuring short throws and direct linkage, offering a highly engaging driving experience for enthusiasts.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A conventional 6-speed automatic transmission with paddle shifters, providing smooth gear changes for everyday commuting and cruising.

## Safety

### Seat Belt

Owner complaints highlight isolated concerns regarding seat belt assembly operation on driver and passenger sides.

**Seat Belt Assembly Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers or passengers may experience mechanical issues, binding, or latching problems with the seat belt assemblies on either side of the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt mechanisms for proper retraction and latching; replace faulty assemblies as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V694000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Fiat&model=124+Spider&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A software error in the air bag control module may cause the front air bags to deploy with excessive force during a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An air bag that deploys with excessive force can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the air bag control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Fiat 124 Spider combines classic roadster charm with responsive turbo performance. While owner reports indicate occasional starting and seat belt operation concerns, informed buyers who verify service histories and conduct a pre-purchase inspection can find an enjoyable and stylish drop-top cruiser.
