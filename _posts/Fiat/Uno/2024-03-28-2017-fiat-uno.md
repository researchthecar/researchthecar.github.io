**Introduction:**

The 2017 Fiat Uno is a subcompact hatchback that offers a budget-friendly option with decent fuel economy. It is available with a 1.3-liter FireFly engine that produces 94 horsepower and 93 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with a 5-speed manual transmission.

Despite its affordability, the Uno has received mixed reviews from drivers. While it is praised for its affordability and fuel efficiency, complaints have been raised about its low power output, poor soundproofing, and lack of advanced safety features.

**Engine**

**1.3-liter FireFly Engine**

* **Timing Chain Failure:** The timing chain can prematurely wear out, leading to engine damage and costly repairs.
    * Symptoms: Engine rattling, reduced power output, Check Engine light
    * Solution: Replacement of timing chain and components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Difficult Shifting:** Gears may be difficult to engage or grind when shifting.
    * Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, grinding or scraping noises
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of clutch, transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Failure:** The front struts may prematurely fail, resulting in poor handling and excessive noise.
    * Symptoms: Knocking or clunking noises, reduced stability, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replacement of front struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain prematurely due to electrical faults.
    * Symptoms: Difficulty starting, loss of electrical power
    * Solution: Diagnosis of electrical system, repair or replacement of faulty components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Malfunction:** The door locks may malfunction, preventing the doors from being opened or closed.
    * Symptoms: Doors cannot be locked or unlocked, difficulty operating door handles
    * Solution: Replacement of door latch assemblies
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:** The dashboard may rattle or make excessive noise due to poor assembly.
    * Symptoms: Loose or vibrating dashboard parts, annoying noises
    * Solution: Tightening or replacement of dashboard components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Fiat Uno has a range of issues, including timing chain failures, transmission problems, suspension issues, electrical faults, door lock malfunctions, and interior rattles. While some of these issues are minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be costly and significantly impact the vehicle's reliability. The severity and cost of these issues should be considered when researching or purchasing this vehicle.
