# 2018 Fiat 500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2018 Fiat 500, buyers will find a compact city car characterized by Italian styling and efficient powertrain options. While the available owner complaint data is sparse, prospective buyers should review available maintenance records and conduct thorough inspections during a test drive to ensure everything is in proper working order.

---

## Engine

### 1.4L Turbocharged MultiAir 4-Cylinder

This 1.4-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 135 horsepower and 150 lb-ft of torque, offering peppy performance for daily city driving. It is generally robust, though regular maintenance and timely oil changes are vital for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission pairs engaging shift action with the turbo engine, providing a direct connection and reliable operation for drivers who prefer shifting gears themselves.

### 6-Speed Aisin Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed conventional automatic transmission delivers smooth gear changes suited for urban commutes. Routine fluid checks help maintain its dependability over time.

## Suspension

### Lug Nuts

Wheel hardware should be inspected during routine maintenance to ensure proper torque and security.

**Lug Nut Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may be informed by service shops or tire centers that the original factory lug nuts require replacement due to wear or swelling.
- **Solution:** Have the lug nuts inspected during tire rotations and replace them with durable aftermarket or OEM replacements if degradation is found.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake components generally perform well, though alignment and hardware condition affect high-speed stopping stability.

**Vehicle Pulling During Hard Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may exhibit a tendency to pull or dive toward one side when brakes are applied aggressively at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, calipers, and alignment settings to ensure balanced braking performance.

## Interior

### Seats

Cabin upholstery and seat padding should be carefully examined during a pre-purchase inspection for cosmetic or structural wear.

**Seat Cushion Indentations**
- **Symptoms:** Occupants might notice depressions, indentations, or internal plastic cord structures pressing through the seat cover material.
- **Solution:** Have the upholstery inspected by an automotive trim specialist or dealer to evaluate potential cushion padding adjustments.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19E044000: Power Train:Manual Transmission:Floor Shift Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Fiat&model=500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the shifter breaks while the vehicle is in motion, it can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V474000: Tires:Pressure Monitoring And Regulating Systems](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Fiat&model=500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The tire pressure monitoring system (TPMS) warning light may not illuminate as intended. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 138, "Tire Pressure Monitoring Systems." A TPMS warning light that fails to illuminate can result in the vehicle being driven with improperly inflated tires.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the TPMS electronic control unit module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Fiat 500 presents as a stylish and efficient option for city drivers, supported by generally stable powertrain options. Because owner-reported complaints are minimal, buyers should focus on standard pre-purchase inspections, verifying vehicle history, and checking all mechanical and interior features prior to purchase.
