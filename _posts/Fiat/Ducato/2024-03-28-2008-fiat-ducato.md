**Introduction**

The 2008 Fiat Ducato is a versatile van offered with various engine and transmission options. It boasts impressive cargo capacity, making it a popular choice for commercial and recreational purposes. The Ducato is powered by a 2.2-liter MultiJet diesel engine paired with a 6-speed manual transmission as standard. Other available engine options include a 2.0-liter MultiJet diesel engine and a 3.0-liter MultiJet Power diesel engine paired with a 6-speed Comfort-Matic automated manual transmission or a 6-speed manual transmission.

**Engine**

**2.2-liter MultiJet Diesel Engine**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, reduced power, increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Replace faulty fuel injector(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whistling noises, blue or black smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter MultiJet Diesel Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Valve Swirl Flap Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, Check Engine Light
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold valve swirl flap
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $750+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0-liter MultiJet Power Diesel Engine**

* **EGR Valve Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, Check Engine Light
    * Solution: Replace EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Gearshift Linkage Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace gearshift linkage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Comfort-Matic Automated Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears
    * Solution: Replace clutch assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, reduced ride quality
    * Solution: Replace front suspension strut(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, reduced load capacity
    * Solution: Replace rear leaf spring(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, difficulty starting engine
    * Solution: Inspect electrical system for parasitic drain, replace battery if necessary
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, discharged battery, electrical system malfunction
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Mirror Defrost Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Mirrors do not defrost properly in cold weather
    * Solution: Replace mirror defrosting element(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door Panel Trim Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from door panels
    * Solution: Apply foam or felt tape to secure trim panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Fan Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No air flow from climate control system
    * Solution: Replace climate control fan motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2008 Fiat Ducato is generally a reliable vehicle, but like any vehicle, it can have some issues over time. The most common engine and transmission issues seem to be fuel injector failure and gearshift linkage failure, respectively. The suspension and electrical systems have been known to have issues with front suspension strut failure and battery discharge, respectively. Other issues include mirror defrost failure, door panel trim rattle, and climate control fan motor failure. Overall, the severity of the issues is moderate, and the cost to fix them varies depending on the severity of the problem and the specific part that needs to be replaced.
