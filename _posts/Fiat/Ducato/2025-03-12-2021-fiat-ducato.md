The 2021 Fiat Ducato is a popular choice for commercial vans and motorhome conversions, known for its spacious interior and versatility. Engine options for the 2021 model year typically include a 2.3-liter MultiJet diesel engine. Power outputs vary, but a common version produces around 140 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. A 6-speed manual transmission is typically standard, with an automatic transmission available as an option. Owners often praise the Ducato's large cargo capacity and customizable nature. However, some common criticisms include relatively basic interior features and potential reliability issues.

**Engine**

**2.3L MultiJet Diesel**

*   **Injector Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, poor fuel economy, black smoke from the exhaust, and potential engine misfires. Fault codes related to fuel injector performance may be present.
    *   Solution: Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **EGR Valve Problems**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, increased emissions, and potential stalling. Check engine light activation with EGR-related fault codes.
    *   Solution: Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

*   **Clutch Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, unusual noises during clutch engagement.
    *   Solution: Clutch disc, pressure plate, and potentially flywheel replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Rough Shifting/Hesitation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Harsh or delayed shifts between gears. Hesitation during acceleration.
    * Solution: Transmission fluid flush, valve body repair or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $2000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Rear Leaf Spring Sagging**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rear of the vehicle sits lower than normal, especially when loaded. Reduced ride quality and potential handling issues.
    *   Solution: Replacement of rear leaf springs, potentially with heavy-duty versions.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Battery Drain Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Frequent dead battery, especially after short periods of inactivity.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis and repair of parasitic drain. May involve replacing faulty modules or wiring repairs.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Sensor Failures (ABS, ESP)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Warning lights on the dashboard for ABS or ESP systems. Potential loss of functionality of these systems.
    *   Solution: Replacement of faulty sensors.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Sliding Door Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing the sliding door. Sticking or jamming.
    *   Solution: Adjustment or replacement of door rollers, hinges, or latch mechanisms.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Dashboard Rattles**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Annoying rattling noises coming from the dashboard area, especially on rough roads.
    *   Solution: Locating and securing loose components behind the dashboard.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300+

    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Fiat Ducato, while offering versatility and space, can be prone to some common issues. Engine-related problems like injector and EGR valve failures can lead to performance issues. Transmission problems, especially with the manual clutch, can be costly. Suspension issues like sagging leaf springs may affect handling. Electrical faults, such as battery drain and sensor failures, are also frequently reported. Finally, exterior and interior problems, such as sliding door malfunctions and dashboard rattles, can cause day-to-day frustration. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but potential buyers should be aware of these common complaints.
