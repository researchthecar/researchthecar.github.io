Here's a breakdown of common issues and complaints reported for the 2022 Fiat Ducato, organized by category and engine/transmission configuration. Please note that due to limited access to comprehensive and verified owner complaint databases for the 2022 Ducato specifically, this information is compiled from general Ducato forums, reviews, and repair databases. Specific costs can vary widely depending on location and repair shop.

The 2022 Fiat Ducato is a popular choice for commercial vans and camper conversions, known for its spaciousness, practicality, and relatively competitive pricing. Engine options generally include a 2.2L Multijet diesel engine with varying power outputs and a 3.0L Multijet diesel engine. Horsepower can range from 120 to 180 depending on the engine variant. Torque figures generally vary between 320 and 450 Nm. Owners appreciate the Ducato's large cargo capacity and suitability for conversions, but some criticisms include concerns about reliability, build quality in some areas, and electronic gremlins. Transmission options are generally a 6-speed manual or a 9-speed automatic, depending on the engine and market.

**Engine**

**2.2L Multijet Diesel**

*   **Injector Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, reduced power, increased fuel consumption, diesel knock.
    *   Solution: Replacement of faulty injectors.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1200+ (depending on the number of injectors needing replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **EGR Valve Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Poor engine performance, black smoke from the exhaust, engine warning light. This is more common in vehicles used for short journeys.
    *   Solution: Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Problems**
    *   Description/Symptoms: DPF light illumination, reduced engine power, inability to regenerate the DPF, potential for engine damage if ignored.
    *   Solution: Forced DPF regeneration, DPF cleaning, or DPF replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 (forced regeneration) - $1500+ (DPF replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**3.0L Multijet Diesel**

*   **Injector Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, reduced power, increased fuel consumption, diesel knock.
    *   Solution: Replacement of faulty injectors.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1200+ (depending on the number of injectors needing replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Timing Chain Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, especially on startup. Potential for serious engine damage if the chain fails.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the timing chain and associated components (guides, tensioners).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $2000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

*   **Clutch Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clutch slippage, difficulty shifting gears, clutch pedal sticking or being difficult to press.
    *   Solution: Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1500
    *   Recalls: None Found

**9-Speed Automatic**

*   **Harsh Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Noticeable hard shifts between gears, particularly at lower speeds. Hesitation when accelerating.
    *   Solution: Software update for the transmission control module (TCM). In some cases, valve body replacement may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300 (software update), $800 - $2000+ (valve body replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Transmission Fluid Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Visible fluid leaks under the vehicle, often near the transmission. Low transmission fluid level can lead to shifting problems and damage.
    *   Solution: Replacement of leaking seals or gaskets.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800 (depending on the severity and location of the leak)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Rear Suspension Sagging**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sits lower in the rear, particularly when loaded. Can affect handling and ride quality.
    *   Solution: Replacement of rear springs or installation of auxiliary air suspension.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1500+ (depending on the solution)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Knocking Noises from Suspension**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clunking or knocking sounds heard when driving over bumps or uneven surfaces. Often caused by worn bushings or ball joints.
    *   Solution: Replacement of worn suspension components (bushings, ball joints, shock absorbers).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+ (depending on the components needing replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Sensor Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Various warning lights on the dashboard (ABS, ESP, engine management), potentially affecting vehicle performance and safety systems.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis of the faulty sensor and replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+ (depending on the sensor)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Infotainment System Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Freezing, crashing, unresponsive touchscreen, Bluetooth connectivity problems.
    *   Solution: Software update, resetting the system, or replacement of the infotainment unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (software update) - $1000+ (replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Battery Problems**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, especially in cold weather. Battery draining quickly.
    *   Solution: Battery replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Sliding Door Problems**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing the sliding door, sticking, unusual noises.
    *   Solution: Adjustment of the door mechanism, lubrication, or replacement of worn rollers or cables.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Rust Issues (Early Stages)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Surface rust appearing in common areas (wheel arches, door sills, underbody).
    *   Solution: Rust removal and treatment, followed by repainting or protective coating.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+ (depending on the extent of the rust)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Dashboard Rattles**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Annoying rattling noises coming from the dashboard area, especially on rough roads.
    *   Solution: Identifying the source of the rattle and securing loose components with foam padding or clips.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200 (depending on the complexity)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Door Panel Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Loose or ill-fitting door panels, potential for vibration noises.
    *   Solution: Re-securing door panels with new clips or fasteners.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Fiat Ducato can be a practical and versatile van, but potential buyers should be aware of some common issues. Engine problems, particularly related to injectors and DPFs, are a concern. Transmission issues, especially with the 9-speed automatic, can be costly to repair. Suspension sagging and electrical gremlins are also reported. The severity of these issues varies, but addressing them can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand, depending on the problem and repair needed. Potential buyers should carefully inspect the vehicle and consider a pre-purchase inspection by a qualified mechanic.
