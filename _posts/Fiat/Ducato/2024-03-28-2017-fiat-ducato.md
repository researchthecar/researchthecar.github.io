**Introduction:**

The 2017 Fiat Ducato is a full-size van available with a variety of engine and transmission options. It offers ample space and a comfortable ride, making it suitable for both personal and commercial use. However, several issues have been reported by owners, ranging from minor inconveniences to more serious problems.

**Engine**

* **2.3L MultiJet II Diesel**
    * **Excessive Oil Consumption:** High oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Description/Symptoms: Decreased oil level on dipstick, blue smoke from exhaust.
    * Solution: Engine may require disassembly and replacement of piston rings or valve seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **3.0L MultiJet II Diesel**
    * **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues:** Fault codes related to DPF regeneration or clogging, causing reduced performance and potential engine damage.
    * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, reduced power output, smoky exhaust.
    * Solution: DPF cleaning or replacement, ECU software updates.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual**
    * **Gearbox Synchronizers:** Worn or damaged synchronizers, causing difficulty shifting gears.
    * Description/Symptoms: Grinding or difficulty engaging gears.
    * Solution: Transmission overhaul or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **9-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Hesitation:** Delayed or hesitant shifting, especially during acceleration or overtaking.
    * Description/Symptoms: Jerking or prolonged pauses while shifting.
    * Solution: Transmission software updates or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Springs:** Premature wear or breakage of rear suspension springs, causing sagging and potential loss of control.
* Description/Symptoms: Rear end sitting lower than front, uneven tire wear.
* Solution: Replacement of rear suspension springs.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Premature battery discharge, resulting in difficulty starting the vehicle.
* Description/Symptoms: Battery going dead after short periods of inactivity.
* Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical drain source (e.g., faulty wiring, parasitic draw).
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sliding Door Lock:** Failure of sliding door lock mechanism, causing the door to open while driving.
* Description/Symptoms: Sudden door opening, warning light on dashboard.
* Solution: Replacement of door lock assembly.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 18V758000

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Panel:** Loose or rattling dashboard panel, creating noise and vibration.
* Description/Symptoms: Rattle or vibration from dashboard area.
* Solution: Removal and reattachment of dashboard panel.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Fiat Ducato presents some issues, ranging from minor inconveniences to more significant mechanical problems. The most common issues include excessive oil consumption, DPF issues, and gearbox synchronizer wear. While these issues can be frustrating and costly to repair, the overall reliability of the Ducato is considered average. Regular maintenance and timely repairs are essential to minimize problems and ensure a safe and reliable driving experience.
