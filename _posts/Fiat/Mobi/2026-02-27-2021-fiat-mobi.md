## Exterior

**Paint chipping/fading**

## Interior

**Squeaks and rattles**

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Fiat Mobi generally presents a reliable profile for its segment, with its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission showing no significant widespread issues. The primary areas where owners might encounter minor concerns are within the electrical system, specifically intermittent warning lights, and the exterior and interior, where minor cosmetic issues like paint wear or interior rattles can emerge. These are often minor and manageable, aligning with the vehicle's budget-friendly nature. Overall, for its intended use as an economical city car, the Mobi offers a dependable ownership experience, provided owners are aware of and address these less critical potential issues.
