**Introduction:**

The 2021 Fiat Mobi is a compact and affordable hatchback designed for urban environments. It is powered by a 1.0-liter four-cylinder engine, mated either to a five-speed manual transmission or an optional automated manual transmission. The Mobi has a spacious interior and a decent amount of cargo space for its compact size. It is praised for its fuel efficiency and maneuverability, but some owners have reported various issues.

**Engine**

**1.0-Liter Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Excessive Carbon Buildup**
    * Description/Symptoms: Insufficient power, decreased fuel efficiency, rough idle
    * Solution: Engine cleaning or replacement of affected components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or clicking noise at startup, engine stalling
    * Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Shifting Difficulties**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard to shift gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Lubrication or replacement of worn components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Automated Manual Transmission**

* **Hesitation or Jerking During Shifts**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips or stutters during gear changes
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of transmission control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Squeaky Suspension**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or popping noises over bumps
    * Solution: Lubrication or replacement of sway bar bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Testing and repair of electrical system or replacement of battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks not functioning properly, doors locking or unlocking without input
    * Solution: Replacement of door lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC malfunctioning, heater not working
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair or replacement of climate control components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Wind Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise at higher speeds
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of door seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 Fiat Mobi is an economical and practical vehicle, but it has been reported to have some issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. While some of the issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more costly. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these issues before making a purchase decision.
