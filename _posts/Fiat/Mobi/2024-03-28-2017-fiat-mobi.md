**Introduction:**

The 2017 Fiat Mobi is a compact city car known for its affordability and practicality. It comes standard with a 1.0-liter gasoline engine paired with a 5-speed manual transmission.

**Engine:**

**1.0-liter Gasoline**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle requires frequent oil top-ups, burning oil at an accelerated rate.
    * Solution: Piston ring replacement or engine rebuild.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant and/or oil leaks from the intake manifold area, poor engine performance.
    * Solution: Replacement of intake manifold gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Timing Belt Failure (if equipped):**
    * Description/Symptoms: Catastrophic engine damage due to timing belt breakage.
    * Solution: Regular timing belt replacement as per manufacturer's recommended intervals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**5-speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping clutch, burning smell.
    * Solution: Clutch disc and pressure plate replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shift Linkage Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting into certain gears, imprecise gear selection.
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of shift linkage components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Front Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive suspension noise, uneven tire wear, poor handling.
    * Solution: Replacement of front struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bouncy ride, poor stability, excessive body roll.
    * Solution: Replacement of rear shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Electrical:**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharge, dim headlights, loss of electrical functions.
    * Solution: Replacement of alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
* **Starter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to start the engine.
    * Solution: Replacement of starter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* **Electrical Short Circuit:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blown fuses, flickering lights, random electrical malfunctions.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Door Lock Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to lock or unlock doors, faulty central locking system.
    * Solution: Replacement or repair of door lock actuators or central locking module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Tail Light Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning tail lights, turn signals, or brake lights.
    * Solution: Replacement of faulty bulbs or tail light assemblies.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+

**Interior:**

* **Dashboard Panel Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks appearing on the dashboard panel, typically around the vents or center console.
    * Solution: Replacement of dashboard panel.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative or intermittent climate control system, difficulty controlling temperature or airflow.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of climate control system components (e.g., blower motor, temperature sensor).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+

**Summary:**

While the 2017 Fiat Mobi is an affordable and practical vehicle, it does have a number of potential issues that can affect its reliability and ownership experience. Engine problems, such as excessive oil consumption and intake manifold gasket failure, are among the most severe and costly to repair. Transmission issues and electrical short circuits can also be problematic. Suspension and brake issues are generally less serious, but can still lead to inconvenience and maintenance expenses. Interior issues, such as dashboard panel cracking and climate control malfunctions, can also detract from the overall ownership experience. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and to factor them into their decision when considering the 2017 Fiat Mobi.
