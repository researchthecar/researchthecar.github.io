The 2019 Fiat 500X is a subcompact crossover SUV known for its Italian styling and available all-wheel drive. It offers a blend of personality and practicality, appealing to buyers seeking a unique vehicle in a crowded segment. The 2019 model year received a refresh, including a new engine. The sole engine option is a 1.3-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing 177 horsepower and 210 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. Some owners appreciate the 500X's distinctive design and nimble handling. Common complaints revolve around the reliability of the 9-speed transmission, fuel economy, and limited cargo space.

**Engine**

**1.3L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

*   **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners report high oil consumption, requiring frequent oil top-offs between scheduled oil changes. This can manifest as the low oil pressure warning light illuminating.
    *   Solution: There is no definitive fix, but monitoring oil levels regularly and using the recommended oil type is crucial. In severe cases, engine components may need replacement or engine replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $5000+ depending on severity.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

*   **Harsh Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Hesitation when accelerating, especially from a stop, and rough or jerky shifts between gears are common complaints. Some owners describe a "clunking" sound during shifts.
    *   Solution: Software updates to the transmission control module (TCM) may improve shift quality. In some cases, valve body replacement or a full transmission rebuild may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$300+ for software update. $800 - $4000+ for valve body replacement or transmission rebuild.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Transmission Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Complete transmission failure. The vehicle will have difficulty moving and the transmission may make loud grinding noises.
    *   Solution: Transmission replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $7,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Uconnect System Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Malfunctions, including freezing, crashing, and unresponsiveness. Bluetooth connectivity problems and navigation errors have also been reported.
    *   Solution: Software updates from Fiat can sometimes resolve these issues. In some cases, the Uconnect module itself needs to be replaced.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 for software updates (if covered under warranty or recall), $500 - $1500+ for module replacement.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Battery Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Premature battery drain or failure, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle or complete starting failure.
    *   Solution: Replacing the battery with a high-quality replacement. Ensuring proper charging system function.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+ for battery replacement.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Rear Hatch Alignment Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Misalignment of the rear hatch, leading to difficulty closing the hatch.
    *   Solution: Readjusting the latch and hinges.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500+

**Interior**

*   **Dashboard Rattles**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rattling or buzzing noises emanating from the dashboard area while driving, especially on rough roads.
    *   Solution: Locating and securing the source of the rattle, which may involve adding padding or tightening loose fasteners.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300+

**Summary**

The 2019 Fiat 500X presents a mixed bag for owners. While its styling and driving dynamics are generally appreciated, reliability concerns, particularly regarding the 9-speed automatic transmission and Uconnect system, are prevalent. The potential for costly repairs to the transmission and engine should be a significant consideration for prospective buyers. The severity of issues ranges from minor inconveniences like dashboard rattles to major mechanical failures. The estimated cost of repairs can vary widely, with transmission and engine work potentially reaching several thousand dollars.
