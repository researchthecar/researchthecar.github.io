# 2019 Fiat 500X Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2019 Fiat 500X means looking at a stylish, Italian-designed crossover that offers standard all-wheel drive and a punchy turbocharged engine. While owner-reported complaints are relatively limited, prospective buyers should review specific mechanical and fueling concerns noted by drivers. Keeping up with routine inspections during a pre-purchase check will help ensure a smoother ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 1.3L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2019 Fiat 500X features a 1.3-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing approximately 177 horsepower and 210 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant replaced the older naturally aspirated engine, providing improved low-end responsiveness and standard all-wheel drive across the lineup.

**Fuel Refueling Overflow**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report that fuel pumps click off prematurely and gasoline spills out onto the ground during refueling attempts.
- **Solution:** Inspection of the fuel filler neck, EVAP system, and fuel tank venting components is required to identify blockages or faulty valves.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission is paired exclusively with the standard all-wheel-drive system on all 2019 models. Buyers should test-drive the vehicle thoroughly to ensure smooth gear engagement and responsive shifting across various speeds.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly shift out of drive and into neutral while driving at highway speeds, resulting in a loss of acceleration.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning for transmission control module (TCM) error codes and software updates or hardware inspections may be necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V287000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Fiat&model=500X&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Incorrect software may cause the occupant restraint controller (ORC) module to disable certain passive safety features. Deactivated passive safety features can increase the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will update the ORC module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V510000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Fiat&model=500X&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A seat belt buckle switch sensor may be improperly connected, preventing the front seat air bag from deploying as intended. A front seat air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Fiat 500X provides distinctive styling and practical all-wheel-drive capability, though buyers should verify proper transmission operation and fuel system function during a test drive. Conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection remains the best way to catch any lingering mechanical or electrical quirks before finalizing a deal.
