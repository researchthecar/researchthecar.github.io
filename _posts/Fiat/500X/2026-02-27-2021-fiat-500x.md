# 2021 Fiat 500X Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Fiat 500X is a stylish subcompact crossover that shares its platform and mechanical foundations with the Jeep Renegade, featuring standard all-wheel drive across the lineup. While early model years faced various growing pains, the 2021 model benefits from mature powertrain offerings and a relatively quiet complaint profile. Used-car buyers looking at this Italian-styled crossover will find standard turbocharged performance, but should still inspect electronic accessories and mechanical components carefully during a pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 1.3L Turbocharged MultiAir 4-Cylinder

The 1.3-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 177 horsepower and 210 lb-ft of torque, providing spirited acceleration for daily driving. It pairs exclusively with an all-wheel-drive system and generally represents a modern, refined powerplant for the 500X lineup.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission distributes power to all four wheels. While earlier iterations of this ZF-sourced unit received mixed reviews regarding shift smoothness, later calibration updates have largely stabilized its highway and city performance.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V510000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Fiat&model=500X&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A seat belt buckle switch sensor may be improperly connected, preventing the front seat air bag from deploying as intended. A front seat air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Fiat 500X enters the used market with a clean slate of reported owner complaints regarding its core powertrain, making it a more dependable prospect than earlier editions. Prospective buyers should still prioritize a thorough inspection of the electrical systems and standard pre-purchase checks to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
