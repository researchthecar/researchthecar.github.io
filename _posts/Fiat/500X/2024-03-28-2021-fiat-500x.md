**Introduction:**

The 2021 Fiat 500X is a subcompact crossover SUV available with two engine and transmission options:

* 1.3L MultiAir Turbo I-4: 177 horsepower, 210 lb-ft of torque
    * 9-speed automatic transmission
* 1.0L GSE T3 Turbo I-3: 120 horsepower, 148 lb-ft of torque
    * 6-speed manual transmission

While the 500X is praised for its stylish design, fuel efficiency, and maneuverability, it has received criticism for its cramped rear seating, stiff ride, and limited cargo space.

**Engine**

**1.3L MultiAir Turbo I-4**

* **Engine Stalling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Random engine stalling while driving, particularly at low speeds.
    * Solution: Software update to reprogram the engine control module (ECM).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0-$100
    * Recalls: 21V362000

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: High oil consumption leading to the need for frequent oil top-offs.
    * Solution: Replace defective piston rings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500-$2,000
    * Recalls: 20V604000

**1.0L GSE T3 Turbo I-3**

* No Issues Found

**Transmission**

**9-speed Automatic**

* **Delayed or Rough Shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slow or jerky gear changes, especially during acceleration or deceleration.
    * Solution: Update transmission software or replace faulty solenoids.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$500
    * Recalls: 21V361000

**6-speed Manual**

* No Issues Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise or clunking from the rear suspension, particularly over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace worn sway bar links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharging rapidly, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace defective battery or alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* No Issues Found

**Interior**

* **Loose Dashboard Trim**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard trim pieces becoming dislodged or rattling.
    * Solution: Reattach or replace trim pieces.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Other**

* **Brake Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature brake pad wear or excessive brake noise.
    * Solution: Replace worn brake pads or rotors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2021 Fiat 500X is a stylish and fuel-efficient SUV, it has experienced some issues with its engine, transmission, and suspension. The severity of these issues varies, and some, like the engine stalling and transmission delay, have been addressed through software updates. Overall, the cost of repairs can range from minor to significant, depending on the issue.
