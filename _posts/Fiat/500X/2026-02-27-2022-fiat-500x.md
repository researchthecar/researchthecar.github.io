# 2022 Fiat 500X Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Fiat 500X is a stylish subcompact crossover that brings Italian flair and standard all-wheel drive to the segment. For used car shoppers, it offers distinctive styling and nimble handling, but buyers should approach with a mindset toward careful inspection, as owner feedback highlights occasional mechanical and electrical quirks.

---

## Engine

### 1.3L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 1.3-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces around 177 horsepower and 210 lb-ft of torque, providing lively pep for daily commutes. While generally modern and efficient, owners should monitor throttle response and powertrain software behavior during test drives.

**Unexpected RPM Surge**
- **Symptoms:** When cresting hills at cruising speeds, the engine may suddenly increase RPMs and cause the vehicle to accelerate unprompted, catching the driver off guard.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the throttle body, powertrain control module (PCM), and transmission shift logic for software anomalies or sensor calibration issues.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 9-speed automatic transmission pairs exclusively with the standard all-wheel-drive system across the lineup. Buyers should ensure smooth gear changes across all speeds during a thorough test drive.

## Safety

### Airbags and Structure

Safety equipment includes a comprehensive suite of modern airbags and structural crumple zones designed to protect occupants in a collision.

**Unspecified Structural or Airbag Concern**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have filed formal documentation noting miscellaneous concerns regarding vehicle structural integrity or airbag deployment systems.
- **Solution:** Verify that all safety recalls have been addressed and have a professional inspect the vehicle for any past collision damage or warning lights.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V510000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Fiat&model=500X&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A seat belt buckle switch sensor may be improperly connected, preventing the front seat air bag from deploying as intended. A front seat air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Fiat 500X is an appealing choice for buyers seeking distinctive European styling and standard all-wheel drive in a small footprint. Prospective owners should perform a detailed inspection focusing on powertrain behavior, ensure all manufacturer safety recalls are complete, and budget for a thorough pre-purchase evaluation.
