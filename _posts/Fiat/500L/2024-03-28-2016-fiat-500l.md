**Introduction:**

The 2016 Fiat 500L is a subcompact crossover SUV offered with a choice of two engine and transmission combinations: a 1.4-liter turbocharged four-cylinder with a 6-speed manual or 6-speed dual-clutch transmission, and a 2.4-liter four-cylinder with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The 1.4-liter engine produces 160 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.4-liter engine generates 180 horsepower and 175 lb-ft of torque. The 500L has received praise for its stylish design, spacious interior, and affordable price point. However, some owners have reported issues with various components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**1.4-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder with 6-Speed Manual Transmission**

**No Issues Found**

**1.4-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder with 6-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**

**Issue 1:** Transmission shuddering during shifts
Description/Symptoms: The transmission shudders or vibrates when shifting gears, especially during low-speed maneuvers.
Solution: Replace the transmission clutch pack assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 19V479000

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Issue 1:** Failing rearview camera display
Description/Symptoms: The rearview camera display freezes, flickers, or becomes distorted.
Solution: Replace the rearview camera module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 19V323000

**Issue 2:** Seat heater malfunction
Description/Symptoms: The seat heater does not work or only partially works.
Solution: Replace the seat heater element.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2016 Fiat 500L has received mixed feedback from owners. While it offers a unique design and spacious interior, it has also been plagued by some reliability issues. The transmission shuddering issue with the 1.4-liter turbocharged engine and dual-clutch transmission is a major concern, as it can affect the drivability of the vehicle. Other issues, such as the failing rearview camera display and seat heater malfunction, are less severe but can still be costly to repair. Potential buyers should carefully consider the potential issues before purchasing this vehicle.
