# 2017 Fiat 500L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Fiat 500L, buyers will find a quirky, spacious Italian hatchback offering distinct styling and a tall greenhouse. While powertrain options are generally straightforward, owners have noted occasional drivability hiccups. Reviewing maintenance records and performing a thorough test drive can help ensure the vehicle has been well-maintained.

---

## Engine

### 1.4L Turbocharged MultiAir Inline-4

The 1.4-liter turbocharged MultiAir inline-4 engine produces approximately 160 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. It provides punchy performance for daily driving, though proper oil maintenance is critical for the MultiAir valve actuation system.

**Loss of Power and Acceleration**
- **Symptoms:** While driving, the vehicle may suddenly fail to accelerate, accompanied by a loss of power and an illuminated check engine light. Cycling the ignition may temporarily clear the issue.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fault codes stored in the Powertrain Control Module (PCM) and inspect throttle body, turbocharger, and sensor connections.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A conventional 6-speed automatic transmission handles power delivery to the front wheels. It offers smoother shifting characteristics compared to earlier automated manual setups.

### 6-Speed Euro TwinPleasure Dual-Dry-Clutch Automatic

An available 6-speed dual-dry-clutch transmission offers direct power transfer, though prospective buyers should inspect shift quality thoroughly during a test drive.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

A 6-speed manual transmission is paired with the turbocharged engine on select trims for a more engaging driving experience, requiring standard clutch wear inspections.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Fiat 500L presents unique European styling and interior space, but buyers should approach with a standard level of caution typical for used European compact cars. Verifying proper powertrain operation during a pre-purchase inspection is strongly recommended.
